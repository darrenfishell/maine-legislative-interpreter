23SUMMARY
24 This bill amends the site location of development laws administered by the Department 
25 of Environmental Protection.  It specifies the time in which the department must process 
26 minor revisions to permits.  It clarifies the exemption for new construction at or 
27 modification of an existing licensed development.  It adds an exemption for rerouting of 
28 storm water if certain criteria are met.24
