21SUMMARY
22 This bill updates references to the United States Internal Revenue Code of 1986 
23 contained in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 36 to refer to the United States Internal 
24 Revenue Code of 1986, as amended through December 31, 2021, for tax years beginning 
25 on or after January 1, 2021 and for any prior tax year as specifically provided by the United
