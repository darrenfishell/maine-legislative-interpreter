16SUMMARY
17 This bill requires the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to ensure 
18 that its rules and guidelines neither preclude business owners from allowing nor require 
19 business owners to allow consumers to supply their own containers for the bulk purchase 
20 of certain food and nonfood items and to provide technical assistance and education 
21 regarding best practices.  The bill requires the department to report on its progress by 
22 February 1, 2022 to the Joint Standing Committee on Innovation, Development, Economic 
23 Advancement and Business.
