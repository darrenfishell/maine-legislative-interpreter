23SUMMARY
24 This bill adds to the membership of the Sexual Assault Forensic Examiner Advisory 
25 Board an additional sexual assault nurse examiner and one representative of law 
26 enforcement.  The bill also clarifies that one member is a representative from a sexual 
27 assault support center and changes the member representing the State Board of Nursing to 
28 a member representing a school of nursing.
