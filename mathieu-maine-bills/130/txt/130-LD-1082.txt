18SUMMARY
19 This bill increases the total enrollment at virtual public charter schools authorized by 
20 the Maine Charter School Commission from 1,000 at all virtual public charter schools to 
21 1,500 at a virtual public charter school.  It allows a virtual public charter school to expand 
22 to serve grade levels beyond the grade levels under the school's initial or renewed charter 
23 contract to include grade levels 5 and 6.  It exempts children who are enrolled in or attend 
24 a virtual public charter school from immunization requirements.  It also allows the Maine 
25 Charter School Commission to adopt safety protocols for physical attendance by such 
26 children at any class or school program.19
