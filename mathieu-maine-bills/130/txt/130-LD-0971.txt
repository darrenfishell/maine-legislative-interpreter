9Sec. 2.  Notification of transmission and distribution utility sale; Public 
10Utilities Commission inquiry.  The Public Utilities Commission shall open an inquiry 
11 to examine and make recommendations regarding a process to provide timely notification 
12 to all entities authorized to own a transmission and distribution plant in the State of any 
13 proposed sale of an investor-owned transmission and distribution utility and to provide the 
14 opportunity for the notified entities to bid on the sale. The commission shall submit a report 
15 on the results of this inquiry to the Joint Standing Committee on Energy, Utilities and 
16 Technology by February 1, 2022. The Joint Standing Committee on Energy, Utilities and 
17 Technology may, after receipt of the report under this section, submit legislation related to 
18 the report to the Second Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.
19SUMMARY
20 This bill requires the Public Utilities Commission to consider the potential benefits of 
21 providing more local control over electricity service and whether local control is feasible 
22 before issuing an order of authorization for the sale or merger of an investor-owned 
23 transmission and distribution utility. It allows the commission to adopt routine technical 
24 rules to implement this proposed change.
25 This bill also directs the commission to open an inquiry to examine and make 
26 recommendations regarding a process to provide timely notification to entities authorized 
27 to own a transmission and distribution plant in the State of any proposed sale of an investor-
28 owned transmission and distribution utility and to provide the opportunity for the notified 
29 entities to bid on the sale. It requires the commission to submit the results of this inquiry to 
30 the Joint Standing Committee on Energy, Utilities and Technology by February 1, 2022.20
