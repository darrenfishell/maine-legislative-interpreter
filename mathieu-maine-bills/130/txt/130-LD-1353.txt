10SUMMARY
11 This bill requires a hospital to post on its publicly accessible website the cost to a 
12 patient of procedures, services, medications and equipment provided by that hospital.  It 
13 requires a hospital to provide notice to a patient of this posting and provide these costs to a 
14 patient upon the request of that patient.  A violation of the provisions of the bill is a Class 
15 E crime for which a fine of $1,000 per day may be adjudged.11
