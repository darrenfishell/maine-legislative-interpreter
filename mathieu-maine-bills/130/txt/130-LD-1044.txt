20SUMMARY
21 The bill provides to a person residing in a correctional or detention facility the right to 
22 have that person's consistently held gender identity respected and acknowledged and to 
23 have staff and representatives of the correctional or detention facility use the pronouns, 
24 titles and names identified by the person.21
