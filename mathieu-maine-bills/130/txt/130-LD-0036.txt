17SUMMARY
18 This bill amends the definition of "timber harvesting" to clarify that timber harvesting 
19 means any cutting or removal of trees or forest products that when cut or removed are 
20 transported to a roundwood processing operation.  It also adds bark to the definition of 
21 "forest products" and log yards and bark processors to the definition of "roundwood 
22 processing operation" in the laws governing forest landowner and wood processor 
23 reporting requirements.
