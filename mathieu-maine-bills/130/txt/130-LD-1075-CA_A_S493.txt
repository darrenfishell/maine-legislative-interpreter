14 §598-C. Process for determination of reduction or substantially altered use of 
15 designated land 
16 The Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Bureau of Parks and Lands 
17 shall adopt rules to establish an objective evaluation process for determining if a proposed 
18 activity on land designated under this chapter and under the jurisdiction of the bureau would 
19 cause the land to be reduced or the uses of the land to be substantially altered. fu adopting 
20 the rules, the bureau shall observe the requirements relating to designated lands in the 
21 Constitution of Maine, Article IX, Section 23 and ensure proper exercise of the bureau's 
22 public trust responsibility. These rules must also include provisions for public notice and 
23 comment before authorizing any such activity and for determining the appropriate 
24 instrument to be used to authorize that activity, including but not limited to whether an 
25 easement, lease, license or other instrument should be used. Rules adopted pursuant to this 
26 section are major substantive rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.' 
27 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section 
28 number to read consecutively.
