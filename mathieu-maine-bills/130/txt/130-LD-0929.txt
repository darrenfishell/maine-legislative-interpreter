28SUMMARY
29 This bill repeals the provision of law that directs the Commission on Governmental 
30 Ethics and Election Practices to adjust the amount of distributions made from the Maine 
31 Clean Election Fund to candidates certified under the Maine Clean Election Act based on 
32 the Consumer Price Index and requires the commission to make all future distributions 
33 under the Act in accordance with the amounts established in Initiated Bill 2015, chapter 1.29
