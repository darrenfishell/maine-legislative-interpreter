Page 1 - 130LR2121(01)1Sec. 1.  Granite Hill Road property in Hallowell; transfer authorized.  
2Resolved: That, in accordance with the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 37-B, section 264, 
3 the Adjutant General may sell the 2-acre parcel of land located off Granite Hill Road in 
4 Hallowell and identified as Lot 019-023 on the Hallowell tax maps to the City of Hallowell 
5 by means of a quitclaim deed, as long as the City of Hallowell agrees to indemnify and 
6 hold harmless the State from all claims, including any environmental clean-up costs that 
7 may arise in connection with the land.
8SUMMARY
9 This resolve authorizes the Maine National Guard to sell to the City of Hallowell a 
10 2-acre parcel located off Granite Hill Road in Hallowell.9
