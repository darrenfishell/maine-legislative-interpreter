9Sec. 2.  Department of Health and Human Services to seek available funds 
10for uninsured patients.  Resolved: That the Department of Health and Human Services 
11 shall seek and apply for available federal funds or funds from any other sources to pay the 
12 costs of providing inpatient psychiatric care to uninsured patients who are discharged from 
13 acute care hospitals.
14Sec. 3.  Report.  Resolved: That, no later than December 1, 2021, the Department 
15 of Health and Human Services shall submit a report to the Joint Standing Committee on 
16 Health and Human Services regarding the progress the department has made in developing 
17 a reimbursement rate to provide inpatient psychiatric care under its rule Chapter 101: 
18 MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 45.03.  The joint standing committee is 
19 authorized to report out legislation related to the report to the Second Regular Session of 
20 the 130th Legislature.
21SUMMARY
22 This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rule 
23 Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 45.03 to provide 
24 reimbursement for patients discharged from Southern Maine Health Care's psychiatric 
25 inpatient unit in the amount of $10,166 per distinct discharge; the amended rule must be 
26 submitted to the United States Department of Health and Human Services, Centers for 
27 Medicare and Medicaid Services in time for the increased rate of reimbursement to apply 
28 from January 1, 2021.
29 The resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to seek and apply 
30 for federal funds and funds from any other sources to pay the costs of providing psychiatric 
31 services to uninsured patients.  It also directs the department to submit a report to the Joint 
32 Standing Committee on Health and Human Services regarding the progress the department 
33 has made in developing a reimbursement rate to provide inpatient psychiatric care under 
34 its rule Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 45.03.  The 
35 committee is authorized to report out legislation related to the report to the Second Regular 
36 Session of the 130th Legislature.22
