12SUMMARY
13 Public Law 2019, chapter 411 requires the Department of Health and Human Services 
14 to issue a request for proposals, or RFP, for technology-supported assessments of 
15 foreseeable harm for the purposes of involuntary hospitalization. The RFP has yet to be 
16 issued. This resolve directs the department to move forward with contracts for assessments 
17 and provides that until the contracts are issued, qualified medical practitioners employed 
18 by the State's psychiatric hospitals shall perform the assessments.13
