32SUMMARY
33 This bill changes the name of the Local Produce Fund within the Department of 
34 Education to the Local Foods Fund and provides that the fund's maximum state match for 
35 the purchase of produce or minimally processed foods is $7,500 per school administrative 
36 unit in fiscal year 2021-22 and subsequent years or $10,000 per school administrative unit 
37 if the school administrative unit sends a food service employee to local foods training 
38 administered by the Department of Education.  It also provides that the food the fund may 
39 be used to help purchase may include value-added dairy and protein and that food may be 
40 purchased from local food processors and food service distributors.33
