23 lease or tenancy at will agreement for the residential building shall provide written notice

25SUMMARY
26 This bill requires landlords, beginning in 2022, to test residential buildings yearly for 
27 radon. If the test reveals a level of radon of 4.0 picocuries per liter of air or above, the 
28 landlord must mitigate within 6 months the level of radon in the residential building until 
29 it is reduced to a level below 4.0 picocuries per liter of air.26
