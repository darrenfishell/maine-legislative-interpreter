Page 1 - 130LR0628(01)1Sec. 1.  Department of Health and Human Services to increase MaineCare 
2reimbursement rates.  Resolved:  That, no later than October 1, 2021, the Department 
3 of Health and Human Services shall amend its rule Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits 
4 Manual, Chapter III, Section 17, Allowances for Community Support Services, and Section 
5 65, Behavioral Health Services, to increase reimbursement rates for daily living support 
6 services and any home and community-based services provided to children and adults by 
7 behavioral health professionals by 20%. The rule must specify that the increase in 
8 reimbursement rates must be applied to wages and benefits for employees who provide 
9 direct care services and not to administrators or managers.  Rules adopted pursuant to this 
10 section are routine technical rules as defined in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 
11 375, subchapter 2-A.
12Sec. 2.  Increase to employee wages. Resolved: That to qualify for the 
13 reimbursement rate increase pursuant to section 1, an agency providing services must 
14 demonstrate, to the satisfaction of the Department of Health and Human Services, that an 
15 increase in wages and benefits has been granted to employees providing direct care services 
16 that equals the amount of the projected increase in reimbursement to be received. The 
17 increase in wages and benefits must be paid or granted retroactively from the date the 
18 department begins reimbursing at the increased rates.
19SUMMARY
20 This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rule 
21 Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Sections 17 and 65 to increase 
22 reimbursement rates for daily living support services and any home and community-based 
23 services provided to children and adults by behavioral health professionals by 20%.  It 
24 requires that the increase in reimbursement rates must be applied to wages and benefits for 
25 employees who provide direct care services and not to administrators or managers.
