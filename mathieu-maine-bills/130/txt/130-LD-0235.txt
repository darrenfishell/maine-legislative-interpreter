5ยง1500-N.  Cancellation of furniture sales before delivery
61. Cancellation period required.  A person who operates a business in this State that 
7 offers to sell and deliver furniture shall offer, and disclose at the time of sale, a cancellation 
8 policy that includes but is not limited to:
9 A.  A reasonable time frame for cancellation;
10 B.  Reasonable costs or fees associated with cancellation, such as shipping costs or 
11 restocking fees; and
12 C.  Reasonable exceptions to the cancellation policy, including but not limited to 
13 custom orders or clearance items.
14 The seller must promptly refund any payments or deposits made prior to delivery, less 
15 reasonable costs or fees associated with cancellation.
162.  Unfair trade practice.  A violation of this section constitutes an unfair or deceptive 
17 act or practice in violation of Title 5, chapter 10.
18SUMMARY
19 This bill requires that any seller of furniture in Maine that offers delivery of the 
20 furniture allow the buyer to cancel an order according to a set policy disclosed to the buyer 
21 at the time of sale.  If the furniture seller fails to do so, it is a violation of the Unfair Trade 
22 Practices Act.
