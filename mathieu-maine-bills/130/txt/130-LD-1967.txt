12Sec. 2.  P&SL 1929, c. 33, §9, last ¶, as amended by P&SL 1995, c. 16, §1, is 
13 further amended by enacting before the last sentence a new sentence to read:
14 If a trustee misses more than 2 regular meetings in a calendar year, the treasurer may deduct 
15 $50 per additional missed meeting from the trustee's compensation.
16SUMMARY
17 This bill authorizes the Gray Water District to build infrastructure on Intervale Road 
18 in the Town of New Gloucester and build a water main into and offer service in the Town 
19 of North Yarmouth along Gray Road.  It also allows the district treasurer to deduct money 
20 from a trustee's total compensation for missed meetings.17
