3ยง3405.  Prohibition on offshore wind energy development projects
4 Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, a department or agency of 
5 the State or a political subdivision thereof may not permit, approve or otherwise authorize 
6 an offshore wind energy development project.
7 For the purposes of this section, "offshore wind energy development project" includes 
8 a community-based offshore wind energy project, as that term is defined in Title 12, section 
9 682, subsection 19; a deep-water offshore wind energy pilot project as described in Public 
10 Law 2009, chapter 615, Part A, section 6, as amended by Public Law 2013, chapter 369, 
11 Part H, sections 1 and 2 and chapter 378, sections 4 to 6; an offshore wind energy 
12 demonstration project, as that term is defined in Title 38, section 480-HH, subsection 1, 
13 paragraph H; and an offshore wind power project, as that term is defined in Title 38, section 
14 480-B, subsection 6-A.

31SUMMARY
32 This bill prohibits any department or agency of the State or any political subdivision 
33 of the State from permitting, approving or otherwise authorizing an offshore wind energy 
34 development project.  The bill also requires the Department of Agriculture, Conservation 
35 and Forestry, Maine Land Use Planning Commission, the Public Utilities Commission and 
36 the Department of Environmental Protection each to submit by December 1, 2021 
37 legislation necessary to align those provisions of law under their respective jurisdictions 
38 with the prohibition on offshore wind energy development projects.
39 Under the bill, the term "offshore wind energy development project" includes 
40 community-based offshore wind energy projects, deep-water offshore wind energy pilot 
41 projects, offshore wind energy demonstration projects and offshore wind power projects, 
42 which are all categories of projects currently authorized by law.
