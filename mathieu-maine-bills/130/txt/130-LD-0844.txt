3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill would require the examination of the reasons individuals undertake actions 
6 that lead to incarceration and recidivism due to a violation of conditions of release or 
7 probation and propose changes, based on the findings of that examination, to reduce 
8 incarceration and recidivism due to such a violation.
