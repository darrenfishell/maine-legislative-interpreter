4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill, as emergency legislation, proposes to enact measures to provide additional 
6 resources to the office of the child welfare services ombudsman to enhance the capacity of 
7 that office to improve child welfare practices through both the review of individual cases 
8 and the provision of information on the rights and responsibilities of families, service 
9 providers and other participants in the child welfare system.4
