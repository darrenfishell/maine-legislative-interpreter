3ยง458.  Pretextual traffic stops
4 A law enforcement officer as defined in Title 17-A, section 2, subsection 17 may not 
5 stop a motor vehicle an occupant of which the law enforcement officer suspects is engaging 
6 in criminal activity for a violation under Title 29-A, chapter 5, 15, 17 or 19 not related to 
7 the suspected criminal activity.  Evidence of criminal activity obtained pursuant to a motor 
8 vehicle stop in violation of this section may not be used in any criminal proceeding.
9SUMMARY
10 This bill prohibits a law enforcement officer from stopping a motor vehicle an occupant 
11 of which the law enforcement officer suspects is engaging in criminal activity for a motor 
12 vehicle violation not related to that criminal activity.  The bill also provides that evidence 
13 obtained in a traffic stop in violation of this provision may not be used in any criminal 
14 proceeding.
