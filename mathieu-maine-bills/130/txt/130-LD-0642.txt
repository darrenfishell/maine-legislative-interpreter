3ยง1653-B.  Parental rights and responsibilities; behavioral health services
4 This section governs the provision of behavioral health services to a minor child when 
5 one parent opposes treatment.  Notwithstanding section 1651, if the parents of a minor child 
6 are unable to agree on the provision of behavioral health services to the child, the parents 
7 of the minor child shall participate in mediation with a 3rd party in a manner that is timely 
8 to the minor child's situation.  For purposes of this section, "behavioral health services" 
9 means all treatment or other services to support a child's mental health, including substance 
10 use disorder treatment.
11 This section does not apply to parents who have a court order establishing parental 
12 rights and responsibilities under section 1653, subsection 2 if that order allocates decision 
13 making among the parents regarding the provision of behavioral health services to the child 
14 or otherwise addresses parental decision making related to behavioral health services.
15SUMMARY
16 This bill provides that in the event one parent of a minor child opposes providing 
17 behavioral health services to the child, the parents are required to enter mediation with a 
18 3rd party in a manner that is timely to the minor child's situation.16
