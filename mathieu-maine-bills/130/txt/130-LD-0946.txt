Page 1 - 130LR1769(01)1Sec. 1.  Nonfederal sponsor with the United States Army Corps of 
2Engineers for the shore damage mitigation project in Saco.  Resolved:  That the 
3 Governor shall take all actions necessary to cause the State to become the nonfederal 
4 sponsor with the United States Army Corps of Engineers under the federal River and 
5 Harbor Act of 1968, Section 111, as amended, 33 United States Code, Section 426i, for the 
6 shore damage mitigation project for the Saco River and Camp Ellis Beach in Saco pursuant 
7 to the report dated July 2017 and known as Alternative 6.  The Governor shall direct an 
8 appropriate agency to work with the City of Saco and the United States Army Corps of 
9 Engineers to provide a letter of intent that is satisfactory to the Federal Government for 
10 project authorization.  The Governor shall take all steps necessary for the State to enter into 
11 a project partnership agreement with the United States Army Corps of Engineers for the 
12 construction of the project under this section.
13SUMMARY
14 This bill requires the Governor to take all steps necessary for the State to be the 
15 nonfederal sponsor with the United States Army Corps of Engineers for the shore damage 
16 mitigation project for the Saco River and Camp Ellis Beach in Saco.14
