25 This amendment allows the Department of Health and Human Services, Maine Center 
26 for Disease Control and Prevention to establish agreements with state, regional and national 
27 amyotrophic lateral sclerosis registries for bidirectional data exchange, in a manner 
28 consistent with applicable state and federal confidentiality laws and policies.
