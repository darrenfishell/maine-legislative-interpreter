8Sec. 2.  Department of Health and Human Services to establish emergency 
9rate review process.  As part of the efforts of the Department of Health and Human 
10 Services to establish a rate-setting procedure, the department shall include a process for 
11 emergency reimbursement rate review to be used at times of extraordinary circumstances.  
12 The department shall determine what constitutes extraordinary circumstances, except that 
13 they must include state and municipal requirements for increased minimum wages and 
14 other increases to wages that affect MaineCare reimbursement rates and widespread 
15 outbreaks of disease that require additional resources to offer services.  The process must 
16 include a method for individual providers or their representative associations to request an 
17 emergency review, deadlines that ensure a timely response from the department to requests 
18 and requirements for clear communications on acceptance or decline of requests for review.
19SUMMARY
20 This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to establish a process 
21 for emergency reimbursement rate review to be used at times of extraordinary 
22 circumstances.  This process must be established as part of the current rate review process 
23 undertaken by the department.20
