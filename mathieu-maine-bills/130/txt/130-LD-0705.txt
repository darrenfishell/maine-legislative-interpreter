2 Department of Education shall amend its rule Chapter 125:  Basic Approval Standards:  
3 Public Schools and School Administrative Units to require standards governing air quality 
4 and ventilation for all public schools, including schools with mechanical and 
5 nonmechanical ventilation systems.  These standards must apply to all public schools and 
6 must apply no earlier than July 1, 2022 and no later than July 1, 2026.  The rules adopted

9 adopted rules for final adoption to the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural 
10 Affairs by January 4, 2022.
11SUMMARY
12 This resolve directs the Department of Education to amend its rule Chapter 125:  Basic 
13 Approval Standards:  Public Schools and School Administrative Units to require standards 
14 governing air quality and ventilation for all schools, including schools with mechanical and 
15 nonmechanical ventilation systems.  It requires these standards to apply to all public 
16 schools and to apply no earlier than July 1, 2022 and no later than July 1, 2026.  It makes 
17 the rules adopted major substantive rules pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, 
18 chapter 375, subchapter 2-A and requires the department to present the provisionally 
19 adopted rules for final adoption to the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural 
20 Affairs by January 4, 2022.12
