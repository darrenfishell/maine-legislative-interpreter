4§6601-A.  Free or reduced-price school meals; Internet-based school meal 
5applications
6 The department shall contract for the development and implementation of an Internet-
7 based application for free or reduced-price meals under the National School Lunch 
8 Program under 7 Code of Federal Regulations, Part 210 and the School Breakfast Program 
9 under 7 Code of Federal Regulations, Part 220.  The department shall make available to 
10 public schools the Internet‑based application for free or reduced-price meals developed 
11 under this section on the department's publicly accessible website.  The department shall 
12 endeavor to provide the Internet-based application in various languages reflecting the 
13 student population of public schools in the State.  A public school may make the Internet-
14 based application available for school meal applications on the public school's publicly 
15 accessible website.  If a public school implements the Internet-based application process 
16 under this section, the  All public  school schools shall continue to distribute paper 
17 applications for school meals to all students.  A public school implementing the 
18 Internet‑based application is solely responsible for processing that school's online 
19 applications.  Data submitted through the Internet-based application may not be visible to 
20 the department and must be transmitted directly to the applicable public school.
21SUMMARY
22 This bill requires the Internet-based application for free or reduced-price meals 
23 developed for the Department of Education to be made available on the department's 
24 publicly accessible website.  It also allows a public school to make the application available 
25 on its publicly accessible website.  It requires the department to endeavor to provide the 
26 application in various languages reflecting the student populations of public schools in the 
27 State.  It requires all public schools to continue to distribute the paper application.    It also 
28 requires that data submitted through the Internet-based application not be visible to the 
29 department and to be transmitted directly to the applicable public school.22
