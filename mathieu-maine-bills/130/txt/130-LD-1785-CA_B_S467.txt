§11682. Maine Public Safety Workers Loan Repayment Program 
1. Establishment. The Maine Public Safety Workers Loan Repayment Program is 
established within the authority for the purpose of attracting and retaining qualified public 
safety workers. The authority shall administer the program. 
2. Eligibility. To be considered eligible for loan repayment pursuant to subsection 4, 
an applicant must, at a minimum: 
A. Be a cun-ent resident of the State or become a resident of the State within 6 months 
of being selected as a recipient under the program; 
B. Be cun-ently employed as a public safety worker within the State or become 
employed as a public safety worker within the State within 6 months of being selected 
as a recipient under the program; and 
C. Possess an outstanding student loan. 
3. Application. An application to the program must be made directly to the authority 
at a time and in a format to be determined by the authority. 
4. Maximum loan repayment. The maximum loan repayment amount available to a 
recipient under the program is $3,000 per year for a maximum of 3 years. 
§11683. Maine Public Safety Workers Loan Repayment Program Fund 
The Maine Public Safety Workers Loan Repayment Program Fund is established 
within the authority as a nonlapsing, interest-earning, revolving fund to cany out the 
purposes of this chapter. The authority may receive, invest and expend on behalf of the 
fund money from gifts, grants, bequests and donations in addition to money appropriated 
or allocated by the State. Money in the fund must be used for the designated purposes of 
the fund and for the payment of administrative costs incun-ed by the authority for the 
operation of the program. Any unexpended balance in the fund can-ies forward for 
continued use under this chapter. 
§11684. Rules 
The authority shall establish rules necessary to implement this chapter. Rules adopted 
pursuant to this section are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, 
subchapter 2-A.

This bill includes a one-time General Fund appropriation of $2,000,000 in fiscal year 2022-23 to a newly established 
Maine Public Safety Workers Loan Repayment Program Fund within the Finance Authority of Maine (FAME) to 
provide student loan repayment ofup to $3,000 per year for a maximum of 3 years to eligible public safety workers in 
the State. This bill authorizes FAME to use monies in the fund to pay for the costs to administer the program. 
Therefore, no additional General Fund appropriations are required to support the operation of the program.
