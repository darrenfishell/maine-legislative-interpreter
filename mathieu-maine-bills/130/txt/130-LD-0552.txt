2013.  Changes to individualized education program.  Require that changes to the 
21 individualized education program be made by consensus of the individualized education

23 individualized education program, the individualized education program in effect at the 
24 time of the proposed change remains in effect.  If a team member seeks to pursue a change

26 requesting mediation under 7206-C, as appropriate, or initiating other available dispute

2914.  Special education services providers; presence at team meeting.  Require an 
30 individualized education program team to allow an individual who provides special 
31 education services to a child with a disability and has regular, direct contact with that child 
32 to attend and participate in an individualized education program team meeting concerning

35Sec. 5.  Rulemaking.  No later than January 1, 2022, the Commissioner of Education 
36 shall submit to the Legislature proposed changes to Department of Education rule Chapter 
37 101, Maine Unified Special Education Regulation Birth to Age Twenty, a major 
38 substantive rule adopted pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20-A, section 7005, 
39 to bring the rule into conformance with this Act.
40SUMMARY
41 This bill requires that changes to the individualized education program for a child with 
42 a disability be made by consensus of the individualized education program team.  The bill 41
