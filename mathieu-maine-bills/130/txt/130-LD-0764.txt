21SUMMARY
22 This bill authorizes the Department of Defense, Veterans and Emergency Management, 
23 Maine Emergency Management Agency to requisition food supplies for emergency use or 
24 special duty assignments and corrects a reference to the Department of Agriculture, 
25 Conservation and Forestry, Bureau of Forestry.
