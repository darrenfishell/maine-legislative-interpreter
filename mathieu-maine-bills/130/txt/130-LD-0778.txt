14SUMMARY
15 This bill amends outdated language regarding electronic submission of reports of 
16 suspected abuse or neglect to the Department of Health and Human Services by removing 
17 the language regarding secure e-mail and fax transmissions.  Instead, electronic reports are 
18 submitted through a portal provided by the department linked to the department's 
19 comprehensive child welfare information system.  The bill also expands the types of 
20 mandated reporters who may report electronically to include school personnel.
