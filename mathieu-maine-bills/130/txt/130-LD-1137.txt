20Sec. 1.  37-B MRSA §742, sub-§1, ¶E is enacted to read:
21 E.  If an order or rule issued by the Governor, pursuant to the powers granted in

Page 2 - 130LR1432(01)1SUMMARY
2 This bill requires the Governor to convene the Legislature if a declared state of 
3 emergency is to be in effect for longer than 90 days.  If the Legislature does not, by a 2/3 
4 vote in each House, vote to extend the state of emergency, the Governor may not declare a 
5 similar, subsequent state of emergency.
6 The bill also requires the Governor to convene the Legislature if, after a state of 
7 emergency is declared, an order or rule issued by the Governor is to be in effect for longer 
8 than 90 days.  The Legislature must, by a 2/3 vote in each House, vote to extend the 
9 Governor's authority to extend that order or rule.
