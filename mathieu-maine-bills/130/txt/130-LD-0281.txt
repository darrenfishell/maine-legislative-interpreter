18SUMMARY
19 This bill adds a provision to the school funding formula to provide an additional 
20 allocation of state funding to school administrative units to address student academic 
21 achievement gaps based on student race or economic status.  It requires the Commissioner 
22 of Education to specify data and metrics to calculate student academic achievement gaps, 
23 to establish eligibility criteria for the adjustment and to determine the adjustment amount 
24 or method for computing the amount.  It also requires eligible school administrative units 
25 to submit a plan to address academic achievement gaps prior to receipt of the adjustment 
26 each year. It authorizes the commissioner to disburse up to $10,000,000 per fiscal year to 
27 provide the adjustments.
