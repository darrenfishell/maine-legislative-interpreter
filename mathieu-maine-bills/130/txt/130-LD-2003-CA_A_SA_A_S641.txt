Required Activity Unit Affected 
Requires municipalities to 1) implement ordinances with respect to allowing Municipality 
accessory dwelling units on the same lot as a single-family dwelling unit in any area 
in which housing is permitted and 2) amend ordinances to comply with certain 
conditions with respect to the number of dwelling units allowed in residential areas.

This amendment eliminates the ongoing General Fund appropriations in the bill totaling $3,000,000 in fiscal year 
2022-23 which included $100,000 to reimburse municipalities for mandated costs. It also eliminates the provisions 
related to the Housing Opportunity Program within the Department of Economic and Community Development 
included in the bill that the funding supported. The Housing Opportunity Program and the related funding, including 
the funding for the mandated costs in this bill, were included in LD 1995, the supplemental budget that was enacted 
on April 19, 2022.
