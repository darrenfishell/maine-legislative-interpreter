Page 2 - 130LR0268(01)1SUMMARY
2 This bill provides waivers of the applicable qualifying examinations for the following 
3 applicants for professional teacher certificates or endorsements:
4 1.  For applicants seeking a professional teacher certificate who have satisfactorily 
5 completed a bachelor's degree program from a regionally accredited college or university 
6 with an overall grade point average of 3.0 or higher, on a scale of 4.0, or its equivalent;
7 2. For applicants seeking endorsements who have satisfactorily completed a bachelor's 
8 degree program from a regionally accredited college or university with an overall grade 
9 point average of 3.0 or higher, on a scale of 4.0, or its equivalent in the content area of the 
10 endorsement being sought; and
11 3. For applicants seeking a professional teacher certificate who are required to take the 
12 pedagogical knowledge and skills assessment and principles of learning and teaching 
13 assessment by Department of Education rule but who have successfully completed 2 full 
14 years of teaching under a conditional certificate. 
15 This bill also provides that rules adopted to implement the qualifying examination 
16 waivers are not subject to the law that provides that an amendment to the teacher 
17 certification rules that revises the qualifications for a credential for a professional teacher 
18 certificate does not apply to certain persons; this change ensures that the qualifying 
19 examination waivers are available to all eligible applicants.2
