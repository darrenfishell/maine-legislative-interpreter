SUMMARY 
This amendment makes the following changes to the Advisory Panel To Better 
Understand and Make Recommendations Regarding the Implications of Genome-editing 
Technology for the Citizens of the State established in the resolve. 
1. It requires members appointed to the panel to be Maine residents. 
2. It requires the legislative members of the panel who are members of the 2nd largest 
party to be appointed by the minority leaders rather than the President of the Senate and 
the Speaker of the House of Representatives. 
3. It changes the date the report is to be submitted from December 31, 2023 to 
December 6, 2023 to comply with the Joint Rules. 
4. It specifies that the report is submitted to the joint standing committee of the 
Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human services matters and the committee 
· is authorized to report out legislation. 
5. It clarifies that the legislative appointments must be current members of the 
Legislature. 
The amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section.
