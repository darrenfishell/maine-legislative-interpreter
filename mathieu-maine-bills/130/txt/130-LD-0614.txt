2Resolved: That the Department of Health and Human Services shall amend its rules in 
3 Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 67, Principles of 
4 Reimbursement for Nursing Facilities to provide that Maine Veterans' Homes nursing 
5 facilities are reimbursed for services provided to residents covered under Title XIX of the 
6 United States Social Security Act based upon the actual cost of services provided through 
7 June 30, 2023.
