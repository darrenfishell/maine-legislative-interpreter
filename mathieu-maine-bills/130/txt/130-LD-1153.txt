22SUMMARY
23 This bill prohibits the use of any watercraft equipped with an internal combustion 
24 engine on Webber Pond in the Town of Bremen, except that it allows current residents with 
25 a valid registration on the effective date of the provision to continue to use and operate 
26 watercraft with a 4-stroke engine that is no more than 6 horsepower.23
