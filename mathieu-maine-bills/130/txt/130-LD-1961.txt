7SUMMARY
8 This bill includes among the state goals for all state and municipal agencies affecting 
9 natural resource management, land use and development preserving the State’s tribal and 
10 cultural resources; addressing significant disparities in housing needs and access to 
11 educational, occupational and other opportunities; furthering the purposes of the federal 
12 Fair Housing Act; and promoting housing choice and economic diversity in housing, 
13 including housing for both low-income and moderate-income households. It also changes 
14 the membership of the Maine State Housing Authority and specifies that appointments to 
15 the Maine State Housing Authority and the appointment of the director of the authority are 
16 subject to review by the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction 
17 over housing matters.8
