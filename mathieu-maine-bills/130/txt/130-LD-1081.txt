9SUMMARY
10 This bill changes the moose hunting permits issued to hunting outfitters through a 
11 separate chance drawing to their issuance through a separate chance drawing to any 
12 licensed hunting guide.  The fee, proceeds and percentage of permits issued for a moose 
13 hunting permit under this section remain the same.  All other provisions related to the sale 
14 and transfer of the permit remain the same, except that the sale does not need to be a part 
15 of a package deal including food and lodging.10
