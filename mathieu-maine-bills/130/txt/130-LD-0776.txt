41.  Furlough authorized.  The sheriff may establish rules for and permit a prisoner 
5 under the final sentence of a court a furlough from the county jail in which the prisoner is 
6 confined.  Furlough may be granted for not more than 3  7 days at one time in order to 
7 permit the prisoner to visit a dying relative, to obtain medical services, to participate in a 
8 program operated by a jail that conditions release on regular daily reporting to the jail of 
9 the prisoner's location and activities or for any other reason consistent with the 
10 rehabilitation of an inmate or prisoner that is consistent with the laws or rules of the sheriff's 
11 department.  Furlough may be granted for a period longer than 3  7 days if required to 
12 provide treatment for a physical or mental condition of the prisoner, including a substance 
13 use disorder, as determined by a qualified licensed professional.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill extends from 3 to 7 days the length of time that a sheriff may furlough a person 
16 who is incarcerated in a county jail.  The bill adds to the purposes for which furlough may 
17 be granted participation in a program operated by a jail that conditions release on regular 
18 daily reporting to the jail of the prisoner's location and activities.
