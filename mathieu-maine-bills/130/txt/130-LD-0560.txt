30SUMMARY
31 This bill amends the safe haven laws regarding abandoned children by including a safe 
32 haven baby box to the list of safe havens that a person may deliver a newborn child to under 
33 the Child and Family Services and Child Protection Act and to serve as an affirmative 
34 defense to the criminal offense of abandonment of a child.  A safe haven baby box is a 
35 device or container that can safely accept delivery of a newborn child that is located in a 
36 hospital, law enforcement facility or fire department facility that is staffed 24 hours a day 
37 by a medical services provider.31
