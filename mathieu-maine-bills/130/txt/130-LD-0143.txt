6SUMMARY
7 This bill makes permanent the provisions of law governing arrearage management 
8 programs implemented by transmission and distribution utilities to assist eligible low-
9 income residential customers who are in arrears on their electricity bills by eliminating the 
10 future repeal of these provisions.
