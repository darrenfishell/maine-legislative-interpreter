SUMMARY

premises under Class I and Class III licenses.

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill adds pool halls to the premises eligible to be licensed to sell spirits for on-premises consumption and lists 
both pool halls and outdoor auditoriums as eligible premises under a Class I and Class II licenses. Any additional 
revenue from adding these entities is not anticipated to be significant.
