33SUMMARY
34 This bill provides that the Finance Authority of Maine may not issue a certificate of 
35 eligibility for a refundable seed capital tax credit to a private venture capital fund until after 
36 certificates have been issued for nonrefundable credits and only if the annual limit on 
37 certificates will not be exceeded.  The bill also provides ongoing appropriations of 
38 $500,000 annually to the Governor's Jobs Initiative Program to be used as matching funds 
39 for worker training or retraining in manufacturing industries.33
