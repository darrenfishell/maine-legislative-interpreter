4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill proposes to amend the laws governing permitting by the Department of 
6 Environmental Protection.  The bill proposes to amend the laws governing:
7 1.  Requirements for who may intervene in a department application review;
8 2.  Deadlines for and limitations on submitting public comments for complex projects;
9 3.  The duration of public comment periods during the permit application process; and
10 4.  Requirements on the ability to appeal a decision of the department.4
