SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment requires the Department of Labor 
to conduct a comprehensive educational campaign focused on the business and nonprofit 
communities on employee overtime laws as described in the Maine Revised Statutes. It 
also requires the department to submit a report regarding that campaign annually to the 
joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over labor matters.
