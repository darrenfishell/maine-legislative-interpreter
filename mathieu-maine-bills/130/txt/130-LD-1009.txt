8SUMMARY
9 This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to designate an 
10 intensive case manager working in the county jails to serve as a health care ombudsman to 
11 oversee county jail inmates with medical and mental health care needs and connect them 
12 to the health care services for which they qualify.  The health care ombudsman also assists 
13 county jail inmates in receiving the medications they need in a timely fashion.9
