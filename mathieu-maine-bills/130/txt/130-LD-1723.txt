29SUMMARY
30 This bill authorizes the Town of Windham to provide winter maintenance, including 
31 snow removal, sanding and salting, on certain designated private ways using town 
32 equipment.  The bill requires the town council of the Town of Windham to enact an 
33 ordinance governing the performance of winter maintenance on private ways, including 
34 establishing eligibility criteria for private ways, creating a roster and map of designated 
35 private ways, developing maintenance standards for designated private ways and setting 
36 conditions for the suspension and termination of winter maintenance.  The bill also limits 
37 the ability of a road association or owners of property benefited by a private way to claim 
38 that the private way has become a town way by virtue of the town's performance of winter 
39 maintenance.1
