SENATE AMENDMENT" A," to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 492, 
L.D. 1581, "An Act To Require Telecommunications Companies To Divulge Location 
Information to Law Enforcement When Necessary To Respond to a 9-1-1 Call or Locate a 
Person in Danger" 
Amend the amendment in section 1 in subsection 4 in the 5th line (page 1, line 22 in 
amendment) by striking out the following: ", and the possessor of the loeation information" 
and inserting the following:', and the possessor of the location information' 
Amend the amendment in section 1 in subsection 4 in the 7th line (page 1, line 24 in 
amendment) by inserting after the following: "~" the following: 'does not believe the 
request is made in bad faith' 
Amend the amendment by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment amends Committee Amendment "A" to provide that, when imminent 
danger of death or serious physical injury is present, the possessor of the location 
information must provide the information to the governmental requester as long as the 
possessor of the location information does not believe the request is made in bad faith.
