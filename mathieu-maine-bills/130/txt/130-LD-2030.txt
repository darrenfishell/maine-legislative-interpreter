19SUMMARY
20 This bill creates an exemption from sales and use tax for sales of commercial energy 
21 storage systems; integral parts and accessories for such a system; and materials for the 
22 construction, repair or maintenance of such a system. The bill also provides that the 
23 business equipment tax exemption is available for qualified energy storage systems.20
