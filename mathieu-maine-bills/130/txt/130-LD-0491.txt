17SUMMARY
18 This bill creates the Class C crime of hate crime false public alarm or report in which 
19 a person gives or causes to be given information the person, motivated by or because of the 
20 actual or perceived race, color, religion, religious practice, disability or origin of any 
21 person, knows or should reasonably know is false to an emergency responder, government 
22 agency or utility with the intent of inducing the responder, agency or utility to respond or 
23 to a law enforcement officer with the intent to cause the belief in the law enforcement 
24 officer that a crime has been committed.18
