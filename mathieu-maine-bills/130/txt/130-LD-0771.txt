31SUMMARY
32 This bill modifies the laws governing the wastewater treatment plant operator 
33 certification program administered by the Department of Environmental Protection.  It 
34 changes the procedure for revocation of a wastewater treatment plant operator certification, 
35 provides authority for suspension of a wastewater treatment plant operator certification, 
36 clarifies how the department may administer the program, updates outdated terminology 
37 and eliminates outdated provisions.  It also clarifies the department's existing authority with 
38 respect to licenses and its authority with respect to wastewater treatment plant operator 
39 certificates.1
