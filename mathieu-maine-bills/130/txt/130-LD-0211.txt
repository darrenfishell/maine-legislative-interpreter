11SUMMARY
16 This bill includes ongoing General Fund appropriations of $3,000,000 per year to the 
17 Shelter Operating Subsidy program within the Maine State Housing Authority to support 
18 operations and capacity at low-barrier emergency homeless shelters across the State. This 
19 bill specifies that the funding is supplemental to the Maine State Housing Authority's 
20 emergency shelter and housing assistance program and is to be delivered outside of the 
21 funding formula set forth in the Maine State Housing Authority's rule under 99-346 C.M.R.
