25SUMMARY
26 This bill amends the laws governing the Small Enterprise Growth Program, also known 
27 as the Maine Venture Fund, to permit the Small Enterprise Growth Board to invest in 
28 certain privately managed venture capital funds and makes certain technical changes to the 
29 statutes.1
