14SUMMARY
15 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to designate Bridge 923 over the 
16 Pleasant River in the Town of Brownville the Stanley Smith Larson Memorial Bridge, 
17 Bridge 3222 over the Pleasant River in Brownville Junction in the Town of Brownfield the 
18 Edward Fredonia Stone Memorial Bridge and Bridge 3355 over the East Branch Pleasant 
19 River in the Town of Brownville the Elden Howard Cail Memorial Bridge.15
