3 further amended by amending subparagraph (2) to read:
4 (2)  Emergency lights used on an ambulance, an emergency medical service 
5 vehicle, a fire department vehicle or a hazardous material response vehicle may 
6 include one blue light mounted facing toward the rear of the vehicle so that the 
7 light is primarily visible to approaching traffic from the rear only.  Any other blue 
8 light used on an ambulance, an emergency medical service vehicle, a fire 
9 department vehicle or a hazardous material response vehicle must be approved by 
10 the chief of the local law enforcement agency of the jurisdiction of the vehicle.
11SUMMARY
12 This bill allows ambulances, emergency medical service vehicles, fire department 
13 vehicles and hazardous material response vehicles to use blue lights other than the one blue 
14 light mounted facing toward the rear of the vehicle presently allowed by law, upon approval 
15 of the chief of the local law enforcement agency of the jurisdiction of the vehicle.12
