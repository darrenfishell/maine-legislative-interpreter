26SUMMARY
27 This bill amends licensing requirements for foreign-trained dentists by allowing dentist 
28 licensure for an applicant with a certification or a degree from a postdoctoral advanced 
29 specialty program and to allow resident dental licensure for an applicant with credentials 
30 from a medical school or program accredited by the national Commission on Dental 
31 Accreditation and a doctoral degree in dentistry from an institute or program from another 
32 country accredited by a country-specific national dentistry board or council.  This bill also 
33 removes the requirement that an applicant for a resident dental license provide verification 
34 of passing the examinations required by the Board of Dental Practices.27
