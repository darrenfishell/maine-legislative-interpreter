61.  Office established.  The State Planning Office is established within the Office of 
7 the Governor and is directly responsible to the Governor.
82.  Purpose.  The State Planning Office shall:
9 A.  Advise the Governor on developing and implementing policy;
10 B.  Assist the Legislature with information and analysis; and
11 C.  Administer financial and technical assistance programs to help build sustainable 
12 communities.
13SUMMARY
14 This bill reestablishes the State Planning Office, eliminated in 2012, within the Office 
15 of the Governor.  The State Planning Office has 3 core functions: to advise the Governor 
16 on developing and implementing policy; to assist the Legislature with information and 
17 analysis; and to administer financial and technical assistance programs to help build 
18 sustainable communities.
