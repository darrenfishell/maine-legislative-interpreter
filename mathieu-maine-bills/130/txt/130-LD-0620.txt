Page 1 - 130LR0627(01)1Sec. 1.  Develop plan for teachers to collect social security. Resolved: That 
2 the Maine Public Employees Retirement System shall examine options and make 
3 recommendations for a plan to allow teachers in the State to contribute, accumulate credit 
4 and collect benefits under the United States Social Security Act in addition to collecting 
5 benefits under the Maine Public Employees Retirement System.  In examining options, the 
6 Maine Public Employees Retirement System shall consider and outline the process for an 
7 amendment to the State's so-called Section 218 Agreement with the United States Social 
8 Security Administration and any other available avenues to allow teachers to collect social 
9 security. In conducting its work under this section, the Maine Public Employees Retirement 
10 System shall consult, as needed, with experts in public retirement and social security issues.  
11 In making recommendations, the Maine Public Employees Retirement System shall 
12 develop implementation timelines and outline statutory and other changes necessary to 
13 implement the plan. For the purposes of this section, "teacher" has the same meaning as in 
14 the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 17001, subsection 42.
15Sec. 2.  Report. Resolved: That no later than December 1, 2022, the Maine Public 
16 Employees Retirement System shall submit to the Joint Standing Committee on Labor and 
17 Housing a report, including its findings and recommendations as required under section 1, 
18 for a plan to allow teachers to collect social security in addition to collecting benefits under 
19 the Maine Public Employees Retirement System. Following receipt and review of the 
20 report, the committee is authorized to submit a bill to the Second Regular Session of the 
21 130th Legislature.
22SUMMARY
23 This resolve directs the Maine Public Employees Retirement System to examine 
24 options and make recommendations for a plan to allow teachers in the State to contribute, 
25 accumulate credit and collect benefits under the United States Social Security Act in 
26 addition to collecting benefits under the Maine Public Employees Retirement System.  It 
27 requires the Maine Public Employees Retirement System to submit a report on its findings 
28 and recommendations, together with any proposed implementing legislation, to the Joint 
29 Standing Committee on Labor and Housing by December 1, 2022 and authorizes the 
30 committee to submit a bill to the Second Regular Session of the 130th Legislature after 
31 reviewing the report.23
