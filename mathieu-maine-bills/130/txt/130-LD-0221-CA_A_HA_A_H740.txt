SUMMARY 
This amendment incorporates the substance of L.D. 496, "An Act To Clear Waiting 
Lists for and Ensure Timely Access to Mental Health Services for Maine Children, 11 as 
amended, which provides funding to increase rates in Depattment of Health and Human 
Services rule Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter Ill, Section 65, 
Behavioral Health Services, Children's Home and Community Based Treatment, and 
Section 97, Appendix D, Principles of Reimbursement for Child Care Facilities, by July 1, 
2021. The rate ofMaineCare reimbursement is increased by 25% for children's home and 
community-based treatment and child care facilities and by 25% for outpatient therapy for 
individuals and families and outpatient group therapy under the MaineCare program.
