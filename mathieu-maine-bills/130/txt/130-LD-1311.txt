32SUMMARY
33 This bill makes the following changes to the laws governing the State Employee Health 
34 Commission and the state employee health insurance program.
35 1.  It changes the name of the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, 
36 Division of State Employee Health Insurance to the Office of Employee Health and 
37 Wellness and changes the name of the office head from the Executive Director of Health 
38 Insurance to the Executive Director of Employee Health and Wellness.
39 2.  It states that the duties of the State Employee Health Commission include providing 
40 counsel to the Executive Director of Employee Health and Wellness and the Director of 
41 the Bureau of Human Resources on dental insurance and the state living resources program.1
