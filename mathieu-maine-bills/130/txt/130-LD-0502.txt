5 This bill proposes to enact measures facilitating a review of how working waterfront 
6 or working waterfront property is defined and treated throughout the Maine Revised 
7 Statutes to assess whether statutory or regulatory changes are needed to better recognize 
8 and account for water-dependent commercial activities associated with working waterfront 
9 or working waterfront property in the State's land use planning, resiliency planning and 
10 climate change adaptation strategies.4
