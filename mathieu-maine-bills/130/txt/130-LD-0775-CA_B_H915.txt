SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, specifies that 
employees of the State are not entitled to a pre-disciplinary·hearing during the required 
probationary period. It also clarifies that a judicial employee may be dismissed, suspended 
or otherwise disciplined without cause during the probationary period.
