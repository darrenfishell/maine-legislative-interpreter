20SUMMARY
21 This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to increase 
22 reimbursement rates for nursing services for certain home-based services by January 15, 
23 2022 and to index the reimbursement rates for certain home-based services provided under 
24 rule Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapters II and III, Section 40 to the low-
