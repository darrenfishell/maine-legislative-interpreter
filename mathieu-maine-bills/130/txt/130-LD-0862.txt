Page 1 - 130LR0651(01)1Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2Sec. 1.  29-A MRSA §1611, sub-§2, ¶G is enacted to read:
3 G.  For for-hire snowplow vehicles and vehicles used by landscaping or gardening 
4 companies:
5 (1)  There is a combined single limit of $125,000; or
6 (2)  There is a split limit of $50,000 per person or $100,000 per occurrence for 
7 bodily injury liability and $25,000 for property damage liability.
8 These vehicles must be covered by commercial policies.
9SUMMARY
10 This bill adds for-hire snowplow vehicles and vehicles used by landscaping or 
11 gardening companies to the provision of law requiring a minimum amount of insurance.  It 
12 requires the vehicles to be covered by commercial policies with a combined single limit of 
13 $125,000 or a split limit of $50,000 per person or $100,000 per occurrence for bodily injury 
14 liability and $25,000 for property damage liability.10
