14Sec. 1.  Adoption.  Resolved:  That final adoption of portions of Chapter 101:  
15 MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 97:  Private Non-Medical Institution

18 Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A outside the legislative rule 
19 acceptance period, is authorized.

21 takes effect when approved.
