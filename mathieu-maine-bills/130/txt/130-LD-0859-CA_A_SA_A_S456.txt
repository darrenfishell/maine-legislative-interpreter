SUMMARY 
This amendment permits municipalities to adopt a different method of determining the 
secret ballot election of town officials or municipal referenda elections if done so in a 
municipal meeting held at least 180 days before the election. 
The committee amendment requires the different method to be adopted at least 180 
days before the annual meeting.
