2of adoption assistance.  Resolved:  That no later than October 1, 2021, the Department 
3 of Health and Human Services shall amend its rules in 10-148, Chapter 13 to require that 
4 an adoptive family receive the same level of financial assistance for long-term or limited-
5 period adoption assistance as the family received when the child was in treatment-level 
6 foster care.  The department shall increase the amount of its long-term adoption assistance 
7 and limited-period adoption assistance to equal the reimbursement rates provided in 10-
8 148, Chapter 14 for a child who has been assessed to require a treatment level of foster care 
9 at level C, D or E.  Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules as 
10 defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
