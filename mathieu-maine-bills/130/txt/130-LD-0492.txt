3ยง13056-I.  Rural Workforce Recruitment Program
41.  Program established.  The Rural Workforce Recruitment Program, referred to in 
5 this section as "the program," is established within the department to provide grants to 
6 entities in rural regions of the State to aid those entities in hiring workforce recruiters to 
7 assist rural businesses in those regions in locating and recruiting qualified staff to meet 
8 workforce needs.
92.  Fund established.  The Rural Workforce Recruitment Fund, referred to in this 
10 section as "the fund," is established as a nonlapsing fund within the department for the 
11 purpose of providing funds for the program.  The fund consists of money appropriated to 
12 the fund by the Legislature and any funds received by the department for the purposes of 
13 the program.
143.  Rulemaking.  The department shall establish by rule the criteria for eligibility for 
15 grants from the program and the process of application.  Rules adopted pursuant to this 
16 subsection are routine technical rules as defined in chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
174.  Reporting.  The department shall report by January 15th of each year to the joint 
18 standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over economic development 
19 matters on the program, including the number of grants given and the results achieved.
