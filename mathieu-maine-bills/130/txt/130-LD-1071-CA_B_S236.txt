SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the committee and changes the total 
homestead property tax exemption to $30,000 for property tax years beginning on or after 
April 1, 2022 and decreases the percentage of state reimbursement of taxes lost by reason 
of the homestead property tax exemption to municipalities to 58%. The amendment also 
adds an appropriations and allocations section.
