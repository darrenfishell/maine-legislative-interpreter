14 'Sec. 1. 37-B MRSA §613 is enacted to read: 
15 §613. Maine Veterans' Homes Stabilization Fund 
16 1. Fund established. The Maine Veterans' Homes Stabilization Fund, referred to in 
17 this section as "the fund," is established in the Department of Defense, Veterans and 
18 Emergency Management, Bureau of Veterans' Services as an Other Special Revenue Funds 
19 account for the purposes of this section. 
20 2. Fund purposes. For the purposes of assisting the board in ensuring the continuous 
21 operation of the Maine Veterans' Homes pursuant to section 604, subsection 6, the 
22 department may enter into agreements or cooperative arrangements with a state or federal 
23 agency or with any person, firm or corporation. The department may receive, administer 
24 and disburse any funds or contributions from a state or federal agency, person, firm or 
25 corporation, either independently or in conjunction with state funds allocated to these 
26 purposes. 
27 3. Nonlapsing. Unexpended funds in the fund do not lapse at the end of any fiscal 
28 year but are carried forward to be used for the same purposes. 
29 4. Fund use; notification. The department shall notify the joint standing committees 
30 of the Legislature having jurisdiction over appropriations and financial affairs and over 
31 veterans affairs each time the department plans to transfer or expend any amount from the 
32 fund or otherwise affect the balance in the fund no less than 14 days prior to any transfer 
33 or expenditure, unless an emergency proclamation issued under section 742 kin effect 
34 The notification must include the amount of funds required and the purpose for which the 
35 funds are required.
