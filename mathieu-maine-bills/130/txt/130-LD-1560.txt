20 Passamaquoddy Tribe's compliance with the terms, conditions, encumbrances and 
21 restrictions described in section 3 releases the Passamaquoddy Tribe of any state 
22 environmental contamination claims, including natural resource damage claims, that have 
23 or in the future may accrue to the State or its agencies as a result of the historic 
24 contamination of the land by parties other than the Passamaquoddy Tribe.
25Sec. 6.  Repeal.  Resolved: That sections 1 to 4 of this resolve are repealed 5 years 
26 from its effective date.
27SUMMARY
28 This resolve authorizes the State to convey to the Passamaquoddy Tribe a certain parcel 
29 of land located on the northern side of State Route 191 in Meddybemps, Washington 
30 County, which is a portion of a site known as the Eastern Surplus Company Superfund site 
31 that has been designated a Superfund site due to the presence of hazardous substances, 
32 materials or wastes.
33 This resolve allows the Passamaquoddy Tribe to take ownership of the land without 
34 assuming liability for historical contamination that the tribe did not cause and permits 
35 continued access to the land by the State and the United States to address remaining and 
36 future contamination at this or nearby lands.28
