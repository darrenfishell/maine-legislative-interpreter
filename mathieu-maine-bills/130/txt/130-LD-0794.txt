3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This emergency bill proposes to direct the Department of Professional and Financial 
6 Regulation, Bureau of Insurance and the Department of Health and Human Services to 
7 develop a state nonprofit risk indemnification trust to provide liability insurance coverage 
8 for entities such as state-contracted providers of therapeutic foster care that are unable to 
9 obtain affordable coverage through ordinary means and when coverage is required by law 
10 or is necessary for the conduct of a public purpose.  The bill proposes to indemnify 
11 nonprofit beneficiary organizations and their officers, directors and agents for financial loss 
12 due to the imposition of legal liability.
