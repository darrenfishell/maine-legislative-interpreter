21SUMMARY
22 This bill amends the information relating to a request for demographic data that an 
23 apprenticeship agreement under the Maine Apprenticeship Program must contain.  It brings 
24 the program into compliance with federal Equal Employment Opportunity Act of 1972 
25 standards.
