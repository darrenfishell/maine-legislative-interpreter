29SUMMARY
30 This bill allows a municipality to impose a local option sales tax of no more than 1%, 
31 which may be seasonal, on prepared food, not including marijuana or marijuana products, 
32 and short-term lodging if approved by referendum of the voters in that municipality.  
33 Revenue from the local option sales tax is distributed at the rate of 85% to the municipality 
34 and 15% to all other municipalities.  Revenue received by a municipality may not be used 
35 to reduce or eliminate funding otherwise due the municipality under other provisions of 
36 law.30
