The bill stabilizes property taxes for eligible property owners by capping the amount of property taxes at the amount 
billed when stabilization was first requested. The bill includes a General Fund appropriation to the Department of 
Administrative and Financial Services of $70,824 in fiscal year 2021-22 and $315,242 in fiscal year 2022-23 for one 
Property Appraiser, one half-time, temporary Property Appraiser, mandate reimbursement costs and All Other costs to 
process and audit applications. It also includes a General Fund appropriation to the Department of Administrative and 
Financial Services of 1,955,000 in :fiscal year 2022-23 for reimbursement to municipalities for property taxes lost due 
to stabilization.
