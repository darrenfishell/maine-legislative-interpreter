5 This bill would require the Department of Health and Human Services to work with 
6 stakeholders to develop and fund a continuum of evidence-based treatment services for 
7 adolescents affected by substance use disorder.  Services would include, but not be limited 
8 to, detoxification beds in hospitals or residential settings and intensive outpatient treatment
