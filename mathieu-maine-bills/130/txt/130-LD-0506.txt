16SUMMARY
17 This bill exempts from sales tax all electricity consumed by residential customers 
18 participating in either a low-income assistance program or arrearage management program 
19 approved by the Public Utilities Commission.  This exemption maximizes the value of the 
20 benefit from the subsidies provided to participants in these programs.
