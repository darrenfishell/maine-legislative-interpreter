25 applies to the battery energy storage system, and includes all parts and accessories that 
26 are integral to such a battery energy storage system. 
27 2. Refund authorized. The assessor shall refund the sales or use tax imposed pursuant 
28 to this Part and paid by a person that purchases a qualifying battery energy storage system 
29 on or after January 1, 2023 and before December 31, 2025.

31 storage system and pays the tax imposed pursuant to this Part may submit a claim for 
32 reimbursement on a form prescribed by the assessor filed within 3 years of the payment of 
33 the sales or use tax to which the reimbursement relates, except that a claim for 
34 reimbursement may not be submitted prior to July 1, 2023.
