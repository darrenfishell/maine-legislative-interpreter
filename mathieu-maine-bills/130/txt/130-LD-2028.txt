Page 1 - 130LR2651(01)1Sec. 1.  Preliminary injunctions in judicial separation and divorce actions. 
2Resolved: That the Family Law Advisory Commission, as established in the Maine 
3 Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 12004-I, subsection 52-A, shall review the elements of 
4 preliminary injunctions in judicial separation and divorce actions in Title 19-A and in the 
5 statutes of other states to analyze appropriate elements and when they should be applied.  
6 The commission shall develop options for improving preliminary injunctions in judicial 
7 separation and divorce actions.  The commission may include interested parties in the 
8 review, analysis and development of options.  The commission shall submit a report, 
9 including any recommendations, to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 
10 jurisdiction over judiciary matters no later than December 15, 2022. The joint standing 
11 committee may report out legislation related to the report to the 131st Legislature in 2023.
12SUMMARY
13 This resolve directs the Family Law Advisory Commission to review the elements of 
14 preliminary injunctions in judicial separation and divorce actions both in this State's 
15 statutes and in the statutes of other states. The commission is required to develop options 
16 for improving those preliminary injunctions. The commission must submit a report to the 
17 joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over judiciary matters no 
18 later than December 15, 2022, and the joint standing committee may report out legislation 
19 related to the report to the 131st Legislature in 2023.13
