8 committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over transportation matters.  The 
9 committee may report out legislation based upon the plan to the First Regular Session of 
10 the 131st Legislature.
11SUMMARY
12 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to develop a plan by January 1, 
13 2023 to redevelop Exit 5 on Interstate 295 in Portland that eliminates redundant and 
14 unnecessary on-ramps and off-ramps and prioritizes restoring the land to be used for 
15 housing or neighborhood amenities.12
