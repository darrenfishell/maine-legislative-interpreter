17SUMMARY
18 This bill repeals the provision of law that terminates the powers, duties and authority 
19 of the Maine School for Marine Science, Technology, Transportation and Engineering 90 
20 days after the adjournment of the Second Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.  The 
21 bill also provides that the phase-in procedures for schools that are funded in the same way 
22 as charter schools are the same as those for charter schools. 18
