13 organization that placed the sign within the public right-of-way and the date the sign 
14 was erected within the public right-of-way.
15SUMMARY
16 This bill provides that temporary road signs may be placed within the public right-of-
17 way for a maximum of 30 days from January 1st to June 30th and for a maximum of 30 
18 days from July 1st to December 31st.16
