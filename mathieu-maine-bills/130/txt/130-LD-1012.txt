5SUMMARY
6 This bill creates a new project category for natural resources agencies that are applying 
7 for a grant from the Maine Outdoor Heritage Fund.  The new category is wildlife 
8 rehabilitation and includes criteria for the board to take into account when considering and 
9 awarding grants to projects under that category.  The new category represents 10% of 
10 available annual grant money, reallocating funding from 2 other existing categories.6
