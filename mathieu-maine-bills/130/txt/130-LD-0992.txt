27SUMMARY
28 This bill prohibits the Commissioner of Professional and Financial Regulation, or any 
29 bureau, board or commission within or affiliated with the Department of Professional and 
30 Financial Regulation, from denying or revoking a professional or occupational license if 
31 the denial or revocation is for a violation unrelated to the practice of the licensed profession 
32 or occupation.  It requires the department to submit to the Joint Standing Committee on 
33 Innovation, Development, Economic Advancement and Business a report identifying 
34 existing statutes that allow for such denial or revocation and gives the committee the 
35 authority to report out legislation repealing these statutes to the Second Regular Session of 
36 the 130th Legislature.
37 The bill also prohibits municipalities from denying or revoking a business license if 
38 the denial or revocation is for a violation unrelated to the conduct of the business.  It 
39 requires the Department of Economic and Community Development to submit to the Joint 
40 Standing Committee on Innovation, Development, Economic Advancement and Business 
41 a report identifying existing statutes that allow for such denial or revocation and gives the 28
