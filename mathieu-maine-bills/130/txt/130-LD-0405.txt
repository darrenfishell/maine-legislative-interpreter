4 A person may not, in order to promote that person's success or the success of another

12SUMMARY
13 This bill provides that a person may not, in order to promote that person's success or 
14 the success of another person as a candidate for nomination or election to any public office 
15 or in connection with any question submitted to the voters, include or cause to be included 
16 in any political advertisement, circular, poster or publication the name of any person as an 
17 endorser or supporter or any statement that gives the appearance of that endorsement or 
18 support except with the express written consent of that person.
