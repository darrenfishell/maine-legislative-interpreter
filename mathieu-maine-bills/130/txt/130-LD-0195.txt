Page 1 - 130LR1692(01)1Sec. 1.  Voluntary licensing system for general contractors for home 
2improvement and construction.  Resolved:  That the Department of Professional and 
3 Financial Regulation, in conjunction with the Department of Labor, shall study the 
4 development of a voluntary licensing system for general contractors for home improvement 
5 and construction.  The study must address costs of the voluntary licensing system, state 
6 agency resources, system implementation and the potential impact on the construction 
7 industry.
8Sec. 2.  Report.  Resolved:  That the Department of Professional and Financial 
9 Regulation shall submit a report on the study conducted under section 1, including 
10 suggested legislation, to the Joint Standing Committee on Innovation, Development, 
11 Economic Advancement and Business by December 1, 2021.  The committee is authorized 
12 to submit a bill based on the report to the Second Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.
13SUMMARY
14 This resolve directs the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation, in 
15 conjunction with the Department of Labor, to study the development of a voluntary 
16 licensing system for general contractors for home improvement and construction.  The 
17 department is required to submit a report to the Joint Standing Committee on Innovation, 
18 Development, Economic Advancement and Business by December 1, 2021, and the 
19 committee is authorized to submit a bill based on the report to the Second Regular Session 
20 of the 130th Legislature.
