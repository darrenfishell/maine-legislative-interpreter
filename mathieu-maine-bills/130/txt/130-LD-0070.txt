10CHAPTER 231
11STUDENTS WHO HAVE BEEN AFFECTED BY COVID-19
12ยง6981.  Students who have been affected by COVID-19
13 The parent or legal guardian of a student may, by written notice to the principal of the 
14 student's school or to the superintendent of the school administrative unit in which the 
15 student attends school, request that the student not be promoted to the next grade level or 
16 graduated due to the various effects of coronavirus disease 2019, also known as COVID-19, 
17 and the related pandemic on the student's learning during the 2019-2020 and 2020-2021 
18 school years.  A principal or superintendent shall approve such a request as long as the 
19 student's school can accommodate adding the student to the affected grade level.
20Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
21 takes effect when approved.
22SUMMARY
23 This bill allows a parent or legal guardian of a student to request, by written notice to 
24 the principal of the student's school or to the superintendent of the school administrative 
25 unit in which the student attends school, that the student not be promoted to the next grade 
26 level or graduated due to the various effects of COVID-19 and the related pandemic on the 
27 student's learning during the 2019-2020 and 2020-2021 school years.  It requires the 
28 principal or superintendent to approve the request as long as the student's school can 
29 accommodate adding the student to the affected grade level.
