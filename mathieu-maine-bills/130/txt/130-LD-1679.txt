8SUMMARY
9 This bill requires the State to pay the difference between the federal requirement for a 
10 free breakfast or lunch and the full price for a breakfast or lunch at no cost to the students 
11 who are ineligible for free or reduced-price school meals.  It also directs schools and school 
12 administrative units to request parents and guardians to complete household income forms 
13 to determine eligibility for various state and federal food assistance programs.  This bill 
14 also directs school administrative units to the maximum extent possible to maximize 
15 participation in federal school meal programs.9
