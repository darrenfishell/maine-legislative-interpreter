20Sec. 2.  34-B MRSA §5006 is enacted to read:
21§5006.  State and municipal minimum wage increases
22 The department shall ensure that reimbursement rates to providers of home and 
23 community-based services to a person receiving services under this chapter must include 
24 any increases to the minimum wage or other wage requirements as required by Title 22, 
25 section 3173.
26SUMMARY
27 This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to increase its 
28 reimbursement rates to providers of home and community-based services to persons with 
29 intellectual disabilities or autism by the same amount of increase required under the 
30 minimum wage law pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 26, section 664, 
31 subsection 1 or by a municipal ordinance.  If a municipal ordinance increases other wage 
32 requirements in the ordinance, such as a requirement for time-and-a-half in certain 
33 circumstances, that amount of increase is also required in the reimbursement rates.27
