14Sec. 2.  5 MRSA §285, sub-§3-E is enacted to read:
153-E.  Enrollment of spouse and dependents of employees who have retired and 
16returned to covered employment.  A retiree who is reemployed as a state employee may 
17 make an election at the time of returning to state employment or during any declared open 
18 enrollment period to enroll a spouse and dependent or dependents for coverage under the 
19 group health plan under the same terms and conditions for enrolling a spouse and dependent 
20 or dependents under the group health plan as an active employee.
21SUMMARY
22 This bill allows retirees who are rehired following retirement to make an election upon 
23 being rehired or during any declared open enrollment period to enroll a spouse and 
24 dependent or dependents in coverage under the group health plan for state employees.22
