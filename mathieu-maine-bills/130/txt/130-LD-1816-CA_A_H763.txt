20 SUMMARY 
21 This amendment amends the bill by clarifying it is the Board of Trustees of the 
22 University of Maine System that is required under the bill to establish a labor and 
23 community education center at the University of Southern Maine focused on providing 
24 lifelong community-based labor education, research and outreach. The amendment also 
25 requires the center to work collaboratively with the provost as well as with the President of 
26 the University of Southern Maine in integrating university curricula and faculty into 
27 education offered by the center, consistent with any applicable policies and collective 
28 bargaining agreements of the board of trustees.

This bill includes ongoing General Fund appropriations totaling $700,000 per year beginning in fiscal year 2022-23 to 
the University of Maine System. Of this amount, $500,000 per year is appropriated for the establishment of a labor 
and community education center at the University of Southern Maine to provide labor education and outreach and 
$200,000 per year is appropriated to the Bureau of Labor Education at the University of Maine to expand the capacity 
for applied research, ongoing labor history and customized education programs for the organized and unorganized 
labor force in Maine.
