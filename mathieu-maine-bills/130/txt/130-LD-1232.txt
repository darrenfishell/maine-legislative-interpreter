20SUMMARY
21 This bill describes training requirements for master barbers, a new license category. It 
22 replaces the term "barber hair stylist" with "barber".  It defines "barber pole" and also 
23 prohibits installation of a barber pole at a place of business unless at least one licensed 
24 barber or master barber practices barbering at the location of the business.21
