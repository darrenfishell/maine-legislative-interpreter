12SUMMARY
13 This bill requires students to complete 40 hours of community service to receive a high 
14 school diploma.  Community service may not be performed during school hours and may 
15 not be advocacy or of a political nature.  School boards may adopt policies to exempt 
16 students with disabilities from the requirement and to provide educational credit for 
17 completed community service hours.13
