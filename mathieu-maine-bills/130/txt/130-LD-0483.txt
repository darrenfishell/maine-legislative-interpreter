35SUMMARY
36 This bill clarifies 3 aspects of the law pertaining to the Maine Civil Legal Services 
37 Fund and the collection of fees and surcharges by the Judicial Department.
38 The bill clarifies that the Maine Civil Legal Services Fund surcharge on debt collection 
39 actions is payable at the commencement of debt collection actions, whether or not they are
