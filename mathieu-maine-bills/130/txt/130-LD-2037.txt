Page 1 - 130LR2701(01)1Emergency preamble.  Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
2 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
3Whereas, Cyr Plantation currently only has one assessor elected to the Board of 
4 Assessors and the functions of the Cyr Plantation Board of Assessors cannot be carried out 
5 without the appointment of temporary staff and election of additional board members to 
6 ensure a quorum exists; and
7Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
8 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
9 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
10 therefore,
11Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
12Sec. 1. Cyr Plantation temporary authority of assessor; filling vacancies 
13through appointment.  The assessor of the Cyr Plantation Board of Assessors may 
14 appoint a municipal clerk, registrar of voters and treasurer to fill existing vacancies for the 
15 purpose of facilitating an election to fill empty seats on the Board of Assessors.  The terms 
16 of these appointments end upon the election of a quorum to the Board of Assessors unless 
17 the appointees are later confirmed as otherwise required by law or procedure.
18Sec. 2. Cyr Plantation authority of assessor; authority to call town meetings 
19and elections and designate shortened paper process.  The assessor of the Cyr 
20 Plantation Board of Assessors may issue a warrant calling a town meeting and an election 
21 to fill the vacancies on the Board of Assessors pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 
22 30-A, sections 2521, 2528 and 7006.  The assessor may also designate a shortened 
23 nomination paper process for the election pursuant to Title 30-A, section 2528, subsection 
24 4, paragraph E and may appoint the necessary number of ballot clerks to carry out the 
25 election pursuant to Title 30-A, section 2528, subsection 8.
26Sec. 3. Cyr Plantation authority of assessor; disbursement warrants.  Until 
27 a quorum of assessors is elected to the Cyr Plantation Board of Assessors, the assessor may 
28 approve and sign disbursement warrants under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, 
29 section 5603, subsection 2.
30Sec. 4. Cyr Plantation authority of assessor; general assistance program 
31administration.  Until a quorum is elected to the Cyr Plantation Board of Assessors, the 
32 assessor may approve any general assistance applications and perform any necessary 
33 functions to administer Cyr Plantation's general assistance program that would otherwise 
34 require a quorum of assessors.
35Sec. 5. Repeal.  This Act is repealed December 31, 2022.
36Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
37 takes effect when approved.
38SUMMARY
39 This bill allows the assessor of the Cyr Plantation Board of Assessors to take action to

41 to the Cyr Plantation Board of Assessors, the bill allows the assessor to approve and sign 39
