Page 1 - 130LR1188(01)1Sec. 1.  Commanding officer of State Bureau of Identification to seal 
2records relating to crimes decriminalized by the 130th Legislature.  Resolved: 
3 That the commanding officer of the State Bureau of Identification within the Department 
4 of Public Safety shall seal and make confidential any record contained in the criminal 
5 history record information related to a person for the conviction of a crime that has been 
6 decriminalized by the 130th Legislature immediately upon the effective date of legislation 
7 decriminalizing the crime.
8SUMMARY
9 This resolve directs the commanding officer of the State Bureau of Identification within 
10 the Department of Public Safety to immediately seal and make confidential any criminal 
11 history record information related to a person for the conviction of a crime that has been 
12 decriminalized by the 130th Legislature upon the effective date of legislation 
13 decriminalizing the crime.9
