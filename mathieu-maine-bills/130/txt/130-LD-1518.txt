33SUMMARY
34 This bill suspends until January 1, 2022 certain minimum requirements for income to 
35 be derived from food sales for restaurants licensed to sell alcohol.
36 This bill also allows a licensed in-state manufacturer of liquor to have a financial 
37 interest in a single retail licensee and allows a retail licensee to have a financial interest in 
38 a single licensed in-state manufacturer.34
