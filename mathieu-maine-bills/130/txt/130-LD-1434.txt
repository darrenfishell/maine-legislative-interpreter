39.  Controlled indoor entry area.  A marijuana store may have a controlled, indoor 
4 entry area directly inside the marijuana store in which the identification of a purchaser may 
5 be verified and a purchaser may await entry into the sales area of the marijuana store.  A 
6 controlled, indoor entry area under this subsection must:
7 A. Be physically separated from the sales area of the marijuana store; 
8 B. Prevent a purchaser in the controlled, indoor entry area from viewing adult use 
9 marijuana or adult use marijuana product displayed for sale in the marijuana store; and
10 C. Have a means to prevent entry of a purchaser from the controlled, indoor entry area 
11 into the sales area except as permitted by an employee of a marijuana store who is 
12 present in the controlled, indoor entry area.
13SUMMARY
14 This bill allows a marijuana store to have a controlled, indoor entry area directly inside 
15 the store where the identification of a purchaser can be verified and the purchaser can await 
16 entry into the store.  The area must be physically separated from the sales area of the store, 
17 prevent a purchaser in the area from viewing adult use marijuana or adult use marijuana 
18 product displayed for sale in the store and have a means to prevent a purchaser from 
19 entering the sales area except as permitted by an employee of the store who is present in 
20 the entry area.14
