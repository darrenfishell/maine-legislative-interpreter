24SUMMARY
25 This resolve provides an exemption to the temporary moratorium on the 
26 commencement, review and approval of new specialty or recognition registration plates by 
27 the Department of the Secretary of State, Bureau of Motor Vehicles by allowing a plate 
28 sponsor whose plate was approved before the effective date of the moratorium to continue 
29 the signature gathering process and bring forth enabling legislation for that plate.25
