Page 1 - 130LR1358(01)1Sec. 1.  Department of Health and Human Services to provide funds to 
2recovery community centers.  Resolved:  That the Department of Health and Human 
3 Services shall provide grant funds in an expedited manner, beginning within 60 days after 
4 the effective date of this legislation, in the amount of $1,000,000, to recovery community 
5 centers located in Franklin, Hancock, Kennebec, Piscataquis, Somerset, Waldo and York 
6 counties serving individuals with substance use disorder.  For purposes of this section, a 
7 "recovery community center" means a peer-operated organization whose primary purpose 
8 is to provide the community-based connections necessary for a person to enter and sustain 
9 recovery from substance use disorder by serving as a source of information on recovery, 
10 making referrals to local resources and providing or referring an individual to mutual-help 
11 or peer-support organization meetings or social activities.
12Sec. 2.  Report.  Resolved: That the Commissioner of Health and Human Services 
13 shall appear periodically before the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 
14 jurisdiction over health and human services matters to report on the implementation of this 
15 legislation.
16Sec. 3.  Funds may not be transferred.  Resolved: That, notwithstanding the 
17 Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 1585 or any other provision of law to the contrary, 
18 funding provided in this legislation may not be transferred to any other appropriation or 
19 program of an appropriation made by the Legislature.
20Sec. 4.  Funds may not lapse.  Resolved; That, notwithstanding the Maine Revised 
21 Statutes, Title 5, section 1589 or any other provision of law to the contrary, any 
22 unencumbered balance of appropriations contained in this legislation remaining at the end 
23 of each fiscal year may not lapse but must be carried forward to be used for the same 
24 purposes.
25Sec. 5.  Request for proposals.  Resolved: That the Department of Health and 
26 Human Services shall provide grant funds using the competitive request for proposal 
27 bidding process set forth in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 155. The 
28 department shall issue a request for proposals no later than October 1, 2021 to award annual 
29 grant funds beginning no later than 60 days after the effective date of this legislation.
30Sec. 6.  Appropriations and allocations.  Resolved:  That the following 
31 appropriations and allocations are made.
