8Sec. 2.  28-A MRSA §1201-B is enacted to read:
9§1201-B.  Transfer wine between retail locations
101.  Authority to transfer wine between retail locations.  Notwithstanding section 
11 1201, subsection 3-A and any other provision of this Title to the contrary, a retailer licensed 
12 to sell wine for off-premises consumption may sell or transfer wine by the case or by the 
13 bottle to another retailer licensed to sell wine for off-premises consumption under the 
14 following conditions:
15 A. The same person or persons must hold a majority ownership interest in both 
16 retailers; and
17 B. The retailers must be located within the territory that has been allocated under 
18 section 1401, subsection 8 to a single wholesale licensee for the purposes of making 
19 wholesale sales of the specific wine product being sold or transferred.
202. Notice; approval. A retailer licensed to sell wine for off-premises consumption shall 
21 seek approval from the bureau, on a form designated by the bureau, in advance of making 
22 an initial sale or transfer of wine to another retailer licensed to sell wine for off-premises 
23 consumption under subsection 1.
243.  Penalty.  In addition to any penalty that may be imposed under chapter 33, the 
25 bureau may prohibit a retailer licensed to sell wine for off-premises consumption that 
26 violates any provision of this section or of any rule adopted by the bureau to implement 
27 this section from engaging in the activities authorized by subsection 1.
28SUMMARY
29 This bill authorizes a licensed off-premises retailer of wine to transfer wine by the case 
30 or by the bottle to another licensed off-premises retailer of wine located within the same 
31 wholesaler's territory as long as the same person or persons hold a majority interest in both 
32 off-premises retail licenses.29
