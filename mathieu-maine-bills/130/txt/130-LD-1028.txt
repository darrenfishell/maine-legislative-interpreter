31SUMMARY
32 Under current law, new construction at a licensed manufacturing facility and new 
33 construction at or modification of a campus of an educational institution and of an existing 
34 licensed development is exempt from review under the laws governing site location of 
35 development if certain criteria are met.  This bill amends those exemptions by increasing 
36 the square footage criteria under the exemptions.32
