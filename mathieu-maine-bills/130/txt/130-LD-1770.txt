7Sec. 5.  12 MRSA §6506 is enacted to read:
8§6506.  Commercial halibut fishing license
91. License required. Beginning January 1, 2023, a person may not engage in the 
10 activities authorized under this section without a current:
11 A. Commercial halibut fishing license for a resident operator;
12 B. Commercial halibut fishing license for a resident operator and all crew members; or
13 C. Commercial halibut fishing license for a nonresident operator and all crew members.
142. Licensed activity; commercial license. The holder of a commercial halibut fishing 
15 license may fish for, take, possess, ship, transport or sell halibut that the holder has taken. 
16 A commercial halibut fishing license for an operator and all crew members also authorizes 
17 the crew members aboard the vessel named on the license to fish for, take, possess, ship or 
18 transport halibut when the license holder is aboard the vessel. 
193. Eligibility. A commercial halibut fishing license may be issued only to an individual. 
204. Fees. Fees for commercial halibut fishing licenses are as follows:
21 A. For a commercial halibut fishing license for a resident operator, $48;
22 B. For a commercial halibut fishing license for a resident operator and all crew 
23 members, $128; and
24 C. For a commercial halibut fishing license for a nonresident operator and all crew 
25 members, $481. 
26 The commissioner shall deposit fees collected pursuant to this subsection in the Halibut 
27 Fund established under section 6039.
285. Halibut tags. The holder of a license issued under subsection 1 must annually 
29 purchase halibut tags to fish for or take halibut. A license holder may purchase an allotment 
30 of halibut tags only once per year.  A vessel may have only one license holder’s tags 
31 assigned to that vessel.   The commissioner shall establish by rule the maximum number of 
32 halibut tags that may be issued to a license holder per year.   The following fees apply to 
33 halibut tags: 
34 A. For 5 halibut tags, $5;
35 B. For 10 halibut tags, $20;
36 C. For 15 halibut tags, $45;
37 D. For 20 halibut tags, $70; and
38 E. For 25 halibut tags, $100; 
39 The commissioner shall deposit fees collected pursuant to this subsection in the Halibut

11SUMMARY
12 This bill creates commercial halibut fishing licenses and halibut tags, with increasing 
13 fees for larger numbers of tags. With the creation of the commercial halibut fishing license, 
14 halibut is removed from the general commercial fishing license. The bill preserves an 
15 exemption for halibut fishing for personal use.12
