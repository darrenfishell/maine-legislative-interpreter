23SUMMARY
24 This bill requires the Commissioner of Education to pay annual national board 
25 certification salary supplements to school administrative units and publicly supported 
26 secondary schools for payment to national board-certified teachers.  Funding for these 
27 salary supplements comes from credential fees paid by teachers.  If these fees are 
28 insufficient to fully fund these salary supplements, general purpose aid must be 
29 appropriated to fund the balance.24
