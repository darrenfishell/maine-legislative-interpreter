132.  Balance billing prohibited.  A physician licensed under this chapter may not 
14 balance bill a Medicare beneficiary for any service, procedure or treatment provided to the 
15 Medicare beneficiary in the physician's office.
163.  Notice; posting.  A physician who treats Medicare beneficiaries shall post a 
17 summary of the provisions of this section in a conspicuous place in the physician's office.  
18 The summary must include the statement that any person aggrieved by a physician's failure 
19 to comply with the provisions of this section may file a complaint with the board and must 
20 include toll-free telephone numbers to be used for this purpose.
214.  Complaints; violations.  An individual may file a complaint with the board seeking 
22 disciplinary action concerning a violation of this section.  The board shall investigate or 
23 cause to be investigated and shall resolve a complaint on its own motion or upon receipt of 
24 a written complaint.  The board shall conduct its actions in accordance with the Maine 
25 Administrative Procedure Act.
