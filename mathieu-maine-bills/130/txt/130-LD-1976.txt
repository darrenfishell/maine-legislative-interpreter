10SUMMARY
11 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to convene a study group 
12 including members representing the department, the National Alliance on Mental Illness 
13 Maine and other interested parties to examine department bridge safety standards and how 
14 they may be improved beyond federal standards to discourage suicide attempts from 
15 bridges in this State.  The department is directed to submit a report to the Joint Standing 
16 Committee on Transportation no later than November 2, 2022 that includes its findings and 
17 recommendations, including suggested legislation, to make bridges in this State safer and 
18 more resistant to suicide attempts.11
