41. Definitions.  For the purposes of this section, the following terms have the following 
5 meanings.
6 A. "Eligible military veterans' organization" means a military veterans' organization 
7 qualified under the federal Internal Revenue Code of 1986, 26 United States Code, 
8 Section 501(c)(19), as amended, excluding a trust or foundation for a post or 
9 organization of past or present members of the Armed Forces of the United States.
10 B.  "Residential customer" means a customer defined as residential under the terms and 
11 conditions of the customer's transmission and distribution utility.
122. Residential rate for military veterans' organizations.  A transmission and 
13 distribution utility shall bill an eligible military veterans' organization at the same rate as 
14 the utility bills a residential customer for electric power or service provided to a facility 
15 owned by that organization or a facility rented by that organization if that organization is 
16 billed directly for electric power or service provided by the utility.
17SUMMARY
18 This bill requires a transmission and distribution utility to treat an eligible military 
19 veterans' organization as a residential customer for the purpose of billing for electric power 
20 or service provided to a facility owned or rented by that organization if that organization is 
21 billed directly for electric power or service.18
