1 SUMMARY 
2 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, makes the following 
3 changes to the bill. 
4 I. It amends the definition in the bill in the shoreland zoning laws for measuring the 
5 height of a structure to limit the alternative measurement for structures located in an area 
6 of special flood hazard to only existing principal or accessory structures. 
7 2. It amends the specific exception in the bill to shoreland zoning height requirements 
8 for legally existing nonconforming structures located in an area of special flood hazard to 
9 provide that such structures may be raised to, but not above, the minimum elevation 
10 necessary to be consistent with the local floodplain management elevation requirement or 
11 to 3 feet above base flood elevation, whichever is greater. 
12 3. It makes other technical changes to the bill.
