33.  Consent agreements. The commission may execute a consent agreement that 
4 resolves a complaint or investigation without further proceedings. A consent agreement 
5 may be entered into only with the consent of the licensee, the alleged violator, the 
6 commission and the Department of the Attorney General. Any remedy, penalty, purse 
7 return or fine that is otherwise available by law may be achieved by consent agreement, 
8 including long-term suspension and permanent revocation of a license or registration. A 
9 consent agreement is not subject to review or appeal and may be modified only by a writing 
10 executed by all parties to the original consent agreement. A consent agreement is 
11 enforceable by an action in Superior Court.  
12SUMMARY
13 This bill provides that the State Harness Racing Commission may enter into consent 
14 agreements to resolve violations of the laws relating to harness racing.13
