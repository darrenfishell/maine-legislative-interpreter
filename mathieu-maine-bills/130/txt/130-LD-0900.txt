4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill proposes to enact measures related to the challenges of and opportunities for 
6 recycling clean energy equipment such as solar panels, wind turbines and battery energy 
7 storage systems in the State.  It also establishes a funding mechanism for the Public Utilities 
8 Commission to subsidize start-up costs for such recycling.
