39.  Trucks and truck tractors; leftmost lane.  An operator driving a truck or truck 
4 tractor on that section of Interstate 95 from the New Hampshire state line to the southern 
5 terminus of the Maine Turnpike or on that section of the turnpike south of mile marker 44

9SUMMARY
10 This bill allows trucks with a registered gross vehicle weight over 10,000 pounds, 
11 including truck tractors, to operate in the leftmost lane only as necessary to safely 
12 accommodate vehicles entering or exiting Interstate 95 from the Maine-New Hampshire 
13 border to the southern terminus of the Maine Turnpike and entering or exiting the Maine 
14 Turnpike south of mile marker 44 where there are at least 3 lanes in each direction.10
