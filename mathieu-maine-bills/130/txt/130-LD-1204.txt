41.  Reimbursement for parent.   A parent of a child who is eligible for in-home 
5 personal care services under the MaineCare program may register as a personal care agency 
6 as defined in section 1717, subsection 1, paragraph C and determined in department rule 
7 and receive reimbursement for providing those services to the child if the following 
8 requirements are met:
9 A. The parent has made reasonable efforts to obtain regular in-home personal care 
10 services; and
11 B. The parent has satisfied the requirements of the applicable background check 
12 required for providers of in-home personal care services.
132.  Employer designation.   In order for a parent to be registered as a personal care 
14 agency under subsection 1, another individual who is not a parent of the child must be 
15 designated the employer. The person designated as the employer must be approved by both 
16 the department and the parent to act in the child's interest.
173.  Rulemaking.   The department shall adopt rules to implement this section. Rules 
18 adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter
