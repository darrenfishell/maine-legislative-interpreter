7SUMMARY
8 This bill requires a person obtaining a permit to hold a regatta, race, boat exhibition or 
9 water ski exhibition to provide protection, as prescribed by the Commissioner of Inland 
10 Fisheries and Wildlife, for wildlife, wildlife habitat and water quality.
