51.  Coverage provided.  The department shall provide the same scope of medical 
6 assistance provided under section 3174-G for comparable enrollment groups to a person 
7 who would be eligible for assistance under the federal Medicaid program under Title XIX 
8 of the federal Social Security Act but for their immigration status.  In accordance with 8 
9 United States Code, Section 1621, the State shall appropriate funds in the state budget to 
10 provide medical assistance through the MaineCare program and the Children's Health 
11 Insurance Program as defined in section 3174-X, subsection 1, paragraph A for noncitizen 
12 individuals who reside in the State and are ineligible for coverage due to federal restrictions 
13 in the federal Medicaid program and the Children's Health Insurance Program.
142.  Maximization of federal funds.  The department shall maximize the receipt of 
15 available federal matching funds for coverage provided under this section, including, but 
16 not limited to, the state option to cover a noncitizen pregnant person by covering that 
17 person's child from conception through birth.
183.  Rulemaking.  The department shall adopt rules to implement this section.  Rules 
19 adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 
20 375, subchapter 2-A.
214.  Waivers and state Medicaid plan.  The department shall seek any waivers and 
22 make amendments to the state Medicaid plan as needed to increase access to any federal 
23 funds that may be available to provide coverage under this section.
24SUMMARY
25 This bill provides MaineCare program and the Children's Health Insurance Program 
26 coverage for noncitizen residents of this State who are ineligible for coverage under the 
27 federal Medicaid program or Children's Health Insurance Program due to their immigration
