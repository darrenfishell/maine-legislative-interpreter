26SUMMARY
27 This bill is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on State and Local 
28 Government pursuant to Resolve 2021, chapter 19.  It reduces the number of paper copies 
29 a state agency or legislative committee must provide to the State Librarian when the agency 
30 or legislative committee publishes a report or other publication.  It requires the State 
31 Librarian to maintain an online repository for permanent digital access to government 
32 documents.
33 The committee has not taken a position on the substance of this bill.  By reporting this 
34 bill out the committee is not suggesting and does not intend to suggest that it agrees or 
35 disagrees with any aspect of this bill.  The committee is reporting the bill out for the sole 
36 purpose of having a bill printed that can be referred to the committee for an appropriate 
37 public hearing and subsequent processing in the normal course.  The committee is taking 
38 this action to ensure clarity and transparency in the legislative review of the proposals 
39 contained in the bill.1
