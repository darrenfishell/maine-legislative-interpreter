29SUMMARY
30 This bill makes the following changes to the State's uncontrolled hazardous substance 
31 sites law.
32 1.  It amends the definition of "hazardous substance" in the laws governing 
33 uncontrolled hazardous substance sites by including substances defined as hazardous 
34 substances or pollutants or contaminants under the federal Comprehensive Environmental 
35 Response, Compensation, and Liability Act of 1980.
36 2.  It provides a limited exemption from liability for publicly owned treatment works 
37 based on the contribution by the publicly owned treatment works of effluent or sewage 
38 sludge to an uncontrolled hazardous substance site.
39 3.  It provides a limited exemption for public water systems based on the contribution 
40 by the public water system of water treatment residuals to an uncontrolled hazardous 
41 substance site.1
