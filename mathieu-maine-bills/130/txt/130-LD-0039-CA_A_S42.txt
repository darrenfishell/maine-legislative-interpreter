13 SUMMARY 
14 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, changes the title of 
15 and replaces the bill. It amends the law regulating single-use carry-out bags at retail 
16 establishments to authorize local governments to adopt ordinances exempting from all 
17 requirements of that law the use and provision of single-use carry-out bags and recycled 
18 paper bags by retail establishments as long as those ordinances do not otherwise regulate 
19 the use and provision of such bags by retail establishments.
