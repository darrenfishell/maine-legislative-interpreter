6SUMMARY
7 This bill implements recommendations of the Right To Know Advisory Committee 
8 that were contained in the 2020 annual report.
9 The bill caps the fee to cover the cost of copying a public record at no more than 10¢ 
10 per page for a standard 8 1/2 inches by 11 inches black and white copy and clarifies that a 
11 per-page copy fee may not be charged for records provided electronically.
12 The bill adds a member to the Right To Know Advisory Committee who has legal or 
13 professional experience in the field of data and personal privacy, to be appointed by the 
14 Governor.
15 The bill makes the following changes to the requirements for training under the 
16 Freedom of Access Act.
17 1.  It clarifies that an official must complete training within 120 days of assuming the 
18 duties of the position.
19 2.  It expands the list of municipal officials required to complete training to include 
20 code enforcement officers, town managers and administrators and planning board members 
21 and clarifies that deputies of municipal clerks, treasurers, managers or administrators, 
22 assessors and code enforcement officers must also complete the training. 
23 3.  It clarifies that school superintendents, assistant superintendents and school board 
24 members are required to complete training.7
