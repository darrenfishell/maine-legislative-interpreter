2Sec. 1.  14 MRSA §1213, sub-§2, ¶D is enacted to read:
3 D.  A person who served in the United States Armed Forces and received an honorable 
4 discharge or general discharge under honorable conditions who does not wish to serve 
5 on a jury is excused from jury service.
6SUMMARY
7 This bill allows a veteran who does not wish to serve as a juror to be excused from jury 
8 duty.  The veteran must have received an honorable discharge or a general discharge under 
9 honorable conditions to qualify for the jury service excuse.
