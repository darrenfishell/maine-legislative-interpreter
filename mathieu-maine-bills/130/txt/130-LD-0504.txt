10SUMMARY
11 This resolve, which is a recommendation from the Working Group on Mental Health, 
12 directs the Department of Health and Human Services to amend, no later than October 1, 
13 2021, its guidelines for the mental health rehabilitation technician/community certification, 
14 also known as an MHRT/C certification, in order to allow an individual who has completed 
15 a 4-year postsecondary educational degree program or obtained a graduate degree in a 
16 mental health-related field to receive the MHRT/C certification notwithstanding any other 
17 guidelines for certification.11
