24Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
25 takes effect when approved.
26SUMMARY
27 Under current law, the Lucerne-in-Maine Village Corporation in the Town of Dedham 
28 is required to construct, operate and maintain a dam at the north end of Phillips Lake at no 
29 higher than 227 feet above sea level.  The bill removes that height restriction and instead 
30 requires the dam to be constructed, operated and maintained at a height adopted by the 
31 Lucerne-in-Maine Village Corporation at an annual meeting.
