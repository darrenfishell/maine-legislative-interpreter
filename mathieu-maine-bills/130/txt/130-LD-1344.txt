25SUMMARY
26 This bill allows the Department of Health and Human Services to impose 
27 administrative fines and license suspensions for violations of department rules regarding 
28 disease control and public health emergencies.  The department is also authorized to close 
29 a business or entity when that business or entity directly and repeatedly violates public 
30 health control measures during an extreme public health emergency.26
