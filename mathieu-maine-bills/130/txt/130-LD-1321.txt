27SUMMARY
28 This bill requires an insurance liability policy required for a motor vehicle to provide 
29 coverage for damage caused by that insured vehicle to a tractor lawfully operating on a 
30 public way.  The bill requires that motor vehicle drivers pass in a lane not adjacent to the 
31 tractor or otherwise pass the tractor at a careful and prudent speed.28
