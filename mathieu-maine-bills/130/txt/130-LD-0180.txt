8Sec. 3.  8 MRSA §223-A, sub-§4-B is enacted to read:
94-B.  Permit to store consumer fireworks.  The commissioner may issue a permit to 
10 store consumer fireworks under subsection 4-A to an applicant who:
11 A.  Is 21 years of age or older;
12 B.  Possesses the permits required under subsection 1;
13 C.  Complies with the provisions of subsection 4-A; and
14 D.  Has not been convicted of an offense or violated a state, federal or municipal law, 
15 rule or regulation involving fireworks or explosives within the 2 years prior to the 
16 application.
17 The term of a permit is one year from date of issuance.  The commissioner shall charge a 
18 fee of $125 for the initial permit issued to an applicant and $125 for each annual permit 
19 renewal.  A separate permit is required for each location at which an applicant seeks to 
20 store consumer fireworks.  Fees collected under this subsection must be deposited in a 
21 nonlapsing account of the Office of the State Fire Marshal to be used for the purpose of 
22 enforcing this section.
23SUMMARY
24 This bill brings the laws governing the storage of consumer fireworks into conformity 
25 with a recent Maine court decision by authorizing the Office of the State Fire Marshal to 
26 regulate consumer fireworks storage by those authorized by law to sell consumer fireworks.
