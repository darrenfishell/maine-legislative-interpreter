This amendment strikes the provisions in the committee amendment that established the Maine Public Safety Workers 
Loan Repayment Program within the Finance Authority of Maine and eliminates the one-time General Fund 
appropriation of $2,000,000 in fiscal year 2022-23 to provide student loan repayment ofup to $3,000 per year for a 
maximum of 3 years to eligible public safety workers in the State. 
As amended, the bill provides General Fund appropriations of $450,000 beginning in fiscal year 2022-23 to. the 
Department of Public Safety to distribute to Penobscot County for 3 Deputy Sheriff positions.
