Page 1 - 130LR2161(01)1Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2Sec. 1.  P&SL 1989, c. 51, as amended, is repealed.
3Sec. 2.  Town of St. Francis to absorb St. Francis Water District.  Title to all 
4 personal property, real property, assets, benefits and liabilities of the St. Francis Water 
5 District, including, without limitation, all lands, waters, water rights, buildings and 
6 improvements, reservoirs, appurtenant easements and other rights appurtenant to the real 
7 property, and all easements in gross and all other interests, rights and duties of the St. 
8 Francis Water District pass to and vest in the Town of St. Francis, and the Town of St. 
9 Francis shall assume, maintain and operate all personal property, real property, assets, 
10 benefits and liabilities of the St. Francis Water District.
11SUMMARY
12 This bill repeals Private and Special Law 1989, chapter 51, as amended, which created 
13 the St. Francis Water District, and directs the Town of St. Francis to assume, maintain and 
14 operate the property, benefits and liabilities of the district.12
