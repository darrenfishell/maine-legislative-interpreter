31-B.  Plans with no deductibles; cost-sharing parameters.  Notwithstanding any 
4 provision of this Title to the contrary, for plan years beginning on or after January 1, 2022, 
5 a carrier that offers individual and group health plans in this State shall make available at 
6 least 50% of the carrier's health plans in each market segment with cost-sharing parameters 
7 providing for no deductible applicable to prescription drugs covered under those health 
8 plans.  For those health plans offered pursuant to this subsection, the cost sharing paid by 
9 an enrollee for any given prescription drug at the point of sale may not exceed the amount 
10 of the copayment or coinsurance specified in the summary of benefits and coverage for that 
11 enrollee's health plan.
12SUMMARY
13 This bill requires carriers offering individual and group health plans in this State to 
14 make available at least 50% of health plans in each market segment without deductibles for 
15 prescription drugs covered under those health plans.  The bill also clarifies the treatment of 
16 copayments and coinsurance for prescription drugs under these plans.  The requirements 
17 of the bill apply to plan years beginning on or after January 1, 2022.13
