11SUMMARY
12 This bill creates a mandatory boating safety course requirement for individuals born 
13 after January 1, 2002 for the operation of motorboats propelled by machinery over 10 
14 horsepower on Maine waters beginning January 1, 2023.  The bill establishes a minimum 
15 age of 16 for operation of a motorboat propelled by machinery over 10 horsepower and 
16 requires the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to establish a program for boating 
17 safety education certification.  This bill also requires a mandatory boating safety course for 
18 an individual born after January 1, 2002 who operates a personal watercraft.1
