26SUMMARY
27 This bill amends the provision in law that requires law enforcement agencies to adopt 
28 written policies regarding procedures to deal with hate or bias crimes by adding a 
29 requirement that such a policy must require the law enforcement agency to select, assign 
30 and secure training for a civil rights officer and to provide the name of and contact 
31 information for the civil rights officer to the Attorney General.  The bill also provides 
32 funding for computer programming costs and related maintenance.26
