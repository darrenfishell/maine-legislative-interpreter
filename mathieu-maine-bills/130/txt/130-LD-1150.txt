21SUMMARY
22 This bill prohibits insurance carriers providing individual health plans or small group 
23 health plans from varying premium rates based on age, geographic location or tobacco use 
24 on or after January 1, 2022.  The bill also reduces the permitted variation based on group 
25 size to 1.5 to 1 for small group plans over time. 1
