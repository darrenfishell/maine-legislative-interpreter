This bill provides an income tax credit to employers who employ certain apprentices and will result in a reduction in 
General Fund revenue of $2,406,250 in fiscal year 2022-23 and a reduction in Local Government Fund revenue of 
$93,750 in fiscal year 2022-23. The bill includes a one-time General Fund appropriation of $33,000 in fiscal year 
2022-23 to the Department of Administrative and Financial Services for computer programming to add lines to the 
individual, fiduciary and corporate income tax returns.
