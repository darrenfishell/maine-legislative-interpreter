11Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
12Sec. 1.  20-A MRSA §13407, as enacted by PL 2019, c. 343, Pt. UU, §2, is amended 
13 to read:
14§13407.  Minimum salaries beginning in 2020-2021 school year
15 Each school administrative unit shall establish a minimum salary for certified teachers; 
16 librarians as defined in section 15672, subsection 17; and career and technical education 
17 teachers as follows:
181.  School year 2020-2021.  For the school year starting after June 30, 2020, the 
19 minimum salary is $35,000;
202.  School year 2021-2022.  For the school year starting after June 30, 2021, the 
21 minimum salary for certified teachers, librarians and career and technical education 
22 teachers is $37,500; and
233.  School years beginning in or after 2022.  For the school year starting after June 
24 30, 2022, and in each subsequent school year, the minimum salary for certified teachers, 
25 librarians and career and technical education teachers is $40,000.
26 A school administrative unit shall provide to the department annually on or before 
27 October 1st the number of certified teachers , librarians and career and technical education 
28 teachers eligible for incremental salary increases as defined in section 15689, subsection 
29 7‑A, paragraph A.
30Sec. 2.  20-A MRSA §15689, sub-§7-A, as amended by PL 2019, c. 616, Pt. C, §6, 
31 is further amended to read:
327-A.  Adjustment for minimum teacher and librarian salary.  Beginning in fiscal 
33 year 2020-21, the commissioner shall, in accordance with this subsection, increase the state 
34 share of the total allocation to a qualifying school administrative unit by an amount 
35 necessary to achieve the minimum salary for certified teachers, librarians and career and 
36 technical education teachers established in section 13407.
37 A.  As used in this subsection, unless the context otherwise indicates, "qualifying 
38 school administrative unit" means a school administrative unit that the commissioner 
39 determines to have a locally established salary schedule with a minimum teacher salary 
40 of less than $40,000 in school year 2019-2020.  As used in this subsection, unless the 
41 context otherwise indicates, "incremental salary increases" means the incremental 
42 increases in the salaries of certified teachers , librarians and career and technical

44 2019-2020 necessary to meet the minimum salary requirements of section 13407 from 
45 fiscal year 2020-21 to fiscal year 2022-23.
4 B.  The commissioner shall allocate the funds appropriated by the Legislature in 
5 accordance with the following.
6 (1)  The amount of increased funds provided to qualifying school administrative 
7 units under this subsection must be the amount necessary to fund the incremental 
8 salary increases specified in this subsection.
9 (2)  The number of certified teachers , librarians and career and technical education 
10 teachers eligible for incremental salary increases in a qualifying school 
11 administrative unit for a fiscal year must be based on the information supplied to 
12 the department pursuant to section 13407 in that fiscal year.
13 (3)  The increased funds provided under this subsection must be issued to 
14 qualifying school administrative units as an adjustment to the state school subsidy 
15 for distribution to the certified teachers , librarians and career and technical 
16 education teachers. Qualifying school administrative units shall use the payments 
17 provided under this subsection to provide salary adjustments to those certified 
18 teachers, librarians and career and technical education teachers eligible for 
19 incremental salary increases.  The department shall collect the necessary data to 
20 allow the funds to be included in a qualifying school administrative unit's monthly 
21 subsidy payments beginning no later than February 1st of each fiscal year.
22 (4)  Funding for incremental salary increases in fiscal year 2020-21 must be based 
23 on data submitted to the department and certified by school administrative units as 
24 of October 1, 2019.
25Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
26 takes effect when approved.
