8SUMMARY
9 This resolve directs the Department of Public Safety to study recent incidents involving 
10 school buses and school bus stops.  It directs the department to report on its findings to the 
11 Joint Standing Committee on Criminal Justice and Public Safety by December 1, 2021.  It 
12 also directs the joint standing committee to report out a bill related to the report to the 
13 Second Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.9
