8SUMMARY
9 This bill allows bona fide nonprofit organizations, agricultural societies and volunteer 
10 fire departments, when conducting an authorized raffle, to offer raffle tickets for purchase 
11 by mail and requires the organization to ensure that any person purchasing a raffle ticket 
12 by mail is at least 21 years of age.9
