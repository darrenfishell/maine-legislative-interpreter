22SUMMARY
23 This bill requires the Director of the Maine Center for Disease Control and Prevention 
24 within the Department of Health and Human Services to include in the director's annual 
25 report to the Legislature, with the assistance of the Commissioner of Public Safety, public 
26 health data concerning firearm fatalities separated by homicides, including the number of 
27 domestic violence homicides, suicides and accidents, further separated by the ages of the 
28 victims of each category, and the type, make and model of the firearm used, and 
29 hospitalizations that occurred as a result of a firearm that did not result in death and the 
30 type, make and model of firearm used, and to make the report publicly available.23
