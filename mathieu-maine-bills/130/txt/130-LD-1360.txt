22SUMMARY
23 This bill:
24 1.  Provides funding to clear the MaineCare Section 29 waiting list through the end of 
25 fiscal year 2021; and
26 2.  Provides funding to reduce waiting lists for MaineCare Section 21 services by 
27 providing services to those individuals who are designated Priority 2 and are receiving no 
28 other MaineCare home and community-based or support services.1
