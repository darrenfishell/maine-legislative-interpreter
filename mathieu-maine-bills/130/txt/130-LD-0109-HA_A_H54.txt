22 Current law provides that a candidate's name listed on the ballot must be the one 
23 approved by the Probate Cou1t or, in the absence of an applicable court order~ the name 
24 consistently used by the candidate during the past 2 years in filings with governmental 
25 agencies and in the transaction of public business. This amendment eliminates the language 
26 that provides a 2-year look-back period. It also eliminates the provisions pertaining to 
27 nicknames.

Removal of Potential Ballot Costs 
This amendment removes the provision providing for a candidate to use nicknames on ballots. The incremental 
impact is to remove the potential ballot costs for additional ballots.
