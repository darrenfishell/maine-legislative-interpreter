8SUMMARY
9 This bill removes the designation as a Class E crime of the following: operating with 
10 expired motor vehicle registrations, failure to change out-of-state registrations and 
11 operating with expired 14-day registration plates.  It requires a warning to be issued for all 
12 traffic stops for expired registrations.  It requires registrations to be obtained or changed 
13 within 10 business days of a warning having been issued.  It establishes a $500 fine for 2nd 
14 and subsequent offenses.  It also requires the Secretary of State to establish a notification 
15 system to inform individuals of registrations that are going to expire within the next 30 
16 days.9
