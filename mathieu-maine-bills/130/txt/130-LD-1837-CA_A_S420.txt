9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" A" to S.P. 655, L.D. 1837, "An Act To Clarify the 
10 Appeals Process for Decisions Related to the Maine Health Insurance Marketplace" 
11 Amend the bill by inserting after the title and before the enacting clause the following: 
12 'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
13 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and 
14 Whereas, this legislation has been submitted by the Department of Health and Human 
15 Services to clarify the appeals process for decisions of the Maine Health fusurance 
16 Marketplace; and 
17 Whereas, enacting this legislation as soon as possible will provide clarity for 
18 consumers who want to appeal decisions of the Maine Health Insurance Marketplace that 
19 affect their eligibility for health care coverage; and 
20 Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
21 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
22 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
23 therefore,' 
24 Amend the bill by inserting after section 1 the following: 
25 'Sec. 2. Application. This Act applies to any decision of the Maine Health Insurance 
26 Marketplace made prior to, on or after the effective date of this Act that may be appealed 
27 to the Department of Health and Human Services' administrative hearings unit.' 
28 Amend the bill by adding before the summary the following: 
29 'Emergency clause. In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
3 0 takes effect when approved.' 
31 Amend the bill byrelettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section 
32 number to read consecutively.
