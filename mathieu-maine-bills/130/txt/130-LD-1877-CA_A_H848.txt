SUMMARY 
This amendment is the majority report of the committee. It clarifies that "serious 
mental illness" does not include opioid disorder, tobacco use disorder or any other 
substance use disorder for the purposes of this legislation. It clarifies that step therapy 
includes a protocol or tiering system. It specifies that the prohibition on required prior 
authorization or step therapy for serious mental illness applies only to assessment or 
treatment of mental illness in adults. The amendment includes a requirement that the 
implementation must be consistent with the requirements of the Medicaid drug rebate 
program and that implementation requires approval by the federal Department of Health 
and Human Services, Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services before taking effect. 
The amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section.
