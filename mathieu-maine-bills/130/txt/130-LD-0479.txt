6SUMMARY
7 This bill provides that a foreign national may not:
8 1. Make, directly or indirectly, a contribution to influence the nomination or election 
9 of any person to state, county or municipal office or a contribution to a party committee;
10 2. Make, directly or indirectly, an expenditure or independent expenditure for an 
11 electioneering communication or to design, produce or disseminate a communication for 
12 the purpose of influencing the nomination or election of any person to state, county or 
13 municipal office;
14 3. Make, directly or indirectly, an expenditure for the purpose of influencing the 
15 initiation or outcome of a state or local ballot measure; or
16 4. Disseminate or republish campaign materials authorized by a candidate or a 
17 candidate's authorized political committee.
18 The bill provides that a person may not:
19 1. Solicit, accept or receive, or provide substantial assistance in the making, 
20 solicitation, acceptance or receipt of, a contribution that a foreign national is prohibited 
21 from making under the bill; or
22 2. Accept, or provide substantial assistance in the making or acceptance of, an 
23 expenditure or independent expenditure that a foreign national is prohibited from making 
24 under the bill.
25 The bill directs each television or radio broadcasting station, provider of cable or 
26 satellite television and Internet platform to establish due diligence policies to prevent the 
27 distribution of communications for which foreign nationals have made prohibited 
28 expenditures or independent expenditures and further directs an Internet platform to, upon 
29 discovery, immediately remove any such communications from its platform.
30 Finally, the bill requires, whenever a foreign national expends money to finance a 
31 communication to influence government officials or the public on issues of state or local 
32 policy or foreign relations, that the communication include a clear and conspicuous 
33 statement naming the foreign national as a sponsor of the communication.7
