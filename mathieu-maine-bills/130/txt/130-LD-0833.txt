37SUMMARY
38 Public Law 2019, chapter 154 removes the exemption from immunization 
39 requirements based on religious belief for students in elementary, secondary and 
40 postsecondary schools and employees of nursery schools and certain health care facilities.  
41 This bill reinstates the religious exemption.38
