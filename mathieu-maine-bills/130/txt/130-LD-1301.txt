20SUMMARY
21 This bill directs the Department of Health and Human Services to coordinate and 
22 arrange for transitional housing and services including job training and health care services 
23 for persons in need of transitional housing, including those experiencing abuse, dangerous 
24 living conditions, economic insecurity due to divorce or separation, chronic homelessness, 
25 substance use disorder or mental disorders, including post-traumatic stress disorder, in 
26 every county of the State or by region if all counties are provided for.  It also provides an 
27 ongoing appropriation of $8,000,000 beginning in fiscal year 2022-23.20
