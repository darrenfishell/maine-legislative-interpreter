2targeted case management services.  Resolved:  That the Department of Health and 
3 Human Services shall amend its rule Chapter 101:  MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter 
4 II, Section 13.03-4 to remove from the eligibility requirements for targeted case 
5 management services for adults with substance use disorder the provision regarding 
6 pregnancy, living with minor children and intravenous drug use.  Rules adopted pursuant 
7 to this section are routine technical rules pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, 
8 chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
9SUMMARY
10 This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rule 
11 Chapter 101:  MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter II, Section 13.03-4 to remove from the 
12 eligibility requirements for targeted case management services for adults with substance 
13 use disorder the provision regarding pregnancy, living with minor children and intravenous 
14 drug use.
