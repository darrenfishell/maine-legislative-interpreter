3ยง752-F.  Perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl substances
4 An action arising out of any harm or injury caused by a perfluoroalkyl or 
5 polyfluoroalkyl substance must be commenced within 6 years after the date the plaintiff 
6 discovers or reasonably should have discovered such harm or injury. For the purposes of 
7 this section, "perfluoroalkyl or polyfluoroalkyl substance" means any member of the class 
8 of fluorinated organic chemicals containing at least one fully fluorinated carbon atom.  This 
9 section does not affect application of notice requirements for filing under section 8107 or 
10 the limitation on actions against a government entity under section 8110.
11SUMMARY
12 This bill provides that an action arising out of any harm or injury caused by a 
13 perfluoroalkyl or polyfluoroalkyl substance must be commenced within 6 years after the 
14 plaintiff discovers or should have discovered such harm or injury.  It does not alter the 
15 notice period or limitation period applicable to claims against a government entity.
