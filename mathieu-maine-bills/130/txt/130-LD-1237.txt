9SUMMARY
10 This bill allows the Governor to declare a state of emergency for the sole purpose of 
11 requesting the President of the United States to declare a major disaster for part or all of 
12 Maine in order to make available federal aid.  If the Governor declares this type of state of 
13 emergency, the Governor is prohibited, pursuant to that declaration, from using the same 
14 powers granted to the Governor under a state of emergency declared because of a disaster 
15 or civil emergency pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 37-B, section 742,
