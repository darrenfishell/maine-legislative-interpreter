10Sec. 1.  Director of Bureau of Parks and Lands authorized to lease certain 
11land within the public reserved lands in Township 11, Range 4 WELS in 
12Aroostook County.  Resolved: That the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands 
13 within the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may lease to Aroostook 
14 Technologies, Inc., on such terms and conditions as the director may direct, certain land 
15 within the public reserved lands in Township 11, Range 4 WELS in Aroostook County 
16 described in the Telecommunications Facilities Lease entered into between the bureau and 
17 Aroostook Technologies, Inc., in effect beginning August 30, 2019, to be used to install 
18 and maintain a radio repeater station consisting of solar panels and an antenna attached to 
19 an existing fire tower for the purpose of repeating and relaying a radio signal.
20SUMMARY
21 This resolve authorizes the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the 
22 Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to lease to Aroostook Technologies, 
23 Inc. certain land within the public reserved lands in Township 11, Range 4 WELS in 
24 Aroostook County to be used to install and maintain a radio repeater station consisting of 
25 solar panels and an antenna attached to an existing fire tower for the purpose of repeating 
26 and relaying a radio signal. 21
