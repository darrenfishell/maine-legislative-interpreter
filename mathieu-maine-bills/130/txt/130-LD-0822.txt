18Sec. 2.  22 MRSA §4301, sub-§1, as amended by PL 2019, c. 126, §2, is further 
19 amended to read:
201.  Basic necessities.  "Basic necessities" means food, seeds, potable water, clothing, 
21 shelter, fuel, electricity, nonelective medical services as recommended by a physician, 
22 nonprescription drugs, telephone where it is necessary for medical reasons and any other 
23 commodity or service determined essential by the overseer in accordance with the 
24 municipality's ordinance and this chapter.  "Basic necessities" do not include security 
25 deposits for rental property, except for emergency purposes.  For the purposes of this 
26 subsection, "emergency purposes" means any situation in which no other permanent 
27 lodging is available unless a security deposit is paid.
28SUMMARY
29 This bill adds seeds to the definitions of "necessities" in the law prohibiting profiteering 
30 during supply disruptions and "basic necessities" in the law governing municipal general 
31 assistance.29
