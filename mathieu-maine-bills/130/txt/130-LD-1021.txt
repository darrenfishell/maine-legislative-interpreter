Page 1 - 130LR0461(01)1Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2Sec. 1.  17-A MRSA §210-A, sub-§2, ¶A, as amended by PL 2007, c. 685, §1, is 
3 further amended to read:
4 A.  "Course of conduct" means 2 or more acts, including but not limited to acts in which 
5 the actor, by any action, method, device or means, including use of an unmanned aerial 
6 vehicle, directly or indirectly follows, monitors, tracks, observes, surveils, threatens, 
7 harasses or communicates to or about a person or interferes with a person’s property.  
8 "Course of conduct" also includes, but is not limited to, threats implied by conduct and 
9 gaining unauthorized access to personal, medical, financial or other identifying or 
10 confidential information.
11Sec. 2.  17-A MRSA §210-A, sub-§2, ¶F is enacted to read:
12 F.  "Unmanned aerial vehicle" means an aircraft operated without a physical human 
13 presence within or on the aircraft.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill adds the use of unmanned aerial vehicles, also known as drones, to the laws 
16 governing stalking in the Maine Criminal Code.15
