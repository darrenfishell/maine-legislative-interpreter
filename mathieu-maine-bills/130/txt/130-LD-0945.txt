49.  Notice of amendments to provider agreements.  A carrier offering or renewing a 
5 health plan in this State shall notify a participating provider of a proposed amendment to a 
6 provider agreement at least 60 days prior to the amendment's proposed effective date.  If 
7 an amendment that has substantial impact on the rights and obligations of providers is made 
8 to a manual, policy or procedure document referenced in the provider agreement, such as 
9 material changes to fee schedules or material changes to procedural coding rules specified 
10 in the manual, policy or procedure document, the carrier shall provide 60 days' notice to 
11 the provider.  After the 60-day notice period has expired, the amendment to a manual, 
12 policy or procedure document becomes effective and binding on both the carrier and the 
13 provider subject to any applicable termination provisions in the provider agreement, except 
14 that the carrier and provider may mutually agree to waive the 60-day notice requirement.  
15 This subsection may not be construed to limit the ability of a carrier and provider to 
16 mutually agree to the proposed change at any time after the provider has received notice of 
17 the proposed amendment.  The notice required by this subsection must be provided in 
18 writing to each provider, and the notice must be the only subject of the written 
19 communication.
