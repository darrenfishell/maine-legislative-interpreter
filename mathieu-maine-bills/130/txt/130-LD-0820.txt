2working group.  Resolved: That the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and 
3 Forestry shall convene a working group of stakeholders to develop plans and consider ways 
4 to discourage the use of land of higher agricultural value and encourage the use of more 
5 marginal agricultural lands when siting a solar array.  The department shall submit its report 
6 and recommendations, including any suggested legislation, to the Joint Standing 
7 Committee on Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry; the Joint Standing Committee on 
8 Energy, Utilities and Technology; and the Joint Standing Committee on Environment and 
9 Natural Resources no later than January 14, 2022.
10SUMMARY
11 This resolve directs the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to 
12 convene a working group of stakeholders to develop plans and consider ways to discourage 
13 the use of land of higher agricultural value and encourage the use of more marginal 
14 agricultural lands when siting a solar array.  The department must submit its report and 
15 recommendations, including any suggested legislation, to the Joint Standing Committee on 
16 Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry; the Joint Standing Committee on Energy, Utilities 
17 and Technology; and the Joint Standing Committee on Environment and Natural Resources 
18 no later than January 14, 2022.11
