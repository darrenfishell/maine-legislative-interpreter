20 takes effect when approved.
21SUMMARY
22 This bill allows the sale, distribution and display of a commemorative vehicle 
23 registration plate celebrating the State's bicentennial until December 31, 2021.  It allows 
24 members of the Maine Bicentennial Commission to continue to serve in 2021 and requires 
25 the commission to make a progress report by December 1, 2021.
