SUMMARY 
2 . This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, removes the language 
3 from the bill that relates to Cub Care coverage for persons under 21 years of age who are 
4 noncitizens. It also keeps the maximum eligibility level for family income under the Cub 
5 Care program at 200% of the federal poverty level, which is the amount established in 
6 current law. The amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section.
