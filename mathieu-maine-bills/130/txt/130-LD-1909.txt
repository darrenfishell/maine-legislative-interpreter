13Sec. 1.  22 MRSA §1341, sub-§1-A is enacted to read:
141-A.  Hypodermic apparatus distribution.  A hypodermic apparatus exchange 
15 program may not limit the number of hypodermic apparatuses given to participants of the 
16 program at any time or require the return of a used hypodermic apparatus as a condition for 
17 receiving a hypodermic apparatus.

23SUMMARY
24 This bill prohibits a hypodermic apparatus exchange program from limiting 
25 distribution of hypodermic apparatuses to program participants or requiring the exchange 
26 of used hypodermic apparatuses.24
