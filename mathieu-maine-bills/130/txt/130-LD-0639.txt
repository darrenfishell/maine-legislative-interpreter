3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill proposes to make changes to the certification requirements for school 
6 counselors to require that applicants demonstrate to the satisfaction of the Department of 
7 Education successful completion of a minimum of 6 hours of course work in family or 
8 intimate partner violence, including course work in spousal or partner abuse that addresses 
9 screening, referral and intervention strategies, including knowledge of community 
10 resources, cultural factors, evidence-based risk assessment and same-gender abuse 
11 dynamics.  The requirements must be based on similar course work requirements for 
12 psychologists, clinical professional counselors and clinical social workers.  The Maine 
13 Coalition to End Domestic Violence must be consulted in the development of the course 
14 work.4
