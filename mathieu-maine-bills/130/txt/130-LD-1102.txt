10SUMMARY
11 This bill amends the law governing preliminary injunctions issued in judicial 
12 separation and divorce actions.  It requires the preliminary injunction to provide more 
13 accountability about financial transactions of the parties and prohibits both parties from 
14 making changes to life, casualty, automobile or health insurance or making any 
15 withdrawals from retirement or other plans or accounts.  It also prohibits both parties from 
16 intentionally or knowingly damaging or destroying property, including electronically 
17 stored materials or data.  It prohibits both parties from opening or diverting mail addressed 
18 to the other party.  It prohibits both parties from signing or endorsing the other party's name 
19 on any negotiable instrument, check or draft.1
