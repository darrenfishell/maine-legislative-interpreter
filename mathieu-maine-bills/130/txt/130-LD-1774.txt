18SUMMARY
19 Under the probate code, an adult subject to guardianship is entitled to retain counsel or 
20 seek court-appointed counsel in a proceeding to terminate a guardianship.  This bill 
21 specifies that an adult subject to guardianship is entitled to retain counsel and to 
22 communicate with other advocates and the court for any reason.19
