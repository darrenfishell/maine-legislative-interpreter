12SUMMARY
13 Current law provides that if a person, firm, corporation or association operating a 
14 residential care facility, assisted housing program, drug treatment center, children's home, 
15 child placing agency, child care facility or adult day care program has received and 
16 maintained accreditation from a national accreditation body approved by the department, 
17 the entity must be deemed in compliance with state licensing rules. This bill provides 
18 instead that the entity may be determined by the department to be in compliance with state 
19 licensing rules. 13
