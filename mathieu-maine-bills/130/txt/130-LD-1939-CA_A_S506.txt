23 SUMMARY 
24 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, replaces the bill and 
25 amends the crime of obstructing government administration within the Maine Criminal 
26 Code to clarify that the term "public servant," as used in the description of that crime, means 
27 a public servant as defined in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 17-A, section 2, subsection 
28 21; a superintendent as defined in Title 20-A, section 1, subsection 39; any member of a 
29 school board as defined in Title 20-A, section 1, subsection 28; a principal as defined in 
30 Title 20-A, section 1, subsection 21; a teacher; school staff; and any other persons 
31 employed by or serving in a school administrative unit as defined in Title 20-A, section 1, 
32 subsection 26.
