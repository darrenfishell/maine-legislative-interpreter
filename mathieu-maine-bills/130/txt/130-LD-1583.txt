26SUMMARY
27 This bill, to enhance the continued tradition of public use of private property in the 
28 State, directs the Office of Information Technology, Office of Geographic Information 
29 Systems to create and conduct a pilot program in Oxford County that uses geographic 
30 information system technology to assist landowners in identifying their property for a 
31 specified public use and allows the engagement of public and private entities to develop 
32 software to carry out the program's purpose.27
