10SUMMARY
11 Under current law a person who violates a protective order issued by a Maine court 
12 through conduct that is reckless and that creates a substantial risk of death or serious bodily 
13 injury to the person named in the protective order or who assaults that person commits a 
14 Class C crime.  This bill extends the prohibited conduct to a violation of an order that is 
15 similar to a protective order and that is issued by a court of the United States or of another 
16 state, territory, commonwealth or tribe.
