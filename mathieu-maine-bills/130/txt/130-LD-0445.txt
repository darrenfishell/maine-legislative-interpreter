11§11452-A.  Deer baiting
121.  Placing of deer bait.  During an open season on deer, a person may not place bait 
13 to hunt deer unless:
14 A.  The person holds a valid license to hunt deer issued under section 11109 and a 
15 permit to place bait to hunt deer issued by the department in accordance with rules 
16 adopted by the department;
17 B.  In addition to the fee required for a license under section 11109, the person pays a 
18 fee of $5 to the department for each bait site established by the person; and
19 C.  The person complies with the provisions of section 11227, except as otherwise 
20 provided in this section.
212.  Hunting over bait.  A person may not hunt over bait unless the person complies 
22 with the provisions of subsection 1.
233.  Hunting over another's bait.  Notwithstanding section 11227, subsection 3, a 
24 person may not hunt at a bait site established by another person.
254.  Penalty.  A person who violates this section commits a civil violation for which a 
26 fine of not less than $500 nor more than $1,000 may be adjudged.
275.  Rules.  The department shall adopt rules to implement this section.  Rules adopted 
28 pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, 
29 subchapter 2-A.
30 For the purposes of this section, "bait" has the same meaning as in section 11227, 
31 subsection 1, paragraph A and "bait site" has the same meaning as in section 11227, 
32 subsection 1, paragraph B.
33SUMMARY
34 This bill authorizes the baiting of deer by licensed hunters.34
