15Sec. 4.  20-A MRSA §9005 is enacted to read:
16§9005.  Regional Fire Service Training Fund
171.  Fund established.  The Regional Fire Service Training Fund, referred to in this 
18 section as "the fund," is established under the Maine Fire Service Institute within the Maine 
19 Community College System as a nonlapsing fund to provide funds to municipalities for 
20 regional fire service training in the State.
212.  Grant program.  The Maine Fire Protection Services Commission, referred to in 
22 this section as "the commission" and established pursuant to Title 5, section 12004-J, 
23 subsection 12, shall establish criteria to award grants to municipalities from the fund for 
24 the purpose of funding regional fire service training for municipal firefighters and public 
25 safety employees.   The commission may adopt rules to implement this subsection. Rules 
26 adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 
27 375, subchapter 2-A.
283.  Procedure.  Municipalities may apply to the commission for grants. The 
29 commission shall evaluate each application pursuant to the criteria developed in subsection 
30 2 and, to the extent funds are available, shall award grants from the fund to support regional 
31 fire service training programs.
324.  Payment of grants.  Upon the award of a grant by the commission, the commission 
33 shall direct the Maine Fire Service Institute within the Maine Community College System 
34 to make the payment of the grant award to the municipality from the fund.
