Page 1 - 130LR0659(01)1Sec. 1.  Department of Education truancy study.  Resolved:  That the 
2 Department of Education shall conduct a study on truancy trends in the State and develop 
3 recommendations for supporting school administrative units in increasing student 
4 attendance and reducing truancy.  In conducting the study under this section, the 
5 department shall seek input from the Maine School Management Association, the Maine 
6 Principals' Association and the Maine Education Association.
7Sec. 2.  Report.  Resolved:  That, by January 4, 2022, the Department of Education 
8 shall submit a report, including recommended legislation, based on its evaluation in section 
9 1 to the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs.  The Joint Standing 
10 Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs is authorized to submit legislation related to 
11 the report to the Second Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.
12SUMMARY
13 This resolve directs the Department of Education to conduct a study on truancy trends 
14 in the State and develop recommendations for supporting school administrative units in 
15 increasing student attendance and reducing truancy and to submit a report with suggested 
16 legislation by January 4, 2022 to the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural 
17 Affairs, which may submit legislation to the Second Regular Session of the 130th 
18 Legislature.
