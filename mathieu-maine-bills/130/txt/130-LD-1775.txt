11SUMMARY
12 This resolve directs the Department of Education to develop a comprehensive system 
13 to support school districts in seeking reimbursement for MaineCare-eligible services.  The 
14 department is required to submit a report with suggested legislation by November 2, 2022 
15 to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over education 
16 matters, which may submit legislation to the First Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.12
