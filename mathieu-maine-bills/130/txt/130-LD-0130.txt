21SUMMARY
22 This bill allows the Secretary of State to refuse to issue or to recall a vanity registration 
23 plate that falsely suggests an association with a government or government agency; is 
24 vulgar or obscene or constitutes racial or ethnic epithets; connotes breasts, genitalia, the 
25 pubic area or buttocks or relates to sexual or eliminatory functions; refers to any intoxicant 
26 or drug; refers to the use, nonuse, distribution or sale of an intoxicant or drug; refers to a 
27 user, nonuser or purveyor of an intoxicant or drug; refers to gender, gender identity, sexual 
28 orientation or disability status; suggests a privilege not given by law in the State; or forms 
29 a slang term, abbreviation, phonetic spelling or mirror image of a word or term otherwise 
30 prohibited.
