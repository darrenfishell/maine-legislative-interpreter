24 adopted by the ambulance service, a nontransporting emergency medical service as defined 
25 in Title 32, section 83, subsection 14 that possesses a current and valid license issued by 
26 the Medical Direction and Practices Board pursuant to Title 32, section 86, in accordance 
27 with policies adopted by the nontransporting emergency medical service, and a municipal 
28 firefighter as defined in Title 30-A, section 3151, subsection 2, in accordance with policies

33 established in Title 32, section 83, subsection 16-B.  The Medical Direction and Practices 
34 Board shall establish medical training protocols for law enforcement officers, corrections 
35 officers, emergency medical services persons, ambulance services, nontransporting 
36 emergency medical services and municipal firefighters pursuant to this subsection.
37SUMMARY
38 This bill authorizes emergency medical services persons, ambulance services and 
39 nontransporting emergency medical services to administer intranasal naloxone 
40 hydrochloride if they have received medical training in accordance with protocols adopted 
41 by the Medical Direction and Practices Board.38
