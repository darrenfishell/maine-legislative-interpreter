32-G.  Motor vehicle speed measurement.  The board shall include in the basic law 
4 enforcement training course, in both a classroom and field setting, a block of instruction on 
5 the use of all means of speed measurement authorized in Title 29-A, section 2075, 
6 subsection 4, including training from the manufacturer of every speed measurement device

11SUMMARY
12 This bill requires the Maine Criminal Justice Academy to provide in the basic law 
13 enforcement training course a block of instruction on the use of speed measurement devices 
14 and methods, including training from the manufacturer of every speed measurement device 
15 authorized for use in the State, and requires a law enforcement officer to be trained in a 
16 speed measurement method or device in the basic law enforcement training course or in-
17 service law enforcement training before using the device or performing the method to 
18 measure the speed of motorists.12
