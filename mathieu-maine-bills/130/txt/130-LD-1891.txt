2Sec. 1.  Grants for well water abatement.  The Maine State Housing Authority 
3 shall provide grants to eligible owners of single-family homes or landlords with private 
4 well water that shows evidence of contamination.  For purposes of this section, 
5 "contamination" means that the level of microorganisms, disinfectants, disinfection by-
6 products, inorganic chemicals, organic chemicals or radionuclides exceeds maximum 
7 contaminant levels for public water systems established by the national primary drinking 
8 water regulations, 40 Code of Federal Regulations, Part 141, or similar standards applicable 
9 to public water systems in the State pursuant to state law or rule, and "private well water" 
10 means water from a private drinking water well as defined in the Maine Revised Statutes, 
11 Title 38, section 1392, subsection 8.
