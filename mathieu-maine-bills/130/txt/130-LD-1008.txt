12Sec. 2.  37-B MRSA §783-A is enacted to read:
13§783-A.  Joint use entity liaisons
141.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
15 following terms have the following meanings.
16 A.  "Facilities" has the same meaning as in Title 35-A, section 2502, subsection 3.
17 B.  "Joint use entity" has the same meaning as in Title 35-A, section 711, subsection 7, 
18 paragraph B.
192.  Joint use entity; liaison designation.  A joint use entity shall designate, in each 
20 county where the joint use entity has facilities, a permanent liaison with the applicable 
21 county emergency management agency that will be responsible for assisting the agency 
22 with the coordination of actions during a disaster or civil emergency and shall communicate 
23 to the agency the contact information for the liaison.
24SUMMARY
25 This bill requires a joint use entity, which includes a public utility, voice service 
26 provider, dark fiber provider, wholesale or retail competitive local exchange carrier, cable 
27 television system, unlit fiber provider, telecommunications service provider or information 
28 service provider, to designate a permanent liaison with each county emergency 
29 management agency in the State in counties in which the joint use entity has facilities in 
30 order to assist in the coordination of efforts during a disaster or civil emergency.25
