2 held by the State for conservation or recreation purposes may not be reduced or its uses 
3 substantially altered except on the vote of 2/3 of all members elected to each House.
4Whereas, the real estate authorized for conveyance by this legislation is under the 
5 designations described in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 12, section 598-A; and
6Whereas, the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the Department of 
7 Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may sell or exchange lands with the approval of 
8 the Legislature in accordance with the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 12, sections 1814, 
9 1837 and 1851; now, therefore, be it
10Sec. 1.  Director of Bureau of Parks and Lands directed to convey certain 
11land in Township 12, Range 13 WELS in exchange for other land in Township 
1211, Range 10 WELS.  Resolved:  That the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands 
13 within the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry shall by quitclaim deed 
14 without covenant convey to Clayton Lake Woodlands Holdings, LLC all of the State's 
15 common and undivided interest in T.12 R.13 WELS, which is approximately 6,000 acres, 
16 in exchange for all right, title and interest in and to approximately 6,000 acres in T.11 R.10 
17 WELS or T.12 R.13 WELS, which is currently owned by Clayton Lake Woodlands 
18 Holdings, LLC.  The resulting state fee ownership is subject to the tax applied to forest 
19 land in Aroostook County under the Maine Tree Growth Tax Law.
20SUMMARY
21 This bill directs the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the Department 
22 of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to enter into a land exchange with Clayton Lake 
23 Woodlands Holdings, LLC to separate common and undivided interest in a parcel of land 
24 in Aroostook County.21
