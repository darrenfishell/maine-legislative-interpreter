22SUMMARY
23 This bill raises the upper age limit from 20 years of age to 22 years of age for voluntary 
24 participation in extended care for persons who attained 18 years of age while in the care 
25 and custody of the State.  It makes a corresponding change in the transition grant program 
26 for individuals exiting extended care and pursuing a postsecondary education by changing 
27 the requirement that to be eligible to participate in the program an individual must be at 
28 least 21 years of age to a requirement that an individual must be at least 23 years of age.23
