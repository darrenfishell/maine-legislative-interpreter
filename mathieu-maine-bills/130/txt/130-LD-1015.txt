41.  Prohibition.  A person may not hunt with ammunition that contains lead.

6 A.  A person who violates subsection 1 for the first time must be issued a written

8 B.  A person who violates subsection 1 after having been issued a written warning

12SUMMARY
13 This bill prohibits the use of ammunition that contains lead when hunting in this State.  
14 Individuals who violate this prohibition receive a warning for the first offense.  Individuals 
15 who repeatedly violate this prohibition commit a civil violation resulting in a $100 fine for 
16 a 2nd offense, with the penalty increasing by $50 for each subsequent offense.13
