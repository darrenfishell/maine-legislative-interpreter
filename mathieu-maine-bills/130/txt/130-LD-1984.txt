9Sec. 1. Director of Bureau of Parks and Lands authorized to lease certain 
10land within Aroostook State Park in Presque Isle.  Resolved:  That the Director 
11 of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and 
12 Forestry is authorized to lease approximately 1/4 of an acre of land at Aroostook State Park 
13 in Presque Isle to the Federal Aviation Administration within the United States Department 
14 of Transportation for the purpose of continuing to provide an outer marker light and 
15 compass locator elements of the airplane landing guidance system for Presque Isle 
16 International Airport in Aroostook County.
