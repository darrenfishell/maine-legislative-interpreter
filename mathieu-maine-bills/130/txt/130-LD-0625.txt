10 determine if additional action is necessary.

13 section 1 to the Joint Standing Committee on Veterans and Legal Affairs.  The committee 
14 is authorized to submit legislation related to the report to the Second Regular Session of 
15 the 130th Legislature.
16SUMMARY
17 This resolve directs the Adjutant General to conduct a review of the implementation of 
18 the recommendations made to the Joint Standing Committee on Veterans and Legal Affairs 
19 in the report submitted pursuant to Resolve 2013, chapter 66 and identify any crucial needs 
20 and lapses in responding to and preventing military sexual trauma in the Maine National 
21 Guard and determine if additional action is necessary.  The Adjutant General is required to 
22 submit a report on the review by November 1, 2021 to the Joint Standing Committee on 
23 Veterans and Legal Affairs, which may submit legislation related to the report to the 
24 Second Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.17
