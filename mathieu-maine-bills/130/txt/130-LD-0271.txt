Page 1 - 130LR0893(01)1Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2Sec. 1.  Wage increase for support workers in Department of Corrections.  
3 Notwithstanding any law to the contrary, beginning October 1, 2021, staff in support, 
4 maintenance and other nonsupervisory positions, but not including guards, in correctional 
5 facilities maintained by the Department of Corrections must be given a $2 per hour increase 
6 in their wages in effect on September 30, 2021.
7SUMMARY
8 This bill requires staff in support, maintenance and other nonsupervisory positions, but 
9 not including guards, in correctional facilities maintained by the Department of Corrections 
10 to be given a $2 per hour increase in their wages.  In 2016, guards in the department were 
11 given a $2 per hour wage increase but other employees in the correctional facilities were 
12 not.
