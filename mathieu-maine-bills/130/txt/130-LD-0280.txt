30SUMMARY
31 This bill changes the resident and nonresident registration fees for snowmobiles and 
32 uses the increased fees to help fund the Snowmobile Trail Fund and the fund responsible 
33 for capital equipment grants.  The fee for resident snowmobile registrations is increased by 
34 $10.  The fees for nonresident snowmobile registrations are increased by $20.
