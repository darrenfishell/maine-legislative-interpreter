17Sec. 3.  30-A MRSA §4741, sub-§20 is enacted to read:
1820.  Affirmatively further fair housing.  The Maine State Housing Authority shall 
19 ensure that any federal funding or any state or local funding is used in a manner that will 
20 affirmatively further fair housing in this State.  For the purposes of this subsection, 
21 "affirmatively further fair housing" means to engage actively in efforts to address barriers 
22 to and create opportunities for full and equal access to housing without discrimination on 
23 the basis of race, color, sex, sexual orientation or gender identity, physical or mental 
24 disability, religion, ancestry, national origin, familial status or receipt of public assistance.
25Sec. 4.  Report to the Legislature.  The Maine State Housing Authority shall 
26 develop a plan to ensure public funds are used to affirmatively further fair housing in this 
27 State in accordance with the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, section 4741, subsection 
28 20 and report the development of that plan to the Joint Standing Committee on Labor and 
29 Housing by January 15, 2022.  The Joint Standing Committee on Labor and Housing may 
30 report out legislation based on the report to the Second Regular Session of the 130th 
31 Legislature.
32SUMMARY
33 This bill requires the Maine State Housing Authority to ensure that public funds are 
34 used to affirmatively further fair housing, which is defined as to engage actively in efforts 
35 to address barriers to and create opportunities for full and equal access to housing without 
36 discrimination on the basis of race, color, sex, sexual orientation or gender identity, 
37 physical or mental disability, religion, ancestry, national origin, familial status or receipt of 
38 public assistance.  The bill requires the Maine State Housing Authority to develop a plan 
39 and report back to the Joint Standing Committee on Labor and Housing by January 15, 
40 2022.33
