2Sec. 1.  35-A MRSA §3483, sub-§5 is enacted to read:
35.  Interconnection of in-state distributed generation resources.  A standard buyer 
4 shall give preference in a list of requests for the interconnection of new, or uprated to 
5 increased capacity, distributed generation resources to those resources of 500 kilowatts or 
6 less owned by a resident of the State or owned by an entity with its principal place of 
7 business in the State.  For purposes of this subsection, “resident of the State” includes a 
8 business or entity with 51% or more ownership by one or more residents of the State.
9SUMMARY
10 This bill requires that a transmission and distribution utility give preference in a list 
11 requesting interconnection to the transmission and distribution utility’s electric distribution 
12 system to renewable electricity generators of 500 kilowatts or less owned by a resident of 
13 the State or owned by an entity that has its principal place of business in the State.10
