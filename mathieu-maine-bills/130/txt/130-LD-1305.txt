3ยง3110.  Facilitating requests for housing assistance
4 The department shall include on all applications for aid programs established under 
5 this Part, including the statewide food supplement program and the MaineCare program, 
6 and for the Temporary Assistance for Needy Families program described in chapter 
7 1053-B, an option for an applicant to request housing assistance from the State, including 
8 assistance provided by the department or the Maine State Housing Authority under Title 
9 30-A, chapter 201.  If an applicant requests housing assistance on the application, the 
10 department shall review and act upon the application or deliver it to the Maine State 
11 Housing Authority, as appropriate.
12SUMMARY
13 This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to include on certain 
14 applications for aid, including those for the statewide food supplement program, the 
15 MaineCare program, and the Temporary Assistance for Needy Families program, an option 
16 for the applicant to request housing assistance from the department or the Maine State 
17 Housing Authority.13
