5ยง2491.  Browntail moth control
61.  Program.  The department shall administer a program to assist a government entity 
7 or nonprofit organization, upon application by that government entity or nonprofit 
8 organization to the department, with the control of browntail moths.  This program must 
9 include the provision of resources, which may include resources for the mechanical, 
10 cultural or chemical control of the browntail moths; education campaigns; and other 
11 activities to mitigate browntail moth populations.
122.  Rules.  The department shall adopt routine technical rules, as described in Title 5, 
13 chapter 375, subchapter 2-A, to implement the provisions of this section.

3SUMMARY
4 This bill requires the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to 
5 administer a program to assist a government entity or nonprofit organization, upon 
6 application by that government entity or nonprofit organization to the department, with the 
7 control of browntail moths.  It also includes an appropriations and allocations section to 
8 provide funding for an Entomologist I position and a Senior Entomology Technician 
9 position in the department to support the management of forest insect pests.1
