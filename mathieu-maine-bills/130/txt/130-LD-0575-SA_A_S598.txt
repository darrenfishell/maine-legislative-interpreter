Fiscal Note 
Removes counties -Eliminates State Mandate 
Removing counties from the provisions of this bill eliminates the potential unfunded state mandate. 
As amended, this bill still shifts the burden of proof that an employee of the State whose regular or incidental d, 
require the care, supervison or custody of a person confined in a state correctional facility contracted heart disease or 
hypertension as a result of that employment from the claimant to the State and may result in increased costs to the 
Workers' Compensation Management Fund within the Department of Administrative and Financial Services 
beginning in fiscal year 2022-23.
