6SUMMARY
7 This bill directs the Department of Education's office of adult education to:
8 1.  Develop and implement a grant process to award grants to adult education programs 
9 to increase English language acquisition instruction in communities experiencing an 
10 increase in immigrant populations or demand for that instruction; and
11 2.  Administer an industry-specific English language acquisition and workforce 
12 training program for immigrants in the State and establish a statewide competitive grant 
13 process to carry out the purposes of the program.
14 The bill also provides funding for the industry-specific English language acquisition 
15 and workforce training program and grants to increase English language acquisition 
16 instruction.1
