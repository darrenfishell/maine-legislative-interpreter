17SUMMARY
18 This bill requires the Office of the Revisor of Statutes, on request, to consult with, and 
19 provide drafting assistance and full drafts to, persons involved in initiating a people's veto 
20 or direct initiative of a bill, resolve or resolution pursuant to the Constitution of Maine and 
21 to provide drafting assistance to persons involved in drafting local referenda.
22 This bill also requires that the office, in consultation with the Revisor of Statutes, 
23 provide classes on the drafting of people's vetoes, direct initiatives of legislation and local 
24 referenda.18
