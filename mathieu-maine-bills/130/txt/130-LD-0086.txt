9SUMMARY
10 This bill provides an exemption from the sales and use tax to area agencies on aging, 
11 as designated by the Department of Health and Human Services, and public and private 
12 nonprofit agencies that are operating under grants provided by the department, that provide 
13 social services in order to secure and maintain maximum independence and dignity in a 
14 home environment for older people capable of self-care with appropriate supportive 
15 services.
