27SUMMARY
28 This bill establishes an interagency task force to develop a statewide plan to assist 
29 immigrants who need housing, job training, language skills and transportation.  The bill 
30 also provides funds to contract with community-based organizations for language 
31 acquisition programs for persons who have recently come to this State as immigrants, 
32 refugees or asylum seekers.  It also provides funds to contract for workforce development 
33 programs for persons who have recently come to this State as immigrants, refugees or 
34 asylum seekers.  14
