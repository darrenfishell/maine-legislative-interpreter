18SUMMARY
19 Current law allows a student covered by an individualized education plan who elected 
20 a philosophical or religious exemption from immunization on or before September 1, 2021 
21 to continue to attend school after September 1, 2021 without being immunized as long as 
22 certain statements regarding acknowledgement of the risks and benefits associated with the 
23 choice to immunize are provided.
24 This bill extends that exemption to any student who elected a philosophical or religious 
25 exemption from immunization on or before September 1, 2021.
