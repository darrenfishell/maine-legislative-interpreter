10SUMMARY
11 This resolve directs the Department of Labor to submit to the United States Secretary 
12 of Labor and the Joint Standing Committee on Labor and Housing a plan to reinstate the 
13 self-employment assistance program pursuant to Public Law 1993, chapter 710 and the 
14 Maine Revised Statutes, Title 26, section 1197. The Joint Standing Committee on Labor 
15 and Housing is authorized to submit a bill regarding the program to the Second Regular 
16 Session of the 130th Legislature.11
