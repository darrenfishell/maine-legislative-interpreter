122.  Violation.  The operator of a motor vehicle on a public way is in violation of this 
13 section if the operator or a passenger in the passenger area of the motor vehicle:
14 A. Consumes marijuana or a marijuana product; or
15 B. Possesses an open marijuana container or marijuana product container.
163.  Penalty.  A person who violates subsection 2 commits a traffic infraction for which 
17 a fine of $100 must be adjudged.
