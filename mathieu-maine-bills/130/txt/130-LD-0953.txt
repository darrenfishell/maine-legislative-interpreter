22SUMMARY
23 This bill permits tax increment financing to be used by a municipality to cover costs 
24 associated with the development of affordable housing in and outside development 
25 districts, for the use in supporting housing services for persons who are homeless, for the 
26 purpose of providing an incentive for development within the municipality.  The bill also 
27 corrects a conflict in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, section 5225, subsection 1, 
28 paragraph C, which was amended by Public Law 2019, chapter 604 and chapter 625, by 
29 incorporating the changes made by both laws.1
