21SUMMARY
22 This bill amends the laws concerning the fees and travel reimbursement paid to sheriffs 
23 and their deputies for service of civil process documents by increasing these fees and 
24 providing that the travel reimbursement rate must be at least the same rate as that paid to 
25 state employees.22
