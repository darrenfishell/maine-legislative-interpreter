23 SUMMARY 
24 This amendment makes changes to the definition of "occupational therapy" to align 
25 with the model definition recommended by the American Occupational Therapy 
26 Association. The amendment also removes the sections of the bill related to continuing 
27 education requirements for license renewals and license reinstatements and replaces them 
28 with a requirement directing the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation, 
29 Board of Occupational Therapy Practice to adopt rules for continuing education . as a 
30 condition of renewal of a license.
