Page 1 - 130LR0934(01)1Sec. 1.  Review of barriers to regional housing solutions.  Resolved:  That the 
2 Department of Economic and Community Development, Office of Community 
3 Development shall conduct a review of barriers to regional solutions to making housing 
4 affordable, accessible, available, functional and compatible with strategies outlined in the 
5 December 2020 climate action plan of the Maine Climate Council, "Maine Won't Wait:  A 
6 Four-year Plan for Climate Action."  The office shall report to the Joint Standing 
7 Committee on Labor and Housing by December 1, 2021 with a summary of these barriers, 
8 their implications and potential solutions.
9 The office shall also include in its report:
10 1.  Proposals to update the planning and land use regulation laws pertaining to growth 
11 management with a focus on regional solutions recognizing the interrelationships of 
12 housing, transportation, climate action and land use;
13 2.  An assessment of the implications of housing choices of state and regional 
14 transportation investments and policies, produced with the advice of the Department of 
15 Transportation and regional transportation agencies;
16 3.  Identification of regional approaches to address significant mill rate differentials 
17 between rural and urban service center hubs and surrounding communities that increase 
18 associated living expenses by driving housing out of established communities; and
19 4.  Strategies for offering choices to local governments about how to meet statewide 
20 housing goals through their comprehensive plans and land use codes.
21SUMMARY

25 outlined in the December 2020 climate action plan of the Maine Climate Council, "Maine 
26 Won't Wait:  A Four-year Plan for Climate Action."  It directs the office to report to the 
27 Joint Standing Committee on Labor and Housing by December 1, 2021 with a summary of 
28 these barriers, their implications and potential solutions.22
