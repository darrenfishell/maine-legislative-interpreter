7 upon receiving an application and evidence of payment of the excise tax required by Title

122.  Design; review; vanity plates.  The Secretary of State, in consultation with the 
13 plate sponsor, shall determine a design for the support multiple sclerosis programs special 
14 registration plates.  The joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over 
15 transportation matters shall review the final design prior to manufacture of the plates.  The 
16 Secretary of State shall issue upon request support multiple sclerosis programs special 
17 registration plates that are also vanity plates.  Support multiple sclerosis programs special 
18 registration plates are issued in accordance with the provisions of this section and section 
19 453.
203.  Contribution; credit to funds.  In addition to the regular motor vehicle registration 
21 fee prescribed by law for the particular class of vehicle registered, the initial contribution 
22 for support multiple sclerosis programs special registration plates is $20, which must be 
23 deposited with the Treasurer of State and credited as follows:
24 A.  Ten dollars to the regional chapter of a nationwide multiple sclerosis society to 
25 support multiple sclerosis programs;
26 B.  Nine dollars to the Highway Fund for administrative and production costs; and
27 C.  One dollar to the Specialty License Plate Fund established under section 469.
284.  Renewal fee.  In addition to the regular motor vehicle registration fee prescribed by 
29 law, the annual renewal contribution for support multiple sclerosis programs special 
30 registration plates is $15, which must be deposited with the Treasurer of State and credited 
31 as follows:
32 A.  Ten dollars to the regional chapter of a nationwide multiple sclerosis society to 
33 support multiple sclerosis programs;
34 B.  Four dollars to the Highway Fund for administrative and production costs; and
35 C.  One dollar to the Specialty License Plate Fund established under section 469.
365.  Payment for costs associated with the production and issuance of the first 2,000
