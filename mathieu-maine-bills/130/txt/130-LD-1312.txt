35SUMMARY
36 This bill requires municipalities to allow one accessory dwelling unit to be included 
37 within or located on the same lot as a single-family dwelling unit.  It prevents municipalities 
38 from imposing any of the following for accessory dwelling units: setback requirements on 
39 accessory dwelling units incorporated within an existing single-family dwelling unit; 
40 setback requirements of more than 5 feet for accessory dwelling units not included within 36
