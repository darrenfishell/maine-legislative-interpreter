22SUMMARY
23 Currently, all the New England states, New York and the Canadian provinces of 
24 Quebec, Newfoundland and Labrador and the Maritimes make up the Northeastern 
25 Interstate Forest Fire Protection Compact.  This bill extends liability protection for the State 
26 and firefighters who travel to fight wildfires or other emergencies outside of the compact 
27 area.
