Page 1 - 130LR1678(01)1Sec. 1.  Department of Health and Human Services to seek waiver. 
2Resolved:  That, by December 1, 2021, the Department of Health and Human Services 
3 shall apply to the United States Department of Health and Human Services, Centers for 
4 Medicare and Medicaid Services for a waiver pursuant to 42 United States Code, Section 
5 1315. The waiver must request the authorization of federal Medicaid matching funds for 
6 the provision of additional medication-assisted treatment for persons with substance use 
7 disorder to eligible persons up to 30 days prior to the person's release from incarceration 
8 so that a qualified health care professional and the person with substance use disorder 
9 receiving care from the professional have access to all forms and formulations of 
10 medications for substance use disorder approved by the United States Food and Drug 
11 Administration as recommended by a national society of addiction medicine and a national 
12 commission on correctional health care.
13SUMMARY
14 This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services, by December 1, 
15 2021, to seek a waiver from the United States Department of Health and Human Services, 
16 Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services authorizing federal Medicaid matching funds 
17 for additional medication-assisted treatment for persons with substance use disorder who 
18 are incarcerated up to 30 days prior to the release of the persons from incarceration.14
