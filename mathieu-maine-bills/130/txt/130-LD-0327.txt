3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill proposes to distribute the funds in the State Treasury by:
6 1.  Placing in the General Fund only the amount necessary to cover the outstanding 
7 obligations of the State required by law, such as for bond debt and payment of contracts 
8 and salaries;
9 2.  After the transfer of funds required to meet the legal obligations of the State, 
10 distributing 75% of the remaining funds to Maine taxpayers on a pro rata basis, based on 
11 tax data for the 2020 tax year; and
12 3.  After the transfer and the distributions, transferring the remaining 25% to the Maine 
13 Budget Stabilization Fund, also known as the Maine Rainy Day Fund.
