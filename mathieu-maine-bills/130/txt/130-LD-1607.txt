2Sec. 1.  14 MRSA §7488 is enacted to read:
3§7488.  Private right of action for race-based false public alarm or report
4 A plaintiff may bring a private small claims action against a defendant who knowingly 
5 gives or causes to be given false information about the plaintiff to a law enforcement 
6 officer, a member of a firefighting agency, including a volunteer fire department, or a 
7 public safety answering point as defined in Title 25, section 2921, subsection 7 or any other 
8 person knowing that the other person is likely to communicate the information to a law 
9 enforcement officer, a member of a firefighting agency or a public safety answering point, 
10 due to race-based profiling of the plaintiff.  The court may award up to $250 in damages to 
11 a plaintiff who prevails in an action brought under this section.  For purposes of this section, 
12 "race-based profiling" means to take an action or cause an action to happen against another 
13 person because of the race, color, ancestry or national origin of the other person.

Page 2 - 130LR0032(01)1SUMMARY
2 This bill adds to the crime of false public alarm or report knowingly giving or causing 
3 to be given false information about another person to a law enforcement officer, a member 
4 of a firefighting agency, including a volunteer fire department, or a public safety answering 
5 point or any other person knowing that the other person is likely to communicate the 
6 information to a law enforcement officer, a member of a firefighting agency or a public 
7 safety answering point, due to race-based profiling of the person about whom the false 
8 information was given.  The bill also creates a private right of action in small claims court 
9 with damages up to $250 for a race-based false public alarm or report.  "Race-based 
10 profiling" is defined as taking an action or causing an action to happen against another 
11 person because of the race, color, ancestry or national origin of the other person.2
