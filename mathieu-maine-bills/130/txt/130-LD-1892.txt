1Sec. 2.  Transfer of allocations.  Any balance of the allocation for "General 
2 Contingency" made by the Legislature for the Maine Turnpike Authority may be 
3 transferred at any time prior to the closing of the books to any other allocation or 
4 subdivision of any other allocation made by the Legislature for the use of the Maine 
5 Turnpike Authority for the same calendar year.  Any balance of any other allocation or 
6 subdivision of any other allocation made by the Legislature for the Maine Turnpike 
7 Authority that at any time is not required for the purpose named in the allocation or 
8 subdivision may be transferred at any time prior to the closing of the books to any other 
9 allocation or subdivision of any other allocation made by the Legislature for the use of the 
10 Maine Turnpike Authority for the same calendar year subject to review by the joint standing 
11 committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over transportation matters.  Financial 
12 statements describing the transfer, other than a transfer from "General Contingency," must 
13 be submitted by the Maine Turnpike Authority to the Office of Fiscal and Program Review 
14 30 days before the transfer is to be implemented.  In the case of extraordinary emergency 
15 transfers, the 30-day prior submission requirement may be waived by vote of the 
16 committee.  These financial statements must include information specifying the accounts 
17 that are affected, amounts to be transferred, a description of the transfer and a detailed 
18 explanation as to why the transfer is needed.
35Sec. 3.  Encumbered balance at year-end.  At the end of each calendar year,
