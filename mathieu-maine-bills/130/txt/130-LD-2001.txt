6SUMMARY
7 The bill requires the Board of Trustees of the Maine Veterans' Homes to obtain 
8 legislative approval for the establishment or closure of a facility managed by the board.  
9 The bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rules to 
10 allocate emergency payments made in fiscal years 2021-22 and 2022-23 to the veterans 
11 homes located in Caribou and Machias.  The bill provides for one-time appropriations and 
12 allocations for the emergency payments.1
