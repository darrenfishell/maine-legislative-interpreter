29SUMMARY
30 This resolve increases reimbursement rates to direct care workers.  The resolve requires 
31 the Department of Health and Human Services to increase reimbursement rates to all direct 
32 care worker services that are provided in home and community-based settings and in 
33 institutional settings by no less than $2 an hour.30
