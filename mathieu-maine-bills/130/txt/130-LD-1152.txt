Page 1 - 130LR0725(01)1Sec. 1.  Postsecondary educational institutions to review student debt and 
2develop plans. Resolved:  That postsecondary educational institutions in this State shall 
3 review the level of student debt at their institutions and develop plans to:
4 1.  Limit student debt to $25,000 over 4 years;
5 2.  Eliminate the gap in funding that exists between the average cost of attending a 
6 postsecondary educational institution and the sum of the average financial aid award 
7 provided to a student and the estimated family contribution;
8 3.  Meet the financial need of their students in their financial aid awards; and 
9 4.  Eliminate the need for students to incur additional debt beyond the $25,000 level.
10 Postsecondary educational institutions shall report the results of their review and their 
11 plans to the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs no later than 
12 December 1, 2021.  The Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs may 
13 report out legislation related to the reports to the Second Regular Session of the 130th 
14 Legislature.
15SUMMARY
16 This resolve requires postsecondary educational institutions in this State to review the 
17 level of student debt at their institutions and develop plans to:
18 1.  Limit student debt to $25,000 over 4 years;
19 2.  Eliminate the gap in funding that exists between the average cost of attending a 
20 postsecondary educational institution and the sum of the average financial aid award 
21 provided to a student and the estimated family contribution;
22 3.  Meet the financial need of their students in their financial aid awards; and 
23 4.  Eliminate the need for students to incur additional debt beyond the $25,000 level.
24 Postsecondary educational institutions are required to report the results of their review 
25 and their plans to the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs no later 
26 than December 1, 2021.  The Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs 
27 may report out legislation related to the reports to the Second Regular Session of the 130th 
28 Legislature.16
