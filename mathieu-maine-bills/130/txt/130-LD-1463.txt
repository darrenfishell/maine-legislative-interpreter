34ยง4330-A.  Health care affordability assessment
351.  Assessment.  A covered entity shall file with the superintendent a report of its net 
36 written premiums for the preceding year by no later than April 1st of each year.  The 
37 superintendent shall calculate and issue to the covered entity a certified assessment, which 
38 must be equal to 2.85% of the covered entity's net written premiums but may not exceed 
39 the amount the covered entity would have paid under Section 9010 of the federal 
40 Affordable Care Act if that section were in effect during that year, and the covered entity 
41 shall pay the assessment.
422.  Deposit to the fund.  The superintendent shall deposit all proceeds of the 
43 assessment to the fund.

13SUMMARY
14 This bill:
15 1.  Establishes the Maine Health Care Affordability Fund to fund activities and 
16 initiatives to reduce the cost of health insurance coverage for Maine residents;
17 2.  Requires the Commissioner of Health and Human Services to adopt rules for 
18 the administration of the Maine Health Care Affordability Fund and for the disbursement 
19 of funds from the fund to assist Maine residents with the cost of health care coverage;
20 3.  Establishes the health care affordability assessment to provide funding to the 
21 Maine Health Care Affordability Fund and requires the Superintendent of Insurance within 
22 the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation to adopt rules for the 
23 administration and enforcement of the health care affordability assessment;
24 4.  Requires state agencies to provide technical assistance to the commissioner and 
25 the superintendent for the administration of the Maine Health Care Affordability Fund or 
26 the health care affordability assessment upon request;
27 5.  Establishes the Affordable Health Care Advisory Group, which consists of the 
28 commissioner, the superintendent and 11 additional members appointed by the President 
29 of the Senate, the Speaker of the House of Representatives and the Governor, to consult on 
30 the development of rules to implement and administer the Maine Health Care Affordability 
31 Fund and the health care affordability assessment; and
32 6.  Requires the commissioner to submit an annual report to the Governor and the 
33 joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over health insurance 
34 matters describing activities and initiatives funded by the Maine Health Care Affordability 
35 Fund to increase the affordability of health insurance for Maine residents and summarizing 
36 the findings and recommendations of the Affordable Health Care Advisory Group by 
37 February 1st.  The commissioner is also required to make the report publicly available 
38 online.14
