26SUMMARY
27 This bill modifies the regional school unit formation process to provide for 
28 comprehensive programming for all of the students from kindergarten to grade 12 with 
29 exceptions for those school administrative units that would benefit from a different grade 
30 level configuration in the regional school unit, allowing for the provision of comprehensive 
31 programming for which the regional school unit is responsible through the use of school 
32 choice, by a contract with another school administrative unit or by some other means.27
