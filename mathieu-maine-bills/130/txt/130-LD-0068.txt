61.  Career management.  The career management component of the course offered 
7 under this section must describe student career options after high school, including but not 
8 limited to 4-year colleges or universities, community colleges, vocational schools, the

112.  Fiscal management.  The fiscal management component of the course offered 
12 under this section must provide information on opening bank accounts, using checking 
13 accounts, using credit cards and mortgages, loans, stocks, bonds, dividends and retirement 
14 accounts.
15SUMMARY
16 This bill requires school administrative units operating a secondary school to offer a 
17 career and fiscal management course to students.
