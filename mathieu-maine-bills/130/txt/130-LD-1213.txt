10SUMMARY
11 This bill requires the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to prescribe a 
12 form and content of bear, deer, moose and wild turkey registration to include the use of a 
13 computer, telephone or mobile electronic device in lieu of presenting the animal at a 
14 tagging station for registration and to provide a person using this system a unique 
15 registration number to attach to the animal in lieu of a seal.11
