41.  Definition.  As used in this section, "reportable accident" means an accident on a 
5 public way or a place where public traffic may reasonably be anticipated, resulting in bodily 
6 injury or death to a person or a domestic animal or apparent property damage of $1,000 or 
7 more.  Apparent property damage under this subsection must be based upon the market 
8 value of the necessary repairs and may not be limited to the current value of the vehicle or 
9 property.
10SUMMARY
11 This bill amends the definition of "reportable accident" in the motor vehicle provisions 
12 by adding an accident that results in the injury or death of a domestic animal.
