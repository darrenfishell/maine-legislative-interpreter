2Sec. 1.  20-A MRSA §2, sub-§1, as enacted by PL 1981, c. 693, §§5 and 8, is 
3 amended to read:
41.  State responsibility for public education.  In accordance with the Constitution of 
5 Maine, Article VIII, the Legislature shall enact the laws that are necessary to assure that all 
6 school administrative units make suitable provisions for the support and maintenance of 
7 the public schools and the education of every person within the age limitations prescribed 
8 by state statutes who resides in the school administrative unit. It is the intent of the 
9 Legislature that every person within the age limitations prescribed by state statutes shall be 
10 provided an opportunity to receive the benefits of a free public education.
11Sec. 2.  20-A MRSA §6355, sub-§5 is enacted to read:
125.  Virtual public charter schools and private schools.  The student is enrolled in or 
13 attends a virtual public charter school as defined in section 2401, subsection 11 or a private 
14 school.
15SUMMARY
16 This bill exempts children who are enrolled in or attend a virtual public charter school 
17 or a private school from the requirement that a child must have evidence of immunization 
18 to be enrolled in or attend school.  The bill also provides that the Legislature is required to 
19 enact laws that are necessary to assure that all school administrative units make suitable 
20 provisions for the support and maintenance of public schools and the education of every 
21 person within the age limits prescribed by statute who resides in the school administrative 
22 unit.
