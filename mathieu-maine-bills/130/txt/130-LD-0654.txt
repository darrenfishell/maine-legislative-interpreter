5ยง4994-A.  Twenty-four-hour Shelter Capital Project program
61.  Program established.  The Maine State Housing Authority shall establish and 
7 administer the Twenty-four-hour Shelter Capital Project program to support new 
8 construction or expansion of emergency 24-hour shelters for persons experiencing 
9 homelessness.  New construction and expansion projects funded by the program must 
10 address an increase in the need for shelter capacity in the region since the construction, or 
11 expansion, of an existing shelter or shelters in the region or since the start of the outbreak 
12 of the coronavirus disease 2019, or COVID-19, or both. The program must allow the 
13 purchase of land and improvements to land as allowable costs.  For the purposes of this 
14 section, "24-hour shelter" means a shelter that provides access to shelter and intake services 
15 24 hours per day to persons experiencing homelessness.
162.  Fund established.  The Twenty-four-hour Shelter Capital Project Program Fund is 
17 established within the Maine State Housing Authority.  The Maine State Housing Authority 
18 may apply money in the fund for purposes authorized by this subchapter.
193.  Rules.  The Maine State Housing Authority may adopt rules to implement this 
20 section.  Rules adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in 
21 Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
22Sec. 2.  Appropriations and allocations.  The following appropriations and
