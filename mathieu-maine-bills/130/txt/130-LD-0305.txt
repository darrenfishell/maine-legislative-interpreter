24SUMMARY
25 This bill adds forest rangers and forest fire prevention specialists in the forest protection 
26 unit within the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Bureau of Forestry 
27 who have contracted cancer to the list of firefighters under the workers' compensation laws 
28 whose cancer is presumed to be caused by their firefighting duties.
