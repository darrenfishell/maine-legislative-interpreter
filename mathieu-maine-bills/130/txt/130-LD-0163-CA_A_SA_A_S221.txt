19 SUMMARY 
20 This amendment changes the frequency, from monthly to qua1terly, at which a facility 
21 must monitor vapor leaks around any aboveground petroleum storage tank with a storage 
22 capacity greater than 39,000 gallons. The amendment changes the frequency, from every 
23 2 years to every 5 years, at which a facility must conduct a complete inspection of the cover 
24 and seal associated with the internal, floating roof of any aboveground petroleum storage 
25 tank equipped with such a roof. The amendment also removes a requirement that the 
26 Department of Environmental Protection submit a report to the Joint Standing Committee 
27 on Environment and Natural Resources regarding the regulation of air emissions at 
28 petroleum storage facilities and removes the appropriations and allocations section 
29 providing funding for that rep01t.
