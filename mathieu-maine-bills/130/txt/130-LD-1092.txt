7SUMMARY
8 This bill adds 3 members to the Board of Trustees of the Maine Criminal Justice 
9 Academy for a total of 6 members who are not and have never been sworn members of a 
10 law enforcement agency, 2 of whom are appointed by the Governor, 2 of whom are 
11 appointed by the President of the Senate and 2 of whom are appointed by the Speaker of 
12 the House of Representatives and all of whom are subject to confirmation by the Senate.  
13 This bill also requires that any change or adoption of an element of the Maine Criminal 
14 Justice Academy basic law enforcement training program be considered only after public 
15 comment, with the members of the board of trustees required to acknowledge receipt of the 
16 comments, and that a complete review of the basic law enforcement training program by 
17 this process be accomplished within 12 months of the enactment of this bill with the 3 new 
18 members present on the board.1
