12SUMMARY
13 This bill reclassifies from Class C to Class B the lower section of the Androscoggin 
14 River from Gulf Island Dam to a line formed by the extension of the Bath-Brunswick 
15 boundary across Merrymeeting Bay in a northwesterly direction.13
