5 The municipal officers of a municipality without a full-time chief of police may

7 for that municipality.  The designation must be made by written agreement with the Chief 
8 of the State Police.  The agreement must include provisions for termination of the 
9 agreement.  During the term of an agreement, the State Police shall perform all the 
10 functions of the issuing authority, including suspension and revocation of permits.  The 
11 State Police are entitled to receive any fees authorized for performing the functions of an 
12 issuing authority.  The Chief of the State Police continues to serve as the issuing authority 
13 until the chief receives from the municipal officers written notice of cancellation or 
14 revocation of the designation.  The Chief of the State Police upon request shall provide 
15 assistance in conducting a background check under this chapter to a municipal issuing 
16 authority that has not designated the Chief of the State Police as the issuing authority under 
17 this section.
18SUMMARY
19 This bill requires the Department of Public Safety, Chief of the State Police upon 
20 request to provide assistance in conducting a background check for a concealed handgun 
21 permit for a municipality that has not designated the Chief of the State Police as the issuing 
22 authority for concealed handgun permits.19
