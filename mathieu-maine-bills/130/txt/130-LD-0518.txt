Page 1 - 130LR1776(01)1Sec. 1.  Department of Education to study educational mandates. Resolved: 
2 That the Department of Education shall study current educational mandates imposed by the 
3 State and the process by which educational mandates are established.
4Sec. 2.  Report. Resolved: That, no later than January 1, 2022, the Department of 
5 Education shall submit a report based on the study under section 1 and any recommended 
6 legislation to the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs. The report 
7 must include, but is not limited to:
8 1. A full list of all educational mandates. For each educational mandate, the report must 
9 identify:
10 A.  The ages of the students the educational mandate affects;
11 B.  The broad academic subject area that the educational mandate affects; and
12 C.  An estimate of the required time and cost to implement each educational mandate; 
13 2.  Identification of and recommendations for continuance of educational mandates that 
14 are appropriate and effective and educational mandates that are onerous, outdated or 
15 otherwise ineffective;
16 3.  Recommendations for improving the process of establishing educational mandates 
17 to ensure that new educational mandates are appropriate, necessary and achievable within 
18 school resources; and
19 4.  Any other findings the Department of Education determines relevant.
20 The Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs may submit 
21 legislation on the subject of the report to the Second Regular Session of the 130th 
22 Legislature.
23SUMMARY
24 This resolve directs the Department of Education to review all current educational 
25 mandates imposed by the State and the process by which educational mandates are 
26 established and requires the Department of Education to submit a report to the Joint 
27 Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs, no later than January 1, 2022, with 
28 a list of all educational mandates and recommendations on educational mandates and 
29 improvements for the process by which educational mandates are established.  The Joint 
30 Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs may submit legislation on the 
31 subject of the report to the Second Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.24
