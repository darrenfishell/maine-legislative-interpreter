15SUMMARY
16 This bill prohibits a person from operating a motor vehicle on a public way without 
17 clearing the motor vehicle of solid precipitation.  Operating a motor vehicle under such 
18 circumstances is a traffic infraction subject to a $250 fine for a first offense and $500 for a 
19 2nd or subsequent offense.  The prohibition does not apply to commercial vehicles until 
20 May 1, 2026.16
