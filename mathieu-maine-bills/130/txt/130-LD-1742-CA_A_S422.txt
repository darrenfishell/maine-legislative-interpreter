SUMMARY 
This amendment requires the Commissioner of Marine Resources to notify. the joint 
standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over marine resources matters 
within 15 days of initiating rulemaking to change minimum and maximum lobster sizes or 
dimensions of vents in lobster traps. It makes other technical changes.
