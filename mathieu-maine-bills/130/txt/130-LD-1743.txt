413-A.  Electronic facsimile  table game.  "Electronic facsimile  table game" means a

1043-A.  Table game.  "Table game" means a card game, dice game or other game of 
11 chance, including, but not limited to, blackjack, poker, dice, craps, roulette, baccarat, 
12 money wheels, wheel of fortune or any electronic facsimile of such a  an electronic table 
13 game located in a casino.  Table games are governed under this chapter and excluded from 
14 the definition of "game of chance" in Title 17, section 330  1831, subsection  2 5.
15SUMMARY
16 This bill amends the statutes governing the Department of Public Safety, Gambling 
17 Control Board to change the defined term "electronic facsimile" to "electronic table game" 
18 and to conform the definition of "table game" to that change. It also corrects a cross-
19 reference.16
