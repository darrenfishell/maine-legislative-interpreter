30SUMMARY
31 This bill provides that the following are not public records under the Freedom of 
32 Access Act:
33 1.  Video and audio recordings made by security or surveillance cameras on school 
34 grounds or in school-related transportation vehicles; and 
35 2.  Live or recorded classes taught by public school staff in which students can be seen 
36 or heard.
