19SUMMARY
20 This bill requires that any project for construction, maintenance or repair of a road 
21 maintain the compliance of, or bring into compliance, the public way with the federal 
22 Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990 and that any facility located in a public way that 
23 must be relocated in order to ensure compliance be relocated at the expense of the facility
