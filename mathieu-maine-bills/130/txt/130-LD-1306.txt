35SUMMARY
36 This bill amends the law regulating the provision of emergency services under the 
37 State's E-9-1-1 system by including crisis response services as part of those emergency 
38 services to be offered to individuals experiencing mental health emergencies, emergencies 
39 relating to a substance use disorder or other emergencies for which fire, emergency medical 
40 or police services are determined not to be required.  It also directs the Public Utilities 
41 Commission, Emergency Medical Services Communications Bureau to develop protocols 
42 and procedures necessary to ensure the efficient dispatch of such crisis response under the 36
