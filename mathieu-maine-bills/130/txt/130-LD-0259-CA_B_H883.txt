SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is a minority report of the committee, changes the title and 
replaces the bill, which is a concept draft. It amends waste handling fees to, beginning 
August 1, 2023, increase the fee imposed on the disposal at a landfill of municipal solid 
waste and municipal solid waste ash from $1 per ton to $2 per ton and increase the fee 
imposed on the disposal at a landfill of construction and demolition debris and residue from 
the processing of construction and demolition debris from $2 per ton to $3 per ton.

This bill increases the fee imposed on landfilled municipal solid waste and municipal solid waste ash from $1 per ton 
to $2 per ton and the fee imposed on landfilled construction and demolition debris and residue from $2 per ton to $3 
per ton, beginning August 1, 2023. The Department of Environmental Protection has indicated that based on the 
cunent volume of materials subject to these fees annual revenue will increase by $1,293,417 starting in fiscal year 
2022-23 to the Maine Solid Waste Management Fund (MSWMF). The bill includes a conesponding allocation to the 
MSWMF to allow expenditure of the additional revenue.
