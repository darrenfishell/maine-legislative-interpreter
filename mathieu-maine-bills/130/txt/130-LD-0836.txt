2Sec. 1.  29-A MRSA §1601-B is enacted to read:
3§1601-B.  Notification of cancellation of liability insurance
41.  Notice to Secretary of State required.  A company providing liability insurance 
5 for a motor vehicle registered in this State shall immediately notify the Secretary of State 
6 when that insurance coverage is cancelled or terminated or lapses.  If the company knows 
7 that replacement coverage has been secured in place of the coverage that has been cancelled 
8 or terminated or has lapsed, the company is not required to notify the Secretary of State.
9 The Secretary of State shall develop by rule standard procedures for a company to use in 
10 providing notice under this subsection.
112.  Determination period.  Upon receipt of notice under subsection 1, the Secretary of 
12 State shall notify the motor vehicle owner whose insurance has been cancelled or 
13 terminated or has lapsed that the motor vehicle owner has 15 days to provide evidence of 
14 financial responsibility in the form of a reinstatement of liability insurance coverage or new 
15 liability insurance coverage.  Evidence may be provided by a company providing liability 
16 insurance for a motor vehicle or the insured motor vehicle owner.
173.  Suspension.  The Secretary of State, upon termination of a 15-day period under 
18 subsection 2 following receipt of the notice provided in subsection 1 and failure of the 
19 motor vehicle owner or a company providing liability insurance for a motor vehicle to 
20 provide proof of financial responsibility, shall suspend the motor vehicle owner's 
21 registration certificate and plates for that motor vehicle, in accordance with section 2458, 
22 subsection 2.  The suspension continues until that motor vehicle owner or a company 
23 providing liability insurance for that motor vehicle for that motor vehicle owner provides 
24 proof of financial responsibility to the Secretary of State pursuant to section 1605.
254.  Rulemaking.  The Secretary of State shall develop rules necessary to implement 
26 this section.  Rules adopted under this subsection are routine technical rules pursuant to 
27 Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
