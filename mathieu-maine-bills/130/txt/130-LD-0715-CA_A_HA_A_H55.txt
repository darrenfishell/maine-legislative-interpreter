Fiscal Note 
Potential current biennium revenue decrease -General Fund 
Fiscal Detail and Notes · 
Assuming an effective date 90 days after the statutory adjournment date of June 16, 2021, removing the emergency 
preamble will delay the bill's proposed reduction in the municipal revenue sharing rate from 5% to 3.75% in fiscal 
year 2()21-22 for three months and will decrease General Fund revenue by $11. 7 million from the levels assumed in 
the bill. Similarly, removing the emergency preamble could delay the availability offunding provided in the bill for 
fiscal year 2021-22, but will not affect overall funding levels provided for each year of the biennium. 
Assuming an effective date 90 days prior to the start of fiscal year 2021-22, removing the emergency preamble will 
not delay the bill's proposed reduction in the municipal revenue sharing rate, will not reduce General Fund revenue 
from the levels assumed in the bill and this amendment will not delay the availability of funding for fiscal year 2021-
22.
