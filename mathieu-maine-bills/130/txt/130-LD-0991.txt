Page 1 - 130LR1628(01)1Sec. 1.  Feasibility analysis for passenger rail service from Portland to 
2Lewiston.  Resolved:  That the Department of Transportation, in consultation with the 
3 Northern New England Passenger Rail Authority and interested municipalities and 
4 planning organizations, shall complete a feasibility analysis in accordance with the United 
5 States Department of Transportation, Federal Transit Administration "New Starts" federal 
6 funding program requirements to implement passenger rail service from the Portland Ocean 
7 Gateway Pier to downtown Lewiston.  The study must identify the state-owned railroad 
8 corridor between Portland and Auburn and into Lewiston as the locally preferred 
9 alternative route for analysis.  The report must include:
10 1.  Identification of municipal station locations and coordination with existing 
11 passenger rail services and with municipality transportation planning agencies;
12 2.  An analysis of market demand and potential economic benefits;
13 3.  A detailed passenger service development plan;
14 4.  An inventory of infrastructure and necessary capital investment, including 
15 electrification;
16 5.  An evaluation of potential financial mechanisms and sources of funds;
17 6.  An environmental impact analysis to meet "New Starts" federal funding program 
18 requirements for the proposed service, including station locations; and
19 7.  An analysis of the potential for service between Boston and both Lewiston and 
20 Montreal, Province of Quebec, Canada.
21 The study must be completed in a timely manner and meet 2021 federal infrastructure 
22 program requirements.
