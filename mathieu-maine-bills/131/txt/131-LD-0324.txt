4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill proposes to enact the Secure Jobs Act, which establishes a framework for 
6 employee discipline and discharge, prohibits the unjust discharge of an employee, requires 
7 employers to use progressive discipline measures, limits the use of electronic monitoring, 
8 provides for severance pay, provides remedies for wrongfully discharged employees and 
9 authorizes the recovery of damages.4
