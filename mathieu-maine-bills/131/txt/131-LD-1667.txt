2Sec. 1.  5 MRSA §1997 is enacted to read:
3§1997.  State Names Authority
4 The Chief Information Officer shall appoint the State Names Authority to serve within

7 United States Geological Survey, United States Board on Geographic Names and makes

5SUMMARY
6 This bill establishes the position of State Names Authority. The State Names Authority 
7 is appointed by the Chief Information Officer within the executive branch and serves in the 
8 Office of Information Technology, Office of Geographic Information Systems. The State 
9 Names Authority is directed to serve as a liaison to the United States Department of the 
10 Interior, United States Geological Survey, United States Board on Geographic Names and 
11 make recommendations for renaming Maine features. The bill also establishes the State 
12 Names Advisory Board to advise the State Names Authority in making those 
13 recommendations.6
