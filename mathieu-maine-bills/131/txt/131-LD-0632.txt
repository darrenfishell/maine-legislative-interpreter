8SUMMARY
9 This bill eliminates the requirement that participants in the Social Work Education 
10 Loan Repayment Program have completed a social work degree within 3 years of the date 
11 of application for the program. The bill adds to the list of practice areas available for 
12 participation in the program. The bill replaces the current priority consideration in the 
13 program with one developed by the Finance Authority of Maine with stakeholder input and 
14 also requires stakeholder input on changes to the application process. The bill requires a 
15 participant to serve residents of this State, increases the number of loans repaid each year 
16 and increases the amount of repayment for each participant.9
