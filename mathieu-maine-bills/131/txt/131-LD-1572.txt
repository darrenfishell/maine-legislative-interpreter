20SUMMARY
21 This bill authorizes the Secretary of State to require the owner of an electric vehicle to 
22 pay an electric vehicle registration surcharge of $72 per year. The money collected from 
23 the surcharge must be transmitted to the Treasurer of State to be credited to the Highway 
24 Fund.21
