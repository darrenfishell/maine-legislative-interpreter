4SUMMARY
5 This bill adds 3rd-party liability requirement provisions for MaineCare claims for 
6 payment and adds a provision to apply MaineCare assignment provisions to out-of-state 
7 carriers who provide coverage to residents of this State. The bill also provides that 
8 payments made under assignment to the MaineCare program by a 3rd party are final after 
9 2 years. 5
