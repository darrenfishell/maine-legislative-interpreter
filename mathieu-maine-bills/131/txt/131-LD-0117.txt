2Sec. 1.  22 MRSA §21, sub-§4, as amended by PL 2011, c. 687, §3, is further

44.  Electronic benefits transfer system or EBT.  "Electronic benefits transfer system" 
5 or "EBT" means a system for the delivery of benefits to recipients by means of credit or 
6 debit card services, automated teller machines, point of sale devices or access to online 
7 systems for the withdrawal of funds or the processing of a payment for merchandise or a

12 cash.
13SUMMARY
14 This bill provides that benefits received through the electronic benefits transfer system 
15 may only be redeemed through purchase by electronic transfer and may not be redeemed 
16 for cash.14
