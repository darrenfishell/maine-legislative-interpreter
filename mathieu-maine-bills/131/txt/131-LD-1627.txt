17SUMMARY
18 This bill requires a lobbyist to file the following reports with the Commission on 
19 Governmental Ethics and Election Practices:
20 1.  Within 48 hours of entering into an agreement with another person for the purpose 
21 of lobbying for that person, a report disclosing the employment agreement; 
22 2.  No later than noon each Friday when the Legislature is in session, a report detailing 
23 each legislative instrument or action on which the lobbyist spent any time lobbying; and
24 3.  No later than noon each Friday, a report detailing each issue before a state agency 
25 on which the lobbyist spent any time lobbying.18
