3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill would update the Uniform Commercial Code with the national 2022 revisions 
6 by: 
7 1.  Creating a new Uniform Commercial Code Article on Controllable Electronic 
8 Records;
9 2.  Updating Article 9 to allow perfection of security interests in digital assets;
10 3.  Promoting new rules for mixed transactions involving both goods and services;
11 4.  Updating rules for electronic negotiable instruments; and
12 5.  Updating terminology to account for electronic signatures and documents.4
