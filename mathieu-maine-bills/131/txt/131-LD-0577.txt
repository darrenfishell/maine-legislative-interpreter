3ยง611.  Online access to election information
4 The Secretary of State, or the secretary's designee, shall develop and maintain a web

7 the Secretary of State, or the secretary's designee, shall post on the web page a link to the

11 web page for use by a municipal or county government.
12SUMMARY
13 This bill requires the Secretary of State, or the secretary's designee, to: develop and 
14 maintain a web page on its publicly accessible website, with general information on state 
15 and local elections; include a link to municipal or county governments' publicly accessible 
16 websites or web pages containing general information on state and local elections; and 
17 provide a template of its web page to municipal and county governments.13
