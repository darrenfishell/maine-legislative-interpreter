22SUMMARY
23 This bill amends the Cannabis Legalization Act in the following ways.  It:
24 1. Creates new definitions for the terms "specified event permit," "permitted premises 
25 for a specified event" and "specified event";
26 2. Amends the requirements applicable to cannabis stores to account for authorized 
27 sales outside of a cannabis store's licensed premises;
28 3. Clarifies the application requirements for a permit for cannabis stores to conduct 
29 sales at a specified event;
30 4. Provides criteria for the Department of Administrative and Financial Services' 
31 approval or denial of a permit application for cannabis stores to conduct sales at a specified 
32 event;
33 5. Provides criteria for the Department of Administrative and Financial Services' 
34 suspension or revocation of a permit for a cannabis store to conduct sales at a specified 
35 event;
36 6. Amends the Department of Administrative and Financial Services' rule-making 
37 authority; and
38 7. Repeals the requirement that the Department of Administrative and Financial 
39 Services issue guidance documents to adult use licensees.23
