28 are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
29SUMMARY
30 This bill provides MaineCare program coverage for noncitizen residents of this State 
31 21 years of age or older with qualifying low incomes who are ineligible for coverage under 
32 the federal Medicaid program due to their immigration status.30
