5§8882.  Forms
6 Forms required under this subchapter must be provided by the bureau and must be 
7 written in an easily understandable format.  In addition to the information required under 
8 section 8883‑B, the bureau may request information regarding business practices and 
9 workers' compensation coverage. A landowner or designated agent may submit any form 
10 required under section 8883-B in an electronic or paper format.
