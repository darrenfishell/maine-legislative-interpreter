127.  Five-year moratorium on mandatory COVID-19 vaccinations.  Notwithstanding 
13 any law to the contrary, mandatory vaccinations for coronavirus disease 2019, which is 
14 also known as COVID-19, are prohibited for 5 years from the date of a vaccine's first 
15 emergency use authorization by the United States Department of Health and Human 
16 Services, Food and Drug Administration in order to allow for safety testing and 
17 investigations into reproductive harm.
