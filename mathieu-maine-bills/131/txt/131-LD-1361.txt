71. Application.  This section applies to behavioral health services that are part of a 
8 nationally recognized model supported by evidence-based research and delivered with 
9 model fidelity.  The services described by this section include, but are not limited to:

14 behavior;
15 E. So-called attachment and biobehavioral catch-up;
16 F. Adolescent community reinforcement approach; and
17 G. Dialectical behavioral therapy.
182. Amount.  A supplement provided under this section for a service must be in an 
19 amount equal to 30% of the reimbursement amount for that service.

25SUMMARY
26 This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to provide a 
27 supplement to MaineCare reimbursement for behavioral health services that are part of a 
28 nationally recognized model supported by evidence-based research and delivered with 
29 model fidelity.  The bill requires the Department of Corrections to contract with providers 
30 who provide behavioral health services that are part of a nationally recognized model 
31 supported by evidence-based research and delivered with model fidelity when contracting 
32 for behavioral health services.26
