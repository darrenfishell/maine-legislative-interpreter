Page 1 - 131LR2164(01)1Sec. 1.  Creation of state employee and legislative child care center.  
2Resolved:  That the Department of Health and Human Services shall conduct a study on 
3 the creation of a state employee and legislative child care center.  The department shall 
4 study practical matters inherent to creating the child care center, including proximity to the 
5 State House, whether to build a new facility or repurpose an existing facility, the time 
6 period for opening the child care center, credits or stipends to pay for child care and staffing 
7 requirements.  As part of the study, the department shall conduct a survey of state 
8 employees and Legislators to determine potential use of the child care center. The 
9 department shall submit a report with findings and recommendations to the Legislature no 
10 later than December 6, 2023.
11SUMMARY
12 This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to conduct a study 
13 on the creation of a state employee and legislative child care center.12
