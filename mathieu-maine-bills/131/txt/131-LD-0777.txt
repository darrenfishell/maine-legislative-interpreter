10 The minimum hourly wage paid to residents manufacturing vehicle registration plates in

12 664, subsection 1.
13SUMMARY
14 This bill provides that the minimum hourly wage for residents at the Maine State Prison 
15 working at tasks related to manufacturing vehicle registration plates must be consistent 
16 with Maine's minimum hourly wage. This bill also changes language in the current law 
17 referring to "inmate" to the wording "resident."14
