2Sec. 1.  Boundary line.  Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, a 
3 portion of the boundary line between Waldo County and Knox County in Penobscot Bay

5 1. Beginning at a point located at N 44º 12' 36" and W 68º 53' 48" in a southwesterly

11SUMMARY
12 This bill clarifies and describes a portion of the common boundary between Waldo 
13 County and Knox County in Penobscot Bay in order to address a discrepancy between 
14 recent maps and the legislative history of the boundary line.12
