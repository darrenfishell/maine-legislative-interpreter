5ยง6691.  Parental notification regarding medical information required
61.  School employee defined.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise 
7 indicates, "school employee" means a school nurse, school counselor, teacher, staff 
8 member, principal or other administrative official at a public or private school.
92.  Prohibitions.  Except in an emergency, a school employee is prohibited from:
10 A.  Withholding from a minor student's parent medical information related to the 
11 student or conversations with the student related to medical issues; or
12 B.  Encouraging or advising a minor student to withhold from the student's parent 
13 medical information related to the student or conversations with the student related to 
14 medical issues.
153.  Civil action.  A civil action may be brought by a parent of a minor student who was 
16 the subject of a violation of this section.  In an action brought pursuant to this section, the 
17 parent may recover the party's actual damages, compensatory damages, punitive damages 
18 or injunctive relief or any combination of those or any other appropriate relief.  A prevailing 
19 parent is entitled to an award of attorney's fees and costs.
20SUMMARY
21 This bill prohibits public and private schools from withholding from a minor student's 
22 parent or encouraging a minor student to withhold from the student's parent medical 
23 information related to the student or conversations with the student related to medical 
24 issues. The prohibition does not apply in an emergency. A parent of a minor student who 
25 was the subject of a violation may bring a civil action to recover the party's actual damages, 
26 compensatory damages, punitive damages, injunctive relief, any combination of those or 
27 any other appropriate relief.  A prevailing parent is entitled to an award of attorney's fees 
28 and costs.21
