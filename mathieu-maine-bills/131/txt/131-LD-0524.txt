3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill would establish requirements for the placement of electric vehicle charging 
6 stations in new construction, including:
7 1. Requiring all new multifamily housing of more than 5 units to have at least 15% of 
8 off-street parking spaces wired for electric vehicle charging; and
9 2.  Requiring all other new construction, with the exception of single-family homes and 
10 retailers that have fewer than 25 parking spaces, to have at least 5% of parking spaces wired 
11 for electric vehicle charging.4
