4SUMMARY
5 This bill:
6 1.  Removes the sales tax exemption for automobiles and parts or accessories related 
7 to automobiles purchased by a person engaged in the business of renting automobiles for 
8 rental of those automobiles for a period of less than one year; 
9 2.  Increases the rate of the sales tax imposed on lodging from 9% to 11%, effective 
10 October 1, 2023;
11 3.  Increases the rate of sales tax imposed on the short-term rental of automobiles from 
12 10% to 15%, effective October 1, 2023; and
13 4.  Increases the size of the middle individual income tax bracket, which has the effect 
14 of increasing the amount of income that may be earned before the top income tax rate 
15 applies.5
