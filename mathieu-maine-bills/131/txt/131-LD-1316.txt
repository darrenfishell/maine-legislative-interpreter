41.  Reporting.  An individual shall report the individual's required work search and 
5 other eligibility activities under section 1192 to the commissioner no later than 5 p.m. on 
6 the Friday of the week in which the activities were completed. The reporting may be 
7 accomplished on a form under subsection 2 online, in writing or at any office operated by 
8 the Department of Labor or by agreement with an organization providing unemployment 
9 or reemployment services to individuals.
102.  Form requirements.  A reporting form under subsection 1 must contain the 
11 following information regarding the individual:
12 A.  The actions taken by the individual to comply with section 1192;
13 B.  The employer, employment office, program or agency the individual interacted with 
14 or participated in with respect to actions taken under paragraph A;
15 C.  The date of every action taken under paragraph A; and
16 D.  A point of contact to verify each action taken under paragraph A.
173.  Verification by commissioner.  Prior to paying benefits to an individual under this 
18 chapter, the commissioner shall verify that the individual completed the required weekly 
19 report of eligibility activities under subsection 1. Each week the commissioner shall select 
20 a sample of at least 1/3 of work search reports received for that week for verification of the 
21 reported information under subsection 2.
22SUMMARY
23 This bill requires individuals receiving unemployment compensation to report weekly 
24 the required work search and other activities the individuals performed and requires the 
25 Commissioner of Labor to select and audit 1/3 of all reports received each week to verify 
26 the statements made in the reports.23
