325.  Copayments.  A carrier offering a health plan in this State is subject to the 
4 following requirements with respect to the copayment for a health care service rendered by 
5 a health care provider other than a primary care provider.
6 A.  The copayment may not be less than a copayment for primary care services.
7 B.  The copayment amount may not be more than 50% of the allowed amount under a 
8 carrier's health plan for that health care service.
9Sec. 2.  Application. The requirements of this Act apply to all health plan policies, 
10 contracts and certificates executed, delivered, issued for delivery, continued or renewed in 
11 this State on or after January 1, 2024.  For purposes of this Act, all policies, contracts and 
12 certificates are deemed to be renewed no later than the next yearly anniversary of the 
13 contract date.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill limits the amount of the copayment that carriers offering health plans in this 
16 State may impose for a health care service rendered by a health care provider, other than a 
17 primary care provider, to no less than the copayment for primary care services and to no 
18 more than 50% of the allowed amount under a carrier's health plan for that health care 
19 service. The requirements apply to health plans beginning on or after January 1, 2024.15
