18SUMMARY
19 This bill states that for purposes of determining public trust rights and common law 
20 rights in intertidal land, the harvesting of naturally occurring seaweed or rockweed is 
21 deemed to be fishing.  The bill exempts obtaining and exercising control over seaweed or 
22 rockweed naturally occurring on intertidal land from the statute governing theft.  The bill 
23 exempts all activities authorized as a public trust right in intertidal land, including 
24 recreation and the harvesting of seaweed and rockweed naturally occurring on intertidal 
25 land, from the statute governing criminal trespass.19
