61.  Definition.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, "eligible 
7 student" means:
8 A.  A high school graduate; or
9 B.  An individual who obtains the equivalent of a high school diploma.
102.  Conditions.  In order to receive a waiver of tuition and fees for up to 2 years at a 
11 community college, an eligible student must:
12 A.  Be enrolled in a community college full-time;
13 B.  Pursue an associate degree or academic credential;
14 C.  Live in the State at the time of enrollment in a community college and for the 
15 duration of that enrollment; and
16 D.  Accept all available federal and state grants, scholarships and other sources of 
17 funding.
183.  Report.  The Maine Community College System shall submit a report annually by 
19 October 1st to the Governor and the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 
20 jurisdiction over education matters on the use and success of enrollment in the Maine 
21 Community College System pursuant to this section.
