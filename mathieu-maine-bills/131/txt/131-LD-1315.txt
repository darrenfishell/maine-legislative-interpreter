5 Annually, a secondary school shall provide to students in each grade an age-appropriate 
6 instructional program on the use and misuse of drugs and controlled substances, the dangers

10 for assistance and training in how to administer an opioid antidote, such as naloxone 
11 hydrochloride, to an overdose victim in an emergency.
12SUMMARY
13 This bill requires secondary schools to annually provide to students an age-appropriate 
14 instructional program on the use and misuse of drugs and controlled substances, the dangers 
15 of substance abuse and substance abuse prevention.  The instructional program must 
16 include a component on providing appropriate assistance to an overdose victim, including 
17 training in how to administer an opioid antidote, such as naloxone hydrochloride.13
