15SUMMARY
16 This bill amends the provisions of law relating to portable electronic device content 
17 information by:
18 1. Replacing the term "portable electronic device" with the term "electronic device" 
19 and redefining the term;
20 2. Replacing the term "content information" with the terms "electronic communication 
21 information" and "electronic device information";
22 3.  Adding definitions of the terms "electronic communication" and "subscriber 
23 information"; and
24 4. Clarifying the circumstances in which a government entity may obtain certain 
25 electronic communication information or access electronic device information pursuant to 
26 a search warrant.16
