3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill would establish a statewide inventory, to be maintained by the Department of 
6 Public Safety, of firearms that were relinquished to law enforcement agencies in civil cases; 
7 the inventory would not include firearms stored as evidence in criminal cases.  The 
8 department would be required to provide data to entities that received federal funds to 
9 address the relinquishment of firearms.4
