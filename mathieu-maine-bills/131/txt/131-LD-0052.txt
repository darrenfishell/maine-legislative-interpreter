15SUMMARY
16 This bill exempts a school employee from the prohibition on possessing a firearm on 
17 public school property or the property of an approved private school or discharging a 
18 firearm within 500 feet of public school property or the property of an approved private 
19 school if the school employee has successfully completed certification and training 
20 prescribed by the Board of Trustees of the Maine Criminal Justice Academy and has been 
21 authorized by the school board to possess or discharge a firearm for the purpose of 
22 enhancing the safety and security of the school and in defense of students, staff and 
23 members of the public on the school premises.  The bill provides that the school employee 
24 is not authorized to exercise law enforcement powers.
25 The bill allows a school board to establish a certification standard and a training 
26 program to arm and supervise certain school employees in a manner and according to 
27 protocols as the school board determines.  The bill specifies that a school board may not 
28 arm a school employee without the employee's voluntary consent and a school employee 
29 may not be censured or discriminated against for unwillingness or refusal to carry a firearm.1
