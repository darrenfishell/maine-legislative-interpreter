2Sec. 1.  22 MRSA §3174-KKK is enacted to read:
3§3174-KKK.  Reimbursement for doula services
41.  Definition.  For purposes of this section, "doula services" means the provision of 
5 services by a nonclinical professional related to the physical, emotional and informational 
6 support of women before, during and after childbirth, including, but not limited to, 
7 guidance and support around pregnancy health, childbirth, breastfeeding and newborn care.
82.  Reimbursement.  The department shall provide reimbursement under the 
9 MaineCare program for doula services.
103.  Rulemaking.  The department shall adopt rules to implement this section. Rules 
11 adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined by Title 5, chapter 
12 375, subchapter 2‑A.
13SUMMARY
14 This bill requires reimbursement under the MaineCare program for doula services.14
