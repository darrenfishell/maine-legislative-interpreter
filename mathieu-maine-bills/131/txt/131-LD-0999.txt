8SUMMARY
9 This bill adds grandparents and great-grandparents with a serious health condition to 
10 the list of persons related to an employee for whom family medical leave may be taken.  It 
11 also provides that an employee may take family medical leave for any of the persons listed 
12 with a serious health condition who are related to the employee by blood, adoption, legal 
13 custody, marriage or domestic partnership. 9
