fund The bill decreases the existing transfer of these revenues to the Adult Use Cannabis Public Health and Safety 
and Mumc1pal Opt-m Fund (PHSMO) from 12% of the revenue from cannabis sales and excise taxes to 12% of the

The bill reduces General Fund revenue by $2,178,000 m fiscal year 2023-24 and $5,633,000 in fiscal year 2024-25 It

decreases revenue to the PHSMO by $297,000 m fiscal year 2023-24 and by $768,000 in fiscal year 2024-25
