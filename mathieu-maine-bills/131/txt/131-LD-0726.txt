29SUMMARY
30 This bill removes the provision of law stating that monetary contributions from party 
31 committees, ballot question committees and political action committees to candidates and 
32 leadership political action committees may not be derived, in whole or in part, from a 
33 business entity.  Under current law, a business entity includes a firm, partnership, 
34 corporation, incorporated association, labor organization or other organization, whether 
35 organized as a for-profit or a nonprofit entity.30
