5ยง4100-A.  Child Care Provider Student Loan Repayment Fund
61.  Fund established.  The Child Care Provider Student Loan Repayment Fund, 
7 referred to in this chapter as "the fund," is established as a dedicated, nonlapsing account 
8 within the department to support child care workers in underserved areas of the State with 
9 the repayment of student loans.
102.  Sources of fund.  The fund consists of funds appropriated or allocated by the State.
113.  Use of fund.  The department shall make distributions from the fund on a first-
12 come, first-served basis to provide up to $6,000 annually to a child care provider that meets 
13 the criteria under subsection 4 for the repayment of student loans.
144.  Award criteria.  In order to be eligible for a distribution under this chapter, a child 
15 care provider must:
16 A. Work in an underserved area of the State;
17 B. Work at least 30 hours per week for a child care facility licensed under section 
18 8301-A, subsection 2 or a family child care provider licensed under section 8301-A, 
19 subsection 3;
20 C. Have worked for a child care facility licensed under section 8301-A, subsection 2 
21 or a family child care provider licensed under section 8301-A, subsection 3 for at least 
22 120 days prior to requesting a distribution under this chapter;
23 D. Have incurred student loans in order to attend an institution of higher education as 
24 defined in Title 20-A, section 11413, subsection 11, whether or not the child care 
25 provider is currently attending the institution; and
26 E. Demonstrate that the child care provider made student loan payments in the amount 
27 of the requested distribution.
285.  Rules.  The department shall adopt rules to implement this section, including, every 
29 2 years, defining the phrase "underserved area of the State."  Rules adopted pursuant to this 
30 subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
