4 behavioral health crisis services under rule Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, 
5 Chapter II, Section 65, Behavioral Health Services, to establish staffing ratios based on a

9 Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules as defined in the 
10 Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
11SUMMARY
12 This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to adopt rules 
13 requiring a facility providing overnight behavioral health crisis services to establish staffing 
14 ratios based on a clinical assessment of the acuity of the residential milieu and not fewer 
15 than one staff person to every 3 clients between the hours of 7 a.m. to 7 p.m. and one staff 
16 person to every 4 clients between the hours of 7 p.m. to 7 a.m.12
