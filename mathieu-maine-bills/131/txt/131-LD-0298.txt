4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill would establish a working group to study the shared use of the public right of 
6 way by competing utilities, new modes of transportation, solar generation and new and 
7 developing modes of electricity distribution.  The working group would be required to 
8 consider expanding the uses of the public right of way as valuable real estate, improving 
9 the appearance of transmission poles and lines and how to maximize revenues from leased 
10 usage of the right of way.4
