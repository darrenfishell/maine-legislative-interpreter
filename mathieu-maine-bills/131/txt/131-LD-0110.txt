11SUMMARY
12 This bill prohibits a parent, guardian or other person legally charged with the care or 
13 custody of a minor who has not attained 10 years of age from intentionally, knowingly or 
14 recklessly leaving that minor in a motor vehicle under conditions that present a substantial 
15 health or safety risk to the minor. Violating this law is a traffic infraction for which a fine 
16 of not more than $50 for the first offense, not more than $100 for the 2nd offense and not 
17 more than $250 for the 3rd and subsequent offenses may be adjudged.12
