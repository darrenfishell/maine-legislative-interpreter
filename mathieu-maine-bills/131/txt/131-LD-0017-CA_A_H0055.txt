COMMITTEE AMENDMENT ,4-,, to H.P. 21, L.D. 17, "An Act to Amend the 
Membership Requirements of the State Claims Commission" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in the first indented paragraph in the 3rd line (page 1, line 
6 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "be" and inserting the following: 'ee have been' 
Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section 
number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment changes "be" to "have been" to clarify that the 2 qualified appraisers 
appointed by the Governor do not need current certifications.
