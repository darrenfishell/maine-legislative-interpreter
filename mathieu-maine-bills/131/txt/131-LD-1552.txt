13 The Lobster Innovation Fund, referred to in this section as "the fund," is established in 
14 the department. Balances in the fund may not lapse and must be carried forward.  The fund 
15 must be used to provide stipends to holders of Class I, Class II or Class III lobster fishing 
16 licenses to participate in the testing of new lobster fishing technologies.

25 This bill establishes the Lobster Innovation Fund to provide stipends to holders of Class 
26 I, Class II or Class III lobster fishing licenses to participate in the testing of new lobster 
27 fishing technologies and appropriates one-time funds of $1 million in each year of the
