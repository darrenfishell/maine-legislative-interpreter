26 SUMMARY 
27 This amendment eliminates the requirement in current law that all financial institutions 
28 must post a copy of their financial statements in their offices. Instead, the amendment 
29 requires a federally insured financial institution to post a notice in its main office that this 
30 information is available to the public upon request. The amendment maintains the 
31 requirement in current law for limited purpose banks.
