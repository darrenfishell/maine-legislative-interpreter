19SUMMARY
20 This bill amends the law governing essential support worker reimbursement under 
21 MaineCare or state-funded home and community-based services and residential services 
22 by increasing the rate for the labor portion of reimbursement to 157% of the minimum 
23 wage as well as reimbursement for taxes and benefits directly related to the labor portion.  
24 As in current law, the reimbursement is tied to any increase in the minimum wage, which 
25 includes an annual cost-of-living increase, and the same cost-of-living increase is applied 
26 to reimbursement for related taxes and benefits.20
