8SUMMARY
9 This bill amends the law governing the system of care for clients with intellectual 
10 disabilities or autism by:
11 1. Requiring the Department of Health and Human Services to pay providers of 
12 services and supports sufficiently to ensure that persons with intellectual disabilities, 
13 autism, brain injury or other related conditions receive the same level of care as the general 
14 population;
15 2. Requiring the Department of Health and Human Services to ensure that waiting lists 
16 under the system are sufficiently short to provide the same level of care as received by the 
17 general population; and
18 3. Creating a cause of action for persons aggrieved by violations by the Department of 
19 Health and Human Services in performing the department’s responsibilities under the 
20 system.1
