15SUMMARY
16 This bill amends the law governing the landowner consideration for the antlerless deer 
17 permit lottery to provide special consideration for all landowners who apply for a permit 
18 and keep their lands open to hunting by the public.  This bill also defines an eligible 
19 landowner as a landowner who owns at least 10 acres of contiguous land and does not post 
20 the landowner's land to prohibit hunting by others.16
