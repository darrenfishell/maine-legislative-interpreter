3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill would establish the Permanent Commission on the Status of Housing in 
6 Maine.  The commission would be a quasi-independent agency funded through the 
7 Department of Economic and Community Development.  Members of the commission 
8 would include housing advocates and organizations, policy makers, housing developers, 
9 housing experts, landlords and tenants.  The commission would identify the hurdles to 
10 developing more housing units and recommend solutions to each branch of government.4
