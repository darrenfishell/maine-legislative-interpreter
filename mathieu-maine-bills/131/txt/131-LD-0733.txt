51. Definitions. As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
6 following terms have the following meanings.
7 A. "Employee benefits" includes, but is not limited to, salary or wages, health insurance 
8 and compensated leave such as vacation, holiday and sick leave.
9 B. "Retirement system" has the same meaning as in section 17001, subsection 36.
102. Public employer to provide employee with annual employee benefits statement.  
11 Beginning January 1, 2024 and annually thereafter, a public employer shall provide to each 
12 public employee, both electronically and by mail, the following information covering the 
13 preceding fiscal year as it applies to that individual public employee:
14 A. An itemized statement of all employee benefits the employee or retiree accrued or 
15 received;
16 B. An itemized statement of all pension benefits the employee or retiree accrued or 
17 received;
18 C. The total amount of employer contributions made from funds appropriated by the 
19 Legislature to the retirement system; and
20 D. The total amount of employer contributions made from funds appropriated by the 
21 Legislature to the applicable health insurance plan, including the group health plan as 
22 described in chapter 13, subchapter 2.
233. Responsibility of administering annual employee benefits statement. Each 
24 public employer is responsible for administering the annual employee benefits statement 
25 described in subsection 2. The public employer shall coordinate with the entities 
26 responsible for administering the employee benefits. 
274. Duties of retirement system. The retirement system shall provide public employers 
28 with the information required in subsection 2 if necessary.  The retirement system shall 
29 provide to retirees the information in subsection 2, paragraphs A to D.
30Sec. 2.  5 MRSA §17103, sub-§15 is enacted to read:
3115.  Create online dashboard for member use.  The board shall create and display 
32 on a publicly accessible website a dashboard for a member to use that will calculate, based 
33 on data provided by the member related to the member’s average monthly expenses, how 
34 much money the member needs in retirement benefits to provide for the member’s average

37SUMMARY
38 This bill requires a public employer to provide to public employees and the Maine 
39 Public Employees Retirement System to provide to retirees an annual statement that 
40 includes the following:38
