3ยง4710-C.  Parental rights regarding removing child from class
41.  Removal from class or activity.  A parent is entitled to remove the parent's child 
5 temporarily from an elementary or secondary school class or other school activity if the 
6 parent considers that the class or school activity espouses, promotes, advances or compels 
7 the parent's child to adopt a viewpoint that conflicts with the viewpoint of the parent.  A 
8 parent temporarily removing a child from a class or other school activity under this 
9 subsection shall deliver to the teacher of the parent's child a written statement authorizing 
10 the removal of the child from the class or other school activity.  A parent is not entitled to 
11 remove the parent's child from a class or other school activity to avoid a test or to prevent 
12 the child from taking a subject for an entire semester.
132.  Alternate class or school activity.  If a child is temporarily removed from a class 
14 or other school activity under subsection 1, the school may require the child to attend an 
15 alternate class or school activity.
