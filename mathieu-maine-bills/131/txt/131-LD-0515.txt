7Sec. 2.  20-A MRSA §1055, sub-§13 is enacted to read:
813.  Administrative procedures or rules.  Upon the adoption of a new policy by the 
9 school board, the superintendent shall establish administrative procedures or rules, as 
10 appropriate, to implement the school board policy and submit the administrative procedures 
11 or rules to the school board for approval.  No later than 7 days before the vote for approval 
12 by the school board, the superintendent shall arrange for notice of the proposed 
13 administrative procedures or rules in a newspaper having general circulation in the area of 
14 the school administrative unit and publish the administrative procedures or rules on the 
15 school administrative unit’s publicly accessible website.  The administrative procedures or 
16 rules may not become effective without the approval of the school board.
17SUMMARY
18 This bill requires school superintendents to establish administrative procedures or rules 
19 to implement school board policies. The proposed administrative procedures or rules must 
20 be published in a local newspaper and published on a school administrative unit's publicly 
21 accessible website at least 7 days prior to the school board vote on the administrative 
22 procedures or rules. The administrative procedures or rules may not become effective 
23 without the approval of the school board.18
