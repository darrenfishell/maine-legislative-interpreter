4§3173-D.  Reimbursement for substance use disorder treatment
5 The department shall provide reimbursement, to the maximum extent allowable, under 
6 the United States Social Security Act, Title XIX, for substance use disorder treatment.  
7 Treatment must include, but need not be limited to, residential treatment and outpatient 
8 care as defined in Title 24‑A, section 2842.  Reimbursement under this section must include 
9 reimbursement for residential treatment for substance use disorder that occurs in another 
10 state when residential treatment is unavailable in the State.
11SUMMARY
12 This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to provide 
13 reimbursement under the MaineCare program for out-of-state residential treatment for 
14 substance use disorder when residential treatment is unavailable in the State.12
