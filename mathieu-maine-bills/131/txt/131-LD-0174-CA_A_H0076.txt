Fiscal Note 
Current biennium cost increase -Maine Community College System 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The Maine Community College System estimates the cost associated with transitioning from the Maine Community 
College System to the Maine Technical College System to be approximately $5,000,000. The cost includes stripping 
and replacing logos on floors; replacing all signage associated with the athletic facilities and all other venues on all 
seven campuses; website redesign and other marketing and rebranding tools used for recruitment, public awareness 
and operations.
