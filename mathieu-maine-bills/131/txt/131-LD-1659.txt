Page 1 - 131LR0910(01)1Preamble.   The Constitution of Maine, Article IX, Section 23 requires that real estate 
2 held by the State for conservation or recreation purposes may not be reduced or its uses 
3 substantially altered except on the vote of 2/3 of all members elected to each House.
4Whereas, certain real estate authorized for leasing by this resolve is under the 
5 designations described in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 12, section 598-A; and
6Whereas, the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the Department of 
7 Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may lease public reserved lands and state park 
8 lands for utilities in accordance with Title 12, section 1814 and section 1852, subsection 4, 
9 respectively; and
10Whereas, the Bureau of Parks and Lands previously issued leases for electric power 
11 and telecommunications pursuant to these laws, and those leases have expired or will soon 
12 expire; and
13Whereas, Title 12, section 1852, subsection 4 provides that poles and electric power 
14 transmission and telecommunications lines and facilities are deemed to substantially alter 
15 public reserved lands and any 25-year lease of public reserved lands for such uses requires 
16 2/3 approval of all members elected to each House; and
17Whereas, the Bureau of Parks and Lands has begun drafting, but not yet provisionally 
18 adopted, the major substantive rules required by Title 12, section 598-C, pursuant to which 
19 the Bureau of Parks and Lands will establish a public administrative process for 
20 determining whether a proposed activity on designated lands under the jurisdiction of the 
21 bureau would cause the land to be reduced or its uses substantially altered; now, therefore, 
22 be it
23Sec. 1. Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands is authorized, but not 
24directed, to lease certain land within Dyer Township, Washington County. 
25Resolved:  That the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the Department of 
26 Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may lease to Greg Tantimonico and Ronald Wright, 
27 on such terms and conditions as the director may direct, certain land within the St. Croix 
28 Waterway in Dyer Township, T.1, R.2, T.S., Washington County. This land is designated 
29 as state park land, and the lease is for an existing aboveground electric line for camp lots.  
30 The lease area occupies approximately 0.5 acres, and the original lease was signed by the 
31 bureau in 2018 and expires on June 30, 2023.
32Sec. 2. Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands is authorized, but not 
33directed, to lease certain land within the Town of Sullivan, Hancock County. 
34Resolved:  That the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the Department of 
35 Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may lease to Versant Power, formerly Bangor 
36 Hydro Electric, on such terms and conditions as the director may direct, certain land within 
37 Donnell Pond Public Reserved Lands in the Town of Sullivan, Hancock County. The lease 
38 is for an existing 30-foot-wide utility corridor and occupies approximately 0.6 acres. The 
39 original lease was signed by the bureau in 1997 and expired on April 1, 2022.
40Sec. 3. Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands is authorized, but not 
41directed, to lease certain land within Township 21, Washington County. 
42Resolved:  That the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the Department of 
43 Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may lease to M&N Operating Co., L.L.C., on such 
Page 2 - 131LR0910(01)44 terms and conditions as the director may direct, certain land on the Township 21 public 
45 reserved lands within Washington County. The lease is for an existing natural gas pipeline 
46 and occupies approximately 5.5 acres. The original lease was signed by the bureau in 1999 
47 and expires on January 31, 2024.
5Sec. 4. Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands is authorized, but not 
6directed, to lease certain land within Upper Enchanted Township, Somerset 
7County. Resolved:  That the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the 
8 Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may lease to the Department of 
9 Administrative and Financial Services, Office of Information Technology, on such terms 
10 and conditions as the director may direct, certain land, formerly leased to the Department 
11 of Transportation, on the Coburn Mountain public reserved lands within Upper Enchanted 
12 Township, Somerset County. The lease is for existing radio telecommunications facilities 
13 and occupies approximately 0.4 acres. The original lease was signed by the bureau in 1999 
14 and expires on June 10, 2024.
15SUMMARY
16 This resolve authorizes the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the 
17 Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to lease certain state park land and
