1SUMMARY
2 This bill transfers funds from the Maine Recovery Fund to the Department of Health 
3 and Human Services, the Department of Corrections and the Department of Education for 
4 substance use and mental health intervention and treatment programs for specific 
5 individuals served by those departments. The bill also provides one-time allocations to the 
6 departments to allow for the expenditure of the transferred funds.1
