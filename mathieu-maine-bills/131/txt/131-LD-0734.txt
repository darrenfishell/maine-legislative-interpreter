34SUMMARY
35 This bill allows the investment in gold, silver or other precious metals by the Treasurer 
36 of State with funds located in pension funds administered by the Treasurer of State and in 
37 the Maine Budget Stabilization Fund, also known as the Rainy Day Fund, and by 
38 municipalities as part of investments lawfully allowed by municipalities.35
