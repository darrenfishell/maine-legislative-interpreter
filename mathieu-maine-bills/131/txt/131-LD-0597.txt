15SUMMARY
16 This bill requires that certified recovery residences have a written discharge and 
17 transfer policy that is approved by the Department of Health and Human Services. It 
18 provides that notwithstanding any landlord and tenant rights and obligations under the 
19 Maine Revised Statutes, Title 14, chapter 709, a recovery residence with an approved 
20 discharge and transfer policy may immediately discharge or transfer a resident in 
21 accordance with the policy if the discharge or transfer is necessary for the resident's 
22 welfare, the resident's needs cannot be met at the recovery residence or the health and safety 
23 of other residents or recovery residence employees are at risk or would be at risk if the 
24 resident continues to live at the recovery residence.  It requires that the department adopt 
25 routine technical rules describing the requirements of a discharge and transfer policy.16
