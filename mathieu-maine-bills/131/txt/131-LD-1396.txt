33SUMMARY
34 This bill proposes statutory changes based on recommendations included in the report 
35 made to the Joint Standing Committee on Health Coverage, Insurance and Financial 
36 Services pursuant to Public Law 2021, chapter 587. The committee has not taken a position 
37 on the substance of this bill.  By reporting this bill out the committee is not suggesting and 
38 does not intend to suggest that it agrees or disagrees with any aspect of this bill.   The 
39 committee is reporting the bill out for the sole purpose of having a bill printed that can be 
40 referred to the committee for an appropriate public hearing and subsequent processing in 
41 the normal course.  The committee is taking this action to ensure clarity and transparency 
42 in the legislative review of the proposals contained in the bill.
