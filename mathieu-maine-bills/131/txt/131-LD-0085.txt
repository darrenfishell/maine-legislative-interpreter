7SUMMARY
8 This resolve directs the Maine Turnpike Authority to conduct a study to evaluate the 
9 potential economic development effects of adding exits to the Maine Turnpike.  The 
10 authority is required to submit a report based on its evaluation by December 6, 2023 for 
11 presentation to the Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.8
