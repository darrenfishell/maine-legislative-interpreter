51.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
6 following terms have the following meanings.
7 A.  "Biodiesel" means the a renewable, biodegradable mono-alkyl esters of long chain 
8 fatty acids derived from plant oils or animal matter fats that meets the requirements of 
9 the American Society of Testing and Materials Standard  most recent ASTM 
10 International standard D6751.   "Biodiesel" includes fuel that otherwise meets the 
11 requirements of this paragraph and also contains up to 1% diesel fuel.
12 A-1.  "Biodiesel blend" means a blend of diesel fuel that contains greater than 5% by 
13 volume of biodiesel and meets the requirements of the most recent ASTM International 
14 standard D7467.
15 B.  "Biomass-based diesel" means a diesel fuel substitute produced from nonpetroleum 
16 renewable resources that meets the registration requirements for fuels and fuel 
17 additives established by the United States Environmental Protection Agency under 42 
18 United States Code, Section 7545 (2012).
19 C.  "Biomass-based diesel blend" and "biodiesel blend" mean means a blend of diesel 
20 fuel that contains greater than 5% by volume of biomass-based diesel  or biodiesel and 
21 petroleum-based diesel fuel.
222.  Number 2 heating oil.  For purposes of this section, all references to diesel include 
23 #2 heating oil.
243.  Transfer document.  A person that sells or otherwise transfers title to a fuel 
25 containing biodiesel, biomass-based diesel  blend or , biomass-based diesel blend or 
26 biodiesel blend to any other person for resale of the product shall prepare a document 
27 evidencing the transfer.  This transfer document may be in the form of an invoice, bill of 
28 lading, bill of sale or other written instrument meeting the requirements of this subsection.  
29 This transfer document must include the name of the transferor, the name of the transferee, 
30 the date of the transfer, the volume in gallons of the product transferred and either the 
31 volume in gallons or the percentage of biomass-based diesel or biodiesel that is contained 
32 in the blended product.  A person making such a transfer shall maintain the transfer 
33 document required by this subsection for a period of 4 years from the transfer date.  As 
34 used in this subsection, the term "resale" does not include a sale of product purchased at a 
35 retail outlet.
364.  Transferee not liable.  A transferee of fuel containing a  biodiesel, biomass-based 
37 diesel, biomass-based diesel blend or biodiesel blend is not liable for failing to verify the 
38 accuracy of the information included in any a transfer document conforming to the 
39 requirements of subsection 3 or for any other liability arising from the transferee's reliance 
40 on such that information.
415.  Supplement other requirements.  The requirements of this section are in addition 
42 to any other requirements or standards in state law.
