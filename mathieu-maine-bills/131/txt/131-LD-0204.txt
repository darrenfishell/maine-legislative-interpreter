4 A school administrative unit shall install interlocking door controller technology in

9 time.
10SUMMARY
11 This bill requires a school administrative unit to install interlocking door controller 
12 technology in every elementary school and secondary school in the unit.  It requires the 
13 State to provide funds to a school administrative unit to install the interlocking door
