2 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
3Whereas, this legislation expands the area in which dogs may be trained for hunting

12Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
13Sec. 1.  12 MRSA §12051, sub-§1, ¶C, as repealed and replaced by PL 2013, c. 
14 588, Pt. A, §16, is amended to read:
15 C.  A resident may train up to 6 dogs at any one time on bear from July 1st to the 4th 
16 day preceding the open season on hunting bear, except in those portions of Washington 
17 County and Hancock County that are situated south of Route 9.
18Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
19 takes effect when approved.
20SUMMARY
21 This bill removes the prohibition on training hunting dogs on bear in those portions of 
22 Washington County and Hancock County that are situated south of Route 9.21
