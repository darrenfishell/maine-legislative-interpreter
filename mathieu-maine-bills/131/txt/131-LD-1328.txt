20ENDOWMENT TAX
21§4951. Definition
22 For purposes of this chapter, "endowment" means a trust or other nonexpendable fund 
23 for investment created and established by private donors for the benefit of a public or 
24 private college or university and held by the trustees of the university or college.
25§4952. Endowment tax
26 A tax is imposed on the annual investment income of an endowment in this State at the 
27 rate of 3%.

Page 2 - 131LR2271(01)1SUMMARY
2 This bill directs the Maine Commission on Indigent Legal Services to develop and 
3 maintain a public defender's office.  It establishes an endowment tax at a rate of 3% on 
4 funds held for investment by the trustees of a public or private college or university that 
5 were generated by trusts created and established by private donors for the benefit of the 
6 university or college.  It establishes the Public Defender's Office Fund within the Maine 
7 Commission on Indigent Legal Services and provides that money received from the 
8 endowment tax must be transferred to that fund until the balance of the fund is $35,000,000.  
9 It provides that any money collected in excess of $35,000,000 must be transferred to the 
10 Maine Civil Legal Services Fund.2
