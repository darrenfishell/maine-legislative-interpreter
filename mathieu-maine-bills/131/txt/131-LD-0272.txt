8Sec. 2. Obstructive sleep apnea working group.  The Secretary of State shall 
9 convene a working group to review the process used by the Office of the Secretary of State 
10 for issuing and renewing a driver's license to a person diagnosed with obstructive sleep 
11 apnea.  The working group must include members of the public diagnosed with obstructive 
12 sleep apnea, physicians and other health care providers who treat patients diagnosed with 
13 obstructive sleep apnea and representatives of the Department of the Secretary of State, 
14 Bureau of Motor Vehicles.  The Secretary of State shall provide a mechanism for relevant 
15 stakeholders to provide input and recommendations to the working group.  By December 
16 6, 2023, the Secretary of State shall submit findings and recommendations regarding the 
17 working group's review to the Joint Standing Committee on Transportation.  The joint 
18 standing committee may report out a bill relating to the findings and recommendations to 
19 the Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature. 
20SUMMARY
21 This bill prohibits the Secretary of State from requiring a holder of a Class C driver's 
22 license who has been diagnosed with obstructive sleep apnea to submit to a driving 
23 evaluation related to obstructive sleep apnea more frequently than every 2 years. The bill 
24 also requires the Secretary of State to convene a working group to review and make 
25 recommendations regarding the process for issuing and renewing a driver's license to a 
26 person diagnosed with obstructive sleep apnea.21
