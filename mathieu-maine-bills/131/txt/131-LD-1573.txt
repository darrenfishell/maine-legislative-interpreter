19SUMMARY
20 This bill requires an application for employment by the State to include a check box to 
21 be checked if the applicant served in an approved national service position under the 
22 Corporation for National and Community Service based in the State, otherwise known as 
23 AmeriCorps, the Maine Service Fellows Program or the Maine Climate Corps Program and 
24 subtracts from federal adjusted gross income for state income tax purposes an amount equal 
25 to a national service educational award received from the National Service Trust or an 
26 approved national service position or an educational loan payment earned through service 
27 with the Maine Service Fellows Program.20
