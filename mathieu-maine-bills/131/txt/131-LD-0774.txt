22SUMMARY
23 This bill requires the Maine State Housing Authority to report annually to the joint 
24 standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over appropriations and financial 
25 affairs with a full accounting of how revenues transferred to the Housing Opportunities for 
26 Maine Fund to be used to support the creation of new housing units through new 
27 construction or adaptive reuse were spent, looking back 10 years.23
