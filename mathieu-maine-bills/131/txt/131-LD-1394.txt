14SUMMARY
15 This bill amends the laws related to the Competitive Skills Scholarship Program to 
16 increase opportunities for program enrollment by increasing revenue available to the 
17 program and providing for coverage of additional services necessary for eligible 
18 individuals to participate in the program.  The bill also requires that registered apprentices 
19 and individuals participating in certified preapprenticeship training programs are eligible 
20 for the program, including for any educational or support services necessary to participate 
21 throughout the period of their apprenticeship or preapprenticeship program.  It also requires 
22 payment by the program of costs required as part of the application process for applicants 
23 who are determined otherwise eligible by the Department of Labor.15
