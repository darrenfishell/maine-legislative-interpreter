41. Recovery residences for mothers and children. The Maine State Housing 
5 Authority shall contract for the construction of at least 15 housing units in certified recovery 
6 residences under section 20057 that provide services to mothers recovering from substance 
7 use disorder and their minor children.
82. Location. The certified recovery residences contracted under this section must be 
9 located in counties with a county seat of no more than 35,000 residents and must be in close 
10 proximity to public transportation or provide transportation to residents of the certified 
11 recovery residences. 
123. Services. A certified recovery residence must provide the following:
13 A. A central shared living and meeting space;
14 B. Recovery support services based on the needs of each resident; and
15 C. Support for reunification services with minor children based on the needs of each 
16 resident.
174. Staffing. Each certified recovery residence contracted under this section must be 
18 staffed by peer-to-peer staff, who must be paid at least livable wages, as defined in Title 
19 26, section 1-A. 
205. Fund. The Rural Recovery Residence Fund is established as a nonlapsing fund to 
21 receive funds to support the activities described in this section. The Maine State Housing 
22 Authority shall distribute funds through a competitive bid process to grantees, who will 
23 provide a minimum of 1:1 matching funds.
24SUMMARY
25 This bill requires the Maine State Housing Authority to contract for the construction of 
26 at least 15 housing units, to be located in certified recovery residences and in counties with 
27 a county seat of no more than 35,000 residents. The certified recovery residences are 
28 required to serve mothers recovering from substance use disorder and promote 
29 reunification with minor children and are required to be staffed with peer-to-peer staff. The 
30 bill establishes the Rural Recovery Residence Fund to receive funds to support the certified 
31 recovery residences. The bill requires that the Maine State Housing Authority distribute 
32 funds through a competitive bid process to grantees, who will provide a minimum of 1:1 
33 matching funds.25
