11SUMMARY
12 This resolve directs the University of Maine System to review, either through clinical 
13 study or by reviewing a compilation of scientific literature, the effects of so-called 5G 
14 wireless technology on bird, bee and insect populations and to study the effects of 
15 long-term exposure to 5G wireless technology and radio frequency radiation on children in 
16 educational settings and to report findings to the Joint Standing Committee on Energy, 
17 Utilities and Technology.12
