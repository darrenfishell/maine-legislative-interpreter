28 percentage change in the Consumer Price Index for the fiscal years ending June 30, 2011,

40SUMMARY
41 This bill, starting with the 132nd Legislature, increases the salary of Legislators to 
42 $25,000 in the first year of each biennium and to $20,000 in the 2nd year of each biennium.41
