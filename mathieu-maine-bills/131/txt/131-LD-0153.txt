34SUMMARY
35 Current law requires that annual estimates of proposed county taxes and notices of 
36 public hearings on proposed county taxes must be sent to the clerk of each municipality in 
37 the county by mail or delivered in person and that proposed county budgets and notices of 
38 public hearings on proposed county budgets must be sent to the clerk of each municipality 
39 in the county by registered or certified mail, return receipt requested, or delivered in person.  
40 This bill allows those proposed county taxes and budgets and public hearing notices to be 
41 sent by electronic or regular mail, with confirmation of receipt by electronic or regular 
42 mail, or delivered in person. 35
