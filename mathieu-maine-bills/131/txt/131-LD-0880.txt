7SUMMARY
8 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to initiate a service development 
9 plan for commuter and passenger train service between Portland and the Lewiston and 
10 Auburn area. The plan must indicate the locations of railway stations in towns along the 
11 corridor route and include an environmental impact statement for each station site. It directs 
12 the department to submit a progress report to the Joint Standing Committee on 
13 Transportation by January 15, 2024.8
