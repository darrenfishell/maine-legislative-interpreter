20SUMMARY
21 This bill provides that the school administrative unit that a student attended 
22 immediately prior to residing at the Long Creek Youth Development Center and attending 
23 the Arthur R. Gould School is responsible for issuing a diploma to that student. The Arthur 
24 R. Gould School is required to forward to the school administrative unit any course work 
25 completed by and academic credits awarded to the student, and the superintendent of the 
26 school administrative unit is required to apply the academic credits to the school 
27 administrative unit's graduation requirements and award a high school diploma upon the 
28 successful completion of all of the graduation requirements.21
