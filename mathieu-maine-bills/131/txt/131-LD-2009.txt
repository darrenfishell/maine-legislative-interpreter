3ยง1823-A.  Release of minors and adults with disabilities
4 A hospital licensed under this chapter that provides services to a minor or an adult with 
5 a disability who is under guardianship in connection with the treatment of that minor or 
6 that adult with a disability shall discharge the minor or the adult with a disability to the care 
7 of a parent or guardian no later than 48 hours after the attending physician has determined 
8 the minor or the adult with disabilities is safe for discharge.  If a parent or guardian does 
9 not take custody of the minor or the adult with a disability within that period, the hospital 
10 shall notify child protective services or adult protective services, as appropriate, which shall 
11 take custody of the minor or the adult with a disability.
12SUMMARY
13 This bill requires a hospital to discharge a minor or an adult with a disability who is 
14 under guardianship to the care of a parent or guardian no later than 48 hours after the 
15 attending physician has determined the minor or the adult with disabilities is safe for 
16 discharge, and if a parent or guardian does not take custody of the discharged minor or the 
17 discharged adult with a disability within that period, the hospital is required to notify child 
18 protective services or adult protective services, as appropriate, which must then take 
19 custody of the minor or the adult with a disability.13
