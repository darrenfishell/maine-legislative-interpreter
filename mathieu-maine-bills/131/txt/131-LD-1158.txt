7§15676-B.  EPS per-pupil rate for career and technical education centers
8 For each career and technical education center, the commissioner shall calculate the 
9 center's EPS per-pupil rate for each year as the applicable per-pupil rate multiplied by an 
10 adjustment calculated by the commissioner based on the costs of:
111. Equipment and infrastructure.  Annual updates and replacements necessary for 
12 equipment specific to centers and infrastructure costs necessary to accommodate these 
13 upgrades in equipment specific to centers; 
142. Transportation.  Transportation for students to and from work sites or centers; and
153.  Staffing.  Staffing necessary to provide a lower ratio between students and staff.
16SUMMARY
17 This bill requires the Commissioner of Education to calculate the per-pupil essential 
18 programs and services rate for career and technical education centers as the applicable per-
19 pupil rate multiplied by an adjustment calculated based on the costs of equipment and 
20 infrastructure, transportation and staffing.17
