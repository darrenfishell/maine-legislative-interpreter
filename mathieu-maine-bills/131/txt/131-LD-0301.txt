4 C.  "Employee" means a person engaged in employment but does not include:

9SUMMARY
10 This bill exempts substitute teachers, other substitute employees, coaches and 
11 temporary employees in school administrative units and part-time or temporary employees 
12 in a retail trade or a service-related industry from being covered by the law that requires 
13 employers to offer earned paid leave to employees.10
