2Sec. 1.  28-A MRSA §713, sub-§2, ¶B is enacted to read:
3 B.  This subsection does not prohibit delivery of malt liquor by a small brewery or 
4 contractor of a small brewery under section 1355-A, subsection 3, paragraph F.
5Sec. 2.  28-A MRSA §1355-A, sub-§3, ¶F is enacted to read:
6 F.  A holder of a small brewery license may deliver to a customer for off-premises 
7 consumption a container of malt liquor produced by the brewery allowed to be 
8 delivered under paragraph B, subparagraph (2). Delivery may be made only to an 
9 individual 21 years of age or older who provides acceptable identification under section 
10 706. The holder may contract with a 3rd party to perform delivery under this paragraph.
11Sec. 3.  28-A MRSA §2073-D, sub-§2, ¶E is enacted to read:
12 E.  A holder of a small brewery license or contractor of the holder may deliver malt 
13 liquor under section 1355-A, subsection 3, paragraph F.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill allows a small brewery or a 3rd-party contractor of a small brewery to deliver 
16 containers of malt liquor produced by the brewery to a customer 21 years of age or older 
17 who provides acceptable identification.15
