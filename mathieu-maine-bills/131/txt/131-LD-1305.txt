Page 1 - 131LR2311(01)1Sec. 1.  Department of Health and Human Services to design and 
2implement a community-based model of care for adolescent mental health.  
3Resolved:  That the Department of Health and Human Services shall design and 
4 implement a community-based model of care that addresses the acute mental health needs 
5 of adolescents with co-occurring disorders.  The model of care must support adolescents 
6 following discharge from residential care for substance use disorder treatment or address 
7 urgent treatment needs during times when residential care for substance use disorder 
8 treatment is not available or desired by the adolescents, the adolescent's family or the 
9 adolescent's treatment team.  The model of care must include the use of an evidence-based 
10 model, such as the so-called assertive continuing care protocol.  No later than December 3, 
11 2025, the Department of Health and Human Services shall submit to the joint standing 
12 committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human services matters a 
13 report demonstrating the efficacy of the model of care and sufficient data to aid in the 
14 development of a future MaineCare reimbursement rate.

22SUMMARY
23 This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to design and 
24 implement a community-based model of care that addresses the acute mental health needs 
25 of adolescents with co-occurring disorders.  It appropriates one-time funds of $965,000 for 
26 this purpose.  It directs the Department of Health and Human Services to, no later than 
27 December 3, 2025, submit to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 
28 jurisdiction over health and human services matters a report demonstrating the efficacy of 
29 the model of care and sufficient data to aid in the development of a future MaineCare 
30 reimbursement rate.22
