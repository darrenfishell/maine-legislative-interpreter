Fiscal Detail and Notes 
/ This bill increases the annual fee to register a pesticide with the Board of Pesticides Control within the Department of 
Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry from $160 to $215. The increased registration fee will result in an additional 
$660,000 in Other Special Revenue Funds revenue starting in fiscal year 2023-24. The bill includes corresponding 
allocations to allow for the expenditure of the additional revenue.
