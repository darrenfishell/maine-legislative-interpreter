3ยง4015.  Monitoring software prohibited
4 A school administrative unit or other public actor may not install monitoring software 
5 on an electronic device that may be used by a student of the school administrative unit 
6 outside of school. For the purposes of this section, "monitoring software" means any device 
7 that may be used by a public actor to observe, photograph, hear, record, amplify or 
8 broadcast sounds or events when the electronic device is being used by a student other than 
9 when the student intends to provide the public actor that access. "Public actor" means any 
10 employee of a state, county, municipal or quasi-municipal government or other actor acting 
11 on behalf of a school administrative unit.
12SUMMARY
13 This bill prohibits a school administrative unit or other public actor acting on behalf of 
14 a school administrative unit from installing monitoring software on an electronic device 
15 that may be used by a student outside of school to monitor that student except when the 
16 student intends to use the electronic device for that purpose.13
