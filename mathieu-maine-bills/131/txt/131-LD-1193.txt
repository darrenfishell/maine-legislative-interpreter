7Sec. 2.  30-A MRSA §4707 is enacted to read:
8§4707.  Pets
91. Definitions. As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
10 following terms have the following meanings. 
11 A. "Common household pet" means a domesticated animal, including but not limited 
12 to a dog or cat, that is commonly kept in the home for pleasure and not commercial 
13 purposes.
14 B. "Public housing" means a residential building funded wholly or partly by the Maine 
15 State Housing Authority or by the housing authority of a municipality.
162.  Pets allowed.  Subject to other applicable federal law and state or local laws 
17 regarding public health, animal control and animal cruelty and rules adopted by the Maine 
18 State Housing Authority or the housing authority of a municipality, the Maine State 
19 Housing Authority, a housing authority of a municipality or other entity in control of public 
20 housing shall allow a resident of public housing to own and maintain one or more common 
21 household pets.
223. Pet deposit allowed.  The Maine State Housing Authority, a housing authority of a 
23 municipality or other entity in control of public housing may charge a reasonable, 
24 refundable pet deposit for each pet to a resident of the public housing who owns one or 
25 more common household pets.
264. Pet rent prohibited. The Maine State Housing Authority, a housing authority of a 
27 municipality or other entity in control of public housing may not charge a resident of the 
28 public housing a monthly fee or other surcharge, other than the pet deposit authorized under 
29 subsection 3, for a common household pet owned and maintained by the resident.
305. Reasonable conditions.  The Maine State Housing Authority, a housing authority 
31 of a municipality or other entity in control of public housing may impose reasonable 
32 conditions on the ownership of a common household pet in the public housing, including 
33 policies on nuisance behaviors, leashing requirements, limitations on the number of 
34 common household pets allowed in a unit based on the unit's size and prohibitions on 
35 dangerous or vicious animals.  A reasonable condition imposed under this subsection may 
36 not include a restriction on the breed or size of the animal or a requirement that an animal 
37 be declawed or devocalized.
386.  Liability. This section may not be construed to affect a landlord's liability.
397. Rules.  The Maine State Housing Authority may adopt rules to implement this 
40 section. Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules as defined in Title 
41 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
