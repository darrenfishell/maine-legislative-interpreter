Page 1 - 131LR0223(01)1Emergency preamble.  Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
2 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
3Whereas, fishing for or taking elvers within the waters of the State may occur only 
4 between March 22nd and June 7th, or until Maine's elver quota, as established by the 
5 Atlantic States Marine Fisheries Commission, has been met, whichever is earlier; and
6Whereas, the elvers taken during the 2022 fishing season were worth nearly 
7 $20,000,000 at the docks; and 
8Whereas, this legislation needs to take effect before the expiration of the 90-day 
9 period in order for it to be in effect for the 2023 fishing season; and
10Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
11 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
12 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
13 therefore,
14Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
15Sec. 1.  12 MRSA §6505-A, sub-§1-A, as enacted by PL 2013, c. 468, §24, is

24Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation

26SUMMARY
27 This bill permits an unlicensed crew member of a holder of an elver fishing license to 
28 empty an elver fyke net.27
