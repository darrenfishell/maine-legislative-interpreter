5ยง3991. Restrictions prohibited
6 Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, a municipality may not:

13SUMMARY
14 This bill prevents a municipality from prohibiting a business, entity or individual from 
15 selling any product or offering any service that is otherwise permitted under state law and 
16 also prohibits a municipality from restricting the operation or licensure of a business or 
17 entity within that municipality based on the type of products or services that the business 
18 or entity plans to provide unless municipalities are provided with the authority to restrict 
19 that type of business or entity under state law.14
