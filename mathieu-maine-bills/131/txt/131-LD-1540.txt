20STABLE HOME FUND PROGRAM
21ยง4767.  Stable Home Fund Program
221.  Fund established.  The Stable Home Fund, referred to in this subchapter as "the 
23 fund," is established within the Maine State Housing Authority.  The fund may receive 
24 money from any available state, federal or private source.  The fund may not lapse, but 
25 must be carried forward to carry out the purposes of this subchapter.
262.  Program purpose. The Stable Home Fund Program, referred to in this subchapter 
27 as "the program," is an eviction prevention support program established within the Maine 
28 State Housing Authority.  The purpose of the program is to provide eligible persons with 
29 $300 per month in rental assistance paid directly to the landlord for up to 24 months or 
30 until those persons secure housing by means of a housing voucher program administered 
31 by the United States Department of Housing and Urban Development under the United 
32 States Housing Act of 1937, Public Law 75-412, 50 Stat. 888, Section 8 or secure a 
33 subsidized housing unit.
343.  Administration. The Maine State Housing Authority shall administer the program 
35 under this section except that the Maine State Housing Authority may delegate the 
36 administration of the program and provide appropriate funding from the fund to a municipal 
37 housing authority with respect to providing assistance to eligible persons located within the 
38 municipal housing authority's jurisdiction. The administrator of the program may not 
Page 2 - 131LR1790(01)39 require an application process for property owners, rental payment standards or unit 
40 inspections.
34.  Eligibility.  To be eligible for the program a person must:
4 A.  Earn less than 60% of the median income for the area as defined by the United 
5 States Department of Housing and Urban Development under the United States 
6 Housing Act of 1937, Public Law 75-412, 50 Stat. 888, Section 8; and
7 B.  At the time of application to the program, not be using a federal housing voucher 
8 to pay rent or live in a subsidized housing unit.
9Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
10 takes effect when approved.
11SUMMARY
12 This bill, which includes an emergency preamble and emergency clause, creates the 
13 Stable Home Fund and the Stable Home Fund Program within the Maine State Housing 
14 Authority.  The purpose of the program is to provide persons earning up to 60% of the 
15 median income for an area as defined by the United States Department of Housing and 
16 Urban Development with $300 per month in rental assistance paid directly to the landlord 
17 for up to 24 months or until those persons secure housing by means of a housing voucher 
18 program administered by the United States Department of Housing and Urban 
19 Development under the United States Housing Act of 1937, Public Law 75-412, 50 Stat. 
20 888, Section 8, as amended, or secure a subsidized housing unit.
21 The bill directs the Maine State Housing Authority to administer the program except 
22 that the Maine State Housing Authority may delegate the administration of the program 
23 and provide appropriate funding from the fund to a municipal housing authority with 
24 respect to eligible persons located within the municipal housing authority's jurisdiction.1
