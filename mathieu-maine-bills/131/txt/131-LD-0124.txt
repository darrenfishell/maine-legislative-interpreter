8SUMMARY
9 This bill requires the Department of Transportation to maintain the right-of-way of a 
10 circular intersection on a state or state aid highway, including the center traffic island and 
11 the perimeter of the circular intersection. It defines the term "circular intersection" to 
12 include rotaries, roundabouts and traffic circles.9
