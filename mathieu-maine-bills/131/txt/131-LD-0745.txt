38-A.  Toy cannons.  The use of a toy cannon is governed by this subsection in addition 
4 to the other requirements of this chapter.   A person may not use a toy cannon in a location 
5 where there is any affected property owner, unless the person has the written permission of 
6 each affected property owner.
7 As used in this subsection, "toy cannon" means a type of consumer fireworks in the shape 
8 of a cannon that, when discharged, emits a sound of more than 150 decibels, as measured 
9 at the cannon. As used in this subsection, "affected property owner" means any person 
10 owning or renting a residence located on property that is within 1,000 feet of the location 
11 of the use of the toy cannon.
12 A person who violates this subsection commits a civil violation for which a fine of $100 
13 may be adjudged for any one offense.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill requires a person using a toy cannon fireworks device, which when discharged 
16 emits a sound of more than 150 decibels, to get permission from affected property owners.15
