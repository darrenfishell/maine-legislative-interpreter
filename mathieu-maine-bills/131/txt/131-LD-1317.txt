Page 1 - 131LR2369(01)1Sec. 1. Commission established. Resolved:  That the Commissioner of Health 
2 and Human Services shall establish the Commission to Promote the Integration of Human 
3 Health and Environmental Health, referred to in this resolve as "the commission."
4Sec. 2. Commission membership. Resolved:  That the Commissioner of Health 
5 and Human Services shall invite members of the Legislature, representatives of the logging 
6 industry, representatives of the manufacturing industry, a representative of a hunting 
7 organization, a representative of the outdoor recreation community and medical 
8 professionals, a representative of a conservation organization, a representative of small 
9 farmers and 4 representatives of small, private landowners, as well as appropriate staff of 
10 the Department of Health and Human Services, to serve on the commission.
11Sec. 3. Commission duties. Resolved:  That the commission shall conduct a 
12 comprehensive review of the overall health of the State's population, health care access and 
13 delivery systems in the State and environmental health in the State and the interrelation of 
14 those public health areas in order to develop recommendations to achieve health equity in 
15 the State through an integrated health care approach.
16Sec. 4. Report. Resolved:  That, by December 6, 2023, the Commissioner of Health 
17 and Human Services shall submit a report that includes the findings and recommendations 
18 of the commission, including suggested legislation, for presentation to the Joint Standing 
19 Committee on Health and Human Services. The committee is authorized to submit 
20 legislation related to the report to the Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.
21SUMMARY
22 This resolve directs the Commissioner of Health and Human Services to establish the 
23 Commission to Promote the Integration of Human Health and Environmental Health, 
24 which is directed to conduct a comprehensive review of the overall health of the State's 
25 population, health care access and delivery systems in the State and environmental health 
26 in the State and the interrelation of those public health areas and develop recommendations 
27 to achieve health equity in the State through an integrated health care approach. The 
28 commissioner is required to submit a report based on the commission's review by 
29 December 6, 2023 for presentation to the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human 
30 Services, which is authorized to submit legislation related to the report to the Second 
31 Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.22
