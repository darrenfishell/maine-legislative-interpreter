18 A.  The requirement for public hearing is not a prerequisite to the valid issuance of any 
19 bond, note or other obligation of a municipality authorized to borrow money by vote 
20 under any such particular article.
21 B.  If a particular article to be voted on by secret ballot requests an appropriation of 
22 money by the municipality, the article, when printed in the warrant and on the ballot, 
23 must be accompanied by a recommendation of the municipal officers.
24 (1)  If by town meeting vote or charter provision, a budget committee has been 
25 established to review proposed town expenditures, the recommendations of the 
26 budget committee shall be printed in addition to those of the municipal officers.
27 (2)  If the action affects the school budget, a recommendation by the school board 
28 shall be printed in addition to those of the municipal officers and the budget 
29 committee, if any.

31 moderator and one or more referendum questions to be voted on by secret ballot, the 
32 municipal officers may specify the same voting places as those used by the town for
