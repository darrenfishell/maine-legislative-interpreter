2Sec. 1.  29-A MRSA §1306-A is enacted to read:
3§1306-A.  Instructor-administered test
4 A person with a valid instructor license under section 1354 who is employed by a driver 
5 education school licensed under section 1354 may administer road tests for Class C 
6 noncommercial driver's licenses in accordance with rules adopted by the Secretary of State. 
7 The Secretary of State shall adopt rules to implement this section. Rules adopted pursuant 
8 to this section are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
9Sec. 2.  29-A MRSA §1307, sub-§5, as amended by PL 2013, c. 381, Pt. B, §17, is 
10 further amended to read:
115.  Exception  Exceptions.  A person required to take an examination pursuant to 
12 section 1309, subsection 1 is not required to pay an examination or cancellation fee. The 
13 Secretary of State may, in accordance with rules adopted under section 1306-A, modify the 
14 fee under subsection 2 related to road tests for Class C noncommercial licenses when the 
15 person administering the road test is an instructor licensed under section 1354.
16SUMMARY
17 This bill allows a person who has a valid driver education instructor license and is 
18 employed by a licensed driver education school to administer road tests to Class C 
19 noncommercial driver's license applicants in accordance with rules adopted by the
