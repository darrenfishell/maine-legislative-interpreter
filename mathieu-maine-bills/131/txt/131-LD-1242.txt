17Sec. 21.  Additional changes to law.  The Department of the Secretary of State, 
18 Bureau of Motor Vehicles shall submit a bill to the Second Regular Session of the 131st 
19 Legislature to make changes in the Maine Revised Statutes to fully abolish inspection 
20 requirements for motor vehicles registered in the State, with the exception of inspection 
21 requirements for commercial motor vehicles, trailers and semitrailers under Title 29-A, 
22 section 1753; inspection of fire trucks under Title 29-A, section 1755; and inspection by 
23 dealers and transporters under Title 29-A, section 1754, which must be retained.
24SUMMARY
25 This bill repeals statutory provisions requiring inspections for motor vehicles registered 
26 in the State, except that the provisions regarding inspection of commercial motor vehicles, 
27 trailers and semitrailers under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 29-A, section 1753; 
28 inspection of fire trucks under Title 29-A, section 1755; and inspection by dealers and 
29 transporters under Title 29-A, section 1754 are not repealed.25
