22SUMMARY
23 This bill does the following.
24 1.  It eliminates provisions relating to the annual reports to the joint standing committee 
25 of the Legislature having jurisdiction over inland fisheries and wildlife matters on deer and 
26 moose management.
27 2. It replaces the provision that requires mandatory submission to complete chemical 
28 tests to determine a person's alcohol level or other chemical use by analysis of blood, breath 
29 or urine after that person has been involved in a hunting accident or operated a watercraft, 
30 snowmobile or ATV and it resulted in serious injury or death.  It also removes the provision 
31 requiring a person under 16 years of age to be accompanied by a parent or guardian in an 
32 ATV training program.
33 3. It provides clarity on how a super pack licensee applies for an antlerless deer permit.
34 4. It resolves a conflict in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 12 regarding the cost of 
35 combination licenses.
36 5. It removes references to specific types of personal flotation device.
37 6. It renames the Black Bear Research Fund the Black Bear Research and Management 
38 Fund and modifies its purpose to allow the fund to be used for both research and 
39 management activities.23
