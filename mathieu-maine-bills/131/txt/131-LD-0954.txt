41.  Child care facility indoor air quality.  The department shall provide on a publicly 
5 accessible website resources and information on strategies to improve indoor air quality at 
6 child care facilities. The department shall provide grants from the fund to child care 
7 facilities to improve indoor air quality at the facilities.
82.  Child Care Facility Indoor Air Quality Grant Fund.  The Child Care Facility 
9 Indoor Air Quality Grant Fund, referred to in this section as "the fund," is established in 
10 and administered by the department.  The fund's purpose is to provide grants to child care 
11 facilities to improve indoor air quality at the facilities. The fund is interest-bearing and may 
12 receive funds from appropriations, allocations, donations, grants and other public or private 
13 sources. Funds in the fund at the end of the fiscal year do not lapse but carry over to the 
14 next fiscal year.
153.  Rules.  The department may adopt rules to carry out the purposes of this section.
