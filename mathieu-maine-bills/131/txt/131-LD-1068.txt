12Sec. 9.  12 MRSA §11229 is enacted to read:
13§11229.  Hunting in an enclosed area
141.  Prohibition.  A person may not instigate, promote, participate in, aid or abet, as a 
15 principal, agent, employee, participant or spectator, participate in the earnings from or 
16 intentionally maintain or allow any place to be used for the shooting, killing or wounding 
17 with a firearm or a deadly weapon of an animal that is confined within artificial boundaries 
18 or tied, staked out, caged or otherwise intentionally restrained, regardless of the animal's 
19 size.
202.  Penalty.  A person who violates subsection 1 commits a Class E crime.

38SUMMARY
39 This bill prohibits hunting animals in an enclosed area and specifies that a person who 
40 violates the prohibition commits a Class E crime. The bill also repeals the law that permits 
41 the establishment, operation and use of commercial large game shooting areas.39
