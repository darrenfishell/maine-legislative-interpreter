32SUMMARY
33 This bill:
34 1. Increases from 50% to 100% the amount of the employment tax increment the State 
35 Tax Assessor is required to deposit into the Loring Job Increment Financing Fund;
36 2. Expands the allowable uses of the Loring Job Increment Financing Fund to include 
37 electricity, telecommunications and the maintenance of buildings and facilities and 
38 provides that covered municipal services and maintenance and restoration must relate to 
39 projects involving the maintenance or restoration of buildings or facilities that will create 
40 or retain jobs and promote economic development;33

2 from 2026 to 2046; and
3 4. Provides that a payment from the Loring Job Increment Financing Fund that an 
4 employer receives or is eligible for based on a percentage of employer withholding taxes
