15SUMMARY
16 This bill requires a candidate to provide a municipality of residence and removes the 
17 requirement to provide a place of residence on primary petitions and nonparty nomination 
18 petitions if that candidate is running for a federal office.  The bill also provides that the 
19 candidate consent for primary and nonparty candidates must redact the place of residence 
20 of a candidate for a federal office when that consent is made available as a public record.16
