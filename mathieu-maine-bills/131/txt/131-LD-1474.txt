7SUMMARY
8 This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rules in 
9 Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 67, Principles of 
10 Reimbursement for Nursing Facilities, to require the department to reimburse at least 75% 
11 of a nursing facility's costs pursuant to the facility's cost reports within 90 days of receipt.8
