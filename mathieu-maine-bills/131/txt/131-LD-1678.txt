Page 1 - 131LR1617(01)1Sec. 1.  Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to study and 
2report on soil carbon sequestration incentive programs.  Resolved:  That the 
3 Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry shall study and report on soil carbon 
4 sequestration incentive programs that can be applied to land in this State, including but not 
5 limited to forest land, agricultural land, conservation land and wetlands, and determine 
6 possible funding sources that may be used to fund the programs. The department shall 
7 submit its report, including any recommended legislation, to the Joint Standing Committee 
8 on Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry no later than December 6, 2023. The committee 
9 may report out legislation regarding the report to the Second Regular Session of the 131st 
10 Legislature.
11SUMMARY
12 This resolve directs the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to study 
13 and report on soil carbon sequestration incentive programs that can be applied to land in 
14 this State, including but not limited to forest land, agricultural land, conservation land and 
15 wetlands, and determine possible funding sources that may be used to fund the programs. 
16 The department must submit its report, including any recommended legislation, to the Joint 
17 Standing Committee on Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry no later than December 6, 
18 2023. The committee may report out legislation regarding the report to the Second Regular 
19 Session of the 131st Legislature.12
