2CONCEPT DRAFT
3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill would:
6 1. Provide funding for the consolidation of the Department of Inland Fisheries and 
7 Wildlife facilities by relocating to a larger building to be used as headquarters in the City 
8 of Augusta, upgrading the wireless technology and installing secure badge-access 
9 technology;
10 2. Authorize the Maine Governmental Facilities Authority to issue securities in an 
11 amount not to exceed $43,450,000 outstanding at any one time for a headquarters facility 
12 for the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife; 
13 3. In the event that financing by the Maine Governmental Facilities Authority is not 
14 feasible,  authorize the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to issue a request for 
15 proposals from private developers for a long-term lease for a headquarters facility for the 
16 department; and
17 4. Authorize the Department of Administrative and Financial Services to purchase the 
18 following parcels of land and any buildings thereon in the City of Augusta for a 
19 headquarters facility for the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife:
20 A. Parcel 1: for $1, the property formerly known as the Statler Tissue Company mill 
21 property; and
22 B. Parcel 2: by eminent domain, an adjacent property located to the south of Parcel 1, 
23 currently owned by A.I.M. Recycling.4
