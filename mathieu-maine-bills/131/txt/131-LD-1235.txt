4 Beginning January 1, 2024, a jail employee may not work more than 18 hours in a 24-
5 hour period except in exceptional circumstances or when the Governor has declared a state 
6 of emergency pursuant to Title 37-B, section 742, subsection 1. For the purposes of this 
7 section, "exceptional circumstances" means circumstances beyond the control of the sheriff 
8 or jail administrator that pose a threat to the safekeeping of persons committed or 
9 transferred to or detained in the custody of the jail, the safe operation of the jail in 
10 accordance with this chapter and the standards set pursuant to Title 34-A, chapter 1, 
11 subchapter 2 or the safe performance by employees of the jail of their duties.
12Sec. 2. Compensation for jail employees. The Treasurer of State shall issue 
13 additional compensation of $2,500 to each employee of a county jail under the Maine 
14 Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, chapter 13.
15SUMMARY
16 This bill prohibits, beginning January 1, 2024, a jail employee from working more than 
17 18 hours in a 24-hour period except in exceptional circumstances or when the Governor 
18 has declared a state of emergency. The bill also directs the Treasurer of State to issue 
19 additional compensation of $2,500 to every jail employee in the State.16
