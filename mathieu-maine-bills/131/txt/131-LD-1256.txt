2Sec. 1.  20-A MRSA §12302, sub-§1, as amended by PL 2009, c. 8, §1, is further 
3 amended to read:
41.  Establishment.  The Maine Dental Education Loan Program, referred to in this 
5 chapter as "the program," is established.  The authority shall administer the program.  
6 Under this program, beginning in fiscal year 2000-01 and ending in fiscal year 2007-08, 
7 the chief executive officer shall award up to 3 loans or loan repayment agreements annually 
8 up to an aggregate of 12.  Beginning in fiscal year 2008-09, the chief executive officer shall 
9 award up to 3 loans or loan repayment agreements annually for doctors of dental medicine 
10 and up to 6 loans or loan repayment agreements annually for dental hygienists, dental 
11 therapists, expanded function dental assistants or dental assistants and may award 
12 additional loans or loan repayment agreements annually as funds permit.  As provided in 
13 subsection 3:
14 A.  Loans are available to Maine residents enrolled in a dental school or enrolled in any 
15 accredited school in a program of study for dental hygienists, dental therapists, 
16 expanded function dental assistants or dental assistants; or
17 B.  A loan repayment agreement is available to a person who is eligible for licensure 
18 as a doctor of dental medicine or as a dental hygienist, dental therapist, expanded 
19 function dental assistant or dental assistant in Maine and who has outstanding dental 
20 education loans.
