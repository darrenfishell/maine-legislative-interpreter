15Sec. 3.  5 MRSA §20005, sub-§23 is enacted to read:
1623.  Treatment centers.  Establish 2 treatment centers for substance use disorder 
17 treatment.  The treatment centers must have at least 10 beds each.  At least 40% of the 
18 occupancy in the treatment centers must be made available to individuals who have 
19 coverage under the MaineCare program.  A treatment center established pursuant to this 
20 subsection shall provide:
21 A.  A receiving center that is open 24 hours per day, 7 days per week to provide 
22 substance use disorder treatment while a patient waits for access to a higher level of 
23 care;
24 B.  Medically managed withdrawal services;
25 C.  Access to medication to treat substance use disorder, including any medication 
26 approved by the United States Food and Drug Administration; and
27 D.  Coordination with services after treatment, including but not limited to local 
28 recovery centers, hypodermic apparatus exchange programs and recovery residences.
29 No later than February 1, 2025 and annually thereafter, the department shall report to the 
30 joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human 
31 services matters on the number of people served, the types of services provided, the 
32 attempts made at community outreach and any recommendations relating to the services 
33 provided by the treatment centers.
34Sec. 4.  Request for proposals.  No later than December 1, 2023, the Department

36 establish and operate the treatment centers required pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes,
