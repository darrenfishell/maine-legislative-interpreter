9SUMMARY
10  This bill requires all licensed nursing facilities to prevent injury or death by providing 
11 silent bed alarms that directly signal nursing home staff to residents who have dementia 
12 and are unable to use a call button if they fall out of a bed or chair. The bill also directs the 
13 Department of Health and Human Services to adopt rules to implement this provision.10
