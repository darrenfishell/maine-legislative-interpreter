8SUMMARY
9 This resolve directs the Department of Education to convene a working group of 
10 stakeholders to examine alternative metrics related to disadvantaged students to use in 
11 distributing funds in the school funding formula under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 
12 20-A, chapter 606-B.  The department is required to submit a report by January 1, 2024 to 
13 the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs.9
