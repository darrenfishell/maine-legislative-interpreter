17SUMMARY
18 This bill accomplishes the following.
19 1.  It repeals provisions relating to the county and municipal detention facility advisory 
20 committee and transfers the function of advising the Commissioner of Corrections with 
21 respect to all jail standards to the County Corrections Professional Standards Council.
22 2.  It provides that lottery winnings are set off against victim restitution owed as a result 
23 of a court order issued under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 17-A.
24 3.  It allows the Commissioner of Corrections to appoint a director of women’s services 
25 to act as the chief administrative officer of any adult correctional facility female housing 
26 units. 
27 4. It renames the chief administrative officer of the Mountain View Correctional 
28 Facility and the chief administrative officer of the Downeast Correctional Facility as the 
29 warden, which is the name of the chief administrative officers of the other adult correctional 
30 facilities with medium or higher security housing units.
31 5. It repeals provisions referring to the Mountain View Correctional Facility as a 
32 juvenile facility.
33 6.  It repeals a provision regarding accreditation of correctional and detention facilities 
34 and instead requires the Department of Corrections to operate those facilities in accordance 
35 with best practices and to have the operation of those facilities reviewed.18
