26Sec. 7.  30-A MRSA §1663 is enacted to read:
27§1663.  Prisoner accounts
28 A county jail that maintains prisoner accounts containing money to be used within the 
29 county jail for optional items and services shall create a fund to provide money in accounts 
30 for indigent prisoners.  Money for this fund may be from gifts, grants and donations to the 
31 county jail for that purpose or from an option for released prisoners to donate the balance 
32 of an account to the fund on release.
33SUMMARY
34 This bill requires county jails to employ a transition coordinator who is responsible for 
35 creating a transition plan for each inmate scheduled for release. The bill requires county 
36 jails to have a written policy regarding prisoner leave for funerals, to provide all prisoners 
37 with a copy of the rules of the jail applicable to furloughs and to have a written policy 
38 regarding once-per-week visitation for prisoners and requires a county jail that maintains 
39 prisoner accounts containing money to be used within the county jail for optional items and 
40 services to create a fund to provide money in accounts for indigent prisoners. The bill 
41 requires the State to pay for certain prisoner education programs.34
