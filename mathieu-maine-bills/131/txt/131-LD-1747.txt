35SUMMARY
36 This bill decreases the general sales tax rate from 5.5% to 5.25% beginning October 1, 
37 2023 and to 5% beginning July 1, 2024. This bill does not change the rate of the sales tax 
38 imposed on prepared food, lodging, rental vehicles, liquor or adult use cannabis.36
