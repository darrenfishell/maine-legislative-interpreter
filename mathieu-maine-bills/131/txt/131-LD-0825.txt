51.  Requirements.  A provider of a transportation service covered by the MaineCare 
6 program to a member of the MaineCare program with multiple disabilities shall:
7 A. Ensure that a vehicle transporting the MaineCare member is equipped in a manner 
8 that accommodates the member's disabilities safely;
9 B.  Ensure that the driver is physically capable of assisting the MaineCare member in 
10 the event of an emergency; and
11 C. To the extent possible, provide consistency in drivers.
122.  Rulemaking.  The department shall adopt rules to implement this section. Rules 
13 adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined by Title 5, chapter 
14 375, subchapter 2-A.
15SUMMARY
16 This bill requires that transportation services provided to members of the MaineCare 
17 program who have multiple disabilities ensure that a vehicle transporting the MaineCare 
18 member is equipped in a manner that accommodates the member's disabilities safely, 
19 ensure that the driver is physically capable of assisting the member in the event of an 
20 emergency and, to the extent possible, provide consistency in drivers.16
