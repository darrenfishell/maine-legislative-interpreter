5 Beginning January 1, 2024, the department shall reimburse under the MaineCare 
6 program for the treatment of personality disorder and emotional dysregulation.  The 
7 department shall establish a bundled reimbursement rate for the treatment of MaineCare 
8 members diagnosed with personality disorder or emotional dysregulation.  In establishing 
9 the bundled reimbursement rate, the department shall consider various bundled 
10 reimbursement rate structures, including a daily rate and a monthly rate. The bundled 
11 reimbursement rate must include participation in a dialectical behavior therapy intensive 
12 outpatient program, psychiatry services, nursing services, case management, residential 
13 services, after-hours skills coaching and supported employment services.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services, beginning January 1, 
16 2024, to reimburse under the MaineCare program for the treatment of personality disorder 
17 and emotional dysregulation.  The bill also requires the department to establish a bundled 
18 reimbursement rate for the treatment of MaineCare members diagnosed with personality 
19 disorder or emotional dysregulation.15
