20SUMMARY
21 This bill amends the provisions of law governing the use of tinted windows in motor 
22 vehicles by allowing the use of a tinted front windshield that allows a light transmittance 
23 of not less than 75% net of glass and material and the use of side or rear tinted windows 
24 that allow a light transmittance of not less than 20% net of glass and material.  The bill also 
25 repeals the provision of law that requires that front and side vehicle windows contain 2-
26 way glass that provides the occupants with a clear view of the road and a person outside 
27 the vehicle with a clear view of the occupants and the interior of the vehicle. 21
