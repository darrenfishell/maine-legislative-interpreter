23SUMMARY
24 This bill requires cannabis stores to label their adult use cannabis and adult use 
25 cannabis products with a message stating that the products contain or may contain 
26 tetrahydrocannabinol, or THC, which is harmful to animals even in residual amounts, and 
27 to place signs in the store with the same message.24
