2Sec. 1.  5 MRSA §13056-K is enacted to read:
3§13056-K.  COVID-19 Illness Mortgage Assistance Program
41.  Program established; administration.  The COVID-19 Illness Mortgage 
5 Assistance Program, referred to in this section as "the program," is established within the 
6 department to provide mortgage assistance to homeowners who are unable to pay a 
7 mortgage due to hardship resulting from illnesses related to coronavirus disease 2019, also 
8 known as COVID-19.  The department shall administer the program. 
92.  COVID-19 Illness Mortgage Assistance Fund.  The COVID-19 Illness Mortgage 
10 Assistance Fund, referred to in this section as "the fund," is established as a fund within the 
11 department for the purpose of providing funds for the program. The fund consists of money 
12 appropriated to the fund by the Legislature and any money received by the department for 
13 the purposes of the program.  
143.  Grants. The department shall solicit applications for grants from the fund and award 
15 grants from the fund based on criteria set forth in rules adopted by the department.
164.  Program reporting.  A recipient of grant funds through the program shall cooperate 
17 with the department in performing reporting requirements.  
185.  Rulemaking.  The department shall establish by rule the criteria for eligibility for 
19 grants from the program and the process of application. Rules adopted pursuant to this 
20 subsection are routine technical rules as defined in chapter 375, subchapter 2‑A.
21SUMMARY
22 This bill creates the COVID-19 Illness Mortgage Assistance Program within the 
23 Department of Economic and Community Development to provide mortgage assistance to 
24 homeowners who are unable to pay a mortgage due to hardship resulting from illnesses 
25 related to COVID-19.22
