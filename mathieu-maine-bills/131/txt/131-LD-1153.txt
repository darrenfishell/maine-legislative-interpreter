16SUMMARY
17 This bill removes the municipal personal property tax exemption for solar energy 
18 equipment that generates heat or electricity if all of the energy is used on the site where the 
19 property is located or the energy is transmitted through the facilities of a transmission and 
20 distribution utility, and a utility customer or customers receive a utility bill credit for the 
21 energy generated by the equipment.17
