16SUMMARY
17 This bill repeals the law restricting the use of single-use carry-out plastic bags by retail 
18 stores and instead allows a retailer to use plastic bags to bag products at the point of retail 
19 sale if the retailer locates inside the store or within 20 feet of the main entrance to the store 
20 a receptacle for collecting any used plastic bags and ensures that the plastic bags collected 
21 are recycled or delivered to a person engaged in recycling plastics.  It also stipulates that a 
22 retailer may provide customers with plastic bags, paper bags or reusable bags at the point 
23 of retail sale and may allow customers to use their own reusable bags at the point of retail 
24 sale.17
