3115.  Machinery and equipment used to provide broadband communications.  
4 Beginning January 1, 2024, sales of machinery and equipment used to provide broadband 
5 communications service to a broadband communications service provider. 
6 For purposes of this subsection:
7 A. "Broadband communications service" means Internet access service, 
8 telecommunications services under section 2551, subsection 20-A or video 
9 programming service;
10 B. "Broadband communications service provider" means a person or an affiliate of a 
11 person who provides broadband communications service;
12 C. "Machinery and equipment used to provide broadband communications service" 
13 includes but is not limited to a wire, cable, fiber, conduit, antenna, pole, switch, router, 
14 amplifier, rectifier, repeater, receiver, multiplexer, duplexer, transmitter, circuit card, 
15 insulating and protective material and case, power equipment, backup power 
16 equipment, storage device, modem, software, cable modem termination system 
17 component, wireless fidelity equipment and other central office or so-called headend 
18 and hub equipment including a channel card, frame and cabinet or equipment used in 
19 successor technology including an item used to monitor, test, maintain, enable or 
20 facilitate qualifying equipment, machinery, ancillary component, appurtenance, 
21 accessory or other infrastructure that is used in whole or in part to provide broadband 
22 communications service; and
23 D. "Video programming service" means video programming and associated audio 
24 signals used to provide consumer entertainment and information service by means of 
25 broadcast cable television or direct-to-home satellite delivery, Internet or other forms 
26 of mass communication.
27SUMMARY
28 This bill exempts from the sales and use tax sales of machinery and equipment used to 
29 provide broadband communications service to a broadband communications service 
30 provider.28
