4Constitution, Art. IX, ยง26 is enacted to read:
5Section 26.  Revenues from motor vehicle sales and use taxes.  Beginning in 
6 July 2024, 50% of the revenue from the sales and use taxes imposed on sales of motor 
7 vehicles and sales related to motor vehicles must be deposited in the Highway Fund for 
8 road and bridge capital improvements. Beginning in August 2024, and every month 
9 thereafter, the State Controller shall transfer to the Highway Fund for road and bridge 
10 capital improvements an amount that is equal to 50% of the revenue from the taxes imposed 
11 on sales of motor vehicles and sales related to motor vehicles for the previous month. The 
12 amount transferred must be based on actual sales for that month and may not consider any 
13 accruals that may be required by law. The State Tax Assessor shall certify to the State 
14 Controller by the 15th day of each month the amounts to be transferred and credited under 
15 this section as of the close of the State Controller's records for the previous month.
16Constitutional referendum procedure; form of question; effective date.  
17Resolved:  That the municipal officers of this State shall notify the inhabitants of their 
18 respective cities, towns and plantations to meet, in the manner prescribed by law for holding 
19 a statewide election, at a statewide election held in November 2023, to vote upon the 
20 ratification of the amendment proposed in this resolution by voting upon the following 
21 question:
22 "Do you favor amending the Constitution of Maine to dedicate 50% of 
23 sales and use tax revenue from sales of motor vehicles and sales related to 
24 motor vehicles to the Highway Fund for road and bridge capital 
25 improvements?"
26 The legal voters of each city, town and plantation shall vote by ballot on this question 
27 and designate their choice by a cross or check mark placed within the corresponding square 
28 below the word "Yes" or "No."  The ballots must be received, sorted, counted and declared 
29 in open ward, town and plantation meetings and returns made to the Secretary of State in 
30 the same manner as votes for members of the Legislature.  The Governor shall review the 
31 returns.  If it appears that a majority of the legal votes are cast in favor of the amendment, 
32 the Governor shall proclaim that fact without delay and the amendment becomes part of 
33 the Constitution of Maine on the date of the proclamation.
34Secretary of State shall prepare ballots.  Resolved:  That the Secretary of State 
35 shall prepare and furnish to each city, town and plantation all ballots, returns and copies of 
36 this resolution necessary to carry out the purposes of this referendum.
37SUMMARY
38 This resolution proposes to amend the Constitution of Maine to dedicate 50% of sales 
39 and use tax revenue from sales of motor vehicles and sales related to motor vehicles to the 
40 Highway Fund for road and bridge capital improvements.38
