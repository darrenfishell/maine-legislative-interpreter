54.  Volunteer firefighter.  "Volunteer firefighter" means an active member of a 
6 volunteer fire association or a volunteer fire department created by a municipality who 
7 receives no compensation from the municipality  other than  up to 20% of the compensation 
8 of a municipal firefighter and may receive injury and death benefits.
9SUMMARY
10 The current definition of "volunteer firefighter" provides that the firefighter receives 
11 no compensation and must belong to a volunteer fire association.  This bill changes that 
12 definition to provide that the firefighter receives up to 20% of the compensation of a 
13 municipal firefighter and may belong to a volunteer fire department created by a 
14 municipality.10
