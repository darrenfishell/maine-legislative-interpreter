39 utilities under this section. The department may use the fund to provide energy rate relief 
40 to eligible customers of a consumer-owned transmission and distribution utility. Any

15SUMMARY
16 This resolve ensures that an eligible customer of an electric utility receives a payment 
17 from the Energy Rate Relief Fund under Resolve 2021, chapter 168 even if that customer 
18 did not receive a February 2022 invoice from that electric utility.16
