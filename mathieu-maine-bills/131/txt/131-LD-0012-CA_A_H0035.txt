SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, provides that the 
definition of "closing" does not include a business consolidation. The amendment also 
keeps the current exclusion from eligibility for severance pay of employees who have not 
been employed by the employer for at least 3 years.

Fiscal Note 
Potential cost to local units of government 
Expanding the definition of "covered establishment11 in the law regarding employee severence pay to include all 
employment sectors, not just industrial and commercial sectors, may result in increased severance pay costs to cert 
local units of government if they would now be required to make severance payments to employees at county and 
municipal-owned facilities that they decide to close or relocate.
