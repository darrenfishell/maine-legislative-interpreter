2 This bill eliminates the imposition of taxes on personal property, except for excise taxes 
3 and sales and use taxes.  Due to the elimination of personal property taxation, this bill also 
4 eliminates the business equipment tax exemption and the Business Equipment Tax 
5 Reimbursement program.  The legislation applies to property tax years beginning on or 
6 after April 1, 2024.2
