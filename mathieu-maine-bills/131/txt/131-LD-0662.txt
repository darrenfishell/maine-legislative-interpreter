41. Definitions. As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
5 following terms have the following meanings.
6 A. "Flavored tobacco product" means any tobacco product that imparts a taste or smell, 
7 other than the taste or smell of tobacco, that is distinguishable by an ordinary consumer 
8 either prior to or during the consumption of a tobacco product, including, but not 
9 limited to, any taste or smell relating to fruit, menthol, mint, wintergreen, chocolate, 
10 cocoa, vanilla, honey or any candy, dessert, beverage, herb or spice.
11 B. "Tobacco product" has the same meaning as in Title 22, section 1551, subsection 3.
12 C. "Tobacco retailer" has the same meaning as in Title 22, section 1551, subsection 
13 3-A.
142. Limitation.  A municipality may not adopt or enforce an ordinance that prohibits a 
15 tobacco retailer licensed in accordance with Title 22, chapter 262-A, subchapter 1 from 
16 selling a flavored tobacco product.
17SUMMARY
18 This bill prohibits a municipality from adopting or enforcing an ordinance prohibiting 
19 a tobacco retailer from selling flavored tobacco products.18
