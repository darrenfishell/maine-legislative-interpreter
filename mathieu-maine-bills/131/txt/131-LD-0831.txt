14Sec. 2.  Rules.  The Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife shall adopt rules

16 the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 12, section 11552, such that if a district had a less than 
17 50% success rate for moose hunts over 2 consecutive seasons, the district must begin its 
18 open hunting season in the first week of the moose hunting season. Wildlife management 
19 districts 7 and 8 must each have 2 independent moose hunting seasons, one in September 
20 and one in October. Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules 
21 pursuant to Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
22SUMMARY
23 This bill requires the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife, when establishing 
24 rules that manage moose hunting seasons, to take into consideration the moose hunting 
25 success rate of each wildlife management district when establishing the beginning date for 
26 moose hunting in that district. The bill also directs the commissioner to establish 2 
27 independent moose hunting seasons in wildlife management districts 7 and 8.23
