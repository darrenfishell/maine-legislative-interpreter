11SUMMARY
12 This resolve directs the Maine State Housing Authority to study strategies for 
13 improving abandoned properties and recommend strategies for municipal governments and 
14 regional organizations to create incentives for land improvements, including the use of state 
15 infrastructure banks or other means to guarantee loans, and a structure for repayment of 
16 those loans, for housing infrastructure projects serving a larger area built by private 
17 developers or others. The resolve requires the Maine State Housing Authority to submit a 
18 report to the Joint Standing Committee on Innovation, Development, Economic 
19 Advancement and Business by December 1, 2024 regarding the study.12
