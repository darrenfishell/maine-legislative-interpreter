41.  Fund established.  The Maine Rural Health Care Education Workforce Fund, 
5 referred to in this chapter as "the fund," is established to support rural primary care and 
6 health professions workforce development as a dedicated, nonlapsing account within the 
7 department.
82.  Sources of fund.  The fund consists of amounts appropriated or allocated by the 
9 State.
103.  Use of fund.  The department shall make distributions from the fund to support the 
11 following purposes:
12 A. Expanding rural longitudinal primary care clinical rotations for 3rd-year medical 
13 students to support long-term professional practice in the rural part of the State;
14 B. Expanding rural primary care clinical rotations for 4th-year medical students to 
15 support long-term professional practice in the rural part of the State;
16 C. Expanding rural primary care clinical residency placements in the State; and
17 D. Supporting the long-term sustainability of rural clinical preceptorship programs to 
18 provide support for students in undergraduate and graduate nursing programs.
194. Rules.  The department may adopt rules to implement this section.  Rules adopted 
20 pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, 
21 subchapter 2-A.
