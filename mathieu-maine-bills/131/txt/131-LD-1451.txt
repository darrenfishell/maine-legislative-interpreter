252. Prohibition. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, a government 
26 entity may not take any action to enforce or assist in the enforcement of federal laws that 
27 restrict a person's right to own, possess and purchase firearms.  This subsection may not be 
28 interpreted to prohibit a government entity from enforcing a state law restricting a person's 
29 right to own, possess and purchase firearms.

10SUMMARY
11 This bill prohibits all state and local government entities, the employees of these 
12 entities and all other state and local government officials from:
13 1.  Enforcing or assisting federal agencies in the enforcement of federal laws that 
14 restrict a person's right to own, possess and purchase a firearm or firearm ammunition or 
15 obtain a related firearms certification without prohibiting these state and local government 
16 officials from enforcing state laws that do the same;
17 2.  Restricting, revoking, suspending or otherwise infringing on a person's right to own, 
18 purchase or possess a firearm or firearm ammunition or to obtain any related firearms 
19 certification based on the person's purchase, possession or use of cannabis; and
20 3.  Providing information to a federal agency or official of a federal agency relating to 
21 a person's ownership, possession or purchase of a firearm or firearm ammunition or the 
22 person's obtaining of any related firearms certification based solely on the person's 
23 purchase, possession or use of cannabis.11
