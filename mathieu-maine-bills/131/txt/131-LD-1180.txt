23SUMMARY
24 Current law provides that a licensed driver under 18 years of age may not carry 
25 passengers other than immediate family members unless accompanied by a licensed 
26 operator who meets certain conditions. This bill exempts such a driver from this restriction 
27 if the driver is employed and can demonstrate employment and is in receipt of a safe driver 
28 discount under the person’s motor vehicle liability insurance policy.24
