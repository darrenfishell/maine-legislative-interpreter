2Sec. 1.  22 MRSA §1599-A, sub-§3 is enacted to read:
33.  Information related to health insurance coverage for 2nd opinion.  A health 
4 care professional, as defined in section 1596, subsection 1, paragraph C, shall provide to a 
5 pregnant person information related to the right to a 2nd opinion when the health care 
6 professional has recommended an abortion due to the health or safety of the pregnant

12 representative to receive the notice.
13Sec. 2.  24-A MRSA §4320-M, sub-§1-A is enacted to read:

16 after the enrollee's provider has recommended an abortion due to the health or safety of the

202.  Limits; deductible; copayment; coinsurance.  A health plan that provides 
21 coverage for the services required by this section may contain provisions for maximum 
22 benefits and coinsurance and reasonable limitations , deductibles and exclusions to the 
23 extent that these provisions are not inconsistent with the requirements of this section. A 
24 health plan may not impose any deductible, copayment, coinsurance or other cost-sharing 
25 requirement for the costs of a 2nd opinion in accordance with subsection 1-A.
26SUMMARY
27 This bill requires a health insurance carrier to provide coverage for the costs of a 2nd 
28 opinion and any additional testing by any provider of the enrollee's choice after the 
29 enrollee's provider has recommended an abortion due to the health or safety of the enrollee 
30 or fetus. The bill also requires health care professionals to inform a patient in writing of the 
31 right to a 2nd opinion and the availability of health insurance coverage for the costs of that 
32 opinion.27
