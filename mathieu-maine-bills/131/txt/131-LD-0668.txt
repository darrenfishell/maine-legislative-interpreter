26SUMMARY
27 This bill requires a person to be a citizen of the United States in order to receive general 
28 assistance benefits. This bill provides that a municipality is ineligible to receive general 
29 purpose aid for local schools, municipal general assistance and state-municipal revenue 
30 sharing if that municipality prohibits or restricts, formally or informally, the exchange of 
31 information with federal immigration authorities or any other federal, state or local 
32 government entity regarding the immigration status, lawful or unlawful, of any individual 
33 or the maintenance of such information.27
