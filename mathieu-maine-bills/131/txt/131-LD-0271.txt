6ยง12993.  Hunting, fishing, recreational shooting and trapping course required
7 The University of Maine System and the Maine Community College System shall offer 
8 a for-credit course about hunting, fishing, recreational shooting and trapping. The course 
9 curriculum must include educational instruction on the historical and contemporary roles 
10 of a North American model of wildlife conservation and a national system of conservation 
11 funding and the effects that hunting, fishing, recreational shooting and trapping have on 
12 fish and wildlife management. The course curriculum must include a field component 
13 appropriate for the season in which students are able to gain hands-on experience with 
14 mentors on hunting, fishing, recreational shooting or trapping. The course curriculum must 
15 satisfy the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife's approved firearms hunter safety 
16 course. The University of Maine System and the Maine Community College System shall 
17 work with the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to establish the necessary course 
18 components.
19ยง12994.  Rulemaking
20 The Chancellor of the University of Maine System and the Board of Trustees of the 
21 Maine Community College System shall adopt rules necessary to carry out the purposes of 
22 this chapter.
23SUMMARY
24 This bill requires the University of Maine System and the Maine Community College 
25 System to offer a for-credit course about hunting, fishing, recreational shooting and 
26 trapping.24
