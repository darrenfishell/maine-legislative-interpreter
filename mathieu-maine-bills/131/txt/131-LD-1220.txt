41.  Lyme disease defined.  For the purposes of this section, unless the context 
5 otherwise indicates, "Lyme disease" has the same meaning as in Title 32, section 3282-B, 
6 subsection 1, paragraph B.
72.  Immunization; Lyme disease vaccines.  A carrier shall provide coverage for Lyme 
8 disease vaccines as follows.  
9 A.  A carrier shall provide coverage for any Lyme disease vaccine licensed or 
10 authorized by the United States Food and Drug Administration that is recommended 
11 by a health care provider for administration to an enrollee.
12 B.  A carrier may not impose any deductible, copayment, coinsurance or other cost-
13 sharing requirement for the cost of Lyme disease vaccines, including all associated 
14 costs of administration.
15 C.  A carrier may not make coverage without cost sharing as required by paragraph B 
16 dependent on any prior authorization requirement.  
17 D.  A carrier may not make coverage without cost sharing as required by paragraph B 
18 dependent on the use of a provider in a carrier's network unless an enrollee is offered 
19 immunization by a network provider without additional delay and the enrollee chooses 
20 instead to obtain immunization from an out-of-network provider.
21Sec. 2.  Application. This Act applies to all policies, contracts and certificates 
22 executed, delivered, issued for delivery, continued or renewed in this State on or after 
23 January 1, 2024.  For purposes of this Act, all contracts are deemed to be renewed no later 
24 than the next yearly anniversary of the contract date.
25SUMMARY
26 This bill requires carriers offering health plans in this State to provide coverage for 
27 vaccines for Lyme disease authorized by the federal Food and Drug Administration.  The 
28 requirements of the bill apply to health plans issued or renewed on or after January 1, 2024.26
