26SUMMARY
27 This bill eliminates the requirement that $3,000,000 of the sales and use tax and income 
28 tax revenues that are credited to the General Fund be deposited in the Maine Clean Election 
29 Fund.  Instead, the bill provides that the $3,000,000 be used to eliminate the waiting list for 
30 services for adults with intellectual disabilities and autism spectrum disorder.12
