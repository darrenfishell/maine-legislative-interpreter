2Sec. 1.  20-A MRSA §6552, sub-§2, ¶D is enacted to read:
3 D.  The prohibition on discharge of a firearm does not apply to a person residing on 
4 private property who discharges a firearm on the private property in self-defense or in 
5 order to protect the person's residence.
6SUMMARY
7 Current law prohibits a person from discharging a firearm within 500 feet of public 
8 school property or the property of an approved private school.  This bill establishes an 
9 exception for the discharge of a firearm by a person residing on private property for the 
10 purposes of self-defense or in order to protect the person's residence.7
