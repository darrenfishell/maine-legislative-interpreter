32SUMMARY
33 This bill allows consumption of adult use cannabis and adult use cannabis products in 
34 a designated separate area of a cannabis store if the municipality in which the cannabis 
35 store is located approves on-premises consumption of adult use cannabis and adult use 
36 cannabis products, the cannabis store obtains approval from the Department of 
37 Administrative and Financial Services and the products sold or offered for sale for on-
38 premises consumption meet all the requirements for packing, labeling and health and safety 
39 requirements for the adult use cannabis and adult use cannabis products and the restrictions 
40 and requirements on employees, customers and security for the on-premises area meet 
41 those of the cannabis stores.33
