21SUMMARY
22 This bill requires that rules adopted to implement school food service programs allow 
23 the greatest flexibility allowed under federal law with respect to meal times and packaging 
24 of meals to send home with students.  The bill also requires the Department of Education 
25 to create a program for the distribution of school cafeteria surplus or leftover food within 
26 the community.22
