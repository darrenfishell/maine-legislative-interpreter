51.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
6 following terms have the following meanings.
7 A.  "Associated facilities" has the same meaning as in section 3451, subsection 1.
8 B.  "Offshore wind energy demonstration project" has the same meaning as in Title 38, 
9 section 480‑HH, subsection 1, paragraph H.
10 C.  "Offshore wind power project" means an offshore project that uses a windmill or 
11 wind turbine to convert wind energy to electrical energy.  "Offshore wind power 
12 project" includes both generating facilities as defined by section 3451, subsection 5 
13 and associated facilities, without regard to whether the electrical energy is for sale or 
14 use by a person other than the generator.
15 D.  "Pilot-scaled, limited duration offshore wind power research and development 
16 project" means an offshore project that uses a wind turbine to convert wind energy to 
17 electrical energy, has a generating capacity of no more than 0.5 megawatts and is 
18 operational for no more than 5 years.
19 E.  "Submerged lands" has the same meaning as in Title 12, section 1801, subsection 
20 9.
21 F.  "Territorial waters" has the same meaning as in Title 12, section 6001, subsection 
22 48‑B.
232.  Prohibition.  Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary and except as 
24 otherwise provided by subsection 3, a state agency or municipality or other political 
25 subdivision of the State may not license, permit or otherwise approve or authorize the 
26 siting, construction or operation of or issue a lease or grant an easement or other real 
27 property interest for a windmill or wind turbine or tower for an offshore wind power project 
28 in state-owned submerged lands or , territorial waters  or federal waters as defined in Title 
29 38, section 570-AA, subsection 3.
303.  Exemption.  The prohibition established under subsection 2 does not apply to:
31 A.  A pilot-scaled, limited-duration offshore wind power research and development 
32 project;
33 B.  An offshore wind energy demonstration project and its associated facilities 
34 proposed for location in the Maine Offshore Wind Energy Research Center designated 
35 by the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry pursuant to Title 12, 
36 section 1868 and for which, prior to the effective date of this section, the commission 
37 has approved the terms of a long-term power purchase agreement.  Subsequent 
38 amendment of the terms of such an agreement does not affect the applicability of this 
39 exemption;
40 C.  The licensing, permitting or approval by a state agency or municipality or other 
41 political subdivision of the State of the siting, construction or operation of or the 
42 issuance of a lease or the grant of an easement or other real property interest for portside

44 governed by paragraph D that are intended to support generation of electricity from 
45 offshore wind energy facilities located seaward of the territorial waters; and
4 D.  The licensing, permitting or approval by a state agency or municipality or other

10 ( 1)  The Governor's Energy Office has completed a strategic plan to inform the

15 ( 2)  The Governor's Energy Office, in consultation with other state agencies, has 
16 conducted a review of applicable state laws and rules to determine whether the 
17 existing offshore wind energy statutory and regulatory framework protects the 
18 State's coastal resources in a manner that avoids or minimizes adverse effects on 
19 coastal resources and users from the development of offshore wind power projects 
20 located seaward of the territorial waters; and
21 ( 3)  The Governor's Energy Office, with input from the advisory board of the 
22 Offshore Wind Research Consortium established in section 3406, has identified 
23 the preliminary research questions the consortium seeks to answer regarding the 
24 development of offshore wind power projects.

2 This bill prohibits a state agency or municipality or other political subdivision of the 
3 State from licensing, permitting or otherwise approving or authorizing the siting, 
4 construction or operation of, or issuing a lease or granting an easement or other real 
5 property interest for, a windmill or wind turbine or tower for an offshore wind power 
6 project in state-owned submerged lands, territorial waters or federal waters. It directs the 
7 Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, the Public Utilities Commission 
8 and the Department of Environmental Protection to submit legislation to align the 
9 provisions of law under the agencies' respective jurisdictions with the prohibition on 
10 offshore wind power projects.2
