11Sec. 1. Working group. Resolved: That the Commissioner of Corrections and the 
12 Commissioner of Health and Human Services shall establish a working group of 
13 stakeholders and interested parties to study and develop a plan to relocate the Department 
14 of Corrections' division of juvenile services from the Department of Corrections to the most 
15 appropriate organizational structure in State Government.  The working group must be 
16 convened by July 1, 2023 and shall provide a report to the Joint Standing Committee on 
17 Criminal Justice and Public Safety, the Joint Standing Committee on Education and 
18 Cultural Affairs and the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human Services by 
19 January 1, 2024.  The commissioners shall designate membership on the working group.
20Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
21 takes effect when approved.
