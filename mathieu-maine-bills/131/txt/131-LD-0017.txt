Page 1 - 131LR0109(01)1Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2Sec. 1.  23 MRSA §152, first ¶, as amended by PL 1999, c. 185, §2, is further 
3 amended to read:
4 The State Claims Commission, established by Title 5, section 12004‑B, subsection 5, 
5 consists of 5 members.  Four of the members must be appointed by the Governor, 2 of 
6 whom must be qualified appraisers certified as general real estate appraisers pursuant to 
7 Title 32, chapter 124 within the 5 years prior to the date of appointment and 2 of whom 
8 must be attorneys-at-law.  The Governor shall designate one of the attorneys-at-law to be 
9 chair.  The members of the commission appointed by the Governor shall serve for terms of 
10 4 years.  They must be sworn, and for inefficiency, willful neglect of duty or for 
11 malfeasance in office may, after notice and hearing, be removed by the Governor on the 
12 address of both branches of the Legislature or by impeachment.  In case of a vacancy 
13 occurring through death, resignation or removal, the Governor shall appoint a successor for 
14 the whole term of the member whose place that successor takes, subject to removal as 
15 provided in this section.
16SUMMARY
17 This bill allows a real estate appraiser whose license has lapsed to be appointed to the 
18 State Claims Commission as long as the real estate appraiser was licensed as a certified 
19 real estate appraiser within the 5 years prior to the date of appointment. 17
