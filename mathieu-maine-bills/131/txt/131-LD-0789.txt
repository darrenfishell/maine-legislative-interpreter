2Sec. 1.  32 MRSA §18302, sub-§2, as repealed by PL 2017, c. 388, §1, is reenacted 
3 to read:
42.  Charitable dentist license.  "Charitable dentist license" means the authority granted 
5 to an individual to provide free dental care as requested by a charitable or social 
6 organization within the State when resident dental services are not available.

19Sec. 3.  32 MRSA §18342, sub-§5, as repealed by PL 2017, c. 388, §8, is reenacted 
20 to read:
215.  Charitable dentist license.  An applicant for a charitable dentist license must 
22 comply with section 18341 and must provide:
23 A.  Verification of a doctoral degree in dentistry from a dental school accredited as 
24 required by board rule;
25 B.  Verification that the applicant has been licensed as a dentist in good standing under 
26 the laws of this State or has an active dental license in good standing issued under the 
27 laws of another state or a Canadian province;
28 C.  Verification of passing all examinations required by board rule; and
29 D.  Verification that the purpose of the license is to offer free dental care in conjunction 
30 with a charitable or social organization.
31SUMMARY
32 This bill reestablishes in law the provision authorizing a license at no cost for a dentist 
33 licensed in another state or a Canadian province for the purpose of providing charitable 
34 care.32
