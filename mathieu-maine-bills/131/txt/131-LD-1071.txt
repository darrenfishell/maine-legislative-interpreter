31SUMMARY
32 This bill provides that a political party, qualified to participate in a primary election, 
33 may nominate a candidate who has been nominated for the same office by a different 
34 political party. Political parties must notify the Secretary of State no later than February 1st 
35 of an election year of that party's decision to allow a person not enrolled in that party to be 
36 that party's nominee for an office.32
