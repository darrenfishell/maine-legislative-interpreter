7SUMMARY
8 This resolve directs the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to examine the 
9 northern pike population in Sabattus Pond in the towns of Sabattus, Greene and Wales and 
10 to submit a report, which may include a recommended fisheries management plan for 
11 Sabattus Pond, to the Joint Standing Committee on Inland Fisheries and Wildlife no later 
12 than December 6, 2023.8
