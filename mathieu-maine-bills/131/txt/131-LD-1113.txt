97.  Return of unused funds.  A school administrative unit shall account for and return

12SUMMARY
13 This bill requires a school administrative unit to produce and make available to the 
14 public an annual accounting of the use of school construction bond proceeds.  It also 
15 requires a school administrative unit to account for and return to each municipality 
16 responsible for the debt the amount of any bond proceeds not used to complete the school 
17 construction project.13
