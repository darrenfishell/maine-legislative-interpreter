33SUMMARY
34 This bill amends the definition of "incoming voting list" in the laws governing elections 
35 to remove the requirement that the list be a printed list.  The bill also provides that the 
36 deadline for receipt of voter registration applications transmitted by the Department of the 
37 Secretary of State, Bureau of Motor Vehicles or by a source agency other than the Bureau 
38 of Motor Vehicles under the automatic voter registration law is midnight on the 7th day 
39 before election day.  Except for the section of the bill that amends the law regarding reports 
40 of registration and enrollment that was amended by Public Law 2021, chapter 750, which 
41 has an effective date of January 1, 2024, the bill establishes an effective date of November 
42 1, 2023 for consistency with the effective date of Public Law 2021, chapter 439, which 
43 amended the law establishing deadlines for receipt of voter registration applications.34
