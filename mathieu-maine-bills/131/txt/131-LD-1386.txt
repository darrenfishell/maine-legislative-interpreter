17SUMMARY
18 This bill requires the settlement agent for the conveyance of residential real property 
19 to provide the buyer a completed application for the Maine resident homestead property 
20 tax exemption.  If the buyer signs the application, the settlement agent is required to submit 
21 it to the municipal assessor for the residential real property being conveyed.18
