32SUMMARY
33 Current law provides up to a $6,000 exemption in just value for veterans who are at 
34 least 62 years of age and who served during a federally recognized war period or veterans 
35 who were disabled during active military service.  This bill provides a complete exemption 
36 from property taxes for veterans who were disabled during active military service and who 
37 are receiving a pension or compensation from the United States Government for total, 
38 service-connected disability, except that the dollar amount of the reduction in taxes due 
39 because of the exemption is limited to $5,000 or the amount of taxes actually due for that 
40 property tax year, whichever amount is less.33
