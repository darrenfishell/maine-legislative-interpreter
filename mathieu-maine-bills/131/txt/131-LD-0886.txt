19SUMMARY
20 This bill expands the list of persons who may receive an absentee ballot after the 3rd 
21 business day before election day to include persons with any disability, rather than persons 
22 with a physical disability as current law provides, and to include persons providing 
23 volunteer or uncompensated care to a person with a disability.20
