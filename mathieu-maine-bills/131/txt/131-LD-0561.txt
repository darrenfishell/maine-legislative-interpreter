25SUMMARY
26 This bill clarifies that the person having power of attorney for a Maine resident who is 
27 a member of the United States Army, Navy, Air Force, Marine Corps, Space Force, Coast 
28 Guard, Army National Guard or Air National Guard who is mobilized or deployed outside 
29 the boundaries of the United States for more than 30 days is obligated to pay the debts and 
30 other financial obligations of the member in accordance with the terms of the debts or other 
31 financial obligations to the extent funds of the member are available to the person.  The bill 
32 requires the Adjutant General of the Department of Defense, Veterans and Emergency 
33 Management to implement a program to reimburse a member who suffers financial loss 
34 because the person having power of attorney for the member failed to fulfill this obligation. 
35 The Adjutant General is required to purchase and maintain surety and fidelity insurance to 
36 ensure that funds are available to reimburse members who suffer a loss under these 
37 circumstances.26
