7SUMMARY
8  This bill provides that, beginning in fiscal year 2024-25, transportation costs for public 
9 school students will no longer be paid as other subsidizable costs and that instead each 
10 school administrative unit must be guaranteed and the State is required to pay, in addition 
11 to the state share of the total allocation, 100% of the per-pupil transportation costs for each 
12 school administrative unit. The bill also provides that per-pupil transportation costs include 
13 transportation costs for cocurricular and extracurricular activities and requires municipal 
14 school units to provide transportation to secondary school students.1
