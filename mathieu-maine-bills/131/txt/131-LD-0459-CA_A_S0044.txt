27 SUMMARY 
28 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, requires that the 
29 electronic transmission of an application for and of a court order granting an application 
30 for emergency involuntary commitment be through secure means. It also authorizes a 
31 Justice of the Superior Court, Judge of the District Court, Judge of Probate or justice of the 
32 peace to endorse the application electronically.
