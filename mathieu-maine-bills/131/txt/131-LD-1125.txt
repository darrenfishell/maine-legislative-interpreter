Page 1 - 131LR1040(01)1Sec. 1.  Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of 
2Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations to study paying bonuses for 
3selling certain winning Powerball tickets. Resolved: That the Department of 
4 Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery 
5 Operations shall study paying bonuses to persons licensed to sell lottery tickets for selling 
6 winning Powerball tickets over $10,000 that are not grand prize winners and develop a 
7 system to pay bonuses to persons selling winning Powerball tickets with prizes over 
8 $10,000 that are not grand prize winners.
9Sec. 2.  Report. Resolved: That, no later than December 6, 2023, the Department 
10 of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery 
11 Operations shall submit a report based upon the study in section 1, along with any 
12 recommended legislation, to the Joint Standing Committee on Veterans and Legal Affairs, 
13 which may report out legislation related to the report to the Second Regular Session of the 
14 131st Legislature.
15SUMMARY
16 This resolve requires the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau 
17 of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations to study paying bonuses to persons licensed 
18 to sell lottery tickets for winning Powerball tickets that are not grand prize winners and 
19 develop a system to pay bonuses to persons selling winning Powerball tickets with prizes 
20 over $10,000 that are not grand prize winners.16
