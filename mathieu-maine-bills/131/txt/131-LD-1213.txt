35  SUMMARY
36 This bill reduces individual income taxes by reducing the rate of tax on the first income 
37 tax bracket from 5.8% to 2.9%, the rate of tax on the 2nd income tax bracket from 6.75% 
38 to 5% and the rate of tax on the 3rd income tax bracket from 7.15% to 7%. The bill 
39 maintains the current 7.15% rate of tax for single filers on income over $80,000, heads of 
40 household on income over $120,000 and married joint filers on income over $160,000. The 
41 rate changes apply to tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2023.1
