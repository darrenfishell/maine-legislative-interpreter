3 a seasonal commercial driver's license examiner pilot program by procuring 3 full-time 
4 equivalent employees to proctor written commercial driver’s license examinations from

10 under section 1.
11Sec. 3.  Report. Resolved: That, by December 3, 2025, the Secretary of State shall 
12 submit a report based upon the evaluation of the pilot program under section 2, along with 
13 any recommended legislation, to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 
14 jurisdiction over transportation matters. The joint standing committee may report out 
15 legislation based upon the report to the Second Regular Session of the 132nd Legislature.
16SUMMARY
17 This resolve requires the Secretary of State to conduct a pilot program to hire 3 full-
18 time equivalent examination proctors to work from May 1st to September 30th in the 
19 calendar years 2024 and 2025 to allow permanent commercial driver’s license examiner 
20 staff to conduct road evaluations. The Secretary of State is directed to submit a report and 
21 evaluation of the pilot program to the 132nd Legislature.17
