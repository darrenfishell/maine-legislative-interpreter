Page 2 - 131LR1911(01)1SUMMARY
2 This bill amends the 24-hour reporting requirements of committees in the election laws 
3 by:
4 1. Eliminating that requirement for political action committees in the State, state party 
5 committees and municipal, district and county party committees; and
6 2. Changing from $1,000 to $5,000 the amount of expenditures required to be reported 
7 by ballot question committees in the State.
8 The bill also increases to $25,000 the aggregate amount that a party committee, a ballot 
9 question committee, a leadership political action committee, a separate segregated fund 
10 committee, a caucus political action committee and any other political action committee 
11 can contribute to a candidate in support of the candidacy of one person.2
