14SUMMARY
15 This bill establishes an affirmative defense to prosecution for the crimes of criminal 
16 trespass and certain instances of aggravated criminal trespass when the person was at the 
17 time the person committed the act an unhoused person attempting to seek shelter in the 
18 dwelling place, structure, place, cemetery or burial ground the person entered or remained 
19 in. The bill defines "unhoused person" as a person who is without shelter or who lacks a 
20 fixed, regular and adequate nighttime residence.15
