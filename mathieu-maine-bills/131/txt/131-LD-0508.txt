Page 1 - 131LR2097(01)1Sec. 1.  Department of Environmental Protection to review environmental 
2permitting laws.  Resolved:  That the Department of Environmental Protection shall 
3 review its environmental permitting authority, including the laws governing site location 
4 of development under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 38, chapter 3, subchapter 1, article 
5 6 and the Natural Resources Protection Act, to consider whether its statutory authority 
6 requires any changes in order to better consider climate change, habitat connectivity, 
7 ecological resilience and the coastal marine environment.  No later than December 6, 2023, 
8 the department shall submit a report of its review, including any suggested legislation, to 
9 the Joint Standing Committee on Environment and Natural Resources.  The committee may 
10 report out legislation related to the report to the Second Regular Session of the 131st 
11 Legislature.
12SUMMARY
13 This resolve requires the Department of Environmental Protection to review its 
14 environmental permitting authority, including the laws governing site location of 
15 development and the Natural Resources Protection Act, to consider whether its statutory 
16 authority requires any changes in order to better consider climate change, habitat 
17 connectivity, ecological resilience and the coastal marine environment.  It requires the 
18 department to submit a report to the Joint Standing Committee on Environment and Natural 
19 Resources by December 6, 2023.  It authorizes the committee to report out legislation 
20 related to the report.13
