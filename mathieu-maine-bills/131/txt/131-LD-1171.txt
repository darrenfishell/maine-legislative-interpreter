7Sec. 2.  38 MRSA §2203-A, sub-§2-B is enacted to read:
312-B.  Support for municipal waste reduction, reuse, recycling and composting.  
32 Five dollars per ton of the fee imposed under subsection 1 for the disposal of municipal 
33 solid waste, municipal solid waste ash, construction and demolition debris and residue from 
34 the processing of construction and demolition debris must be distributed to municipalities 
35 to support the priorities under section 2101, subsection 1, paragraphs A, B, C and D.
36SUMMARY
37 This bill increases from $2 per ton to $7 per ton the fee imposed on the disposal of 
38 municipal solid waste, municipal solid waste ash, construction and demolition debris and 
39 residue from the processing of construction and demolition debris.  It also provides that $5 
40 per ton of that fee must be distributed to municipalities to support the solid waste 
41 management hierarchy priorities of reduction of waste generated at the source, reuse of 
42 waste, recycling of waste and composting of biodegradable waste.7
