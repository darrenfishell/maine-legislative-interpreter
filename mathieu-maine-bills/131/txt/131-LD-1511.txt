21§125.  Board of Agriculture
221.  Establishment; duties.  The Board of Agriculture, referred to in this section as "the 
23 "board," as established in Title 5, section 12004‑G, subsection 4‑A, is created within the 
24 University of Maine System.  The board shall advise the Chancellor of the University of 
25 Maine System and the President of the University of Maine at Orono on matters concerning 
26 the direction, operation and management of agricultural research conducted by the Maine 
27 Agricultural and Forest Experiment Station and university farm-based programs, including 
28 those of the University of Maine Cooperative Extension Service.  The board's duties are 
29 limited to advising the chancellor and the president on research and programs relating to 
30 agriculture and aquaculture.  The board does not advise the Director of the Maine 
31 Agricultural and Forest Experiment Station or have a role in the operation of research and 
32 programs within the Maine Agricultural and Forest Experiment Station that relate to 
33 forestry, wildlife, or fisheries  and aquaculture.  The board shall assist the chancellor and 
34 the president in articulating the mission of the Maine Agricultural and Forest Experiment 
35 Station and the University of Maine Cooperative Extension Service as it pertains to 
36 agriculture and the State's rural economy.  The director, with the agreement of advice from 
37 the board, shall develop a budget for the station.  The board and the director shall seek 
38 agreement on all issues. In the event that agreement can not be reached, final authority rests 
39 with the director.  The board may not interfere with funding and grants for commodity 
40 research programs brought to the University of Maine System directly or through the efforts 
41 of commodity groups.  The board shall respect the expertise of the various commodity 
42 groups and shall maintain the integrity of the research being recommended and reviewed 
43 by specific commodity groups.  The board shall assist in the coordination of activities with 
44 commodity groups interested in or supporting agricultural research.  The board shall invite

29SUMMARY
30 This bill renames the Maine Agricultural Experiment Station the Maine Agricultural 
31 and Forest Experiment Station and requires the Director of the Maine Agricultural and 
32 Forest Experiment Station to perform the director's duties with advice from the University 
33 of Maine System's Board of Agriculture.  The bill also adds aquaculture and the State's 
34 rural economy to the board's advisory duties to the Chancellor of the University of Maine 
35 System and the President of the University of Maine at Orono.  It amends the membership 
36 of the board and requires the board to invite appropriate individuals and conduct a public, 
37 facilitated discussion at least annually to better understand the needs and trends in 
38 agriculture in the State that would benefit from new or additional research.  It also requires 
39 the board to include in its annual report to the Legislature an outline of emerging trends in 
40 agriculture, to submit annual proposals for additional funding for program needs to the 
41 Board of Trustees of the University of Maine System and to update its long-range plan for 
42 operation of the Maine Agricultural and Forest Experiment Station every 5 years.  30
