24SUMMARY
25 This bill removes any statute of limitations in a civil or criminal matter for an offense 
26 classified as a sexual assault or as sexual exploitation of a minor.  For a criminal offense, 
27 the provisions of this legislation apply to an offense committed on or after the effective 
28 date of the provisions of this bill if the victim had not attained 18 years of age at the time 
29 of the offense.25
