3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill would create a fund to reimburse school administrative units that pay tuition 
6 to state-approved schools after the October student counts are taken by the Department of 
7 Education. The bill would require that school administrative units that enroll tuition 
8 students in state-approved schools receive subsidy allocations that reflect actual tuition 
9 rates paid to the state-approved schools. The bill would require adjustments to state 
10 subsidies to be made at the end of the school year to fund students that enroll in a state-
11 approved school after the October enrollment deadline.4
