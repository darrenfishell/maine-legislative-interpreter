19SUMMARY
20 This bill defines the term "cannabis paraphernalia" for purposes of the Maine Medical 
21 Use of Cannabis Act.  It clarifies that cannabis paraphernalia is not a tobacco product under 
22 the laws governing the retail sale of tobacco.  It permits a caregiver to sell or provide 
23 cannabis paraphernalia to a qualifying patient for the patient's medical use of cannabis.20
