21SUMMARY
22 This bill requires the Chief Justice of the Supreme Judicial Court or the Chief Justice's 
23 designee to reimburse counties for the cost of maintenance, supplies, cleaning, heat and 
24 electricity at a rate per square foot to be determined by each county and the Chief Justice 
25 or the Chief Justice's designee annually for certain county buildings used by the courts.22
