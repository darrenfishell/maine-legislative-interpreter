6§13408.  Minimum salaries beginning in 2024-2025 school year
7 Each school administrative unit shall establish a minimum salary for certified teachers 
8 and career and technical education teachers as follows: 
91.  School year 2024-2025.  For the school year starting after June 30, 2024, the 
10 minimum salary is $42,500;
112.  School year 2025-2026.  For the school year starting after June 30, 2025, the 
12 minimum salary is $45,000;
133. School year 2026-2027. For the school year starting after June 30, 2026, the 
14 minimum salary is $47,500;
154. School year 2027-2028. For the school year starting after June 30, 2027, the 
16 minimum salary is $50,000; and
175.  School years beginning in or after 2028.  For the school year starting after June 
18 30, 2028, the minimum salary for certified teachers and career and technical education 
19 teachers must increase by multiplying the amount of the salary by the cost-of-living 
20 adjustment.  For each subsequent year, the previous year's adjusted minimum salary for 
21 certified teachers and career and technical education teachers must increase by multiplying 
22 the amount of the adjusted minimum salary by the cost-of-living adjustment.  For purposes 
23 of this subsection, "cost-of-living adjustment" means the Chained Consumer Price Index 
24 for the 12-month period ending June 30th of the preceding calendar year divided by the 
25 Chained Consumer Price Index for the 12-month period ending June 30th of the calendar 
26 year in which the adjustment is effective and "Chained Consumer Price Index" has the same 
27 meaning as in Title 36, section 5402, subsection 1.
28 A school administrative unit shall provide to the department annually on or before 
29 October 1st the number of certified teachers and career and technical education teachers 
30 eligible for incremental salary increases as defined in section 15689, subsection 7‑A, 
31 paragraph A.
