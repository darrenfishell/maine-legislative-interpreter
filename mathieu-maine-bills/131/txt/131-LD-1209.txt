7SUMMARY
8 Public Law 2019, chapter 154 removed the exemption from immunization 
9 requirements based on religious or philosophical beliefs for students in elementary, 
10 secondary and postsecondary schools and employees of nursery schools and certain health 
11 care facilities.  This bill reinstates the religious and philosophical exemptions for students 
12 enrolled in private schools or virtual public charter schools.8
