SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, requires the 
stakeholder group convened by the Department of Education, Maine School Safety Center 
to develop best practices for and evaluate active shooter drills in addition to lockdown 
drills. 
The amendment provides that the Department of Education's rules must be amended to 
allow parents to opt out of active shooter drills, rather than lockdown drills, for their child. 
The amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section.
