15SUMMARY
16 This bill requires the Department of Education to develop an application process, 
17 procedures and criteria for implementing the requirements for designation of community 
18 schools pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20-A, chapter 333.  It also provides 
19 additional one-time funding of $2,000,000 in fiscal years 2023-24 and 2024-25 to expand 
20 the community school program.15
