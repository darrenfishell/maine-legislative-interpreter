Page 1 - 131LR0087(01)1Sec. 1.  Sokokis Trail bridge in Cornish renamed.  Resolved:  That the 
2 Department of Transportation shall designate Bridge 3859 on Sokokis Trail in the Town of 
3 Cornish, which crosses the Little River and is currently known as the Route 5/Little River 
4 Bridge, the Harriet Tubman Memorial Bridge.
5Sec. 2.  Main Street bridge in Cornish renamed.  Resolved:  That the 
6 Department of Transportation shall designate Bridge 3024 on Main Street in the Town of 
7 Cornish, which crosses Douglas Brook and is currently known as the Tannery Bridge, the 
8 9/11 Memorial Bridge.
9Sec. 3.  Depot Road bridge in Cornish renamed.  Resolved:  That the 
10 Department of Transportation shall designate Bridge 3025 on Depot Road in the Town of 
11 Cornish, which crosses the Saco River and is currently known as the Depot Bridge, the 
12 Veterans Memorial Bridge.
13SUMMARY
14 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to designate Bridge 3859 over 
15 the Little River in the Town of Cornish the Harriet Tubman Memorial Bridge, Bridge 3024 
16 over Douglas Brook in the Town of Cornish the 9/11 Memorial Bridge and Bridge 3025 
17 over the Saco River in the Town of Cornish the Veterans Memorial Bridge.14
