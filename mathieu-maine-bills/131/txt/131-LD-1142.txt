16SUMMARY
17 This bill exempts a volunteer firefighter and a volunteer emergency medical services 
18 provider from paying registration fees for a vehicle that is the primary means of 
19 transportation. The bill directs the Secretary of State to define "volunteer firefighter" and 
20 "volunteer emergency medical services provider." The bill provides for the Secretary of 
21 State to adopt rules related to this exemption.
22 Current law provides an exemption from motor vehicle registration fees for a person 
23 applying for a registration certificate and a set of gold star family plates.  This bill moves 
24 that provision to a new statutory section for exemptions from registration fees.17
