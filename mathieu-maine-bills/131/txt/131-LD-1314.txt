2 This bill establishes, starting with the school years after June 30, 2024, a minimum 
3 hourly wage for educational specialists, educational technicians and other school teaching 
4 professionals equal to 200% of the state minimum hourly wage.  As with the adjustment 
5 for minimum teacher salaries, this bill requires the Commissioner of Education, based on 
6 a list provided annually by each school administrative unit, to allocate funds provided by 
7 the Legislature to each school administrative unit necessary to fund the incremental salary 
8 increases.2
