8SUMMARY
9 This resolve directs the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Bureau 
10 of Parks and Lands to conduct a study to examine the issue of persons who go missing in 
11 state parks. The bureau is required to submit a report based on its evaluation by December 
12 6, 2023 for presentation to the Joint Standing Committee on Agriculture, Conservation and 
13 Forestry.9
