3ยง410-B.  Public Art Fund
4 There is established the Public Art Fund, an interest-bearing nonlapsing General Fund 
5 account, referred to in this section as "the fund." The fund is administered by the Maine 
6 Arts Commission. Money deposited in the fund and the earnings on that money remain in 
7 the fund to be used to provide a grant to a public entity, including a school, library or 
8 government office, that applies for a grant for the installation or performance of art that is 
9 publicly accessible.  For the purposes of awarding grants, "public art" must be construed 
10 broadly, and may include a mural, sculpture, painting, audio or visual presentation, digital 
11 presentation, musical or theatrical performance, demonstration of or instruction in an 
12 activity or performing a task or lecture.  A recipient of a grant under this section shall 
13 endeavor to contract or commission a provider, maker or performer of art that resides or is 
14 located in the State to the extent possible.
