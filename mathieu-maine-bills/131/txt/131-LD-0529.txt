18SUMMARY
19 This bill amends the laws governing property tax stabilization for senior citizens to 
20 eliminate the requirement for an annual application.  It provides that a new application is 
21 required when an individual establishes a new homestead in the State.  It requires an 
22 individual or the personal representative of the individual's estate to notify the municipality 
23 when there is a change in ownership of the individual's homestead.19
