15 SUMMARY 
16 The bill authorizes a conservator to sign a transfer on death deed for the primary 
17 dwelling of the individual subject to the conservatorship, with court approval. This 
18 amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, also authorizes a conservator to 
19 revoke a transfer on death deed for the primary dwelling of the individual subject to the 
20 conservatorship, with court approval.
