2for ultrasound and fetal nonstress test procedures through telehealth under 
3MaineCare program. Resolved: That the Department of Health and Human Services 
4 shall amend its rules regarding reimbursement under the MaineCare program to allow 
5 reimbursement for ultrasound procedures and fetal nonstress tests performed remotely in a 
6 residence or other off-site location through telehealth under the MaineCare program. For 
7 purposes of this resolve, "telehealth" has the same meaning as in the Maine Revised 
8 Statutes, Title 22, section 3173-H, subsection 1, paragraph D.  
9SUMMARY
10 This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rules 
11 regarding reimbursement under the MaineCare program to allow reimbursement for 
12 ultrasound procedures and fetal nonstress tests performed remotely in a residence or other 
13 off-site location through telehealth under the MaineCare program.10
