Page 1 - 131LR2070(01)1Sec. 1. Department of Health and Human Services to amend rules 
2regarding substance use disorder.  Resolved: That the Department of Health and 
3 Human Services shall establish within the MaineCare program a health home model, which 
4 is a model of care that involves care coordination, case management, individual and family 
5 support and health promotion and education, that includes:
6 1. Services for substance use disorder and co-occurring mental health diagnoses in one 
7 model; and
8 2. A new payment model that allows a MaineCare member to have access to services 
9 as needed beyond the services provided by the health home team.
10 The department shall combine its rules in Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, 
11 Chapter II, Section 92, Behavioral Health Home Services, and Section 93, Opioid Health 
12 Home Services, in order to establish the new health home model. 
13SUMMARY
14 This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to establish a new 
15 health home model that includes services for substance use disorder and co-occurring 
16 mental health diagnoses in one model and a new payment model.14
