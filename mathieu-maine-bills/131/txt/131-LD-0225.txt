19SUMMARY
20 Current law requires the Department of Health and Human Services to provide 
21 reimbursement to hospitals other than critical access hospitals for each day after the 10th 
22 day that a MaineCare-eligible individual is in the care of a hospital while awaiting 
23 placement in a nursing facility.  This bill provides that the department also is required to 
24 provide reimbursement to hospitals other than critical access hospitals for each day after 
25 the 10th day that a MaineCare-eligible individual is in the care of a hospital while awaiting 
26 placement in a residential care facility.  The bill removes the provision that repeals the 
27 current law on December 31, 2023.20
