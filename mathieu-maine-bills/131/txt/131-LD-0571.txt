4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill would amend the statutes, and require the amendment of relevant rules, that 
6 inhibit or prevent individuals who are 16 or 17 years of age from entering the workforce to 
7 make it less burdensome on those individuals to find employment and for businesses to 
8 hire those individuals.4
