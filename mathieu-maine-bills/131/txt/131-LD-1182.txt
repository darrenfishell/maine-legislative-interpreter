18SUMMARY
19 This bill amends the laws governing tax increment financing by removing the 
20 requirement that the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry and the 
21 Department of Transportation review proposals for the designation of downtown tax 
22 increment financing districts. The bill also clarifies the approval condition related to a 
23 property being suitable for commercial or arts district uses.19
