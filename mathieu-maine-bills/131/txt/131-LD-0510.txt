23SUMMARY
24 This bill permits a municipality to hire associate health officers, as defined, to assist 
25 the local health officer during an actual or threatened epidemic or other public health threat.  
26 An associate health officer must have appropriate education, training or experience, is not 
27 required to meet the qualifications of a local health officer and must work under the direct 
28 supervision of the local health officer.24
