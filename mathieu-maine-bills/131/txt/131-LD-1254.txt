18SUMMARY
19 This bill allows a provider with a pending enrollment application to apply for a prior 
20 authorization of medications and services and requires the Department of Health and 
21 Human Services to make available prior authorization criteria to providers with a pending 
22 enrollment application.  It also requires the Department of Health and Human Services to 
23 cover medications and services delivered by a provider during the time the provider's 
24 application is pending based on the determination of the prior authorization request.19
