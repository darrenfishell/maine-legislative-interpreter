20SUMMARY
21 This bill requires the Secretary of State to establish and operate a program to provide 
22 mobile services for vehicle registration and renewal or replacement of a driver's license or 
23 nondriver identification card. The program must provide services in rural areas and to 
24 unhoused, immigrant and refugee populations. In order to provide the services, the 
25 Secretary of State must acquire 2 large vehicles to accommodate the needs of the program. 
26 Each vehicle must be staffed by 4 individuals, who, to the maximum extent feasible, must 
27 operate the program, including driving the vehicle and providing the services.21
