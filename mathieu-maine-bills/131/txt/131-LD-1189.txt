17SUMMARY
18 This bill amends provisions in the Maine Revised Statutes regarding criminal 
19 restitution by requiring a convicted person to establish by a preponderance of the evidence 
20 that the person cannot make restitution in order to move the court to modify the time or 
21 method of payment or service of the restitution, and provides an additional remedy of 
22 otherwise modifying or terminating the restitution. The bill also allows the court to modify 
23 or terminate restitution upon default.18
