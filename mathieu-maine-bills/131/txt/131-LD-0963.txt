3ยง2919.  Domestic terrorism reports
4 The Department of Public Safety shall report annually to the joint standing committee 
5 of the Legislature having jurisdiction over criminal justice and public safety matters on 
6 domestic terrorism acts and domestic terrorism threats in the State, including the activities 
7 and growth of known and suspected violent extremist groups.  The annual report must 
8 include, but is not limited to, information about threats against individuals belonging to a 
9 protected class under the Maine Human Rights Act, local and state governmental bodies, 
10 public officials and critical infrastructure. 
11 For purposes of this section, "domestic terrorism" means an activity that involves acts 
12 dangerous to human life that are a violation of the crime of causing a catastrophe under 
13 Title 17-A, section 803-A; that appears to be intended to intimidate or coerce a civilian 
14 population, influence the policy of a government by intimidation or coercion or affect the 
15 conduct of a government by mass destruction, assassination or kidnapping; and that occurs 
16 primarily within the State. 
17Sec. 2.  Maine Criminal Justice Academy domestic terrorism threat 
18response training and information dissemination.  The Maine Criminal Justice 
19 Academy shall develop minimum standards regarding domestic terrorism threat response 
20 training and information dissemination.  For purposes of this section, "domestic terrorism" 
21 has the same meaning as in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 25, section 2919.
22SUMMARY
23 This bill requires the Department of Public Safety to report annually to the joint 
24 standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over criminal justice and public 
25 safety matters on domestic terrorism acts and domestic terrorism threats in the State, 
26 including the activities and growth of known and suspected violent extremist groups.  The 
27 bill also directs the Maine Criminal Justice Academy to develop minimum standards 
28 regarding domestic terrorism threat response training and information dissemination.23
