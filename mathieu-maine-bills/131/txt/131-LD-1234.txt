41. Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
5 following terms have the following meanings.
6 A. "Authorized handler" means a current or former law enforcement officer who was 
7 the handler of a retired law enforcement dog during the dog's active service for the law 
8 enforcement agency and who is currently responsible for the well-being and health care 
9 of the dog.
10 B. "Department" means the Department of Public Safety.
11 C. "Law enforcement dog" has the same meaning as in Title 14, section 164-B, 
12 subsection 1, paragraph B.
132. Health care for retired law enforcement dogs. The department shall pay the health 
14 care expenses of a retired law enforcement dog that was used in service by the State Police. 
15 Upon submission of receipts of bona fide health care expenses on behalf of the dog, the 
16 department shall reimburse the authorized handler of the dog up to $5,000 per calendar 
17 year.
183. Administration.  The department shall administer the payment of health care 
19 expenses for retired law enforcement dogs under subsection 2.
204. Rules.  The department may adopt rules to carry out the purposes of this section. 
21 Rules adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules under Title 5, chapter 
22 375, subchapter 2-A.
23SUMMARY
24 This bill requires the Department of Public Safety to reimburse authorized handlers of 
25 retired law enforcement dogs that were used in service by the State Police up to $5,000 per 
26 year for health care expenses of their dogs.24
