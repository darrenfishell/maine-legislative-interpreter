6SUMMARY
7 This resolve directs the Maine-Canadian Legislative Advisory Commission and the 
8 New England and Eastern Canada Legislative Commission to examine restoring passenger 
9 rail from Boston, Massachusetts to Montreal, Quebec, Canada.  The commissions are 
10 directed to submit a report with their findings to the Joint Standing Committee on 
11 Transportation by December 6, 2023.7
