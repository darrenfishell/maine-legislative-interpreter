13 following: 
14 'Sec. 1. 38 MRSA §1304, sub-§1-D is enacted to read: 
15 1-D. Rules; beneficial use of solid waste. The board may adopt or amend rules 
16 relating to the beneficial use of solid waste. Rules adopted or amended pursuant to this 
17 subsection are major substantive rules, as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A, 
18 except that rules adopting or amending screening levels for beneficial use that are derived 
19 using the standard risk protocols of the United States Environmental Protection Agency are 
20 routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A. 
21 Sec. 2. PL 2011, c. 304, Pt. F, §2 is repealed.' 
22 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section 
23 number to read consecutively. 
24 SUMMARY 
25 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, replaces the bill and 
26 provides that the Board of Environmental Protection may adopt or amend rules relating to 
27 the beneficial use of solid waste. Those rules are designated major substantive rules except 
28 for certain rules adopting or amending screening levels for beneficial use, which are 
29 designated routine technical rules. The amendment also repeals a prior enacted unallocated 
30 law that contains a similar rule-making authority.
