10SUMMARY
11 This bill changes the total amount of revenues that may be distributed to Maine Clean 
12 Election Act candidates for contested general elections from $60,000 to $32,000 for State 
13 Senate candidates and from $15,000 to $8,200 for State House of Representative 
14 candidates.11
