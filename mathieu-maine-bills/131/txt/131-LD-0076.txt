9SUMMARY
10 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to convene a study group 
11 including members representing the department and other interested parties to examine the 
12 width of road shoulders on state roads and how they may be made consistent across the 
13 State.  The department is directed to submit a report to the Joint Standing Committee on 
14 Transportation no later than December 6, 2023 that includes its findings and 
15 recommendations, including suggested legislation, to make the width of road shoulders on 
16 state roads more consistent.10
