41.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
5 following terms have the following meanings.
6 A.  "Agreement" means when a buyer and a seller reach an agreement for the purchase 
7 and the sale of a firearm.
8 B.  "Firearm" has the same meaning as in Title 17-A, section 2, subsection 12-A.
9 C.  "Firearm dealer" means a person that is licensed as a dealer under 18 United States 
10 Code, Section 923 or that is required to be licensed as a dealer under that section.
112.  Waiting period.  A person may not knowingly deliver a firearm pursuant to a sale 
12 without withholding delivery of the firearm for at least 72 hours after agreement.
133.  Penalty.  This subsection applies to violations of subsection 2.
14 A.  A person who violates subsection 2 commits a civil violation for which a fine of 
15 not less than $200 and not more than $500 may be adjudged.
16 B.  A person who violates subsection 2 after having previously been adjudicated as 
17 violating subsection 2 commits a civil violation for which a fine of not less than $500 
18 and not more than $1,000 may be adjudged.
194.  Application.  This section does not apply to:
20 A.  The sale of a firearm if the seller knows that the person to whom the seller is selling 
21 the firearm is:
22 (1)  A law enforcement officer; or
23 (2)  A person who desires to purchase a firearm for use in promoting the public 
24 interest incidentally to the person's employment as a bank guard or armed truck 
25 guard or as a similar type of employee; or
26 B.  The sale of a firearm to a firearm dealer.
27 A firearm dealer is subject to the requirements of conducting national instant criminal 
28 background checks under 18 United States Code, Section 922(t).
29SUMMARY
30 This bill requires a 72-hour waiting period between an agreement for the purchase and 
31 sale of a firearm and its delivery to the purchaser and makes violation of the waiting period 
32 a civil violation with a $200 to $500 fine for the first violation and a $500 to $1,000 fine 
33 for a subsequent violation.30
