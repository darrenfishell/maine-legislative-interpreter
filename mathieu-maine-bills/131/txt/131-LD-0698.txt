3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill proposes to direct the Public Utilities Commission to examine its previous 
6 reports made pursuant to the Maine Energy Cost Reduction Act on the status and future of 
7 the natural gas supply in the State and, using current natural gas supply information as well 
8 as future projections, submit a report of the commission's findings and recommendations 
9 to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over energy, utilities 
10 and technology matters.4
