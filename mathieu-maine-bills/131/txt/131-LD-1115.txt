24SUMMARY
25 This bill:
26 1.  Provides for 10% of the available funds in the Community Development Block 
27 Grant Program to be provided on an equitable basis to the federally recognized Indian tribes 
28 in this State; and
29 2.  Directs the Department of Economic and Community Development to develop, in 
30 consultation with federally recognized Indian tribes in this State, a plan for ensuring those 
31 tribes have equitable access to the New Markets Tax Credit Program administered through 
32 the United States Department of the Treasury's Community Development Financial 
33 Institutions Fund.25
