25SUMMARY
26 This bill makes the following changes to the laws governing licensing and registration 
27 by the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife.
28 1.  It provides that all members of the Advisory Board for the Licensing of Guides 
29 except state employees are entitled to receive compensation and increases the daily rate of 
30 compensation for examiners of the Advisory Board for the Licensing of Guides and the 
31 Advisory Board for the Licensing of Taxidermists from $50 per day to $150 per day and 
32 per diem. 
33 2.  It increases the guide examination fee from $100 to $125 and the taxidermist 
34 application fee from $50 to $125 and clarifies that the taxidermist application fee also 
35 applies to examinations for general classification.
36 3.  It repeals the provision of law that allows a guide applicant to take a 2nd test for 
37 free.
38 4.  It clarifies that the owner of a snowmobile is not required to register that snowmobile 
39 if it is operated on land that the owner lives on or land that the snowmobile operator owns 
40 or leases. 26
