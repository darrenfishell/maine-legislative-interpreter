25SUMMARY
26 This bill directs the Department of Administrative and Financial Services to adopt 
27 major substantive rules to develop a graduated enforcement plan for violations of the Maine 
28 Medical Use of Cannabis Act or rules adopted under the Act, to include a stakeholder 
29 process in the adoption of those rules and to make the enforcement plan publicly available.26
