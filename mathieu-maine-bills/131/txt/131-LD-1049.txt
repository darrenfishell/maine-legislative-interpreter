41 commissioners to require a vertical barrier at least 4 feet tall in the fishway located at the 
42 dam on the Piscataquis River at Brown's Mill in the Town of Dover-Foxcroft to prevent 
43 the upstream passage of invasive fish species.  It also prohibits the owners, lessors or other 38
