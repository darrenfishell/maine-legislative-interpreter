41. Access to services. In accordance with this chapter, the department shall provide 
5 funding to maintain continued statewide access to family planning services. The 
6 department shall issue a request for proposals from qualified providers of family planning 
7 services. The department shall select one provider to provide management and oversight 
8 of the delivery of family planning services in this State, including, but not limited to, the 
9 coordination and management of any subrecipients, and shall distribute funds appropriated 
10 for that purpose.
112. Fund established. The Fund to Maintain Access to Statewide Family Planning 
12 Services, referred to in this section as "the fund," is established as a dedicated, nonlapsing 
13 fund within the department to provide family planning services under this section. The 
14 department may accept funds into the fund from any public or private source for the 
15 purposes of awarding a grant under this section. Any funds appropriated to the fund may 
16 not supplant other sources of funding.
173. Grant. The department shall make funds available from the fund in the form of a 
18 grant and may not distribute any funds under a fee-for-service format.
