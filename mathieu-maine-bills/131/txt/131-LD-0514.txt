Page 1 - 131LR1248(01)1Sec. 1. State Auditor to compile a report that ranks all nonprofit 
2corporations in the State that have received state funds. Resolved: That the State 
3 Auditor shall compile a report that ranks all nonprofit corporations in the State that have 
4 received state funds. The report must be completed using existing resources of the Office 
5 of the State Auditor. In compiling the report, the State Auditor shall:
6 A. Analyze the past 20 years of state funding to nonprofit corporations in the State;
7 B. Rank per 5-year period the nonprofit corporations in the State from the nonprofit 
8 corporation receiving the most state funds to the nonprofit corporation receiving the 
9 least state funds; and
10 C. State the percentage per state budget that was disbursed to nonprofit corporations in 
11 the State over the past 20 years. 
12Sec. 2. Report. Resolved: That, no later than December 6, 2023, the State Auditor 
13 shall submit the report under section 1 to the Joint Standing Committee on Appropriations 
14 and Financial Affairs.
15SUMMARY
16 This resolve directs the State Auditor to compile a report by December 6, 2023 of state 
17 funds received by nonprofit corporations in the State for the past 20 years, break down the 
18 report into 5-year increments, rank the nonprofits from the most state funds received to the 
19 least state funds received and state the percentage of the state budget that was disbursed to 
20 nonprofit corporations in the State.16
