Page 1 - 131LR1370(01)1Sec. 1.  Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to study distribution 
2of gasoline tax revenue for nonhighway recreational vehicle programs.  
3Resolved:  That the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife shall study the 
4 distribution of gasoline tax revenue for nonhighway recreational vehicle programs as 
5 described in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 36, section 2903-D. The department shall 
6 invite the participation of interested parties identified by the department. The study must 
7 explore changing the distribution of gasoline tax revenues among programs supporting the 
8 various types of recreational vehicles so that those programs are supported proportionately 
9 based on the number of registrations of each type of recreational vehicle. The department 
10 shall submit a report and any recommended changes to the distribution of gasoline tax 
11 revenue for nonhighway recreational vehicle programs to the Joint Standing Committee on 
12 Inland Fisheries and Wildlife no later than December 6, 2023. The committee may report 
13 out legislation related to the report to the Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.
