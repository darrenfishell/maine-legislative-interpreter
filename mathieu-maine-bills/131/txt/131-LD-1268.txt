51. Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
6 following terms have the following meanings.
7 A.  "Emergency medical services person" has the same meaning as in Title 32, section 
8 83, subsection 12, except that the person receives no or nominal compensation or 
9 reimbursement for actual costs incurred for responding to emergency medical services 
10 calls or participating in required training and meetings.
11 B.  "Volunteer firefighter" means a member of a separately incorporated volunteer fire 
12 department or municipal fire department who receives no or nominal compensation or 
13 reimbursement for actual costs incurred for responding to fire calls or participating in 
14 required training and meetings. 
152.  Optional exemption.  A municipality may by ordinance exempt from the annual 
16 excise tax imposed pursuant to section 1482 one vehicle owned or jointly owned by a 
17 resident who is a volunteer firefighter or a volunteer emergency medical services person.  
18 To qualify for the exemption, the resident volunteer firefighter or resident volunteer 
19 emergency medical services person annually must present to a designated municipal 
20 official documentation demonstrating that during the previous 12 months, the resident 
21 actively served as a volunteer firefighter or volunteer emergency medical services person 
22 and used the vehicle for which the exemption is being requested to respond to fire calls or 
23 emergency medical services calls.  A municipality may impose additional eligibility 
24 criteria, including, but not limited to, duration of service as a volunteer firefighter or 
25 emergency medical services person, required response to a minimum number of fire or 
26 emergency services calls and training requirements.
27SUMMARY
28 This bill allows a municipality to provide an exemption from annual excise tax for one 
29 vehicle owned, separately or jointly, by a resident of that municipality who is a volunteer 
30 firefighter or volunteer emergency medical services person, as long as that vehicle is used 
31 to perform those volunteer services.28
