3CHAPTER 279
4DIAPER CHANGING STATIONS
5ยง1700-C.  Diaper changing stations in public restrooms required
61.  Definition.  As used in this section, "diaper changing station" means a table or other 
7 device suitable for changing the diaper of a child who has not attained 4 years of age.
82.  Requirement.  Except as provided in subsection 3, all public restrooms must be 
9 equipped with a diaper changing station that is physically safe, sanitary and appropriate.  
10 The diaper changing station must be accessible to individuals with disabilities.
113.  Exceptions.  A public restroom is not subject to the requirements of subsection 2 if 
12 the restroom:
13 A.  Has conspicuous signage directing individuals to the location of another restroom 
14 on the same floor with a diaper changing station that is accessible to the individual; or
15 B.  Would require construction in order to install a diaper changing station and the cost 
16 of construction is not feasible.
174.  Rulemaking.  The department shall adopt rules to implement this section.  Rules 
18 adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 
19 375, subchapter 2-A.
20SUMMARY
21 This bill requires, with certain exceptions, all public restrooms to be equipped with a 
22 diaper changing station.  It directs the Department of Health and Human Services to adopt 
23 rules to implement this requirement.21
