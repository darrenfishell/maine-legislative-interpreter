14SUMMARY
15 This resolve directs the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to develop a 
16 software application that a person can download onto the person's electronic device to allow 
17 the person to access the department's publicly accessible website pages regarding hunting 
18 and fishing information, licenses and permits. The department is required to complete 
19 development of the application by December 21, 2024 to be used by the public for the 2025 
20 hunting and fishing seasons.14
