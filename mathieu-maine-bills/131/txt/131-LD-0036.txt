17SUMMARY
18 This bill amends the Maine Insurance Code in the following ways.
19 1.  It clarifies the laws governing domestic insurer investments concerning investments 
20 in stocks of subsidiaries.
21 2.  It  removes the prohibition on members of the Continuing Education Advisory 
22 Committee being reappointed and allows the Superintendent of Insurance to remove a 
23 member of the committee for cause.
24 3.  It removes from the small and large group health insurance rating laws obsolete 
25 references to the Dirigo Health savings offset payments program.
26 4.  It requires licensees under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 24-A and Title 24 to 
27 respond to all inquiries of the superintendent within 14 days of receipt of the inquiry. 
28 Current law requires licensees under Title 24-A to respond to inquiries that relate to 
29 resolution of consumer complaints involving the licensee within the 14-day time frame and 
30 to all other inquiries within 30 days. The bill also requires licensees to respond to follow-
31 up inquiries within 5 days of receipt.
32 5.  It makes other technical corrections.18
