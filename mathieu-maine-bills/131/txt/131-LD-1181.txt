4 Except as otherwise provided by the municipality's ordinances or charter, an An elected

8Sec. 2.  30-A MRSA §2505, sub-§9, as enacted by PL 2011, c. 324, §1, is repealed.
9SUMMARY
10 This bill allows a municipality to use the statutory procedure or a provision of the 
11 municipality's ordinances or charter for recalling an elected official from office and 
12 removes the provision requiring that an elected official may only be recalled if the official 
13 is convicted of a crime.10
