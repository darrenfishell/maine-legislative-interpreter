14SUMMARY
15 Under current law, premiums for direct insurance and annuity considerations are 
16 subject to tax at the rate of 3% or whatever rate is imposed in the jurisdiction in which the 
17 insurance company is incorporated, whichever is greater, except for certain nonadmitted 
18 insurance premiums.
19 This bill removes the imposition of a higher rate of tax on premiums for direct 
20 insurance and annuity considerations for insurers incorporated outside of the State. Under 
21 the bill, all premiums are subject to a 3% tax rate.15
