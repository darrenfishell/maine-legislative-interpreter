24 SUMMARY 
25 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, reduces the number of 
26 signatures required on a nomination petition for a candidate for United States Senator from 
27 at least 4,000 and not more than 5,000 to at least 2,000 and not more than 2,500 and a 
28 candidate for United States Representative from at least 2,000 and not more than 2,500 to 
29 at least 1,000 and not more than 1,250. The amendment also removes the provision that 
30 moved the date by which a nomination petition needs to be filed with the Secretary of State.
