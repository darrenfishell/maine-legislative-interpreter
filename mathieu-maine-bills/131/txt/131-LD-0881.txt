2wood products. Resolved: That the University of Maine System shall study the 
3 development of a course for midcareer architects and engineers on incorporating 
4 manufactured wood products into their work.  In conducting the study, the University of 
5 Maine System shall examine the market demand for manufactured wood products and other 
6 climate-friendly building materials and pertinent midcareer training that already exists for 
7 architects and engineers.
8Sec. 2. Report. Resolved: That, by December 6, 2023, the University of Maine 
9 System shall submit a report based on its study in section 1, including a description of the 
10 proposed course, for presentation to the Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.
11SUMMARY
12 This resolve directs the University of Maine System to study the development of a 
13 course for midcareer architects and engineers on incorporating manufactured wood 
14 products into their work.  The University of Maine System is required to submit a report 
15 based on its study by December 6, 2023 for presentation to the Second Regular Session of 
16 the 131st Legislature.12
