33SUMMARY
34 This bill allows a person to be a candidate for sheriff who is not and has not been 
35 certified as a law enforcement officer as long as the person is enrolled in the Department 
36 of Public Safety's preservice law enforcement training and meets all required in-service 
37 law enforcement training standards within one year of taking office.  This bill also requires 
38 that a new sheriff under this provision enroll in a jail administration certification program 
39 administered by the Commissioner of Corrections and obtain certification from the 
40 program within 6 months of taking office.34
