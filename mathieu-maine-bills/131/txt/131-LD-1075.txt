34SUMMARY
35 This bill makes multiple changes to the tax credits available to visual media production 
36 companies operating in the State.  The bill makes the available tax credits refundable and 
37 transferable, increases the credits for certain expenses incurred in Maine and for hiring 
38 residents of Maine and offers additional credits for visual media productions that are 
39 located in certain Maine counties, are set in Maine or have a lead cast member, writer or 
40 director who is a Maine resident.  The bill also reduces the total expenditures needed to 
41 qualify for the credits, extends the time period for visual media production companies to 1
