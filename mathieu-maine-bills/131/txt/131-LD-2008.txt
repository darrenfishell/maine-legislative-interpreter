Page 1 - 131LR2528(01)1Sec. 1.  Live plants in state-owned buildings.  Resolved:  That the Department 
2 of Administrative and Financial Services shall amend its rules to allow live plants in state-
3 owned buildings, except that the department may prohibit live plants that develop mold or 
4 bacteria or are contributing to an insect infestation.
5SUMMARY
6 This resolve requires the Department of Administrative and Financial Services to 
7 amend its rules to allow live plants in state-owned buildings unless the plants develop mold 
8 or bacteria or are contributing to an insect infestation.6
