2sunrise review regarding proposal to license genetic counselors.  Resolved:  
3 That the Commissioner of Professional and Financial Regulation shall conduct an 
4 independent assessment, as described in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 12015, 
5 subsection 3, paragraph B pursuant to the sunrise review requirements in Title 32, chapter 
6 1-A, subchapter 2, of a proposal to license genetic counselors.
7Sec. 2. Report.  Resolved:  That, no later than January 10, 2024, the Commissioner 
8 of Professional and Financial Regulation shall submit a report describing the results of the 
9 independent assessment under section 1 to the Joint Standing Committee on Health 
10 Coverage, Insurance and Financial Services.  The committee is authorized to submit 
11 legislation on the subject matter of the report to the Second Regular Session of the 131st 
12 Legislature.
13SUMMARY
14 This resolve is a recommendation from the Advisory Panel To Better Understand and 
15 Make Recommendations Regarding the Implications of Genome-editing Technology for 
16 the Citizens of the State established pursuant to Resolve 2021, chapter 177.  It requires the 
17 Commissioner of Professional and Financial Regulation to conduct an independent 
18 assessment of a proposal to license genetic counselors.  The resolve requires the 
19 commissioner to submit a report to the Joint Standing Committee on Health Coverage, 
20 Insurance and Financial Services and allows the committee to submit legislation to the 
21 Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.14
