51.  Fund established.  The Statewide Radio and Network System Reserve Fund, 
6 referred to in this section as "the "fund," is established as an internal service fund in the 
7 Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Office of Information Technology, 
8 referred to in this section as "the "office," for the purposes of managing the fund and 
9 acquiring, expanding, upgrading and replacing a statewide radio and network system for 
10 use by state agencies and Aroostook County.  The office may charge a fee to agencies using 
11 the statewide radio and network system and Aroostook County in accordance with an 
12 established rate structure.  Revenues derived from operations must be used to pay the costs 
13 of the lease-purchase to acquire a system, expand, upgrade and replace the system, and to 
14 manage the fund.
15 A.  The office shall work closely with all state departments and agencies and Aroostook 
16 County to identify radio and network requirements for the statewide system to ensure 
17 that agency and county program requirements are met to the maximum extent possible.  
18 The office shall:
19 (1)  Ensure that the annual costs of the lease or lease-purchase are paid in a timely 
20 manner and that the financial affairs of the fund are properly managed;
21 (2)  Maintain records of radio and network system requirements for all state 
22 agencies using the system and Aroostook County and make this information 
23 available to state agencies and Aroostook County;
24 (3)  Require state agencies to become part of the statewide radio and network 
25 system when replacing their current systems or purchasing new systems;
26 (4)  Acquire, expand, upgrade or replace the statewide radio and network system 
27 in accordance with an established replacement plan; and
28 (5)  Transfer radio equipment and network infrastructure into the fund from state 
29 agencies using the system and Aroostook County, purchase, lease, lease-purchase 
30 or enter into other financing agreements, in accordance with section 1587, for the 
31 acquisition, expansion, upgrade or replacement of the system or any of its 
32 components in accordance with paragraph B when it can be demonstrated that any 
33 such action or agreement provides a clear cost or program advantage to the State.
34 B.  The Chief Information Officer, in conjunction with the state agencies using the 
35 statewide radio and network system and Aroostook County, operating as a board that 
36 may be referred to as "the Statewide Radio Network Board," shall establish the 
37 following:
38 (1)  Standards for statewide radio and network system operations;
39 (2)  Specifications for systems and components to be acquired by the State; and
40 (3)  Standards for the exemption or waiver of state agencies from the requirements 
41 of this section.

29SUMMARY
30 This bill requires the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Office of 
31 Information Technology to authorize Aroostook County to use the statewide radio and 
32 network system and amends the provisions of law governing the Statewide Radio and 
33 Network System Reserve Fund to include Aroostook County.30
