11SUMMARY
12 This bill expands the authorized use of traffic surveillance cameras to school zones and 
13 portions of a public way under construction. The bill makes the data of the cameras 
14 confidential and restricts the dissemination of the data to only the purposes of investigation 
15 or prosecution of a violation of the law.1
