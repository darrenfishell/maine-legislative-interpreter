35SUMMARY
36 This bill allows 14 days for the processing of absentee ballots instead of 7 as in current 
37 law, adjusts the hours for a member of the public to make a written request of the clerk to 
38 inspect ballot applications and envelopes before they are processed, requires a municipality 
39 with a population greater than 7,000 to provide an opportunity for absentee voting in the 
40 presence of a clerk for at least 8 hours on one Saturday and 8 hours on one Sunday in the 
41 18 days before the date of an election and requires the Secretary of State to provide grants 36
