27SUMMARY
28 This bill amends the laws governing harness racing to clarify that, for the purpose of 
29 the sale of pari-mutuel pools and common pari-mutuel pools for simulcast races, the 
30 enclosure of a racetrack consists of all contiguous land and buildings owned or leased by a 
31 commercial track.  The bill also delays the repeal of the law governing the sale of pari-
32 mutuel pools and common pari-mutuel pools for simulcast races to January 1, 2026.28
