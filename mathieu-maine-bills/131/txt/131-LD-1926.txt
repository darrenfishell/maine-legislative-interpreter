Page 1 - 131LR2493(01)1Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2Sec. 1.  Lower Presumpscot River; discharge license moratorium.  
3 Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, until January 1, 2028, the 
4 Department of Environmental Protection may not issue a waste discharge license under the 
5 Maine Revised Statutes, Title 38, section 413 for a new discharge to the section of the 
6 Presumpscot River from Saccarappa Falls to tidewater as identified in Title 38, section 467, 
7 subsection 9, paragraph A, subparagraph (4).
8SUMMARY
9 This bill imposes a moratorium, until January 1, 2028, on the issuance of waste 
10 discharge licenses for new discharges to the Presumpscot River from Saccarappa Falls to 
11 tidewater.9
