Page 1 - 131LR1958(01)1Sec. 1.  Banking and cannabis industry study. Resolved:  That the Treasurer 
2 of State shall, in consultation with the Finance Authority of Maine, the Department of 
3 Administrative and Financial Services' office of cannabis policy and any other state agency 
4 or organization that the Treasurer of State determines to be appropriate, review options for 
5 meeting the banking needs of individuals and businesses involved in the adult use cannabis 
6 and medical cannabis industries in the State, including examining banking solutions in 
7 other states that have legalized cannabis use, banking solution efforts under way at the 
8 federal level and actions the Legislature can take to encourage federal action on banking 
9 solutions.
10Sec. 2.  Report. Resolved:  That, by December 6, 2023, the Treasurer of State shall 
11 submit a report based on the review in section 1 to the Joint Standing Committee on 
12 Veterans and Legal Affairs. The committee is authorized to submit legislation based on the 
13 report to the Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.
14SUMMARY
15 This resolve directs the Treasurer of State to review options for meeting the banking 
16 needs of individuals and businesses involved in the adult use cannabis and medical 
17 cannabis industries in the State, including examining banking solutions in other states that 
18 have legalized cannabis use, banking solution efforts under way at the federal level and 
19 actions the Legislature can take to encourage federal action on banking solutions.  The 
20 treasurer is required to submit a report based on the review by December 6, 2023 to the 
21 Joint Standing Committee on Veterans and Legal Affairs, which is authorized to submit 
22 legislation based on the report to the Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature. 15
