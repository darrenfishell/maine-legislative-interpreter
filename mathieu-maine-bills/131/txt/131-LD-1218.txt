3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill would create an independent office to manage appeals to administrative 
6 actions made by the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Bureau of 
7 Parks and Lands regarding leases of or easements held on land managed by the bureau and 
8 management plans adopted or updated by the bureau.  The appeals process would include 
9 a mechanism to stop or reverse administrative actions found to be harmful to the public.4
