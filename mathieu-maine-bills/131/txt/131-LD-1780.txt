20SUMMARY
21 This bill defines a micromobility device as a small, low-speed human-powered or 
22 electric-powered transportation device, including a scooter, skateboard, electric scooter, 
23 electric skateboard or other lightweight, wheeled conveyance that has a speed of 28 miles 
24 per hour or less.  This bill applies the rules of the road and helmet standards to 
25 micromobility devices as the rules and standards are applied to bicycles.21
