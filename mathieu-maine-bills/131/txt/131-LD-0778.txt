15SUMMARY
16 This bill, beginning January 1, 2024, prohibits the Department of Health and Human 
17 Services from requesting certain waivers and exemptions under the federal Supplemental 
18 Nutritional Assistance Program, or SNAP.  It prohibits the department from applying for a 
19 waiver under 7 United States Code, Section 2015(o)(4), which allows for waivers in 
20 specific geographical areas that have unemployment over 10% or with insufficient jobs 
21 available.  It also prohibits the department from providing exemptions to work 
22 requirements for up to 12% of individuals receiving SNAP benefits pursuant to 7 United 
23 States Code, Section 2015(o)(6)(E).16
