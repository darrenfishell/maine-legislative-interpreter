Page 1 - 131LR0746(01)1Sec. 1.  Amendment of Principles of Reimbursement for Nursing Facilities 
2rule.  Resolved:  That the Department of Health and Human Services shall amend its rule 
3 Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 67, Principles of 
4 Reimbursement for Nursing Facilities, no later than January 1, 2024 as follows.
5 1.  The costs of health insurance for those personnel currently included in the direct 
6 care cost component in section 16.1.7(3) must be removed from the direct care cost 
7 component and included in the fixed costs component in section 18.
8 2.  The costs of health insurance for those personnel currently included in the routine 
9 cost component in section 17.4.1(o)(3) must be removed from the routine cost component 
10 and included in the fixed costs component in section 18.
11 Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules as defined in the 
12 Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
13SUMMARY
14 This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rule 
15 Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 67, Principles of 
16 Reimbursement for Nursing Facilities, no later than January 1, 2024 to move health 
17 insurance costs for personnel from direct care and routine cost components to fixed costs 
18 components.  14
