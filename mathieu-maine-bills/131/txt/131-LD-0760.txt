2Sec. 1.  5 MRSA §145, as amended by PL 1991, c. 780, Pt. Y, §15, is further 
3 amended to read:
4§145.  Registered bonds
5 The Treasurer of State may issue registered bonds, transferable by assignment, in 
6 pieces of not less than $1,000, and of any multiple of 1,000, in exchange for, and in place 
7 of, any coupon bonds issued under the laws of this State, bearing the same rate of interest 
8 and maturing at the same time as the bonds that the Treasurer of State may receive therefor 
9 in exchange. The place of payment prescribed therein must be the State Treasury. Said 
10 Those bonds must bear the facsimile of the signature of the Governor and must be signed 
11 by the Treasurer of State or the Treasurer of State's deputy and attested by the 
12 Commissioner of Administrative and Financial Services, or such agent as the commissioner 
13 may designate.
14Sec. 2.  5 MRSA §145-B, sub-§2, as amended by PL 1991, c. 780, Pt. Y, §17, is 
15 further amended to read:
162.  Signatures.  Registered bonds must bear the facsimile signatures of the Governor 
17 and signature of the Treasurer of State , or the Treasurer of State's deputy , and must be 
18 attested by the facsimile signature of the Commissioner of Administrative and Financial 
19 Services or such agents as the commissioner may designate.  Whenever signatures on 
20 registered bonds of other state officials are required, their facsimile signatures may be used.
21SUMMARY
22 This bill removes the requirement that registered bonds issued by the Treasurer of State 
23 bear the facsimile signature of the Governor.22
