20SUMMARY
21 This bill amends the so-called cascade, which describes how any General Fund 
22 unappropriated surplus at the close of a fiscal year is to be distributed.  This bill reduces 
23 from 80% to 40% the amount distributed to the Maine Budget Stabilization Fund, also 
24 known as the Rainy Day Fund, and distributes 20% to the School Revolving Renovation 
25 Fund and 20% to the Education Stabilization Fund.21
