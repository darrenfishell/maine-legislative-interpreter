19SUMMARY
20 This bill decreases the rate of tax imposed on the lowest bracket of individual income 
21 for single individuals and married persons filing separate returns, heads of households and 
22 persons filing married joint returns from 5.8% to 4.5%. The decreased rate applies to tax 
23 years beginning on or after January 1, 2023. 1
