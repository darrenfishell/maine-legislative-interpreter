32SUMMARY
33 This bill provides authority for the Legislative Council to establish an internship 
34 program.  The program must provide for the placement of policy interns with Legislators 
35 to assist the Legislators with their legislative work.  The program must also provide an 
36 appropriate hourly wage to each policy intern for the internship work.  Under the program, 
37 policy interns may not work more than 10 hours in any week.  Under the program, a policy 
38 intern must either be a student at an accredited institution of higher education in this State 
39 or be a resident of this State and a student at an accredited institution of higher education 
40 in another state.  No more than 185 policy interns at any one time may be placed under the 
41 program. 33
