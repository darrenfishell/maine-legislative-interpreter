41. Program established. The Department of Transportation shall develop a program 
5 to:
6 A. Cooperate with the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Bureau 
7 of Parks and Lands to identify the state parks with the highest number of visitors;
8 B. Construct shoulders of not less than 5 feet in width on state and state aid highways 
9 that serve the state parks identified under paragraph A or other destinations of statewide 
10 or regional significance; and
11 C. Identify state and state aid highways serving high-use state parks near population 
12 centers and prioritize the construction of separated bicycle lanes on those state and state 
13 aid highways.
14 The program must construct the shoulders and bicycle lanes described by this subsection 
15 on state and state aid highways beginning with the highways already scheduled for 
16 improvement and include a plan for completing the construction required by this subsection 
17 before January 1, 2044.
182.  Fund established.  The Shoulder and Bicycle Lane Program Fund is established as 
19 a dedicated account within the Department of Transportation. The fund must be used for 
20 the administration of the program established pursuant to subsection 1. The department 
21 shall administer the fund.
