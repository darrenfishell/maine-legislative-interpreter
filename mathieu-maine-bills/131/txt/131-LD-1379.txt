3CHAPTER 225-A
4CELLULAR TELEPHONE DO-NOT-CALL REGISTRY ENROLLMENT
5ยง1499-C. Cellular telephone do-not-call registry enrollment
61. Definitions.  As used in this chapter, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
7 following terms have the following meanings.
8 A. "Division" means the Department of the Attorney General, consumer protection 
9 division. 
10 B. "Registry" means the do-not-call registry maintained by the division that lists the 
11 names of persons who do not wish to receive telephone sales calls.
12 C. "Retail transaction" means a transaction in which a customer purchases a wireless 
13 telephone service from a wireless telephone service provider and the customer is 
14 assigned a telephone number.
15 D. "Telecommunications carrier" has the same meaning as in 47 United States Code, 
16 Section 153(51) as in effect on January 1, 2023. 
17 E.  "Telephone sales call" means a solicitation call made to a customer for:
18 (1)  Solicitation of a sale of consumer goods or services; or
19 (2)  Obtaining information that will or may be used for the direct solicitation of a 
20 sale of consumer goods or services or an extension of credit for such purposes.
21 F. "Wireless telephone service" means any mobile telecommunications services as 
22 defined in Title 35-A, section 102, subsection 9-A.
23 G. "Wireless telephone service provider" means a telecommunications carrier that 
24 provides wireless telephone service.
252. Point of sale requirements.  At the time of engaging in a retail transaction in the 
26 State, a wireless telephone service provider shall:
27 A. Advise the customer that the wireless telephone service provider will submit the 
28 customer's telephone number to the division for inclusion in the registry unless the 
29 customer opts out of the inclusion of the customer's telephone number in the registry 
30 in writing; and
31 B. Provide the customer with a form that the customer may sign to opt out of having 
32 the customer's telephone number included in the registry.
333. Registry. If a customer does not opt out of the inclusion of the customer's telephone 
34 number in the registry, the wireless telephone service provider shall submit the customer's 
35 telephone number to the division within 30 days of the retail transaction. If a customer opts 
36 out of inclusion in the registry, the wireless telephone service provider may not submit the 
37 customer's telephone number to the division.
Page 2 - 131LR0595(01)14. Civil violation.  A violation of this chapter constitutes a civil violation for which a 
2 fine of not less than $250 may be adjudged.
3Sec. 2.  Effective date. This Act takes effect January 1, 2024.
4SUMMARY
5 This bill requires a wireless telephone service provider to submit the telephone number 
6 associated with a customer's mobile telecommunications service to the Department of the 
7 Attorney General, consumer protection division for inclusion in the do-not-call registry 
8 unless the customer opts out of the inclusion of the customer's telephone number to the do-
9 not-call registry in writing. Violations of the chapter are subject to a minimum fine of $250.
10 This legislation takes effect January 1, 2024.5
