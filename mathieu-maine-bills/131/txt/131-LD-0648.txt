5ยง11681.  Fund to Provide Public Service Student Loan Forgiveness
6 The Fund to Provide Public Service Student Loan Forgiveness is established as a 
7 nonlapsing fund under the jurisdiction and control of the Finance Authority of Maine.  All 
8 revenues credited to this fund must be distributed for the purpose of student loan 
9 forgiveness based on need for residents of the State who are local and county law 
10 enforcement officers, firefighters, emergency medical services persons, dispatchers and 
11 public health workers. The Finance Authority of Maine shall award funds for the purpose 
12 of student loan forgiveness and adopt rules for determining eligibility for and terms and 
13 conditions of student loan forgiveness. Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine 
14 technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A. Interest earned on 
15 amounts in the fund may be used for the costs of administering the distribution of funds for 
16 the purpose of student loan forgiveness.

1SUMMARY
2 This bill establishes the Fund to Provide Public Service Student Loan Forgiveness in 
3 the Finance Authority of Maine to provide funds for student loan forgiveness for local and 
4 county law enforcement officers, firefighters, emergency medical services persons, 
5 dispatchers and public health workers.  It appropriates $5,000,000 to the fund.  It also 
6 appropriates $5,000,000 to the Finance Authority of Maine to provide no-interest home 
7 mortgages to local and county law enforcement officers, firefighters, emergency medical 
8 services persons, dispatchers and public health workers while they are serving in those 
9 positions.1
