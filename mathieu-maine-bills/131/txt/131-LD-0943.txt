51.  Prohibition.  Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, a common 
6 carrier or contract carrier for transportation or shipment may not keep or cause to be kept 
7 a record, registry or database of privately owned firearms in this State; a record, registry or 
8 database of firearm owners in this State; or a record, registry or database of firearm 
9 possessors in this State that contains sufficient information to establish a connection 
10 between any specific sender or receiver of a shipment in this State containing a firearm or 
11 firearm ammunition, including, but not limited to, information regarding the name and 
12 address of an individual and information regarding the make, model, caliber or serial 
13 number of a firearm.
142.  Penalty.  A common carrier or contract carrier for transportation or shipment that 
15 violates subsection 1 commits a civil violation for which a fine of $1,000 for each item of 
16 information stored in a record, registry or database in violation of subsection 1 must be 
17 adjudged.
18SUMMARY
19 This bill makes it unlawful for common carriers or contract carriers for transportation 
20 or shipment to maintain a record, registry or database containing information relating to 
21 firearms, firearm owners and firearm possessors that would be sufficient to establish a 
22 connection between any person and any specific firearm or firearm ammunition. The 
23 penalty for violating this law is a civil violation for which a fine of $1,000 must be adjudged 
24 for each item of information stored in a record, registry or database by a common carrier 
25 or contract carrier for transportation or shipment.19
