13Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
14Sec. 1.  5 MRSA §13203, sub-§4, ¶C, as enacted by PL 2021, c. 631, §1, is 
15 amended by amending the first blocked paragraph to read:
16 The terms of the members of the board of directors who are not ex officio members  
17 must be staggered as specified in the bylaws of the corporation  are appointed for 3-
18 year terms.   A vacancy must be filled in the same manner as the original appointment 
19 in accordance with this paragraph for the balance of the unexpired term.  A member of 
20 the board of directors continues to hold office until a successor is appointed and 
21 qualified, but the term of the successor is not altered from the original expiration date 
22 of the holdover member's term.
23Sec. 2.  Staggered terms.  Notwithstanding the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, 
24 section 13203, subsection 4, paragraph C, with regard to the initial appointments of the 
25 members of the Board of Directors of the Maine Space Corporation, the Governor shall 
26 appoint 4 members to a one-year term, 4 members to a 2-year term and 3 members to a 3-
27 year term. 
28Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
29 takes effect when approved.
30SUMMARY
31 This bill requires that the Governor, in making initial appointments of the members of 
32 the Board of Directors of the Maine Space Corporation, appoint the members for staggered 
33 terms. 31
