2Sec. 1.  28-B MRSA §1501, sub-§3 is enacted to read:
33.  Conduct allowed for individuals on probation or parole and released pending 
4trial.  Unless the court makes a finding that the cultivation, possession or use of cannabis 
5 by an individual creates a risk of danger to the individual or another person, the individual’s 
6 conduct allowed under this chapter or a positive test of the individual indicating the 
7 presence of cannabis, delta-9-tetrahydrocannabinol or any other cannabinoid or metabolite 
8 of cannabis may not be considered a violation of probation or parole under Title 34-A, 
9 chapter 5 or release pending trial under Title 15, section 1026.
10SUMMARY
11 This bill prohibits an individual’s legal cultivation, possession or use of cannabis or 
12 testing positive for cannabis from being a violation of probation, parole or release pending 
13 trial unless the court finds that the cultivation, possession or use of cannabis by the 
14 individual creates a risk of danger to the individual or others.11
