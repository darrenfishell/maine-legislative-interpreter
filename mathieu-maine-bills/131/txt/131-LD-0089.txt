9SUMMARY
10 This bill, for property tax years beginning on or after April 1, 2024, expands eligibility 
11 for the property tax stabilization program for individuals 65 years of age or older to 
12 surviving spouses of eligible individuals as long as they were married for at least 10 years 
13 before the eligible individual's death.1
