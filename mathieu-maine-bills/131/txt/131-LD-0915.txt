Page 1 - 131LR0259(01)1Sec. 1. Commission established. Resolved: That the Blue Ribbon Commission to 
2 Study the Organization of and Service Delivery by the Department of Health and Human 
3 Services, referred to in this resolve as "the commission," is established.
4Sec. 2. Commission membership. Resolved: That, notwithstanding Joint Rule 
5 353, the commission consists of 13 members as follows:
6 1. The Commissioner of Health and Human Services, or the commissioner's designee;
7 2. Two departmental directors within the Department of Health and Human Services, 
8 appointed by the Commissioner of Health and Human Services;
9 3.  Two members appointed by the President of the Senate who are members of the 
10 Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human Services;
11 4. Three members appointed by the Speaker of the House of Representatives who are 
12 members of the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human Services;
13 5. One member appointed by the President of the Senate who represents an 
14 organization that advocates for individuals with disabilities;
15 6. One member appointed by the Speaker of the House of Representatives who 
16 represents an organization that represents at-risk groups served by the Department of 
17 Health and Human Services;
18 7.  One member appointed by the Speaker of the House of Representatives who 
19 represents an organization that advocates for individuals who are affected by mental health 
20 disorders;
21 8. One member of the public appointed by the President of the Senate; and
22 9. One member of the public appointed by the Speaker of the House of Representatives.
23Sec. 3. Chairs. Resolved: That, notwithstanding Joint Rule 353, the first-named 
24 Senate member of the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human Services and the 
25 first-named House member of the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human 
26 Services shall serve as cochairs of the commission.
27Sec. 4. Appointments; convening of commission. Resolved: That, 
28 notwithstanding Joint Rule 353, all appointments must be made no later than 30 days 
29 following the effective date of this resolve. The appointing authorities shall notify the 
30 Executive Director of the Legislative Council once all appointments have been completed. 
31 After appointment of all members, the chairs shall call and convene the first meeting of the 
32 commission. If 30 days or more after the effective date of this resolve a majority of but not 
33 all appointments have been made, the chairs may request authority and the Legislative 
34 Council may grant authority for the commission to meet and conduct its business.
35Sec. 5. Duties. Resolved: That the commission shall examine the organizational 
36 structure of the Department of Health and Human Services and the services provided by 
37 the department. The commission shall review the organizational structure of and service 
38 delivery at similar agencies in other states and in nongovernmental organizations.
39Sec. 6. Staff assistance. Resolved: That, notwithstanding Joint Rule 353, the Office 
40 of Program Evaluation and Government Accountability shall provide support and 
41 necessary staffing services to the commission.
Page 2 - 131LR0259(01)1Sec. 7. Report. Resolved: That, no later than December 1, 2024, the commission 
2 shall submit a report that includes its findings and recommendations, including suggested 
3 legislation, for presentation to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 
4 jurisdiction over health and human services matters. The committee may submit legislation 
5 to the 132nd Legislature in 2025.
6SUMMARY
7 This resolve creates the Blue Ribbon Commission to Study the Organization of and 
8 Service Delivery by the Department of Health and Human Services to examine the 
9 organizational structure of the Department of Health and Human Services and the services 
10 provided by the department and review the organizational structure of and service delivery 
11 at similar agencies in other states and in nongovernmental organizations.7
