2Sec. 1.  12 MRSA §12458, sub-§3 is enacted to read:
33.  Washington County rivers.  A person may not be limited in the number, amount, 
4 weight or size of largemouth bass or smallmouth bass fished in the rivers of Washington 
5 County.  A person may not be limited in the possession of largemouth bass or smallmouth 
6 bass fished in the rivers of Washington County. For purposes of this subsection, the rivers 
7 of Washington County include, but are not limited to, all or portions of Dennys River, St. 
8 Croix River, Machias River, East Machias River, Pleasant River, Narraguagus River, 
9 Union River, Tunk Stream and Orange River.

26SUMMARY
27 This bill removes the limit on the number, amount, weight and size of largemouth bass 
28 and smallmouth bass fished in the rivers of Washington County. The bill also removes the 
29 limit on possessing largemouth bass or smallmouth bass fished in the rivers of Washington 
30 County. The bill specifies that the rivers of Washington County include, but are not limited 
31 to, all or portions of Dennys River, St. Croix River, Machias River, East Machias River, 
32 Pleasant River, Narraguagus River, Union River, Tunk Stream and Orange River.27
