27SUMMARY
28 This bill changes the distribution of revenue from the real estate transfer tax on property 
29 transfers by deeds to provide that beginning in fiscal year 2023-24 revenue received by the 
30 State would be distributed entirely to the Maine State Housing Authority for deposit in the 
31 Housing Opportunities for Maine Fund after the transfer of funds identified to be used by 
32 the Maine State Housing Authority for deposit in the Maine Energy, Housing and 
33 Economic Recovery Fund to meet the authority's obligations relating to bonds issued or 
34 planned to be issued by the authority for the Maine Energy, Housing and Economic 
35 Recovery Program.  For fiscal years 2025-26 to 2030-31, as long as the revenue to the 
36 Maine State Housing Authority does not fall below the level of available revenues of fiscal 
37 year 2021-22, 50% of the remaining funds received in the Housing Opportunities for Maine 
38 Fund must be used to support the creation of new housing units that are affordable to low-
39 income households.1
