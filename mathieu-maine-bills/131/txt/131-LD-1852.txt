7SUMMARY
8 Under current law, the rental of living quarters in tourist camps and trailer camps is 
9 subject to a sales tax of 9%.  This bill removes living quarters in tourist camps and trailer 
10 camps from inclusion under the 9% sales tax rate, which will result in the rental of living 
11 quarters in trailer camps and tourist camps being subject to the general sales tax rate, which 
12 is currently 5.5%.8
