Page 1 - 131LR2450(01)1Sec. 1.  Office of Tax Policy to study State's mining excise tax; report.  
2Resolved:  That the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of 
3 Revenue Services, Office of Tax Policy shall examine and evaluate the State's taxation of 
4 metallic mineral mining business activity, including the mining excise tax, and shall review 
5 mining taxes and fees from other states, including severance taxes, excise taxes, extraction 
6 fees and royalties and corresponding potential uses of collected revenue, and consult with 
7 national mining tax experts as appropriate.  No later than January 15, 2024, the Office of 
8 Tax Policy shall submit a report to the Joint Standing Committee on Taxation that includes 
9 its findings and recommendations, including suggested legislation, that are consistent with 
10 the policy and purposes in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 36, section 2853.
11SUMMARY
12 This resolve directs the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau 
13 of Revenue Services, Office of Tax Policy to examine and evaluate the State's taxation of 
14 metallic mineral mining business activity and to report to the Joint Standing Committee on 
15 Taxation with its findings and recommendations.12
