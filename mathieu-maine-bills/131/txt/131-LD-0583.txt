26Sec. 2.  17 MRSA §3210 is enacted to read:
27§3210.  Local option; grocery stores
28 A municipality may by ordinance allow grocery stores with no more than 10,000 square 
29 feet of interior customer selling space to be open on Easter Day, Thanksgiving Day and 
30 Christmas Day. For purposes of this section, "grocery store" has the same meaning as in 
31 Title 22, section 2491, subsection 7-B.
