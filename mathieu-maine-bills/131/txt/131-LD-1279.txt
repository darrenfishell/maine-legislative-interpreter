33SUMMARY
34 This bill amends the provisions of law allowing a court to appoint a guardian for a 
35 minor, amends the definitions of jeopardy and serious harm under the Child and Family 
36 Services and Child Protection Act and establishes a presumption of jeopardy in a jeopardy 
37 proceeding for circumstances in which the parent of a child or a person in contact with the 
38 child has been convicted or adjudicated of a crime of violence of any classification and 
39 there is no documented record of successful completion of a certified domestic violence 
40 intervention program.34
