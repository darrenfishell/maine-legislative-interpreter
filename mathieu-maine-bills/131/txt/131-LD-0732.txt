11SUMMARY
12 This bill prohibits a person from operating a snowmobile off a snowmobile trail in an 
13 area that is posted as being closed to off-trail snowmobile operation.  It provides for a fine 
14 of not less than $100 and not more than $500 for the first violation of the prohibition and 
15 that a violation after 3 or more civil violations of the inland fisheries and wildlife laws is a 
16 Class E crime.12
