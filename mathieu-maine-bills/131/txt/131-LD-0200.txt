24SUMMARY
25 This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to provide Medicaid 
26 coverage to a child under 7 years of age, regardless of the child's family's nonfarm income 
27 official poverty line, if the child is not covered by an employer-sponsored or private health 
28 plan.  It directs the department to seek any waivers and make amendments to the state 
29 Medicaid plan as needed, but does not make coverage contingent on federal approval.25
