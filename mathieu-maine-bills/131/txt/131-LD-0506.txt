18SUMMARY
19 This bill increases the number of family law magistrates to 24 by August 1, 2025 and 
20 increases related staff.  It authorizes family law magistrates to hear and dispose of all 
21 pretrial and interim elements of child protection proceedings, except for final prehearing 
22 conferences.19
