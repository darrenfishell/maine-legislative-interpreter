19Sec. 3.  29-A MRSA §2075, sub-§3, ¶G is enacted to read:
20 G.  Create a bicycle and pedestrian zone without conducting a speed study in a 
21 downtown, as defined in Title 30-A, section 4301, subsection 5-A, where the posted 
22 speed limit is 35 miles per hour or less and where not more than 6,000 motor vehicles 
23 per day pass through the area and set the speed limit in that zone at 25 miles per hour. 
24 In order to determine whether or not to create a bicycle and pedestrian zone, the 
25 municipality shall consult with a law enforcement agency regarding the number of 
26 speeding violations, complaints about speeding motor vehicles, accidents and other 
27 hazards present in the proposed bicycle and pedestrian zone.
28 A municipality may deploy traffic calming measures in the bicycle and pedestrian 
29 zone.
30SUMMARY
31 This bill would allow municipalities to create bicycle and pedestrian zones in which 
32 the speed limit is 25 miles per hour.31
