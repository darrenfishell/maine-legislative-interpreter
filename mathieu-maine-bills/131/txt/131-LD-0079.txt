534-B.  State agency client.  "State agency client" means a child with a disability who 
6 is 3 years of age or older and under 22 years of age who requires an individualized 
7 education program in order to access a free, appropriate public education and who is:
8 A.  In the care or custody, or both, of the Department of Health and Human Services 
9 and whose placement, either with a person who is not the child's parent, legal guardian 
10 or relative or in a residential setting, is facilitated by a caseworker from the Department 
11 of Health and Human Services and funded, in whole or in part, through the MaineCare 
12 program or the Department of Health and Human Services, and that placement is for 
13 reasons other than educational reasons; or
14 B.  In the custody or under the supervision of the Department of Corrections, including, 
15 but not limited to, a juvenile on conditional release, an informally adjusted juvenile, a 
16 probationer or a juvenile on community reintegration status from the Long Creek 
17 Youth Development Center and who is placed, for reasons other than educational 
18 reasons, pursuant to a court order or with the agreement of an authorized agent of the 
19 Department of Corrections, outside the juvenile's home.
20 Notwithstanding paragraphs A and B, "state agency client" also means a child who is under 
21 3 years of age and has a diagnosed, established condition or a biological factor that has a 
22 high probability of resulting in developmental delay.
