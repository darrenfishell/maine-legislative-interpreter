24Sec. 11.  Public Transit Advisory Council to set goals; report.  The Public 
25 Transit Advisory Council, referred to in this section as "the council," shall identify 
26 measurable goals for expansion, efficiency, affordability and environmental impact in each 
27 geographic region under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 23, section 4209.  The goals 
28 must include strategies to achieve the goals and methods to measure progress in achieving 
29 the goals.  The council shall identify funding needs to achieve the goals and propose 
30 funding sources.  No later than December 30, 2023, the council shall submit a report 
31 containing the information in this section to the Department of Transportation.  No later 
32 than January 30, 2024, the department shall submit a report to the Joint Standing Committee 
33 on Transportation with the report from the council and the department's plans based on the 
34 report.  The department may engage with relevant stakeholders in developing its report, 
35 including large employers in the State.  The committee may report out legislation relating 
36 to the report to the Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.

21SUMMARY
22 This bill:
29 1. Requires the Public Transit Advisory Council to meet 4 times per year;
30 2. Directs the Public Transit Advisory Council to identify measurable goals for 
31 expansion, efficiency, affordability and environmental impact in each geographic region 
32 and to report that information to the Department of Transportation.  It requires the 
33 department to submit a report to the Joint Standing Committee on Transportation on its 
34 planning activities related to the council's report;
35 3. Amends the transportation policy in the Sensible Transportation Policy Act to 
36 include facilitating and supporting public transportation systems in the State and reducing 
37 greenhouse gas emissions;
38 4.  Amends the membership of the Public Transit Advisory Council;
39 5.  Authorizes the Public Transit Advisory Council to establish a steering committee 
40 with the Commissioner of Transportation to direct the work of the council;1
