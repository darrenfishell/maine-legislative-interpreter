2Sec. 1.  35-A MRSA §307, as amended by PL 1999, c. 398, Pt. A, §13 and affected 
3 by §§104 and 105, is further amended by enacting after the first paragraph a new paragraph 
4 to read:
5 A public utility seeking an increase in rates greater than 1% shall send a notice of the 
6 increase to the public utility's customers by either first-class mail or the method by which 
7 the customer receives bills from the public utility.  The commission shall prepare the notice 
8 for an increase in rates greater than 1% and may not approve an increase for the public 
9 utility greater than the increase described in the notice.
10SUMMARY
11 This bill requires a public utility seeking an increase in rates greater than 1% to send a 
12 notice of the increase to the utility's customers and prescribes details for that notice. The 
13 bill also prohibits the Public Utilities Commission from approving an increase in rates 
14 greater than the increase described in the notice sent to customers.11
