20 §10056. Division of Public Information and Education 
21 The Division of Public Information and Education is established within the 
22 Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife and is responsible for the administration of 
23 programs to increase the public's knowledge and understanding of inland fisheries and 
24 wildlife resources and the management of these resources, including the adminish·ation of

26 watercraft and all-terrain vehicles. The division's responsibilities include public 
27 education, promotion of inland fisheries and wildlife resources and the dissemination of

COMMITTEE AMENDMENT 
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A "to H.P. 103, L.D. 121 
1 SUMMARY 
2 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. The amendment requires the 
3 Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife, Division of Public Information and 
4 Education to annually undertake campaigns to promote safety in the handling and use of 
5 firearms, watercraft, all-terrain vehicles and snowmobiles. The division is also directed 
6 to promote safety with respect to other outdoor activities to the extent the division 
7 determines such campaigns would help improve safety. The campaigns must be designed 
8 to reach the target audiences effectively through television, radio, Internet or other 
9 communication mediums.
