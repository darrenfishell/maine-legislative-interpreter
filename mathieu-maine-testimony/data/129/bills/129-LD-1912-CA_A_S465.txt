9COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “      ” to S.P. 659, L.D. 1912, Bill, “An Act To 
10Conform the Maine Apprenticeship Program to the Federal Equal Employment 
11Opportunity Act of 1972”
12Amend the bill by striking out all of section 2 and inserting the following:
13'Sec. 2.  26 MRSA §3205, sub-§10, as enacted by PL 2011, c. 491, §13, is 
14amended to read:
1510.  Equal opportunity.  A statement that the apprentice will be accorded equal 
16opportunity in all phases of apprenticeship employment and training, without 
17discrimination because of race, color, religion, national origin or gender, sex, sexual 
18orientation, gender identity, genetic information or disability or age; and' 
19Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
20section number to read consecutively.
21SUMMARY
22This amendment requires that the statement regarding equal opportunity required in 
23an apprenticeship agreement specify that an apprentice cannot be discriminated against 
24because of gender identity. It also specifies an apprentice cannot be discriminated against 
25because of age but does not specify, like the bill, that age discrimination is only 
26applicable if the apprentice is 40 years of age or older.Date: (Filing No. S-          )
