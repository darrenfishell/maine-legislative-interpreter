25SUMMARY
26This resolve directs the Commissioner of Professional and Financial Regulation to 
27create a working group to study credentialing skilled individuals with foreign credentials 
28and those with licenses in other states. The working group consists of 8 to 10 persons 
29representing licensing boards and professional organizations served by the licensing 
30boards in professions in the State for which there is a need for and an opportunity to 
31credential skilled individuals with foreign credentials and those with out-of-state licenses. 
32The working group is required to submit its report along with any recommendations and 
33suggested legislation to the Commissioner of Professional and Financial Regulation. The 
34commissioner is required to submit the report with any recommendations and suggested 
35legislation to the Joint Standing Committee on Innovation, Development, Economic 
36Advancement and Business. The committee is permitted to report out a bill relating to the
