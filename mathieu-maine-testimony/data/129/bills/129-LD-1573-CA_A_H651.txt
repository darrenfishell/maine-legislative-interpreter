18 SUMMARY 
19 The bill requires juvenile case records to be kept confidential and only disclosed, 
20 disseminated, inspected or obtained by certain parties or certain agencies or by court 
21 order. This amendment clarifies the disclosure, dissemination and inspection of juvenile 
22 case records. 
23 The amendment provides that when juvenile case records are inspected by or 
24 disseminated to anyone other than parties to the juvenile's case or victims, the court may 
25 impose reasonable limitations to protect the identity and safety of 3rd parties, including, 
26 but not limited to, victims and other accused or adjudicated juveniles, and the interests of 
27 justice. 
28 The amendment provides that the dissemination of juvenile intelligence and 
29 investigative record information is subject to limitations. It prohibits the further 
30 dissemination of juvenile intelligence and investigative record information unless such 
31 further dissemination is authorized. 
32 The amendment prohibits a criminal justice agency from confirming the existence or 
33 nonexistence of juvenile intelligence and investigative record information that is 
34 confidential.
