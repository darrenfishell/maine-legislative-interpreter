14Sec. 2.  Maine Charter School Commission membership; staggered terms.  
15A member of the Maine Charter School Commission serving on the effective date of this 
16Act who is a member of the State Board of Education may continue to serve until that 
17member's term has expired.  The terms of members of the Maine Charter School 
18Commission serving on the effective date of this Act who are not members of the State 
19Board of Education end on the effective date of this Act.  Notwithstanding the Maine 
20Revised Statutes, Title 20-A, section 2405, subsection 8, paragraph A, of the initial 
21gubernatorial appointments to the Maine Charter School Commission, 2 must be for 
22terms of 4 years and 2 must be for terms of 2 years. 
23SUMMARY
24This bill changes the appointing authority for 4 members of the Maine Charter School 
25Commission from the State Board of Education to the Governor and increases the terms 
26of those members from 3 years to 4 years.
