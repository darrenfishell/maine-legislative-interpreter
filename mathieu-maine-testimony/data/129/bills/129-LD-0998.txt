9SUMMARY
10This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services, Maine Center for 
11Disease Control and Prevention through its division of data, research and vital statistics to 
12report by December 4, 2019 to the Joint Standing Committee on Judiciary on data on the 
13incidence of marriage of minors in the State.  The joint standing committee is authorized 
14to report out a bill based on the report to the Second Regular Session of the 129th 
15Legislature.
