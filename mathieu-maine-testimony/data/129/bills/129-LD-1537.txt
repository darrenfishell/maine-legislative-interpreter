2Sec. 1.  5 MRSA §17806, sub-§1, ¶A, as amended by PL 2015, c. 334, §1, is 
3further amended to read:
4A.  Except as provided in paragraphs A-1 and A-2, whenever there is a percentage 
5increase in the Consumer Price Index from July 1st to June 30th, the board shall 
6automatically make an equal percentage increase in retirement benefits, beginning in 
7September, up to a maximum annual increase of 3%.  Effective July 1, 2011 to June 
830, 2020, the increase applies to that portion of the retirement benefit up to $20,000, 
9which amount must be indexed in subsequent years by the same percentage 
10adjustments granted under this paragraph and paragraph A-2.  Effective July 1, 2020, 
11the increase applies to that portion of the retirement benefit up to $30,000, which 
12amount must be indexed in subsequent years by the same percentage adjustments 
13granted under this paragraph and paragraph A-2.
14SUMMARY
15This bill increases the portion of retirement benefits of retired state employees, 
16teachers and beneficiaries of either to which the cost-of-living adjustment applies from 
17$20,000 to $30,000 effective July 1, 2020.
