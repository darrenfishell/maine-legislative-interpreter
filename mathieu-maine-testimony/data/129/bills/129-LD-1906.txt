3SUMMARY
4This bill changes the composition of the Shellfish Advisory Council by:
51.  Eliminating the requirement that 3 of the 4 members who are commercial shellfish 
6license holders be soft-shell clam harvesters;
72.  Expanding the representation from a member representing the interests of 
8municipalities with wastewater treatment systems to a member who is a municipal 
9official involved in pollution permitting or mitigation;
103.  Changing the requirement that 2 members be municipal shellfish wardens to 
11instead provide that 2 members must be municipal shellfish officials, including, but not 
12limited to, a municipal shellfish conservation warden or a member of a municipal 
13shellfish management committee;
144.  Allowing a person who is designated by the Department of Marine Resources as 
15an authorized representative of the holder of a shellfish depuration certificate to be a 
16member; and
175.  Adding a nonvoting member who has a demonstrated knowledge of biological 
18science and holds at least a bachelor's degree.
