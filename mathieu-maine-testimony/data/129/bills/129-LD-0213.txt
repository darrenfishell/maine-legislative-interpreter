15SUMMARY
16This bill requires automobiles to be equipped with snow tires or all-weather tires 
17from the 2nd day of October to the last day of April and requires the Chief of the State 
18Police within the Department of Public Safety to adopt rules to define "snow tires" and 
19"all-weather tires."
