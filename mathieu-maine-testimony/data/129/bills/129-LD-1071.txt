11SUMMARY
12This bill makes sale of a high-capacity magazine a Class D crime.  A high-capacity 
13magazine is a firearm magazine or other device that is used to load ammunition into the 
14chamber of a firearm and that has the capacity to contain or accept more than 10 rounds 
15of ammunition.
