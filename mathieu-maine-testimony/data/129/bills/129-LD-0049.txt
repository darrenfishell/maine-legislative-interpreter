61.  Deaf or hard-of-hearing.  The Secretary of State shall issue, at the request of a 
7person who is deaf or hard-of-hearing, a sticker to that person to place in a location 
8designated by the Secretary of State on the back of the person's driver's license to indicate 
9that the person is deaf or hard-of-hearing. The Secretary of State may require appropriate 
10documentation that a person is deaf or hard-of-hearing before issuing the sticker. For 
11purposes of this subsection, "deaf person" and "hard-of-hearing person" have the same 
12meanings as in section 1358, subsection 1.
132.  Traumatic brain injury.  The Secretary of State shall issue, at the request of a 
14person who has a traumatic brain injury, a sticker to that person to place in a location 
15designated by the Secretary of State on the back of the person's driver's license or 
16nondriver identification card to indicate that the person has a traumatic brain injury. The 
17Secretary of State may require appropriate documentation that a person has a traumatic 
18brain injury before issuing the sticker.
19SUMMARY
20This bill requires the Secretary of State, upon request, to issue a sticker to be placed 
21on a person's driver's license or nondriver identification card to indicate that the person 
22has a traumatic brain injury.
