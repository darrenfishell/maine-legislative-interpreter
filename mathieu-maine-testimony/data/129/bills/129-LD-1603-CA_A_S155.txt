12 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
13 'Resolve, To Coordinate a Plan To Expand Cellular Telephone Service' 
14 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and inserting the following: 
15 'Sec. 1. Plan to expand cellular telephone service. Resolved: That the 
16 Department of Economic and Community Development, in coordination with the 
17 ConnectME Authority established in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 
18 12004-G, subsection 33-F, the Department of Administrative and Financial Services and 
19 the University of Maine System, Networkmaine, shall develop a plan to leverage state 
20 assets to expand cellular telephone service to underserved areas of the State. The plan 
21 may be developed in connection with any plans to expand broadband service prepared by 
22 the ConnectME Authority pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 35-A, chapter 93. 
23 Sec. 2. Report; authority for legislation. Resolved: That, no later than 
24 February 1, 2020, the Department of Economic and Community Development shall 
25 submit a report on the plan developed under section 1 to the Joint Standing Committee on 
26 Energy, Utilities and Technology. The committee may report out a bill to the Second 
27 Regular Session of the 129th Legislature related to the report.' 
28 SUMMARY 
29 This amendment replaces the bill with a resolve. The amendment requires the 
30 Department of Economic and Community Development, in coordination with the 
31 ConnectME Authority, the Department of Administrative and Financial Services and the 
32 University of Maine System, Networkmaine, to develop a plan to leverage state assets to 
33 expand cellular telephone service to underserved areas of the State. It requires the 
34 Department of Economic and Community Development to submit a report on the plan to 
35 the Joint Standing Committee on Energy, Utilities and Technology and authorizes the
