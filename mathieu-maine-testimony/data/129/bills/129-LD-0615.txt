28program or the federal State Children's Health Insurance Program if the department fails 
29to comply with this section.
30SUMMARY
31This bill provides that, without authorization from the Legislature by proper 
32enactment of a law, the Department of Health and Human Services may not implement 
33changes in the Medicaid program or the federal State Children's Health Insurance 
34Program that significantly change enrollment in the program, the category or scope of 
35covered services or the funding mechanisms for the program, unless required by federal 
36law.
