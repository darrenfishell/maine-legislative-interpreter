23SUMMARY
24This bill allows a laboratory operated by a licensed waste discharge facility to 
25analyze waste discharges for enterococcus without being certified by the Department of 
26Health and Human Services, Maine Center for Disease Control and Prevention for the use 
27of programs of the department or the Department of Environmental Protection.
