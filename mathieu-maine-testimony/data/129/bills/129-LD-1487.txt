2Sec. 1.  29-A MRSA §501, as amended by PL 2015, c. 206, §4 and c. 267, Pt. 
3BBBB, §1, is further amended by adding at the end a new paragraph to read:
4A person possessing or applying for a registration certificate and a set of gold star 
5family registration plates pursuant to section 524-B is exempt from registration fees under 
6this section for the motor vehicle registered or to be registered.
7Sec. 2.  29-A MRSA §524-B, sub-§1, as amended by PL 2015, c. 17, §1 and 
8affected by §3, is further amended to read:
91.  Eligibility.  Notwithstanding the requirements in section 468-A, the Secretary of 
10State, upon application and upon evidence of payment of the excise tax required by Title 
1136, section 1482 and the annual motor vehicle registration fee required by section 501, 
12shall issue a registration certificate and a set of gold star family registration plates, to be 
13used in lieu of regular registration plates, to:
14A.  A person who is eligible to receive a gold star lapel button under 10 United States 
15Code, Section 1126 (2010); 
16B.  A grandparent of a member of the United States Armed Forces, if that member 
17dies after March 28, 1973 as a result of:
18(1)  An international terrorist attack against the United States or a foreign nation 
19friendly to the United States, recognized as such an attack by the United States 
20Secretary of Defense; or
21(2)  Military operations while serving outside the United States, including the 
22commonwealths, territories and possessions of the United States, as a part of a 
23peacekeeping force;
24C.  A person who is not eligible to receive a gold star lapel button under 10 United 
25States Code, Section 1126 (2010) but who is eligible for a lapel button for next of kin 
26of deceased personnel under 32 Code of Federal Regulations, Section 578.63 (2006); 
27and
28D.  A grandparent of a member of the United States Armed Forces, if that member 
29dies while serving on active duty or while assigned in the reserve components of the 
30United States Armed Forces or Army National Guard unit in a drill status.
31SUMMARY
32This bill exempts a person possessing or applying for a registration certificate and a 
33set of gold star family registration plates from the annual motor vehicle registration fee.
