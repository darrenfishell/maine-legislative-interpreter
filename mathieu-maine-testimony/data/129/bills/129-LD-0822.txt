13SUMMARY
14This bill prohibits a distributor, blender or retail dealer from selling, consigning or 
15distributing motor fuel containing more than 10% ethanol by volume unless the motor 
16fuel is sold, consigned or distributed in a sealed container that contains one quart or less 
17of the motor fuel.  It also repeals a contingent prohibition on the sale of motor fuel 
18containing more than 10% ethanol by volume.
