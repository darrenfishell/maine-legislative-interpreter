11SUMMARY
12This bill increases the penalty for overtaking a school bus when the bus has stopped 
13with its red lights flashing from a $250 minimum fine to a $500 minimum fine for the 
14first offense and increases the driver's license suspension from 30 days to 60 days for a 
152nd offense.
