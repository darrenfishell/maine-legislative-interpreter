34SUMMARY
35This bill makes a retired employee of an academy approved for tuition purposes in 
36accordance with the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20-A, sections 2951 to 2955 eligible 
37for coverage under the group health plan available to state employees and other eligible 
38persons.
