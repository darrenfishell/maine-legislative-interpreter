24SUMMARY
25This bill changes the contractual commitment to maintain state-protected benefits 
26regarding cost-of-living adjustments for retired state employees and teachers by 
27specifying that it constitutes a solemn contractual commitment of the State that is 
28protected under the Constitution of Maine and the United States Constitution.
