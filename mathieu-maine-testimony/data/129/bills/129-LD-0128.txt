2Sec. 1.  Restored operation of Downeast Correctional Facility.  
3Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the Department of Corrections 
4shall reopen the Downeast Correctional Facility. The facility must remain open and 
5operational, except that the facility may be closed if legislation approving the closure is 
6enacted into law.
7Sec. 2.  Appropriations and allocations.  The following appropriations and 
8allocations are made.
9CORRECTIONS, DEPARTMENT OF 
10Downeast Correctional Facility 0542
11Initiative: Provides funds to restore all positions and for related All Other costs to reopen 
12and operate the Downeast Correctional Facility beginning October 1, 2019.

30SUMMARY
31This bill requires the Department of Corrections to reopen the Downeast Correctional 
32Facility.  The facility must remain open and operational, and the bill permits the closure 
33of the facility only if legislation approving the closure is enacted into law.
