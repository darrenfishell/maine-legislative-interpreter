2Sec. 1.  22 MRSA §1542, sub-§2, ¶O is enacted to read:
3O.  Smoking tobacco is not prohibited in a designated outdoor eating area under 
4section 1550, subsection 4.
5Sec. 2.  22 MRSA §1550, sub-§2, as enacted by PL 2009, c. 140, §2, is amended 
6to read:
72.  Smoking prohibited.  Smoking Except for a designated outdoor eating area under 
8subsection 4, smoking is prohibited in an outdoor eating area if the outdoor eating area or 
9any portion thereof is open and available for dining and beverage service.
10Sec. 3.  22 MRSA §1550, sub-§4 is enacted to read:
114.  Smoking permitted.  An eating establishment may permit smoking tobacco in a 
12designated outdoor eating area if no one under 21 years of age is permitted in the 
13designated outdoor eating area.
14SUMMARY
15This bill allows an eating establishment to permit smoking tobacco in a designated 
16outdoor eating area if no one under 21 years of age is allowed in the designated outdoor 
17eating area.
