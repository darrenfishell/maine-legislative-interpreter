9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" A "to S.P. 719, L.D. 2029, Bill, "An Act To Make 
10 March Maine Childhood Cancer Awareness Month" 
11 Amend the bill by inserting after the title and before the enacting clause the 
12 following: 
13 'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
14 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and 
15 Whereas, Maine has one of the highest rates of childhood cancer in the nation; and 
16 Whereas, federal funding for childhood cancer research is historically less than 5% 
17 of the total amount allocated for cancer research; and 
18 Whereas, Public Law 2019, chapter 433, ,iAn Act To Advance Children's Cancer 
19 Research in Maine,11 added an option on Maine's tax return form allowing a Maine 
20 taxpayer entitled to a tax refund to designate a portion of that refund to be paid into the 
21 Maine Children's Cancer Research Fund; and 
22 Whereas, many Maine taxpayers will file tax forms before the expiration of the 90-
23 day period; and 
24 Whereas, if this Act becomes effective before March 1st, the Governor will issue a 
25 proclamation in March that will increase the public's awareness of childhood cancer and 
26 may elicit contributions by taxpayers to the Maine Children's Cancer Research Fund; and 
27 Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
28 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
29 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
30 therefore,' 
31 Amend the bill by adding before the summary the following: 
32 'Emergency clause. In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
33 legislation takes effect when approved.'
