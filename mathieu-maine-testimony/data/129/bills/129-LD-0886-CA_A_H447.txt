SUMMARY 
This amendment makes the bill consistent with the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 26, 
section 809, concerning absence for emergency response. It adds a definition for 
"recognized organization" and changes terminology from "volunteer search and rescue" 
to "search and rescue volunteer."
