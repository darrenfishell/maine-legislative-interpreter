Page 2 - 129LR2311(01)-11SUMMARY
2This bill requires the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of 
3Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations to set off lottery winnings against monetary 
4fines, surcharges and assessments imposed by a court of the State.  The bill also 
5establishes an order of priority the bureau must follow if there is more than one claim to 
6the lottery winnings pursuant to state law or court order.
