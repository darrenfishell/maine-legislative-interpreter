12SUMMARY
13This bill exempts children who are enrolled in or attend a virtual public charter 
14school from the requirement that a child may not be enrolled in or attend school without 
15evidence of immunization.  The bill also amends the law governing the Universal 
16Childhood Immunization Program to include persons who are 18 years of age or younger 
17and are enrolled in or have been offered enrollment in a private or public postsecondary 
18educational institution in the State.
19The bill also corrects an error in Public Law 2019, chapter 154 by providing that the 
20exemption from immunization requirements based on religious or philosophical beliefs 
21for employees of health care facilities takes effect September 1, 2021.  The correction is 
22contingent on Public Law 2019, chapter 154 being ratified by a majority of voters and 
23taking effect in accordance with the Constitution of Maine, Article IV, Part Third, Section 
2417.
