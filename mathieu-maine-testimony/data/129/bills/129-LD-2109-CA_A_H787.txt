12SUMMARY
13This amendment replaces the bill and makes it a resolve.  It implements a 
14recommendation of the Commission To Study Long-term Care Workforce Issues to 
15increase reimbursement rates to direct care workers.  The amendment requires the 
16Department of Health and Human Services to increase reimbursement rates to all direct 
17care worker services that are provided in home and community-based settings and in 
18institutional settings by no less than $2 an hour.  The amendment adds an emergency 
19preamble and emergency clause.  The amendment also adds an appropriations and 
20allocations section.
