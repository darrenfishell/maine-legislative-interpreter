18 SUMMARY 
19 This amendment replaces the bill, which is a concept draft, and changes the title. It 
20 allows the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to accept monetary donations 
21 to support hunting opportunities for disabled veterans. It requires the commissioner to 
22 transfer those donations to the Department of Defense, Veterans and Emergency 
23 Management, Bureau of Maine Veterans' Services to be deposited into the Hunting 
24 Opportunities for Disabled Veterans Fund, a nonlapsing fund to be used for the purpose 
25 of making funds available to organizations that provide disabled veterans with hunting 
26 opportunities. The fund may also receive any other monetary gifts, donations or other 
27 contributions from public or private sources. The amendment also adds an appropriations 
28 and allocations section.
