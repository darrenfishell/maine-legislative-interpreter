1 SUMMARY 
2 This amendment replaces the bill which was a concept draft. It creates the Shellfish 
3 Research Fund as a nonlapsing fund administered by the Commissioner of Marine 
4 Resources for the purpose of funding shellfish research projects in the State. It provides 
5 ongoing funding of $50,000 per fiscal year from the General Fund. It also allows the 
6 commissioner to accept and deposit into the fund any monetary gifts, donations or other 
7 contributions from public or private sources. It requires that the commissioner consult 
8 with the Shellfish Advisory Council before deciding upon research projects and awarding 
9 grants from the fund. 
10 It also increases the membership of the Shellfish Advisory Council from 13 to 14 
11 members. It directs the commissioner to appoint as a member of the Shellfish Adviso1y 
12 Council a person who has a demonstrated knowledge of a marine science and, at 
13 minimum, a bachelor's degree in a field of marine science.
