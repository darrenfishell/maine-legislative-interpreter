4SUMMARY
5This bill amends the law governing name changes to allow a probate judge to limit 
6the notice requirement for anyone who shows by a preponderance of the evidence that the 
7person seeking the name change is in reasonable fear of the person's safety, not just for 
8domestic abuse victims.
