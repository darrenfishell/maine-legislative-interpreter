24 SUMMARY 
25 This amendment adds a definition of "eligible person"; modifies the definition of 
26 "account holder"; adds language allowing the University of Maine System to solicit 
27 proposals from community development organizations on a schedule established by the 
28 system; allows, instead of requires, the system to adopt rules; allows, instead of requires, 
29 enforcement of the penalty for unauthorized withdrawals; changes the makeup of the 
30 Advisory Committee on Family Development Accounts from 12 members to 10 members 
31 and changes the descriptions of members; and adds a section describing the transition of 
32 the program from the Finance Authority of Maine to the system.
