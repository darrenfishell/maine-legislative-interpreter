8SUMMARY
9This bill allows for municipalities to conduct early voting in person during the same 
10period as is permitted for absentee voting.  Early voting takes place in the municipal 
11offices with the same requirements and restrictions regarding voting places, voting 
12booths, ballot boxes and ballot challenges as apply to regular voting.  At the end of early 
13voting, the municipal clerk delivers the sealed ballot boxes to the voting place as 
14presently required by law.
