Page 1 - 129LR2425(01)-11Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
2become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
3Whereas, this legislation proposes a referendum to ratify changing the name of the 
4unorganized territory referred to currently as Township 17, Range 3, WELS, and located 
5in Aroostook County to Van Buren Cove, and requires the referendum to be held no later 
6than 6 months after adjournment of the First Regular Session of the 129th Legislature; 
7and
8Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
9the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
10immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
11therefore,
12Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
13Sec. 1.  Van Buren Cove.  Wherever the designation Township 17, Range 3, 
14WELS, appears or reference is made to that unorganized territory, that designation or 
15reference means Van Buren Cove.
16Sec. 2.  Referendum for ratification.  This Act must be submitted to the legal 
17voters residing in the unorganized territory of Township 17, Range 3, WELS, in 
18Aroostook County. The date of the submission must be determined by the Aroostook 
19County Board of Commissioners but may not be later than 6 months after adjournment of 
20the First Regular Session of the 129th Legislature. The Aroostook County commissioners 
21are authorized to expend such funds as necessary to implement the referendum.
22The county commissioners shall cause the preparation of the required ballots, on 
23which they shall state the subject matter of this Act in the following question:
24"Do you favor changing the name of Township 17, Range 3, WELS, to 
25Van Buren Cove?"
26The voters shall indicate by a cross or a check mark placed against the word "Yes" or 
27"No" their opinion of the question.
28The result of the election must be declared by the Aroostook County commissioners 
29and due certificate filed with the Secretary of State.
30Sec. 3.  Emergency clause; effective date.  In view of the emergency cited in the 
31preamble, this legislation takes effect when approved only for the purpose of submitting it 
32to the legal voters residing in the unorganized territory of Township 17, Range 3, WELS, 
33in Aroostook County no later than 6 months after adjournment of the First Regular 
34Session of the 129th Legislature.  The name change in section 1 of this Act takes effect 
35immediately upon its acceptance by a majority of the legal voters voting at the election 
36held pursuant to section 2.
