SUMMARY

state-municipal revenue sharing percentage is 2.5% for fiscal year 2019-20 and 3% for 
fiscal year 2020-21 and returns to 5% in fiscal year 2021-22.

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This amendment reduces the revenue sharing percentage to 2.5% in fiscal year 2019-20 and 3% in fiscal year 2020-
21, returning to 5% in fiscal year 2021-22. It would result in an increase in General Fund revenue of $85,218,902 in 
fiscal year 2019-20 and $74,524,016 in fiscal year 2020-21, with a corresponding decrease in Local Government Fund 
revenue.
