9SUMMARY
10This bill directs the Commission on Governmental Ethics and Election Practices to 
11conduct random audits of political action committees that are required to file campaign 
12finance reports with the commission and candidates for state office, including candidates 
13for Governor, State Senator, State Representative and presidential elector.  The bill 
14directs the commission to adopt rules to implement this requirement.  The rules must 
15direct the commission to audit an equal percentage of candidates for state office who are 
16certified as Maine Clean Election Act candidates, candidates for state office who are not 
17certified as Maine Clean Election Act candidates and political action committees.  The 
18rules must also establish standard auditing requirements to be applied to each candidate 
19and political action committee.
