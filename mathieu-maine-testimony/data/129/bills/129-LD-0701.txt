30SUMMARY
31Current law requires the Department of Education to make information available to 
32public schools regarding Internet-based applications for free or reduced-price meals under 
33the National School Lunch Program.  This bill requires the department to develop and 
34make available to public schools a specific Internet-based application for the National 
35School Lunch Program as well as for the School Breakfast Program.  A public school that 
36implements the Internet-based application process is required to continue to distribute 
37paper applications for school meals to all students.
