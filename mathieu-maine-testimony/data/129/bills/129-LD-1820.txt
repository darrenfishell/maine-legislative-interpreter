34SUMMARY
35This bill amends the laws governing investigations by school entities, which includes 
36approved private schools, school administrative units, public charter schools, school 
37management and leadership centers, schools in the unorganized territory and schools 
38operated by the State, of credential holders.  This bill:
391.  Removes language requiring a school entity to notify the Department of Education 
40within 15 business days of the initiation of a covered investigation by that school entity;
Page 3 - 129LR2508(01)-112.  Requires a school entity to immediately notify the Department of Education if a 
2credential holder who is the subject of a covered investigation leaves the school entity's 
3employment for any reason prior to the conclusion of the covered investigation;
43.  Eliminates the requirement that the school entity notify the department in writing 
5of the final outcome of the investigation within 5 days of its completion and provide to 
6the department a copy of any final report and instead requires that the school entity 
7provide to the department a copy of any final report produced in support of the school 
8entity's decision to discipline, suspend or terminate the credential holder;
94.  If a credential holder left employment prior to the completion of a covered 
10investigation and provides consent as part of that credential holder's application for 
11employment with a school entity, directs the department to notify the superintendent or 
12the chief administrative officer of that school entity of the fact that the credential holder 
13left employment with a school entity prior to the completion of a covered investigation of 
14that credential holder; and
155.  Repeals language requiring the commissioner to adopt rules governing 
16confidentiality of information received under the provisions concerning covered 
17investigations.
