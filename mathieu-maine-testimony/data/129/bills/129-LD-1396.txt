20SUMMARY
21This bill makes administrative updates to the laws governing regional library 
22systems.  It promotes cooperation between library systems by replacing library districts 
23with library regions, consolidating the district councils by establishing the Maine Library 
24Advisory Council to serve as an advisory body to the library regions and directing staff of 
25the Maine State Library holding the position of consultant or specialist to provide 
26services to library regions as appointed by the State Librarian.  The bill also revises the 
27membership and terms of the Maine Library Commission.
