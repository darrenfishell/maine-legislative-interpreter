9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 215, L.D. 291, Bill, "An Act 
10 Regarding Responsibility for the Overpayment of Claims" 
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
12 'An Act Regarding Responsibility for the Duplicative or Incorrect Payment of 
13 Health Insurance Claims' 
14 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the 
15 following: 
16 'Sec. 1. 24-A MRSA ยง4303, sub-ยง23 is enacted to read: 
17 23. Duplicative or incorrect claims payments. If a carrier has made a duplicative 
18 or incorrect payment on a claim with respect to a health plan: 
19 A. If the claim payment was made to a provider. the carrier shall retroactively seek 
20 collection related to that payment directly from the provider: and 
21 B. The carrier may not attempt to retroactively seek collection related to the claim 
22 payment from an enrollee unless the enrollee was already paid directly for the 
23 services identified in the claim and a provider submits evidence to the carrier that the 
24 enrollee did not forward payment to the provider. After a provider has submitted 
25 evidence that the enrollee did not forward payment to the provider. a carrier may 
26 require an enrollee to provide evidence of payment to the provider.' 
27 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
28 section number to read consecutively. 
29 SUMMARY 
30 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. It clarifies that the provisions 
31 of the bill are intended to apply to duplicative or incorrect claims payments made with 
32 respect to health insurance coverage.
