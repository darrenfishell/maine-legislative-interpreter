Page 3 - 129LR1763(01)-11SUMMARY
2This bill makes it unlawful for a member of the clergy who is in a position of trust or 
3authority over another person to cause the other person to submit to or participate in a 
4sexual act, sexual contact or sexual touching by exploiting the person's emotional 
5dependency on the member of the clergy.  It also places the current definition of 
6"domestic partner" in the definition section of the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 17-A, 
7chapter 11 and deletes repetitive definitions of "domestic partner" found throughout the 
8chapter.
