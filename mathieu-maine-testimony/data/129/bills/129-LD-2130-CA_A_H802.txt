Fiscal Detail and Notes
This bill increases ATV registration fees for residents from $45 to $70, nonresident 7-day registration fees from $75 
to $100 and nonresident annual registration fees from $90 to $115 starting May 1, 2021. The additional $25 from each 
registration will be deposited in the ATV Recreational Management Fund (ARMF) within the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry (ACF).  Based on an  estimated 72,026 ATV registrations, it is anticipated that 
the fee increases will generate an additional $600,033 in  Other Special Revenue Funds revenue to the ARMF 
beginning in fiscal year 2020-21.  This bill also includes an accompanying allocation to ACF for the expenditure of 
the additional revenue. Fiscal Note129th MAINE LEGISLATURE
