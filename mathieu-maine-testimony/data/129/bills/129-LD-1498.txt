25§2354-F.  Eight-axle limits
261.  Definition.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, "B-
27train double" means a truck tractor-semitrailer-semitrailer combination vehicle in which 
28the 2 trailing units are connected with a B-train assembly.  The B-train assembly is a rigid
