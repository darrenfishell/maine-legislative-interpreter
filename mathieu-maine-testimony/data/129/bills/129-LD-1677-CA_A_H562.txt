SUMMARY 
This amendment requires a reentry house to meet all state and local building and life 
safety codes for the type of building in which the reentry house is located.

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill authorizes the Department of Corrections (DOC) to contract with private employers for reentry houses. The 
DOC has not requested additional funding at this time and has indicated it could request funding as it pursues 
contracts with private employers.
