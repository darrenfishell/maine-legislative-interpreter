16Sec. 3.  Secretary of State to request that United States Secretary of 
17Transportation place Maine in Atlantic Time Zone.  The Secretary of State shall 
18request that the United States Secretary of Transportation place the State in the Atlantic 
19Time Zone.
20Sec. 4.  Contingent effective date.  Sections 1 and 2 of this Act do not take effect 
21until the United States Secretary of Transportation places the State in the Atlantic Time 
22Zone pursuant to section 3.
23SUMMARY
24This bill creates an exemption to federal provisions regarding the observation of 
25so-called Eastern Daylight Saving Time in the State and requires the Secretary of State to 
26request that the United States Secretary of Transportation place the State in the Atlantic 
27Time Zone.
