8SUMMARY
9This bill clarifies the Maine Medical Use of Marijuana Act by:
101.  Standardizing the term "caregiver retail store" by creating a definition of the term 
11and replacing other variations of "retail store" in the Act with "caregiver retail store"; and 
122.  Defining "municipal approval" in the provisions regarding marijuana for medical 
13use caregiver retail stores, dispensaries and facilities operating before the effective date of 
14the Act as a specific examination and approval of the underlying use of the store, 
15dispensary or facility, including a conditional use approval, site plan approval or issuance 
16of a marijuana-specific business license and not including the issuance of a building, 
17electrical or other similar permit that does not address the use of the structure or facility 
18for which the permit was issued.
