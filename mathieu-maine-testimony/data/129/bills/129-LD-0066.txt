16Sec. 2.  22 MRSA §1716-A is enacted to read:
17§1716-A.  Communication about alternative payment options with hospitalized 
18patients
191.  Definition.  As used in this section, "alternative payment options" means a 
20hospital charity care or financial assistance program, a state or federal program of 
21medical assistance, a health insurance or health maintenance organization plan, a 
22prescription drug coverage program or any other payment arrangement intended to 
23provide payment for the costs associated with a patient's hospitalization.
242.  No communication about alternative payment options with patients during 
25hospitalization.  A hospital licensed by the State under chapter 405 may not 
26communicate with a patient regarding alternative payment options at any time during a 
27patient's hospitalization unless that communication is requested by the patient.
283.  Permissible communication.  A hospital may communicate with a patient about 
29alternative payment options prior to or upon the patient's admission to the hospital or at 
30the time of discharge.
31SUMMARY
32This bill prohibits a hospital from communicating with a patient regarding alternative 
33payment options at any time during a patient's hospitalization unless that communication 
34is requested by the patient.  The bill permits communication with a patient prior to or 
35upon the patient's admission to the hospital or at the time of discharge.
