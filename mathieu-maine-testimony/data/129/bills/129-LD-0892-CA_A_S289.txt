9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A " to S.P. 258, L.D. 892, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Exempt MaineCare Appendix C Private Nonmedical Institutions from the Service 
11 Provider Tax" 
12 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
13 'Resolve, To Require the Examination of Alternatives to the Service Provider Tax' 
14 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and inserting the following: 
15 'Sec. 1. Examination of alternatives to the service provider tax. Resolved: 
16 That the Department of Health and Human Services in partnership with the Department 
17 of Administrative and Financial Services and other state agencies that the departments 
18 determine should be included shall examine the service provider tax imposed pursuant to 
19 the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 36, chapter 358 and alternatives to that tax. The 
20 departments shall submit a report on their findings and recommendations to the Joint 
21 Standing Committee on Taxation by March 1, 2020 describing the advantages and 
22 disadvantages of the service provider tax and alternatives that were examined. The 
23 committee may submit a bill related to the report to the Second Regular Session of the 
24 129th Legislature.' 
25 SUMMARY 
26 This amendment replaces the bill with a resolve that directs the Department of Health 
27 and Human Services in partnership with the Department of Administrative and Financial 
28 Services and other state agencies that the departments determine should be included to 
29 examine the service provider tax and alternatives to the tax and submit a report on their 
30 findings to the Joint Standing Committee on Taxation by March 1, 2020. The committee 
31 is authorized to submit a bill to the Second Regular Session of the 129th Legislature.
