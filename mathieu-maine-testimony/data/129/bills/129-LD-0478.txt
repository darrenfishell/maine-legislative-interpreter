6SUMMARY
7This bill, beginning in 2020, allows the operation of an all-terrain vehicle, or ATV, 
8on a public way as long as the ATV is registered and meets specified safety equipment 
9requirements and the operator is licensed and has insurance.  This bill establishes an 
10annual registration fee of $33, the same as for ATVs under current law.  The bill does not 
11affect the operation of ATVs off of public ways.
12This bill prohibits the operation of an ATV on a public way with a posted speed limit 
13of more than 50 miles per hour.  The Department of Transportation may adopt rules or a 
14municipality or county may adopt an ordinance to restrict or prohibit the operation of an 
15ATV on a way if the department, county or municipality determines that the prohibition 
16or restriction is necessary in the interest of public safety.
