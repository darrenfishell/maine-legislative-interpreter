4SUMMARY
5This bill limits, by definition, the size of an all-terrain vehicle to 65 inches in width 
6and 2,000 pounds in weight, prohibits operation of motor vehicles on a designated all-
7terrain vehicle trail that is not on a gravel road system and prohibits operation of all-
8terrain vehicles on trails posted with size or weight limitations unless that use has been 
9authorized by the landowner or the landowner's agent.
