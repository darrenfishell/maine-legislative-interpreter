22SUMMARY
23This amendment strikes and replaces the bill to reallocate the language establishing 
24the Maine Workforce, Research, Development and Student Achievement Institute from 
25the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20-A to Title 5.  The amendment changes the 
26membership of the Maine Workforce, Research, Development and Student Achievement 
27Institute Steering Committee.  It also adds a requirement that the institute align its work 
28with the priorities established by the State's economic development strategy developed by 
29the Department of Economic and Community Development.
