15SUMMARY
16This bill extends the eligibility period for closure costs for a landfill that currently 
17applies only to a landfill licensed on or before September 1, 1989.  The bill provides that 
18if a license application for a landfill was accepted for processing on or before September 
191, 1989 and the application was approved by the Commissioner of Environmental 
20Protection within one year of accepting the application for processing, the landfill is 
21eligible for payment of 75% of certain closure costs by the Department of Environmental 
22Protection.
