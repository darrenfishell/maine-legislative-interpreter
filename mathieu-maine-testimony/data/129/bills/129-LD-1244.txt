33SUMMARY
34This bill authorizes the Department of Public Safety, Gambling Control Board to 
35accept an application from the Passamaquoddy Tribe to operate 50 slot machines at a 
36gambling facility in Washington County at which high-stakes beano is conducted by the 
37Passamaquoddy Tribe. The bill raises the limit on the number of slot machines allowed in 
38the State by 50 to accommodate the slot machines that may be operated by the 
39Passamaquoddy Tribe.  The bill requires deposit of 25% of net slot machine income in 
Page 3 - 129LR1610(01)-11the General Fund and sets the initial application fee to operate the 50 slot machines at 
2$10,000 and the renewal fee at $5,000.  The Passamaquoddy Tribe is exempted from 
3paying a $250,000 nonrefundable privilege fee and a $5,000,000 license fee.  Slot 
4machines operated by the Passamaquoddy Tribe would be subject to the oversight of the 
5Gambling Control Board and subject to the same laws and rules as other slot machines 
6operated in this State.
