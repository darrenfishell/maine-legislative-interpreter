18SUMMARY
19This bill changes the eligibility requirements for the retirement of a state fire marshal 
20investigator, state fire marshal senior investigator and state fire marshal sergeant under 
21the Maine Public Employees Retirement System from 55 years of age and 25 years of 
22creditable service to just 20 years of creditable service.
