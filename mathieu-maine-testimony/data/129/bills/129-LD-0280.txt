61.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
7following terms have the following meanings.
8A.  "Cellular telephone" means a device used to access wireless telephone service.
9B.  "Portable electronic device" means any portable electronic device that is not part 
10of the operating equipment of a motor vehicle, including but not limited to an 
11electronic game, device for sending or receiving e-mail, text messaging device, 
12cellular telephone, video device, global positioning system device and computer.
13C.  "Text messaging" means reading or manually composing electronic 
14communications, including text messages, instant messages and e-mails, using a 
15portable electronic device.  "Text messaging" does not include using a global 
16positioning or navigation system.
17D.  "Operate" means driving a motor vehicle on a public way with the motor running, 
18including while temporarily stationary because of traffic, a traffic light or a stop sign 
19or otherwise stationary.  "Operate" does not include operating a motor vehicle with or 
20without the motor running when the operator has pulled the motor vehicle over to the 
21side of, or off, a public way and has halted in a location where the motor vehicle can 
22safely remain stationary.
232.  Prohibition.  A person may not operate a motor vehicle while engaging in text 
24messaging or using a portable electronic device.
253.  Penalties.  The following penalties apply to a violation of this section.
26A.  A person who violates this section commits a traffic infraction for which a fine of 
27not less than $250 may be adjudged.
28B.  A person who violates this section after previously having been adjudicated as 
29violating this section within a 3-year period commits a traffic infraction for which a 
30fine of not less than $500 may be adjudged, and the Secretary of State shall suspend 
31the license of that person without right to hearing.  The minimum periods of license 
32suspension are:
33(1)  Thirty days, if the person has 2 adjudications for a violation of this section 
34within a 3-year period;
35(2)  Sixty days, if the person has 3 adjudications for a violation of this section 
36within a 3-year period; and
37(3)  Ninety days, if the person has 4 or more adjudications for a violation of this 
38section within a 3-year period.

4SUMMARY
5This bill adds a prohibition on the use of a portable electronic device such as a video 
6device or a global positioning system device to the prohibition on text messaging while 
7operating a motor vehicle.
