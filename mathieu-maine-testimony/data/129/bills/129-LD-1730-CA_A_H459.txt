32 SUMMARY 
33 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, makes the following 
34 changes to the bill. 
35 1. It removes the provisions of the bill that authorize the Secretary of State to omit 
36 the write-in space on a ballot for any office in which there is no declared write-in 
37 candidate. 
38 2. It removes the provisions of the bill that change the laws restricting certain 
39 activities at and around the polls on election day.
