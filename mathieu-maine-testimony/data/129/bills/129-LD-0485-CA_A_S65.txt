1 SUMMARY 
2 This amendment replaces the bill and provides a new title. The amendment requires 
3 the owner or keeper of a dog that assaults a person and causes an injury that requires 
4 medical attention to secure aid for the injured person, contain the dog, if necessary, and 
5 provide the owner's or keeper's name, current address and contact information to the 
6 injured person, a person acting for the injured person or a law enforcement officer before 
7 the owner or keeper may leave the scene of the assault. A violation of the section is a 
8 Class D crime.
