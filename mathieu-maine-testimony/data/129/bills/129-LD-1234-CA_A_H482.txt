COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A " to H.P. 895, L.D. 1234, Bill, "An Act To 
Expand the Value of the Homestead Exemption to $25,000 and State Reimbursement to 
100 Percent of Lost Property Tax Revenue" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Expand the Value of the Homestead Exemption to $25,000 and State 
Reimbursement to 70 Percent of Lost Property Tax Revenue' 
Amend the bill in section 2 in subsection 3 in the 3rd from last line (page 1, line 18 in 
L.D.) by striking out the following: "100%" and inserting the following: '70%' 
Amend the bill in section 2 in subsection 4 in the 4th from last line (page 1, line 27 in 
L.D.) by striking out the following: "100%" and inserting the following: '70%' 
Amend the bill by striking out all of section 3. 
Amend the bill in section 4 in subsection 2 in paragraph C in the first line (page 2, 
line 6 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "100%" and inserting the following: '70%'' 
Amend the bill by inserting after section 4 the following: 
'Sec. 5. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and 
allocations are made. 
ADMINISTRATIVE AND FINANCIAL SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF 
Homestead Property Tax Exemption Reimbursement 0886 
Initiative: Provides funding to increase the homestead property tax exemption from 
$20,000 to $25,000 and to increase the reimbursement to municipalities from 62.5% to 
70%.

8 SUMMARY 
9 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, changes to 70% the 
10 percentage of state reimbursement of lost property tax revenue due to the increase in the 
11 homestead exemption in the bill. It also removes from the bill the provision that the tax 
12 assessed on a homestead eligible for the homestead exemption may not be less than $100. 
13 The amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section.
