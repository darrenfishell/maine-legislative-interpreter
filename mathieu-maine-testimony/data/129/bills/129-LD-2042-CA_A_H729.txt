Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill allows termination of a memorandum of understanding between the Department of Public Safety and the 
LifeFlight Foundation and transfers a motor vehicle, computer training equipment and $163,000 from the Department 
of Public Safety to the LifeFlight Foundation. This money is the balance remaining in an account at the Depaitment of 
Public Safety that has been dedicated to activities carried out by the LifeFlight Foundation. It is anticipated that the 
LifeFlight Foundation will continue to provide the same services it has been providing prior to enactment of this bill.
