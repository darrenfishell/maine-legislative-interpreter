9 SUMMARY 
10 This amendment clarifies that the Department of Health and Human Services must 
11 seek approval from the United States Department of Health and Human Services, Centers 
12 for Medicare and Medicaid Services to amend the brain injury waiver to increase rates for 
13 Home Support (Residential Habilitation) Level I to no less than $8.63 per quarter hour. It 
14 also requires the department to include any findings from exploring telehealth 
15 opportunities for brain injury services to be submitted to the joint standing committee of 
16 the Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human services matters as part of the 
17 annual telehealth report. The amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations 
18 section.
