20 property tax exemption for farm machinery, provides administrative provisions for the

22 reimbursement amounts and requires the State to reimburse municipalities for 100% of 
23 property taxes lost as a result of the increase in the exemption.

This bill increases the property tax exemption for farm machinery from $10,000 to $45,000 and provides for 100% 
reimbursement to municipalities of the property tax loss. The bill includes a one-time General Fund appropriation of 
$17,500 in fiscal year 2020-21 to the Department of Administrative and Financial Services to reimburse 
municipalities for costs associated with increasing this property tax exemption. The Department will require annual 
General Fund appropriations of $525 ,000 beginning in fiscal year 2021-22 to reimburse municipalities 100% of the 
property tax loss.
