27 SUMMARY 
28 This amendment strikes and replaces the bill to reallocate the language establishing 
29 the Maine Workforce, Research, Development and Student Achievement Institute from 
30 the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20-A to Title 5. It changes the membership of the 
31 Maine Workforce, Research, Development and Student Achievement Institute Steering 
32 Committee. It also adds an appropriations and allocations section.

Provides an appropriation of $250,390 per year beginning in 2019-20 to the Legislature for the costs of the Legislature 
to contract on an ongoing basis with the University of Maine System to establish and maintain the Maine Workforce, 
Research, Development and Student Achievement Institute.

Board, the Maine Community College System and the Finance Authority of Maine to serve on the steering committee 
can be absorbed within existing budgeted resources.
