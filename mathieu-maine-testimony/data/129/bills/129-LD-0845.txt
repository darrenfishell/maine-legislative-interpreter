4If the Secretary of State is a candidate in an election under the Secretary of State's

10elected Secretary of State under Title 5, section 81.
11SUMMARY
12This bill prohibits the Secretary of State from overseeing an election in which the 
13Secretary of State is a candidate and transfers all responsibilities of the Secretary of State 
14for that election to the first deputy secretary of state.
