COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A "to H.P. 59, L.D. 62, Bill, "An Act To Enhance 
the Senio1· Volunteer Benefit Pro gram" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 1-A in the 3rd and 4th lines (page 1, lines 6 
and 7 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "or 100 times the state minimum hourly 
wage under Title 26. section 664. subsection 1, whichever is greate.r.." 
SUMMARY 
This amendment removes from the bill a provision that requires a maximum payment 
under the senior volunteer benefit program of 100 times the state minimum hourly wage 
if that amount is greater than $1,000.
