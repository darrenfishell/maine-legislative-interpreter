4Sec. 2.  20-A MRSA §2405, sub-§10 is enacted to read:
510.  Limit on number of public charter schools approved by the commission.  No 
6more than 10 public charter schools approved by the commission may operate at any 
7time.  Any time the cap is reached, the commissioner may not accept further registrations 
8from the commission and only local school boards and collaboratives of local school 
9boards may approve charters.
10SUMMARY
11Under current law, the number of public charter schools approved by the Maine 
12Charter School Commission that may operate at any time is capped at 10 until July 1, 
132022.  This bill makes that cap permanent.
