3SUMMARY
4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to establish a task force to study the benefits, assessments and 
6expansion of continuing education programs for young adults with disabilities after high 
7school.  The members of the task force would include a variety of experts, providers and 
8parents, and the task force would be charged with developing recommendations to 
9enhance the coordination of programs and recommend targeted reforms to ensure the 
10most efficient and effective provision of services.  This bill would also implement 
11targeted reforms that have been recommended by existing or previous task forces.
