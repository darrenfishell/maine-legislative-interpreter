Page 1 - 129LR0166(01)-11Sec. 1.  State plan amendment.  Resolved:  That the Department of Health and 
2Human Services shall prepare and submit a Medicaid state plan amendment to the United 
3States Department of Health and Human Services, Centers for Medicare and Medicaid 
4Services seeking to disregard under the federal Social Security Act, Section 1902(r)(2) 
5assets held in qualifying retirement and education accounts when determining Medicaid 
6eligibility for individuals with disabilities under 65 years of age who have dependent 
7children living in the home.
8SUMMARY
9This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to prepare and 
10submit a state plan amendment to the United States Department of Health and Human 
11Services, Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services in order to make a change in 
12Medicaid eligibility requirements for individuals with disabilities under 65 years of age 
13who have dependent children living in the home by disregarding assets held in qualifying 
14retirement and education accounts.
