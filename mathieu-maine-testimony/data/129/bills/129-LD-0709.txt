9SUMMARY
10This bill exempts from sales and use tax meals, up to a maximum cost to the 
11employer of $6 per day, provided to an employee of an eating establishment while that 
12employee is working.  It provides that, beginning in 2020, the amount of the exemption 
13must be adjusted for inflation.
