8SUMMARY
9This bill specifies that liquefied propane gas distribution systems that have 
10underground pipes are subject to the so-called dig safe law.
11It also increases the administrative penalties for violations of the so-called dig safe 
12law from $500 to $1,000 for a violation and from $5,000 to $10,000 for a subsequent 
13violation occurring within 12 months of an earlier violation.
