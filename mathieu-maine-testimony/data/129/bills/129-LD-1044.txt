18research;

225, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
234.  Transfers for children's cancer research.  The Secretary of State shall transfer 
24quarterly to the entity designated pursuant to subsection 3 the total amount deposited into 
25the Maine Children's Cancer Research Fund pursuant to subsection 2. All funds 
26transferred under this subsection must be used exclusively for children's cancer research.
27SUMMARY
28This bill creates the option for an individual to make a donation upon vehicle 
29registration or renewal in support of children's cancer research and establishes the Maine 
30Children's Cancer Research Fund for collection of the donations. All donations are 
31transferred quarterly to an entity determined by the Secretary of State and must be used 
32exclusively for children's cancer research.
