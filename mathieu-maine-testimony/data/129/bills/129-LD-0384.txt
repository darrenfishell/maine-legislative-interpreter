Page 1 - 129LR2152(01)-11Sec. 1.  Secretary of State to review uniform legislation. Resolved:  That the 
2Secretary of State shall review the uniform law entitled "Revised Uniform Law on 
3Notarial Acts," which was approved and recommended for enactment by the National 
4Conference of Commissioners on Uniform State Laws on November 15, 2010 and any 
5subsequent amendments to that uniform law approved by the National Conference of 
6Commissioners on Uniform State Laws.  In conducting this review, the Secretary of State 
7may consult with state and national associations for notaries public, as well as other 
8experts in the field of notarial law and practice.  Based on that review, the Secretary of 
9State shall submit a report including a recommendation regarding whether the Legislature 
10should adopt the uniform law, with or without changes, together with any proposed 
11implementing legislation, to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 
12jurisdiction over judiciary matters no later than January 15, 2021.  Following receipt and 
13review of the report, the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction 
14over judiciary matters is authorized to report out a bill to the First Regular Session of the 
15130th Legislature.
16SUMMARY
17This resolve directs the Secretary of State to review uniform legislation governing 
18notarial practice and to submit a report including a recommendation regarding adoption 
19of the uniform law, along with any proposed implementing legislation, to the joint 
20standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over judiciary matters, which is 
21authorized to report out a bill to the 130th Legislature.
