15SUMMARY
16This amendment adds language requiring from any business entity acting as a 
17supervising travel producer without being specifically licensed the provision of certain 
18notices to the superintendent. The amendment clarifies that travel insurance remains a 
19limited line of insurance and that it must be classified and filed as inland marine 
20insurance.  The amendment updates the definition of "marine and transportation 
21insurance" to include travel insurance. The amendment also makes other clarifying 
22changes to the bill.
