9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "11 " to S.P. 664, L.D. 1917, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Eliminate Direct Retail Competition for the Supply of Electricity to Residential 
11 Consumers" 
12 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
13 'Resolve, Directing the Public Utilities Commission To Examine Direct Retail 
14 Competition for the Supply of Electricity to Residential Customers' 
15 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and inserting the following: 
16 'Sec. 1. Report; retail market for residential electricity supply. Resolved: 
17 That, no later than January 31, 2021, the Public Utilities Commission shall submit to the 
18 joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over energy, utilities and 
19 technology matters a report regarding the competition in the retail market for the supply 
20 of electricity to residential customers. The report must be based on an analysis of the 
21 market, including, but not limited to, the level of competition, the costs and benefits to 
22 residential customers of the current market structure and opportunities to enhance 
23 competition. The report may include findings and recommendations, including suggested 
24 legislation. The committee may report out a bill to the First Regular Session of the 130th 
25 Legislature related to the report.' 
26 SUMMARY 
27 This amendment replaces the bill with a resolve. The amendment directs the Public 
28 Utilities Commission to submit a report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature 
29 having jurisdiction over energy, utilities and technology matters regarding the 
30 competition in the retail market for the supply of electricity to residential customers. The 
31 amendment authorizes the committee to report out a bill to the First Regular Session of 
32 the 130th Legislature related to the report.
