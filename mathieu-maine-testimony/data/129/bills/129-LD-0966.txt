3SUMMARY
4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to require that patient intake forms used by all health care facilities, 
6including hospitals, emergency rooms, urgent care facilities, health care clinics and 
7doctor’s offices, include a question on the form regarding the date of the patient’s most 
8recent vaccine.  This vaccine-related information will then be provided to the United 
9States Department of Health and Human Services to aid its efforts to ensure safer 
10childhood vaccines.
