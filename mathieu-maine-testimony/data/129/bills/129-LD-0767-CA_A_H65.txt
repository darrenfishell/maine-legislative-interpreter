SUMMARY 
This amendment authorizes, instead of requfres as in the bill, the sheriff of a county 
jail to provide for in-person visitation between a prisoner and a visitor of the prisoner, 
subject to any conditions and limitations required for the safety and security of the jail as 
determined on a case-by-case basis by the sheriff. The amendment also authorizes, 
instead of requires as in the bill, the sheriff to provide opportunities for in-person 
visitation involving physical contact between a prisoner and a visitor of the prisoner, 
subject to any conditions and limitations required for the safety and security of the jail as 
determined on a case-by-case basis by the sheriff.
