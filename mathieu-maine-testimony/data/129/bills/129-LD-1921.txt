33SUMMARY
34This bill makes the following changes to the laws governing inland fisheries and 
35wildlife.
361.  It waives the 7-day notice requirement for meetings of the Inland Fisheries and 
37Wildlife Advisory Council in cases of emergency rulemaking.
Page 4 - 129LR3048(01)-112.  It changes the name of the supervisor of an apprentice hunter from a youth hunter 
2supervisor to an apprentice supervisor and changes the name of the supervisor of a junior 
3hunter from an adult supervisor to a junior hunter supervisor.
43.  It removes 2 cross-references in the laws governing permits to possess wildlife in 
5captivity.
