35SUMMARY
36This bill raises the betting limit for games of chance from $1 to $5.  It also allows a 
37bona fide nonprofit organization to operate a licensed card game or registered game of 
38chance to which the general public has access for 4 consecutive days instead of 3 days 
39and removes the current 3-month waiting period between game operations.
