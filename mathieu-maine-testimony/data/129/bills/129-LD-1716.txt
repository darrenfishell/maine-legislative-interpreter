20SUMMARY
21This bill updates language in the laws governing occupational therapy practice, 
22including occupational therapists and occupational therapy assistants, establishes an 
23inactive license status for licensees and repeals the continuing education requirement for 
24licensees.  The bill also repeals a residency provision for applicants and the character 
25reference requirement for foreign-trained applicants.
