14 §576-A. Greenhouse gas emissions reductions 
15 1. 2030 annual emissions level. By January 1, 2030, the State shall reduce gross 
16 annual greenhouse gas emissions to at least 45% below the 1990 gross annual greenhouse 
17 gas emissions level. 
18 2. Interim emissions level. By January 1, 2040, the gross annual greenhouse gas 
19 emissions level must, at a minimum, be on an annual trajectory sufficient to achieve the 
20 2050 annual emissions level in accordance with subsection 3. 
21 3. 2050 annual emissions level. By January 1, 2050, the State shall reduce gross 
22 annual greenhouse gas emissions to at least 80% below the 1990 gross annual greenhouse 
23 gas emissions level.

25 shall adopt rules to track and report to the Legislature on gross annual greenhouse gas 
26 emissions and net annual greenhouse gas emissions.

36 achieved by each sector since 1990 measured as a percentage of statewide gross
