3ยง12764.  Municipal stocking of fish in inland waters within the municipality; 
4notification
5A municipality may stock an inland water within the municipality for the first time or 
6with a new species of fish, subject to the department's authority relating to culture and 
7research of appropriate fish species for that inland water.  Prior to stocking an inland 
8water under this section, the municipality shall notify the Bureau of Resource 
9Management.  The bureau shall review the stocking plan and, if the bureau determines the 
10species of fish to be stocked is an appropriate species for that inland water, follow the 
11public notice requirements under section 12758-A.
12SUMMARY
13This bill allows a municipality to stock with fish an inland water of the municipality, 
14subject to a determination by the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife, Bureau of 
15Resource Management that the species of fish is appropriate to be stocked in that inland 
16water and to the public notification requirements that apply when the bureau proposes to 
17stock an inland water with fish.
