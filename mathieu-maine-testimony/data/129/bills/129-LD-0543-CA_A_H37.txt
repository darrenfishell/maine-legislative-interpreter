SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, changes the title of 
the bill and changes the bill into a resolve directing the Department of Health and Human 
Services, in consultation with the Department of Environmental Protection, to submit to 
the Joint Standing Committee on Environment and Natural Resources on or before 
January 15, 2020 a report containing a variety of information regarding septic system 
inspections and the certification of septic system inspectors. After reviewing the report, 
the committee may report out a bill related to the information contained in the report to 
the Second Regular Session of the 129thLegislature.
