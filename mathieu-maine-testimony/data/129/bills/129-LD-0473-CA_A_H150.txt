COMMITTEE AMENDMENT ,.fl.,, to H.P. 358, L.D. 473, Bill, "An Act To Allow 
Flexibility in Residential Rental Agreements" 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the 
following: 
'Sec. 1. 14 MRSA ยง6032, as amended by PL 2009, c. 566, ยง22, is further amended

ยง6032. Maximum security deposit 
A lease or tenancy at will agreement for a dwelling intended for human habitation
