3SUMMARY
4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to establish the Maine Broadband Initiative to encourage, promote, 
6stimulate, invest in and support universal high-speed broadband to unserved and 
7underserved areas of the State.  The bill would also establish the Maine Broadband 
8Initiative Fund to provide ongoing funding for high-speed broadband through funding 
9sources that would be identified in the bill.
