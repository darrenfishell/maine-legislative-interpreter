This bill includes an ongoing allocation of $500,0000 starting in fiscal year 2020-21 from the Maine Ground and 
Surface Waters Clean-up and Response Fund (MGSWCR) within the Department of Environmental Protection for 
loans and grants for rebate programs to retrofit, repair, replace or remove aboveground and underground oil storage 
tanks and associated piping at residential dwellings. The MGSWCR has sufficient resources to fund additional 
expenditures.
