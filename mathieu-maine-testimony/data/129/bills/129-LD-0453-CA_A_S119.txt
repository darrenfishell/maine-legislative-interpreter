18 §441. Removal or suspension of sheriff 
19 Whenever the county commissioners find that the sheriff is not faithfully or 
20 efficiently performing any duty imposed by this chapter or that the sheriff is improperly 
21 exercising or acting outside the sheriffs authority, the commissioners may file a 
22 complaint with the Governor describing in detail the facts of those actions or omissions 
23 and requesting that the Governor to remove the sheriff from offioe and appoint another 
24 sheriff in that offioe for the remainder of the term., pursuant to the Constitution of Maine, 
25 Article IX, Section 10: 
26 1. Remove the sheriff. Remove the sheriff from office and appoint another sheriff 
27 to that office for the remainder of the term; or 
28 2. Suspend the sheriff. Suspend the sheriff until the Governor determines whether 
29 to remove the sheriff from office or dismiss the complaint filed against the sheriff.'
