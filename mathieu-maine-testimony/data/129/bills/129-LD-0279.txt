4§1215.  Mileage and compensation of jurors
5A juror is entitled to paid mileage at the rate of 15¢ per mile for travel expenses from 
6the juror's residence to the place of holding court and return, except that, beginning July 
71, 2016, a juror is entitled to paid mileage at the rate established in Title 5, section 8.  A 
8juror is entitled to compensation at the rate of $15 $50 for each day of required 
9attendance at sessions of the court.
10SUMMARY
11This bill increases a juror's daily compensation rate from $15 to $50.
