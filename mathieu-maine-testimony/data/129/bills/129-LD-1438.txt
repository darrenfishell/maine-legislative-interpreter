9SUMMARY
10This bill requires the Secretary of State, with the assistance of the Attorney General 
11and the Revisor of Statutes, to prepare a summary of each referendum question that 
12explains the referendum question to the voter and to place this summary on the ballot 
13after the corresponding referendum question.  This summary may not exceed 250 words, 
14except that additional words may be used if the Secretary of State considers it necessary 
15and space on the ballot permits.
