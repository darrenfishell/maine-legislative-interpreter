The bill requires the Public Utilities Commission (PUC) to limit utility rate increases to less than 10% in any 12-
month period and prohibits the costs of an en-or on the utilities pmi from being billed to ratepayers. The bill also 
changes the term of service for appointed members of the PUC and the Public Advocate from a six-year term to 
service at the pleasure of the Governor and sets the compensation of the members of the PUC to the average annual 
wage within the State. The decrease in salaries for the Chair of the PUC and the other members will necessitate 
deallocations in Other Special Revenue Funds of$272,726 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $379,207 in fiscal year 2020-
21.
