5CHAPTER 723
6HEATING FUEL FEE
7§4951.  Imposition of fee
8Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, a fee is imposed on the 
9wholesale sale in the State of heating oil, propane, coal, kerosene and dyed diesel fuel 
10that is intended for residential or commercial heating purposes in the amount of 1% of the 
11wholesale sale price.
12§4952.  Administration
13The fee imposed by this chapter is administered as provided in chapter 7 and Part 3, 
14with the fee imposed pursuant to this chapter to be considered as imposed under Part 3.
15The revenue derived from the fee imposed by this chapter must be deposited in the 
16Heating Fuels Efficiency and Weatherization Fund established in Title 35-A, section 
1710119, which must reimburse the General Fund for the administrative costs of the fee as 
18certified by the bureau.
19§4953.  Effective date
20This chapter takes effect July 1, 2020.
21SUMMARY
22Starting July 1, 2020, this bill imposes a fee of 1% on the wholesale sale price of 
23heating oil, propane, coal, kerosene and dyed diesel fuel that is intended for residential or 
24commercial heating purposes.  The bill requires the revenue raised through the imposition 
25of this fee to be used to provide additional funding for the Heating Fuels Efficiency and 
26Weatherization Fund, which is administered by the Efficiency Maine Trust for the 
27purpose of reducing heating fuel consumption and providing energy efficiency and 
28weatherization products and services to consumers.
