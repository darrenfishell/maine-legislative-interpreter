11SUMMARY
12This bill eliminates municipal paper record retention requirements for maintaining 
13death disposition permits and requires those permits to be maintained in the electronic 
14death registration system.  A copy of a permit must be made available to a member of the 
15public upon a request made to the municipal clerk.
