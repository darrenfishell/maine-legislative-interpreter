19SUMMARY
20This bill amends the Board of Environmental Protection's authority to adopt and 
21enforce new motor vehicle emission standards by prohibiting the adoption of standards 
22requiring that new aftermarket catalytic converters sold or installed in the State be 
23certified to any standards adopted by the State of California or the California Air 
24Resources Board.  The bill also requires the Department of Environmental Protection, as 
25soon as practicable, to amend its rule Chapter 127 relating to new motor vehicle emission 
26standards in accordance with this statutory prohibition.
