26 SUMMARY 
27 This amendment does the following. 
28 1. It strikes the references in the bill to "enrollee originating site" and "provider 
29 distant site" and removes those definitions. 
30 2. It requires that a clinical evaluation be required before a provider may write a 
31 covered prescription through telehealth. The bill requires a physical exam. 
32 3. It removes the prohibition for coverage of prescribed schedule I, II or III 
33 controlled substances. 
34 4. It clarifies the telemonitoring requirements and provides that telephonic services 
35 are covered when scheduled telehealth services are technologically unavailable at the 
36 time of the scheduled telehealth service for an existing enrollee. 
37 5. It removes the exclusions section of the bill.
