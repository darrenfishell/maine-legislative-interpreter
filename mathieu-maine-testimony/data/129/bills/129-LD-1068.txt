20SUMMARY
21This bill allows the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of 
22Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations to license up to 6 agency liquor stores in a 
23municipality with a population of at least 10,001 but less than 20,001; current law allows 
24such a municipality a maximum of 5 agency liquor stores.
