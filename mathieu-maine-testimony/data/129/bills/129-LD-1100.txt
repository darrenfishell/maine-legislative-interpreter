2Sec. 1.  5 MRSA §11006, sub-§1, ¶A, as enacted by PL 1977, c. 551, §3, is 
3amended to read:
4A.  In the case of the failure or refusal of an agency to act or of alleged irregularities 
5in procedure before the agency which that are not adequately revealed in the record, 
6discovery may be conducted by the petitioner as permitted by the Maine Rules of 
7Civil Procedure and evidence thereon may be taken and determination made on the 
8matter by the reviewing court.
9SUMMARY
10This bill permits a petitioner under the Maine Administrative Procedure Act to 
11conduct discovery if any agency fails or refuses to act or there are alleged irregularities 
12that are not adequately revealed in the record of a proceeding.
