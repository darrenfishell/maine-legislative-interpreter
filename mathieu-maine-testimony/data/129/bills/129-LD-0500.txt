4SUMMARY
5Current law prohibits an individual licensed to prescribe opioid medication from 
6prescribing more than a 30-day supply of an opioid medication to a patient under 
7treatment for chronic pain.  This bill allows an individual licensed to prescribe opioid 
8medication to prescribe no more than a 6-month supply of an opioid medication to a 
9patient under treatment for chronic pain who has been prescribed medication for chronic 
10pain continually for at least 5 years or is 63 years of age or older.
