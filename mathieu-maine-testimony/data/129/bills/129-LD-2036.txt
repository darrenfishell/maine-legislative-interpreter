18Sec. 1.  Commission established. Resolved:  That the Blue Ribbon Commission 
19To Continue Studying and Recommend Funding Solutions for the State's Transportation 
20Systems, referred to in this resolve as "the commission," is established.
21Sec. 2.  Commission membership. Resolved:  That, notwithstanding Joint Rule 
22353, the 15 members and chairs appointed to the Blue Ribbon Commission To Study and 
23Recommend Funding Solutions for the State's Transportation Systems, established 
24pursuant to Resolve 2019, chapter 97 and referred to in this resolve as "the previous 
25commission," serve as the members and chairs of the commission.
26Sec. 3.  Duties. Resolved:  That the commission shall continue the work of the 
27previous commission to study how to reform and adequately supplement funding for the 
28State's transportation infrastructure to promote equity, sustainability and predictability so 
29that the State can responsibly provide safe and reliable state transportation systems.  The 
30commission shall continue to focus on funding the state highway and bridge system and 
31shall also develop findings or recommendations on the need and potential funding 
32solutions for multimodal transportation infrastructure. Specific study topics may include, 
33but are not limited to, those listed in Resolve 2019, chapter 97, section 5.
34The commission shall meet up to 4 times over the course of the Second Regular 
35Session of the 129th Legislature and may hold public hearings and review 
36recommendations from the people of the State and qualified experts when appropriate.  
37The chairs may also provide the opportunity for knowledgeable stakeholders to submit 
38written comments throughout the study process and to provide oral testimony on the 
39commission's draft recommendations.

23SUMMARY
24This resolve is reported by the Joint Standing Committee on Transportation pursuant 
25to Resolve 2019, chapter 97, section 7 to implement the recommendation of the Blue 
26Ribbon Commission To Study and Recommend Funding Solutions for the State's 
27Transportation Systems.  This resolve establishes the Blue Ribbon Commission To 
28Continue Studying and Recommend Funding Solutions for the State's Transportation 
29Systems to continue the work of studying funding solutions for the State's transportation 
30systems as recommended by the report of the previous commission created by Resolve 
312019, chapter 97.
