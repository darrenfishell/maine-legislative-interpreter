22 SUMMARY 
23 This amendment changes the bill as follows: 
24 1. It modifies the definition of "high-impact electric transmission line" to include a 
25 transmission line that is: 
26 A. Greater than 50 miles in length; 
27 B. Capable of operating at 200 kilovolts or more; 
28 C. Not a generator interconnection transmission facility; and 
29 D. Not constructed primarily to provide electric reliability within the State; 
30 2. It replaces the requirement that a high-impact electric transmission line provide 
31 significant tangible benefits with requirements that: 
32 A. The petitioner for a certificate of public convenience and necessity for the line 
33 pay an amount of tax benefits projected by the petitioner; and 
34 B. The petitioner for a certificate of public convenience and necessity for the line 
35 provide a community benefits package with a total value of no less than $20,000 per 
36 year per megawatt of capacity of the line;
