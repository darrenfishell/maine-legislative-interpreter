COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "/}" to H.P. 819, L.D. 1130, Bill, "An Act To 
Promote Equity in the Elver Aquaculture Quota" 
Amend the bill by striking out all of the emergency preamble (page 1, lines 1 to 9 in 
L.D.). 
Amend the bill by striking out all of section 1 and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. 12 MRSA §6575-M is enacted to read: 
§6575-M. Aquaculture quota; eligible harvesters 
The following provisions govern elver quota allocated specifically for use in 
aquaculture. 
1. Annual list of license holders. A person who receives an elver quota to be 
allocated specifically for use in aquaculture shall submit to the commissioner annually a 
list of the elver license holders who provided that person with their elver quota. 
2. Eligibility. An elver license holder chosen to provide a person with any portion 
of the elver quota allocated specifically for use in aquaculture is not eligible again for a 
period of 3 years to provide elvers to a person who receives an elver quota to be used 
specifically in aquaculture. 
3. Prior violations. A person who has an elver fishing violation under section 
6505-A or chapter 621, subchapter 2, article 5 in the previous 6 years is not eligible to 
provide elvers to a person who receives an elver quota to be allocated specifically for use 
in aquaculture.' 
Amend the bill by striking out all of the emergency clause. 
Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively.

SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill. It specifies that a person receiving an elver quota 
to be allocated specifically for use in aquaculture shall provide to the Commissioner of 
Marine Resources a list of elver license holders who provided that person with their elver 
quota. Once an elver license holder is chosen, that license holder is not eligible to 
provide elvers to a person allocated an elver quota to be used in aquaculture for a period 
of 3 years. A person who has any elver fishing violations in the previous 6 years is not 
eligible to provide elvers to a person who receives an elver quota to be used in 
aquaculture.
