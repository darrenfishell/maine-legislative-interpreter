9SUMMARY
10This bill clarifies that funds presumed abandoned under the Uniform Unclaimed 
11Property Act in a lawyer's trust account for which identifying client information can be 
12found must be delivered to the Treasurer of State.  It provides that funds presumed 
13abandoned in a lawyer's trust account for which no identifying client information can be 
14found must be transferred to the lawyer's trust account program manager to be used to 
15provide funding to organizations whose primary purpose is to provide civil legal aid to 
16low-income residents of the State.
