27 Initiative: Provides one-time funding in fiscal year 2019-20 only to school administrative

30 which at least 50% of students qualified for a free or reduced-price lunch during the

32 guidelines to allocate and disburse the funding to participating schools. Guidelines must 
33 be developed within 90 days following the effective date of this Act. Funding

35 fiscal year to be used only for the purpose for which it was provided.

This amendment adds a one-time General Fund appropriation of $500,000 in fiscal year 2019-20 to school 
administrative units that start or expand alternative breakfast delivery services that provide breakfast after the start of 
the school day. 
It reduces the General Fund appropriations by $66,000 per year from $216,000 to $150,000 beginning in fiscal year 
2019-20 to the Department of Education for the cost to develop and implement the Internet-based application. 
This amendment also adds one-time transfers of$650,000 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $150,000 in fiscal year 2020-21 
to the unappropriated surplus of the General Fund from the Medical Use of Marijuana Fund.
