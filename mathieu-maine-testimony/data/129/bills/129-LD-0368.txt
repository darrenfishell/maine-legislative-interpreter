20SUMMARY
21This bill replaces the requirement that a multiple-employer welfare arrangement 
22based on geographic association must be established by an association with a principal 
23office in a location within a 40-mile radius of the principal place of business of eligible 
24employers with a requirement that the arrangement must be established by an association 
25with a principal office located in the State.
