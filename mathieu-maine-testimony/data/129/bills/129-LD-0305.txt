3ยง598-A.  Prospective employee's social security number
4An employer may not request a social security number from a prospective employee 
5on an employment application or during the application process for employment except 
6for the purposes of substance abuse testing under subchapter 3-A or a preemployment 
7background check.  This section does not apply to an employer's request for a social 
8security number after the employee has been hired.
9SUMMARY
10This bill prohibits the request of a social security number from a prospective 
11employee by an employer on an employment application or during the application 
12process.  Under this bill, an employer may still request a social security number from a 
13prospective employee for purposes of a substance abuse test or preemployment 
14background check, and an employer is not prohibited from asking for a social security 
15number from an individual for any reason after the individual has been hired.
