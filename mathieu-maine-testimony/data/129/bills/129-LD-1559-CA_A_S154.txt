9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT " A-" to S.P. 494, L.D. 1559, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Require the Public Utilities Commission To Open an Investigation Whenever It Initiates a 
11 Management Audit" 
12 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
13 'An Act To Require the Public Utilities Commission To Permit the Public Advocate 
14 To Access Certain Information Related to a Management Audit' 
15 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the 
16 following: 
17 'Sec. 1. 35-A MRSA §113, sub-§5 is enacted to read: 
18 5. Public Advocate; access to information. Throughout the course of a 
19 management audit performed pursuant to this section, the commission, upon request of 
20 the Public Advocate, shall: 
21 A. Permit the Public Advocate to access information supplied by a public utility to 
22 the auditor and information supplied by the auditor to the commission; and 
23 B. Provide the Public Advocate the opportunity to comment on information received 
24 under this subsection.' 
25 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
26 section number to read consecutively. 
27 SUMMARY 
28 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. The amendment requires the 
29 Public Utilities Commission, upon request of the Public Advocate, to permit the Public 
30 Advocate to access certain information in connection with a management audit of a 
31 public utility and to provide the Public Advocate the opportunity to comment on 
32 information received.
