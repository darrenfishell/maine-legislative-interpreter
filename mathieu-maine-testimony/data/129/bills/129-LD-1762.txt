10Sec. 1.  Director of Bureau of Parks and Lands to convey land.  Resolved:  
11That the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the Department of Agriculture, 
12Conservation and Forestry shall by quitclaim deed without covenant convey or release to 
13the Town of Allagash, County of Aroostook for community development purposes the 
14parcel of land situated on Route 161, otherwise known as Allagash Road, and Old Route 
15161 in the Town of Allagash and described in the records of the bureau as the Allagash 
16Southeast project, Original Reservation parcel and having identification number 86-2298.
17SUMMARY
18This resolve directs the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the 
19Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to transfer a parcel of land situated 
20on Route 161 and Old Route 161 in the Town of Allagash to the Town of Allagash for 
21community development purposes.
