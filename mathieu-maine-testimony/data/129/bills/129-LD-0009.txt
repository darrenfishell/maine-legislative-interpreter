4 §1215.  Mileage and compensation of  jurors
5 A juror is entitled  to paid mileage at the rate of 15¢ per mile for travel expenses from  
6 the juror's residence to the place of holding court and return, except that, beginning July 
7 1, 2016, a juror  is entitled to paid mileage at the rate established in Title 5, section 8.  A 
8 juror is entitled to compensation at the rate of $15 $40 for each day of required 
9 attendance at  sessions of  the court.
10 SUMMARY
11 This bill increases a juror's  daily compensation  rate from $15 to $40.
