8SUMMARY
9This bill limits the amount of money that may be retained under a construction 
10contract, pending the completion of contractor or subcontractor performance, to 5% of the 
11contract price.  The limit applies only to private contracts and not to contracts entered into 
12by governmental entities and only to contracts entered into on or after the effective date 
13of the legislation.
