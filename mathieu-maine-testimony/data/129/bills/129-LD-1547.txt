18SUMMARY
19This bill directs the Public Utilities Commission, Emergency Services 
20Communication Bureau to use 10% of the statewide E-9-1-1 surcharges to fund certain 
21staff positions and capital equipment projects of public safety answering points operated 
22by county governments or other local units of government.
