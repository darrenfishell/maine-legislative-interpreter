24SUMMARY
25This bill increases the Governor's salary from $70,000 per year to $150,000 per year 
26beginning January 2023.  The bill also increases the legislative meal and housing 
27allowances from $32 and $38 to $50 and $75, respectively.  It also increases the mileage 
28allowance from $38 to $75.  The increases in the expense allowances for Legislators take 
29effect December 2, 2020.
