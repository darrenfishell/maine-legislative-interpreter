13SUMMARY
14This bill prohibits certain convicted sex offenders who were convicted of an offense 
15against a minor under 14 years of age from intentionally or knowingly establishing 
16residence in a dwelling unit of a multiunit residential building in which another dwelling 
17unit that shares the same entryway is occupied by a person under 18 years of age.  The 
18bill makes violation of this provision a Class E crime.
