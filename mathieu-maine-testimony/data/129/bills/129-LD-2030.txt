16SUMMARY
17This bill provides the Secretary of State discretion as to whether to suspend the 
18driver's license of a driver under 18 years of age who has violated restrictions that apply 
19only to drivers under 18 years of age.  It also provides such a driver whose license has 
20been suspended the opportunity to request a hearing.
