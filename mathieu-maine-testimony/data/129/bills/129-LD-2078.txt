14Sec. 1.  Adoption.  Resolved:  That final adoption of portions of Chapter 100: 
15Enforcement Procedures, a provisionally adopted major substantive rule of the Maine 
16Health Data Organization that has been submitted to the Legislature for review pursuant 
17to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A, is authorized.

21This resolve provides for legislative review of portions of Chapter 100: Enforcement
