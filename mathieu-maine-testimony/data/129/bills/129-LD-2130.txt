32SUMMARY
33This bill is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on Inland Fisheries and 
34Wildlife pursuant to Resolve 2019, chapter 35, section 1.  The bill increases the 
35registration fees for all-terrain vehicles by $25 for each type of registration.  The bill 
36provides that the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Bureau of Parks 
37and Lands must use the registration fees deposited into the ATV Recreational 
Page 2 - 129LR3239(01)-11Management Fund to oversee construction and maintenance of designated state-approved 
2all-terrain vehicle trails.
