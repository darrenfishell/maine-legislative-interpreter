17 SUMMARY 
18 The bill requires health education instruction in elementary, middle, junior high and 
19 high school to include instruction in mental health and the relationship between physical 
20 and mental health. This amendment clarifies that health education must include 
21 instruction that addresses the relationship between physical and mental health in order to 
22 enhance student understanding of attitudes toward and behavior relating to mental illness 
23 and to eliminate the stigma associated with mental illness.
