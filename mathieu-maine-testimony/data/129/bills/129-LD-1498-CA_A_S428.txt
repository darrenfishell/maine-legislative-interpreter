following: 
'Sec. 1. 29-A MRSA §2354-C, sub-§1, first 1, as amended by PL 2015, c. 119, 
§ § 1 and 2, is further amended to read: 
1. Canadian gross vehicle weight limits. Notwithstanding section 2354, except as

10 SUMMARY 
11 This amendment does the following: 
12 1. It states that after December 31, 2025 the Department of Transportation may not 
13 authorize certain routes from the United States-Canada border to certain points in this 
14 State for commercial vehicles transporting wood at Canadian gross vehicle weight limits; 
15 and 
16 2. It requires the department to submit a report to the joint standing committee of the 
17 Legislature having jurisdiction over transportation matters by January 1, 2024 regarding 
18 the effects on road conditions and recommendations for continuance, discontinuance or 
19 modification of allowing certain commercial vehicles to travel at Canadian gross vehicle 
20 weight limits. It allows the joint standing committee to submit legislation based on the 
21 findings and recommendations in the report to the Second Regular Session of the 131st 
22 Legislature.
