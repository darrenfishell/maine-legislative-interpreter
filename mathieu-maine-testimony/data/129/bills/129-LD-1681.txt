19SUMMARY
20This bill requires the Public Utilities Commission to establish the statewide E-9-1-1 
21surcharge and the prepaid wireless E-9-1-1 surcharge but limits the surcharges to no more 
22than 45¢ per month per line or number for the statewide E-9-1-1 surcharge and 45¢ per 
23retail transaction for the prepaid wireless E-9-1-1 surcharge, which are the same amounts 
24as in the current law.  The bill requires the commission to establish the surcharges by 
25routine technical rules or through other commission proceedings.
