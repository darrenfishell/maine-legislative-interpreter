2Sec. 1.  Recalculation of benefits for certain Maine Public Employees 
3Retirement System retirees subject to merit pay freezes; retroactivity.  The 
4changes to the law governing the calculation of retirement benefits for members of the 
5Maine Public Employees Retirement System under Public Law 2015, chapter 267, Part 
6CCCC, section 1 and Public Law 2015, chapter 385, which allowed members who retired 
7on or after June 30, 2015 to pay the necessary member contributions to include in their 
8retirement benefit calculations wages lost due to merit pay freezes and longevity pay 
9freezes, apply retroactively to a member who retired on or after March 1, 2014 and prior 
10to June 30, 2015 back to the date of the member's retirement, except that such lost wages 
11may only include frozen merit pay and not frozen longevity pay.
12SUMMARY
13The law governing the calculation of retirement benefits for members of the Maine 
14Public Employees Retirement System was amended by Public Law 2015, chapter 267, 
15Part CCCC, section 1 and Public Law 2015, chapter 385 to allow members who retired 
16on or after June 30, 2015 to pay the necessary member contributions to include in their 
17retirement benefit calculations wages lost due to merit pay freezes and longevity pay 
18freezes.
19This bill authorizes a member of the Maine Public Employees Retirement System 
20who retired on or after March 1, 2014 and prior to June 30, 2015 to include in the 
21member's retirement benefit calculations wages lost due to merit pay freezes, retroactive 
22to the date of the member's retirement.  This provision does not include lost wages due to 
23longevity pay freezes.
