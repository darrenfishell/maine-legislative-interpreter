18SUMMARY
19This bill limits, by definition, the size of an all-terrain vehicle to 65 inches in width 
20and 2,000 pounds in weight, prohibits operation of motor vehicles on a designated all-
21terrain vehicle trail that is not on a gravel road system and prohibits operation of all-
22terrain vehicles on trails posted with size or weight limitations unless that use has been 
23authorized by the landowner or the landowner's agent.  It also increases all-terrain vehicle 
24registration fees by $12, which must be transferred to the ATV Recreational Management 
25Fund.
