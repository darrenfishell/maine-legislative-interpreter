SUMMARY 
This amendment makes the following changes to the bill. 
1. With regard to the fine for violating a court order banning temporary possession 
under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 17, section 1021 or 1034, the amendment 
removes the mandatory minimum fine of $50. 
2. With regard to the crime of cruelty to animals, the amendment adds to the 
prohibition on giving drugs, poison or alcohol to an animal that the act was done with the 
intent to harm or intoxicate the animal.
