12SUMMARY
13This resolve directs the Department of Economic and Community Development, 
14Office of Tourism, as established in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 13090-C, 
15to conduct a study on the feasibility of creating a state-operated online reservation service 
16in order to retain reservation booking commissions in the State.  The resolve also directs 
17the Office of Tourism to report the study findings and recommendations to the Joint 
18Standing Committee on Innovation, Development, Economic Advancement and Business 
19no later than January 15, 2020, and authorizes the joint standing committee to submit a 
20bill relating to the subject matter of the report to the Second Regular Session of the 129th 
21Legislature.
