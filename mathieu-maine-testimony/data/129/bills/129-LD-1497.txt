25SUMMARY
26Under current law, for the 2019-20 fiscal year, a system administration allocation of 
27$141 per pupil is established, with $47 being allocated to each school administrative unit 
28and an additional $94 being allocated as a targeted amount to school administrative units 
29that have established regionalized administrative services.
30This bill maintains the $141 allocation for fiscal year 2019-20, but provides $94 to 
31each school administrative unit and an additional $47 to school administrative units that 
32have established regionalized administrative services and to those school administrative 
33units that have been identified as high-performing, efficient school administrative units.
