22(2)  The clerk shall note the date of each petition form issued.  All petitions must 
23be filed within 120 days of the date of issue or they are void.
24(3)  Each petition form shall must have printed on its back an affidavit to be 
25executed by the circulator, stating:
26(a)  That the circulator personally circulated the form;
27(b)  The number of signatures on the form;
28(c)  That all the signatures were signed in the circulator's presence;
29(d)  That the circulator believes them to be genuine signatures of the persons 
30whose names they purport to be;
31(e)  That each signer has signed no more than one petition; and
32(f)  That each signer had an opportunity to read the petition before signing.

8SUMMARY
9This bill clarifies that a petition to form a charter commission to establish a new 
10municipal charter uses different language than a petition to form a charter commission to 
11revise a charter.
