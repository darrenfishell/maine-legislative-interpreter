SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill with a resolve that directs the Commissioner of 
Education and the Commissioner of Public Safety to jointly convene a working group to 
examine the use of extreme risk protection orders regarding individuals who pose a 
serious threat to themselves or others, as well as other proposals to improve school safety, 
and to present the findings and recommendations of the working group to the Joint 
Standing Committee on Judiciary by January 1, 2020. The amendment authorizes the 
committee to submit legislation relating to the recommendations of the working group to 
the Second Regular Session of the 129th Legislature.
