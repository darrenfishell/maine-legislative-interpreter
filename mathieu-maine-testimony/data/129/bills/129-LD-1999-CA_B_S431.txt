13 'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
14 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and 
15 Whereas, the Legislature established the Maine Education Savings Program, also 
16 known as NextGen 529, as Maine's Section 529 plan, so-called after Section 529 of the 
17 federal Internal Revenue Code of 1986, to be administered by the Finance Authority of 
18 Maine; and 
19 Whereas, the Maine Education Savings Program provides account owners and 
20 beneficiaries the opportunity to realize potential tax advantages if assets are used for 
21 qualified higher education expenses; and 
22 Whereas, the United States Congress has recently expanded the federal definition of 
23 qualified higher education expenses to include certain expenses related to registered and 
24 certified apprenticeship programs and limited repayments of qualified education loans; 
25 and 
26 Whereas, under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20-A, section 11483, the 
27 authority is authorized to take necessary action to ensure that the Maine Education 
28 Savings Program complies with federal law, and the authority considers it prudent to 
29 make statutory changes to conform with the expanded federal definition and to make 
30 certain other amendments to the law; and 
31 Whereas, in order to avoid confusion in the application and use of the program, it is 
32 necessary that these conforming changes take effect as soon as possible; and 
33 Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
34 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as

18 SUMMARY 
19 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, removes the change 
20 from mandatory to permissive the investment of fund dollars by the Finance Authority of 
21 Maine in state-based financial institutions. This amendment only amends certain 
22 provisions of the law in order to conform elements of the Maine Education Savings 
23 Program to recent changes to federal law affecting so-called Section 529 programs. This 
24 amendment also adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause to the bill.
