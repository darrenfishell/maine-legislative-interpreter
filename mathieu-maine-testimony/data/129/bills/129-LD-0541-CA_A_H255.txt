COMMITTEE AMENDMENT .. IJ,, to H.P. 398, L.D. 541, "Resolve, To Reduce 
Food Waste in Schools" 
Amend the resolve by adding after section 1 the following: 
' 'Sec. 2. Food share table guidance. Resolved: That the Department of 
Education and the Department of Health and Hmnan Services, Maine Center for Disease 
Control and Prevention shall collaborate to revise the Department of Health and Human 
Services, Maine Center for Disease Control and Prevention's health inspection program 
guidance titled "Food Sharing Tables -Guidance for Schools" in a manner that is less 
restrictive than current guidance and meets the requirements of the United States 
Department of Agriculture's Food and Nutrition Service guidance for the use of share 
tables in school nutrition programs. The Department of Education and the Department of 
Health and Human Services, Maine Center for Disease Control and Prevention shall 
disseminate the revised state guidance for the use of share tables, by January 1, 2020, to 
public school food service programs throughout the State.' 
Amend the resolve by relettering or renmnbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section nmnber to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment directs the Department of Education and the Department of Health 
and Hmnan Services, Maine Center for Disease Control and Prevention to collaborate to 
revise and disseminate by January 1, 2020 to public school food service programs 
throughout the State the Maine Center for Disease Control and Prevention's health 
inspection program guidance titled "Food Sharing Tables -Guidance for Schools."
