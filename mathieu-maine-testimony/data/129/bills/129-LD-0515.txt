5ยง2597.  Commercial food producer; retail location; ingredient information
61.  Definitions.  As used in this chapter, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
7following terms have the following meanings.
8A.  "Commercial food producer" means a person who prepares or processes food or 
9beverages for human consumption.
10B.  "Commissioner" means the Commissioner of Agriculture, Conservation and 
11Forestry.
12C.  "Retail location" means a place or establishment or premises where food or 
13beverage items are sold or offered for sale for consumption on or off the premises.
142.  Ingredient information.  A commercial food producer with a retail location shall 
15provide upon request a list of ingredients of each food and beverage offered for sale at the 
16retail location.
173.  Authority.  The commissioner shall enforce this chapter in the same manner as is 
18authorized for enforcement of chapter 551, subchapter 1.
19SUMMARY
20This bill requires a commercial food producer with a retail location to provide upon 
21request a list of ingredients of each food and beverage item offered for sale at the retail 
22location.
