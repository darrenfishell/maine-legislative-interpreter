16SUMMARY
17This bill includes ongoing General Fund appropriations of $3,000,000 per year to the 
18Shelter Operating Subsidy program within the Maine State Housing Authority to support 
19operations and capacity at low-barrier emergency homeless shelters across the State.  
20This bill specifies that the funding is supplemental to the Maine State Housing 
21Authority's emergency shelter and housing assistance program and is to be delivered 
22outside of the funding formula set forth in the Maine State Housing Authority's rule under 
2399-346 C.M.R. Chapter 19, Homeless Solutions Rule.GENERAL FUND 2019-20 2020-21
