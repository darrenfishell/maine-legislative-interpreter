2resolve and pursuant to its authority under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 35-A, 
3section 1321, the Public Utilities Commission shall amend "Order Granting Certificate of 
4Public Convenience and Necessity and Approving Stipulation," entered by the Public 
5Utilities Commission on May 3, 2019 in Docket No. 2017-00232 for the New England 
6Clean Energy Connect transmission project, referred to in this resolve as "the NECEC 
7transmission project."  The amended order must find that the construction and operation 
8of the NECEC transmission project are not in the public interest and that there is not a 
9public need for the NECEC transmission project.  There not being a public need, the 
10amended order must deny the request for a certificate of public convenience and necessity 
11for the NECEC transmission project.
12SUMMARY
13This initiated bill directs the Public Utilities Commission to amend "Order Granting 
14Certificate of Public Convenience and Necessity and Approving Stipulation," entered by 
15the Public Utilities Commission on May 3, 2019 for the New England Clean Energy 
16Connect transmission project.  The amended order must find that the construction and 
17operation of the NECEC transmission project are not in the public interest and that there 
18is not a public need for the NECEC transmission project.  There not being a public need, 
19the amended order must deny the request for a certificate of public convenience and 
20necessity for the NECEC transmission project.
