19SUMMARY
20This bill amends the school funding formula to fund after-school programs based on 
21the state share percentage and to fund public preschool programs at 50% of the cost of the 
22programs, or if a school administrative unit's state share percentage is greater than 50%, 
23to fund the unit's public preschool program at the state share percentage.  The bill also 
24increases the additional weight for economically disadvantaged students from 0.15 to 1.5.  
25The bill also raises the maximum debt service limit for school construction projects from
