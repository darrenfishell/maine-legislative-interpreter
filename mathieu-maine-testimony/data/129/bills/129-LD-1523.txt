9SUMMARY
10This bill adds definitions of "person recovering from substance use disorder" and 
11"recovery residence" to the laws governing the substance use disorder programs of the 
12Department of Health and Human Services and directs the department to establish a 
13voluntary certification process for recovery residences.  It also directs the Maine State 
14Housing Authority to create a pilot project to provide a short-term rental subsidy to a 
15person recovering from substance use disorder to reside in a certified recovery residence 
16that provides medication-assisted treatment.
17This bill also adds "recovery" to "substance use disorder prevention and treatment" in 
18the context of activities and services under the laws addressing alcohol and drug use.  The
