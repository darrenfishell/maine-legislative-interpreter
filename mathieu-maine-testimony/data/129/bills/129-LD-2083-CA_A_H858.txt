9COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “      ” to H.P. 1484, L.D. 2083, Bill, “An Act To 
10Require the Board of Pesticides Control To Annually Publish Certain Information 
11Regarding Pesticides and To Prohibit Certain Uses of Neonicotinoids”
12Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
13'Resolve, Directing the Board of Pesticides Control To Prohibit the Use of Certain 
14Neonicotinoids for Outdoor Use' 
15Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and inserting the following:
16'Sec. 1.  Prohibit the use of certain neonicotinoids for outdoor use.  
17Resolved: That, pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 7, section 610, the 
18Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Board of Pesticides Control shall 
19prohibit the use of any product containing the active ingredient dinotefuran, clothianidin, 
20imidacloprid or thiamethoxam used for application in outdoor residential landscapes such 
21as on lawn, turf or ornamental vegetation.  Products used for preserving wood, controlling 
22or treating indoor insects and treating pets, as defined under Title 7, section 712, 
23subsection 16, are specifically exempt from the prohibition under this section.  Rules 
24adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 
25375, subchapter 2-A.' 
26SUMMARY
27This amendment strikes and replaces the bill.  The amendment directs the Department 
28of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Board of Pesticides Control to prohibit the use 
29of any product containing certain neonicotinoids used for application in outdoor 
30residential landscapes such as on lawn, turf or ornamental vegetation.  The amendment 
31also provides that products used for preserving wood, controlling or treating indoor 
32insects and treating pets are specifically exempt from the prohibition.
