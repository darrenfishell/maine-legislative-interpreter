31 SUMMARY 
32 This amendment, which is the unanimous report of the committee, requires the 
33 Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages 
34 and Lottery Operations to notify each municipality in the State of the bureau's 
35 preliminary determination, based on the bureau's records of local option elections 
36 conducted in that municipality, whether the bureau may issue licenses for the sale of 
37 liquor by retail establishments in that municipality. Unlike the bill, this requirement 
38 applies not only to municipalities in which retail establishments are currently licensed for 
39 the sale of liquor but also to municipalities where liquor is not currently sold by licensed 
40 retail establishments. If a municipality disagrees with the bureau's preliminary 
41 determination that a type of retail liquor establishment may not be licensed in the 
42 municipality, it may, by July 1, 2022, either provide the bureau with proof of a previous 
43 local option election authorizing the sale of liquor by that type of retail establishment or
