8SUMMARY
9This bill requires the Public Utilities Commission, whenever it initiates a 
10management audit of a public utility, to also open an investigation into the same issues 
11that are the subject of the audit.  This investigation is an adjudicatory proceeding with full 
12rights of intervention and participation as allowed under the commission's procedural 
13rules.
