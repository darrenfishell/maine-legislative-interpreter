Page 1 - 129LR0093(01)-11 Sec. 1.  Universal job application system.   Resolved:  That the Department of 
2 Labor shall  create  a universal  job application  system for use in the State's career centers 
3 and make universal job applications available at each of the State's career centers.  The 
4 department shall work with public and private  employers in the State  in the creation and 
5 maintenance of  the universal job application system.
6 SUMMARY
7 This resolve directs the Department of Labor to create  a universal job application 
8 system for use in the State's  career centers  and make universal job applications  available 
9 at each of the centers.   It also requires the department to work  with public and private 
10 employers in the  creation and maintenance of the system.
