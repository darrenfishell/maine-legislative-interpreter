COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 325, L.D. 416, Bill, "An Act To Allow 
Eating Establishments To Permit Smoking Tobacco in Designated Outdoor Eating Areas" 
Amend the bill by strildng out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the 
following: 
'Sec. 1. 22 MRSA §1541, sub-§1, as amended by PL 2005, c. 257, §1, is further 
amended to read: 
1. Designated smoking area. "Designated smoking area" means an enclosed area 
or outdoor eating area designated as a place for smoking. A designated area must be 
designed to prevent smoke escaping from the designated area into a public place. 
Sec. 2. 22 MRSA §1541, sub-§2-A is enacted to read: 
2-A. Outdoor eating area. "Outdoor eating area" means a patio, deck or other 
property that is partially enclosed or open to the sky that is permitted for outdoor eating 
or drinking under the control of an eating establishment. as defmed in section 2491. 
subsection 7. as long as food or drink is served by the eating establishment to the public 
for consumption on the premises. 
Sec. 3. 22 MRSA §1542, sub-§1, as amended by PL 2009, c. 140, §1, is further 
amended to read: 
1. Prohibition. Smoking is prohibited in all enclosed areas of public places, om:door 
eating areas as pwvided in section 1550 and all rest rooms made available to the public. 
In the case of a child care facility that is not home"based, smoking is also prohibited in a 
facility-designated motor vehicle within 12 hours before transporting a child who is in the 
care of the child care facility, and whenever such a child is present in the vehicle. 
Smoking is also prohibited in outdoor areas of the facility where children may be present. 
Sec. 4. 22 MRSA §1542, sub-§2, 10 is enacted to read: 
0. Smoking is not prohibited in an outdoor eating area as provided by section 1550.

33 SUMMARY 
34 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, replaces the bill. It 
35 amends the laws about smoking in public places to not prohibit smoking in an outdoor 
36 eating area of an eating establishment as long as no one under 21 years of age is 
37 permitted in the outdoor eating area. It requires an eating establishment that allows 
38 smoking to prohibit employees of the eating establishment from providing dining and 
39 beverage service in an outdoor eating area, require its patrons to obtain and purchase food
