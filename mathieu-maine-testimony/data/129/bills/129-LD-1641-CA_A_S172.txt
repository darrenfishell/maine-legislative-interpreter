24 SUMMARY 
25 This amendment replaces the bill with a resolve that directs the Department of 
26 Education, in collaboration with the Department of Labor, to comprehensively examine 
27 issues associated with the school transportation workforce, including, but not limited to, 
28 determining the best strategies for hiring, training and retaining school transportation 
29 personnel. The department is required to submit a report with findings and 
30 recommendations to the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs and 
31 the Joint Standing Committee on Labor and Housing. Each committee is authorized to 
32 report out a bill on the subject matter of the report to the Second Regular Session of the 
33 129th Legislature.
