2Sec. 1.  29-A MRSA §664-A, sub-§1, as amended by PL 2009, c. 598, §29, is 
3further amended to read:
41.  Vehicle sold by dealer.  A vehicle that is sold by a dealer must be accompanied 
5by a properly assigned and valid certificate of title or certificate of salvage or a copy of a 
6valid certificate of title at the time of its sale. A dealer may retain and process certificates 
7of title and certificates of salvage at the dealer’s primary facility if in the case when the 
8dealer displays a vehicle at an annex facility the dealer maintains a copy of the certificate 
9of title or certificate of salvage at the annex facility.
10SUMMARY
11This bill allows a dealer selling a motor vehicle to use a copy of the valid certificate 
12of title instead of the original valid certificate of title.
