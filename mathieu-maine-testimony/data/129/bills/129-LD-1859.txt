2Sec. 1.  4 MRSA §807, sub-§5 is enacted to read:
35.  Practice by law students.  Notwithstanding any of the other provisions of this 
4chapter and under such terms, conditions, limitations, qualifications and supervision as 
5the Supreme Judicial Court by rule requires, a law student who is enrolled in and has 
6completed 3 or more full semesters or the equivalent at a law school accredited by the

8an agency of the State or under the supervision of an organization providing legal 
9services to the indigent approved by the Supreme Judicial Court on behalf of an 
10individual receiving services through the organization.

12repealed.
13SUMMARY
14This bill provides that a law student who is enrolled in and has completed 3 or more 
15full semesters or the equivalent at a law school accredited by the American Bar 
16Association may appear in court on behalf of the State or under the supervision of an 
17approved organization providing legal services to the indigent.
