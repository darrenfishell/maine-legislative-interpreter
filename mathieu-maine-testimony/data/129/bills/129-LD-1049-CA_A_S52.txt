8 SUMMARY 
9 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. The amendment allows, for a 
10 dog or cat with a life expectancy ofless than one year as determined by a veterinarian, the 
11 purchaser to choose to retain the dog or cat and receive a full refund for the original 
12 purchase price of the dog or cat. The amendment also provides that reimbursement of 
13 veterinarian fees by the seller is not required when a purchaser chooses to retain the dog 
14 or cat and receive a full refund for the original purchase price of the dog or cat. 
15 The amendment provides that sellers may not, contractually or otherwise, exempt 
16 themselves from the remedies provided for deaths or health problems in dogs and cats 
17 caused by hereditary or congenital defects.
