SQMMARY 
This amendment is the majority report of the committee and specifies that if a 
candidate requests that the candidate's nickname appear on the ballot for an election in the 
State, the candidate must include the nickname on the candidate's declaration of consent 
or written acceptance filed with the Secretary of State and must declare that the nickname 
is actually the name by which the candidate is lmown to others. The amendment also 
specifies that if a candidate requests that the candidate's nickname appear on the ballot for 
an election in the State, the Secretary of State must set off the candidate's nickname by 
quotation marks and it must be placed on the ballot following the candidate's legal last 
name, first initial and middle initial, if any.
