16SUMMARY
17This bill allows civilian employees in the employment of the Department of Public 
18Safety, Maine State Police Crime Laboratory or computer crimes unit to elect to 
19participate in the 1998 Special Plan of the Maine Public Employees Retirement System.  
20Under that plan, a person may retire at 55 years of age with 10 years of creditable service 
21or may retire before 55 years of age with 25 years of creditable service and at a reduced 
22benefit.
