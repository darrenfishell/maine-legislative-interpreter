27SUMMARY
28This bill defines "emergency services telecommunicator" and requires all emergency 
29services telecommunicators that provide dispatch for calls involving emergency medical 
30conditions to be trained in the delivery of cardiopulmonary resuscitation methods by 
31telecommunications technology.  The training must incorporate recognition protocols for 
32out-of-hospital cardiac arrest and compression-only cardiopulmonary resuscitation 
33instruction and provide for continuing education.  The bill requires the Department of 
34Public Safety and the Emergency Services Communication Bureau within the Public 
35Utilities Commission to establish a procedure to monitor compliance and allows the 
36department to sanction noncompliance by adjusting funding.
