51.  Definitions.  As used in this subchapter, unless the context otherwise indicates, 
6the following terms have the following meanings.
7A.  "Characterizing flavor" means a distinguishable taste or aroma of candy, 
8chocolate, vanilla, fruit, berry, nut, herb, spice, honey or an alcoholic drink that is 
9imparted to tobacco or tobacco smoke either prior to or during consumption. 
10"Characterizing flavor" does not include a taste or aroma from tobacco.  A cigar is 
11deemed to have a characterizing flavor if the cigar is advertised or marketed as 
12having or producing the taste or aroma of candy, chocolate, vanilla, fruit, berry, nut, 
13herb, spice, honey or an alcoholic drink, other than the taste or aroma of tobacco, 
14imparted either prior to or during consumption of a tobacco product or component 
15part thereof, including, but not limited to, tastes or aromas relating to any fruit, 
16chocolate, menthol, mint, wintergreen, vanilla, honey, candy, cocoa, dessert, 
17alcoholic beverage, herb or spice; however, a tobacco product may not be considered 
18to have a characterizing flavor solely because of the use of additives or flavorings or 
19the provision of ingredient information.
20B.  "Component part" includes but is not limited to the tobacco, filter and paper in a 
21cigar means any element of a tobacco product, including, but not limited to, the 
22tobacco, filter and paper, but not including any constituent.
23C.  "Constituent" means any ingredient, substance, chemical or compound, other than 
24tobacco, water or reconstituted tobacco sheet, that is added by the manufacturer to the 
25tobacco, paper or filter of a cigar a tobacco product during the processing, 
26manufacture or packing packaging of the cigar tobacco product. "Constituent" 
27includes a smoke constituent.
28C-1.  "Electronic smoking device" means a device used to deliver nicotine or any 
29other substance intended for human consumption that may be used by a person to 
30simulate smoking through inhalation of vapor or aerosol from the device, including, 
31without limitation, a device manufactured, distributed, marketed or sold as an 
32electronic cigarette, electronic cigar, electronic pipe, electronic hookah or so-called 
33vape pen.  "Electronic smoking device" includes any component, part or accessory of 
34such a device, whether or not sold separately, and includes any substance intended to 
35be aerosolized or vaporized during the use of the device.  "Electronic smoking 
36device" does not include drugs, devices or combination products authorized for sale 
37by the United States Food and Drug Administration, as those terms are defined in the 
38Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act.
39D.  "Flavored cigar tobacco product" means a cigar or any component part of the 
40cigar that contains a constituent that imparts a characterizing flavor tobacco product 
41or a component part of a tobacco product that contains a constituent that imparts a 
42characterizing flavor.

72.  Prohibition on sale or distribution of flavored tobacco products.  Except as 
8provided in subsection 5-A, a A person may not sell or distribute or offer to sell or 
9distribute in this State any flavored cigar unless the cigar is a premium cigar tobacco 
10product.
11A public statement or claim made or disseminated by the manufacturer of a tobacco 
12product, or by a person authorized or permitted by the manufacturer to make or 
13disseminate public statements concerning a tobacco product, that a tobacco product has or 
14produces a characterizing flavor constitutes presumptive evidence that the tobacco 
15product is a flavored tobacco product.
163.  Violation.  A person who violates this section commits a civil violation for which 
17fines may be imposed under subsection 4.
