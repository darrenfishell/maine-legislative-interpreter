4ยง3209-A.  Net energy billing
5The commission may adopt or amend rules governing net energy billing.  The 
6commission may not limit the installed capacity of an eligible facility or the number of 
7accounts or meters a customer or shared ownership customer may designate for net 
8energy billing. Rules adopted or amended under this section are routine technical rules as 
9defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.  "Net energy billing" means a billing and 
10metering practice under which a customer is billed on the basis of net energy over the 
11billing period taking into account accumulated unused kilowatt-hour credits from the 
12previous billing period.
13SUMMARY
14This bill provides that the Public Utilities Commission may not limit the installed 
15capacity of an eligible facility or the number of accounts or meters a customer or shared 
16ownership customer may designate for net energy billing.  Current commission rules limit 
17the capacity to 660 kilowatts and the number of meters or accounts to 10.
