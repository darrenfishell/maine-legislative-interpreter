36 SUMMARY
37 This bill directs the Commissioner of Marine Resources to ensure that a seat on the 
38 Shellfish Advisory Council is held by a member who has a demonstrated knowledge of a 
39 marine science and, at minimum, a bachelor's degree in a field of marine science.
