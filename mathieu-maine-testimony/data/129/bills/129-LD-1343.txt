14read:
15§4712.  Junior high school or middle school course of study
16The basic course of study for the junior high schools or middle schools must provide 
17for the instruction of all students in career and education development, English language 
18arts, health education and physical education, mathematics, science and technology,

24Sec. 3.  20-A MRSA §4723, as enacted by PL 1983, c. 859, Pt. C, §§5 and 7, is 
25amended to read:
26§4723.  Health and physical education
27The secondary course of study shall must include instruction in health, safety and 
28physical education, as prescribed by the commissioner, and physiology and hygiene, with 
29special reference to the effects of alcoholic drinks, stimulants and narcotics upon the 
30human system.  Instruction in health, safety and physical education must include at least 
311/2 hour per day of instruction in health education and one hour per day of instruction in 
32physical education.
33SUMMARY
34This bill requires instruction in elementary, junior high and secondary schools to 
35include at least 1/2 hour per day of instruction in health education and one hour per day 
36of instruction in physical education.
