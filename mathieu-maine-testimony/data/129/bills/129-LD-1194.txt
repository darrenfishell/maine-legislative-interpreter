25Sec. 2.  Application.  This Act applies to property tax years beginning on or after 
26April 1, 2020.
27SUMMARY
28This bill increases the property tax exemption beginning on or after April 1, 2020 
29from $6,000 to $10,000 of the just value of a home of a veteran who served in the Armed 
30Forces of the United States during specified periods or who is receiving compensation 
31from the United States Government for total, service-connected disability.
