3SUMMARY
4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to require the collection and cataloging of data on the history of 
6public buildings regarding abatement and contaminant testing, create new standards 
7regarding the level of contaminants allowed in public buildings, improve testing 
8requirements and better reinforce oversight of those buildings identified as having 
9contaminant issues.  This bill also seeks to improve the ability of state workers in public 
10buildings to raise and resolve safety concerns.
