13SUMMARY
14This resolve, which is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on Veterans and 
15Legal Affairs pursuant to Joint Order 2019, Senate Paper 381, does the following.
161.  It repeals Resolve 2017, chapter 18, which directs legislative staff to prepare a 
17recodification and revision of the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 28-A and to submit it to 
18the Joint Standing Committee on Veterans and Legal Affairs no later than January 15, 
192019.
202.  It directs the Office of Policy and Legal Analysis to prepare an analysis regarding 
21inconsistencies, duplications and ambiguities contained within the text of Title 28-A and, 
22on or before January 1, 2020, to submit that analysis to the Joint Standing Committee on 
23Veterans and Legal Affairs.
243.  It authorizes the Joint Standing Committee on Veterans and Legal Affairs to report 
25out legislation to the Second Regular Session of the 129th Legislature proposing revisions 
26to Title 28-A in response to the analysis submitted by the Office of Policy and Legal 
27Analysis.
