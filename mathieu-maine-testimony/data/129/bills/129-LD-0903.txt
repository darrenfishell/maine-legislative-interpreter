Page 3 - 129LR0868(01)-11SUMMARY
2This bill establishes 2 new tax brackets and 3 new rates for corporate income tax 
3purposes for tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2020.  Under the bill, corporate 
4income between $2,000,000 and $3,000,000 is taxed at the rate of 8.5% and corporate 
5income between $3,000,000 and $3,500,000 is taxed at the rate of 8.75%; such income is 
6taxed under current law at the rate of 8.33%.  The top tax rate, which is imposed on 
7corporate income over $3,500,000, is increased from 8.93% to 9%.
