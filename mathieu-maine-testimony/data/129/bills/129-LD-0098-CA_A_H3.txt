13 Amend the resolve by striking out all of section 1 and inserting the following: 
14 'Sec. 1. Adoption. Resolved: That final adoption of portions of Chapter 122: 
15 Grant Application and Award Procedure: Fund for the Efficient Delivery of Educational 
16 Services, a provisionally adopted major substantive rule of the Department of Education 
17 that has been submitted to the Legislature for review pursuant to the Maine Revised 
18 Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A within the rule acceptance period, is 
19 authorized only if the following changes are made. 
20 1. The rule must be amended in Section 5 as follows: the scoring of the applicant's 
21 capacity in Section 4, subsection' 2, paragraphs A and B will make up 20% of the total 
22 score; the scoring of the programmatic criteria in Section 4, subsection 2 will make up 
23 50% of the total score; the scoring of the budget and budget criteria in Section 4, 
24 subsection 3 will make up 25% of the total score; and the scoring of priority areas 
25 refe11Â·ed to in Section 4, subsection 4 will make up 5% of the total score. 
26 2. The rule must be amended in Section 6, subsection 2 by providing that an 
27 applicant or partner who receives a grant award from the fund in a given fiscal year is not 
28 eligible to apply for another grant award from the fund until one fiscal year after the end 
29 of the program, function or service funded by the initial grant, and any subsequent 
30 application from that applicant or partner must be for a new program, service or function 
31 that was not funded by the previous award from the fund. 
32 3. The rule must be amended in Section 6 by adding a subsection on the replication 
33 of grants that provides that the replication of a project of similar scope or service 
34 previously funded by an award from the fund is permissible by a new applicant and 
35 partners.
