22subchapter 2-A.
23SUMMARY
24This bill prohibits a postsecondary institution from assessing a student fees unless the 
25institution uses the standardized financial aid offer form developed by the Commissioner 
26of Education.  The bill directs the Commissioner of Education to develop a standard 
27format for written and electronic financial aid offers to students enrolled or accepted for 
28enrollment in a postsecondary institution.  The bill specifies that the standardized 
29financial aid offer form must clearly identify each student fee to be paid to the 
30postsecondary institution.
