Page 1 - 129LR1402(01)-11Sec. 1.  Department of Health and Human Services to amend rules.  
2Resolved:  That the Department of Health and Human Services shall amend its rules in 
3Chapter 101:  MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 67, Principles of 
4Reimbursement for Nursing Facilities to provide that Maine Veterans' Homes nursing 
5facilities are reimbursed for services provided to residents covered under Title XIX of the 
6United States Social Security Act based upon the actual cost of services provided.
7SUMMARY
8This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its 
9rules governing MaineCare reimbursement in order to provide cost-based reimbursement 
10to Maine Veterans' Homes nursing facilities.
