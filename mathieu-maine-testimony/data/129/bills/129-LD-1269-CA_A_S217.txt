18 SUMMARY 
19 This amendment makes the following changes to the bill. 
20 1. It defines "convertible child restraint system" and allows a child under 2 years of 
21 age properly secured in a convertible child restraint system to ride in a forward-facing 
22 position if the child exceeds the manufacturer recommended weight limit for a rear-facing 
23 position. 
24 2. It retains fmes for the violation of seat belt laws at current statutory levels. 
25 3. It removes the changes in the bill regarding the inadmissibility of evidence for 
26 failure to properly secure a child.
