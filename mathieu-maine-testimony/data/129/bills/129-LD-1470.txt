24SUMMARY
25This bill allows a political subdivision to prohibit the carrying of dangerous weapons 
26at public proceedings and at voting places.  It provides an exception to allow the carrying 
27of a handgun by an on-duty law enforcement officer.  It defines "political subdivision" as 
28any municipality, plantation, county, quasi-municipal corporation and special purpose 
29district, including, but not limited to, any water district, sanitary district, hospital district, 
30municipal transmission and distribution utility and school administrative unit.
