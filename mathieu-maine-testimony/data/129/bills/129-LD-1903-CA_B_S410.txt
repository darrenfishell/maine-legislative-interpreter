9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT ")?" to S.P. 655, L.D. 1903, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Amend the Laws Governing Activities at or near the Polls on Election Day" 
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
12 'An Act To Prohibit the Collection of Campaign Contributions at or near the Polls 
13 on Election Day' 
14 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the 
15 following: 
16 'Sec. 1. 21-A MRSA §682, sub-§1-A is enacted to read: 
17 1-A. Collection of campaign contributions prohibited. On election day, on public 
18 property within 250 feet of the entrance to the voting place as well as within the voting 
19 place itself, a person may not solicit or accept contributions for a campaign as defined in 
20 section 1052, subsection 1 or for the purposes of qualifying a Maine Clean Election Act 
21 candidate pursuant to section 1125. For purposes of this subsection, "contribution" 
22 includes a contribution as defined in section 1012, subsection 2 and a contribution 
23 described in section 1052, subsection 3, whether made to a political action committee or 
24 to a ballot question committee.' 
25 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
26 section number to read consecutively. 
27 SUMMARY 
28 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, strikes and replaces 
29 the bill and provides a new title. The amendment prohibits the solicitation or acceptance 
30 of contributions for a campaign for the nomination or election of a candidate or a 
31 campaign to initiate or influence a ballot measure on public property within 250 feet of 
32 the entrance to the voting place as well as within the voting place itself on the day of an 
33 election.
