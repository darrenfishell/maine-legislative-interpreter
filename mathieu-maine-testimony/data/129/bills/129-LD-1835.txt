25SUMMARY
26This bill requires the State Tax Assessor, upon the recommendation of the 
27Commissioner of Economic and Community Development or at the direction of the 
28Governor, to transfer anticipated funds to the Loring Job Increment Financing Fund prior 
29to the July 31, 2019 payment date.
