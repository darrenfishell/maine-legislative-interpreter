41SUMMARY
42This bill amends the child support enforcement laws to implement background 
43investigative checks for employees, prospective employees and contractors with access to 
44federal tax information in compliance with the United States Internal Revenue Services' 
Page 5 - 129LR2317(01)-11Publication 1075, which requires fingerprinting of all affected individuals. This bill 
2amends provisions relating to the Department of Public Safety, Bureau of State Police, 
3State Bureau of Identification that set forth the procedures for taking and processing 
4fingerprints. This bill authorizes the department to adopt rules to conduct background 
5checks.  This bill also corrects a conflict created by Public Law 2017, chapters 452 and 
6457, which affected the same provision of law, by incorporating the changes made by 
7both laws.
