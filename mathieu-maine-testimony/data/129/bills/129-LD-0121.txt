313.  Firearm safety and firearm violence prevention program.  Beginning July 1, 
42020 and annually thereafter, the commissioner shall develop and implement a program 
5focused on increasing the awareness and education of the public on matters relating to 
6firearm safety and the prevention of firearm violence.  In developing and implementing 
7the program, the commissioner may consult with public and private entities with 
8expertise in firearm safety and the prevention of firearm violence, including, but not 
9limited to, the Department of Public Safety.  The program may include, but is not limited 
10to, print, radio, television and Internet advertising, the distribution of written materials 
11and the offering or facilitation of public events.
12SUMMARY
13This bill requires the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to develop and 
14implement a program focused on increasing the awareness and education of the public on 
15matters relating to firearm safety and the prevention of firearm violence.  The program 
16may include, but is not limited to, print, radio, television and Internet advertising, the 
17distribution of written materials and the offering or facilitation of public events.
