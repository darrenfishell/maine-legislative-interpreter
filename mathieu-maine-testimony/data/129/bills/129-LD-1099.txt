41.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
5following terms have the following meanings.
6A.  "Agreement" means when a buyer and a seller reach an agreement for the 
7purchase and the sale of a firearm.
8B.  "Firearm" has the same meaning as in Title 17-A, section 2, subsection 12-A.
9C.  "Firearm dealer" means a person that is licensed as a dealer under 18 United 
10States Code, Section 923 or that is required to be licensed as a dealer under that 
11section.
122.  Waiting period.  A person may not knowingly deliver a firearm pursuant to a sale 
13without withholding delivery of the firearm for at least 72 hours after agreement.
143.  Penalty.  This subsection applies to violations of subsection 2.
15A.  A person that violates subsection 2 commits a civil violation for which a fine of 
16not less than $200 and not more than $500 may be adjudged.
17B.  A person that violates subsection 2 after having previously been adjudicated as 
18violating subsection 2 commits a civil violation for which a fine of not less than $500 
19and not more than $1,000 may be adjudged.
204.  Application.  This section does not apply to:
21A.  The sale of a firearm if the seller knows that the person to whom the seller is 
22selling the firearm is:
23(1)  A law enforcement officer; or
24(2)  A person who desires to purchase a firearm for use in promoting the public 
25interest incidentally to the person's employment as a bank guard or armed truck 
26guard or as a similar type of employee; or
27B.  The sale of a firearm to a firearm dealer.
28A firearm dealer is subject to the requirements of conducting national instant criminal 
29background checks under 18 United States Code, Section 922(t).
30SUMMARY
31This bill requires a 72-hour waiting period between an agreement for the purchase 
32and sale of a firearm and its delivery to the purchaser and makes violation of the waiting 
33period a civil violation with a $200 to $500 fine for the first violation and a $500 to 
34$1,000 fine for a subsequent violation.
