1 SUMMARY 
2 This amendment changes the title and strikes the bill, adding an emergency preamble 
3 and clause. It amends the statutory reporting requirements of the State Compensation 
4 Commission, eliminating alternate year interim reports and changing the fmal report 
5 submission to require the State Compensation Commission to submit a report to the 
6 Legislature and the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over 
7 state and local government matters by January 15th of every even-numbered year. The 
8 amendment authorizes the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction 
9 over state and local government matters to introduce legislation based on the 
10 commission's report.
