17SUMMARY
18This bill requires the State to fund a minimum of 50% of the costs of an approved 
19public preschool program for each year a school administrative unit operates the program, 
20other than the year in which the school administrative unit receives start-up funds for the 
21program.  If funds for essential programs and services do not provide the funding 
22necessary to fund a minimum of 50% of approved public preschool programs, the State is 
23directed to use other funding sources and the Commissioner of Education is directed to 
24include the funding levels necessary in the Department of Education's request to the 
25Legislature for appropriations from the General Fund.
