SUMMARY 
This amendment changes from 4 mils to 2.25 mils the thickness of the plastic bags 
that qualify as reusable. This amendment also provides that the revenue collected by 
retail establishments for providing recyclable or reusable bags must be distributed to a 
conservation organization identified in major substantive rules adopted by the 
Department of Environmental Protection.
