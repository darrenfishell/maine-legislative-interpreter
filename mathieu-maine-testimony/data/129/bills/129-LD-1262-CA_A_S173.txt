9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A " to S.P. 382, L.D. 1262, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Allow Funds from the Federal E-Rate Program To Be Applied to Maine Preschool 
11 Programs" 
12 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the 
13 following: 
14 'Sec. 1. 20-A MRSA §1, sub-§23-A, as enacted by PL 2007, c. 141, §2, is 
15 amended to read: 
16 23-A. Public preschool program. "Public preschool program" means a program 
1 7 offered by a public elementary school pursuant to chapter 203 that provides instruction to 
18 children who are 4 years of age, including but not limited to a Head Start program that is 
19 approved as a component of the public preschool program.' 
20 SUMMARY 
21 This amendment strikes and replaces the bill, which is a concept draft. The 
22 amendment enables public preschool programs to be eligible for the Federal E-Rate 
23 Program by amending the definition of "public preschool program" to mean a program 
24 offered by a public elementary school that provides instruction to children who are 4 
25 years of age, including but not limited to a Head Start program that is approved as a 
26 component of the public preschool program.
