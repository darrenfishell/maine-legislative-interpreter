40Sec. 4.  12 MRSA §6502-C is enacted to read:
Page 2 - 129LR3017(01)-11§6502-C.  Commercial menhaden fishing license
21.  License required.  A person may not engage in the activities authorized under 
3this section without a current commercial menhaden fishing license.
42.  License category.  At the time of purchase an applicant for a commercial 
5menhaden fishing license shall select a license category from the following options:
6A.  State allocation; or
7B.  Small scale.
8A person may select only one license category for a commercial menhaden fishing 
9license.  The small scale license category may be selected only by an individual who 
10holds a license issued under section 6421, subsection 1, paragraph A, B, C or E.
113.  Licensed activity.  The holder of a commercial menhaden fishing license may 
12fish for or take or possess, ship, transport or sell menhaden that the holder has taken.  The 
13commercial menhaden fishing license also authorizes the crew members aboard the 
14vessel named on the license to fish for or take or possess, ship or transport menhaden 
15when the license holder is aboard the vessel. 
164.  Exemption.  The licensing requirement under subsection 1 does not apply to a 
17person who fishes for, takes, possesses or transports menhaden that have been taken by 
18speargun, harpoon, minnow trap, hand dip net or hook and line and are only for personal 
19use.
205.  Rules.  The commissioner shall determine by rule any limitations on the license 
21categories for a commercial menhaden fishing license. Rules adopted pursuant to this 
22subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
236.  Eligibility.  A commercial menhaden fishing license may be issued only to an 
24individual who is a resident.  An individual may not hold more than one commercial 
25menhaden fishing license.
267.  Fees.  Fees for commercial menhaden fishing licenses are:
27A.  One hundred twenty-eight dollars for a commercial menhaden fishing license, 
28state allocation category; and
29B.  Forty-eight dollars for a commercial menhaden fishing license, small scale 
30category.
318.  Surcharges.  A $50 surcharge is assessed on a holder of a commercial menhaden 
32fishing license issued by the department.  The commissioner shall deposit surcharges 
33collected pursuant to this subsection in the Pelagic and Anadromous Fisheries Fund 
34established under section 6041.
359.  Violation.  A person who violates this section commits a civil violation for which 
36a fine of not less than $100 nor more than $500 may be adjudged.

2This bill removes the authority to fish for Atlantic menhaden from the commercial 
3pelagic and anadromous fishing license and creates a new commercial menhaden fishing 
4license with 2 license categories.
