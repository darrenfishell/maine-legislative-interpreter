20SUMMARY
21This amendment makes the following changes to the bill.
221.  It adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause to the bill.
232.  It changes the definition of "lodging place" as a place offering stays that consist of 
2490 or fewer consecutive days as in the bill to one offering stays of fewer than 183 
25aggregate days a year.  It changes the definition of "permanent residence" used in the 
26licensing laws to mean the primary location where an individual lives for 183 days or 
27more in a year.
283.  It clarifies that the term "lodging place" does not include assisted living programs, 
29residential care facilities or nursing facilities.
304.  It includes public pools and public spas in the fee section of the licensing laws to 
31clarify the fee charged for licensing a public pool or public spa.
325.  It removes the section on confidential information from the bill.
336.  It corrects the placement of sections regarding license renewal and conditional 
34licenses within the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 32.
