SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill. It changes the references in the bill to cite the 
Adult Protective Services Act in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 22, chapter 958-A 
when requiring that the Department of Health and Human Services include reports of 
persons who have exploited, abused or neglected incapacitated and dependent adults in its 
system as provided in Title 34-B.
