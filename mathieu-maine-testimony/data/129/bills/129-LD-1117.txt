20Wildlife shall amend the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife's rules to change 
21the wild turkey bag limit during the spring open season to 4 male wild turkeys and during 
22the fall open season to 8 wild turkeys of either sex pursuant to the Maine Revised 
23Statutes, Title 12, section 11701.
24SUMMARY
25This bill eliminates the turkey hunting permit and fee and the requirement to tag wild 
26turkeys at registration stations.  A hunter is directed to inform the Department of Inland 
27Fisheries and Wildlife at the conclusion of each turkey hunting season of how many wild 
28turkeys the hunter harvested.  It directs the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and 
29Wildlife to amend the department's rules to increase the spring season bag limit to 4 male 
30wild turkeys and the fall season bag limit to 8 wild turkeys of either sex.
