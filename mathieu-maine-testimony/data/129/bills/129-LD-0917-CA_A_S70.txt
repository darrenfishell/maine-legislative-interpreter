22 SUMMARY 
23 This amendment allows, but does not require, municipal agents to charge a service 
24 fee for registration of a motor vehicle, up to $5 for a renewal and up to $6 for a new 
25 registration. It also allows, but does not require, municipal agents to charge a service fee 
26 for registration of an all-terrain vehicle, snowmobile or watercraft, up to $3 for a renewal 
27 and up to $5 for a new registration.
