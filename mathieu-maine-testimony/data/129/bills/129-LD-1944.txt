25SUMMARY
26This bill extends the current home accessibility adaptations available to MaineCare 
27members receiving services under rule Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, 
28Chapters II and III, Sections 21 and 29 to persons providing shared living housing to a 
29member receiving these services.  The bill also requires that the Department of Health 
30and Human Services evaluate the effectiveness of contracts for services for adults with 
31diagnoses of intellectual disabilities or other developmental disabilities.
