Page 1 - 129LR1274(01)-11Sec. 1.  Air emissions report revision.  Resolved:  That the Department of 
2Environmental Protection shall review and update its report submitted to the Legislature 
3dated January 15, 2005 and concerning issues related to air emissions from marine 
4vessels.  As part of the revision and update under this section, the department shall 
5consult with stakeholders in determining the issues to be addressed and recommendations 
6for action by the Legislature.
7Sec. 2.  Findings and report.  Resolved:  That the Department of Environmental 
8Protection shall present its findings and the updated report pursuant to section 1, 
9including suggested legislation to implement any proposals, by January 15, 2020 to the 
10Joint Standing Committee on Environment and Natural Resources and the Joint Standing 
11Committee on Marine Resources.  The Joint Standing Committee on Environment and 
12Natural Resources and the Joint Standing Committee on Marine Resources may submit 
13legislation related to the report to the Second Regular Session of the 129th Legislature.
14SUMMARY
15This resolve requires the Department of Environmental Protection to review and 
16update its report submitted to the Legislature dated January 15, 2005 and concerning 
17issues related to air emissions from marine vessels.  The department is required to present 
18its findings and the updated report by January 15, 2020 to the Joint Standing Committee 
19on Environment and Natural Resources and the Joint Standing Committee on Marine 
20Resources.  The committees are authorized to submit legislation to the Second Regular 
21Session of the 129th Legislature.
