4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to enact measures designed to provide flexibility in the regulation 
6of development near the shore.  The purpose of the bill is to encourage the development 
7of creative approaches to protect beaches and critical near-shore infrastructure threatened 
8by sea level rise and storm damage.
