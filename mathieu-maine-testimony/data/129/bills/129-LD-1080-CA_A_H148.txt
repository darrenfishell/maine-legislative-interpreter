This bill requires the Governor's Energy Office (GEO) to direct a study on the environmental and economic feasibility 
of setting a requirement for the percentage ofbiofuel to be used in various heating fuels. The GEO has indicated that 
it does not have the current capacity to complete this study within existing budgeted resources and the required 
timeframe. The bill includes a one-time appropriation to the GBO of$100,000 in fiscal year 2019-20 for contracted 
services to complete the study. Additional costs to various other state agencies to consult with the GEO can be 
absorbed within existing budgeted resources.
