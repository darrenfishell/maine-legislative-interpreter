SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, amends the bill by 
removing the requirement that the Department of Economic and Community 
Development, Office of Community Development provide both basic and advanced 
training to code enforcement officers in the technical and legal aspects of code 
enforcement and instead removes from existing law the designation that the training 
program required by statute be basic.
