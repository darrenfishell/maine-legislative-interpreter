11SUMMARY
12This bill amends the law governing raffles for certain nonprofit organizations in the 
13following ways:
141.  Increasing the amount of total value of all prizes for which the nonprofit 
15organization is not required to register the raffle with the Gambling Control Unit from 
16$2,500 to $10,000;
172.  Allowing noncash raffle prizes to be in the alternate form of cash or to be 
18exchanged for cash;
193.  Removing the limitation allowing only one raffle with a noncash prize up to 
20$75,000 and a cash prize up to $20,000 in a 12-month period; and
214.  Clarifying that the nonprofit organization may conduct more than one raffle at a 
22time.
