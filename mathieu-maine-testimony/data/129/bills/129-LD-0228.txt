10Sec. 3.  32 MRSA §6216-A is enacted to read:
11§6216-A.  Time frame for granting or denying licenses
12The board shall grant or deny a license under this subchapter within 30 days of the 
13filing of the application under this subchapter.
14SUMMARY
15This bill requires the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation, State 
16Board of Alcohol and Drug Counselors to grant or deny a license to practice as an alcohol 
17and drug counseling aide, certified alcohol and drug counselor, licensed alcohol and drug 
18counselor or certified clinical supervisor within 30 days of receipt of the application for 
19that license.  This bill also clarifies the authority of the State Board of Alcohol and Drug 
20Counselors to license certified clinical supervisors.
