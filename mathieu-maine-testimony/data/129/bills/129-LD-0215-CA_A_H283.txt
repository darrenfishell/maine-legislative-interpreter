The bill provides general fund appropriations of$52,500 in FY 2019-20 and $70,000 in FY 2020-21 to increase the 
reimbursement rate for ambulance services paid for by the Department of Corrections. According to data provided by 
the department, total reimbursements for ambulance services have averaged about $55,600 per fiscal year. Increasing 
this amount by 225% to reflect an increased reimbursement rate would be $125,100, raising total reimbursements by 
$70,000 annually.
