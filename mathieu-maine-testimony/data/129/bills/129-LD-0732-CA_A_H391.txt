The bill provides a sales tax exemption for sales to career and technical student organizations and would result in a 
reduction in General Fund revenue of $1,3 3 0 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $1,957 in fiscal year 2020-21. It would also 
result in a reduction in Local Government Fund revenue of $70 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $103 in fiscal year 2020-21. 
The bill includes a one-time General Fund appropriation of $5,000 in fiscal year 2019-20 to the Depatiment of 
Administrative and Financial Services for computer programming costs to create an exemption certificate.
