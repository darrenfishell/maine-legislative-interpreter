13SUMMARY
14Currently, under the Educators for Maine Program, an eligible undergraduate may 
15receive a loan of up to $3,000 per academic year or $12,000 total.  This bill increases 
16those loan amounts to $9,000 per academic year and $36,000 total.  It also increases from 
17$20,000 to $44,000 the total loan amount under the program a student may receive in 
18undergraduate and postbaccalaureate loans.
