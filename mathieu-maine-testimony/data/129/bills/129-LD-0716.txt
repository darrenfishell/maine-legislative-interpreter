8SUMMARY
9This bill requires a railroad company to ensure that there are at least 2 employees on 
10a freight train traveling on a railroad line within the State, except for a freight train 
11traveling for hostler service.  It provides that a railroad company that violates this 
12provision is subject to a fine of not more than $500 for each violation.
