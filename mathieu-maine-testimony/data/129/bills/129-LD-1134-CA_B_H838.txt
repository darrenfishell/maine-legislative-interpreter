11SUMMARY
12This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, replaces the bill with 
13a resolve.  It directs the Department of Health and Human Services to, no later than 90 
14days after the effective date of this legislation, enter into a memorandum of understanding 
15with the federally recognized Indian nations, tribes and bands in the State to define and 
16implement meaningful tribal consultation for all block grants received by the State.  It 
17also requires the department to report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature 
18having jurisdiction over health and human services matters on the status of the 
19memorandum of understanding no later than January 31, 2021.
