17SUMMARY
18This bill amends the Maine State Grant Program, which is a grant program 
19administered by the Finance Authority of Maine to award grants to Maine residents who 
20are students at postsecondary institutions of higher education in this State based on 
21financial need.  Under the program, the minimum grant is $1,000 per year.
22This bill allows the authority, using the same need and residency criteria as for the 
23grants, to award up to an additional $500 grant for textbooks per year.
