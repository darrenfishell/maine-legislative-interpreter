17calculation methodology and statute or other anomalies affecting comparative 
18outcomes.
19SUMMARY
20This bill directs the Commissioner of Education to annually post on the Department 
21of Education's publicly accessible website statewide historical financial data on education 
22funding for a period of no less than 10 years.
