49.  Stationary vehicles.  The operator of a vehicle passing a stationary authorized 
5emergency vehicle using an emergency light or a stationary public service vehicle using 
6its authorized lights, with due regard to the safety and traffic conditions, shall:
7A.  Pass in a lane not adjacent to that of the authorized emergency vehicle or public 
8service vehicle, if possible; or
9B.  If passing in a nonadjacent lane is impossible or unsafe, pass the emergency 
10vehicle or public service vehicle at a careful and prudent speed reasonable for passing 
11the authorized emergency vehicle or public service vehicle safely.
12A violation of this subsection is a traffic infraction for which a minimum fine of $250 
13$350 must be adjudged.
14SUMMARY
15This bill increases from $250 to $350 the minimum fine for passing a stationary 
16authorized emergency vehicle using an emergency light or a stationary public service 
17vehicle using its authorized lights and failing to pass in a lane that is not adjacent to the 
18stationary vehicle or, if passing in a nonadjacent lane would be impossible or unsafe, 
19failing to pass at a careful and prudent speed.
