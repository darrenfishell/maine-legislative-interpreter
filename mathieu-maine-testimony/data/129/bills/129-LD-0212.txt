19SUMMARY
20This bill increases ATV registration fees from $33 to $45 for annual registrations for 
21residents, from $53 to $65 for 7-day registrations for nonresidents and from $68 to $80 
22for one-year registrations for nonresidents. The bill provides that the increased fee 
23revenue be deposited in the ATV Recreational Management Fund.
