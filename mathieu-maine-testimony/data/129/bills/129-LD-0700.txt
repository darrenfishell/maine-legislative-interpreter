25SUMMARY
26This bill creates the crime of organized electronic theft, of which a person is guilty if 
27the person commits 2 or more thefts under the Maine Criminal Code pursuant to a 
28scheme or course of conduct involving thefts committed by electronic means.  "Thefts 
29committed by electronic means" includes, but is not limited to, thefts committed using 
30Internet-based sales platforms and Internet-based fundraising, including so-called 
31crowdfunding platforms.  The severity of punishment for the crime of organized 
32electronic theft is dependent upon the total value of the property stolen.  The bill also 
33includes an appropriation to establish a computer forensic analyst position within the 
34Department of Public Safety dedicated to the investigation of organized electronic theft.GENERAL FUND 2019-20 2020-21
