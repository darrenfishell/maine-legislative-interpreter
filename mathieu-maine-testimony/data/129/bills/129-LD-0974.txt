10Class D E crime.
11SUMMARY
12This bill amends the penalty provision for the crime of engaging in prostitution.  For 
13a first offense, the bill retains the current law designation of the crime as a Class E crime.  
14For a 2nd or subsequent conviction within 2 years of engaging in prostitution, the bill 
15changes the designation from a Class D crime to a Class E crime.
