6SUMMARY
7The bill provides that:
81.  Farm owners or operators in the State may not knowingly confine an egg-laying 
9hen in an enclosure that is not a cage-free housing system or in an enclosure with less 
10than a certain amount of usable floor space per egg-laying hen; and
112. Business owners or operators may not knowingly sell in the State eggs from egg-
12laying hens that are not kept in cage-free housing systems.
13This amendment, which is the minority report, changes the date the prohibitions will 
14go into effect from after December 31, 2024 to after December 31, 2029.
15The amendment also provides that the prohibitions does not apply to a farm owner or 
16operator or a business owner or operator who owns or leases fewer than 3,000 adult egg-
17laying hens at a given time in the State and whose hens' eggs will be consumed by 
18humans.
