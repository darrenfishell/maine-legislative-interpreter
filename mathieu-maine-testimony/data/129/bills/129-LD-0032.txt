Page 1 - 129LR0424(01)-11Sec. 1.  Designate portion of U.S. Route 1.  Resolved:  That the Department of 
2Transportation shall designate U.S. Route 1 from the point 0.78 mile north of the Dodge 
3Road in the Town of Whiting to the boundary line between the Town of Whiting and 
4Edmunds Township the Brigadier General John Crane Memorial Highway. 
5Sec. 2.  New Crane Bridge renamed.  Resolved:  That the Department of 
6Transportation shall designate Bridge 2194 in the Town of Whiting, currently known as 
7the New Crane Bridge, the Purple Heart Veterans Memorial Bridge.
8SUMMARY
9This resolve requires the Department of Transportation to designate a portion of U.S. 
10Route 1 in the Town of Whiting the Brigadier General John Crane Memorial Highway 
11and to rename the New Crane Bridge in the Town of Whiting the Purple Heart Veterans 
12Memorial Bridge.
