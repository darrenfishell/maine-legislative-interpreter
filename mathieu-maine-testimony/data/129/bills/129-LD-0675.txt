30SUMMARY
31This bill amends the provisions regarding surcharges on lobster and crab fishing 
32licenses that fund the Lobster Promotion Fund by replacing the current 3-tiered structure 
33for promotion surcharges on the 3 classes of lobster and crab fishing licenses with a 
34single promotion surcharge of $310 for all 3 classes of licenses.
