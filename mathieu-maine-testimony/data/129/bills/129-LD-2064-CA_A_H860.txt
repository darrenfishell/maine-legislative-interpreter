9SUMMARY
10This amendment removes the provision of the bill that states the Department of 
11Transportation's payment of the remaining portion of the cost of a capital improvement to 
12a bridge on a town way is subject to available funds.  The amendment also makes minor 
13changes to clarify provisions in the bill and clarifies that, if a culvert or a minor span is 
14changed into a bridge, that change is subject to the department's approved design plans 
15and specifications.
