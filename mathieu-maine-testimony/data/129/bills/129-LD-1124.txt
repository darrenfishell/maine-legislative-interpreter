2Sec. 1.  12 MRSA §10308, sub-§5, ¶B, as enacted by PL 2003, c. 414, Pt. A, §2 
3and affected by c. 614, §9, is amended to read:
4B.  The board shall review and award annually funding requests for specific projects 
5from natural resources agencies.  The board may award grants only to proposals that 
6conform to the strategic plan adopted pursuant to paragraph A.  Grant proposals that 
7establish a public-private partnership are encouraged.  Grant proposals must include a 
8stated purpose, a time line, potential outcomes, a budget and an explanation of need.  
9No more than 20% of all grant funds may be awarded to projects of natural resources 
10agencies.  Members of the board who are members of natural resources agencies may 
11not participate in decisions awarding funding to their natural resources agencies.
12SUMMARY
13This bill limits the Maine Outdoor Heritage Fund Board to awarding no more than 
1420% of all grant funds to projects of the State's natural resources agencies.  It also 
15prohibits members of the Maine Outdoor Heritage Fund Board who are members of 
16natural resources agencies from participating in decisions awarding funding to their 
17natural resources agencies.
