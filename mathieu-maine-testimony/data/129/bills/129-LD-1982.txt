Page 1 - 129LR3047(01)-11Sec. 1.  Study to develop best practices for improving the security of 
2municipal properties.  Resolved:  That the Department of Public Safety shall 
3conduct a study to develop best practices that municipalities may reference to improve 
4the security of municipal properties, including, but not limited to, buildings, libraries, 
5parks and public open spaces.
6Sec. 2.  Report.  Resolved:  That, no later than December 31, 2020, the 
7Department of Public Safety shall submit a report of its study to develop best practices 
8under section 1 to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over 
9criminal justice and public safety matters.  The joint standing committee may submit a 
10bill based on the department's report to the First Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.
11SUMMARY
12This resolve directs the Department of Public Safety to conduct a study to develop 
13best practices that municipalities may reference to improve the security of municipal 
14properties. The resolve requires the department to submit a report of its study to the joint 
15standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over criminal justice and public 
16safety matters no later than December 31, 2020.
