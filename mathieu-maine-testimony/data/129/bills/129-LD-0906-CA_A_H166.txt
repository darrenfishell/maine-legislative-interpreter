SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, amends the bill as 
follows. 
1. It amends the definition of "polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons" to include a 
reference to the federal Comprehensive Environmental Response, Compensation, and 
Liability Act of 1980. 
2. It clarifies the scope of the bill's sales and application prohibitions or coal tar 
sealant products.
