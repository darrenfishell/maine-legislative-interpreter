13 'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not

20 SUMMARY 
21 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, retains the change 
22 from mandatory to permissive the investment of fund dollars by the Finance Authority of 
23 Maine in state-based financial institutions. This amendment also amends certain 
24 provisions of the law in order to conform elements of the Maine Education Savings 
25 Program to recent changes to federal law affecting so-called Section 529 programs. This 
26 amendment also adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause to the bill.
