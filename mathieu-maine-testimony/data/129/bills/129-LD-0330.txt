28SUMMARY
29This bill clarifies that massage therapists and massage practitioners are health care 
30practitioners and that massage therapy is a health care practice.  It prohibits unlicensed 
31persons from claiming to provide procedures that are described as "massage," 
32"bodywork," "body therapy," "manual therapy," "neuromuscular therapy," "touch 
33therapy" or "myotherapy" or related terms.
