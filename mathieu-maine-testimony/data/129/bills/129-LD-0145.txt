27SUMMARY
28This bill increases the membership of the Wild Blueberry Commission of Maine from 
298 members to 14 members and requires that one member be a member of a federally 
30recognized Indian nation, tribe or band in the State, one member be an organic grower, 
31one member be a fresh packer, one member be the leader of a cooperative and one 
32member be a representative of a value-added organization or company.  The bill also 
33increases the number of members who are grower representatives and decreases the 
34number of members who are processor representatives who process 1,000,000 pounds or 
35more of wild blueberries in a calendar year.  The bill specifies that members may not 
36serve consecutive terms.
