12SUMMARY
13This bill adds dispatchers to the categories of participating local district employees 
14for which a special retirement plan is available.  Participating local districts that elect to 
15cover their dispatchers by a more favorable special plan are responsible for the payment 
16of any increased employer costs associated with such an election.  Depending upon the 
17plan that is elected, dispatchers may also pay an increased member contribution rate for 
18participation in the more favorable special plan.
