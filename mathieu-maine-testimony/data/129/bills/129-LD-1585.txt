Page 1 - 129LR2460(01)-11Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
2become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
3Whereas, the Legislature has established a municipal tax increment financing 
4program, pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, chapter 206, as a local 
5economic development tool to permit municipalities to finance new development 
6projects; and 
7Whereas, the City of Augusta may suffer financial liability because it did not timely 
8complete the steps statutorily required for designation of a municipal tax increment 
9financing district; and
10Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
11the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
12immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
13therefore,
14Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
15Sec. 1.  Authorization to adjust the definition of original assessed value.  
16Notwithstanding the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, section 5222, subsection 13, the 
17City of Augusta may define "original assessed value" for the Performance Food Group 
18municipal tax increment financing district designated on July 2, 2016 as the assessed 
19value of the district as of March 31, 2016.
20Sec. 2.  Authorization to adjust the start date of the district.  
21Notwithstanding the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, chapter 206, the effective date 
22of the Performance Food Group municipal tax increment financing district is July 1, 
232017, with a duration of up to 30 consecutive years.
24Sec. 3.  Assessment, tax commitment and tax collection of property taxes 
25dedicated for the Performance Food Group district for fiscal years 2018-19 
26and 2019-20 are ratified, made legal and valid.  Notwithstanding the Maine 
27Revised Statutes, Title 36, section 504, the assessment, commitment and collection of the 
28portion of the assessed property taxes for the fiscal years 2018-19 and 2019-20 dedicated 
29to the Performance Food Group municipal tax increment financing district designated on 
30July 2, 2016 is ratified, made legal and valid. 
31Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
32legislation takes effect when approved.
33SUMMARY
34This bill authorizes the City of Augusta to adjust the definition of "original assessed 
35value" for the City of Augusta's Performance Food Group municipal tax increment 
36financing district and to validate the assessment, commitment and collection of property 
37taxes dedicated for the Performance Food Group municipal tax increment financing 
38district for the fiscal years 2018-19 and 2019-20.
