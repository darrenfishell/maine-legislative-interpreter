SENATE AMENDMENT "B "to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 292, 
L.D. 1013, Bill, "An Act To Clarify the Disqualification from Unemployment Benefits of 
a Person Who Is Terminated from Employment for Being Under the Influence of 
Marijuana" 
Amend the amendment by striking out all of subparagraph ( 6) (page 1, lines 14 and 
15 in amendment) and inserting the following: 
'( 6) Intoxication while on duty or when reporting to work., or unauthorized use of 
alcohol or marijuana while on duty except for the use of marijuana permitted 
under Title 22, chapter 558-C;' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment excepts from the disqualification from unemployment benefits for 
marijuana use the use of marijuana permitted under the laws governing the medical use of 
marijuana.
