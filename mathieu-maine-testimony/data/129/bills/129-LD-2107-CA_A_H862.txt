11Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the 
12following:
13'Sec. 1.  22 MRSA §1826, sub-§3, as amended by PL 1997, c. 329, §1, is further 
14amended to read:
153.  Other contract provisions.  The contract or agreement may contain other 
16provisions that do not violate state law or rule or federal law or regulation and that are 
17specifically allowed by the standardized contract under subsection 4.  A facility may add 
18to the standardized contract an additional contract or rider that includes, but is not limited 
19to, the following:
20A.  A provision that requires a resident's agent who has legal access to a resident's 
21income or financial resources to pay for facility services for the resident from that 
22income or resources to the extent available for this purpose, except that the resident's 
23agent may not be held personally liable for the resident's facility expenses; and
24B.  A provision that provides that if a resident's agent fails in the agent's obligation to 
25ensure that the facility is paid in a timely manner for the costs of a resident's monthly 
26care or fails to perform any of the agent's obligations described in the contract or the 
27rider, the facility may pursue a legal action against the agent in Probate Court or 
28District Court for review of the agent's performance.  The provision may also provide 
29that, in addition to other relief that the facility may request from the court under such 
30action, if the facility prevails, the resident's agent is responsible to pay reasonable 
31costs of the facility's attorney's fees and costs with the resident's income or financial 
32resources.
33For the purposes of this subsection, "agent" means a person acting as a power of attorney 
34as set forth in Title 18-C, section 5-914 or as a conservator appointed under Title 18-C, 
35section 5-401.' Date: (Filing No. H-          )

3SUMMARY
4This amendment replaces the bill.  The bill requires the Department of Health and 
5Human Services to amend its rules to change the standard admission contract for nursing 
6facilities.  The amendment amends law to provide that a nursing facility may add the 
7provisions to an additional contract or rider to the standard admission contract.
