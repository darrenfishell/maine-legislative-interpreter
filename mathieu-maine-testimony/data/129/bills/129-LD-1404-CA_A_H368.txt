8 SUMMARY 
9 This amendment clarifies that the Department of Health and Human Services is 
10 required to examine opportunities to provide home and community-based services to 
11 individuals experiencing homelessness using a state plan amendment option under 
12 Section 1915(i) of the federal Social Security Act or any other Medicaid-funded 
13 mechanism that may be appropriate. It requires the department to report its progress in 
14 examining options and submitting a waiver to the Joint Standing Committee on Health 
15 and Human Services. The amendment also corrects the appropriations and allocations 
16 section to reflect funding from the Temporary Housing Assistance Fund and changes the 
17 initiative language to be more consistent with the statute governing the fund.
