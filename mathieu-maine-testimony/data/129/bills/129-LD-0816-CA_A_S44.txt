9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A " to S.P. 252, L.D. 816, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Implement the National Popular Vote for President of the United States" 
11 Amend the bill by adding after section 3 the following: 
12 'Sec. 4. Contingent effective date. That section of this Act that amends the 
13 Maine Revised Statutes, Title 21-A, section 805, subsection 2 takes effect upon the 
14 effective date of Title 21-A, chapter 17.' 
15 SUMMARY 
16 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, clarifies that Maine's 
17 presidential electors are not obligated to cast their votes in favor of the presidential 
18 candidate and vice presidential candidate that are declared the winners of the national 
19 popular vote until the interstate compact to elect the President of the United States by 
20 national popular vote takes effect as described in the bill.
