11SUMMARY
12This bill prohibits the operation of an all-terrain vehicle at a speed of more than 15 
13miles per hour within 50 yards of a stream or pond, unless the ground is frozen and 
14sufficiently covered with snow. It specifies that a person who violates this prohibition 
15commits a civil violation, except that a person who violates this prohibition after having 
16been adjudicated as having committed 3 or more violations of this prohibition within the 
17previous 5-year period commits a Class E crime.
