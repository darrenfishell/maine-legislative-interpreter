10SUMMARY
11This bill requires the provision of comprehensive access to sexual and reproductive 
12health care and education for a person who is a female or who has a uterus admitted to or 
13detained or incarcerated in a jail or county correctional facility or a state detention or 
14correctional facility. The bill establishes the Sexual and Reproductive Health Care 
15Advisory Committee to review and develop adequate standards, policies and materials for 
16the provision of sexual and reproductive health care and education for persons who are 
17incarcerated and requires a report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature 
18having jurisdiction over criminal justice matters by January 1, 2021.
