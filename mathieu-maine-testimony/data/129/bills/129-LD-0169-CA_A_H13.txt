COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "I}" to H.P. 132, L.D. 169, Bill, "An Act To 
Provide Occupants of Motor Vehicles with Gold Star Family Registration Plates Free 
Entry to State Parks, Camping Areas and Beaches" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Provide Occupants of Motor Vehicles with Gold Star Family 
Registration Plates Free Entry to State Parks and Historic Sites' 
Amend the bill in section 1 in the first paragraph in the last 2 lines (page 1, lines 6 
and 7 in L.D.) by striking out the following: ". camping area or beach" and inserting the 
following: 'or historic site managed by the State; the free entry is for day use only. For 
purposes of this paragraph, "day use" does not include camping' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies that free entry to state parks and historic sites for a person 
displaying gold star family registration plates is for day use only.
