16SUMMARY
17This bill specifies that an individual, organization or institution, including a public 
18school, that donates food waste to a swine producer for use in swine feed is not required 
19to verify that the swine producer has a license to feed garbage to swine.  The bill also 
20specifies that it is private or public property owned or operated by a swine producer that 
21the Commissioner of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may enter to investigate 
22conditions relating to the treating or feeding of garbage to swine.
