5 SUMMARY 
6 This amendment provides that the maximum bet for a licensed card game in which 
7 bets are placed per hand or per deal is $5, inclusive of any raises made during the hand or 
8 deal. The bill increases the maximum bet for a licensed card game from $1 to $5. The 
9 amendment also provides that a bona fide nonprofit organization may operate a licensed 
10 card game or registered game of chance to which the general public has access for 4 
11 consecutive days no more than 4 times in a calendar year. Current law includes a 3-
12 month waiting period between such game operations, which the bill removes. The 
13 amendment also adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause.

Minor cost increase -General Fund 
Any additional costs to the Gambling Control Unit, within the Department of Public Safety, as a result of changes to 
the betting limit for games of chance, the number of days a nonprofit organization can operate a licensed card game 
or registered game of chance or the waiting period between game operations are anticipated to be minor and can be 
absorbed within existing budgeted resources.
