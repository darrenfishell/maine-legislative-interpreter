COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "/j" to H.P. 1326, L.D. 1855, Bill, "An Act To 
Include Student Absences for Mental Health or Behavioral Health Needs as Excusable 
Absences" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in paragraph A in the first line (page 1, line 4 in L.D.) by 
inserting after the following: "person's" the following: 'physical,' 
Amend the bill in section 2 in paragraph A in the first line (page 1, line 7 in L.D.) by 
inserting after the following: "person's" the following: 'physical,' 
Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment specifies that a person's absence from school is excused when the 
absence is due to reasons of physical health, as well as the person's mental and behavioral 
health.
