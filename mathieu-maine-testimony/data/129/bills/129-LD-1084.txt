14SUMMARY
15This bill defines "pedal-powered tour vehicle" and permits such vehicles to be used 
16on public ways.  The bill provides that a municipality may restrict the operation of a 
17pedal-powered tour vehicle on a public way within the municipality only by requiring 
18evidence of liability insurance and compliance with equipment standards and traffic 
19restrictions established by the municipality.
