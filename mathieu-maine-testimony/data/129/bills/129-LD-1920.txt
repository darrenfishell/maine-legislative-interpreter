15A license issued to a resident paraplegic under this subsection remains valid for the life of

17under section 10001, subsection 53 and the license is not revoked or suspended. A 
18nonresident paraplegic may apply for and be qualified to be issued the complimentary 
19licenses and permits referred to in this subsection as long as the state where the person 
20resides provides a reciprocal privilege for resident paraplegics of this State.

37This bill:

39has lost, or who has permanently lost the use of, both lower extremities to provide that

53.  Exempts from the State's lake and river protection sticker requirement New
