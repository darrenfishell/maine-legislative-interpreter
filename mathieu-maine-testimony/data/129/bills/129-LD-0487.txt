9SUMMARY
10This resolve directs the Department of Transportation, in consultation with the Town 
11of Old Orchard Beach, to study the corner of Smithwheel Road and Ocean Park Road, 
12which connects to the I-195 spur, and the intersection of State Route 5, which is also 
13known as Saco Avenue, with Temple Avenue and Old Orchard Road and determine 
14whether a roundabout or an alternative layout is needed.
