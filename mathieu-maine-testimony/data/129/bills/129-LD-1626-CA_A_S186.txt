5 SUMMARY 
6 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, strikes the provision 
7 of the bill mandating that each party's presidential primary elections are closed to any 
8 voter not enrolled in that party. Under the amendment, by December 1st of the year prior 
9 to a presidential election year, a party must notify the Secretary of State whether 
10 unenrolled voters are eligible to vote in the party's presidential primary election. The 
11 amendment also corrects cross-references in the bill.
