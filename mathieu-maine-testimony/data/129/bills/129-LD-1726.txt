31SUMMARY
32This bill provides for penalties for violation of Department of Agriculture, 
33Conservation and Forestry, Bureau of Forestry rules governing plant or wood products 
34that may cause the introduction or spread of a dangerous forest insect or disease and rules 
35governing the quarantine of forest or shade trees or part of a forest or shade tree capable 
36of supporting a disease or insect infestation.
37The bill sets fines at $100 to $1,000 for each day of a violation and from $1,000 to 
38$2,000 for each day of a violation by a violator that violated the rules within the previous
