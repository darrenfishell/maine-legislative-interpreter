6SUMMARY
7This bill directs the Department of Health and Human Services to provide 
8reimbursement under the MaineCare program for palliative care.  It also requires the 
9department to adopt rules that support and standardize the delivery of palliative care in 
10the State, including but not limited to strategies for the distribution of public educational 
11documents and the distribution by health care providers of information regarding the 
12availability of palliative care to patients.  It also requires the department to consult with 
13the Maine Hospice Council, the Palliative Care and Quality of Life Interdisciplinary 
14Advisory Council and other stakeholders when developing educational documents and 
15rules related to palliative care.
