21SUMMARY
22This bill updates references to the United States Internal Revenue Code of 1986 
23contained in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 36 to refer to the United States Internal 
24Revenue Code of 1986, as amended through December 31, 2019, for tax years beginning 
25on or after January 1, 2019 and for any prior tax years as specifically provided by the 
26United States Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended. This bill primarily affects the 
27State's income tax laws.
