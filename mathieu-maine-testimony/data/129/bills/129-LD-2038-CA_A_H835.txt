26SUMMARY
27 This amendment is the majority report of the Joint Standing Committee on Judiciary.
28 This amendment replaces the bill.  The amendment requires court authorization for the 
29 Department of Health and Human Services to release confidential information related to 
30 child protective activities to an administrator of a social media service to report, investigate 
31 or remove a threat or serious intimidation attempt directed at a department employee, an 
32 employee of the Attorney General's Office, a guardian ad litem, a court employee or an 
33 officer of the court, including prosecuting and other attorneys.
