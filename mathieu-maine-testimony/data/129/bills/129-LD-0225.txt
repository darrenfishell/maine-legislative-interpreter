Page 1 - 129LR0501(01)-11Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2Sec. 1.  20-A MRSA §5401, sub-§15, ¶E is enacted to read:
3E.  Notwithstanding any other provision of this subsection, the commissioner shall 
4approve a request submitted by a school administrative unit to replace a conventional 
5school bus or transit-style school bus if the bus has:
6(1)  At least 150,000 accumulated miles; and
7(2)  Been in operation for 10 or more years, as determined by the commissioner.
8The commissioner shall by rule define "conventional school bus" and "transit-style 
9school bus." Rules adopted pursuant to this paragraph are routine technical rules as 
10defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
11Sec. 2.  20-A MRSA §5401, sub-§18 is enacted to read:
1218.  School transportation operations.  The department may not require a school 
13administrative unit to use a specific brand of routing and transportation management 
14software.
15SUMMARY
16This bill establishes in statute the criteria for the replacement of conventional and 
17transit-style school buses through the Department of Education and prohibits the 
18department from requiring a school administrative unit to use a specific brand of routing 
19and transportation management software.
