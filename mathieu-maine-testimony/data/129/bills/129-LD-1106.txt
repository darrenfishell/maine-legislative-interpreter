6SUMMARY
7This bill removes the asset test for the Medicare savings program and the elderly low-
8cost drug program.  It also increases the income eligibility levels for the Medicare savings 
9program and the elderly low-cost drug program to the levels in effect prior to Public Law 
102011, chapter 657.  The Department of Health and Human Services is required to submit 
11any necessary state plan amendments for approval for the increases in income eligibility.
