2Sec. 1.  Expansion of collaborative workspaces.  The Department of Economic 
3and Community Development shall provide funds from the Maine Coworking 
4Development Fund, as established in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 
513056-G, to entities that maintain collaborative workspace businesses to allow those 
6entities to expand those collaborative workspace businesses.  In providing funds pursuant 
7to this section, notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the department shall 
8give preference to applicants with collaborative workspace businesses located in 
9municipalities and counties in which the unemployment rate exceeds the State's 
10unemployment rate.
