24SUMMARY
25This bill simplifies the definition of "handheld electronic device" in recently enacted 
26law prohibiting the use of handheld electronic devices while driving.  It adds parking 
27areas to the places where the use of mobile telephones and handheld electronic devices 
28while driving is prohibited.  It changes the penalty provisions to provide a fine of $50 for 
29the first offense and $250 for a 2nd or subsequent offense.
