SUMMARY 
This amendment changes the title and provides a seat on the Board of Trustees of the 
Maine Criminal Justice Academy for a person appointed by the Governor who is 
knowledgeable about public safety and who has been recommended to the Governor by 
the Wabanaki tribal governments of the Aroostook Band of Micmacs, the Houlton Band 
of Maliseet Indians, the Passamaquoddy Tribe at Motahkmikuk, the Passamaquoddy 
Tribe at Sipayik and the Penobscot Nation. The term of this member of the board of 
trustees is 2 years.
