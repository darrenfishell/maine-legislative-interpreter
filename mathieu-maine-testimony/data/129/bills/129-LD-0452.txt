21transferor of the land.
22SUMMARY
23This bill provides that if land classified under the Maine Tree Growth Tax Law is to 
24be transferred, the owner must notify the assessor of the intended transfer and the 
25intended transferee of a potential withdrawal penalty.  If land is transferred, a withdrawal 
26penalty must be assessed against the transferor unless the intended transferee notifies the 
27assessor of intent to maintain eligibility of the land for tree growth classification.
