15 'Sec. 1. Adult family care home reimbursement for bed-hold days. 
16 Resolved: That, no later than January 1, 2020, the Department of Health and Human 
17 Services shall amend its rule Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, 
18 Section 2, Adult Family Care Services to reimburse for days a resident is absent from a

21 reimbursement must be at least equal to the rate for bed-hold days provided to residential 
22 care facilities under the department's rule Chapter 115: Principles of Reimbursement for 
23 Residential Care Facilities.
