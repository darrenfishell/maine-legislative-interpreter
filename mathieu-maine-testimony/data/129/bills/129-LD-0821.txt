11SUMMARY
12This bill requires that the Department of Health and Human Services ensure that a 
13caseworker in the Office of Child and Family Services is not assigned a number of cases 
14that exceeds a number established by the department by rule.  The number established by 
15the department must be a number recommended by a national organization with expertise 
16in maximum case loads that ensures caseworkers can adequately focus on each case.  The 
17bill also provides that the number of caseworkers assigned to any individual support staff 
18may not exceed 8.
