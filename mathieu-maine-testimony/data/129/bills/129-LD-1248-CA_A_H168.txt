9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A "to H.P. 909, L.D. 1248, "Resolve, To Direct 
10 the ConnectME Authority To Report on the Progress of the Detailed 2019-2021 Strategic 
11 Plan for Broadband Service in Maine" 
12 Amend the resolve by striking out all of section 1 and inserting the following: 
13 'Sec. 1. Direction to report. Resolved: That in 2020, 2021 and 2022 the 
14 ConnectME Authority established under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 35-A, section 
15 9203 shall include in its annual report due January 15th to the joint standing committee of 
16 the Legislature having jurisdiction over energy, utilities and technology matters, as 
17 required under Title 35-A, section 9208, information outlining the progress of the 
18 authority in meeting the goals of its Detailed 2019-2021 Strategic Plan for Broadband 
19 Service in Maine developed pursuant to Title 35-A, section 9218, including but not 
20 limited to an explanation of the measures that the authority has taken to: 
21 1. Build partnerships with private businesses and state, county and municipal 
22 planning organizations, including but not limited to regional transportation planning 
23 authorities; and 
24 2. Coordinate and communicate with the Department of Transportation in order to 
25 facilitate the installation of broadband infrastructure along roadways.' 
26 Amend the resolve by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
27 section number to read consecutively. 
28 SUMMARY 
29 This amendment incorporates and aligns the reporting requirement in the resolve with 
30 the ConnectME Authority annual report, instead of requiring a separate report to the 
31 Legislature on the progress on the strategic plan.
