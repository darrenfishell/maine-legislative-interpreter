9SUMMARY
10This amendment changes the membership of the Criminal Records Review 
11Committee. It removes the Commissioner of Corrections, the President of the Maine 
12Sheriffs' Association, a representative of an organization that provides legal assistance on 
13immigration, a representative of an adult and juvenile prisoners' rights organization and a 
14representative of a nonprofit organization whose mission includes advocating for victims 
15and survivors of sexual assault from the committee. It adds the President of the Maine 
16Chiefs of Police Association or the president's designee and a member of the press to the 
17committee. It adds the following to the list of individuals and organizations from whom 
18the committee must invite comments and suggestions: juvenile justice reform advocates, 
19restorative justice advocates, individuals or organizations that provide legal assistance in 
20immigration matters, the Secretary of State and the Department of Professional and 
21Financial Regulation.
