Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill makes changes to the state tax laws and would result in a decrease in General Fund revenue of $449,487 in 
fiscal year 2020-21 and a decrease in Other Special Revenue Funds revenue of $17,513 in fiscal year 2020-21. The 
bill includes a one-time General Fund appropriation to the Department of Administrative and Financial Services of 
$5,000 in fiscal year 2020-21 for computer programming costs.
