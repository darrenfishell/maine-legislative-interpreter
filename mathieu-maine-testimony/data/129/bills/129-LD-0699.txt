25SUMMARY
26This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to contract with 
27a community-based nonprofit organization to develop outreach and educational programs 
28regarding reproductive and sexual health care for women and adolescents at highest risk 
29of experiencing an unintended pregnancy.  The programming must emphasize the right to 
30individual self-determination regarding family planning and childbearing and be targeted 
31to women and adolescents experiencing substance use disorder, homelessness or other 
32circumstances that indicate a need for family planning services or who are involved in the 
33correctional system.GENERAL FUND 2019-20 2020-21
