17Sec. 2.  Review and recommendations.  Resolved:  That, in its review of 
18transmission grid reliability and electric rate stability for the northern Maine service 
19territory, the stakeholder group convened pursuant to section 1 shall, at minimum, 
20address and develop recommendations concerning:
211.  The continued need to assess reliability in the northern Maine service territory;

243.  The region's fuel security, competitive supply and rate volatility resulting from its 
25reliance on generation resources in the region; and
264.  Opportunities for transmission and nontransmission alternatives to address the 
27current and projected reliability and rate stability needs of the region.

6SUMMARY
7This resolve directs the Governor's Energy Office to convene a stakeholder group to 
8identify and develop strategies to address the transmission grid reliability and electric rate 
9stability for the northern Maine service territory.  The Governor's Energy Office is 
10directed to submit a report that includes its findings, together with any proposed 
11implementing legislation, to the Joint Standing Committee on Energy, Utilities and 
12Technology, which is authorized to report out a bill to the Second Regular Session of the 
13129th Legislature.
