14Sec. A-1.  22 MRSA §1912 is enacted to read:
15§1912.  Continued access to family planning services
161.  Access to services.  In accordance with this chapter, the department shall provide 
17 funding to ensure continued access to family planning services.  The department shall issue 
18 a request for proposals from qualified providers of family planning services.  The 
19 department shall select one provider to provide management and oversight of the delivery 
20 of family planning services in this State, including, but not limited to, the coordination and 
21 management of any subrecipients, and shall distribute funds appropriated for that purpose.
222.  Grant.  The department shall make funds available from the fund in the form of a 
23 grant and may not distribute any funds under a fee-for-service format.
243.  Fund established.  The Fund To Ensure Access to Family Planning Services, 
25 referred to in this section as "the fund," is established as a dedicated, nonlapsing fund within 
26 the department to provide family planning services under this section.  The department may 
27 accept funds into the fund from any public or private source for the purposes of awarding 
28 a grant under this section.  Any funds appropriated to the fund may not supplant other 
29 sources of funding.
304.  Prohibition.  The department may not select a provider to provide management and 
31 oversight of the delivery of family planning services under this section if that provider 
32 provides, promotes, refers for or supports abortion as a method of family planning.  A 
33 provider selected by the department is also prohibited from distributing any funds under 
34 this section to a subrecipient that provides, promotes, refers for or supports abortion as a 
35 method of family planning.
