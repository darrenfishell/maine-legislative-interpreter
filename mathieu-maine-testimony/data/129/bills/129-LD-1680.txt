10commissioners of an unincorporated place in which an entertainment district has been

26SUMMARY
27This bill authorizes the issuance of auxiliary liquor licenses for the consumption of 
28spirits, wine and malt liquor within entertainment districts authorized by and located 
29within municipalities or unincorporated places.
