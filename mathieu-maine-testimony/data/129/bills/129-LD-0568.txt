162.  Appointment of members representing a county. One member must be 
17appointed by each of the  8  6 counties with the most acreage in the unorganized or 
18deorganized areas subject to the jurisdiction of the commission.  The board of county 
19commissioners for each of the counties shall appoint by majority vote a resident of that

21candidate for appointment to serve on the commission may not vote on that appointment.

14SUMMARY
15This bill amends the laws governing the composition of the Maine Land Use 
16Planning Commission to increase the number of members appointed by the Governor 
17from one to 3 and to decrease the number of members appointed by the counties from 8 
18to 6.
