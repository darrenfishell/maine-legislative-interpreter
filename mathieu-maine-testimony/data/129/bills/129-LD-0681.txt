15§12306.  Stakeholder consultation
16In administering the program and assessing its effectiveness, the chief executive

18to, representatives of dental education and practitioner communities in the State and 
19organizations representing the interests of low-income communties in the State.
20SUMMARY
21This bill amends the Maine Dental Education Loan Program by clarifying the 
22definition of underserved population areas; increasing maximum loan amounts under the 
23program; eliminating the advisory committee on dental education; and enabling the Chief 
24Executive Officer of the Finance Authority of Maine to consult key stakeholders in 
25administering the program and evaluating its effectiveness.
