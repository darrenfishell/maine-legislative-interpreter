12SUMMARY
13This bill exempts from the corporate income tax a manufacturer of alcoholic spirits 
14located outside of this State whose only nexus with the State is the storage of that 
15manufacturer's spirits in a warehouse operated or approved for operation by the 
16Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages 
17and Lottery Operations.
