22SUMMARY
23This bill eliminates the requirement that the Secretary of State must issue annual 
24registration plates or decals.
25It repeals language setting the price for replacement decals for plates at 50¢ each.
26It repeals a provision that makes it a Class E crime to attach or display on a vehicle 
27registration plate a decal issued for another vehicle.
