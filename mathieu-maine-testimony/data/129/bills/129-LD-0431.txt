5No court Court may not be held on Sunday or any day designated for the annual

112nd Monday in October; the day of the general election, as defined in Title 21-A, section

13of the Supreme Judicial Court may order that court be held on a legal holiday when he the 
14Chief Justice finds that the interests of justice and judicial economy in any particular case 
15will be served. The public offices in county buildings may be closed to business on the 
16holidays named in this section. When any one of the holidays named in this section falls 
17on Sunday, the Monday following shall must be observed as a holiday, with all the 
18privileges applying to any of the days named in this section.
19Sec. 2.  5 MRSA §59 is enacted to read:
20§59.  General election day established as state holiday
21The day of the general election, as defined in Title 21-A, section 1, subsection 19, is a 
22state holiday.  All nonessential state offices must be closed on the day of the general 
23election.

30SUMMARY
31This bill designates the day of the general election, which is the day of the regular 
32election of state and county officials occurring biennially in November, as a state holiday.
