14SUMMARY
15This bill provides specificity for the use and expenditure of funds by county 
16governments by:
171.  Limiting the payment of expenditures for the priority-designated social service of 
18transportation to transportation for access to health care services;
192.  Limiting grants that are paid to agencies outside of the regular county departments 
20to those that have been statutorily authorized;
213.  Requiring the Office of the State Auditor, when conducting an audit of a county, 
22to report any transaction not authorized by statute to the district attorney for that county; 
23and
244.  Specifying that the ability of county commissioners to approve county 
25expenditures is limited to those expenditures that are statutorily authorized.
