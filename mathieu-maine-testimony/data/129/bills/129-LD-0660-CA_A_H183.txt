The bill provides a sales tax exemption for sales to parent-teacher organizations and would result in a reduction in 
General Fund revenue of $7,600 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $10,450 in fiscal year 2020-21. It would also result in a 
reduction in Local Government Fund revenue of $400 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $550 in fiscal year 2020-21. The bill 
includes a one-time General Fund appropriation of $5,000 in fiscal year 2019-20 to the Department of Administrative 
and Financial Services for programming costs to create an exemption certificate.
