4provide a voluntary, short-term residential program designed to support individuals 
5experiencing, or at risk of, a psychiatric crisis.
6SUMMARY
7This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services, office of 
8substance abuse and mental health services to implement by means of a request-for-
9proposals process at least one peer respite program in the State to provide a voluntary, 
10short-term residential program designed to support individuals experiencing, or at risk of, 
11a psychiatric crisis.
