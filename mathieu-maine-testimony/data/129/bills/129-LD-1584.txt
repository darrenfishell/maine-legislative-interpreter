182.  Comprehensive scholarships.  Providing comprehensive scholarships that allow

253.  Apprenticeships.  Expanding the number of apprenticeships available in early

30criteria established by rule adopted pursuant to this section; and
314.  Salary supplements.  Establishing a system of salary supplements for providers 
32of child care and early childhood educators working with children up to 5 years of age.

4SUMMARY
5This bill requires the Commissioner of Health and Human Services, the 
6Commissioner of Education and the Commissioner of Labor jointly to develop and 
7implement an early childhood educators workforce support program to recruit and retain 
8early childhood educators working with children up to 5 years of age.  Components of the 
9program include:
101.  Expansion of educational programs at career and technical education centers and 
11financial support of those programs;
122.  Comprehensive scholarships for persons taking classes toward the attainment of 
13an early childhood education credential or an associate or bachelor's degree that allow the 
14persons to graduate without student debt as long as the persons agree to work for a year 
15with an approved employer;
163.  An increased number of apprenticeships; and
174.  Salary supplements awarded to individuals who provide child care or who are 
18early childhood educators.  The amount of the supplement is based on the level of 
19education and experience of the individual and other factors.
