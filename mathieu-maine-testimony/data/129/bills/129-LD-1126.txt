11SUMMARY
12This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rule 
13Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 67, Principles of 
14Reimbursement for Nursing Facilities no later than January 1, 2020 to move health 
15insurance costs for personnel from direct care and routine cost components to fixed costs 
16components.  This was a majority recommendation of the Commission To Study Long-
17term Care Facilities, which reported in December 2013.
