2Sec. 1.  20-A MRSA §1, sub-§46 is enacted to read:
346.  Vocational preparation and practical life skills.  "Vocational preparation and 
4practical life skills" means experiential instruction of students, regardless of career choice 
5or pathway, that develops their understanding of interests, aptitudes and options related to 
6work and study; develops core workplace skills in areas such as planning, 
7communication, problem solving, teamwork and computer applications; and includes 
8practical workplace and home economics experiences that maximize learning through 
9hands-on application.

22A.  Career Vocational preparation and education development practical life skills;
23SUMMARY
24This bill changes the requirement in the system of learning results from "career and 
25education development" to "vocational preparation and practical life skills."  The bill 
26defines "vocational preparation and practical life skills" to mean experiential instruction 
27of students, regardless of career choice or pathway, that develops their understanding of 
28interests, aptitudes and options related to work and study; develops core workplace skills 
29in areas such as planning, communication, problem solving, teamwork and computer 
30applications; and includes practical workplace and home economics experiences that 
31maximize learning through hands-on application.
