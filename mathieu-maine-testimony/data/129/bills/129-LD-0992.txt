Page 1 - 129LR1217(01)-11Sec. 1.  Extension of Down East Sunrise Trail from Ayers Junction to 
2Calais.  Resolved:  That the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the 
3Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry shall develop a fully off-road trail 
4corridor linking the East Coast Greenway's northern terminus at the Ferry Point 
5International Bridge in Calais with the Down East Sunrise Trail's eastern terminus at 
6Ayers Junction.
7SUMMARY
8This resolve directs the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the 
9Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to develop a fully off-road trail 
10corridor linking the East Coast Greenway's northern terminus at the Ferry Point 
11International Bridge in Calais with the Down East Sunrise Trail's eastern terminus at 
12Ayers Junction.
