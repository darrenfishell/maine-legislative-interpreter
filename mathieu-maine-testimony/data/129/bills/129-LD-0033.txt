34.  Special youth season; Thanksgiving.  The commissioner shall establish by rule a 
4special youth hunting season for hunting wild turkeys. The season must be from the 
5Monday prior to Thanksgiving Day to the Saturday following Thanksgiving Day. Youth 
6hunters in possession of a valid junior hunting license may harvest one wild turkey in any 
7area where wild turkey hunting is permitted during the season.
8SUMMARY
9This bill directs the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to establish a 
10special youth hunting season from the Monday prior to Thanksgiving Day to the Saturday 
11following Thanksgiving Day during which youth hunters with a valid junior hunting 
12license may harvest one wild turkey.
