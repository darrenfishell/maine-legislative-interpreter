4ยง3174-JJ.  MaineCare reimbursement for ambulance services
5The department shall reimburse for ambulance services under MaineCare at a level 
6that is not less than the average allowable reimbursement rate under Medicare for such 
7services or at the highest percent of that level that is possible within resources 
8appropriated for those purposes.  Beginning March 1, 2015, the department shall 
9reimburse for ambulance services under MaineCare at a level that is not less than 65% of 
10the average allowable reimbursement rate under Medicare for such services.  Beginning 
11September 1, 2019, the department shall reimburse for ambulance services under 
12MaineCare at a level that is not less than the average allowable reimbursement rate under 
13Medicare for such services and shall reimburse for neonatal transport services under 
14MaineCare at the average rate for critical care transport services under Medicare.
15SUMMARY
16This bill specifies that beginning September 1, 2019 the reimbursement rate for 
17ambulance services under the MaineCare program may not be less than the average 
18allowable reimbursement rate under Medicare and reimbursement for neonatal transport 
19services under MaineCare must be at the average rate for critical care transport services 
20under Medicare.
