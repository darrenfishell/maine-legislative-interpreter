13SUMMARY
14This bill removes references to the age of the patient for purposes of requiring 
15coverage for colorectal cancer screening for asymptomatic individuals who are at average 
16risk for colorectal cancer and instead requires coverage in accordance with the most 
17recently published guidelines of a national cancer society.  In addition, this bill requires 
18coverage of all colorectal examinations and laboratory tests recommended by a health 
19care provider in accordance with those published guidelines.
