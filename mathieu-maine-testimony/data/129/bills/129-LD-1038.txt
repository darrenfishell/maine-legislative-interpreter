3SUMMARY
4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to improve the training and retention of oil and hazardous materials 
6emergency response workers and their ability to respond to oil and hazardous materials 
7emergencies by requiring the State to:
81.  Pay or waive the fee for workers for the cost of necessary training courses and any 
9materials required for those courses;
102.  Compensate workers fairly for the time spent in training courses, while on call and 
11when responding to hazardous materials emergencies; and
123.  Purchase hazardous materials emergency response equipment for use by 
13municipalities or reimburse municipalities that purchase hazardous materials emergency 
14response equipment.
