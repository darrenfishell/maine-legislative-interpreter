13SUMMARY
14This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to provide coverage 
15to a MaineCare member for legal abortion services.  The bill provides that abortion 
16services that are not approved Medicaid services must be funded by the State.  The bill 
17also directs the Department of Health and Human Services to adopt rules no later than 
18March 1, 2020.
