2Sec. 1.  12 MRSA §12853, sub-§4-A, ¶F is enacted to read:
3F.  A person who may renew a guide license at no cost under section 10851, 
4subsection 1, paragraph D is exempt from the requirements of this subsection if a 
5background check has been conducted on the person when the person was 68 years of 
6age or older.
7SUMMARY
8This bill exempts a registered Maine guide who holds a senior lifetime hunting, 
9fishing, trapping or archery license from the requirement to undergo a background check 
10when renewing the guide license if the guide has undergone a background check when 
11the guide was 68 years of age or older.
