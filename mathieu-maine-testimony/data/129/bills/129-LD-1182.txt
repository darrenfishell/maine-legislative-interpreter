9SUMMARY
10This bill requires that a public school be visited at least 2 times per week by a law 
11enforcement officer from the State Police, a county sheriff's office or a municipal law 
12enforcement agency when that public school is in session.  Visits by law enforcement 
13officers must be unannounced and occur at varying times of the day and week at each 
14public school.  The county sheriff's office is responsible for coordinating these visits.
