9§7501-A.  Regulation of junkyards and automobile graveyards authorized 
10The county commissioners of a county may enact an ordinance for the purpose of 
11regulating junkyards and automobile graveyards in the same manner as a municipality 
12may pursuant to chapter 183, subchapter 1.
13SUMMARY
14This bill grants to plantations the power to control junkyards and automobile 
15graveyards in the same manner as municipalities may control junkyards and automobile 
16graveyards.  The bill also grants to county commissioners the power to control junkyards 
17and automobile graveyards in unorganized territories in the same manner as 
18municipalities may control junkyards and automobile graveyards.
