6SUMMARY
7This bill requires the Commissioner of Health and Human Services to submit a 
8waiver under Section 1332 of the federal Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act to 
9establish a MaineCare purchase option so that residents of Maine who are not otherwise 
10eligible for the MaineCare program may participate in the program.  The waiver must 
11include authority for individuals who qualify for advance tax credits and cost-sharing 
12credits to use them to purchase coverage through the MaineCare program.  Individuals 
13participating in the MaineCare purchase option may access the same services as other 
14MaineCare members.  The commissioner is required to implement mechanisms to ensure 
15the long-term sustainability of the MaineCare purchase option.  Rates are set by the 
16Department of Health and Human Services and determined actuarially, and the open 
17enrollment period is the same as the period for individuals purchasing insurance on the 
18federal exchange.  The department is required to submit a progress report on the request 
19for the waiver as well as annual reports to the joint standing committees of the 
20Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human services matters and insurance 
21matters.
