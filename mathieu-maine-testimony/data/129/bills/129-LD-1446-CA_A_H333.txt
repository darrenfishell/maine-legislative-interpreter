Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill provides a sales and use tax exemption for sales of coins with gold or silver content or refined gold or silver 
bullion and would result in a reduction in General Fund revenue of $332,500 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $489,250 in 
fiscal year 2020-21. It would also result in a reduction in Local Government Fund revenue of $17,500 in fiscal year 
2019-20 and $25,750 in fiscal year 2020-21.
