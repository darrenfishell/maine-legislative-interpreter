21technical rules as defined by Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
22SUMMARY
23This bill requires that firefighter training include preventive and post-trauma 
24counseling, at which a counseling professional licensed under the Maine Revised 
25Statutes, Title 32, chapter 119 or the fire department chaplain must be available to work 
26with the firefighters, as required by the fire chief.  The bill provides that, with advice 
27from and in consultation with each regional council and its medical control committee 
28and with the statewide emergency medical services' medical director, the Emergency 
29Medical Services' Board may adopt routine technical rules setting mandatory 
30requirements for preventive and post-trauma counseling for basic and advanced training 
31for emergency medical services' persons for initial licensing and relicensing.  The 
32requirements may include the services of a counseling professional licensed under Title 
3332, chapter 119 or a chaplain to be provided by ambulance services and nontransporting 
34emergency medical services.
