101-A.  Comprehensive family life education.  "Comprehensive family life education" 
11means education in kindergarten to grade 12 regarding human development and sexuality,

24comprehensive knowledge of factors pertinent to this determination.

22Sec. 3.  22 MRSA §1907, as enacted by PL 1973, c. 624, §1, is amended to read:
23§1907.  Refusal
24The refusal of any person to accept family planning services shall does not affect the 
25right of such that person to receive public assistance or public health services or to avail 
26himself of access any other public benefit. The employees of agencies engaged in the 
27administration of this chapter shall recognize that the right to make decisions concerning 
28family planning is a fundamental personal right of the individual, and nothing in this 
29chapter shall in any way abridge abridges such right nor shall may any individual be 
30required to receive family planning services or to state his reasons for refusing the offer 
31of family planning services.
