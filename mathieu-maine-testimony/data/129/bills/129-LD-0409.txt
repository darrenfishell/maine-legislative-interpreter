19SUMMARY
20This bill allows confidential information related to services provided by a child 
21advocacy center established pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 22, section 
224019 to be disclosed to an attorney representing a person charged with committing a 
23crime related to an allegation of child sexual abuse or other child abuse and neglect 
24against a child who is the subject of confidential records and an attorney in a child 
25protection proceeding representing a parent of a child who is the subject of confidential 
26records.
