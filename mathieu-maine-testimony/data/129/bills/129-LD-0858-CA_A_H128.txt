9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A "to H.P. 632, L.D. 858, "Resolve, Directing the 
10 Department of Education To Study and Make Recommendations for the Establishment of 
11 a Maine School Safety Center" 
12 Amend the resolve by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
13 'Resolve, Directing the Department of Education To Study and Make 
14 Recommendations Relating to School Safety and Security' 
15 Amend the resolve by striking out all of section 1 and inserting the following: 
16 'Sec. 1. Study establishment of a Maine School Safety Center. Resolved: 
17 That the Department of Education shall study and make recommendations relating to 
18 school safety and security, includlng, but not limited to, planning to mitigate the potential 
19 risks associated with opening school facilities to the public when a school is used as a 
20 polling place and the establishment of a Maine School Safety Center based on a report 
21 created by Safe Havens International pursuant to Resolve 2013, chapter 76, the top 
22 recommendation of which was the establishment of such a center, as detailed in the Safe 
23 Havens International report entitled "Excerpts from the School Security Repo1is 
24 Submitted to the Maine Depaiiment of Education January, 2014." The depaitment shall 
25 submit a preliminary report regarding the study with initial findings to the Joint Standing 
26 Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs by December 15, 2019 and shall submit a 
27 final report, including its recommendations relating to school safety and security and any 
28 suggested legislation, to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 
29 jurisdiction over education matters by December 15, 2020. The joint standing committee 
30 may submit a bill to the First Regular Session of the 130th Legislature. 
31 Amend the resolve by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Patt letter or 
32 section number to read consecutively.' 
33 SUMMARY

35 make recommendations relating to school safety and security, including, but not limited
