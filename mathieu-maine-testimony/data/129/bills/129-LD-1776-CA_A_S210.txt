COMMITTEE AMENDMENT " A,, to S.P. 600, L.D. 1776, Bill, "An Act To 
Reduce the Membership of the Clean-up and Response Fund Review Board" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 1 by striking out all of paragraphs E and F 
(page 1, lines 21 to 24 in L.D.) and inserting the following: 
'E. One member representing marine fisheries interests appointed by the President of 
the Senate; 
F. One member familiar with oil sp~ll technology appointed by the Speaker of the 
House of Representatives;' 
Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively. 
SUM:MARY 
This amendment retains as a member of the Clean-up and Response Fund Review 
Board the member familiar with oil spill technology, which the bill removes, and instead 
removes the member representing marine fisheries interests, which the bill retains.
