8SUMMARY
9This bill amends the general hunting season provisions to direct the Commissioner of 
10Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to extend the open seasons on upland game by one 
11additional day, the last Saturday of September, annually.  It defines the term "upland 
12game" as snowshoe hare, gray squirrel, ring-necked pheasant, ruffed grouse and bobwhite 
13quail.
