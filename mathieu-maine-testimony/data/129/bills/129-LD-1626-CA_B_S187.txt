3 SUMMARY 
4 This amendment, which is a minority report of the committee, strikes the provision of 
5 the bill mandating that each party's presidential primary elections are closed to any voter 
6 not enrolled in that party. Under the amendment, an unenrolled voter may choose to vote 
7 in one party's presidential primary election. The amendment also corrects cross-
8 references in the bill.
