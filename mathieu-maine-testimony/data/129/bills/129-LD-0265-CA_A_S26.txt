SUMMARY 
2 This amendment replaces the bill and adds an emergency preamble and an emergency 
3 clause. It establishes that the open season on upland game must begin on the last Saturday 
4 in September and authorizes the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to 
5 establish the length of the season. It also removes ring-necked pheasant from the 
6 definition of "upland game."
