27SUMMARY
28This bill reestablishes a ceiling that was repealed in 2013 on energy efficiency 
29spending from the Regional Greenhouse Gas Initiative Trust Fund; provides for transfers 
30from the trust fund to Maine-based energy-intensive businesses, in addition to transfers to 
31affected customers; requires that funds over a certain amount be distributed to ratepayers; 
32and authorizes the Public Utilities Commission to adopt implementing rules.
