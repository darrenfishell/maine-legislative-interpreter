8 SUMMARY 
9 This amendment requires the Department of Health and Human Services to issue a 
10 request for proposals to conduct the time study. It also requires the Department of Health 
11 and Human Services to submit a report, together with recommendations and suggested 
12 legislation, to the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human Services describing 
13 the department's activities and actions regarding the time study and of the Alzheimer's 
14 and dementia care and other memory care work group established to review training of 
15 direct care staff. The amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section.
