11opportunities for young adults with disabilities after high school.

13provide the task force with staff assistance from existing resources.

15funding to support the work of the task force.

12SUMMARY
13This resolve establishes the Task Force To Study the Coordination of Services and 
14Expansion of Educational Programs for Young Adults with Disabilities. The membership 
15of the task force consists of Legislators who serve on the joint standing committees of the 
16Legislature having jurisdiction over education and cultural affairs, health and human 
17services matters and labor and housing matters, the Commissioner of Education, the 
18Commissioner of Health and Human Services and the Commissioner of Labor, members 
19of organizations or associations knowledgeable about services for young adults with 
20disabilities after high school, a parent or guardian of a young adult with a disability and a 
21a young adult with a disability who is receiving services from the State. The 
22Commissioner of Education convenes the task force, which must hold a minimum of 4 
23meetings and submit a report to the joint standing committees of the Legislature having 
24jurisdiction over education and cultural affairs, health and human services matters and 
25labor and housing matters on recommendations and targeted reforms to improve the 
26efficiency and effectiveness of services provided by different agencies and continuing 
27educational opportunities for young adults with disabilities after high school.OTHER SPECIAL REVENUE FUNDS 2019-20 2020-21
