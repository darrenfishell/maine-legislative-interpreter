HOUSE AMENDMENT "!l "to H.P. 1049, L.D. 1437, "RESOLUTION, Proposing 
an Amendment to the Constitution of Maine Concerning Alternative Signatures Made by 
Persons with Disabilities" 
Amend the resolution in section 20 in the 14th and 15th lines (page 1, lines 18 and 19 
. in L.D.) by striking out the following: ", except when the Legislature by proper 
enactment has authorized alternative signatures for persons with disabilities," and 
inse1iing the following: ', or, as authorized by law, the alternative signatures of persons 
with physical disabilities that prevent them from signing their own names.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment revises the language in the resolution that allows alternative 
signatures for persons with disabilities signing a petition for a people's veto and for a 
direct initiative to clarify that the alternative signatures are authorized for use by persons 
with physical disabilities that prevent them from signing their own names.
