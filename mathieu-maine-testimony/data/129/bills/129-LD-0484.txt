Page 1 - 129LR0420(01)-11Sec. 1.  Department of Education, in consultation with the State Board of 
2Education, to establish a Maine state high school diploma. Resolved:  That the 
3Department of Education, in consultation with the State Board of Education, shall 
4develop a Maine state high school diploma designed to ensure consistency of academic 
5standards for high school graduates.  The department shall define the criteria for the 
6Maine state high school diploma, which must include a statewide exam that is defined, 
7proctored and graded at the state level and overseen by the Department of Education 
8while still respecting the role and value of local control.  The Maine state high school 
9diploma must be designed to be in addition to a high school diploma awarded by a 
10secondary school.
11Sec. 2.  Reporting date established.  Resolved:  That the Department of 
12Education shall report its findings and recommendations with respect to the Maine state 
13high school diploma developed pursuant to section 1, including recommended changes to 
14the law needed to implement the Maine state high school diploma, to the Joint Standing 
15Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs no later than January 15, 2020.  After 
16receipt and review of the report, the joint standing committee may report out legislation 
17regarding the implementation of the Maine state high school diploma to the Second 
18Regular Session of the 129th Legislature.
19SUMMARY
20This resolve directs the Department of Education, in consultation with the State 
21Board of Education, to develop a Maine state high school diploma and report  to the Joint 
22Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs on its findings and 
23recommendations no later than January 15, 2020.
