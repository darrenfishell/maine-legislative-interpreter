6 This amendment, which is the unanimous report of the committee, clarifies the 
7 provisions regarding the exemption of an account established under a qualified ABLE 
8 program that complies with the requirements of the federal Achieving a Better Life 
9 Experience Act of 2014, Public Law 113-295 from Medicaid estate recovery to the extent 
10 permitted under federal law.
