32SUMMARY
33This bill prohibits insurance carriers providing individual health plans or small group 
34health plans from varying premium rates based on age, geographic location or tobacco 
35use on or after January 1, 2020.  The bill also reduces the variation based on group size to 
361.5 to 1 for small group plans over time.
