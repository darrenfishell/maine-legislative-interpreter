9COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “      ” to S.P. 686, L.D. 1984, Bill, “An Act To 
10Eliminate Waiting Lists for Home and Community-based Services for Adults with 
11Intellectual Disabilities, Autism, Brain Injury and Other Related Conditions”
12Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
13'An Act To Eliminate Waiting Lists for Support Services for Adults with Intellectual 
14Disabilities or Autism and Reduce Waiting Lists for Home and Community-based 
15Services for Adults with Intellectual Disabilities or Autism' 
16Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and inserting the following:
17'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
18become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
19Whereas, persons with disabilities have the right to dignity and to receive the 
20support they need to work and to be otherwise included in their communities; and
21Whereas, many persons with disabilities have an urgent need for adult home and 
22community-based services to provide support and to enable their family members to 
23work; and
24Whereas, as of December 2019, more than 180 persons on the waiting list for 
25services under the MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapters II and III, Section 21 were 
26receiving no services at all; and
27Whereas, providing and increasing services for all MaineCare members eligible for 
28services under the MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapters II and III, Section 29 protects 
29their dignity and ensures their safety; and
30Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
31the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
32immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
33therefore,
34Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:Date: (Filing No. S-          )

12SUMMARY
13This amendment replaces the bill and provides funding to:
141.  Clear the waiting list for services under the MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapters 
15II and III, Section 29 through the end of fiscal year 2020-21;
162.  Increase the annual cap for a person receiving services under the MaineCare 
17Benefits Manual, Chapters II and III, Section 29 to $70,000; and
183.  Reduce waiting lists for services under the MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapters 
19II and III, Section 21 by providing services to those individuals who are designated 
20Priority 2 and are receiving no other MaineCare home and community-based or support 
21services.
