24Department of Health and Human Services shall cover the costs using General Fund 
25money.
26SUMMARY
27This bill allows nonemergency medical transportation services that are currently 
28available to MaineCare members for transportation to and from providers of medical 
29services to be used by parents or guardians of MaineCare members under 18 years of age 
30to and from providers of medical services when no other means of transportation is 
31available to the parents or guardians.  The bill also requires the Department of Health and 
32Human Services to adopt or amend rules to implement this provision.  The Department of 
33Health and Human Services is required to apply for any Medicaid waivers or submit any 
34state plan amendments to the United States Department of Health and Human Services, 
35Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services to receive matching funds.  In the event that 
36no matching federal funds are available, the Department of Health and Human Services is 
37required to cover the cost using General Fund money.
