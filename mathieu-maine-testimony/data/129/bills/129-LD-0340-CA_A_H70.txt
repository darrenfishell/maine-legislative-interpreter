10 SUMMARY 
11 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. It clarifies that a lobster and 
12 crab fishing license holder who has a substantial illness or medical condition and has 
13 been granted a temporary medical allowance may continue to fish under the license as 
14 long as the license holder does not fish concurrently with the individual authorized to fish 
15 under the authority of that license holder through the temporary medical allowance. It 
16 adds domestic partners to the list of individuals authorized to fish under this provision. It 
17 also clarifies that the license holder is liable for the activities of the individual fishing 
18 under the temporary medical allowance whether or not the license holder is present on the 
19 vessel.
