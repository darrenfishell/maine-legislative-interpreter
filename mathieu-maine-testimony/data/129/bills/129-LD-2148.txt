9SUMMARY
10This bill, which is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on Environment and 
11Natural Resources pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 38, section 1772, 
12subsection 5, implements the recommendations of the Department of Environmental 
13Protection regarding the State’s plastic bag reduction law, as contained in its annual 
14report on the State’s product stewardship programs pursuant to Title 38, section 1772, 
15subsection 2.  The bill amends in that law the definition of "single-use carry-out bag" to 
16clarify that this definition includes bags provided within a retail establishment other than 
17at the point of sale and includes bags used for packaging, protecting or otherwise 
18containing merchandise within the retail establishment.
