SUMMARY 
This amendment changes the entity reporting information to the Legislature regarding 
the access to justice credit from the State Tax Assessor to the Supreme Judicial Court and 
clarifies that the authorized disclosure by the assessor of tax inf mmation related to the 
credit is to the Supreme Judicial Court for purposes of making the report to the 
Legislature.

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill provides an income tax credit to eligible attorneys and would result in a reduction in General Fund revenue of 
$23, 750 and a reduction in Local Government Fund revenue of $1,250 in fiscal year 2020-21. 
Any additional costs to the Supreme Judicial Court, within the Judicial Department, to report to the Legislature can be 
absorbed within existing budgeted resources.
