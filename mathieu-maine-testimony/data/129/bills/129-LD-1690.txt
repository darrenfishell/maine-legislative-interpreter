3SUMMARY
4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to create a "Maine Made" certification depicted by a seal or a 
6stamp placed upon products produced in the State and used or marketed worldwide to 
7promote the products.  The certification would be used in conjunction with or in 
8substitution for the current Maine Made America's Best program administered by the 
9Department of Economic and Community Development, office of business development 
10and innovation.
