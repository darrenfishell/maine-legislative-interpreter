31SUMMARY
32This bill, which is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on Veterans and 
33Legal Affairs pursuant to Public Law 2019, chapter 409, section 7, implements the 
34recommendations of the Secretary of State for implementation of the automatic voter 
35registration system. The committee has not taken a position on the substance of the bill 
36and by reporting this bill out the committee is not suggesting and does not intend to 
37suggest that it agrees or disagrees with any aspect of this bill. The committee is reporting 
38the bill out for the sole purpose of obtaining a printed bill that can be referred to the 
39committee for a public hearing and subsequent committee action in the normal course.  
Page 2 - 129LR3220(01)-11The committee is taking this action to ensure clarity and transparency in the legislative 
2review of the proposal.
3The bill replaces the requirement that the Department of the Secretary of State, 
4Bureau of Motor Vehicles scan documentation that will be used to create a pending voter 
5registration record for an individual who has not opted out of automatic voter registration 
6and who applies for a driver's license or nondriver identification card with a requirement 
7that the bureau record the individual's documentation for pending voter registration 
8record purposes.  The bill also authorizes the bureau to create a pending voter registration 
9record for an individual who has previously applied for a driver's license or nondriver 
10identification card and submitted documentation sufficient to create a pending voter 
11registration record if the individual appears before the bureau to conduct another 
12transaction and does not opt out of automatic voter registration.
13The bill directs the Secretary of State to submit, by January 1, 2021, a report to the 
14joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over voter registration 
15matters on the progress made toward implementing automatic voter registration and the 
16estimated time required to complete all activities necessary for implementation. The joint 
17standing committee may report out legislation to the First Regular Session of the 130th 
18Legislature based on the report.
