32SUMMARY
33This bill allows a municipality to impose a local option sales tax, which may be 
34seasonal, if approved by referendum of the voters in that municipality.  The referendum 
35question must identify the rate of the local option sales tax, specify the months during 
36which it will be imposed if not year-round and identify the purposes for which the 
37revenue will be used.  The local option sales tax would be collected and administered by 
38the State in the same manner as the sales and use tax.  Revenue from the local option 
39sales tax is distributed to the municipality imposing the local option sales tax.  Revenue 
40received by a municipality may not be used to reduce or eliminate funding otherwise due 
41the municipality under other provisions of law.
