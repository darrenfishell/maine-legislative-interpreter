Page 1 - 129LR1270(01)-11Sec. 1.  Study establishment of a Maine School Safety Center.  Resolved:  
2That the Department of Education shall study and make recommendations for the 
3establishment of a Maine School Safety Center based on a report created by Safe Havens 
4International pursuant to Resolve 2013, chapter 76, the top recommendation of which was 
5the establishment of such a center, as detailed in the Safe Havens International report 
6entitled "Excerpts from the School Security Reports Submitted to the Maine Department 
7of Education January, 2014."  The department shall issue a report regarding the study, 
8including its recommendations for the establishment of a center and any suggested 
9legislation, to the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs by 
10October 15, 2019.  The joint standing committee may submit a bill to the Second Regular 
11Session of the 129th Legislature.
12SUMMARY
13This resolve directs the Department of Education to study and make 
14recommendations for the establishment of a Maine School Safety Center based on a 
15report created by Safe Havens International, the top recommendation of which was the 
16establishment of such a center.  The department is directed to issue a report, including its 
17recommendations for the establishment of a center and any suggested legislation, to the 
18Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs by October 15, 2019.  The 
19joint standing committee is authorized to submit a bill to the Second Regular Session of 
20the 129th Legislature.
