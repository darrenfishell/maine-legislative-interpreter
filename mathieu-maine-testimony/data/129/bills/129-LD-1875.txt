11SUMMARY
12This bill requires the Department of Transportation to designate Bridge 5818 on 
13Interstate 95, which crosses Main Street in the City of Waterville, the Specialist Wade A. 
14Slack Memorial Bridge.  The bill also requires the department, when designating a bridge 
15in this State with a specific name, to deem separate bridges that run parallel to one 
16another on the same highway as one bridge for the purposes of that designation.
