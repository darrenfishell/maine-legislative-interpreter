41.  Qualifications.  The Director of the Maine Center for Disease Control and 
5Prevention, referred to in this section as "the director," must:

11or both.
122.  Annual report.  The director shall report annually to the joint standing committee 
13of the Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human services matters on:
14A.  Challenges and threats to the health of the residents of the State; and
15B.  The ways in which the Maine Center for Disease Control and Prevention has 
16responded to those challenges and threats and has aided in keeping the residents of 
17the State healthy and safe.
18SUMMARY
19This bill establishes qualifications for the Director of the Maine Center for Disease 
20Control and Prevention within the Department of Health and Human Services, and 
21requires that the director report annually to the joint standing committee of the 
22Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human services matters on challenges and 
23threats to public health and ways in which the Maine Center for Disease Control and 
24Prevention has responded to those challenges and threats.
