2SUMMARY
3This bill:
41.  Increases the one-time fee for an out-of-rotation registration plate and for a 
5reserved registration number from $15 to $25, the same as for a vanity registration plate 
6fee;
72.  Increases the registration transfer fee for a trailer under 2,000 pounds from $5 to 
8$8, making the fee the same as for other registration transfers;
93.  Increases the motorcycle rider education school and instructor license fees to 
10match fees for other business licenses issued by the Department of the Secretary of State, 
11Bureau of Motor Vehicles, such as certain dealer licenses;
124.  Increases driver education school and instructor license fees to match other 
13business license fees;
145.  Increases the fee for a duplicate registration certificate from $2 to $5; and
156.  Reduces the driver's license fees for persons 65 years of age and over by $1 to 
16provide equity in the fees assessed on driver's licenses issued to this demographic.
