This bill moves the Kim Wallace Adaptive Equipment Loan Program Fund from the Finance Authority of Maine to 
the Treasurer of State. The purpose of the fund does not change, but continues to be to provide loans to qualified 
bonowers to acquire adaptive equipment. The bill includes ongoing Other Special Revenue Funds allocations of $500 
to establish the fund with the Treasurer of State and to allow expenditures to be made from the fund. 
This change is not expected to have any significant impact on the Finance Authority of Maine.
