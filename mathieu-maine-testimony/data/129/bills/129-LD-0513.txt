4SUMMARY
5This bill limits total enrollment at all virtual public charter schools authorized by the 
6commission to the total enrollment at the end of the 2018-2019 school year.  It also 
7prohibits a virtual public charter school authorized by the commission from expanding to 
8serve a grade level not included in the school's initial charter contract or, for a school 
9whose charter was renewed prior to November 1, 2019, the renewed charter contract.
