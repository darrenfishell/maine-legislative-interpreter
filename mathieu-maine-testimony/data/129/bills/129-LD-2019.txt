2Sec. 1.  26 MRSA §976 is enacted to read:
3§976.  Obligations during interim between contracts
4During the interim after the expiration of a collective bargaining agreement and 
5before the effective date of any subsequent collective bargaining agreement, public 
6employees covered by the expired collective bargaining agreement remain eligible for 
7and must receive merit increases in accordance with the terms and conditions set forth in 
8the expired collective bargaining agreement.

23SUMMARY
24This bill provides the same protections to municipal, judicial and public higher 
25education employees that are provided to state employees upon the expiration of labor 
26contracts by requiring that, during an interim between the expiration of a public employee 
27collective bargaining agreement and before the effective date of any subsequent 
28collective bargaining agreement, those employees covered by the expired collective 
29bargaining agreement remain eligible for and must receive merit increases in accordance 
30with the terms and conditions set forth in the expired collective bargaining agreement.
