21SUMMARY
22This bill consolidates reporting requirements regarding Department of Health and 
23Human Services planning for and activities concerning the State's aging population and 
24incapacitated and dependent adults and the department's quality assurance review 
25committee's recommendations and activities.  It also repeals requirements for annual 
26press releases and reports by the Commissioner of Health and Human Services regarding 
27department spending, welfare fraud-related statistics, contracting services, grants received 
28from the Federal Government and total out-of-state travel costs for employees.
