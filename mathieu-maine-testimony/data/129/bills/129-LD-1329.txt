3ยง1154.  Temporary roadside memorials
41.  Placement.  A person may place a roadside memorial on a public way, as defined 
5in section 1903, subsection 11, within 20 feet of the outside edge of the paved portion of 
6the public way for a period not to exceed 180 days.  For purposes of this section, 
7"roadside memorial" means decorations, flags, flowers and other lightweight objects or 
8ornamentation commonly used at funerals or grave sites that are used to mark the site of 
9the death of a person on the public way.
102.  Prohibitions.  A roadside memorial under this section may not:
11A.  Interfere with the use of the public way or with the visibility of directional and 
12other official signs and notices or with road safety, construction or maintenance;
13B.  Display a political or commercial message; or
14C.  Use reflective material or any item that is distracting to drivers and other users of 
15the public way.
163.  Notification.  A person who places a roadside memorial under this section shall 
17inform the Department of Transportation or the municipality containing the location of 
18the roadside memorial of the location of the roadside memorial within 7 days of the 
19placement of the roadside memorial and provide that person's contact information.
204.  Tampering with or unauthorized removal of roadside memorial.  A person 
21may not destroy, mutilate, deface or injure a roadside memorial or remove a roadside 
22memorial without the written authorization of the person who placed the roadside 
23memorial under subsection 1 to remove the roadside memorial.  A person who violates 
24this subsection commits a civil violation for which a fine of up to $100 may be adjudged.
255.  Removal by State or municipality.  Notwithstanding subsections 1 and 4, the 
26State or the municipality in which a roadside memorial is placed under this section may 
27remove a roadside memorial at any time.
286.  Liability.  The State and the municipality in which a roadside memorial is placed 
29under this section are not liable for damage or injury resulting from the presence of the 
30roadside memorial.
317.  Rules.  The Department of Transportation shall adopt rules to administer this 
32section.  Rules adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules under Title 
335, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
