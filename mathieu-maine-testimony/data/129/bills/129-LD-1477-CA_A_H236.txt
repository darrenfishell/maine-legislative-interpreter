3 SUMMARY 
4 This amendment allows the Legislature, or the people using the direct initiative, to 
5 establish the method for determining the winners of general elections for Governor, State 
6 Senators and State Representatives. The amendment also clarifies that, after local 
7 officials form records of the votes received in a general election for Representatives, 
8 Senators and Governor, the ballots may be forwarded to the Secretary of State at the 
9 direction of the Secretary of State.
