24 SUMMARY 
25 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. It establishes September 11th 
26 as First Responders Day. It requires the Governor to issue a proclamation inviting and 
27 urging the people of the State to observe this day through appropriate ceremony, 
28 celebration and activity. First Responders Day honors the significant contributions of the 
29 men and women of Maine who put their lives in danger to keep the people of Maine safe, 
30 including law enforcement officers, firefighters, emergency medical personnel, game 
31 wardens, forest rangers and marine patrol officers.
