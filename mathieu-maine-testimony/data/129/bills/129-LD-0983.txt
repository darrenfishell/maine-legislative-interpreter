3Sec. 3.  38 MRSA §480-Q, sub-§2-E is enacted to read:
42-E.  Low-head dams.  A permit is not required for the repair of any extent of a low-
5head dam that is not a hydropower dam, whether privately or publicly owned, including

8SUMMARY
9This bill exempts low-head dams from the permitting requirements for repair under 
10the Natural Resources Protection Act.
