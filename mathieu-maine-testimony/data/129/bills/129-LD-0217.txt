17SUMMARY
18This bill allows a licensed veterans' organization, at the discretion of and by 
19agreement with the Commissioner of Administrative and Financial Services, to sell liquor 
20to the general public, subject to time-of-day and seasonal limitations defined at the time 
21of license approval, if the organization has a valid license and is located in a municipality 
22with fewer than 5,000 residents or is located on an island off the coast of the State that is
