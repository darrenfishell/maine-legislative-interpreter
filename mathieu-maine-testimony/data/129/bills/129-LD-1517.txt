41.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
5following terms have the following meanings.
6A.  "Small wireless facility" means a wireless facility each antenna of which could fit 
7within an enclosure of no more than 3 cubic feet and of which all associated wireless 
8equipment other than antennas, electric meters and concealment elements has a 
9cumulative volume of no more than 28 cubic feet.
10B.  "Wireless facility" means equipment at a fixed location that enables wireless 
11communications between user equipment and a communications network, including 
12equipment associated with wireless communications; radio transceivers, antennas, 
13coaxial or fiber-optic cable, regular and backup power supplies and rectifiers; and 
14comparable equipment, regardless of technological configuration.  "Wireless facility" 
15includes a small wireless facility.  "Wireless facility" does not include the structure or 
16improvements on, under, within or adjacent to which the equipment is colocated or 
17coaxial or fiber-optic cable that is between wireless support structures or poles or that 
18is otherwise not immediately adjacent to or directly associated with a particular 
19antenna.
202.  Small wireless facilities.  Notwithstanding any zoning or land use ordinance to 
21the contrary, a small wireless facility must be a permitted use within the public 
22right-of-way, subject to any duly adopted, nondiscriminatory conditions otherwise 
23applicable to permitted uses within the municipality and consistent with state and federal 
24law, including, without limitation, any permitting requirements in Title 35-A, chapter 25.
25SUMMARY
26This bill provides that a small wireless facility must be a permitted use within the 
27public right-of-way, subject to any duly adopted, nondiscriminatory conditions otherwise 
28applicable to permitted uses within the municipality and consistent with state and federal 
29law, including, without limitation, any permitting requirements in the Maine Revised 
30Statutes, Title 35-A, chapter 25.
