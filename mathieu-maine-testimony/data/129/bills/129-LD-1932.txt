6SUMMARY
7This bill allows private elementary and secondary schools and public and private 
8postsecondary educational institutions in the State to, without a guide license, conduct 
9camping trips for students and adults associated with those institutions. The bill requires 
10the staff member in charge of such a trip to obtain a camp trip leader permit.
