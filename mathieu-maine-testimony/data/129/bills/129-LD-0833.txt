3SUMMARY
4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to amend the laws governing the Maine Public Employees 
6Retirement System to provide the same retirement benefits as are provided to law 
7enforcement officers to state employees working in law enforcement, including these job 
8classifications:
91.  Emergency dispatchers;
102.  Staff in the Maine State Police computer crimes unit;
113.  Staff in the Maine State Police Crime Laboratory;
124.  Law enforcement staff at the Attorney General's office; and
135.  Forest rangers.
