Reclassify Certain Waters of the State" 
Amend the bill in section 3 in paragraph A by striking out all of subparagraph (5) 
(page 2, lines 10 and 11 in L.D.) and inserting the following: 
'(5) From the West Enfield Dam, inoluding the Stillwater Branch, to the~

section number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies the description under the State's water classifica(ion 
program of the portion of the Penobscot River, main stem, from the West Enfield Dam to 
the Milford Dam and the Stillwater Branch.
