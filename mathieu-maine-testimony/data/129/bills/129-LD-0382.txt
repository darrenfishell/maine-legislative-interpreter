29SUMMARY
30This bill establishes the Blind and Visually Impaired News Access Fund as a 
31dedicated fund within the Maine State Library to be used to provide access to the 
32National Federation of the Blind's news network for blind and visually impaired persons.  
33This bill directs the Public Utilities Commission to transfer $40,000 per year from the 
34state universal service fund to the Blind and Visually Impaired News Access Fund to
