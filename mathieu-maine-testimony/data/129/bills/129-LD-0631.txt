2Sec. 1.  7 MRSA §2405, as enacted by PL 2001, c. 497, §3, is amended to read:
3§2405.  Integrated Pest Management Fund
4There is created a dedicated, nonlapsing Integrated Pest Management Fund.  The 
5commissioner shall credit funds from any source to the Integrated Pest Management Fund 
6for the purpose of developing and implementing integrated pest management programs.  
7Funds received pursuant to Title 8, section 374, subsection 6 must be used solely to 
8support the operation of a diagnostic and research laboratory focused on ticks and tick-
9borne diseases in the State.  Appropriations from the General Fund may not be credited to 
10the Integrated Pest Management Fund.
11Sec. 2.  8 MRSA §374, sub-§6 is enacted to read:
126.  Lottery game to fund tick identification lab.  The commission, in consultation 
13with the University of Maine Cooperative Extension Pest Management Office, shall 
14develop and initiate a lottery game designed to raise funds for the Integrated Pest 
15Management Fund established pursuant to Title 7, section 2405.  Tickets for the instant 
16lottery game established under this subsection must be sold for $2 and must be changed 
17by the commission periodically throughout the year.  The sales commission paid to agents 
18for the sale of the tickets must be one percentage point higher than the sales commission 
19paid to agents for the sale of other instant lottery game tickets except tickets under 
20subsection 5.  The commission shall provide the net proceeds of the lottery game 
21established under this subsection to the Integrated Pest Management Fund annually.
22SUMMARY
23This bill directs the State Liquor and Lottery Commission, in consultation with the 
24University of Maine Cooperative Extension Pest Management Office, to develop and 
25initiate a $2 instant lottery game designed to raise funds to support the operation of a 
26diagnostic and research laboratory focused on ticks and tick-borne diseases in the State.
