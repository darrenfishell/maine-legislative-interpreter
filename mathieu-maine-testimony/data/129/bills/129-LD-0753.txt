2Sec. 1.  21-A MRSA §753-A, sub-§7 is enacted to read:
37.  Application for ongoing absentee voter status.  A voter may apply, in writing, 
4for status as an ongoing absentee voter in a municipality that has approved the use of 
5ongoing absentee voter status.  Each qualified applicant must automatically receive an 
6absentee ballot for each ensuing statewide and municipal election for which the voter is 
7entitled to vote and need not submit a separate request for each election.
8A.  An application must be made in writing by the voter on a form designed by the 
9Secretary of State.  This form must contain a statement notifying the voter that if the 
10voter moves out of the municipality, that voter's status as an ongoing absentee voter 
11in that municipality terminates.
12B.  The Secretary of State shall send a copy of an approved application under this 
13subsection to the clerk of the municipality where the absentee voter is registered and 
14a copy to the address of the absentee voter.
15C.  The Secretary of State shall terminate a voter's ongoing absentee voter status 
16upon:
17(1)  The written request of the voter;
18(2)  The death or disqualification of the voter;
19(3)  The cancellation of the voter's registration record in the central voter 
20registration system;
21(4)  The return of an absentee ballot as undeliverable; or
22(5)  The designation of a voter's status as inactive in the central voter registration 
23system.

20SUMMARY
21This bill provides a process for a voter to request ongoing absentee voter status in a 
22municipality that has approved the use of ongoing absentee voter status, which allows the 
23voter to automatically receive an absentee ballot for each statewide and municipal 
24election until the status is terminated.
