15SUMMARY
16This bill requires a recovery residence located in a house to be considered a one-
17family dwelling under rules concerning safety to life from fire if the recovery residence 
18has no more than 6 occupants and contains a fire extinguisher and smoke detector in each 
19room.
20This bill also defines "recovery residence" as an alcohol-free and illegal substance-
21free shared living residence for persons recovering from substance use disorder that 
22provides peer support and connects residents to support services and community 
23resources.
