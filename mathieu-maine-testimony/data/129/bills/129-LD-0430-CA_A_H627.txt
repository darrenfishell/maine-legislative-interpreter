The bill includes a one-time General Fund appropriation of $43,500 in fiscal year 2019-20 to the Department of 
Public Safety for the purchase of a new high temperature thermal destruction incinerator to be used for the destruction

associated with establishing an education campaign and updating the website can be absorbed within existing
