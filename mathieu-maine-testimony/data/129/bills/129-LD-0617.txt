8SUMMARY
9This bill authorizes the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to establish 2 
10additional youth deer hunting days, the Monday and Tuesday following the last Saturday 
11of the regular firearms season on deer, to the current youth deer hunting day, which 
12occurs on the Saturday preceding the residents-only Saturday of the regular deer hunting 
13season.
