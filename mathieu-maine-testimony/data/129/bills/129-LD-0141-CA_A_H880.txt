23SUMMARY
24This amendment is the majority report of the committee.  The amendment provides 
25that the glove compartment of a motor vehicle is considered to be within the passenger 
26area only if the glove compartment is unlocked.  The amendment, in the wording on what 
27constitutes a violation of the law, changes "places marijuana or a marijuana product" in a 
28manufacturer's nonmarijuana container to "possesses marijuana or a marijuana product" 
29in a manufacturer's nonmarijuana container.  The amendment limits the exception for a 
30passenger who possesses an open container or a passenger consumes marijuana or a 
31marijuana product in the living quarters of a motor home, trailer, semitrailer or truck 
32camper to a passenger who engages in that conduct while the motor home, trailer, 
33semitrailer or truck camper is not in motion.Date: (Filing No. H-          )
