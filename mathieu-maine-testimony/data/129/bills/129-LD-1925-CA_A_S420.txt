29 SUMMARY 
30 This amendment clarifies language related to the elver quota. Specifically, it does the 
31 following. 
32 1. It allows the Commissioner of Marine Resources to use data collected from the 
33 elver transaction cards to determine whether the overall annual quota has been reached 
34 for federally recognized Indian tribes in the State. 
35 2. It specifies that a person may not possess or sell elvers when that person's elver 
36 transaction card has been used to record transactions equal to or in excess of the elver 
37 quota allocation to that person. 
38 3. It specifies that a person may not fish for or possess elvers for the remainder of the 
39 season when that person's elver transaction card has been used to record sales of elvers in 
40 an amount equal to or in excess of the elver quota allocation to that person.
