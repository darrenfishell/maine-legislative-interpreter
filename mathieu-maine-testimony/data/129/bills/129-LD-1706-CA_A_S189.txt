10 Ensure Public Notification of Air Quality Violations" 
11 Amend the bill by striking out all of section 1 and inserting the following: 
12 'Sec. 1. 38 MRSA §589-A is enacted to read: 
13 §589-A. Notification of violation to affected municipality 
14 When the department issues a notice of violation for a violation of this chapter 
15 pursuant to section 347-A or receives an air quality-related notice of violation from the 
16 United States Environmental Protection Agency, the department shall provide notice of 
17 that violation to each municipality in which the violation occurred. The department may 
18 provide the information required under this section electronically.' 
19 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
20 section number to read consecutively. 
21 SUMMARY 
22 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, amends the bill as 
23 follows. 
24 1. It removes language from the bill requiring the Department of Environmental 
25 Protection to notify affected municipalities regarding the department's initiation of an air 
26 quality-related enforcement action. 
27 2. It adds language requiring the department to notify affected municipalities 
28 regarding the department's receipt of an air quality-related notice of violation from the 
29 United States Environmental Protection Agency.
