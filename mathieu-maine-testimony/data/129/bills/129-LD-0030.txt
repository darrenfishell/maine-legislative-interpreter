4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to enact measures designed to improve the State's capacity to use 
6data regarding health care costs and health care systems.  The State's ability to better 
7analyze and use data regarding health care utilization and health care quality will serve to 
8inform the State's health care policy.
