21 SUMMARY 
22 This amendment provides that an out-of-state secondary school that was included on 
23 the list of approved out-of-state secondary schools maintained by the Department of 
24 Education for the 2017-2018 school year continues to receive tuition payments for any 
25 student who resides in Maine in a school administrative unit that does not maintain a 
26 secondary school and who was enrolled at that out-of-state secondary school for the 
27 2018-2019 school year. Tuition payments must continue for such a student until that 
28 student graduates or terminates enrollment.
