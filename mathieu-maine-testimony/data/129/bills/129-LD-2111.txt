19SUMMARY
20This bill makes the following changes.
211.  The bill requires health care entities, which includes health care practitioners and 
22facilities, to disclose the average cost in the State for the service for which a patient has 
23been scheduled and the entities offering the service at the highest and lowest rates in the 
24State if the patient has been scheduled or referred for one of the 25 highest cost services 
25or procedures.
262.  The bill requires health care entities to disclose that a health care facility use fee 
27will be charged and identify that fee separately on any bill provided to a patient.
283.  The bill prohibits a health care entity from charging a patient when a billing 
29statement has not been provided within 6 months of the date the patient received the 
30services.
314.  The bill requires a health care entity to disclose to a federal Medicare patient who 
32is on observation status that the patient's observation status may increase the patient's out-
33of-pocket costs associated with a stay at a health care entity and the estimated increase in 
34the patient's out-of-pocket costs.
355.  The bill provides that a carrier must require a provider receiving a referral to 
36disclose to the patient whether the provider is an out-of-network provider.
