3 SUMMARY 
4 This amendment, which is the unanimous report of the committee, clarifies that the 
5 department must establish a bundled rate for coordinated specialty care. It also provides 
6 that the department may review, develop or apply for any source of funds that may be 
7 available to implement reimbursement for the coordinated specialty care model. The 
8 amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section.
