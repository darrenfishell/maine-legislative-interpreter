136.  Refundability of credit.  The credit under this section is refundable for eligible

16subsection 1, paragraph B.
17SUMMARY
18This bill provides that the income tax credit for homestead modifications to improve 
19accessibility for an individual with a disability or physical hardship is refundable if the 
20individual is 65 years of age or older and the individual's Maine adjusted gross income is 
21equal to or less than 138% of the federal poverty level.
