15 'Sec. 1. Energy planning. Resolved: That the Governor's Energy Office shall, 
16 as a complement to the State's overall interagency and regional planning goals and in 
17 coordination with development of the state energy plan prepared pursuant to Title 2, 
18 section 9, subsection 3, paragraph C or other planning initiatives, conduct an analysis for 
19 at least one scenario through which the State, by 2030, can become a net exporter of 
20 energy through the development and expansion of energy generating capacity within the 
21 boundaries of the State and its coastal waters, energy conservation and energy efficiency 
22 at levels sufficient to offset the total value of the State's domestic energy consumption 
23 across all sectors. This analysis must identify economic benefits to the State from 
24 becoming a net exporter and policies that would be necessary to achieve this outcome. 
25 Sec. 2. Report.~ Resolved: That the Governor's Energy Office shall provide a 
26 report on progress regarding the state energy plan and the analysis required under section 
27 1, along with any recommended policy initiatives, to the Joint Standing Committee on 
28 Energy, Utilities and Technology by December 31, 2019.'
