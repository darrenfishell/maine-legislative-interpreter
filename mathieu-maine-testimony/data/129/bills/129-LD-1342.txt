11§12.  Maine Workforce, Research, Development and Student Achievement Institute
12The Maine Workforce, Research, Development and Student Achievement Institute, 
13as established in Title 5, section 12004-I, subsection 6-I and referred to in this section as 
14"the institute," is established to collect and analyze information related to workforce 
15training, research and development, student debt and economic advancement in the State, 
16to make policy recommendations to the Legislature and to conduct targeted research for 
17the Legislature.

9SUMMARY
10This bill establishes the Maine Workforce, Research, Development and Student 
11Achievement Institute and a related steering committee to advise the Legislature on 
12matters related to workforce training, research and development, student debt and 
13economic advancement in the State. The bill directs the Legislature, through the joint 
14standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over innovation, development, 
15economic advancement and business matters, to contract with the University of Maine 
16System to establish the steering committee. The bill describes the 8 required steering 
17committee members and establishes that each member serves a 2-year term and that the 
18steering committee will meet at least 4 times per year.
