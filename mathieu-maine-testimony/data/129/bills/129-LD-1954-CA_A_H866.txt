27SUMMARY
28 This amendment, which is the unanimous report of the committee, clarifies that the 
29 provisions regarding estate recovery relating to hospital and prescription drug services do 
30 not apply to those services provided to MaineCare members generally, but only to a 
31 MaineCare member who is receiving nursing facility services or home and community-
32 based services.  The amendment also provides that actions that are pending as of the 
33 effective date of this legislation are not affected by the changes to estate recovery provided 
34 in this legislation.
35 This amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section.
