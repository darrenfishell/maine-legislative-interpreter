14(b)  The surviving spouse of an eligible veteran who had a subsequent

17(c)  A minor child of an eligible veteran.  For purposes of this division, a

19(i)  Has not attained 21 years of age; or
20(ii)  Has not attained 23 years of age and is pursuing a full-time course of 
21instruction at an educational institution offering an accredited 
22postsecondary educational degree program; and
23(d)  An unmarried adult child of an eligible veteran if that child became 
24permanently physically or mentally disabled and incapable of self-support:
25(i)  Before attaining 21 years of age; or
26(ii)  Before attaining 23 years of age if supporting documentation exists 
27that the adult child was pursuing a full-time course of instruction at an 
28educational institution offering an accredited postsecondary educational 
29degree program.
30(2)  "Eligible veteran" means any person who:
31(a)  Served in the active United States Armed Forces and who:
32(i)  If discharged, received an honorable discharge or a general discharge 
33under honorable conditions, provided that as long as the discharge was 
34not upgraded through a program of general amnesty; and
35(ii)  If having served as an enlisted person after September 7, 1980 or as 
36an officer after October 16, 1981, served for a minimum of 24 continuous 
37months or the full period for which the person was called to active duty;
Page 2 - 129LR3038(01)-11(b)  Served in the Maine National Guard and died as a result of injury, 
2disease or illness sustained while serving on active state service as provided 
3in chapter 3, subchapter 3;
4(d)  Served in the Reserve Components of the United States Armed Forces 
5and was entitled to retired pay under 10 United States Code, chapter 1223, 
6section 12731 or would have been entitled to retired pay under chapter 1223, 
7section 12731 except that the person was under 60 years of age; or
8(e) Died while serving in the Active Guard Reserve and whose death is 
9determined to be in the line of duty.
10SUMMARY
11This bill clarifies the requirements for eligibility for burial in the Maine Veterans' 
12Memorial Cemetery System for veterans and dependents of veterans by amending the 
13definitions of "eligible veteran" and "eligible dependent."
