7 SUMMARY 
8 This amendment replaces the title and the bill and adds an emergency preamble and 
9 emergency clause. It allows a person who holds a valid moose permit to transfer that 
10 permit to an eligible disabled veteran. If a person does not identify a specific disabled 
11 veteran to receive the transferred permit, the permit may be returned to the department, 
12 which provides for the transfer of the permit to a disabled veteran.
