29SUMMARY
30This bill changes the method for funding teacher retirement costs.  It repeals those 
31provisions of law enacted pursuant to Public Law 2013, chapter 368 that require school 
32administrative units and private schools to pay a portion of the costs for teacher 
33retirement.
