9SUMMARY
10This bill amends the definition of "authorized emergency vehicle" in the motor 
11vehicle laws to include a Department of Corrections vehicle operated by an investigative 
12officer or other employee of the department who is certified by the Board of Trustees of 
13the Maine Criminal Justice Academy as a law enforcement officer.
