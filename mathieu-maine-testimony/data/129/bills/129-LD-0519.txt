17SUMMARY
18This bill expands the availability of coverage for comprehensive dental services.
19Part A of the bill broadens the scope of required coverage under the MaineCare 
20program for adults. Under current law, coverage for adults over age 21 is limited to 
21certain services.
22Part B of the bill requires health insurance carriers to provide coverage for 
23comprehensive dental services. Part B applies to policies and contracts issued or renewed 
24on or after January 1, 2020.
25Part C of the bill requires dentists as a condition of license renewal to adopt and 
26implement policies to provide comprehensive dental services through charity care or 
27financial assistance to persons without MaineCare coverage or private health insurance.  
28Part C also authorizes the Board of Dental Practice to adopt rules relating to this 
29requirement.
