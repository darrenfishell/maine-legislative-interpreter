COMMITTEE AMENDMENT Â·A" to H.P. 1117, L.D. 1534, Bill, "An Act To 
Amend Maine Fireworks Laws To Include Flame Effects" 
Amend the bill by inserting after the title and before the enacting clause the 
following: 
'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and 
Whereas, the summer and fall seasons for flame effects shows will begin before the 
expiration of the 90-day period; and 
Whereas, the potential for harm to the public from an uninspected flame effects 
show is very real and dangerous; and 
Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
therefore, 1 
Amend the bill by inserting at the end before the summary the following: 
'Emergency clause. In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
legislation ta1ces effect when approved.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause in order for the 
bill to take effect when approved and provide inspections for flame effects shows during 
the summer and fall 2019 seasons.
