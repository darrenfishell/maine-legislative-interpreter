27Sec. 2.  24-A MRSA §4321, sub-§5 is enacted to read:
285.  Assistance to employers with 50 or fewer employees.  The division shall 
29provide education and resources to assist employers with 50 or fewer employees 
30regarding health care options and obtaining health care coverage and services for their 
31employees, including, but not limited to, options for coverage through the commercial 
32group market, through association group coverage, through small business health options 
33through the federal Affordable Care Act and through self-insurance.
34SUMMARY
35This bill requires the Bureau of Insurance, Consumer Health Care Division to provide 
36education and resources to employers with 50 or fewer employees to assist with making 
37decisions related to health care coverage for their employees.
