This amendment reduces the General Fund appropriations to the Department of Health and Human Services by 
$216,903 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $224, 118 in fiscal year 2020-21 by eliminating one Health Program Manager to 
be the Oral Health Coordinator in the oral health program within the Maine Center for Disease Control and 
Prevention, rural health and primary care division, to lead the center's public health functions related to oral health, 
one Planning and Research Associate II in the Maine Center for Disease Control and Prevention and one half-time 
Office Assistant II position to provide logistical and administrative support within the Maine Center for Disease 
Control and Prevention.
