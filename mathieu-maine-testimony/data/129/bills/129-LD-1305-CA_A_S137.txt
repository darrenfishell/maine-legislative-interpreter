The bill creates a tax credit for taxpayers who make contributions to family development account reserve funds and 
would result in a reduction in General Fund revenue of $9,500 in fiscal year 2020-21 and a reduction in Local 
Government Fund revenue of $500 in fiscal year 2020-21. The bill includes a one-time General Fund appropriation of 
$33,000 in fiscal year 2020-21 to the Department of Administrative and Financial Services for computer programming 
to add one additional line to the income tax returns.
