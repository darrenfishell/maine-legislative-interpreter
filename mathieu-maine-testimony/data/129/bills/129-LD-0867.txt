16ยง1112.   Public health impact statements
17When requested by the chairs of a committee, the department shall prepare and 
18provide to the committee an impact statement for the legislation being considered by the 
19committee.  The department shall provide the impact statement no later than the work 
20session scheduled for that legislation.
21SUMMARY
22This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services, when requested by 
23the chairs of a joint standing committee of the Legislature, to prepare and provide a 
24public health impact statement for legislation before the committee.  The public health 
25impact statement must include the potential positive and negative public health effects of 
26the legislation and considerations necessary to the decision-making process regarding the 
27legislation and provide practical recommendations to increase the positive health effects 
28and minimize negative health effects of the legislation.
