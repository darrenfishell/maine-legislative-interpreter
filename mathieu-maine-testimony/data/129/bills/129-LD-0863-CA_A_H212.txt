Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill provides a sales tax exemption for diapers and diaper products and would result in a reduction of General 
Fund revenue of $1,202,198 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $1,634,990 in fiscal year 2020-21. It would also reduce Local 
Govemment Fund revenue by $63,274 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $86,052 in fiscal year 2020-21.
