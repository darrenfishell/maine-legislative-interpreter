11"Do you favor increasing the number of Franklin County commissioner 
12districts from 3 to 5 and staggering the terms of the 5 county 
13commissioners following redistricting conducted pursuant to the Maine 
14Constitution in 2021?"

20gubernatorial election.

32SUMMARY
33Public Law 2019, chapter 362 increases the number of Franklin County 
34commissioners, subject to referendum by the voters of Franklin County.  This bill amends 
35that law to authorize submission of the referendum question to voters of Franklin County 
36in the statewide election to be held in November 2019.  This bill also makes technical 
37changes to the designations of new County Commissioner Districts Number 1 and 
38Number 5.
