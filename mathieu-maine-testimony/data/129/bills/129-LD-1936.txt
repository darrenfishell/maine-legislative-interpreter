32SUMMARY
33This bill allows for the reimbursement of a parent providing in-home personal care 
34services to the parent's child by allowing the parent to register as a personal care agency.  
35The child must be eligible for the MaineCare program.  An individual who is not a parent 
36of the child must be designated as the employer and must be approved as the employer by 
37both the parent and the Department of Health and Human Services.  The department is 
38required to request the necessary state plan amendments or waivers from the federal
