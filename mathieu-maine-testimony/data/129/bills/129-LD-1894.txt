21SUMMARY
22This bill directs the Department of Education to endeavor to meet a goal of 
23transitioning the public school bus fleet to 100% all-electric school buses by 2040 and 
24directs the department to adopt rules relating to bid requirements, electric charging 
25stations and uniform school bus specifications.  It requires the department to set aside a 
26percentage of available resources for the purchase of electric small school buses, as 
27replacements in or additions to the school bus fleet, in the 2020 request for proposals.  It 
28requires the department to develop an electric school bus training program to train school 
29bus mechanics, school bus drivers and school transportation directors and it requires 
30annual training by school bus mechanics, school bus drivers and school transportation 
31directors.  It requires the department to assess the need to expand transportation 
32administration staff to manage the transition of the fleet of public school buses in the 
33State to electric school buses.
