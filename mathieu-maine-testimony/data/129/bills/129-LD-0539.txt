Page 1 - 129LR1176(01)-11Sec. 1.  Personal needs allowance for residents of nursing facilities.  
2Resolved:  That, by January 1, 2020, the Department of Health and Human Services 
3shall amend the rules for the MaineCare program to increase the personal needs 
4allowance for persons residing in nursing facilities.  The rules must provide for an 
5increase in the personal needs allowance from $40 per month to $70 per month.  Rules 
6adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules as defined in the Maine 
7Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
8Sec. 2.  Personal needs allowance for residents of residential care 
9facilities.  Resolved:  That, by January 1, 2020, the Department of Health and Human 
10Services shall amend the rules for the MaineCare program to increase the personal needs 
11allowance for persons residing in residential care facilities.  The rules must provide for an 
12increase in the personal needs allowance from $70 per month to $100 per month.  Rules 
13adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules as defined in the Maine 
14Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
15SUMMARY
16This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its 
17MaineCare rules to provide for increases in the personal needs allowances of residents in 
18nursing facilities and residential care facilities.  The rules are designated as routine 
19technical rules.
