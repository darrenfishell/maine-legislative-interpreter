2work requirements under the Temporary Assistance for Needy Families 
3program.  Resolved:  That, by January 1, 2020, the Department of Health and Human 
4Services shall amend its rule 10-144 C.M.R. Chapter 607 to include a Temporary 
5Assistance for Needy Families program participant's attendance at the medical and mental 
6health appointments of the participant's children as a life management skill and job 
7readiness activity.
8SUMMARY
9This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rules 
10to include a Temporary Assistance for Needy Families program participant's attendance 
11at the medical and mental health appointments of the participant's children as a life 
12management skill and job readiness activity.
