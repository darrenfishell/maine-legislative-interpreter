COMMITTEE AMENDMENT .ff:. to H.P. 631, L.D. 857 
1 SUMMARY 
2 This amendment changes the fine structure proposed in the bill from a $2,500 fine for 
3 each subsequent violation of the various wage and hour laws to a fine of at least $500 but 
4 not more than $2,500 for each subsequent violation. It also strikes new language added 
5 by the bill regarding a civil action remedy for violations of various wage and hour laws. 
6 It reduces the liquidated damages available in a civil action to collect unpaid minimum 
7 wages from 3 times the amount of unpaid wages, as proposed by the bill, to twice the 
8 amount of unpaid wages, and it reduces the Labor and Safety Inspector positions added to 
9 the bill from 10 to 4.
