Fiscal Detail and NotesFiscal Note
This bill includes allocations of $251,000 in fiscal y ear 2020-21 to the Public Utilities Commission (PUC) for 2 
Utility Analyst positions, one Senior Consumer Assistance Specialist position and one Public Service Coordinator II 
position and associated costs.  The PUC is funded by an assessment set to produce sufficient revenue for the 
expenditures allocated by the Legislature for operating the PUC.  The increased allocations in this bill require a 
corresponding increase in revenue from the assessm ent on transmission and distribution utilities.  129th MAINE LEGISLATURE
