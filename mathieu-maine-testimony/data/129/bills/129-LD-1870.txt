41.  Maine Lighthouse Trust registration plates. The Secretary of State, upon 
5receiving an application and evidence of payment of the excise tax required by Title 36, 
6section 1482, the annual motor vehicle registration fee required by section 501 or 504 and 
7the contribution provided for in subsection 3, shall issue a registration certificate and a set

102.  Design; review; vanity plates.  The Secretary of State, in consultation with the 
11Maine Lighthouse Trust, shall determine a design for the Maine Lighthouse Trust special 
12registration plates.  The joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction 
13over transportation matters shall review the final design prior to manufacture of the 
14plates.  The Secretary of State shall issue upon request Maine Lighthouse Trust special 
15registration plates that are also vanity plates.  Maine Lighthouse Trust special registration 
16plates are issued in accordance with the provisions of this section and section 453.
173.  Contribution; credit to funds.  In addition to the regular motor vehicle 
18registration fee prescribed by law for the particular class of vehicle registered, the initial 
19contribution for Maine Lighthouse Trust special registration plates is $20, which must be 
20deposited with the Treasurer of State and credited as follows:
21A.  Ten dollars to the Maine Lighthouse Trust to support lighthouse restoration and 
22preservation efforts;
23B.  Nine dollars to the Highway Fund for administrative and production costs; and
24C.  One dollar to the Specialty License Plate Fund established under section 469.
254.  Renewal fee.  In addition to the regular motor vehicle registration fee prescribed 
26by law, the annual renewal contribution for Maine Lighthouse Trust special registration 
27plates is $15, which must be deposited with the Treasurer of State and credited as 
28follows:
29A.  Ten dollars to the Maine Lighthouse Trust to support lighthouse restoration and 
30preservation efforts;
31B.  Four dollars to the Highway Fund for administrative and production costs; and
32C.  One dollar to the Specialty License Plate Fund established under section 469.
335.  Payment for costs associated with the production and issuance of the first 
342,000 plates.  The sponsor of the Maine Lighthouse Trust special registration plates shall 
35provide $50,000 to the Secretary of State for costs associated with the production and 
36issuance of Maine Lighthouse Trust special registration plates.  The Secretary of State 
37shall deposit these funds in the Specialty License Plate Fund established under section 
38469.  In accordance with section 468, subsection 3-A, the Secretary of State shall provide 
392,000 credit receipts to the sponsor.

9SUMMARY
10This bill creates the Maine Lighthouse Trust special registration plate to support 
11lighthouse restoration and preservation efforts.
