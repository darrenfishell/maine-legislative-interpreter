12disbursements only upon written direction from the board.  This fund is a nonlapsing 
13revolving fund.  All money in the fund must be continuously applied to carry out the 
14purposes of this chapter.

26SUMMARY
27This bill transfers the Kim Wallace Adaptive Equipment Loan Program Fund from 
28the Finance Authority of Maine to the Treasurer of State and allows the Treasurer of State 
29to make disbursements from the fund only upon written direction of the Kim Wallace 
30Adaptive Equipment Loan Program Fund Board.  This bill also provides funds to
