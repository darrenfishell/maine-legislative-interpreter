12 SUMMARY 
13 This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment adds language to the law 
14 governing the telecommunications education access fund to: 
15 1. Specify that the use of the fund for Internet access includes mobile Internet access 
16 through a portable wireless access point, or hotspot, that provides Internet access over a 
17 cellular network; and 
18 2. Add a guideline for allocation of money from the fund to provide, within existing 
19 resources, support for qualified libraries in rural areas of the State with greatest need, as 
20 determined in consultation with the State Librarian, the Commissioner of Education and 
21 the ConnectME Authority, to offer portable wireless access points, or hotspots, for 
22 mobile Internet access.
