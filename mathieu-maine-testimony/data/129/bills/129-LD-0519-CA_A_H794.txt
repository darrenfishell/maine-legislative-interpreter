8SUMMARY
9This amendment replaces the bill and is the majority report of the committee.  The 
10amendment turns the bill into a resolve and changes the title.  The amendment directs the 
11Department of Professional and Financial Regulation, Bureau of Insurance to conduct 
12pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 24-A, section 2752 a review and evaluation 
13of the expansion of adult dental health insurance coverage, provides funds for that 
14purpose and requires the bureau to submit a report to the joint standing committee of the 
15Legislature having jurisdiction over health coverage, insurance and financial services 
16matters no later than March 1, 2021.  The joint standing committee is authorized to report 
17out a bill based on the report to the First Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.

Fiscal Detail and Notes
The bill requires the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation, Bureau of Insurance, to review and 
evaluate the expansion of adult dental insurance coverage . The department has indicated it will require, and the bill 
includes in fiscal year 2020-21 only, an allocation of $12,500 to contract out for this review. This allocation will be 
funded by existing resources within the Bureau of Insurance. Fiscal Note129th MAINE LEGISLATURE
