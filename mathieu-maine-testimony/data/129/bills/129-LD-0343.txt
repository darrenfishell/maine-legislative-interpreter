17for sale.  A cigar lounge shall provide notice to all applicants for employment and 
18employees that working in a cigar lounge may cause serious negative health effects, 
19including an increased risk of cancer and heart disease and that no level of exposure 
20to environmental tobacco smoke is safe.

30spirits, wine and malt liquor to be consumed on the premises.
31SUMMARY
32This bill allows a tobacco specialty store to be licensed as a cigar lounge, which may 
33serve nonalcoholic and alcoholic beverages.  A cigar lounge may not sell cigarettes or 
34prepare food on premises for sale.  A tobacco specialty store that is a cigar lounge must 
Page 2 - 129LR0963(01)-11provide notice about the dangers of environmental tobacco smoke to applicants for 
2employment and employees.  The cigar lounge license fee is $100 per year.
