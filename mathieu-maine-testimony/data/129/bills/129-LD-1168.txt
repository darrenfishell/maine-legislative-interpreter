Page 1 - 129LR1789(01)-11Sec. 1.  Task force on childhood trauma response guidance.  Resolved:  
2That the Commissioner of Education shall convene a task force, inviting the participation 
3of experts and interested parties, to develop guidance for kindergarten to grade 12 
4administrators on appropriate training and responses to childhood trauma.  In developing 
5the guidance, the task force shall consider the following topics:
61.  Mandated training for preschool and kindergarten to grade 12 teachers, 
7administrators and other faculty on adverse childhood experiences and trauma, including 
8the establishment of state certification rules requiring training hours in trauma and the 
9effect of trauma on learning and behavior;
102.  A response-to-intervention process that includes behavioral health and social and 
11emotional screening and referral to support procedures;
123.  A policy that sets a standard ratio for students to support staff to ensure adequate 
13staffing to meet the behavioral and emotional needs of students;
144.  A mandated social and emotional learning curriculum for kindergarten to grade 8;
155.  An attendance policy and discipline policy that is established with a trauma-
16informed perspective; and
176.  A policy that ensures publicly funded schools and preschools have the resources 
18and funds necessary to develop school and school administrative unit action plans for 
19addressing the needs of traumatized children and creating trauma-informed school 
20environments.
21Sec. 2.  Report.  Resolved:  That the Commissioner of Education shall submit a 
22report on the work of the task force under section 1 to the Joint Standing Committee on 
23Education and Cultural Affairs by December 4, 2019.  The committee is authorized to 
24submit legislation related to the report to the Second Regular Session of the 129th 
25Legislature.
26SUMMARY
27This resolve directs the Commissioner of Education to convene a task force, inviting 
28the participation of experts and interested parties, to develop guidance for kindergarten to 
29grade 12 administrators on appropriate training and responses to childhood trauma and to 
30submit a report to the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs by 
31December 4, 2019.  The committee is authorized to submit legislation related to the report 
32to the Second Regular Session of the 129th Legislature.
