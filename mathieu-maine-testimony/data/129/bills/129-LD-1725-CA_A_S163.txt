9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT " {\-" to S.P. 574, L.D. 1725, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Create a Minimum Age To Hold a Limited-purpose Aquaculture License" 
11 Amend the bill by inserting after the title and before the enacting clause the 
12 following: 
13 'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
14 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and 
15 Whereas, in order to avoid an influx of applications for limited-purpose aquaculture 
16 licenses by individuals who are under 12 years of age, this legislation needs to become 
17 effective before 90 days after the adjournment of the First Regular Session of the 129th 
18 Legislature; and 
19 Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
20 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
21 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
22 therefore,' 
23 Amend the bill by adding before the summary the following: 
24 'Emergency clause. In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
25 legislation takes effect when approved.' 
26 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
27 section number to read consecutively. 
28 SUMMARY 
29 This amendment adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause to the bill in 
30 order to avoid an influx of applications for limited-purpose aquaculture licenses by 
31 individuals who are under 12 years of age.
