28were issued with the professional teacher certificate originally issued to that teacher.
29SUMMARY
30This bill provides that a rule adopted that amends specifications for grades or subject 
31areas endorsements for a professional teacher certificate does not apply to the 
32endorsements on a professional teacher certificate held by a person if the endorsements 
33were issued prior to or during the school year preceding the adoption of the rules and that 
34a teacher may renew the teacher's professional teacher certificate with the same grades 
35and subject areas endorsements as were originally issued to that teacher.
