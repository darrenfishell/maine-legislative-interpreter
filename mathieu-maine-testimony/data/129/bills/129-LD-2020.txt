7SUMMARY
8This bill amends the provision of law terminating the Maine School for Marine 
9Science, Technology, Transportation and Engineering 90 days after adjournment of the 
10Second Regular Session of the 129th Legislature to provide that the school is terminated 
1190 days after adjournment of the Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.  It also 
12removes references to residential students and amends reporting requirements for the 
13school by incorporating reporting requirements similar to those of charter schools.
