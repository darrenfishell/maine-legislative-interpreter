11SUMMARY
12This bill prohibits an investor-owned transmission and distribution utility located in 
13an area administered by the Northern Maine Independent System Administrator from 
14charging a person generating or selling electricity for the transmission, or wheeling, of 
15that electricity to or from Canada over the utility's transmission system.
