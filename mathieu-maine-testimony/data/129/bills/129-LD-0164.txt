6SUMMARY
7This bill increases the total exemption under the Maine resident homestead property 
8tax exemption program to $50,000 for property tax years beginning on or after April 1, 
92020.  This bill also increases state reimbursement to municipalities for homestead 
10property tax exemptions from 62.5% to 100% for property tax years beginning on or after 
11April 1, 2020.
