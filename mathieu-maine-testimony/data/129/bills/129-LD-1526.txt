17SUMMARY
18This bill eliminates the requirement that the State Fire Marshal inspect a family foster 
19home and certify that it meets all elements of the fire safety code before the Department 
20of Health and Human Services may issue a license to operate as a family foster home.  
21The bill moves the inspection responsibility to the Department of Health and Human 
22Services, which is directed to adopt rules governing the method of inspection.
