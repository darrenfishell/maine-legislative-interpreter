12SUMMARY
13This bill enacts a recommendation of the stakeholder group established pursuant to 
14Public Law 2019, chapter 515, section 4.  The bill requires the Department of Health and 
15Human Services to reimburse a municipality for the amount of general assistance 
16provided to an individual in an emergency situation that is in excess of the maximum 
17level of assistance established by that municipality.
