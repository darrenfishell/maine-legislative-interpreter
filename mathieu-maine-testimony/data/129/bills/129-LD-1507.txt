282.  Prohibition.  Except as provided in subsection 3, a homeowners association may 
29not prohibit or restrict the construction or operation of an amateur radio station by a 
30homeowner who is an amateur radio operator as long as the outdoor antenna used by the 
31amateur radio operator is concealed from public view or constructed to occupy minimal 
32space and is located on property under exclusive control of the amateur radio operator.

7SUMMARY
8This bill prohibits a homeowners association, which is defined in this bill, from 
9restricting the construction and operation of an amateur radio station by an amateur radio 
10operator licensed by the Federal Communications Commission.  This bill allows 
11homeowners associations to establish certain requirements concerning the construction 
12and appearance of amateur radio station antennas.
