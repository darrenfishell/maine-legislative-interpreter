Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This amendment removes the requirement that the Department of Health and Human Services must reimburse 
municipalities 100% of direct GA costs after a person has spent 90 consecutive nights homeless. This will decrease 
the amount of additional assistance payments made by municipalities due to this bill.
