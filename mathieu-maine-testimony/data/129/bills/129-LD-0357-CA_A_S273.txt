Fiscal Note 
Maine Government Facilities Authority bonding authorized 
This bill authorizes the issuance ofup to $6,000,000 in Maine Government Facilities Authority securities to construct 
a building to accommodate the office of the district attorney of York County. The building will be connected to a new 
courthouse that was previously authorized by earlier legislation. The county will pay the debt service on the bonds 
until they are paid in full, at which time the judicial branch will transfer the property to York County.
