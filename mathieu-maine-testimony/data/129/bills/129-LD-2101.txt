29SUMMARY
30This bill implements the recommendations of the Right To Know Advisory 
31Committee in the Fourteenth Annual Report of the Right to Know Advisory Committee 
32concerning the membership of the Archives Advisory Board.
33This bill adds 3 members to the Archives Advisory Board to ensure that journalists, 
34newspapers, broadcasters and other news media as well as personal privacy protection 
35advocates are represented in the expertise involved in the development of records 
36retention schedules.
