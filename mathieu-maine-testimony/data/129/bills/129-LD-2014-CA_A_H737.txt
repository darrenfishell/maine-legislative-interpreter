Current biennium cost increase -Maine State Grant Program 
Increasing the number of semesters that an eligible adult learner may receive grants from the Maine State Grant 
Program within the Finance Authority of Maine from 10 semesters to 12 semesters will result in increased 
expenditures to the program.
