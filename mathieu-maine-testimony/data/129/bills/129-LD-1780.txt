12SUMMARY
13This bill increases opportunities for property owners to replace at-risk home heating 
14oil tanks by authorizing money in the Maine Ground and Surface Waters Clean-up and 
15Response Fund to be disbursed for loans and grants for department-approved rebate 
16programs to retrofit, repair, replace or remove aboveground and underground oil storage 
17tanks and associated piping at residential dwellings.
