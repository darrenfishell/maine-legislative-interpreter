29SUMMARY
30This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, changes the First 4 
31ME Early Care and Education Program from a pilot project to an ongoing program within 
32the Department of Health and Human Services.  It directs the department to seek and 
33apply for available federal funds or funds from any other sources to pay the costs of 
34projects under the program and provides that the department, to the maximum extent 
35possible, must use state funds received for the projects to maximize its receipt of federal 
36funds to be used for the projects.  It also amends some of the participant qualification and 
37provider requirements under the program.
