19the report to the Second Regular Session of the 129th Legislature.
20SUMMARY
21This resolve requires the Department of Environmental Protection and the 
22Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to study the feasibility of 
23establishing incentives or mandates to promote composting in the State.  The departments 
24are required to submit a joint report of their findings and recommendations, together with 
25any necessary implementing legislation, to the Joint Standing Committee on Agriculture, 
26Conservation and Forestry and the Joint Standing Committee on Environment and 
27Natural Resources, which may submit legislation relating to the report to the Second 
28Regular Session of the 129th Legislature.
