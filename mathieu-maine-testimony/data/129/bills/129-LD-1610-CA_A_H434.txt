COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" A» to H.P. 1162, L.D. 1610, "Resolve, To Modify 
the Deed for a Parcel of Property in the Town of Carrabassett Valley" 
Amend the resolve in section 1 in section 4 in the 17th line (page 1, line 20 in L.D.) 
by inserting after the following: "camping" the following: ', including commercial 
camping if development plans for commercial camping are approved by the State' 
Amend the resolve by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies that camping, including commercial camping, is an 
acceptable use of the property as long as development plans for commercial camping are 
approved by the State.
