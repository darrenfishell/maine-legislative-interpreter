11Sec. 1.  17-A MRSA §§208-D, 208-E and 208-F are enacted to read:
12§208-D.  Domestic violence aggravated assault
131.  A person is guilty of domestic violence aggravated assault if:
14A.  The person violates section 208; and
15B.  The victim is a family or household member as defined in Title 19-A, section 
164002, subsection 4.
172.  Violation of this section is a Class B crime.
18§208-E.  Domestic violence elevated aggravated assault
191.  A person is guilty of domestic violence elevated aggravated assault if:
20A.  The person violates section 208-B; and
21B.  The victim is a family or household member as defined in Title 19-A, section 
224002, subsection 4.
232.  Violation of this section is a Class A crime.
24§208-F.  Domestic violence elevated aggravated assault on pregnant person
251.  A person is guilty of domestic violence elevated aggravated assault on a pregnant 
26person if:
27A.  The person violates section 208-C; and
28B.  The victim is a family or household member as defined in Title 19-A, section

302.  Violation of this section is a Class A crime.

7SUMMARY
8This bill creates the crimes of domestic violence aggravated assault, domestic 
9violence elevated aggravated assault and domestic violence elevated aggravated assault 
10on a pregnant person.  The bill also makes violation of a protection from abuse order a 
11Class C crime if the person has 2 or more prior convictions for violating a protection from 
12abuse order.
