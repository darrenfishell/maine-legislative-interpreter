25SUMMARY
26Current law governing the measurement of damages for trees that are unlawfully cut, 
27damaged or destroyed allows an owner of property zoned for residential use to recover 
28the costs of replacing, replanting or restoring any tree.  This bill limits recovery of the 
29costs of replacement to trees that are ornamental or fruit trees.
