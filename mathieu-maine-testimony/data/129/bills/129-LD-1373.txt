Page 1 - 129LR0947(01)-11Sec. 1.  Department of Health and Human Services to seek demonstration 
2waiver.  Resolved:  That the Department of Health and Human Services shall file an 
3application with the United States Department of Health and Human Services, Centers for 
4Medicare and Medicaid Services to request a demonstration waiver under the federal 
5Social Security Act, Section 1115 to allow medically tailored food and nutrition 
6interventions to be a reimbursable covered service.  Medically tailored food and nutrition 
7interventions may be used when a health care provider determines that certain elements 
8of nutrition or foods based upon a nutrition plan developed by a licensed dietitian are 
9necessary for a patient's health.  The determination by the health care provider may be for 
10reasons of preventive health care or to support treatment for a patient, may be for a 
11patient who is homebound and unable to prepare nutritious foods and is at a high risk for 
12admission or readmission to a health care facility or may be to assist in a patient's health 
13outcome.
14Sec. 2.  Report.  Resolved:  That the Department of Health and Human Services 
15shall report annually for 5 years by January 1st to the joint standing committee of the 
16Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human services matters on the use of 
17services provided under a demonstration waiver under section 1, including how many 
18individuals have qualified for a nutrition intervention under section 1 and the duration of 
19and conditions treated under the nutrition intervention.  The report must also include data 
20on hospital readmission rates for 2 categories of individuals with similar health 
21conditions: individuals who have received nutrition interventions and individuals who 
22have not received nutrition interventions.
23Sec. 3.  Department of Health and Human Services to file application for 
24home-delivered meal coverage. Resolved:  That the Department of Health and 
25Human Services shall file the application by October 1, 2019 with the United States 
26Department of Health and Human Services, Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services 
27to request that home-delivered meals be reimbursable under MaineCare as directed by 
28Resolve 2015, chapter 54.
29SUMMARY
30This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to file an 
31application with the United States Department of Health and Human Services, Centers for 
32Medicare and Medicaid Services for a demonstration waiver to allow for reimbursement 
33of medically tailored food and nutrition interventions when a health care provider 
34determines that certain elements of nutrition or foods based upon a nutrition plan 
35developed by a licensed dietitian are necessary for a patient's health.  This resolve also 
36directs the Department of Health and Human Services to file the application by October 
371, 2019 with the United States Department of Health and Human Services, Centers for 
38Medicare and Medicaid Services to request that home-delivered meals be reimbursable 
39under MaineCare as directed by Resolve 2015, chapter 54.
