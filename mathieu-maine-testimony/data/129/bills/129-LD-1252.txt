37SUMMARY
38This bill requires an annual additional $30 snowmobile registration fee, unless the 
39registrant can show proof of membership in a snowmobile club in the State. It specifies 
Page 3 - 129LR1788(01)-11that for a resident family residing in the same household the additional fee is $30 for up 
2to 4 snowmobiles. Lastly, it requires the transfer of $20 collected from this additional fee 
3to the Snowmobile Trail Fund and the transfer of $10 collected from this additional fee to 
4the Snowmobile Enforcement Fund.
