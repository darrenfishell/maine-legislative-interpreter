14SUMMARY
15This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, amends the resolve 
16by reducing the reimbursement rate per discharge to cover the cost of providing care to 
17MaineCare members in Southern Maine Health Care's psychiatric inpatient unit and 
18changes the effective date of the new reimbursement rate.  The amendment directs the 
19Department of Health and Human Services to seek and apply for federal funds and funds 
20from any other sources to pay the costs of providing psychiatric services to uninsured 
21patients.  The amendment also directs the Department of Health and Human Services to 
22submit a report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over 
23health and human services matters regarding the progress the department has made in 
24developing a reimbursement rate to provide inpatient psychiatric care under its rule 
25Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 45.03.  The committee is 
26authorized to report out legislation related to the report to the Second Regular Session of 
27the 130th Legislature.
28This amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section.
