121.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
13following terms have the following meanings.
14A.  "Home repair, modification and weatherization services" means services, 
15materials and programs available to make a residence safe, warm, dry and efficient, 
16including repairs to the physical structure, the installation of safety equipment, 
17including bathroom grab bars, improved lighting and smoke and carbon monoxide 
18detectors, weatherization services and the installation of energy-efficient lighting 
19fixtures.
20B.  "Housing navigation assistance" means assistance with:
21(1)  Finding affordable home repair, modification and weatherization services;
22(2)  Finding affordable and adequate alternative housing options, including 
23housing with services;
24(3)  Managing a transition between housing settings; and
25(4)  Accessing information and resources regarding housing and transitioning 
26housing.
27C.  "Qualified person" means a person with a disability or a person 60 years of age or 
28older.
292.  Housing navigator.  There is established within the department the position of 
30housing navigator.  The housing navigator:
31A.  Provides housing navigation assistance to qualified persons;
32B.  Collaborates with other state agencies, aging and disability service providers and 
33organizations and others to develop, maintain, update and make publicly available 
34information and resources regarding housing navigation assistance; and
35C.  Identifies gaps in housing assistance needs of qualified persons and periodically 
36submits a report on any gaps to the director of the department's office of aging and

3SUMMARY
4This bill creates within the Department of Health and Human Services a housing 
5navigator who:
61.  Assists disabled and elderly persons in locating housing, transitioning between 
7housing settings and accessing home repair and home modification services and 
8materials;
92.  Maintains and makes publicly available housing assistance information and 
10resources; and
113.  Identifies gaps in housing assistance needs of elderly persons and persons with 
12disabilities and periodically submits a report on the gaps to the director of the Department 
13of Health and Human Services' office of aging and disability services for inclusion in the 
14state plan on aging.
