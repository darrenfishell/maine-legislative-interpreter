3SUMMARY
4This amendment, which is the unanimous report of the committee, adds one member 
5representing municipal officers to the membership of the Frequent Users System 
6Engagement Collaborative.  This amendment also changes the reporting date for the 
7collaborative and the session of the 130th Legislature to which legislation may be 
8submitted.
