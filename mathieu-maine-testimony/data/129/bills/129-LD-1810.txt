29SUMMARY
30This bill changes the responsibility for reviews pursuant to the State Government 
31Evaluation Act of boards responsible for the licensing of health care professions from the 
32joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over business, research 
33and economic development matters to the joint standing committee of the Legislature 
34having jurisdiction over professional licensing of health care professions matters.
