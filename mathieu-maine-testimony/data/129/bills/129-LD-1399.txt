2SUMMARY
3This bill establishes within the Department of Health and Human Services one Oral 
4Health Coordinator position in the oral health program within the Maine Center for 
5Disease Control and Prevention, rural health and primary care division to lead the State's 
6work on oral health; one Planning and Research Associate II position in the rural health 
7and primary care division within the Maine Center for Disease Control and Prevention 
8tasked with data analysis, performance management reporting and program planning and 
9evaluation; and one Early Periodic Screening Diagnosis and Treatment Dental 
10Coordinator position in the Office of MaineCare Services.  The bill also provides funding 
11to expand preventive oral health services provided in schools through the oral health 
12program within the Maine Center for Disease Control and Prevention, rural health and 
13primary care division to all schools in the State and a half-time Office Assistant II 
14position to provide logistical and administrative support for that expansion.DEPARTMENT TOTAL - ALL FUNDS $790,650 $800,308
