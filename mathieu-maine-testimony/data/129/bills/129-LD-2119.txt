8A.  The panel coordinator shall review the deaths of all women during pregnancy or 
9within 42 days one year of giving birth, the majority of cases in which a fetal death 
10occurs after 28 weeks of gestation and the majority of deaths of infants under one 
11year of age, with selection of cases of infant death based on the need to review 
12particular causes of death or the need to obtain a representative sample of all deaths.
13SUMMARY
14This bill amends the laws governing the maternal, fetal and infant mortality review 
15panel to require the review of maternal deaths that occur within one year of giving birth.  
16Current law requires the panel to review maternal deaths that occur within 42 days of 
17giving birth.
