5 SUMMARY 
6 This amendment, which is the majority report, requires the Director of the Bureau of 
7 Parks and Lands within the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to 
8 offer to convey, by quitclaim deed with covenant to Roosevelt Conference Center doing 
9 business as Eagle Lake Sporting Camps, a 12.86-acre parcel of land in Township 16, 
10 Range 6. If Eagle Lake Spotting Camps accepts the offer, the amendment requires the 
11 director at the State's expense and the purchaser at the purchaser's expense to each obtain 
12 a professional appraisal of the property and the higher of the 2 appraisals is the sale price. 
13 The amendment also stipulates that the use of the property is restricted to the operation of 
14 a commercial sporting camp, in keeping with the historical traditions of the area in 
15 general and particularly of this property. The amendment provides that if the property is 
16 no longer used for the purpose of a commercial sporting camp, title to the property reverts 
17 to the State without cost and any buildings that occupy the site must be removed by the 
18 property owner at the property owner's own cost.
