23SUMMARY
24This bill requires that, for applicants to the Department of Economic and Community 
25Development or municipalities for assistance for the improvement and development of 
26housing, community and economic development opportunities, the name of the applicants 
27and the amount of assistance being requested not be withheld as confidential but be 
28subject to disclosure.
