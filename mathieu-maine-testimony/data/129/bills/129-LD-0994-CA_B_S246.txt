9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" J3" to S.P. 284, L.D. 994, "Resolve, To Require 
10 the Approval by the Public Utilities Commission of a Proposal for a Long-term Contract 
11 for Deep-water Offshore Wind Energy" 
12 Amend the resolve by striking out everything after the title and inserting the 
13 following: 
14 'Sec. 1. Public Utilities Commission to approve long-term contract 
15 between Maine Aqua Ventus and Central Maine Power Company. Resolved: 
16 That, within 30 days of the effective date of this resolve, the Public Utilities Commission 
17 shall approve a long-term contract for capacity and associated energy between Maine 
18 Aqua Ventus I, GP, LLC and Central Maine Power Company. The approved contract 
19 must be the same as the long-term contract between Maine Aqua Ventus and Central 
20 Maine Power Company filed with the commission in December 2017, except for 
21 revisions necessary to ensure the contract price is equal to the ISO-New England 
22 wholesale energy market price.' 
23 SUMMARY 
24 This amendment removes the emergency preamble and emergency clause. The 
25 amendment requires that the contract between Maine Aqua Ventus and Central Maine 
26 Power Company to be approved by the Public Utilities Commission under the resolve be 
27 revised to set the price in the contract equal to the ISO-New England wholesale energy 
28 market price.
