8SUMMARY
9This bill exempts from Maine income tax net capital gains on the sale of securities 
10held for 10 years or longer and that are subject to federal income tax for individuals with 
11federal adjusted gross income of $300,000 or less if filing married joint returns and 
12$150,000 or less for all other individuals.
