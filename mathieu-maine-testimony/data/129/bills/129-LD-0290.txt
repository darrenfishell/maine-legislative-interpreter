3ยง6959-B.  Operating a motor vehicle in the intertidal zone
41.  Violation.  A person may not operate a motor vehicle as defined in Title 29-A, 
5section 101, subsection 42 in the intertidal zone, except:
6A.  To launch or retrieve watercraft as defined in section 13001, subsection 28;
7B.  To engage in an activity that has received any necessary state or local permits and 
8for which a motor vehicle is necessary; or
9C.  If the vehicle is an authorized emergency vehicle as defined in Title 29-A, section 
102054, subsection 1, paragraph B.
112.  Penalty.  The following penalties apply to violations of this section.
12A.  A person who violates this section commits a civil violation for which a fine of 
13not less than $100 and not more than $500 may be adjudged.
14B.  A person who violates this section after having been adjudicated as having 
15committed 3 or more civil violations under this section within the previous 5-year 
16period commits a Class E crime.
17SUMMARY
18This bill prohibits a person from operating a motor vehicle in the intertidal zone 
19except to launch or retrieve a watercraft, to engage in an activity that requires the use of a 
20motor vehicle and for which any necessary state or local permits have been obtained or if 
21the vehicle is an authorized emergency vehicle.  It specifies that a person who violates the 
22prohibition on the operation of a motor vehicle in the intertidal zone commits a civil 
23violation, except that a person who is adjudicated as having committed 3 or more civil 
24violations for operating a motor vehicle in the intertidal zone within the previous 5-year 
25period commits a Class E crime.
