SENATE AMENDMENT "A" to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 
1167, L.D. 1615, Bill, "An Act To Enact Laws Governing Private Vehicle Rentals" 
Amend the amendment is section 1 in § 7 403 in subsection 4 by striking out all of 
paragraph A (page 3, lines 20 to 27 in amendment) and inserting the following: 
'A. Procure insurance coverage of the shared vehicle during the car sharing period. 
At a minimum, the insurance must provide: 
(1) Primary liability coverage for the shared vehicle owner and secondary 
liability coverage for the shared vehicle driver in an amount not less than 3 times 
the minimum requirements for liability in Title 29-A, section 1605; and 
(2) Property and casualty coverage, including comprehensive and collision 
protection, for physical loss to the shared vehicle during the time that the vehicle 
is in the custody of the provider or shared vehicle driver in an amount not less 
than the replacement cost of the shared vehicle;' 
Amend the amendment by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter 
or section number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies the requirement for a peer-to-peer car sharing program 
provider to procure liability insurance coverage. The amendment also replaces the 
requirement that property and casualty insurance coverage for physical loss to a shared 
vehicle be provided to the same extent that coverage for physical loss is provided under a 
shared vehicle owner's policy with a requirement that coverage be provided in.an amount
