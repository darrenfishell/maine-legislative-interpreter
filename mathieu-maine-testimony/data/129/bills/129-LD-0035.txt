17SUMMARY
18This bill increases the homestead exemption to $50,000 for veterans of the United 
19States Army, Navy, Air Force, Marines or Coast Guard who are receiving disability 
20compensation from the United States Government due to a service-connected disability 
21and provides an income tax deduction for all income of those individuals.
