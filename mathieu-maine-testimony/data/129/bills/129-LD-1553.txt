9SUMMARY
10This bill requires that, notwithstanding any applicable notice requirements, each of 
11the towns of Abbot, Atkinson, Bradford, Cambridge, Dexter, Dover-Foxcroft, Guilford, 
12Milo, Monson, Parkman, Sangerville, Sebec and Willimantic hold an advisory vote no 
13later than April 30, 2019 on the proposed merger of Hospital Administrative District No. 
144 with Northern Light Health.  Unless a town's charter otherwise provides, the vote must 
15be taken at a town meeting.  The results of the vote in each town must be declared by the 
16municipal officers of the town and transmitted to the board of directors of Hospital 
17Administrative District No. 4.
