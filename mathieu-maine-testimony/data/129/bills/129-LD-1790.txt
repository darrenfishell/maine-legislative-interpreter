9SUMMARY
10This bill amends the law governing the confidentiality of personal information of 
11municipal employees to parallel the same protections provided for state employees and 
12establishes as confidential any genetic information and information about the sexual 
13orientation of employees contained in the records of the municipality.  This bill also 
14amends the state employee personnel records provisions to include confidentiality of 
15genetic information and sexual orientation and amends the laws governing county and 
16municipal employee personnel records to match.
