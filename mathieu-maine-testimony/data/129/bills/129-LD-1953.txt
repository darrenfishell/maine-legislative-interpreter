19SUMMARY
20This bill does the following.
211.  It removes the provisions of law that provide for the suspension of a driver's 
22license or permit for failure to pay a fine for an offense not related to driving and strikes 
23the October 1, 2021 repeal provisions.
242.  It repeals the provision of law that provides for the issuance of a restricted driver's 
25license for failure to pay a fine for offenses not related to driving.
263.  It repeals the provision, effective on October 1, 2021, that provides the statutory 
27exemptions from attachment and execution for certain property do not apply to 
28enforcement of fines owed to the State.
