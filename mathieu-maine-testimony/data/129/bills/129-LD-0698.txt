10SUMMARY
11This bill clarifies that when one or more citizens sue the Governor or any executive 
12branch agency to enforce federal or state law and the citizen or citizens prevail or 
13substantially prevail, the citizen or citizens may petition the court for the State to pay all 
14costs.  If the citizen or citizens demonstrate that the agency or Governor knowingly 
15violated the law, the court is required to also award reasonable attorney's fees.
