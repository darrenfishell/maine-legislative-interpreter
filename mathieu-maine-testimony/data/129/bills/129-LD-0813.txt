30SUMMARY
31This resolve directs the Department of Economic and Community Development to 
32design and implement a one-year pilot program to encourage remote workers to relocate 
33to Maine. The pilot program, the New Remote Worker Grant Program, will award grants 
34of up to $2,500 per qualifying individual and up to $125,000 in total funds for qualifying 
35expenses. The resolve directs the department to report back to the joint standing 
36committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over innovation, development, economic 
37advancement and business matters and to the joint standing committee of the Legislature 
38having jurisdiction over labor and housing matters and gives these committees authority 
39to report out legislation based on the department's report.
