20SUMMARY
21This bill provides that an auctioneer is not required to record the serial numbers on 
22paper money that is subject to purchase.  It retains the provision in current law that an 
23auctioneer record a brief description of property the auctioneer purchases.  It also allows 
24an auctioneer to keep certain records regarding the purchase of used personal property in 
25electronic form.
