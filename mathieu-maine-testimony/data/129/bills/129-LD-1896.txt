11SUMMARY
12This bill provides that retail electricity sales pursuant to a supply contract or standard-
13offer service arrangement executed by a competitive electricity provider that is in effect 
14on September 19, 2019 are exempt from the requirement for the purchase of thermal 
15renewable energy credits until the end date of the existing term of the supply contract or 
16standard-offer service arrangement.  It also allows the Public Utilities Commission to set 
17separate alternative compliance payment rates for Class I resources, Class IA resources 
18and thermal renewable energy credits under the laws governing renewable resources.
