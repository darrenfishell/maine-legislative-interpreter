18local energy production by amending current use tax laws or other state and 
19local policies. Resolved: That the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry 
20 shall establish a working group to study the challenges and opportunities facing farmers 
21 and farmland owners considering the installation of solar or other local energy generation.  
22 The working group shall consider the impact of property taxes, solar energy siting and 
23 design decisions and other factors on the use and long-term conservation of agricultural 
24 land that is enrolled in the farm and open space tax program under the Maine Revised 
25 Statutes, Title 36, chapter 105, subchapter 10 where solar energy installations are located.  
26 The working group must include representatives designated by the State Tax Assessor and 
27 by statewide organizations representing municipal governments, solar energy developers, 
28 farmers and farmland conservation.  The working group shall consider and recommend 
29 policies to encourage solar energy installations to be sited on farmland and incorporated 
30 into farming activities in ways that will help keep land in farming and protect prime 
31 farmland.  At a minimum, the policies that are considered must include, but are not limited 
32 to, tax policy, farm and municipal viability, dual use methods of production and soil health 
33 as means to achieve this objective.  The department shall submit a report on the findings 
34 and recommendations of the working group to the joint standing committees of the 
35 Legislature having jurisdiction over taxation matters and agriculture, conservation and

5SUMMARY
6 This amendment changes the bill to a resolve directing the Department of Agriculture, 
7 Conservation and Forestry to form a working group to study the impact of property taxes 
8 and other factors on the use of agricultural land that is enrolled in the farm and open space 
9 tax program where solar energy installations are located and to recommend policies to 
10 support and encourage the generation and use of solar energy on farmland in ways that will 
11 help protect farmland.
