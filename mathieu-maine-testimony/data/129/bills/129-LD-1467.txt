3SUMMARY
4This bill, which is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on Environment and 
5Natural Resources pursuant to Joint Order 2019, House Paper 883, implements the 
6Department of Environmental Protection's recommendations regarding the State's cellular 
7telephone recycling law as included in the department's annual report on the State's 
8product stewardship programs.
9The bill amends the State's cellular telephone recycling law to remove the 
10requirement that cellular telephone service providers submit annual reports to the 
11department regarding the collection and disposal, reuse or recycling of used cellular 
12telephones.
13The committee has not taken a position on the substance of the bill and by reporting 
14this bill out, the committee is not suggesting and does not intend to suggest that it agrees 
15or disagrees with any aspect of this bill.  The committee is reporting the bill out for the 
16sole purpose of obtaining a printed bill that can be referred to the committee for a public 
17hearing and subsequent committee action in the normal course.
