11 Amend the resolve by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
12 'Resolve, Directing the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, 
13 Bureau of Parks and Lands To Work with the Town of Allagash Regarding the 
14 Conveyance of a Plot of Land from the State to the Town of Allagash' 
15 Amend the resolve by striking out everything after the title and inserting the 
16 following: 
17 'Sec. 1. Director of Bureau of Parks and Lands to work with the Town of 
18 Allagash. Resolved: That the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the 
19 Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry shall work with the Town of 
20 Allagash, County of Aroostook regarding the siting and plans for the establishment of a 
21 fueling facility in the Town of Allagash for the local logging industry and off-road 
22 recreational vehicles, including, but not limited to, snowmobiles and all-tenain vehicles. 
23 The Bureau of Parks and Lands and the Town of Allagash shall work in collaboration 
24 with the Department of Environmental Protection, Bureau of Remediation and Waste 
25 Management. By February 1, 2020, the Bureau of Parks and Lands shall submit a report 
26 with findings and recommendations to the Joint Standing Committee on Agriculture, 
27 Conservation and Forestry. The committee may submit a bill relating to the subject 
28 matter of the report to the Second Regular Session of the 129th Legislature.' 
29 Amend the resolve by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
30 section number to read consecutively.

34 work with the Town of Allagash, in collaboration with the Department of Environmental
