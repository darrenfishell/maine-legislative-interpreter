6Whereas, a major substantive rule has been submitted to the Legislature outside the 
7legislative rule acceptance period; and
8Whereas, immediate enactment of this resolve is necessary to record the 
9Legislature's position on final adoption of the rule; and
10Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
11the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
12immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
13therefore, be it
14Sec. 1.  Adoption.  Resolved:  That final adoption of Chapter 348: On Farm 
15Raising, Slaughter and Processing of Less Than 1,000 Ready-to-Cook Whole Poultry 
16Carcasses, a provisionally adopted major substantive rule of the Department of 
17Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry that has been submitted to the Legislature for 
18review pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A 
19outside the legislative rule acceptance period, is authorized.
20Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
21legislation takes effect when approved.
22SUMMARY
23This resolve provides for legislative review of Chapter 348: On Farm Raising, 
24Slaughter and Processing of Less Than 1,000 Ready-to-Cook Whole Poultry Carcasses, a 
25major substantive rule of the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry that 
26was filed outside the legislative rule acceptance period.
