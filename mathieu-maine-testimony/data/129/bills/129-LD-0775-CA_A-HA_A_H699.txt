SUMMARY 
This amendment removes the mandate that the Department of Health and Human 
Services amend its rule Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter II, Section 17, 
Community Support Services, instead authorizing the department to amend the rule, This 
amendment also requires the department to report, by January 15, 2021, to the joint 
standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human services 
matters on the following: 
1. Whether the department amended the rule and, if so, the justification for the 
changes; 
2. Data regarding eligibility criteria and the number of applicants for community 
support services under the rule, including the number initially accepted and rejected and, 
of those rejected, how many appealed and were accepted after appeal; and 
3. Whether the department is meeting the intended purpose of the rule and its plan 
for communicating to providers how the department plans to ensure the provision of 
community support services pursuant to the rule.
