7SUMMARY
8This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, resolves a conflict in 
9the law by clarifying that, when an individual provides documentation that provides proof 
10of voter eligibility in the course of applying for a driver's license or nondriver 
11identification card from the Department of the Secretary of State, Bureau of Motor 
12Vehicles, the individual must be given the opportunity to opt out of voter registration 
13before the bureau creates an electronic pending voter registration record for the 
14individual.
