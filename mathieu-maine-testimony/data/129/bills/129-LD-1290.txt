21SUMMARY
22Current law requires pawnbrokers to provide before the 15th day of every month to 
23the law enforcement agency of jurisdiction a summary of the pawn transactions entered 
24into during the preceding month.  This bill requires pawnbrokers within 10 days of a 
25pawn transaction to submit a report of the transaction to the law enforcement agency of 
26jurisdiction and to a regional property and recovery tracking system administered by a 
27regional law enforcement agency, currently the Regional Information Sharing Systems 
28Program administered by the New England State Police Information Network, listing 
29information concerning the consumer or seller in the transaction and of every item 
30pawned or sold in the transaction.  This bill makes a violation of the reporting 
31requirements a Class E crime.
