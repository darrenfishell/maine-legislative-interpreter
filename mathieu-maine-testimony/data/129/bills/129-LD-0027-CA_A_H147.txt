COMMITTEE AMENDMENT 
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 28, L.D. 27 
1 SUMMARY 
2 This amendment replaces the bill and the title. For a period of 3 years, beginning in 
3 2020, it allows a person, in accordance with rules adopted by the Commissioner of Inland 
4 Fisheries and Wildlife, to hunt deer with a cro~sbow during the open archery season on 
5 deer and the fall open season on hunting wild turkey. It specifies that a person using a 
6 crossbow during the open arche1y season may not harvest an antlerless deer unless that 
7 person possesses an antlerless deer permit. It also clarifies that, outside of this limited 
8 time period, the use of a crossbow to hunt wild turkey is allowed only during the spring 
9 open season on hunting wild turkey. Lastly, it requires the commissioner to submit a 
10 report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over inland 
11 fisheries and wildlife matters that includes a recommendation on whether to continue to 
12 permit the use of a crossbow during the regular archery season and the fall open season 
13 on hunting wild turkey and whether the commissioner allowed the use of a crossbow 
14 during a special archery season on deer and, if so, whether the use should continue to be 
15 permitted. The committee may report out a bill based on the report.

Fiscal Note 
Minor cost increase -General Fund 
This bill would allow the use of a crossbow during certain hunting seasons and under specific conditions starting in 
2020 and ending in 2022. The bill requires the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife (IFW) to submit a repott 
at the conclusion of this period with a recommendation on whether to continue allowing the use of a crossbow during 
certain hunting seasons and under specific conditions. Any additional costs to IFW associated with this legislation 
are anticipated to be minor and can be absorbed within existing budgeted resources.
