23SUMMARY
24This bill expands the membership of the Acquired Brain Injury Advisory Council 
25who are persons with acquired brain injury or family caregivers to 50% of the voting 
26membership of the council and adds representatives from the long-term care ombudsman 
27program, an aging and disability resource center and a center for independent living.
