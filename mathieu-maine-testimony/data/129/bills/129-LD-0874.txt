16SUMMARY
17This bill codifies the decision of the Superior Court in Maine Citizens for Clean 
18Elections v. LePage, No. CV-18-112, 2018 (Me. Super. Ct., Ken. Cty., August 2, 2018). 
19The bill authorizes the Commission on Governmental Ethics and Election Practices to 
20expend existing revenues from the Maine Clean Election Fund in excess of the current 
21year allocations without first obtaining a financial order approved by the Governor, solely 
22for the purpose of making distributions required by the Maine Clean Election Act to 
23certified candidates.
