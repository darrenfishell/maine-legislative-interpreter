9 SUMMARY 
10 This amendment does the following. 
11 1. It requires that a child restraint system for children 2 years of age or older and 
12 weighing less than 55 pounds must have an internal harness. 
13 2. It clarifies that children less than 8 years of age who weigh less than 80 pounds 
14 and are less than 57 inches in height must be properly secured in a belt positioning seat or 
15 other child restraint system. 
16 3. It clarifies that a child with a medical condition that, in the written opinion of a 
17 physician, nurse practitioner, physician assistant or child passenger safety technician with 
18 special needs training, necessitates that a different child restraint system be used is 
19 required to be secured in a child restraint system recommended in the opinion.
