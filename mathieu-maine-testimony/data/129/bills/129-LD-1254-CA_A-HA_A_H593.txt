Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This floor amendment removes meals from the local option sales tax. It would reduce the revenue increase to 
municipalities and Maine Rural Development shown in the committee amendment by approximately $22.8 million 
and $7.6 million, respectively. It would also reduce the revenue reduction to the General Fund by approximately $1.5
