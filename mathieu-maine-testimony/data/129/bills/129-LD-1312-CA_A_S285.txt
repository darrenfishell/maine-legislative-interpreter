Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill creates an extreme risk protection order process and includes a one-time General Fund appropriation of 
$39,000 and a one-time Highway Fund allocation of $21,372 in fiscal year 2019-20 to the Department of Public 
Safety for computer programming to update the protection order database.
