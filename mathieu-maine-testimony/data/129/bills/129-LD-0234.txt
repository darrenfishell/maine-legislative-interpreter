Page 1 - 129LR0869(01)-11Sec. 1.  Department of Health and Human Services to increase 
2MaineCare rates.  Resolved:  That, no later than January 1, 2020, the Department of 
3Health and Human Services shall amend its rules in Chapter 101:  MaineCare Benefits 
4Manual, Chapter III, Section 15 to increase reimbursement rates for chiropractic services 
5for manipulative treatments under procedure codes 98940, 98941 and 98942 to no less 
6than 70% of the federal Medicare reimbursement rate for these services as long as the rate 
7is no lower than the rate reimbursed as of January 1, 2019.
8Sec. 2.  Rate study.  Resolved:  That, notwithstanding section 1, if the 
9Department of Health and Human Services conducts a rate study of chiropractic services 
10for manipulative treatments, the department may adopt new rates for manipulative 
11treatments that are recommended by the rate study by amending its rules in Chapter 101:  
12MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 15.
13Sec. 3.  Routine technical rules.  Resolved:  That rules adopted pursuant to 
14section 1 or 2 are routine technical rules as defined in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, 
15chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
16Sec. 4.  Appropriations and allocations.  Resolved:  That the following 
17appropriations and allocations are made.
