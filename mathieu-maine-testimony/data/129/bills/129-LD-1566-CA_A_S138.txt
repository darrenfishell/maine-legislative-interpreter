12 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
13 'Resolve, To Determine Ways To Increase the Number of Recipients under the 
14 Tuition Waiver Program for Participants in Foster Care' 
15 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and inserting the following: 
16 'Sec. 1. Public Higher Education Systems Coordinating Committee to 
17 review the tuition waiver program for participants in foster care. Resolved: 
18 That the Public Higher Education Systems Coordinating Committee established pursuant 
19 to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20-A, section 9 shall review the tuition waiver 
20 program for participants in foster care. In its review, the committee shall consult with the 
21 Finance Authority of Maine, the Department of Health and Human Services, Office of 
22 Child and Family Services and any other public or private organizations that work with 
23 children in the foster care system. The committee shall report to the Joint Standing 
24 Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs no later than January 15, 2020 on the 
25 identification of barriers to the tuition waiver program for dependent students who have 
26 been adopted and independent students who have not been adopted with 
27 recommendations and any suggested legislation to support both communities through the 
28 tuition waiver program. After receiving the report, the Joint Standing Committee on 
29 Education and Cultural Affairs may submit a bill related to the report to the Second 
30 Regular Session of the 129th Legislature.' 
31 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
32 section number to read consecutively.
