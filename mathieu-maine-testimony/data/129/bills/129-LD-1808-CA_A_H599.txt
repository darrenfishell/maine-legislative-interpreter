Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill provides a sales tax exemption to certain nonprofit charitable organizations and would result in a reduction in 
General Fund revenue of$133,000 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $199,500 in fiscal year 2020-21. It would also result in 
a reduction in Local Government Fund revenue of $7,000 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $10,500 in fiscal year 2020-21. 
The bill includes a one-time General Fund appropriation of$15,000 in fiscal year 2019-20 to the Department of 
Administrative and Financial Services for programming costs related to creating a sales tax exemption certificate.
