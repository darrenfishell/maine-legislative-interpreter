COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 786, L.D. 1063, Bill, "An Act To 
Support the Role of Municipalities in ~x~anding Broadband Infrastructure" 
Amend the bill in section 5 in subsection 15 in the 4th to 6th lines (page 2, lines 2 to 
4 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "A municipality may not provide retail 
communications services through community broadband systems to nonmunicipal 
entities." 
Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment removes the provision in the bill that prohibits a municipality from 
providing retail communication services through community broadband systems to 
nonmunicipal entities.
