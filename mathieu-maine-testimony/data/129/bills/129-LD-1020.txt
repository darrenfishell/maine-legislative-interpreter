20SUMMARY
21This bill allows the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of 
22Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations to license up to 8 agency liquor stores in a 
23municipality with a population of at least 16,001 but less than 30,001; current law 
24requires a population of at least 20,001 before a municipality qualifies for 8 agency liquor 
25stores.  The population threshold to qualify for 5 agency liquor stores remains at 10,001.
