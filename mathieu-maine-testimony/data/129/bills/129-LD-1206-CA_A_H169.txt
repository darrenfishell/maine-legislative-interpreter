SUMMARY 
2 This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment adds language to the law 
3 governing the safety and convenience of highways, town ways and streets to specify that 
4 utility poles and facilities are not defects in the public way, in order to relieve municipal 
5 officials from liability for utility poles and facilities in the public way. The amendment 
6 also directs the Public Utilities Commission to report to the Joint Standing Committee on 
7 Energy, Utilities and Technology in January 2020 regarding actions the commission has 
8 taken to address issues related to abandoned utility poles and any associated facilities in 
9 the public right-of-way.
