SUMMARY 
This amendment adds to committee membership personnel from the Department of 
Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Agricultural Water Management Board and a 
member of the public with expertise in stormwater management or wastewater 
management. The amendment strikes the requirement that a member of the public with 
expertise in water advocacy serves on the committee The amendment strikes the 
requirement that the committee focus on developing a strategy for protecting and 
managing groundwater resources in this State if worldwide clean water resources are 
threatened. The amendment also strikes phases 2 and 3 in which the committee is 
required to focus effort.
