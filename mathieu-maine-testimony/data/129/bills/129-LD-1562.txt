5following goal:  that, by January 1, 2050, 100% of the energy consumed in this State will

10to achieve the goal.  By January 1, 2020 and annually thereafter, the director shall submit 
11a report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over energy 
12matters on the program and the progress toward the goal.
