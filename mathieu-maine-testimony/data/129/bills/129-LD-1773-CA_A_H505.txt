Fiscal Note 
No change to debt service limit -major capital school construction projects 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This legislation will not impact the total cost of public education from kindergarten to grade 12 or the State's share of 
that cost. School management and leadership centers will follow the same approval process as school administrative 
units for major capital projects in order to receive state funding and the statut01y debt service ceiling for state-funded 
major capital constrnction projects will not change due to the provisions in this bill.
