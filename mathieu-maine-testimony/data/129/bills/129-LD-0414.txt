4Sec. 2.  Department of Health and Human Services to increase the 
5number of the State's intensive case manager positions.  The Department of

8requirements of the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 34-B, section 1227.
9SUMMARY
10This bill directs the Department of Health and Human Services to create additional 
11intensive case manager positions so that counties that do not have a county jail or 
12regional jail will have an intensive case manager.  An intensive case manager oversees 
13persons who are detained by a law enforcement officer and who have intellectual 
14disabilities or mental health conditions or have misused substances and connects them to 
15the services for which they qualify, works with the court system to ensure that they 
16receive due process and speedy trials and assists persons who qualify for the MaineCare 
17program to apply for and receive MaineCare benefits and services while being detained 
18or incarcerated, including during the implementation of diversion and reentry plans.
