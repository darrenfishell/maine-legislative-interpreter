41.  Subcontractor chosen from preapproved list.  A proposer, as defined in section 
51743, that is awarded a capital improvement contract subject to competitive bidding in 
6accordance with this subchapter with a value greater than $100,000 and that included in 
7its bid the use of a subcontractor must be assigned a subcontractor from a list of approved 
8subcontractors compiled by the Director of the Bureau of General Services as described 
9in this section.  If the work of the subcontractor included in the proposer's bid has a 
10good-faith estimate value of $10,000 or less, the proposer may use a subcontractor of the 
11proposer's choosing.
122.  Compilation of preapproved subcontractor list.  The Director of the Bureau of 
13General Services shall submit, at regular intervals, a general request for proposals for the 
14components of future capital improvement projects listed in subsection 3.  The form for a 
15general request for proposals may be determined by the director.  The request for 
16proposals must indicate that the bidder is seeking to be included on a list of preapproved 
17subcontractors to be selected to complete a component of capital improvement projects, 
18as listed in subsection 3, for which future contracts may be awarded.  The subcontractor's 
19bid must be submitted in a manner that allows the director to estimate the cost to 
20complete the subject component of a capital improvement project.  A subcontractor that 
21submits a bid to be included on the preapproved list may indicate any general contractors 
22with whom the subcontractor prefers to work and any general contractors with whom the 
23subcontractor chooses not to work.

64.  Rules.  The Director of the Bureau of General Services shall adopt rules to

9SUMMARY
10This bill requires the use of preapproved subcontractors for certain components of 
11work included in bids awarded to general contractors for public improvement 
12construction projects subject to competitive bidding.
