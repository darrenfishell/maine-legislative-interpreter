21Sec. 2.  Transfer from General Fund unappropriated surplus to the Fund

23Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the State Controller shall transfer 
24$1,000,000 from the General Fund unappropriated surplus to the Fund for the Treatment 
25of Pregnant Women with Opioid Use Disorder established within the Department of 
26Health and Human Services pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 22, section 
271728, subsection 3 to be used for the purposes of that fund.
28SUMMARY
29This bill directs the Department of Health and Human Services to administer a 
30program to provide grants for the treatment of pregnant women with opioid use disorder.  
31It establishes the Fund for the Treatment of Pregnant Women with Opioid Use Disorder 
32and directs the State Controller to transfer $1,000,000 from the General Fund 
33unappropriated surplus to that fund.
