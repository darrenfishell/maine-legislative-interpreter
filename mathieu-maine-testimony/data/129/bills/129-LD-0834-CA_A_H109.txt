30 SUMMARY 
31 The amendment changes the provision in the bill regarding the metrics for evaluating 
32 service interruptions for transmission and distribution utilities and the threshold for 
33 requiring corrective action. Rather than calculating a statewide average service 
34 interruption rate and establishing the threshold for corrective action at 50% above that 
35 statewide average, as in the bill, the amendment requires the Public Utilities Commission
