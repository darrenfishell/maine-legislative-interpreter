3ยง4317-C.  Coverage for prescription insulin drugs; limit on out-of-pocket costs
4A carrier that provides coverage for prescription insulin drugs may not impose any 
5deductible, copayment, coinsurance or other cost-sharing requirement on an enrollee for 
6that coverage that results in out-of-pocket costs to the enrollee that exceed $100 per 30-
7day supply of covered prescription insulin drugs, regardless of the amount, type or 
8number of insulin drugs needed to fill the enrollee's prescription.
9Nothing in this section prevents a carrier from reducing an enrollee's cost-sharing 
10requirement by an amount greater than the amount specified in this section.
11The superintendent may adopt rules to implement and administer this section to align 
12with applicable federal requirements.  Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine 
13technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
14SUMMARY
15This bill provides that a health insurance carrier that provides coverage for 
16prescription insulin drugs may not impose a cost-sharing requirement on the enrollee that 
17results in out-of-pocket costs to the enrollee in excess of $100 per 30-day supply of 
18insulin.
