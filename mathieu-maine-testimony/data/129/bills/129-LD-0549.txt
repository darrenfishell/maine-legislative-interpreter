2Sec. 1.  20-A MRSA §6602, sub-§1, ¶A, as enacted by PL 2007, c. 539, Pt. IIII, 
3§1, is amended to read:
4A.  A public school shall participate in the National School Lunch Program in 
5accordance with 7 Code of Federal Regulations, Part 210 (2007) and provide Type A 
6meals as determined by the United States Department of Agriculture.  A public 
7school participating in the National School Lunch Program pursuant to this paragraph 
8shall provide lunch at no cost to all students who are eligible for free and reduced-
9price meals.  The State shall provide funding equal to the difference between the 
10federal reimbursement for a reduced-price lunch and the federal reimbursement for a 
11free lunch.
12SUMMARY
13This bill directs the State to fund the difference between the federal reimbursements 
14for reduced-price and free lunches in order to provide all children from families with 
15incomes under 185% of the federal poverty level free lunch at school.
