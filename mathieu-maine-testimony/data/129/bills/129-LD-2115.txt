7SUMMARY
8This bill is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on State and Local 
9Government pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 3, section 2, subsection 2.  The 
10committee has not taken a position on the substance of this bill.  By reporting out this bill, 
11the committee is not suggesting and does not intend to suggest that it agrees or disagrees 
12with any aspect of this bill.  The committee is reporting out the bill for the sole purpose of 
13having a bill printed that can be referred to the committee for an appropriate public 
14hearing and subsequent processing in the normal course.  The committee is taking this 
15action to ensure clarity and transparency in the legislative review of the proposals 
16contained in the bill.
17This bill includes all the recommendations of the State Compensation Commission's 
18January 2020 final report including increases in the:
191.  Salary of the Governor from $70,000 per year to $135,000 per year to take effect 
20after the election of a Governor not in office on December 2, 2020;
212.  The expense account of the Governor from $30,000 per year to $40,000 per year;
223.  Salary of Maine Legislators, beginning with the 130th Maine Legislature, from 
23$14,862 in the first year and $10,582 in the 2nd year of a biennium, after adjustment for 
24inflation, to $16,000 in each year of the biennium;
254.  Mileage allowance for Maine Legislators from 44¢ per mile to 58¢ per mile;
265.  Lodging rate for Maine Legislators from $38 per day to $50 per day;
276.  Salary of the Chief Justice of the Supreme Judicial Court from $116,000 per year 
28to $184,000 per year;
297.  Salary of the Associate Justices of the Supreme Judicial Court from $96,000 to 
30$169,000 per year;
318.  Per diem compensation for Active Retired Justices from $350 per day to $500 per 
32day;
339.  Salary of the Chief Justice of the Superior Court from $94,000 per year to 
34$160,000 per year;
Page 7 - 129LR3213(01)-1110.  Salary of the Justices of the Superior Court from $90,000 per year to $150,000 
2per year;
311.  Salary of the Chief Judge of the District Court from $94,000 per year to 
4$160,000 per year;
512.  Salary of the Deputy Chief Judge of the District Court from $92,000 per year to 
6$155,000 per year; and
713.  Salary of the Associate Judges of the District Court from $90,000 per year to 
8$150,000 per year.
9The bill gives the Chief Justice of the Supreme Judicial Court the authority to 
10develop criteria and rates for longevity pay for all justices and judges.  It moves the initial 
11meeting of the State Compensation Commission from within 15 days after appointment 
12of the members to within 15 days after adjournment of a legislative session.  It requires 
13the President of the Senate and the Speaker of the House to appoint the 5th member of the 
14commission in alternating odd-numbered years.  It removes the following positions from 
15the commission's review: Secretary and Assistant Secretary of the Senate and Clerk and 
16Assistant Clerk of the House of Representatives.
