11SUMMARY
12This bill permits a rabbit producer to sell whole rabbit carcasses without inspection at 
13the farm on which the rabbits were raised, at farmers' markets, by delivery to consumers' 
14homes, to members of community supported agriculture farms with which the rabbit 
15producer has a direct marketing relationship, to locally owned grocery stores and to 
16locally owned restaurants as long as the rabbit producer slaughters fewer than 1,000 
17rabbits annually and the rabbit producer is registered.
