15SUMMARY
16This bill provides an income tax credit for the purchase of a new plug-in electric-
17drive motor vehicle that is eligible for a federal income tax credit.  The credit is $300 plus 
18$50 for each kilowatt-hour of battery capacity in excess of 5 kilowatt-hours up to a 
19maximum credit of $1,500.
