Fiscal Note 
Potential increase in number and amount ofloans granted -Educators for Maine Program 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Changes made to certain provisions of law governing the Educators for Maine Program administered by the Finance 
Authority of Maine (FAME) in this legislation may increase the number of loans or the amount of loans granted under 
the program. The impact can not be determined at this time. However, FAME has indicated that the funding 
available for the program is sufficient to cover any increase in costs and no additional funding is required.
