20SUMMARY
21This bill amends provisions regarding the Maine Uniform Building and Energy Code 
22by requiring the Technical Building Codes and Standards Board to adopt and maintain an 
23appendix to the Maine Uniform Building and Energy Code that contains energy 
24conservation and efficiency requirements that exceed the requirements in the code, to 
25make the appendix available for voluntary adoption by municipalities and to maintain a 
26list of those municipalities on the board’s publicly accessible website.
