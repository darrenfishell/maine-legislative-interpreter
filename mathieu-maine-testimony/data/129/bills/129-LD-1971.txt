26SUMMARY
27This bill allows a school administrative unit to employ as a school security officer a 
28person who is a trained law enforcement officer or a person who meets the qualifications 
29for a law enforcement officer as set forth in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 25, chapter 
30341 or a person who has retired in good standing from a position as a certified law 
31enforcement officer for a state, county, municipal or federal law enforcement agency and 
32who meets the requirements for criminal history record information of Title 20-A, section 
336103.
