24SUMMARY
25This amendment makes the following changes to the bill.
261.  It clarifies the definitions of "authorized individual," "insurance carrier" and 
27"nonpublic information."
282.  It extends the time period for notification of a cybersecurity event from 72 hours 
29to no later than 3 business days.
303.  It changes the date that an insurance carrier annually certify compliance with the 
31requirements for an information security program from February 15th to April 15th.
324.  It clarifies the exemption for small business licensees and for certain licensees 
33subject to federal law.
345.  It changes the effective date to June 1, 2021.
