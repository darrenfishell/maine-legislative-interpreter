22SUMMARY
23This bill amends the law restricting the sale of residential upholstered furniture 
24treated with flame-retardant chemicals to align Maine's requirements with standards 
25recently enacted in California.  The bill also specifies that the Department of 
26Environmental Protection is authorized, rather than required, to adopt rules to implement 
27the section of law governing residential upholstered furniture.
