21form as developed by the Maine Board of Dental Examiners.
22SUMMARY
23This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rule 
24Chapter 101:  MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter II, Section 25, Dental Services to add 
25certain procedures as covered services and to remove the requirement that independent 
26practice dental hygienists must have 2 written agreements with dentists, one regarding 
27temporary fillings and another regarding dental radiographs.
