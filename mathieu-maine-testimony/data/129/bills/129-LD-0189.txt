2Sec. 1.  24-A MRSA §5083, sub-§3, ¶C is enacted to read:
3C.  When an insurer has received all necessary written documentation and 
4information identified in subsection 2 to support payment of a claim, the insurer may 
5not delay or withhold payment of the claim by requiring that an insured or the 
6insured's representative request payment of the claim by telephone as a prerequisite 
7for releasing funds to pay the claim for benefits. 
8SUMMARY
9This bill prohibits an insurer from delaying or withholding payment of a claim for 
10long-term care insurance benefits by requiring that an insured or the insured's 
11representative request payment of the claim by telephone as a prerequisite for releasing 
12funds to pay the claim for benefits if the insurer has received all necessary written 
13documentation and information to support payment of the claim.
