2. Sale of nonprescription drugs by vending machines. The Maine Board of 
2 Pharmacy shall adopt rules to allow a phannacy licensed as a vending machine outlet in 
3 accordance with section 13751 to sell or distribute nonprescription drugs by vending 
4 machines. The rules must include, but are not limited to, the following: 
5 A. A requirement that only nonprescription drugs may be dispensed by a vending 
6 machine; 
7 B. A requirement that nonprescription drugs dispensed by a vending machine must 
8 be stored in accordance with manufacturer recommendations, including those that 
9 require a stable temperature; 
10 C. A requirement that nonprescription drugs dispensed by a vending machine must 
11 be sold only in the manufacturer's clearly labeled, original, unbroken, tamper-proof 
12 and expiration-dated packaging; 
13 D. A requirement that nonprescription drugs dispensed by a vending machine may 
14 not be older than the manufacturer's expiration date; 
15 E. A limitation that no more than 12 different nonprescription drugs may be 
16 dispensed by any single vending machine; 
17 F. A requirement that a vending machine tlu·ough which nonprescription drugs are 
18 dispensed must have an obvious and legible statement on the machine that identifies 
19 the owner of the machine, a toll-free telephone number at which the consumer may 
20 contact the owner of the machine, a statement advising the consumer to check the 
21 expiration date of the product before using the product and the telephone number of 
22 the board; 
23 G. Identification of any nonprescription drugs that may not be dispensed by a 
24 vending machine; and 
25 H. Identification of locations at which a vending machine dispensing nonprescription 
26 drugs may not be located, including the following:
