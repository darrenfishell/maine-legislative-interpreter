13SUMMARY
14This bill amends the law regarding the temporary licensure of physicians to require 
15the temporary licensure of a physician within 60 days of application for a temporary 
16license when the Board of Licensure in Medicine determines temporary licensure of the 
17physician is necessary to provide relief for a local or national emergency or for a situation 
18in which the number of physicians is insufficient to supply adequate medical services or 
19for the purpose of permitting the physician to serve as locum tenens for another 
20physician.  It reduces the fee for the temporary license from $400 to $100.
