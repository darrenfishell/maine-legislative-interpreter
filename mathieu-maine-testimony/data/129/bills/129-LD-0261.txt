8C.  Impose restrictions that prevent a commercial entity from operating the entity's 
9vehicles on the public way where it is headquartered or where it is conducting its 
10business activities.
11SUMMARY
12This bill requires publicly available written justification for imposing restrictions on a 
13public way for more than 6 weeks, prohibits the imposition of size and weight restrictions 
14for vehicles on a public way when the ambient air temperature is below 31 degrees 
15Fahrenheit and protects the ability of a commercial entity to operate its vehicles on the 
16public way where it is headquartered or where it is conducting its business activities.
