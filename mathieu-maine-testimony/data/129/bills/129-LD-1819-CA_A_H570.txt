A voids revenue decrease from federal changes -Local Government Fund 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Changes to federal regulations related to audits oflarge or complex partnerships have put at risk the collection of 
certain revenues under current Maine law. This bill is in response to the federal changes and will ensure that revenue 
collections from audited partnerships will not be reduced.
