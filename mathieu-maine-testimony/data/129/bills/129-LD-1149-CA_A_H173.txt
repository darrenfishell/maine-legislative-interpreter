This bill includes General Fund appropriations totaling $925,000 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $325,000 in fiscal year 
2020-21 to the Maine State Libra1y to support resource sharing services for Maine's libraries. Of these amounts, 
$600,000 in one-time funds is appropriated in fiscal year 2019-20 to accelerate libraries' access to the Maine 
statewide catalog ($500,000) and for an in-depth analysis of statewide libraiy services and resource sharing 
($100,000). Additionally, $325,000 in ongoing funds is for increased costs associated with the Van Delivery and 
Interlibrary Lending program ($75,000 per year) and for increased access to educational, career and economic 
development content with the Digital Maine Libra1y ($250,000 per year)
