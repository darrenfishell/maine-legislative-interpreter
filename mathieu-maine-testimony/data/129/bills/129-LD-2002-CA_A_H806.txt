COMMITTEE AMENDMENT1SUMMARY
2This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee:
31.  Clarifies that a public official or employee or agent of the Department of 
4Administrative and Financial Services, office of marijuana policy who inspects, reviews, 
5divulges, makes known in any manner or otherwise uses any individual's social security 
6number for any purpose other than the conduct of official duties commits a Class E crime 
7if such conduct is intentional or knowing.  Under the bill, such violation is based on 
8conduct described as willful; and
92.  Strikes from the bill provisions requiring the dismissal from office of a public 
10official or employee or agent of the office of marijuana policy who willfully uses an 
11individual's social security number for an unauthorized purpose.
