25SUMMARY
26This bill provides the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife the authority to 
27change the closing date of the recreational ice-fishing season by rule.  It also amends the 
28rule-making provision of the law governing open seasons for fishing to explicitly provide 
29for adoption of emergency rules.
