13'Sec. 6.  Board of Licensure in Medicine to review licensing laws and 
14recommend changes. The Board of Licensure in Medicine established by the Maine 
15Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 12004-A, subsection 24, in consultation with interested 
16parties, shall review Title 32, chapter 48, any rules adopted by the board and any 
17emerging issues identified by the board and recommend changes to update the licensing 
18chapter and any rules or to address any emerging issues. The board shall submit its report 
19and recommendations to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 
20jurisdiction over health coverage, insurance and financial services matters no later than 
21February 15, 2021. The joint standing committee may report out a bill to the First Regular 
22Session of the 130th Legislature based on the board's recommendations.
