17SUMMARY
18Under current law, a marriage license may be issued to parties who are under 16 
19years of age with the written consent of their parents, guardians or legal custodians and 
20the consent of the probate judge in the county where each minor resides. This bill amends 
21the law to prohibit the issuance of a marriage license to a person under 16 years of age.
