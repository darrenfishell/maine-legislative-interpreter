3SUMMARY
4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to increase funding to the ConnectME Authority from $1,000,000 
6to $5,000,000 to expand universal broadband and high-speed Internet into rural areas 
7identified as the 6% of the State unserved by high-speed Internet.  This bill proposes to 
8make expanding high-speed Internet into unserved rural areas a key emphasis in the 
9economic development of and to multiply the return to the State by directing the 
10ConnectME Authority to use the increased funding to increase the rate of strategic 
11broadband investment and leverage additional federal funding to provide middle-mile and 
12last-mile infrastructure in the unserved areas and to correct broadband deficiencies 
13identified in the ConnectME Authority's baseline update of 2013.
