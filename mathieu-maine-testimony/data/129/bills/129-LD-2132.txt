7legislative rule acceptance period; and
8Whereas, immediate enactment of this resolve is necessary to record the 
9Legislature's position on final adoption of the rule; and
10Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
11the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
12immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
13therefore, be it
14Sec. 1.  Adoption.  Resolved:  That final adoption of Chapter 104: Certain 
15Payments Not Immediate, a provisionally adopted major substantive rule of the Office of 
16the Treasurer of State that has been submitted to the Legislature for review pursuant to 
17the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A outside the legislative 
18rule acceptance period, is authorized.
19Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
20legislation takes effect when approved.
21SUMMARY
22This resolve provides for legislative review of Chapter 104: Certain Payments Not 
23Immediate, a major substantive rule of the Office of the Treasurer of State that was filed 
24outside the legislative rule acceptance period.
