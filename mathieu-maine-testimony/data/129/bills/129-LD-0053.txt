2Sec. 1.  30-A MRSA §2501, sub-§4 is enacted to read:
34.  Ballot questions.  Notwithstanding Title 21-A, section 906, subsection 7, 
4municipal initiative and referendum questions must be listed on the ballot using 
5sequential capital letters of the alphabet instead of sequential numbers.
6Sec. 2.  30-A MRSA §2528, sub-§6, ¶C, as enacted by PL 1987, c. 737, Pt. A, §2 
7and Pt. C, §106 and amended by PL 1989, c. 6; c. 9, §2; and c. 104, Pt. C, §§8 and 10, is 
8further amended to read:
9C.  Any question or questions required by law to be submitted to a vote shall must be 
10printed either below the list of candidates or on a separate ballot from the ballot 
11listing candidates.  Notwithstanding Title 21-A, section 906, subsection 7, the 
12questions must be listed on the ballot using sequential capital letters of the alphabet 
13instead of sequential numbers.  If a separate ballot is used, this ballot must be a 
14different color than the ballot listing candidates.
15SUMMARY
16This bill requires that municipal initiative and referendum questions be listed on 
17election ballots using sequential capital letters of the alphabet rather than sequential 
18numbers.
