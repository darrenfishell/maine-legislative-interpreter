19SUMMARY
20 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, reduces the 
21 reimbursement rate per discharge to cover the cost of providing care to MaineCare 
22 members in Southern Maine Health Care's psychiatric inpatient unit and limits the new 
23 reimbursement rate to the period beginning on the effective date of this legislation and 
24 ending January 15, 2022.  The amendment directs the Department of Health and Human 
25 Services to seek and apply for federal funds and funds from any other sources to pay the 
26 costs of providing psychiatric services to uninsured patients.  The amendment also directs 
27 the Department of Health and Human Services to submit a report to the joint standing 
28 committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human services matters 
29 regarding the progress the department has made in developing a reimbursement rate to 
30 provide inpatient psychiatric care under its rule Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, 
31 Chapter III, Section 45.03.  The committee is authorized to report out legislation related to 
32 the report to the Second Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.  This amendment 
33 includes a provision to repeal the resolve January 15, 2022.
34 This amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section.
