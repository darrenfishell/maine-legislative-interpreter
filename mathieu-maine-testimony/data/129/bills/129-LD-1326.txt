22The exemptions provided in this paragraph apply to the property of that veteran,

25SUMMARY
26This bill allows persons who served in the Armed Forces of the United States during 
27the period from February 1, 1955 to February 27, 1961 to qualify for the veterans' 
28property tax exemption based on dates of service.
