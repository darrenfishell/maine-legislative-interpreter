SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the unanimous report of the committee, adds an 
emergency preamble and emergency clause to the bill. It removes the requirement that a 
visiting qualifying patient obtain valid written certification pursuant to Maine law from 
the visiting patient's medical provider. It also directs the Department of Administrative 
and Financial Services to maintain a list of other jurisdictions that authorize the medical 
use of marijuana and the images of the valid medical marijuana certifications from those 
jurisdictions and make that information available to registered caregivers and registered 
dispensaries.
