152.  Open archery and crossbow season on deer.  The commissioner shall by rule 
16establish a regular archery-only archery and crossbow season beginning at least 30 days 
17prior and extending to the beginning of the regular deer hunting season, as described in 
18section 11401, subsection 1, paragraph A, for the purpose of hunting deer with bow and 
19arrow only and crossbow.  During the regular archery-only archery and crossbow season 
20on deer the following restrictions apply.
21A.  A person may not take a deer with bow and arrow during a regular archery-only 
22archery and crossbow season unless that person uses a hand-held bow and broadhead 
23arrow in accordance with section 11214, subsection 1, paragraph P.
24A-1.  A person may not take a deer with a crossbow during a regular archery and 
25crossbow season unless that person uses a crossbow in accordance with section 
2610953, subsection 3.
27B.  A person may not carry firearms of any kind while hunting any species of wildlife 
28with bow and arrow or crossbow during the regular archery-only archery and 
29crossbow season on deer, except that a person who holds a license that allows hunting 
30with firearms may carry a handgun.  This paragraph may not be construed to prohibit 
31a person from carrying a concealed weapon in accordance with Title 25, section 
322001-A.
33C.  Except as provided in section 11109-A, subsection 3, if a person takes a deer with 
34bow and arrow or crossbow during the regular archery-only archery and crossbow 
35season on deer, that person is precluded from further hunting for deer during that 
36year.
37D.  Except as provided in this subsection, the provisions of this Part concerning deer 
38are applicable to the taking of deer with bow and arrow or crossbow, including the 
39transportation, registration and possession of deer taken by this method.
