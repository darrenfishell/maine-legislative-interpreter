7SUMMARY
8This bill makes changes to the laws governing marriage records and the filing, 
9correction and amendment of death records, including removing language from the 
10statutes that describes marriage as the union of a man and a woman to conform with the 
11laws in the State.  This bill also authorizes people to record their intentions to marry with 
12the State Registrar of Vital Statistics.
