COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 991, L.D. 1369, Bill, "An Act To 
Create an Additional Pathway To Certify Industrial Alts Teachers To Foster Career and 
Technical Subjects in Maine Schools" 
Atnend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the 
following: 
'Sec. 1. 20-A MRSA §13011, sub-§6-C is enacted to read: 
6-C. Additional pathway for industrial arts certification. The state board shall 
adopt rules relating to the credentialing of education personnel to create a pathway for a 
teacher to obtain a conditional ce1tificate and a pathway for a teacher to obtain an 
endorsement on a teaching certificate for a component of industrial arts as defined by the 
department with an experiential lab that includes, but is not limited to automotive body 
repair, diagnostics and mechanics; welding; electrical; carpentiy; and computer-aided 
design. This endorsement must be separate from other endorsements. 
A. At a minimum, rules adopted pursuant to this subsection to obtain a conditional 
certificate must include the following qualifications: 
(1) A completed apprenticeship registered with a statewide or national 
apprenticeship and training organization: and 
(2) A completed 2-year degree or ce1tificate from a technical institution 
accredited by a national association of career and technical schools or similar 
organization. 
Rules adopted pursuant to this subsection are major substantive rules as defined in Title 
5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.' 
Atnend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively.
