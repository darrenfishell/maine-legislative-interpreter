2Sec. 1.  12 MRSA §11209, sub-§1, ¶A, as amended by PL 2015, c. 71, §1, is 
3further amended to read:
4A.  Unless a relevant municipal ordinance provides otherwise and except as provided 
5in subsection subsections 3 and 4 and sections 12401 and 12402, discharge a firearm, 
6including a muzzle-loading firearm, or crossbow or cause a projectile to pass as a 
7result of that discharge within 100 yards of a building or residential dwelling without 
8the permission of the owner or, in the owner's absence, of an adult occupant of that 
9building or dwelling authorized to act on behalf of the owner; or
10Sec. 2.  12 MRSA §11209, sub-§4 is enacted to read:
114.  Sport shooting ranges.  Unless otherwise prohibited, a person may discharge a 
12firearm on a sport shooting range as defined in Title 30-A, section 3011, subsection 1 that 
13is within 100 yards of a building if the sport shooting range was established and in regular 
14operation prior to the erection of the building.
15SUMMARY
16This bill allows the discharge of a firearm on a sport shooting range that is within 100 
17yards of a building if the sport shooting range was established and in regular operation 
18prior to the erection of the building.
