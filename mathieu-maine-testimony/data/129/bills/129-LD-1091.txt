13SUMMARY
14Current law provides that the personal liability of an employee of a governmental 
15entity for negligent acts or omissions within the course and scope of employment is 
16subject to a limit of $10,000.  This bill provides an exception to this limit for an employee 
17who is covered by liability insurance covering the negligent acts or omissions, in which 
18case the limit is equal to the limits of the policy.
