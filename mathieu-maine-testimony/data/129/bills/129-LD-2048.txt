10Sec. 1.  Director of Bureau of Parks and Lands authorized to convey land.

12Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may, by a boundary line agreement and on such 
13other terms and conditions as the director may direct by quitclaim deed without covenant,

17adjustment to create a straight southeast boundary line at Vaughan Woods Memorial 
18State Park located in South Berwick.
19SUMMARY
20This resolve authorizes certain land transactions by the Director of the Bureau of 
21Parks and Lands within the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry in 
22order to execute a boundary line adjustment to create a straight southeast boundary line at 
23Vaughan Woods Memorial State Park located in South Berwick.
