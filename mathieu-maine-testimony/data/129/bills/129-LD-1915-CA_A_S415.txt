27 SUMMARY 
28 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, changes the title of 
29 the resolve and language in the resolve to broaden the scope of the study to all 
30 aboveground petroleum storage tanks instead of just fixed-roof, heated tanks. The 
31 amendment also requires the Department of Environmental Protection, as part of the 
32 study required in the resolve, to identify methods or programs for assisting municipalities 
33 in the use and application of mobile air quality monitoring devices to identify the release 
34 of hazardous air pollutants from aboveground petroleum storage tanks. It also requires
