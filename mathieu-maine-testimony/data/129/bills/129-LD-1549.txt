41.  Department of Education salary supplement.  Notwithstanding any other 
5provision of law, the Department of Education shall provide a public school teacher or a 
6teacher in a publicly supported secondary school who has attained certification from the 
7National Board for Professional Teaching Standards, or its successor organization, with 
8an annual national board certification salary supplement for the life of the certificate.  The 
9salary supplement must be added to the teacher's base salary and must be considered in 
10the calculation for contributions to the Maine Public Employees Retirement System.  If a 
11nationally certified teacher is no longer employed as a teacher, the supplement ceases.  
12The amount of the salary supplement is:
13A.  For fiscal year 2012-13, $2,500;
14B.  For fiscal year 2013-14, $2,750; and
15C.  For fiscal year 2014-15 and succeeding years, except for a teacher under 
16paragraph D, $3,000.; and
17D.  For fiscal year 2020-21 and succeeding years, for a teacher who is employed in a 
18school in which at least 50% of students qualify for a free or reduced-price lunch 
19under chapter 223, subchapter 7 during the year that the supplement is provided, 
20$9,000.
21SUMMARY
22This bill increases, from $3,000 to $9,000, the supplement paid to a teacher who has 
23attained certification from the National Board for Professional Teaching Standards and 
24who is employed in a school in which at least 50% of students qualify for a free or 
25reduced-price lunch during the year that the supplement is provided.
