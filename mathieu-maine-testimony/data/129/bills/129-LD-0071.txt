2Sec. 1.  36 MRSA §5122, sub-§2, ¶QQ, is enacted to read:
3QQ.  The portion of contributions to a qualified tuition program established under

5not be claimed on returns when federal adjusted gross income exceeds $100,000 for 
6returns with a filing status of single or married filing separately or $200,000 for 
7returns with a filing status of married joint or head of household.
8SUMMARY
9This bill reinstates the income tax deduction for certain contributions to qualified 
10tuition programs under Section 529 of the Internal Revenue Code up to $250 per 
11designated beneficiary.  This deduction was in effect from 2006 to 2015.
