Page 1 - 129LR2830(01)-11Sec. 1.  Lafayette Trail designated.  Resolved:  That the Department of 
2Transportation shall erect and maintain suitable signs and other markers at or near each 
3portion of the route followed by Gilbert du Motier, the Marquis de Lafayette, while he 
4was in Maine in 1824 and 1825, including locations in Kittery, South Berwick, Berwick, 
5Wells, Kennebunk, Biddeford, Saco, Scarborough and Portland, and designate the route 
6as the Lafayette Trail.  The signs and markers must bear the Lafayette Trail logo used by 
7The Lafayette Trail, Inc.
8SUMMARY
9This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to erect and maintain suitable 
10signs and other markers at or near each portion of the route followed by the Marquis de 
11Lafayette while he was in Maine, to be designated the Lafayette Trail.
