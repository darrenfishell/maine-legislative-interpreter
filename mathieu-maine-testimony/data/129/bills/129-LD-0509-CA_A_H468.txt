This bill includes ongoing General Fund appropriations totaling $9,450,000 per year beginning in fiscal year 2019-20 
to the Student Financial Assistance Programs within the Finance Authority of Maine. Of that amount, $8,450,000 
per year is provided to support the increase in the minimum grant award under the Maine State Grant Program from 
$1,000 to $2,500 and $1,000,000 per year is appropriated to support adult learners in returning to school and 
completing their credentials. 
LD 1001, the Governor's proposed 2020-2021 biennial budget, includes a General Fund appropriation of $1,000,000 
in fiscal year 2019-20 and ongoing appropriations of $2,000,000 per year beginning in fiscal year 2020-21 to support 
adult learners in returning to school and completing their credentials.
