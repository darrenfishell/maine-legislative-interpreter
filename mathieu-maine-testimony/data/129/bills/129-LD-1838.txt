Fiscal Detail and NotesFiscal Note
The bill includes General Fund appropriations to the Department of Health and Human Services of $26,000 in fiscal 
year 2019-20 for the department to contract with a third party to examine upper payment limit options to increase federally approved limits for services provided under MaineCare. Federal Expenditures Fund allocations are also included for the FMAP match.129th MAINE LEGISLATURE
