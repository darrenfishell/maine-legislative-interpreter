19SUMMARY
20This bill expands the membership of the Wild Blueberry Commission of Maine by 
21adding 2 additional members who are grower representatives and requires the 
22Commissioner of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to formally call for nominations 
23for grower representative members.  The bill clarifies the requirements governing grower 
24representative members and processor representative members.  The bill prohibits former 
25employees of the commission from serving as paid lobbyists for wild blueberry 
26businesses for a period of one year following employment by the commission.  The bill 
27also allows members of the University of Maine System Wild Blueberry Advisory 
28Committee to be reappointed for additional terms.
