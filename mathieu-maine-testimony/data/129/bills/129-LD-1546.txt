12SUMMARY
13This bill provides that, during an interim between the expiration of a state employee 
14collective bargaining agreement and before the effective date of any subsequent 
15collective bargaining agreement, state employees covered by the expired collective 
16bargaining agreement remain eligible for and must receive merit increases in accordance 
17with the terms and conditions set forth in the expired collective bargaining agreement and 
18the public employer is required to pay any increase in employee health insurance 
19premiums occurring during the interim.
