COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 1164, L.D. 1612, Bill, "An Act 
Regarding the Presumption of Abandonment of Gift Obligations" 
Amend the bill by inserting after section 1 the following: 
'Sec. 2. Effective date; application. This Act takes effect January 1, 2021 and 
applies to gift obligation cards sold on or after January 1, 2021.' 
Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment provides that this legislation, which exempts gift obligation cards 
from the Uniform Unclaimed Property Act by establishing that a gift obligation card is 
never presumed abandoned, is effective January 1, 2021 and applies to gift obligation 
cards sold on or after January 1, 2021.

Fiscal Note 
Future biennium revenue decrease -General Fund 
This bill exempts gift obligation cards from the Uniform Unclaimed Property Act and applies to gift obligation cards 
sold on or after January 1, 2021. The Office of Treasurer of State estimates the revenue reduction to the General Fund 
would be $3,500,000 or more annually beginning in fiscal year 2023-24 from reduced balances being available in the 
Unclaimed Property Fund at the time of the normal transfer to the General Fund each year.
