21SUMMARY
22This bill directs the Commissioner of Marine Resources to authorize new zone 
23entrants for a limited-entry lobster zone who have been on a waiting list for 10 or more 
24years and have met certain eligibility requirements. A person authorized as a new zone 
25entrant under this bill must adhere to specific trap tag limits.
