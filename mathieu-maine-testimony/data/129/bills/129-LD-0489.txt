115.  Noise control ordinance exception; authorization.  A municipality may adopt a 
12noise control or other ordinance that applies to an existing sport shooting range if the 
13Commissioner of Public Safety approves the ordinance prior to adoption.  The 
14commissioner shall establish the authorization process, including criteria to allow

17375, subchapter 2-A.
18SUMMARY
19Current law prohibits the application of a municipal noise control or other ordinance 
20to an existing sport shooting range.  This bill gives the Commissioner of Public Safety the 
21power to authorize the application of a municipal noise ordinance to a sport shooting 
22range that existed prior to the adoption of the ordinance.  The Commissioner of Public 
23Safety is required to adopt major substantive rules to establish the criteria and the process 
24for the authorization process.
