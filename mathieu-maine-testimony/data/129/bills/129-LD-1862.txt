9Sec. 1.  Review.  Resolved:  That the Probate and Trust Law Advisory 
10Commission established under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 12004-I, 
11subsection 73-B is directed to conduct a review of the existing laws governing trusts and 
12the Uniform Trust Decanting Act from the National Conference of Commissioners on 
13Uniform State Laws and develop legislative recommendations based on the review.  The 
14Probate and Trust Law Advisory Commission shall invite the participation of interested 
15parties in the review and in the development of recommendations.
16Sec. 2.  Report.  Resolved:  That the Probate and Trust Law Advisory 
17Commission is authorized to submit a bill related to its review under section 1 to the 
18Second Regular Session of the 129th Legislature.
19Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
20legislation takes effect when approved.
21SUMMARY
22This resolve directs the Probate and Trust Law Advisory Commission to review the 
23existing trust laws of this State and the Uniform Trust Decanting Act from the National 
24Conference of Commissioners on Uniform State Laws and develop legislative 
25recommendations based on the review.  The Probate and Trust Law Advisory 
26Commission may submit a bill to the Second Regular Session of the 129th Legislature.
