11SUMMARY
12This bill amends the provisions of the Maine Public Employees Retirement System 
13regarding compensation and service retirement benefits for retired state employees and 
14retired teachers who return to service as classroom-based employees or school 
15administrators in the following ways.
161.  It allows a retired state employee or retired teacher to be restored to service as a 
17classroom-based employee or school administrator beyond the current 5-year limit.
182.  It removes the cap of 75% of compensation established for the position that the 
19retired state employee or retired teacher is filling.
203.  It allows a retired state employee or retired teacher who returns to service as a 
21classroom-based employee or school administrator to receive full retirement, health, 
22dental and life insurance benefits as offered for the position to be filled and suspends the 
23provisions of retiree health, dental and life insurance benefits for retired state employees 
24or retired teachers during the period of reemployment.
254.  It retains the current provisions that a retired state employee or retired teacher who 
26returns to service is not a member and therefore may not accrue additional creditable 
27service or change the retired state employee's or retired teacher's earnable compensation 
28for benefit calculation purposes.
295.  It requires full employee and employer contributions to the retirement system for 
30the unfunded liability and the state group health plan for retiree health care based upon 
31the retired state employee's or retired teacher's compensation.
