11SUMMARY
12This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to adopt rules for 
13children’s residential care facilities that include procedures for fingerprint-based 
14background checks and child abuse and neglect registry checks for all staff working in the 
15facility to ensure compliance with the requirements governing fingerprint-based 
16background checks contained in federal family first prevention services legislation.
