20chapter 375, subchapter 7.
21Sec. 4.  Transition.  Notwithstanding the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 38, section 
22568-B, appointed members of the Clean-up and Response Fund Review Board serving on 
23the effective date of this Act may serve for the remainder of their terms.
24SUMMARY
25This bill reduces the membership of the Clean-up and Response Fund Review Board 
26from 14 to 9 members and amends quorum requirements and the membership of the 
27board's appeals panel.
