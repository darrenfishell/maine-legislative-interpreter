3SUMMARY
4This bill repeals the requirement that a seller of nonresidential real estate provide the 
5purchaser a property disclosure statement that includes information about any abandoned 
6or discontinued roads, public easements or private roads on or abutting the property, if 
7known by the seller, and who is responsible for maintenance of such roads or easements, 
8including any responsible road association, if known by the seller.
