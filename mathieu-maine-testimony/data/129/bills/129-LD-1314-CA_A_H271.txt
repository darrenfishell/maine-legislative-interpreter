SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment requires that an insurer obtain the 
informed written consent of an individual before requesting, requiring, purchasing or 
using any information from an entity providing direct-to-consumer genetic testing in 
connection with the issuance, withholding, extension or renewal of an insurance policy 
for life, credit life, disability, long-term care, accidental injury, specified disease, hospital 
indemnity or credit accident insurance or an annuity.
