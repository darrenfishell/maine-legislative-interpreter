9COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “      ” to H.P. 1485, L.D. 2084, Bill, “An Act 
10Prohibiting Certain Confinement of Egg-laying Hens and the Sale of Their Eggs”
11Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12'Resolve, Directing the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry To 
13Update Best Management Practices for Poultry Facilities' 
14Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and inserting the following:
15'Sec. 1.  Best management practices for poultry facilities.  Resolved:  That 
16the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry shall update department best 
17management practices for poultry facilities in the State and establish guidelines for cage-
18free production of eggs.  The department may cross-reference 3rd-party certifiers' 
19specifications for keeping egg-laying hens humanely in cage-free housing systems.' 
20Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
21section number to read consecutively.
22SUMMARY
23This amendment, which is the majority report, directs the Department of Agriculture, 
24Conservation and Forestry to update its best management practices for poultry facilities in 
25the State and to establish guidelines for cage-free production of eggs.
