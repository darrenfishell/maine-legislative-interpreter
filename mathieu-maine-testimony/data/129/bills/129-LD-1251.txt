16SUMMARY
17This bill provides that agricultural employees and seasonal employees are subject to 
18the laws that place limits on mandatory overtime.  It also provides that agricultural 
19employees are subject to the laws that set a minimum wage and overtime rate.  It provides 
20that the laws that set an overtime rate apply to certain activities related to agricultural 
21produce, meat and fish products and perishable foods.
