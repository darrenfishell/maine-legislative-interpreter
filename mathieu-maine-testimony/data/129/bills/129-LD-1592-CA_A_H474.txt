25 SUMMARY 
26 This amendment clarifies the process for a municipality to withdraw from a regional 
27 school unit that is composed of a single municipality and provides that upon withdrawal 
28 of a municipality from a regional school unit that is composed of a single municipality, 
29 the regional school unit is dissolved.
