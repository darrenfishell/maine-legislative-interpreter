18 SUMMARY 
19 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, replaces the title and 
20 the bill and authorizes a municipality to increase from 30 days up to 60 days the notice 
21 that a party must provide to terminate a tenancy at will. It also authorizes a municipality 
22 to increase from 45 days up to 75 days the notice that a landlord must provide to increase 
23 the rent of a residential tenant.
