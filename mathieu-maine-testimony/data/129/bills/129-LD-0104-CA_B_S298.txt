27 SUMMARY 
28 This amendment, which is the minority report, expands the earned income tax credit 
29 by increasing the credit from 5% of the federal earned income tax credit to 60% for 
30 individuals with no qualifying children and 15% of the federal earned income tax credit 
31 for all other eligible individuals for tax years beginning in 2020 and increasing each year 
32 until it reaches 23% for tax years beginning in 2023 and after. 
33 This amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section.

The bill expands the earned income tax credit and would result in a reduction in General Fund revenue of $25,336,500 
in fiscal year 2020-21 and a reduction in Local Government Fund revenue of $1,333,500 in fiscal year 2020-21. It 
includes a General Fund appropriation of $109 ,209 in fiscal year 2020-21 for one Senior Tax Examiner position and 
related costs to process and audit income tax returns. 
Additional costs to the Department of Labor to produce and furnish the required poster or notice can be absorbed
