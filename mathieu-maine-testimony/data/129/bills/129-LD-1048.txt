44.  Determining rates.  In determining just and reasonable rates, the commission: 
5A.  Shall provide such revenues to the utility as may be required to perform its public 
6service and to attract necessary capital on just and reasonable terms; and
7B.  Shall, to a level within the commission's discretion, consider whether the utility is

10C.  Shall, when determining natural gas rates and rate structures for a gas utility, 
11consider the effect of rates and rate structures on energy conservation, including, but 
12not limited to, financial incentives for customers to reduce natural gas consumption.
13SUMMARY
14This bill requires the Public Utilities Commission, in determining rates and rate 
15structures for natural gas utilities, to consider energy conservation and financial 
16incentives for customers to reduce natural gas consumption.
