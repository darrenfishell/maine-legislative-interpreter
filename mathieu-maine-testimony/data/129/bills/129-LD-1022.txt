9SUMMARY
10This bill amends the elements of the crime of tampering with a witness, informant, 
11juror or victim by adding the instance of committing an unlawful act in retaliation against 
12a witness, informant, juror or victim for acting in the capacity of a witness, informant, 
13juror or victim.
