16SUMMARY
17This bill provides that for public works construction contracts that involve funding 
18from the Federal Government the prevailing wage requirements in state law apply unless 
19the prevailing wage requirements that would otherwise apply under the federal Davis-
20Bacon Act would result in higher total wages under the contract.  An exception is 
21provided for funds received under the United States Housing Act of 1937 if the 
22application of a state prevailing wage is expressly preempted by federal law.
