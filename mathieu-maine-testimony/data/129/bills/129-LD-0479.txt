10SUMMARY
11Under current law, the court may terminate spousal support when the payee and 
12another person have entered into a relationship that is the functional equivalent of 
13marriage for at least 12 months of a period of 18 consecutive months.  This bill changes 
14the time frame of cohabitation necessary for cessation of spousal support to 5 years over a 
15period of 7 consecutive years.
