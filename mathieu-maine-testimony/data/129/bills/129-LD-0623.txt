2Sec. 1.  P&SL 1925, c. 4, §4, 2nd ¶, 5th sentence is repealed and the following 
3enacted in its place:
4Each trustee of the City of Brewer High School District receives compensation annually 
5as determined by the council of the City of Brewer, except that, beginning in the 2018-19 
6fiscal year, the president, treasurer and clerk receive additional compensation annually as 
7determined by the council of the City of Brewer.
8SUMMARY
9This bill amends the charter of the City of Brewer High School District to provide 
10that each trustee of the district receives annual compensation as determined by the city 
11council and to provide that the president, treasurer and clerk receive additional annual 
12compensation as determined by the city council.  Currently, the district charter provides 
13that only the treasurer receives compensation.
