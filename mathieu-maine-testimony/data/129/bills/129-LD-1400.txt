19SUMMARY
20This bill allows a member of the State Employee and Teacher Retirement Program or 
21the Participating Local District Retirement Program to purchase up to 4 years of service 
22credit for time served as a full-time law enforcement officer prior to membership.
23It also allows a member of either program who served as a full-time active duty 
24member of the United States Armed Forces during the Cold War, measured from January 
251, 1947 to December 27, 1991, before becoming a member and who separated from the 
26armed forces under conditions other than dishonorable to purchase service credit.  This 
27service credit is capped at 4 years for members of the State Employee and Teacher 
28Retirement Program and 5 years for members of the Participating Local District 
29Retirement Program.
