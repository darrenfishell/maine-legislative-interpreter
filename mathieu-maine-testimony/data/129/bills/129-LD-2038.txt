7SUMMARY
8This bill amends the records confidentiality statute within the Child and Family 
9Services and Child Protection Act to ensure that information may be disclosed to a social 
10media service when necessary to report, investigate or remove a threat or serious 
11intimidation attempt against an employee of the Department of Health and Human 
12Services, an employee of the Attorney General's Office or an employee of the court.
