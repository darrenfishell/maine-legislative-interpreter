15SUMMARY
16This bill amends the property tax fairness credit under the income tax laws for tax 
17years beginning on or after January 1, 2020 by changing the formula for calculation of 
18benefits to reflect the calculation that applied under the Maine Residents Property Tax 
19Program, referred to as "the circuitbreaker program," which was in effect prior to 
20enactment of the property tax fairness credit.
