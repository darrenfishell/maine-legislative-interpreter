26SUMMARY
27This bill defines an autocycle as a 3-wheeled motorcycle that has a steering wheel or 
28handlebars, floor pedals for automotive-style controls and seating that does not require 
29the operator to straddle or sit astride a seat and establishes provisions for registration and 
30operator licensing.
