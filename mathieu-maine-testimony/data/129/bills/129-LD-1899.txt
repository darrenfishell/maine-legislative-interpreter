3SUMMARY
4This bill makes the following changes to the motor vehicle laws.
51.  It clarifies the law with respect to weight requirements for heavy duty recovery 
6vehicles.
72.  It allows the Secretary of State to assist law enforcement entities by providing 
8driver's license biometric technology records.  The bill prohibits the Secretary of State 
9from allowing an outside entity to use biometric technology to search driver's license 
10records.
113.  It repeals the law establishing the Motor Carrier Review Board in order to remove 
12conflicts with the motor carrier review process conducted by the Department of the 
13Secretary of State, Bureau of Motor Vehicles.
