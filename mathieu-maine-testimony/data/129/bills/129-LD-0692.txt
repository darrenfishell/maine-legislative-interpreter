Page 1 - 129LR0999(01)-11Sec. 1.  Department of Health and Human Services to increase 
2MaineCare rates. Resolved:  That, no later than January 1, 2020, the Department of 
3Health and Human Services shall amend its rule Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits 
4Manual, Chapter III to increase reimbursement rates for counseling services provided by 
5licensed clinical social workers as defined in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 32, section 
67001-A to no less than the lowest reimbursement rate for the same counseling services 
7paid by an insurance carrier licensed in this State in accordance with Title 24-A. Rules 
8adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules pursuant to Title 5, chapter 
9375, subchapter 2-A.
10SUMMARY
11This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rule 
12Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III to increase reimbursement rates for 
13counseling services provided by licensed clinical social workers to no less than the lowest 
14reimbursement rate for the same counseling services paid by an insurance carrier licensed 
15in this State. The rules are routine technical rules and must be amended no later than 
16January 1, 2020.
