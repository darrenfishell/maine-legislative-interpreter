19SUMMARY
20This bill replaces language regarding the bonding authority of the Madison 
21Department of Electric Works to provide that it is the electric works that issues bonds for 
22its purposes and not the Town of Madison.  The bill also removes the $1,200,000 cap on 
23the issuance of bonds or notes for current operating expenses.
