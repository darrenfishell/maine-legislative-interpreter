Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This amendment removes the requirement from the bill that all initiators of deposit enter into a commingling 
agreement by January 1, 2023. Unclaimed deposits for redeemable beverage containers not subject to a commingling 
agreement would continue to be turned over to the State and the revenue loss beginning in fiscal year 2022-23 is 
reversed.
