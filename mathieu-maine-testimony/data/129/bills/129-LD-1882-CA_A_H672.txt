SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill. It clarifies that a qualified resident disabled veteran 
may obtain a noncommercial lobster and crab fishing license or a noncommercial scallop 
license at no cost, and it adds reservists to the definition of "qualified resident disabled 
veteran." It also exempts a qualified resident disabled veteran from paying the scallop 
license surcharge that is assessed on a noncommercial scallop license.
