28SUMMARY
29This bill requires the disclosure of the name of each member of a limited liability 
30company formed in Maine to be included in the company's certificate of formation and 
31the name of each member to be included in the company's annual report to the Secretary 
32of State.  The bill also requires the disclosure of the name of each member of a foreign 
33limited liability company in the company's statement of foreign qualifications to conduct 
34activities.  The bill also requires that if any member of a limited liability company or 
35foreign limited liability company is an organization, the names of each person who forms 
36that organization's governing body be listed.
