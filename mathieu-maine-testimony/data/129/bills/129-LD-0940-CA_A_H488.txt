10 SUMMARY 
11 This amendment requires that, upon approval by the voters of Franklin County at 
12 referendum held in November 2020, when the redistricting of the county commissioner 
13 districts is done in 2021 pursuant to the Constitution of Maine, Franklin County must be 
14 divided into 5 county commissioner districts. This amendment requires that the 
15 reapportionment for each of the new 5 districts reflect, as closely as practicable, the 
16 specified geographical composition of each district.
