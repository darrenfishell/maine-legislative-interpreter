10SUMMARY
11This bill provides an exemption from the sales and use tax to area agencies on aging, 
12as designated by the Department of Health and Human Services, and public and private 
13nonprofit agencies that are operating under grants provided by the department, that 
14provide social services in order to secure and maintain maximum independence and 
15dignity in a home environment for older people capable of self-care with appropriate 
16supportive services.
