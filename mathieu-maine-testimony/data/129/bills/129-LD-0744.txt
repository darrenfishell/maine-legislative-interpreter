18Sec. 2.  Property interests that may be conveyed.  Resolved:  That the state 
19property authorized to be sold or leased is a parcel or parcels of land located in the Town 
20of East Millinocket known as "the Dolby Landfill," consisting of approximately 3,384 
21acres conveyed to the State by Katahdin Paper Company LLC on September 28, 2011, 
22recorded in the Penobscot County Registry of Deeds, Book 12608, Page 97-104 and 
23identified on the Town of East Millinocket's Tax Assessor's Map 01 as Lot 09-011.
24Sec. 3.  Property to be sold as is.  Resolved:  That the Commissioner of 
25Administrative and Financial Services may negotiate and execute leases and purchase and 
26sale agreements upon terms the commissioner considers appropriate; however, the state 
27property must be sold "as is," with no representations or warranties.  Title must be 
28transferred by quitclaim deed without covenant or release deed and executed by the 
29commissioner.
30Sec. 4.  Exemptions.  Resolved:  That any lease or conveyance pursuant to this 
31resolve is exempt from any statutory or regulatory requirement that the state property 
32must first be offered to the Maine State Housing Authority or another state or local 
33agency or offered through competitive bidding.
34Sec. 5.  Appraisal.  Resolved:  That the Commissioner of Administrative and 
35Financial Services shall have the current market value of the state property determined by 
36a broker opinion of value and current comparative market analysis.  The commissioner 
37may list the state property for sale or lease with a private real estate broker and negotiate 
38any sale or lease, solicit bids, sell directly to a purchaser or enter directly into a lease with 
39a tenant.  The commissioner may reject any offer.
Page 2 - 129LR1075(01)-11Sec. 6.  Proceeds.  Resolved:  That any proceeds from the sale or lease of the state 
2property pursuant to this resolve must be deposited into the Department of Administrative 
3and Financial Services, Bureau of General Services' capital repair and improvement 
4account for capital improvements.
5Sec. 7.  Repeal.  This resolve is repealed 5 years from its effective date.
6SUMMARY
7This resolve authorizes the Commissioner of Administrative and Financial Services 
8to lease or sell the state property known as "the Dolby Landfill" located in the Town of 
9East Millinocket.
