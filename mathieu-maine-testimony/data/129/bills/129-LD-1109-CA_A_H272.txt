COMMITTEE AMENDMENT 
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 813, L.D. 1109 
SUMMARY 
2 This amendment: 
3 1. Amends the definition of "ATV" to limit that definition to a motor-driven, off-
4 road, recreational vehicle that was designed by the manufacturer for and is capable of 
5 cross-country travel. It preserves that portion of the bill that amends the definition of 
6 "ATV" to exclude a motor vehicle as defined in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 29-A, 
7 section 101, subsection 42. It removes the size and weight limits added by the bill to the 
8 definition of "ATV"; and 
9 2. Removes that section of the bill that prohibits the operation of an ATV on a trail 
10 posted with size or weight restrictions without authorization of the landowner.
