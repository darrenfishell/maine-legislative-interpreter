COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 367, L.D. 510, Bill, "An Act To 
Authorize Funding for Transitional Housing for Women Veterans and Their Families" 
Amend the bill by inserting after the title and before the enacting clause the 
following: 
'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and 
Whereas, the need to support women veterans in transition and their families is 
immediate and crucial for their health, safety and welfare; and 
Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
therefore,' 
Amend the bill by striking out all of section 1 and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and 
allocations are made.
