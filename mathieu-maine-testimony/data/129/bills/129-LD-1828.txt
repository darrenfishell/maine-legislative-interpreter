10SUMMARY
11Current law specifically exempts certain employees from the laws requiring the 
12payment of overtime.  This bill adds to the list of exempted employees those state 
13employees in the executive branch and the judicial branch engaged in fire protection 
14activities and in law enforcement activities and conforms state law to the federal Fair 
15Labor Standards Act.
