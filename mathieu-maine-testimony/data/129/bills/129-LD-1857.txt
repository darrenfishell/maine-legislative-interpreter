3ยง13203.  Punitive or retaliatory transfers
4A school administrative unit may not transfer a teacher to another location or 
5teaching assignment for a punitive or retaliatory reason unless the teacher consents to the 
6transfer.  A teacher who has been involuntarily transferred and believes the transfer is for 
7a punitive or retaliatory reason may request a hearing with the school board.  The school 
8board shall hold a hearing within 15 days of the receipt of the teacher's request.  If the 
9teacher demonstrates that the transfer was for a punitive or retaliatory reason, the school 
10board must reverse the transfer.
11SUMMARY
12This bill prohibits the transfer of a teacher to another location or teaching assignment 
13for a punitive or retaliatory reason without the teacher's consent.  It authorizes a teacher 
14who has been involuntarily transferred to request a hearing with the school board and 
15requires the school board to reverse the transfer if the teacher demonstrates that the 
16transfer was for a punitive or retaliatory reason.
