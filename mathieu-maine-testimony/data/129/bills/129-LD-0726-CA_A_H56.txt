Amend the bill by inserting after section 1 the following: 
'Sec. 2. 36 MRSA §2557, sub-§38, as amended by PL 2017, c. 445, §2 and 
affected by § 5, is :further amended to read: 
38. Nonprofit library collaboratives. Sales to nonprofit collaboratives of 
academic, public, school and special libraries that provide support for library resource 
sharing, promote quality library information services and suppo1t the cultural, educational 
and economic development of the State; end 
Sec. 3. 36 MRSA §2557, sub-§39, as enacted by PL 2017, c. 445, §3 and 
affected by § 5, is amended to read: 
39. Certain support organizations for combat-injured veterans. Sales to 
incorporated nonprofit organizations organized for the primary purpose of operating a

40. Area agency on aging. Sales to an area agency on aging designated under Title

supportive services.'
