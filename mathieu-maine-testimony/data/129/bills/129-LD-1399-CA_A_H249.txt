17 SUMMARY 
18 This amendment makes the following changes to the bill. 
19 1. It replaces the appropriations and allocations section to remove the funding to 
20 expand preventive oral health services provided in schools. 
21 2. It removes the descriptions of the responsibilities of 2 positions created in the bill 
22 to provide the Department of Health and Human Services flexibility. 
23 3. It removes the interim report on the oral health program. 
24 4. It requires a report on the status of the oral health program rather than on the 
25 expansion of the program since the funding to expand the program has been removed.
