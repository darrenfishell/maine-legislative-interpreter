8SUMMARY
9This amendment does the following:
101.  It adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause;
112.  It adds language clarifying that a Legislator initially appointed to the Maine 
12Spaceport Complex Leadership Council who subsequently ceases to be a member of the 
13Legislature following an election will be replaced on the council by the appointing 
14authority;
153.  It removes the language conditioning the meeting of the council on the availability 
16of funding;
174.  It adds language clarifying that the development of the strategic plan by the 
18council is subject to the availability of funding;
195.  It restructures the language describing the funding arrangements to make clear that 
20the council is responsible for all costs other than reimbursement of Legislators and of 
21council members who are not able to secure reimbursement through their employers; and
226.  It changes the plan report date to November 1, 2021.
