COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 689, L.D. 934, "Resolve, To Review 
the Implementation of the Maine Background Check Center Act" 
Amend the resolve in section 1 by striking out all of subsections 3 and 4 and inserting 
the following: 
13. Ways to allow for the portability of a background check so that it can travel with 
an employee from one job to another within a 5-year time frame; 
4. The necessity of subjecting to the background check system current employees 
who had been subject to a background check within the previous 5 years; and 
5. Any barriers to implementing all required background checks under one 
background check system.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the unanimous report of the committee, adds a directive to 
the study group to consider any barriers to implementing all required background checks 
under one background check system.
