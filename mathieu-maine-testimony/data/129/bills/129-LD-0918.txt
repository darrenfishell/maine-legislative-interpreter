11§316.  Utility line extensions to developments; cost sharing
12The commission by rule shall establish requirements for transmission and distribution 
13utilities to share the cost of utility line extensions to developments in accordance with this 
14section.
151.  Definition.  For the purposes of this section, "development" means property 
16owned by a person, entity or association for the purpose of creating a subdivision as 
17defined in Title 30-A, section 4401, subsection 4.
182.  Cost sharing required.  Rules adopted pursuant to this section must require a 
19transmission and distribution utility to pay the cost of a portion of a line extension to a 
20development as follows:
21A.  For a utility-constructed line extension, the utility shall provide a predetermined 
22portion of the line extension free of charge to the owner of the development; and
23B.  For a privately constructed line extension, the utility shall provide a credit or 
24reimbursement to the owner of the development upon transfer of ownership of the 
25line extension to the utility, in an amount equivalent to the amount that would have 
26been provided free of charge under paragraph A.
27Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, 
28chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
29SUMMARY
30This bill proposes to require transmission and distribution utilities to share the cost of 
31utility line extensions to subdivision developments.  The bill directs the Public Utilities 
32Commission, by rule, to require transmission and distribution utilities to cover the cost of 
33a portion of a line extension either by providing a portion of the line extension free of 
34charge, in the case of utility construction of the line extension, or providing a credit or 
35reimbursement upon transfer of ownership of the line extension to the utility, in the case 
36of a privately constructed line extension.
