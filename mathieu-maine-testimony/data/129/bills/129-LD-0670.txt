3amended to read:
41.  Minimum wage.  The minimum hourly wage is $7.50 per hour.  Starting January 
51, 2017, the minimum hourly wage is $9.00 per hour; starting January 1, 2018, the 
6minimum hourly wage is $10.00 per hour; starting January 1, 2019, the minimum hourly 
7wage is $11.00 per hour; and starting January 1, 2020, the minimum hourly wage is 
8$12.00 per hour.  On January 1, 2021 and each January 1st thereafter, the minimum 
9hourly wage then in effect must be increased by the increase, if any, in the cost of living 
10except that for an employee whose employer employs 50 or more full-time employees, 
11the minimum hourly wage is $13.00 starting January 1, 2021, the minimum hourly wage 
12is $14.00 starting January 1, 2022 and the minimum hourly wage is $15.00 starting 
13January 1, 2023.  On January 1, 2024 and each January 1st thereafter, for an employee 
14whose employer employs 50 or more full-time employees, the minimum hourly wage 
15then in effect must be increased by the increase, if any, in the cost of living. The increase 
16in the cost of living must be measured by the percentage increase, if any, as of August of 
17the previous year over the level as of August of the year preceding that year in the 
18Consumer Price Index for Urban Wage Earners and Clerical Workers, CPI-W, for the 
19Northeast Region, or its successor index, as published by the United States Department of 
20Labor, Bureau of Labor Statistics or its successor agency, with the amount of the 
21minimum wage increase rounded to the nearest multiple of 5¢.  If the highest federal 
22minimum wage is increased in excess of the minimum wage in effect under this section, 
23the minimum wage under this section is increased to the same amount, effective on the 
24same date as the increase in the federal minimum wage, and must be increased in 
25accordance with this section thereafter.
26SUMMARY
27This bill increases the minimum wage of the employees of employers employing 50 
28or more full-time employees to $13.00 on January 1, 2021, to $14.00 on January 1, 2022 
29and to $15.00 on January 1, 2023.
