4REIMBURSEMENT FOR RECOVERED HEATING FUEL
5ยง1500-O.  Reimbursement for recovered heating fuel
61.  Definitions.  As used in this chapter, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
7following terms have the following meanings.
8A.  "Dealer" means an entity engaged in the sale and delivery of heating fuel.
9B.  "Heating fuel" means home heating oil, kerosene or liquefied petroleum gas.
102.  Reimbursement required.  A dealer shall reimburse a customer for any heating 
11fuel recovered from the customer upon the closing of the customer's account not later 
12than 30 days after the fuel is recovered.  The rate of reimbursement is the lesser of the 
13price per gallon paid by the customer for the recovered fuel and the retail price per gallon 
14at the time the fuel is recovered.
153.  Unfair trade practice.  A dealer that fails to comply with any provision of this 
16section commits an unfair and deceptive act that is a violation of the Maine Unfair Trade 
17Practices Act.
18SUMMARY
19This bill requires dealers of heating fuel to reimburse customers within 30 days for 
20any fuel recovered following the closing of their accounts and makes failure to do so a 
21violation of the Maine Unfair Trade Practices Act.
