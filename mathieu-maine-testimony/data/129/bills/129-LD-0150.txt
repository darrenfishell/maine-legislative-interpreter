33SUMMARY
34This bill provides that unless a person 5 years of age or older and under 7 years of 
35age who is enrolled in a public day school withdraws from the school, the person is 
36required to attend the school during the time it is in session.  It provides that students who 
37are at least 5 years of age and have not completed grade 6 and are enrolled in a public day 
38school are subject to the same provisions regarding truancy as students who are at least 7 
39years of age and have not completed grade 6 who are required to attend a public day 
40school.  It provides that a person 5 years of age or older and under 7 years of age is not 
Page 4 - 129LR0918(01)-11required to meet the alternatives to attendance requirements set forth in the Maine 
2Revised Statutes, Title 20-A, section 5001-A, subsection 3.
