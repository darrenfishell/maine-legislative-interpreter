Page 2 - 129LR1243(01)-11SUMMARY
2This bill changes the distance requirements under the adult use marijuana provisions 
3from 1,000 feet to 300 feet for a marijuana establishment to a preexisting public or 
4private school and the method by which the distance is measured to make the adult use 
5marijuana distance requirements consistent with liquor law requirements.
