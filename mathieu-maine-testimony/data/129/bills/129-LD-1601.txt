39SUMMARY
40This bill amends the laws governing the Educators for Maine Program by expanding 
41certain definitions to include home-schooled students and educator service at publicly 
42supported secondary schools and special education facilities; adding service in 
Page 3 - 129LR1069(01)-11underserved geographic areas as an eligibility criterion; adding a loan repayment 
2provision to the program; removing the provision whereby the Governor announces 
3selected recipients annually; authorizing the Finance Authority of Maine to increase 
4maximum loan amounts based on available funding; establishing limits on the duration a 
5recipient may benefit under the program; removing the preference for loans to 
6undergraduate students enrolled in a course of study related to underserved subject areas; 
7and increasing from 5 to 10 years the amount of time a recipient has to complete the 
8return service requirement.
