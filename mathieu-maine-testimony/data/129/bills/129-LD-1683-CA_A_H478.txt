SUMMARY 
This amendment amends the definition of "consumer-owned transmission and 
distribution utility" in the bill. The amendment specifies that a consumer-owned 
transmission and distribution utility is wholly owned by its consumers, including its 
consumers served by the utility in this State. The amendment also adds a provision to 
amend the definition of "consumer-owned water utility" in the same way and makes other 
clarifying changes to the language in the bill.
