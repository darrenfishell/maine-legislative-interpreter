7Sec. 1.  Department of Administrative and Financial Services to conduct 
8an inventory of surplus state-owned land and structures.  Resolved:  That the 
9Department of Administrative and Financial Services shall conduct an inventory of 
10surplus state-owned land and structures, pursuant to the authority and process established 
11in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 1742, subsection 23.  The inventory must 
12include all surplus vacant land, unimproved structures and improved structures.  The 
13department shall consult with the Maine State Housing Authority in determining the 
14suitability of land and structures for use in the development of affordable housing, 
15consistent with Title 30-A, section 4754, subsection 3.  The department shall report on 
16the outcomes of the inventory and make recommendations regarding affordable housing 
17to the Joint Standing Committee on Labor and Housing by February 1, 2020.
18SUMMARY
19This resolve directs the Department of Administrative and Financial Services to 
20conduct an inventory of surplus state-owned land and structures pursuant to the authority 
21and process established in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 1742, subsection 
2223 and to work with the Maine State Housing Authority to determine suitability of 
23identified surplus land and structures for development of affordable housing.  It directs 
24the department to report to the Joint Standing Committee on Labor and Housing 
25regarding the inventory and to make recommendations regarding affordable housing by 
26February 1, 2020.
