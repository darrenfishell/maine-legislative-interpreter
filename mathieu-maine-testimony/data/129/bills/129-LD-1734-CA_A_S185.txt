Fiscal Note 
Minor cost increase -General Fund 
Minor revenue increase -General Fund 
This bill creates a postsecondary educational institution program license which would allow postsecondary 
educational institutions to purchase and permit sampling of liquor in conjunction with an educational curriculum. 
Any additional costs to the Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lotte1y Operations related to the license are 
anticipated to be minor and can be absorbed within existing budgeted resources. Any additional revenue generated 
from the license is not expected to be significant.
