21SUMMARY
22This bill allows the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of 
23Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations to license up to 11 agency liquor stores in a 
24municipality with a population over 60,000.  Under current law, the maximum number of 
25agency liquor stores that may be licensed in a municipality with a population over 45,000 
26is 10.
