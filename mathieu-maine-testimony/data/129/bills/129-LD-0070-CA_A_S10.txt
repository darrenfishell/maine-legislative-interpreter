The bill provides for an income tax deduction for employers participating in approved apprenticeship programs and 
would result in a loss of General Fund revenue of$1,187,500 and a loss of Other Special Revenue Funds revenue of 
$62,500 in fiscal year 2020-21. The bill includes a .one-time General Fund appropriation to the Department of 
Administrative and Financial Services of $33,000 'in fiscal year 2020-21 for computer programming costs. 
This bill also includes General Fund appropriations of$140,590 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $186,356 in fiscal year 
2020-21 to the Employment Services Activity program within the Department of Labor for one Labor Program 
Specialist position and one Office Specialist I position and related All Other costs needed due to an expected increase 
in workload as a result of the tax credit for employers who employ an apprentice participating in an approved 
apprenticeship program. This assumes an effective date of October 1, 2019.
