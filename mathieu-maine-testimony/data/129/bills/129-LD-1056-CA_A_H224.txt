Fiscal Note 
Increased loan liability -Finance Authority of Maine 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This legislation allows the Finance Authority of Maine (FAME) to increase its maximum loan insurance amount 
through its Commercial Loan Insurance Program from $7.0 million to $7.5 million per entity. FAME has indicated 
that there are sufficient funds in its loan insurance reserve fund to support the increase.
