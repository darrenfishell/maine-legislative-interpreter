11Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the 
12following:
13'Sec. 1.  1 MRSA §408-A, sub-§8, ¶A, as enacted by PL 2011, c. 662, §5, is 
14amended to read:
15A.  The agency or official may charge a reasonable fee to cover the cost of copying.  
16A reasonable fee to cover the cost of copying is no more than 10¢ per page for a 
17standard 8 1/2 inch by 11 inch black-and-white copy of a record.  A per page copy 
18fee may not be charged for records provided electronically.' 
19SUMMARY
20This amendment replaces the bill.  It amends the Freedom of Access Act to cap the 
21fee to cover the cost of copying a public record at no more than 10¢ per page for a 
22standard 8 1/2 inch by 11 inch black-and-white copy of a record and clarifies that a per 
23page copy fee may not be charged for records provided electronically.
