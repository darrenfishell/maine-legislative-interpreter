COMMITTEE AMENDMENT1SUMMARY
2This amendment is the majority report of the committee.  The amendment removes 
3from the bill the provision that changes the law regarding service of a portion of the term 
4of imprisonment as a requirement for supervised community confinements and shortens 
5the remaining period of imprisonment to 24 months.
