This amendment removes the annual appropriations of $1,500,000 and instead provides a one-time appropriation 
consisting of $1,000,000 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $1,000,000 in fiscal year 2020-21 to the Department of 
Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to contract with a nonprofit organization that provides statewide hunger relief 
services. 
This amendment also adds one-time transfers of $1,000,000 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $1,000,000 in fiscal year 2020-
21 to the unappropriated surplus of the General Fund from the Medical Use of Marijuana Fund.
