10SUMMARY
11This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rule 
12Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 45.03 to provide 
13reimbursement for patients discharged from Southern Maine Health Care's psychiatric 
14inpatient unit in the amount of $13,396.47 per distinct discharge; the amended rule must 
15be submitted to the United States Department of Health and Human Services, Centers for 
16Medicare and Medicaid Services in time for the increased rate of reimbursement to apply 
17beginning July 1, 2020.
