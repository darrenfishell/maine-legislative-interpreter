31SUMMARY
32This bill requires school administrative units to test schools and other buildings for 
33radon every 5 years.  The radon testing must comply with the United States 
34Environmental Protection Agency's recommended testing standards for schools.  A 
35school administrative unit must notify parents, faculty and staff of the test results and 
36must report test results to the Department of Education and the Department of Health and 
37Human Services.  The Department of Health and Human Services must report these 
38results every 5 years to the Governor and the Legislature.
