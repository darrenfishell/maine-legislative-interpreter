COMMITTEE AMENDMENT .. IJ,, to H.P. 243, L.D. 318, Bill, "An Act To 
Prepare All Students for Work and Life by Requiring That Students Receive Instruction 
in Vocational Preparation and Practical Life Skills" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Prepare All Students for Work and Life by Requiring That Students 
Receive Instruction in Career and Technical Education and Practical Life Skills' 
Amend the bill by stdking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the 
following: 
'Sec. 1. 20-A MRSA ยง4721, sub-ยง1, as enacted by PL 2009, c. 313, ยง13, is 
amended to read: 
1. Comprehensive program of instruction. A secondary school shall provide a 
comprehensive program of instruction of at least 2 years in length, which must meet the 
requirements of this chapter and the parameters for essential instruction and graduation 
requirements established under section 6209. The program must include instruction for 
all students in career and education development, English language arts, health education 
and physical education, mathematics, science and technology, social studies, visual and 
performing arts and world languages. A secondary school shall provide one semester of 
instruction in career and technical education and practical life skills, whenever possible, 
as determined by the local school board. For purposes of this section, 11career and 
technical education and practical life skills" means expedential instruction of students, 
regardless of career choice or pathway, that develops their understanding of interests, 
aptitudes and options related to work and study; develops core workplace skills in areas 
such as planning, communication, problem solving, teamwork and computer applications: 
and includes practical workplace and home economics expedences that maximize 
learning through hands-on application.' 
Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively.
