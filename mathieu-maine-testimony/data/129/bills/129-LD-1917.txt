27SUMMARY
28This bill eliminates retail-level competition for residential electricity customers and 
29requires a more robust competitive process for selecting standard-offer service providers 
30for residential and small commercial electricity customers.  It also requires the Public 
31Utilities Commission to designate or hire an employee to assist the commission in 
32administering standard-offer service.
