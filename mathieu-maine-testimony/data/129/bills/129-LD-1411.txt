5SUMMARY
6This bill amends provisions related to the federal Workforce Innovation and 
7Opportunity Act.  It makes appointment of members to the State Workforce Board 
8subject to confirmation by the Legislature.  It requires the State Workforce Board to 
9submit the state workforce development plan to the joint standing committee of the 
10Legislature having jurisdiction over labor matters prior to the plan being submitted to the 
11Federal Government.  It directs the Treasurer of State to disburse federal funds received 
12pursuant to the federal Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act and state funds made 
13available to be used to implement that Act if the Governor does not authorize 
14disbursement of the funds within 30 days after the funds are received or made available.
