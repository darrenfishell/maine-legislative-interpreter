9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "fr" to S.P. 296, L.D. 1017, Bill, "An Act 
10 Regarding the Elimination of Highly Toxic and Hazardous Chemicals in the Workplace" 
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
12 'Resolve, To Direct the Department of Labor To Develop a Framework for 
13 Encouraging Employers To Identify Safer Alternatives to Hazardous Chemicals' 
14 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and inserting the following: 
15 'Sec. 1. Development of framework for safer alternatives to hazardous 
16 chemicals. Resolved: That the Department of Labor shall, in collaboration with 
17 interested parties and employers and employees in industries that are likely to utilize 
18 hazardous chemicals, develop a framework for identifying hazardous chemicals used in 
19 the workplace and identify safer alternatives to those hazardous chemicals. The 
20 department shall submit a report to the Joint Standing Committee on Labor and Housing 
21 by December 20, 2019 that includes a proposed framework, a list of participants who 
22 participated in developing the proposed framework and a summary of the process used to 
23 develop the framework. The committee may report out a bill to the Second Regular 
24 Session of the 129th Legislature based on the report.' 
25 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
26 section number to read consecutively. 
27 SUMMARY 
28 This amendment replaces the bill with a resolve and replaces the title. It requires the 
29 Department of Labor to develop a framework for identifying hazardous chemicals used in 
30 the workplace and identifying safer alternatives to those chemicals in collaboration with 
31 interested parties and employers and employees in industries that are likely to utilize 
32 hazardous chemicals. The amendment requires the department to submit a report to the 
33 Joint Standing Committee on Labor and Housing by December 20, 2019 that includes a 
34 proposed framework, summarizes the process used to develop the framework and
