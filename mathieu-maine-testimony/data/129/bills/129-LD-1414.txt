18SUMMARY
19This bill replaces the existing penalty provision of the Freedom of Access Act to 
20establish a tiered schedule of fines for the civil violation of willfully violating the 
21Freedom of Access Act.
22A state government agency or local government entity is subject to a fine of up to 
23$500 for a first violation, a fine of up to $1,000 for a 2nd violation and a fine of up to 
24$2,000 for 3rd and subsequent violations committed within 4 years of an adjudication for 
25a willful violation.  For the purposes of determining previous adjudications, a willful 
26violation can be committed by any employee of the agency or entity.
