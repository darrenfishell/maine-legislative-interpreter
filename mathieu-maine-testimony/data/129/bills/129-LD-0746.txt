17SUMMARY
18This bill provides local control to municipalities over the duration of municipal 
19development districts that are funded by assessments and are not tax increment financing 
20districts.  Municipal development districts that are tax increment financing districts 
21continue to be subject to the 30-year duration limitation under current law.
