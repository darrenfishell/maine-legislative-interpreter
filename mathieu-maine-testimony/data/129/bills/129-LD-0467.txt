29SUMMARY
30This bill amends the state retirement system laws governing creditable service for 
31service in the United States Armed Forces to provide credit for service during operations 
32in Lebanon, August 21, 1982 to February 26, 1984; operations in Grenada, October 25, 
331983 to December 15, 1983; and operations in Panama, December 21, 1989 to February 
3413, 1990.
