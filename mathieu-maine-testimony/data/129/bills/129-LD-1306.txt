7§6555.  Safe School Climate Council
81.  Council established.  The Safe School Climate Council, as established in Title 5, 
9section 12004-I, subsection 18-F and referred to in this section as "the council," shall 
10develop model policies to address bullying, harassment and overall safety in schools.
112.  Membership.  The council consists of up to 20 members appointed as follows:
12A.  A representative of the department, appointed by the commissioner;
13B.  A representative of the civil rights team project in the Office of the Attorney 
14General, appointed by the Attorney General;
15C.  A representative of the Maine Principals' Association or successor organization, 
16appointed by the Governor;
17D.  A representative of the Maine School Management Association or successor 
18organization, appointed by the Governor;
19E.  A representative of the Maine Education Association, appointed by the Governor;
20F.  A representative of an organization that advocates for lesbian, gay, bisexual and 
21transgender students, appointed by the Governor;
22G.  One or more members with expertise in bullying prevention, appointed by the 
23Governor; and
24H.  Representatives of underrepresented groups, including minority communities and 
25immigrant communities, appointed by the Governor.
263.  Terms; vacancy.  The members of the council appointed pursuant to subsection 2 
27serve for 2-year terms and serve until their successors are appointed and qualified.  On 
28the expiration of a term of a member under subsection 2, a successor must be appointed 
29to a 2-year term.  A member of the council is eligible for reappointment to the council.  A 
30vacancy in the council does not impair the right of a quorum of the members to exercise 
31all the rights and perform all the duties of the council.  In the event of a vacancy 
32occurring in the membership, the appointing authority shall appoint a replacement 
33member for the remainder of the unexpired term in the same manner as the original 
34appointment was made.
354.  Duties.  The council is authorized to meet twice a year.  At the first meeting of the 
36council and annually thereafter, the members shall elect from the membership a chair and Education Safe School Climate

15SUMMARY
16This bill establishes the Safe School Climate Council, which is authorized to meet 
17twice a year and to present to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 
18jurisdiction over education matters every 2 years recommendations on model policies to 
19address bullying, harassment and overall safety in schools.
