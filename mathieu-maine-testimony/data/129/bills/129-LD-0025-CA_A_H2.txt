1 SUMMARY 
2 This amendment makes several changes to the requirement that the Director of the 
3 Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations within the Department of 
4 Administrative and Financial Services certify financial information related to 
5 administration of the State's spirits business and the bureau's oversight of the 
6 manufacture, distribution and sale of iiquor in the State monthly. The amendment 
7 requires that the director make those monthly certifications to the State Liquor and 
8 Lottery Commission in addition to the Commissioner of Administrative and Financial 
9 Services, as is current practice. The amendment also repeals a duplicative reporting 
10 requirement.
