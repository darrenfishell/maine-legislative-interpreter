19which is sugar or corn syrup.

20SUMMARY
21This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services, Maine Center for 
22Disease Control and Prevention to develop a comprehensive state plan relating to the 
23reduction of unhealthy weight and obesity.  The plan must include coordination of 
24activities within the Department of Health and Human Services and among state 
25departments, efforts to monitor rates of unhealthy weight and obesity, including a survey 
26of food and drink consumption and physical exercise, and a media plan to reduce the 
27consumption of sugary drinks.  The department is required to submit the plan to the joint 
28standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human services 
29matters no later than January 1, 2020 and every 2 years thereafter.  The bill also includes 
30a position within the Maine Center for Disease Control and Prevention, or contracted for 
31by the department, as a coordinator of the state plan.  The bill also appropriates funds to 
32develop and implement the plan, to improve surveillance and epidemiology related to 
33obesity and to fund one Obesity Care Coordinator position in the Maine Center for 
34Disease Control and Prevention.GENERAL FUND 2019-20 2020-21
