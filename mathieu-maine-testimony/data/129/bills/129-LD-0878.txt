18SUMMARY
19This bill changes the date on which a candidate who is not enrolled in a party must 
20file to participate in the Maine Clean Election Act from April 20th to June 1st for a 
21candidate for the Legislature and from April 1st to June 1st for a candidate for Governor.  
22Current law provides that a nomination petition must be filed in the office of the 
23Secretary of State by June 1st.
