4SUMMARY
5This bill reclassifies the Public Utilities Commission's director of emergency services 
6communication to be consistent with other commission directors.  The bill also adds 4 
7positions to the commission: a Utility Analyst to perform utility rate case revenue 
8requirement analysis and other activities; a Utility Analyst to perform communications 
9and customer outreach activities; a Senior Consumer Assistance Specialist to perform 
10administrative work in the receipt, analysis and resolution of consumer complaints; and a 
11Public Service Coordinator II to perform 9-1-1 standards compliance, contract 
12management and other tasks related to the administration of the Emergency Services 
13Communication Bureau.OTHER SPECIAL REVENUE FUNDS $0 $501,998
