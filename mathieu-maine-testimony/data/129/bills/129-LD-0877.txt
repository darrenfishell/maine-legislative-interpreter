25SUMMARY
26Current law distributes 0.9045% of total gasoline tax revenues to programs related to 
27snowmobiles and 0.1525% to programs related to all-terrain vehicles.  This bill amends 
28the law to provide that 0.5285% of gasoline tax revenues funds programs related to 
29snowmobiles and 0.5285% funds programs related to all-terrain vehicles.
