11 Amend the resolve by striking out everything after the title and before the summary 
12 and inserting the following:
13 'Sec. 1.  Department of Health and Human Services to review nonmedical 
14transportation programs; pilot project.  Resolved:  That the Department of Health 
15 and Human Services shall review nonmedical transportation programs in other states that 
16 provide transportation services under Medicaid to older adults with disabilities and other 
17 adults with disabilities, brain injury or other related conditions and determine options for 
18 providing these services in this State.  The department shall review the New York Medicaid 
19 program that provides up to $2,000 annually per individual for nonmedical transportation 
20 costs for services in an individual's plan of care as well as any other similar or relevant 
21 methods of financing such services.  The department shall assess options for modifying or 
22 amending home and community-based waiver programs, in particular the waiver programs 
23 provided under Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Section 19, to provide 
24 nonmedical transportation services to recipients of current services.  The department shall 
25 meet with relevant stakeholders as necessary to assess programs in other states and develop 
26 programs for use in this State, including pilot projects.  The department shall submit a 
27 report with its findings, including recommendations for at least one potential pilot project, 
28 to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over health and 
29 human services matters no later than February 28, 2021.  The joint standing committee of 
30 the Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human services matters may report out 
31 legislation based on the report to the First Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.'
32 Amend the resolve by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
33 section number to read consecutively.
