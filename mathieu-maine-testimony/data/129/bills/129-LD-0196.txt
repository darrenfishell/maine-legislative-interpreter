14Sec. 1.  Adoption.  Resolved:  That final adoption of portions of Chapter 320: 
15Electric Transmission and Distribution Utility Service Standards, a provisionally adopted 
16major substantive rule of the Public Utilities Commission that has been submitted to the 
17Legislature for review pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, 
18subchapter 2-A, is authorized.
19Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
20legislation takes effect when approved.
21SUMMARY
22This resolve provides for legislative review of portions of Chapter 320: Electric 
23Transmission and Distribution Utility Service Standards, a major substantive rule of the 
24Public Utilities Commission.
