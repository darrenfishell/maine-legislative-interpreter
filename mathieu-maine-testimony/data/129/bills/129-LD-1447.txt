31SUMMARY
32This bill suspends the use of ranked-choice voting until elections held after December 
331, 2023.  The bill provides that the laws governing ranked-choice voting are repealed 
34December 1, 2023 unless the Constitution of Maine is amended to authorize the 
35Legislature to determine the method by which the Governor and members of the 
36Legislature are elected.
