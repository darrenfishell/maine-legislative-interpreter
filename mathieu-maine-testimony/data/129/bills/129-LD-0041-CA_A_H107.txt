26 SUMMARY 
27 This amendment does the following. 
28 1. It changes the end date of eligibility to elect net energy billing from December 31, 
29 2019 to December 31, 2020. 
30 2. It allows net energy billing arrangements entered into prior to December 31, 2020 
31 to remain in effect until December 31, 2035. 
32 3. It limits to 100, rather than 50 in the bill, the number of eligible customers that 
33 may participate in a single shared interest in an eligible facility or the number of meters 
34 associated with a single shared interest under net energy billing and under the market-
3 5 based crediting system. 
36 4. It clarifies the exceptions in the bill for northern Maine to specify that the 
37 exception applies to the portion of a transmission and distribution utility's service territory 
3 8 that is in an area administered by the independent system administrator for northern 
39 Maine.
