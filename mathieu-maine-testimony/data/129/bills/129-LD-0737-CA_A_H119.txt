1 SUMMARY 
2 This amendment adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause and authorizes 
3 agency liquor stores and off-premises retail licensees to conduct up to 15 taste-testing 
4 events per month of spirits, wine and malt liquor. The amendment allows, but does not 
5 require, agency liquor stores and off-premises retail licensees to submit a single request to 
6 the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages 
7 and Lottery Operations listing all of the taste-testing events the agency liquor store or 
8 off-premises retail licensee plans to conduct in a single calendar month. The amendment 
9 also allows agency liquor stores and off-premises retail licensees to conduct taste testing 
10 of spirits, wine and malt liquor at the same taste-testing event, as long as the agency 
11 liquor store or off-premises retail licensee is licensed to sell the types of liquor being 
12 offered at the event.
