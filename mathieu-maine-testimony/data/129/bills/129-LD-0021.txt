4LIMITATIONS ON USE OF ELECTROCONVULSIVE THERAPY
5ยง1971.  Limitations on use of electroconvulsive therapy
6A health care provider may not use electroconvulsive therapy on a child, a person 65 
7years of age or older or a person who is pregnant.  For the purposes of this subchapter, 
8"health care provider" means a licensed mental health services provider, a physician 
9licensed under Title 32, chapter 36 or 48 and operating in this State or a corporation or 
10business entity engaged primarily in the provision of physician health care services.
11SUMMARY
12This bill prohibits the use of electroconvulsive therapy on a child under 18 years of 
13age or a person over 65 years of age or a person who is pregnant.
