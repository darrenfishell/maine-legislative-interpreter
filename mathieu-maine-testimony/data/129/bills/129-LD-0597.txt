Page 1 - 129LR0175(01)-11Sec. 1.  Pilot project.  Resolved:  Notwithstanding the Maine Revised Statutes, 
2Title 29-A, section 2117, the Department of Transportation shall establish a pilot project 
3allowing the use of traffic surveillance cameras to prove or enforce a violation of Title 
429-A on Castine Road in the Town of Orland. The Department of Transportation shall by 
5January 15, 2021 submit a report evaluating the results of the pilot project to the joint 
6standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over transportation matters.
7SUMMARY
8This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to establish a pilot project 
9allowing the use of traffic surveillance cameras to prove or enforce a violation of the 
10Maine Revised Statutes, Title 29-A on Castine Road in Orland and requires the 
11department to submit a report evaluating the results of the pilot project to the joint 
12standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over transportation matters by 
13January 15, 2021.
