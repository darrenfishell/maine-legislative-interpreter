20SUMMARY
21This bill establishes cost-of-living adjustments for workers' compensation benefits.  
22Prior to the 1992 revision of the laws governing workers' compensation, benefits for total 
23incapacity were adjusted annually based on the percentage increase or decrease in the 
24state average weekly wage.
