22SUMMARY
23This bill amends the definition of "law enforcement officer" in the laws governing the 
24Maine Criminal Justice Academy to establish that the Commissioner of Public Safety is a 
25law enforcement officer in the employ of the Department of Public Safety if the 
26commissioner is certified as a law enforcement officer and completes in-service training 
27in order to maintain that certification.  The bill also exempts the commissioner from 
28certain training standards and policy development requirements.
