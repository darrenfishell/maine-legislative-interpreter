3-A. License. The license fees for tournament game licenses are as follows:

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill would create a new tier of licenses for games of chance tournaments with fewer than 50 players with license 
fees of $40 per tournament license, $100 for a monthly license and $7 50 for an annual license while retaining the 
current license fees for games of chance tournaments with 51 or more players. The reduction in license fees would 
reduce license revenue by $11,200 in fiscal year 2019-20 and by $12,800 in fiscal year 2020-21.
