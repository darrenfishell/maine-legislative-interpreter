17 L. Temporary signs placed within the public right-of-way for a maximum of 12 
18 weeks per calendar year, except that a temporary sign may not be placed within the 
19 public right-of-way for more than 6 weeks from January 1st to June 30th or for more 
20 than 6 weeks from July 1st to December 31st. A temporary sign may not be placed 
21 within 30 feet of another temporary sign bearing the same or substantially the same 
22 message. A temporary sign may not exceed 4 feet by 8 feet in size. A sign under this 
23 paragraph must include or be marked with the name and address of the individual, 
24 entity or organization that placed the sign within the public right-of-way and the date 
25 the sign was erected within the public right-of-way.' 
26 SUMMARY 
27 This amendment prohibits a temporary sign from being placed within the public 
28 right-of-way for more than 6 weeks from January 1st to June 30th or for more than 6 
29 weeks from July 1st to December 31st.
