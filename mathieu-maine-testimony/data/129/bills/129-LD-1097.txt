25SUMMARY
26This bill creates a rebuttable presumption that a forcible entry and detainer action was 
27commenced in retaliation against a tenant if the tenant had made a complaint of an act of 
28sexual harassment by the landlord or landlord's agent.  The bill defines "sexual 
29harassment."
