5SUMMARY
6This bill requires that, in order for a candidate for President or a candidate for Vice 
7President to appear on a general election ballot, the candidate for President or the 
8candidate for Vice President must disclose the previous 3 years of that candidate's federal 
9income tax returns, which may be redacted to remove personally identifying information 
10other than the candidate's name.  It directs the Secretary of State to post the tax returns on 
11the Secretary of State's website.  The legislation takes effect upon enactment of this 
12requirement in other states.
