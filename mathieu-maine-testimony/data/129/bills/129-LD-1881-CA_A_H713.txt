Future biennium revenue increase -Other Special Revenue Funds 
Minor cost increase -Other Special Revenue Funds 
This bill requires the Public Utilities Co1mnission (PUC) and the Office of the Public Advocate (OPA) to apportion 
the existing assessments collected from public utilities between investor-owned utilities and consumer-owned 
utilities. It also requires the PUC to report on any filing fees or penalties collected during the previous year that have 
not been adjusted in the previous five years and to recommend adjustments based on the consumer price index. It also 
requires the PUC and OP A to include information regarding their activities devoted to investor-owned utilities and 
consumer-owned utilities in aimual reports. Finally, the bill authorizes the PUC to increase filing fees for 
reorganizations of utilities from $50,000 to an amount not to exceed 5/100 of 1 percent of the estimated total value of 
the reorganization. 
Rec01mnendations to change the filing fees or penalties based on the consumer price index and changes to the filing 
fees for reorganizations of utilities will increase Other Special Revenue Funds revenue to the PUC. No estimate of the 
amount is made at this time. Any additional costs to the PUC and OP A from the activities required in this bill are 
anticipated to be minor and can be absorbed within existing budgeted resources.
