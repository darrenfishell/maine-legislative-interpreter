29SUMMARY
30This bill lowers from 70 years of age to 65 years of age the age of eligibility for 
31municipal property tax deferral programs for homesteads of low-income seniors and 
32requires the State to reimburse municipalities for the taxes deferred.  The State acquires a 
33lien on the property for the amount of taxes deferred plus interest.GENERAL FUND 2019-20 2020-21
