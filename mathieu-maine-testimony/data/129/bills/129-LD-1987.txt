2to amend Maine Motor Carrier Safety Regulation.  Resolved:  That, pursuant to 
3the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 29-A, section 555, the Department of Public Safety, 
4Bureau of State Police shall adopt major substantive rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 
5375, subchapter 2-A to amend rule Chapter 4, Maine Motor Carrier Safety Regulation, by 
6amending the rule adopting by reference 49 Code of Federal Regulations, Part 395 to 
7exempt intrastate motor carriers, vehicles and drivers delivering live lobsters from hours-
8of-service restrictions requiring a 30-minute rest break after 8 hours of driving.
9SUMMARY
10This resolve directs the Department of Public Safety, Bureau of State Police to adopt 
11major substantive rules amending its rules governing motor carrier safety to exempt 
12intrastate motor carriers, vehicles and drivers delivering live lobsters from hours-of-
13service restrictions requiring a 30-minute rest break after 8 hours of driving.
