11Sec. 2.  23 MRSA §4404, as enacted by PL 1981, c. 456, Pt. A, §88, is amended to 
12read:
13§4404.  Tolls
14The Department of Transportation shall, in a manner consistent with the Maine 
15Administrative Procedure Act, Title 5, chapter 375, establish adopt rules establishing tolls 
16for the use of such the ferry line route or lines routes under this subchapter by vehicles, 
17freight and passengers.  Tolls must be calculated based upon the cost of service for 
18vehicles, freight and passengers on each route of travel.  In order to ensure affordability 
19for island residents and the viability of the island community, the department may 
20provide discounted tolls to island residents and other frequent users of the service based 
21upon their daily travel needs.  Tolls collected from the operation of the Maine State Ferry 
22Service shall must at all times be kept in a separate fund distinct from all other moneys 
23money of the State and shall must be used for the operation and debt retirement of the 
24ferry service.
25Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, 
26chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
27SUMMARY
28This bill requires the Department of Transportation, by rule, to establish ferry tolls for 
29each route of travel that are based upon the cost of service for vehicles, freight and 
30passengers, and allows the department to provide discounted tolls for frequent usage of 
31the Maine State Ferry Service. This bill also requires that the Marine Highway account 
32must fund 100% of the capital costs and no more than 75% of the operating costs of the 
33Maine State Ferry Service.
