25SUMMARY
26This bill allows municipal agents to charge higher service fees for registrations for 
27motor vehicles, from $3 to $5 for renewals and from $4 to $6 for new registrations.  It 
28also allows municipal agents to charge higher service fees for registrations for all-terrain 
29vehicles, snowmobiles and watercraft, from $1 to $3 for renewals and from $2 to $5 for 
30new registrations.
