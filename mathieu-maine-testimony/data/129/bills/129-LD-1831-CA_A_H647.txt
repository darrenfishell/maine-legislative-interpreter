SUMMARY 
This amendment removes from the bill sections 1, 10, 12, 13, 14, 15, 16, 17, 33, 35, 
36 and 38 because there is pending legislation that may affect the changes proposed in 
each of these sections. The amendment also adds 4 new sections. One new section 
corrects clerical errors. The 2nd new section corrects an incorrect cross-reference. The 
3rd new section removes a cross-reference to a repealed provision. The 4th new section 
corrects a clerical error.
