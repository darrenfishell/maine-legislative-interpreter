Page 6 - 129LR1666(01)-11SUMMARY
2This bill removes limitations on food supplement program benefits and Temporary 
3Assistance to Needy Families program benefits, provides MaineCare coverage and 
4modifies language for state-funded supplemental security income to maintain consistency 
5throughout the law for certain noncitizens who are lawfully present in the United States 
6or pursuing a lawful process to apply for immigration relief.
