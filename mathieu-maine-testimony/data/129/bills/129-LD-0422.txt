Page 1 - 129LR0829(01)-11Sec. 1.  Mt. Katahdin Trail signs.  Resolved:  That the Department of 
2Transportation shall develop a plan to increase the number of directional and 
3identification signs on the Mt. Katahdin Trail, as established by Private and Special Law 
41957, chapter 90.  In developing the plan under this section, the department shall consider 
5the placement of signs on the trail in the Town of Dexter, on the State Route 7 portion of 
6the trail and at the end of the trail at Baxter State Park.
7Sec. 2.  Report.  Resolved:  That, by January 1, 2020, the Department of 
8Transportation shall submit a report detailing the plan developed under section 1 to the 
9Joint Standing Committee on Transportation. 
10SUMMARY
11This resolve requires the Department of Transportation to develop a plan to increase 
12the number of directional and identification signs on the Mt. Katahdin Trail and to submit 
13the plan to the Joint Standing Committee on Transportation by January 1, 2020.
