3SUMMARY
4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to allow persons working in agriculture to organize for the 
6purposes of collectively bargaining for wages, hours, other working conditions and 
7benefits, without fear of reprisal.  The bill would exempt farms under a certain size and 
8with a limited number of employees.
