3ยง614.  Municipal option to exempt all personal property from taxation
4Notwithstanding the provisions of this subchapter or any other provision of law to the 
5contrary, by referendum vote conducted in accordance with the provisions of the 
6municipality's charter or Title 30-A, section 2528 or 2532, even if the municipality has 
7not accepted the provisions of Title 30-A, section 2528, a municipality may adopt an 
8ordinance to exempt personal property from municipal assessment and collection of tax.  
9The exemption must be uniform in application and include all personal property including 
10business equipment.  A referendum to remove the exemption on the assessment and 
11collection of tax on personal property must be conducted in the same manner as the 
12referendum exempting personal property from assessment and collection of tax and must 
13comply with the provisions of this chapter.
14SUMMARY
15This bill allows a municipality, by referendum, to exempt all personal property, 
16including business equipment, located in that municipality from assessment and 
17collection of tax by that municipality.
