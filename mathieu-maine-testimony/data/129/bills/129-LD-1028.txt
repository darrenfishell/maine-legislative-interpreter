32SUMMARY
33This bill increases the cigarette tax from $2.00 to $3.50 per pack of 20 cigarettes and 
34increases the tax on all other tobacco products including electronic cigarettes to 81% of 
35the wholesale sales price, beginning November 1, 2019.  The bill provides that, if the tax 
36on cigarettes is increased on or after November 1, 2019, the tax on all other tobacco 
37products will be adjusted by a rate that is equivalent to the percentage change in the tax 
38rate for one cigarette.  The bill provides ongoing funding, $11,100,000 per year, to the GENERAL FUND 2019-20 2020-21
