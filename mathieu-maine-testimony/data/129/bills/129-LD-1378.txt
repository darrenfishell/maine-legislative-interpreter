18SUMMARY
19This bill requires that, when a child is ordered into the custody of the Department of 
20Health and Human Services, the department must provide to the foster home in which the 
21child is placed an overview of the child's medical condition and the name and contact 
22information of the child's health care provider at the time of placement, if known. The bill 
23also requires the department to ensure that a child receives an appointment for a medical 
24examination within 3 working days of when the department's custody commences, 
25instead of within 10 days as in current law, and requires that the department inform the 
26foster parent of the appointment.
