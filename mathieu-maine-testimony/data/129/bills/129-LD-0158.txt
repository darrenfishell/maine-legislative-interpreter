20SUMMARY
21This bill removes the requirement that beano conducted by organizations such as 
22volunteer fire departments, agricultural fair associations and nonprofit, charitable, 
23educational, political, civic, recreational, fraternal, patriotic, religious or veterans 
24organizations be conducted by members of those organizations and instead requires that 
25beano conducted by these organizations be conducted in the presence of at least one 
26member of the organization.
