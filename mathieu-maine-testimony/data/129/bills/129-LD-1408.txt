6ยง3851.  Military insignia on the uniforms of law enforcement officers
7Notwithstanding any other provision of law, a law enforcement officer employed by 
8the State, a county or a municipality may wear insignia on the law enforcement officer's 
9uniform to indicate that the law enforcement officer is a veteran of the Armed Forces of 
10the United States.  The Department of Public Safety shall adopt rules to implement this 
11section.  Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules pursuant to 
12Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
13SUMMARY
14This bill allows a law enforcement officer employed by the State, a county or a 
15municipality to wear insignia on the law enforcement officer's uniform to indicate that the 
16law enforcement officer is a veteran of the Armed Forces of the United States.  It directs 
17the Department of Public Safety to adopt routine technical rules to implement the change 
18in law.
