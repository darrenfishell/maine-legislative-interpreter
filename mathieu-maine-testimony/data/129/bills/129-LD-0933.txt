Page 1 - 129LR0218(01)-11Sec. 1.  Authorization to sue the Town of Kittery.  Resolved:  That, 
2notwithstanding any other provision of law, those persons who claim to have suffered 
3damages arising from the Kittery Community Center transport van accident on Interstate 
495 in Greenland, New Hampshire on August 10, 2018, or their legal representatives, are 
5authorized to bring a civil action against the Town of Kittery, Maine.
6This action may be brought in the York County Superior Court within one year from 
7the effective date of this resolve.  The action may be heard by a Justice of the Superior 
8Court or by a jury.  The Maine Rules of Civil Procedure govern the conduct of the action.
9The municipal officers of the Town of Kittery shall pay any judgment from funds of 
10the town, including costs and interest, upon final process issued by the Superior Court or, 
11if applicable, the Supreme Judicial Court.
12Sec. 2.  Waiver of statutory limitations.  Resolved:  That limitations on 
13damages pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 14, section 8105, subsection 1 are 
14waived for the action described under section 1.
15SUMMARY
16This resolve authorizes persons who claim to have suffered damages arising from the 
17Kittery Community Center transport van accident on Interstate 95 in Greenland, New 
18Hampshire on August 10, 2018 to sue the Town of Kittery for damages resulting from the 
19accident and waives the statutory limitations on damage recovery.
