17SUMMARY
18This bill increases the total annual aggregate amount of credits that may be issued 
19under the Maine Seed Capital Tax Credit Program from $5,000,000 to $10,000,000, 
20decreases the total aggregate credits that may be authorized for any one business from 
21$5,000,000 to $3,500,000 and requires that eligible businesses that provide a product or 
22service that is sold or rendered predominantly outside the State maintain more than 50% 
23of their employees in positions within the State.
