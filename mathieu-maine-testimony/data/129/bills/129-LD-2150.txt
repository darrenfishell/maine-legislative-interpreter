20SUMMARY
21This bill establishes a moratorium until April 1, 2023 on the issuance of new 
22wholesale seafood licenses with lobster permits. Under this bill, the Commissioner of 
23Marine Resources may not issue a wholesale seafood license with a lobster permit to an 
24individual unless that individual landed at least 1,000 pounds of lobster under a wholesale 
25seafood license with a lobster permit held by that individual at some point during the 
26period of April 1, 2017 to March 31, 2020.
