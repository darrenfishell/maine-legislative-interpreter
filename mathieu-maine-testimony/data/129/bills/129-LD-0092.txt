30An individualized education plan may not be used to measure student growth for the

13SUMMARY
14This bill clarifies that the use of student learning and growth data to inform 
15instruction is included as part of the multiple measures of educator effectiveness.  The bill 
16also requires the Department of Education to submit a provisionally adopted rule to the 
17Legislature by January 10, 2020.
