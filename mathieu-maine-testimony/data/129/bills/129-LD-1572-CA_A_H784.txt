9SUMMARY
10This amendment replaces the bill with a resolve that directs the Maine State Housing 
11Authority in collaboration with stakeholders to examine fair chance housing policies 
12considered or adopted in other jurisdictions to evaluate options for the State to adopt that 
13would address housing issues and expand housing opportunities for formerly incarcerated 
14individuals without placing an excessive burden on housing providers.  It requires the 
15authority to submit a report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 
16jurisdiction over housing matters that includes findings and recommendations and 
17includes a summary of any meetings that occur regarding the examination, a list of 
18participants in the examination, suggestions to increase the awareness among housing 
19providers of the challenges faced by formerly incarcerated individuals in finding housing 
20and any other relevant information.  It allows the authority to include suggested 
21legislation in its report.  Lastly, it allows the joint standing committee of the Legislature 
22having jurisdiction over housing matters to report out legislation based on the report.
