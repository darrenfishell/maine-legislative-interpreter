7SUMMARY
8This bill amends the definition of "private passenger motor vehicle" in the laws 
9governing personal automobile insurance and rental vehicle coverage in the Maine 
10Insurance Code.  It clarifies that "private passenger motor vehicle" includes a sport utility 
11vehicle, a pickup truck and a van, which are commonly rented vehicles.
