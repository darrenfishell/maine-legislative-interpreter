14Sec. 4.  21-A MRSA c. 5, sub-c. 8 is enacted to read:
15SUBCHAPTER 8
16PRESIDENTIAL PRIMARY ELECTIONS
17§441.  Determination and date of primary; voter eligibility
181.  Determination of primary.  No later than November 1st of the year prior to a 
19presidential election year, the state committee of each party shall certify whether there is 
20a contest among candidates for nomination as the presidential candidate.  Upon receiving 
21the certification from one or more parties, the Secretary of State shall announce the 
22parties that will have a presidential primary election, which must be held on the first 
23Tuesday after the first Monday in March of the presidential election year. 
242.  Eligible voter.  Notwithstanding section 340, subsection 1, only a voter who is 
25enrolled in a party may vote in the party's presidential primary election.
26§442.  Petitions
27On or before November 1st of the year prior to a presidential election year, the 
28Secretary of State shall prepare and make available petitions for circulation by a person 
29desiring to be a candidate in the state presidential primary election of any party.  This 
30petition must be completed and filed no later than 5:00 p.m. on December 21st of the year 
31prior to a presidential election year in the manner provided in sections 335 and 336.
Page 2 - 129LR2289(01)-11§443.  Ballot preparation
2The Secretary of State shall prepare ballots for a presidential primary election. A 
3ballot must include the name of a person who files a petition with the Secretary of State 
4in accordance with section 432. The Secretary of State shall determine if a petition meets 
5the requirements of sections 335, 336 and 432, subject to challenge and appeal under 
6section 337.
7SUMMARY
8This bill implements a presidential primary election to be held on the first Tuesday 
9after the first Monday in March of the presidential election year, and provides a process 
10for the parties to participate if they certify to the Secretary of State by November 1st of 
11the year prior to the presidential election year that they have a contest among candidates 
12for nomination.
