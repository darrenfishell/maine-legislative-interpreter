5ยง264.  Help Me Grow program established
61.  Program established.  The Help Me Grow program, referred to in this chapter as 
7"the program," is established in the department as a comprehensive, statewide, 
8coordinated system of early identification, referral and follow-up for children from 
9prenatal care up to 8 years of age and their families with an emphasis on increasing 
10access to the early periodic screening, diagnosis and treatment services required by 
11Medicaid and on a schedule set by the department.  In developing and administering the 
12program, the department shall maintain affiliate status with a national center that offers a 
13program that includes technical assistance and a model for strengthening early childhood 
14systems and services and meet any requirements necessary to maintain fidelity to the 
15program offered by the center.
162.  Staffing; coordination.  The department shall appoint a coordinator of the 
17program to work collaboratively across offices within the department, the Children's 
18Cabinet established in Title 5, section 19131, the Department of Education and other 
19applicable departments.
20ยง265.  Duties of Help Me Grow program
21The Help Me Grow program shall:
221.  Expansion of services.  Increase the rate of children screened at all appropriate 
23locations, including, but not limited to, early childhood education facilities, child care 
24facilities, Head Start facilities, Early Head Start facilities, regional sites of the Child 
25Development Services System under Title 20-A, section 7209 and health care providers 
26to ensure access to early periodic screening, diagnosis and treatment and other related 
27services to promote children's healthy development;
282.  Coordinated system.  Develop a coordinated system of early identification, 
29referral and follow-up services across early childhood education, child care facilities, 
30Head Start, Early Head Start, the Child Development Services System under Title 20-A, 
31section 7209, health care providers and family supports;
323.  Delivery of services.  Improve the delivery of services covered by early periodic 
33screening, diagnosis and treatment required by Medicaid and other related services to 
34promote children's healthy development;
354.  Centralized access point.  Develop a centralized access point for families, 
36caregivers and professionals to obtain information about early periodic screening, 
37diagnosis and treatment services.  The centralized access point must be available by 
38telephone, the Internet and other communication platforms;

9SUMMARY
10This bill establishes the Help Me Grow program in the Department of Health and 
11Human Services.  The program is a comprehensive, statewide, coordinated system of 
12early identification, referral and follow-up for children from prenatal care up to 8 years of 
13age and their families.  The program ensures access to early periodic screening, diagnosis 
14and treatment services and related services to promote healthy development and develop 
15a coordinated system of screening, referral and services.  The bill establishes 5 positions 
16within the Department of Health and Human Services and provides funding for software 
17and program evaluation.  The department must submit annual reports to the joint standing 
18committees of the Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human services and 
19education matters.
