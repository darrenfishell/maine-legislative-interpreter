2Sec. 1.  26 MRSA §1221, sub-§10, ¶H is enacted to read:
3H.  A governmental entity who elects to make payments in lieu of contributions into 
4the unemployment compensation fund as provided in this section is not liable to make 
5such payments with respect to benefits paid to any individual who voluntarily 
6separates from employment.
7SUMMARY
8This bill specifies that a governmental entity that elects to make payments in lieu of 
9contributions into the unemployment compensation fund is not liable to make payments 
10with respect to an individual who voluntarily separates from employment.
