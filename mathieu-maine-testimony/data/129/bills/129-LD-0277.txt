Page 2 - 129LR0799(01)-11SUMMARY
2This bill makes it an unfair trade practice for a telemarketer to misrepresent the 
3telemarketer's phone number when making a solicitation. It also prohibits the use of 
4prerecorded or artificial voices by telemarketers. It also provides that the current 
5exceptions to the prohibition against using an automated telephone calling device also 
6apply to the use of an artificial or prerecorded voice and provides additional exceptions 
7for public and private schools, emergency messages by governmental entities and calls 
8made to deliver information with the prior express written consent of the recipient of the 
9call.
