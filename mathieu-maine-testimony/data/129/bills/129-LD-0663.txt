Page 2 - 129LR1659(01)-11SUMMARY
2This bill allows the holder of a registration certificate and a set of gold star family 
3registration plates pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 29-A, section 524-B to be 
4issued a complimentary license to hunt, trap and fish, including permits, stamps and other 
5permissions needed to hunt.  It also exempts a person with a registration certificate and a 
6set of gold star family registration plates from the annual motor vehicle registration fee.
