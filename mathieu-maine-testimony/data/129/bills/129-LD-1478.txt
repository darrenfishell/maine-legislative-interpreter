2become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
3Whereas, the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A requires 
4legislative authorization before major substantive agency rules may be finally adopted by 
5the agency; and
6Whereas, a major substantive rule has been submitted to the Legislature outside the 
7legislative rule acceptance period; and
8Whereas, immediate enactment of this resolve is necessary to record the 
9Legislature's position on final adoption of the rule; and
10Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
11the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
12immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
13therefore, be it
14Sec. 1.  Adoption.  Resolved:  That final adoption of portions of Chapter 115: The 
15Credentialing of Education Personnel, a provisionally adopted major substantive rule of 
16the Department of Education that has been submitted to the Legislature for review 
17pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A outside the 
18legislative rule acceptance period, is authorized.
19Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
20legislation takes effect when approved.
21SUMMARY
22This resolve provides for legislative review of portions of Chapter 115: The 
23Credentialing of Education Personnel, a major substantive rule of the Department of 
24Education that was filed outside the legislative rule acceptance period.
