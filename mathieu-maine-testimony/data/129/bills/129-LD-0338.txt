102.  Labels; nonrefillable containers; nonexclusive distributorships.  With respect 
11to nonrefillable beverage containers the deposits for which are initiated pursuant to 
12section 3103, subsection 3, the refund value and the word "Maine" or the abbreviation 
13"ME" must be clearly indicated on every refundable beverage container sold or offered 
14for sale by a dealer in this State, by permanently embossing or permanently stamping the 
15beverage containers, except in instances when the initiator of the deposit has specific 
16permission from the department to use stickers or similar devices.  The refund value may 
17not be indicated on the bottom of the container.  Metal beverage containers must be 
18permanently embossed or permanently stamped on the tops of the containers.
19SUMMARY
20Current law requires that returnable metal beverage containers be labeled with the 
21refund value and the word "Maine" or "ME" embossed or stamped on the tops of the 
22containers.  This bill allows that information to appear anywhere except on the bottom of 
23the container.
