Fiscal Note 
No net fiscal impact -Efficiency Maine Trust 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The permanent removal of the provision requiring the Efficiency Maine Trust to dedicate 50% of the funds in the 
Regional Greenhouse Gas Initiative Trust Fund (RGGI) toward residential programs and 50% toward commercial 
and industrial programs may change the ratio of funds dedicated to each program but will not change the overall level 
of expenditures from the RGGI Fund.
