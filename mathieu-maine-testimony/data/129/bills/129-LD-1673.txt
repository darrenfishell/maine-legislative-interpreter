13SUMMARY
14This bill amends current law, which allows the advertising of prescription drugs only 
15if the advertisement is not misleading, to ban completely, beginning January 1, 2020, the 
16advertising of prescription drugs, either by broadcast by a television or radio station in 
17this State, over the Internet from a location in this State or in a magazine or newspaper 
18printed, distributed or sold in this State.  A violation is a violation of the Maine Unfair 
19Trade Practices Act.
