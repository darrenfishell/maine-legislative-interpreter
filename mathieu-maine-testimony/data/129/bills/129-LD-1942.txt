2Sec. 1.  8 MRSA §223-A, sub-§8, ¶C is enacted to read:
3C.  Consumer fireworks may not be used within the shoreland zone, except during:
4(1)  The calendar week that includes July 4th;
5(2)  New Year's Eve and until 12:30 a.m. on New Year's Day;
6(3)  Labor Day; and
7(4)  Memorial Day.
8This paragraph does not apply to a permitted fireworks display under section 227-A.
9As used in this paragraph, "shoreland zone" has the same meaning as "shoreland 
10areas" under Title 38, section 435; terms used to describe "shoreland areas" under 
11Title 38, section 435 have the same meanings as under Title 38, section 436-A.
12SUMMARY
13This bill bans the use of consumer fireworks in the shoreland zone except during the 
14calendar week that includes July 4th and on New Year's Eve, Labor Day and Memorial 
15Day.  It exempts from this prohibition fireworks displays that have a permit issued by the 
16Commissioner of Public Safety or the commissioner's designee.
