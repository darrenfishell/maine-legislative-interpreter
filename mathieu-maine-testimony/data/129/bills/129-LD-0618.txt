27SUMMARY
28This bill allows a person who holds a lobster and crab fishing license to raise and 
29haul lobster traps during any time of day between Labor Day and Memorial Day in an 
30area in the Bay of Fundy encompassing approximately 70 square miles around Machias 
31Seal Island, as long as that person is authorized to fish in that area.
