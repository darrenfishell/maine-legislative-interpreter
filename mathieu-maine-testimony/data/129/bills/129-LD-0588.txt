3SUMMARY
4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to allow a resident of this State or a corporation incorporated in 
6this State that enters into a contract in this State for services or products to be provided 
7exclusively in this State to bring suit or defend against suit in this State.  It proposes to 
8provide that contractual language that requires a resident of this State or a corporation 
9incorporated in this State that has entered into a contract in this State for services or 
10products to be provided exclusively in this State to submit to the jurisdiction of a court 
11outside this State is void.
