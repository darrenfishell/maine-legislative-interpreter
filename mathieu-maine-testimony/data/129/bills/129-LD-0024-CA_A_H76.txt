COMMITTEE AMENDMENT ,4" to H.P. 25, L.D. 24, "Resolve, Regarding 
Legislative Review of Chapter 26: Producer Margins, a Major Substantive Rule of the 
Maine Milk Commission" 
Amend the resolve in section 1 in the last line (page 1, line 18 in L.D.) by inserting 
after the following: "is" the following: 'not' 
Amend the resolve by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment provides that final adoption of Chapter 26: Producer Margins, a 
provisionally adopted major substantive rule of the Maine Milk Commission, is not 
authorized.
