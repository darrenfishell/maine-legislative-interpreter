Page 1 - 129LR1330(01)-11Sec. 1.  MaineCare reimbursement rates for outpatient opioid treatment. 
2Resolved:  That the Department of Health and Human Services shall amend its rules in 
3Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 65, Behavioral Health 
4Services regarding the reimbursement rate paid to outpatient opioid treatment providers 
5to set the rate at $110 per week, except that if the department determines that a higher 
6reimbursement rate is justified, the department shall set the reimbursement rate at that 
7higher rate.
8SUMMARY
9This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to set the weekly 
10MaineCare reimbursement rate paid to outpatient opioid treatment providers at $110 per 
11week, or at a higher rate if the department determines a higher rate is justified.
