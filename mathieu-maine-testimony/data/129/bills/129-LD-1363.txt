32SUMMARY
33This bill prohibits the Public Utilities Commission from issuing a certificate of public 
34convenience and necessity for a high-impact electric transmission line unless the 
35commission finds significant tangible public benefits will result from the construction and 
36use of the line, and all municipalities through which the high-impact electric transmission 
37line will pass have held a local referendum and certified to the commission that a 
38majority of the voters voting at the election voted in favor of the construction of the line 
39through that municipality.
40This bill also establishes a moratorium on the issuance of any permit, certificate or 
41other approval by the Public Utilities Commission or the Department of Environmental 
42Protection for a high-impact electric transmission line until 90 days after the adjournment
