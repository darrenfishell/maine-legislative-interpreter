20Sec. 4.  Maine State Housing Authority pilot project.  The Maine State 
21Housing Authority shall amend the income eligibility level under its pilot project to assist 
22income-eligible older adults or persons with disabilities to stay in their homes by 
23providing financial assistance with certain home modifications.  The income eligibility 
24level for the pilot project must be set at the area median income of the homeowner as 
25determined by the United States Department of Housing and Urban Development for the 
26county or municipality in which the home is located.
27SUMMARY
28This bill changes the maximum income eligibility level for the income tax credit for 
29home modifications to improve accessibility and the Maine State Housing Authority pilot 
30project to assist older adults and persons with disabilities with certain home 
31modifications.  The new income eligibility level for each program is changed to the area 
32median income as determined by the United States Department of Housing and Urban 
33Development for the county or municipality in which the home is located.
