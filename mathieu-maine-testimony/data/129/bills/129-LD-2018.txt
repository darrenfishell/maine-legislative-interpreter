3SUMMARY
4This bill requires each state department, state agency and quasi-independent state 
5entity to ensure that parking areas serving state-owned or state-leased buildings housing 
6that state department, state agency or quasi-independent state entity meet the 2010 federal 
7standards related to the marking of parking space access aisles under the federal 
8Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990 by November 1, 2020.  It requires each state 
9department, state agency or quasi-independent state entity to examine each parking area 
10by June 1, 2020.
