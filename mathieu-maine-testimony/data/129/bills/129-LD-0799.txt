21SUMMARY
22This bill amends the nursing education loan repayment program as follows:
231.  It extends eligibility to apply to individuals who are currently enrolled in master's 
24or doctoral degree programs; 
252.  It specifies that applicants must indicate an intention to work as full-time nursing 
26faculty in a nursing education program in the State, and increases the required 
27commitment from 3 to 5 years after acceptance into the nursing education loan repayment 
28program;
293.  It increases the maximum amount of loans eligible for repayment under the 
30program from $4,500 to $30,000 for a master's degree, and from $6,000 to $60,000 for a 
31doctoral degree.
