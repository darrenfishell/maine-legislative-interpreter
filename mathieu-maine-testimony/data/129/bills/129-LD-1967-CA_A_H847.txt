3SUMMARY
4The resolve requires the commissioners of Hancock and Washington counties to 
5submit the plans based on the result of the referendum question concerning the 
6dissolution of Prosecutorial District Number 7 to the joint standing committee of the 
7Legislature having jurisdiction over state and local government matters.  This amendment 
8instead requires the commissioners to report to the joint standing committee of the 
9Legislature having jurisdiction over judiciary matters because of that committee's 
10traditional jurisdiction overseeing district attorneys.  The amendment directs the joint 
11standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over judiciary matters to 
12introduce legislation in the 130th Legislature implementing the plans for the prosecutorial 
13districts.
14This amendment clarifies that the purpose of the resolve is to permit the submission 
15of the question to the voters of Hancock and Washington counties.
16This amendment adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause to provide 
17sufficient time to place the referendum question on the ballot.
