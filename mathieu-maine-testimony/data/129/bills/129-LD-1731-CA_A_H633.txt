COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "fl" to H.P. 1233, L.D. 1731, Bill, "An Act 
Regarding Representation of the Department of Marine Resources in Libel Proceedings" 
Amend the bill in section 3 in subsection 3 in the first line (page 1, line 17 in L.D.) by 
striking out the following: "person" and inserting the following: 'marine patrol officer' 
Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies that the person who is not an attorney but is representing 
the Department of Marine Resources in a libel proceeding in District Court must be a 
marine patrol officer.
