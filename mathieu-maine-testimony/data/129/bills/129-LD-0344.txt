11SUMMARY
12This bill increases the penalties for passing a stopped school bus with its red lights 
13flashing.  The new penalties are:
141.  A $500 fine and a suspension of a driver's license for not more than 30 days for 
15the first offense; and
162.  A fine of not less than $1,000 and a suspension of a driver's license for not less 
17than 30 days for a 2nd or subsequent offense occurring within 5 years of the first offense.
