13 0. No later than July 1, 2020, adopt, amend and maintain an appendix to the Maine

16 national voluntary efficiency standards that exceed the energy code requirements

26 SUMMARY

28 updated the Technical Building Codes and Standards Board ensure that the appendix

30 Maine Uniform Building and Energy Code.
