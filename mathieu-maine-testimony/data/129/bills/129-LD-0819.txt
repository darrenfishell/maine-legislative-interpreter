15SUMMARY
16This bill requires a worker who is requesting predetermination of independent 
17contractor status from the Workers' Compensation Board to submit all of the 
18miscellaneous income forms provided by the United States Department of the Treasury, 
19Internal Revenue Service received by the worker for the previous calendar year or, if the 
20worker did not receive a miscellaneous income form for the previous year, an explanation 
21as to why the worker did not receive a form.
