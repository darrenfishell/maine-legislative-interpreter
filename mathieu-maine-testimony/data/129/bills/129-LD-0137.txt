3SUMMARY
4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to enact measures designed to make the Department of Education's 
6Maine Learning Technology Initiative, or "MLTI," more cost-effective for schools and 
7for the State, thus allowing participation by increased numbers of students.  The cost-
8saving measures may include, but are not limited to, eliminating the ability of school 
9administrative units to choose higher-cost technology options.
