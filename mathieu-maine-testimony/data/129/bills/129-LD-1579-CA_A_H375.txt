7 SUMMARY 
8 This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment allows the Commissioner of 
9 Inland Fishedes and Wildlife to issue a limited permit to a local government. A limited 
10 permit allows a local government to introduce fish only into a great pond within the 
11 jurisdiction of the local government that was previously stocked by the department and in 
12 which stocking was suspended prior to January 1, 2019 and has not been resumed by the 
13 department due to inadequate public access. The permit allows the introduction of only 
14 the same species of fish that was stocked at the time the department suspended stocking. 
15 The permit allows only the introduction of fish obtained by the local government at its 
16 own expense from an in-state commercial facility that meets testing and health guidelines 
17 approved by the department. The permit may be issued only if the local government 
18 identifies public access to the great pond that is at least suitable for the hand carrying of 
19 boats to the water, includes a parking area and has been marked with signage adequate to 
20 ensure public awareness of the public access. "Local government" is defined as a 
21 municipality or, in the unorganized territory, a county.
