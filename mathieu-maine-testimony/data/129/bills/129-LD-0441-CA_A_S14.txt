20 SUMMARY 
21 This amendment directs the Commissioner of Education to adopt rules authorizing 
22 students who attend public school to possess and use a topical sunscreen product while on 
23 school property or at a school-sponsored event without a note or prescription from a 
24 licensed health care professional if the product is regulated by the federal Food and Drug 
25 Administration for over-the-counter use for the purpose of limiting skin damage from 
26 ultraviolet radiation.
