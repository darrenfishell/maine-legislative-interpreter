9SUMMARY
10This bill amends the Maine Bail Code to provide that a defendant who is not 
11dangerous, is not a flight risk in the absence of bond and is otherwise eligible for bail may 
12not be detained solely due to financial inability to post a money or property bond and may 
13file a motion with the court requesting relief from the requirement to post a money or 
14property bond.  This bill requires the court to determine the financial capacity of the 
15defendant and rule on the motion in an expedited manner.
