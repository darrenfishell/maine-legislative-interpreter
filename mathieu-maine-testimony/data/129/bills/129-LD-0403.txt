28Sec. 2.  Rules.  The State Tax Assessor shall adopt rules to determine the income or

30file a consolidated federal return and to prevent double taxation or double deduction of

32Title 36, section 5200, subsection 5.  Rules adopted pursuant to this section are major 
33substantive rules as described in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
34Sec. 3.  Application.  This Act applies to tax years beginning on or after January 1, 
352020.
36SUMMARY
37This bill requires corporations that file unitary income tax returns in Maine to include 
38income from certain jurisdictions outside the United States in net income when 
39apportioning income among tax jurisdictions.  The State Tax Assessor is required to 
40adopt major substantive rules to determine the income or loss attributable to such
