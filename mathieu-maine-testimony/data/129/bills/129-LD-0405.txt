2Sec. 1.  20-A MRSA §13406, as enacted by PL 2005, c. 635, §6, is amended to 
3read:
4§13406.  Minimum certified teacher salaries
5Each school administrative unit shall establish a minimum salary of $30,000 for 
6certified teachers for the school year starting after June 30, 2007 and in each subsequent 
7school year through the school year ending June 30, 2020.  Each school administrative 
8unit shall establish a minimum salary of $40,000 for certified teachers for the school year 
9starting after June 30, 2020 and in each subsequent school year.
10Sec. 2.  20-A MRSA §15689, sub-§7-A is enacted to read:
117-A.  Adjustment for minimum teacher salary.  Beginning in fiscal year 2020-21 
12and in each subsequent fiscal year, the commissioner shall increase the state share of the 
13total allocation to a qualifying school administrative unit in the current year by an amount 
14necessary to achieve the minimum salary for certified teachers established in section 
1513406.
16SUMMARY
17This bill increases the minimum salary for certified teachers, beginning with the 
182020-2021 school year.  This bill also requires the Commissioner of Education to 
19increase the state share of the total allocation to a qualifying school administrative unit to 
20achieve the minimum salary for certified teachers.
