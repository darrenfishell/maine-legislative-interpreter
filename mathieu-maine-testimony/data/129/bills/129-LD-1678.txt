12SUMMARY
13Current law allows for the collection of biological samples that are not blood 
14samples, such as saliva samples, from convicted adults and adjudicated juveniles by 
15various staff of the Department of Corrections, including corrections officers and 
16probation officers and also including other staff if they work at a department facility and 
17are designated by the Commissioner of Corrections and are trained to collect the samples.  
18This bill allows for collection by any staff member of the Department of Corrections who 
19is designated by the commissioner and is trained to collect biological samples, not just a 
20staff member of a facility.
