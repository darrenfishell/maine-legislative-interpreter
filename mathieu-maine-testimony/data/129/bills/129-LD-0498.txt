Page 1 - 129LR1734(01)-11Sec. 1.  Department of Health and Human Services to increase 
2MaineCare reimbursement rates. Resolved:  That, no later than October 1, 2019, 
3the Department of Health and Human Services shall amend its rules in Chapter 101: 
4MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Sections 68 and 85 to increase reimbursement 
5rates for occupational therapy and physical therapy services to no less than 70% of the 
6federal Medicare reimbursement rate for these services as long as the rate is no lower 
7than the rate reimbursed as of January 1, 2019.
8Sec. 2.  Routine technical rules. Resolved:  That rules adopted pursuant to this 
9resolve are routine technical rules as defined in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, 
10chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
11SUMMARY
12This resolve sets the reimbursement rates for occupational therapy and physical 
13therapy services under the Department of Health and Human Services rule Chapter 101: 
14MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Sections 68 and 85 at 70% of the federal 
15Medicare reimbursement rate as long as the rate is no lower than the rate in effect on 
16January 1, 2019.
