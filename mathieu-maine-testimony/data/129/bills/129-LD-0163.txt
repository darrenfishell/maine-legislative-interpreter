24SUMMARY
25This bill provides enhanced property tax exemptions for certain veterans.  A veteran 
26regardless of age becomes eligible for a $25,000 exemption if the veteran has served at 
27least 90 days of active service and has served in a combat zone.  A veteran with a service-
28connected disability becomes eligible for a $50,000 exemption if the disability is rated 
2950% to 90% and a $100,000 exemption if the disability is rated 100%.  A surviving 
30unremarried spouse, minor child or parent who is receiving a pension based on the 
31service of a deceased veteran qualifies for the same exemption the veteran would have 
32been eligible for. A municipality is reimbursed for the constitutionally mandated 50% of 
33the property tax revenue loss as a result of the enhanced exemption.
