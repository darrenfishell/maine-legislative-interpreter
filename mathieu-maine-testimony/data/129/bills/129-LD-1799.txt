10Sec. 1.  Director of Bureau of Parks and Lands authorized, but not 
11directed, to convey certain land in Little Moose Unit, Moosehead Junction 
12Township. Resolved:  That the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the 
13Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may, by quitclaim deed without 
14covenant, for appraised fair market value and other compensation and on such other terms 
15and conditions as the director may direct, convey a parcel of land, totaling approximately 
160.23 acre, situated in Little Moose Unit, off Route 15 in Moosehead Junction Township 
17and described in a boundary survey performed by Heart of Maine Surveying entitled 
18"Proposed Land to be conveyed to Charles J. Benevento, Moosehead Jct. Twp." dated 
19February 17, 2016 to an abutter, Charles Benevento.  The purpose of the conveyance is to 
20resolve a boundary issue.  The conveyance repeals the designation of the parcel as public 
21reserved lands as required by the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 12, section 598-A.
22SUMMARY
23This resolve authorizes the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the 
24Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to convey 0.23 acre of land in 
25Little Moose Unit, Moosehead Junction Township to an abutter, Charles Benevento, to 
26resolve a boundary issue.
