16SUMMARY
17This bill amends the definition of "epinephrine autoinjector" in different Titles of the 
18Maine Revised Statutes to include devices approved by the federal Food and Drug 
19Administration that deliver a specific dose of epinephrine by means other than automatic 
20injection of epinephrine into the human body.  The bill changes references to epinephrine 
21pen to epinephrine autoinjector.
