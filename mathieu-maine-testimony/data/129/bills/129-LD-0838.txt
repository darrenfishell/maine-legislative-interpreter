5SUMMARY
6Resolve 2017, chapter 56 requires the Department of Health and Human Services to 
7continue certain contractual agreements entered into by the department with Community 
8Partnerships for Protecting Children until January 31, 2019.  This resolve amends that 
9resolve to require the department to extend the contractual agreements until at least the 
10termination date specified in the contractual agreement.  This resolve applies retroactively 
11to January 31, 2019.
