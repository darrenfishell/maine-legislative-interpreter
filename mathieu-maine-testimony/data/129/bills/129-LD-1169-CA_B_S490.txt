16 'Sec. 1.  25 MRSA §1536 is enacted to read:
17§1536.  Registry of automated external defibrillators
18 The director shall establish a registry of publicly accessible automated external 
19 defibrillators, as defined in Title 22, section 2150-C, that are located within the State for 
20 the purpose of assisting a person or a law enforcement officer, firefighter or emergency 
21 medical services person who calls for assistance in an emergency situation.
22 The director may accept grants, funds, equipment and services to establish, operate and 
23 maintain the registry of publicly accessible automated external defibrillators.  The 
24 Department of Public Safety shall adopt rules regarding information collected for, 
25 maintained by and released by the registry.  Rules adopted pursuant to this paragraph are 
26 routine technical rules as defined by Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.'
27 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section 
28 number to read consecutively.
29SUMMARY
30 This amendment is the majority report of the committee.  The amendment provides a 
31 new title, An Act To Create a Registry To Improve Access to Automated External 
32 Defibrillators, and replaces the bill.  The amendment requires the Director of the Bureau of 
33 Consolidated Emergency Communications to establish a registry of publicly accessible 
34 automated external defibrillators located within the State for the purpose of assisting a
