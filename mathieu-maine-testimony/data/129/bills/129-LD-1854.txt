12§1605-A.  Medical payments
13A motor vehicle liability policy issued for a motor vehicle registered or principally 
14garaged in this State must provide coverage in an amount equal to or greater than $2,000 
15$5,000 per person for medical costs incurred as a result of injuries sustained in an 
16accident involving the insured vehicle by the driver and passengers in that vehicle.  The 
17coverage required by this section only applies to medical costs incurred during one year 
18following the date the injuries are sustained.  This section does not apply to a policy 
19insuring more than 4 motor vehicles, nor to any policy covering a garage, automobile 
20sales agency, repair shop, service station or public parking place.
21Sec. 3.  Application.  This Act applies to all policies, contracts and certificates 
22executed, delivered, issued for delivery, continued or renewed in this State on or after 
23January 1, 2021.  For purposes of this Act, all contracts are deemed to be renewed no 
24later than the next yearly anniversary of the contract date.
25SUMMARY
26This bill increases from $2,000 to $5,000 the amount of minimum medical payment 
27coverage for motor vehicle liability policies issued or renewed on or after January 1, 
282021.
