COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "I}" to H.P. 694, L.D. 939, Bill, "An Act 
Regarding the Regulation of Rabbit Production for Local Consumption" 
Amend the bill in section 5 in §2517-E in subsection 3 in the last line (page 2, line 33 
in L.D.) by striking out the following: "major substantive" and inserting the following: 
'routine technical' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment provides that rules adopted by the Commissioner of Agriculture, 
Conservation and Forestry to establish requirements for the physical facilities and 
sanitary processes used by rabbit producers whose rabbit products are exempt from 
inspection are routine technical rules rather than major substantive rules as in the bill.
