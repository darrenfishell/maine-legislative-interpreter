6Sec. 2.  20-A MRSA §15689, sub-§1-C is enacted to read:
71-C.  Adjustments to state contributions to member municipalities in certain 
8school administrative units.  A member municipality of a school administrative unit 
9must be guaranteed a minimum state contribution of 15% of the municipality's total cost 
10of education, as calculated pursuant to section 15688, subsection 2, which must be made 
11to the school administrative unit and deducted from the municipality's required 
12contribution.  The minimum state contribution attributable to a member municipality 
13pursuant to this subsection must be calculated prior to determining whether the school 
14administrative unit is eligible for the minimum state allocation pursuant to subsection 1.
15SUMMARY
16This bill changes the formula for determining the minimum state allocation for a 
17school administrative unit to be the unit's total cost of education, as adjusted, multiplied 
18by 15% or the unit's special education costs, as adjusted, whichever is greater.  This bill 
19also guarantees a member municipality a minimum state contribution of 15% of the 
20municipality's total cost of education, as adjusted, and requires the funds to be paid to the 
21school administrative unit and deducted from the municipality's required contribution.
