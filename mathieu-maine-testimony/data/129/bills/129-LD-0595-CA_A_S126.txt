7 SUMMARY 
8 The bill repeals the increased damages that a prope1ty owner is allowed to seek when 
9 trees located in a residential zone are unlawfully cut. 
10 This amendment provides the option of increased damages equal to the damages 
11 available under current law in a residential zone when the trees are ornamental or fruit 
12 trees or when the trees are located within 400 feet of a dwelling. The court has discretion 
13 to reduce the damages awarded for good cause shown when the cutting of the trees was 
14 done negligently or without fault, which under current law is not applicable in residential 
15 zones.
