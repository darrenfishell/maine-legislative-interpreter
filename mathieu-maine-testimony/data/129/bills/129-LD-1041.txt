5SUMMARY
6This bill amends provisions relating to collective bargaining by public employees 
7covered by municipal public employees labor relations laws, including:
81.  Repealing the provision that prohibits public employees from stopping or slowing 
9work, striking or blacklisting public employers to prevent the employers from filling 
10vacancies; and
112.  Requiring both parties to collective bargaining to have an individual present who 
12is authorized to reach a tentative agreement.
