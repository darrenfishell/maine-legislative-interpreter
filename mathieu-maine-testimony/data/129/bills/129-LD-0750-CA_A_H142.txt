Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Provide for Certification and Endorsement for Junior Reserve Officers' 
Training Corps Instructors To Instruct Students in Grade 9 to Grade 12' 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the 
following: 
'Sec. 1. 20-A MRSA §13003, sub-§5 is enacted to read: 
5. Certification and endorsement for Junior Reserve Officers' Training Corps 
instructor. The state board by rnle shall provide for a certification and endorsement for a 
federal Junior Reserve Officers' Training Corps instructor, pursuant to 10 United States 
Code, Section 2031, certified by the United States Department of Defense, to provide 
Junior Reserve Officers' Training Corps instruction to students in grade 9 to grade 12. 
The rnles must provide alternate pathways to certification, include requirements for 
background checks and fingerprinting and provide separate endorsements as follows: 
A. For senior military officers who have a bachelor's degree, the endorsement must 
be a professional teacher certificate endorsement; and 
B. For military officers who have an associate degree, the endorsement must be an 
educational technician endorsement.' 
Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively.
