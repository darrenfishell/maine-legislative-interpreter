28SUMMARY
29This bill changes the standards of conduct for directors under the Maine Nonprofit 
30Corporation Act and for directors and officers under the Maine Business Corporation Act 
31to add compliance with the laws governing monopolies and profiteering and acting in 
32accordance with the purpose for which the corporation is organized and not in a manner 
33that facilitates bias and partiality in governance.  It specifies that officers and directors of 
34business corporations may not act to cause a detrimental effect on the purpose of 
35nonprofit corporations.  It requires a corporation under the Maine Nonprofit Corporation 
36Act to include a list of private contributions received in its annual report.  It requires a 
37corporation under the Maine Business Corporation Act that receives a business 
38equipment tax exemption or participates in a tax increment financing or credit

2corporations or public entities in its annual report.
