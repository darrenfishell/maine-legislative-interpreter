SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill, which is a concept draft. The amendment requires 
that 33% of sales and use tax revenue collected by the State Tax Assessor from 
marketplace facilitators, with respect to marketplace-facilitated sales, after required 
transfers to other funds be transferred to the ConnectME Authority to further deployment 
of high-speed Internet and broadband infrastructure to unserved and underserved areas of 
the State. 
This amendment adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause to the bill.

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill transfers 33% of the sales and use tax revenue related to marketplace facilitated sales from the General Fund 
to the ConnectME Authority. The transfer will reduce General Fund revenue by $7,010,000 and increase Other 
Special Revenue Funds revenue by a corresponding amount starting in fiscal year 2020-21. The bill also includes an 
accompanying allocation to the Office of Broadband Development within the ConnectME Authority in the 
Department of Economic and Community Development to allow for expenditure of the funds.
