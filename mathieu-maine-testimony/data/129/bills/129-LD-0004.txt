20SUMMARY
21This bill amends the law governing municipal shellfish conservation programs. It 
22provides that a municipal shellfish conservation ordinance may allow a research entity to 
23conduct applied research in conjunction with Department of Marine Resources staff or an 
24approved professional entity to support a municipal shellfish conservation program.  It 
25also requires a municipality to require a research entity conducting approved research 
26projects in the intertidal zone to issue an annual report on research findings to the 
27Commissioner of Marine Resources by January 1st.
