2Sec. 1.  20-A MRSA §5403 is enacted to read:
3§5403.  Fund for Transportation Training
41.  Fund created.  The Fund for Transportation Training, referred to in this section as 
5"the fund," is created to assist in financing the cost of local and regional training for 
6public school bus drivers.  The fund is a dedicated, nonlapsing account within the 
7department.
82.  Use of fund.  The department shall award grants from the fund to school 
9administrative units for training developed and delivered by them to school bus drivers 
10between academic years.
113.  Sources of money.  The fund consists of amounts appropriated or allocated by the 
12State and any gifts or grants made to the department for the purpose of deposit in the 
13fund.
144.  Rules.  The department may adopt rules to implement this section.  Rules adopted 
15pursuant to this subsection are major substantive rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, 
16subchapter 2-A.

34SUMMARY
35This bill allows public school bus drivers to collect unemployment benefits over the 
36summer.  It also creates a nonlapsing fund to award grants to school administrative units 
37for training for school bus drivers between academic years.
