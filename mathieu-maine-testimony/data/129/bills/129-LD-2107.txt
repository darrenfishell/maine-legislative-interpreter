27SUMMARY
28This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its nursing 
29facility rules, Chapter 110: Regulations Governing the Licensing and Functioning of 
30Skilled Nursing Facilities and Nursing Facilities, to modify the standard admission 
31contract for the residents of nursing homes to specifically include a requirement for a 
32resident's legal agent to use the resident's financial resources to pay for nursing facility 
33services and to clarify that a nursing facility seeking relief when a resident's agent fails to 
34perform the agent's obligations may also request attorney's fees and costs.  The 
35department is required to amend the rule no later than January 1, 2021.
