3SUMMARY
4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to provide funding for additional case managers in the Department 
6of Defense, Veterans and Emergency Management, Bureau of Maine Veterans' Services 
7to provide information and assistance to veterans regarding the availability of benefits 
8and services for veterans such as health care, home financing, property tax exemptions 
9and income tax credits.
