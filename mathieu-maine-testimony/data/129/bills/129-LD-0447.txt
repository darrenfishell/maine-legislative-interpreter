3SUMMARY
4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to provide support for persons with substance use disorder along a 
6continuum of care that includes prevention, law enforcement diversion, treatment, harm 
7reduction and recovery.  The bill proposes to provide:
81.  Funding for programs that reduce the use of marijuana and so-called "vaping" by 
9youth in Maine in order to reduce the likelihood of neural pathway changes that can lead 
10to addiction later in life;
112.  Ongoing support for detoxification as a path to recovery;
123.  Reimbursement under the MaineCare program for substance use disorder peer 
13recovery coaches;
144.  Funding to the Bangor Area Recovery Network for addiction recovery support;
155.  Support for regional 2-1-1 hotlines to offer referrals to persons with substance use 
16disorder for local services; and
176.  Access to evidence-based approaches to prevent substance use and treat substance 
18use disorder in rural areas.
