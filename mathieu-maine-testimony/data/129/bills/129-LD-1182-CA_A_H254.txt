COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "fl" to H.P. 856, L.D. 1182, Bill, "An Act To 
Improve School Safety by Requiring Law Enforcement Visits" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Improve School Safety by Allowing Law Enforcement Visits' 
Amend the bill in section 1 in §6555 in the first paragraph in the first line (page 1, 
line 4 in L.D.) by strildng out the following: "must be visited at least 2 times" and 
inserting the following: 'may be visited at least one time' 
Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, removes the 
requirement that a public school must be visited at least twice a week by a law 
enforcement officer and instead provides that a public school may be visited at least one 
time per week by a law enforcement officer.
