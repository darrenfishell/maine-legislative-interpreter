122.  Juvenile detainee.  The commissioner has all the power over a juvenile detainee

17commissioner and the staff and rules of the Mountain View Correctional Facility until 
18release from the Mountain View Correctional Facility.
19SUMMARY
20This bill clarifies that the statutory guardianship power of the Commissioner of 
21Corrections over detainees under 18 years of age extends not only to necessary medical 
22care but also to necessary mental health care.
