19 SUMMARY 
20 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, amends the 
21 membership of the Maine Children's Cabinet Early Childhood Advisory Council to 
22 include 2 members of the Senate and 2 members of the House of Representatives, one 
23 from each of the 2 political parties having the greatest number of members in the House 
24 and Senate. It also requires that the member who is the parent of a young child be 
25 appointed by the Speaker of the House and the member representing child abuse and 
26 neglect prevention be appointed by the President of the Senate and adds 2 members who 
27 represent public and private family child care providers nominated by their organizations 
28 and appointed by the Children's Cabinet. 
29 The amendment also requires the council to submit its annual report to the joint 
30 standing committees of the Legislature having jurisdiction over education and cultural 
31 affairs and health and human services matters in addition to the Children's Cabinet. 
32 The amendment also adds a member of the Maine Children's Cabinet Early 
33 Childhood Advisory Council to the Education Research Institute Steering Committee in 
34 place of the member of the Maine Children's Growth Council. The amendment adds an 
35 appropriations and allocations section.

Includes adjustments to appropriations to the Legislature to reflect the elimination of the Child Care Adviso1y Council 
and the Maine Children's Growth Council and the creation of the Maine Children's Cabinet Early Childhood Advisory 
Council. These adjustments result in a net deappropriation for the Legislature of $3,040 per year. 
The bill also establishes allocation in the Federal Expenditures Fund and the Other Special Revenue Funds to allow 
the council to expend any funding received from the sources specified in the bill. 
Additional costs to the Governor's Office, the Department of Education and the Department of Health and Human 
Services associated with serving on the council can be absorbed within existing budgeted resources.
