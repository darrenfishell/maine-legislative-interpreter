17SUMMARY
18This bill authorizes the Superintendent of Insurance, the Superintendent of Consumer 
19Credit Protection, the Superintendent of Financial Institutions and the Director of the 
20Office of Professional and Occupational Regulation to lower or suspend certain fees 
21specified in law by order.
