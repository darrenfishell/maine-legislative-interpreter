SUMMARY 
This amendment requires, in fiscal year 2019-20 and fiscal year 2020-21, the State 
Controller to transfer $200,000 from available balances in the Bureau of Insurance Other 
Special Revenue Funds account within the Department of Professional and Financial 
Regulation to the Department of the Attorney General. The amendment also provides an 
Other Special Revenue Funds allocation to the Department of the Attorney General and 
makes other technical changes.
