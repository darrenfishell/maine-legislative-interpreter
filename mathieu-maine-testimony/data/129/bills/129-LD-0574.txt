25SUMMARY
26This bill includes in the definition of "indigent legal services" the filing, on behalf of 
27an indigent party or defendant or a juvenile, of a petition for certiorari to the Supreme 
28Court of the United States from an adverse decision of the Law Court on a case for which 
29indigent legal services were provided.  This bill also requires the Maine Commission on 
30Indigent Legal Services to develop a procedure for approving requests by counsel for 
31authorization to file a petition for certiorari.  Compensation for the preparation and filing 
32of the petition may not exceed $1,500.
