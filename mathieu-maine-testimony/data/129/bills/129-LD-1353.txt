8SUMMARY
9This bill requires insurers to report primary care expenditures to the Maine Health 
10Data Organization and for the Maine Quality Forum to use this data to report annually to 
11the Department of Health and Human Services and the Legislature the percentage of total 
12medical expenditures paid for primary care by commercial insurers, the MaineCare 
13program, Medicare, the organization that administers health insurance for state employees 
14and the Maine Education Association benefits trust respectively, the average of the 
15percentage of total medical expenses paid for primary care across all organizations and 
16the methods used by these organizations to pay for primary care.  This bill also requires 
17the Maine Quality Forum to conduct a study on best practices in health care spending 
18reporting and submit its findings to the Department of Health and Human Services and 
19the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human Services.
