6Initiative: Provides one-time Capital Expenditures funding for construction costs for a 
7long-term solution to the morning and evening congestion on Route 112 off I-95, Exit 36.  
8This allocation to the Department of Transportation represents 40% of the estimated 
9$60,000,000 that will be required for construction costs, with another 40% to be provided 
10by the Maine Turnpike Authority and the remaining 20% to be provided by the City of

17SUMMARY
18This bill, based on the recommendations of the study conducted jointly by the 
19Department of Transportation, the Maine Turnpike Authority and the City of Saco, 
20provides 40% of the estimated cost of the construction of a new exit and spur from the 
21Maine Turnpike to serve communities near Exit 36 of the Maine Turnpike, such as Saco, 
22Old Orchard Beach and Hollis.  The recommendation of the study was for the cost to be 
23split as follows:  40% to be provided by the Department of Transportation; 40% to be 
24provided by the Maine Turnpike Authority; and 20% to be provided by the City of Saco.HIGHWAY FUND 2019-20 2020-21
