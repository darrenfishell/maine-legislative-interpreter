13SUMMARY
14This bill establishes the Department of Substance Use Disorder Services as a separate 
15cabinet-level department.  It removes from the Department of Health and Human 
16Services, and transfers to the Department of Substance Use Disorder Services, functions 
17and services for individuals with substance use disorders, prevention programs and 
18administrative assistance for the Substance Use Disorder Services Commission.
