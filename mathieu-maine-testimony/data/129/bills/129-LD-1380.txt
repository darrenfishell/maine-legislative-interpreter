15PART B
16Sec. B-1.  Secretary of State to review Maine Revised Statutes and issue 
17recommended legislation; report.  The Secretary of State shall review the Maine 
18Revised Statutes to identify any amendments to provisions of law needed to carry out the 
19purposes of this Act. No later than January 15, 2020, the Secretary of State shall submit a 
20report on the changes found pursuant to the review under this section with recommended 
21legislation to correct those changes to the Joint Standing Committee on Judiciary.  The 
22committee may report out legislation based upon the report to the Second Regular 
23Session of the 129th Legislature.
24SUMMARY
25This bill transfers the responsibilities of the violations bureau, which processes traffic 
26infractions, from the judicial branch to the Office of the Secretary of State.
