Landowners Using Businesses Based in the United States" 
Amend the amendment in section 1 in §2729 in subsection 2 in the first line (page 1, 
line 21 in amendment) by striking out the following: "An" and inserting the following: 
'For taxes based on the status of property on or after April 1. 2022. an' 
Amend the amendment in section 1 in §2729 in subsection 3 in paragraph A in the 
first line (page 1, line 31 in amendment) by striking out the following: "At11 and inserting 
the following: 'For taxes based on the status of property on April 1. 2022 and April 1. 
2023. at least 90% of the employees of the business or contractor are residents of the 
United States and for taxes based on the status of property on or after April 1. 2024. at' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment changes the starting date for the credit to tax years based on the 
status of property on or after April 1, 2022 and changes the percentage of employees who 
must be residents of the United States from 7 5% to 90% for the first 2 years of the credit.
