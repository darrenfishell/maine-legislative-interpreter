9SUMMARY
10This bill makes any legally domiciled resident of the State eligible for coverage under 
11the group health plan available to state employees and other eligible persons.  The bill 
12permits any resident enrolling in coverage under this provision to do so during an open 
13enrollment period.
14The bill also adds a reporting requirement to track the experience of those persons 
15enrolling in coverage under the group health plan and the relative effect of that 
16experience on the overall costs of the group health plan.
