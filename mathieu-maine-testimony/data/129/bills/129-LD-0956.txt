2Sec. 1.  12 MRSA §6421, sub-§3-A, ¶E, as amended by PL 2015, c. 68, §3, is 
3further amended to read:
4E.  A student license authorizes the license holder to engage in the licensed activities 
5under subsection 2.  Notwithstanding section 6472 or any other provision that 
6establishes a closed season on fishing for or taking lobsters, a student license holder 
7is authorized to fish for or take lobsters during a closed season if that closed season 
8occurs during an interim between school years.  A student license may be issued only 
9to a person who, at the time of application, is 8 years of age or older and under 23 
10years of age.  A person issued a student license may not submerge more than:
11(1)  Ten lobster traps in the coastal waters of the State if the person is 8 years of 
12age or older and under 11 years of age;
13(2)  Fifty lobster traps in the coastal waters of the State if the person is 11 years 
14of age or older and under 14 years of age; or
15(3)  One hundred and fifty lobster traps in the coastal waters of the State if the 
16person is 14 years of age or older and under 23 years of age.
17A person issued a student license is enrolled in the apprentice program under section 
186422.  When applying for a license the person must designate a sponsor and may 
19designate up to 3 sponsors.  For the purposes of this paragraph, "sponsor" means a 
20person who holds a Class I, Class II or Class III lobster and crab fishing license 
21issued under this section.
22SUMMARY
23This bill allows a person with a student lobster and crab fishing license to fish for or 
24take lobsters during a closed season on fishing for or taking lobsters if that closed season 
25occurs during an interim between school years.
