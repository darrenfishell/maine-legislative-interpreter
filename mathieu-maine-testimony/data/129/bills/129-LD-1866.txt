16Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
17legislation takes effect when approved.
18SUMMARY
19This bill increases the standard automatic draft authority for licensed insurance 
20producers from $5,000 to $10,000 and allows for a temporary increase of $20,000 in the 
21event of a severe weather occurrence.
