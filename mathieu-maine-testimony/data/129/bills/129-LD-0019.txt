Page 1 - 129LR0003(01)-11 Be it enacted  by the People of  the State of Maine as  follows:
2 Sec. 1.  29-A MRSA §2301, sub-§5-C is  enacted to read:
3 5-C.  School bus crossing arm.  "School  bus crossing arm" means a device mounted  
4 on the front bumper of a school  bus that when activated extends outward 8 to 10 feet in 
5 front of the school bus to require students to walk at least 10 feet in front  of the school 
6 bus to cross the  roadway.
7 Sec. 2.  29-A MRSA §2302,  as amended by PL 2003, c. 293, §2, is further 
8 amended to read:
9 §2302.  School  bus markings;  lights; mirrors; school bus crossing arms
10 1.  Identifications.  Each school bus:
11 A.  Must be identified with the words, "school bus":
12 (1)  Printed  in letters not less than 8 inches high; and
13 (2)  Located between  the warning  signal  lamps as high as possible without 
14 impairing front and rear visibility of  the lettering;
15 B.  Must have no other lettering on the front or rear, except lettering not more than 4 
16 inches high  indicating an  emergency exit and a bus  number;
17 C.  Must be painted national school bus glossy yellow,  except that the hood may be 
18 lusterless black;
19 D.  Must have bumpers  of glossy black unless painting is impracticable through use 
20 of rubber,  reflective material  or other devices;
21 E.  Must be equipped with a system of signal  lights  that conform  to school bus 
22 requirements approved  by the Commissioner of  Education;
23 F.  Must be equipped with a system of mirrors that give the seated  operator a view of 
24 the way to each side of the bus, and of the area immediately in front of the front  
25 bumper;
26 G.  May be equipped with a system of stop arms to be operated  only with the red 
27 signal lights; and
28 H.  May  be equipped with reflective strips of national school bus yellow.
29 1-A.  School bus crossing arms.  A school  bus must  be equipped with a school bus 
30 crossing arm.
31 3.  Other  purposes.   A school bus permanently converted wholly to other  purposes 
32 must be painted  a color  other than national school  bus glossy yellow  and have  the words 
33 "school bus," school bus  signal lights  and stop arms  removed.
34 4.  Other passengers.   A school bus operated on a public way and transporting  
35 passengers who do not include school-age  persons  must  have the words "school bus" 
36 removed or  concealed and  the school  bus signal  lamps may not be operable.
Page 2 - 129LR0003(01)-11 5.  Application.  A vehicle operated  on a public  way displaying the words "school 
2 bus" or with the equipment required by this section may only be used to transport school-
3 age persons,  as defined  in section 2301.
4 SUMMARY
5 This bill requires school buses to be equipped with a school bus crossing arm, which  
6 is a device  requiring students to walk  at least 10 feet in front  of the school  bus to cross the 
7 roadway.
