5ยง1299.  Cancellation of subscriptions
61.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
7following terms have the following meanings.
8A.  "Automatic renewal subscription" means an agreement to provide any of the 
9following goods or services for a specified time and price that is automatically 
10renewed at the end of a definite term for a subsequent term unless the consumer 
11cancels the agreement:
12(1)  Online magazines, journals and periodicals;
13(2)  Online media players;
14(3)  Mobile apps;
15(4)  Social networking services; and
16(5)  Internet game services.
17B.  "Internet game service" means an online service that provides information, 
18software, data, text, photographs, graphics, audio or video that may be accessed by a 
19consumer on a paid subscription basis for the purpose of allowing that consumer to 
20play a single-player or multiplayer game through the Internet or to download a game 
21for that consumer to play offline.  "Internet game service" does not include online 
22gambling or other gaming in which a person participates to win money.
23C.  "Mobile app" means a software application designed to be operated on a mobile 
24device such as a smartphone.
25D.  "Online media player" means an online service that delivers audio or video 
26content.
27E.  "Social networking service" means an online service that facilitates the building of 
28social relations and the sharing of information among specified groups of people.
292.  Method of cancellation of automatic renewal subscription.  A business that 
30makes an automatic renewal subscription offer to a consumer in this State shall present 
31that consumer with an easily accessible disclosure of the methods that consumer may use 
32to cancel the subscription.  One of the methods of cancellation must provide for online 
33cancellation of the subscription by any means of communicating information over a 
34computer network.
353.  Exception.  This section does not apply to an entity that provides the host 
36platform on the website of an Internet game service.
Page 2 - 129LR1855(01)-114.  Violation.  A violation of this section is a violation of the Maine Unfair Trade 
2Practices Act.
3Sec. 2.  Application.  This Act applies only to an agreement entered into or 
4renewed after January 1, 2020 under which a business makes an automatic renewal 
5subscription offer to a consumer in this State.
6SUMMARY
7This bill requires a business that makes an automatic renewal subscription offer of an 
8online magazine, journal or periodical, online media player, mobile app, social 
9networking service or Internet game service to present a consumer in this State with an 
10easily accessible disclosure of the methods that consumer may use to cancel the 
11subscription, which must include online cancellation.
12The bill applies only to agreements entered into or renewed after January 1, 2020 and 
13does not apply to an entity that provides the host platform on the website of an Internet 
14game service.
