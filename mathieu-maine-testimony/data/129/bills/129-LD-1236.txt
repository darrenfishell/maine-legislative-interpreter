22SUMMARY
23This bill defines paramedic and paramedic emergency medical treatment in the laws 
24governing the provision of emergency medical treatment.  It also authorizes the 
25Emergency Medical Services' Board to establish by rule appropriate licensure levels for 
26paramedics and the qualifications for persons to hold those licenses.
