6ยง3851.  Submission of sexual assault cases for review for prosecution
7A law enforcement agency in possession of a complaint for an alleged sexual assault 
8under Title 17-A, chapter 11 shall within 60 days of receiving the complaint inform the 
9appropriate prosecutor of any evidence and submit the complaint for review and a 
10decision by the prosecutor regarding further investigation and commencement of 
11prosecution.  Failure of a law enforcement agency to inform the appropriate prosecutor of 
12any evidence and submit a complaint to the appropriate prosecutor within 60 days as 
13required by this section does not affect the validity of a later submission and prosecution.
14SUMMARY
15This bill requires a law enforcement agency, within 60 days of receiving a complaint 
16for an alleged sexual assault, to inform the appropriate prosecutor of any evidence and 
17submit the complaint to the appropriate prosecutor for review and a decision by the 
18prosecutor regarding further investigation and commencement of prosecution.  Failure of 
19a law enforcement agency to do so does not affect the validity of a later submission and 
20prosecution.
