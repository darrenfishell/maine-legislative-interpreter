2Sec. 1.  Immigrant workforce development council.  The Department of 
3Labor shall establish an immigrant workforce development council composed of relevant 
4stakeholders.  The department, after consultation with the council, shall provide funds to 
5entities that promote integration of immigrants into the workforce and community 
6through English language acquisition programs, job training and educational programs, 
7housing and transportation assistance and assistance in acquiring appropriate credentials.  
8Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the Department of Labor in 
9consultation with the council shall develop a competitive process in which funds provided 
10to the Department of Labor in section 2 are to be distributed to qualified entities.
