3ยง51.  Block grant set aside; vulnerable communities and Indian tribes
4Notwithstanding section 5327 or any other provision of law to the contrary, for each 
5block grant received by the department from the Federal Government, the department 
6shall annually set aside an amount equal to 20% of the total amount of the block grant to 
7be awarded to the most vulnerable communities in the State, as determined by the 
8department by rule, and an amount equal to 10% of the total amount of the block grant to 
9be awarded to federally recognized Indian nations, tribes and bands in the State.  The 
10department shall adopt rules to implement this section.  Rules adopted pursuant to this 
11section are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
12SUMMARY
13This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to annually set aside 
1420% of each federal block grant it receives for the most vulnerable communities in the 
15State and 10% of each federal block grant it receives for federally recognized Indian 
16nations, tribes and bands in the State.
