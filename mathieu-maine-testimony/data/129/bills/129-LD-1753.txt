25SUMMARY
26This bill amends the charter of the Boothbay-Boothbay Harbor Community School 
27District to require that bond issues in the principal amount of $250,000 or greater go to a 
28referendum vote, to add a competitive bidding requirement for district procurements of 
29$20,000 or greater and to remove the cap on contributions to the capital reserve fund.
