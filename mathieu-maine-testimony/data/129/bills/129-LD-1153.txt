38SUMMARY
39This bill requires a review team determining whether to approve a behavior 
40modification or behavior management program for a person with an intellectual disability 
41or autism to consider the successful use of the program for the person in another state.  It 
42requires that one member of the review team must be qualified by training and experience 
Page 3 - 129LR0289(01)-11in the use of behavior change procedures and the assessment and treatment of severe 
2problem behavior.  It specifies criteria that a behavior modification or behavior 
3management program submitted for review must meet.  It provides for a process for 
4appealing a review team's determination.  It allows for temporary restrictions on the 
5possession or use of personal property through the use of reinforcement procedures under 
6a behavior modification or behavior management program.
