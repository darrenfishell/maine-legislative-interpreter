6SUMMARY
7This bill directs the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to establish and 
8coordinate a volunteer corps of search and rescue specialists whose initial and ongoing 
9training is delivered by the department and who are deputized to engage in search and 
10rescue operations.
