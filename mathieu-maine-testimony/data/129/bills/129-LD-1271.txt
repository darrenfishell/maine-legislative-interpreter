2Sec. 1.  36 MRSA §653, sub-§1, ¶D-4 is enacted to read:
3D-4.  For property tax years beginning on or after April 1, 2020, the estates, having a 
4taxable situs in the place of residence, of a veteran who provides to the assessor of the 
5place in which the veteran resides certification from the United States Department of 
6Veterans Affairs or any branch of the Armed Forces of the United States that the 
7veteran is permanently disabled and receives benefits based on a rating of 100% for a 
8service-connected disability.
9The exemption provided in this paragraph also applies to property held in a revocable 
10living trust for the benefit of the veteran.
11SUMMARY
12This bill provides a complete property tax exemption in the municipality of residence 
13for a veteran receiving benefits based on a rating of 100% for a service-connected 
14disability.
