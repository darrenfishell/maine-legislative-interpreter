1 SUMMARY 
2 This amendment replaces the bill and does the following. 
3 1. The amendment amends and repeals certain definitions and changes terminology 
4 in the emergency medical services field regarding first responders and paramedics to 
5 general terminology that correlates with emergency medical services licensing. 
6 2. The amendment adds 3 positions to the Medical Direction and Practices Board: a 
7 pediatric physician, an emergency medical services person licensed to provide basic life 
8 support and an emergency medical services person licensed to provide advanced life 
9 support. 
10 3. The amendment changes the job title "statewide assistant emergency medical 
11 services medical director" to "statewide associate emergency medical services medical 
12 director." 
13 4. The amendment makes the statewide associate emergency medical services 
14 medical director an ex officio member of the Emergency Medical Services Board and 
15 adds to the board a representative in the field of pediatrics. 
16 5. The amendment gives the Emergency Medical Services Board the authority to 
17 deny or refuse to renew an emergency medical services person license or revoke a 
18 license.
