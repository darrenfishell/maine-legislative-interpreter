SUMMARY 
The amendment clarifies that the annual allocation to the capital reserve fund after 
fiscal year 2020 must be the greater of the previous year's allocation or that year's 
allocation adjusted by the percentage change in the Consumer Price Index for the most 
recently concluded calendar year.
