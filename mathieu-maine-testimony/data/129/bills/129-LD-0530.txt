28SUMMARY
29This bill amends the subrogation law related to medical payments coverage to require 
30a health care provider to notify the insured and any other person reasonably likely to be 
31able to submit a claim prior to submitting any claim for payment of any hospital, nursing, 
32medical or surgical services or of any expenses paid or reimbursed under the medical 
33payments coverage in a policy.  The bill also prohibits an insurer from paying a claim for 
34payment of any hospital, nursing, medical or surgical services or of any expenses paid or 
35reimbursed under the medical payments coverage in a policy if an insured is entitled to 
36coverage under any other health insurance policy or contract and requires an insurer to 
37provide at least 14 days' notice to an insured before paying such a claim.
