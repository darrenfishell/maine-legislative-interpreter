8SUMMARY
9This bill provides for each local workforce development board in the State to receive 
10an annual appropriation equal to $25 multiplied by the number of unemployed persons in 
11the local workforce development board's service area for the prior calendar year divided 
12by 12 and requires that on or before September 1st of each even-numbered year, the 
13Commissioner of Labor is required to prepare and submit the annual appropriation 
14requirement for each year of the ensuing biennium to the State Budget Officer to be 
15included in the Governor's biennial budget proposal.
16This bill includes General Fund appropriations totaling $465,252 in fiscal year 2019-
1720 and $465,252 in fiscal year 2020-21 to provide funding for the local workforce
