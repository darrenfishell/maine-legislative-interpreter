13SUMMARY
14This bill requires an insurer who overpays a claim to attempt to retroactively collect 
15that overpayment directly from the provider, not the insured, unless the insured was 
16directly paid by the insurer for the services identified in the claim and the insured did not 
17forward payment to the provider.
