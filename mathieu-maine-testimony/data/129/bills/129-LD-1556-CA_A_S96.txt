Fiscal Note 
Current biennium revenue increase -Other Special Revenue Funds 
Current biennium cost increase -Other Special Revenue Funds 
This bill will increase the filing fee paid to the Office of the Public Advocate (OPA) when filing a petition of 
approval with the Public Utilities Commission to erect, rebuild or relocate a transmission line from 1/100 of 1 % of 
the estimated cost of the project to 2/100 of 1 % of the cost. The bill will also allow the OPA to bill the applicant if its 
expenses exceed the initial filing fee paid. The OPA must return any p01iion of the filing fee revenue that exceeds 
expenses related to that filing. The bill will provide additional revenue to the OPA to accommodate a higher level of 
expenditures.
