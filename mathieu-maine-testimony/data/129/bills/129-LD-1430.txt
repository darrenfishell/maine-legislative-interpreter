Page 3 - 129LR1414(01)-11SUMMARY
2This bill provides clarification related to the eligibility of business investments in 
3renewable energy facilities for purposes of the business equipment tax exemption and 
4provides personal property tax and real estate tax exemptions for renewable energy 
5facilities installed for noncommercial use.  Additionally, the bill directs the Department 
6of Administrative and Financial Services, Maine Revenue Services to provide guidance 
7documents to assist municipalities with the assessment of renewable energy facilities 
8included in these provisions.
