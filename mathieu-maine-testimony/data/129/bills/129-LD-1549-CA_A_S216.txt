Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This legislation increases the amount of the salary supplement paid to certain eligible teachers who have attained 
certification from the National Board for Professional Teaching Standards from $3,000 to $5,000 beginning in fiscal 
year 2020-21. The bill requires the Department of Education to detennine the cost of this provision and report the 
amount to the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs no later than January 1, 2020 and 
authorizes the committee to submit a bill to the Second Regular Session of the 129th Legislature. 
The cost of providing the national board certification salary supplement is included in the calculation of the total cost 
of public education from kindergarten to grade 12 as part of the targeted education funds. Unless the State provides 
100% of the funding needed to cover the cost of the increase in the supplement beginning in fiscal year 2020-21, a 
portion, or all, of the cost will be borne by local school administrative units.
