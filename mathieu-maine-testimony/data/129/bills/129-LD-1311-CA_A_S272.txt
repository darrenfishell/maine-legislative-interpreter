32 SUMMARY

34 1. It allows pet shops that are currently lawfully offering dogs or cats for sale to 
35 continue to offer dogs and cats for sale as long as the ownership of the pet shop does not 
36 change and the number of dogs and cats offered for sale does not increase. It allows for 
37 limited transfers of ownership of a family-owned pet shop to a spouse, domestic partner
