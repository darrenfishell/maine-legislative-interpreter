2Sec. 1.  30-A MRSA §5225, sub-§1, ¶B, as amended by PL 2011, c. 101, §13, is 
3further amended to read:
4B.  Costs of improvements that are made outside the tax increment financing district 
5but are directly related to or are made necessary by the establishment or operation of 
6the district, including, but not limited to:
7(1) That portion of the costs reasonably related to the construction, alteration or 
8expansion of any facilities not located within the district that are required due to 
9improvements or activities within the district, including, but not limited to, 
10sewage treatment plants, water treatment plants or other environmental protection 
11devices; storm or sanitary sewer lines; water lines; electrical lines; improvements 
12to fire stations public safety facilities; and amenities on streets;
13(2) Costs of public safety improvements made necessary by the establishment of 
14the district; and
15(3) Costs of funding to mitigate any adverse impact of the district upon the 
16municipality or plantation and its constituents.  This funding may be used for 
17public facilities and improvements if:
18(a)  The public facilities or improvements are located in a downtown tax 
19increment financing district; and
20(b)  The entire tax increment from the downtown tax increment financing 
21district is committed to the development program of the tax increment 
22financing district;
23SUMMARY
24This bill expands the types of projects eligible for tax increment financing for 
25municipalities and plantations by including public safety facilities instead of just fire 
26stations, as in the current law.
