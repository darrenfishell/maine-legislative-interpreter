12For every intentional violation of this section, the department, agency or entity of the 
13State whose employee committed the violation is liable for a civil violation for which a 
14fine of not less than $100 and not more than $500 may be adjudged.
15SUMMARY
16This bill prohibits the State, as an employer, from inquiring about a prospective 
17employee's compensation history until after an offer of employment that includes all 
18terms of compensation has been negotiated and made to the prospective employee.  The 
19bill also prohibits the State from requiring that a prospective employee's compensation 
20history meet certain criteria.  A department, agency or entity of the State that violates this 
21provision is subject to a fine of not less than $100 and not more than $500 per intentional 
22violation.
