2Sec. 1.  22 MRSA §§3174-BBB and 3174-CCC are enacted to read:
3§3174-BBB.  Medical nutritional therapy; rules
4The department shall provide for the reimbursement under the MaineCare program 
5for medical nutritional therapy services to treat obesity, unhealthy weight, hypertension, 
6diabetes, prediabetes, disorders of lipid metabolism, HIV infection, unintended weight 
7loss in older adults, chronic kidney disease, gastrointestinal conditions, malnutrition and 
8inappropriate eating habits.  Medical nutritional therapy services may be provided by 
9physicians licensed pursuant to Title 32, chapter 36 or 48, dietitians licensed pursuant to 
10Title 32, chapter 104 or nurse practitioners licensed pursuant to Title 32, chapter 31 who 
11practice in hospitals, outpatient settings or private offices.
12As used in this section, "medical nutritional therapy" means a therapeutic approach to 
13treating medical conditions and their associated symptoms by use of counseling and a 
14specifically tailored diet devised and monitored by a physician licensed pursuant to Title 
1532, chapter 36 or 48, a dietitian licensed pursuant to Title 32, chapter 104 or a nurse 
16practitioner licensed pursuant to Title 32, chapter 31.
17The department shall adopt rules to implement this section.  Rules adopted pursuant 
18to this paragraph are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 
192-A.
20§3174-CCC.  Prescription drug therapy; rules
21The department shall provide for the reimbursement under the MaineCare program 
22for prescription drug therapy services to treat obesity, unhealthy weight, hypertension, 
23diabetes, prediabetes, disorders of lipid metabolism, HIV infection, unintended weight 
24loss in older adults, chronic kidney disease, gastrointestinal conditions, malnutrition and 
25inappropriate eating habits.  Prescription drug therapy services may be provided by 
26physicians licensed pursuant to Title 32, chapter 36 or 48 or nurse practitioners licensed 
27pursuant to Title 32, chapter 31 who practice in hospitals, outpatient settings or private 
28offices.
29The department shall adopt rules to implement this section.  Rules adopted pursuant 
30to this paragraph are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 
312-A.
32SUMMARY
33This bill provides for reimbursement under the MaineCare program for medical 
34nutritional therapy and prescription drug therapy.
