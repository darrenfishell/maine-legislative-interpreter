7SUMMARY
8This bill amends the chapter of the Maine Revised Statutes concerning interbranch 
9communication and coordination to prohibit the Governor from limiting access to 
10department commissioners and other executive branch personnel when the Legislature is 
11seeking information necessary for the Legislature to do the work of the people of Maine.
