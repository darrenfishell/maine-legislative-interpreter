37SUMMARY
38This bill allows the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to establish bear 
39hunting and trapping season dates by rule.  It reduces the cost of a resident bear hunting 
Page 4 - 129LR0514(01)-11permit and a resident bear trapping permit from $27 to $10, and it also repeals the 2-bear 
2limit in statute and instead refers to the bag limit set in rule.
