COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 368, L.D. 511, Bill, "An Act To Create 
an Alzheimer's Disease and Dementia Coordinator Position within the Department of 
Health and Human Services" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Create a Position within the Department of Health and Human Services 
To Coordinate Dementia Programs and Services' 
Amend the bill by strildng out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the 
following: 
'Sec. 1. 22 MRSA §5106, sub-§4-A is enacted to read: 
4-A. Coordinator for dementia oro!!rams and services. Coordinate and 
implement programs that provide services to the State's individuals and families affected 
by dementia. The department shall designate a coordinator to coordinate efforts across 
departments and branches of State Government and to oversee imp1ementlltion of any 
related state plans developed by the department and plans developed by the United States 
Department of Health and Human Services, Centers for Disease Control and Prevention 
and national organizations with a recognized expertise related to dementias that are

2020 to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over health

17 SUMMARY 
18 This amendment replaces references to "Alzheimer's disease and other dementias" 
19 from the bill and uses the term "dementia" instead. It also clarifies that the coordinator 
20 oversees the implementation of any state or federal plans related to dementia and that 
21 programs and services are for both individuals with dementia and their families.
