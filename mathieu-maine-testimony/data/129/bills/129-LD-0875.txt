Page 2 - 129LR1861(01)-11SUMMARY
2This bill requires that warranties for manufactured homes include covering damage to 
3a manufactured home during transportation or handling, for which the dealer is required 
4to take corrective action.  This bill also requires a dealer of manufactured housing to 
5place an amount equal to 10% of the purchase price in escrow that may not be released to 
6the dealer until both the dealer and consumer execute a release subsequent to the 
7installation and final inspection of the manufactured housing.
