4A.  "Earnable compensation" includes:
5(1)  Workers' compensation benefits;
6(2)  Maintenance, if any; 
7(3)  Any money paid by an employer to a 3rd party under a tax sheltered annuity 
8contract or a deferred compensation plan for the future benefit of an employee 
9provided that as long as the money is not derived from amounts excluded from 
10earnable compensation by paragraph B; and
11(4)  Pick-up contributions.; and
12(5)  Stipends received as part of the member's compensation for services rendered 
13in an employment position.
14SUMMARY
15This bill requires that stipends paid to a member of the Maine Public Employees 
16Retirement System be counted as earnable compensation for purposes of calculating 
17retirement benefits.
