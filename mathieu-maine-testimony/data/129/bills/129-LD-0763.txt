Page 1 - 129LR0753(01)-11Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
2become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
3Whereas, community integration services are important for the overall health and 
4well-being of recipients of those services; and
5Whereas, low rates of reimbursement for these services create barriers to providers 
6willing to provide these services; and
7Whereas, instability of willing providers reduces access to services; and
8Whereas, this legislation requires that an amendment to rules to address this issue 
9be made by July 1, 2019; and
10Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
11the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
12immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
13therefore,
14Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
15Sec. 1.  Department of Health and Human Services to increase 
16community integration services rates.  The Department of Health and Human 
17Services shall amend rule Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 
1817, Allowances for Community Support Services to increase the reimbursement rate for 
19community integration services, effective July 1, 2019, to $24.25 per quarter-hour.
