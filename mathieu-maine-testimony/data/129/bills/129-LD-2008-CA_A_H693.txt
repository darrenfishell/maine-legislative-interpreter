SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies the existing law that provides that the 10% sales tax rate 
applies to 3 categories of motor vehicles rented for less than one year: automobiles; 
pickup trucks or vans weighing less than 26,000 pounds that are rented from a person 
primarily engaged in the business ofrenting automobiles; and certain loaner vehicles.
