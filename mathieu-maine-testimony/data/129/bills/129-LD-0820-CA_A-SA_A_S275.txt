This amendment includes General Fund appropriations to the Department of Health and Human Services of $227,546 
in fiscal year 2019-20 and $375,843 in fiscal year 2020-21 to provide coverage to MaineCare members for legal 
abortion services and for oversight, development and testing of technology changes. Federal Expenditures Fund 
allocations will not be required, as these services are not eligible for federal match.
