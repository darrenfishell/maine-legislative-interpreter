8SUMMARY
9This bill increases from $25,000 to $50,000 the minimum amount motor vehicle 
10liability insurance policies must cover for damage to property in order for the policies to 
11be accepted as proof of financial responsibility to drive, including for vehicles used 
12exclusively to transport passengers for hire between points within the State and seating no 
13more than 3 passengers behind the driver and for rental trucks with a registered gross 
14weight of 26,000 pounds or less, rented or leased for fewer than 30 days.  It also amends 
15the law regarding satisfied judgments to increase to $50,000 the amount that must be 
16credited on a judgment for injury to or destruction of property of others as a result of one 
17accident rendered in excess of that amount.
