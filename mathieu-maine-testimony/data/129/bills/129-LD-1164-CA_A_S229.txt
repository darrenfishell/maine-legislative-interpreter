1 SUMMARY 
2 This amendment removes the requirement that a taxpayer's degree was received after 
3 2007 to receive a credit for student loan repayment and provides that taxpayers who were 
4 eligible for a refundable credit under the credit for educational opportunity may continue 
5 to receive a refundable credit for tax years beginning before January 1, 2022. The 
6 amendment also increases from $50,000 per year to $75,000 per year the funds provided 
7 to market the Job Creation Through Educational Opportunity Program.
