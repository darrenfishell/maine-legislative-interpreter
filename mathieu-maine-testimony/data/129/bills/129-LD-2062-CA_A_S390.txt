17 SUMMARY 
18 This amendment, which is the unanimous report of the committee, clarifies that the 
19 only licensed entities that may register a slot machine or slot machine associated 
20 equipment are licensed slot machine distributors and licensed gambling services vendors. 
21 The amendment also changes the definition of "associated equipment" to clarify that 
22 only mechanical, electromechanical or electronic components or machines that are used 
23 in or intended for use in a slot machine or table game and that affect the outcome of the 
24 game or that are involved in the handling of money, tokens, credits or similar objects or 
25 things of value used to play a slot machine or table game or the calculation of or 
26 distribution of payoffs of the slot machine or table game must be registered.
