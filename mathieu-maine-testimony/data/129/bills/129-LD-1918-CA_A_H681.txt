SUMMARY 
This amendment adds to what a reserve fund may be established for to include 
providing funds for expenditures such as regular instruction, special education, career and 
technical education, other instruction including summer school and extracurricular 
instruction, student and staff support, system administration, school administration, 
transportation and buses, facilities maintenance, debt service and other commitments and 
other expenditures, including school lunch. 
The amendment also adds that, in order to expend funds by a vote of the boards or 
committees of school administrative districts, regional school units, community school 
districts and career and technical education regions, the respective boards and committees 
must provide public notice of the meeting at which the vote to expend funds from the
