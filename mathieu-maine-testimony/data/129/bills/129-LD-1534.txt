8SUMMARY
9This bill amends fireworks laws in the following ways:
101.  By adding the term "flame effects" to the definition of "display";
112.  By adding flame effects to the types of displays for which a permit must be 
12obtained; and
133.  By removing the requirement that indoor pyrotechnic events be monitored by the 
14State Fire Marshal or the State Fire Marshal's designee and instead requiring that indoor 
15pyrotechnic and flame effects events be inspected by the State Fire Marshal or the State 
16Fire Marshal's designee.
