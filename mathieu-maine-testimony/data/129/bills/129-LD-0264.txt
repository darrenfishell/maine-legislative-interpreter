19blood at the request of a law enforcement officer pursuant to this chapter.
20SUMMARY
21This bill allows an emergency medical services person or law enforcement officer 
22whose training allows that person to draw blood samples to draw a specimen of blood 
23from the operator of a motor vehicle involved in a fatal traffic accident for the purpose of 
24determining the blood-alcohol level or the presence of a drug or drug metabolite.  This 
25bill extends to those same persons the current law shielding persons from liability for acts 
26done or omitted in collecting or withdrawing specimens of blood.
