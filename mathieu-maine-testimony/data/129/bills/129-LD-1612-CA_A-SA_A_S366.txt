Fiscal Note 
Additional revenue reduction -General Fund 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This amendment begins the revenue loss earlier than the committee amendment by lowering the presumption of 
abandonment earlier in stages. It also restricts abandonment for additional cards. This is expected to reduce General 
Fund revenue over the committee amendment by $1,700,000 in fiscal year 2021-22, $3,500,000 in fiscal year 2022-23 
and $1,500,000 in fiscal year 2023-24 and after.
