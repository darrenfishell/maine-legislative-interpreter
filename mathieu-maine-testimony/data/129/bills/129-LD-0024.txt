14 Sec. 1.  Adoption.  Resolved:  That final adoption of Chapter 26:  Producer 
15 Margins, a provisionally  adopted  major substantive  rule of the Department of 
16 Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Maine Milk  Commission that has been  submitted 
17 to the Legislature for review  pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter  375, 
18 subchapter 2- A, is authorized.
19 Emergency clause.  In view  of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
20 legislation takes effect when  approved.
21 SUMMARY
