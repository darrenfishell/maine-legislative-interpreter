13SUMMARY
14This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to establish and 
15maintain a program through the Health and Environmental Testing Laboratory offering 
16free well water testing for low-income residents of the State.  It also requires the 
17department to review recent research regarding arsenic toxicity and levels suitable for 
18consumption and to amend its rules to revise the maximum contaminant level for arsenic 
19to be consistent with that research.
