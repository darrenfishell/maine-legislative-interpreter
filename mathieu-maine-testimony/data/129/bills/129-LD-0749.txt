10SUMMARY
11This bill requires the State Controller to maintain a publicly accessible website with 
12up-to-date information concerning the State's payroll and vendor information, which is 
13currently in existence and called the "Maine Open Checkbook."  The bill requires the 
14website to include the names of all recipients of state grants and track and record all 
15payments made by quasi-public entities of the State and the dollar value of credits 
16awarded through all state economic development programs.
