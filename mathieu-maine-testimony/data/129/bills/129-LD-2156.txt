13SUMMARY
14The Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs unanimously voted 
15to report out this committee bill pursuant to Resolve 2019, chapter 44. This resolve 
16requires the Department of Education to report back to the joint standing committee of 
17the Legislature having jurisdiction over education matters on issues relating to bullying in 
18schools and the progress of any work groups or task forces that are currently studying this 
19issue. The report must include any findings, recommendations and suggested legislation. 
20The joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over education 
21matters may report out a bill on the subject matter of the report to the First Regular 
22Session of the 130th Legislature.
