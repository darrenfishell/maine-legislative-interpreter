13SUMMARY
14This bill will reduce the amount of opioid prescribing education required of 
15prescribers from 3 hours per license cycle of 2 years to one hour per license cycle of 2 
16years after the licensee has completed an initial 3-hour requirement. It will also require 
17the licensing boards to adopt rules consistent with the reduction in the amount of required 
18prescribing education.
