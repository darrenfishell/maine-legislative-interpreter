6SUMMARY
7This bill directs the Commission on Governmental Ethics and Election Practices to 
8amend its rules no later than October 1, 2020 to allow a certified candidate under the 
9Maine Clean Election Act to use money disbursed from the Maine Clean Election Fund 
10for expenses related to an election recount.
