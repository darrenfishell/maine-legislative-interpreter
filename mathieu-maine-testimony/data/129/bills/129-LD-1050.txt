4Instruction in the Holocaust must be provided in and required for graduation from all

8program of discriminatory and genocidal laws, policies or actions targeted against 
9discrete groups of individuals based on race, religion, ethnicity, sexual orientation, 
10physical disability or national origin, from January 1, 1933 to December 31, 1945, in 
11Nazi Germany or in any European country allied with or occupied by Nazi Germany.
12SUMMARY
13This bill requires instruction in the Holocaust developed by the Commissioner of 
14Education to be provided in and required for graduation from all elementary and 
15secondary schools, both public and private.
