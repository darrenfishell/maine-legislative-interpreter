24SUMMARY
25This bill allows the assignment of state vehicles to field personnel directly concerned 
26with the maintenance and operation of Maine National Guard facilities who are 
27frequently called for emergency duty outside of regular hours.  It also allows Department 
28of Defense, Veterans and Emergency Management, Military Bureau employees 
29designated by the Commissioner of Defense, Veterans and Emergency Management to 
30use state vehicles to commute between home and work.
