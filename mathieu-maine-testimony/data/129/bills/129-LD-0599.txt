13§4404.  Tolls
14The Department of Transportation shall, in a manner consistent with the Maine 
15Administrative Procedure Act, Title 5, chapter 375, establish adopt rules establishing tolls 
16for the use of such the ferry line route or lines routes under this subchapter by vehicles, 
17freight and passengers.  Tolls must be calculated separately for each route of travel. The 
18department may not apply a surcharge or transaction fee to the payment of tolls.  Tolls 
19collected from the operation of the Maine State Ferry Service shall must at all times be 
20kept in a separate fund distinct from all other moneys money of the State and shall must 
21be used for the operation and debt retirement of the ferry service.
22Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, 
23chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
24Sec. 3.  Rates. No later than November 1, 2019, the Department of Transportation 
25shall adopt rules pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 23, section 4404 
26establishing the tolls for the Maine State Ferry Service at the rates that were in effect on 
27May 20, 2018.
28SUMMARY
29This bill requires that state support to the Marine Highway account must fund 100% 
30of the capital and operating costs of the Maine State Ferry Service.  It also requires the 
31Department of Transportation to establish, by rule, ferry tolls separately for each route of 
32travel and to adopt no later than November 1, 2019 the toll rates that were in effect on 
33May 20, 2018.
