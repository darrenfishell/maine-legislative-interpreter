41.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
5following terms have the following meanings.
6A.  "Kinship care" means the placement of a child with a relative on a temporary or 
7permanent basis.  The placement may or may not involve the participation of the 
8department.
9B.  "Relative placement" means the use of a relative as a temporary placement for a 
10child in the custody of the department or a comparable department in another state 
11while the child and the child's parents are engaged in a rehabilitation and 
12reunification process under subchapter 5.
132.  Administrator.  The commissioner shall appoint one person to administer all 
14issues related to kinship care and relative placement.
15SUMMARY
16This bill requires the Commissioner of Health and Human Services to appoint one 
17person to administer all issues related to the placement of a child with a relative.
