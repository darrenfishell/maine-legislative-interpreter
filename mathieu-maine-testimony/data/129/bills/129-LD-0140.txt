61.  Criteria for certification.  The department shall, in coordination with the 
7Department of Health and Human Services, identify best practices for employers to 
8support individuals with substance use disorders. The department shall develop criteria 
9for certification of an employer and shall develop a process for an employer to apply to 
10the department for certification. The department shall certify an employer who 
11demonstrates that the employer has met the department's criteria for working with 
12employees with substance use disorders.
132.  Online database.  The department shall create a publicly accessible online 
14database of employers certified by the department under this section.
153.  Rules.  The department shall adopt rules to implement this section. Rules adopted 
16pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, 
17subchapter 2-A.
18SUMMARY
19This bill directs the Department of Labor to establish a process to certify employers 
20as having met the criteria established by the department for working with employees with 
21substance use disorders. The bill also directs the department to create a publicly 
22accessible database of certified employers.
