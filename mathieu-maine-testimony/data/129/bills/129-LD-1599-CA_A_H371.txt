Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill authorizes cettain agencies within the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation (DPFR) to lower 
or suspend certain fees specified in law by order, including licensing and other fees collected from regulated 
professions and industries. Any reduction to these fees from current levels will decrease total revenue collected by 
DPFR.
