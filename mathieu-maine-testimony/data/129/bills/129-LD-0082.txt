4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to implement recommendations relating to the bond requirements 
6for public guardians and conservators under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 18-C, 
7section 5-710.  The recommendations must be based on a review of the bond 
8requirements to determine whether the requirements should be changed.
