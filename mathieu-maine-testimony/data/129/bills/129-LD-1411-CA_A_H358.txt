1 SUMMARY 
2 This amendment requires the state workforce development plan to be submitted to the 
3 joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over labor matters at the 
4 same time the plan is posted for public comment pursuant to the federal Workforce 
5 Innovation and Opportunity Act. It requires that the appointments of certain members of 
6 the State Workforce Board are subject to review by the joint standing committee of the 
7 Legislature having jurisdiction over labor matters and to confirmation by the Legislature, 
8 but clarifies that members such as the Governor or commissioners of state agencies and 
9 county commissioners are not required to be confirmed by the Legislature. It specifies 
10 that when the term of a member expires, that member remains on the board until 
11 replaced. Lastly, it requires the Governor to make federal funds available to the local 
12 workforce boards to be used to implement the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act 
13 within 30 days after the date funds are made available to the Governor and in accordance 
14 with state procurement rules and the federal Cash Management Improvement Act of 
15 1990.
