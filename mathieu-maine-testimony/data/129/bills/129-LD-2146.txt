15SUMMARY
16This bill implements the recommendations of the Board of Dental Practice that were 
17included in the report required by Public Law 2019, chapter 388.  The bill makes the 
18following changes.
191.  It makes changes to the definitions of "direct supervision" and "general 
20supervision" and makes corresponding changes to the activities that may be performed 
21under direct or general supervision by a person licensed by the Board of Dental Practice.
222.  It makes changes to provisions governing the delegation authority of dentists to 
23unlicensed persons and to persons licensed by the Board of Dental Practice.
243.  It adds a definition of "teledentistry" and authorizes oral health care services and 
25procedures to be provided through teledentistry in accordance with rules adopted by the 
26Board of Dental Practice.
274.  It directs the Board of Dental Practice to recommend changes to the scope of 
28practice for dental hygienists and dental therapists for the purpose of aligning current 
29practice settings and reflecting advancements in the profession and technology. The 
30Board of Dental Practice is required to submit its recommendations to the joint standing 
31committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over health coverage, insurance and 
32financial services matters no later than February 1, 2021. The joint standing committee 
33may report out a bill to the First Regular Session of the 130th Legislature based on the 
34board's recommendations.
