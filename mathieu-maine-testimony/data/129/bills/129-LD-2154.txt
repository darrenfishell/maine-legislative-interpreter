18SUMMARY
19This bill implements the recommendations of the stakeholder group established 
20pursuant to Resolve 2019, chapter 41.  The bill removes the asset tests for the elderly 
21low-cost drug program, the Medicare savings program, the statewide food supplement 
22program and the Temporary Assistance for Needy Families program to the extent 
23allowable under federal law.
