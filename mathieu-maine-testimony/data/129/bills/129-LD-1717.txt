21SUMMARY
22This bill changes the inland fisheries and wildlife laws to:
231.  Define the terms "airbow," "crossbow" and "hand-held bow" or "bow" and 
24establish an open season for hunting deer with an airbow during the open firearm season 
25on deer;
262.  Specify that lifetime license holders must meet eligibility requirements for hunting 
27with bows and crossbows;
283.  Make technical and grammatical corrections to the law regarding having a loaded 
29firearm or crossbow in a motor vehicle or hunting or shooting from a motor vehicle or 
30motorboat;
314.  Repeal outdated language regarding carrying a handgun during the regular 
32archery-only season on deer;
335.  Clarify that it is only beaver, and no other species, that may be trapped by the 
34holder of a nonresident trapping license who is not a citizen of the United States;
356.  Require the court to impose a fine of $20 for each quart of smelts rather than for 
36each fish unlawfully possessed over the limit adopted by the Commissioner of Inland 
37Fisheries and Wildlife by rule; and

3"abandoned ice-fishing shack."
