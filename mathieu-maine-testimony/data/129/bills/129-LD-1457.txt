3SUMMARY
4This bill changes language in the Maine Revised Statutes to make certain references 
5to the Governor and the Chief Justice of the Supreme Judicial Court and associate justices 
6gender neutral.  The Revisor of Statutes is directed to review the Maine Revised Statutes 
7to determine where further references to the Governor and the Chief Justice of the 
8Supreme Judicial Court and associate justices need to be made gender neutral and to 
9implement these revisions when updating, publishing or republishing the statutes.
