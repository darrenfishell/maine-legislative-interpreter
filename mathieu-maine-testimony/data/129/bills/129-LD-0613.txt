Page 1 - 129LR1010(01)-11Sec. 1.  Adoption of rules.  Resolved:  That, by January 1, 2020, the Department 
2of Health and Human Services shall adopt rules pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, 
3Title 34-B, section 3011 for the Bridging Rental Assistance Program in order to ensure 
4fairness, equity and access to the program for those persons with mental illness who 
5qualify for the program.
6Sec. 2.  Delay in adoption of rules.  Resolved:  That if the Department of 
7Health and Human Services anticipates a delay in the adoption of rules under section 1, 
8the department shall submit a report by January 1, 2020 to the Joint Standing Committee 
9on Health and Human Services detailing the reason for the delay and the progress made.
10SUMMARY
11This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to adopt rules for 
12the Bridging Rental Assistance Program in order to ensure fairness, equity and access to 
13the program for those persons with mental illness who qualify for the program.  The 
14department is required to submit a report to the Joint Standing Committee on Health and 
15Human Services if the department anticipates a delay in the adoption of the rules.
