3ยง1697.  Compensation for propane upon termination of agreement
4Upon the termination of an agreement between a propane dealer and a customer, the 
5propane dealer shall compensate the customer for the propane remaining in the customer's 
6tank at a rate equal to the market price of the propane on the date that the agreement is 
7terminated or the price that the customer paid for the propane, whichever is less, if the 
8customer has paid for the propane, the amount of propane is more than 5 gallons and the 
9customer has not received compensation pursuant to this section in the previous 12 
10months.
11SUMMARY
12This bill provides that upon the termination of an agreement between a propane 
13dealer and a customer, the propane dealer must compensate the customer for the propane 
14remaining in the customer's tank at a rate equal to the market price of the propane on the 
15date that the agreement is terminated or the price that the customer paid for the propane, 
16whichever is less, if the customer has paid for the propane, the amount of propane is more 
17than 5 gallons and the customer has not received such compensation in the previous 12 
18months.
