17§51.  Block grant set aside; federally recognized Indian nations, tribes and bands in 
18the State
19Notwithstanding section 5327 or any other provision of law to the contrary, for each 
20block grant received by the department from the Federal Government, the department 
21shall annually set aside an amount equal to at least 12% of the total amount of the block 
22grant to be awarded to federally recognized Indian nations, tribes and bands in the State.  
23The administration of such grants is by the tribal district.  The department shall adopt 
24rules to implement this section.  Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine 
25technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.  For purposes of this 
26section, "tribal district" has the same meaning as in section 411, subsection 12.' 
27Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
28section number to read consecutively.
