3SUMMARY
4This amendment increases from 2 to 4 the number of public members of the 
5commission appointed by the President of the Senate and includes a representative of 
6statewide agricultural interests and one that is in the building trades.  It also increases 
7from 2 to 4 the number of public members appointed by the Speaker of the House and 
8includes one that is a residential developer and one that is a low-income or middle-
9income renter or homeowner.  It provides that, instead of being required to appoint a 
10public member representing a regional planning association, the Speaker of the House is 
11required to appoint a public member representing a regional planning association or a 
12statewide organization that advocates for smart growth policies and projects.  Lastly, it 
13requires that the report be submitted to the Joint Standing Committee on Labor and 
14Housing instead of to the entire Legislature.
