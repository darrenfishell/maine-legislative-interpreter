28Sec. 5.  Duties.  Resolved:  That the committee shall explore providing a more 
29affordable health insurance option to middle-income individuals and small businesses.  
30The committee shall:
311.  Contact relevant government representatives in all New England states and inquire 
32as to their interest in working together to create a nonprofit health insurance cooperative.  
33If fewer than 3 states respond with interest, the committee shall reach out to Mid-Atlantic 
34states; and

4Sec. 6.  Staff assistance.  Resolved:  That the Department of Health and Human 
5Services shall provide necessary staffing services to the committee.
6Sec. 7.  Report.  Resolved:  That, no later than December 1, 2021, the committee

8proposal under section 5, for presentation to the Second Regular Session of the 130th 
9Legislature.
10SUMMARY
11This resolve establishes the Committee To Explore Regional Cooperation for 
12Affordable Health Insurance, which is required to study providing a more affordable 
13health insurance option to middle-income individuals and small businesses, including 
14convening a multistate conference to draft a proposal for a nonprofit health insurance 
15cooperative.
