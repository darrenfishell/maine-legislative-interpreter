8SUMMARY
9This bill requires that the Commissioner of Public Safety appoint a technical codes 
10coordinator, an office specialist and a building codes trainer. It also requires that 
11municipalities impose a $3 surcharge on building permits and remit those funds to the 
12Department of Public Safety to fund the Uniform Building Codes and Standards Fund.
