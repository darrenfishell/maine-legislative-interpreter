25 SUMMARY 
26 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, amends the bill as 
27 follows. 
28 1. It amends program staffing requirements to allow the Commissioner of 
29 Environmental Protection to approve a lesser staffing requirement than the minimum 
30 half-time employee required under the bill. 
31 2. It authorizes the Department of Environmental Protection to recommend in its 
32 annual report to the Legislature that a program be required to provide financial incentives 
33 or a deposit and refund system. 
34 3. It makes a number of other technical changes to the bill.
