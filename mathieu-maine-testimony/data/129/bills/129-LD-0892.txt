8SUMMARY
9This bill repeals the service provider tax for so-called MaineCare Appendix C private 
10nonmedical institutions, which are residential care facilities maintained wholly or partly 
11for the purpose of providing residents with medical and remedial treatment services.  The 
12service provider tax continues to apply to private nonmedical institution services that are 
13provided by MaineCare Appendix B, D, E or F private nonmedical institutions.
