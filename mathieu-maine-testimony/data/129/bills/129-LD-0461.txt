22SUMMARY
23This bill provides additional funding to the Maine State Grant Program in the 2020-
242021 biennium.  It requires the Finance Authority of Maine to expand the Maine State 
25Grant Program to enable more low-income residents of the State to attend an institution 
26of higher education within the State.  It also requires the Finance Authority of Maine and 
27the Department of Health and Human Services to jointly examine ways to maximize the 
28delivery of federally funded support services to postsecondary students, including, but not 
29limited to, the federal supplemental nutrition assistance program.GENERAL FUND 2019-20 2020-21
