16SUMMARY
17Retirement benefits under state, local or federal government retirement plans that are 
18based on employment compensation for which contributions are not made to the federal 
19Social Security system result in reductions in the amount of Social Security benefits that a 
20retiree is eligible to receive under the federal Windfall Elimination Provision.  This bill 
21provides an income tax exemption for those retirement benefits.
