17 'Sec. 1.  Restorative justice pilot program.  Resolved:  That the Commissioner 
18 of Corrections shall, after consultation with advocates for the principles and practice of 
19 restorative justice, advocates for victims and offenders and representatives of prosecutors

21 incarcerated in all of Maine's correctional facilities.  The commissioner shall report to the 
22 Legislature by December 1, 2020 on the pilot program.'
23SUMMARY
24 This amendment replaces the resolve.  The amendment directs the Commissioner of 
25 Corrections, after consultation with advocates for the principles and practice of restorative 
26 justice, advocates for victims and offenders and representatives of prosecutors and defense 
27 attorneys, to establish a restorative justice pilot program to serve adults incarcerated in all 
28 of Maine's correctional facilities.  The amendment requires the commissioner to report to 
29 the Legislature by December 1, 2020 on the pilot program.
