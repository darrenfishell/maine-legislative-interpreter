12SUMMARY
13This bill amends the child care services provisions in the following ways:
141.  It establishes graduated quality differential rates for steps 2 to 4 in the 4-step child 
15care quality rating system currently required by law;
162.  It requires that contracts with providers of child care services prioritize infants, 
17toddlers and preschool children up to 4 years of age in a variety of ways; and
183.  It directs the Department of Health and Human Services to develop a shared 
19services program for providers of child care services to realize efficiencies and achieve 
20financial sustainability by sharing administrative and program services and costs.
