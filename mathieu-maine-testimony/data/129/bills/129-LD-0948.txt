2Sec. 1.  17-A MRSA §402, sub-§4, ¶F is enacted to read:
3F.  A municipality or political subdivision of the State may not enact an ordinance, 
4law or rule regulating the posting of property by marking with signs or paint that is 
5different from the provisions regulating the posting of property by marking with signs 
6or paint in this subsection.
7SUMMARY
8This bill provides that a municipality or political subdivision of the State may not 
9enact an ordinance, law or rule regulating the posting of property by marking with signs 
10or paint that is different from the provisions in state law regulating the posting of property 
11by marking with signs or paint.
