10SUMMARY
11This bill adds a new requirement for the renewal of professional licenses for 
12psychologists, social workers and licensed clinical professional counselors that each must 
13show proof upon renewal that the licensee has completed a minimum of 6 hours of course 
14work every 10 years in suicide prevention, evidence-based suicide risk assessment or the 
15treatment and management of suicidal persons, including knowledge of community 
16resources and cultural factors.  These courses may be used to satisfy the licensee's other 
17continuing education requirements.  The bill also changes, from January 1, 2020 to 
18January 1, 2022, the date that licensed clinical professional counselors must begin 
19demonstrating successful completion of a minimum of 12 hours of course work in family 
20or intimate partner violence.
