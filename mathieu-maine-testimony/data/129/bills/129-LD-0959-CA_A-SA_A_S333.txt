This amendment.reduces appropriations in the bill by $75,000. As amended, the bill includes annual appropriations 
to the Department of Environmental Protection of $150,000 starting in fiscal year 2019-20 for funding for grants to 
the Lake Stewards of Maine and the Maine Lakes Society and $25,000 starting in fiscal year 2019-20 to the New 
England Interstate Water Pollution Control Commission to increase funding for pollution control training programs.
