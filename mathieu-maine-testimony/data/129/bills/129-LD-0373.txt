14SUMMARY
15This bill directs the Department of Health and Human Services to extend MaineCare 
16dental services to a person 21 years of age or older who receives support services for 
17adults with intellectual disabilities or autism spectrum disorder.  Dental services provided 
18to such a person must be the same as those provided under the MaineCare program to an 
19eligible person under 21 years of age.
