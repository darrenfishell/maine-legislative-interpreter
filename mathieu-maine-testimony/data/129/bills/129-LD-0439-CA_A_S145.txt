21 SUMMARY 
22 This amendment clarifies that the Commissioner of Health and Human Services is 
23 required to convene a task force of stakeholders to evaluate the need for long-term acute 
24 care beds in the State. It changes the reporting date for the department from December 1, 
25 2019 to January 2, 2020.
