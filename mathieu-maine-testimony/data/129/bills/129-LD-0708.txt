13Sec. 2.  12 MRSA §11154, sub-§16 is enacted to read:
1416.  Moose permit transfer; wounded service members.  A resident or nonresident 
15who is eligible to obtain a Maine hunting license or who will be eligible to obtain a 
16Maine hunting license by the opening day of the open moose season may apply for a 
17moose hunting permit that, if issued, would be issued to a wounded service member or 
18veteran. An applicant under this subsection must indicate on the application filed 
19pursuant to subsection 6 that the permit is intended to go to a wounded service member or 
20veteran. The applicant may indicate on the application the identity of the wounded service 
21member or veteran or request the commissioner choose the wounded service member or 
22veteran from a list maintained by the commissioner.  The commissioner shall maintain a 
23list of wounded service members and veterans who submit their names to the 
24commissioner for consideration for a moose hunting permit under this subsection and 
25shall choose a wounded service member or veteran from the list upon the request of an 
26applicant on a random basis. A wounded service member or veteran must meet the 
27requirements of subsection 4 to obtain a moose hunting permit under this subsection.  For 
28purposes of this subsection, "wounded service member or veteran" means a person who 
29incurred a physical or mental injury, illness or wound while serving in the Armed Forces 
30of the United States and meets the requirements of subsection 5.
31SUMMARY
32This bill allows a person eligible to participate in the public chance drawing for a 
33moose hunting permit to indicate on the application for a permit the person's intention 
34that the permit be issued to a wounded service member or veteran.  The person may 
35indicate the identity of the wounded service member or veteran or request that the 
36commissioner randomly draw the name of a wounded service member or veteran from a 
37list maintained by the commissioner of wounded service members and veterans who have 
38submitted their names for consideration to receive a moose hunting permit under this 
39process.
