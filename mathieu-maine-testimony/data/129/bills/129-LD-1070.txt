38SUMMARY
39This bill, for the purpose of reducing the incidence of domestic violence and suicides, 
40increases the excise taxes imposed on spirits, malt liquor, fortified wines and hard cider 
41and the sales tax imposed on the sale of liquor sold in establishments for consumption on 
42or off premises.
