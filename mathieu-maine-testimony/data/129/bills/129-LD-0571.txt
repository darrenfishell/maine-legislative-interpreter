7SUMMARY
8This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to initiate a service 
9development plan for commuter and passenger train service between Portland and the 
10Lewiston and Auburn area.  The plan must indicate the locations of railway stations in 
11towns along the corridor route and include an environmental impact statement for each 
12station site.  It directs the department to submit a progress report to the Joint Standing 
13Committee on Transportation by January 15, 2020.
