9SUMMARY
10This bill amends the law governing attorney's fees under the Maine Workers' 
11Compensation Act of 1992.  The bill:
121.  Requires employers to pay reasonable costs and attorney's fees if the employee 
13prevails on a disputed petition for payment of medical and related expenses or if the 
14employee prevails in an appeal that is decided by the Appellate Division or by the Law 
15Court; and 
162.  Removes language governing the specific computation of attorney's fees for lump-
17sum settlements.
