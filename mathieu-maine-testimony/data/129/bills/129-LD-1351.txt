14SUMMARY
15This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services, Maine Center for 
16Disease Control and Prevention and the Department of Education, by October 1, 2019, to 
17collaborate to revise the Department of Health and Human Services, Maine Center for 
18Disease Control and Prevention's health inspection program guidance titled "Food 
19Sharing Tables - Guidance for Schools" and disseminate the new guidance to public 
20schools in the State.
21The bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services, Maine Center for 
22Disease Control and Prevention and the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and 
23Forestry to grant public school food service programs a variance under the departments' 
24jointly adopted rules regulating food safety in order to allow public school food service 
25programs to collect food that was taken from the food service line by students and placed 
26on a share table and to redistribute that food through the food service line; to allow 
27students to take food from a share table and consume that food in the same breakfast, 
28lunch or snack period or at another time; to allow students to place unwanted, eligible 
29food items on a share table at any time during their breakfast, lunch or snack period; and 
30to allow school staff members under certain circumstances to collect and redistribute food 
31from a share table.
32The bill does not mandate the creation of share tables within schools or require 
33schools to purchase additional food or materials.
