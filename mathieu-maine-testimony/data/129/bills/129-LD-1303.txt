3SUMMARY
4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to establish the State Board of Dental Hygiene as a board affiliated 
6with the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation that is separate from, but 
7shares resources with, the Board of Dental Practice.  The State Board of Dental Hygiene 
8would regulate the licensing, practice and discipline of dental hygienists.
