SUMMARY 
This amendment changes the title of the bill and provides that the sales tax exemption 
applies to sales of watercraft to an incorporated nonprofit transportation company that has 
a written understanding with a municipality that the watercraft will be available at all 
times to transport an emergency medical services patient from an island to a licensed 
ambulance service on the mainland.
