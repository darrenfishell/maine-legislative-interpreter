This bill includes General Fund appropriations totaling $465,252 in both fiscal year 2019-20 and fiscal year 2020-21 
to provide support for each local workforce development board in the State. The bill provides that each local 
workforce development board receive an annual appropriation equal to $25 multiplied by the number of unemployed 
persons in each board's service area for the prior year. Future costs of this provision cannot be determined at this 
time.
