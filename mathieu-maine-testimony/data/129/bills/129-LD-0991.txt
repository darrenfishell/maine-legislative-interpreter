Page 1 - 129LR1447(01)-11Sec. 1.  Department of Public Safety to expunge all records relating to 
2eligible marijuana convictions and violations.  Resolved:  That, by July 1, 2020, 
3the Department of Public Safety shall review all records possessed by any state criminal 
4justice or law enforcement agency, the Department of Public Safety, Bureau of State 
5Police, State Bureau of Identification or the Federal Bureau of Investigation that contain 
6criminal history record information pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 16, 
7section 703, subsection 3 and expunge all records that relate to criminal convictions and 
8civil violations for conduct that is authorized under Title 28-B, chapter 3.  For purposes 
9of this section, "expunge" means to permanently delete or to physically destroy or 
10obliterate.
11SUMMARY
12This bill requires the Department of Public Safety to expunge, by July 1, 2020, all 
13records relating to criminal convictions and civil violations for conduct now authorized 
14by the adult use of marijuana provisions in the Maine Revised Statutes.
