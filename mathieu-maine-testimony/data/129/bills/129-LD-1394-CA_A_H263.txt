SUMMARY 
This amendment removes an inaccurate clause from the preamble. It adds a 
requirement that the Department of Administrative and Financial Services provide an 
inventory of surplus state-owned land and structures to the Maine State Housing 
Authority. The amendment also provides the Joint Standing Committee on State and 
Local Govermnent with the option to introduce a bill in the Second Regular Session of 
the 129th Legislature related to the resolve.

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This resolve directs the Department of Administrative and Financial Services (DAFS) to conduct an inventory of 
surplus state-owned land and structures and to work with the Maine State Housing Authority to determine the 
suitability of certain land and structures for development of affordable housing. Any additional costs to DAFS and the 
Maine State Housing Authority to implement the provisions of this bill are expected to be minor and can be absorbed 
within existing budgeted resources.
