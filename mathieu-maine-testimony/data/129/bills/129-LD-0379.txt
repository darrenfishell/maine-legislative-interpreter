23SUMMARY
24This bill creates the Class E crime of unlawful storage of a firearm, which a person is 
25guilty of if the person stores or leaves on premises under the person's control a loaded 
26firearm; the person knows or reasonably should know that a child is likely to gain access 
27to the loaded firearm without the permission of the child's parent or guardian; and a child 
28in fact gains access to the loaded firearm and uses the loaded firearm in a reckless or 
29threatening manner, uses the loaded firearm during the commission of a crime or 
30recklessly or negligently discharges the loaded firearm.
31The bill also requires a firearm dealer to post a warning at each purchase counter 
32regarding the need to secure firearms from unauthorized use.
