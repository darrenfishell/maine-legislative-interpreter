33SUMMARY
34This bill increases the total exemption amount under the Maine resident homestead 
35property tax exemption program from the current $20,000 to $30,000 for the property tax 
36year beginning April 1, 2020 and to $40,000 for property tax years beginning on or after 
37April 1, 2021.  This bill also increases the reimbursement rate by the State for the revenue 
38lost by a municipality due to the exemption from 62.5% to 75%, beginning with the 
392020-2021 property tax year.
