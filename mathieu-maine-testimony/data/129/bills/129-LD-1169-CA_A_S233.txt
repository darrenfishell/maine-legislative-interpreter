25 SUMMARY 
26 This amendment is the majority report of the committee. The amendment requires 
27 that required automated external defibrillators be clearly marked. The amendment 
28 removes from the requirements for pharmacies that an individual trained in the use of the 
29 defibrillator be present at all times the pharmacy is open to the public.
