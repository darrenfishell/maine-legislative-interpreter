3SUMMARY
4This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5This bill proposes to provide funding to update screening equipment at courthouses 
6located throughout the State and to replace outdated screening equipment that may be 
7causing exposure to radiation.  It proposes to establish a protocol for testing and repairing 
8existing screening equipment.  It also proposes to develop a long-range plan for testing 
9and replacing screening equipment in a timely manner.
