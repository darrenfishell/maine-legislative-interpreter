8Sec. 2.  10 MRSA §1310-H, sub-§4 is enacted to read:
94.  Reporting of overdue medical expenses on consumer report.  Notwithstanding 
10any provision of federal law, a consumer reporting agency may not report debt from 
11overdue medical expenses on a consumer's consumer report in a manner that adversely 
12affects the consumer's credit history or credit rating as long as the consumer is making 
13regular, scheduled periodic payments toward the debt.
14SUMMARY
15This bill prohibits a consumer reporting agency from reporting debt from overdue 
16medical expenses on a consumer report in a manner that adversely affects the consumer's 
17credit history or credit rating as long as that consumer is making regular, scheduled 
18periodic payments toward the debt.
