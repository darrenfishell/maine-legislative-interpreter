14SUMMARY
15This bill repeals An Act to Implement the Maine Indian Claims Settlement.  The 
16repeal does not take effect unless approved by the Houlton Band Council of the Houlton 
17Band of Maliseet Indians, the Tribal Chief and the Council of the Penobscot Nation and 
18the Joint Tribal Council of the Passamaquoddy Tribe within 90 days after the 
19adjournment of the First Regular Session of the 129th Legislature.
