Fiscal Note 
Vehicle already provided 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill authorizes the Department of Defense, Veterans and Emergency Management to provide a vehicle to a senior 
facility manager position. This position is already being provided with a vehicle, which is included in the department's 
budget at a cost of $3,600 per year. The purpose of the bill is to put language into statute to clarify that this position 
can have the use of a vehicle.
