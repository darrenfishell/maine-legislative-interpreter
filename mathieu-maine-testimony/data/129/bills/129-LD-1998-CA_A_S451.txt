Fiscal Detail and NotesFiscal Note
This bill includes Other Special Revenue Funds dealloca tions of $4,925,000 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $5,000,000 in 
fiscal year 2020-21 to the Waste Motor Oil Disposal Site  Remediation Program within  the Finance Authority of 
Maine (FAME) due to the repeal of the program and the prem ium on motor vehicle oil sold or  distributed in the State. 
The bill requires FAME to transfer the balance remaining in the Waste Motor Oil Revenue Fund to the Uncontrolled 
Sites Fund within the Department of Environmental Prot ection (DEP) by June 30, 2020,  except that FAME shall 
reserve $65,000 for payment due in 2020 under the motor vehicle oil premium reimbursement program and $10,000 for the cost of administering the fund in 2020. The amount  available to transfer is estimated to be $3,466,094.129th MAINE LEGISLATURE
