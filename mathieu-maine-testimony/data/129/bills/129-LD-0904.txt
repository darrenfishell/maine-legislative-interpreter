13SUMMARY
14This bill requires the Commissioner of Professional and Financial Regulation to 
15conduct an assessment pursuant to the sunrise review requirements in the Maine Revised 
16Statutes, Title 32, chapter 1-A, subchapter 2 of the licensing of heavy equipment 
17operators.  The bill requires the commissioner to submit a report to the Joint Standing 
18Committee on Innovation, Development, Economic Advancement and Business and 
19allows the committee to submit legislation to the Second Regular Session of the 129th 
20Legislature.
