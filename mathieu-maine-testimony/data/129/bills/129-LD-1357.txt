13SUMMARY
14This bill amends the law regarding the number of agency liquor stores allowed in a 
15municipality from the number being determined based upon the population of the 
16municipality to being determined by the municipality, with existing agency liquor store 
17licenses grandfathered if the municipality authorizes a number of agency liquor stores 
18less than the number of operating liquor stores previously licensed by the Department of 
19Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery 
20Operations or the municipality does not make a determination.  This bill also requires an 
21applicant for an agency liquor store license to be a business licensed in the municipality 
22in which the agency liquor store will be located that has been in existence for at least the 
233 years immediately prior to application and that has been in good standing with every 
24agency of the State for the 3 years immediately prior to application.
