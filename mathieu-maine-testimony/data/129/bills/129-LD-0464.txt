25Notwithstanding any other limitation on the time frame for filing a request for a due 
26process hearing, an action against a school administrative unit to recover the costs of a 
27unilateral special education placement may be commenced by a parent, surrogate parent 
28or guardian only by requesting a due process hearing pursuant to this section within 90 
29days of the unilateral special education placement.  For the purposes of this subsection, 
30"unilateral special education placement" means the unilateral placement of a student by a 
31parent, surrogate parent or guardian in a private school.
32SUMMARY
33This bill removes a requirement that rules adopted by the Commissioner of Education 
34governing due process hearings include a maximum period within which due process 
35hearings and appeals may be requested.  It provides that an action against a school 
36administrative unit to recover the costs of a unilateral special education placement in a 
37private school may be commenced only by requesting a due process hearing within 90 
Page 2 - 129LR0669(01)-11days of the placement.  It also provides that rules adopted by the Commissioner of 
2Education governing the procedures for conducting due process hearings must include 
3procedures for discovery, including rules for the production of documents.
