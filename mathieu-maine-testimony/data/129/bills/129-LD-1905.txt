33Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
34legislation takes effect when approved.
35SUMMARY
36This bill allows a person 65 years of age or older and, under certain circumstances, a 
37person with a permanent physical disability to take an antlerless deer with a crossbow 
38without an antlerless deer permit during the open archery season on deer.
