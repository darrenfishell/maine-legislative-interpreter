41.  Information to consumers.  The department shall implement a program that 
5provides to consumers information regarding live lobsters sold in the State.  The 
6information, which may be conveyed through labeling, must inform a consumer whether 
7the lobster was:
8A.  Stored in a tube or other confined compartment that does not allow the lobster to 
9move freely within the tube or compartment;
10B.  Caught or landed in a location other than in this State or the waters of this State 
11and, if so, where; and
12C.  Stored in a lobster pound for more than 7 days.
132.  Rules.  The department shall adopt rules to implement this section.  Rules adopted 
14pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, 
15subchapter 2-A.
16SUMMARY
17This bill directs the Department of Marine Resources to implement a program to 
18provide to consumers information regarding live lobsters sold in the State.  The 
19information, which may be conveyed through labeling, must convey to the consumer 
20certain information regarding the location in which the lobster was landed and the method 
21and length of time of storage.
