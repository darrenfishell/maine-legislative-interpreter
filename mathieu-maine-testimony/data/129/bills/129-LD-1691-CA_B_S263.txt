6 SUMMARY 
7 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, strikes and replaces 
8 the bill. The amendment prohibits the aerial application of glyphosate or other synthetic 
9 herbicides for the purpose of silviculture, including reforestation, regeneration or 
10 vegetation control after a timber harvest.
