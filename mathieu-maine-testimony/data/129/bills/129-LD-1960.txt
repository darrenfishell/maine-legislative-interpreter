20SUMMARY
21This bill makes communications between a bargaining agent and a municipal or state 
22employee confidential in proceedings before the Maine Labor Relations Board to the 
23same extent that such communications would be subject to the lawyer-client privilege 
24under the Maine Rules of Evidence if the bargaining agent were a lawyer.
