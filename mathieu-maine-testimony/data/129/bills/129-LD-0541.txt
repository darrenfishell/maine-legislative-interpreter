Page 1 - 129LR0836(01)-11Sec. 1.  Food sharing policy.  Resolved:  That, by January 1, 2020, the 
2Department of Education shall develop a school food sharing policy to encourage schools 
3and food banks to work together to collect whole and packaged school cafeteria surplus 
4or leftover food and share it with the community.  In developing the policy, the 
5department shall consult the food recovery database maintained by the Department of 
6Environmental Protection pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 38, section 
72137-A, which includes materials relating to the development and implementation of 
8programs for the sharing of surplus or leftover food, including, but not limited to, share 
9tables and food donation practices and programs.
10SUMMARY
11This resolve directs the Department of Education to develop a school food sharing 
12policy to encourage schools and food banks to work together to collect whole and 
13packaged school cafeteria surplus or leftover food and share it with the community.
