11SUMMARY
12Under current law, the Department of Health and Human Services has a claim against 
13the estate of a MaineCare recipient when, after the death of the recipient, it is determined 
14that the recipient was 55 years of age or older when the person received MaineCare 
15assistance.  This bill provides that the department has a claim against the estate only for 
16MaineCare assistance received by the person that was for nursing facility services, home 
17and community-based services or related hospital and prescription drug services.
