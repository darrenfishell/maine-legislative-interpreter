17SUMMARY
18This bill requires that a district attorney be elected on a nonpartisan ballot and that a 
19vacancy in the office must be filled without regard to political affiliation.  A candidate for 
20district attorney must qualify by filing a nomination petition, although the candidate is not 
21required to unenroll from a party.  A candidate for district attorney may not include party 
22affiliation on the nomination petition.
