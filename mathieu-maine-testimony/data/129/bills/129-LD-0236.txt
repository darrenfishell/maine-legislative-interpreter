26additional subsidy a school administrative unit board does not raise 100% of the 
27required local contribution pursuant to section 15690, section 1, the commissioner 
28shall waive the required proration of the state share pursuant to section 15690, 
29subsection 1, paragraph C.
30SUMMARY
31This bill requires the warrant presented to the legislative body of the regional school 
32unit to include an article describing how unanticipated school aid will be used.  In cases 
33where the adjustment prevents the regional school unit board from raising 100% of the 
34required local share, the Commissioner of Education must waive the required proration of 
35the state share.
