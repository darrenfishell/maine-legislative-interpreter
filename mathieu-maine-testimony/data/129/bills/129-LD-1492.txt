6SUMMARY
7This bill amends the Maine Criminal Code provisions regarding scheduled drugs by 
8relaxing or eliminating provisions regarding trafficking and furnishing, unlawful 
9possession of scheduled drugs, trafficking, furnishing or possession of hypodermic 
10apparatuses, use of drug paraphernalia and trafficking or furnishing of imitation drugs.
