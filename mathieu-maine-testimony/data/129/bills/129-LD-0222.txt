19SUMMARY
20This bill prohibits a nursery school, a certified family child care provider, a licensed 
21child care facility or a person who provides day care in that person's home for one or 2 
22children whose care is paid for by state or federal funds from charging any fee for any 
23period of time the provider is closed because the provider is on vacation.  It prohibits the 
24Department of Health and Human Services from reimbursing providers of child care 
25services or recipients for child care services for any period of time the provider is closed 
26because the provider is on vacation.
