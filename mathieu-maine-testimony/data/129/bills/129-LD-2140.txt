22SUMMARY
23PART A
24This Part makes appropriations and allocations.
25PART B
26This Part amends a provision in Public Law 2019, chapter 415, Part C to require the 
27State Controller, at the end of fiscal years 2018-19 and 2019-20, to carry forward any 
28unexpended balances in the Personal Services and All Other line categories in the 
29Department of Secretary of State, Administration - Motor Vehicles program, after all 
30financial commitments for salary, benefits and other obligations and budgetary 
31adjustments have been made, to the All Other line category in the Department of 
32Secretary of State, Administration - Motor Vehicles program to be used for the 
33procurement and implementation of hardware and software for computer modernization 
34projects.  Public Law 2019, chapter 415, Part C authorized the balances to be used for the 
35procurement and implementation of an automated driver's license testing system.
36PART C
37This Part amends a provision in Public Law 2019, chapter 415, Part G to authorize 
38the Commissioner of Transportation, for fiscal years ending June 30, 2020 and June 30,
