2Sec. 1.  17 MRSA §1832, sub-§1, as amended by PL 2017, c. 284, Pt. KKKKK, 
3§15, is further amended to read:
41.  License or registration required.  A Except as provided in subsection 9, a 
5person, firm, corporation, association or organization may not hold, conduct or operate a 
6game of chance without a license issued by or, as applicable, without registering with the 
7Gambling Control Unit in accordance with this section.  A license is not required when a 
8game of chance constitutes social gambling.
9Sec. 2.  17 MRSA §1832, sub-§9 is enacted to read:
109.  Exception for cribbage tournaments conducted by fraternal and veterans' 
11organizations.  The following organizations may conduct a cribbage tournament in 
12which cash prizes are not awarded without obtaining a license from or registering with 
13the Gambling Control Unit:
14A.  A nonprofit fraternal or veterans' organization that has been in existence and 
15founded, chartered or organized in the State for at least 2 consecutive years prior to 
16operating the cribbage tournament; and
17B.  An auxiliary organization associated with an organization described in paragraph 
18A that has been in existence for a least 2 consecutive years prior to operating the 
19cribbage tournament.
20For the purposes of this subsection, "cribbage" has the same meaning as in section 1836, 
21subsection 4-B.
22SUMMARY
23This bill authorizes nonprofit fraternal and veterans' organizations to conduct 
24cribbage tournaments in which no cash prizes are awarded without obtaining a license 
25from or registering with the Department of Public Safety, Gambling Control Unit.
