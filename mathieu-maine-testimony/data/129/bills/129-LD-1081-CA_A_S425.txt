1 SUMMARY 
2 This amendment changes the title of and replaces the bill. Under the motor vehicle 
3 laws, smoking in a motor vehicle by the operator or a passenger is prohibited when a 
4 person who has not attained 16 years of age is present in the motor vehicle. The 
5 amendment amends that prohibition to provide that smoking in a motor vehicle by the 
6 operator or a passenger is prohibited when a minor is present in the motor vehicle. Under 
7 the motor vehicle laws, "minor" is defmed as a person who has not attained 18 years of 
8 age.
