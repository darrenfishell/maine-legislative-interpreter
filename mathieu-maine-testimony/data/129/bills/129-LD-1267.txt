Page 2 - 129LR1342(01)-11SUMMARY
2This bill allows career and technical education centers and regions to award credit 
3toward graduation requirements for courses and programs that are approved by the 
4Commissioner of Education.  The commissioner is required to establish the credit to be 
5awarded and the content area toward which the school administrative unit must apply the 
6credit.  Finally, the commissioner must adopt routine technical rules that develop a 
7procedure for career and technical education centers and regions to submit courses and 
8programs for approval and criteria in each content area that the course or program must 
9meet to be approved.
