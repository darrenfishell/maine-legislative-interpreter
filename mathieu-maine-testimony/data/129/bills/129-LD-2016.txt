26SUMMARY
27This bill provides that a student code of conduct may not prescribe suspension, 
28expulsion or the withholding of recess as a consequence of a violation of the student code 
29of conduct by any student enrolled in grade 5 or below except as provided under the 
30federal Gun-Free Schools Act.  The bill also provides that any student enrolled in grade 6 
31or above who is eligible for free or reduced-price school meals is entitled to 
32representation at state expense during an expulsion proceeding.
