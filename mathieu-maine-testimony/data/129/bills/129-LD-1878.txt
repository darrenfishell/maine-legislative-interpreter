11SUMMARY
12This resolve establishes the Commission To Study the Use of Adjunct Professors in 
13Maine's Public University and College System.  The commission is directed to examine 
14and make recommendations on the use of adjunct professors in the University of Maine 
15System and the Maine Community College System, including, but not limited to, an 
16examination of recruitment and retention issues, creation of a career path for adjunct 
17professors, expansion of the role of adjunct professors in student retention and success, 
18expansion of access to health care for adjunct professors and identification of funding 
19needs for the recommendations of the commission.  The commission must report its 
20findings and recommendations to the Joint Standing Committee on Education and 
21Cultural Affairs and the Joint Standing Committee on Labor and Housing.
