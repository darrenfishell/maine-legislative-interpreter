This bill provides a sales tax exemption to nonprofits providing food or other pet supplies at no charge to owners and

fiscal year 2020-21. The bill includes a one-time General Fund appropriation of $5,000 in fiscal year 2019-20 to the 
Depaiiment of Administrative and Financial Services for computer programming costs to update the sales tax filing
