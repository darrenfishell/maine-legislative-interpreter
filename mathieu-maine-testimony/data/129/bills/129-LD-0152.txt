2Sec. 1.  22 MRSA §1578-B, sub-§1, ¶D, as enacted by PL 1987, c. 687, is 
3repealed and the following enacted in its place:
4D.  "Tobacco use" includes:
5(1) Smoking, which means carrying or having in one's possession an electronic 
6smoking device, as defined in section 1541, subsection 1-A, or a lighted cigarette, 
7cigar, pipe or other object giving off or containing any substance giving off 
8smoke; and
9(2) The use of smokeless tobacco.
10SUMMARY
11This bill includes in the prohibition against tobacco use in elementary or secondary 
12schools the possession of an electronic smoking device, which is defined as a device used 
13to deliver nicotine or any other substance intended for human consumption that may be 
14used by a person to simulate smoking through inhalation of vapor or aerosol from the 
15device, including, without limitation, a device manufactured, distributed, marketed or 
16sold as an electronic cigarette, electronic cigar, electronic pipe, electronic hookah or so-
17called vape pen.
