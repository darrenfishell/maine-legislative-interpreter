SENATE AMENDMENT" ft" to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 567, 
L.D. 762, Bill, "An Act To Bring Maine's Laws Concerning Implied Consent in 
Operating a Motor Vehicle into Compliance with Recent Opinions of the United States 
Supreme Court" 
Amend the amendment in section 5 in subsection 3 in paragraph B in the 2nd and 3rd 
lines (page 3, lines 14 and 15 in amendment) by striking out the following: "except that, 
when the person has refused or failed to submit to a blood test in the absence of a 
warrant, the refusal or failure is not admissible in evidence at trial" 
SUMMARY 
This amendment removes from the committee amendment wording regarding the 
admissibility of evidence at trial, correcting a drafting error in the committee amendment.
