11SUMMARY
12This bill allows detectives in the Office of the Attorney General to elect to participate 
13in the 1998 Special Plan of the Maine Public Employees Retirement System.  Under that 
14plan, a person may retire at 55 years of age with 10 years of creditable service or may 
15retire before 55 years of age with 25 years of creditable service and at a reduced benefit.
