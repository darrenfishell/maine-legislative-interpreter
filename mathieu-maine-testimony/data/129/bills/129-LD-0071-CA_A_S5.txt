The bill provides for an income tax deduction for certain contributions to qualified tuition programs up to $1,000 per 
designated beneficiary and would result in a loss of General Fund revenue of $950,000 and a loss of Other Special 
Revenue Funds revenue of $50,000 in fiscal year 2020-21. The bill includes a one-time General Fund appropriation to 
the Department of Administrative and Financial Services of $22,000 in fiscal year 2020-21 for computer programming 
costs.
