21SUMMARY
22This bill allows the Department of Marine Resources to establish by rule an 
23apprentice program for entry into the scallop fishery that includes an apprentice license 
24allowing an apprentice to engage in dragging for scallops under the supervision of a 
25scallop dragging license holder.  It sets the apprentice license fee at $250 and creates a 
26$250 license surcharge for a scallop dragger to sponsor an apprentice.  It limits 
27participation to residents and makes 18 years of age the minimum age to participate as an 
28apprentice.  It also changes rules to implement the scallop license limited entry system 
29from major substantive rules to routine technical rules.
