19 4. The commissioner also shall develop resources relating to instruction in the history of 
20 genocide, including the Holocaust, and make them readily available on the Department of 
21 Education's publicly accessible website. For purposes of this section, "Holocaust" is the

23 discrete groups of individuals based on race, religion, ethnicity, sexual orientation, 
24 physical disability or national origin, from January 1, 1933 to December 31, 1945, in 
25 Nazi Germany or in any European country allied with or occupied by Nazi Germany.

This bill includes a one-time General Fund appropriation of $9,000 in fiscal year 2019-20 to the Learning Systems 
program within the Department of Education for the costs associated with holding meetings with stakeholders to

studies standards and, therefore, has not requested funding in the 2020-2021 biennial budget to conduct another 
review.
