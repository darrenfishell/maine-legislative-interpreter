22Sec. 2.  39-A MRSA §213, sub-§1-B, as enacted by PL 2011, c. 647, §8, is 
23repealed.
24SUMMARY
25Current law limits to 520 weeks the duration of workers' compensation benefits for 
26partial incapacity due to injuries occurring on or after January 1, 2013.  This bill removes 
27that durational cap.
