12prohibited
131.  Definition.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, 
14"foreign national" means a foreign national as defined in 52 United States Code, Section 
1530121(b) (2020) and an entity with respect to which a foreign national holds, owns, 
16controls or otherwise has direct or indirect beneficial ownership of 50% or more of the 
17total equity, outstanding voting shares, membership units or other applicable ownership 
18interests.
192.  Contribution prohibited.  A foreign national may not make, directly or 
20indirectly, a contribution of money or anything of value to influence a referendum.
213.  Solicitation or acceptance of contribution prohibited.  A person may not 
22solicit, accept or receive a contribution described in subsection 2 from a foreign national.
234.  Expenditure prohibited.  A foreign national may not make, directly or indirectly, 
24an expenditure to influence a referendum.
255.  Participation prohibited.  A foreign national may not direct, dictate, control or 
26directly or indirectly participate in the decision-making process of any person with regard 
27to that person's activities to influence a referendum, such as decisions concerning the 
28making of contributions or expenditures to influence a referendum.
296.  Violation; penalty.  Notwithstanding section 1004, subsection 1, a person that 
30violates this section commits a civil violation for which a fine of not more than $100,000 
31or twice the amount of the contribution or expenditure involved in the violation, 
32whichever is greater, may be adjudged.
33Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
34legislation takes effect when approved.
