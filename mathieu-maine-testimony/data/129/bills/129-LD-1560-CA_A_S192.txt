11 SUMMARY 
12 This amendment limits the application of the requirement in the bill that there be net 
13 benefits to ratepayers under a reorganization to only those reorganizations that involve 
14 transfer of ownership and control of a public utility or the parent company of a public 
15 utility, rather than all reorganizations.
