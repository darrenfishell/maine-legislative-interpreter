COMMITTEE AMENDMENT .. ff,, to H.P. 1269, L.D. 1784, Bill, "An Act To 
Increase Land Permit by Rule Application Fees" 
Amend the bill by inserting after the title and before the enacting clause the 
following: 
'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and 
Whereas, the additional funds that will result from the increase in land permit by 
rule application fees proposed in this legislation are critical to sustaining current staffing 
in the Bureau of Land Resources within the Department of Environmental Protection and 
for providing necessary staff training to the staff in that bureau; and 
Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
therefore,' 
Amend the bill by inserting after section 1 the following: 
'Sec. 2. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and 
allocations are made. 
ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION, DEPARTMENT OF 
Maine Environmental Protection Fund 0421 
Initiative: Provides allocation to administer licensing and permitting programs.

This bill increases the processing fee for a Board 
of Environmental Protection permit-by-rnle application from $50 to 
$250. Based on the average number of annual permits in previous years, beginning in fiscal year 2019-20 this 
increase will generate an estimated $296,820 in additional Other Special Revenue Funds revenue for the Department 
of Environmental Protection. The bill includes a conesponding allocation to allow for the expenditure of the 
additional revenue.
