Page 2 - 129LR2338(01)-11SUMMARY
2This bill clarifies in the laws governing forest practices and forest landowner and 
3wood processor reporting requirements that the parties responsible for compliance with 
4those laws include the landowner, the landowner's designated agent, the professional 
5forester, the professional forester's employee and the person who conducts timber 
6harvesting activities.
