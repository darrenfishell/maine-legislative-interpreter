26 SUMMARY 
27 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, clarifies that a 
28 committee established to finance a Governor-elect's transition to office and inauguration 
29 must file financial disclosure statements with the Commission on Governmental Ethics 
30 and Election Practices until it not only disposes of all surplus funds but also satisfies all 
31 outstanding debts and loans. 
32 The amendment also clarifies that, when the Commission on Governmental Ethics 
33 and Election Practices decides whether to assess a penalty for violations of the law 
34 governing transition committees, it must consider the factors established in the bill both 
35 when the violation was committed by the transition committee and the penalty will be
