The bill provides a sales tax exemption for the purchase of trailers purchased by nonresidents and then driven or 
transported outside of the state and would result in a General Fund reduction in revenue of $117,049 in fiscal year 
2019-20 and $175,750 in fiscal year 2020-21. It would also result in a reduction in Local Government Fund revenue 
of $6,161 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $9,250 in fiscal year 2020-21.
