2Sec. 1.  20-A MRSA §9923, as enacted by PL 2015, c. 267, Pt. GGG, §1, is 
3amended to read:
4§9923.  Designation of community schools
5The department is authorized to designate 3 community schools established in 
6accordance with this chapter as part of a 5-year pilot project beginning in the 2016-2017 
7school year.  For the 2020-2021 school year, the department is authorized to designate 5 
8community schools established in accordance with this chapter.  Beginning in the 2021-
92022 school year, the department may biannually designate 10 additional community 
10schools established in accordance with this chapter.  The department shall annually 
11inform school administrative units of the application process and deadlines and make that 
12information and appropriate forms available on its publicly accessible website.  The 
13commissioner shall provide state funding to the school administrative units in which the 
14designated community schools are located and may employ a state community school 
15coordinator to implement this pilot project designation program.  Annual state allocations 
16for this pilot project may not exceed $150,000.
17This section is repealed July 1, 2021.

2025.  Community schools.  The commissioner may expend and disburse funds for the

23and expansion.
24This subsection is repealed July 1, 2021.
25SUMMARY
26This bill makes the Department of Education's community school pilot project 
27permanent.  The department is authorized to designate 5 community schools in the 2020-
282021 school year and 10 additional schools biannually beginning in the 2021-2022 school 
29year.  The Commissioner of Education is required to apply for available federal funds in 
30support of community school implementation and expansion.
