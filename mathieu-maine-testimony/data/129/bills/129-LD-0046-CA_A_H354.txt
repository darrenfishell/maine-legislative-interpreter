COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" A" to H.P. 47, L.D. 46, Bill, "An Act To Establish 
a Substance Use Disorder Clinic at the Cumberland County Jail" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'Resolve, To Establish the Cumberland County Jail Substance Use Disorder 
Rehabilitation Pilot Project' 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. Cumberland County jail substance use disorder rehabilitation 
pilot project. Resolved: That the Department of Corrections shall contract with the 
Cumberland County jail for a 24-month pilot project to assist in the rehabilitation of 
individuals incarcerated or detained at the Cumberland County jail who have a substance 
use disorder. The pilot project must focus on the health of the individual and must 
provide services including, but not limited to, counseling, medical services, medication­
assisted treatment, education and employment opportunities and community service. The 
pilot project must be designed to include the involvement of community-based services 
for individuals on work release. The contract must include outcome measures for 
individuals who received services in the jail as described under this section and were 
released from the jail into the community, including, but not limited to, case management 
enrollment, participation in substance use disorder treatment, employment, recidivism 
and overdose death.
