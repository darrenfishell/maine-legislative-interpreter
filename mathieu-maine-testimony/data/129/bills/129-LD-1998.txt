20SUMMARY
21This bill amends the Finance Authority of Maine's waste motor oil disposal site 
22remediation program by directing all remaining funds in the Waste Motor Oil Revenue 
23Fund to the Department of Environmental Protection's Uncontrolled Sites Fund and by 
24repealing the laws governing the program.  Except for the premium imposed on the sale 
25or distribution of motor vehicle oil, the laws governing the program are repealed June 30, 
262020.  The repeal of the premium imposed on the sale or distribution of motor vehicle oil 
27is effective when the legislation is approved.  The bill also amends other sections of law 
28by correcting cross-references and making technical changes resulting from the repeal of 
29the laws governing the program.
