20SUMMARY
21This bill changes the Maine Uniform Probate Code, taking effect July 1, 2019, in the 
22provision governing professional evaluation in an adult guardianship matter to replace the 
23term "licensed physician or psychologist" with the term "medical practitioner," the 
24definition of which is added to the provision by the bill and is the same as under the 
25Maine Revised Statutes, Title 34-B, section 3801, which provides definitions for 
26provisions governing hospitalization by psychiatric hospitals.
