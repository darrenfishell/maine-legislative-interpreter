SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the committee and replaces the bill. It 
amends the provisions of the Maine Public Employees Retirement System regarding 
compensation and service retirement benefits for retired state employees and retired 
teachers who return to service to remove the 5-year limit on a retired state employee or 
retired teacher to be restored to service.
