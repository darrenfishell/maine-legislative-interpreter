CHAPTER 1065 
KINSHIP CARE NAVIGATOR PROGRAM 
§3931. Kinship care navigator program 
1. Kinship care defined. As used in this section, unless the context otherwise 
indicates, "kinship care" means the care of a child on a temporary or permanent basis, by 
a person who is not the child's parent but who is either a relative or a person who has a 
close relationship with the child, whether or not the department was involved with the 
placement of the child with the person. 
2. Program established. The kinship care navigator program, referred to in this 
chapter as "the program," is established within the department to provide resources and 
information to persons providing kinship care to children in the State. The commissioner 
shall designate one person to administer the program. 
3. Services. The program may: 
A. Identify and coordinate existing resources available to persons providing kinship 
care to children;

27 SUMMARY 
28 This amendment, which is the unanimous report of the committee, replaces the bill. 
29 It establishes within the Department of Health and Human Services the kinship care 
30 navigator program to provide resources and information to persons providing kinship care 
31 to children in the State. It requires the Commissioner of Health and Human Services to 
32 designate one person to administer the program. It also adds an appropriations and 
33 allocations section.
