2Sec. 1.  22 MRSA §3172, sub-§1-C is enacted to read:
31-C.  Community support services.  "Community support services" means 
4rehabilitation services that promote a person's recovery and integration into the 
5community and that support the person in a living situation of that person's choice.  
6"Community support services" includes, but is not limited to, community integration 
7services, community rehabilitation services, intensive case management, daily living 
8support services, skills development services, day support services, wellness recovery 
9planning, trauma recovery, behavioral therapy and interpreter services.

20Sec. 4.  Rules; MaineCare eligibility.  The Department of Health and Human 
21Services shall amend the rules in Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter II, 
22Section 17, Community Support Services to include access to services to persons who 
23have a diagnosis of bipolar disorder, major depressive disorder, panic disorder or 
24post-traumatic stress disorder.  Pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 22, section 
253174-FF, subsection 3, paragraph I, these rules are major substantive rules as defined in 
26Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A and must be provisionally adopted by the department 
27and submitted to the Legislature no later than January 1, 2020.
28SUMMARY
29This bill directs the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rules in 
30Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter II, Section 17, Community Support 
31Services to include access to services to persons who have a diagnosis of bipolar disorder, 
32major depressive disorder, panic disorder or post-traumatic stress disorder.
