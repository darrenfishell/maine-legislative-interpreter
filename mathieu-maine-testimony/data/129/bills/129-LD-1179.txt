12SUMMARY
13This bill abolishes the coyote night hunting permit fee, allows hunters to hunt coyotes 
14on Sundays during the regular hunting season, which under current Department of Inland 
15Fisheries and Wildlife rules is from January 1st to December 31st, and eliminates any 
16license fee that may be charged in connection with coyote hunting for the 2019 regular 
17season.
