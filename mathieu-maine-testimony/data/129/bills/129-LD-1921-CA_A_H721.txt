16 SUMMARY 
17 This amendment changes the term that refers to the person who supervises a junior 
18 trapper from "adult trapper supervisor" to "junior trapper supervisor." The amendment 
19 clarifies that a person 16 years of age who obtained a junior trapping license before that 
20 person reached 16 years of age must either successfully complete a trapper education 
21 course prior to trapping or be supervised by a junior trapper supervisor while trapping 
22 after the person turns 16 years of age. 
23 Additionally, the amendment changes a statutory reference to the hunter safety course 
24 a junior hunter is required to complete if not supervised by a junior hunter supervisor.
