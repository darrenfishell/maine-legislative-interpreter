Fiscal Note 
Eliminates current biennium cost increase -General Fund 
Eliminates future biennium cost increase -School Administrative Units 
This amendment removes the provisions in the bill that require local school administrative units (SAU's) to increase 
the minimum salary for certified teachers to $40,000 beginning in fiscal year 2020-21 and that the state fund 100% of 
the incremental cost in fiscal year 2020-21, 66% of the incremental cost in fiscal year 2021-22 and 33% of the 
incremental cost in fiscal year 2022-23. 
As amended, this bill has no fiscal impact on the State.
