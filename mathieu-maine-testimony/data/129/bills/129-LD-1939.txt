Page 1 - 129LR2926(01)-11Sec. 1.  Maine Veterans' Homes staff ratios.  Resolved:  That the Board of 
2Trustees of the Maine Veterans' Homes, established by the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 
35, section 12004-G, subsection 34, shall increase the required staff-to-patient ratio at 
4Maine Veterans' Homes for long-term care to 3 staff to 15 patients.
5Sec. 2.  Certified nursing assistant rates.  Resolved:  That the Department of 
6Health and Human Services shall amend its rule 10-144, Chapter 101: MaineCare 
7Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 96, Private Duty Nursing and Personal Care 
8Services to increase reimbursement rates for certified nursing assistants by 10%.
9SUMMARY
10This resolve directs the Board of Trustees of the Maine Veterans' Homes to increase 
11the required staff-to-patient ratio at Maine Veterans' Homes for long-term care to 3 staff 
12to 15 patients and the Department of Health and Human Services to increase MaineCare 
13reimbursement rates for certified nursing assistants by 10%.
