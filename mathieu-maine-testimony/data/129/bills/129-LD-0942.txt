11SUMMARY
12Part A of this bill requires a health insurance carrier to provide coverage for 
13marijuana for medical use for a health plan enrollee who has received certification for the 
14medical use of marijuana from an authorized medical provider.  Carriers are required to 
15directly reimburse a health plan enrollee for the costs of obtaining a medical marijuana 
16certificate and the costs of medical marijuana.  The requirements apply to all individual 
17and group policies and contracts issued or renewed on or after January 1, 2020.
18Part A also applies the same requirements to individual and group accidental injury 
19and disability insurance.
20Part B of the bill requires an employer to reimburse an employee eligible for workers' 
21compensation benefits for the costs of obtaining a medical marijuana certificate and the 
22costs of medical marijuana.
