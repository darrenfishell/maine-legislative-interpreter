4further amended to read:
526.  Nonpublic offerings under 4(2).  A security offered in a nonpublic offering 
6under Section 4(2) of the federal Securities Act of 1933, 15 United States Code, Section 
777d(2) if, no later than 15 days after the first sale in this State, a notice on "Form D," as 
8promulgated by the Securities and Exchange Commission, is filed with the administrator 
9together with a consent to service of process complying with section 16611, signed by the

12need not be filed for this purpose, and if the consent to service of process on the Form D 
13is executed in a manner accepted by the Securities and Exchange Commission, it is 
14deemed to comply with the requirement in this section and section 16611, subsection 1 
15that the consent be signed. An additional nonrefundable late filing fee of $500 must be 
16paid for a filing made between 16 and 30 or more days after the first sale in this State.

22PART B
23Sec. B-1.  32 MRSA c. 99, as amended, is repealed.
24SUMMARY
25Part A corrects a late fee provision that is currently applied to certain securities 
26filings that are made between 16 and 30 days after the first sale of the securities in the 
27State.  This Part applies the late fee to any applicable filing made 16 or more days after 
28the first sale of the securities in the State.
29Part B makes a technical correction by repealing a statute enacted in 1985 governing 
30financial planners.
