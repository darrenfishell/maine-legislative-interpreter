17SUMMARY
18This bill removes from the laws governing water districts and standard water districts 
19language referencing the appointment of standard district trustees pursuant to standard 
20district charters.  It amends the law regarding appointments of trustees to provide that, if 
21sufficient and appropriate nominations for the election of trustees have not occurred,
