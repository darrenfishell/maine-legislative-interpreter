26SUMMARY
27This resolve directs the Department of Labor to establish a new permanent position in 
28the Bureau of Employment Services to expedite the processing of employer applications 
29for certification required for the federal work opportunity tax credit under Section 51 of 
30the United States Internal Revenue Code of 1986.  The resolve also requires the 
31Department of Labor to submit a report to the Joint Standing Committee on Labor and 
32Housing with the most recent information available concerning applications submitted by 
33employers, including information on the extent of any backlog in application processing, 
34by February 1, 2020.GENERAL FUND 2019-20 2020-21
