8 SUMMARY 
9 This amendment clarifies that the provisions of the bill relating to persons attending 
10 schools outside of the unorganized territory also apply to a person who resides in the 
11 unorganized territory. The amendment provides that unless a person in the unorganized 
12 territory 5 years of age or older and under 7 years of age who is enrolled in a public day 
13 school withdraws from the school, the person is required to attend the school during the 
14 time it is in session. It provides that students in the unorganized territory who are at least 
15 5 years of age and have not completed grade 6 and are enrolled in a public day school are 
16 subject to the same provisions regarding truancy as students who are at least 7 years of 
17 age and have not completed grade 6 who are required to attend a public day school. It 
18 provides that a person in the unorganized territory 5 years of age or older and under 7 
19 years of age is not required to meet the alternatives to attendance requirements set forth in 
20 the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20-A, section 3271, subsection 2.
