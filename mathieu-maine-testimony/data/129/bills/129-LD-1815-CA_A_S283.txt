19 SUMMARY 
20 This amendment transfers the administration of the statewide online advanced 
21 placement course program from the Department of Education in partnership with the 
22 University of Maine at Fort Kent to solely the University of Maine System. Accordingly, 
23 the amendment provides ongoing funds to continue the statewide online advanced 
24 placement program to the University of Maine System, instead of the Department of 
25 Education, and directs the University of Maine System to contract with the national 
26 governing body of the advanced placement course program, the College Board, to 
27 continue the program for the 2019-2020 school year. The amendment also requires the 
28 University of Maine System to submit annual reports to the joint standing committee of 
29 the Legislature having jurisdiction over education matters on the status of the statewide 
30 online advanced placement course program. 
31 This amendment adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause and adds an 
32 appropriations and allocations section.
