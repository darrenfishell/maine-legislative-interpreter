21SUMMARY
22This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, adds an emergency 
23preamble and clause, strikes and replaces the bill and does the following.
241.  It amends the school approval requirements to authorize the Department of 
25Education to adopt rules on physical restraint and seclusion.
262.  It enacts a provision to restore language that was repealed on July 1, 2020 to 
27continue to allow the Commissioner of Education to approve on a case-by-case basis 
28waivers of the age requirements to allow a student who has reached 20 years of age 
29before the start of the school year to be enrolled as a public secondary school student.
303.  It amends the parameters for the system of learning results to reflect that the career 
31and development standard is now called the life and career ready standard and the science 
32and technology standard is now called the science and engineering standard.
334.  It authorizes the Commissioner of Education to adopt major substantive rules on 
34reasonable accommodations for students who hold written certification for the medical 
35use of marijuana.
