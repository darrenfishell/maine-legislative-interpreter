SUMMARY 
This amendment is the majority report of the committee. It provides that the limit in 
the bill for the amount that may be retained under a construction contract applies only to 
private contracts and not to contracts entered into by governmental entities. It also 
specifies that the provision applies to contracts entered into on or after the effective date 
of the legislation.
