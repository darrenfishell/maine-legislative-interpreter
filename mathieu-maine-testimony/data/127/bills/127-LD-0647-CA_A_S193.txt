SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, establishes an 
ongoing working group to evaluate research on treatments for children with cognitive 
impairments and functional limitations to determine treatments that are evidence-based 
and that may be eligible for MaineCare and to submit recommendations to the 
Commissioner of Health and Human Services. The working group shall submit a report 
on findings, current level of evidence and actions taken by the Department of Health and 
Human Services to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction 
over health and human services matters by January 15th of each odd-numbered year. It 
adds a requirement for the Department of Health and Human Services to publish a list of 
evidence-based treatments for children with cognitive impairments and functional 
limitations that are reimbursed by MaineCare. The amendment retains the rule-making 
requirement in the bill.
