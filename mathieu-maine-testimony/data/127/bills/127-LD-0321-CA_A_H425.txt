SUMMARY 
2 This amendment clarifies that a person or entity may be named as nominee to hold a 
3 mortgage. This amendment provides that the provisions of the bill apply to the 
4 following: 
5 1. A discharge or partial release issued prior to the effective date of this legislation, 
6 whether made by a nominee mortgagee or by a subsequent assignee; 
7 2. A discharge or partial release issued on or subsequent to the effective date of this 
8 legislation, whether made by a nominee mortgagee or by a subsequent assignee; and 
9 3. An assignment or other instrument affecting title to a mortgaged property that is 
10 the subject of a foreclosure judgment or other legal judgment affecting title to a 
11 mortgaged property for which, as of the effective date of this legislation, either the period 
12 for appeal has run with no appeal having been filed or all rights of appeal have been
