Sec. 1.  27 MRSA §410 -A is enacted to read:  2 
§410 -A.  Private support organization  3 
1.  Designation of private support organization.  The Director of the Maine Arts 4 
Commission shall designate a no nprofit organization as the private support organization 5 
for the Maine Arts Commission. The designated organization must be incorporated as a 6 
nonprofit corporation under the laws of the State, and its sole purpose, as reflected in its 7 
bylaws, must be to or ganize and foster support for the Maine Arts Commission and its 8 
programs.  9 
2.  Member on board of directors.  The Director of the Maine Arts Commission, or 10 
the director’s designee, shall serve as a member of the private support organization's 11 
board of direc tors. 12 
3.  Plan of work.  The Director of the Maine Arts Commission shall negotiate an 13 
annual memorandum of understanding between the Maine Arts Commission and the 14 
private support organization that outlines a plan of work identifying priority projects of 15 
mutual benefit and cooperation.  16 
4.  Use of property.  The Director of the Maine Arts Commission may permit the 17 
appropriate use of fixed property, equipment and facilities of the Maine Arts Commission 18 
by the private support organization. Such use must be dire ctly in keeping with the 19 
purpose of the private support organization as set out in subsection 1 and must comply 20 
with all appropriate state policies and procedures.  21 
SUMMARY  22 
This bill requires the Director of the Maine Arts Commission to designate a nonprofi t 23 
organization as the private support organization for the Maine Arts Commission to 24 
organize and foster support for the commission and its programs.  25
