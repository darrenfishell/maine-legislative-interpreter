Sec. B-2. WorIdng group. Resolved: That the Department of Health and 
Human Services shall convene and staff a working group of 9 members, referred to in this

Sec. B-3. Pilot project criteria. Resolved: That evaluation criteria developed 
by the working group under section 2 of this Part must provide for objective assessment

project explores ways to make locally produced fruit and vegetable products without

ensure that a project participant is informed of any educational services provided by the

practical food preparation techniques for a person living within a limited budget; and be it 
further

23 SUMMARY 
24 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, removes the 
25 emergency preamble and emergency clause from the resolve. It changes the date by 
26 which the Department of Health and Human Services must request a waiver from 
27 October 1, 2015 to January 1, 2016. It requires the Department of Health and Human 
28 Services to establish the Healthy Local Foods Initiative Pilot Program to engage in efforts 
29 to seek out and support, sustain or assist eligible applicants in submitting proposals for 
30 federal grants or funding for pilot projects to improve the diets of low-income persons 
31 enrolled in a statewide food supplement program administered as part of the federal 
32 Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program. The department is required to create a 
33 working group to make recommendations concerning best practices and criteria for 
34 evaluating proposals for federal grants or funding to conduct pilot projects designed to 
35 provide incentives to participants of the federal Supplemental Nutrition Assistance 
36 Program to improve their diets, reduce obesity and support farmers and businesses in the 
37 State. The amendment directs the department to provide the matching funds required of 
38 eligible applicants, The amendment establishes within the department the Healthy Local 
39 Foods Incentive Fund, which may include funds received from hospital organizations 
40 fulfilling United States Internal Revenue Service requirements to meet community health 
41 needs,
