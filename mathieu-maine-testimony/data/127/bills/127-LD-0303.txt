SUMMARY  21 
This bill allows public disclosure of otherwise c onfidential records and information 22 
related to an executive session regarding a public employee when that employee publicly 23 
discloses information about the matter discussed in that executive session.  This bill 24 
applies to records and information held by th e Department of Administrative and 25 
Financial Services, Bureau of Human Resources for an employee of the executive or 26 
legislative branch, as well as records and information held by a county or municipality.  27
