 SUMMARY  1 
This bill prohibits a person from intentionally displaying, distributing or offering a 2 
photograph, videotape, film or digital recording of another person in a state of nudity or 3 
engaged in a sexual act if that person knows or should have known that the depicted 4 
person is 18 years of age or older, is identifiable and has not consented to the disclosure.  5 
A violation of this prohibition is a Class D crime.  6
