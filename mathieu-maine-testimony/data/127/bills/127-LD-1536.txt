SUMMARY  19 
This bill requires that all active law enforcement officers employed by the State, 20 
including game wardens, forest rangers, marine patro l officers and investigative officers 21 
and detectives in the Office of the Attorney General, the Department of the Secretary of 22 
State and the Department of Corrections, be equipped with well -fitting, contemporary 23 
ballistic vests.  This bill also requires su ch vests to be provided for dogs used in law 24 
enforcement by the State.  The cost of each vest must be paid by the political subdivision 25 
of the State employing the law enforcement officer or using the dog.  The State is 26 
permitted to accept private or public  funds to offset the cost of the vests.  27
