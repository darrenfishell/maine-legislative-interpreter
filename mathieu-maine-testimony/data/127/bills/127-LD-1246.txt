SUMMARY  18 
This bill strengthens laws regarding the manufacture and sale of methamphetamine 19 
and other drugs.  It:  20 
1.  Provides that possession of 2 grams or more of fentanyl or 90 or  more individual 21 
containers containing fentanyl constitutes "trafficking";  22 
2.  Provides that possession of at least one gram but less than 2 grams of fentanyl or 23 
at least 45 but fewer than 90 individual containers of fentanyl constitutes "furnishing";  24 
3.  Establishes the crime of aggravated unlawful operation of a clandestine 25 
methamphetamine laboratory; and  26 
4.  Prohibits the unlawful possession of a scheduled drug containing fentanyl, 7 27 
grams or more of cocaine or 2 grams or more of cocaine in the form of c ocaine base.  28
