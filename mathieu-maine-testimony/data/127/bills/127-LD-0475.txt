 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 1 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and  2 
Whereas, home and community services for adults with intellectual disabilities or 3 
autistic disorder  can exceed $100,000 annually; and  4 
Whereas, support services for adults with intellectual disabilities or autistic disorder 5 
are critical to allowing those individuals to remain in their homes for as long as possible 6 
and to delaying their entry to residenti al care facilities; and  7 
Whereas, increasing support services allows family members living with adults with 8 
intellectual disabilities or autistic disorder to maintain employment; and  9 
Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergen cy within 10 
the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 11 
immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 12 
therefore, be it  13 
Sec. 1.  Increasing reimbursement for support services for adults with 14 
intellectual disabilities or autistic disorder.  Resolved:  That, by April 1, 2015, 15 
the Department of Health and Human Services shall amend its rules in the MaineCare 16 
Benefits Manual, Chapter 101, Chapter II, Section 29:  Support Services for A dults with 17 
Intellectual Disabilities or Autistic Disorder to increase the combined annual limit for 18 
reimbursement for home support, community support, work support -individual or work 19 
support -group, assistive technology and career  planning to $47,550.  The  department 20 
shall determine the number of hours of specific support services on the basis of the 21 
increased combined annual limit.  Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine 22 
technical rules pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 3 75, subchapter 23 
2-A. 24 
Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 25 
legislation takes effect when approved.  26 
SUMMARY  27 
This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its 28 
rules in the MaineCare Benefits Manua l, Chapter 101, Chapter II, Section 29:  Support 29 
Services for Adults with Intellectual Disabilities or Autistic Disorder, by April 1, 2015, to 30 
increase the combined annual limit for service reimbursement to $47,550 from the current 31 
limit of $23,771.  32
