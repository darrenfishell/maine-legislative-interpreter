SUMMARY  14 
This bill establishes an annual $15 snowmobile trail fund sticker that must be 15 
purchased for each snowmobile registered in the State by a nonresident and that is 16 
voluntary for residents, the proceeds of which are transferred  to the Snowmobile Trail 17 
Fund of the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Bureau of Parks and 18 
Lands for snowmobile trail maintenance and grooming.  19
