SUMMARY  13 
This bill provides that a county may obtain loans up to one ten -thousandth of the 14 
county’s total property valuation instead of being limited to $10,000.  It repeals the 15 
exception f or Aroostook County whereby Aroostook County is allowed to obtain loans up 16 
to $95,000, but loans over $10,000 are limited to the purpose of building, rebuilding, 17 
altering or improving county -owned real estate and personal property in that real estate.  18
