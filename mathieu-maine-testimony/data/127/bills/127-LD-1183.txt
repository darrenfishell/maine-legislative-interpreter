SUMMARY  9 
This bill caps the tax the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, State 10 
Liquor and Lottery Commission may levy on spirits produced by a distillery in the S tate 11 
that distills 50,000 gallons per year or less at no more than 18% of the price the State 12 
pays the distillery for the spirits.  The bill requires that the distillery must use state -grown 13 
products, not including water, as a majority of the spirits' subs trate, which is the sugary 14 
mixture from which alcohol or spirits are distilled.  15
