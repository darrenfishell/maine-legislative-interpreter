SUMMARY  14 
This bill expands the list of statements that are inadmissible in proceedings under the 15 
Maine Workers' Compensation Act of 1992 to include statements obtained after 16 
contin ued questioning of an employee by an investigator, employer or an employer's 17 
representative after that employee's request to have a representative of the employee's 18 
choice present has been denied.  19
