SUMMARY  7 
This bill rescinds the statutory restriction of only agricultural or forestry purposes on 8 
2 parcels of former state land in Greenbush currently owned by Anita P. Haskell and 9 
directs the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to issue a deed to each 10 
parcel to Anita P. Haskell releasing the restriction on those parcels.  Anita P. Haskell and 11 
the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry are required to agree that the 12 
State must be held harmless regarding any and all claims related to the property and the 13 
ownership of the property by the State and any previous o wners. It also requires that the 14 
release of the deed restriction and hold harmless clause become part of the deed and bind 15 
all successors in title. The bill also requires that Anita P. Haskell pay all legal costs 16 
associated with the release of the deed res triction and the hold harmless agreement.  The 17 
bill also requires, pursuant to the Constitution of Maine, an affirmative vote of 2/3 of the 18 
members of the Legislature.  19
