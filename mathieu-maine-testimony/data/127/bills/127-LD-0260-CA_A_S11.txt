centennial. 
Sales of these plates will increase Highway Fund revenue by $9,000 in fiscal year 2015-16 and Other Special Revenue 
Funds revenue to the Department of Defense, Veterans and Emergency Management by $18,000 in fiscal year 2015-
16.
