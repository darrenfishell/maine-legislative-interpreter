35 SUMMARY 
36 This amendment authorizes, instead of requiring, the Finance Authority of Maine to 
37 establish the Maine Capital Investment Program and reduces the amount of funding 
38 required for the bill to take effect from $250,000,000 to $50,000,000. 
39 This amendment eliminates the provision of the bill that provides that investments 
40 into the Maine Capital Investment Fund may be eligible for the seed capital investment 
41 tax credit.

Fiscal Note 
Maine Capital Investment Program authorized 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Contingent upon receipt of at least $50,000,000 in appropriations, allocations or other funding sources, this bill 
authorizes the Maine Capital Investment Program within the Finance Authority of Maine and establishes a nonlapsinp 
revolving loan and equity fund to provide loans or bond funding to certain eligible business projects. The Finance 
Authority of Maine has indicated that it will be able to implement and administer the program within the provisions 
established in this legislation.
