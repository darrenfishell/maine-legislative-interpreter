5 SUMMARY 
6 This amendment provides that a recycler, salvage vehicle dealer or scrap processor 
7 may accept a vehicle without a certificate of title or certificate of salvage if certain 
8 conditions are met. 
9 1. The vehicle model year is 1995, 1996, 1997, 1998 or 1999. 
10 2. The recycler, salvage vehicle dealer or scrap processor obtains the seller's name 
11 and address and maintains the seller's name and address and vehicle identification number 
12 of the scrapped vehicle for a period of at least one year. 
13 3. The recycler, salvage vehicle dealer or scrap processor reports the destruction of 
14 the vehicle to the Secretary of State within 30 days. 
15 This amendment also provides an effective date of July 1, 20 IS.
