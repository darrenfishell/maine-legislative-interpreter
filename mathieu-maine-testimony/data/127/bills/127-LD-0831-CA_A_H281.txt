COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 565, L.D. 831, "Resolve, To Reduce 
MaineCare Spending through Targeted Prevention Services" 
Amend the resolve by striking out everything after the title and before the summary 
and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. Home-delivered meals. Resolved: That the Department of Health and 
Human Services shall file an application with the United States Department of Health and 
Human Services, Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services to request that homeÂ­
delivered meals be a reimbursable covered service under Rule Chapter 101, MaineCare 
Benefits Manual, Chapter II, Section 19. Individuals receiving the home-delivered meal 
services under the waiver must both qualify for Section 19 services and be experiencing a 
transition of care, have a debilitating or acute illness or be primarily homebound and 
unable to prepare nutritious meals. 
Sec. 2. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and 
allocations are made.

The bill includes General Fund appropriations to the Department of Health and Human Services of $55,523 in fiscal
