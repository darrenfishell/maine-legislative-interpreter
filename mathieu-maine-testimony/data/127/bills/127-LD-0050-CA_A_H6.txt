Veterans' 
Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 2 in the first paragraph in the 4th line from 
the end (page 1, line 12 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "1" and inserting the 
following: '£' 
SUMMARY 
Current law provides that a 100% disabled veteran may receive, at no fee, a 
registration certificate and set of special designating plates for one vehicle. This 
amendment provides that a 100% disabled veteran may receive, at no fee, a registration 
certificate and set of special designating plates for each of up to 2 vehicles rather than 3 
vehicles as proposed in the bill.
