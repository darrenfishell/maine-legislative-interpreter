This bill addresses the practice of rehoming children by creating a new crime 35 
prohibiting the transfer of the long -term care and custody of a child without a court order.  36 
The new crime is described as a parent, guardian or other person legally charged with the 37 
long-term care and custody of a child under 16 years of age, or a person to whom the 38
