Fiscal Note 
Contingent increased loan liability -Finance Authority of Maine 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This legislation allows the Finance Authority of Maine (FAME) to increase its maximum loan insurance amount 
through its Commercial Loan Insurance Program from $7.0 million to $10.0 million per entity. The increased 
authority is contingent upon FAME's receipt of an appropriation, allocation or other funding of at least $37.0 million.
