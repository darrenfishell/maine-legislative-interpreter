SUMMARY  17 
This bill is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural 18 
Affairs pursuant to Resolve 2013, chapter 114, section 10.  The bill incorporates changes 19 
to the educ ation statutes and rules proposed by the Commission To Strengthen the 20 
Adequacy and Equity of Certain Cost Components of the School Funding Formula that 21 
the commission's report indicated are necessary to implement its recommendations.  22 
The joint standing com mittee has not taken a position on the substance of the report 23 
or this bill, and by submitting this bill the joint standing committee is not suggesting and 24 
does not intend to suggest that it agrees or disagrees with any aspect of the 25 
recommendations and th e necessary changes to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20 -A, 26 
included in the report or this bill.  The committee is submitting the bill for the sole 27 
purpose of turning the proposals in the report into a printed bill that can be referred to the 28 
joint stan ding committee for an appropriate public hearing and subsequent processing in 29 
the normal course of business.  The joint standing committee is taking this action to 30 
ensure clarity and transparency in the legislative review of the proposals included in the 31 
report submitted by the commission.  32
