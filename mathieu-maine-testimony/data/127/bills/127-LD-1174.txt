 Sec. 1.  Rail service to City of Bangor. Resolved:  That the Department of 1

the City of Bangor and shall study:  3 
1.  The condition of, ownership of and ability to acce ss rail lines to the City of 4 
Bangor;  5 
2.  Long -term maintenance costs of passenger rail service to the City of Bangor;  6 
3.  Possible savings from reduced traffic on and use of roads;  7 
4.  The likelihood and ability of the State to draw on federal funding for rail 8 
expansion;  9 
5.  The economic benefits derived from expanded passenger rail service; and  10 
6.  Possible rail expansion options beyond the City of Bangor in the future including 11 
seasonal and excursion routes.  12
