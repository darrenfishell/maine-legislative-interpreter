SUMMARY  16 
This bill repeals the definition of the Coordinator of the Natural Areas Program and 17 
removes the Coordinator of the Natural Areas Program in the Department of Agriculture, 18 
Conservation and Forestry as a member of the Maine Outdoor Heritage Fund Board and 19 
replaces that member with the Commissioner of Marine Resources.  20
