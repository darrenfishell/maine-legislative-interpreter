The Municipal Veterans' Graves Fund, referred to in this section as "the fund," is 4 
established as a nonlapsing,  interest -bearing fund administered by the bureau.  The fund 5 
consists of appropriations and allocations for inclusion into the fund, gifts and grants 6 
from outside sources and interest from investments of the fund as the bureau determines 7 
appropriate.  The bureau shall disburse grants from the fund to municipalities for the 8 
purpose of maintaining veterans' graves under Title 13, section 1101 on an as -needed 9 
basis as determined by the bureau.  The bureau shall adopt routine technical rules as 10 
defined in Title  5, chapter 375, subchapter 2 -A to carry out the purposes of this section.  11

SUMMARY  25 
This bill creates the Municipal Veterans' Graves Fund, administered by the 26 
Department of Defense, Veterans and Emergency Management, Bureau of Maine 27 
Veterans' Services , and appropriates $1,000,000 to provide grants to municipalities for 28 
the maintenance of veterans' graves in municipal cemeteries.  29
