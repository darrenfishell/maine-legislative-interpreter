COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "R" to H.P. 303, L.D. 464, Bill, "An Act To

Amend the bill in section 3 in the last indented paragraph in the 4th and 5th lines 
(page 1, lines 40 and 41 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "and assessment beginning 
with the 2016-2017 school year" 
Amend the bill in section 3 in the last indented paragraph in the next to the last line 
(page 2, line 2 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "in 2016" and inserting the 
following: 'for the Second Regular Session of the 127th Legislature' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment removes the requirement that the assessment of the Next Generation 
Science Standards for kindergarten to grade 12 must be implemented beginning with the 
2016-2017 school year. The amendment also changes the language to clarify that the 
amendments to the Department of Education rules chapters must be provisionally adopted 
and submitted to the Legislature for legislative review during the Second Regular Session 
of the 127th Legislature.
