 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  26 MRSA §773 -A is enacted to read:  2 
§773 -A.  Minors; refereeing at youth athletic programs  3 
A minor 12 years of age or older may work for a stipend as a referee, umpire or 4 
official in a y outh athletic program pursuant to the requirements under this article, as long 5 
as the minor is a referee, umpire or official for an age group younger than the minor’s 6 
own age and an adult representing the youth athletic program is on the premises where 7 
the athletic event is occurring.  8 
SUMMARY  9 
This bill allows a minor 12 years of age or older to referee, umpire or officiate in a 10 
youth athletic program for a stipend if the minor referees, umpires or officiates for an age 11 
group younger than the minor’s own age  and an adult representing the youth athletic 12 
program is on the premises.  13
