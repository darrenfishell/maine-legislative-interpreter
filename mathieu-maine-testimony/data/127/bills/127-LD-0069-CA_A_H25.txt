The bill includes a Highway Fund allocation of $49,044 in fiscal year 2016-17 to the Department of Public Safety for 
conducting blood tests to determine the blood alcohol level and the presence of any drug or drug metabolite for 
accidents involving a fatality. 
The Department of Health and Human Services will receive additional Other Special Revenue Funds revenue of 
$41,175 annually beginning in FY 2016-17 to test blood samples obtained from the operators of motor vehicles 
involved in accidents that result in, or may result in, death to determine the blood alcohol level and the presence of 
any drug or drug metabolite and report results to the Bureau of Highway Safety.
