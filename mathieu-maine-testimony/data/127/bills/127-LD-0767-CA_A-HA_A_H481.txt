SUMMARY 
Current law requires that a qualified employee in a Pine Tree Development Zone or 
under the Maine Employment Tax Increment Financing Act be paid a wage greater than 
the annual per capita personal income in the county in which the qualified employee is 
employed. This amendment changes this requirement to require that such an employee in 
Aroostook County or Washington County be paid a weekly wage greater than the average 
weekly wage in certain counties. With respect to Aroostook and Washington counties, in
