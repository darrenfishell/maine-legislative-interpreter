SUMMARY  12 
The purpose of this bill is to ensure that Maine is in compliance with 23 United States 13 
Code, Section 159(a)(3)(A), a federal law that requires the withholding of federal fun ds 14 
unless a state provides for a mandatory 6 -month revocation or suspension of the driver's 15 
license of a person convicted of violating the federal Controlled Substances Act or a drug 16 
offense.  17 
This bill:  18 
1.  Requires a court to suspend for a minimum of 6 mo nths the driver's license of a 19 
person convicted of a crime specified in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 17 -A, chapter 20 
45, except for section 1116, which prohibits trafficking or furnishing imitation scheduled 21 
drugs;  22 
2.  If a person who is convicted of a crime specified in Title 17 -A, chapter 45, except 23 
for section 1116, does not have a driver's license, or the person's driver's license is already 24 
suspended, requires the court to delay the issuance or reinstatement of the license of that 25 
person for at leas t 6 months after the person applies for issuance or reinstatement of a 26 
driver's license;  27 
3.  Requires the Secretary of State, on receipt of an attested copy of a court record of 28 
a suspension of the driver's license of a person convicted of violating the fe deral 29 
Controlled Substances Act or of a crime specified in Title 17 -A, chapter 45, except for 30 
section 1116, to immediately record the suspension and send written notice of the 31 
suspension to the person whose license has been suspended.  If that person does not have 32 
a driver's license or that person's driver's license is already suspended, the Secretary of 33 
State is required to delay the issuance or reinstatement of that person's license for the 34 
period specified by the court; and  35 
4.  Increases the minimum peri od of suspension for a person convicted of operating 36 
under the influence of intoxicants, including scheduled drugs, who has no prior 37 
convictions for operating under the influence within a 10 -year period, from 150 days to 38 
180 days.  39
