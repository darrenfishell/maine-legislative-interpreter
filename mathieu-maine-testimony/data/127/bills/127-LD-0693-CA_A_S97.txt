SUMMARY 
The bill adds a provision to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 15 regarding counter­
drug activities by the Maine National Guard. This amendment retains that provision but 
moves it to Title 37-B. The amendment also strikes the provision of the bill that specifies 
that records of the Department of Defense, Veterans and Emergency Management, 
Military Bureau may not be disclosed to the public. The amendment also adds an 
appropriations and allocations section.

This legislation allows the Department of Defense, Veterans and Emergency Management to receive seized money 
and assets for counter-drug activities and could result in increased revenue to the department. The bill includes an 
Other Special Revenue Funds allocation to the department of$500 beginning in fiscal year 2015-16 to allow the 
expenditure of any monies received. Any assets or proceeds received must be used to support counter-drug activities. 
The amount received in any given year will depend on the frequency and nature of the cases that arise and cannot be 
predicted at this time.
