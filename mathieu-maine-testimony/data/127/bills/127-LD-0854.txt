 SUMMARY  1 
This bill expands medical coverage under the MaineCare program to adults who 2 
qualify under federal law with incomes up to 133% of the nonfarm income official 3 
poverty line, with the 5% federal income adjustment for family size, and qualifies Maine 4 
to receive federal funding for 100% of the cost of coverage for members who enroll 5 
under the expansion.  Adults who will be eligible are those 21 to 64 years of age 6 
beginning January 1, 2016 and adults 19 and 20 years of age beginning October 1, 2019.  7
