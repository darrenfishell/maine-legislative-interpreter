Fiscal Note 
Potential current biennium cost increase -Multiple Funds 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This legislation could increase Highway Fund costs to the Department of Transportation and General Fund, Other 
Special Revenue Funds and Federal Expenditures Fund costs to the Department of Environmental Protection if they 
are required to pay landowners compensation to enforce land use regulations. Although these costs cannot be 
estimated, it is anticipated they could be substantial.
