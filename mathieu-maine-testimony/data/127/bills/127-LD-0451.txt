SUMMARY  15 
This bill provides for the removal of the sunset provisions contained in Public Law 16 
2013, chapter 150.  It clarifies that the amendment s made by Public Law 2013, chapter 17 
150 apply to small claims court disclosure proceedings and allows the judgment creditor 18 
to obtain Department of Labor wage information when the judgment debtor has not 19 
conformed to the requirements of an installment payme nt order.  20
