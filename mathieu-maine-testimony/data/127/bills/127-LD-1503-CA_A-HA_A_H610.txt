HOUSE AMENDMENT • .ff,, to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 1026, 
L.D. 1503, Bill, "An Act To Create a Class II Limited Lobster and Crab Fishing License 
and Improve the Limited-entry System" 
Amend the amendment in section 9 in paragraph A-3 in subparagraph (1) in the 2nd 
line (page 4, line 31 in amendment) by inserting after the following: "6422" the 
following: 'before attaining 18 years of age' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment amends Committee Amendment "A" by adding a provision to the 
requirement that a person under 23 years of age has logged time fishing in the apprentice 
program, in addition to other requirements, to be eligible to declare a limited-entry zone 
as that person's declared lobster zone to also require that the person log time prior to 
attaining 18 years of age.
