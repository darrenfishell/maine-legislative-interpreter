COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 576, L.D. 842, Bill, "An Act To 
Establish Peer Center Reimbursement" 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
summary and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. 34-B MRSA §3613 is enacted to read: 
§3613. Compensation for peer centers 
1. Peer center. For the purposes of this section, "peer center" means an entity or 
organization that has a contract with the department to provide peer support services for 
individuals experiencing mental health conditions and other conditions that may co-occur 
with a mental health condition. Services include, but are not limited to, community 
integration, social support, leisure and avocational activities and recovery coaching. 
2. Compensation; rules. A peer center must be reimbursed at a rate established by 
the department pursuant to this subsection. The department may not establish a so-called 
flat rate. This subsection applies to all funds, including federal funds, paid by any agency 
of the State to a peer center. The department shall establish rules concerning 
reimbursement that: 
A. Take into account the costs of providing services in conformity with applicable 
state and federal laws, rules, regulations and quality and safety standards; and 
B. Are reasonable and adequate to meet the costs incurred by an efficiently and 
economically run peer center. 
Rules adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, 
chapter 375, subchapter 2-A. 
Sec. 2. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and 
allocations are made.
