16 SUMMARY 
17 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. Beginning October 1, 2015, 
18 the amendment eliminates the fees that may be charged by a consumer reporting agency 
19 when a consumer requests a security freeze and permits the parent or guardian of a minor 
20 under 16 years of age to place a security freeze on the consumer report of that minor.
