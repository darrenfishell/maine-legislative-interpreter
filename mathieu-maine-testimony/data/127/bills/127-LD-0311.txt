SUMMARY  20 
This bill gi ves school boards the option of reducing the minimum age required for 21 
school attendance from 7 years of age to 5 years of age.  The bill also provides that 22 
students who are at least 5 years of age and enrolled in a public day school are subject to 23 
the same  provisions regarding truancy as students who are 7 years of age or older and 24 
who are required to attend a public day school.  25
