SUMMARY  12 
This bil l transfers administration of the Kim Wallace Adaptive Equipment Loan 13 
Program Fund to a program administrator, eliminates the Kim Wallace Adaptive 14 
Equipment Loan Program Fund Board, authorizes the establishment of an advisory board 15 
to assist the program ad ministrator and transfers authority over the fund from the Finance 16 
Authority of Maine to the Treasurer of State.  17
