SUMMARY  26 
Public Law 2013, chapter 595, Part U, section 4 repealed the Maine Revised Statutes, 27 
Title 8, chapter 19, which governed the licensing of exhibitions, performances and 28 
amusement shows, including the authority of the Dep artment of Public Safety, Office of 29 
the State Fire Marshal to inspect amusement shows and amusement devices.  This bill 30 
restores the repealed language and specifies that a hayride is included within the 31 
definition of an "amusement device" over which the De partment of Public Safety, Office 32 
of the State Fire Marshal has licensing and inspection authority.  33
