SUMMARY  30 
This bill defines the term "broadband service provider." This bill provides authority 31 
for the Public Utilities Commission to regulate service standards for providers of mobile 32 
telecommunications services and bro adband services and to investigate consumer 33 
complaints. This bill also allows the Public Utilities Commission, through an 34 
adjudicatory proceeding, to assess an administrative penalty on a mobile 35 
telecommunications service provider or broadband service prov ider for violations of 36 
standards adopted by the Public Utilities Commission. The bill allows the Public Utilities 37 
Commission to establish a reasonable assessment on providers to implement these 38 
provisions. This bill creates a nonlapsing fund for assessment s. This bill provides the 39 
authority for the Public Utilities Commission to enact major substantive rules to 40
