SUMMARY  21 
This bill prohibits the use of the power of eminent domain for the development, 22 
operation, management, ownership, leasing or maintenance of a transportation facility as 23 
a public -private  partnership project.  It also prohibits the use of the power of eminent 24 
domain by a private business entity when the entity is involved in a public -private 25 
partnership.  26
