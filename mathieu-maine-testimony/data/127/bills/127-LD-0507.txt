SUMMARY  15 
This bill changes the law governing a candidate's nomination by primary election fo r 16 
the office of Governor, United States Senator or Representative to Congress.  It changes 17 
the number of signatures required on a primary petition for the office of Governor or 18 
United States Senator to be 1% of the voters enrolled in the candidate's party and residing 19 
in the candidate's electoral district as of December 1st of the year before the election or 20 
the current minimum requirement of 2,000 voters, whichever is less.  It also changes the 21 
number of signatures required on a primary petition for the of fice of Representative to 22 
Congress to be 1% of the voters so enrolled or the current minimum of 1,000 voters, 23 
whichever is less.  24
