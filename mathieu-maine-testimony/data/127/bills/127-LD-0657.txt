SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to increase funding for mental health care services for the county 5 
prison population, including educating and training prison employees in behavioral health 6 
assessment and protocols on an annual basis.  It would also require that every county jail 7 
have a mental health worker on site at least 8 hours a day.  8
