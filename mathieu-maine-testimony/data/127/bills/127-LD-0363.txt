SUMMARY  24 
This bill allows a designee of the Secretary of State or a person licensed as a driver 25 
education instructor to do the following:  26 
1.  Collect fees for a learner's permit for a driver's license;  27 
2.  Collect application materials for a learner 's permit;  28 
3.  Administer an examination for a learner's permit; and  29 
4.  Issue a learner's permit.  30 
Current law allows only the Secretary of State to perform these functions.  31
