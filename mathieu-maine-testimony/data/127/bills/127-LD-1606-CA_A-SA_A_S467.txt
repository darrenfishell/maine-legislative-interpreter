SENATE AMENDMENT "A " to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 647, 
L.D. 1606, Bill, "An Act To Provide Funding to the Maine Budget Stabilization Fund" 
Amend the amendment in Part C by inserting after section 2 the following: 
'Sec. C-3. Salary schedule for senior motor vehicle detectives changed. 
The Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Human Resources 
shall amend by August 1, 2016 its rules regarding compensation to ensure that the fixed 
salary schedule for positions classified as senior motor vehicle detectives within the 
Department of the Secretary of State is increased by 5%.' 
Amend the amendment by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter 
or section number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment adds senior motor vehicle detectives to Part C of Committee 
Amendment "A," which provides salary increases to certain law enforcement personnel. 
This position was inadvertently omitted from the amendment.

Fiscal Detail and Notes Fiscal Note 
Current biennium cost increase -General Fund 
Future biennium cost increase -Highway Fund 
This amendment adds Senior Motor Vehicle Detective positions to the list of employees whose salary schedule will be 
increased by 5%. The 2015-16 and 2016-17 cost is estimated to be $2,561 and$ 13,315, respectively, and will be 
funded from the General Fund Salary Plan. These positions were inadvertently omitted from the majority committee 
amendment.
