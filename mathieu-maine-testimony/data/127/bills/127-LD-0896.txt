SUMMARY  26 
This bill provides that the holder of a Class I, Class II or Class III lobster and crab 27 
fishing license may transfer the license to the buyer of the transferor's vessel  and lobster 28 
and crab fishing traps if the transferee has completed the apprentice program under the 29 
Maine Revised Statutes, Title 12, section 6422.  This bill further provides that a retired 30 
marine patrol officer is eligible for a lobster and crab fishing  license with a 400 trap limit.  31 
This bill also provides that a logbook maintained by a holder of a lobster and crab fishing 32 
license may not be inspected by the Commissioner of Marine Resources more than 4 33 
times in any 10 -year period.  34
