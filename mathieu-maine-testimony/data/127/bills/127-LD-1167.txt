SUMMARY  31 
This bill requires the ConnectME Authority to review its definition of broadband 32 
service on an annual bas is and consider how broadband service is defined by the Federal 33 
Communications Commission. This bill also requires the ConnectME Authority to 34 
consider other indicators of nationally competitive broadband performance expectations 35 
when defining broadband ser vice. This bill requires that if the ConnectME Authority 36 
amends its definition of broadband service, it must strengthen the definition to ensure it 37 
contributes to the State's nationally competitive economic development status. This bill 38 
also requires that the ConnectME Authority enhance broadband service in Maine to 39
