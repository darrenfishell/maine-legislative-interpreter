SUMMARY  5 
This bill authorizes a person, firm, partnership, association or cor poration that offers 6 
for purchase an electronic downloadable product to require, as a condition of accepting a 7 
credit card for payment, that a consumer provide personal identification information only 8 
if that person, firm, partnership, association or corpo ration requires that information 9 
solely for the detection, investigation or prevention of fraud, theft, identity theft or 10 
criminal activity or for enforcement of the terms of sale. The bill requires any personal 11 
identification information collected for the se purposes to be destroyed after it is no longer 12 
needed and also prohibits the further sharing of that information.  13 
The bill authorizes a person, firm, partnership, association or corporation that offers 14 
for purchase an electronic downloadable product to require that a consumer establish an 15 
account as a condition for the purchase of a downloadable product and to require that a 16 
consumer provide personal identification information, as long as the consumer is 17 
provided an opportunity to opt out of the collecti on of personal identification information 18 
before completing the online transaction.  19
