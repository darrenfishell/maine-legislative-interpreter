SUMMARY  21 
This bill updates references to the United States Internal Revenue Code of 1986 22 
contained in the Maine Revi sed Statutes, Title 36 to refer to the United States Internal 23 
Revenue Code of 1986, as amended through December 31, 2015, for tax years beginning 24 
on or after January 1, 2015 and for any prior tax years as specifically provided by the 25 
United States Internal  Revenue Code of 1986, as amended. This bill primarily affects the 26 
State's income tax laws.  27
