(4)  Artificial insemination;  13

(6)  Zygote intrafallopian transfer;  15 
(7)  Intracytoplasmic sperm injection;  16 
(8)  Four completed egg retrievals per lifetime; and  17 
(9)  Low tubal ovum transfer.  18 
2.  Required coverage.   All individual and group nonprofit hospital and medical 19 
services plan policies, contracts and certificates and all nonprofit health care plan 20 
policies, c ontracts and certificates must provide coverage for the treatment of infertility.  21 
A contract, policy or certificate that provides coverage for the services required by this 22 
section may contain provisions requiring a 50% or lower copayment by the insured.  23 
3.  Limits.   The coverage required by this section is subject to the following 24 
conditions:  25

SUMMARY  34 
This bill requires that health insurance policies include coverage for the treatment of 35 
infertility if:  36

 1.  The covered individual is married;  1 
2.  The covered individual's infertility is not the result of a sexually transmitted 2

3.  The number of embryos implanted does not exceed the number set forth in rules 4 
adopted by the Departm ent of Professional and Financial Regulation, Bureau of 5 
Insurance.  6 
Under this bill, a policy that provides such coverage may require a 50% or lower 7 
copayment by the insured.  8 
This bill applies to all policies in effect on or after January 1, 2016.  9
