standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over transportation matters.  28 
Financial statements describing the transfer, other than  a transfer from "General 29 
Contingency," must be submitted by the Maine Turnpike Authority to the Office of Fiscal 30

specifying the accounts that are affected, amounts to be transferred, a description of the 34 
transfer and a detailed explanation as to why the tran sfer is needed.  35
