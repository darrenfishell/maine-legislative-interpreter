SUMMARY  36 
This bill amends the motor vehicle laws as follows.  37 
It clarifies that a combination vehicle may include a truck -trailer configuration.  38 
 
 Page 7 - 127LR2332(01) -1 
 It allows municipal agents to issue registrations for pickup trucks up to 10,000 1 
pounds rather than the current 9,000 -pound limit.  2 
It amends the vehicle registration laws to require registration information on a motor 3 
vehicle's motive power for vehicles that do not use motor fuels, such as electric vehicles.  4 
It makes clear that a registration plate may not be obscured by a plate frame or 5 
covering or other device that prevents the plate from being read.  6 
It repeals the authorization for truck and truck tractor lobster registration plat es and 7 
farm truck agriculture education registration plates.  8 
It removes the word "commercial" from the laws regarding registration of trucks and 9 
truck tractors so as to include trucks and truck tractors that are used privately.  10 
It removes an obsolete requi rement that an agent who issues temporary registered 11 
gross weight increases be either a municipal tax collector or town or city manager.  12 
It corrects a discrepancy regarding the maximum length of time for which a 13 
permanent disability parking placard may be issued.  The limit is 6 years.  14 
It requires antique autos and antique motorcycles manufactured after 1991 to be 15 
subject to a biennial inspection.  16 
It removes the word "commercial" from the laws regarding the operation of a vehicle 17 
exceeding its registered we ight. 18 
It amends the definition of "related entity" to include a domestic partner in the 19 
provision regarding suspension or revocation of the license or authority to engage in a 20 
business or commercial activity under the motor vehicle major offenses laws.  21 
It removes the requirement for a noncommercial driver education school to obtain a 22 
surety bond if the school offers driver education as a course credit and does not charge a 23 
fee for the driver education course.  24 
It repeals the provision of law that allows a pe rson who is at least 15 years of age to 25 
obtain a special restricted license under certain conditions without having held a permit 26 
for a period of 6 months.  27 
It changes the motorcycle driver education requirement from an 8 -hour block of 28 
instruction to classr oom and hands -on instruction.  29
