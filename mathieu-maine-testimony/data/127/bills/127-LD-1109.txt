 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 1 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and  2 
Whereas, state law requires cosmetology students to obtain 1,500 hours of 3 
instruction in an approve d cosmetology school or 2,500 hours of training in order to 4 
receive a license to practice cosmetology; and  5 
Whereas, Mr. Bernard's School of Hair Fashion, Inc., with campuses in Bangor and 6 
Lewiston, closed unexpectedly leaving a number of cosmetology studen ts unable to finish 7 
their licensing requirements in instruction hours; and  8 
Whereas, many cosmetology students of Mr. Bernard's School of Hair Fashion, 9 
Inc., have completed a majority of the instruction hours required for licensing but cannot 10 
practically fi nish the requirements within a reasonable period of time due to limited space 11 
and waiting lists for admission at other approved cosmetology schools and programs; and  12 
Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 13 
the me aning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 14 
immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 15 
therefore,  16 
Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  17 
Sec. 1.  Students o f Mr. Bernard's School of Hair Fashion, Inc., permitted 18 
to finish cosmetology instruction hour requirements through experience in 19 
the practice of cosmetology as trainees.   A student of Mr. Bernard's School of Hair 20 
Fashion, Inc., who was attending the schoo l at the time of the school's closure and has 21 
completed at least 1,000 instruction hours in cosmetology may qualify for a license under 22 
the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 32, section 14226, subsection 3 by fulfilling the 23 
student's remaining required instruc tion hours for licensure through experience in the 24 
practice of cosmetology as a trainee at a rate of one training hour for every .6 of an 25 
instruction hour.  26 
Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 27 
legislation takes effect whe n approved.  28 
SUMMARY  29 
This bill allows a cosmetology student of the now -closed Mr. Bernard's School of 30 
Hair Fashion, Inc., who has at least 1,000 hours of the required 1,500 instruction hours 31 
for licensure in cosmetology to fulfill the remaining required ins truction hours through 32 
experience in the practice of cosmetology as a trainee at a rate of one training hour for 33 
every .6 of an instruction hour.  34
