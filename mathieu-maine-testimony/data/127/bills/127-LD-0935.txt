SUMMARY  13 
This bill creates a liquor manufacturing license for a research facility at a 14 
postsecondary educational institution.  Under the bill, othe r liquor manufacturers, known 15 
as subject manufacturers, may produce malt liquor, wine, sparkling wine, fortified wine 16 
and spirits at the research facility and the research facility may itself manufacture malt 17 
liquor, wine, sparkling wine, fortified wine an d spirits for research or instructional 18 
purposes, which the research facility also may sell to the State, wholesalers or retailers or 19 
directly to the public under certain circumstances.  20
