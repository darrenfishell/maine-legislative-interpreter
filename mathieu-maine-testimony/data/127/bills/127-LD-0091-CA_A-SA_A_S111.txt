SUMMARY 
2 Committee Amendment "A" allows dental hygienists, dental hygienists with public 
3 health supervision status, independent practice dental hygienists and dental hygiene 
4 therapists to prescribe, dispense or administer fluoride dentifrice and antibacterial rinse. 
5 This amendment requires the prescription, dispensation or administration of these 
6 substances to occur under protocols developed by the Board of Dental Examiners and 
7 requires a dental hygienist, dental hygienist with public health supervision status, 
8 independent practice dental hygienist or dental hygiene therapist to have a written 
9 agreement with a licensed dentist in order to prescribe, dispense or administer these 
10 substances,
