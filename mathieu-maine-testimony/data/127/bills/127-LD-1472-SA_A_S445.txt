SENATE AMENDMENT "A_,, to S.P. 570, L.D. 1472, "Resolve, To Enhance the 
Administration of the Child and Adult Care Food Program by Creating Clear Guidelines 
for Organizations and Streamlining the Application Process" 
Amend the resolve in section 1 in subsection 2 in the first line (page 1, line 21 in 
L.D.) by inserting after the following: "website" the following: 'no later than August 1, 
2017' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment requires that the forms for an organization to apply to participate in 
the federal Child and Adult Care Food Program be made available on the Department of 
Health and Human Services' publicly accessible website no later than August 1, 2017.
