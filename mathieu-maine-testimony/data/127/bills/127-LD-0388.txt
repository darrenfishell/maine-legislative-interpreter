This bill prohibits the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Bureau 21 
of Parks and Lands from contracting for timber harvesting on land under its managemen t 22 
if the contractor uses persons employed under the federal labor certification process for 23 
employment of foreign workers in logging for that purpose.  24
