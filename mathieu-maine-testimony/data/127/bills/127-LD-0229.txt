 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  7 MRSA §2900, sub -§16-A is enacted to read:  2 
16-A.  Raw milk.  "Raw milk" means milk that has not been pasteurized or 3 
homogenized.  4 
Sec. 2.  7 MRSA §2901 -C, sub -§1, as enacted by PL 1999,  c. 679, Pt. A, §5, is 5 
amended to read:  6 
1.  Milk distributor license.  A milk distributor shall obtain a license from the 7 
commissioner and shall renew that license annually.  The license fee is based on the 8 
annual volume of milk or milk products sold or di stributed in the State.  The annual fee 9 
may not be lower than $25 or exceed $300.  The commissioner shall deposit all money 10 
received from milk distributor license fees into the General Fund.  A milk distributor 11 
selling raw milk or a raw milk product under section 2910 -C is exempt from the 12 
provisions of this subsection.  13 
Sec. 3.  7 MRSA §2908 -A, sub -§1, as amended by PL 2005, c. 270, §7, is further 14 
amended to read:  15 
1.  Violation.  A firm, person, corporation or society may not sell milk or milk 16 
products in th e State , except raw milk or a raw milk product under section 2910 -C, 17 
without the license or permits provided in sections 2901 -C and 2902 -A, violate sections 18 
2901 -A to 2903 -B or neglect, fail or refuse to comply with those sections and the rules, 19 
regulation s and standards of identity and quality issued pursuant to section 2910.  20 
Sec. 4.  7 MRSA §2910 -C is enacted to read:  21 
§2910 -C.  Sales of raw milk  22 
1.  Exemption from licensing requirement.  A milk distributor who daily produces 23 
for sale less than 20 gallons of raw milk or daily processes less than 20 gallons of raw 24 
milk into cheese aged at least 60 days, yogurt, cream, butter or kefir or other dairy 25 
product is not required to obtain a license under section 2901 -C if the milk and any 26 
products are sold directly  to the consumer from the milk distributor's own farm or farm 27 
stand or at a farmers' market within the State.  28 
2.  Labeling.  Raw milk or a raw milk product sold under subsection 1 must be 29 
clearly labeled with the name, address and phone number of the milk distributor's farm, 30 
the name of the product and the following statement:  "This product is made with raw 31 
milk and is exempt from State of Maine licensing.''  If the raw milk or a raw milk product 32 
is sold in a container provided by the milk distributor, the  container must be labeled 33 
pursuant to the requirements of this subsection.  34 
3.  Sign.  A milk distributor selling raw milk or a raw milk product under subsection 35 
1 shall place a sign at the point of sale that contains the name, address and phone number 36 
of the milk distributor's farm and the following statement:  "Products from this farm made 37 
from raw milk are exempt from State of Maine licensing.''  38
