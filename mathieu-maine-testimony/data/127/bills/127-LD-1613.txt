SUMMARY  18 
This bill provides an exemption from the sales and use tax for meals served by an 19 
incorporated nonprofit organization, including a regularly organized church or house of 20 
religious worship, when the proceeds of the sale of the meals are used to fund the purpose 21 
of the organization.  22
