SUMMARY  17 
This bill provides that individual and group health insurance policies must ensure that 18 
inpatient coverage with respect to breast cancer treatment is provided for a hospital stay 19 
of no less than 48 hours insofar as the attending physician, in consultation with the 20 
patient, determines it to be medically appropriate following a mastectomy or lumpectomy 21 
or of no less than 24 hours following a lymph node dissection.  Under current law, the 22 
minimum hospital stay is not prescribed but must be provided for a period of time 23 
determined to be medically appropriate by the attending physician in consultation with 24 
the patient.  25
