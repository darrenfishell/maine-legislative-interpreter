SUMMARY  15 
This bill author izes a person who has been issued a B.Y.O.B. function permit from 16 
the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages 17 
and Lottery Operations to hold multiple functions over a period of 2 calendar years as 18 
long as the bure au is provided notice at least 24 hours prior to each function.  The bill 19 
also provides that a B.Y.O.B. function may be held outside of established premises, 20 
including but not limited to open space and parking lots adjacent to a facility where 21 
sporting eve nts take place.  22
