13 SUMMARY 
14 This amendment strikes and replaces the bill. The amendment strikes from the bill: 
15 1. The increase in the required minimum age of the accompanying licensed driver, for 
16 a driver of a motor vehicle under a leamer's permit, from 20 to 25 years of age; 
17 2. The increase in the required length of time that the accompanying licensed driver, 
18 for a driver of a motor vehicle under a leamer's pennit, has held a driver's license from 2 
19 to 3 years; 
20 3. The increase in the length of time a pennit holder is required to have a leamer's 
21 permit before obtaining a driver's license from 6 to 12 months; and 
22 4. The increase in the fine for using a mobile telephone while operating a motor 
23 vehicle with an intennediate license from not less than $250 and not more than $500 to 
24 $500. 
25 The amendment amends the current laws prohibiting the holder of a leamer's pennit 
26 or intermediate license from using a mobile telephone while operating a motor vehicle to 
27 also prohibit such a person from using a handheld electronic device while operating a 
28 motor vehicle. The amendment provides that the definitions in these provisions and in 
29 the provisions of current law that prohibit minors from using certain electronic devices 
30 while operating a motor vehicle are consistent. 
31 The amendment strikes from the bill the prohibition on a driver who is operating a 
32 motor vehicle with an intermediate license from operating a motor vehicle between the 
33 hours of 10 p.m. and 5 a.m., unless traveling directly between the driver's place of 
34 employment and residence. The amendment leaves current law in place, which prohibits 
35 a driver who is operating a motor vehicle with an intennediate license from operating a 
36 motor vehicle between midnight and 5 a.ill. 
37 The amendment strikes from the bill the prohibition on a driver who is operating a 
38 motor vehicle with an intermediate license from can-ying any passengers unless 
39 accompanied by a licensed operator. The amendment leaves CllTent law in place, which 
40 provides that a driver who is operating a motor vehicle with an interrnediate license may
