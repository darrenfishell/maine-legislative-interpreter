SUMMARY  9 
This bill makes the Adjutant General the next of kin to a deceased veteran who has 10 
no known living spouse or adult relatives and direc ts the Adjutant General to ensure the 11 
veteran receives all death benefits, including a grave marker from the United States 12 
Department of Veterans Affairs.  13
