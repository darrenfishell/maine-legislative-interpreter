The purpose of this bill is to help ensure that patients with complex medical 5 
conditions who are in hospitals are p laced in more appropriate nonhospital settings.  6

settings for:  8 
1.  Patients with severe brain injuries;  9 
2.  Bariatric patients;  10 
3.  Patients who are dependent on a ventilator fo r the long -term;  11 
4. Young adult patients with substance abuse issues who receive extended 12 
intravenous therapy due to infections;  13 
5.  Young adult patients with spinal cord injuries;  14 
6.  Young adult patients who have had strokes;  15 
7.  Homeless patients who ne ed preoperative care such as feeding tubes; and  16 
8.  Patients who are violent and have been previously discharged from a health care 17 
facility due to violence.  18
