SUMMARY  34 
This bill requires the Aroostook County Commissioners to rename B Stream in and 35 
near the Town of Houlton in Aroostook  County to Captain Ambrose Bear Stream, in 36 
honor of Captain Ambrose Bear, a Maliseet Indian chief who served in the Revoluti onary 37 
War.  The bill also requires the county commissioners to notify the appropriate authorities 38
