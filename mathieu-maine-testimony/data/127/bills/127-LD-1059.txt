SUMMARY  17 
This bill allows for t he operation of marijuana testing facilities.  These facilities may 18 
possess marijuana regulated under the Maine Medical Use of Marijuana Act.  19 
Dispensaries and registered primary caregivers may own and operate marijuana testing 20 
facilities for research and development purposes.  If a label for medical marijuana refers 21 
to potency or cannabinoid profile, the label must be verified by a marijuana testing 22 
facility.  23
