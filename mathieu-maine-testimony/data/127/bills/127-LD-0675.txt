SUMMARY  18 
Under the current law, a person who receives or is scheduled to receive remuneration 19 
in the form of vacation pay in excess of 4 weeks' wages or holiday pay is disqualified 20 
from receiving unemployment benefits for the week that remuneration is due.  This bill 21 
removes those disqualifications.  22
