14 SUMMARY 
15 This amendment clarifies that the operator of a motor vehicle approaching a railroad 
16 crossing must stop the vehicle not less than 15 feet and not more than 50 feet from the 
17 nearest rail of the railroad track and may not proceed if a clearly visible electric or 
18 mechanical signal device warns of the approach of a train; a crossing gate is lowered or a 
19 flagger gives or continues to give a signal or warning of the approach or passage of a 
20 train; a train is visible and is in hazardous proximity to the crossing; or a sign, device or 
21 law requires the vehicle to stop.
