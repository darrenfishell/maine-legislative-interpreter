SUMMARY  32 
This bill prohibits the State or any political subdivision of the State from adopting or 33 
implementing policies originating in the United Nations Agenda 21 or other international 34 
laws that restrict private property rights without due process.  Because the United Nations 35 
has accredited and enlisted numerous nongovernmental and intergovernmental 36 
organizations to assist in the implementation of its policies related to Agenda 21 around 37
