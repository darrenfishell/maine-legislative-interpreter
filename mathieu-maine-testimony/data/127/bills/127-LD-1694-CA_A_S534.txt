9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 699, L.D. 1694, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Authorize a General Fund Bond Issue To Improve Highways, Bridges and Multimodal 
11 Facilities" 
12 Amend the bill by inserting after section 9 the following: 
13 'Sec. 10. Contingent transfer for additional ballot. If the number or length 
14 of referendum questions to be submitted to the voters at the general election in November 
15 2016 requires the production and delivery of more than a single ballot by the Secretary of 
16 State, the State Controller by August 1, 2016 shall transfer from the unappropriated 
17 surplus of the General Fund to the Department of the Secretary of State, Bureau of 
18 Administrative Services and Corporations program, General Fund account, $107,500 for 
19 each ballot in addition to the initial ballot required by the Secretary of State, unless the 
20 Secretary of State has already received a transfer of funds for that additional ballot.' 
21 SUMMARY 
22 This amendment provides for a transfer to the Secretary of State if the number or 
23 length of the referendum questions to be submitted to the voters at the general election in 
24 November 2016 requires the Secretary of State to produce and distribute more than one 
25 ballot to accommodate those questions.
