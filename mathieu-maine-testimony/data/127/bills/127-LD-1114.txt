SUMMARY  13 
This bill amends the law regarding solicitation of a child to commit a prohibited act 14 
and creates the crimes of exploitive travel and unlawful electronic transmission of an 15 
image or video depicting sexually explicit conduct. The bill also amends the  criminal 16 
forfeiture provisions of the sexual exploitation of minors laws to include conveyances and 17 
money instruments as items in addition to computer equipment that may be subject to 18 
forfeiture proceedings.  19
