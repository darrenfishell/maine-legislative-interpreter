SUMMARY  13 
This bill creates a digital portal for education, which makes digital texts and online 14 
courses available to all students through a common statewide platform administered by 15 
the Commissioner of Education.  This bill allows prov iders of digital texts and online 16 
courses to install them onto the portal at their own expense and set the fee charged for 17 
enrollment in or subscription to the digital text or online course.  The commissioner is 18 
required to maintain a catalog of available digital texts and online courses.  19
