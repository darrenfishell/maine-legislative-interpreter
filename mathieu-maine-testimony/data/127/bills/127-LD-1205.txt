 SUMMARY  1 
The funds provided by this bond issue, in the amount of $65,000,000, will be used to 2 
assist in the provision of energy -efficient affordable homes to low -income Maine seniors.  3 
The bulk of the bond proceeds will be used to construct new homes for seniors and to 4 
adaptively reuse existing structures to provide homes for seniors.  At least one project 5 
will be located in each of Maine's 16 counties.  Preference wil l be given to home 6 
locations that have access to health care services, other essential goods and services and 7 
public transportation.  A portion of the bond proceeds will provide for home repair and 8 
weatherization of the existing homes of low -income seniors .  The bond proceeds will 9 
leverage an estimated $100,000,000 in private and other funds.  10
