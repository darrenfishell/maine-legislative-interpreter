SUMMARY  9 
Current law allows an overwidth light farm tractor to operate on a public way or 10 
bridge without a permit when the tractor is equipped with adequate lights or reflectors 11 
warning other highway users of the tractor’s extreme width.  This  bill allows all 12 
overwidth farm tractors to operate on public ways and bridges.  13
