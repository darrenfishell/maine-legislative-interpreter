Fiscal Note 
Potential for fewer grants at higher amounts -Maine State Grant Program 
Increasing the minium grant award from the Maine State Grant Program administered by the Finance Authority of 
Maine from no less than $1,000 to no less than $2,500 for first year students, with the minimum amount increasing by 
$1,000 for each consecutive year until students reach their 4th year, for those eligible students with no expected 
family contributions beginning with the 2016-2017 academic year will result in fewer grants being awarded at higher 
amounts than without this bill.
