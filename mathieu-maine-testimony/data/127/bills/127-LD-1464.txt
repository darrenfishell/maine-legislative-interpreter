SUMMARY  6 
This bill amends the laws regarding the criminal history record information of 7 
educational personnel to consolidate and update language.  It requires the Commissioner 8 
of Education to set annually fees for criminal history record checks for and certification 9 
of educational personnel that currently are set in statute.  It extends from fiscal year 2014 - 10 
15 to all subsequent fiscal years the crediti ng of up to $335,000 of fees from the initial 11 
and renewal certification of educational personnel to the National Board Certification 12 
Salary Supplement Fund, Other Special Revenue Funds account within the Department of 13 
Education.  It allows course work and testing for targeted need area and conditional 14 
certificates and transitional endorsements issued preceding the amendment of rules 15 
regarding qualifications to be completed within 3 years instead of annually.  It also 16 
requires the department in conjunction w ith the State Board of Education to review all 17 
educational personnel certification rules and report by January 7, 2017 to the joint 18 
standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over education and cultural 19 
affairs.  The joint standing committee  is authorized to report out legislation to the First 20 
Regular Session of the 128th Legislature.  21
