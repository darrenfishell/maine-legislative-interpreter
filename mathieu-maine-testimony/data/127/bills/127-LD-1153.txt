SUMMARY  12 
This b ill repeals the laws governing proficiency -based graduation requirements.  It 13 
provides that a school administrative unit may elect to opt out of the so -called Common 14 
Core State Standards and statewide student assessments.  It provides that a school 15 
adminis trative unit may establish a method of transferring funds to a public charter school 16 
that is an alternative to the current method.  It makes it optional, instead of mandatory, for 17 
a school administrative unit to develop and implement a performance evaluati on and 18 
professional growth system for educators.  19
