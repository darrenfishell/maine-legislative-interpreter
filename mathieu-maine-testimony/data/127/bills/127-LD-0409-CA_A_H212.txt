Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill phases out individual income taxes and would result in a loss of General Fund revenue of $155,952,000 in 
fiscal year 2015-16 and $395,770,000 in fiscal year 2016-17 and a loss to the Local Government Fund of $8,208,000 
in fiscal year 2015-16 and $20,830,000 in fiscal year 2016-17.
