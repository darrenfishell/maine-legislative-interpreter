This chapter may be known and cited as "the Automatic Contract Renewal Act."  6 
ยง1500 -I.  Definitions  7 
As used in this chapter, unless the context otherwise indicates, the following terms 8 
have the following meanings.  9 
1.  Consumer.  "Consumer" means an individual, business, corporation or other legal 10 
entity receiving products, services or repai r under a contract.  11 
2.  Contract.  "Contract" means a written agreement between 2 or more parties.  12 
3.  Parties.  "Parties" includes individuals and other legal entities, but does not 13 
include the Federal Government, the State or another state, a unit of loc al government or 14 
a school district.  15 
ยง1500 -J.  Automatic renewal; requirements  16 
1.  Automatic renewal.  A person, firm, partnership, association or corporation that 17 
sells or offers to sell a product or service to a consumer pursuant to a contract that 18 
automa tically renews unless the consumer cancels the account shall disclose the 19 
automatic renewal clause clearly and conspicuously in the contract, including the 20 
cancellation procedure.  The automatic renewal clause must be printed in upper case 21 
letters, undersc ored and in bold print of a print size larger than the text of the rest of the 22 
contract.  23 
2.  Written notice.  A person, firm, partnership, association or corporation that sells 24 
or offers to sell a product or service to a consumer pursuant to a contract tha t has a 25 
specified term of 12 months or more and that automatically renews for a specified term of 26 
more than one month unless the consumer cancels the contract shall notify the consumer 27 
in writing of the automatic renewal.  Written notice under this subsect ion must be 28 
provided to the consumer no fewer than 30 days and no more than 60 days before the 29 
cancellation deadline pursuant to the automatic renewal clause and must disclose clearly 30 
and conspicuously:  31 
A.  That unless the consumer cancels the contract it will automatically renew; and  32 
B.  Where the consumer may obtain details regarding the automatic renewal 33 
provision and cancellation procedure.  34

SUMMARY  12 
This bill requires any entity who sells or provides services through an automatic 13 
renewal contract to disclose to the consumer the automatic renewal clause clear ly and 14 
conspicuously in the contract, including the cancellation procedure.  The bill also requires 15 
that written notice be provided to the consumer no fewer than 30 days and no more than 16 
60 days before the cancellation deadline pursuant to the automatic re newal clause.  17
