3.  Definitions.   As used in this sec tion, the following terms have the following 5

5.  Surcharge.   A retailer shall collect a surcharge o f 5¢ from a person for each 18

The retailer shall include an itemized sal es receipt documenting the surcharge.  A retailer 21

SUMMARY  8 
This bill requires a retailer to assess a 5¢ surcharge for a plastic bag designed for one - 9 
time use distributed to a customer at the point of retai l sale.  A retailer retains 2¢ from the 10 
surcharge for administrative costs.  The revenues from the plastic bag surcharge are 11 
credited to the Plastics Recycling Fund administered by the Department of Environmental 12 
Protection for the purpose of promoting rec ycling efforts related to plastics.  A retailer 13 
may provide recyclable paper bags to bag products at the point of sale. A retailer must 14 
provide reusable bags for purchase by a customer.  15
