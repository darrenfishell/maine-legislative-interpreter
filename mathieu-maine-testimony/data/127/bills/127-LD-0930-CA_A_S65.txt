Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill expands the income tax credit for child care expenses by increasing it from 25% to 100% of the federal tax 
credit allowed and making the credit fully refundable and would result in a reduction in General Fund revenue of 
$12,302,500 in fiscal year 2015-16 and $12,160,000 in fiscal year 2016-17 and a reduction in Local Government Fund 
revenue of $647,5000 in fiscal year 2015-16 and $640,000 in fiscal year 2016-17.
