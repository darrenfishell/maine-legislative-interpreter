Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill includes one-time appropriations and allocations in fiseal year 2014-15 to various programs within the 
Department of Education, the Maine Arts Commission and the Maine State Museum to support position 
reorganizations, reclassifications and payroll obligations. 
This bill also amends the Lease-purchase authorization for the Maine learning technology initiative enacted in Public 
Law 2013, c. 368, Pt. JJ, section I to revise the principal and interest caps to include not only leases that the State 
pays for but leases that schools pay for. There is no fiscal impaet to the State.
