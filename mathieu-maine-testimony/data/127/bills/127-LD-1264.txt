 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 1

Whereas, the Maine Quality Forum is currently the only program overseen by 3

care quality are aligned with the activities of the Maine Health Data Organization; and  6

 Sec. 7.  22 MRSA §§8718 and 8719 are enacted to read:  1 
§8718.  Maine Quality Forum  2 
Effective July 1, 2015, the Maine Quality Forum, referred to in this chapter as "the 3 
forum," is established within the Maine Health Data Organization.  The forum is 4 
governed by the board with advice from the Maine Quality Forum Advisory Council 5 
pursuant to section 8719.  The forum must be funded, at least in part, through the funds 6 
remaining after June 30, 2014 from the access payments made to Dirigo Health pursuant 7 
to Title 24 -A, section 6917. Except as otherwise provided in section 8707, subsection 2, 8 
information obtain ed by the forum is a public record as provided by Title 1, chapter 13, 9 
subchapter 1.  The forum shall perform the following duties.  10 
1.  Research dissemination.   The forum shall collect and disseminate research 11 
regarding health care quality, evidence -based medicine and patient safety to promote best 12 
practices.  13 
2.  Quality and performance measures.   The forum shall adopt a set of measures to 14 
evaluate and compare health care quality and provider performance.  The measures must 15 
be adopted with guidance from the  advisory council pursuant to section 8719. The quality 16 
measures adopted by the forum must be the basis for the rules for the collection of quality 17 
data adopted by the organization pursuant to section 8708 -A. 18 
3.  Data coordination.   The forum shall coordin ate the collection of health care 19 
quality data in the State.  The forum shall work with other entities that collect health care 20 
data to minimize duplication and to minimize the burden on providers of data.  21 
4.  Reporting.   The forum shall work collaborative ly with health care providers, 22 
health insurance carriers and others to report in useable formats comparative health care 23 
quality information to consumers, purchasers, providers, insurers and policy makers. The 24 
forum shall produce annual quality reports pur suant to section 8712.  The forum shall 25 
make provider -specific information regarding quality of services available on its publicly 26 
accessible website.  27 
5.  Consumer education.   The forum shall conduct education campaigns to help 28 
health care consumers make i nformed decisions and engage in healthy lifestyles.  29 
6.  Technology assessment.   The forum shall conduct technology assessment reviews 30 
to guide the use and distribution of new technologies in this State. The forum shall make 31 
recommendations to the certifica te of need program under chapter 103 -A. 32 
7.  Electronic data.   The forum shall encourage the adoption of electronic 33 
technology and assist health care practitioners to implement electronic systems for 34 
medical records and submission of claims.  The assistance  may include, but is not limited 35 
to, practitioner education, identification or establishment of low -interest financing options 36 
for hardware and software and system implementation support.  37 
8.  Annual report.   The forum shall make an annual report to the pub lic.  The forum 38 
shall provide the report to the joint standing committees of the Legislature having 39

SUMMARY  15 
This bill transfers responsibility for the Maine Quality Forum from Dirigo Heal th to 16 
the Maine Health Data Organization effective July 1, 2015. The bill also transfers 2 17 
positions authorized for staffing the Maine Quality Forum to the Maine Health Data 18 
Organization.  19
