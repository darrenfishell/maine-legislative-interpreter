SUMMARY  21 
This bill allows a health care practitioner to disclose health care information to the 22 
parent or guardian of an individual with a mental health diagnosis who withholds consent 23 
because of the individual's compromised view of that individual's mental  health.  A health 24 
care practitioner is not liable for failing to make a disclosure if the practitioner determines 25 
in good faith that there is no serious or imminent threat and the disclosure would interfere 26 
with providing effective care.  27
