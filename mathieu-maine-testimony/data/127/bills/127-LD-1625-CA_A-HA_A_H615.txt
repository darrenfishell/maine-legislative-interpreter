§353-B. Tuition benefit for member 
A member who meets the prerequisites of section 354 is entitled to a 100% tuition 
benefit at a state postsecondary education institution. The benefit applies to tuition for a 
member enrolled or accepted for admission to a state postsecondary education institution 
on a full-time or part-time basis. To be eligible for the benefit. a member must be enrolled 
full time or part time at a state postsecondary education institution. The benefit may be 
used to earn one credential at the following levels: baccalaureate. associate or certificate 
and licensure. The benefit must be reduced by any other tuition assistance received by a 
member not related to housing costs or non-tuition expenses. 
§353-C. Waiver required 
If the cost of providing the tuition benefit under this subchapter exceeds the amount 
of money available in the Maine National Guard Postsecondary Fund established in 
section 353-A, the tuition benefit must be provided in the form of a tuition waiver 
provided by the state postsecondary education institution.

7 Like Committee Amendment "A", Part A of this amendment replaces the bill with 
8 language directing the University of Maine System and the Maine Community College 
9 System to identify the needs of student-veterans on their campuses, including needs 
10 related to admission, degree completion, transitioning to civilian life and meeting 
11 personal and financial obligations, and directs the University of Maine System and the 
12 Maine Community College System to identify existing services that meet the needs 
13 identified, assess the effectiveness of those services, determine what services are not 
14 being offered that, if offered, would meet those needs and propose services and solutions 
15 that fulfill those needs across campuses that are based upon best practices. 
16 Part B of this amendment establishes the Maine National Guard Postsecondary Fund 
17 and amends existing law regarding education assistance grants to Maine National Guard 
18 members. The amendment provides that the Maine National Guard Postsecondary Fund 
19 will provide for 100% of an eligible member's tuition costs. If the tuition benefit costs 
20 exceed the amount in the fund, the amendment states that the benefit will be provided in 
21 the form of a waiver from the state postsecondary education institution. The amendment 
22 clarifies that tuition does not include lab fees or other mandatory fees charged to students. 
23 The amendment specifies that the benefit entitles a member to one postsecondary 
24 education credential, whether it be at the associate, baccalaureate, certificate or licensure 
25 level. The amendment specifies that a member must first pursue all other state, federal 
26 and employer-provided benefits available and have not previously earned a bachelor's 
27 degree in order to qualify for the tuition benefit.
