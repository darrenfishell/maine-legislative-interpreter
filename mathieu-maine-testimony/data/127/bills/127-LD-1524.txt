SUMMARY  11 
This bill updates the laws governing the Maine Veterans' Homes and repeals outdated 12 
provisions.  It removes references to specific veterans' home locations.  It increases the 13 
borrowing authority of the Maine Veterans' Homes from  $15,000,000 to $50,000,000.  It 14 
repeals a requirement that the Maine Veterans' Homes develop and implement a geriatric 15 
training program.  It changes the title of the position of the person who administers the 16 
homes from administrator to chief executive of ficer.  It repeals a requirement relating to 17 
payment of costs by veterans admitted to the homes.  It repeals a requirement that excess 18 
funds be transferred to the General Fund.  It repeals a provision relating to the use of 19 
stipend funds.  It makes changes  to an annual reporting requirement.  It also makes 20 
technical changes to provide consistent reference to the Maine Veterans' Homes.  21
