SUMMARY  12 
Current law provides a property tax exemption to certain property of benevolent and 13 
charitable institutions, including land trusts, incorp orated in this State.  14 
This bill allows a municipality, by referendum, to deny tax -exempt status to a land 15 
trust that is at least 20 acres in size.  If the municipality denies tax -exempt status for a 16 
land trust, it must deny tax -exempt status to all land tr usts of at least 20 acres located in 17 
that municipality.  18
