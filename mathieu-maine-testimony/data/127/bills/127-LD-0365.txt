SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to provide a means to offset the costs incurred by a person with a 5 
disability who modifies a res idence, such as by installing ramps or stairlifts, widening 6 
entryways or remodeling, to make it more accessible for that person.  The offset could be 7 
in the form of an income tax credit or an exemption from property taxes.  8
