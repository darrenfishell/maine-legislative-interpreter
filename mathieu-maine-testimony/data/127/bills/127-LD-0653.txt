SUMMARY  21 
Current law requires state and school purchasers to buy meat, fish, dairy products, 22 
excluding milk and eggs, and species of fruit s and fresh vegetables directly from Maine 23 
food producers or food brokers. This bill establishes a minimum percentage of Maine 24 
foodstuffs, including milk and eggs, that must be purchased, requiring at least 15% by 25 
December 31, 2021, at least 20% by Decembe r 31, 2026, at least 25% by December 31, 26 
2036 and at least 35% by December 31, 2037. The bill exempts from the requirements 27 
school purchasers at schools participating in the National School Lunch Program.  The 28 
bill also requires competitive bidding when mo re than one producer, broker or wholesaler 29 
can supply a given foodstuff.  30
