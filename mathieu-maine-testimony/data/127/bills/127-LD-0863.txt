This bill makes the following changes to the laws governing liability claims reports 26 
under the Maine Health Security Act:  27 
1.  It amends the law to provide that reports of claims against a health care provider 28 
need not be forwarded to the department or board that regulates the health care provider; 29 
and 30 
2.  It amends the law to provide that reports of disposition of claims against a health 31 
care provide r must be forwarded to the department or board that regulates the health care 32
