SUMMAR Y 17 
This bill permits rabbit producers to sell rabbit products without inspection at the 18 
farm on which the rabbits were raised, at farmers' markets, by delivery to consumers' 19 
homes, to community supported agriculture farms with which the rabbit producer has a 20 
direct marketing relationship, to locally owned grocery stores and to locally owned 21 
restaurants.  22
