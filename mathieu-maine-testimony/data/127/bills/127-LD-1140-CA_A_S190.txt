Fiscal Note 
Contingent increased loan liability -Finance Authority of Maine 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This legislation allows the Finance Authority of Maine (FAME) to increase the maximum loan amount available 
through its Economic Recovery Program from $1.0 million to $2.0 million per project. The increased authority is 
contingent upon the program's receipt of an appropriation, allocation or other funding of at least $13.0 million.
