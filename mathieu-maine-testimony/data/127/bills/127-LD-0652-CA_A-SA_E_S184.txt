SUMMARY 
This amendment provides a municipal legislative body with the authority to adopt an 
ordinance that restricts the carrying of firearms in the municipality's essential municipal 
offices and places of legislative assembly just as the State imposes those restrictions in its 
capitol area. It also provides a municipal legislative body with the authority to adopt an 
ordinance that restricts the carrying of firea ms in a Class A lounge.
