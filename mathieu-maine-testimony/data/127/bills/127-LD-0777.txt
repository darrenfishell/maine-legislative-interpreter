SUMMARY  8 
This bill establishes a seasonal agency liquor store license.  It authorizes the 9 
Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Alcoholic Beverag es 10 
and Lottery Operations to issue one seasonal license in a municipality with a year -round 11 
population of at least 5,001 if between June 1st and October 1st the population increases 12 
by a number that would otherwise qualify the municipality for an additiona l agency 13 
liquor store license.  14
