SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies that a power of sale foreclosure may be used only if a 
mortgage deed executed on or after October 1, 1993 states that it is given primarily for 
one or more of the following purposes: business, commercial or agricultural. The deed is 
not required to name all 3 purposes. 
This amendment amends the bill to retain a cross-reference to existing notice 
requirements. 
This amendment clarifies a cross-reference to provisions listing foreclosure 
procedures.
