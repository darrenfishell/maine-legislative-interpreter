This resolve requires the Finance Authority of Maine, through a request for proposal 29 
process, to select and contract with a statewide entity to purchase, process, sto re and 30 
transport agricultural products and seafood harvested in the State in order to provide 31 
access to those agricultural products and seafood to residents of the State with food 32 
insecurity.  The resolve requires an annual report regarding the progress of  the entity 33 
selected and the efforts needed to achieve the purpose of providing food to residents of 34 
the State with food insecurity.  The initiative is funded using $3,000,000 transferred on a 35 
one-time basis from the Fund for a Healthy Maine; the authority  is responsible for the 36 
administration of the funds and may retain a portion of the funds for the reasonable 37
