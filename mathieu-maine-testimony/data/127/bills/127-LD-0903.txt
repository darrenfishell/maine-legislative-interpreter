SUMMARY  23 
This bill allows the operator of a motorcycle, moped or bicycle, inclu ding a 24 
motorized bicycle, motorized tricycle or motorized scooter, facing a steady and circular 25 
red light, after stopping for a reasonable amount of time, to proceed cautiously through 26 
the intersection before the light on the traffic -control device turns g reen if no other 27 
vehicles are present at the intersection to actuate the traffic -control device.  28
