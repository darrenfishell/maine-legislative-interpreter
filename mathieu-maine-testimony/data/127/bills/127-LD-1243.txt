SUMMARY  23 
This bill amends the definition of "critical incident" to include employees of and 24 
emergency dispatchers for a criminal justice agency.  The bill also allows the chief law 25 
enforcement off icer of a law enforcement agency or the chief law enforcement officer's 26 
designee to designate critical incident stress management team members.  27
