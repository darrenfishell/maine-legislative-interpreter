SUMMARY  21 
This bill raises the per diem compensation for active retired judges  and justices from 22 
$300 per day to $500 per day.  It fixes the per diem compensation for active retired 23 
family law magistrates at 75% of the per diem compensation paid to active retired judges.  24 
It also limits per diem compensation received by an active re tired judge, justice or family 25 
law magistrate in any calendar year to 75% of the annual salary of a full -time judge, 26 
justice or family law magistrate, respectively, and provides that an active retired judge, 27 
justice or family law magistrate does not accrue  additional creditable service for benefit 28 
calculation purposes and is not entitled to any other employee benefit, including health, 29 
dental and life insurance.  30
