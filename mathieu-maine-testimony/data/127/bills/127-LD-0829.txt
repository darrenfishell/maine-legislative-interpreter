SUMMARY  15 
This bill establishes within each division of the District Court a railroad bureau and a 16 
violations clerk to accept written appearances, waivers of trial, pleas of guilty and 17 
payments of fines and costs for civil violations of railroad trespass laws.  This bill 18 
designates as a Class E crime trespassing on railroad property after 3 convictions for civil 19 
violations of the railroad trespass laws.  This bill increases the minimum fines for civil 20 
violations of railroad trespass laws.  21
