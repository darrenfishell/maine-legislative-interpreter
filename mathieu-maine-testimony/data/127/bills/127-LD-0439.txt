SUMMARY  15 
This bill prohibits a passenger train engine from operating for more than 30 minutes 16 
while the train is stopped except for during repairs or servicing requiring the engine to be 17 
running or delivering or accepting merchandise or passengers requi ring engine -assisted 18 
power and includes a fine of $2,500 for a violation of these provisions.  19
