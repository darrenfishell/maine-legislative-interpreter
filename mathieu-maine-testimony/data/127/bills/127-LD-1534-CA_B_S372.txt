17 SUMMARY 
18 This amendment is the minority report of the committee. This amendment specifies 
19 that the Maine State Police Drug Interdiction Unit in the Department of Public Safety, 
20 Bureau of State Police must be established using existing resources and vacant positions 
21 within the Maine State Police.

This bill establishes the Maine State Police Drug Interdiction Unit and provides that it be established within existing 
budgeted resources. The Department of Public Safety is required to use currently vacant positions to dedicate 3 Stat~ 
Police Officer positions and one State Police Sergeant position to the new unit. The impact on existing programs and 
services cannot be determined at this time.
