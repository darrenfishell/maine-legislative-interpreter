SUMMARY  9 
This bill provides for the allocation of funding among Maine's federally qualified 10 
health centers to support access to primary medical, behavioral health and dental services 11 
for residents in rural and underserved communities. This fundin g is targeted to support 12 
the provision of primary care services for the uninsured and underinsured, as well as to 13 
assist with provider recruitment and retention.  14
