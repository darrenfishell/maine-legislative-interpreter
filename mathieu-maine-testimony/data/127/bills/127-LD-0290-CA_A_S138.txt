Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill provides a sales and use tax refund or exemption for fuel used in commercial wood harvesting or commercial 
agricultural production. It would result in a loss of General Fund revenue of $9,963,600 in fiscal year 2015-16 and 
$13,019,750 in fiscal year 2016-17 and a loss to the Local Government Fund of $524,400 in fiscal year 2015-16 and 
$685,250 in fiscal year 2016-17.
