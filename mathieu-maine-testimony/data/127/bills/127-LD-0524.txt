 SUMMARY  1 
This bill proposes to decrease the cost of and increase access to substance abuse 2 
treatment services by requiring the Department of Health and Human Services to increase 3 
the number of federally qualified health centers that provide methadone t reatment 4 
services, to establish a pilot clinic location in a rural area at least 30 miles from any 5 
currently licensed methadone treatment clinic and to work to facilitate access to services 6 
and distribution of services across the State.  The bill requires the department to amend 7 
the methadone clinic rules to eliminate the requirement that the centers be open for 8 
administration of methadone treatment on Sundays.  The bill designates the rules as 9 
routine technical rules.  The department is required to work wi th stakeholders to address 10 
current rules and policies that act as barriers to achieve the intent of this legislation.  11
