SUMMARY  15 
This bill allows certificate of approval holders under the liquor licensing laws to 16 
donate a gift certificate to purchase their products or donate their products to an  17 
incorporated civic organization for use by the organization as a prize, gift or award. It 18 
also allows alcohol producers and wholesalers to provide their products, free of charge or 19 
at a reduced price, to a licensee to be served at a licensed on -premises e vent for the 20 
benefit of an incorporated civic organization.  21
