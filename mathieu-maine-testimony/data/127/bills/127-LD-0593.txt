SUMMARY  17 
This bill exempts an electric vehicle charging station provider from being considered 18 
a competitive electricity provider.  The bill defines what constitutes an electric vehicle 19 
charging station provider and allows a provider to install an electrica l submeter and to 20 
charge a submeter user only for kilowatt hours used.  21
