SUMMARY  27 
This bill requires a court to sentence a person who is convicted  of unlawful 28 
possession of a scheduled drug to a definite term of imprisonment but suspend all of the 29 
sentence if the person has no prior convictions for certain drug -related offenses or other 30 
specified crimes, such as assault or sexual assault.  The perso n must be sentenced to a 31 
definite term of probation with the condition that the person successfully complete a 32 
community substance abuse program.  33 
This bill specifies that probation may be imposed for a Class D or Class E crime of 34 
unlawful possession of a s cheduled drug.  35 
This bill requires each county sheriff to establish a community substance abuse 36 
program at that sheriff's regional or county jail.  Programs are administered by the 37 
counties and must meet certification standards established by the Department  of 38 
Corrections by rule.  The bill establishes the Community Substance Abuse Fund to 39

 annually reimburse counties for the cost of the community substance abuse programs that 1 
meet the department's certification requirements.  2 
A person participating in a progr am must comply with certain requirements in order 3 
to successfully complete the program, including submitting to electronic monitoring and 4
