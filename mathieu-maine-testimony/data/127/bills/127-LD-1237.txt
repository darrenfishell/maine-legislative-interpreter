SUMMARY  8 
This bill makes the following changes to the laws governing the filing of death and 9 
marriage records:  10 
1.  It authorizes the parties to a marriage, or the legal representatives of the parties to 11 
a marriage, that occurred mo re than one year previously to apply for a delayed certificate 12 
of marriage;  13 
2.  It establishes a procedure for registering an official record of death when a death is 14 
presumed to have occurred in the State but the body has not been located.  15
