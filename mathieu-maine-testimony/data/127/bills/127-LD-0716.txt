SUMMARY  16 
This bill makes the following changes related to snowmobile registration and sales 17 
tax on diesel fuel used in snowmobile trail -grooming machinery.  18 
1. It increases the resident snowmobile registration fee from $40 to $50 and allocates 19 
the additional $10 to the Snowmobile Trail Fund of the Department of  Agriculture, 20 
Conservation and Forestry, Bureau of Parks and Lands.  21 
2. The bill increases the 3 -day nonresident snowmobile registration fee from $43 to 22 
$49 and increases the seasonal nonresident registration from $88 to $99 and allocates the 23 
additional rev enue to the Snowmobile Trail Fund of the Department of Agriculture, 24 
Conservation and Forestry, Bureau of Parks and Lands.  25 
3. It creates a 7 -day nonresident snowmobile registration with a fee of $75.  26 
4. It amends the law governing the special fuel tax to ma ke it clear that fuel 27 
qualifying for a refund under that law does not lose the sales tax exemption created in the 28 
bill for diesel fuel used in snowmobile trail -grooming machinery.  29
