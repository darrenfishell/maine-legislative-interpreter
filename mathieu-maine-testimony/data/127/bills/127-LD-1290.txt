SUMMARY  25 
This bill, which takes effect January 1, 2016, repeals the Maine Clean Election Act 26 
and transfers the remaining balance in the Maine Clean Election Fund to the Department 27 
of Education to be used for the State's contribution toward the costs of education funding.  28 
This bill also corrects the numerous cross -references to the Ma ine Clean Election Act in 29 
the Maine Revised Statutes.  30
