SUMMARY  29 
This resolution proposes to amend the Constitution of Maine to reduce the size of the 30 
Senate from no more than 35 members to 32 members as a result of reapportioning to 2 31 
Senators per county.  The resolution also requires the redrawing of district lines in 2016.  32
