HOUSE AMENDMENT" 11-" to H.P. 994, Order, "Joint Order, That Additional 
Specified Matters Be Held Over to Any Special or Regular Session of the 127th 
Legislature" 
Amend the order in that part relating to "Veterans and Legal Affairs" by inserting 
before the following: "H.P. 876, L.D. 1280 -An Act To Provide Income Tax Relief by 
Expanding Gaming Opportunities" the following: 
'S.P. 272, L.D. 742 -RESOLUTION, Proposing an Amendment to the Constitution 
of Maine To Require That 5 Percent of Signatures on a Direct Initiative of 
Legislation Come from Each County' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment amends the so-called carry-over order to add S.P. 272, L.D. 742, 
RESOLUTION, Proposing an Amendment to the Constitution of Maine To Require That 
5 Percent of Signatures on a Direct Initiative of Legislation Come from Each County.
