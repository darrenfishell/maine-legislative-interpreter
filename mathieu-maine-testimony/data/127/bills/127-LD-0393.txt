SUMMARY  37 
This bill replaces the Board of Trustees of the University of Maine System, the Board 38 
of Trustees of the Ma ine Community College System and the Board of Trustees of the 39 
 
 Page 5 - 127LR0908(01) -1 
 Maine Maritime Academy with the Board of Trustees of Higher Education to provide a 1 
unified body for the governance of those institutions.  2 
Part A makes changes to the law to replace some select r eferences to the individual 3 
boards of trustees with references to the unified board and enacts a new chapter in the 4 
Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20 -A with provisions that establish the new unified board.  5 
Part B establishes a transition team to recommend f urther changes to the law to fully 6 
accomplish the transition to the unified board.  The transition team will specifically 7 
recommend changes to determine the qualifications of trustees and the powers and duties 8 
of the unified board in a report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 9 
jurisdiction over education and cultural affairs.  The report of the transition team must 10 
also recommend changes to the administration or organization of and the existing law 11 
governing the University of Maine Sy stem, the Maine Maritime Academy and the Maine 12 
Community College System to ensure the efficient operation under the unified board and 13 
to avoid any potential conflicts with the law establishing the unified board.  The joint 14 
standing committee is authorized to submit a bill to the Second Regular Session of the 15 
127th Legislature based upon the report.  16
