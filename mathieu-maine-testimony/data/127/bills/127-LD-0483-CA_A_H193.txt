Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 7 in the last line (page 1, line 15 in L.D.) by

SUMMARY 
This amendment provides an additional exception, beyond the exception established 
in the bill, to the law mandating a report to the 'Department of Health and Human 
Services of suspected abuse or neglect of a child under 6 months of age for bums or other 
injuries occurring as a 'result of medical treatment following the delivery of the child
