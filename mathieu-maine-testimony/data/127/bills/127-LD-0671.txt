 SUMMARY  1 
Under a pilot project that is scheduled to expire on March 15, 2015, independent 2 
practice dental hygienists are allowed to expose and process radiographs in areas of the 3 
State that have been designated by the United States Department of Health and Human 4 
Services as dental health professional shortage areas.  5 
This bill continues the authority of these independent practice dental hygienists to 6 
expose and process radiographs indefinitely, under the same restrictions as imposed under 7 
the pilot project, except th at the authorization applies in the entire State.  8
