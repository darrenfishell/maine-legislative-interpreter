 Sec. 1.  Koi added to list.  Resolved:  That the Department of Inland Fisheries 1 
and Wildlife is directed to amend its rules in chapters 2 and 7 to add koi, Cyprinus carpio 2 
haematopterus, to the list of unrestricted fish that do not require an importation p ermit or 3 
possession permit.  Rules adopted must specify that the keeping of koi is allowed only in 4 
a closed aquarium or pond system.  For the purpose of these rules, "closed system" means 5 
a type of water system where the water is recirculated, no water is discharged into the 6 
environment and the wildlife or marine species held in the system cannot escape.  7 
SUMMARY  8 
This resolve directs the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to amend its 9 
rules to add koi, Cyprinus carpio haematopterus, to the list of u nrestricted fish that do not 10 
require an importation permit or possession permit and to allow koi to be kept only in a 11 
closed aquarium or pond system.  12
