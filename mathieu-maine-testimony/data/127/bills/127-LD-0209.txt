SUMMARY  25 
This bill changes the apportionment of the percentage of the Maine Economic 26 
Improvement Fund that goes to the University of Maine at Augusta, the University of 27 
Maine at Farmingto n, the University of Maine at Fort Kent, the University of Maine at 28 
Machias, the University of Maine at Presque Isle and the Maine Maritime Academy to 29 
support research and development by removing the equal apportionment to all of the 30 
institutions of a mini mum of 3% of the fund and, beginning July 1, 2015, providing a 31 
minimum 2% of the fund to be disbursed to the University of Maine at Machias to 32 
support a marine field station and a minimum of 1% to be apportioned among the 33 
remainder of the institutions.  34
