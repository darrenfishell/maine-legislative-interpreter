2.  New American Start -up Fund.  The New American Start -up Fund, referred to in 13 
this section as "the fund," administered by the department, is established to carry out the 14 
purposes under subsection 3.  The fund consists of appropriati ons or allocations for 15 
inclusion in the fund, any money received as contributions or grants from private and 16 
public sources, any money received in repayment of loans from the fund and all interest, 17 
dividends or other pecuniary gains from investment of fund s from the fund.  The fund, to 18 
be accounted for within the department, must be held separate and apart from all other 19 
money, funds and accounts. Any balance remaining in the fund at the end of a fiscal year 20 
does not lapse but must be carried forward to the  next fiscal year.  21 
3.  Fund purposes.  The purposes of the fund include providing:  22 
A.  Capital financing for a start -up and expanding business owned by a low -income 23 
immigrant or refugee in the State.  Capital financing provided under this section may 24 
be in  the form of an interest -bearing loan or, if required by the person's religious 25 
faith, in the form of a noninterest -bearing fee -for-service loan; and  26 
B.  Operational support for a nonprofit corporation incorporated in the State under 27 
Title 13 -B for a progr am that assists low -income immigrants and refugees in starting 28 
and developing businesses.  29 
4.  Outside contract.  The department shall contract the administration of subsection 30 
3, paragraph A to a nonprofit corporation incorporated in the State under Title 13-B that 31 
assists low -income immigrants and refugees in starting and developing businesses.  32
