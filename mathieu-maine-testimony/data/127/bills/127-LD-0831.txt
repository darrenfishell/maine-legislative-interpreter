SUMMARY  9 
This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to file an 10 
application with the United States Department of Health and Human Services, Centers for 11 
Medicare and Medicaid Services to request that home -delivered meals be a reimbursable 12 
covered service under the State's home -based and community -based services waivers.  13 
The purpose of this resolve is to improve health and delay  entry into facility -based care.  14
