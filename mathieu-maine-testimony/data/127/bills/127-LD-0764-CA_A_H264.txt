COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "II "to H.P. 517, L.D. 764, "Resolve, To Impose a 
2-year Delay on the Use of Standardized Tests To Evaluate Teachers" 
Amend the resolve by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'Resolve, To Impose a One-year Delay on the Use of Standardized Tests To Evaluate 
Teachers' 
Amend the resolve in the emergency preamble in the 4th paragraph in the first line 
(page 1, line 7 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "effect" and inserting the following: 
'affect' 
Amend the resolve in section lin the 4th line (page 1, line 16 in L.D.) by striking out 
the following: "2017-18" and inserting the following: '2016-2017' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the committee. The amendment changes 
the resolve's provision from a 2-year delay to a one-year delay prohibiting school 
administrative units from using the Maine Educational Assessment for Mathematics and 
English Language Arts/Literacy standardized tests to measure educator effectiveness.
