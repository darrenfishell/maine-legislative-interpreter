the student is enrolled.  17

SUMMARY  15 
This legislation is an emergency bill that includes certain recommendations proposed 16 
by the Commission To Study College Affordability and College Completion for 17 
consideration by the Joint Stan ding Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs.  18 
The joint standing committee has not taken a position on the substance of the report 19 
or this bill and by submitting this bill the committee is not suggesting and does not intend 20 
to suggest that it agrees or  disagrees with any aspect of the recommendations and the 21 
necessary changes to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20 -A, chapter 419 -A included in 22 
the report or this bill. The joint standing committee is submitting the bill for the sole 23 
purpose of turning th e proposals in the report into a printed bill that can be referred to the 24 
committee for an appropriate public hearing and subsequent processing in the normal 25 
course of business. The joint standing committee is taking this action to ensure clarity and 26 
trans parency in the legislative review of the proposals included in the report submitted by 27 
the Commission To Study College Affordability and College Completion.  28
