SUMMARY 
This amendment removes the mandate preamble and exempts a school administrative 
unit, the University of Maine System, the Maine Community College System and the 
Maine Maritime Academy from the requirement of using insulation with a global­
warming potential of 10 or less if it is demonstrated that use of such materials will 
increase the cost of the state-funded new construction or substantial renovations.
