SUMMARY  22 
This bill allows the next of kin of a deceased person, in addition to a lineal 23 
descendent, to authorize the repair, maintenance or removal of a memorial to the 24 
deceased.  This bill also allows a memorial to be moved if it is in poor condition or the  25 
preservation of the memorial cannot be guaranteed in its present location.  26
