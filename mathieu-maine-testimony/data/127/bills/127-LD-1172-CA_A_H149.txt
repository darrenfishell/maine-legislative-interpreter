Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Adoption of this rule could increase Highway Fund and Other Special Revenue Funds expenditures if a proposal 
regarding truck configuration and weight is received and the department determines it would require capital 
improvements to the infrastructure in order for it to withstand the proposed configuration. The rule includes a 
provision requiring the entity requesting the change to pay for 50% of the improvements, which would increase Other 
Special Revenue Funds revenue.
