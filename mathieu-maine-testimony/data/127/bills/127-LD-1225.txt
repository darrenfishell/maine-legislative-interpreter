SUMMARY  18 
This bill provides that a person may not establish or maintain a swim line or a 19 
devel oped swim area unless the length of the developed swim area or of the area 20 
delineated by a swim line is equal to or less than 50% of the entire length of the shore 21 
frontage of the property from which the developed swim area or the area delineated by a 22 
swim  line extends or 200 feet, whichever is greater.  The length of a developed swim area 23 
or of an area delineated by a swim line is measured parallel to the shore.  The length of 24 
the swim line will determine where a line, rope or series of buoys used to delin eate an 25 
area of surface water for the purpose of swimming may be placed.  26
