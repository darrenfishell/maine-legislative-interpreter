SUMMARY  29 
This bill changes the date by which the Department of Education's working group is 30 
required to develop and submit for review standards, assessments and assessment criteria 31 
for determining student proficiency as part of the develo pment of a standards -based 32 
system of education. The bill also requires the working group to begin meeting as soon as 33 
practicable and that all meetings be open to the public.  34
