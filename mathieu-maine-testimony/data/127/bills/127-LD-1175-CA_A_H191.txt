17 SUMMARY 
18 This amendment: 
19 1. Modifies the provision of the bill regarding brake lights that requires that all 
20 factory-installed brake lights must be operational and meet certain standards; 
21 2. Replaces the provision of the bill that prohibits a person from operating a motor 
22 vehicle if certain video equipment is visible to the operator. The amendment clarifies that 
23 video equipment relating to operation of the vehicle and video equipment used by law 
24 enforcement and emergency personnel may be visible to the vehicle operator; and 
25 3. Removes the provision of the bill that repeals a law prohibiting persons from 
26 riding in a towed vehicle or trailer. It instead amends the law to remove the prohibition 
27 on a person riding in a vehicle being towed by a wrecker or by a motor vehicle using a 
28 tow bar but preserves the other prohibitions in current law.
