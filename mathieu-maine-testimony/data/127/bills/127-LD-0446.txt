SUMMARY  10 
This bill adds "and safety" to referenc es to the Public Utilities Commission's 11 
consumer assistance division to reflect that the division now also oversees the State's gas 12 
safety regulation and enforcement and enforcement of the underground facility damage 13 
prevention law.  14
