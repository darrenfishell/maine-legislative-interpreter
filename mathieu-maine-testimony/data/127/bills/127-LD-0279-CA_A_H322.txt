16 SUMMARY 
17 This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment directs the State Tax Assessor to 
18 suspend reimbursement under the Business Equipment Tax Reimbursement program that 
19 would otherwise be issued if a taxpayer is delinquent in the payment of personal property 
20 taxes. If the taxpayer does not pay the tax due by the end of the reimbursement 
21 application period, the taxpayer's eligibility for the suspended reimbursement is 
22 terminated.

Fiscal Note 
Minor revenue increase -General Fund 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill requires the State Tax Assessor to suspend reimbursement under the Business Equipment Tax 
Reimbursement program if the taxpayer's delinquent balance is $10,000 or more and certification is received from the 
municipality. This provision could result in a minor increase in General Fund revenue not requiring any change to the 
budget.
