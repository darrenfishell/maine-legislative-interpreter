SUMMARY  10 
This bill adds a new requirement for the renewal of professional licenses for social 11 
workers, licensed clinical professional counselors and psychologists that each must show 12 
proof upon renewal that the licensee has comple ted a minimum of 6 hours of course work 13 
every 10 years in suicide prevention, evidence -based suicide risk assessment or the 14 
treatment and management of suicidal persons, including knowledge of community 15 
resources and cultural factors.  These courses may be  used to satisfy the licensee's other 16 
continuing education requirements.  17
