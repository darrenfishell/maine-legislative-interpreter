SUMMARY  25 
This bill provides that, beginning with the 2016 tax year, it is the goal and policy of 26 
the State that the amount of property taxes distributed by a municipality for  education 27 
under the Essential Programs and Services Funding Act may not exceed 55% of the state 28 
valuation of the municipality's property subject to the valuation filed with the Secretary of 29 
State.  The bill also provides that a municipality is not require d to pay more than 55% of 30 
the funds raised by local property taxes for the municipality's required contribution to the 31 
school administrative unit's total cost of education unless a majority of the elected 32 
officials of the municipality approve raising and e xpending funds appropriated through 33 
local taxation for educational purposes that exceed 55% of the state valuation of the 34 
municipality's property.  35
