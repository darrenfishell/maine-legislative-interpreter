§12924.  Loan agreement; repayment  18 
A STEM student applying for a loan under section 12922 shall enter into an 19 
agreement with the authority that includes t he following provisions.  20 
1.  Principal; interest.  Upon completion of postsecondary education, the STEM 21 
student shall repay the entire principal of the loan plus simple interest.  Interest does not 22 
begin to accrue until 6 months following completion of the  loan recipient's education or 23 
withdrawal from school.  24 
2.  Term of loan.  Loans must be repaid over a term no longer than 10 years, except 25 
that the authority may extend a loan recipient's term as necessary to ensure repayment of 26 
the loan.  27

education, withdrawal from school or discontinuance in school.  29

SUMMARY  23 
The purpose of this bill is to increase the number of students pursuing graduate and 24 
undergraduate degrees in the fields of science, computer science, technology, engineering 25 
and mathematics.  This bill creates the Maine Science, Technology, Enginee ring and 26 
Mathematics Loan Program administered by the Finance Authority of Maine.  Under the 27 
bill, loans with varying interest rates will be provided to participating students depending 28 
on their future employment: loans with 0% interest rates will be avail able to students who 29 
remain in or return to the State and work in the fields of science, computer science, 30 
technology, engineering and mathematics upon graduation; loans bearing an interest rate 31 
of 5% will be available to students who remain in or return t o the State upon graduation 32 
but are not employed in the fields of science, computer science, technology, engineering 33 
and mathematics; and loans bearing an interest rate of 8% annually will be available for 34 
students who live and work outside the State upon graduation.  This bill appropriates 35 
$10,000,000 to fund the program for fiscal year 2015 -16. 36
