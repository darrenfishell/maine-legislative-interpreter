 SUMMARY  1 
This bill provides an hourly rate increase for guardians ad litem and makes the 2 
following adjustments to appropriations from the General Fund for certain expenditures 3 
of the judicial branch for the fiscal years ending June 30, 2016 and J une 30, 2017.  4 
1.  It provides funding for the guardian ad litem hourly rate increase from $50 per 5 
hour to $60 per hour to be consistent with the rate paid to court -appointed counsel.  6 
2.  It provides funding for an increase in the workweek of certain judici al branch 7 
employees from 37.5 hours per week to 40 hours per week.  8 
3.  It provides funding for an increase in fees charged by the Department of 9 
Administrative and Financial Services, Office of Information Technology to the judicial 10 
branch.  11 
4.  It deappropr iates unused feasibility study funds.  12 
5.  It deappropriates funds no longer needed for debt services.  13
