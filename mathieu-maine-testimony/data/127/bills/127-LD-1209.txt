 SUMMARY  1 
This bill establishes a peer support services program in the office of substance abuse 2 
and mental health services within the Department of Health and Human Services. The bill 3 
requires each assertive community treatment team to i nclude at least one full -time 4 
intentional peer support specialist certified by the department.  "Intentional peer support 5 
specialist" is defined.  The bill requires the department to appoint and convene the 6 
Intentional Peer Support Advisory Committee. The bill requires the department to adopt 7 
necessary rules and designates the rules as routine technical rules.  The bill requires the 8 
costs of intentional peer support services and the advisory committee to be met through 9 
the transfer of funding from the Menta l Health - Community account and the Mental 10 
Health - Community Medicaid account and through the discontinuance of 2 full -time 11 
positions within the office of substance abuse and mental health services.  12
