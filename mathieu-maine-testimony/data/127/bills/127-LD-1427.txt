SUMMARY  7 
This resolve provides for final adoption by the Department of Environmental 8 
Protection of portions of Rule Chapter 500:  Stormwater Management, a major 9 
substantive rule of the department that was submitted for legislative review on January 8, 10 
2015 and approved for final adoption by the Legislature pursuant to Resolve 2015, 11

accordance with Resolve 2015, chapter 12 as amended by this resolve, as long as final 14
