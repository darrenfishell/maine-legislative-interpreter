SUMMARY  31 
This bill adds the office of the state judge advocate to the definition of "criminal 32 
justice agency."  It also amends the laws regarding rights and liabilities of state military 33 
force s members by providing members with immunity for the use of deadly force under 34 
certain conditions and by providing that Army National Guard and Air National Guard 35 
military police officers and security forces have certain rights of law enforcement 36 
officers.  37
