SUMMARY  9 
This bill creates a new hire tax credit for an employer in the amount of 50% of the 10 
wages of an employee who was a recipient of benefits  under the Temporary Assistance 11 
for Needy Families program and who is employed at least 20 hours per week on average 12 
by the employer.  This bill also directs the Department of Health and Human Services to 13 
develop recommended legislation to adjust the level  of assistance under state benefits 14 
programs based upon earned income for low -income recipients of state assistance who 15 
are employed by an employer who receives a new hire tax credit and to recommend other 16 
measures in statute, rule and policy that will ena ble low -income recipients of state 17 
assistance to obtain stable and lasting employment.  18
