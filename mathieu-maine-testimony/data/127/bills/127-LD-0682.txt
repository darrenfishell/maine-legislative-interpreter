 SUMMARY  1 
This bill provides that the Secretary of State may not waive any component of the 2 
driver's license examination req uirements except for an applicant who possesses a valid 3 
driver's license from another state or jurisdiction.  The bill also clarifies that fees and any 4 
required application materials for a learner's permit must be collected by the Secretary of 5 
State, any e xamination for a learner's permit must be administered by the Secretary of 6 
State and a learner's permit must be issued by the Secretary of State.  7
