26 SUMMARY 
27 This amendment changes the title and strikes most of the bill, retaining those sections 
28 that include references to aggravated sex trafficking, sex trafficking and prostitution of a 
29 minor or a person with a disability as crimes for which an enhanced charge applies. The 
30 amendment also retains the changes to the Child and Family Services and Child 
31 Protection Act. The amendment adds sexual exploitation of a minor, sex trafficking and 
32 aggravated sex trafficking to the list of aggravating factors in the child welfare laws.
