SUMMARY  35 
This bill repeals the definitions of "tourist camp" and "trailer camp" and replaces 36 
those terms with the term "campground" in the Sales and Use Tax Law.  This bill 37 
exempts from sales and use ta x rental fees charged to a person who resides for 28 days or 38 
longer at one campground.  This bill also exempts from the sales and use tax the rental of 39 
a campsite as long as nothing else of value is given by the owner of that campsite to the 40 
renter.  41
