25 SUMMARY 
26 This amendment makes changes to the' provisions in the bill that apply to the school 
27 budget approval process for alternative organizational structures. Current law requires 
28 that a change in who approves a budget must be approved at the next statewide election. 
29 The bill requires that such a determination of who approves a budget must be approved at 
30 a special meeting 'authorized by a majority of the governing body of the alternative 
31 organizational structure rather than at the next statewide election. The amendment 
32 provides that the detennination of the school budget approval process must be approved 
33 at the next regular election or special election of all of the member entities of the 
34 alternative organizational structure. The, amendment also provides that a referendum 
35 authorized for this purpose must be called and conducted by the governing body of the
