SUMMARY  9 
Current law divides slot machine revenue credited to the Agricultural Fair Support 10 
Fund between commercial harness racing tracks and agricultural fairs licensed to accept 11 
wagers on harness racing.  This bill requires that slot machine re venue from the casino 12 
located in Oxford County and credited to the fund be distributed only to agricultural fairs 13 
licensed to accept wagers on harness racing and not to commercial tracks.  14
