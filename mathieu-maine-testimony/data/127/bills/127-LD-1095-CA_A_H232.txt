25 SUMMARY 
26 This amendment, which is a minority report of the committee, removes the provisions 
27 in the bill that change the calculation of rent constituting property taxes and decrease 
28 from 6% to 5% the income threshold for purposes of calculation of the property tax 
29 fairness credit. This amendment also removes incorrect references to "property tax year."

the property tax fairness credit and would result in a reduction in General Fund revenue of $37,952,500 in fiscal year 
2016-17 and a reduction in Local Government Fund revenue of $1,997,500 in fiscal year 2016-17.
