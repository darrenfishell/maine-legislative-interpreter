SUMMARY  4 
This bill repeals the Maine Guaranteed Access Reinsurance Association Act, 5 
effective January 1, 2019.  Prior to the repeal, it requires the joint standing committee 6 
having jurisdiction over insurance and financial services matters to review and evaluate 7 
the transitional reinsurance program operating in the State between Janu ary 1, 2014 and 8 
December 31, 2016 pursuant to the federal Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act 9 
and federal regulations adopted pursuant to that Act and the differences between the 10 
transitional reinsurance program and the Maine Guaranteed Access Reins urance 11 
Association and recommend to the Superintendent of Insurance whether the Maine 12 
Guaranteed Access Reinsurance Association should resume operations.  The committee is 13 
authorized to submit a bill to the Second Regular Session of the 128th Legislature b ased 14 
on the committee’s evaluation.  15
