SUMMARY  36 
This bill amends the laws regarding dealers in secondhand precious metals by:  37 
1.  Including palladium and platinum as precious metals subject to regulation;  38

 2.  Allowing a dealer to take one digital photograph of all the items in a transaction, 1

4.  Reducing from 15 days to 5 business days the amount of time a dealer must wait 5

5.  Removing a reference to an electronic database designed to catalog stolen 7

6.  Requiring a dealer to register as a precious metals dealer with the municipality in 9 
which the dealer will conduct business, i nstead of requiring the dealer to obtain a permit 10
