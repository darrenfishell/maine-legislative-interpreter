SUMMARY  14 
This bill expands turkey hunting opportunities by:  15 
1. Eliminating the turkey permit requirements;  16 
2. Allowing the hunting of turkey with a big game or small game h unting license;  17 
3. Requiring deer registration stations to also register turkeys; and  18 
4. Providing for electronic or telephonic registration of turkeys.  19
