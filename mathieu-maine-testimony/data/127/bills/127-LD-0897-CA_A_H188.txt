COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" f.J " to RP. 616, L.D. 897, Bill, "An Act To Move 
a Township from House District 150 to House District 151" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 151 in paragraph A in the last 2 lines (page 
1, lines 11 and 12 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "Oxbow, Portage Lake, St. 
Francis, St. Jolm, Wallagrass and Winterville, including Township 14, Range 6" and 
inserting the following: 'including Township 14, Range 6, Oxbow, Portage Lake, St. 
Francis, St. John, Wallagrass and Winterville' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies that Township 14, Range 6 is included in the unorganized 
territory of Northwest Aroostook in House District 151.
