COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "H" to H.P. 906, L.D. 1331, Bill, "An Act To 
Provide Enhanced Enforcement of the Laws Governing Alcoholic Beverages" 
Amend the bill by striking out all of section 6 and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 6. Director of the Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery 
Operations to hire liquor inspectors who have completed law enforcement 
training at the Maine Criminal Justice Academy. No later than July 1,2016, the 
Director of the Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations within the 
Department of Administrative and Financial Services shall hire no fewer than 5 liquor 
inspectors and require that no fewer than 3 of those liquor inspectors must have 
completed training as qualified law enforcement officers as defined by the Maine Revised 
Statutes, Title 25, section 280 I-A, subsection 5. 
Sec. 7. Director of the Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery 
Operations to report on enhanced liquor enforcement. No later than March 1, 
2017, the Director of the Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations within 
the Department of Administrative and Financial Services shall report to the joint standing 
committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over alcoholic beverage matters 
regarding the changes to and enhancement of liquor enforcement functions in the State, 
including, but not limited to, increases in the number of compliance checks, 
investigations related to on-premises retail licensees selling spirits not purchased from the 
State, sales to minors and forensic audits of licensee operations. 
Sec. 8. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and 
allocations are made. 
ADMINISTRATIVE AND FINANCIAL SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF 
Alcoholic Beverages -General Operation 0015 
Initiative: Provides appropriations in fiscal years 2015-16 and 2016-17 for 5 Liquor 
License Inspector positions and related support costs. Three of these positions must have 
law enforcement authority.

Department of Administrative and Financial Services to hire 5 additional Liquor License Inspector positions, 3 of
