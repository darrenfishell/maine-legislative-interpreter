SUMMARY  4 
This bill amends the education statutes related to the method by which the 5 
Department of Education determines the state and local sh ares of the major capital costs 6 
for a state -approved school construction project.  The bill requires that the amount of the 7 
state and local shares for the approved major capital costs of the school construction 8 
project approved for a school year beginning on or after July 1, 2016 must be based on 9 
the same percentages as the state and the school administrative unit's contributions to the 10 
total cost of education as determined by the school administrative unit's state share 11 
percentage as calculated in accordan ce with the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20 -A, 12 
section 15672, subsection 31.  The bill also requires that a school administrative unit 13 
establish a reserve fund to finance capital improvement or capital equipment for a state - 14 
approved school construction pr oject approved for a school year beginning on or after 15 
July 1, 2016.  16
