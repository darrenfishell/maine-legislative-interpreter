COMMITTEE AMENDMENT " A" to S.P. 495, L.D. 1362, Bill, "An Act 
Concerning Membership on the Board of Directors of the Lewiston-Auburn Water 
Pollution Control Authority" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in section 14 in the II th and 12th lines (page 1, lines 14 
and 15 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "president Auburn Sewerage District 
Trustees shall select" and inserting the following: 'president shall select, subject to 
confirmation by the Auburn Sewerage District Trustees,' 
Amend the bill in section 1 in section 14 in the 18th line (page 1, line 21 in L.D.) by 
striking out the following: "the president of the Auburn Sewerage District Trustees" and 
inserting the following: 'the president of the Auburn Sewerage District' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment provides that, if the president of the Auburn Sewerage District 
Trustees declines to serve or resigns as a member of the board of the Lewiston-Auburn 
Water Pollution Control Authority, the president must select, subject to confirmation by 
the Auburn Sewerage District Trustees, another trustee or resident of Auburn to serve the 
remainder of the term; the bill provides that the trustees select the president's 
replacement.
