SUMMARY  31 
This bill expands the time period within which a wrongful death action may be 32 
brought in a case of a death caused by a homicide.  The bill also provides that the 33 
expansion of the time period applies to wrongful death actions that have not been barred 34 
by the statute of limitations in force immediately prior to the effective date o f this 35 
legislation.  36
