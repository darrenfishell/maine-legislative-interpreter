Committee: Labor, Commerce, Research and Economic Development

Fiscal Note 
Potential current biennium revenue increase -Federal Expenditures Fund 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This legislation will allow the Bureau of Labor Standards within the Department of Labor to remain in compliance 
with injury and illness recordkeeping requirements of the United States Occupational Safety and Health 
Administration. Being out of compliance with the federal requirements may result in the loss of up to $400,000 in 
federal Occupational Safety and Health Administration grant funds that are used to reimburse the Bureau of Labor 
Standards for the costs associated with conducting public sector consultation and enforcement services.
