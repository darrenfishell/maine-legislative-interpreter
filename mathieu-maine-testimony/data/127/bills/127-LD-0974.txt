SUMMARY  18 
This bill provides for a supplemental benefit paid to a corrections officer who is 19 
injured by the acts of a patient or prisoner of the jail, prison or state correctional facility 20 
where t he corrections officer works if the injury qualifies the corrections officer for 21 
workers' compensation benefits.  The amount of the supplemental benefit is the difference 22 
between the corrections officer's workers' compensation benefits and the corrections 23 
officer's regular salary.  In addition, the work time the corrections officer misses is not 24 
chargeable against available sick leave credits.  25
