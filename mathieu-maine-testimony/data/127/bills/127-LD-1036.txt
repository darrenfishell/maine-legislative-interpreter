SUMMARY  23 
This bill makes an applicant for general assistance who voluntarily abandons or 24 
refuses to use an available resource without just cause ineligible to receive general 25 
assistance to replace the abandoned resource for a period of 120 days from the date the 26 
applicant abandons the resource.  The bill defines "available resource" as a re source that 27 
is immediately available or can be secured without delay.  The bill also makes an 28 
applicant who forfeits an available resource due to fraud, misrepresentation or intentional 29 
violation or refusal to comply with rules without just cause ineligibl e to receive general 30 
assistance to replace the forfeited resource for the duration of the sanction imposed on the 31 
applicant for violation of a rule or 120 days, whichever is greater.  The bill identifies 32 
circumstances relating to use of an available resour ce under which just cause must be 33 
found.  34
