SUMMARY  5 
Current law permits funds held in prearranged funeral and burial plans to be invested 6 
in certain listed permissible investment categories. This bill expands those options by 7 
permitting these funds to be invested in the same categories of investments that are 8 
authorized under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 39 -A, section 403, subsection 9 as  9 
permissible investments for trust funds for self -insurers under the laws governing 10 
workers' compensation.  11
