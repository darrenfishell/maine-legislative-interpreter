14 'Sec. 1. Department of Health and Human Services; reimbursement for 
15 days awaiting placement; reimbursement for hospitals other than critical 
16 access hospitals. Resolved: That the Department of Health and Human Services shall 
17 amend the rules under Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 45, 
18 Hospital Services to provide reimbursement to hospitals other than critical access 
19 hospitals for each day after the 10th day that a MaineCare-eligible individual is in the 
20 care of a hospital while awaiting placement in a nursing facility. The Department of 
21 Health and Human Services shall reimburse hospitals prospectively at the statewide 
22 average rate per member day for nursing facility services. The Department of Health and 
23 Human Services shall compute the statewide average rate per member day based on the 
24 simple average of the nursing facility rate per member day for the applicable state fiscal 
25 year or years and prorated for the hospital's fiscal year. Reimbursement for days awaiting 
26 placement pursuant to this section is limited to a period of 5 years and to a maximum of 
27 $500,000 of combined General Fund funds and federal funds for each year of the 5-year 
28 period. Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules as defined in the 
29 Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A; and be it further
