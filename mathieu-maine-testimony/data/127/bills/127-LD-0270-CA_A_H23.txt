9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 188, L.D. 270, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Reduce the Annual High-stakes Beano Fee from $50,000 to $12,500" 
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
12 'An Act To Temporarily Reduce the Annual High-stakes Beano Fee from $50,000 to 
13 $25,000' 
14 Amend the bill by striking out all of section 1 and inserting the following: 
15 'Sec. 1. 17 MRSA §314-A, sub-§4, as amended by PL 2011, c. 410, §2, is further 
16 amended to read: 
17 4. Term of license; fees. A license issued under this section is valid for a period of 
18 one year. The annual license fee for a high-stakes beano license is $50,000, except that 
19 the annual license fee due in 2008 to;w.g 2016 is $25,000. License fees may be paid in 
20 advance in quarterly installments. All license fees must be paid to the Treasurer of State 
21 to be credited to the General Fund.' 
22 SUMMARY 
23 This amendment strikes the provision in the bill that reduces the annual fee for high-
24 stakes beano from $50,000 to $12,500. Existing law states that the annual fee was to be 
25 set at $25,000 from 2008 to 2013. The amendment provides that the fee be set at $25,000 
26 through 2016. The amendment retains the provision of the bill that makes the change 
27 retroactive to apply to the 2014 fee.

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill reduces the annual license fee for a high-stakes beano license from $50,000 to $25,000 through 2016. This 
will reduce Other Special Revenue Funds revenue to the Department of Public Safety by an estimated $25,000 in each 
of fiscal years 2014-15, 2015-16 and 2016-17.
