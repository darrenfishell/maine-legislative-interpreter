SUMMARY  18 
This bill provides that the Director of the Bureau  of Parks and Lands within the 19 
Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may not issue a permit for a swim 20 
area the length of which, measured parallel to the shore, is more than 50% of the length 21 
of the shore frontage of the property from which the swim area extends or 200 feet, 22 
whichever is greater.  23
