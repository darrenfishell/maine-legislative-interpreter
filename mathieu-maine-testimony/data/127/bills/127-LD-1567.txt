SUMMARY  11 
This bill allows the Commissioner of Transportation to permit the construction of all - 12 
terrain vehicle and snowmobile trails within the right -of-way limits of controlled access 13 
highways in areas of new construction.  It also permits the operations of all -terrain 14 
vehicles and snowmobiles on those trail segments.  15
