SUMMARY  12 
This bill requires that a motor vehicle not otherwise subject to inspection be inspected 13 
for working lights and brakes within one  year before transporting a member of the 14 
general public.  Prior to transporting a member of the general public in the vehicle, the 15 
owner of the vehicle must certify by affidavit that the inspection requirements have been 16 
met. The owner is subject to a fin e of up to $500 for executing a false affidavit or 17 
otherwise violating the inspection requirements.  18
