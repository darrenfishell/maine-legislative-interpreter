SUMMARY 
This amendment provides that a person is prohibited from advertising or promoting 
for emergency response services any telephone number other than 9-1-1. The amendment 
also provides a process for the imposition of a penalty for a person that uses a number 
other than 9-1-1 to advertise or promote emergency response services.
