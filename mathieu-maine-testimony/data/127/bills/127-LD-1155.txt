SUMMARY  29 
This bill requires a minimum cash bail for defendants charged with certain crimes 30 
under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 17 -A.  The bill requires a minimum cash bail of 31 
$4,000 for a cha rge of assault under section 207, domestic violence assault under section 32 
207-A, aggravated assault under section 208, elevated aggravated assault under section 33 
208-B or elevated aggravated assault on a pregnant person under section 208 -C.   The bill 34 
requi res a minimum cash bail of $2,000 for a charge of criminal threatening under section 35 
209, domestic violence criminal threatening under section 209 -A, terrorizing under 36 
section 210, stalking under section 210 -A, domestic violence terrorizing under section 37 
210-B, domestic violence stalking under section 210 -C, reckless conduct under section 38 
211, domestic violence reckless conduct under section 211 -A or aggravated reckless 39 
conduct under section 213.  40
