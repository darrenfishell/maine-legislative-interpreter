SUMMARY  3 
This bill amends Maine law prohibiting the possession of firearms to conform the law 4 
to federal law.  This bill adds the following persons to the list of persons who may n ot 5 
possess firearms:  6 
1.  Fugitives from justice;  7 
2.  Persons who are unlawful users of or are addicted to any controlled substance and 8 
as a result are prohibited from possession of a firearm under 18 United States Code, 9 
Section 922(g)(3);  10 
3.  Aliens who ar e illegally or unlawfully in the United States or who were admitted 11 
under nonimmigrant visas and who are prohibited from possession of a firearm under 18 12 
United States Code, Section 922(g)(5);  13 
4.  Persons who have been discharged from the United States Arm ed Forces under 14 
dishonorable conditions;  15 
5.  Persons who have renounced United States citizenship; and  16 
6.  Persons who have been convicted in any court of a crime of domestic violence 17 
designated as a Class D or Class E crime or an equivalent crime of that jurisdiction.  18
