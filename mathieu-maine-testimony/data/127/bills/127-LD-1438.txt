SUMMARY  32 
Current law authorizes the court to prohibit a defendant who is subject to a protection 33 
from abuse order from posses sing a firearm or other dangerous weapon during the 34 
duration of the order.  This bill amends that law to specifically include bows and 35 
crossbows as weapons that the court may prohibit a defendant from possessing during the 36 
duration of a protection from abu se order.  37
