This amendment reduces the General Fund appropriations in the bill by $150,000 per year in both fiscal year 2015-16 
and fiscal year 2016-17. 
As amended, the bill still includes General Fund appropriations of $100,000 per year beginning in fiscal year 2015-16 
to the Maine Coworking Development Program within the Department of Economic and Community Development to 
support collaborative workspace businesses. It also includes base allocations of $500 in fiscal year 2015-16 and $500 
in fiscal year 2016-17 to establish the Maine Coworking Development Fund as a dedicated account within the 
department to accept contributions and grants from public and private sources.
