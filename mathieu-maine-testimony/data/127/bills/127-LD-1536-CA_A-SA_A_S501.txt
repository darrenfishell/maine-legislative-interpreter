SUMMARY 
This amendment eliminates the funding for ballistic vests for forest rangers and 
reduces the funding for law enforcement officers in the Department of Marine Resources, 
Bureau of Marine Patrol.

The amendment eliminates the $54,672 General Fund appropriation to the Department of Agriculture, Conservation 
and Forestry in fiscal year 2016-1 7 and reduces the General Fund appropriation to the Department of Marine 
Resources (DMR) by $27 ,336 in fiscal year 2016-17. DMR will instead receive a General Fund appropriation of 
$12,060 in fiscal year 2016-17 and require the same appropriation in subsequent years. 
Overall, as amended, the bill includes a General Fund appropriation of $171,312 in fiscal year 2016-17 for ballistic 
vests and vest carriers.
