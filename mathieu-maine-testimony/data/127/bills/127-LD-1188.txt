SUMMARY  4 
This bill directs the Maine State Housing Authority to implement a rental assistance 5 
program for low -income households and individua ls that allocates assistance throughout 6 
the State based upon the local area's availability, affordability, condition and cost burden 7 
of housing. Under the bill, 100% of available funds are awarded to participants with 8 
incomes at or below 30% of the median income of the local area.  9
