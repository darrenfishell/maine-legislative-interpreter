Sec. 2.  10 MRSA §1120 -A is enacted to read:  6 
§1120 -A.  Indemnification agreements against public policy  7 
It is against public policy and is void and unenforceable if, notwithstanding any other 8 
provision of this chapter, a provisi on, clause, covenant or agreement contained in, 9 
collateral to or affecting a construction contract purports to require a contractor or 10 
subcontractor, a contractor's or subcontractor's surety or that surety's insurer to indemnify 11 
the promisee against liabil ity for damages for loss, damage or expense arising from the 12 
negligence or willful misconduct of the promisee or an employee, agent or servant of the 13 
promisee or an independent contractor who is directly responsible to the promisee.  14 
SUMMARY  15 
This bill prohi bits certain indemnification agreements, including those by which a 16 
promisee requires a contracting party to indemnify the promisee from the promisee's own 17 
negligence or willful misconduct.  18
