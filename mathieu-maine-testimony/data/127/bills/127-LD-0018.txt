SUMMARY 9 
This resolve directs the Office of Program Evaluation and Government 10 
Accountability to review and audit the University of Maine System's finances and 11 
governance practices and submit a report of its findings to the Joint Standing Committee 12 
on Education and Cultural Affairs no later than January 15, 2016.  The joint standing 13 
committee may submit legislation on the topic of the report to the Second Regular 14 
Session of the 127th Legislature. 15
