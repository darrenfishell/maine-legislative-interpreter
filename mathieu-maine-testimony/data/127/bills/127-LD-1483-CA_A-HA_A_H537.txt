SUMMARY 
Committee Amendment "A" provides that the motor vehicle excise tax paid to the 
Secretary of State by a nonresident with no temporary or occasional residing place in the 
State must be credited to the Highway Fund. This amendment adds a cross-reference to 
clarify that that excise tax is not required to be deposited in the General Fund.
