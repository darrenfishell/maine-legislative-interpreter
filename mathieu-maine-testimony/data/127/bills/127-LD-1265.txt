SUMMARY  33 
This bill proposes to implement the recommendations of the Maine Health Exchange 34 
Advisory Committee pursuant to Joint Order 2013, House Paper 1136. The bill 35 
establishes  the Maine Health Exchange Advisory Committee on a permanent basis.  36 
The Joint Standing Committee on Insurance and Financial Services has not taken a 37 
position on the substance of the recommendations or the bill, and by reporting out this bill 38 
the committee is not suggesting and does not intend to suggest that it agrees or disagrees 39 
with any aspect of the advisory committee's recommendations or this bill. The Joint 40 
Standing Committee on Insurance and Financial Services is reporting out the bill for the 41

 sole p urpose of turning the advisory committee's proposal into a printed bill that can be 1 
referred to the Joint Standing Committee on Insurance and Financial Services for an 2

clarity and transparency in the legislative review of the advisory committee's proposal.  5
