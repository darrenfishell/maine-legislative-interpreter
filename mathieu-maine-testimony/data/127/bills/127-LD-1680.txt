SUMMARY  22 
This resolution proposes to amend the Constitution of Maine to reduce the size of the 23 
House of Representatives from 151 members to 100 members and the size of the Senate 24 
from no more than 35 members and no fewer than 31 members to 25 members, beginning 25 
with the Legislature elected in 2018.  26
