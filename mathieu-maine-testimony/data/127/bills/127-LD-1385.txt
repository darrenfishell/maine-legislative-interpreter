SUMMARY  18 
This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to pay 100% of the 19 
child care expenses incurred by a foster parent for child care provided at a licensed child 20 
care provider selected by the  foster parent, subject to certain determinations of the 21 
department.  The bill is in response to a proposal by the department to cap daycare 22 
payments.  23
