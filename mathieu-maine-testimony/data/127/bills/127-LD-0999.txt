This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to prohibit rule -making authority for rules having the force of law 5 
when the provisions of those  rules are not enacted by the Legislature or are otherwise 6 
inconsistent with the legislative powers enumerated in the Constitution of Maine, Article 7 
IV. 8 
As proposed in this bill, departments, agencies, boards, commissions and quasi - 9 
governmental entities ma y establish rules only for the purposes of performing the duties 10 
charged to them; any rules outside the scope of this authority must be submitted to the 11 
Legislature within one year of the effective date of this bill for enactment into law by the 12 
Legislatur e.  Any such rules not enacted by the Legislature within 2 years of the effective 13 
date of this bill are considered void and without force of law.  14
