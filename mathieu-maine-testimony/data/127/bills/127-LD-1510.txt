SUMMARY  30 
This b ill amends the law governing campaign financing and reporting by:  31 
1.  Clarifying that the definition of "political action committee" does not apply to an 32 
individual;  33 
2.  Requiring political action committees to register with the Commission on 34 
Governmental Ethics and Election Practices within 7 days of receiving contributions 35 
totaling more than the applicable threshold of $1,500 or $5,000;  36 
 
 Page 5 - 127LR2490(01) -1 
 3.  Requiring ballot question committees to register with the Commission on 1 
Governmental Ethics and Election Practices i n a manner similar to political action 2 
committees, including appointing a principal officer who would be jointly liable with the 3 
committee and the committee treasurer for penalties assessed against the committee;  4 
4.  Establishing a uniform threshold of $10 0 for both political action committees and 5 
ballot question committees regarding political contributions in campaign finance reports; 6 
and 7 
5.  Modifying the criteria for when a political action committee must report a 8 
contribution made for the purpose of inf luencing a candidate or ballot question election.  9
