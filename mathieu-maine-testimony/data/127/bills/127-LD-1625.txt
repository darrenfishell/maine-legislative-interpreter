SUMMARY  20 
This bill implements one of the recommendations made by the final report of the 21 
Commission To Strengthen and Align the Services Provided to Maine's Veterans and is 22 
submitted pursuant to Resolve 2015, chapter 48.  The bill requires  each campus of the 23 
University of Maine System with a significant number of student -veterans to include an 24 
office that provides assistance to those veterans with the intent of facilitating transition 25 
from military life and supporting successful completion of the veterans' postsecondary 26 
education.  The bill requires that any office established pursuant to this bill must use, to 27 
the greatest extent possible, the office at the University of Southern Maine as a model.  28
