The amendment allows landfills that sent applications received by the Department of Environmental Protection (DEP) 
on or before September 1, 1989 to become eligible to have DEP pay 75% of closure costs incurred between July 1, 
1994 and before December 31,2025. DEP estimates that at least one additiona11andfill would become eligible. DEP 
will expend an estimated $365,600 for these closure costs. This amendment includes Other Special Revenue Funds 
allocations of $91,400 in each of fiscal years 2015-16 and 2016-17 for landfill closure costs. All costs to the DEP will 
be funded from the Maine Solid Waste Management Fund within DEP. The fund has sufficient resources to absorb 
these costs.
