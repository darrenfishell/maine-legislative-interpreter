11 This amendment is the minority report of the committee and replaces the bill. The 
12 amendment provides a new title for the bill. The amendment provides immunity to a 
13 minor from prosecution under the alcoholic beverages laws applicable to minors when 
14 there is a risk of alcohol overdose. The bill proposes to provide a defense to prosecution 
15 for a minor when there is a risk of alcohol overdose.
