SUMMARY  18 
This bill amends the Child and Family Services and Child Protection Act to comply 19 
with the federal Preventing Sex Trafficking and Strengthening Families Act.  The bill 20 
requires the Department of Health and Human Services to disclose certain in formation on 21 
missing or abducted children or youth to the National Crime Information Center database 22 
of the Federal Bureau of Investigation and to a national information clearinghouse for 23 
missing and exploited children and to provide notification of the re moval of a child from 24 
the custody of a parent or custodian to all parents of a sibling of the child who have legal 25 
custody of the sibling.  The bill also requires that permanency plans for children who are 26 
14 years of age and older must determine the servi ces needed to assist the children to 27 
make the transition from foster care to independent living.  28
