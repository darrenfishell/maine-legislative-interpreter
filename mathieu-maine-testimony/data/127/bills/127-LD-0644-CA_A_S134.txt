This bill reduces the income tax on annuities by .5% each year until it becomes zero in 2019. It would reduce General 
Fund revenue by $795,150 in fiscal year 2015-16 and $1,867,700 in fiscal year 2016-17 and Local Government Fund 
revenue by $41,850 in fiscal year 2015-16 and $98,300 in fiscal year 2016-17. The bill includes a one-time General 
Fund appropriation of$33,600 to the Department of Administrative and Financial Services for computer programming 
charges.
