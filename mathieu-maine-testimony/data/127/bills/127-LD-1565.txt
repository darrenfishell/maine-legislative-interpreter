SUMMARY  4 
This bill increases the maximum fee a llowed to be paid to a nonsalaried medical 5 
examiner or a nonsalaried medicolegal death investigator for an inspection and view.  6 
It increases the fees charged by the Department of the Attorney General, Office of 7 
Chief Medical Examiner for providing report d ocuments and histological slides.  It also 8 
increases the fee charged by a medical examiner for a certificate that is required for 9 
cremation and allows this fee to be waived at the discretion of the Chief Medical 10
