This bill provides a one-time General Fund appropriation of $300,000 in fiscal year 2015-16 for the Judicial 
Department to provide architectural feasibility studies to improve court facilities in Oxford County, Waldo County 
and York County. The Judiciary Committee recommendations in the Governor's proposed budget for the 2016-2017 
biennium, LD 1019, also includes $300,000 in fiscal year 2015-16 for feasibility studies. This fiscal note may need to 
be revised after final legislative action on the biennial budget hill.
