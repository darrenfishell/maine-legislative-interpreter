SUMMARY  3 
This bill provides funding in fiscal years 2014 -15 and 2015 -16 in the amount of 4 
$225,000 for the Lake and River Pro tection Fund within the Department of Inland 5 
Fisheries and Wildlife and in the amount of $900,000 for the Invasive Aquatic Plant and 6 
Nuisance Species Fund within the Department of Environmental Protection.  These 7 
General Fund distributions are intended to correspond to the annual average funds 8 
deposited into each of these accounts representing fees collected on motorboat and 9 
personal watercraft lake and river protection stickers issued pursuant to the Maine 10 
Revised Statutes, Title 12, section 13058, subsect ion 3.  Pursuant to statute, these funds 11 
are used by each department to engage in activities relating to the containment, 12 
eradication and management of the spread of invasive aquatic plants and nuisance species 13 
in Maine, to assist municipalities and other entities in accomplishing these goals and to 14 
aid in the enforcement of laws and rules pertaining to invasive aquatic plants and 15 
nuisance species.  16
