The Commissioner of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestrv shall establish a

§8204. Firewood Infestation Education Fund 
The Firewood Infestation Education Fund. referred to in this section as "the fimd," is 
established. The fund is a nonlapsing. fund administered by the Department of 
Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry for the purposes of educating the public about and 
promoting public awareness of the dangers of infestation of invasive species tlrrough the

resources and tools for landowners. Money in the fimd consists of appropriations or 
allocations for the purpose of the fund and any other money, including federal money and 
other public and private contributions, deposited into the fund. Income from investment 
of the fund must be deposited to the credit of the fund.

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The biIl requires the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry (ACF) to establish a firewood emergency 
response group to respond to reports of infestation of firewood by invasive species and establishes the Firewood 
Infestation Education Fund within ACF for educational and promotional purposes. The bill includes Other Special 
Revenue Funds allocations of $500 in each of fiscal years 2015-16 and 2016-17 to establish the fund. No revenue 
source is provided in the bill. Any additional costs to ACF to establish a firewood emergency response group are 
expected to be minor and can be absorbed within existing budgeted resources.
