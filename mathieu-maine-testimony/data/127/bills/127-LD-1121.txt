SUMMARY  22 
This bill repeals the requirement that the Commissioner of Public Safety and the 23 
Attorney General pursue federal funding to establish a cold case homicide unit.  The bill 24 
also repeals the provision of law that makes establishment of a cold case homicide unit 25 
contingent upon availability of  federal funding.  The provision of law directing the 26 
Attorney General in collaboration with the Commissioner of Public Safety to establish a 27 
cold case homicide unit is not repealed.  The bill provides funding for the fiscal year 28 
2016 -17. 29
