SUMMARY  8 
This bill creates a 90 -pound per day, per person harvesting limit on scallops; 9 
however, this bill exempts scallops harvested through aquaculture from this limit.  The 10 
bill provides that a hand fishing scallop license or scallop draggin g license must be issued 11 
to an applicant who previously held a hand fishing scallop license or scallop dragging 12 
license, which had expired.  The bill also imposes a maximum width of 5 feet, 6 inches 13 
for scallop drags.  14
