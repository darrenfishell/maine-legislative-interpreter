COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" A" to H.P. 582, L.D. 851, "Resolve, Regarding 
Legislative Review of Portions of Chapter 2: Standards for Qualifications of Assigned 
Counsel, a Late-filed Major Substantive Rule of the Maine Commission on Indigent 
Legal Services" 
Amend the resolve in section I in the last line (page I, line 18 in L.D.) by striking out 
the following: "authorized." and inserting the following: 'authorized only if the 
following change is made:' 
Amend the resolve in section 1 by inserting at the end the following: 
'I. The rule must be amended in section 2, subsection I, paragraph c) to provide that 
an attorney must inform the commission in writing within 5 days of the filing of any 
criminal charge against the attorney.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment requires that the Maine Commission on Indigent Legal Services 
amend the proposed rule on standards for qualifications of assigned counsel to require 
that an attorney on the roster inform the commission in writing within 5 days of the filing 
of any criminal charge against the attorney. The provisionally adopted rule requires the 
report to be made promptly.
