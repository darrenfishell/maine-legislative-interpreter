SUMMARY  15 
This resolve, which is a recommendation of the Commission To Study Difficult -to- 16 
place Patients, directs the Department of Health and Human Services to amend the rules 17 
under Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 45, Hospital 18 
Services to provide reimbursement to hospitals other than critical access hospitals for 19 
each day that a MaineCare -eligible individual is in the care of a hospital other than a 20 
critical access hospital while awaiting placement in a nursing facility.  The 21 
reimbursement is to be paid prospectively at the statewide average rate per member day 22 
for nursing facility service s.  The department is directed to implement this reimbursement 23 
for days awaiting placement for a period of no more than 5 years and is limited to a 24 
maximum of $500,000 of combined General Fund funds and federal funds for the entire 25 
5-year period.  26
