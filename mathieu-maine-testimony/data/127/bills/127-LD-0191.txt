SUMMARY  16 
This bill provides that, when land taxed under the tree growth tax program is 17 
transferred, the new landowner may file a sworn statement that a new forest management 18 
and harvest plan has been  prepared. If the new landowner does not file a new plan, the 19 
landowner must continue to manage the land in accordance with the plan prepared for the 20 
previous landowner or the land is considered to have been withdrawn from the program.  21
