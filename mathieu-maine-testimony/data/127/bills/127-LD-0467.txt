SUMMARY  8 
This bill reduces the size of the Saco River Corridor .  Under current law, the corridor 9 
includes lands adjacent to the rivers within the corridor to a distance of 500 feet from the 10 
high water line of the rivers or the edge of the floodplain if that extends beyond 500 feet, 11 
up to a maximum of 1,000 feet.  The  bill changes the distance to 250 feet from the high 12 
water line or to the edge of the floodplain if that extends beyond 250 feet, up to a 13 
maximum of 250 feet.  14
