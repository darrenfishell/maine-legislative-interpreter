6.  Ownership of generation assets. Notwithstanding any other provision in this 6 
chapter, an investor -owned transmission and distribution utility may own, have a 7 
financial interest in or control generation or generation -related assets in the State if the 8 
utility or an affiliated interest applies for and receives approval from the commission 9 
prior to the time that the investor -owned transmission and distribution utility owns, 10 
obtains a financial interest in or controls the generation or generation -related assets.  The 11 
commission may approve an application under this subsection if the applicant establishes 12 
that the ownership, financial interest in or control of the generation or generation -related 13 
asset is consistent with the interests of the utility's  ratepayers.  In granting approval under 14 
this subsection, the commission shall impose terms, conditions or requirements the 15 
commission determines are necessary to protect the interests of the utility's ratepayers.  16 
SUMMARY  17 
This bill allows an investor -owned  transmission and distribution utility to own 18 
generation assets if the Public Utilities Commission determines that ownership is 19 
beneficial to the utility's ratepayers.  20
