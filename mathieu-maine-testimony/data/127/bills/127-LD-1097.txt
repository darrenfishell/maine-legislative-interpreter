SUMMARY  28 
This bill restricts the use of benefits for recipients of temporary assistance for needy  29 
families under the electronic benefits transfer system by prohibiting use of the electronic 30 
benefits transfer system outside of the State, except for in New Hampshire, withdrawals 31 
of cash per month in an amount over 25% of a recipient's monthly benefits a nd 32 
expenditures on items such as tobacco products, liquor and lottery tickets and several 33 
other similar items.  This bill also directs the Department of Health and Human Services 34 
to hire 5 additional fraud investigators.  35
