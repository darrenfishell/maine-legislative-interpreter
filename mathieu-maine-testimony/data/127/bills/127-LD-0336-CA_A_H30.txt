10 Combine Outstanding Student Loan Debt with a Home Mortgage" 
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
12 'Resolve, Directing the Finance Authority of Maine To Convene a Stakeholder 
13 Group To Develop Solutions To Reduce the Negative Effect of Student Loan Debt 
14 on Home Ownership' 
15 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and before the summary and 
16 inserting the following: 
17 'Sec. 1. Finance Authority of Maine to convene stakeholder group. 
18 Resolved: That the Finance Authority of Maine, referred to in this resolve as "the 
19 authority," shall convene a stakeholder group to study the issues surrounding outstanding 
20 student loan debt and its negative effect on the ability of residents of the State to own 
21 homes and to develop solutions to reduce outstanding student loan debt to facilitate home 
22 ownership. The authority shall invite the participation of Legislators; the Department of 
23 Professional and Financial Regulation, Bureau of Financial Institutions and Bureau of 
24 Consumer Credit Protection; the Maine State Housing Authority; and representatives of 
25 financial institutions, credit unions, mortgage lenders, real estate agents and brokers, 
26 students and other interested parties; and be it further 
27 Sec. 2. Report. Resolved: That, no later than January 1,2016, the authority shall 
28 report the findings and recommendations of the study under section 1 along with any 
29 suggested legislation to the Joint Standing Committee on Insurance and Financial 
30 Services. The Joint Standing Committee on Insurance and Financial Services may submit 
31 a bill related to the subject matter of the report to the Second Regular Session of the 
32 I27th Legislature.'
