SUMMARY  24 
This bill amends the provisions that apply to the appointment of the 4 members of the 25 
Maine Charter School Commission, who are currently nominated and appointed by the 3 26 
members of the State Board of Education subject only to review by the joint standing 27 
committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over education matters and majority vote 28 
of the state board, to provide instead for nomination by the 3 members of the state board 29 
for appointment by the Governor, subject to review by the joint standing committee of the 30 
Legislature having jurisdiction over education matters and confirmation by t he Senate.  31
