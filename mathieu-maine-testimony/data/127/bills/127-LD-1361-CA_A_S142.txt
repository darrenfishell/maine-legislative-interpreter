Fiscal Note 
Potential future savings -All funds 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Prohibiting municipalities from enacting ordinances related to a minimum wage could save the State future 
expenditures in any instance where a municipality might choose to impose a minimum wage, indexed or otherwise, 
that could exceed the lowest salary paid to a state employee within that municipality. No estimate is made at this time 
on the amount or probability of any savings.
