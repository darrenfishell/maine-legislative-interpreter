SUMMARY  15 
This bill allows a taxpayer, either an individual or corporation, that o perates a 16 
registered dispensary of medical marijuana pursuant to the Maine Medical Use of 17 
Marijuana Act to deduct from income the costs of operating that registered dispensary.  18 
The bill also allows a qualified patient to deduct from income the costs of me dical 19 
marijuana purchased by that qualifying patient.  20
