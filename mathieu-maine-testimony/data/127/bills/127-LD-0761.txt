Sec. 3.  28-A MRSA §1055 is enacted to read:  8 
§1055.  Wine samples at restaurants  9 
1.  Wine samples at restaurants.   A restaurant, Class A restaurant or Class A 10 
restaurant/lounge may offer a complimentary sample of wine to a customer, subject to the 11 
following conditions:  12 
A.  A sample may not be given to a person under 21 years of age;  13 
B.  A perso n may not be served more than a total of 5 ounces of wine having an 14 
alcohol content of 14% or less or more than a total of 3 ounces of wine having an 15 
alcohol content greater than 14%;  16 
C.  A person who is visibly intoxicated may not be served;  17 
D.  The wine sampling must be conducted within the hours of retail sale for liquor 18 
established in this Title;  19 
E.  The wine sampling must take place on the licensed premises; and  20 
F.  Wine served as a wine sample must be purchased from a wholesale licensee.  21 
SUMMARY  22 
This bill allows a restaurant, Class A restaurant or Class A restaurant/lounge to offer 23 
complimentary samples of wine to a customer subject to certain conditions.  24
