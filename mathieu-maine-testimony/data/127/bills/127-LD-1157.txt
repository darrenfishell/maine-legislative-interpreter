SUMMARY  16 
This bill prohibits employers from obtaining information regarding the 17 
creditworthiness, credit standing, credit capacity, debts or check -writing experience of a 18 
prospective employee as part of the hiring process or de cision.  There are exceptions from 19 
this prohibition in the bill for circumstances in which the job requires management of the 20 
company's finances or a customer's financial assets, the employer is in the financial 21 
services industry or the employer is otherwi se required by law to obtain this information.  22 
Violation of this prohibition is a civil violation for which a fine from $500 to $1,000 for 23 
each violation may be adjudged.  The prohibition is enforced by the Director of Labor 24 
Standards within the Departmen t of Labor.  25
