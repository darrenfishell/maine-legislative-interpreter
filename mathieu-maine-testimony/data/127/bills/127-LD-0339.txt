SUMMARY  25 
This bill clarifies that the telephone number 9 -1-1 is the primary number advertised 26 
or promoted for emergency response services.  The  bill also eliminates certain outdated 27 
language regarding the publication of the 9 -1-1 number in telephone directories.  28
