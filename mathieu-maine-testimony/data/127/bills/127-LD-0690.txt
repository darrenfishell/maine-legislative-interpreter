SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
The purpose of this bill is to ensure the safety of women who choose to give birth at 5 
home or in freestanding birth ing centers attended by certified professional midwives.  6 
This bill proposes to license and regulate certified professional midwives practicing in 7 
Maine.  Among other things, the bill will establish:  8 
1.  Criteria for licensure and oversight;  9 
2.  A regulato ry body including certified professional midwives, clients, certified 10 
nurse midwives and physicians to oversee complaint and disciplinary processes;  11 
3.  Procedures to allow other health care providers to consult with, collaborate with or 12 
accept transfer of  care from a licensed certified professional midwife;  13 
4.  Procedures to allow for protected peer review for licensed certified professional 14 
midwives; and  15 
5.  Requirements for data collection and submission for quality improvement 16 
purposes.  17
