SUMMARY  11 
Current law provides that if a person discharges oil into or upon certain water bodies, 12 
lands adjacent to certain water bodies, public or privat e water supplies or sewers that 13 
person is not subject to fines or civil penalties if that person, in addition to taking other 14 
actions, reports the discharge.  This bill exempts discharges of less than 10 gallons from 15 
the reporting requirement.  16
