SUMMARY  10 
This bill allows a holder of a liquor license to sell or deliver liquor from 5 a.m. on 11 
any day until 1 a.m. of the following day except in those areas in which liquor may not be 12 
sold on Sundays.  The bill also amends sections of law that deal with the sale of liquor on 13 
Sundays to reflect this change.  14
