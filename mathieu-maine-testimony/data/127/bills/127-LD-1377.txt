SUMMARY  11 
This resolve establishes the Commission To Study the Reduction of Unfunded and 12 
Outdated Municipal Mandates.  The commission is required to meet at least twice a year 13 
for 2 years to review unfunded and  outdated municipal mandates in order to provide 14 
recommendations and to report out a list of mandates for the Legislature to consider 15 
eliminating or revising.  16
