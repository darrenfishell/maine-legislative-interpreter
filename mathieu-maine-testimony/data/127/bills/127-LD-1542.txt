SUMMARY  15 
This bill authorizes an employer to provide its employees a group disability income 16 
protecti on plan, which is a group policy instituted by an employer that provides income 17 
benefits to an employee who is unable to work for an extended period of time because of 18 
sickness or an accident.  The group disability income protection plan may be either a 19 
short-term plan offering at least 6 months of benefits or a long -term plan offering at least 20 
24 months of benefits.  The premium paid by an employee for participation in an 21 
employer -sponsored group disability income protection plan is considered a premium th at 22 
the employee has agreed to pay, as long as certain conditions are met.  23 
An employer is entitled to a tax credit of $50 for each employee enrolled in a group 24 
disability income protection plan after January 1, 2017. The credit may be taken by an 25 
employer f or no more than 5 years.  26
