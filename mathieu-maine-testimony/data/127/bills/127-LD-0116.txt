SUMMARY  9 
This bill allows a municipality or plantation that forms a tax increment financing 10 
district to use the re venue generated by that district to pay for the costs of vital public 11 
services, as long as the costs of the services do not exceed 50% of the tax increment 12 
revenue and the costs are not paid for more than 2 fiscal years during the life of the tax 13 
increment  financing district and its development program.  Vital public services are fire 14 
and rescue, law enforcement and waste disposal services and local road improvements.  15
