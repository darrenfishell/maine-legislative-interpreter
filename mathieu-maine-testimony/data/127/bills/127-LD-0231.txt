SUMMARY  12 
This bill defines dyslexia as a specific learning disability that is neurological in 13 
origin.  It requires sch ool administrative units to conduct screenings of students from 14 
kindergarten to grade 2 for dyslexia, except that, for the 2016 -2017 school year, school 15 
administrative units must screen students from kindergarten to grade 3.  It requires school 16 
administrat ive units to screen certain transfer and older students.  It also creates the 17 
position of dyslexia consultant within the Department of Education and provides for the 18 
dyslexia consultant to provide school administrative units with dyslexia professional 19 
awar eness training.  20
