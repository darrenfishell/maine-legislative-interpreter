17 'Sec. 1. Bureau of Insurance report. Resolved: That the Department of 
18 Professional and Financial Regulation, Bureau of Insurance shall conduct a review and 
19 evaluation pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 24-A, section 2752 of a 
20 mandated health benefit proposing to require a carrier offering a health plan in this State 
21 to include coverage for the costs of providing emergency medical services to an enrollee, 
22 including, but not limited to, the transportation costs required to provide medical 
23 assistance even if the enrollee is not transported to a hospital or other health care facility. 
24 The bureau shall submit the review and evaluation required by this section to the Joint 
25 Standing Committee on Insurance and Financial Services on or before January 1, 2016. 
26 The joint standing committee may report out a bill based on the review and evaluation to 
27 the Second Regular Session of the 127th Legislature. '
