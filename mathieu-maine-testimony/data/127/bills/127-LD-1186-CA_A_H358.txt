Fiscal Note 
Current biennium revenue increase -Other Special Revenue Funds 
Current biennium cost increase -Other Special Revenue Funds 
The bill authorizes the State Court Administrator to establish fees on lawyers, guardians ad litem, interpreters, 
mediators and other professionals who participate in court proceedings to cover the costs of training, orientation, 
continuing education, background investigations, entry screening and security. Fees are also authorized on 3rd parties 
to cover the costs of the use of court facilities for purposes not related to court functions by those 3rd parties.
