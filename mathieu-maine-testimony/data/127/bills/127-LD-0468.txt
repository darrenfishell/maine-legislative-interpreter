SUMMARY  33 
The purpose of this bill is to protect the environment and wildlife of the State and to 34 
reduce the increasing costs of litter colle ction and solid waste disposal by prohibiting the 35 
sale or distribution of disposable polystyrene food service containers, which constitute a 36 
sizable portion of nondegradable litter and solid waste in the State and which fragment 37

Beginning January 1, 2016, this bill prohibits the sale or distribution, at retail or 3 
wholesale, in the State of disposable food service containers composed in whole or in part 4 
of poly styrene foam, as well as disposable food service containers that are not recyclable 5 
or compostable unless there is no recyclable or compostable product available at a 6 
comparable cost.  This bill requires the Department of Environmental Protection to adopt 7
