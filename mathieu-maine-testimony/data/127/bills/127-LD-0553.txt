 SUMMARY  1 
This bill makes the statutory changes necessary to include a representative of the 2 
Aroostook Band of Micmacs in the Maine House of Representatives beginning with the 3 
128th Legislature.  It requires the Tribal Clerk of the Aroostook Band of Micmacs to 4 
furnish the outgoing Clerk of the House of Representativ es with a certification of the 5 
name and residence of the Representative -elect of the Aroostook Band of Micmacs to the 6 
Legislature.  It also authorizes the Representative of the Aroostook Band of Micmacs to 7 
be compensated in the same manner as other members  of the House of Representatives.  8 
It also provides that the changes do not take effect unless the Aroostook Band of 9 
Micmacs approves them and provides certification of that approval to the Secretary of 10 
State within 90 days of the adjournment of the First Regular Session of the 127th 11 
Legislature.  12
