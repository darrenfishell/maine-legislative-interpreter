 Sec. 1.  Department of Public Safety to study the feasibility of developing 1 
statewide and regional training facilities for firefighting. Resolved:  That the 2 
Department of Public Safety shall hire an independent consultant to study the feasibility 3 
of establ ishing a statewide, centrally located firefighter training facility and several 4 
regional firefighter training facilities.  The regional training facilities must be located so 5 
as to be within reach of at least 90% of the firefighters of the State within one  hour.  At a 6 
minimum, the study must identify:  7 
1.  The best location for each regional firefighter training facility based on 8 
demographics and environmental impact;  9 
2.  Other potential users of the firefighter training facilities;  10 
3.  The type of training to be provided at each facility;  11 
4.  The daily structuring of the operations of each facility;  12 
5.  The annual operational cost per facility;  13 
6.  Opportunities for the facilities to generate other revenue to offset annual 14 
operational costs;  15 
7.  The benefits  of the facilities to public safety; and  16 
8.  The funding and grant options available on a statewide and federal basis for 17 
construction and maintenance of the facilities.  18 
The Department of Public Safety shall submit a report based on the results of the 19 
study to the Joint Standing Committee on Criminal Justice and Public Safety by January 20 
6, 2016; and be it further  21
