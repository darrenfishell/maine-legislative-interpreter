SUMMARY 
This amendment reduces the amount of the bond issue for the grant program to invest 
in projects that contribute to economic activity, environmental protection and community 
development along the State's waterfronts. This amendment specifies that the projects 
pertain to the State's ocean and river waterfronts. This amendment also provides for a 
transfer to the Secretary of State if the number or length of the referendum questions to be 
submitted to the voters at the general election in November 2016 requires the Secretary of 
State to produce and distribute more than one ballot to accommodate those questions.
