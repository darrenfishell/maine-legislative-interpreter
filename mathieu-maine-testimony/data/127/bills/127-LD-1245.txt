 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  35-A MRSA §752, sub -§§1-A and 4 -A are enacted to read:  2 
1-A.  Dynamic sign.  "Dynamic sign" means an electronic sign capable of displaying 3 
a changing message.  4 
4-A.  Traffic beacon.  "Traffic beacon" means a light used for traffic control.  5 
Sec. 2.  35-A MRSA §761, sub -§4 is enacted to read:  6 
4.  Municipalities; state agencies.  This chapter does not apply to an employee or 7 
independent contractor engaged on behalf of a municipality or state agency to maintain or 8 
install traffic signals, traffic beacons, dynamic signs or luminaires.  9 
SUMMARY  10 
This bill provides an exemption from the Overhead High -voltage Line Safety Act to 11 
an employee or independent contractor engaged on behalf of a municipality  or state 12 
agency to maintain or install traffic signals, traffic beacons, dynamic signs or luminaires 13 
within 10 feet of overhead high -voltage lines.  14
