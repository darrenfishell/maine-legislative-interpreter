SUMMARY 
This amendment strikes the emergency preamble and emergency clause and adjusts 
certain dates and funding accordingly. Specifically, this amendment: 
1. Provides for an upward adjustment of salary schedules for certain law 
enforcement positions in fiscal year 2016-17, instead of fiscal year 2015-16; 
2. Provides one-time funding for the operation of the State's county jails and regional 
jail in fiscal year 2016-17, instead of fiscal year 2015-16; 
3. Removes funding for the last 3 months of fiscal year 2015-16 for an increase in 
reimbursement rates to eligible MaineCare providers; and
