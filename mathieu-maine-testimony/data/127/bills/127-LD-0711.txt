SUMMARY  24 
This bill requires that, by September 1, 2017, each school administrative unit ensure 25 
that its secondary schools' school days start no earlier than 8:30 a.m. and that there be at 26 
least 11 consecutive hours of uninterrupted time f rom the end of a class, extracurricular 27 
activity, athletic practice or any other school activity to the start of the next school day.  28 
A school administrative unit is required to submit a compliance plan.  Units that do not 29 
comply face reductions in the st ate share of funding attributable to the cost of the 30 
components of essential programs and services.  31
