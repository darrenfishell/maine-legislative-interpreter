SUMMARY  30 
This bill restricts the jurisdiction of the Board of Trustees of the Maine Public 31 
Employees Retirement System in making final and determining administ rative decisions 32 
regarding claims of certain participating local district employees.  This bill also makes 33 
changes to the laws governing participation and claims for prior service credit for certain 34 
participating local district employees.  35
