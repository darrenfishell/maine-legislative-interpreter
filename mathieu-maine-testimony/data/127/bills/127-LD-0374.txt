 SUMMARY  1 
This bill provides that, beginning July 1, 2017, the statutory provisions related to the 2 
renewal of certificates for school administrators are amended to include the completion of 3 
at least 50 hours of direct student instruction as a q ualification for renewal of the 4 
administrator's certificate.  The bill also directs the Department of Education and the State 5 
Board of Education to provisionally adopt rules related to the renewal of certificates for 6 
school administrators no later than Dec ember 31, 2016.  7
