SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to enact measures designed to facilitate the ability of so -called 5 
"one-industry towns" to plan f or, and recover from, the closure of that one industry.  The 6 
measures proposed by the bill may include, but are not limited to:  7 
1.  The imposition of a fee on any ancillary commodity produced by the industry, 8 
such as hydropower produced by a mill, and remi tting the amount of the fee to the town;  9 
2.  A requirement that municipalities that are reliant on a single industry develop, 10 
with representatives of the industry, a long -term plan for the industry and any ancillary 11 
commodity produced by the industry; and  12 
3.  A requirement that an industry provide municipal officials written notice of its 13 
plan to terminate its business at least 90 days prior to the intended termination.  14
