SUMMARY  18 
This bill creates the Hunting and Fishing Law Revie w Advisory Committee under the 19 
Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to review inland fisheries and wildlife laws 20 
and rules and make recommendations to the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and 21 
Wildlife regarding any necessary changes and conduct an a nnual survey of holders of 22 
fishing and hunting licenses and permits on various inland fisheries and wildlife issues in 23 
the State.  24
