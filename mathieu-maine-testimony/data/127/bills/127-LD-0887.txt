 §12260 -B.  Unlawful trapping of bear  1

SUMMARY  4 
This bill prohibits the use of dogs while hunting bear and it prohibits trapping be ar, 5 
unless necessary for the trapping of nuisance bears as permitted by the Commissioner of 6 
Inland Fisheries and Wildlife.  7
