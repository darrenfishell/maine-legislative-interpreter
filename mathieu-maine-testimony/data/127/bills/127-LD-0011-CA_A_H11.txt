Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill exempts from sales tax sales of the United States flag as long as the flag is made of cloth. It would result in a 
loss of General Fund revenue of $93,337 in fiscal year 2015··16 and $129,926 in fiscal year 2016-17 and a loss to the 
Local Government Fund of $4,913 in fiscal year 2015-16 and $6,838 in fiscal year 2016-17.
