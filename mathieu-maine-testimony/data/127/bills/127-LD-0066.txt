 Sec. 1.  Bridges in Machias named.  Resolved:   That the Department of 1 
Transportation shall designate bridges numbered 1469, 1470 and 2191 on U.S. Route 1 2 
that span the Machias River in the Town of Machias the Jeremiah O'Brien Memorial 3 
Bridge.  4 
SUMMARY  5 
This resolve names the 3 bridges that span the Machias River in the Town of Machias 6 
as the Jeremiah O'Brien Memorial Bridge, after the Revolutionary War naval hero.  7
