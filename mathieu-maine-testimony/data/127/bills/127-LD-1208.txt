Sec. 2.  38 MRSA §419 -D is enacted to read:  12 
§419 -D.  Coal tar sealant products  13 
1.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 14 
following terms have the following meanings.  15 
A.  "Coal  tar" means a viscous substance obtained by the destructive distillation of 16 
coal and containing levels of polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons in excess of 10,000 17 
milligrams per kilogram. "Coal tar" includes, but is not limited to, refined coal tar, 18 
high tempe rature coal tar, coal tar pitch and coal tar pitch volatiles.  19 
B.  "Coal tar sealant product" means a surface -applied sealant product that contains 20 
coal tar and is for use on an asphalt or concrete surface, including a driveway or 21 
parking area.  22 
2.  Sale of coal tar sealant products prohibited.   Beginning October 1, 2016, a 23 
person may not sell or offer for sale a coal tar sealant product that is labeled as containing 24 
coal tar.  25 
3.  Application of coal tar sealant products prohibited.   Beginning October 1, 26 
2016 , a person may not apply a coal tar sealant product on a driveway or parking area.  27 
4.  Exemptions.   A person may request an exemption from the prohibitions in 28 
subsections 2 and 3 by submitting a written request to the commissioner.  The request 29 
must includ e the reason an exemption is needed.  The commissioner may exempt a 30 
person from the prohibitions in subsections 2 and 3 if the commissioner determines that  31 
the person is researching the effects of a coal tar sealant product on the environment or 32 
the perso n is developing an alternative technology and the use of a coal tar sealant 33 
product is required for research or development.  34 
5.  Penalties.   A person who violates the provisions of this section is subject to 35 
penalties under section 349.  36
