SUMMARY  32 
This resolve amends Resolve 1999, chapter 41, which authorized the conveyance of 33 
1,203 acres from the State t o the Town of Carrabassett Valley.  The conveyance is subject 34 
to use restrictions, such as a requirement that the land be used for public outdoor 35 
recreation and that development for overnight accommodations, except camping, and 36 
commercial uses is expressly  prohibited.  37

 This resolve allows the town to lease no more than 2 acres of the conveyed parcel to a 1 
nonprofit corporation for the development of an outdoor recreational and educational 2 
center for use by persons with developmental and physical disabilities.   The development 3 
may include residential and overnight accommodations and dining facilities for the use of 4 
staff and guests at the center, and the nonprofit corporation may charge a fee for the use 5 
of the accommodations.  The Director of the Bureau of Par ks and Lands in the 6 
Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry is directed to change the deed in 7 
accord with the changes made by this resolve.  8
