SENATE AMENDMENT" A " to S.P. 419, L.D. 1192, Bill, "An Act Regarding 
Campaign Finance Refonn" 
Amend the bill in section 4 in subsection 6 in the last line (page 2, line 14 in L.D.) by 
inserting after the following: "race" the following: 'or a race of a candidate who is not a 
Maine Clean Election Act candidate or in both types of races' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment changes the provision in the bill that requires the Commission on 
Governmental Ethics and Election Practices to provide a fonn to political action 
committees on which the treasurer or principal officer of a political action committee may 
pledge not to spend money in a Maine Clean Election Act candidate's race to also provide 
that the treasurer or principal officer of a political action committee may pledge not to 
spend money in a race of a candidate who is not a Maine Clean Election Act candidate or 
in both types of races. \ .
