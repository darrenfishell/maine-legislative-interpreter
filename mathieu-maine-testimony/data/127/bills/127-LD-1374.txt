SUMMARY  5 
This bill allows a graduate of the Maine Maritime Academy who is a resident of 6 
Maine but is employed in a position outside of Maine on a vessel at sea to qualify for the 7 
educational opportunity tax credit, which is currently available only t o residents who are 8
