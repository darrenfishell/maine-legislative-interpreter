SUMMARY  31 
This bill provides for a 40% discount in the price of electricity for electricity 32 
consumed during off -peak periods.  It also requires  the Public Utilities Commission to 33 
establish a discount transmission and distribution rate for residential and small business 34 
customers who purchase electricity from resources that qualify as new renewable 35 
capacity resources.  36 
It establishes a study commis sion to examine the sources and costs of electricity in 37 
Maine, how Maine costs compare to those in other states and how Maine can lower 38
