9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT " A " to S.P. 575, L.D. 1477, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Protect Victims of Sexual Assault" 
11 Amend the bill in section 1 in § 165 8 by striking out all of the first indented paragraph 
12 (page 1, lines 6 to 8 in L.D.) and inserting the following: 
13 'This section applies to the termination of parental rights and responsibilities with 
14 respect to a specific child conceived as a result of an act of sexual assault by the parent of 
15 that child.' 
16 Amend the bill in section 1 in § 1658 by striking out all of subsection 3 (page 1, lines 
17 18 to 21 in L.D.) and inserting the following: 
18 '3. Termination. Except as provided in subsection 4, if the petitioner proves the 
19 allegation in subsection 2, paragraph A by a preponderance of the evidence, the court 
20 shall terminate the parental rights and responsibilities of the parent. If the petitioner 
21 proves the allegation in subsection 2, paragraph B by clear and convincing evidence, the 
22 court may terminate the parental rights and responsibilities of the parent.' 
23 Amend the bill in section 2 by striking out all of subsection 1-B (page 1, lines 30 to 
24 37 in L.D.) and inserting the following: 
25 '1-B. Conception by sexual assault as grounds for termination. The court may 
26 order termination of parental rights if the court finds, based on clear and convincing 
27 evidence, that the child was conceived as a result of an act by the parent of sexual assault 
28 or a comparable crime in another jurisdiction. For purposes of this subsection, "sexual 
29 assault" has the same meaning as in Title 17-A, section 253, 254 or 556. A guilty plea or 
30 conviction for sexual assault is considered clear and convincing evidence for purposes of 
31 this subsection.'

4 COMMITIEE AMENDMENT" A" to S.P. 575, L.D. 1477 
This amendment allows a court, instead of requires as in the bill, to terminate the 
parental rights of a person if the court finds by clear and convincing evidence that the 
child was conceived as a result of an act of sexual assault by that person. The amendment 
applies the same standard in the child protection laws.
