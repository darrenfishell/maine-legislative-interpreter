23 SUMMARY 
24 This amendment clarifies the emergency rule-making authority of the Commissioner 
25 of Marine Resources to adopt or amend rules to comply with changes to federal or 
26 interstate fisheries management plans. The amendment provides that the commissioner 
27 must follow the procedures set forth in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375 
28 when adopting or amending emergency rules.
