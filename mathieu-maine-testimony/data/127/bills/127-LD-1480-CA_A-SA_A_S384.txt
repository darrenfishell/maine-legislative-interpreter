Fiscal Note 
Eliminates any state or public funding sources 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This amendment eliminates any state or public funding for the Maine Capital Investment Program within the Finance 
Authority of Maine and is now contingent upon receipt of at least $50,000,000 from only private sources. The 
program still establishes a nonlapsing revolving loan and equity fund to provide loans to certain eligible business 
projects.
