 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 1

Whereas, this resolve establishes the Task Force on School Leadership to conduct a 3

Sec. 5.  Duties. Resolved:  That th e task force shall conduct a comprehensive 18 
study on excellence in school leadership in prekindergarten to grade 12 public schools.  19 
In performing its work, the task force shall research the various aspects of the issues 20 
related to excellence in school lead ership and arrange presentations by recognized experts 21 
and practitioners in school leadership including an expert from a school administration 22 
preparation program at the University of Maine.  The task force shall develop strategies 23 
to enhance the identific ation, recruitment, preparation, mentoring, evaluation, 24 
professional development and retention of effective public school principals and other 25 
public school leaders; and be it further  26
