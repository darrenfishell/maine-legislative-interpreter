11 Amend the bill by inserting after the title and before the enacting clause the 
12 following: 
13 'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
14 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and 
15 Whereas, many new Americans who had hair braiding businesses in their countries 
16 of origin would like to start hair braiding businesses in Maine and current Maine 
17 cosmetology and barbering regulations are burdensome and inapplicable for individuals 
18 who want to open within their communities shops that offer only hair braiding services; 
19 and 
20 Whereas, an exemption from licensure for hair braiding services would help many 
21 individuals start small businesses and assist with their participation in the Maine 
22 economy, which benefits all citizens of the State; and 
23 Whereas, this legislation needs to take effect before the end of the 90-day period in 
24 order to benefit the economy as soon as possible; and 
25 Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
26 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
27 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
28 therefore,' 
29 Amend the bill by striking out all of sections 1 to 3. 
30 Amend the bill by adding before the summary the following: 
31 'Emergency clause. In view of the emergency cited ill the preamble, this 
32 legislation takes effect when approved.' 
33 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
34 section number to read consecutively.

SUMMARY 
2 This amendment adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause to the bill. It 
3 also strikes provisions in the bill that exclude the practice of hair braiding from the 
4 practices of barbering, limited barbering and cosmetology. It retains the provision that 
5 allows a person who provides only hair braiding services to be exempt from barbering 
6 and cosmetology licensure requirements.
