SUMMARY  28 
This bill allows the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of 29 
Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations to issue to a holder of an auctioneer license 30 
a permit to conduct a wine auction for privately held wine. The bill establishes the fee for 31 
and sets specific conditions on the wine auction permit.  32
