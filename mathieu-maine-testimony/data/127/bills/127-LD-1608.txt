 SUMMARY  1 
This bill allows a public, civic, philanthropic, charitable or religious organization to 2 
erect signs in the public right -of-way that announce a fund -raising  event for that 3 
organization at which military personnel, active and retired, and the families of military 4 
personnel are permitted entry free of charge.  5
