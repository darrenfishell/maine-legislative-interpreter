 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 1 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and  2 
Whereas, the State faces significant challenges caring for residents living with 3 
serious and persis tent mental illness and it lacks adequate community -based and hospital - 4 
based treatment resources to address the needs of its civil and forensic patient 5 
populations; and  6 
Whereas, the current structure and staffing at Riverview Psychiatric Center may not 7 
be fully optimized for the successful rehabilitation of its civil and forensic patient 8 
populations; and  9 
Whereas, these structural and staffing challenges continue to contribute to the 10 
problems with morale and staffing at Riverview Psychiatric Center as well a s to the fiscal 11 
problems facing Riverview Psychiatric Center; and  12 
Whereas, this resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to 13 
seek the input of a variety of providers, stakeholders, staff and interested parties in order 14 
to evaluate altern atives to the current situation at Riverview Psychiatric Center and to 15 
bring information about those alternatives to the Legislature for consideration in order for 16 
the Legislature to design solutions that are fiscally responsible and meet the legal and 17 
treatment needs of all patients at Riverview Psychiatric Center; and  18 
Whereas, the severity of the issues at Riverview Psychiatric Center require action as 19 
soon as possible on the development of solutions; and  20 
Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these  facts create an emergency within 21 
the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 22 
immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 23 
therefore, be it  24
