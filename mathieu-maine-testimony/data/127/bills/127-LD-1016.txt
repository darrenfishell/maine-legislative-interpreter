SUMMARY  4 
This bill provides that a retired marine patrol officer who applies for a Class I, Class 5 
II or Class III lobster and crab fishing license, an elver fishing license, a hand fishing 6 
scallop license, a scallop dragging licens e, a handfishing sea urchin license, a sea urchin 7 
dragging license or a sea urchin hand -raking and trapping license must be issued up to 2 8 
of the licenses, but in no case 2 of the same license, if certain conditions are met.  To be 9 
issued a license, the ap plicant must be eligible for service retirement benefits under the 10 
State Employee and Teacher Retirement Program and the issuance of the license must not 11 
result in an increase in the total number of licenses outstanding in the fishery for which 12 
the license  is issued.  13
