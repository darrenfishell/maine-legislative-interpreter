COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 1149, L.D. 1679 
SUMMARY 
2 This amendment is the minority report of the committee. It removes language from 
3 the bill that removes the requirement that the Governor keep the Governor's office at the 
4 State House. It also removes the section that increases the salaries of members of the 
5 Senate and House of Representatives by 25% if the number of members of the Senate is 
6 reduced to 25 or fewer and the number of members of the House of Representatives is 
7 reduced to 100 or fewer. It retains the section that increases the Governor's salary from 
8 $70,000 per year to $150,000 per year beginning January 2019.
