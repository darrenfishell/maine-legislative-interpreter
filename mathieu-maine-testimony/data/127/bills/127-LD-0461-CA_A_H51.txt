COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 300, L.D. 461, Bill, "An 
Change the Notification Deadline for the Nonrenewal of a Teacher's Contract" Act To 
11 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
12 summary and inserting the following: 
13 'Sec. 1. 20-A MRS A §13201, 2nd ~, as amended by PL 2011, c. 420, Pt. F, § 1 
14 and affected by §2, is further amended to read: 
15 After a probationary period of 3 years, subsequent contracts of duly certified teachers 
16 must be for not less than 2 years. Unless a duly certified teacher receives written notice 
17 to the contrary at least 6 months before the terminal date of the contract, the contract must 
18 be extended automatically for one year and similarly in subsequent years, except for duly 
19 certified teachers who received a· summative effectiveness rating indicating 
20 ineffectiveness pursuant to chapter 508 for the preceding school year. The right to an 
21 extension for a longer period of time through a new contract is specifically reserved to the 
22 contracting parties. Unless a duly certified teacher who received a summative 
23 effectiveness rating indicating ineffectiveness pursuant to chapter 508 for the preceding 
24 school year receives written notice to the contrary from the superintendent not later than 
25 May 15th, the contract must be extended automatically for one year.' 
26 SUMMARY 
27 This amendment strikes and replaces the bill to provide that the deadline for a 
28 superintendent to notifY a teacher of the superintendent's decision not to renew the 
29 teacher's contract is at least 6 months before the terminal date of the contract except for 
30 teachers who received a summative effectiveness rating indicating ineffectiveness 
31 pursuant to the performance evaluation and professional growth system established under 
32 the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20-A, chapter 508 for the preceding school year. The 
33 amendment also requires that unless the superintendent provides a written notice to the 
34 contrary not later than May 15th to a teacher who received a suminative effectiveness 
35 rating indicating ineffectiveness pursuant to chapter 508 for the preceding school year, 
36 the teacher's contract must be extended automatically for one year.
