SUMMARY  6 
This bill requires the disclosure of the names of businesses benefiting from economic 7 
development spending and tax expenditures in excess of $5,000 annually.  The 8 
Department of Economic and Community Development is required to submit information 9 
regarding reportable economic development spending to the Department of 10 
Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Revenue Services, Office of Tax Policy 11 
annually.  The B ureau of Revenue Services is required to identify businesses receiving 12 
$5,000 or more annually from economic development tax expenditures.  The Office of 13 
Tax Policy is required to submit a report annually to the Legislature regarding economic 14 
development s pending and tax expenditures and make the information available on its 15 
publicly accessible website.  16
