SUMMARY 
This amendment makes the following changes to the bill. 
1. It establishes an order of persons from whom authorization or approval must be 
sought prior to repairing or removing a memorial. After obtaining approval of the owner 
or operator of the cemetelY or burial ground, if one exists, a person first must seek the 
authorization of the owner of the burial lot or a lineal descendant of the deceased buried 
there; if unable to obtain that authorization, the person ml,Jst seek the authorization of a 
next of kin of the deceased; if unable to obtain that authorization, the person must obtain 
the approval of the municipality or, if the memorial is in unorganized ten-itOlY, the county 
in which the cemetelY or burial ground is located.
