Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This legislation proposes a sales tax exemption on the purchase of fuel used in commercial agricultural production, 
commercial fishing, commercial aquacultural production and commercial wood harvesting and would result in a loss 
of General Fund and Local Government Fund revenue in fiscal year 2016-17 of $8, 771,980 and $179,020, 
respectively.
