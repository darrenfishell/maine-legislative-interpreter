27 SUMMARY 
28 This amendment strikes and replaces the bill. Like the bill, it shortens the time

30 Department of Health and Human Services from 10 days after the department's custody 
31 of the child commences to 3 days. The bill strikes the provision requiring under certain 
32 conditions a psychological assessment within 30 days of the required physical 
3 3 examination. This amendment restores that requirement. It also requires the department
