SENATE AMENDMENT" A " to S.P. 245, L.D. 652, Bill, "An Act To Authorize 
the Carrying of Concealed Handguns without a Permit" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in paragraph B in the 3rd line from the end (page 1, line 9 
in L.D.) by inserting after the following: "firearm" the following: 'and has completed a 
course that includes handgun safety offered by or under the supervision of a federal. state, 
county or municipal law enforcement agency or a firearms instructor certified by a 
private firearms association that is knowledgeable in matters of handgun safety" 
Amend the bill in section 2 in paragraph A-I in the last line (page 1, line 14 in L.D.) 
by inserting after the following: "firearm" the following: 'and has completed a course 
that includes handgun safety offered by or under the supervision of a federal, state, 
county or municipal law enforcement agency or a firearms instructor certified by a 
private firearms association that is knowledgeable in matters of handgun safety" 
SUMMARY 
This amendment requires a person to complete a course in handgun safety in order to 
carry a concealed handgun without a pertt.
