 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  29-A MRSA §2057, as amended by PL 2007, c. 348, §§21 and 22, is 2 
further amended by adding at the end a new paragraph to read:  3 
A traffic -control device must default to rapid intermittent  flashes of the colored lights 4 
between the hours of 12 midnight and 6 a.m. with yellow flashing lights on primary roads 5 
and red flashing lights on secondary roads.  6 
SUMMARY  7 
This bill requires that traffic lights default to a flashing mode between the hours of 12 8 
midnight and 6 a.m.  9
