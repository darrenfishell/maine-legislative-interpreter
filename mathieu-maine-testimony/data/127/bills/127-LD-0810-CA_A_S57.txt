24 SUMMARY 
25 This amendment retains the provision of the bill that requires certain health insurance 
26 carriers to allow prescriptions to be dispensed by a pharmacist for less than a 30-day 
27 supply if it is in the best interest of the patient, but excludes prescriptions for solid oral 
28 doses of antibiotics and solid oral doses dispensed in their original container. 
29 The amendment makes the change apply to all policies and contracts issued or 
30 renewed on or after January 1, 2016.
