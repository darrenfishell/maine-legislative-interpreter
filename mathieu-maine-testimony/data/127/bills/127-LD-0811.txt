 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  12 MRSA §11152, sub -§1-B is enacted to read:  2 
1-B.  Residents 70 years of age or older; antlerless deer.   Notwithstanding any 3 
other provision of law, a resident of the State who is 70 ye ars of age or older and 4 
possesses a valid resident license under this Part may take an antlerless deer without a 5 
permit during the open season on deer under section 11401.  The department shall 6 
designate which hunting zones are open to the taking of antler less deer pursuant to this 7 
subsection based on the department’s management of the deer herd statewide.  8 
SUMMARY  9 
This bill allows a resident of the State who is 70 years of age or older and possesses a 10 
hunting license to lawfully take an antlerless deer with out a permit during the open 11 
season on deer.  The bill requires the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to 12 
designate which hunting zones are open to the taking of antlerless deer by such residents.  13
