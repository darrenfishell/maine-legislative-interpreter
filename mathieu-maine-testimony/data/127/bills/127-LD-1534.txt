SUMMARY  28 
This bill establishes the Maine State Police Drug Interdiction Unit in the Department 29 
of Public Safety, Bureau of Sta te Police to conduct drug trafficking patrols on state roads 30 
and ways and at access points to the State to prevent the trafficking of illegal drugs in the 31 
State.  The drug interdiction unit consists of at least 3 state police officers and a 32 
supervising sta te police officer, all of whom must have training and experience in illegal 33 
drug interdiction techniques.  34
