21 SUMMARY 
22 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. The amendment extends the 
23 suspension of the operations of the Maine Guaranteed Access Reinsurance Association 
24 for one year, until December 31, 2017, and removes statutory provisions that are no 
25 longer necessary given the extended suspension. The amendment also directs the 
26 Superintendent of Insurance to make a recommendation before February 15, 2017 to the 
27 joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over insurance and 
28 financial services matters relating to the continued operation or dissolution of the 
29 association.
