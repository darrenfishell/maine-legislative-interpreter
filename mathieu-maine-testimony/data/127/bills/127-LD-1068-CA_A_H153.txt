COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" A" to H.P. 737, L.D. 1068, Bill, "An Act To 
Allow Certain Active Duty Military Personnel To Pay the Resident Application Fee for a 
Concealed Handgun Permit" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Require That Certain Active Duty Military Personnel Are Eligible for 
the Resident Application Fee for a Concealed Handgun Permit' 
Amend the bi1I in section 2 in subsection 18 in the 2nd line (page 1, line 10 in L.D.) 
by striking out the following: "pays income taxes to this State" and inserting the 
following: 'qualifies as a resident of the State under the Department of Administrative and 
Financial Services, Bureau of Revenue Services rules' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment removes from the bill the requirement that a member of the United 
States Armed Forces pay income taxes to this State in order to pay a resident's application 
fee for a permit to carry a concealed handgun and instead requires that the member 
qualify as a resident of the State under the Department of Administrative and Financial 
Services, Bureau of Revenue Services rules.
