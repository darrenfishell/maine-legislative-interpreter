COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A-" to H.P. 430, L.D. 617, Bill, "An Act To 
Change Municipal Campaign Contribution Limits" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Change County and Municipal Campaign Contribution Limits' 
Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 1 in the 4th and 5th lines (page 1, lines 7 
and 8 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "candidate for municipal office and 
beginning January 1, 2012 more than $750 for a oandidate for munioipal offioe or more 
than $750 in any election for any other candidate" and inserting the following: 'candidate 
for a county or municipal office and beginning January 1, 2012 more than $750 for a 
oandidate for munioipal offioe or more than $750 in any eleotion for any other eandidate' 
Amend the bill in section 2 in subsection 2 in the 5th to 7th lines (page 1, lines 23 to 
25 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "candidate for municipal office and beginning 
January 1,2012 more than $750 for a oandidate for munieipal offioe or more than $750 in 
any election for any other candidate" and inserting the following: 'candidate for a county 
or municipal office and beginning January 1, 2012 more than $750 for a oandidate for 
municipal office or more than $750 in any eleotion for any other oandidate' 
Amend the bill by adding after section 2 the following: 
'Sec. 3. Effective date. This Act takes effect January 1, 2016.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment adds to the bill a provision that applies the $350 campaign 
contribution limit in the bill for candidates for municipal office to candidates for a county 
office and establishes an effective date for the bill of January 1,2016.
