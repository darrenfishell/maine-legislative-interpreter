SUM MARY  18 
This bill amends and adds definitions in the inland fisheries and wildlife laws.  It 19 
establishes a reporting requirement for persons licensed to fish for eels, suckers, lampreys 20 
or yellow perch.  The reported information will be used for scientific pu rposes and is 21 
confidential.  It provides for suspension or revocation of a permit for noncompliance with 22 
a permit restriction.  The bill repeals a provision of law allowing eel permits to be issued 23 
to trappers.  24
