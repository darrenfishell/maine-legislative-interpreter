 SUMMARY  1 
This bill provides notice to the municipal officers of a municipa lity when the 2 
Emergency Medical Services' Board investigates or takes action with regard to a violation 3 
of law or rule in connection with the provision of emergency medical services in the 4 
municipality.  The bill provides an exception to Emergency Medical Services' Board 5 
confidentiality protections when notice is given to municipal officers.  6
