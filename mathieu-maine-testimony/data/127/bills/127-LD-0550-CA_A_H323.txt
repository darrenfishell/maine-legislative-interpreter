'An Act To Improve the Process for Obtaining an Adjustment in State Valuation 
Due to Sudden and Severe Reduction in Municipal Valuation'

SUMMARY 
This amendment strikes the provisions of the bill amending the school funding' 
formula and regarding the Town of Bucksport and instead amends one of the conditions 
of eligibility for an adjustment of state valuation for sudden and severe disruption of 
municipal valuation by providing that a municipality is eligible if the municipality's 
equalized tax rate of residential property following the sudden and severe disruption in 
municipal valuation exceeds the most recent state average of residential property for 
which data is available.
