SUMMARY  15 
This bill provides:  16 
1.  That if a l aw enforcement officer transports or causes to be transported to a 17 
hospital or mental health facility a person who is in the custody of that law enforcement 18 
officer, the hospital or mental health facility may not discharge that person from care 19 
unless the person leaves in the custody of a licensed law enforcement officer and the 20 
hospital or mental health facility must provide to that law enforcement officer or the 21 
relevant law enforcement agency a report that includes, if known, the name, residence, 22 
sex and  age of the person and the date and time at which the person is scheduled to be 23 
discharged;  24 
2.  That law enforcement officers be given full access to hospitals and mental health 25 
facilities in order to serve defendants with protection from abuse orders; and  26 
3.  That a hospital, mental health facility or other person who in good faith makes a 27 
report, provides information or cooperates in an investigation in accordance with the 28 
Maine Revised Statutes, Title 34 -B, section 1207, subsection 10 is immune from civi l or 29 
criminal liability or professional licensure action arising out of that action or actions.  30
