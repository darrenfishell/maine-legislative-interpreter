SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to implement recommendations from the Department of 5 
Environmental Protection relating to solid w aste handling, management and disposal 6 
issues identified in a letter dated March 24, 2014 to the Department of Environmental 7 
Protection from the Joint Standing Committee on Environment and Natural Resources.  8 
Specifically, this bill would enact measures des igned to:  9 
1.  Provide incentives for and encourage the increased use of methods of source 10 
reduction, reuse, recycling and composting as a means of achieving the state -mandated 11 
recycling goal of 50% under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 38, section 2132, together 12 
with a method for accurately measuring future achievements toward that goal;  13 
2.  Achieve economic stabilization of the 3 existing waste -to-energy facilities in the 14 
State by January 1, 2016, with options for funding that stabilization;  15 
3.  Develop a plan to minimize the need for future expansion of landfill capacity in 16 
Maine, including state -owned landfills;  17 
4.  Identify additional strategies to increase the beneficial use of waste materials, 18 
particularly where there are potential economic benefits to be derived from these 19 
materials; and  20 
5.  Develop other components of an overall comprehensive implementation plan 21 
necessary and appropriate to better advance the goals of the State's solid waste 22 
management hierarchy under Title 38, section 2101.  23
