SUMMARY  18 
This bill amends the Maine Fair Debt Collection Practices Act to provide protection 19 
to debtors with regard to collection actions by debt buyers.  A debt buyer may not collect 20 
on a debt without providing specified information that includes the name of the original 21 
creditor and all intervening creditors, as well as the sources of added fees and interest.  22 
The information must be included in the complaint to initiate the cause of ac tion to 23 
collect the debt.  24 
This bill establishes a 3 -year statute of limitations that replaces all other limitations 25 
unless the existing limitations is a shorter period.  26 
This bill provides that a debt may not be collected if a debt buyer violates the Act.  27
