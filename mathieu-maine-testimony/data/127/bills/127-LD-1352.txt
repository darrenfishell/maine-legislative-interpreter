SUMMARY  3 
This bill:  4 
1.  Requires a hospital that is part of a health care system that includes at least one 5 
other hospital to include in its strategic plan as an integral part of its m ission the provision 6 
of telemedicine and telehealth;  7 
2.  Requires that services under MaineCare that are provided through telemedicine or 8 
telehealth be reimbursed at the same rates as those services that are not provided through 9 
telemedicine or telehealth;  10 
3.  Requires that a telemedicine facility fee must be shared between the site at which 11 
the patient is physically located and the site at which the health care provider providing 12 
service is located;  13 
4.  Expands the duties of the ConnectME Authority to incl ude facilitation of the 14 
availability of communications technology infrastructure necessary to support the 15 
delivery of health care services through telemedicine and telehealth;  16 
5.  Removes restrictions on the ability of the ConnectME Authority to undertake a 17 
project or make an investment unless taken on behalf of, in partnership with or in support 18 
of one or more communications service providers that are remitting assessments to the 19 
authority;  20 
6.  Changes the designation of rules adopted by the ConnectME Auth ority from 21 
major substantive to routine technical;  22 
7.  Repeals the broadband sustainability fee;  23 
8.  Directs the Department of Health and Human Services to adopt rules requiring 24 
that, in order to obtain licensing, a newly constructed residential long -term care facility 25 
must include space designed to accommodate the receipt by residents of health care 26 
delivered through telemedicine and telehealth;  27 
9.  Directs the ConnectME Authority to apply to the Federal Communications 28 
Commission for funding from the commi ssion's Universal Service Fund to improve the 29 
quality of health care available to patients in rural communities by ensuring access to 30 
telecommunications and broadband service for use in the delivery of health care services 31 
through telemedicine and teleheal th; 32 
10.  Directs the ConnectME Authority to develop a strategic plan to facilitate the 33 
availability of communications technology infrastructure necessary to support the 34 
delivery of health care services through telemedicine and telehealth; and  35
