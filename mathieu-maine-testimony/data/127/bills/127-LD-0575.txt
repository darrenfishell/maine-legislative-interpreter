SUMMARY  34 
This bill increases th e exemption amount available under the Maine resident 35 
homestead property tax exemption from $10,000 to $20,000.  Currently, the State 36 
reimburses municipalities 50% of the taxes lost by reason of the homestead exemption.  37 
In order to offset the increased co st to the State of the doubling of the homestead 38 
exemption, this bill also imposes the sales and use tax on amusement, entertainment and 39 
recreation services, such as admission fees to entertainment venues and performances, 40
