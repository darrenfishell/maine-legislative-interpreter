SUMMARY  7 
This bill clarifies that information obtained or gathered by the Maine Commission on 8 
Indigent Legal Services when the commission is p erforming an investigation of an 9 
attorney is confidential.  The bill retains the provision of current law that provides that 10 
information obtained or gathered by the commission when performing an evaluation of an 11
