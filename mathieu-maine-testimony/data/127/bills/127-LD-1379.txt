§7302.  Definitions  8 
As used in this chapter, unless the context otherwise indicates, the following terms 9 
have the following meanings.  10 
1.  Digital network.   "Digital network" means any online -enabled application, 11 
software, website or system of fered or used by a transportation network company that 12 
enables the provision of prearranged rides by transportation network company drivers.  13 
2.  Personal vehicle.   "Personal vehicle" means a vehicle that:  14 
A.  Is used by a transportation network company dri ver; 15 
B.  Is owned, leased or otherwise authorized for use by the transportation network 16 
company driver; and  17 
C.  Is not a taxicab, as defined in Title 29 -A, section 101, subsection 79, a limousine, 18 
as defined in Title 29 -A, section 101, subsection 32 or for -hire transportation as 19 
defined in Title 29 -A, section 101, subsection 25.  20 
3.  Prearranged ride.   "Prearranged ride" means transportation provided by a 21 
transportation network company driver to a transportation network company rider, 22 
beginning when the driv er accepts a transportation request through a digital network and 23 
ending when the rider departs from the driver's personal vehicle.  "Prearranged ride" does 24 
not include transportation provided using a taxi, limousine or other for -hire vehicle.  25 
4.  Transpor tation network company.   "Transportation network company" means a 26 
corporation, partnership, sole proprietorship or other entity that is licensed pursuant to 27 
this chapter and is operating in the State and that uses a digital network to connect 28 
transportatio n network company riders to transportation network company drivers who 29 
provide prearranged rides.  30 
5.  Transportation network company driver; driver.   "Transportation network 31 
company driver" or "driver" means an individual who:  32

transportation network company in exchange for payment of a fee to the 34

§7304.  Financial respons ibility  12

2.  Minimum insurance requirements for driver while on digital network.   A 18

company digital network but is not engaged in a prearranged ride in amounts that are no 21 
less than  set forth in paragraph A or paragraph B, whichever is higher:  22

§7306.  Automobile insurance provisions  4 
1.  Exclude coverage.   Notwithstanding section 2902 or Title 29 -A, section 1605, an 5

C.  Medical payments coverage;  14

SUMMARY  5 
This bill establishes requirements for insurance coverage relating to the operation of 6 
transportation net work companies in the State, which are companies that provide 7 
prearranged transportation services for compensation using a digital network to connect a 8 
passenger with a driver using a personal vehicle.  9
