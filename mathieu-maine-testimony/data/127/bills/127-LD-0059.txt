SUMMARY  18 
This bi ll directs the Commissioner of Education to adopt rules regarding 19 
dissemination of student information by schools not covered under current law.  These 20 
rules are major substantive rules and must include substantially the same prohibitions and 21 
standards reg arding dissemination of student information as applicable to publicly funded 22 
schools.  The commissioner is required to recommend an appropriate penalty for a 23 
violation of the rules to the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs 24 
no later than January 15, 2016.  25
