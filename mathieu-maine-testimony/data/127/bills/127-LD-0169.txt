SUMMARY  18 
This bill creates an exc ise tax of 1¢ per gallon on the extraction of groundwater or 19 
surface water from springs or other underground sources in this State by a bottled water 20 
operator that extracted more than 1,000,000 gallons in the previous calendar year if the 21 
water is packaged  for sale in containers of 5 gallons or less.  The revenue from the tax is 22 
applied 25% to watershed and water quality protection, 50% to essential programs and 23 
services for kindergarten to grade 12 and 25% to the municipality where the water was 24 
extracted.  25
