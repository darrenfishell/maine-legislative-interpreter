SUMMARY  17 
This bill changes the fee for petitioning th e Public Utilities Commission for approval 18 
to erect a transmission line capable of being operated at 69 kilovolts or more from 4/100 19 
of 1% of the cost to erect the line to 4/100 of 1% or $100,000, whichever is higher.  It 20 
also requires a fee of 2/100 of 1%  of the estimated cost of a transmission line and its 21 
associated infrastructure capable of operating at less than 69 kilovolts and projected to 22 
cost in excess of $20,000,000 to be paid by a person petitioning the commission to build 23 
such a line and infrast ructure.  24
