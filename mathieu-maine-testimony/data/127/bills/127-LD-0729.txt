SUMMARY  10 
This bill adds acetylfentanyl to the list of Schedule W drugs.  Acetylfentanyl is not 11 
approved by the United States Food and Drug Administration and is closely related to the 12 
prescription opioid fentanyl, which is a Schedule W drug.  13
