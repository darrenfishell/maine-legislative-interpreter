7 SUMMARY 
8 This amendment allows a person whose license has been suspended for conviction of 
9 a drug crime, as proposed in the bill, or for operating under the influence of alcohol or 
10 drugs to get a restricted license to allow that person to transport a child to and from a 
11 child care facility. The restricted license is similar to a work-restricted license, with 
12 similar requirements and penalties for violating the restrictions. 
13 The amendment defines "child care facility" as a child care center, small child care 
14 facility or nursery school, but does not include in the definition a facility operated by a 
15 family child care provider, a youth camp, programs offering instruction to children for the 
16 purpose of teaching a skill such as karate, dance or basketball, a formal public or private
