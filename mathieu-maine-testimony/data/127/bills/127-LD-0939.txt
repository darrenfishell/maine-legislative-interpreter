SUMMARY  25 
The purpose of this bill is to enhance transparency in funding the University of Maine 26 
System.  This bill requires that, beginning in fiscal year 2016 -17, appropriations and 27 
allocations in the state budget document and the budget  bills for the Board of Trustees of 28 
the University of Maine System must be separated into 2 separate line items: one 29 
reflecting those amounts to be allocated to the individual campuses of the University of 30 
Maine System and one reflecting those amounts to b e used by the University of Maine 31 
System for administrative costs.  This bill also requires that the terms and conditions of 32 
invitations for bid proposals by the University of Maine System for purchases of $10,000 33 
or more be posted on a publicly accessible  website.  34
