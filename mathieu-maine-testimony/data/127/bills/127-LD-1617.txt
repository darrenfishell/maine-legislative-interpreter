SUMMARY  26 
This bill, which is a recommendation of the Commission To Study Difficult -to-place 27 
Patients, amends the law governing the long -term care ombudsman program to clarify 28 
that the long -term care ombudsman has the authority to act as a resource during the 29 
hospital discharge process to assist patients with complex medical needs who experience  30 
significant barriers to admission in a residential care facility, nursing facility or assisted 31 
living facility or program.  It also provides funds to allow the program to contract for 2 32 
new full -time positions within the program.  33
