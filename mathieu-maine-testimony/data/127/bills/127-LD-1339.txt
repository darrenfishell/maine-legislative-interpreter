SUMMARY  13 
This bill requires the Public Utilities Commission to reject all bids to provide 14 
standard of fer service for a customer class in a transmission and distribution utility's 15 
service territory if none of the bids for that customer class are less than 10¢ per kilowatt 16 
hour.  When this occurs, the commission is required to suspend the portfolio requirem ents 17 
for renewable capacity resources and issue a new request for proposals for each customer 18 
class that did not obtain a bid less than 10¢ per kilowatt hour.  The suspension of the 19 
portfolio requirements remains in effect for the duration of the new stand ard offer period.  20
