Sec. B -1.  Department of Health  and Human Services to review 2 
reimbursement levels at residential care facilities.  Resolved:   That the 3 
Department of Health and Human Services shall review the reimbursement levels at 4

the greater levels of care required by residents in those facilities who choose to age in 6

SUMMARY  20 
This resolve increases by up to 40 the number of available residential care beds in 21 
Maine. This resolve also directs the Department of Health and Human Services to review 22 
the reimbursement levels at residenti al care facilities to identify if an increase in 23 
MaineCare funding is necessary to account for higher levels of care at these facilities and 24 
directs the department to develop a demonstration program in Washington County for 25 
suite -type settings that allow c ouples to remain living together. The department is also 26 
required to submit a report to the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human 27 
Services no later than January 15, 2016.  28
