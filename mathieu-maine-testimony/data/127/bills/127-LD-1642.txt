 SUMMARY  1 
Public Law 2015, chapter 21 amended the law governing theft by deception to 2 
specifically include as deception false claims of being a veteran or a member of the 3 
Armed Forces of the United States or a state military force.  This bill specif ies instead 4 
that the intentional creation or reinforcement of a false impression that a person is such a 5 
veteran or member constitutes deception under this law.  This bill also specifies that any 6 
fine imposed on such a person must be deposited in the Maine  Military Family Relief 7 
Fund.  8
