SUMMARY  4 
Current law provides a property tax exemption to a veteran of the Vietnam War as 5 
long as the veteran served on active duty for at least 180 days during the period between 6 
February 27, 1961 and May 8, 1975.  7 
This bill re peals that limitation.  8
