CHAPTER 712-A 3 
LOCAL OPTION REAL ESTATE TRANSFER TAX 4 
§4661.  Definitions 5 
As used in this chapter, unless the context otherwise indicates, the following terms 6 
have the following meanings.  Other terms have the same meanings as in chapter 711- A. 7 
1.  Eligible property.   "Eligible property" means real property subject to the tax 8 
imposed under chapter 711- A. 9 
2.  Local option real estate transfer tax.   "Local option real estate transfer tax" 10 
means the real estate transfer tax imposed by a participating municipality on eligible 11 
property pursuant to section 4662. 12 
3.  Participating municipality.   "Participating municipality" means a municipality 13 
that elects to impose a local option real estate transfer tax pursuant to section 4664. 14 
§4662.  Imposition and collection of local option real estate transfer tax 15 
A participating municipality may impose a local option real estate transfer tax on the 16 
transferor of eligible property of up to 1% of the value of the eligible property transferred.  17 
The local option real estate transfer tax must be collected in the same manner and at the 18 
same time as the tax collected pursuant to section 4641-B, subsection 1.  The tax imposed 19 
pursuant to this section is in addition to the real estate transfer tax imposed pursuant to 20 
section 4641- A. 21 
§4663.  Determination and payment of municipal share; administrative costs 22 
On the last day of each month, the register of deeds shall identify the amount of 23 
revenue under this chapter attributable to each participating municipality, based on the 24 
location of the properties being transferred and the amount of tax collected under section 25 
4662 for those properties, and certify the net amount due the municipality to the county 26 
treasurer.  The county treasurer shall, on or before the 10th day of each month, pay over 27 
to each municipality that municipality's share of the tax collected pursuant to this chapter 28 
for the previous month.  If the tax collected is not paid over by the 10th day of the month, 29 
the municipality may impose interest pursuant to section 186. 30 
§4664.  Referendum 31 
The question of whether to impose a local option real estate transfer tax and the 32 
amount of the tax must be submitted to the legal voters of a municipality that seeks to 33 
impose the local option real estate transfer tax. 34

The local option real estate transfer tax may be discontinued by referendum 8 
conducted in the same manner as the referendum adopting the tax under this section. 9 
A vote by a municipality to adopt or repeal a local option real estate transfer tax may 10 
not be held more often than once each year.  The municipality must notify the assessor 11 
and the register of deeds within 30 days of the outcome of any election. 12 
§4665.  Effective date of tax 13 
The local option real estate transfer tax authorized by this chapter takes effect 120 14 
days after the municipal referendum vote under section 4664 if it is accepted by a 15 
majority of the local voters voting at the election and the total number of votes cast equals 16 
or exceeds 20% of the total number of votes cast in that municipality in the most recent 17 
gubernatorial election. 18 
SUMMARY 19 
This bill allows a municipality, by referendum, to establish a local option real estate 20 
transfer tax of up to 1% on the transfer of real property that is subject to the state real 21 
estate transfer tax. 22
