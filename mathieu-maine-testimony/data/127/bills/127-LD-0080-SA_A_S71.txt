SENATE AMENDMENT" It "to S.P. 28, L.D. 80, "RESOLUTION, Proposing an 
Amendment to the Constitution of Maine To Lower the Age Requirement To Run for 
Legislative Office" 
Amend the resolution by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'RESOLUTION, Proposing an Amendment to the Constitution of Maine To Lower 
the Age Requirement To Run for the Maine House of Representatives' 
Amend the resolution by striking out all of the 4th and 5th paragraphs after the title 
(page 1, lines 12 to 15 in L.D.). 
Amend the resolution by striking out all of the question (page 1, lines 23 to 26 in 
L.D.) and inserting the following: 
"'Do you favor amending the Constitution of Maine to lower the age 
requirement for State Representatives from 21 to 18 years of age?'" 
SUMMARY 
This amendment strikes from the resolution the provision that lowers the age 
requirement for State Senators, and retains the provision in the resolution that lowers the 
age requirement for State Representatives.
