§341.  Unenrolled voter participation in primary elections allowed  6 
A registered voter not enrolled in a political party may participate, subject to the 7 
restrictions of section 144, in a party's primary election without enrolling in that political 8 
party .  An unenrolled voter may vote in only one party's primary election.  Municipal 9 
clerks shall establish procedures to ensure that each voter voting in a party's primary 10 
election is offered a ballot for that primary election.  11 
SUMMARY  12 
This bill allows unenro lled voters to vote in primary elections without having to 13
