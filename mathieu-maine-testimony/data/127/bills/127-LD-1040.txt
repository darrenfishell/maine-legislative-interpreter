SUMMARY  15 
This bill creates the program to reduce shoreline erosion and protect lake quality 16 
administered by the Department of Environmental Protection.  The bill directs the 17 
Department of Environmental Protection to contract with the Lakes Environmental 18 
Association for implementation of the program and use volunteer labor from the Youth 19 
Conservation Corps for an 8 -week period in the summer of 2016 and the summer of 20 
2017.  The scope of the work is determined by the amount of outside fun ds deposited into 21 
the Erosion Control Fund from which the Department of Environmental Protection is 22 
directed to disburse $3 from the fund for every $2 received from the outside source up to 23 
$150,000 for each year.  24
