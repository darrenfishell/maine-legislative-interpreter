Part A provides for the use of electronic licenses and permits.  24 
Part B requires ATV registration numbers to be displayed by means of stickers.  25 
Part C provides that, beginning January 1, 2016, a license holder, including a holder 26 
of a complimentary license, who hunts during a special season on deer implemented by 27 
the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to maintain a deer population must be 28 
issued one antlerless deer permit and one either -sex permit, which is a new permit title 29 
established in the law gov erning special seasons.  30 
Part D allows holders of junior hunting licenses, after they turn 16 years of age, to 31 
hunt pheasants and migratory waterfowl and to hunt with a bow and arrow for the 32 
remainder of the calendar year for which their licenses are issued  without their having to 33 
purchase pheasant permits, migratory waterfowl permits or archery hunting licenses.  34 
Part E removes references to big game licenses, changes references to crossbow and 35 
muzzle -loading licenses to crossbow and muzzle -loading permits a nd clarifies that 36 
hunting licenses allow the hunting of all legal species, subject to permit requirements.  37
