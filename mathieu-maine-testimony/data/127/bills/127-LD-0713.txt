SUMMARY  9 
This resolve directs the Department of Environmental Protection to, as soon as 10 
practicable, amend its Rule Chapter 1000: Guidelines for Municipal Shoreland Zoning 11 
Ordinances in effect on the effective d ate of this resolve to reflect a number of changes, 12 
including changes to setbacks for new principal and accessory structures, phosphorus 13 
mitigation requirements in conjunction with the expansion of the footprint of a structure 14 
within the setback area and r equirements for the location of wells within the setback area.  15 
The resolve also directs the Commissioner of Environmental Protection to convene a 16 
stakeholder group to conduct a study regarding the effectiveness of the department's 17 
shoreland zoning rules s ince January 1, 2005 and to report its findings and any 18 
recommendations for legislation to the Joint Standing Committee on Environment and 19 
Natural Resources by January 1, 2016.  The committee may report out a bill relating to 20 
those recommendations to the S econd Regular Session of the 127th Legislature.   21
