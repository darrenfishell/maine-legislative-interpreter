SUMMARY  17 
This bill amends the Freedom of Access Act to clarify that the date of receipt of a 18 
request to copy or inspect a public record is the date a sufficient description of the public 19 
record is received by the age ncy or official at the office responsible for maintaining the 20 
public record.  An agency or official that receives a request for a public record that is not 21 
maintained by that office must forward the request to the appropriate office without 22 
willful delay.  23
