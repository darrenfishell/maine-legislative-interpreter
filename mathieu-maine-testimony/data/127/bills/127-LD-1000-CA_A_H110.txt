COMMITTEE AMENDMENT ,,4 " to H.P. 695, L.D. 1000, Bill, "An Act To

Amend the bill in section I in subsection 8-D in the last line (page I, line 5 in L.D.) 
by inserting after the following: "practitioner" the following: 'as defined in Title 24,

SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies the term "health care practitioner" in the bill by adding a 
cross-reference to its definition in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 24, section 2502.
