SUMMARY  9 
This bill makes the following changes to the consumer fireworks law.  10 
1.  It prohibits the use of consumer fireworks whenever the Governor issues a 11 
proclamation prohibiting out -of-door fires.  12 
2.  It c larifies that the use of consumer fireworks may constitute loud and 13 
unreasonable noise under the disorderly conduct law pursuant to the Maine Revised 14 
Statutes, Title 17 -A, section 501 -A. 15
