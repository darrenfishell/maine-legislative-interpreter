SUMMARY 
2 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, adds appropriations 
3 and allocations of $16,000,000 in all funds to reduce the number of people on the waiting

6 Intellectual Disabilities or Autistic Disorder and Chapter III, Section 21: Allowances for 
7 Home and Community Benefits for Members with Intellectual Disabilities or Autistic 
8 Disorder. It adds another appropriations and allocations section to provide funding to 
9 increase the combined annual limit for certain MaineCare Services.
