SUMMARY  3 
This bill repeals existing allocations of lobster and crab fishing licenses, commerci al 4 
sea urchin licenses, commercial scallop licenses and elver fishing licenses to the 5 
Passamaquoddy Tribe, the Penobscot Nation, the Houlton Band of Maliseet Indians and 6 
the Aroostook Band of Micmacs, and provides for an equal per capita distribution of 7 
those licenses among the Penobscot Nation, the Houlton Band of Maliseet Indians, the 8 
Aroostook Band of Micmacs, the Passamaquoddy Tribe at Sipayik and the 9 
Passamaquoddy Tribe at Motahkmikuk.  10
