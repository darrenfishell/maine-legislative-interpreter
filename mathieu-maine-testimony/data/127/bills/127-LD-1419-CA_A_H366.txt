COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 966, L.D. 1419, "RESOLUTION, 
Proposing an Amendment to the Constitution of Maine To Change the Selection Process 
for the Treasurer of State" 
Amend the resolution on page 1 In line 34 by striking out the following: "the date of 
the proclamation" and inserting the following: 'December 1, 2016' ' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report and changes the date the amendment becomes 
part of the Constitution of Maine from the date of the proclamation, as stated in the 
resolution, to December 1,2016, to allow the current Treasurer of State, who was elected 
by the Legislature, to finish the treasurer's term.
