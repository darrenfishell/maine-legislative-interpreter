COMMITTEE AMENDMENT ·A " to H.P. 1081, L.D. 1590, Bill, "An Act To 
Exempt from the Sales and Use Tax Sales to Certain Veterans' Organizations Funding 
Direct Supportive Services to Veterans and Their Families" 
Amend the bill by striking out all of section 1 and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. 36 MRSA §1760, sub-§100 is enacted to read: 
100. Certain veterans' service organizations. Sales to an organization that 
provides services to veterans and their families that is chartered under 36 United States 
Code, Subtitle II, Part B, including posts or local offices of that organization, and that is 
recognized as a veterans' service organization by the United States Department of 
Veterans Affairs.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the sales tax exemption provided in the bill with a sales tax 
exemption to all federally chartered veterans' service organizations.
