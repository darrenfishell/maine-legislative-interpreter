SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to enact measures that would require sea level model predictions to 5 
be taken into account in the  design phase of any development project on the coast or in a 6 
flood zone if more than 10% of the total funding for the project is state funding.  7 
"Development project" includes, but is not limited to, the development of buildings, land, 8 
walkways, bridges, roads, culverts and piers.  9 
The bill also proposes to enact measures to require coordination among state and 10 
federal agencies, universities and stakeholders concerning development projects on the 11 
coast or in a flood zone that may be affected by sea level ri se. 12
