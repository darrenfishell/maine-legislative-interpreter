SUMMARY  9 
This bill makes the following changes to  the law relating to recruitment, hiring and 10 
retention of state employees.  11 
Current law provides that the Director of Human Resources within the Department of 12 
Administrative and Financial Services may establish policies and procedures to allow 13 
departments a nd agencies to administer the tests and to interview persons taking the tests.  14 
This bill provides that the director may establish policies and procedures to allow 15 
departments and agencies to administer tests and conduct interviews.  16 
Current law provides th at an application for a position in State Government may not 17 
be rejected solely because the applicant lacks educational qualifications and specifies that 18 
acceptable equivalent combinations of appropriate experience or education may be 19 
substituted for forma l educational qualifications, except where the educational 20 
qualifications are reflected in necessary registrations or federal standards.  This bill 21 
repeals that provision.  22
