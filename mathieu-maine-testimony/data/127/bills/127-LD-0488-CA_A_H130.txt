COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 327, L.D. 488, Bill, "An Act To 
Expand the Scope of Practice for Denturists" 
Amend the bill by striking out all of sections 6 to 8 and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 6. 32 MRS A ยง1100-B, sub-ยง3, ~ is enacted to read: 
D. The making. placing, constructing, altering, reproducing or repamng of 
nonorthodontic removable sports mouth guards and the provision of teeth whitening

SUMMARY 
This amendment removes provisions of the bill that allow denturists to take x-rays 
and fit removable prostheses, with the exception of orthodontic appliances, and instead 
authorizes a licensed denturist to make, place and repair nonorthodontic removable sports 
mouth guards and provide teeth whitening services.
