SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies that the 8 or more members of the Legislative Council 
required to vote to appoint or to remove from office an Executive Director of the 
Legislative Council, a State Law Librarian and other office directors must be present at a 
public meeting to vote and makes a correction to punctuation.
