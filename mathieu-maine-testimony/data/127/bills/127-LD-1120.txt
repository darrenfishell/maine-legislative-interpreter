SUMMARY  15 
This bill repeals the laws establishing the Maine Uniform Building and Energy Code 16 
and makes adjustment s to certain other laws to make them consistent with law that 17 
existed prior to the enactment of Public Law 2007, chapter 699, which created the 18 
uniform code.  This bill does not reenact the Maine Model Building Code or the energy 19 
efficiency building standa rds that were repealed by Public Law 2007, chapter 699 and 20 
replaced by the Maine Uniform Building and Energy Code.  21
