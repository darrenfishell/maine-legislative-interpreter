Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill includes a General Fund appropriation to the Department of Health and Human Services of $1,016,590 in 
fiscal year 2016-17 to pay fines imposed by the federal government due to the State's failure to comply with federal 
requirements related to the ASPIRE-TANF program.
