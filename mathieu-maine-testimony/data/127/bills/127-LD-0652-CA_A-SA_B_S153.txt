SUMMARY 
This amendment requires a person who is authorized under the bill to cany a 
concealed handgun without a permit to sign an acknowledgment that the person was 
provided a frrearm safety brochure upon purchase of a handgun. A firearm safety 
brochure is currently required to be provided with every firearm sold. The amendment 
adds to the information provided in the firearm safety brochure information developed by 
the Department of Public Safety related to locations where handguns are prohibited and 
information concerning the use of handguns for self-defense. The amendment requires 
the purchaser to retain the acknowledgment. It also requires the Department of Public 
Safety to post a firearm safety brochure and the acknowledgment form and a list of 
certified safety programs on the department's publicly accessible website.
