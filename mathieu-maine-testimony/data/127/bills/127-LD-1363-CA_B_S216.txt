22 SUMMARY 
23 This amendment is a minority report of the committee. It removes the directive to the 
24 Public Utilities Commission to order transmission and distribution utilities to undertake 
25 actions to ensure their systems are protected from or able to negate effects of 
26 electromagnetic pulses and limits the directive to severe geomagnetic disturbances only. 
27 It also requires the commission to direct transmission and distribution utilities to monitor 
28 their 345-ldlovolt transfonners to ensure they are protected from severe geomagnetic 
29 disturbances. This amendment specifies that any action directed by the commission must 
30 be cost-effective and consistent with the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission's 
31 reliability standards. It removes language that specifies that all costs inculTed by 
32 transmission and distribution utilities as a result of the requirements imposed under the 
33 Maine Revised Statutes, Title 35-A, section 3144 are just and reasonable for rate-making 
34 purposes. It also removes language that prohibits a transmission and distribution utility
