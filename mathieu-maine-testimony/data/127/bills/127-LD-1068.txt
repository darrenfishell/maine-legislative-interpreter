SUMMARY  14 
This bill provides that a per son on active duty in the United States Armed Forces who 15 
pays income taxes to this State and is otherwise qualified to be issued a permit to carry a 16 
concealed handgun is eligible for a permit issued by the Chief of the State Police upon 17 
payment of the appl ication fee for a resident.  18
