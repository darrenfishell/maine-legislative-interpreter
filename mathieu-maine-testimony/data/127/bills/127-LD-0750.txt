SUMMARY  34 
This bill provides for a moratorium on metallic mineral exploration or mining in the 35 
State.  It directs the Department of Environmental Protection, by January 1, 2017, to 36 
provisionally adopt and submit for legislative review major substantive rules related to 37 
the Maine Metallic Mineral Mining Act.  These rules must:  38 
1.  In clude standards necessary to protect the public health and safety and the 39 
environment;  40
