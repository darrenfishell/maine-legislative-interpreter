SUMMARY  15 
This bill makes changes to the laws governing the withdrawal and replacement of 16 
candidates for an office other than United Sta tes Senator, Representative to Congress or 17 
Governor.  It amends the requirements that must be met in order for a political committee 18 
to make a replacement nomination.  It requires notification of a meeting to name a 19 
replacement candidate and it requires th at a minimum of 5 residents of the electoral 20 
district who are members of the political party making the replacement attend the 21 
meeting.  22
