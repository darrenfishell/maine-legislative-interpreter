SUMMARY  22 
This bill requires that a seller of residential real  property provide to the purchaser a 23 
form that allows the purchaser to formally decline to have the water at the property tested 24 
for arsenic.  25 
The bill imposes a 3% surcharge on the retail sale of water filters.  The proceeds are 26 
to be used by the Maine Sta te Housing Authority for the home repair program 27 
administered by the authority that provides loans and grants to low -income homeowners 28 
for necessary home repairs.  29
