 Sec. 1.  Department of Labor to conduct pilot program.  Resolved:  That 1 
the Department of Labor shall conduct a pilot program to provide funding to employers in 2 
the forest products industry who hire a high school graduate or holder of a general 3 
equivalency  diploma who is 18 to 20 years of age and not enrolled in a postsecondary 4 
education program at the time of hiring. The program provides, for a period of up to one 5 
year, an amount up to 50% of the full -time or part -time annual salary or, if after hiring an 6 
eligible person the employer sends the eligible person to a postsecondary education or 7 
business training program, employer -paid stipend of an eligible person hired by an 8 
employer in the forest products industry.  The percentage of funding for an employer 9 
under the pilot program is determined by funds available to the department.  An employer 10 
who qualifies for this program is responsible for recruiting and hiring a student eligible to 11 
participate in the program; and be it further  12
