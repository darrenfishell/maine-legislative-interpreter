 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  Department to create 3 new peer centers.   The Department of Health 2 
and Human Services shall create 3 new peer centers in different parts of the State to 3 
coordinate and run peer support programs to help persons in recovery from drug 4 
addiction.  In order to serve populations in rural parts of the State, 2 of these peer centers 5 
must be located in currently underserved areas that are outside of Maine's largest cities.  6 
Funding for each peer center must be used to support the hiring of a coordinator who 7 
shall support recovery group facilitation, peer mentoring and peer recovery resource 8 
connections.  The peer centers may be coordinated and housed within existing health care 9 
settings, such as a  rural health care center.  10
