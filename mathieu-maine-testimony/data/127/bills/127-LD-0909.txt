SUMMARY  15 
This bill encourages municipalities to develop policies that assist older adults with 16 
aging in place and that create age -friendly communities.  It amends the law governing  17 
comprehensive plans by encouraging municipalities to plan for the needs of older adults 18 
in their communities.  19
