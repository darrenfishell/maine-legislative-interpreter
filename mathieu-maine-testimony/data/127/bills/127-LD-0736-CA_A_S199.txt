13 I An Act To Allow Access to Certain Death Records I 
14 Amend the bill by striking out all of section 2. 
15 SUMMARY 
16 This amendment strikes section 2 of the bill, which requires that when a physician or 
17 clinical psychologist has evaluated a person for the purpose of providing a 2nd opinion on 
18 whether the person meets the criteria for emergency admission to a psychiatric hospital 
19 and determines that the person does not meet the criteria, the physician or clinical 
20 psychologist must destroy the written application for emergency admission and 
21 accompanying certificate.
