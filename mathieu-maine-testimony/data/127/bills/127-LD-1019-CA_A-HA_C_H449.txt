SUMMARY 
This amendment provides that, for fiscal year 2015-16 and beyond, in the event a 
municipality is approved for an adjustment to its state property valuation due to a sudden 
and severe disruption in its municipal valuation, that adjusted state valuation is the 
property fiscal capacity for purposes of determining municipal contributions under the 
school funding formula.
