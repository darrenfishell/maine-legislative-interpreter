SUMMARY  31 
This bill makes t he following changes in the laws governing provider of last resort 32 
service:  33 
1.  Removes the requirement that a provider of provider of last resort service have the 34 
capacity to maintain uninterrupted voice service during a power failure, either through the 35 
incorporation into the network or network interface devices of suitable battery backup or 36 
through electric current;  37 
2.  Provides that after December 31, 2015 and until December 31, 2021:  38
