SUMMARY 17 
This bill requires the Commission on Governmental Ethics and Election Practices to 18 
provide campaign finance information for legislative elections on a publicly accessible 19 
website, including all contributions and independent expenditures used to support or 20 
oppose a candidate, sorted by candidate and district. 21
