Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill includes General Fund appropriations of$33,325 in fiscal year 2015-16 and $45,431 in fiscal year 2016-17 to 
the Department of Health and Human Services for a Comprehensive Health Planner II position to work as the liaison 
between DOE, DHHS and the school administrative units to help schools alleviate the challenges in navigating the 
complexity of Mainecare billing and improve communication. Federal Expenditures Fund allocations will also be 
required for the administrative federal match.
