SUMMARY  35 
This bill amends the laws governing the licensure renewal requirements for mental 36 
health clinicians, including professional counselors and social workers, to require 6 37 
continuing education hours in suicide risk assessment and treatment of suicidal persons as 38 
a requirement for license renewal.  39
