industrial hemp cultivation to a federally recognized Indian tribe for the purpose of 6

SUMMARY  9 
This resolve directs the Commissioner of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry 10 
upon application to issue a  license to conduct a pilot project in industrial hemp cultivation 11 
to a federally recognized Indian tribe.  12
