SUMMARY  7 
This bill r equires that if a coastal municipality or multimunicipal region that includes 8 
a coastal municipality adopts a growth management program under the State's growth 9 
planning and land use laws, its comprehensive plan must include information on and a 10 
plan to ad dress the impacts of changes in sea level on buildings, transportation 11 
infrastructure, sewage treatment facilities and other relevant municipal, multimunicipal or 12 
privately held infrastructure or property.  This bill also amends the State's growth 13 
planning  and land use laws to reflect that addressing the impacts of sea level rise is a state 14 
planning and regulatory goal.  This bill amends the laws regarding the State's coastal 15 
management policies to reflect a new state policy of addressing and planning for t he 16 
impacts of sea level rise.  17
