SUMMARY  33 
This bill amends the property tax fairness credit for property tax years beginning on 34 
or after January 1, 2015 in the following ways.  35 
1.  For purposes of calculating the benefit base upon which the credit is based, it 36 
increases the amount of property taxes paid or rent constituting property taxes paid that 37 
may be used in calculating the credit.  38 
2.  It incre ases the percentage of rent paid that may be considered rent constituting 39 
property taxes from 15% of the gross rent actually paid to 18% of the gross rent actually 40 
paid.  41
