SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to reduce the regulatory burden on a physician assistant who is 5 
currently required to be license d by both the Board of Licensure in Medicine and the 6 
Board of Osteopathic Licensure if that physician assistant is supervised by both allopathic 7 
and osteopathic physicians.  The bill proposes to require one of the physician licensing 8 
boards to issue all th e licenses needed by a physician assistant, regardless of which board 9 
licenses the supervising physician.  10
