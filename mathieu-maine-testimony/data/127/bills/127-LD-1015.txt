SUMMARY  26 
This bill requires employers who employ over 100 workers  in the State to report 27 
annually to the Bureau of Labor Standards within the Department of Labor the total 28 
compensation of the chief executive officer of the employer and the total compensation of 29 
the full -time employee of the employer with the lowest rate  of pay.  The bill requires the 30 
bureau to submit an annual report including, for each employer required to report under 31 
this bill, the employers' information and the ratio of the total compensation of the chief 32 
executive officer of the employer to the tota l compensation of the full -time employee of 33
