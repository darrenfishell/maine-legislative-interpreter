 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  10 MRSA §4013 is enacted to read:  2 
§4013.  Removal of lien  3 
A holder of a lien against property pursuant to this Part or under any other Title shall 4 
remove the lien within 30 days of sati sfaction or discharge of the lien by the debtor or 5 
owner of the property or agent of the debtor or owner.  The holder of the lien shall 6 
provide to any person who was provided notice of the lien at the time the lien was 7 
originally filed written notice that the lien has been discharged.  8 
SUMMARY  9 
This bill requires a lienholder to remove a lien no later than 30 days after a lien has 10 
been satisfied and to provide written notice of the lien’s discharge to any person provided 11 
notice at the time the lien was origin ally filed.  12
