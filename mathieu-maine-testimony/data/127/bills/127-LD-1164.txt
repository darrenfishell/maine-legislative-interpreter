§5219 -NN.  Certified major visual media production credit  28 
1.  Credit allowed.  A major visual media production company, as defined in Title 5, 29 
section 13090 -L, subsection 2 -A, paragraph B -2, is allowed a credit against the ta xes 30 
imposed by this Part in an amount equal to a percentage of major visual media production 31 
expenses, as defined in Title 5, section 13090 -L, subsection 2 -A, paragraph B -3, up to 32 
25% of major visual media production expenses.  33 
2.  Limitation.  Taxpayers cl aiming a credit under section 5219 -W or 5219 -Y are not 34 
eligible for the credit under this section.  The credit allowed by this section may be used 35 
only for the taxable year in which the major visual media production, as defined in Title 36 
5, section 13090 -L, subsection 2 -A, paragraph B -1, is completed.  37 
3.  Refundable.  The credit allowed under this section is fully refundable.  38 
Sec. 3.  Application.  This Act applies to tax years beginning on or after January 1, 39

 SUMMARY  1 
This bill provides a fully refunda ble income tax credit for expenses and wages 2 
directly incurred in Maine by a major visual media production company, including for 3 
content that is produced for the Internet, in an amount of up to 25% of production 4 
expenses if the overall budget is greater t han $1,000,000, not including wages for starring 5 
cast or the director, writer or producers.  The credit must be claimed in the taxable year in 6 
which the major visual media production is completed.  7 
A major visual media production company must submit a nonre fundable application 8 
fee, which is 0.2% of the estimated tax credit, but may be no less than $200 and no more 9 
than $5,000.  The Department of Economic and Community Development may deny a 10 
certificate for a visual media production or a major visual media pr oduction for any 11 
reason.  The department is required to deny a certificate if it would qualify the production 12 
for tax credits in excess of the funds appropriated for that purpose.  13
