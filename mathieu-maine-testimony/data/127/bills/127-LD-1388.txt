SUMMARY  30 
This bill adds to the laws regarding used car information definitions of "livery 31 
service," "rideshare" and  "vehicle history report."  The bill also exempts a car dealer from 32 
liability for providing a consumer a vehicle service history report that the dealer did not 33 
prepare if the dealer also provides the consumer with a disclosure.  Finally, this bill 34 
increase s the minimum amount of damage required to be disclosed from $2,000 to 35 
$3,000.  36
