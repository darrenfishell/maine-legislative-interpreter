This bill includes General Fund appropriations of $7,850 per year beginning in fiscal year 2015-16 to the School 
Finance and Operations program within the Department of Education to develop and implement annual competitive 
skill-oriented school food service recognition events that emphasize creative and effective use of local foods. The 
estimate assumes 3 events being held in different regions of the state with approximately 15 people participating as 
well as a final recognition event. 
This bill also includes Federal Expenditures Fund base allocations of $500 per year beginning in fiscal year 2015-16 
to the School Finance and Operations program to establish a Local Produce Fund program account that can expend 
any federal grants received by the deparhnent for the purposes provided in this legislation.
