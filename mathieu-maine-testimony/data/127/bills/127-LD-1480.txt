 SUMMARY  1 
This bill creates the Maine Capital Investment Program within the Finance Authority 2 
of Maine to provide loans or bond funding to eligible business development projects that 3 
have projected costs of at least $50,000,000 or are p rojected to result in the creation or 4 
retention of at least 250 full -time employment positions that pay at least 125% of the state 5 
annual average weekly wage.  The bill does not take effect until the authority receives 6 
funds for the program in the amount o f at least $250,000,000.  7
