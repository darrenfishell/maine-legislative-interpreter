SUMMARY  33 
This bill caps the interest rate for commercial or business loans a t 25% per year.  The 34 
bill provides that violations are subject to criminal penalties of up to $5,000 or 35 
imprisonment for not more than one year or both.  The bill also allows a court to void a 36 
loan issued in violation of the interest rate caps upon the pet ition of the person to whom 37 
the loan was issued.  38
