Correctional and Judicial Impact Statements 
Definition of theft by deception now to include false impressions instead of false claims. 
The collection of fines for certain offenses will no longer go to the General Fund but be dedicated to an Other Speci~ 
Revenue Funds account. 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Any fines imposed on a person convicted of theft by deception for creating the impression they are a veteran or a 
member of the Armed Forces of the United States or a state military force will be credited to the Maine Military 
Family Relief Fund within the Department of Defense, Veterans and Emergency Management. No estimate of the 
amount of these fmes has been made.
