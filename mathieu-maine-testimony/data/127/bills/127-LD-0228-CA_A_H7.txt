Fiscal Note 
Current biennium cost increase -Maine Turnpike Authority 
This bill makes allocations from Maine Turnpike Authority funds of $40,655,249 for the authority's operating expenses for the 
calendar year ending December 31,2016. Maine Turnpike Authority revenue of $131,995,373 is budgeted to fund those 
allocations. Included in the budgeted revenue but not allocated is $3,700,613 to pay off bonds authorized by the Legislature for 
specified Department of Transportation projects. These bonds were issued by the Maine Turnpike Authority pursuant to the Maj· 
Revised Statutes, Title 23, section 1968, subsection 2-A. This bill also includes budgeted revenue of $37,000,000 for the Reservt.. 
Maintenance Fund, $35,089,696 for the Debt Service Fund and $15,549,815 for capital improvements at new interchanges. The 
funds that are not allocated in the bill are not subject to the transfer limitations in section 2 of the bill.
