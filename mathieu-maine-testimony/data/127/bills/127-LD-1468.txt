SUMMARY  28 
This bill amends the membership of the Maine State Ferry Advisory Board and 29 
requires the board to review Department of Transportation policies related to the Maine 30 
State Ferry Service pr ior to implementation.  The bill requires the Department of 31 
Transportation to implement recommendations contained in the "Maine State Ferry 32 
Service, MaineDOT, Operational Safety Assessment (OSA) Report" dated April 4, 2008.  33 
The bill also requires the Depa rtment of Transportation to adopt rules in connection with 34 
the Maine State Ferry Service regarding staffing levels and duties, transportation of 35 
hazardous materials, transportation of medical samples, customer service and crew 36 
stations and duties.  37
