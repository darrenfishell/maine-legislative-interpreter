Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill changes the laws governing the expedited pennitting of grid-scale wind energy development and allows the 
Maine Land Use Planning Commission (LUPC) within the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to 
add and remove specific locations to the designated wind energy pennitting area under certain circumstances. The bill 
contains a one-time General Fund appropriation of $2,500 in fiscal year 2015-16 to the LUPC for rulemaking related 
to these changes in law.
