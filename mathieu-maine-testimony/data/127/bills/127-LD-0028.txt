SUMMARY 24 
This bill requires that 50% of a fine or forfeiture collected for a violation of the motor 25 
vehicle laws must be remitted to the municipality in which the violation occurred if the 26 
fine or forfeiture resulted from a summons issued by a municipal law enforcement 27 
agency. 28
