SUMMARY  22 
This bill defines duties on the part of persons who hold powers of attorney or act as 23 
agents for residents of long -term care facilities or for persons applying to become 24 
residents of long -term care facilities and requires them to promptly apply for coverage of 25 
services under the MaineCare and other applicab le payment programs and comply with 26 
all requirements governing these programs.  It also provides remedies for breach of those 27 
duties.  28 
The bill further requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend the 29 
terms and requirements of the standardi zed nursing facilities admissions contract set forth 30 
in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 22, section 1826 and the standard contract for 31 
licensed assisted living programs set forth in applicable rules to incorporate these several 32 
duties and requirements.  33 
The bill also permits collection of attorney's fees and costs from persons who breach 34 
the duties established by this bill.  35
