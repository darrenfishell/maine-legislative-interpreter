SUMMARY  7 
This bill amends the laws governing the expedited permitting of grid -scale wind 8 
energy development by changing the terms "expedited permitting area" and "expedited 9 
wind energy development" to "designated wind energy permitting area" and "designated 10 
wind energy  development," respectively.  The bill authorizes the Department of 11 
Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Maine Land Use Planning Commission to add 12 
specified places to or remove specified places from the designated wind energy 13 
permitting area only if the  commission receives the written consent of the owner or 14 
owners of the land proposed for addition or removal.  It also repeals the current provision 15 
of law governing additions to the area.  16
