SUMMARY  8 
This bill makes the following chang es to the educational opportunity tax credit for tax 9 
years beginning on or after January 1, 2016.  10 
1.  It expands eligibility to persons graduating after December, 31, 2015 from non - 11 
Maine community colleges, colleges and universities.  12 
2.  It expands the cre dit to cover graduate degrees.  13 
3.  It removes the requirement of Maine residency while attending school.  14 
4.  It permits coverage of refinanced and consolidated loans.  15
