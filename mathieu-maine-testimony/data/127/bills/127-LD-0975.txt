§7051.  Short title  18 
This chapter may be known and cited as "the Limited Lines Travel Insurance Act."  19 
§7052.  Definitions  20 
As used in this chapter, unless the context otherwise indicates, the following terms 21 
have the following meanings.  22 
1.  Designated responsible producer.   "Designated responsible producer" means the 23 
individual licensed producer responsible for ensuring compliance by the limited lines  24 
travel insurance producer with travel insurance laws and rules of the State.  25 
2.  Limited lines travel insurance producer.  "Limited lines travel insurance 26 
producer" means an insurance producer that is designated by an insurer as the travel 27 
insurance supe rvising entity that is a licensed:  28 
A.  Managing general underwriter;  29 
B.  Managing general agent or 3rd -party administrator; or  30 
C.  Insurance producer designated by an insurer.  31

 3.  Offer and disseminate.  "Offer and disseminate" means providing general 1 
information, including a description of the coverage and price, as well as processing the 2 
application, collecting premiums and performing other nonlicensed activities permitted 3 
by the State.  4 
4.  Travel insurance.  "Travel insurance" means insurance coverage fo r personal 5 
risks incident to planned travel, including but not limited to:  6 
A.  Interruption or cancellation of a trip or event;  7 
B.  Loss of baggage or personal effects;  8 
C.  Damages to accommodations or rental vehicles; or  9 
D.  Sickness, accident, disability  or death occurring during travel.  10 
"Travel insurance" does not include a major medical plan that provides comprehensive 11 
medical protection for travelers with trips lasting 6 months or longer, including those 12 
working abroad and military personnel.  13 
5.  Trave l retailer.   "Travel retailer" means a business entity that makes, arranges or 14 
offers travel services and may offer and disseminate travel insurance as a service to its 15 
customers on behalf of and under the direction of a limited lines travel insurance 16 
producer.  17 
§7053.  Licensure  18 
1.  Issuance of license.   Notwithstanding any other provision of this Title, upon filing 19 
an application for a limited license in the form and manner prescribed by the 20 
superintendent, the superintendent may issue a limited lines trav el insurance producer 21 
license to an individual or business entity authorizing the individual or business entity to 22 
sell, solicit or negotiate travel insurance as a limited lines travel insurance producer 23 
through a licensed insurer.  24 
§7054.  Requirements  25 
A limited lines travel insurance producer may authorize a travel retailer to offer and 26 
disseminate travel insurance only after the limited lines travel insurance producer has met 27 
the following requirements.  28 
1.  Disclosure.   The limited lines travel insurance producer or travel retailer shall 29 
provide to purchasers of travel insurance:  30 
A.  A description of the material terms or the actual material terms of the insurance 31 
coverage;  32 
B.  A description of the process for filing a claim;  33 
C.  A description of the revie w or cancellation process for the travel insurance policy; 34 
and 35 
D.  The identity and contact information of the designated responsible producer and 36 
limited lines travel insurance producer.  37

§7055.  Brochures  22 
1.  Brochures.   Any travel retailer offering or disseminating travel insurance shall 23 
make available to prospective purchasers brochures or other written materials that:  24 
A.  Provide the identity and contact information of the insurer and the limited lines 25 
travel insurance producer;  26 
B.  Explain that the purchase of travel insurance is not required in order to purchase 27 
any other product or service from the travel retailer; and  28 
C.  Explain that a travel retailer that is not licensed as an insurance producer is 29 
permitted to provide general information about the insurance offered by the travel 30 
insurance producer or insurer, including a description of the coverage and price, but 31 
is not qualified or authorized to answer technical questions about the terms and 32 
conditions of the insurance offered by the travel retailer or to evaluate the adequacy 33 
of the customer's existing insurance coverage.  34

SUMMARY  17 
This bill establishes a limited lines travel insurance license under the Maine 18 
Insurance Code. The bill regulates the practice of providing travel insurance to consumers 19 
in the State and sets out the requirements for allowing a travel retailer to offer and 20 
disseminate travel insurance.  21
