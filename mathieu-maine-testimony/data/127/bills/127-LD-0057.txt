 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  14 MRSA §1215, as amended by PL 1991, c. 528, Pt. E, §13 and affected 2 
by c. 528, Pt. RRR and amended by c. 591, Pt. E, §13, is further amended to read:  3 
§1215.  Mileage and compensation of jurors  4 
A juror is entitled to paid mileage at the rate of 15¢  per mile specified for state 5 
employees in Title 5, section 8  for travel expenses from the juror's residence to the place 6 
of holding court and return and to compensation at the rate of $10 $20 for each day of 7 
required attendance at sessions of the court.  8 
SUMMARY  9 
This bill increases the mileage reimbursement for citizens who serve as jurors from 10 
15¢ per mile to 44¢ per mile. It restores the per diem compensation to $20 per day, the 11 
rate paid to jurors until 1991.  12
