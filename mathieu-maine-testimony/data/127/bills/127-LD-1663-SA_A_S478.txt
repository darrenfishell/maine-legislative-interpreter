SUMMARY 
This amendment strikes the provision in the bill that provides that, in hiring an 
employee who has not been employed by the executive branch within the immediately 
preceding 12 months, an agency may offer and pay a one-time recruitment bonus to the 
new employee that is subject to the discretion of the agency and is removed from 
collective bargaining.~ ~
