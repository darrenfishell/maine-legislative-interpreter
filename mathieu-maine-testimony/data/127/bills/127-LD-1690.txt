SUMMARY  3 
This bill authorizes the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau 4 
of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations to issue licenses to public service 5 
berthing vesse ls for the sale of spirits, wine and malt liquor on overnight voyages of at 6 
least one night.  The bill establishes requirements and fees for such licensing.  7
