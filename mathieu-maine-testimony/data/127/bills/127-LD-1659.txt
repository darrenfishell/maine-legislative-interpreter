Fiscal Detail and NotesFiscal Note
Minor cost increase - General Fund
The bill directs the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry (ACF) to convey a deed modification to the 
Sinclair Sanitary District concerning land that ACF conveyed to the sanitary district. This deed modification 
acknowledges the authority of the Sinclair Sanitary District to lease a portion of the land to construct, operate, 
maintain and replace a commercial telecommunications tower. Any additional costs to ACF are expected to be minor 
and can be absorbed within existing budgeted resources.127th MAINE LEGISLATURE
