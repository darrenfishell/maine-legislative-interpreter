SUMMARY  7 
This bill allows the Town of  Gray to exempt certain strips of land from shoreland 8 
zoning setback requirements and vegetation clearing standards for the purpose of 9 
operating the Maine Narrow Gauge Railroad Company and Museum. The bill also 10 
exempts those strips of land from the require ment to obtain a Natural Resources 11 
Protection Act permit for the clearing of vegetation associated with the operation of the 12 
Maine Narrow Gauge Railroad Company and Museum.  13
