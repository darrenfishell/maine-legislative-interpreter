SUMMARY  23 
This bill eliminates the arborist licensing exemption for highway contractors, 24 
subcontractors and their employees who remove trees during the performance of 25 
contracts for the construction or maintenance of highways  and general contractors who 26 
remove interfering shade or ornamental trees or interfering parts of shade or ornamental 27 
trees in the conduct of their regular business.  This bill also increases the penalty for 28 
violating a provision of the laws regarding arbo rists from up to $500 per day to up to 29 
$1,000 per day but provides for a warning for a first offense.  30
