23 SUMMARY 
24 This amendment amends Committee Amendment "A" to strike and replace the 
25 provision in the bill that provides that a child upon a recommendation from the child's 
26 health care provider may not be denied eligibility to attend school solely because the 
27 child requires medical marijuana in a nonsmokeable form as a reasonable accommodation 
28 necessary for the child to attend school. 
29 This amendment provides that a child who holds a written certification for the use of 
30 medical marijuana from a medical provider under the Maine Medical Use of Marijuana 
31 Act may not be denied eligibility.
