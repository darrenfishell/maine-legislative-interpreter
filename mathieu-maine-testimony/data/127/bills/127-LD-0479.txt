SUMMARY  20 
This bill allows a person to possess hunting equipment on Sunday on a landowner's 21 
property if the person possessing the equipment has the landowner's written consent to 22 
hunt on that property.  The bill also allows hunting on a landowner' s property on Sunday 23 
with the landowner's written consent.  24
