SUMMARY 
This amendment makes the following changes to section 6 of the bill: 
1. It eliminates the definition of "entry"; 
2. It eliminates the requirement that the designation of a caregiver needs to be made 
within the first 24 hours; 
3. It changes the notice requirements to caregivers from a requirement to notify to a 
requirement to make reasonable efforts to notify; 
4. It eliminates what a discharge plan must include and defers to the hospital's 
established policy; and 
5. It eliminates the instruction requirements.
