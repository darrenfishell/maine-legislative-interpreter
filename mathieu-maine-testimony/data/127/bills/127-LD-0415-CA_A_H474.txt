SUMMARY 
This amendment is the majority report of the committee. The bill proposes a 
mandatory $500 fme for a person who sells or transfers a firearm to a prohibited person. 
The amendment changes the mandatory fme to a discretionary fine of up to $500. The 
bill changes the fine in existing law in which it is a civil violation to give a false or 
fictitious name to a firearms dealer from a discretionary fine of $50 to a mandatory fine 
of up to $1,000; the amendment provides for a discretionary fme of up to $1,000.
