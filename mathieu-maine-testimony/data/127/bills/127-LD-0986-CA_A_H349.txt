D. Spirits produced by a holder of a distillery or small distillery license must be sold

Sec. 2. 28-A MRSA §1357 is enacted to read: 
§1357. Sales of sphits from small distillery manluacturing location 
A small distillery licensed under section 1355-A that produces less than 25,000 
gallons of spirits per year may, in accordance with rules adopted by the bureau, sell its 
own spirits on the licensed premises where those spirits are produced without the 
distillery being required to transport those spirits away from the distillery. Ru1es initially 
adopted in accordance with this section are major substantive rules in accordance with 
Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A. Subsequent rules adopted in accordance with this 
section that are nonsubstantive, clarifying amendments to the initially adopted rules are 
routine technical rules.
