21 SUMMARY 
22 This amendment removes the Republic of Ireland from the list of jurisdictions 
23 considered tax havens and adds a requirement that the State Tax Assessor submit an 
24 annual report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over 
25 taxation matters regarding whether jurisdictions should be added to or deleted from the 
26 list of tax havens based on specified criteria.

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill requires corporations who file unitary income tax returns to include income from certain jurisdictions outside 
the United States in net income when apportioning income among tax jurisdictions. It would result in an increase in 
General Fund and Local Government Fund revenue of $5,272,500 and $277,500, respectively, in fiscal year 2016-17.
