SUMMARY  16 
This bill requires the Department of Public Safety, Office of the State Fire Marshal, 17 
Technical Building Codes and Standards Board, the entity responsible for maintenance of 18 
the Maine Uniform Building and Energy Code, to exempt buildings used to house 19 
livestock or harvested crops from the code.  20
