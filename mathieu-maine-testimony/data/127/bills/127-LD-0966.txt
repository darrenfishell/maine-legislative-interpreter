SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to take steps to help provide acute psychiatric care in an inpatient 5 
setting by increasing the a vailability of inpatient beds.  6 
The bill will seek to do the following:  7 
1.  Create and fund additional psychiatric beds for geriatric patients;  8 
2.  Review and make changes to the bed hold regulations for nursing homes and 9 
group homes to create incentives to  take difficult mental health patients back after a 10 
hospital stay;  11 
3.  Review and make changes to the bed hold regulations for nursing homes and 12 
group homes to create penalties for facilities that refuse to take difficult mental health 13 
patients back after a hospital stay;  14 
4.  Provide psychiatric urgent care centers with accompanying medically supervised 15 
crisis beds;  16 
5.  Create and fund additional psychiatric observation units;  17 
6.  Create an effective and professional mental health placement rapid response t eam 18 
or ombudsman in the Department of Health and Human Services; and  19 
7.  Provide additional MaineCare reimbursement for long -stay mental health 20 
emergency department patients and patients awaiting placement in psychiatric units.  21
