SUMMARY  8 
This bill repeals the service provider tax for so -called Mai neCare Appendix C private 9 
nonmedical institutions, which are residential care facilities maintained wholly or partly 10 
for the purpose of providing residents with medical and remedial treatment services.  The 11 
service provider tax continues to apply to privat e nonmedical institution services that are 12 
provided by MaineCare Appendix B, D, E or F private nonmedical institutions.  13
