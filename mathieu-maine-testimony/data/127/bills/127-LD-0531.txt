SUMMARY  18 
This bill prohibits the State and its political subdivisions from assisting, participating 19 
with or provid ing material support or resources to enable or facilitate a federal agency in 20 
the collection or use of a person's electronic data or metadata without the person's 21 
informed consent, without a warrant based upon probable cause that particularly 22 
describes the  person, place or thing to be searched or seized or without acting in 23 
accordance with a legally recognized exception to the warrant requirements.  24
