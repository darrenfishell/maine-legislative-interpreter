Fiscal Detail and Notes ($170,455) ($185,257) ($193,646) ($202,370) 
This bill includes Federal Expenditures Fund deallocations to the Employment Security Services program within the 
Department of Labor of $194,916 in fiscal year 2015-16 and $209,718 in fiscal year 2016-17 from personal services 
savings due to changing the way 2 members of the Maine Unemployment Insurance Commission who are 
representatives of employers and labor are compensated from a fixed salary basis to a per diem basis. This bill also 
includes Federal Expenditures Fund allocations of $24,461 in both fiscal year 2015-16 and fiscal year 2016-17 for the 
per diem and other expenses of the 2 commission members.
