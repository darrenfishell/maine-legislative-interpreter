 SUMMARY  1 
This bill amends the Sex Offender Registration and Notification Act of 1999 and the 2 
Sex Offender Registration and Notification Act of 2013 to make technical changes.  3 
With respect to the Sex Of fender Registration and Notification Act of 1999, the bill:  4 
1.  Amends the Act to provide that, with respect to individuals convicted in other 5 
jurisdictions, only those individuals convicted before September 18, 1999 may apply for 6 
relief from the registrat ion requirement, in order to parallel the provision applicable to 7 
registrants with Maine convictions.  The bill also amends the law to require persons 8 
found not criminally responsible to be included in reinstating the registration 9 
requirement;  10 
2.  Amends t he Act to require registration of a person sentenced after September 17, 11 
1999 for a crime added after that date to the Act;  12 
3.  Amends the Act to not require verifications to be sent if the verification 13 
requirement has been suspended and to change the time  period from 90 days to 3 months 14 
in order to require only 4 verifications a year.  The bill also amends the law to clarify that 15 
in-person verification should occur every 5 years on the anniversary of the initial 16 
registration;  17 
4.  Amends the Act to require verifications from lifetime registrants who move to 18 
Maine from other jurisdictions;   19 
5.  Amends the Act to begin the registration period only after the duty to register has 20 
occurred;  21 
6.  Amends the Act to allow the Department of Public Safety, State Burea u of 22 
Identification to suspend the verification requirement for a registrant who is incarcerated, 23 
incapacitated or hospitalized; and  24 
7.  Amends the Act to provide for notice if a custodian of records certification is 25 
challenged.  26 
With respect to the Sex Off ender Registration and Notification Act of 2013, the bill:  27 
1.  Amends the Act to make the crime of gross sexual assault against a person with 28 
intellectual disabilities or autism receiving services from a program of the Department of 29 
Health and Human Servic es a Tier II offense regardless of the age of the victim;  30 
2.  Amends the Act to include incest with prior convictions as a Tier III offense;  31 
3.  Amends the Act to clarify that the bureau's duty to collect and post information  32 
occurs after the initial regi stration form is received;  33 
4.  Amends the Act to not require verifications to be sent if the verification 34 
requirement has been suspended, to change the time period from 90 days to 3 months for 35 
 
 Page 11 - 127LR1947(01) -1 
 Tier III registrants in order to require only 4 verifications a  year and to change the time 1 
period from 180 days to 6 months for Tier II registrants;  2 
5.  Amends the Act to allow the bureau to suspend the verification requirement for a 3 
registrant who is incapacitated or hospitalized;  4 
6.  Amends the violations section i n the Act to include cross -references to relevant 5 
provisions of the Sex Offender Registration and Notification Act of 1999; and  6 
7.  Amends the Act to provide for notice if a custodian of record certification is 7 
challenged.  8
