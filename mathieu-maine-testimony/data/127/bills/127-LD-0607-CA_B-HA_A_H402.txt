L.D. 607, Bill, "An Act To Stop the Abuse of Electronic Benefits Transfer Cards"

following: 'Notwithstanding Title 5, section 8072, subsection 11, final adoption by the 
department of the rules adopted pursuant to this subsection or any part of these rules is 
prohibited unless legislation authorizing adoption of these rules or any part of these rules 
becomes law.'
