SUMMARY  25 
This bill allows as a pilot project until January 1, 2018 hunting on Sundays in wildlife 26 
manage ment districts 1 to 14, 17, 19 and 28 for any species during the open season on 27 
that species. This bill requires the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to submit 28 
by January 1, 2017 a report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having  29 
jurisdiction over inland fisheries and wildlife matters regarding the status of the pilot 30 
project and recommendations as to whether the project should be expanded, modified, 31 
reduced or terminated.  32
