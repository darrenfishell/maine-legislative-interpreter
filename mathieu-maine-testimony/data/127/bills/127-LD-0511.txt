SUMMARY  11 
This bill amends the law concernin g licensed sales representatives at liquor tasting 12 
events by allowing a sales representative to provide spirits to be sampled under certain 13 
conditions and by allowing a sales representative to pour spirits or wine at a liquor tasting 14 
event.  15
