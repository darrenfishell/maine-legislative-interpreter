SUMMARY  13 
This bill establishes terms of appointment of no more than 5 years for medical 14 
examiners and provides for those terms to be renewed indefinitely, in order to allow the 15 
Chief Medical Examiner to review the activity status of the medical examiners and ensure 16 
that their qualifications are updated to meet any new guidelines and office policies.  17
