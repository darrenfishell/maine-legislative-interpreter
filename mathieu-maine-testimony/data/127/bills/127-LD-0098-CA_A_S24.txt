Correctional and Judicial Impact Statements 
Increasing the trap limit from 550 traps to 600 will eliminate Class D violations related to that range. 
A reduction in fines will decrease General Fund revenue by minor amounts. 
Fiscal Detail and Notes $1,100 
The bill raises the number of traps that may be placed or maintained by individuals registered to obtain Swans Island 
Lobster Conservation Area trap tags from 550 to 600. This wilI increase Other Special Revenue Funds revenue from 
increased trap tag sales by an estimated $1,100 to the Lobster Management Fund within the Department of Marine 
Resources in fiscal year 2014-15 and in every subsequent fiscal year.
