27 SUMMARY 
28 This amendment replaces the resolve and directs the Maine Arts Commission, the 
29 Maine Historic Preservation Commission and the Maine State Museum Commission to 
30 study the public's interest in replacing one or both statues contributed by Maine currently 
31 in the National Statuary Hall Collection in the United States Capitol and the procedures 
32 for and the feasibility of accomplishing that process. The commissions are required to 
33 report their findings to the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs 
34 by January 15, 2016, and the committee is authorized to report out a bill related to the 
35 subject matter ofthe report to the Second Regular Session of the 127th Legislature. 
36 The amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section.
