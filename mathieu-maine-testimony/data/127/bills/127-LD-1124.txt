SUMMARY  18 
This bill enacts construction requirements in statute for the construction of natural 19 
gas pipelines and establishes these require ments for gas utilities.  The requirements 20 
govern clearance between underground facilities, crossings of other facilities and 21 
consultation with owners of proximate underground facilities.  The bill also requires that 22 
the application process include providi ng evidence of notice to owners of facilities in the 23 
applicable public way.  24
