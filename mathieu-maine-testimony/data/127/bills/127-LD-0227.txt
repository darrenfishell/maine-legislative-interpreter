SUMMARY  24 
This bill allows the Department of Public Safety, Bureau of State Police to create a 25 
program of limited duration for truck drivers 18 to 21 years of age under an exemption 26 
from federal regulations concerning transporting hazardo us materials adopted by the 27 
bureau that encourages the drivers to enter the commercial trucking profession and 28 
reduces the regulatory barriers for and monitors and records the skills and driving records 29 
of the drivers.  30
