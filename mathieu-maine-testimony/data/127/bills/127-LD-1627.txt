SUMMARY  8 
This bill is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural 9 
Affairs pursuant to Joint Rule  353.  The bill includes certain recommendations proposed 10 
in the report submitted by the Maine Proficiency Education Council established pursuant 11 
to Resolve 2015, chapter 41, section 3 for consideration by the joint standing committee.  12 
The joint standing c ommittee has not taken a position on the substance of the report 13 
or this bill and by submitting this bill the committee is not suggesting and does not intend 14 
to suggest that it agrees or disagrees with any aspect of the recommendations and the 15 
necessary ch anges to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20 -A included in the report or this 16 
bill.  The joint standing committee is submitting the bill for the sole purpose of turning 17 
certain proposals in the report into a printed bill that can be referred to the commit tee for 18 
an appropriate public hearing and subsequent processing in the normal course of 19 
business. The joint standing committee is taking this action to ensure clarity and 20 
transparency in the legislative review of the proposals included in the report submit ted by 21 
the Maine Proficiency Education Council.  22
