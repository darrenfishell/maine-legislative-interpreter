Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The Department of Administrative and Financial Services has identified the need for additional General Fund 
appropriations for 5 positions and associated costs to implement recommendations of the Government Oversight 
Committee related to ethics practices and procedures for executive branch employees. The bill includes appropriations 
of$495,307 in fiscal year 2015-16 and $660,697 in fiscal year 2016-17. 
Any additional costs to the Commission on Governmental Ethics and Election practices are expected to be minor and 
can be absorbed within existing budgeted resources.
