SUMMARY  9 
This bill modi fies the acceptable investments and diversification requirements for 10 
workers' compensation self -insurance trusts, providing for the portfolio to contain up to 11 
40% in bonds issued or generated by United States government agencies, while 12 
decreasing the permi ssible percentage of corporate or municipal bonds.  13
