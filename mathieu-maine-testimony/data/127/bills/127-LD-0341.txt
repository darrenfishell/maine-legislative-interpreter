SUMMARY  30 
This bill requires corporations that file unitary income tax returns in Maine to include 31 
income from certain jurisdictions outside the United States in net income when 32 
apportioning income among tax jurisdictions.  The State Tax Assessor is required to 33 
adopt major substantive rules to determine the income or loss attributable to such 34 
corporations and to prevent double taxation or deduction of income.  35
