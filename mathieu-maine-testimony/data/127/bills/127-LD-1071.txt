SUMMARY  28 
This bill:  29 
1.  Amends the laws regarding damaging cemeteries and burial grounds by including 30 
damage to metal plaques, flags, flag holders, veteran's grave markers, vegetation and 31 
plants and add ing damage created by an animal owned by or in the control of a person 32 
and by increasing the class of the crime from Class D to Class C;  33 
2. Designates as a Class D crime a person's removing a flower, flag, flag holder or 34 
other memorial token from a burial ground without permission to do so or owning or 35 
controlling an animal that damages or destroys property in a cemetery or burial ground;  36 
 
 Page 4 - 127LR1579(01) -1 
 3. Adds metal plaques, flags, flag holders and veteran's grave markers to the crime of 1 
illegal possession or sale of gra vestones;  2 
4. Amends the restitution provisions in the law by expanding the list of authorized 3 
claimants to the crimes of interference with a cemetery or burial ground and illegal 4 
possession or sale of gravestones and removes from the consideration of the c ourt the 5 
defendant's inability to pay; and  6 
5. Expands the list of claimants who may bring a civil suit with treble damages for 7 
the illegal possession or sale of gravestones.  8
