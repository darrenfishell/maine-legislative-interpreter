14 SUMMARY 
15 This amendment provides that when the Maine Governmental Facilities Authority, 
16 the Maine Health and Higher Educational Facilities Authority, the Maine State Housing 
17 Authority and the Maine Municipal Bond Bank conduct public proceedings with one or 
18 more members of the board or commission participating via remote access technology, 
19 each member, to the extent reasonably practicable, must be able to see all other members 
20 by videoconferencing or other similar means of communication and members of the 
21 public attending the public proceeding at the location identified in the required notice are 
22 able, to the extent reasonably practicable, to see all members participating from other
