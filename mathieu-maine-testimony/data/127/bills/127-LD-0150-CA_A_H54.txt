9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" A " to H.P. 108, L.D. 150, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Provide Anger Management Courses or Additional Imprisonment for Domestic Violence 
11 Offenders" 
12 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
13 'Resolve, Requiring a Review of and a Report on Pretrial and Post-conviction Use of 
14 Batterers' Intervention Programs' 
15 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and before the summary and 
16 inserting the following: 
17 'Sec. 1. Review of pretrial and post-conviction use of batterers' 
18 intervention programs. Resolved: That the Department of Corrections and the 
19 Maine Commission on Domestic and Sexual Abuse, as established in the Maine Revised 
20 Statutes, Title 5, section 12004-1, subsection 74-C, shall review pretrial and post-
21 conviction use of batterers' intervention programs. The review must include best 
22 practices for batterers' intervention programs, including the length of successful programs 
23 and sanctions and incentives to encourage full participation. The review must consider 
24 the potential for use of batterers' intervention programs before trial, during a period of 
25 deferred disposition and after conviction; and be it further 
26 Sec. 2. Report on pretrial and post-conviction use of batterers' 
27 intervention programs. Resolved: That, after completing the review required under 
28 section 1, the Department of Corrections and the Maine Commission on Domestic and 
29 Sexual Abuse shall report to the Joint Standing Committee on Criminal Justice and Public 
30 Safety by January 1, 2016 on the results of the review. The report may include 
31 recommendations and suggested legislation. Following receipt of the report the Joint 
32 Standing Committee on Criminal Justice and Public Safety may submit a bill on pretrial 
33 and post-conviction use ofbatterers' intervention programs to the Second Regular Session 
34 of the 127th Legislature.'
