SUMMARY 
This amendment amends Committee Amendment "A" by replacing the increases to 
the application and renewal fees for a concealed handgun permit. This amendment 
provides that the application and renewal fee is $40 and $24, respectively, for a resident 
and $72 for a nonresident for either an application or a renewal. The amendment replaces 
the appropriations and allocations section. The amendment incorporates the provisions of 
House Amendment "A" to Committee Amendment '''A.''
