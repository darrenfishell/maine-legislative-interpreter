SUMMARY  16 
This bill provides MaineCare coverage for dental services including diagnostic and 17 
preventive services to pregnant and postpartum women and dental services necessary to 18 
avoid more costly medical or dental care as identified by a stakeholder group.  It requires 19 
the Department of Health and Human Services to provide information concerning adult 20 
dental benefits to adult MaineCare members and providers.  It requires the department to 21 
adopt rules relating to the dental coverage by October 1, 2015.  22
