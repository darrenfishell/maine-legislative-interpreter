SUMMARY  8 
This bill is submitted by the Joint Standing Committee on Veterans and Legal Affairs 9 
pursuant t o the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 3, section 955 and in response to the report 10 
submitted by the State Liquor and Lottery Commission and the Department of 11 
Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery 12 
Operations as requi red by the State Government Evaluation Act under Title 3, section 13 
959, subsection 1, paragraph J. The purpose of the bill is to ensure the committee has 14 
adequate information to facilitate its role of overseeing and monitoring the operation of 15 
the Maine Sta te Lottery Commission and the State's spirits business.  16 
The bill requires the Director of the Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery 17 
Operations within the Department of Administrative and Financial Services to submit 18 
annual reports to the joint standing  committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over 19 
alcoholic beverages and lottery operations matters.  Beginning January 15, 2017, the 20 
director is required to submit a report of expenditures made to promote lottery sales 21 
through advertising and market ing and a report on the gross revenues and operating 22 
profits from the sale of spirits in the State.  23
