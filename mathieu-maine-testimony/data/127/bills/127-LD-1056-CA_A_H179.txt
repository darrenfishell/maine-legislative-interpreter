COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 725, L.D. 1056, Bill, "An Act To 
Improve Snowmobiling in Maine" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Exempt the Sale of Snowmobiles to Nonresidents from Sales Tax' 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
summary and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. 36 MRS A §1760, sub-§25-C is enacted to read: 
2S-C. Snowmobiles. Sale of a snowmobile, as defined in Title 12, section l3001, 
subsection 25, to an individual who is not a resident of this State;' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. It exempts the sales of 
snowmobiles to nonresidents from sales tax.

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill exempts sales of snowmobiles to nonresidents from the sales tax and would result in a reduction in General 
Fund revenue of$58,572 in fiscal year 2015-16 and $65,075 in fiscal year 2016-17. Local Government Fund revenue 
would be reduced by $3,083 and $3,425 in those fiscal years.
