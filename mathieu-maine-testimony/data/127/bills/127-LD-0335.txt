SUMMARY  17 
This bill prohibits a pet shop from selling cats or dogs that have not been born and 18 
raised in the pet shop.  The prohibition does not apply to a public or private charitable 19 
nonprofit an imal shelter, humane society or animal rescue organization, a pet shop where 20 
a dog or cat owned by a public or private organization excepted by the law is being 21 
offered for adoption or a pet shop that is selling a cat or dog that was born and raised by 22 
the owner of the pet shop in a separate facility.  The penalty for violating these provisions 23 
is $2,500 and the pet shop's license may be revoked.  24
