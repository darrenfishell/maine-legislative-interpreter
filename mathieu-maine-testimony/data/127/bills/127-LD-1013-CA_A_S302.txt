27 SUMMARY 
28 This amendment is the majority report of the committee. The amendment replaces 
29 the bill. The amendment clarifies that the Commissioner of Corrections establishes 
30 mandatory minimum standards for using restraints applicable to pregnant prisoners and 
31 pregnant juveniles in jails by adopting routine technical rules. The amendment clarifies 
32 that notice of the rules is provided only to female prisoners and female juveniles. The bill 
33 places responsibility for certain provisions on corrections officials, and the amendment 
34 makes those provisions applicable to the jail administrator in a jail and the chief 
35 administrative officer in a state correctional facility.
