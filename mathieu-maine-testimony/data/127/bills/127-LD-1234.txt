 SUMMARY  1 
This bill provides for a bond issue to be presented to the voters in the amount of 2 
$2,000,000 to be used for the acquisition of the Frances Perkins homestead in Newcastle, 3 
which is on the National Register of Historic Places, is a National Hist oric Landmark and 4 
is the ancestral home of Frances Perkins, who served as the United States Secretary of 5 
Labor longer than any other person in that office and became the first woman to hold a 6 
cabinet position in the United States.  This bill directs that a  preservation easement or 7 
covenant be held by the Maine Historic Preservation Commission.  8
