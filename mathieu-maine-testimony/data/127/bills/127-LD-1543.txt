SUMMARY  13 
This bill prohibits a municipality from adopting an ordinance that specifically applies 14 
to pesticide storage, distribution or use unless the ordinance exempts farms, nurseries an d 15 
golf courses and the municipality establishes a municipal reviewing authority that is 16 
similar to the Board of Pesticides Control within the Department of Agriculture, 17 
Conservation and Forestry.  18
