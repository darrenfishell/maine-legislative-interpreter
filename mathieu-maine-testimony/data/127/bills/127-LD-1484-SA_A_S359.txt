13 SUMMARY 
14 This amendment removes the provision of the bill that allows a municipality to 
15 process absentee ballots as early as the 4th day before the election and authorizes the 
16 Secretary of State to make available high-speed ballot tabulators for absentee ballots and 
17 to allow a municipality to bring absentee ballots to a state-designated central location for 
18 tabulating by the high-speed ballot tabulators as long as security guidelines are properly 
19 followed.
