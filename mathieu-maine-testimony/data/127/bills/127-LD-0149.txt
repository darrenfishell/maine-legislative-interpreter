 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  8 MRSA §223 -A, sub -§8, ¶¶C and D are enacted to read:  2 
C.  A person may not use consumer fireworks where noise from the fireworks would 3 
disturb livestock or poultry or within 1/4 mile of  a property where livestock or 4 
poultry are present without the consent of that property's owner.  As used in this 5 
paragraph, "livestock" has the same meaning as in Title 7, section 81, subsection 8 6 
and "poultry" has the same meaning as in Title 7, section 81, subsection 9.  7 
D.  A person may not allow debris from the use of consumer fireworks to enter or 8 
land on the property of a person who has not given consent for the debris to enter or 9 
land on that property.  10 
SUMMARY  11 
This bill prohibits the use of consumer fireworks where noise from the fireworks 12 
would disturb livestock or poultry or within 1/4 mile of a property where livestock or 13 
poultry are present without the consent of that property's owner and prohibits a person 14 
from allowing debris from the use of con sumer fireworks to enter or land on the property 15 
of a person who has not given consent for the debris to enter or land on the property.  16
