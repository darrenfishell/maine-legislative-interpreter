The Maine Coworking Development Fund is established within the department to 4

1.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 10 
following terms have the following meanings.  11 
A.  "Business" means a corporation, partnership, limited liability corporation, 12 
professional corporation or other legal business entity recognized under the laws of 13 
the State engaging or proposing to engage in economic activity within the State.  14 
B.  "Collaborativ e workspace" means coworking, shared working environments that 15 
promote collaboration, interaction, socialization and coordination among tenants 16 
through the clustering of multiple businesses or individuals within the shared work 17 
environment.  18 
C.  "Fund" mean s the Maine Coworking Development Fund established in this 19 
section.  20 
D.  "Transformative development" means redevelopment on a scale and of a 21

sustained basis.  26

SUMMARY  5 
This bill establishes the Maine Coworking Development Fund within the Department 6 
of Economic and Community Development.  The fund is est ablished to strengthen 7 
opportunities for entrepreneurship, stimulate innovation in the State by increasing the 8 
availability of collaborative workspace environments and address a regional market 9 
demand for affordable work environments that support communica tion, information 10 
sharing and networking opportunities.  The fund will match public and private funds to 11 
create collaborative workspaces in order to foster innovative communities and spur 12 
creative business growth and economic activity.  The bill includes a n appropriations and 13 
allocations section that provides for a $250,000 General Fund appropriation to the fund 14 
for fiscal year 2015 -16 and fiscal year 2016 -17. 15
