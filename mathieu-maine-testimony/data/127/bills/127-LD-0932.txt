SUMMARY  21 
This bill, beginning in 2016, establishes an annual sales tax holiday on the 2nd 22 
Saturday in August to exempt from sales tax sales of items of clothing with a price of 23 
$100 or less, items of school supplies with a price of $100 or less and individual 24 
computers, not including those used in a trade or business, with a price of $1,500 or less.  25
