SUMMARY  14 
Current law requires an agency or political subdivision of the State that buys and uses 15 
engine fuel to be refunded th e tax paid on that fuel; this right to a refund may be assigned 16 
to a person with whom that agency or political subdivision has a contract.  This bill 17 
provides a fuel tax exemption to all ambulance service companies and associations in this 18
