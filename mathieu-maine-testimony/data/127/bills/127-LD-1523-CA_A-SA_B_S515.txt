Resolved: That the funds in the Salary Plan program, General Fund account within the

SUMMARY 
This amendment provides for a 5% upward adjustment of salary schedules for certain 
law enforcement positions in the Department of Public Safety, the Department of Inland 
Fisheries and Wildlife and the Department of Marine Resources. 
This amendment also incorporates the salary increases for law enforcement

This amendment eliminates the General Fund appropriations contained in the bill of$45,480 in fiscal year 2015-16 
and $249,926 in fiscal year 2016-17 and eliminates the Highway Fund allocations contained in the bill of $8,991 in
