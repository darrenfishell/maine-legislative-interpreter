22 SUMMARY 
23 This amendment replaces the bill and is the majority report of the committee. The 
24 amendment provides that a B'y.O.B. permit holder may be issued a 12-month permit that 
25 allows for multiple events over that period as long as the events are held at the same 
26 location. The bill allows a 2-year permit and provides that a B.Y.O.B. function may be 
27 held outside of the established premises.
