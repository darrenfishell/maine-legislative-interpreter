 SUMMARY  1 
This bill provides that documents submitted to a municipal board of appeals or a 2 
municipal code enforcement officer that describe or verify the disability of a person who 3 
is seeking a variance from municipal zoning  ordinances in order to accommodate the 4 
disability are not public records pursuant to the Freedom of Access Act.  5
