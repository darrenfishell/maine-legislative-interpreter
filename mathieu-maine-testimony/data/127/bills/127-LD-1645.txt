 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 1 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and  2 
Whereas, the state mental health institutions are an integral part of mental health 3 
care in the Sta te; and  4 
Whereas, employee recruitment and retention is important to the stability of the 5 
state mental health institutions; and  6 
Whereas, this legislation provides for an increase in wages for certain personnel at 7 
the state mental health institutions beginni ng July 1, 2016; and  8 
Whereas, the 90 -day period will not expire until after July 1, 2016; and  9 
Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 10 
the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation  as 11 
immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 12 
therefore,  13 
Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  14 
Sec. 1.  Department to increase wages at state mental health institutions.  15 
The Department of Health and Human Services shall increase wages for select personnel 16 
at the state mental health institutions.  Wages must be increased by $2 per hour for all 17 
Acuity Specialist positions, Licensed Practical Nurse positions, Mental Health Worker I 18 
position s, Mental Health Worker II positions and Mental Health Worker III positions. 19 
Wages must be increased by $4 per hour for all Nurse I positions, Hospital Nurse II 20 
positions, Hospital Nurse III positions, Hospital Nurse IV positions, Psychologist III 21 
position s and Psychologist IV positions.  22
