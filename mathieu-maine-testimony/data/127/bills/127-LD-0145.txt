 SUMMARY  1 
This bill removes the  duty of verifying and certifying petitions in direct initiatives of 2 
legislation and people's veto referenda from municipal clerks and registrars and instead 3 
requires the Secretary of State to verify and certify all petitions. The Secretary of State is 4 
also required to send a random sampling of 10% of the petitions back to the 5 
municipalities to review for accuracy for audit purposes.  The bill also creates 3 positions 6 
in the Secretary of State's office to assist in the verification and certification process  for 7 
petitions in direct initiatives and people's veto referenda.  8
