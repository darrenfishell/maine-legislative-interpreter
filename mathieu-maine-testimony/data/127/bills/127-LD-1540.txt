SUMMARY  20 
This bill removes the age limit on the victim of the crime of unlawful sexual contact, 21 
unlawful sexual touching or gross sexual assault when the victim is a student at an 22 
elementary, secondary or special education school and the actor is a person at the school 23 
who has authority over the student.  24 
The bill also specifies that a  parent convicted of the crime of unlawful sexual contact, 25 
unlawful sexual touching or gross sexual assault is subject to stricter scrutiny by the court 26 
when it determines residence for and contact with a child of the parent when the victim 27 
was a student a t an elementary, secondary or special education school and the parent was 28 
a person at the school who had authority over the student.  29
