14 SUMMARY 
15 This amendment gives the Secretary of State the authority to refuse to file or record a 
16 record that is not required or authorized to be filed or recorded with the Secretary of 
17 State. The Secretary of State may also refuse to file or record a record if the Secretary of 
18 State has reasonable cause to believe the record is materially false or fraudulent. 
19 The amendment gives the Secretary of State the authority to remove a record that was 
20 filed or recorded erroneously because the record was not required or authorized to be 
21 filed or recorded with the Secretary of State or the Secretary of State has reasonable cause 
22 to believe that the record is materially false or fraudulent. 
23 The amendment gives the person who presented the refused or removed record the 
24 option of asking the Superior Court to require the Secretary of State to file or record the 
25 refused record, or reinstate the filing or recording of the removed record from the original 
26 date of the filing or recording.
