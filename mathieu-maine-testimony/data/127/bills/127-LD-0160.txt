§1215.  Mileage and compensation of juror s 4 
A juror is entitled to paid mileage at the rate of 15¢ 44¢ per mile for travel expenses 5 
from the juror's residence to the place of holding court and return and to compensation at 6 
the rate of $10 $32 for each day of required attendance at sessions of the court.  7 
SUMMARY  8 
This bill increases the mileage reimbursement for citizens who serve as jurors from 9 
15¢ per mile to 44¢ per mile and the per diem compensation to $32 per day, the amount 10 
paid to Legislators for meals.  11
