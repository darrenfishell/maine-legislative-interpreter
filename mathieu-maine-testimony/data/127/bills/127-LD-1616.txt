SUMMARY  26 
This bill, which is a recommendation of the Commission To Study Difficult -to-place 27 
Patients, requires the Department of Health and Human Services to increase the number 28 
of geropsychiatric beds by up to 25 beds.  The beds may be in nursing facilities, private 29 
nonmedical institutions or a combination of both.  The beds must be made ava ilable in 30 
underserved parts of the State, some specifically to be made available in the northern part 31 
of the State.  The beds are funded and therefore are exempt from MaineCare budget 32 
neutrality provisions.  33
