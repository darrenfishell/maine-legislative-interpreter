SUMMARY  23 
This resolve creates a commemorative simulated motor vehicle registration plate in 24 
celebr ation of Maine's bicentennial.  The Secretary of State is directed to design and issue 25 
the plate, which may be displayed beginning January 1, 2018 to December 31, 2020 26 
covering the front registration plate.  Revenue generated by the sale of the plate in ex cess 27 
of the cost of production and issuance will be given to the Maine Historic Preservation 28 
Commission.  29
