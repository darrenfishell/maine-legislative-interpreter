SUMMARY 
This amendtnent is one of 3 reports of the committee. The amendtnent reduces 
unlawful possession of certain schedule W drugs from a Class B crime to a Class D crime 
and of certain other schedule W drugs from a Class C crime to a Class D crime when 
there is no prior drug conviction in Maine or another jurisdiction. The amendment directs 
the court in sentencing a person for a Class D mug offense to consider imposing a 
sentencing alternative that includes medical and mental health treatment for addiction, 
when appropriate.
