SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the committee. The amendment reduces 
the required annual deposit or investment for capital improvement or capital equipment 
from 2%, as proposed in the bill, to 1% for the reserve fund to be established by a school 
administrative unit that receives a state share percentage of 20% or more for a school 
construction project approved for state funding on or after July 1,2016.
