9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" A " to S.P. 372, L.D. 1046, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Penalize Legislators for Excessive Absences" 
I I Amend the bill in section 1 in the first paragraph in the last line (page 1, line 8 in 
12 L.D.) by inserting after the following: "without leave." the following: 'At the beginning 
13 of each Legislature, the Legislative Council shall establish policies and procedures to 
14 record attendance for each legislative day. and set the salary reduction for members who 
15 violate this paragraph.' 
16 SUMMARY 
17 This amendment requires the Legislative Council at the beginning of each Legislature 
18 to establish policies and procedures to record attendance for each legislative day and set 
19 the salary reduction mentioned in the bill for members who are absent without leave for 
20 more than 5 legislative days in the first regular session and for more than 3 legislative 
21 days in the second regular session.
