COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 972, L.D. 1426, Bill, "An Act 
Regarding the Maine Registry of Certified Nursing Assistants and Direct Care Workers" 
Amend the bill in section 1 by striking out all of paragraph A-3 (page 1, lines 9 to 13 
in L.D.) and inserting the following: 
'A-3. "Direct care worker" means an individual who by virtue of employment 
generally provides to individuals direct contact assistance with personal care or 
activities of daily living or has direct access to provide care and services to clients, 
patients or residents regardless of setting. "Direct care worker" does not include a 
certified nursing assistant employed in that person's capacity as a certified nursing 
assistant.' . 
Amend the bill in section 9 in §1812-G in subsection I-B by striking out all of 
paragraph D (page 4, lines 13 to 17 in L.D.) and inserting the following: 
'D. "Direct care worker" means an individual who by viltue of employment generally 
provides to individuals direct contact assistance with personal care or activities of 
daily living or has direct access to provide care and services to clients, patients or 
residents regardless of setting. "Direct care worker" does not include a certified 
nursing assistant employed in that person's capacity as a certified nursing assistant.' 
Amend the bill in section 9 in §1812-G in subsection 6-A in paragraph A in the 2nd 
line (page 8, line 24 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "secure and" and inserting the 
following: 'secure or' 
Amend the bill by striking out all of section 10 (page 11, lines 36 and 37 in L.D.) 
SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies the definition of "direct care worker" in the bill in 
provisions relating to registration of personal care agencies and placement agencies as
