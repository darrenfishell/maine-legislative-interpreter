SUMMARY 
The resolution proposes to amend the Constitution of Maine to require that signatures 
on a petition to directly initiate legislation b<;: of voters from every county of the State. 
This amendment instead proposes to amend the Constitution of Maine to require that the 
number of signatures on a petition to directly initiate legislation be of voters from each of 
the State's 2 congressional districts in an amount not less than 10% of the total votes for 
Governor cast in that congressional district in the previous gubernatorial election.
