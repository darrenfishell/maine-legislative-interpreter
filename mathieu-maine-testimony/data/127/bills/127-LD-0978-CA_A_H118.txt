renewed no later than the next yearly anniversary of the contract date.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment prohibits health insurance carriers 
offering individual and group health plans from refusing to reimburse for services 
provided by a chiropractor acting within the scope of the chiropractor's license if the 
services are covered by the carrier and the chiropractor participates in the carrier's 
provider network. The amendment applies to all policies and contracts issued or renewed 
on or after January 1,2016.
