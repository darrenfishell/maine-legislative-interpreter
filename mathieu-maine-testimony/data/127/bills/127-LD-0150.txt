SUMMARY  16 
This bill provides for anger management courses or specif ied jail time in addition to 17 
the sentences for certain domestic violence offenders.  The bill directs the Department of 18 
Corrections, in consultation with the Maine Commission on Domestic and Sexual Abuse, 19 
to develop standards and procedures for certificati on of anger management programs and 20 
to certify those programs.  The bill has an effective date of January 1, 2016.  21
