The bill renames the Governor's Energy Office as the Maine Energy Office (MEO). It requires an annual transfer of 
$300,000 from the Efficiency Maine Trust to the MEO and establishes a Deputy Commissioner position to be 
appointed by the Govemor. This bill includes Other Special Revenue Funds allocations to the MEO of $300,000 in 
each of fiscal years 2015-16 and 2016-17 for a new Deputy Commissioner position costing $90,483 in fiscal year 
2015-16 and $92,340 in fiscal year 2016-17 and to fund celiain activities relating to energy resources, planning and
