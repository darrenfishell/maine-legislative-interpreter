SUMMARY  23 
This bill amends the Act To Implement the Maine Indian Claims Settlement by:  24 
1. Transfe rring jurisdiction over violations of a tribal ordinance from the State to the 25 
Passamaquoddy Tribe and the Penobscot Nation over a person who is not a member of 26 
either tribe or nation in accord with and to the extent authorized by federal law;  27 
2. Increasin g the level of certain criminal offenses from a maximum period of 28 
imprisonment of one year and a maximum amount of $5,000 to a maximum period of 29 
imprisonment of 3 years and a maximum amount of $15,000 over which the Penobscot 30 
Nation has the right to exerci se exclusive jurisdiction as authorized by the federal Tribal 31 
Law and Order Act of 2010; and  32 
3.  Clarifying that the Penobscot Nation has concurrent jurisdiction with the State 33 
over criminal offenses as authorized by the federal Violence Against Women 34 
Reau thorization Act of 2013.  35
