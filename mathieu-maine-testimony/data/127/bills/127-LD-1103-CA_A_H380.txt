SUMMARY 
This amendment is the majority report of thy committee. It allows the Joint Standing 
Committee on State and Local Government to report out a bill related to the Department 
of Administrative and Financial Services report in the resolve to the Second Regular 
Session of the 127th Legislature.

The Department of Administrative and Financial Services has identified the need for General Fund appropriations for 
3 additional positions for the 2016-2017 biennium to perform the data collection and review required by this resolve. 
Since this resolve does not contain emergency language, its effective date is assumed to be close to October 1,2015. 
With a report deadline of January 4, 2016, any positions would be limited-period positions or contracted professional 
services for a short duration.
