SUMMARY  5 
This bill provides that members of the Houlton Band of Maliseet Indians and the 6 
Aroostook Band of Micmacs may hunt for any wild animal or bird at any time in 7 
Aroostook County for s ustenance use.  Tribal members engaging in this sustenance 8 
hunting are largely exempt from general wild game laws, except that moose and bear 9 
hunting must be done by special permits issued by the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries 10 
and Wildlife.  The commissi oner is required to annually issue 33 moose permits and 33 11 
bear permits to members of each respective tribe.  The commissioner is also directed to 12 
annually issue 33 moose permits and 33 bear permits for sustenance hunting in Aroostook 13 
County to 100% disabl ed United States veterans residing in Aroostook County, who are 14 
bound by the general wild game laws except that they may hunt from a motor vehicle.  15
