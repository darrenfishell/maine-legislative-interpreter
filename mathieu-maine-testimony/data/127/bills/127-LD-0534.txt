SUMMARY  23 
This bill requires that, beginning January 2017, at least 20% of law enforcement 24 
officers in a municipal police department and in a county sheriff department must 25 
complete the so -called Memphis model of crisis intervention team training.  Beginning 26 
June 2017, officers who have not completed this training mu st be certified in mental 27 
health first aid.  28
