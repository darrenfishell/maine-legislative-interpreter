20 SUMMARY 
21 This amendment is the majority report of the committee. The amendment does the 
22 following. 
23 1. It retains the provision in the bill removing the requirement that a health care 
24 practitioner display a copy of the practitioner's license so the license is visible to patients. 
25 2. It amends the requirement that a health care practitioner wear a name badge to 
26 clarify that either the practitioner's first name or first and last name may be disclosed, 
27 except for a physician, whose name badge must include the physician's first and last 
28 name. 
29 3. It removes the requirement that a health care practitioner's name badge include the 
30 practitioner's medical staff position.
