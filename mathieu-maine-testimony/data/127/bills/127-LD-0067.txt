SUMMARY  19 
This bill makes the following changes regarding the statutory requirements regarding 20 
annual municipal reports:  21 
1.  It allows a summary of all financial transactions in lieu of a record of all financial 22 
transactions;  23 
2.  It makes the listing o f delinquent taxpayers and engineering and survey reports 24 
optional; and  25 
3.  It eliminates the penalty for a municipal official who refuses or neglects to 26 
perform a duty related to annual reporting.  27
