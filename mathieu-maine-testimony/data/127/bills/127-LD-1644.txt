SUMMARY  23 
This resolve establishes the Commission To Study Ways To Support and Strengthen 24 
the Direct Care Workforce across the Long -term Care Continuum. The commission is 25 
required to study current challenges to recruiting and retaining direct care workers and 26 
recommend ways to support and strengthen that workforce across the long -term care 27 
continuum. The commission's duties include reviewing related studies, legislation and 28 
Department of Health and Human Services' initiatives; determining current demand for 29 
direct care workers across long -term care settings; identifying career pathways for direct 30 
care workers within and across long -term care settings; developing worker incentive 31 
programs; and developing strategies to create high -quality work environments. The 32 
commission must submit its report, including suggested legislation, to the joint standing 33 
committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human services matters 34 
no later than December 2, 2016. The committee may report out legislation to the First 35 
Regular Session of the 128th Legislature.  36
