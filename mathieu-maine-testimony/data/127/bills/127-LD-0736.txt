SUMMARY  21 
This bill requires that when a physician or clinical psychologist has evaluated a 22 
person for the purpose of providing a 2nd opinion on whether the person meets the 23 
criteria for emergency admission to a psychiatric hospital and determines that the person 24 
does not meet the criteria, the physician or clinical psychologist must destroy the w ritten 25 
application for emergency admission.  The bill also authorizes the names and dates of 26 
death of individuals who died while patients at the Pineland Hospital and Training Center 27 
to be made available to the public in accordance with rules adopted by th e Department of 28 
Health and Human Services.  29
