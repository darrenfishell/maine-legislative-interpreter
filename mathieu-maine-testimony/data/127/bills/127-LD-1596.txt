This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to recodify the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 32, chapter 16, which 5 
governs the practice of dent istry by dentists, expanded function dental assistants, 6 
independent practice dental hygienists, dental hygiene therapists, dental hygienists, dental 7 
auxiliaries, denturists and dental radiographers.  In addition to restructuring the so -called 8 
Dental Practi ce Act, this bill proposes to implement the recommendations of the Board of 9 
Dental Examiners, including restructuring of the board, increasing statutory authority for 10 
the subcommittees of the board, expanding scopes of practice of the various professionals  11 
licensed by the board and making changes to ownership requirements of dental practices.  12
