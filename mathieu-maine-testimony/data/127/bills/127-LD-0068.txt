SUMMARY  24 
This bil l provides for a bond issue in the amount of $10,000,000 to be used to expand 25 
high-speed municipal broadband infrastructure.  The bill also establishes the High -speed 26 
Municipal Broadband Infrastructure Fund to assist municipalities in developing and 27 
improv ing their broadband infrastructure, contingent upon passage of the bond issue.  28
