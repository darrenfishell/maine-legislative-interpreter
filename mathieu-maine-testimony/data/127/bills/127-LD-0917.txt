 Sec. 1.  Pediatric medical benefit.  Resolved:   That the Department of Health 1 
and Human Services shall amend Chapter 101:  MaineCare Benefits Manual to ensure 2 
equity between payment for preventive dental services provided for pediatric members, 3 
including f luoride varnish applications and oral evaluations, by medical providers at 4

SUMMARY  8 
This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rules 9 
in order to provide for equity between payment for preventive pediatric dental services, 10 
inclu ding fluoride varnish applications and oral evaluations, provided by medical 11 
providers at federally qualified health centers, which must be set at a rate greater than the 12 
ambulatory encounter rate used for medical providers, and payment for such services b y 13 
medical providers in other settings.  14
