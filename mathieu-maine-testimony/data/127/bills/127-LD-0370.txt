SUMMARY  20 
This bill amends the laws governing disclosures made by lobbyists by:  21 
1.  Permitting a lobbyist to notify the Commission on Governmental Ethics and 22 
Election Practices of the termination of a lobbying relationship rather than requiring 23 
notice by the lobbyist's employer;  24 
2.  Removing the requirement that state employees must sig n annual registration 25 
forms in order to facilitate electronic submission of registrations; and  26 
3.  Requiring the commission to deposit the entire registration fee paid by lobbyists 27 
and lobbyist associates into a special revenue account to be spent on admin istrative and 28 
technology costs to facilitate disclosure of lobbying and campaign finance information to 29 
the public.  30
