SUMMARY  17 
This bill eliminates boards and commissions that have reported inactivity during 2014 18 
and 2015.  The boards and commissions elim inated for inactivity are:  19 
1.  The Aquaculture Advisory Council;  20 
2.  The ATV Trail Advisory Council;  21 
3.  The Citizens' Code of Conduct Working Group;  22 
4.  The Judicial Compensation Commission;  23 
5.  The Legislative Youth Advisory Council;  24 
6.  The Maine Agricu ltural Water Management Board;  25 
7.  The Maine Biomedical Research Board;  26 
8.  The Maine Quality Forum Advisory Council; and  27 
9.  The Tobacco Prevention and Control Advisory Council.  28 
This bill is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on State and Local 29 
Government pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 12006, subsection 2. 30 
As authorized by the law, and based on the Secretary of State's submission of 31 
recommendations regarding which boards and commissions to eliminate, the bill 32
