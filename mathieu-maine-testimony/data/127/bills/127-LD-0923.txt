§1209.  Sale of wine by auction  8 
Notwithstanding any provision to the contrary in section 1201, chapter 55 or chapter 9 
57, this section governs wine auctions.  10 
1.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 11 
following terms have the following meanings.  12 
A.  "Auction li censee" means a wine auction house issued a license pursuant to this 13 
section.  14 
B.  "Fine and rare wine" means fine and rare wine that is not available for purchase in 15 
this State through a retailer or wholesaler.  16 
C.  "Private collection" means fine and rare wine owned for consumption or 17 
investment purposes by a person that is not a retailer or wholesaler.  18 
D.  "Wine auction" means an event at which a wine auction house sells by auction 19 
fine and rare wine from a private collection to private collectors, retaile rs and 20 
wholesalers.  21 
E.  "Wine auction house" means a person that is not a retailer or wholesaler and that 22 
conducts a wine auction of fine and rare wine owned by or consigned to the wine 23 
auction house.  24 
2.  Prohibition.  A wine auction house may not conduct a wine auction without a 25 
license pursuant to this section.  26 
3.  License; fee.  Subject to the conditions in this section, the bureau may issue a 27 
license to conduct a wine auction to a wine auction house.  A license issued pursuant to 28 
this subsection is vali d for one calendar year.  The license fee per calendar year is $50.  29 
4.  Conditions.  The following conditions apply to a wine auction licensed under this 30 
section.  31 
A.  The auction licensee shall notify the bureau of the date and time scheduled for the 32 
wine auction and shall submit a list of fine and rare wines to be auctioned.  The list of 33 
wines to be auctioned must identify the country of origin of each fine and rare wine.  34 
B.  The auction licensee shall collect registration information as determined by the 35 
bureau by rule from each person attending the wine auction.  The registration 36

SUMMARY  29 
This bill allows a person who  is not a retailer or wholesaler licensed to sell wine or 30 
malt liquor in the State to auction fine and rare wines from a private collection to private 31 
collectors, retailers and wholesalers under certain conditions.  32
