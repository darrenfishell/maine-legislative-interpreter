13 Fisheries and Wildlife Resources' 
14 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the

16 'Sec. 1. 12 MRSA §10051, first ,r, as enacted by PL 2003, c. 414, Pt. A, §2 and 
17 affected by c. 614, §9, is amended to read: 
18 The Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife is established to preserve, protect 
19 and enhance the inland fisheries and wildlife resources of the State; to encourage the wise 
20 use of these resources; to ensure coordinated planning for the future use and preservation

22 regulated hunting, fishing and trapping as the basis for the management of these resources 
23 whenever feasible.' 
24 SUMMARY 
25 Current law provides that the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife is 
26 established to preserve, protect, enhance and effectively manage the inland fisheries and 
27 wildlife resources of the State. This amendment adds using regulated hunting, fishing and 
28 trapping as the basis for the management of these resources whenever feasible.
