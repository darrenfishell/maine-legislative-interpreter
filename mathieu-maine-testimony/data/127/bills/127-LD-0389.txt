SUMMARY  17 
Current law permits an agricultural supply store to sell hypodermic apparatuses and a 18 
livestock owner to possess hypodermic apparatuses for administering antibiotics, 19 
vitamins and vaccines to treat medical conditions or promote the health of that person's 20 
livestock.  21 
This bill cl arifies that an agricultural supply store may furnish or sell, without limit in 22 
number, hypodermic apparatuses to a livestock owner and that the sale by the agricultural 23 
supply store and the possession by the person who owns livestock expressly fall within  24 
the exceptions to the laws restricting possession and furnishing of hypodermic 25 
apparatuses.  26
