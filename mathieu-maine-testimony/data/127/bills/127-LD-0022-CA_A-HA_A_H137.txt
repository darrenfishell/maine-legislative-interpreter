HOUSE AMENDMENT "If" to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 21, 
L.D. 22, Bill, "An Act To Require Removal of Mooring Equipment" 
Amend the amendment in section I in the last 3 lines (page I, lines 24 to 26 in 
amendment) by striking out all of the last sentence. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment removes the provision in Committee Amendment "A" regarding the 
authority of a harbor master, code enforcement officer or board of county commissioners 
to direct the removal of a federally state-owned mooring or floating dock.
