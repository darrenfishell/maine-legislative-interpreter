SUMMARY  36 
Current law allows members of the Maine Public Employees Retirement System to 37 
purchase military time to add those years to their years of creditable service for purposes 38 
of calculating their retirement benefits.  The cost of purchasing military time depends in 39 
part on whether a member served during a federally recognized period of conflict.  This 40 
bill updates the definition of "federally recognized period of conflict" to incl ude service 41 
during operations in Lebanon, August 21, 1982 to February 26, 1984; operations in 42
