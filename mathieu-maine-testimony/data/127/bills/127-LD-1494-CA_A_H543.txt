Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill exempts discharges of less than 10 gallons from the requirement they be reported to the Department of 
Environmental Protection (DEP) as long as the discharge is promptly removed according to the DEP rules. The bill 
includes an Other Special Revenue Funds deallocation to the DEP of $47,400 in fiscal year 2016-17 due to a reduced 
number of responses to these discharges.
