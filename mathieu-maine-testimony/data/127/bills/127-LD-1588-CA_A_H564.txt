Fiscal Note 
Potential increase in heating fuel costs -Public postsecondary educational institutions 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Requiring the University of Maine System, the Maine Community College System and Maine Maritime Academy, 
when entering into contracts for heating fuel, to give preference to companies in the State that produce that type of 
heating fuel may result in increased costs to these higher educational institutions. The intent of the bill is that it not 
apply to users of types of fuel that are not produced in Maine. The cost will depend on the actual prices negotiated 
under this requirement versus the prices that could have been obtained had this requirement not been in place.
