SUMMARY  20 
This bill establishes a consumer advisor position in the Office of the Public Advocate 21 
to advise citizens participating in proceedings before the Public Utilities Commission. 22 
The position is established to address a recommendation in the Office of Program 23 
Evaluation and Government Accountability's 2013 report on  the Public Utilities 24 
Commission that the Public Utilities Commission and the Public Advocate explore ways 25 
to facilitate consumers' ability to effectively represent themselves in adjudicatory 26 
proceedings before the Public Utilities Commission.  27
