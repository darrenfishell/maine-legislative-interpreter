SUMMARY  9 
This bill establishes the Homeless Veterans Housing Fund within the Maine State 10 
Housing Authority to provide housing for homeless veterans and, for fiscal year 2015 -16, 11 
directs the Treasure r of State to credit $1,000,000 derived from the real estate transfer tax 12 
to the Homeless Veterans Housing Fund to support, with a 3 -to-1 match by outside 13 
sources, the construction by the Volunteers of America of housing for homeless veterans 14 
on the ground s of the United States Department of Veterans Affairs medical center in the 15 
City of Augusta.  16
