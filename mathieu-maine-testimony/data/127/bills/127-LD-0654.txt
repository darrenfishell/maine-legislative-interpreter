SUMMARY  11 
This bill allows detectives in the Office of the Attorney General to elect to participate 12 
in the 1998 Special Plan of the Maine Public Employees Retirement System.  Under that 13 
plan, a person may retire at 55 years of age with 10 years of creditable service, o r may 14 
retire before 55 years of age with 25 years of creditable service and at a reduced benefit.  15
