4 SUMMARY 
5 This amendment is one of 2 minority reports of the committee. The bill increases the 
6 driver's license suspension period from 150 days to 180 days for a person who is 
7 convicted of operating under the influence and who has no previous operating under the 
8 influence convictions within the prior 10 years. The amendment makes the change from 
9 150 to 180 days in 2 other provisions of operating under the influence law. 
10 This amendment also adds an allocation for the cost of computer programming 
11 necessitated by the changes made to the bill.
