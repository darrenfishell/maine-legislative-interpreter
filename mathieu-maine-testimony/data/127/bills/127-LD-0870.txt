SUMMARY  3 
This bill updates the Maine Spruce Budworm Management Act to eliminate outda ted 4 
language, such as that referring to pesticide spraying, and to add language governing 5 
presalvage and salvage harvesting of forest stands that have a significant risk of damage 6 
from spruce budworm.  7
