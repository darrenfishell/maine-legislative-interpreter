This bill proposes to create well -paying jobs and to reduce the State's costs for 5

investing $1,000,000 in a pilot block grant program for nonprofit home health care 7 
providers to provide light housekeeping, shopping, cooking, budget and medication 8 
management and referral services and ass istance with other activities of daily living under 9 
the supervision of a primary care physician.  Providers would apply via a competitive 10 
grant application process.  11
