SUMMARY  29 
This bill  amends the Advanced Technology Infrastructure Act.  It amends the 30 
definitions of "communications service" and "communications service provider."  It 31 
makes changes to the State's goals and policies in the Act.  It amends the membership of 32 
the ConnectME Aut hority and amends the duties of the authority.  It requires the 33 
authority to provide funds for broadband planning grants.  It abolishes the ConnectME 34 
Advisory Council.  It requires the authority to establish a triennial strategic plan for 35 
broadband service  and to report to the Legislature on progress toward meeting the plan's 36 
goals and objectives.  37
