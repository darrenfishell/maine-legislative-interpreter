SUMMARY  17 
This bill aims to correct an inconsistency in the so -called Dig Safe Law. It requires 18 
that an excavation must commence within 30 days afte r notification by an excavator to 19 
the underground facility damage prevention system and requires that if work is not 20 
completed within 60 calendar days of notification, an excavator must again notify the 21 
system in accordance with the Maine Revised Statutes,  Title 23, section 3360 -A. 22 
This bill is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on Energy, Utilities and 23 
Technology pursuant to Public Law 2013, chapter 557, section 3.  The law directed the 24 
Public Utilities Commission to review Public Utilities Commi ssion Rule Chapter 895:  25 
Underground Facility Damage Prevention Requirements. The commission submitted a 26 
report to the committee and made recommendations based on this review and after 27 
receiving stakeholder input.  28
