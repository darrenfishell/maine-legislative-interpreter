SUMMARY  34 
This bill proposes to limi t the costs for special education transportation services for 35 
each school administrative unit that incurs special education costs that exceed $20,000 for 36 
transportation services required to assist a child with a disability.  The bill provides that 37 
the Comm issioner of Education must pay 100% of the costs authorized for special 38 
education transportation services that exceed $20,000 annually for each eligible child 39 
with a disability within the jurisdiction of the school administrative unit.  40
