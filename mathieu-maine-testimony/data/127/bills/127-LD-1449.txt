SUMMARY  23 
This bill makes the following changes to the election laws.  24 
1.  It allows the Secretary of State to accept voted absentee ballots from uniformed 25 
service voters and overseas voters by an electronic means authorized by the Secretary of 26 
State. 27 
2.  It allows for the connection of individual voting devices to a central server 28 
operated or managed by the Secretary of State for voting systems equipped for 29 
individuals with disabilities by other than a wired, point -to-point telephone connection.  30 
3.  It allows for the use of the Internet to provide a ballot -marking system or software 31 
that is used for voters with disabilities, uniformed service voters or overseas voters to 32 
mark a ballot and transmit the marked ballot online, as long as the system or  software 33 
does not tabulate the ballots or transmit the ballot tabulations online.  34
