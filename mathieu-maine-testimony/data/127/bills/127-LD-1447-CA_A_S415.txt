29 Justice and Public Safety. It changes the title and strikes the bill. It authorizes the Maine

31 improvements to the Maine Correctional Center in South Windham and a facility owned 
32 by the Department of Corrections in Washington County up to an amount not to exceed 
33 $149,700,000 outstanding at any one time.
