SUMMARY  14 
This bill allows for the prescription of naloxone hydrochloride by standing order.  It 15 
allows for the prescription of naloxone h ydrochloride to friends of and other persons in a 16 
position to assist an individual at risk of experiencing an opioid -related drug overdose in 17 
addition to immediate family members.  It provides for immunity from civil and criminal 18 
liability for the prescrip tion and administration of naloxone hydrochloride for prescribers 19 
and those who administer the drug.  It creates a limited immunity from criminal 20 
prosecution for those who seek medical assistance when a person is experiencing an 21 
opioid -related drug overdos e. 22
