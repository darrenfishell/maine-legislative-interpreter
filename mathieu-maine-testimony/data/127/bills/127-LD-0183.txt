SUMMARY  32 
This bill in creases the amount exempt from property tax for homesteads from 33 
$10,000 to $20,000 and requires that municipalities be reimbursed for 100% of the taxes 34 
lost due to the increase.  The bill also increases from 5% to 6% the sales tax on tangible 35 
personal prop erty and taxable services and products transferred electronically with a 36 
value of $5,000 or less.  37
