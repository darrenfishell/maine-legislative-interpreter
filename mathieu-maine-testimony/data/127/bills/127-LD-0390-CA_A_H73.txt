COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 256, L.D. 390, Bill, "An Act To 
Enforce Restrictions in Parking Spaces and Access Designated for Persons with a 
Walking Disability" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Enforce Restrictions in Parking Spaces and Access Aisles Designated for 
Persons with a Walking Disability' 
Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 9-A by striking out all of the last sentence 
(page 1, lines 15 to 18 in L.D.) and inserting the following: 'Testimony under oath with 
clear photographic evidence from a person with a disability or the driver of a vehicle 
transporting a person with a disability that a vehicle was parked in violation of this 
subsection is prima facie evidence of a violation of this subsection. For purposes of this 
subsection, "person with a disability" has the same meaning as in subsection I.' 
Amend the bill by adding after section 1 the following:
