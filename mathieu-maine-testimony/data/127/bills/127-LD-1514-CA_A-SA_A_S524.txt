4 SUMMARY 
5 This amendment corrects conflicts created by the enactment of Public Law 2015, 
6 chapter 429, which repealed and replaced the laws governing dental professionals. This 
7 amendment makes the following changes to the provisions of law enacted in chapter 429: 
8 I. It changes the term "dental hygiene therapist" to "dental therapist"; 
9 2. It changes the term "dental hygiene therapy" to "dental therapy"; 
10 3. It specifies that an applicant who has successfully completed a master's degree, 
11 instead of a bachelor's degree or higher, in a dental therapy education program that is 
12 accredited by either the Board of Dental Practice or the American Dental Association 
13 Commission on Dental Accreditation or a successor organization approved by the board 
14 meets the requirements for having completed a dental therapy education program; 
15 4. It increases the number of hours of supervised clinical practice that an applicant 
16 for dental therapist authority must complete from 2,000 to 2,500; 
17 5. It removes the requirements that a dental therapy education program be consistent 
18 with a specified model curriculum, be consistent with programs in other states and be 
19 approved by the board; 
20 6. It limits to 5 the number of dental therapists that a single dentist may supervise at 
21 one time; and
