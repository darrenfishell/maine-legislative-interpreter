COMMITTEE AMENDMENT " f\ " to S.P. 447, L.D. 1242, Bill, "An Act To 
Protect Maine Consumers from Medical Identity Theft" . 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'Resolve, Directing the Bureau of Insurance To Monitor Developments Related to 
Cybersecurity Standards for Insurance Companies of the National Association of 
Insurance Commissioners' 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and before the summary and 
inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. Monitor development related to cybersecurity standards for 
insurance companies. Resolved: That the Department of Professional and Financial 
Regulation, Bureau of Insurance shall monitor the development of cybersecurity 
standards for insurance companies by the National Association of Insurance 
Commissioners and consult with the Joint Standing Committee on Insurance and 
Financial Services about the legislative and regulatory activities in other states related to 
cybersecurity for insurance companies to protect against security breaches involving 
personal information and health care information collected by or in the possession of 
insurance companies. The Joint Standing Committee on Insurance and Financial Services 
may report out a bill to the Second Regular Session of the 127th Legislature related to 
cybersecurity standards for insurance companies.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the committee and replaces the bill with a 
resolve. This amendment directs the Department of Professional and Financial 
Regulation, Bureau of Insurance to monitor the development of cybersecurity standards 
for insurance companies by the National Association of Insurance Commissioners and 
consult with the Joint Standing Committee on Insurance and Financial Services about the 
legislative and regulatory activities in other states related to cybersecurity for insurance 
companies to protect against security breaches involving personal information and health
