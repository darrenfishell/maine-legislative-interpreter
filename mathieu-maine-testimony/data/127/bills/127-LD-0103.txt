This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to create a program using a $5,000,000 fund to help employers 5 
lower their energy costs through e nergy efficiency and weatherization initiatives.  This 6 
program would be modeled after the Governor's Jobs Initiative Program administered by 7 
the Department of Labor's bureau of employment services, which provides financial 8 
assistance to employers that are expanding, reorganizing or locating in the State or are 9 
training new or current employees to upgrade worker skills.  10
