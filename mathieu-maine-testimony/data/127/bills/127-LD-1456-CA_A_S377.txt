12 Amend the resolve by striking out everything after the title and before the summary

14 'Sec. 1. Definitions. Resolved: That, as used in this resolve, the following terms 
15 have the following meanings. 
16 1. "Commissioner" means the Commissioner of Administrative and Financial 
17 Services. 
18 2. "State property" means the real estate described in section 3 with any buildings

24 organized nonprofit corporation; and be it further 
25 Sec. 3. Property interests that may be conveyed. Resolved: That the state 
26 property subject to transfer is described as follows:

19 SUMMARY 
20 
21 
22 This amendment retains the substance of the resolve and adds the following 
provisions. 
1. It defines "state property" and "commissioner" for the purposes of the resolve. 
23 2. It makes a clarification regarding the ownership of a parcel of land abutting the 
24 parcel to be sold. 
25 3. It clarifies that the State acquired the parcel of land upon the deorganization of the 
26 Town of Madrid. 
27 4. It authorizes the Commissioner of Administrative and Financial Services to 
28 negotiate and execute purchase and sale agreements and provides that the title of the 
29 property must be transferred from the State by quitclaim deed without covenant or release 
30 deed to the Madrid Historical Society. 
31 5. It exempts the transfer from any statutory or regulatory requirement that the 
32 property be offered to a state or local agency or offered through competitive bidding. 
33 6. It specifies that any proceeds from a sale must be deposited in the Unorganized 
34 Territory Education and Services Fund. 
35 7. It provides that the resolve is repealed 5 years after its effective date.

Fiscal Note 
Current biennium revenue increase -Other Special Revenue Funds 
This resolve authorizes the Commissioner of Administrative and Financial Services to convey the interest of the State 
in state property acquired upon the deorganization of the Town of Madrid. The funds received as a result of the sale 
will accrue as dedicated revenue to the Unorganized Territory Education and Services Fund. The amount received 
from the sale will depend on the sale price and cannot be estimated at this time.
