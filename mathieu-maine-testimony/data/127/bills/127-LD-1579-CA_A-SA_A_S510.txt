Fiscal Note 
Change in timing of transfer to Maine Clean Election Fund 
This amendment removes both the emergency preamble and emergency clause from the bill and changes the date of the $500,000 
General Fund transfer to the Maine Clean Election Fund from August 1, 2016 to September 1, 2016. There is no net fiscal impact to 
this change within the same fiscal year.
