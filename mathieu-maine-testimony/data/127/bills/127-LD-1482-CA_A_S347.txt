9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A " to S.P. 580, L.D. 1482, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Revise the Charter of the Rumford Water District" 
11 Amend the bill by striking out all of section 15 (page 4, lines 38 and 39 and page 5, 
12 lines 1 and 2 in L.D.) and inserting the following: 
13 'Sec. 15. Retains rights, powers and authorities. Nothing in this Act may be 
14 construed to affect the district's property rights, powers or authorities acquired through 
15 the district's acquisition of Union Construction Company, Virginia Spring Water 
16 Company, Rumford Falls Light and Water Company and the Mexico Water Company 
17 unless those rights, powers or authorities are inconsistent with this Act.' 
18 SUMMARY 
19 This amendment clarifies that property rights, powers or authorities the Rumford 
20 Water District acquired through the acquisition of Union Construction Company, 
21 Virginia Spring Water Company, Rumford Falls Light and Water Company and the 
22 Mexico Water Company are not affected by the revised charter of the Rumford Water 
23 District unless those rights, powers or authorities are inconsistent with the revised charter 
24 of the Rumford Water District.
