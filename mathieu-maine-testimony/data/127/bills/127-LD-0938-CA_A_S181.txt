SUMMARY 
2 This unanimous committee amendment eliminates the per ton fees collected by the 
3 Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry on agricultural liming materials 
4 and on commercial fertilizer sold in the State, increases the per product commercial 
5 fertilizer registration fee by $25, directs the increase to a separate account to be used for 
6 carrying out the Maine Commercial Fertilizer Law and the Maine Agricultural Liming 
7 Materials Act and requires the submission of an annual report to the joint standing 
8 committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over agricultural matters, which is 
9 authorized to report out a bill.

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill eliminates the $1 per ton fee on both commercial fertilizer and agricultural liming material. This bill increases 
the registration fee on commercial fertilizer from $100 to $125. Altering these fees is expected to result in an 
estimated Other Special Revenue Funds revenue loss of $7,797 in fiscal year 2015-16 and in every subsequent fiscal 
year.
