SUMMARY  14 
This bill requires health insurance carriers to reimburse for the costs of emergency 15 
medical services, including calls for emergency medical assistance when a person is not 16 
transported to a hospital.  The requirements of the bill apply to all health insurance 17 
policies issued or renewed on or after January 1, 2016.  18
