SUMMARY 
This amendment amends Committee Amendment "A." It replaces the language on 
security freezes involving minors under 16 years of age in Committee Amendment "A" 
with language that is consistent with the laws of other states that permit security freezes 
for minors under 16 years of age. The amendment permits a consumer reporting agency 
to charge a fee of up to $10 for each placement of or removal of a security freeze for a
