SUMMARY  2 
This bill requires the Secretary of State to implement a system that allows a person to 3 
register to vote online.  The system requires an appl icant to provide certain information, 4 
which is verified using driver’s license or nondriver identification card data maintained 5 
by the Department of the Secretary of State, Bureau of Motor Vehicles.  An applicant 6 
must submit an affidavit attesting that all  information provided in the electronic voter 7 
registration application is true.  The bill authorizes the Secretary of State to adopt rules to 8 
administer the electronic voter registration system and requires the Secretary of State to 9 
submit an annual report  to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 10 
jurisdiction over voter registration matters.  11
