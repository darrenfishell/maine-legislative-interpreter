SUMMARY  3 
This bill expands the law creating the right to a leave of absence for employees who 4 
are victims of violence by requiring that a returning employee be placed in the employee's 5 
former employment position or a position of equivalent employme nt status.  The bill 6 
prohibits discrimination or retaliation against an employee for exercising the right to take 7 
a leave of absence or enforcing the provisions of the law and creates a private right of 8 
action to enforce the law, and to seek injunctive or equitable relief and compensatory 9 
damages.  10
