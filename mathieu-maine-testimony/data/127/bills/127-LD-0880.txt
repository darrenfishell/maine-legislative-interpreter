SUMMARY  12 
This bill provides the Public Utilities Commission with the authority to create a 13 
mechanism for a water utility to request an adjustment to rates charged to ratepayers to 14 
reconcile actual revenues or costs with proje cted revenues or costs, either on a total or per 15 
customer basis. This bill also requires the Public Utilities Commission, as part of its 16 
annual report, to list all rate adjustments requested and all those that have been granted.  17
