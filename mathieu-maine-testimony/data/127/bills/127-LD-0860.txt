 Sec. 1.  Rules. Resolved:   That the Department of Health and Human Services 1 
shall annually review and incrementally adjust during the 5 years following the effective 2 
date of this resolve the reimbursement rates under the MaineCare program for dental 3 
codes D0120, D0140, D0150, D0220, D0272, D0330, D1110, D1120, D1208, D1351, 4 
D2140, D2150, D2160, D2330, D2331, D2332, D2391, D2392, D2393, D3330, D7140 5 
and D7210 until the reimbursement rates reflect the 10th percentile of the fees for the 6 
New England region in the most recent "Survey of Dental Fees" published by the 7 
American Dental Association.  After the reimbursement rates reach the 10th percentile, 8 
the department shall amend Rule Chapter 101, MaineCare Benefits Manual to require that 9 
the rates be adjusted ann ually for inflation in accordance with the United States 10 
Department of Labor, Bureau of Labor Statistics Consumer Price Index.  Rules adopted 11 
pursuant to this section are routine technical rules pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, 12 
Title 5, chapter 375,  subchapter 2 -A. 13 
SUMMARY  14 
This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to incrementally 15 
adjust the MaineCare reimbursement rates for certain dental services annually over the 16 
next 5 years until the rates reach the 10th percentile of the fees for the New England 17 
region in the most recent "Survey of Dental Fees" published by the American Dental 18 
Association. The resolve provides that the rates must then be adjusted annually for 19 
inflation.  20
