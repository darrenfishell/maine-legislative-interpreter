SUMMARY 
This amendment eliminates the proposed General Fund appropriation in fiscal year 
2016-17 for the County Jail Operations Fund and reduces the General Fund appropriation 
to Medical Care -Payments to Providers by $2,465,896 in fiscal year 2016-17 as a result 
of the increase in the Federal Medical Assistance Percentage for federal fiscal year 2017.
