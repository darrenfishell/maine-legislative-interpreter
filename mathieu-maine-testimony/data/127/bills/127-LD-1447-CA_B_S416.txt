The legal voters of each city, town and plantation shall vote by ballot on this question 
and designate their choice by a cross or check mark placed within a corresponding square 
below the word "Yes" or "No." The ballots must be received, sorted, counted and 
declared in open ward, town and plantation meetings and returns made to the Secretary of

SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the Joint Standing Committee on Criminal 
Justice and Public Safety. The amendment changes the title and replaces the bill with 
legislation authorizing a General Fund bond issue. The bonds, in the amount of 
$149,700,000, will be used for capital construction, repairs and improvements to the 
Maine Correctional Center in South Windham and a facility owned by the Department of 
Corrections in Washington County.
