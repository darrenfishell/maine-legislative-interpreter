SUMMARY  10 
This bill  clarifies that the Secretary of State and not the Treasurer of State manages 11 
the Maine Organ and Tissue Donation Fund and distributes revenue to the Organ 12 
Donation Advisory Council, that all interest accrued by the fund becomes part of the fund 13 
and that t he fund is nonlapsing.  14
