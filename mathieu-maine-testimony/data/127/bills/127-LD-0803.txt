SUMMARY  30 
This reso lve creates the Task Force To Develop Strategies To Protect Towns.  The 31 
task force will examine ways of assisting municipalities in developing and strengthening 32 
their commercial bases.  The task force is required to submit a report to the Joint Standing 33 
Committee on Labor, Commerce, Research and Economic Development.  34
