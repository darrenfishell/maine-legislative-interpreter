This bill requires the Secretary of State to produce and issue a simulated registration plate commemorating the 
bicentennial of the State for a fee of$25. $16 will go to the Maine State Cultural Affairs Council and $9 will go to the 
Secretary of State. This fiscal note assumes a total of 1,800 plates will be produced, with 900 issued in fiscal year 
2017-18 and 900 issued in fiscal year 2018-19. 
The Maine State Cultural Affairs Council will require an Other Special Revenue Funds allocation of$14,400 in each 
of fiscal years 2017-18 and 2018-19 to allow expenditure of the revenue expected to be received through the sale of 
these plates. 
The Secretary of State will require an Other Special Revenue Funds allocation of $2,311 in fiscal year 2017-18 in 
order to produce and issue the commemorative plates. Other Special Revenue Funds revenue to the department is 
expected to increase by $8,100 in fiscal years 2017-18 and 2018-19 from the sale ofthese plates.
