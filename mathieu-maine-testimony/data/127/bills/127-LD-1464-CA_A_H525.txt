SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies the bill's proposed changes to the Commissioner of 
Education's authority to establish and assess fees for initial and renewal certificates. The 
amendment requires the Department of Education to annually post the fees required for 
the.initial issuance of and the renewal of teacher, education specialist and administrator 
certificates on the department's publicly accessible website. The amendment also 
requires the commissioner to adopt major substantive rules to carry out the establishment 
and assessment of these certification fees.

Fiscal Note 
Potential current biennium revenue impact -General Fund 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Requiring the Commissioner of Education to set fees for certification of educational personnel that are currently set in 
statute may change the amount of revenue that accrues to the General Fund from certification fees. The impact to 
General Fund revenues will depend on the fees established. 
Additional cost to the Department of Education associated with the rulemaking process can be absorbed within 
existing budgeted resources.
