13 SUMMARY 
14 This amendment replaces the bill, which eliminates wild turkey pennit requirements. 
15 Current law requires that a person hold a big game license to be eligible to purchase a· 
16 wild turkey permit. This amendment makes. the holder of a small game license also 
17 eligible to hold a wild turkey permit. It also directs the COlmnissioner of Inland Fisheries 
18 and Wildlife to establish a November wild turkey hunting season that is in addition to the 
19 current fall wild turkey hunting season in October. The amendment eliminates the bill's 
20 provisions regarding registration of wild turkeys. The amendment provides an effective 
21 date ofJanuary 1, 2016.
