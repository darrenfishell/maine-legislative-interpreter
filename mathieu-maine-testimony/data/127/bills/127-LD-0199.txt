SUMMARY  24 
This bill amends the law regarding mandated reporters of suspected child abuse and 25 
neglect and of the suspicious death of a child by striking language allowing those 26 
reporters to cause someone else to make a report.  Language requiring the individual 27 
mandated reporter to make a re port is retained.  28
