Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This amendment permits instead of requires the Department of Education to collect and aggregate data regarding the 
issuance of certificates of content area proficiency. The Department of Education has indicated that, to the extent that 
the data can be collected and aggregated as part of the existing duties of its staff, no General Fund appropriations are 
needed.
