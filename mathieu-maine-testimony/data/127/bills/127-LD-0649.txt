SUMMARY  18 
This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its 19 
Rule Chapter 101,  MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter II, Section 75 to increase the 20 
reimbursement rate for eyeglasses for children by 50% by January 1, 2016.  This resolve 21 
also directs the department to convene a task force to review the reimbursement rates for 22 
certain services to MaineCare recipients and to report the findings to the Joint S tanding 23 
Committee on Health and Human Services.  24
