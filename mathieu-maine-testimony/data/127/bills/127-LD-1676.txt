SUMMARY  9 
This bill is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on Energy, Utilities and 10 
Technology pursuant to Joint Order 2016, Senate Paper 668.  This bill dir ects the Public 11 
Utilities Commission to conduct competitive solicitations and negotiate the procurement 12 
of new or existing renewable resources.  The commission is directed to procure by 13 
September 1, 2016, through an expedited proceeding, 80 megawatts of ne w or existing 14 
renewable resources for contracts of 5 years and, by September 1, 2017, 60 megawatts of 15 
new or existing renewable resources for contracts of no longer than 10 years.  This bill 16 
provides that any facility that generates new or existing renewab le resources that are 17 
procured by the commission is deemed to produce zero greenhouse gas emissions.  18 
The committee has not taken a position on the substance of this bill.  By reporting 19 
this bill out, the committee is not suggesting and does not intend to s uggest that it agrees 20 
or disagrees with any aspect of this bill.  The committee is reporting the bill out for the 21 
sole purpose of having a bill printed that can be referred to the committee for an 22 
appropriate public hearing and subsequent processing in the  normal course.  The 23 
committee is taking this action to ensure clarity and transparency in the legislative review 24 
of the proposals contained in the bill.  25
