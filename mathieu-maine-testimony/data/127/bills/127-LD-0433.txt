 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  22 MRSA §2843 -A, sub -§10, ¶F is enacted to read:  2 
F.  A funeral director or practitioner of funeral service who, in good faith, acts upon 3 
the instructions of a party that misrepresents t hat party's custody and control may not 4 
be held civilly or criminally liable and is not subject to disciplinary action for acting 5 
in accordance with those instructions.  6 
SUMMARY  7 
This bill provides immunity from disciplinary action and civil and criminal lia bility 8 
for a funeral director or a practitioner of funeral service who, with respect to the remains 9 
of a deceased person, acts in good faith upon the instructions of an individual who 10 
misrepresents that individual's custody and control of that deceased per son. 11
