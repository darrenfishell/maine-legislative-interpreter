COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" Pt" to H.P. 749, L.D. 1088, Bill, "An Act To 
Implement Recommendations of the Right To Know Advisory Committee" 
Amend the bill in Part C by striking out all of section 4. 
Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment removes the section of the bill that removes language authorizing 
the Secretary of State to adopt rules regarding the maintenance and use of data processing 
information files required to be kept confidential.
