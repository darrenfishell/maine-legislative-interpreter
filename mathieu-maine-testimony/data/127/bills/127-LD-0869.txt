Sec. 1.  17 MRSA §3203, sub -§1, as enacted by PL 2003, c. 452, Pt. I, §54 and 2 
affected by Pt. X, §2, is amended to read:  3 
1.  Sales of motor vehicles on Sunday prohibited.   Except as provided in section  4 
sections  3203 -A and 3203 -B, on Sunday a person may not:  5 
A.  Carry on or engage in the business of buying, selling, exchanging, dealing or 6

B.  Open any place of business or lot in which that person attempts to or does engage 8 
in the business of buying, selling, exchanging, dealing or trading in new or used 9 
motor vehicles; or  10 
C.  Buy, sell, exchange, deal or trade in new or used motor vehicles.  11 
Sec. 2.  17 MRSA §3203 -B is enacted to read:  12 
§3203 -B.  Antique autos  13 
Notwithstanding section 3203, a person whose primary business involves buying, 14 
selling, exchanging, dealing in or trading in antique autos as defined in Title 29 -A, 15 
section 101, subsection 3 may buy, sell, exchange, deal in and trade in antique autos on 16 
Sunday. 17 
SUMMARY  18 
This bill exempts from the law prohibiting the sale of motor vehicles on Sunday the 19 
sale of antique autos by antique auto dealers.  20
