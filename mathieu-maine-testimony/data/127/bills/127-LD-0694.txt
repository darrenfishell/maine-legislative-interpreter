SUMMARY  23 
This bill provides that a person is ineligible for interment in the Maine Veterans' 24 
Memorial Cemetery System if that person has been proven to have committed a serious 25 
crime, such as murder, a sexual offense or any crime punishable by imprisonment for life.  26 
It also allows Department of Labor personnel to access veterans' military service records.  27
