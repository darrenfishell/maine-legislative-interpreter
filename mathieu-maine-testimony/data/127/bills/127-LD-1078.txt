Sec. 2.  Annual cost -of-living adjustment.  The Department of Health and 13 
Human Services shall amend its rules in Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, 14 
Chapter II I, Section 2, Adult Family Care Services; Chapter III, Section 2, Section 97, 15 
Appendix C: Principles of Reimbursement for Medical and Remedial Service Facilities; 16 
and Chapter 115, Principles of Reimbursement for Residential Care Facilities - Room and 17 
Board  Costs, to provide for ongoing, annual rate changes beginning July 1, 2015 to adjust 18 
for inflation.  For each of the state fiscal years ending July 1, 2016 and July 1, 2017, the 19 
amount of the adjustment is 4%.  For each year thereafter, the department shal l set the 20 
inflation adjustment cost -of-living percentage change in reimbursement each year in 21 
accordance with the United States Department of Labor, Bureau of Labor Statistics 22 
Consumer Price Index medical care services index.  23 
SUMMARY  24 
This bill provides fun ds to give adult family care homes and Appendix C private 25 
nonmedical institutions a 4% cost -of-living rate increase in funding in each of the next 2 26 
fiscal years.  Annual cost -of-living adjustments are provided by rule for each fiscal year 27 
thereafter in ac cordance with the United States Department of Labor, Bureau of Labor 28 
Statistics Consumer Price Index medical care services index.  29
