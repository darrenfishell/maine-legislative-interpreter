 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 1 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and  2 
Whereas, the federal Naval Appropriations Act of 1916, dated March 3, 1915, 3 
established a United St ates Naval Reserve, and March 3, 2015 marks the centennial 4 
celebration of what is now called the United States Navy Reserve; and  5 
Whereas, this legislation honors and recognizes this important anniversary and the 6 
men and women who serve and have served in t he United States Navy Reserve; and  7 
Whereas, the anniversary date of the United States Navy Reserve is prior to the 8 
effective date of nonemergency legislation; and  9 
Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 10 
the meani ng of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 11 
immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 12 
therefore, be it  13 
Sec. 1.  Navy Reserve centennial plate.  Resolved:  That, notwithstanding the 14 
Maine Revised Statutes, Title 29 -A, section 454, the Secretary of State shall issue, at no 15 
cost to the Secretary of State, a reflectorized, commemorative, simulated registration 16 
plate in recognition and celebration of the centennial of the United States Na vy Reserve 17 
on March 3, 2015.  The Secretary of State shall design the plate in consultation with the 18 
United States Navy Reserve, and the plate must bear the date of its expiration, which is 19 
March 3, 2016.  The United States Navy Reserve centennial commemor ative plate may 20 
be displayed by covering, but not removing, the front registration plate on a motor 21 
vehicle, including a motor vehicle registered outside this State and operated within it, 22 
until March 3, 2016.  A truck tractor as defined in Title 29 -A, sec tion 101, subsection 90 23 
may not display the plate under this section; and be it further  24 
Sec. 2.  Administrative fee; distribution.  Resolved:  That the Secretary of 25 
State shall develop a plan for the sale and distribution of the United States Navy Reserve 26 
centennial commemorative plate under section 1.  The fee for the United States Navy 27 
Reserve centennial commemorative plate is $15, which must be distributed as follows:  28 
1.  Five dollars per plate to the Secretary of State for the costs associated with 29 
production and issuance of the plates; and  30 
2.  Ten dollars per plate to the Maine Military Family Relief Fund established in the 31 
Maine Revised Statutes, Title 37 -B, section 158; and be it further  32 
Sec. 3.  Other uses prohibited.  Resolved:  That a United States  Navy Reserve 33 
centennial commemorative plate may not be sold or displayed except as provided in this 34 
resolve; and be it further  35

SUMMARY  10 
This resolve creates a special commemorative, simulated motor vehicle registration 11 
plate in celebra tion of the centennial of the United States Navy Reserve, which takes 12 
place March 3, 2015.  The Secretary of State is directed to design and issue the plate, 13 
which may be displayed until March 3, 2016 covering the front registration plate.  14 
Revenue generat ed by the sale of the plate in excess of the cost of production and 15 
issuance will be given to the Maine Military Family Relief Fund.  16
