 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  36 MRSA §1817 is enacted to read:  2 
§1817.  Sale of United States flags  3 
The State Controller shall deposit the tax collected under this chapter from the sale of 4 
a United States flag into the Maine Veterans' Memorial Cemetery System Care Fund 5 
under Title 37 -B, section 512 for the purpose of maintaining the cemeteries within the 6 
Maine Veterans' Memorial Cemetery System.  7 
Sec. 2.  37-B MRSA §512, sub -§1, as amended by PL 2013, c. 128, §2, is further 8 
amended to read:  9 
1.  Maine Veterans' Memorial Cemetery System Care Fund establishment; 10 
purpose.   The Maine Veterans' Memorial Cemetery System Care Fund, known in this 11 
section as "the fund," is established for the purpose  purposes  of maintaining the 12 
cemeteries and  ensuring ongoing care and maintenance of veterans' graves within the 13 
Maine Veterans' Memorial Cemetery System after plot interment allowances for burials 14 
within the system are no longer received from the United States Department of Veterans 15 
Affairs.  The fund is established from deposits of 1/3 of the funds received from the 16 
United States Department of Veterans Affairs for plot interment allowances and from 17 
annual deposits from the Coordinated Veterans Assistance Fund established by section 18 
514 and sales tax revenue received pursuant to Title 36, section 1817 .  The fund may also 19 
accept private and public donations.  The fund is separate from other perpetual care or 20 
cemetery maintenance funds that support veterans' cemeteries and were establish ed prior 21 
to the effective date of this section.  22 
SUMMARY  23 
This bill requires that the sales tax collected from the sale of United States flags be 24 
deposited into the Maine Veterans' Memorial Cemetery System Care Fund for the 25 
purpose of maintaining the cemeter ies in the Maine Veterans' Memorial Cemetery 26 
System.  27
