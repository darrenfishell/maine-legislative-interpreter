SUMMARY  13 
Under current law, municipal clerks are authorized to process absentee ballots on the 14 
Monday before  the election as long as established notice, inspection, processing and 15 
security procedures are followed.  This bill allows municipal clerks to process absentee 16 
ballots on the Saturday or Monday before the election, or both, as long as all these 17 
procedures  are followed.  18
