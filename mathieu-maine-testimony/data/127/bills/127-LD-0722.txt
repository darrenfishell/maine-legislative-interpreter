SUMMARY  17 
This bill provides that a person who makes a false representation of a material fact to 18 
obtain municipal general assistance is ineligible to receive further assista nce for a period 19 
of the longer of 120 days and until that reimbursement is made.  Current law provides for 20 
a period of ineligibility of 120 days.  21
