SUMMARY  17 
This bill provides that a retail establishment licensed to sell alcoholic beverages for 18 
off-premises consumption may also be licensed as a Class A restaurant or an 19 
establishment licensed to sell beer and wine f or consumption on the premises as long as 20 
alcoholic beverages are served as individual servings by an employee of the on -premises 21 
establishment.  This bill repeals the existing dual liquor license provision in law.  22
