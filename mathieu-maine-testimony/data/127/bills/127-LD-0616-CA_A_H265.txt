35 This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment establishes a tenant winery

37 tenant winery must have approval from the United States Department of the Treasury, 
38 Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau authorizing an alternating proprietorship 
39 with another winery. The amendment allows up to 9 tenant wineries per host winery. A 
40 tenant winery may share or rent the facilities and equipment of the host winery under
