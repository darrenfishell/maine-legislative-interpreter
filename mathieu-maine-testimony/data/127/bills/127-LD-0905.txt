SUMMARY  10 
This bill establishes an ongoing commission to review whether allocations of the 11 
Fund for a Healthy Maine are properly aligned with the State's public and prev entive 12 
health priorities and goals and recommend adjustments to allocations as necessary.  13 
Under the bill, the commission is authorized to meet up to 6 times every 3 years when the 14 
Legislature is not is session and the commission's first report must be sub mitted by 15 
December 7, 2015 to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction 16 
over appropriations and financial affairs and the joint standing committee having 17 
jurisdiction over health and human services matters.  Money in the Fund for a Healthy 18 
Maine may be used to fund the activities of the commission.  19
