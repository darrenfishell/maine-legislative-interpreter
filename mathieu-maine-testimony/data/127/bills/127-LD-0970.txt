SUMMARY  33 
This bill revises statutory language regarding advanced practice registered nursing.  34 
The bill provides for prescriptive authority for a ll 4 roles of advanced practice, including 35 
certified nurse practitioner, certified nurse midwife, certified registered nurse anesthetist 36 
and clinical nurse specialist.  Currently, nurse practitioners and certified nurse midwives 37 
have prescriptive authority .  The bill also requires population -specific practice to prevent 38 
the proliferation of specialties within the nurse practitioner and clinical nurse specialist 39 
categories.  The bill includes a grandfather provision.  40
