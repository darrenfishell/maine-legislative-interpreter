SUMMARY  12 
This bill increases hunting license fees by $1 and directs the Department of Inland 13 
Fisheries and Wil dlife to use that revenue on an annual basis to educate the public on the 14 
management of game species.  It also directs the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and 15 
Wildlife to convene a stakeholders group to develop a 5 -year public outreach campaign 16 
on the eff orts of the department to manage game species, including a plan on how to use 17 
the increased revenue.  The commissioner is directed to report by February 1, 2016 to the 18 
Joint Standing Committee on Inland Fisheries and Wildlife, which may report out a bill t o 19 
the Second Regular Session of the 127th Legislature.  20
