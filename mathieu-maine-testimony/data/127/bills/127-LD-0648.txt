SUMMARY  12 
This bill increases the earned income tax credit for Maine residents from 5% of the 13 
federal earned income credi t to 20% of the federal earned income credit.  This bill also 14 
repeals the eligibility of nonresidents and part -year residents for the Maine earned income 15 
tax credit.  16
