CONCEPT DRAFT  2 
SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to enact measures to encourage municipalities to review and 5 
reassess municipal regulations that create barriers to redevelopment in downtowns and on 6 
main streets.  As part of a review and reassessment, a municipality shall consider:  7 
1.  Assessing current community needs and environmental impacts of the regulations;  8 
2.  Lessening the impact of excessi ve parking requirements for buildings in 9 
downtowns and on main streets; and  10 
3.  Extending so -called grandfather provisions relating to the reuse of upper floors of 11 
buildings in downtowns and on main streets.  12
