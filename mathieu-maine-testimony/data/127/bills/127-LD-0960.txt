SUMMARY  7 
Under current law, family medical leave may be taken by an employee to care  for a 8 
child, domestic partner's child, parent, domestic partner, sibling or spouse with a serious 9 
health condition.  This bill adds to this list "grandparent" and "great -grandparent" and 10 
further provides that family medical leave may be taken in connectio n with the serious 11 
health condition experienced by any of these listed individuals related to the employee by 12 
blood, adoption, legal custody, marriage or domestic partnership.  13
