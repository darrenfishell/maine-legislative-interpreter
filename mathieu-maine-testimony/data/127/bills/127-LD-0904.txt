SUMMARY  36 
Current law limits the amount individuals, political committees, political action 37 
committees and other entities may contribute to a candidate for Governor to $1,500 in 38 
any primary, general or special election.  The limit for a legislative candidate is $350 per 39 
election.  This bill provides that individuals, political committees, political action 40 
committees and other entities may contribute a total of $3,000 to a candidate for 41
