Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill reduces the amount of state-municipal revenue sharing received by municipalities and increases General Fund 
revenue by $64,462,043 in fiscal year 2015-16 and $42,974,695 in fiscal year 2016-17. Under current law revenue 
sharing is being reduced by $86,000,000 in fiscal year 2014-15, but will not be reduced in the years affected by this 
bill. This bill may be in conflict with certain other proposals before the Legislature.
