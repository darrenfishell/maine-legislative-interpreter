SUMMARY  31 
Current law allows exemptions because of a person's philosophical beliefs from 32 
immunization requirements for students in elementary and secondary schools and 33 
postsecondary schools and employees of nursery schools and health care  facilities.  This 34 
bill removes those exemptions.  The bill also directs the Department of Health and 35 
Human Services to remove any immunization exemptions because of philosophical 36 
beliefs from its rules.  37
