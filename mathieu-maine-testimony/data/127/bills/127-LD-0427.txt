CONCEPT DRAFT  2 
SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to enact measures to address and mitigate plastic pollution in the 5 
marine environment, including  but not limited to microdebris pollution.  For the purpose 6 
of this resolve, "microdebris" means particles of plastic approximately 5 to 10 microns in 7 
length that may be ingested by filter -feeding organisms in the marine environment.  8
