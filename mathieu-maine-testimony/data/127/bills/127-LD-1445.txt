 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 1 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and  2

SUMMARY  24 
This resolve extends the authority to issue bonds for the University of Maine' s 25 
offshore wind energy demonstration site previously authorized in June 2010.  These 26 
funds are required to complete construction of the offshore wind energy demonstration 27 
site already under construction at the university.  28
