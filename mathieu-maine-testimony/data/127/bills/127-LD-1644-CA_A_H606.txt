This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, makes the following 
changes to the Commission To Study Ways To Support and Strengthen the Direct Care 
Workforce across the Long-term Care Continuum. 
1. It adds 3 members, including a representative of a labor intermediary, a

2. It clarifies that the Commissioner of Health and Human Services and the

Fiscal Note 
Legislative Cost/Study 
Legislative Cost/Study 
The general operating expenses of this study are projected to be $625 infiscal year 2015-16 and $2,125 in fiscal year 
2016-17. The Legislature's budget includes $10,000 in fiscal year 2015-16 and $10,000 in fiscal year 2016-17 for 
legislative studies. Whether the amounts are sufficient to fund all studies will depend on the number of studies 
authorized by the Legislative Council and the Legislature. The additional costs of providing staffing assistance to the 
study during the interim can be absorbed utilizing existing budgeted staff resources. 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Additional costs to the Department of Labor and the Department of Health and Human Services associated with the 
Commissioner or the commissioner's designee serving on the commission can be absorbed within existing budgeted 
resources.
