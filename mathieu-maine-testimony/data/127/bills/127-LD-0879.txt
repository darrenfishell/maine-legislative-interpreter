SUMMARY  12 
This bill makes several changes in the regulation of tele communications utilities.  It:  13 
1.  Permits the Public Utilities Commission to eliminate alternative forms of 14 
regulation if the commission determines that service deficiencies are occurring;  15 
2.  Requires the Public Utilities Commission to adopt rules that p rovide for automatic 16 
penalties if service quality standards are not met;  17 
3.  Repeals a provision regarding the reporting of unscheduled outages to the Public 18 
Utilities Commission;  19 
4.  Reduces the number of exemptions a telephone utility has from customer s ervice 20 
and regulatory requirements; and  21 
5.  Removes the exemption from regulation for telecommunications services provided 22 
using interconnected voice over Internet protocol technology.  23
