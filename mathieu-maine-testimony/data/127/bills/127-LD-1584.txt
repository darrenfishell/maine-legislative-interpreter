SUMMARY  9 
This bill requires the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Bureau 10 
of Parks and Lands to issue a free group exemption pass for day use at state parks and 11 
historic sites to an organization that serves persons with disabilities.  The pass entitles 12 
persons with disabilities and persons assisting them to admission free of charge to all 13 
state parks and historic sites for visits arranged by the organization holding the pass.  14
