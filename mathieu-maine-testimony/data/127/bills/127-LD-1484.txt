 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 1 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and  2 
Whereas, this legislation makes necessary changes to improve the administration of 3

SUMMARY  24 
This bill makes the following changes to the election laws.  25 
1.  It adds a restriction that a registrar may not serve during an election when an 26 
immediate family member of the registrar is a candidate for federal office and clarifies 27 
the language regarding compensation.  The current restriction applies only to candidates 28 
for state, local or county offices.  29 
2.  It removes provisions regarding the use of a supplemental incoming voting list.  30 
3.  It clarifies the requirements for how a voter’s name and prior voting residence 31 
address must be provided on a voter registration application.  32 
4.  It updates the list of the outside agencies that must provide voter registration 33 
pursuant to the National Vo ter Registration Act of 1993.  34

 5.  It clarifies that an individual or organization is entitled to obtain voter registration 1

6.  It provides that when  there is no candidate for a primary election office who has 4 
qualified by primary petition or as a declared write -in candidate, the Secretary of State is 5 
not required to list that office on the primary ballot.  6 
7.  It provides that the written acceptance fo r a replacement candidate must include a 7

8.  It changes the deadline for a candidate to withdraw and be replaced from 60 day s 10 
to 70 days before the primary or general election.  11 
9.  It clarifies that a candidate may withdraw and be replaced if the candidate is 12 
incapacitated by a condition or injury and requires a certificate signed by a single licensed 13 
physician to accompany the  withdrawal request.  Current law provides only for 14 
incapacitation due to an illness and requires a certificate signed by 2 licensed physicians.  15 
10.  It changes the deadline for a nonparty candidate for Vice President to withdraw 16 
and be replaced from 60 da ys to 70 days before the general election.  17 
11.  It provides that if a candidate or nominee for a federal or gubernatorial office 18 
withdraws less than 70 days before any election, the Secretary of State is not required to 19 
produce new ballots.  20

14.  It clarifies the voting procedure by combining the steps of checking voter names 27

It prohibits the practice of handing out the ballot at a separate location from the incoming 29

15.  It clarifies that it is a violation of law to display, as well as distribute, campaign 31
