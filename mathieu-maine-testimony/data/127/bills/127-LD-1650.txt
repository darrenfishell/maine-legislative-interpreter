SUMMARY  8 
This bill enacts recommendations of the study of the allocations of the Fund for a 9 
Healthy Maine conducted pursuant to Resolve 2015, chapter 47.  The bill requires the 10 
Department of Health and Human Services to submit an annual report by January 1st to 11 
the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over appropriations 12 
and financial affairs and the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 13 
jurisdiction over health and human services matters on the Fund for a Healthy Maine.  14 
The report must include annual expenditures in all programs, progress made toward 15 
improvin g the health status of persons in the State using the fund, a description of how 16 
funds were targeted to prevention and health -related purposes outlined in statute and 17 
information on any audits in the previous year.  The requirement for the Department of 18 
Administrative and Financial Services to provide an annual report regarding the 19 
expenditure of the Fund for a Healthy Maine on prevention and health -related purposes is 20 
removed from the statute.  21 
The bill also repeals several provisions of law requiring repor ts by the Department of 22 
Health and Human Services that are no longer necessary.  23
