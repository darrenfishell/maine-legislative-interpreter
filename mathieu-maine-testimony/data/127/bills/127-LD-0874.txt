SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to establish a public online registry of all state and federal grants 5 
and low -interest or no -interest loans given to residents, businesses, municipalities and 6 
nonprofit organizations in this State.  The registry would be searchable by a resident's 7 
name, municipality name, business name or organization's name.  The registry would 8 
include the state or federal agency giving the grant or loan and the amount, terms and 9 
purpose of the grant or loan.  10
