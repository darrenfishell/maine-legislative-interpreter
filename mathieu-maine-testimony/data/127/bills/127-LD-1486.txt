 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 1 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and  2 
Whereas, the Treasurer of State may not sell the remaining bonds authorized but not 3 
yet issued from  the Land for Maine's Future Board bond issue authorized by the voters in 4 
November 2010 unless the Legislature reauthorizes the issuance of those bonds; and  5 
Whereas, the Land for Maine's Future Board requires the funds to meet its 6 
obligations for previousl y authorized land conservation projects with state, municipal and 7 
private entities and to fulfill its obligations under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, 8 
chapter 353 in order to protect public recreational opportunities, farmland and the State's 9 
environ ment; and  10 
Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 11 
the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 12 
immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safet y; now, 13 
therefore, be it  14 
Sec. 1.  Extension for issuing.  Resolved:  That, pursuant to the provisions of the 15 
Constitution of Maine, Article IX, Section 14, the period for issuance of bonds or of bond 16 
anticipation notes for the acquisition of land and inter ests in land for purposes such as 17 
conservation, wildlife and fish habitat, outdoor recreation and farmland preservation 18 
authorized but unissued pursuant to Public Law 2009, chapter 414, as amended by Public 19 
Law 2009, chapter 645, and authorized by the vote rs in a statewide election held in 20 
November 2010 is further extended for a 2 -year period.  21 
Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 22 
legislation takes effect when approved.  23
