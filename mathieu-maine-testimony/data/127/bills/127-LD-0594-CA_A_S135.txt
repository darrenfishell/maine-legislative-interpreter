An Act To Allow the Creation of a Local Option Sales Tax by Referendum 
Fiscal Detail and Notes Fiscal Note for Bill as Amended by Committee Amendment 'W' (S -)35 ) 
Committee: Taxation 
Fiscal Note Required: Yes 
Fiscal Note 
Potential current biennium cost increase -General Fund 
Potential current biennium revenue increase -General Fund 
This bill allows a municipality to impose a local option sales tax of no more than I % by referendum. The Department 
of Administration and Financial Services would incur costs to administer the program which would be covered by the 
sales tax revenue. The amounts would depend on the number of municipalities that choose to have a local sales tax 
and on the amount of sales realized.
