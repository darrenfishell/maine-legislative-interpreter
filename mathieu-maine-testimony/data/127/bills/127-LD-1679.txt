SUMMARY  18 
This bill increases the Governor's salary from $70,000 per year to $150,000 per year 19 
beginning January 2019.  20 
The bill also increases the salaries of members of the Senate and House of 21 
Representatives by 25% if the number of members of the Senate is reduced to 25 or fewer 22 
and the number of members of the House of Representatives is reduced to 100 or fewer.  23
