9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" A " to S.P. 398, L.D. 1129, "Resolve, To Support 
10 Home-based Care" 
11 Amend the resolve by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
12 'Resolve, To Change the Requirements for Nursing Services in Home Health Care' 
13 Amend the resolve by striking out everything after the title and before the summary 
14 and inserting the following: 
15 'Sec. 1. Amend the rules governing nursing services in home health care 
16 services. Resolved: That the Department of Health and Human Services shall amend 
17 Rule Chapter 119, Regulations Governing the Licensing and Functioning of Home Health 
18 Care Services, in Chapter 7, Section 7.B.2 to allow nursing services to be provided by 
19 new nursing graduates who have successfully completed a home health care orientation 
20 program approved by the department prior to commencing independent home health care 
21 nursing practice. The orientation, provided by the agency employing the nursing 
22 graduate, must include all components of the home health agency core orientation and, in 
23 addition, a standard orientation curriculum that includes, but is not limited to, intravenous 
24 therapy including central and peripherally inserted central catheter line management, 
25 comprehensive health assessment, chronic disease self-management strategies, goal 
26 setting; patient-centered care, wound care assessment and techniques, evidence-based 
27 practice in clinical care, interprofessional collaborative practice, critical thinking and 
28 clinical judgment, health care ethics and medication assessment, reconciliation and 
29 administration and management in the community setting.' 
30 SUMMARY 
31 The amendment strikes and replaces the resolve. It requires the Department of 
32 Health and Human Services to amend the rules governing the licensing and functioning 
33 of home health care services to allow nursing services to be provided by new graduates 
34 who have successfully completed a home health care orientation program approved by 
35 the department prior to commencing independent home health care nursing practice.
