SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill, which allows a phalmacist to administer vaccines 
licensed and recommended by the United States Food and Drug Administration. Current 
law allows a pharmacist to administer an influenza vaccine to a person 9 years of age or 
older. The amendment lowers that age to 7 years of age.
