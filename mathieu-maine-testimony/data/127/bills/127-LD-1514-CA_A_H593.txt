SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee: 
1. Changes the term "dental hygiene therapist" to "dental therapist"; 
2. Eliminates the provision of the bill that moves dental therapist examination 
requirements to the statutory subsection on education requirements; 
3. Eliminates the provision of the bill that allows licensure of dental therapists by 
endorsement; 
4. Limits the number of dental therapists that a single dentist may supervise; 
5. Corrects a cross-reference; 
6. Requires that an applicant for a dental therapy license engage in at least 2 years 
and 2,000 hours of supervised clinical practice; and
