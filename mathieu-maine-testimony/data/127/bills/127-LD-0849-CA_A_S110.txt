9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" A " to S.P. 298, L.D. 849, Bill, "An Act To Protect 
10 Young Bucks" 
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
12 'Resolve, Directing the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife To Examine the 
13 Use of an Antler Point Restriction System To Increase the Age, Size and Number of 
14 Antlered Deer in Maine' 
15 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and before the summary and 
16 inserting the following: 
17 'Sec. 1. Examine the use of an antler point restriction system to increase 
18 the age, size and number of antlered deer. Resolved: That the Department of 
19 Inland Fisheries and Wildlife shall study issues relating to increasing the number, size 
20 and age of antlered deer in this State. The department shall collect and examine relevant 
21 information from other states regarding the use of an antler point restriction system. The 
22 department shall submit a report of its findings and recommendations to the Joint 
23 Standing Committee on Inland Fisheries and Wildlife by January 15, 2016. The Joint 
24 Standing Committee on Inland Fisheries and Wildlife may report out a bill relating to the 
25 report to the Second Regular Session of the 127th Legislature.' 
26 SUMMARY 
27 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, replaces the bill with 
28 a resolve that directs the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to study issues 
29 relating to increasing the number, size and age of antlered deer in this State, collect and 
30 examine relevant information from other states regarding the use of an antler point 
31 restriction system and submit a report of its findings and recommendations to the Joint 
32 Standing Committee on Inland Fisheries and Wildlife by January 15,2016.
