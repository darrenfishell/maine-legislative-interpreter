Amend the resolution on page 1 in line 30 by striking out tht; following: "the date of

SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report and changes the effective date the amendment 
becomes part of the Constitution of Maine from the date of the proclamation, as stated in 
the resolution, to December 1, 2016 to allow the current Attorney General, who was 
elected by the Legislature, to finish the term.
