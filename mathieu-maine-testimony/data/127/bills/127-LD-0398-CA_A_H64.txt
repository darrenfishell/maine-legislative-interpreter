COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A " to H.P. 264, L.D. 398, Bill, "An Act To Allow 
Consumers of Cable Television To Purchase Channels Individually" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in paragraph F in the last line (page 1, line 4 in L.D.) by 
inserting after the following: "individually" the following: " except for channels included 
in the basic service tier' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, retains the provision 
of the bill that requires a cable system operator to offer subscribers the' option of 
purchasing access to cable channels individually and adds an exception for those channels 
that are provided in the basic service tier.
