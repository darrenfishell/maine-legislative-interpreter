SUMMARY  19 
This bill allows a person 70 years of age or older to hunt with a crossbow during any 20 
open season.  Until January 1, 2015, a provision of law allowed this, but included 21 
language that repealed the provision on that date.  The bill enacts t he substance of that 22 
provision but without the repeal language.  23
