SUMMARY  16 
This bill am ends the Uniform Commercial Code.  Under the bill, a filing office may 17 
refuse to file a record on the basis that the record is fraudulent or appears to be fraudulent 18 
and that the record pertains to a matter outside of the scope of the Uniform Commercial 19 
Code.  This bill also clarifies that a filing office's refusal to file a record is a final agency 20 
action subject to judicial review under the Maine Administrative Procedure Act.  21
