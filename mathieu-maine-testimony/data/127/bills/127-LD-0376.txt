SUMMARY  14 
This bill requires the funds generated by forfeited firearms that are auctioned to 15 
federally licensed  firearms dealers or the public to be used to fund educational programs 16 
taught by law enforcement officers that target prevention of substance abuse, violence or 17 
high-risk behavior and teach students good decision -making skills to help them lead safe 18 
and h ealthy lives.  These educational programs are known as the Drug Abuse Resistance 19 
Education program.  20
