SUMMARY  12 
This bill prohibits a person from operating a motor vehicle at 40 miles per hour or 13 
more on a public way without clearing the motor vehicle of solid precipitation.  Operating 14 
a motor vehicle under such circum stances is a traffic infraction subject to a $250 fine for 15 
a first offense and $500 for a 2nd or subsequent offense.  16
