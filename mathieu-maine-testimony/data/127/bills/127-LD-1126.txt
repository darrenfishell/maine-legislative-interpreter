SUMMARY  17 
This bill  reinstates the State's property tax deferral program, which was in effect for 18 
applications filed before April 1, 1991.  The bill modifies the program to include 19 
households with at least one individual who is 65 years of age or older or who retired 20 
from ga inful employment due to disability and to surviving spouses who are at least 60 21 
years of age, and provides that household income must be less than $40,000.  This limit is 22 
indexed for inflation in 2016 and annually thereafter.  23
