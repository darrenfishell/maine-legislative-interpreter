SUMMARY  10 
This bill provides that school administrative units may annually extend up to 25 11 
school days by one hour each day.  Five one -hour extensions count as one additional 12 
school day.  Schools may use the extensions only to make up school days missed because 13 
of weather or emergency closures.  14
