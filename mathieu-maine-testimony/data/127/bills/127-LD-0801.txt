SUMMARY  3 
This bill prohibits a person from hunting bear with the use of dogs.  The bill also 4 
makes a violation of that prohibition a Class D crime and a Class C crime for a 3rd or 5 
subsequent offense.  It pr ovides an exemption for the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries 6 
and Wildlife to hunt bear with the use of dogs for public safety or research purposes.  7
