SUMMARY  14 
This bill provides a civil remedy for victims of crime when restitution has not been 15 
paid as ordered.  The bill deems an order to make restitution a money judgement and sets 16 
requirements for the enforcement.  Specifically, the bill requires that prior to entry of the 17 
order to pay restitution, the court informed the defendant of the right to a judicial 18 
determination of the amount of restitution and the restitution hearing was held, or the 19 
defendant  waived the hearing or stipulated the amount of restitution and that after the 20 
court ordered payment of restitution, the clerk of the court entered the order to pay 21 
restitution in the same manner as a judgment in a civil action is entered.  22
