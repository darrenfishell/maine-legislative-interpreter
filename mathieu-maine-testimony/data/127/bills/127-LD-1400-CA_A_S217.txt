'A. The commission may enter into contracts for energy efficiency capacity

SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the committee. This amendment changes 
the date of repeal of the renewable portfolio standard from January 1, 2016 to January 1, 
2017. This amendment removes portions of the bill that direct the Public Utilities 
Commission to select a distributed generation aggregator. Lastly, this amendment 
authorizes the Public Utilities Commission to contract for energy efficiency capacity 
resources, either through a competitive solicitation or through a contract with the 
Efficiency Maine Trust.
