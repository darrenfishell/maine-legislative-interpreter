This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to establish an affordable market -based program to provide health 5 
insurance coverage to low -income uninsured Maine citizens who earn less than 133% of 6 
the federal poverty level.  The program would be modeled after the Insure Tennessee 7 
program and the SHARE Plan program in Wyoming.  8
