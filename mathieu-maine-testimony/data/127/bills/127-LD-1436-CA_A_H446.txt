SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority repolt of the committee. Under the bill, on-premises 
licensees are permitted to sell liquor until 2 a.m. regardless of any local option decisions 
to the contrary. The amendment provides that a municipality may prohibit sales after 1 
a.m. if the legislative body of the municipality votes for the restriction and notifies the 
Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages 
and Lottery Operations ofthat decision.
