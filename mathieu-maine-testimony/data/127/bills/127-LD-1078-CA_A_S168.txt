21 Sec. 2. Rate adjustment. The Department of Health and Human Services shall 
22 amend its rules in Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 2, 
23 Adult Family Care Services and Chapter III, Section 97, Appendix C: Principles of 
24 Reimbursement for Medical and Remedial Service Facilities and Rule Chapter 115:

29 SUMMARY 
30 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, makes the following 
31 changes to the bill. 
32 1. It provides that adult family care homes and Appendix C private nonmedical 
33 institutions receive a 4% rate increase in funding in each of the next 2 fiscal years, rather 
34 than a cost-of-living rate increase.
