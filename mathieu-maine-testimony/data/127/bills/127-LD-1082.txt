SUMMARY  24 
This bill eliminates the exemption for member municipalities in School 25 
Administrative District No. 6 and School Administrative District No. 44 from the 26 
standard municipal cost -sharing arrangement.  For purposes of the cost -sharing 27 
calculation between these member municipalities in these 2 school administrative 28 
districts, the valuation and per -pupil figures for the member municipalities must both be 29 
set to 50%.  Th e bill also repeals the prohibition against the withdrawal of the Town of 30 
Frye Island from School Administrative District No. 6.  31
