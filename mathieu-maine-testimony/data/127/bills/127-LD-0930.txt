SUMMARY  4 
This bill amends the income tax credit for child care expenses by increasing the credit 5 
from 25% of the federal tax credit allowable for ch ild and dependent care expenses to 6 
100% of that federal tax credit for tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2015.  This 7 
bill also makes the credit completely refundable.  8
