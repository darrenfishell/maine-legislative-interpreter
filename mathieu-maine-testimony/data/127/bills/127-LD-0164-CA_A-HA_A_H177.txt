SUMMARY 
This amendment amends Committee Amendment "A" to eliminate the dedicated 
funding from the State. This amendment also strikes the appropriations and allocations 
section added by the committee amendment. This amendment retains the elimination of 
the 10% sales tax on the value of consumer fireworks by Committee Amendment" A" 
The effect of these changes is to remove all funding for the Maine Length of Service 
Award Program.

This amendment reverses all fiscal impacts of the bill as amended by committee A. It eliminates the 50% dedication 
of the sales tax on consumer fireworks, increasing General Fund revenue by $109,250 in fiscal years 2015-16 and 
2016-17. It also restores transfers to Municipal Revenue Sharing by $5,750 in both fiscal years 2015-16 and 2016-17. 
All allocations are removed. As amended here, the bill does no! contain any funding to support the establishment of 
the Maine Length of Service Award Program and the mandated activities are still in place.
