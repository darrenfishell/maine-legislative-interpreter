Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Requiring the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, the Maine State Housing Authority, the 
University of Maine System, Maine Maritime Academy, the Maine Community College System and the Efficiency 
Maine Trust to install insulation with a global-wanning potential of 10 or less in state-funded new construction or 
substantial renovation projects that begin on or after October 1, 2017 may increase the cost of each project. The 
additional cost to each entity cannot be detennined at this time and will depend on the price differential of the "global­
wanning potential" insulation and the insulation that would be used absent this requirement.
