SUMMARY  16 
This bill extends from 12 months to 18 months the period of time within which an 17 
application for a refund of taxes paid on internal combustion engine fuel or on special 18 
fuel may be made by a person who purchases and uses the fuel for any use other than the 19 
operation of a registered motor vehicle on the highways of the State and has paid the tax 20 
imposed on that fuel for highway use.  21
