17 SUMMARY 
18 This amendment strikes the restriction that a temporary sign may be placed within the 
19 public right-of-way only during the time period from May 1st to November 15th. It 
20 provides that a temporary sign may not be placed within the public right-of-way within 
21 30 feet, instead of 200 feet as proposed in the bill, of another temporary sign bearing the 
22 same or substantially the same message.
