SUMMARY  27 
This bill provides a definition of "liquor inspector" and provides that the Director of 28 
the Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations within the Department of 29 
Administrative and Financial Services may re quire liquor inspectors to receive law 30 
enforcement training as a condition of employment.  The bill also requires the director to 31 
ensure that no less than 1/3 of the liquor inspector positions under the bureau include a 32 
requirement that the liquor inspecto r must complete law enforcement training.  33
