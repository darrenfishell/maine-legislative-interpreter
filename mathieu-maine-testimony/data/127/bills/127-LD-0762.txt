SUMMARY  36 
This bill authorizes the Penobscot Nation to use electronic beano terminal s for the 37 
operation of high -stakes electronic beano.  It specifies that an electronic beano terminal is 38 
not a slot machine or an illegal gambling machine.  An electronic beano terminal plays a 39 
game of chance resembling a beano game using a draw of no more than 75 letters and 40 
numbers that correspond to the game.  The element of chance is determined by a central 41

 computer server system that is subject to testing by an independent testing company and 1 
to which the Chief of the State Police is provided access via  the Internet for the purpose 2 
of conducting audits and monitoring play.  An electronic beano terminal is not permitted 3 
to dispense cash or prizes.  Instead, prizes for high -stakes electronic beano are awarded 4 
by voucher.  The bill also clarifies that, unli ke traditional beano, beano conducted on an 5 
electronic beano terminal is not required to be a group game.  6
