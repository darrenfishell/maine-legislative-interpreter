The bill requires the Board of Environmental Protection within the Department of Environmental Protection to have 
rules allowing people to retrofit single-walled underground oil storage tanks and to require site assessments when a 
tank is retrofitted in a certain manner. The bill includes a one-time General Fund appropriation of $2,500 in fiscal year 
2015-16 to fund these rulemaking costs.
