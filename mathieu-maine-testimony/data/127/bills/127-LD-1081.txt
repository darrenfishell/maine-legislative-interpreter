This bill requires that the notification to a parent of a child with a disability informing 21 
the parent of the parent's right to be a member of the child's individualized education 22 
program team must include notice that the parent has 14 days to object to any proposal by 23 
a school administrative unit for the placement of the child and that the parent or school 24 
administrative unit may request alternative dispute  resolution.  25
