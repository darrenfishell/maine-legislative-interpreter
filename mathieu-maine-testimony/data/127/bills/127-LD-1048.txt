SUMMARY  3 
This emergency bill authorizes the board of directors of School Administrative 4 
District No. 27 to transfer ownership of the St. Francis Ele mentary School building and 5 
its site to the Town of St. Francis, subject to the approval of the voters of the Town of St. 6 
Francis.  The bill authorizes the municipal officers of the Town of St. Francis to lease a 7 
portion of the St. Francis Elementary Schoo l building and its site back to School 8 
Administrative District No. 27 for educational purposes, and, subject to applicable law, 9 
the town’s municipal officers are authorized to renovate, use, lease, sell, mortgage or 10 
otherwise convey the property, subject t o any leasehold rights of School Administrative 11 
District No. 27 for educational purposes.  The bill also provides that, as long as School 12 
Administrative District No. 27 leases any portion of the property, the other uses of the 13 
property must be compatible w ith use of the property for educational purposes as 14 
reasonably determined by the district’s board of directors.  15
