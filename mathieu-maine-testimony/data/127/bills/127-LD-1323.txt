SUMMARY  31 
This bill allows municipalities and counties to create corporate entities with bonding 32 
authority to issue bonds for the purpose of funding construction or providing loans for the 33 
construction of broadband infrastructure to serve the municipali ty or county and ensure 34 
comprehensive broadband coverage within the municipality or county. The bill further 35 
clarifies that the expansion of broadband using optical fiber is an authorized expense 36 
under the state universal service fund administered by the P ublic Utilities Commission.  37 
Finally, this bill amends the State's goals for broadband policy by requiring that the 38
