The amendment extends the date that a license must have been issued for a landfill from September 1, 1989 to January 
10, 1990 in order for the Department of Environmental Protection (DEP) to pay 75% of closure costs for that landfill. 
DEP estimates that at least one additional landfill would become eligible with the extension. DEP will expend an 
estimated $365,600 for these closure costs. This amendment includes Other Special Revenue Funds allocations of 
$91,400 in each of fiscal years 2015-16 and 2016-17 for landfill closure costs. All costs to the DEP will be funded 
from the Maine Solid Waste Management Fund within DEP. The fund has sufficient resources to absorb these costs.
