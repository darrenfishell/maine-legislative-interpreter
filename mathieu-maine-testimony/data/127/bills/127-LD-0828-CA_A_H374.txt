The Maine Land Use Planning Commission may, by rule adopted in accordance with

1. Transition process for remoyal. The Maine Land Use Planning Commission

A. The specified place is a township, plantation, municipality or portion thereof that 
has been identified pursuant to section 3451, subsection 3, paragraph B; and

Maine Land Use Planning Commission or a form otherwise determined to be 
sufficient for the purposes of this subsection by the Maine Land Use Planning

SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, replaces the bill and 
amends the laws governing expedited permitting of wind energy development in the 
Maine Revised Statutes, Title 35-A, chapter 34-A, as follows: 
1. It amends existing and adds new definitions; and 
2. It provides the Maine Land Use Planning Commission with the authority to 
remove, by rule, a specified place within the unorganized and deorganized areas from the 
expedited pennitting area. It provides that the Maine Land Use Planning Commission is 
not required to provide notice to the Legislature when it adopts a rule to remove a 
specified place from the expedited pennitting area. 
The amendment also provides corresponding cross-references in Title 12 and in the 
public law that enacted Title 35-A, chapter 34-A to the new removal process.
