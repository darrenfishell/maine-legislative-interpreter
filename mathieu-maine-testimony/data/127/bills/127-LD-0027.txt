Sec. 2.  36 MRSA §5126- A is enacted to read: 4 
§5126-A.  Personal exemptions; deductions 5 
1.  General.   For income tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2013, a resident 6 
individual is allowed a deduction equal to the total amount of deductions allowed for 7 
personal exemptions in accordance with the Code, Section 151. 8 
2.  Individuals 67 years of age or older.   For tax years beginning on or after January 9 
1, 2015, a resident individual who has reached 67 years of age by the beginning of the tax 10 
year is allowed a deduction equal to the amount of any otherwise taxable income received 11 
by that individual during the tax year.  This deduction does not apply to income received 12 
jointly with a person who has not reached 67 years of age at the beginning of the tax year. 13

B.  The dollar amount of each withholding allowance in this State must be equivalent 16

the individual is a resident or a nonresident. 18 
SUMMARY 19 
This bill exempts from the state income tax income of residents who are 67 years of 20 
age or older unless the income is received jointly with a person who has not reached 67 21 
years of age. 22
