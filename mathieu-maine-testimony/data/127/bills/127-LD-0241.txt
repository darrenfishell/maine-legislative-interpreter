SUMMARY  13 
This resolve directs the Departmen t of Environmental Protection to resubmit for 14 
legislative review Rule Chapter 200:  Metallic Mineral Exploration, Advanced 15 
Exploration and Mining, a major substantive rule of the Department of Environmental 16 
Protection previously provisionally adopted and s ubmitted to the 126th Legislature for 17 
review.  During the Second Regular Session of the 126th Legislature, the department 18 
submitted its provisionally adopted mining rule for legislative review, but the Legislature 19 
did not enact into law legislation authori zing final adoption of the rule.  This resolve 20 
directs that the Legislature review the rule in accordance with the Maine Administrative 21 
Procedure Act, but stipulates that the rule may not be finally adopted by the department 22 
unless legislation submitted to  the 127th Legislature authorizing final adoption becomes 23 
law. 24
