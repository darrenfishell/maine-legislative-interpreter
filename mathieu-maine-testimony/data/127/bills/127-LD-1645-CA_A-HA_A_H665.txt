Fiscal Note 
Current biennium cost increase -General Fund 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This amendment delays the date for the Department of Health and Human Services to increase wages for selected 
positions at state mental health facilities from the pay period closest to July 1, 2016 to the pay period closest to 
August 1, 2016. The amendment is estimated to decrease costs to the General Fund Salary Plan by $78,698 in fiscal 
year 2016-17.
