Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill establishes the Maine State Police Drug Interdiction Unit and includes a General Fund appropriation to the 
Department of Public Safety of $449,040 and a Highway Fund allocation of$245,608 in fiscal year 2016-17 for 3 
State Police Troopers and one State Police Sergeant, as well as related costs, to run the unit.
