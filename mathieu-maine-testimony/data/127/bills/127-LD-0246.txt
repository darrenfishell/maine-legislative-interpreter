ยง2120.  Handling mobile telephone while operating motor vehicle; prohibition  3 
1.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 4 
following terms have the following meanings.  5 
A.  "Mobile telephone" means a device used to access wireless telephone service.  6 
B.  "Handling" means manipulating, talking into or otherwise physically interacting 7 
with a mobile telephone.  8 
2.  Prohibition.  A person may not operate a motor vehicle while handling a mobile 9 
telephone.  10 
3.  Penalty.  The following penalties apply to violations of this section.  11 
A.  A person who violates this section commits a traffic infraction for which a fine of 12 
not less than $50 may be adjudged.  13 
B.  A person who violates this section after having been previously adjudicated as 14 
violating this section commits a traffic infraction for which a fine of not less than 15 
$250 may be adjudged.  16 
4.  Exceptions.  This section does not apply to the following persons operating a 17 
motor vehicle within the scope of their employment:  18 
A.  Drivers of authorized emergency vehicles, as defined in section 2054, subsection 19 
1, paragraph B;  20 
B.  Physicians;  21 
C.  Holders of commercial driver's licen ses; and  22 
D.  Municipal public works personnel, Maine Turnpike Authority personnel and state 23 
transportation personnel, including all employees and contractors of the department.  24 
SUMMARY  25 
This bill prohibits operating a motor vehicle while handling a mobile t elephone.  The 26 
bill allows certain people to handle mobile telephones while driving within the scope of 27 
their employment, including drivers of authorized emergency vehicles, holders of 28 
commercial driver's licenses, physicians, municipal public works person nel, Maine 29 
Turnpike Authority personnel and state transportation personnel, including all employees 30 
and contractors of the Department of Transportation.  The bill makes the offense a traffic 31 
infraction.  32
