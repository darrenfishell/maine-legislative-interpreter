SUMMARY  26 
This bill p rovides for the upward adjustment of salary schedules in fiscal year 2015 - 27 
16 by 12% to 18% for certain law enforcement positions in the Department of Public 28 
Safety, the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife and the Department of Marine 29 
Resources begi nning with the pay period closest to May 1, 2016. The bill provides for a 30 
similar salary schedule adjustment for certain law enforcement confidential employees in 31 
those departments and allows the Governor to make similar salary schedule adjustments 32 
to uncl assified law enforcement positions in those departments that are subject to the 33 
Governor's adjustment or approval.  The bill authorizes use of the Salary Plan program to 34 
fund the adjustments and provides that certain law enforcement positions supported fro m 35 
other funds must be funded whenever possible from those other sources.  36
