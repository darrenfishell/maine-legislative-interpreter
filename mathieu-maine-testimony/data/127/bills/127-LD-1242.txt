SUMMARY  26 
This bill directs an insurance carrier that provides insurance in th e State to take 27 
certain measures to protect the data and identity of a person insured by the carrier on or 28 
after January 1, 2004, including encrypting all medical and personal data of the insured, 29 
providing medical identify theft protection and a guarantee  of recompense for 10 years 30 
following the breach, aligning all policies with a national medical fraud alliance approved 31 
by the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation, Bureau of Insurance and 32 
requiring all carriers to develop and implement a po licy approved by the bureau that 33 
ensures strict adherence to the compliance rules under the federal Health Insurance 34 
Portability and Accountability Act of 1996.  35
