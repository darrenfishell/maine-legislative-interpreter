SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the Joint Standing Committee on Judiciary. 
It replaces the bill to eliminate references to United Nations Agenda 21 and any 
international law or ancillary plan of action that contravenes the United States 
Constitution or the Constitution of Maine, but still focuses on the protection of private 
property rights. The amendment requires the State and political subdivisions to identify 
the effect of planning and zoning policies on private propeliy rights. It requires the State 
and political subdivisions to ensure paliicipation of private property owners in the process 
of developing planning and zoning policies. It provides that the State and political 
subdivisions may provide an estimate of the effect ofplamling and zoning policies on the 
fair market value of private propeliy.
