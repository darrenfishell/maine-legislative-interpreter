SUMMARY  20 
This bill amends the Maine Criminal Code by striking language that defines as 21 
trafficking or furnishing the possession of a certain amount of heroin and all permissible 22 
inferences based on possession of schedule W or X d rugs.  This bill also downgrades 23 
felony -level trafficking, furnishing, possession and hypodermic needles offenses.  24
