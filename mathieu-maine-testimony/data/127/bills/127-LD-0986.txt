SUMMARY  15 
This bill allows a small distillery that produces less than 25,000 gallons of spirits per 16 
year to fulfill the provision of law requiring the distillery to sell its product to the State by 17 
accounting for spirits it sells or provides as samples on the premises of the distillery 18 
without being required to transport those spirits from the distillery by filing a monthly 19 
accounting with the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of 20 
Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations of the spirits sold or provided on the 21 
premises for that month and paying the difference between the price at which the 22 
distillery sells its spirits to the State and the price at which the distillery purchases back 23 
its own spirits from the State and any associated fees and taxes.  24
