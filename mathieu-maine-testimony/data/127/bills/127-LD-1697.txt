SUMMARY  7 
This bill is submitted by the Joint Standing Committee on Criminal Justice and Public 8 
Safety p ursuant to Joint Order 2016, Senate Paper 695.  It is the majority report of the 9 
committee.  This bill establishes for calendar year 2017 a minimum tax assessment for 10 
Oxford County for correctional services of $1,228,757 and a maximum tax assessment of 11 
$2,050,000.  This bill addresses a critical need for funding for the Oxford County jail, 12 
which can be satisfied through county taxes if the base assessment for correctional 13 
services is increased.  14
