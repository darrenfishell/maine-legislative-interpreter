SUMMARY  6 
This bill authorizes a regional school unit to include an article in the warrant for its 7 
annual budget meeti ng providing that, in the event that the regional school unit receives 8 
more state education subsidy than the amount included in its budget, the regional school 9 
unit board is authorized to increase expenditures for school purposes in cost center 10 
categories approved by the regional school unit board, without a special budget meeting 11 
and budget validation referendum.  12
