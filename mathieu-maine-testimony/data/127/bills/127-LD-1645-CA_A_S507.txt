9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A " to S.P. 670, L.D. 1645, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Address Employee Recruitment and Retention Issues at State Mental Health Institutions" 
11 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
12 emergency clause and inserting the following: 
13 'Sec. 1. Department to increase wages at state mental health institutions. 
14 Notwithstanding any other provision of law, effective at the beginning of the first pay 
15 period commencing on or after July 1, 2016, the Department of Health and Human 
16 Services shall increase wages for select personnel as specified in this section at the state 
17 mental health institutions. Wages must be increased by $2 per hour for all Acuity 
18 Specialist positions, Licensed Practical Nurse positions, Mental Health Worker I 
19 positions, Mental Health Worker II positions and Mental Health Worker III positions. 
20 Wages must be increased by $4 per hour for all Nurse I positions, Hospital Nurse II 
21 positions, Hospital Nurse III positions, Hospital Nurse IV positions, Psychologist III 
22 positions and Psychologist IV positions. 
23 Sec. 2. Transfer from Salary Plan program and special account funding. 
24 The funds in the Salary Plan program, General Fund account within the Department of 
25 Administrative and Financial Services may be used as needed in allotment by financial 
26 order upon the recommendation of the State Budget Officer and approval of the Governor 
27 to be used for the economic items contained in section 1 in fiscal year 2016-17. Positions 
28 supported from sources other than the General Fund must be funded from those other 
29 sources. Transfers from the Salary Plan program pursuant to this section may not exceed 
30 $944,379 in fiscal year 2016-17.'

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill authorizes the Department of Health and Human Services to increase wages for selected positions at state 
mental health facilities beginning with the pay period closest to July 1, 2016. It provides for the funding of the 
General Fund costs of the bill from the General Fund Salary Plan program within the Department of Administrative 
and Financial Services. The cost to the General Fund Salary Plan is estimated to be $944,3 79 in fiscal year 2016-17.
