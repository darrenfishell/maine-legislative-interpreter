SUMMARY  15 
This bill changes the laws governing the Office of the Governor, Industry Partnership 16 
Assistance Collaborative's grant program. It changes eligibility requirements for the grant 17 
program by reducing the grant program's required private sector matching funding from 18 
50% to 25% and allowing the collaborative discretion to exempt businesses with fewer 19 
than 25 employees from the  matching funding requirement.  20
