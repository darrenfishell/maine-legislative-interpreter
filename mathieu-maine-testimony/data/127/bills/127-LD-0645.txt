SUMMARY  8 
In order to promote partnerships, including interlocal agreements under the Maine 9 
Revised Statutes, Title 30 -A, chapter 115, among municipalities located on state and state 10 
aid highways on peninsulas and to address the unique transportation needs of 11 
municipalities located on peninsulas with one road in and out, this bill requires the 12 
Department of Transportation to assign a higher priority classification for a state or state 13 
aid highway located on a peninsula if the relevant municipality or group of municipalities 14 
develops a 10 -year work plan that describes and addresses local and regional 15 
transportation infrastructure needs and considers the regional effect of the capital 16 
improvement of the state or state aid highway.  17
