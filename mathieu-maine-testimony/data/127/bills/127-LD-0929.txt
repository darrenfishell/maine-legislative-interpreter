SUMM ARY  35 
This bill amends the Uniform Unclaimed Property Act to establish a special 36 
procedure for the escheat of unclaimed United States savings bonds to the State.  37 
An unclaimed United States savings bond is presumed abandoned 3 years after its 38 
date of final ma turity.  A United States savings bond is presumed abandoned in this State 39 
if the last known address of the owner of the United States savings bond is in this State 40 
and the United States savings bond has remained unclaimed for 3 years after its date of 41 
final maturity.  42
