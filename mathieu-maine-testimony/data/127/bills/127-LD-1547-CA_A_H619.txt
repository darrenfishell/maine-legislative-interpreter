SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, replaces the bill, 
which establishes a fund for bulk purchases of naloxone hydrochloride for use by first 
responders. 
This amendment directs the Maine Board of Pharmacy to establish by rule procedures 
and standards for authorizing pharmacists to dispense naloxone hydrochloride. The rules 
must establish adequate training requirements and protocols for dispensing naloxone 
hydrochloride by prescription drug order or standing order or pursuant to a collaborative 
practice agreement. 
This amendment clarifies current law to allow first responders to obtain the naloxone 
hydrochloride that they are authorized to administer.
