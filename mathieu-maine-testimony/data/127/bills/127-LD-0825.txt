 SUMMARY  1 
This bill  allows a consumer -owned transmission and distribution utility to apply for 2 
funding from the Efficiency Maine Trust to support the development of a distributed 3 
generation facility or combined heat and power facility as long as the facility will reduce 4 
electricity consumption or reduce greenhouse gas emissions and lower energy costs, the 5 
facility will be owned and operated by an entity other than the consumer -owned 6 
transmission and distribution utility, the facility will meet certain electrical efficiency 7 
standards and the owner of the facility provides performance guarantees to insulate 8 
ratepayers from any risks associated with the construction of the facility.  9
