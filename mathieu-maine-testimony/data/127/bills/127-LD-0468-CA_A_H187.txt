COMMITTEE AMENDMENT 
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 307, L.D. 468 
SUMMARY 
2 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, amends the bill as 
3 follows: 
4 1. It changes the definition of "compostable" to remove the phrase "at a rate 
5 consistent with other materials that undergo degradation by biological processes"; 
6 2. It changes the definition of "disposable food service container" to provide a cross-
7 reference for the term "prepared food" and to clarify that "disposable food service 
8 container" does not include items composed entirely of paper or aluminum and does not 
9 include polystyrene foam coolers and ice chests that are intended for reuse or for the 
10 shipping or transportation of prepared food; and 
11 3. It provides that the prohibition in the bill does not apply to the use of disposable 
12 food service containers necessary for compliance with applicable state or federal food 
13 safety laws, rules or regulations.
