SUMMARY  16 
This bill reduces taxes for retirees by phasing out over 4 years the income tax on 17 
retirement benefit plan and individual retirement account benefits.  The bill also phases in 18 
over 4 years complete conformity of the Maine estate tax with the exclusion amount 19 
under the federal estate tax.  20
