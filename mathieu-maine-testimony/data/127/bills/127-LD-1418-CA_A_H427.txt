SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the committee. The amendment: 
1. Delays the effective date for the changes approved by the voters at the November 
2015 election until January 2018. The changes apply to the statewide election in

The Secretary of State's budget includes sufficient funds to accommodate one ballot of average length for the general 
election in November. If the number or size of the referendum questions requires production and delivery of a second

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
If approved by the voters, this Constitutional amendment would replace the position of Secretary of State with the 
position of Lieutenant Governor effective in January 2019. It is unknown at this time whether the salaries of the two 
positions would be comparable or whether additional costs might be incurred.
