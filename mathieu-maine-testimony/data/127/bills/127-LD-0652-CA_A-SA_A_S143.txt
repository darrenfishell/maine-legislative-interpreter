concealed handgun without a permit to sign an acknowledgment that the person was 
provided a firearm safety brochure upon purchase of a handgun. A firearm safety 
brochure is currently required to be provided with every firearm sold. The amendment 
requires the firearm dealer to retain the acknowledgment for one year. It also requires the
