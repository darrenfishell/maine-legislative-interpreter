SUMMARY  26 
This bill defines "overnight occupancy" in the laws governing lodging places and 27 
requires renters who provide overnight occupancy to the public to be licensed by the 28 
State.  It also provides th at a property rented as a vacation rental must be rented for a 29 
minimum of 7 days.  30
