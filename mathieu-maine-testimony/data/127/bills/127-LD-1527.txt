SUMMARY  28 
This bill provides funds to give adult family care homes, residential care facilities and 29 
Appendix C private nonmedical institutions a 4% cost -of-living rate in crease in funding 30 
in the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2016.  Annual cost -of-living adjustments are to be 31 
provided by rule for each fiscal year thereafter in accordance with the United States 32 
Department of Labor, Bureau of Labor Statistics Consumer Price I ndex medical care 33 
services index.  34
