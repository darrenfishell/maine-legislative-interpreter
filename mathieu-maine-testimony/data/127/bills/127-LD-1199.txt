SUMMARY  35 
This bill changes the laws governing the Department of Labor, Bureau of 36 
Rehabilitation Services by:  37 
 
 Page 4 - 127LR0449(01) -1 
 1.  Repealing the laws governing the blind -made products  program, a program that no 1 
longer exists;  2 
2.  Changing references to the Federal Rehabilitation Act to the federal Rehabilitation 3 
Act of 1973;  4 
3.  Specifying the Department of Labor as the designated state agency to provide 5 
rehabilitation services under t he federal Rehabilitation Act of 1973 and naming the 6 
Division of Vocational Rehabilitation and the Division for the Blind and Visually 7 
Impaired as the designated state units;  8 
4. Defining "gainful employment" as it pertains to the different standards within  the 9 
Division of Vocational Rehabilitation and the Division for the Blind and Visually 10 
Impaired;  11 
5. Changing the phrase "handicapped or disadvantaged individual" to "person with a 12 
disability"; and  13 
6. Reducing the number of members on the Commission for the  Deaf, Hard of 14 
Hearing and Late Deafened from 24 members and 3 at large members to up to 23 15 
members to reflect the bylaws voted on by the commission. It also provides that members 16 
serve 3 -year terms and may serve multiple terms without limit.  17
