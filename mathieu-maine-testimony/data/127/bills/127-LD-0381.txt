SUMMARY  26 
This bill changes the annual inspection requirement for motor vehicles, except for 27 
commercial vehicle s, trailers and semitrailers, to a biennial inspection requirement.  The 28 
bill provides that a new motor vehicle that has had an inspection is not required to have 29 
another inspection until 4 years from the last day of the month in which it was initially 30 
registered.  The bill provides that a new motor vehicle does not include a commercial 31 
motor vehicle, trailer or semitrailer.  The bill also changes the annual enhanced inspection 32 
requirement for vehicles registered in Cumberland County in a similar manner.  33
