SUMMARY  31 
This bill establishes the Affordable Heating from Maine's Forests Fund, administered 32 
by the Efficiency Maine Trust.  The fund receives revenue from the sale of timber and 33 
other things of value from Maine's public reserved lands.  In 2015 a min imum of 34 
$1,000,000 must be transferred from the Public Reserved Lands Management Fund to the 35 
Affordable Heating from Maine's Forests Fund.  Subsequent transfers are based on 36 
revenue accrued over the 2012 revenue amounts of the Public Reserved Lands 37 
Managem ent Fund, subject to some limitations. The Efficiency Maine Trust is required to 38 
use the funding for measures that reduce residential heating costs and must prioritize rural 39 
and low -income residences.  40
