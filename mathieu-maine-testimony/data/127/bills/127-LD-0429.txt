SUMMARY  13 
This bill changes the apportionment of the percentage of the Maine Economic 14 
Improvement Fund that goes to the University of Maine at Augusta, the University of 15 
Maine at Farmington, the  University of Maine at Fort Kent, the University of Maine at 16 
Machias, the University of Maine at Presque Isle and the Maine Maritime Academy to 17 
support applied research and development by removing the apportionment to all 18 
institutions of a minimum of 3% o f the fund and instead requiring a minimum of 2% of 19 
the fund to be disbursed to the University of Maine at Machias to support applied marine 20 
research and development at that university's marine field station and a minimum of 1% 21 
to be apportioned among the remainder of the institutions.  22
