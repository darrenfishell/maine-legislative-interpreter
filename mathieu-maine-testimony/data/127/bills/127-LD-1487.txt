SUMMARY  30 
This bill amends the protection from abuse laws and the laws concerning 31 
unauthorized dissemination of certain private images.  The bill pro vides a process for 32 
sealing certain private images and any written information describing and directly 33 
pertaining to the images that are contained in court records.  The bill adds unauthorized 34 
dissemination of certain private images to the definition of "a buse" for the purposes of the 35 
protection from abuse laws.  The bill adds to the protection from abuse laws remedies 36 
pertinent to the unauthorized dissemination of certain private images.  37
