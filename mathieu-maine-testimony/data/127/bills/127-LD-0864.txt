 SUMMARY  1 
This bill establishes the requirement that courts incorporate a parenting plan into any 2 
order that addresses parental rights and responsibilities.  Each party must submit a 3 
temporary or permanent parenting pl an prior to mediation, or the parties may submit a 4 
temporary or permanent parenting plan jointly.  The court shall incorporate a temporary 5 
parenting plan into any order issued during the parental rights and responsibilities 6 
litigation, and that parenting p lan applies until a final order is issued.  Each party must 7 
submit a permanent parenting plan prior to the pretrial conference or the judicial 8 
settlement conference, or the parties may submit a permanent parenting plan jointly.  The 9 
court shall incorporate  a permanent parenting plan into the final judgment.  10 
The parenting plan must address each parent's specific responsibilities for the child, 11 
as well as establish both a decision -making mechanism and dispute resolution method for 12 
issues not specifically cove red or about which the parents cannot agree.  13
