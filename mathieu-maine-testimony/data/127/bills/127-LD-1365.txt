 SUMMARY  1 
This bill requir es licensed child care facilities, certified family child care providers 2 
and licensed nursery schools to report incidents that result or could result in serious harm 3 
to the physical or mental health, safety or well -being of a child being served by these 4 
entities. Incidents must be reported to the Department of Health and Human Services, 5 
Division of Licensing and Regulatory Services.  The bill requires written notification by 6 
the next business day after the incident occurred.  7
