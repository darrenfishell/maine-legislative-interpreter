SUMMARY  16 
This bill addresses the effect of the Maine Supreme Judicial Court decision in 17 
Francis Small Heritage Trust, Inc. v. Town of Limington et al.,  2014 ME 102, by 18 
specifying that holding land primarily for conservation or public  access purposes is not a 19 
benevolent and charitable purpose, which is a condition necessary to obtaining an 20 
exemption from property tax. The change applies to property tax years beginning on or 21 
after April 1, 2017.  22
