SUMMARY  17 
This bill requires the Office of the State Fire Marshal to inspect and approve all 18 
sprinkler system plans for townhouses constructed after January 1, 2016.  The bill 19 
establishes a definition for the term "townhouse" that mirrors the definition used in the 20 
International Residential Code.  21
