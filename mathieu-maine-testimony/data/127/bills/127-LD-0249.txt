SUMMARY  5 
This bill establishes the Home Weatherization and Repair for Seniors Program in the 6 
Department of Economic and Community Develo pment to assist low -income seniors in 7 
remaining in their homes.  It also establishes the Home Weatherization and Repair for 8 
Seniors Fund and appropriates money to the fund.  The department is required to disburse 9 
money from the fund to a statewide network that provides weatherization and home 10 
repair services, including but not limited to repairs to meet the standards of the federal 11 
Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990, to replace substandard fixtures and hardware 12 
and to reduce ongoing maintenance and hea ting costs, to low -income residents of the 13 
State.  14
