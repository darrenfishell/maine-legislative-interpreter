SUMMARY 
This amendment creates a pennitting process for transportation network companies to 
provide transportation services, establishes requirements for drivers providing 
transportation services on behalf of transportation network companies, establishes 
minimum motor vehicle liability insurance requirements for transportation network 
companies and drivers and prohibits municipalities from regulating transportation 
network companies and drivers. 
This amendment adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause to the bill.
