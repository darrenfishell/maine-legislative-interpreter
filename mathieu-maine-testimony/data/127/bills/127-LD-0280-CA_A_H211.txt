COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 198, L.D. 280, BiJ1, "An Act To 
Exempt Military Pensions and Survivor Benefits from Maine Income Tax" 
Amend the bill in section 2 in paragraph M-2 in the first line (page 2, line 2 in L.D.) 
by inserting after the following; "January 1, 2015," the following; 'to the extent included 
in federal adjusted gross income,' . 
SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies that the exemption for military benefits applies to those 
benefits included in federal adjusted gross income.
