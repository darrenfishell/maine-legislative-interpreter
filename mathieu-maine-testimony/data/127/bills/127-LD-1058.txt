SUMMARY  7 
This bill establishes in the med ical use of marijuana laws the registration of testing 8 
laboratories that perform testing on marijuana samples for the cannabinoid profile and for 9 
possible contaminants.  Registered testing laboratories must be located in the State, and 10 
the laboratories and  their principal officers, board members, agents and employees are 11 
given the same immunity from prosecution, search, seizure and penalty currently granted 12 
to registered dispensaries.  The Department of Health and Human Services is required to 13 
establish an application form and fees for registered testing laboratories by December 15, 14 
2015.  15
