Sec. 2.  17-A MRSA §555, sub -§2, as repealed and replaced by PL 2005, c. 431, 22 
§1, is repealed and the following enacted in its place:  23 
2.  Definitions.   As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 24 
following terms have the following meanings.  25 
A.  "Endanger" includes a failure to act only when the defendant has a legal duty to 26 
protect the health, safety or mental welfare of the depend ent person. For purposes of 27 
this paragraph, a legal duty may be inferred if the defendant has assumed 28 
responsibility in whole or in part for the care of the dependent person.  29 
B.  "Dependent person" means a person, regardless of where that person resides, w ho 30 
is wholly or partially dependent upon one or more other persons for care or support 31 
because the person suffers from a significant limitation in mobility, vision, hearing or 32 
mental functioning or is unable to perform self -care because of advanced age or 33 
physical or mental disease, disorder or defect.  34

 SUMMARY  1 
This bill strengthens the protections for senior citizens in the State.  2 
1.  It amends the purposes of the criminal sentencing provisions to specifically 3 
reference the factor of a victim's ability to self -protect due to age.  4 
2.  It defines "dependent person."  5 
3.  It specifically includes financial exploitation in the definition of "abuse."  6 
4.  It provides funds for one Detective position and one Assistant Atto rney General 7 
position.  8 
5.  It authorizes the Attorney General to accept funds to fund those positions.  9
