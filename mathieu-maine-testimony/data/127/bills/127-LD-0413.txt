SUMMARY  4 
This bill eliminates restrictions on the issuance of absentee ballots after the 3rd day 5 
before an election, removes deadlines for the return of an absentee ballot by a 3rd person 6 
and allows a voter to vote by absentee ballot in the presence of t he clerk until 8:00 p.m. 7 
on the day of any election.  8
