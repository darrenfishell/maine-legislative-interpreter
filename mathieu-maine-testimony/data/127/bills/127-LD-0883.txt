SUMMARY  18 
This bill requires cellular telephone manufacturers that include safety notifications in 19 
their owner's manuals to ensure that cellular telephone p ackaging includes those safety 20 
notifications or a label indicating where the safety notifications may be found in the 21 
owner's manual.  It prohibits retailers from selling at retail in this State cellular telephones 22 
of manufacturers that include safety noti fications in their owner's manuals but do not 23 
include on the product packaging the safety notifications or a label indicating where the 24 
safety notifications may be found.  It requires manufacturers of cellular telephones to 25 
provide the safety notifications  to retailers at no cost to the retailers.  It also prohibits 26 
retailers from selling at retail in this State a cellular telephone that does not bear a label 27 
warning that the device emits radiofrequency electromagnetic fields.  It also requires 28 
retailers to  provide an information bulletin to the purchaser of a cellular telephone 29 
informing the purchaser of potential health risks associated with the use of cellular 30 
telephones.  The bill provides that a violation of these provisions is a violation of the 31 
Maine Unfair Trade Practices Act.  32
