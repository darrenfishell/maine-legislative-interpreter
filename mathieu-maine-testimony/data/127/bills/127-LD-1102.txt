SUMMARY  13 
This resolve creates the Commission To Study the Needs, Opportunities and 14 
Efficiency Associated with the Production of Salmonid Sport Fish in Maine.  The 15 
commission is required to submit its report and any accompanying legislation to the Joint 16 
Standing Committee on Inland Fisheries and Wildlife by January 15, 2016.  17
