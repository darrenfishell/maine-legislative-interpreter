Committee: Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry 
Fiscal Note Required: Yes 
Fiscal Note 
Potential increase in grants -Other Special Revenue Funds 
The bill requires the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry (ACF) to establish the Maine Food 
Infrastructure Advisory Committee and requires ACF to award at least one grant of up to $30,000 from the 
Agricultural Development Grant Fund for conducting market feasibility studies and developing business plans for 
local food infrastructure. This bill will not impact the amount of funds allocated to the Agricultural Development 
Grant Fund and the fund has sufficient resources to absorb the potential costs from this legislation.
