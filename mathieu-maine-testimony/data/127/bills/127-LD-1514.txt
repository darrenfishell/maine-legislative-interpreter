 SUMMARY  1 
This bill amends the licensing and practice requirements for a dental hygiene 2 
therapist by:  3 
1.  Specifying that an applicant who has successfully completed a dental hygiene 4 
therapy education program that is accredited by eithe r the American Dental Association 5 
Commission on Dental Accreditation or a successor organization or the Board of Dental 6 
Examiners meets the requirements for having completed a dental hygiene therapy 7 
education program;  8 
2.  Specifying that a degree in dental  hygiene therapy above a Bachelor of Science 9 
degree is acceptable;  10 
3. Removing the requirements that a dental hygiene therapy education program be 11 
consistent with a specified model curriculum, be consistent with programs in other states 12 
and be approved by the board;  13 
4.  Removing the requirement that an applicant hold an associate degree in dental 14 
hygiene before entering a dental hygiene therapy education program;  15 
5.  Removing the requirement that a dental hygiene therapist be under the direct 16 
supervision of  a dentist; and  17 
6.  Allowing a person to be licensed as a dental hygiene therapist in this State if that 18 
person was graduated from a dental hygiene therapy education program, is licensed to 19 
practice as a dental hygiene therapist in another state and has en gaged in active clinical 20 
practice for at least 3 years prior to applying for a license in this State.  21
