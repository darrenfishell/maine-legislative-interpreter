SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill and allows a nonresident who owns 25 or more 
acres of land in Maine and leaves that property open to hunting and holds a valid hunting 
license to hunt on the Saturday preceding the first day of the open season on deer, 
otherwise known as residents-only deer hunting day. It also repeals this provision on 
September 15, 2018.
