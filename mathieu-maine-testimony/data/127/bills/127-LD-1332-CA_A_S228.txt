13 'A, "Collaborative workspace" means coworking, shared working environments that 
14 promote collaboration, interaction, socialization and coordination among tenants 
15 through the clustering of multiple businesses or individuals within the shared work 
16 environment. 
17 B. "Collaborative workspace business" means a corporation, partnership, limited 
18 liability corporation, professional corporation or other legal business entity 
19 reco gnized under the laws of the State engaging or proposing to engage in economic 
20 activity within the State. 
21 C. "Fund" means the Maine Coworking Development Fund established in this 
22 section. 
23 D. "Participant" means a sole proprietorship, business partnership or corporation 
24 operating a business for profit through which the owner accesses business 
25 development services in a collaborative workspace. 
26 E. "Tenant" means a sole proprietorship, business partnership or corporation 
27 operating a business for profit and leasing or otherwise occupying collaborative 
28 workspace from a collaborative workspace business. 
29 F. "Transformative development" means redevelopment on a scale and of a character 
30 capable of catalyzing significant follow-on private investment, leading over time to 
31 transformation of an entire downtown or urban neighborhood and consistent with 
32 local plans. Transformative development may involve major investment in new 
33 construction, rehabilitation and adaptive reuse or multiple smaller investments on a 
34 sustained basis.'

This bill includes General Fund appropriations of $250,000 per year in both fiscal year 2015-16 and fiscal year 2016-
17 to the Maine Coworking Development Program within the Department of Economic and Community Development 
to support collaborative workspace businesses. 
This bill includes base allocations of $500 in fiscal year 2015-16 and $500 in fiscal year 2016-17 to establish the 
Maine Coworking Development Fund as a dedicated account within the department to accept contributions and grants 
from public and private sources.
