SUMMARY  33 
This bill requires the Commissioner of Marine Resources to establish by rule a 34 
minimum amount of lobster or crab landings required of a Class I, Class II  or Class III 35 
lobster and crab fishing license holder in order for the license holder to purchase the 36 
maximum number of trap tags available.  If the license holder does not report the required 37 
amount of landings, the license holder is not eligible to purch ase more than 300 trap tags 38 
in any subsequent license year.  This bill also provides that a holder of a Class I, Class II 39 
or Class III lobster and crab fishing license who has not reported any lobster or crab 40
