SUMMARY  15 
This bill makes the following changes to the laws governing specific bonds for the 16 
Land for Maine's Future Board:  17 
1.  It authorizes the  reissuance of bonds authorized by the voters in November 2010 18 
for the Land for Maine's Future Board that have not yet been issued;  19 
2.  It requires that the Governor authorize the issuance of the specific bonds for the 20 
Land for Maine's Future Board that we re  ratified by the voters of the State in statewide 21 
elections held in November 2010 and November 2012, unless one of 5 specific conditions 22 
exists;  23 
3.  It requires the Treasurer of State to initiate borrowing in accordance with Public 24 
Law 2009, chapter 414 , Part E, section 6, as amended by Public Law 2009, chapter 645, 25 
Part J, section 3, and Public Law 2011, chapter 696, section 6, ratified by the voters of the 26 
State in statewide elections held in November 2010 and November 2012, respectively; 27 
and 28 
4.  It re quires the Commissioner of Administrative and Financial Services to 29 
effectuate the release to the Land for Maine's Future Board of funds resulting from the 30 
issuance of those bonds, to be expended for the purposes described in Public Law 2009, 31 
chapter 414, Part E, section 6, as amended by Public Law 2009, chapter 645, Part J, 32 
section 3, and Public Law 2011, chapter 696, section 6 and ratified by the voters of the 33 
State in statewide elections held in November 2010 and November 2012.  34
