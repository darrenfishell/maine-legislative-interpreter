SUMMARY  17 
This bill amends the requirements related to a qualified domestic relations order to 18 
allow the payment of retirement benefits to an alternate payee before the retirement of a 19 
member of the State Employee and Teacher Retirement Program and when the payee 20 
reaches the member's normal retirement age.  21
