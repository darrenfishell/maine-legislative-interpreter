SUMMARY  6 
Current law allows a municipality to grant a setback variance for single -family 7 
dwellings in cases of undue hardship, as long as the hardship is not the result of action 8 
taken by the applicant or a prior owner of the property.  This bill allows a set back 9 
variance based on hardship to be granted even if the hardship is the result of action taken 10 
by a prior owner.  11
