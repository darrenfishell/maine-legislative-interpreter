2.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 9 
following terms have the following meanings.  10

C.  "Eligible producer"  means a person located in a participating municipality who 15

F.  "Participating municipality" means the towns of Blue H ill, Brooksville, 22

G.  "Sale" means a transfer, through barter or otherwise.  24

SUMMARY  25 
This bill establishes the Local Food Producers and Processors to Consumers Pilot 26 
Program. The pilot program exempts local producers and processors in the towns of Blue 27 
Hill, Brooksvill e, Penobscot, Sedgwick and Brooklin, all of which have adopted local 28 
food self -governance ordinances, from all state licensure and inspection requirements 29 
with respect to the production and processing of local foods for sale directly to 30 
consumers.  The pil ot program is repealed in 2022.  Each year the Department of 31 
Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry is required to submit a report on the pilot program 32 
to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over agricultural 33 
matters includi ng any assessment of or comments about the pilot program provided by 34 
interested persons, including producers, processors and consumers participating in the 35 
pilot program.  Upon receipt of the report, the committee may report out a bill relating to 36 
the pilo t program.  37
