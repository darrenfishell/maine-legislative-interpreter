Sec. 15 -A.  Capital reserve fund.  The trust ees may establish a capital reserve 16 
fund by appropriating money or by authorizing the transfer of unencumbered surplus 17 
funds at the end of any fiscal year for the purposes of maintaining, rehabilitating, 18 
upgrading or replacing aging infrastructure.  19 
1.  The  annual appropriation for the purposes of the capital reserve fund may not 20 
exceed $500,000.  21 
2.  The maximum amount that may be kept in the capital reserve fund is $2,000,000.  22 
3.  When the trustees determine that a project relates to maintaining, rehabilita ting, 23 
upgrading or replacing aging infrastructure, they may order the withdrawal and 24 
expenditure of the necessary amount from the capital reserve fund to cover the 25 
expenditure of the project.  If funds are committed to cover an expenditure that will run 26 
beyond a given fiscal year, those funds will not be included in the $2,000,000 cap 27 
established under subsection 2.  28

223, subchapter 3 -A.  Any interest earned or capital gains realized accrue to and become 31 
part of the capital reserve fund.  32
