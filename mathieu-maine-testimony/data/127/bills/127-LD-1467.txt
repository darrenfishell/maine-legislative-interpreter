SUMMARY  24 
Under current law, on -premises retailers are required to report their liquor purchases 25 
from reselling agents to the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau 26 
of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations.  This bill instead requires reselling 27 
agents to report sales of spirits to on -premises retail licensees.  This bill further provides 28 
that sales of spirits to on -premises retail licensees reported to the bureau from reselling 29 
agents are not confidential except for the names of the reselling agents.  30
