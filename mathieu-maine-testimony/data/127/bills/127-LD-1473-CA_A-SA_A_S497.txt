This amendment requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its 
rules, as proposed in Committee Amendment "A," by August 1, 2016 and specifies that 
the reimbursement rate increase applies for the period July 1, 2016 to December 31, 
2017. This amendment also removes the General Fund appropriation to the Department 
of Health and Human Services of $568,471 in fiscal year 2016-17 as the appropriation is 
no longer required as a result of the increase in the federal fiscal year 2017 Federal 
Medical Assistance Percentage, or FMAP, to 64.38% from 62.67%. The Federal 
Expenditures Fund allocation also increases due to the FMAP change in fiscal year 2016-
17.
