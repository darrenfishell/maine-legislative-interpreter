SUMMARY  15 
This bill authorizes a person who is not prohibited from possessing a firearm to carry  16 
a concealed handgun without a permit.  This bill also authorizes a person to possess a 17 
loaded pistol or revolver while in a motor vehicle or a trailer or other vehicle being 18 
hauled by a motor vehicle.  19
