SUMMARY

Fisheries and Wildlife to use that revenue to educate the public on the management of

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill establishes the Species Management Education Fund within the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife 
(IFW) and raises certain hunting and trapping licenses by $1 and directs $1 from these licenses to go to this fund. The 
fund is to be used to educate the public on the management of game species. Other Special Revenue Funds revenue to 
this new fund will be an estimated $203,516 in fiscal year 2015-16 and $203,516 in every subsequent fiscal year. 
Additional costs to IFW to convene a stakeholders group to develop recommendations for a 5-year public outreach 
campaign on IFW's efforts to manage game species are expected to be minor and can be absorbed within existing 
budgeted resources.
