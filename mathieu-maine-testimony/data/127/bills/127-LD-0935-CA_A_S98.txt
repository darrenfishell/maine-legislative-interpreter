17 SUMMARY 
18 This amendment replaces the bill. It establishes a research manufacturer license for a 
19 state-sponsored postsecondary educational institution that offers course work or degree 
20 programs in food sciences or agricultural sciences. The license authorizes the 
21 manufacture of malt liquor. wine or spirits by a licensee for educational purposes and as 
22 part of an outreach extension program of the institution that provides food science and 
23 food processing consultation and educational services through application of the 
24 institution's research to promote business development.
