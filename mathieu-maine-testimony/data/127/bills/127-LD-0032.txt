 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows: 1 
Sec. 1.  23 MRSA §1912-C, sub-§5, ¶¶F and G, as enacted by PL 2013, c. 549, 2 
§3, are amended to read: 3 
F.  A transportation facility if signs for the facility significantly benefit the 4 
transportation system; and 5 
G.  A veterans, police or firefighters memorial that is located within 20 miles of an 6 
exit.; and 7 
Sec. 2.  23 MRSA §1912-C, sub-§5, ¶H is enacted to read: 8 
H.  A cultural destination or historic destination that is located within 10 miles of an 9 
exit if that cultural destination or historic destination had a sign prior to August 1, 10 
2014.  For the purposes of this paragraph, "historic destination" includes but is not 11 
limited to a destination listed on the National Register of Historic Places or listed as a 12 
national historic landmark and "cultural destination" includes but is not limited to a 13 
museum. 14 
SUMMARY 15 
This bill allows for directional signs on interstate highways in Maine for a destination 16 
that is within 10 miles of the highway if the destination is cultural or historic and had a 17 
sign on those roads prior to August 1, 2014. 18
