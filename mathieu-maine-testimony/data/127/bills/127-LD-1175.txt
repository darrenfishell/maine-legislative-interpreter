SUMMARY  12 
This bill:  13 
1.  Makes texting and handheld mobile telephone use while driving a traffic 14 
infraction for the purposes of Maine's commercial motor vehicle motor safety laws;  15 
2.  Makes a violation of federal regulations regarding rest breaks, logbook 16 
information and filing of drivers' records of duty status a traffic infraction for the 17 
purposes of Maine's commercial motor vehicle motor safety laws;  18 
3.  Requires vehicles operated in Maine to be equip ped with brake lights;  19 
4.  Restricts persons operating motor vehicles from being able to view a television, 20 
screen or other device capable of receiving or showing visual content.  For the purposes 21 
of the provision, "visual content" includes, but is not lim ited to, television broadcasts, 22 
recorded video and video streamed through electronic or other means.  "Visual content" 23 
does not include content displayed on a device that was installed for the effective or safe 24 
operation of a motor vehicle or on a device u sed for global positioning or navigation;  25 
5.  Repeals the provision of law prohibiting persons from riding in a trailer that is 26 
being towed; and  27 
6.  Prohibits persons from attaching or displaying on a vehicle registration plate a 28 
registration validation de vice issued for another vehicle.  29
