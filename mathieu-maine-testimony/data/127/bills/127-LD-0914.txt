SUMMARY  20 
This bill amends definitions under Maine's accountancy laws and makes conforming 21 
changes to the accountancy laws regarding attest services to reflect th e most recent 22 
version of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants and National 23 
Association of State Boards of Accountancy Uniform Accountancy Act.  24
