Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill expands the educational opportunity tax credit to Maine residents working on a vessel at sea and would result 
in a reduction in General Fund revenue of $65,360 in fiscal year 2015-16 and $98,040 in fiscal year 2016-17 and a 
reduction in Local Government Fund revenue of $3,440 in fiscal year 2015-16 and $5,160 in fiscal year 2016-17.
