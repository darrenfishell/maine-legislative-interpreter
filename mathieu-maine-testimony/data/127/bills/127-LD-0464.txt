SUMMARY  4 
This bill requires the Department of Education to include the so -called Next 5 
Generation Science Standards for kindergarten to grade 12 in the State's system of 6 
learning results and assessment and directs the Commissioner of Education  to amend 7 
Department of Education rules on or before December 31, 2015 in order to include the 8 
science standards as part of the State's system of learning results and assessment 9 
beginning with the 2016 -2017 school year.  10
