SUMMARY  12 
This bill permits an authorized employee of a financial institution or credit union 13 
instead of an attorney t o attend a foreclosure mediation on behalf of the financial 14 
institution or credit union, allows a defendant to affirmatively decline attending the 15 
mediation, grants authority to the mediator to determine the location of the mediation and 16 
clarifies that the  financial institution or credit union is required to appear only at a 17 
required mediation scheduled by the court.  18
