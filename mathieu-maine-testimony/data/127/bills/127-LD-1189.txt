SUMMARY  20 
This bill creates an open, nonpartisan petition and primary process for nomination of 21 
candidates for election to the positions of register of probate, district attorney and sheriff.  22 
If more than 2 candidates are qualified for an office thr ough the petition process, an open 23 
primary is held in which all voters, regardless of party affiliation, are eligible to vote.  24 
The 2 candidates getting the highest number of votes are then nominated to appear on the 25 
general election ballot.  If 2 or fewer  candidates are qualified through the petition process, 26 
no primary is held and those candidates appear on the general election ballot.  27
