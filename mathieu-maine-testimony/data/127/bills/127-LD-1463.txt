SUMMARY  12 
This bill provides that a denial of a disability retirement bene fit application to the 13 
Maine Public Employees Retirement System does not bar the filing and approval of a 14 
subsequent application for the same or different medical conditions if the applicant has 15 
returned to service with an employer covered under the Maine Public Employees 16 
Retirement System.  17 
It authorizes the Board of Trustees of the Maine Public Employees Retirement 18 
System, beginning January 5, 2017, to procure and offer long -term disability insurance to 19 
the retirement system's members and nonmembers who pa rticipate in the retirement 20 
system defined contribution plan.  Persons who elect this coverage or their employers are 21 
responsible for premiums and other 3rd -party costs.  22 
It directs the Maine Public Employees Retirement System to study how it would 23 
procure and offer long -term disability insurance and to report the results of its study to 24 
the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over appropriations 25 
and financial affairs, which is authorized to report out a bill to the First Regular Session 26 
of the 128th Legislature.  27
