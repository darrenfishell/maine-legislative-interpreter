If a statute provides that an entity or individual provide to the Governor names of 4 
candidates for  appointment to a position, but does not specify the number of names of 5 
candidates that must be provided, that statute is construed to mean that the entity or 6

SUMMARY  8 
This bill  clarifies the appointment process in those instances when an individual or 9 
entity is required by statute to provide the Governor names of candidates for appointment, 10 
but the number of candidates is not specified, to require that a list of at least 3 quali fied 11 
candidates be provided to the Governor.  12
