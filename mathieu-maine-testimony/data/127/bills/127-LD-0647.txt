SUMMARY  29 
This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to ann ually update 30 
MaineCare coverage for rehabilitative and community support services for children with 31 
cognitive impairments and functional limitations.  The bill also requires the department to 32 
amend MaineCare rules for those services, specifically amending the definitions of and 33 
coverage for specialized services and specialized services for children with cognitive 34 
impairments and functional limitations.  35
