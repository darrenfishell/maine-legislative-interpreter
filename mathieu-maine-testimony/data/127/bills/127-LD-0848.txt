SUMMARY  23 
This bill:  24 
1.  Imposes a mandatory $500 penalty for a violation of disch arging a firearm or 25 
crossbow within 100 yards of a building or residential dwelling;  26 
2.  Increases the minimum distance for discharging a firearm to 100 feet from the 27 
center of a paved road and sets a penalty of $500 for a violation; and  28 
3.  Requires that a firearm with a rifled barrel in or on a motor vehicle during deer 29 
hunting season be enclosed in a case.  30
