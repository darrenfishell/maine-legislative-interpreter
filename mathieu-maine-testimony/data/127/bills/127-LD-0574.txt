SUMMARY  18 
This bill allows the designee of a county sheriff appointed to the Maine Commission 19 
on Domestic and Sexual Abuse to serve on the com mission and replaces one at -large 20 
member with the executive director of a tribal coalition against sexual assault and 21 
domestic violence.  The current at -large member will serve until the expiration of that 22 
member's term.  23
