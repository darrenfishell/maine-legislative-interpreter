SUMMARY  26 
This bill requires an adult applicant for or an adult recipient of benefits under the 27 
Temporary Assistance for  Needy Families, or TANF, program to be screened and 28 
possibly physically tested for the unlawful use of drugs.  The outcome of testing may 29 
result in that person's being denied or losing TANF benefits if subsequent substance use 30 
disorder treatment is not su ccessfully completed.  The bill also repeals provisions that 31 
prohibit the State's denying food assistance and TANF assistance to a person who has 32 
been convicted of a drug -related felony.  33
