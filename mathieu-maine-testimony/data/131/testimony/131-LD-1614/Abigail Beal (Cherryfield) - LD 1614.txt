 
LD 1614  
An Act to Require an Ultrasound and Certain Counseling 
Before an Abortion  
Presented by Rep. Abigail Griffin  
 
Senator Carney, Representative Moonen, and members of the Maine Committee on Judiciary,  
My name is Abigail Beal of Cherryfield, Maine.  Thank you for the opportunity to testify.  
I urge you to SUPPORT LD 1614,  An Act to Require an Ultrasound and Certain Counseling 
Before an Abortion.  
This bill would provide women with more information as they make such a vital and life -
changing choice.  
If you want what’s best for women and their babies, please vote “Ought to Pass” on this bill.  
Thank you for your time.
