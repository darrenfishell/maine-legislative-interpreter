LD 992, An Act to Create a Fund for the Abatement and Disposal of Polychlorinated Biphenyls 
in School Construction Projects, directs the Department of Education to establish a fund to 
defray the costs for the abatement and disposal of polychlorinated biphenyls directly related toa 
Department approved school construction project. 
The Department has worked directly with the stakeholders involved and has resolved the issues 
related to this legislation with those stakeholders. We believe this legislation is no longer 
necessary and understand that the sponsor and stakeholders agree.
