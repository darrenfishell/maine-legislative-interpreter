BEFORE THE JOINT STANDING COMMITTEE ON CRIMINAL JUSTICE AND PUBLIC 
SAFETY  
Public Hearing: Tuesday, May 4, 2023 
 
Senator Beebe Center , Representative Salisbury, and honorable members of the J oint Standing 
Committee on Criminal Justice and Public Safety:  
 
My name is Sam Hurley , and I am the Directo r of Maine Emergency Medical Services, Maine 
EMS, within the Department of Public Safety. I am testifying  on behalf of Maine Department of 
Public Safety and Maine EMS  neither for nor against LD  1515, “ An Act to Fund Delivery of 
Emergency Medical Services .” 
 
Maine EMS continues to work closely with House Speaker Rep . Talbot Ross, Rep. Salisbury, the 
Governor’s Office, and other stakeholders on similar legislation that provides fiscal support to 
EMS agencies throughout the State of Maine. Our current understanding is that the bill sponsor 
intends on requesting that this bill be tabled pending work on other pieces of legislation.   
I am more than happy to answer any questions that you may have , but please feel free to reach 
out to me  anytime if there is any additional information that  we can offer you and/or the 
Committee . Thank you.
