TESTHVIONY BY WORKERS’ COMPENSATION BOARD 
Senator Tipping, Representative Roeder and members of the Joint Standing Committee on Labor 
and Housi11g, ‘I am John Rohde, the Executive Director of the Workers’ Compensation Board. On 
behalf of the Maine Workers’ Compensation Board, I am here to testify Neither for Nor Against 
L.D. 53. 
The decision to testify Neither For Nor Against L.D. 53 was reached after a discussion during 
Which: The management members voiced their desire to oppose L.D. 53 as drafted because the 
proposed exception is very broad; and, the labor members stated they Wanted more information 
about the issue this bill seeks to address before taking a position. 
Consensus was reached to testify Neither For Nor Against L.D. 53, raise the concern about the 
breadth of the bill and, depending on the issue this bill seeks to address, indicate the Board’s 
willingness to discuss the possibility of a more narrowly tailored approach to the identiﬁed issue. 
Thank you for your time this afternoon. I am happy to try and answer questions you may have.
