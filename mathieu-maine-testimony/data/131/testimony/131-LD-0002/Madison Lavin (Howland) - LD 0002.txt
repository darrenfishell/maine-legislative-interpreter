Senator Pierce and Representative Gere, and honorable members of the Joint Select 
Committee on Housing: 
My name is Madison Lavin and I am submitting my testimony in favor of LD 2. I am 
a resident of Howland, but currently live in Orono as I pursue my Bachelor’s degree 
in Social Work at the University of Maine. Per my internship at the Department of 
Health and Human Services, I frequently interact with individuals who are members 
of the homeless community. Besides homelessness, many of these individuals also 
face other challenges, such as substance abuse and various mental health disorders. 
These issues largely impact people all across the state of Maine. I believe that 
providing safe housing will allow these individuals to achieve a healthier, more stable
lifestyle that will be beneficial to both them, as well as the community around them. 
This will serve as an opportunity to regain control over their lives and begin making 
changes for the better. The passing of this bill will create a safer environment for 
current residents and future generations to come. With this being said, I urge you all 
to vote “Yes” on LD 2. Thank you for your consideration.
Respectfully submitted, 
Madison Lavin
School of Social Work
University of Maine
