Living Wage Calculation for Maine  
The living wage shown is the hourly rate that an individual in a household must earn to support his or 
herself and their family. The assumption is the sole provider is working full -time (2080 hours per year). 
The tool provides information for individuals, and households with one or two working adults and zero to 
three children. In the case of households with two working adults, all values are per working adult, single 
or in a family unless otherwise noted.
