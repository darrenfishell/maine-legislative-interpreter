Summary 
This amendment changes the Maine standard deduction from an amount equal to the 
federal standard deduction to $12,000 for single ﬁlers, $18,000 for heads of household, and 
$24,000 for individuals ﬁling married joint returns, subject to an inﬂation adjustment. This will 
maintain the Maine standard deduction at current levels for tax years 2026 and later, as opposed 
to current law which provides for a reduction i11 the Maine and federal standard deduction for 
those tax years. 
In addition, this amendment changes references in the Maine dependent exemption tax 
credit from the federal child tax credit to the federal personal exemption. This will maintain the 
Maine dependent exemption tax credit at current levels for tax years 2026 and later, as opposed 
to current law which provides for a reduction in the credit due to the expiration of the referenced 
federal child tax credit provisions.
