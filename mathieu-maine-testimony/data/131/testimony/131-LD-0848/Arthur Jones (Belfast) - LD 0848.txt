Re: Public Hearing Testimony on LD 848
Dear Senator Carney, Representative Moonen and Distinguished Members of The 
Judiciary Committee;
My name is Dr. Arthur Jones, and I am writing in support of LD 848, “An Act to 
Expunge Certain Nonviolent Drug Crimes.” 
Simply stated, this bill prevents a conviction on a drug crime from wrecking the 
remainder of an individual’s life by expunging the record of the crime, if and only if a
person has successfully served their sentence and has not been convicted of any 
crimes in the subsequent five years.
The Maine Department of Corrections states their mission as: “Making our 
communities safer by reducing harm through supportive intervention, empowering 
change and restoring lives.” I urge you to help make the “restoring lives” part of this 
statement more truthful than it currently is by voting in support of LD 848.
Sincerely,
Arthur Jones, PhD
