LD 833
LD833 HP522  An Act to Prohibit Persons Who Donate to a Governor's Transition 
Committee and Inaugural Committee from Registering as Lobbyists
LINDA HASKELL, GARLAND. TESTIMONY TO SUPPORT LD833
A person with such monetary influence in the  Governor's  Transition Committee and 
Inaugural Committee should not be a lobbyist for that Governor's positions in his 
office. There needs to be barriers to keep non-partisan parties from taking total 
control of the State of Maine affairs. 
TESTIMONY TO SUPPORT LD833 HP522 LINDA HASKELL, GARLAND.
