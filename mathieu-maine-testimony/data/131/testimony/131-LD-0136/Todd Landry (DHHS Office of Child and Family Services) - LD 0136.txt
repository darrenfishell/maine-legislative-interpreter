Re:  LD 136 – An Act to Clarify Court Jurisdiction of Actions Involving Children 
Brought Under the Maine Uniform Probate Code  
 
Senator Carney , Representative Moonen , and Members of the Joint Standing 
Committee on Judiciary : 
 
This letter is to provide information regarding LD 136, An Act to Clarify Court 
Juris diction of Actions Involving Children Brought Under the Maine uniform Probate 
Code . The bill makes changes proposed by the Family Law Advisory Commission  
(FLAC) regarding jurisdiction of the District Court and probate courts.  
 
OCFS understands that the Off ice of the Attorney General has submitted a letter 
outlining concerns and options  for a proposed amendment  to address those concerns . 
OCFS would urge the Committee to carefully consider the input of the Attorney 
General , as OCFS legal counsel, on the best statutory structure to allow OCFS to 
ensure the safety and stability of Maine children and families and would respectfully 
request that the Committee incorporate the Attorney General’s suggestions into the 
language of t he bill with an amendment.  
 
We wanted you to be aware of the above information as you consider this bill going 
forward. If you have any further questions, please feel free to contact me.  
 
Sincerely,
