Oppose Youth Hunting "weekend" LD 672
Members of the Committee and Chairs
Maine residents have made it quite plain many many times that they do not want 
hunting on Sundays in any way, shape or form.
Thus we have a bill to allow hunting on (a) Sunday, which does not even mention 
Sunday anywhere in the text, yet that is obviously the intention of this bill, to get a 
foothold into Sunday hunting using Children to do the dirty work.
No Sunday hunting, period. That is what Mainers want. NO. SUNDAY. HUNTING.
And shame on the parents who are putting their kids up to this-
