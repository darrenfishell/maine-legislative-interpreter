LD 19, An Act to Authorize the National Institute of Funeral Service to Grant Associate Degrees  
(EMERGENCY ), authorizes the National Institute of Funeral Service to grant the degree of 
Associate of Applied Science in Funeral Service in Maine.   
The MRS , Chapter 409 , Degree -Granting Institutions , ยง10704 Initial authority to confer certain 
degrees and ยง10706-A For-profit college and university re view apply in this situation.  The 
National Institute of Funeral Service , an online for -profit college,  is currently in the approval 
process as described in Maine Department of Education Rule Chapter 149 , Procedures for 
Obtaining Authorization for Institutions of Higher Education to Confer Academic Degrees or to 
Offer Degree Courses/Programs in the State of Maine .  The Department supports the State 
Board in this process.  The approval process is not expected to conclude until at least  June of this 
year.   
The Department will be available to provide additional information  on LD 19 and respond to  any 
questions during the upcoming work session.
