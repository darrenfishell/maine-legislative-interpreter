Re: Public Hearing Testimony on LD 739
Dear Senator Carney, Representative Moonen and Distinguished Members of The 
Judiciary Committee;
My name is Dr. Arthur Jones, and I am writing in support of LD 739,  
“RESOLUTION, Proposing an Amendment to the Constitution of Maine to Empower
the Legislature to Allow the Expungement or Sealing of Certain Criminal Records.” 
Simply stated, this bill allows the expungement or sealing of the record of certain 
crimes upon completion of the sentence served. It would prevent those certain crimes,
that have been fully paid for according to the letter of the law, from wrecking the 
remainder of an individual’s life..
The Maine Department of Corrections states their mission as: “Making our 
communities safer by reducing harm through supportive intervention, empowering 
change and restoring lives.” I urge you to help make the “restoring lives” part of this 
statement more truthful than it currently is by voting in support of LD 848.
Sincerely,
Arthur Jones, PhD
