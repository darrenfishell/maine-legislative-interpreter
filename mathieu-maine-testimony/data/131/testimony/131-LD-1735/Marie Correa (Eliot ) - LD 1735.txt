Marie Correa
Eliot 
LD 1735
I am testifying in opposition to LD1735 “An Act to Safeguard Gender-affirming 
Healthcare”
1) It will usurp parental rights.
2) I have heard testimony of several adults who transitioned as adults and they 
regretted having gone through the procedure.  One transgendered person said that she 
was dying of estrogen induced cancer.  She also stated that this procedure did not cure
her mental disorder.
The State of Maine has no right to take Children away from Parents to give children 
both physical and mental/emotional illnesses.
