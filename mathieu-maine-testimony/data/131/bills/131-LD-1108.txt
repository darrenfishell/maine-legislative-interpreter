36SUMMARY
37 This bill requires nomination petitions for state and county offices to have the same 
38 number of signatures as for a primary petition for the same office, to be signed only by 
39 unenrolled voters and to be filed with the Secretary of State by March 15th of the year of 
40 the election.37
