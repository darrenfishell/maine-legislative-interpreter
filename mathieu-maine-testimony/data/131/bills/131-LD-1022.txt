34 The regional school unit board may call a special budget meeting when it declares that 
35 an emergency exists. The voters of the regional school unit may authorize the regional 
36 school unit board at a special regional school unit budget meeting to expend additional 
37 funds from the regional school unit's undesignated fund balance or to pledge the credit of
