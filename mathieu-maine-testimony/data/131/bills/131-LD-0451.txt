20SUMMARY
21 This bill requires temporary nurse agencies to renew registration annually and to update 
22 the registration if a temporary nurse agency changes ownership, operation or location. The 
23 bill increases the cost of registration for a temporary nurse agency from $25 to $1,000.   
24 The bill requires that temporary nurse agencies ensure that each employee it refers to a 
25 provider for a position meets the state and federal qualification requirements for that 
26 position, and maintains records to that effect. This bill prohibits agencies from requiring 
27 employees to recruit other employees.
28 The bill requires temporary nurse agencies to submit an annual report to the 
29 Department of Health and Human Services, which is then required to submit a summary 
30 report to the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human Services.21
