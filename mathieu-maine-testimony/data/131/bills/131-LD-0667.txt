2Sec. 1.  36 MRSA §5111, sub-§7 is enacted to read:
37.  Income tax surcharge to fund education and rural economic development.  An 
4 income tax surcharge is established and administered as follows.
5 A.  For tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2024, in addition to any other tax 
6 imposed by this chapter, a surcharge at the rate of 3% is imposed on that portion of the 
7 taxpayer's Maine taxable income in excess of $1,000,000 and not more than 
8 $10,000,000 and 6% on that portion of the taxpayer’s Maine taxable income in excess 
9 of $10,000,000.
10 B.  Seventy-five percent of the revenue collected under this subsection must be used to 
11 fund public kindergarten to grade 12 education.  Twenty-five percent of the revenue 
12 collected under this subsection must be used to fund rural economic development.
13SUMMARY
14 This bill establishes an income tax surcharge of 3% on taxable income in excess of 
15 $1,000,000 and 6% on taxable income in excess of $10,000,000 and requires that the 
16 revenue collected from the surcharge must be spent on public kindergarten to grade 12 
17 education and rural economic development.14
