12SUMMARY
13 This bill requires a landlord or landlord's agent, prior to obtaining any information 
14 about a prospective tenant, to notify the prospective tenant in writing what types of 
15 information will be accessed to conduct the tenant screening, including, but not limited to, 
16 background checks and credit checks, and what criteria may result in denial of the rental 
17 application. The bill also prohibits a landlord or landlord's agent from charging a 
18 prospective tenant any payment, fee or charge for the processing, review or acceptance of 
19 a rental application more than one time in a 12-month period.13
