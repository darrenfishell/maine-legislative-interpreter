312.  Recycling fees for nonprofit organizations.  Notwithstanding any provision of 
4 this section to the contrary, a municipality or consolidator that accepts covered electronic 
5 devices for recycling in accordance with this section may not charge a covered entity that 
6 is a nonprofit organization exempt from taxation under the United States Internal Revenue 
7 Code of 1986, Section 501(c)(3) that employs 100 or fewer individuals a fee to recycle a 
8 covered electronic device that is a television.
9 For the purposes of this subsection, "television" means an electronic device that has a 
10 display and receives video programming via broadcast, cable or satellite transmission; 
11 recorded transmissions from videotape, digital video disc and similar video players; or 
12 video from surveillance or other similar cameras.
13SUMMARY
14 This bill amends the electronic waste law to prohibit a municipality or consolidator that 
15 accepts electronic waste for recycling from charging a nonprofit organization exempt from 
16 taxation under the United States Internal Revenue Code of 1986, Section 501(c)(3) that 
17 employs 100 or fewer individuals a television recycling fee.14
