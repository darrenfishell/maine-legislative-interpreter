Page 1 - 131LR1509(01)1Sec. 1. Department of Education to amend rules regarding athletic director 
2qualifications.  Resolved: That the Department of Education shall amend its rules in 
3 Chapter 115: The Credentialing of Education Personnel, Part II, Requirements for Specific 
4 Certificates and Endorsements, Section 1.14, Certificate 215: Athletic Director regarding 
5 renewal requirements to allow a person to serve as an athletic director if the person:
6 1. Holds a valid Maine administrator certificate other than a Teaching Principal's 
7 certificate or completes a minimum of 3 semester hours in diversity-centered content 
8 related to today's classroom, such as culturally responsive teaching, multicultural 
9 education, intercultural education, second language acquisition or world language teaching 
10 methods; and
11 2. Has a basic level of knowledge in all of the following areas:
12 A. Interscholastic Athletics: Philosophy, Organization, and Programs;
13 B. Athletic Administration: Principles, Strategies, and Methods; and
14 C. Athletic Administration: Legal Issues.
15SUMMARY
16 This resolve directs the Department of Education to amend its rules regarding athletic 
17 directors to allow a person to either hold a valid Maine administrator certificate other than 
18 a Teaching Principal's certificate or complete a minimum of 3 semester hours in diversity-
19 centered content related to today's classroom in order to be an athletic director. The 
20 department's current rules require a person to do both.16
