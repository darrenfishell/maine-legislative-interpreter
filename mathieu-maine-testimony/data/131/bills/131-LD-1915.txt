Page 1 - 131LR2422(01)1Sec. 1.  Grant program established. Resolved: That the Department of Marine 
2 Resources shall create a program to award grants for shellfish restoration projects, 
3 including shellfish seeding, to municipalities that agree to reserve municipal shellfish 
4 licenses for qualified disabled veterans, referred to in this section as "the program."  The 
5 department shall require that a municipality seeking to receive a grant from the program 
6 amend the appropriate ordinances to authorize the issuance of licenses under the program. 
7 The department shall determine eligibility for participation in the program by 
8 municipalities, create an application procedure for the program and alert municipalities of 
9 the existence of the program.  In order to qualify for a license issued under the program, a 
10 person must be a qualified disabled veteran.  For the purposes of this resolve, "qualified 
11 disabled veteran" means a person who:
12 1.  Was honorably discharged from the Armed Forces of the United States, the National 
13 Guard or the Reserves of the United States Armed Forces;
14 2.  Has a service-connected disability evaluated at 50% or more; and
15 3.  Is a resident of the State.
16 In order to receive a license under the program, an applicant must provide satisfactory 
17 evidence that the applicant is a qualified disabled veteran.
