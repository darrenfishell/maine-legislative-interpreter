2education subsidy formula.  Resolved:  That the Department of Education shall 
3 contract with a 3rd party to conduct a study on the equity of the state education subsidy 
4 formula used to determine the size of the state subsidy for municipalities and the way the 
5 state subsidy funds capital projects.  The department may use its own funding or seek other 
6 funding to carry out the purposes of this resolve.
7Sec. 2.  Report.  Resolved:  That, by December 6, 2023, the Department of 
8 Education shall report the results of the study conducted under section 1 and its 
9 recommendations, including suggested legislation, to the Joint Standing Committee on 
10 Education and Cultural Affairs.  The joint standing committee may report out legislation 
11 based upon the report to the Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.
12SUMMARY
13 This resolve directs the Department of Education to contract with a 3rd party to conduct 
14 a study on the equity of the state education subsidy formula used to determine the size of
