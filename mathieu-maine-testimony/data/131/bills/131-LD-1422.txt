142. Program established.  The authority shall establish a housing voucher program for 
15 homeless students in elementary school and secondary school.  The authority shall adopt 
16 rules to implement this program.  Rules adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine 
17 technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
18Sec. 2.  Site-based housing program.  The Maine State Housing Authority shall 
19 identify 3 sites in the State to be used for a program under the authority of the Department 
20 of Health and Human Services that provides comprehensive support services to provide 
21 and maintain housing to an individual in the individual's residence.

19SUMMARY
20 This bill establishes a housing voucher program for homeless students under the 
21 authority of the Maine State Housing Authority and appropriates one-time funding of $2 
22 million in fiscal years 2023-24 and 2024-25.  The bill directs the Maine State Housing 
23 Authority to identify 3 sites in Maine to be used for a site-based housing program and 
24 appropriates one-time funding of $3.5 million in fiscal years 2023-24 and 2024-25 to the 
25 Department of Health and Human Services to support the program.1
