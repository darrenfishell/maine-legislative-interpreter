310-C.  Culturally informed reentry and reintegration halfway houses.  The 
4 commissioner shall establish and operate or subcontract with an appropriate entity to 
5 establish and operate culturally informed halfway houses for persons who have not attained 
6 21 years of age to provide housing services, referrals to community-based services and 
7 supports and education and employment resources. The operation of halfway houses 
8 established or subcontracted under this subsection may be funded from resources available 
9 to the department, including any public or private grants, donations and other funding 
10 received for this purpose.  Grants, donations and other funding received by the department 
11 for the purposes of this subsection must be placed in a separate nonlapsing account and 
12 may be used only for the purposes of this subsection and in accordance with any restrictions 
13 placed on the funds by the source of the grant, donation or funding.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill directs the Commissioner of Corrections to establish and operate culturally 
16 informed halfway houses for persons under 21 years of age to provide housing services, 
17 referrals to community-based services and supports and education and employment 
18 resources.  It allows the commissioner to subcontract with an entity for the establishment 
19 and operation of the halfway houses.15
