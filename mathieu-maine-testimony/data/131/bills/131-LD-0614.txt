16SUMMARY
17 This bill amends the law governing the landowner consideration for the antlerless deer 
18 permit lottery to provide an antlerless deer permit at no cost for all eligible landowners who 
19 apply for a permit and keep their lands open to hunting by the public. This bill also defines 
20 an eligible landowner as a landowner who owns at least 20 acres of contiguous land and 
21 does not post the landowner's land to prohibit hunting by others.17
