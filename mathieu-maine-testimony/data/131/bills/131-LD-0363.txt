27SUMMARY
28 This bill expands the jurisdiction of the Sentence Review Panel of the Supreme Judicial 
29 Court from reviewing terms of imprisonment of one year or more to reviewing terms of 
30 imprisonment of any duration, terms of probation, fines or restitution.  It authorizes, for an 
31 applicant seeking sentence review, a stay of execution of the sentence and post-conviction 
32 bail.28
