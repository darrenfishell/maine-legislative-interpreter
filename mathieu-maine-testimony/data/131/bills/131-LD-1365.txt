13SUMMARY
14 This bill requires the presentation of proof of identity in the form of a photograph 
15 identification document when voting in person or by absentee ballot.  Acceptable 
16 photograph identification is a valid driver's license or nondriver identification card issued 
17 in this State, a United States passport, a military identification or a permit to carry a 
18 concealed handgun issued in this State if that permit includes a photograph.  An 
19 identification issued by a college or university in this State may not be accepted for voter 
20 identification when voting or when registering to vote.  The bill also allows a person who 
21 does not possess a photograph identification document to request a free special voter 
22 photograph identification card from the Secretary of State.14
