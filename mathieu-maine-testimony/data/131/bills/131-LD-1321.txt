3ยง1728.  Hospital executive pay
4 The annual wages or total annual compensation of an executive of a hospital licensed 
5 under chapter 405 may not exceed an amount equal to 5 times the median compensation of 
6 the full-time registered professional nurses as defined in Title 32, section 2102, subsection 
7 5 who work at the hospital. For purposes of this section, "executive" includes a president 
8 of the board of directors, director, chief executive officer, chief operating officer, chief 
9 financial officer, owner or partner of the hospital. For purposes of this section, "executive" 
10 does not include a member of the administrative staff or a person primarily employed by 
11 the hospital as a health care practitioner or to provide health care services.
12SUMMARY
13 This bill limits total annual compensation for an executive at a hospital to no more than 
14 5 times the median compensation of the full-time registered professional nurses at the 
15 hospital. For the purposes of this provision, "executive" does not include administrative 
16 staff or persons primarily employed by the hospital as health care practitioners or to provide 
17 health care services.13
