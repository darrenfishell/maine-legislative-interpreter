38SUMMARY
39 This bill makes changes to provisions of law regarding affordable housing and 
40 accessory dwelling units by:39
40
Page 4 - 131LR1001(01)1 1. Removing from the definition of "affordable housing development" that the 
2 developer designates the development as affordable;
3 2. Clarifying that municipal approval of an affordable housing development for 
4 purposes of timing of the execution of a restrictive covenant means granting final approval, 
5 including issuing an occupancy permit;
6 3. Clarifying that setback requirements are just one type of dimensional requirements;
7 4. Changing language stating where housing is allowed or permitted to refer to where 
8 residential uses, including as a conditional use, are allowed or permitted;
9 5. Allowing accessory dwelling units on nonconforming lots, including in shoreland 
10 zones, if the accessory dwelling units do not further increase the nonconformity;
11 6. Allowing accessory dwelling units built without municipal approval, including 
12 accessory dwelling units in shoreland zones, if the accessory dwelling units otherwise meet 
13 state and municipal requirements for accessory dwelling units; and
14 7. Allowing municipalities to approve accessory dwelling units through a permitting 
15 process that does not require planning board approval.
