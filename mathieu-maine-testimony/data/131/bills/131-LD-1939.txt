14Sec. 1.  3 MRSA §227, as corrected by RR 1991, c. 1, §1, is amended to read:
15§227.  Maine-Canadian Legislative Advisory Commission
16 The Maine-Canadian Legislative Advisory Commission, as authorized by Title 5, 
17 section 12004‑K, subsection 10, shall consist  consists of  8 12 members, all of whom  shall  
18 must be citizens of this State. The Speaker of the House shall appoint  4 6 members, 2 for a 
19 term of one year and 2  4 who must be members of the House of Representatives and who 
20 shall each hold office from the date of appointment until the term of election to the 
21 Legislature expires. The President of the Senate shall appoint 4  6 members, 2 for a term of 
22 one year and 2  4 who must be Senators and who  shall each hold office from the date of 
23 appointment until the term of election to the Legislature expires. At least one member 
24 appointed by the President of the Senate and one member appointed by the Speaker of the 
25 House shall  must be fluent in the French language.  The members appointed must equally 
26 represent the 2 parties holding the largest number of seats in the Legislature. In the event 
27 of the death or resignation of any member, the vacancy shall  must be filled for the 
28 remainder of the term in the same manner as the original appointment.
29 Members shall  must be compensated as provided in Title 5, chapter 379. Four  Six 
30 members shall constitute a quorum. The commission shall designate one of its members as  
31 chairman  chair.
