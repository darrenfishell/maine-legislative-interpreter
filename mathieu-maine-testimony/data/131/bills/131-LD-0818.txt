3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill would establish an emergency fuel reserve for up to 90 days of fuel that the 
6 State could use in an emergency event, such as when the cost of heating oil for state 
7 residents is excessive.  The State would be required to purchase fuel to resupply the reserve 
8 when the cost of fuel is low.  The State would be authorized to contract with a vendor to 
9 store and manage the fuel reserve.4
