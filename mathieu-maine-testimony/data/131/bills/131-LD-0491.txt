Page 1 - 131LR1850(01)1Sec. 1.  Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to develop a plan for 
2communication regarding municipal regulation. Resolved: That the Department 
3 of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife shall develop a plan to inform municipalities about the 
4 limits on local regulation of hunting, fishing and trapping and the operation of watercraft, 
5 snowmobiles and all-terrain vehicles.  The department shall coordinate with a statewide 
6 organization representing municipalities to create a distribution scheme for the information. 
7 The department shall submit a report with the plan and distribution scheme, together with 
8 any necessary legislation, to the Joint Standing Committee on Inland Fisheries and Wildlife 
9 no later than January 1, 2024. The committee is authorized to report out a bill based on the 
10 report to the Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.
11SUMMARY
12 This resolve requires the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to develop and 
13 report by January 1, 2024 a plan to inform municipalities about the limits on local 
14 regulation of hunting, fishing and trapping and the operation of watercraft, snowmobiles 
15 and all-terrain vehicles and a distribution scheme for the information.12
