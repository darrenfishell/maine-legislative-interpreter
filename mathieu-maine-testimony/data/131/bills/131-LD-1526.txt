5ยง2661. Reading Proficiency Grant Fund
61.  Reading Proficiency Grant Fund.  The Reading Proficiency Grant Fund, referred 
7 to in this section as "the fund," is established in the department as a nonlapsing fund to 
8 provide grants to strengthen reading proficiency programming.
92.  Grants.  The department shall establish criteria to award grants to school 
10 administrative units from the fund.   In awarding grants, the department shall give special 
11 consideration to school administrative units serving populations of students with identified 
12 reading proficiency achievement gaps, including, but not limited to, reading achievement 
13 gaps for economically disadvantaged students and racial and ethnic populations.
143. Rulemaking. The department shall adopt rules to implement this section, including 
15 but not limited to rules governing procedures, criteria and priorities for awarding grants. 
16 Rules adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, 
17 chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
18Sec. 2. Report on Reading Proficiency Grant Fund.  By December 6, 2023,  the 
19 Department of Education shall submit to the Joint Standing Committee on Education and 
20 Cultural Affairs a report on the Reading Proficiency Grant Fund under the Maine Revised 
21 Statutes, Title 20-A, section 2661, the criteria to award grants to school administrative units 
22 from the fund and the rules adopted to implement the awarding of grants from the fund.  
23 The committee may report out a bill to the Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.
