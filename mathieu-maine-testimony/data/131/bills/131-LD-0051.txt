22SUMMARY
23 Public Law 2019, chapter 154 removed the exemption from immunization 
24 requirements based on religious or philosophical beliefs for students in elementary, 
25 secondary and postsecondary schools and employees of nursery schools and certain health 
26 care facilities.  This bill reinstates the religious and philosophical exemptions.23
