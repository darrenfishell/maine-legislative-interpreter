13SUMMARY
14 This bill prohibits the expiration of unused kilowatt-hour credits accumulated by a 
15 residential customer participating in a net energy billing arrangement and strikes the 
16 provision that requires the Public Utilities Commission to adopt rules providing that unused 
17 kilowatt-hour credits must be applied for the benefit of participants in the utility's arrearage 
18 management program. The bill also prohibits the expiration of unused credits accrued by a 
19 commercial or institutional net energy billing customer.14
