4SUMMARY
5 This bill establishes a rebuttable presumption:
6 1. Of disability under the Maine Public Employees Retirement System for a law 
7 enforcement officer who suffers a disability as a result of a cardiovascular injury or a 
8 cardiovascular or pulmonary disease; and 
9 2. That a law enforcement officer's cardiovascular injury or disease or pulmonary 
10 disease arose out of and in the course of employment under the Maine Workers' 
11 Compensation Act of 1992.5
