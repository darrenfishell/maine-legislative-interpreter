2Sec. 1.  14 MRSA §8102, sub-§1, as amended by PL 2003, c. 489, §1, is repealed 
3 and the following enacted in its place:
41.  Employee.  "Employee" means a person acting on behalf of a governmental entity 
5 in any official capacity, whether temporarily or permanently, and whether with or without 
6 compensation from local, state or federal funds, including:
7 A.  Elected or appointed officials;
8 B.  Volunteer firefighters as defined in Title 30‑A, section 3151, subsection 4;
9 C.  Emergency medical services personnel, except that a person who is a private 
10 firefighter, a private emergency medical services employee or an employee of another 
11 private service that provides an emergency response is an employee for purposes of 
12 this chapter only if the person is acting without compensation and either at the request 
13 of a governmental entity or in response to a life-threatening emergency;
14 D.  Members of the Maine National Guard but only while performing state active 
15 service pursuant to Title 37‑B;
16 E.  Sheriff's deputies as described in Title 30‑A, section 381 when they are serving 
17 orders pursuant to section 3135; and
18 F.  Persons while performing a search and rescue activity when requested by a state, 
19 county or local governmental entity.
20 "Employee" does not mean a person or other legal entity acting in the capacity of an 
21 independent contractor under contract to the governmental entity.
22SUMMARY
23 This bill specifies that a person who is a private firefighter, a private emergency 
24 medical services employee or an employee of another private service that provides an 
25 emergency response is an employee for purposes of the Maine Tort Claims Act only under 
26 certain conditions.23
