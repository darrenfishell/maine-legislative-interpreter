71.  Requirements.  The holder of a commercial driver's license issued pursuant to this 
8 section must comply with all of the requirements of section 1253, except that the Secretary 
9 of State may not deny a person a commercial driver's license under this section on the basis 
10 of the person's authorized use of cannabis pursuant to Title 22, chapter 558-C or Title 28-B.  
11 This section may not be construed to authorize a person to use cannabis while operating a 
12 motor vehicle.
132.  Exception.  A person may not obtain a commercial driver's license pursuant to this 
14 section for the operation of a school bus.
15SUMMARY
16 This bill establishes a commercial driver's license for use only within the State and 
17 prohibits the Secretary of State from denying a person that commercial driver's license on 
18 the basis of the person's authorized use of cannabis.  The bill clarifies that the prohibition 
19 on denying a commercial driver's license may not to be construed as authorizing a person 
20 to use cannabis while operating a motor vehicle. The bill provides that a person may not 
21 obtain the commercial driver's license for the operation of a school bus.16
