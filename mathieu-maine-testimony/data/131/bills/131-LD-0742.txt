41.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
5 following terms have the following meanings.
6 A.  "Board" has the same meaning as in section 17001, subsection 7.
7 B.  "Boycotting company" means any corporation or company that trades in lobsters 
8 and that has publicly stated it will not trade in lobsters caught in Maine waters, whether 
9 by not purchasing, not selling or otherwise not trading in such Maine lobsters, or caught 
10 by any person licensed by the Department of Marine Resources to fish for, take or catch 
11 lobsters.
12 C.  "Retirement system" means the Maine Public Employees Retirement System.
132.  Board may not invest.  The board, in accordance with sound investment criteria 
14 and consistent with fiduciary obligations, may not invest the assets of the retirement system 
15 in any stocks, securities or other obligations of any boycotting company.  Nothing in this 
16 subsection precludes de minimis exposure of any funds held by the board to the stocks, 
17 securities or other obligations of any boycotting company.
183.  Board to divest.  The board shall review the extent to which the assets of the 
19 retirement system are invested in any stocks, securities or other obligations of any 
20 boycotting company and shall, in accordance with sound investment criteria and consistent 
21 with fiduciary obligations, divest any such holdings.  Nothing in this subsection precludes 
22 de minimis exposure of any funds held by the board to the stocks, securities or other 
23 obligations of any boycotting company.
24SUMMARY
25 This bill requires the Board of Trustees of the Maine Public Employees Retirement 
26 System to divest any stocks, securities or other obligations of any corporation or company 
27 that trades in lobsters and that has publicly stated it will not trade in lobsters caught in 
28 Maine waters, whether by not purchasing, not selling or otherwise not trading in such 
29 Maine lobsters, or caught by any person licensed by the Department of Marine Resources 
30 to fish for, take or catch lobsters.25
