2Sec. 1.  22 MRSA §2353, sub-§5, ¶B, as enacted by PL 2015, c. 508, §4, is

13 C.  A person not listed in this section, acting in good faith and with reasonable care, is

16 naloxone hydrochloride to an individual whom the person believes in good faith is 
17 experiencing an opioid-related drug overdose or for any outcome resulting from such 
18 actions.
19SUMMARY
20 This bill extends immunity from civil or criminal liability or professional disciplinary 
21 action to any person who provides or administers naloxone hydrochloride to an individual 
22 whom the person believes in good faith is experiencing an opioid-related drug overdose.20
