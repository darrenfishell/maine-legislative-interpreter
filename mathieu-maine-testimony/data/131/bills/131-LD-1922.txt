33.  Merger of bargaining agents.  Two or more bargaining agents who are certified 
4 by the executive director of the board and who are members or affiliates of the same public 
5 employee organization may elect to merge. Bargaining agents seeking to merge shall file 
6 with the executive director of the board, or a designee, a petition describing the proposed 
7 merger. On receipt of a petition under this subsection, the executive director of the board 
8 shall conduct an election among the employees represented by the petitioning bargaining 
9 agents in which the only question on the ballot is the proposed merger of the bargaining 
10 agents. On an affirmative vote of the majority of the employees represented by each 
11 petitioning bargaining agent, the executive director of the board shall order the merger. 
12 After a merger is ordered, the parties to a contract in which one other party to that contract 
13 is one of the merged bargaining agents shall honor the terms of the contract unless the 
14 public employer and the merged bargaining agent agree to different terms.
15SUMMARY
16 This bill allows 2 or more bargaining agents who are certified by the Executive Director 
17 of the Maine Labor Relations Board and who are members or affiliates of the same public 
18 employee organization to merge.16
