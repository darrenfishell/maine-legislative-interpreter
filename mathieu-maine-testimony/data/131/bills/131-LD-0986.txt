7SUMMARY
8 This bill amends the Maine Criminal Code as follows:
9 1.  It increases the penalty for trafficking fentanyl and other scheduled drugs containing 
10 a detectable amount of fentanyl from a Class B crime to a Class A crime; and 
11 2.  It establishes a new Class B crime of unlawful use of an electronic communication 
12 device in furtherance of fentanyl trafficking.  A person is guilty of the crime if the person 
13 uses an electronic communication device in the furtherance of trafficking in fentanyl or 
14 other scheduled drugs containing a detectable amount of fentanyl.1
