3ยง2093.  Disabled motor vehicles
41. Disabled motor vehicle. The operator of a motor vehicle that is disabled on a public 
5 way due to a malfunction of the motor vehicle shall activate the motor vehicle's hazard 
6 lights and, if available, place one or more external warning devices, including a warning 
7 triangle or flare, in position to alert an approaching motor vehicle.
82. Passing a disabled motor vehicle. The operator of a motor vehicle passing a 
9 disabled motor vehicle under subsection 1, with due regard to the safety and traffic 
10 conditions, shall:
11 A. Pass in a lane not adjacent to that of the disabled motor vehicle, if possible; or
12 B. If passing in a nonadjacent lane is impossible or unsafe, pass the disabled motor 
13 vehicle at a careful and prudent speed reasonable for passing the disabled motor vehicle 
14 safely.
153. Violation. A violation of subsection 2 is a traffic infraction for which a minimum 
16 fine of $275 must be adjudged.
17SUMMARY
18 This bill requires the operator of a disabled motor vehicle on a public way to activate 
19 the motor vehicle's hazard lights and place warning devices such as a warning triangle or 
20 flares, if available, in a position to alert approaching motor vehicles. The bill also requires 
21 an operator of a motor vehicle to pass a disabled motor vehicle in a lane not adjacent to the 
22 disabled motor vehicle or, if not possible or safe to pass in that nonadjacent lane, to pass 
23 the disabled motor vehicle safely at a careful and prudent speed.18
