Page 1 - 131LR2399(01)1Sec. 1.  Pilot program established. Resolved: That the Department of 
2 Corrections, in cooperation with the Department of Labor, no later than January 1, 2024, 
3 shall establish a pilot program to provide vocational apprenticeships for inmates in order 
4 to provide opportunities for inmates to perform meaningful work while incarcerated.
5Sec. 2.  Report to Legislature. Resolved: That, no later than January 1, 2026, the 
6 Commissioner of Corrections shall submit a report regarding the pilot program under this 
7 resolve and make recommendations, including any suggested legislation, to the joint 
8 standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over criminal justice and public 
9 safety matters. The joint standing committee may report out legislation on the subject 
10 matter of the report to the Second Regular Session of the 132nd Legislature.
11SUMMARY
12 This resolve directs the Department of Corrections, in cooperation with the Department 
13 of Labor, no later than January 1, 2024, to establish a pilot program to provide vocational 
14 apprenticeships for inmates.12
