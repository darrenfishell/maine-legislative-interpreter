3ยง3174-KKK.  Coverage for gender-affirming care
41.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
5 following terms have the following meanings.  
6 A.  "Gender expression" means an individual's gender-related appearance and 
7 behavior, whether or not stereotypically associated with the sex assigned to the 
8 individual at birth.
9 B.  "Gender identity" means an individual's internal sense of that individual's gender, 
10 regardless of the sex assigned to the individual at birth.
11 C.  "Transgender individual" means an individual who identifies as a gender different 
12 from the sex assigned to the individual at birth.
132.  Reimbursement.  The department shall provide reimbursement for medically 
14 necessary treatment for or related to gender dysphoria as defined by the Diagnostic and 
15 Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders, 5th edition, published by the American Psychiatric 
16 Association or a comparable or equivalent diagnosis.
173.  Discrimination prohibited. The department may not discriminate in its 
18 reimbursement of medically necessary treatment on the basis of a MaineCare member's 
19 gender identity or gender expression or on the basis that the MaineCare member is a 
20 transgender individual.
21SUMMARY
22 This bill prohibits the MaineCare program from discriminating in its reimbursement 
23 for medically necessary treatment on the basis of a MaineCare member's gender identity, 
24 gender expression or on the basis that the MaineCare member is a transgender individual 
25 and requires that the program cover medically necessary treatment for or related to gender 
26 dysphoria.22
