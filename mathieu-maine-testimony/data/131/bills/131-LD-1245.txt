7Sec. 2.  29-A MRSA §458-C is enacted to read:
8§458-C.  Motorized bicycle or tricycle or motorized scooter
9 A motorized bicycle or tricycle or motorized scooter may be registered in accordance 
10 with the provisions of this section.
111.  Registration fee.  The fee for a motorized bicycle or tricycle or motorized scooter 
12 registration is the same as the motor vehicle registration fee required under section 501, 
13 subsection 1. 
142.  Registration plate.  The Secretary of State shall issue a registration certificate and 
15 a unique motorized bicycle or tricycle or motorized scooter registration plate in a 3-number 
16 and 3-letter combination sequence.
173.  Application for registration.  An application for registration must contain 
18 information requested by the Secretary of State, including the legal name, residence and 
19 address of the registrant and the maker, the serial number if listed on the frame, the year of 
20 manufacture, the type of motor fuel or motive power and a brief description of the 
21 motorized bicycle or tricycle or motorized scooter.  A registrant that is a corporation, trust, 
22 limited partnership or other similar entity shall provide either a federal taxpayer 
23 identification number or an identification number issued by the department. An initial 
24 application for registration must be signed by the registrant or the registrant's legal 
25 representative. The Secretary of State shall keep the initial application on file until that 
26 registration is terminated. 
274.  Registration.  The registration must indicate the year and make of the motorized 
28 bicycle or tricycle or motorized scooter. 
295.  Vanity plates.  The Secretary of State may not issue vanity plates for motorized 
30 bicycles or tricycles or motorized scooters.
316.  Inspection.  A motorized bicycle or tricycle or motorized scooter is not subject to 
32 the inspection requirements of section 1751, except that the Chief of the State Police may 
33 provide certain inspection requirements for motorized bicycles or tricycles or motorized 
34 scooters pursuant to section 1769.
357.  Rules.  The Secretary of State may adopt rules to enforce this section. Rules adopted 
36 pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, 
37 subchapter 2‑A.

7SUMMARY
8 This bill requires a motorized bicycle or tricycle or motorized scooter to be registered 
9 to operate on a public way.8
