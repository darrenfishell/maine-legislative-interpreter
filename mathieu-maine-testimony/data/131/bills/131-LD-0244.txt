Page 1 - 131LR1158(01)1Sec. 1.  Emergency medical services stakeholder group established.  
2Resolved:  That the Department of Public Safety, Maine Emergency Medical Services 
3 shall convene a stakeholder group to explore career pathways and educational opportunities 
4 for emergency medical services providers in the State.  Maine Emergency Medical Services 
5 shall invite the participation of the Maine Community College System, the University of 
6 Maine System, other public and private entities that provide emergency medical services 
7 education or training programs in the State and individuals with relevant backgrounds and 
8 experiences in emergency medical services education and training and in the delivery of 
9 emergency medical services.  No later than December 6, 2023, Maine Emergency Medical 
10 Services shall submit a report to the Joint Standing Committee on Criminal Justice and 
11 Public Safety that outlines the activities of the stakeholder group and any recommendations 
12 of the group, including any proposed legislation.  The joint standing committee may report 
13 out legislation to the Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.
14SUMMARY
15 This resolve directs the Department of Public Safety, Maine Emergency Medical 
16 Services to convene a stakeholder group to explore career pathways and educational 
17 opportunities for emergency medical services providers in the State and to submit a report 
18 to the Legislature.  Stakeholders include the Maine Community College System and the 
19 University of Maine System.15
