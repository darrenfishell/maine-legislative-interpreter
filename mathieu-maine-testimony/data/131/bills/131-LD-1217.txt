Page 1 - 131LR1898(01)1Sec. 1. Creation of pilot program for alternative teacher certification.  
2Resolved:  That the Department of Education shall create a pilot program to provide 
3 alternative teacher certification that targets demographic groups that are underrepresented 
4 in the education workforce or who hold unique expertise and experience. The pilot program 
5 must allow potential educators to acquire and demonstrate the knowledge and skills that 
6 are defined within nationally recognized standards for beginning educators. The program 
7 must allow an individual who receives an alternative teacher certification to renew that 
8 certification for a reasonable amount of time until the individual can achieve traditional 
9 certification. The department shall submit a report with findings and recommendations 
10 concerning the program to the Legislature no later than December 6, 2023.
11SUMMARY
12 This resolve requires the Department of Education to create a pilot program for 
13 alternative teacher certification.12
