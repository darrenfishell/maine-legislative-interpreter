3SUMMARY
4 This bill repeals the provision of the Maine Revised Statutes that generally prohibits a 
5 prosecutor from communicating with an unrepresented defendant concerning the facts, 
6 circumstances, merits or disposition of a pending criminal charge against that defendant 
7 unless the defendant has been notified by the court of the defendant's right to counsel and 
8 has executed in court a written waiver of the right to counsel.4
