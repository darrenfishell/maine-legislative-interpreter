29SUMMARY
30 This bill clarifies the law governing school bus driver endorsements.  It provides that 
31 the endorsement is contingent on the applicant meeting certain federal requirements only 
32 if there is no waiver of those requirements in place.  It also clarifies that the requirement 
33 that the applicant pass an examination of the person's ability to operate a bus does not 
34 include an ability to operate a truck tractor with semitrailer.
35 The bill also directs the Secretary of State to seek from the Federal Motor Carrier Safety 
36 Administration a waiver of the engine compartment component of the pre-trip vehicle 
37 inspection skills testing requirement in 49 Code of Federal Regulations, Section 
38 383.113(a)(1)(i), known as the "under-the-hood" component, for commercial driver's 
39 license applicants seeking the school bus and passenger endorsements as well as a waiver 
40 of the requirement in 49 Code of Federal Regulations, Section 383.133(c)(1) that the State 
41 administer this portion of the pre-trip vehicle inspection test in accordance with a Federal 
42 Motor Carrier Safety Administration pre-approved examiner information manual.  The 
43 Secretary of State may seek additional waivers of requirements that the Secretary of State 30
