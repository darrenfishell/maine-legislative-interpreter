313.  Battery electric vehicle purchases or leases.  In addition to the requirements of 
4 subsection 12, the Central Fleet Management Division and the Department of Public Safety 
5 shall purchase or lease only battery electric vehicles to the extent a battery electric vehicle 
6 that meets the need for which the vehicle is being purchased or leased is commercially 
7 available. For purposes of this subsection, "battery electric vehicle" has the same meaning 
8 as in Title 35-A, section 10126, subsection 1, paragraph A.
9SUMMARY
10 Current law establishes certain goals for the Department of Administrative and 
11 Financial Services, Bureau of General Services, Central Fleet Management Division and 
12 the Department of Public Safety in acquiring by purchase or lease plug-in hybrid electric 
13 vehicles and zero-emission vehicles. This bill provides, in addition, that the Central Fleet 
14 Management Division and the Department of Public Safety must purchase or lease only 
15 zero-emission battery electric vehicles, to the extent a battery electric vehicle that meets 
16 the need for which the vehicle is being purchased or leased is commercially available.10
