27SUMMARY
28 This bill adds to the exceptions to the registration requirement for labels of malt liquor, 
29 wine, hard cider or low-alcohol spirits products sold in the State labels approved by the 
30 United States Department of the Treasury, Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau. 
31 This bill requires a manufacturer of products sold in this State that bear such a label to 
32 notify the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Alcoholic 
33 Beverages and Lottery Operations of that fact.28
