14SUMMARY
15 This bill requires that driver education instruction for all classes of driver's licenses 
16 include mandatory education in the risks associated with the use of alcohol, cannabis or 
17 other substances and requires the Secretary of State to examine the laws, rules and other 
18 efforts of other jurisdictions to incorporate cannabis awareness within their driver 
19 awareness course curricula.15
