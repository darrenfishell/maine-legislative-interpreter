Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill includes General Fund appropriations to the Judicial Department of $75,870 in fiscal year 2023-24 and 
$100,697 in fiscal year 2024-25 to establish a courtroom technology assistant position effective October 1, 2023 to 
implement the recording of all state grand jury proceedings.
