20SUMMARY
21 This bill amends Maine's Good Samaritan laws concerning drug-related medical 
22 assistance by adding possession of a firearm by a prohibited person, unlawful trafficking 
23 in scheduled drugs and unlawfully furnishing scheduled drugs to the list of crimes that do 
24 not qualify for immunity under those laws.21
