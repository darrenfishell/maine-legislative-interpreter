13Sec. 1.  Department of Health and Human Services to develop a plan for 
14children's behavioral health care services.  Resolved:  That the Department of 
15 Health and Human Services shall develop a plan to ensure that medically necessary home-
16 based and community-based behavioral health care services for children are available in 
17 the scope, intensity and duration necessary to meet the needs of children and their families 
18 in each county and region in the State.  The plan must include an assessment of and plan 
19 for the needs of children currently in institutional settings within the State and outside the 
20 State.  The plan must identify clear steps and timelines for implementation and it must 
21 identify any additional resources needed to implement the plan.  The department shall 
22 submit the plan to the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human Services no later 
23 than October 1, 2023.
24Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
25 takes effect when approved.
26SUMMARY
27 This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to develop a plan 
28 to ensure that medically necessary home-based and community-based behavioral health 
29 care services are available in the scope, intensity and duration necessary to meet the needs 
30 of children and their families in the State.  It directs the department to submit the plan to 
31 the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human Services no later than October 1, 2023.27
