24SUMMARY
25 This bill provides that the Commissioner of Marine Resources may not issue a lease 
26 for marine finfish aquaculture if the stocking density exceeds 22 kilograms per cubic meter 
27 or if the biomass exceeds 780 metric tons and that existing leases must abide by these limits 
28 upon renewal.  25
