4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill proposes to establish measures to ensure that residents in the rural parts of the 
6 State receive adequate law enforcement services, including law enforcement services 
7 provided through the Maine State Police and county sheriffs.  This bill would appropriate 
8 funds to establish these measures.4
