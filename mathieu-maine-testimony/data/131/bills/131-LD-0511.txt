19§3705.  Filing of complaint
201.  Who may file complaint.  A forest products harvester or hauler or forest landowner

25SUMMARY
26 This bill applies the provisions governing the State Board of Arbitration and 
27 Conciliation, Logging Dispute Resolution Board to persons hauling trees from forest land
