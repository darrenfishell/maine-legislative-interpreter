29SUMMARY
30 This bill excludes from eligibility for the business equipment tax exemption and the 
31 business equipment tax reimbursement a person that, based on 3rd-party certifications, 
32 bans, boycotts or otherwise restricts or prevents the sale or distribution of any product that 
33 is legally produced, harvested or grown in the State.30
