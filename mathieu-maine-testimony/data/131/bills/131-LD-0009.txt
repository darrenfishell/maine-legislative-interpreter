10SUMMARY
11 This bill exempts applications for site location of development from the 20-day time 
12 period in current law for the Commissioner of Environmental Protection to decide whether 
13 an application meets the permit by rule provisions and instead requires the commissioner 
14 to determine whether an application for site location of development meets the permit by 
15 rule provisions within the applicable time period established annually by the commissioner 
16 pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 38, section 344-B.11
