35SUMMARY
36 This bill provides that, by October 1, 2023, and by October 1st of every odd-numbered 
37 year thereafter, the Hancock County legislative delegation must select, by majority vote, a 
38 member of the delegation to serve on the Hancock County Budget Advisory Committee 
39 for a period of 2 years.  It provides that if the selected member resigns from the Legislature 
40 or the budget advisory committee prior to the expiration of the 2-year term, the delegation 
41 may select another member to fulfill the remaining term of office.  The delegation may also 
42 replace its selected member by a 2/3 vote of the delegation.  36
