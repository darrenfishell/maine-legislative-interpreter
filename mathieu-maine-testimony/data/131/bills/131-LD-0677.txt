5ยง1301. Resale of tickets
61. Definitions. As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
7 following terms have the following meanings.
8 A. "Entertainment event" means a performance, concert, exhibit, game or contest.
9 B. "Place of entertainment" means a facility used to host an entertainment event 
10 including, but not limited to, a theater, stadium, arena, racetrack, museum or 
11 amusement park.
12 C. "Ticket" means documentation of a right to attend an entertainment event.
132. Refunds required. A person or entity that engages in the resale of a ticket to a place 
14 of entertainment shall, upon the request of the customer, refund the amount paid by the 
15 customer for the ticket in any of the following circumstances:
16 A. The entertainment event is cancelled;
17 B. The ticket is not accepted by the entity holding the event because it is counterfeit or 
18 does not conform with the requirements established by the entity holding the 
19 entertainment event;
20 C. The ticket is cancelled by the entity holding the entertainment event for any reason; 
21 or
22 D. The person who purchased the ticket does not receive the ticket in time to attend the 
23 entertainment event.
24SUMMARY
25 This bill requires that anyone engaged in the resale of tickets to entertainment events 
26 must refund the amount paid by the customer if the event is cancelled, the ticket is not 
27 accepted by the entity holding the event, the ticket is cancelled by the entity holding the 
28 entertainment event or the person who purchased the ticket does not receive the ticket in 
29 time to attend the event.25
