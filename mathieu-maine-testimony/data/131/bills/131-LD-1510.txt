5ยง2661. Innovative Learning Fund
61.  Innovative Learning Fund.  The Innovative Learning Fund, referred to in this 
7 section as "the fund," is established in the department as a nonlapsing fund to provide grants 
8 to promote innovative learning strategies to address student achievement gaps.
92.  Grants.  The department shall establish criteria to award grants to school 
10 administrative units from the fund.  In awarding grants, the department shall give special 
11 consideration to school administrative units that demonstrate instructional partnerships 
12 with professional organizations or companies and partnerships with institutions of higher 
13 education and that provide specific data on closing the achievement gap for economically 
14 disadvantaged students, racial and ethnic populations and students whose learning 
15 achievements were negatively affected due to restrictions during the pandemic related to 
16 coronavirus disease 2019, referred to as COVID-19. 
173. Rulemaking.  The department shall adopt rules to implement this section, including 
18 but not limited to rules governing procedures, criteria and priorities for awarding grants.  
19 The criteria for awarding grants must include demonstration that the school administrative 
20 unit is able to implement strategies that use interdisciplinary, project-based and accelerated 
21 learning approaches. Rules adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules 
22 as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
