20SUMMARY
21 This bill establishes detoxification beds and inpatient treatment beds for children and 
22 adults with substance use disorder and requires the Department of Health and Human 
23 Services to fund those beds.  It requires that 50% of the beds be reserved for MaineCare 
24 members or individuals eligible for Medicare.  It directs the department to amend its rules 
25 to provide for the reimbursement of transportation of MaineCare members to detoxification 
26 facilities.21
