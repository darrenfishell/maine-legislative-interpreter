10SUMMARY
11 This bill allows a company to subtract the reduction in its base level of employment 
12 established for an incentive program related to the locations that were affected by a 
13 catastrophic occurrence, lowering the base level of employment the company needs to 
14 maintain due to the catastrophic occurrence. It affects the Pine Tree Development Zone 
15 program and the tax credit for major food processing and manufacturing facility expansion.11
