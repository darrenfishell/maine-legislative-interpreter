3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill would provide incentives, including grants, to large employers that have a 
6 base in this State to provide subsidized, on-site child care for employees. The bill would 
7 require large employers that receive tax credits or other state money to provide on-site child 
8 care for employees in order to continue receiving the tax credit or state money. The bill 
9 would allow smaller employers to participate in the on-site child care paid for by the 
10 incentives by means of a sharing agreement. The bill would also make the incentives 
11 available to a large employer that is unable to provide on-site child care that pays at least 
12 50% of the cost of its employees' expenses for child care obtained from a child care center 
13 in the community.4
