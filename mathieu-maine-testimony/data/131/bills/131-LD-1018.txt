27SUMMARY
28 This bill adds one member to the State Board of Education, who must be a current or 
29 former recipient of the Department of Education's Maine Teacher of the Year award who 
30 is nominated and appointed by the state board and serves for a 3-year term.
31 It directs the Department of Education to establish a working group of stakeholders and 
32 other interested parties to review the responsibilities, functions and staffing of the State 
33 Board of Education.  The department is directed to report to the Joint Standing Committee 
34 on Education and Cultural Affairs on the findings of the working group by January 15, 
35 2024.28
