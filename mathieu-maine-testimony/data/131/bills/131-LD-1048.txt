34SUMMARY
35 This bill clarifies that the definition of "timber harvesting activities" under the forest 
36 practices laws does not include activities that result, within 5 years of the completion of 
37 such activities, in a change of land use.
38 The bill provides that a municipal timber harvesting ordinance must adopt definitions 
39 for forestry terms that are consistent with definitions in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 
40 12, section 8868 and with forestry terms adopted by the Commissioner of Agriculture, 
41 Conservation and Forestry pursuant to Title 12, chapter 805, subchapter 3-A.35
