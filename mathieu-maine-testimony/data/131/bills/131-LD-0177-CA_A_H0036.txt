7 SUMMARY 
8 This amendment replaces the bill and allows the Public Utilities Commission to deny 
9 or limit access to information by an attorney in a proceeding involving one or more special 
10 contracts if the information is customarily considered confidential business information, 
11 the information is relevant to the reasons or justification for the parties' entering into the 
12 special contract and the party represented by the attorney is not a party to the special rate 
13 contract. The commission may deny or limit access to information by any attorney in a 
14 proceeding involving one or more special contracts after providing the attorney with an 
15 opportunity to be heard and upon finding that the potential for harm from disclosure of the 
16 information outweighs the need for disclosure.
