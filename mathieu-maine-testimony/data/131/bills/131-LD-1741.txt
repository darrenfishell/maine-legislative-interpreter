4SUMMARY
5 This bill clarifies that a school administrative unit must make suitable provisions for 
6 the education of every person within the age limits prescribed by law who reside in the 
7 school administrative unit. The bill repeals provisions setting a limit on the total enrollment 
8 of virtual public charter schools, a limit on the number of public charter schools and a limit 
9 on the expansion of virtual public charter schools.  The bill adds 2 categories of authorizers 
10 of public charter schools. The bill creates the educational choice tax credit program to allow 
11 for tax credits for donations made to certain scholarship organizations.  The bill also 
12 provides an exception to school immunization requirements for students of virtual public 
13 charter schools or private schools.5
