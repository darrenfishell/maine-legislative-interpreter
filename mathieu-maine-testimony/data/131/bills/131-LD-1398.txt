6§13408.  Minimum salaries beginning in 2024-2025 school year
7 Using available federal funds, or, if federal funds are not available, using money 
8 provided by the State, each school administrative unit shall establish a minimum salary for 
9 certified teachers and career and technical education teachers as follows: 
101.  School year 2024-2025.  For the school year starting after June 30, 2024, the 
11 minimum salary is $42,500;
122.  School year 2025-2026.  For the school year starting after June 30, 2025, the 
13 minimum salary is $45,000;
143. School year 2026-2027. For the school year starting after June 30, 2026, the 
15 minimum salary is $47,500;
164. School year 2027-2028. For the school year starting after June 30, 2027, the 
17 minimum salary is $50,000;
185.  School year 2028-2029.  For the school year starting after June 30, 2028, the 
19 minimum salary is $52,500;
206.  School year 2029-2030.  For the school year starting after June 30, 2029, the 
21 minimum salary is $55,000;
227. School year 2030-2031. For the school year starting after June 30, 2030, the 
23 minimum salary is $57,500;
248. School year 2031-2032. For the school year starting after June 30, 2031, the 
25 minimum salary is $60,000.
269.  School years beginning in or after 2032.  For the school year starting after June 
27 30, 2032, the minimum salary for certified teachers and career and technical education 
28 teachers must increase by multiplying the amount of the salary by the cost-of-living 
29 adjustment.  For each subsequent year, the previous year's adjusted minimum salary for 
30 certified teachers and career and technical education teachers must increase by multiplying 
31 the amount of the adjusted minimum salary by the cost-of-living adjustment.  For purposes 
32 of this subsection, "cost-of-living adjustment" means the Chained Consumer Price Index 
33 for the 12-month period ending June 30th of the preceding calendar year divided by the 
34 Chained Consumer Price Index for the 12-month period ending June 30th of the calendar 
35 year in which the adjustment is effective and "Chained Consumer Price Index" has the same 
36 meaning as in Title 36, section 5402, subsection 1.
37 A school administrative unit shall provide to the department annually on or before 
38 October 1st the number of certified teachers and career and technical education teachers 
39 eligible for incremental salary increases as defined in section 15689, subsection 7‑A, 
40 paragraph A.
