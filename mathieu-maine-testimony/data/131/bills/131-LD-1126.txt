Page 1 - 131LR1819(01)1Sec. 1. Community action grant amount increase.  Resolved:  That the Office 
2 of Policy Innovation and the Future shall increase to $100,000 the maximum community 
3 action grant amount that a project of a single community may receive.  For the purposes of 
4 this section, "community action grant" means a grant to support projects that reduce energy 
5 use and costs or make the community more resilient to climate change effects, such as 
6 flooding, extreme weather, drought and public health impacts.
7SUMMARY
8 This resolve directs the Office of Policy Innovation and the Future to increase to 
9 $100,000 the maximum community action grant amount that a project of a single 
10 community may receive.  A community action grant is a grant to support projects that 
11 reduce energy use and costs or make the community more resilient to climate change 
12 effects.8
