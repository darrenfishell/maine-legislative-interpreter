15SUMMARY
16 This bill provides that the Government Oversight Committee has access to documents 
17 in the possession of the Department of Health and Human Services relating to an 
18 investigation of the death of a child with previous department involvement, including any 
19 information regarding department contact or other involvement with the child, and that 
20 those documents are exempt from public disclosure.16
