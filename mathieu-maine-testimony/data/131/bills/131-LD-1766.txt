4 under this section shall compensate a provider for reasonable expenses to provide the

6 A.  The provider shall make every reasonable effort to provide the records and 
7 documents requested by a carrier at no cost.
8 B.  The provider shall give the carrier the estimated cost of reasonable expenses to

10 requested by the carrier.
11 C.  The provider may require the carrier to submit reimbursement for the reasonable 
12 costs under paragraph B within a reasonable time.
13 For purposes of this subsection, "reasonable expenses" means the actual costs attributable 
14 to the gathering of the requested medical records or documents, making and providing 
15 copies or transmitting the records or documents.
16SUMMARY
17 This bill requires a carrier requesting medical records or documents for utilization
