15SUMMARY
16 This bill requires the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation to establish 
17 a streamlined application process that allows a person to submit a single application in 
18 order to apply for licenses to work as a plumber and licenses to install or service solid fuel 
19 burning equipment or propane or natural gas burning equipment.16
