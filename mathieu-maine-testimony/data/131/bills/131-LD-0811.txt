9SUMMARY
10 This bill establishes a restricted senior lobster and crab fishing license. A restricted 
11 senior lobster and crab fishing license can be issued only to a person 70 years of age or 
12 older who has previously held a Class I, Class II or Class III license for at least 5 
13 consecutive years.  A restricted senior lobster and crab fishing licensee may not submerge 
14 at any one time more than 100 lobster traps and the applicant for the license or the license 
15 holder must declare the vessel that will be used for lobster and crab fishing at the time of 
16 issuance or renewal.  The license holder's trap tags are allocated to that vessel and the 
17 Department of Marine Resources is not authorized to issue more than 100 restricted senior 
18 lobster and crab fishing trap tags to a declared vessel regardless of the number of restricted 
19 senior lobster and crab fishing license holders fishing from that vessel.  The bill also assigns 
20 a license fee and license surcharge of $126 and $20 respectively for a restricted senior 
21 lobster and crab fishing license.10
