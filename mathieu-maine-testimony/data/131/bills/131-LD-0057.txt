10SUMMARY
11 This bill makes amendments to the State's list of endangered and threatened species 
12 pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 12, section 12803.  It removes the box turtle 
13 and the rapids clubtail from the endangered species list.  It adds the Ashton's cuckoo bumble 
14 bee and the saltmarsh sparrow to the endangered species list.  It adds the bank swallow, the 
15 Bicknell's thrush, the blackpoll warbler, the cliff swallow, the margined tiger beetle and the 
16 tricolored bat to the threatened species list. It also makes some taxonomic nomenclature 
17 changes.11
