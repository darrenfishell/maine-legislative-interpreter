28SUMMARY
29 This bill clarifies that hemp in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 7, cannabis in Title 
30 28-B and the leaves, stems, flowers and seeds of a cannabis plant in Title 22, chapter 558-C 
31 are food protected as a right under the Constitution of Maine, Article I, Section 25. The bill 
32 removes the limitation on the amount of cannabis a qualifying patient may possess, 
33 cultivate or harvest for that patient's medical use of cannabis under Title 22, chapter 558-C. 
34 It removes limitations in Title 28-B, chapter 3 on the amount of:
35 1.  Cannabis plants or seedlings a person may transfer or furnish, without renumeration, 
36 to another person;
37 2.  Cannabis plants and seedlings a person may possess, cultivate or transport;
38 3.  Cannabis produced by cannabis plants and seedlings permitted at a person's place 
39 of residence or at the location where the cannabis was cultivated;29
