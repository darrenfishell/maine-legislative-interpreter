4§1819-A.  Day use passes for certain veterans; spouses and children
5 Notwithstanding section 1819, the commissioner shall enter into a memorandum of 
6 agreement with the Department of Defense, Veterans and Emergency Management for the 
7 issuance of a free day use pass to state parks and historic sites to each veteran determined 
8 by the Department of Defense, Veterans and Emergency Management to meet the criteria 
9 established in Title 37‑B, section 8.  The pass entitles the holder, and the holder's spouse 
10 and children when accompanied by the holder, to admission free of charge to all state parks 
11 and historic sites.
12SUMMARY
13 This bill allows the spouse and children of a veteran holding a free day use pass to state 
14 parks and historic sites admission free of charge when they are accompanied by the pass 
15 holder.13
