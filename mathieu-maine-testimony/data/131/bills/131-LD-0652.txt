8SUMMARY
9 This bill allows that a person whose commercial driver's license has been permanently 
10 suspended due to a 2nd or subsequent failure to submit to a drug or alcohol test may after 
11 10 years petition the Secretary of State for a review and reinstatement of that license for 
12 good cause shown.9
