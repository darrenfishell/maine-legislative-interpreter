12SUMMARY
13 This bill amends the Maine Metallic Mineral Mining Act to clarify that the term 
14 "metallic mineral" as used in that law includes lithium.  It also prohibits the Department of 
15 Environmental Protection from issuing a mining permit under that law for a mining 
16 operation that involves the extraction or removal of ore or other material that contains 
17 lithium.  This prohibition is repealed October 1, 2028.13
