2Sec. 1.  21-A MRSA §112, sub-§14, as amended by PL 1997, c. 436, §21, is further 
3 amended to read:
414.  Persons incarcerated in correctional facilities.  The Except as provided in this 
5 subsection, the residence of a person incarcerated in a correctional facility, as defined in 
6 Title 34‑A, section 1001, or in a county jail does not include the municipality where a 
7 person is incarcerated unless the person had resided in that municipality prior to 
8 incarceration.
9 A person incarcerated in a correctional facility may apply to register to vote in any 
10 municipality where that person has previously established a fixed and principal home to 
11 which the person intends to return or in the municipality where that person is incarcerated 
12 if the person has been incarcerated in a correctional facility in that municipality for at least 
13 the 6 months prior to the election.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill allows the residence of an incarcerated person for voting registration purposes 
16 to be the municipality where that person is incarcerated if the person has been incarcerated 
17 in a correctional facility in the municipality for at least the 6 months prior to the election.15
