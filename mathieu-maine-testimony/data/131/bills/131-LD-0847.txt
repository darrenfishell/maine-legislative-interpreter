9ยง8001.  Trauma-informed training and embedded clinical social workers program
101.  Definitions.  As used in this chapter, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
11 following terms have the following meanings.
12 A.  "Trauma-informed training" means training to recognize the presence of trauma 
13 symptoms and acknowledge the role that trauma can play in people's lives, including 
14 by engaging with individuals with histories of trauma.
152.  Training program and embedded clinical social workers.  The Department of 
16 Public Safety shall develop and implement a program to provide law enforcement officers 
17 with trauma-informed training. The department shall work with municipalities to develop 
18 and implement a program to employ clinical social workers to work embedded within 
19 municipal police departments.
203.  Rules.  The Commissioner of Public Safety may adopt rules to implement this 
21 section. Rules adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in 
22 Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
23SUMMARY
24 This bill requires the Department of Public Safety to develop and implement a program 
25 to provide law enforcement officers with trauma-informed training and requires the 
26 department to work with municipalities to develop and implement a program to employ 
27 clinical social workers to work embedded within municipal police departments.24
