22SUMMARY
23 This bill exempts persons who use or possess cannabis pursuant to the Cannabis 
24 Legalization Act or the Maine Medical Use of Cannabis Act from the prohibition on the 
25 possession of a firearm provided in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 15, section 393. It 
26 prohibits a state employee or municipal official from assisting a federal agency or federal 
27 official with locating, apprehending, arresting or charging a person alleged to have violated 
28 18 United States Code, Section 922(g)(3) because the person possesses or uses cannabis 
29 pursuant to Title 22, chapter 558-C or Title 28-B.23
