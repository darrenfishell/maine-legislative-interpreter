Page 1 - 131LR0906(01)1Sec. 1.  Maine Technology Institute review.  Resolved: That the Maine 
2 Technology Institute, established in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 12004-G, 
3 subsection 33-D, shall review the effectiveness and relevancy of the targeted technology 
4 sectors and the industry clusters that have developed within those sectors to determine 
5 whether the laws governing the sectors and clusters should be amended.  The institute shall 
6 solicit public input during the review.  By December 6, 2023, the institute shall submit a 
7 report on the results of the review, including findings, recommendations and suggested 
8 legislation, to the Joint Standing Committee on Innovation, Development, Economic 
9 Advancement and Business.  The committee may report out a bill to the Second Regular 
10 Session of the 131st Legislature.
11SUMMARY
12 This resolve directs the Maine Technology Institute to review the targeted technology 
13 sectors and the industry clusters to determine whether the laws governing the sectors and 
14 clusters should be amended.  The institute is required to solicit public input during the 
15 review.  The resolve requires the institute to submit a report on the results of the review, 
16 including findings, recommendations and suggested legislation, to the Joint Standing 
17 Committee on Innovation, Development, Economic Advancement and Business, and the 
18 committee may report out a bill to the Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.12
