2Sec. 1.  Working group regarding large structures.  The Department of 
3 Environmental Protection, in conjunction with other state agencies and interested parties 
4 as determined by the department, shall convene a working group to determine the need for 
5 a statewide special permitting requirement for large structures over 700 feet in height.  The 
6 working group shall consider but is not limited to special environmental concerns regarding 
7 such structures, impact on the surrounding area and natural landscape, impacts on 
8 surrounding communities and infrastructure, navigation and aerial hazards, impact on 
9 wildlife, additional safety requirements and decommissioning procedures.  The department 
10 shall report the findings of the working group, including suggested legislation to carry out 
11 the working group's recommendations, to the Joint Standing Committee on Environment 
12 and Natural Resources and the Joint Standing Committee on State and Local Government 
13 by December 31, 2023.  The Joint Standing Committee on Environment and Natural 
14 Resources and the Joint Standing Committee on State and Local Government may report 
15 out legislation to the Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature concerning the report 
16 of the working group.
17Sec. 2.  Moratorium.  There is a moratorium on the building of structures over 700 
18 feet in height until October 1, 2024.  This moratorium does not apply to any project carried 
19 out by a state agency.
20SUMMARY
21 This bill requires the Department of Environmental Protection to convene a working 
22 group to determine the need for a statewide special permitting requirement for large 
23 structures over 700 feet in height.  The working group must consider but is not limited to 
24 special environmental concerns regarding such structures, impact on the surrounding area 
25 and natural landscape, impacts on surrounding communities and infrastructure, navigation 
26 and aerial hazards, impact on wildlife, additional safety requirements and decommissioning 
27 procedures.  The department must report the findings of the working group, including 
28 suggested legislation to carry out the working group's recommendations, to the Joint 
29 Standing Committee on Environment and Natural Resources and the Joint Standing 
30 Committee on State and Local Government by December 31, 2023.  The Joint Standing 
31 Committee on Environment and Natural Resources and the Joint Standing Committee on 
32 State and Local Government may report out legislation to the Second Regular Session of 
33 the 131st Legislature concerning the report of the working group.  The bill also imposes a 
34 moratorium on the building of structures over 700 feet in height until October 1, 2024.  This 
35 moratorium does not apply to any project carried out by a state agency.21
