15SUMMARY
16 Current law provides an exemption from the sales and use tax and the service provider 
17 tax for sales to nonprofit youth organizations whose primary purpose is to provide athletic 
18 instruction in a nonresidential setting.  Beginning January 1, 2024, this bill extends those 
19 exemptions to all nonprofit youth organizations.16
