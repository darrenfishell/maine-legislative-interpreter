51.  Prohibition.  A school administrative unit may not provide instruction in critical 
6 race theory, social and emotional learning and diversity, equity and inclusion in school 
7 curricula. For the purposes of this section, "critical race theory, social and emotional 
8 learning and diversity, equity and inclusion" means instruction that:
9 A.  One race or sex is inherently better than another race or sex;
10 B.  By virtue of the individual's race or sex, an individual is inherently racist, sexist or 
11 oppressive, whether consciously or unconsciously;
12 C.  An individual should be discriminated against or receive adverse treatment solely 
13 or partly because of the individual's race or sex;
14 D.  Members of one race or sex cannot and should not attempt to treat others without 
15 respect to race or sex;
16 E.  An individual's moral character is necessarily determined by the individual's race 
17 or sex;
18 F.  An individual should feel discomfort, guilt, anguish or any other form of 
19 psychological distress solely because of the individual's race or sex; and
20 G.  Meritocracy or traits, such as having a work ethic, are racist or sexist or were created 
21 by members of a particular race to oppress members of another race.
222.  Rulemaking.  The department may adopt rules to implement this section. Rules 
23 adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 
24 375, subchapter 2-A.
25SUMMARY
26 This bill prohibits school administrative units from providing instruction in critical race
