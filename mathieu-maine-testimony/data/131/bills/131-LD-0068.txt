27SUMMARY
28 This bill exempts from the sales and use tax and from the service provider tax sales to 
29 a nonprofit organization that has been determined by the United States Internal Revenue 
30 Service to be exempt from income taxation under Section 501(c)(3) of the federal Internal 
31 Revenue Code of 1986 if the property or services sold are to be used primarily for the 
32 purposes for which the nonprofit organization was organized.  The bill requires the 
33 Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Revenue Services, Office 
34 of Tax Policy to review sales and use tax and service provider tax exemptions to identify 
35 provisions that should be repealed or amended because they are duplicative or otherwise 
36 unnecessary and to submit a report including suggested legislation repealing or modifying 
37 those exemptions to the Joint Standing Committee on Taxation.28
