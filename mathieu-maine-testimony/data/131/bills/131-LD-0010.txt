31SUMMARY
32 This bill includes in the definition for certain conservation and environmental 
33 protection laws of "invasive aquatic plant" all Myriophyllum species that are 
34 nonindigenous to the State, as well as all Trapa species, all Cabomba species and the plants 
35 water soldier, giant salvinia, swollen bladderwort and starry stonewort.  Sales of invasive 
36 aquatic plants are prohibited in the State under current law. This bill also prohibits the sale 
37 in this State of any plant of the genus Myriophyllum that is indigenous to the State.32
