61.  Contract authorized.  A school administrative unit may contract with an 
7 appropriate entity to provide social work and family therapy services for students within 
8 the school administrative unit. Services provided as authorized by this section must be 
9 provided by an individual with one of the following licenses:
10 A.  Licensed master social worker;
11 B.  Licensed master social worker, clinical conditional;
12 C.  Licensed clinical social worker;
13 D.  Licensed clinical social worker, clinical conditional;
14 E.  Licensed marriage and family therapist; or
15 F.  Licensed marriage and family therapist, clinical conditional.
16Sec. 2.  Grant program required.  The Department of Education shall administer 
17 a grant program to provide money to school administrative units that contract for social 
18 work and family therapy services as authorized by the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20-A, 
19 section 6701 according to the population of the school administrative unit as follows:
20 1.  Two hundred fifty or fewer students, $3,000;
21 2.  Two hundred fifty-one to 500 students, $6,000; and 
22 3.  More than 500 students, $9,000.
23 Money received under the grant program may be used only to pay the cost of the 
24 contract to provide social work and family therapy services.
