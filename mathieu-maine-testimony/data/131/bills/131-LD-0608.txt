Page 1 - 131LR1356(01)1Sec. 1. Antlerless deer permit lottery.  Resolved:  That, pursuant to the Maine 
2 Revised Statutes, Title 12, section 11152, subsection 3, the Department of Inland Fisheries 
3 and Wildlife shall amend its rule Chapter 16: Hunting to change the antlerless deer permit 
4 lottery to provide that as applicants' names are drawn, applicants are assigned to their 
5 highest choice of available wildlife management districts until all permits are awarded and 
6 that if the name of an applicant is drawn and all of the applicant's preferred wildlife 
7 management districts' permits have been assigned, that applicant will be assigned to a 
8 district as geographically close as possible to the applicant's first choice, unless the 
9 applicant has indicated the applicant is not interested in any other areas.  The department 
10 shall model the changes to the antlerless deer permit lottery required under this section on 
11 the relevant procedures of the department's moose public chance drawing.
12SUMMARY
13 This resolve requires the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to change the 
14 department's antlerless deer permit lottery to allow an applicant whose name is drawn to 
15 be assigned to another wildlife management district if the applicant's first choices of district 
16 have already been assigned.  It directs the department to model the changes on the relevant 
17 procedures of the department's moose public chance drawing.13
