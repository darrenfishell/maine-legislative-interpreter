9 The Chief of the State Police may make available, but may not require the use of, an

11 For the purposes of this section, "electronic inspection program" means a program, 
12 designated by the Chief of the State Police by rule, that uses electronically generated data 
13 as part of an inspection and permits the creation and exchange of an electronic record for 
14 maintaining inspection information.

26SUMMARY
27 This bill makes the following changes to Maine's motor vehicle inspection program. 
28 1.  It changes the annual vehicle inspection requirement to an inspection every other 
29 year. 
30 2.  It allows the Chief of the State Police to make available, but does not authorize the 
31 chief to require the use of, an electronic inspection program for the purpose of performing 
32 motor vehicle inspections.
33 3.  It defines "electronic inspection program" as a program that uses electronically 
34 generated data as part of an inspection and permits the creation and exchange of an 
35 electronic record for maintaining inspection information.27
