SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies that the ratios established in the bill are applicable to the 
number of students at the career and techmcal education approved student grade levels in 
the career and technical education center or region catchment area and also provides that 
the student-to-teacher ratios for each program established by the Commissioner of 
Education for the purpose of the direct instruction component of the career and technical 
education funding model may not be less than the ratios established in the career and 
techmcal education full-time employee ratio provision of law
