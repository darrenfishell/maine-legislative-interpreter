8SUMMARY
9 This resolve directs the University of Maine System to establish a dental therapy degree 
10 program at the dental hygiene program at the University of Maine at Augusta and by 
11 February 15, 2024 request accreditation for the dental therapy degree program from the 
12 American Dental Association Commission on Dental Accreditation.9
