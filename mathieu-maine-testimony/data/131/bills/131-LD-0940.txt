92.  Remote dispensing at federally qualified health center.  Notwithstanding the 
10 requirements of this section or any other provision of law to the contrary, a pharmacist 
11 licensed under this chapter or a pharmacist who holds an active license in good standing in 
12 another state may provide telehealth services for a patient of a federally qualified health 
13 center in this State, including without limitation the delivery of such services by means of 
14 a pharmaceutical vending machine located at a federally qualified health center and 
15 operated in conjunction with counseling and dispensing services provided by the 
16 pharmacist.
17SUMMARY
18 This bill authorizes a pharmacist licensed in this State or licensed in another state to 
19 provide telehealth services for patients of a federally qualified health center in this State, 
20 including delivering those services through a pharmaceutical vending machine located at 
21 the health center.18
