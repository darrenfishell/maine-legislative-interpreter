3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 In Public Law 2021, chapter 151, the 130th Legislature enacted the Maine Revised 
6 Statutes, Title 35-A, chapter 34-D, which requires certain solar energy developments to 
7 have decommissioning plans.  Chapter 34-D applies to solar energy developments on which 
8 construction began on or after October 1, 2021 and to solar energy developments that 
9 undergo a change in ownership on or after October 1, 2021.  This bill proposes to address 
10 the issue of decommissioning plans for solar energy developments of a size similar to the 
11 size of developments that are subject to the provisions of chapter 34-D and on which 
12 construction began before October 1, 2021.  It also proposes to address the issue of 
13 decommissioning plans for stored energy systems developments.4
