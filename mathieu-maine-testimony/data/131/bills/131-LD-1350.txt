Page 2 - 131LR1378(01)1SUMMARY
2 On November 5, 2024, current law changes the number of commissioner districts in 
3 Franklin County from 3 districts to 5 districts. This bill changes the composition of the 
4 Franklin County Budget Advisory Committee to reflect the change in commissioner 
5 districts by changing the composition of the committee from 3 to 5 districts and from 3 
6 members for each district serving 3-year terms to 2 permanent members for each district 
7 serving 3-year terms and one rotating member serving a one-year term, with each district 
8 selecting the rotating member for one year of the 5-year period.  The bill also changes the 
9 vote for the final adoption of the Franklin County budget from a unanimous vote to a 3/5 
10 vote of the entire membership of the county commissioners. The legislation takes effect 
11 November 5, 2024.2
