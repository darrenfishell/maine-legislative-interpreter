Page 1 - 131LR2344(01)1Sec. 1.  Department of Health and Human Services to amend child care 
2subsidy program rules.  Resolved:  That the Department of Health and Human 
3 Services shall amend its rules in 10-148 C.M.R. Chapter 6: Child Care Subsidy Program 
4 Rules to:
5 1. Increase the eligibility threshold for the program from 85% of the State's median 
6 income to 150% of the State's median income;
7 2. Increase the parent fee from a maximum of 10% to a maximum of 13%; and
8 3. Set additional graduated parent fees, up to the maximum in subsection 2, for income 
9 ranges resulting from the changes in eligibility required by subsection 1. 
10SUMMARY
11 This bill directs the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its child care 
12 subsidy program rules to expand eligibility and increase the parent fee.11
