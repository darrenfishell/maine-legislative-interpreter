14Sec. 1.  Adoption.  Resolved:  That final adoption of portions of Chapter 301:  Fee 
15 Schedule and Administrative Procedures for Payment of Court or Commission Assigned 
16 Counsel, a provisionally adopted major substantive rule of the Maine Commission on 
17 Indigent Legal Services that has been submitted to the Legislature for review pursuant to 
18 the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A outside the legislative rule 
19 acceptance period, is authorized.
20Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
21 takes effect when approved.
22SUMMARY
23 This resolve provides for legislative review of portions of Chapter 301:  Fee Schedule 
24 and Administrative Procedures for Payment of Court or Commission Assigned Counsel, a 
25 major substantive rule of the Maine Commission on Indigent Legal Services that was filed 
26 outside the legislative rule acceptance period.23
