420.  School board meeting public comment period.  A school board shall provide the 
5 opportunity for the public to provide meaningful comment on school and education matters 
6 at a school board meeting. Nothing in this subsection restricts the school board from 
7 establishing reasonable standards for the public comment period, including time Time 
8 limits and conduct standards established by the school board may not limit meaningful 
9 public comment or participation in the meeting. For purposes of this subsection, "school 
10 board meeting" means a full meeting of the school board and does not include meetings of 
11 subcommittees.
12SUMMARY
13 This bill requires school boards to provide an opportunity for the public to provide 
14 meaningful comment on school and education matters at a school board meeting and 
15 prohibits time limits and conduct standards established by the school board from limiting 
16 meaningful public comment and participation.13
