32SUMMARY
33 Under current law, a veteran of a federally recognized war period, upon reaching 62 
34 years of age or receiving a pension or compensation for total disability, is entitled to an 
35 exemption on the first $6,000 of value of property owned by that veteran, as long as that 
36 veteran is a resident of this State.  This bill increases that exemption to $12,000.
37 The bill also provides a $6,000 property tax exemption to all veterans upon reaching 
38 62 years of age as long as they served on active duty in the Armed Forces of the United 
39 States, were not dishonorably discharged and are residents of this State. Under current law, 
40 a veteran is only entitled to one exemption.33
