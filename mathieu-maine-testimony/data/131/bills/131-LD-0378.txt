2regarding eligibility for children's residential services.  Resolved:  That, no later 
3 than September 29, 2023, the Department of Health and Human Services shall amend the 
4 eligibility requirements for children's residential care facilities of rule Chapter 101: 
5 MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter II, Section 97, Private Non-Medical Institution 
6 Services, in order to allow eligibility for individuals who are unable to access services in 
7 the home or community or who are hospitalized and unable to secure clinical 
8 documentation that the individual's treatment needs cannot be met by outpatient or home 
9 and community-based treatment models and in other circumstances in which an individual 
10 cannot be safely maintained at home or in the community. Rules adopted pursuant to this 
11 section are routine technical rules as defined in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 
12 375, subchapter 2-A.
13SUMMARY
14 This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rules 
15 regarding eligibility for children's residential services to include individuals who are unable 
16 to access services in the home or community or who are hospitalized and unable to secure 
17 clinical documentation that the individual's treatment needs cannot be met by outpatient or 
18 home and community-based treatment models and in other circumstances in which an 
19 individual cannot be safely maintained at home or in the community.14
