Page 1 - 131LR0759(01)1Sec. 1.  Accessory dwelling units program.  Resolved:  That the Finance 
2 Authority of Maine shall establish and implement a 2-year pilot program to encourage the 
3 development of accessory dwelling units in the State.  The program must buy down interest 
4 rates to levels below market rates for up to 100 community bank loans for accessory 
5 dwelling units until the program ends.  The authority shall submit annual reports to the joint 
6 standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over housing matters regarding 
7 the usage of the program, the first such report to be made no later than November 6, 2024.
8SUMMARY
9 This resolve directs the Finance Authority of Maine to establish a 2-year pilot program 
10 to encourage the development of accessory dwelling units in the State.  The program must 
11 buy down interest rates to levels below market rates for up to 100 community bank loans 
12 for accessory dwelling units until the program ends.  The authority must submit annual 
13 reports to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over housing 
14 matters regarding the usage of the program, the first such report to be made no later than 
15 November 6, 2024.9
