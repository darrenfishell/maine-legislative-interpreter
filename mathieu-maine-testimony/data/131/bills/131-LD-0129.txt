9SUMMARY
10 This resolve directs the University of Maine System to conduct a study to evaluate the 
11 feasibility of establishing a public allopathic medical school in Penobscot County.  The 
12 University of Maine System is required to submit a report based on its evaluation by 
13 December 6, 2023 to the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs, 
14 which may report out legislation related to the report to the Second Regular Session of the 
15 131st Legislature.10
