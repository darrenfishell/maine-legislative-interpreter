41. Disclaimer. An advertisement, solicitation, informational brochure, mailer or other 
5 promotional material by a private person or entity using the terms "Medicare," "Medicaid" 
6 or "MaineCare" must contain:
7 A. A disclaimer printed on the top and bottom and both front and back of the material 
8 in type size no smaller than the largest type size on the material stating "This is an 
9 advertisement and solicitation";
10 B. Contain in a type size no smaller than the second largest type size on the material 
11 stating "[Name of person or entity sponsoring the promotional material] is a private 
12 company that is not Medicare, Medicaid or MaineCare or any other governmental 
13 agency"; and
14 C. Contain in a type size no smaller than the second largest type size on the material 
15 any other disclaimer on the material.
16 The type color of a disclaimer under this section may not be in grayscale or other faded 
17 tone, and the font may not mimic or be similar to a font used by the United States Internal 
18 Revenue Service.
19SUMMARY
20 This bill requires, on promotional material by private entities using the terms 
21 "Medicare," "Medicaid" or "MaineCare," prominent disclaimers stating the promotional 
22 material is an advertisement or solicitation by a private company that is not Medicare, 
23 Medicaid or MaineCare or any other governmental agency, and that these disclaimers and 
24 any other disclaimer on the material be in a large type size and distinctive type color.20
