29Sec. 2.  Application. This Act applies to property tax years beginning on or after 
30 April 1, 2024.
31SUMMARY
32 This bill provides a complete exemption from property taxes for veterans who were 
33 disabled during active military service and who are receiving a pension or compensation 
34 from the United States Government for total, service-connected disability.  Current law 
35 provides up to a $6,000 exemption.32
