28SUMMARY
29 This bill, for taxable years beginning on or after January 1, 2023, adds manufacturing 
30 facilities, cannabis establishments and testing facilities to the list of cannabis businesses 
31 licensed by the State that are eligible to take deductions under the state income tax laws.29
