29SUMMARY
30 This bill increases the property tax exemption for farm machinery and provides a time 
31 frame for implementation of the increase.  It provides administrative provisions for the 
32 Bureau of Revenue Services to enforce the exemption and verify required state 
33 reimbursement amounts. It requires the State to reimburse municipalities for 100% of 
34 property taxes lost as a result of the increase in the exemption.30
