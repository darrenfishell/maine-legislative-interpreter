6 A health care provider licensed under Title 32, chapter 31, 36 or 48:

8 resources in the State for survivors of domestic violence and sexual assault as part of the

13SUMMARY
14 This bill requires a health care provider licensed under the Maine Revised Statutes, 
15 Title 32, chapter 31, 36 or 48 to receive training on adverse childhood experiences and 
16 resources in the State for survivors of domestic violence and sexual assault as part of the 
17 provider's requirements for licensure and screen all patients 18 years of age or older for 
18 adverse childhood experiences at annual physical exams and at the time behavioral or 
19 reproductive health services are provided.14
