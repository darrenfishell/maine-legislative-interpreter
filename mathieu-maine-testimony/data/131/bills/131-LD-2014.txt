20SUMMARY
21 This bill is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on Veterans and Legal Affairs 
22 pursuant to Public Law 2021, chapter 622. 
23 The bill removes the provision in current law directing the Department of 
24 Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery 
25 Operations to make recommendations to the State Liquor and Lottery Commission 
26 regarding the retail prices of spirits sold in the State and instead requires the bureau to 
27 establish the wholesale and retail prices of spirits sold in this State through major 
28 substantive rulemaking. The rulemaking must:
29 1.  Seek to ensure the bureau receives sufficient, but not excessive, revenues to meet 
30 the requirements of the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 28-A;
31 2.  Seek to ensure equitable prices for similar products across product price and bottle 
32 size;
33 3.  Seek to ensure equitable price markups based on product price or bottle size; and
34 4.  Seek to avoid disproportionate impacts on spirits suppliers and agency liquor stores 
35 due to product price or bottle size.
