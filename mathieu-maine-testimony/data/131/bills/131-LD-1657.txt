30SUMMARY
31 This bill changes the terms "expressly or impliedly acquiesced" and "acquiesced" to 
32 "consented" in the elements of the criminal offenses of gross sexual assault, unlawful 
33 sexual contact and unlawful sexual touching and provides a definition of consent for these 
34 offenses as "a word or action by a person that indicates a freely given agreement."31
