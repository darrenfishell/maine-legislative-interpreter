37SUMMARY
38 Under the Maine Metallic Mineral Mining Act, a Department of Environmental 
39 Protection-issued mining permit is required for a person to engage in any mining-related 
40 activity, excluding those activities that fall under the statutory definition of "exploration."  
41 This bill amends definitions within that Act and related provisions to require the issuance 
42 of a mining permit for a person to engage in any mining-related activity.38
