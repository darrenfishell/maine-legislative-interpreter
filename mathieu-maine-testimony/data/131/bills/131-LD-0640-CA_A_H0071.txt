SUMMARY 
This amendment retains language in the Lewiston-Auburn Water Pollution Control 
Authority charter providing that the board of the authority must assess the City of Lewiston 
and the Auburn Sewerage District for each entity's proportionate share of the expenses. It 
also retains language regarding remedies available to the authority in the event of a default 
and updates the statutory reference.
