278.  State Employee Compensation Stabilization Fund.  The State Employee 
28 Compensation Stabilization Fund, referred to in this subsection as "the fund," is established 
29 and administered by the Department of Administrative and Financial Services. Amounts in 
30 the fund must be expended to augment the salaries of state employees to provide parity 
31 between the salaries of state employees and the salaries of comparable positions in the 
32 public and private sectors as determined by a market pay study under subsection 4, 
33 paragraph B or other market pay study conducted, contracted or reviewed by the 
34 Department of Administrative and Financial Services and as agreed upon through

36 by revenue received pursuant to section 1536, subsection 1, paragraph H.  Amounts in the 
37 fund may be invested as provided by law, and interest earned by the fund is credited to the

39 year. For purposes of this subsection, "state employee" has the same meaning as in Title 
40 26, section 979-A, subsection 6.
