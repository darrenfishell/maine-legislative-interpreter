18SUMMARY
19 This bill allows the Commissioner of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, subject 
20 to the approval of the Governor, to apply for and accept on behalf of the State emergency 
21 relief funds.  It specifies that funds and other personal or real property accepted by the 
22 commissioner on behalf of the State be for the purpose of economic opportunity, business 
23 growth and other strategic investments.  It allows the commissioner to hold hearings and 
24 adopt routine technical rules with respect to the implementation and administration of 
25 authorized grant or fund disbursement programs of the Department of Agriculture, 
26 Conservation and Forestry.19
