3SUMMARY
4 This bill requires able-bodied adults without dependents enrolled in the Supplemental 
5 Nutrition Assistance Program, the MaineCare program or a municipal general assistance 
6 program for longer than 3 months to participate in certain work requirements, including:
7 1.  Working an average of 20 or more hours per week;
8 2.  Participating in an employment or training program or a job search for an average 
9 of 20 hours or more per week; or
10 3.  Participating in a placement with public or private sector employers, including as a 
11 volunteer.1
