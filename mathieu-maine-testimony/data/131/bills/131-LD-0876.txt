26SUMMARY
27 This bill provides for a limited license for a dentist credentialed under the laws of 
28 another state, a Canadian province or a foreign country to be licensed to practice in Maine 
29 for the purposes of providing services in dental clinics when dental services from licensed 
30 resident dentists are not available. The bill also directs the Board of Dental Practice, in 
31 conjunction with the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation, to review 
32 options for a similar limited license for dental professionals other than dentists credentialed 
33 under the laws of another state, a Canadian province or a foreign country and to report their 
34 findings and recommendations to the Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.27
