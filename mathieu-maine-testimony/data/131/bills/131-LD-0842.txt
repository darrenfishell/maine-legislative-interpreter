41.  Board of directors.  The authority consists of a board of 7 directors.  The 7 directors 
5 are the Commissioner of Transportation, who is a director ex officio, the Commissioner of 
6 Economic and Community Development, who also is a director ex officio, and 5 directors 
7 who are members of the public, appointed by the Governor and confirmed by the 
8 Legislature for 5-year staggered terms, who shall serve until their respective successors are 
9 appointed and qualified.  A vacancy in a position held by a director who is a member of the 
10 public occurring other than by the expiration of a term must be filled by the Governor and 
11 confirmed by the Legislature for the unexpired term. The authority may invite the State of 
12 New Hampshire and the Commonwealth of Massachusetts to appoint a person each to serve 
13 as directors on the board.
14Sec. 2. Out-of-state funding.  The Northern New England Passenger Rail Authority 
15 shall negotiate with the appropriate entities in the State of New Hampshire and the 
16 Commonwealth of Massachusetts to obtain funding from that state and commonwealth for 
17 the operation of the Amtrak Downeaster passenger rail service.
18SUMMARY
19 This bill allows the Northern New England Passenger Rail Authority to invite 
20 participation on the authority's board from the states of New Hampshire and Massachusetts 
21 and requires the authority to negotiate with those states to obtain funding for the operation 
22 of the Amtrak Downeaster passenger rail service.19
