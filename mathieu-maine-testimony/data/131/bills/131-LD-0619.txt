7SUMMARY
8 This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to provide 
9 nonemergency transportation services to MaineCare members regardless of whether a 
10 member resides in a rural or nonrural setting. It also requires the department to reimburse 
11 providers of behavioral health services and providers of physical health services for time 
12 spent engaged in coordination of care for MaineCare members, including for critical 
13 collateral care coordination for hospitalized members. It requires the department to provide 
14 reimbursement for representative payee services to members eligible for targeted case 
15 management and community support services.  It requires the department to provide 
16 reimbursement for transportation to and from psychosocial clubhouse services.  It requires 
17 the department to provide certified recovery coach and certified intentional peer support 
18 specialist services to MaineCare members eligible for targeted case management or 
19 community support services. It requires the department to submit any waivers or state plan 
20 amendments required for approval for federal match for services to the United States 
21 Department of Health and Human Services, Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services.  
22 It allows the department to adopt routine technical rules.1
