17SUMMARY
18 This bill provides that lots or portions of lots that are within the watershed of a water 
19 source that has received a waiver from filtration pursuant to federal regulations as 
20 determined by the Department of Health and Human Services are exempt from certain 
21 restrictions related to affordable housing density, the allowed number of dwelling units and 
22 accessory dwelling units.18
