333.  Immunity.  A financial institution authorized to do business in the State or its 
34 affiliate or a credit union authorized to do business in the State or its affiliate that in good 
35 faith discloses financial records to the Department of Health and Human Services pursuant 
36 to section 162, subsection 5 or the Department of Administrative and Financial Services,
