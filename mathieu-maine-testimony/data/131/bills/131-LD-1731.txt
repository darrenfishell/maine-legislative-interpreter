10SUMMARY
11 This bill repeals the provision of law that allows to be disclosed publicly an 
12 adjudication and resulting disposition of a juvenile who has been adjudicated to have 
13 committed a juvenile crime that would constitute murder or a Class A, B or C crime if the 
14 juvenile were an adult.11
