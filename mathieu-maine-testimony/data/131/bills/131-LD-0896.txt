10Sec. 2.  24-A MRSA §704, sub-§4 is enacted to read:
114.  Nonprofit agricultural membership organization.  As used in this Title and Title 
12 24, the use of "health insurance" and related terms such as "accident and health insurance," 
13 "accident and sickness insurance," "carrier," "health," "health benefit plan," "health care," 
14 "health insurer" or "insurer" do not include, unless specifically provided otherwise in the 
15 law, a nonprofit agricultural membership organization providing health care benefits in 
16 accordance with this subsection, and a nonprofit agricultural membership organization 
17 providing health care benefits in accordance with this subsection may not be considered to 
18 be engaged in the business of insurance for the purposes of this Title.  
19 A.  For the purposes of this subsection, "nonprofit agricultural membership 
20 organization" means a statewide nonprofit agricultural membership organization that 
21 is exempt from taxation under the federal Internal Revenue Code and that:
22 (1)  Has been in existence continuously since December 31, 1951;
23 (2)  Provides health care benefits coverage for the payment of medical expenses to 
24 or for members of the organization, their dependents and others; and
25 (3)  Conducts an annual audit that is performed by an independent certified public 
26 accountant in accordance with generally accepted accounting principles and that is 
27 made available to the public upon request.  
28 B.  At the time an initial application for coverage for health care benefits is taken with 
29 respect to new applicants and upon renewal of coverage with respect to persons 
30 previously covered, the nonprofit agricultural membership organization shall provide 
31 to the applicant or covered person a written notice stating that:
32 (1) The coverage is not provided by an insurance company;
33 (2) The plan is not subject to the laws and rules relating to insurance companies;
34 (3) The plan is not under the jurisdiction of the superintendent; and
35 (4) If the plan does not pay medical expenses that are eligible for payment under 
36 the plan for any reason, the individuals covered by the plan may be liable for such 
37 expenses.
