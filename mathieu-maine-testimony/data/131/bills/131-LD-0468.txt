12 The law libraries in Portland and Bangor must make their resources available to other 
13 libraries in the State and to the public.

9SUMMARY
10 This bill amends the laws governing the system of law libraries administered by the 
11 Administrative Office of the Courts.  It requires the Administrative Office of the Courts to 
12 allocate and disburse funds to a law library in Portland and a law library in Bangor for the 
13 purchase of resources, equipment and supplies and to fund the costs of necessary personnel.  
14 It requires the board of directors of each of the 2 law libraries to establish operating policies 
15 and to exercise supervision over the expenditures of funds.10
