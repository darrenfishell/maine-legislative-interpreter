3ยง13090-O.  Small Communities Tourism Fund
41.  Fund established.  The Small Communities Tourism Fund, referred to in this 
5 section as "the fund," is established in the Office of Tourism to issue grants to small 
6 communities to promote tourism and events.  The fund consists of money received as 
7 appropriations, allocations and contributions from private and public sources.  The fund, to 
8 be accounted within the Office of Tourism, must be held separate and apart from all other 
9 money, funds and accounts.  Eligible investment earnings credited to the assets of the fund 
10 become part of the assets of the fund.  Any unexpended balances remaining in the fund at 
11 the end of any fiscal year do not lapse and must be carried forward to the next fiscal year.
122.  Rules.  The Office of Tourism may adopt routine technical rules as defined in 
13 chapter 375, subchapter 2-A to carry out the purposes of the fund.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill establishes the Small Communities Tourism Fund in the Department of 
16 Economic and Community Development, Office of Tourism to issue grants to small 
17 communities to promote tourism and events.15
