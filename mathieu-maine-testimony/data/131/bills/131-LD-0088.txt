12SUMMARY
13 This bill establishes the Local Government Cannabis Revenue Fund and requires the 
14 transfer to the fund of revenue from the sales tax on adult use cannabis and adult use 
15 cannabis products and the cannabis excise tax.  The fund must be distributed to a 
16 municipality where cannabis establishments are operating in proportion to the ratio of 
17 revenues generated by the sales and excise taxes on adult use cannabis and adult use 
18 cannabis products within the municipality to the revenues generated by all cannabis 
19 establishments operating in the State.13
