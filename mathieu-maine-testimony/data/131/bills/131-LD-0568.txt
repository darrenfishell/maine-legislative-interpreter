151.  Fund established.  The Fund To Address Food Insecurity and Provide Nutrition 
16 Incentives, referred to in this section as "the fund," is established in the department to 
17 provide incentives to federal food and nutrition assistance program participants for the 
18 purchase of locally grown fruits and vegetables and to support outreach for and 
19 administration of programs that offer nutrition incentives to participants of federal food and 
20 nutrition assistance programs.  The fund is established to match contributions from private 
21 and public sources of up to $50,000 annually to further the purposes of this section.  The 
22 fund, to be accounted within the department, must be held separate and apart from all other 
23 money, funds and accounts.  Eligible investment earnings credited to the assets of the fund 
24 become part of the assets of the fund.  Any unexpended balances remaining in the fund at 
25 the end of any fiscal year do not lapse and must be carried forward to the next fiscal year. 
26 For purposes of this section, "local" means within the State.
