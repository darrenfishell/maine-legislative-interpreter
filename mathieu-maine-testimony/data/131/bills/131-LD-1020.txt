14SUMMARY
15 This resolve directs the Department of Education, no later than January 1, 2024, to 
16 update its rules for certification of kindergarten to grade 12 industrial arts or engineering 
17 and technology teachers to align the technical requirements for certification with course 
18 offerings of the University of Maine System and to allow licensed engineers to be eligible 
19 for certification as kindergarten to grade 12 industrial arts or engineering and technology 
20 teachers without being required to take the courses. The resolve also amends Resolve 2019, 
21 chapter 101 regarding final legislative approval of rules for credentialing education 
22 personnel to direct the department to finally adopt the rules no later than January 1, 2024.15
