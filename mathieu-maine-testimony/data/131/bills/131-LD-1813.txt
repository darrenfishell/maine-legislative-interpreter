Page 1 - 131LR2391(01)1Sec. 1.  State to reimburse for passenger rail service for one month.  
2Resolved: That the Department of Administrative and Financial Services shall reimburse 
3 residents of the State for the cost of traveling on passenger rail service provided by the 
4 National Railroad Passenger Corporation, doing business as Amtrak, within the State 
5 during the period from December 1, 2023 to December 31, 2023.  The department  shall 
6 adopt rules to implement this section.  Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine 
7 technical rules as defined in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 
8 2-A.
9SUMMARY
10 This bill directs the Department of Administrative and Financial Services to reimburse 
11 residents of the State for the cost of traveling on passenger rail service provided by Amtrak, 
12 within the State during the period from December 1, 2023 to December 31, 2023.10
