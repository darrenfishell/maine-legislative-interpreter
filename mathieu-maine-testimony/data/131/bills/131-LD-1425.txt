20 A public official who willfully violates this paragraph or willfully unduly hinders the 
21 public's ability to record a remote meeting is subject to the provisions of section 410.
22SUMMARY
23 This bill provides that a public body must allow for remote recording of a public 
24 meeting by members of the public on the same platform that is used to conduct the meeting 
25 as long as additional costs are not incurred by the public body.23
