10 University of Maine Graduate School, the University of Maine, the University of Maine at

16 shall serve for 2-year terms and until successors are qualified.
17SUMMARY
18 This bill replaces the current student member of the Board of Trustees of the University 
19 of Maine System with 10 student representatives to the board, as elected by their student 
20 bodies or selected by their student governments, who serve as full voting members of the 
21 board, with all the rights of other appointed members but with 2-year terms.18
