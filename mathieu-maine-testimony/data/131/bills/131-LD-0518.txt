Page 1 - 131LR2211(01)1Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2Sec. 1.  20-A MRSA §1005 is enacted to read:
3§1005.  Armed security
4 A school board may employ or engage by contract a school resource officer, private 
5 contractor or volunteer with applicable background and experience to provide armed 
6 security at a school within the school board's school administrative unit. The school board 
7 may establish qualifications and wage rates for any security personnel employed or 
8 engaged under this section. For purposes of this section, "school resource officer" has the 
9 same meaning as in section 6556.
10Sec. 2.  20-A MRSA §6552, sub-§2, ¶D is enacted to read:
11 D.  The prohibition on possession and discharge of a firearm does not apply to a person 
12 employed or engaged by a school board to provide security for a school in the school 
13 board's school administrative unit under section 1005.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill allows a school board to employ or engage by contract a school resource 
16 officer, private contractor or volunteer with applicable background and experience to 
17 provide armed security at a school within the school board's school administrative unit.15
