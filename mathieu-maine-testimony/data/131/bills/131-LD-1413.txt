4SUMMARY
5 This bill establishes an income eligibility requirement for the senior property tax 
6 stabilization program and provides for annual indexing of the income thresholds.  The bill 
7 also requires certification of eligibility by the State Tax Assessor and repeals the provision 
8 in current law that eligibility for the program may be transferred to a different municipality 
9 if an eligible individual changes homesteads.1
