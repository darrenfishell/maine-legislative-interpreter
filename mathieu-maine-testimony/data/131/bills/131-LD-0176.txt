25SUMMARY
26 This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to establish and 
27 maintain certain staffing levels at the Riverview Psychiatric Center and the Dorothea Dix 
28 Psychiatric Center and to update and maintain certain technology at the psychiatric centers. 
29 It requires the department to report annually to the joint standing committee of the 
30 Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human services matters regarding injuries 
31 of staff and residents of the psychiatric centers.26
