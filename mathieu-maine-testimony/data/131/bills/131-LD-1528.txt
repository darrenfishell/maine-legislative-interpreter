14 legislation.  The committee may report out a bill related to the report to the Second Regular

29SUMMARY
30 This resolve requires the Department of Education to establish a pilot program for 
31 school administrative units to deliver and administer the services provided by the Child 
32 Development Services System pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20-A, section 
33 7209, subsection 3 including child find activities for children from birth to under 6 years 
34 of age, ensuring the provision of early intervention services for eligible children from birth 
35 to under 3 years of age and ensuring a free, appropriate public education for eligible 
36 children at least 3 years of age and under 6 years of age.  The resolve also requires the 
37 Department of Education to convene a work group to review eligibility standards for 
38 children from birth to under 3 years of age for services by the Child Development Services 
39 System.30
