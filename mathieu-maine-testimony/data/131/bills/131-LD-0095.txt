30SUMMARY
31 This bill authorizes a designee of the Director of the Maine Emergency Management 
32 Agency within the Department of Defense, Veterans and Emergency Management to serve 
33 as chair of the State Emergency Response Commission.  The bill increases from $50 to 
34 $100 the maximum fee for registering a facility that is required by federal law to report to 
35 the commission. It also makes other clerical changes.31
