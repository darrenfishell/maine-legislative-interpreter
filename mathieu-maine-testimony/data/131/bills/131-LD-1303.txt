19SUMMARY
20 This bill exempts from sales tax sales of boats and all-terrain vehicles used directly and 
21 exclusively in commercial fishing or commercial agricultural production to a qualified 
22 purchaser.  The bill defines "qualified purchaser" to mean a purchaser who, pursuant to the 
23 law governing the refund of sales tax on depreciable machinery and equipment purchases, 
24 has obtained a certificate from the State Tax Assessor stating that the purchaser is engaged 
25 in commercial fishing or commercial agricultural production.  It also requires the seller to 
26 obtain from the purchaser an affidavit as prescribed by the assessor attesting to the 
27 qualification of the purchase for exemption.20
