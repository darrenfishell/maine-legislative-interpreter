13SUMMARY
14 This bill amends the law authorizing the adoption of state drinking water rules by the 
15 Commissioner of Health and Human Services to require that those rules establish a 
16 maximum contaminant level equivalent to zero nanograms per liter for certain 
17 perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl substances.14
