Page 1 - 131LR0834(01)1Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2Sec. 1.  8 MRSA §521, sub-§3-B is enacted to read:
33-B.  Kickboxing.  "Kickboxing" means a combative sport that is a form of boxing in 
4 which blows are delivered with the hand and any part of the leg below the hip, including 
5 the bare foot, with the intent to disable or cause injury to an opponent in a contest, 
6 exhibition or event.
7Sec. 2.  8 MRSA §521, sub-§4, as enacted by PL 2009, c. 352, §2, is amended to 
8 read:
94.  Mixed martial arts.  "Mixed martial arts" means a combative sport for 
10 compensation that features a mixture of karate, jiu-jitsu, muay thai  Muay Thai, tae kwon 
11 do, boxing, kick boxing  kickboxing, wrestling, judo and striking and grappling techniques.
12Sec. 3.  8 MRSA §521, sub-§5 is enacted to read:
135.  Muay Thai.  "Muay Thai" means a combative sport that is a form of boxing in 
14 which blows are delivered with the fist, elbow, knee and shin and in which various 
15 clinching techniques are employed, with the intent to disable or cause injury to an opponent 
16 in a contest, exhibition or event.
17SUMMARY
18 This bill adds definitions for "kickboxing" and "Muay Thai" to the laws governing 
19 mixed martial arts and boxing.18
