21SUMMARY
22 This bill requires that a member of the staff of a public or private medical institution, 
23 agency or facility or person in charge of the institution, agency or facility or the designated 
24 agent who made a mandated report disclose the same information the member or person 
25 reported to the Department of Health and Human Services to a law enforcement officer 
26 upon request of the law enforcement officer.  The bill also provides immunity for a person 
27 who made a report of suspected abuse or neglect who is participating in good faith in a law 
28 enforcement investigation or criminal justice proceeding relating to suspected abuse or 
29 neglect.22
