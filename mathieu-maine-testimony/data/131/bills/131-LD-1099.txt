5ยง1700-C.  Backup power for certain residential facilities
6 A residential facility licensed under this Title that services or houses elderly persons, 
7 persons with disabilities or other vulnerable populations must be equipped with a backup 
8 source of electrical power to power an on-site area that at a minimum allows for warming, 
9 cooling, charging electronic devices and other basic needs for the entire population of the 
10 facility.
11SUMMARY
12 This bill requires that every residential facility that services or houses elderly persons, 
13 persons with disabilities or other vulnerable populations be equipped with a backup source 
14 of electrical power to power an on-site area that at a minimum allows for warming, cooling, 
15 charging electronic devices and other basic needs for the entire population of the facility.12
