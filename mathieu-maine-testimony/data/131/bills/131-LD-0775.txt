4WATER TREATMENT EQUIPMENT
5ยง2660-AA.  Requirements for water treatment equipment
61.  Definitions.  As used in this subchapter, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
7 following terms have the following meanings.
8 A.  "Water treatment company" means a company that installs or services water 
9 treatment equipment.
10 B.  "Water treatment equipment" means equipment designed to alter the condition of 
11 water for human consumption, including by means of filtration, purification, softening 
12 and reverse osmosis.
132.  Labeling requirements for installation.  An employee of a water treatment 
14 company shall affix to equipment installed by the water treatment company a label that 
15 contains the following information:
16 A.  The name of the water treatment company;
17 B.  The full name of the employee that installed the water treatment equipment;
18 C.  The date of installation of the water treatment equipment;
19 D.  A plumbing permit number associated with the installation of the water treatment 
20 equipment, if applicable;
21 E.  The condition the water treatment equipment is intended to treat;
22 F.  The manufacturer's recommended service interval for the water treatment 
23 equipment;
24 G.  A service record on which an employee performing service on the equipment can 
25 enter the employee's initials and date of service; and
26 H.  Any applicable safety precautions or warnings.
273.  Labeling requirements for servicing.  If an employee of a water treatment 
28 company services a piece of equipment with an affixed service record as described by 
29 subsection 2, paragraph G, the employee shall enter the employee's initials and date of 
30 service. If the employee services a piece of equipment that does not have a label containing 
31 the information required in subsection 2 or that has a label with incomplete information, 
32 the employee shall affix a label containing the information required by subsection 2, to the 
33 extent the employee can ascertain the information.
34SUMMARY
35 This bill imposes labeling requirements on the installation and servicing of water 
36 treatment equipment.35
