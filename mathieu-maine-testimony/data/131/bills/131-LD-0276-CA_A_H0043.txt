7 1-A. Protection or restoration of protected natural resources through repairs to 
8 certain private roads, ways, bridges or storm water management systems. For the

10 community and economic development organization may appropriate funds to repair a

3 SUMMARY 
4 This amendment replaces the bill and authorizes a municipality or a regional 
5 community and economic development organization to appropriate funds to repair a private 
6 road, way, bridge or storm water management system for the purpose of protecting or 
7 restoring a protected natural resource by preventing storm water runoff pollution from 
8 reaching the protected natural resource subject to certain specific requirements, including 
9 a requirement that the private road, way, bridge or storm water management system is 
10 located wholly or partially within or immediately adjacent to a military installation closed 
11 pursuant to the federal Defense Base Realignment and Closure Act of 1990.
