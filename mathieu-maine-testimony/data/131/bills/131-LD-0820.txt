2proposals for quality child care services. Resolved: That, no later than January 1, 
3 2024, the Department of Health and Human Services shall issue a request for proposals to 
4 establish a quality child care program for children from birth to 3 years of age in Kennebec 
5 County in order to alleviate the long waiting lists for child care services.  The child care 
6 program must focus on play, movement, nutrition, social engagement and exploration with 
7 toys and materials and provide education for parents on their children's development.
