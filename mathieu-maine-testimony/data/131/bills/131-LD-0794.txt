28SUMMARY
29 This bill increases the membership of the Permanent Commission on the Status of 
30 Women from 17 members to 19 members and requires that the Governor appoint the 2 
31 additional members, with one member representing the interests of lesbian, gay, bisexual, 
32 transgender, queer, questioning, intersex or asexual persons and one member a person who 
33 has not attained 25 years of age.  The bill also updates terminology describing other 
34 appointees and provides funding for one Public Service Coordinator I position to serve as 
35 executive director of the commission.  28
