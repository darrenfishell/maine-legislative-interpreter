4Sec. 2.  28-A MRSA §1403-A, sub-§6-A is enacted to read:
56-A.  Shipment limit.  A direct shipper may ship no more than 9,000 milliliters per 
6 shipment no more than 12 times in a calendar year.
7SUMMARY
8 Current law authorizes a direct shipper of wine to ship no more than 12 cases, each of 
9 which may contain no more than 9 liters or an equivalent volume, to any one recipient 
10 address in a calendar year. This bill changes the allowable shipment limit to no more than 
11 9,000 milliliters per shipment no more than 12 times in a calendar year. The bill removes 
12 the bottle size limitation.8
