29SUMMARY
30 This bill excepts from the definition of "home health care provider" a licensed 
31 emergency medical services provider providing community paramedicine services 
32 approved by the Emergency Medical Services' Board and exempts from Department of 
33 Health and Human Services home health services licensing requirements a provider 
34 providing approved community paramedicine services.30
