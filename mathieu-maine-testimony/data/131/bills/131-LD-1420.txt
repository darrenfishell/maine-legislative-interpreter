4SUMMARY
5 This bill adds compliance with the Maine Human Rights Act to the standard of review 
6 of public and private schools, adds investigation of a complaint to the purposes for 
7 inspecting a private school and provides for a comprehensive review of public schools and 
8 school administrative units and private schools approved for tuition purposes.5
