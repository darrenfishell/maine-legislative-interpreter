10§12002.  Seasons and restrictions for hunting coyotes
111. Day season. There is an open season for hunting coyotes 30 minutes before sunrise 
12 to 30 minutes after sunset in all counties of the State from October 1st to March 31st. A 
13 person may not hunt, chase, pursue or kill a coyote by any means 30 minutes before sunrise 
14 to 30 minutes after sunset from April 1st to September 30th. 
15 A person who violates this subsection commits a civil violation for which a fine of $500 
16 must be adjudged. The hunting license of a person who violates this subsection must be 
17 revoked and that person is ineligible to obtain any hunting license for a period of one year 
18 from the date of the violation.
192. Night season. Notwithstanding the prohibitions in section 11206, there is an open 
20 season for hunting coyotes 30 minutes after sunset to 30 minutes before sunrise the 
21 following day in all counties of the State from December 16th to March 31st. A person may 
22 not hunt, chase, pursue or kill a coyote by any means 30 minutes after sunset to 30 minutes 
23 before sunrise the following day from April 1st to December 15th. 
24 A.  A person may not hunt coyotes 30 minutes after sunset to 30 minutes before sunrise 
25 the following day without possessing an electronic, hand-held or mouth-operated 
26 predator calling device. A person who hunts coyotes in violation of this paragraph 
27 commits a Class E crime. 
28 B.  Notwithstanding section 11214, subsection 1, paragraph M, the commissioner may 
29 appoint agents to hunt coyotes 30 minutes after sunset to 30 minutes before sunrise the 
30 following day using artificial illumination from October 1st to December 15th. The 
31 commissioner shall develop policies to make the affected public and affected law 
32 enforcement officers aware of any night hunting operations.
33SUMMARY
34 This bill does the following.
35 1. Current Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife rules allow for year-round 
36 hunting of coyotes during the day. This bill limits the open season for daytime hunting of 
37 coyotes to October 1st to March 31st and prohibits daytime hunting of coyotes at all other 
38 times during the year. For daytime hunting of coyotes during periods of the year outside 
39 the open season, the bill imposes a $500 fine and a revocation of a person’s hunting license 
40 for one year. 34

2 to August 31st, to December 16th to March 31st. 
3 3.  It amends the period of time in which the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and 
4 Wildlife may appoint agents to hunt for coyotes at night, which is currently September 1st
