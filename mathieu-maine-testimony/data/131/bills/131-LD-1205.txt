32SUMMARY
33 This bill amends the national standards by which certified professional midwives and 
34 certified midwives are required to practice and changes from permissive to mandatory the 
35 rulemaking of the Board of Complementary Health Care Providers relating to the scope of 
36 practice of certified professional midwives and certified midwives and directs the board to 
37 replace the terms "collaboration" and "consultation" with "referral" in the board's rules 
38 regarding certified professional midwives.33
