13SUMMARY
14 This bill, starting in 2027, bases the salary of the Governor on the average of the annual 
15 salary of all chief executives of states in the United States.  
16 This bill also, beginning with the 132nd Legislature, bases the salary of Legislators for 
17 each biennium on the average salary paid to legislators for states in the United States, 
18 prorated for the number of months that the Legislature is in session each year.  The salary 
19 of Legislators must be paid in 26 equal installments over a 52-week period.14
