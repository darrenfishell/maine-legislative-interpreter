3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill would revise the definition of "perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl substances" 
6 to align with federal law, require downstream notification and allow for a safe harbor, 
7 implement a process for banning harmful chemicals that include exemptions for de minimis 
8 concentrations and replacement parts, require the consideration of limiting access to areas 
9 contaminated by perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl substances and prohibit certain bulk 
10 chemicals, components and parts containing perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl 
11 substances.4
