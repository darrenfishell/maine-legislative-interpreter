14Sec. 1.  Funding for food security hub from so-called budget cascade.  
15 Notwithstanding the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 1536, subsection 1, for fiscal 
16 year 2022-23 only, prior to making any transfer to the Maine Budget Stabilization Fund 
17 pursuant to Title 5, section 1536, subsection 1, paragraph A, the State Controller shall 
18 transfer $2,000,000 to the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry for the 
19 construction and implementation of a food security hub.
20Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
21 takes effect when approved.
22SUMMARY
23 This bill provides $2,000,000 to the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and 
24 Forestry for the construction and implementation of a food security hub. The funds come 
25 from a one-time distribution in fiscal year 2022-23 from the unappropriated surplus of the 
26 General Fund, prior to the transfers to the Maine Budget Stabilization Fund.23
