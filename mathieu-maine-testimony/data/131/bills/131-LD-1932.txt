81.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
9 following terms have the following meanings.
10 A. "Broadband Internet access service" has the same meaning as in section 9301, 
11 subsection 1, paragraph A.
12 B. "Customer" has the same meaning as in section 9301, subsection 1, paragraph B.
13 C. "Provider" has the same meaning as in section 9301, subsection 1, paragraph D.
142.  Service cancellation.  A provider in the State shall provide a customer with a pro 
15 rata credit or rebate for the days of the monthly billing period after the cancellation of 
16 broadband Internet access service if that customer requests cancellation of service 3 or 
17 more working days before the end of the monthly billing period.
183.  Interruption of service.  If broadband Internet access service to a customer is 
19 interrupted for 6 or more consecutive hours in a 30-day period, the provider shall, upon 
20 request, grant that customer a pro rata credit or rebate.
214.  Notice to customers.  A provider shall include on each customer bill for broadband 
22 Internet access service a notice regarding the customer's right to a pro rata credit or rebate 
23 for interruption of service upon request in accordance with subsection 3 or cancellation of 
24 service in accordance with subsection 2. The notice must include a toll-free telephone 
25 number and a telephone number accessible by a teletypewriter device, or TTY, for 
26 contacting the provider to request the pro rata credit or rebate. The notice must be in 
27 nontechnical language, understandable by the general public and printed in a prominent 
28 location on the bill in boldface type.
29SUMMARY
30 This bill requires a provider of broadband Internet access service to provide a pro rata 
31 credit or rebate for:
32 1. The days of the monthly billing period after the cancellation of service if the 
33 customer requests cancellation of service 3 or more working days before the end of the 
34 monthly billing period; and
35 2. Upon customer request, interruptions of service to a customer for 6 or more 
36 consecutive hours in a 30-day period.30
