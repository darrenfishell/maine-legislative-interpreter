29SUMMARY
30 This bill allows a person registering an automobile, pickup truck or sport utility vehicle 
31 to choose a 2-year registration for a fee in an amount 2% more than the fee for a one-year 
32 registration or $20, whichever is less. The excise tax for the entire 2-year period is due at 
33 the time of registration.30
