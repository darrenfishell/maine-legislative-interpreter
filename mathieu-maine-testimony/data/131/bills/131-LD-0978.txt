10SUMMARY
11 This bill removes the provision of law that, on November 1, 2025, repeals the 
12 requirement that major substantive rules provisionally adopted by the Department of 
13 Administrative and Financial Services relating to the Maine Medical Use of Cannabis Act 
14 and submitted for legislative review may not be finally adopted by the department unless 
15 legislation authorizing final adoption of those rules is enacted into law.11
