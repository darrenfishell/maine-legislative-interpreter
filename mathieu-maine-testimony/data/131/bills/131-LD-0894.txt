4 Unless otherwise authorized by statute, a municipality may not adopt any ordinance or 
5 regulation that prohibits or has the effect of prohibiting an individual or entity from 
6 installing, connecting or resupplying a safe and commercially available heating or energy 
7 system of the individual's or entity's choice to serve the individual's or entity's heating or 
8 energy needs or engaging the services of a person or energy distributor to install, connect 
9 or resupply such a system. As used in this section, "energy distributor" means an individual 
10 or entity allowed to distribute or supply oil, propane, natural gas or wood or renewable 
11 resources or other related energy services to consumers in the State and "renewable 
12 resources" has the same meaning as in Title 35-A, section 3210, subsection 2, paragraph 
13 C.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill prohibits municipalities from prohibiting an individual or entity from 
16 installing a heating or energy system for that individual's or entity's own heating or energy 
17 needs or engaging the services of an energy distributor of that individual's or entity's choice 
18 unless the prohibition is otherwise authorized by statute.15
