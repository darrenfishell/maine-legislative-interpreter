2Sec. 1.  12 MRSA §6557 is enacted to read:
3§6557.  Smelts; limits; personal use
4 A person may not take more than 4 quarts of smelts for personal use in any one day.  
5 For the purposes of this section, one day is a 24-hour period beginning at noon on a given 
6 day and ending at 11:59 a.m. the following day.

21SUMMARY
22 Current Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife rules impose a 2-quart bag limit 
23 on the taking of smelts for recreational purposes. This bill increases that bag limit to 4 
24 quarts of smelts. It also provides that a person may take up to 4 quarts of smelts for personal 
25 use under the Department of Marine Resources laws on finfish.22
