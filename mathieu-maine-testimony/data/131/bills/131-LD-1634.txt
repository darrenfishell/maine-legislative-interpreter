8SUMMARY
9 This bill authorizes municipalities to change a speed limit on a single road or section 
10 of road without assuming responsibility for setting or managing speed limits on all roads 
11 within the municipality and reduce posted speed limits by up to 10 miles per hour in 
12 business, residential, compact and growth areas to no lower than 20 miles per hour and to 
13 make physical changes to a road in those areas to promote better compliance with posted 
14 speed limits. The bill also requires a municipality that has changed a speed limit to review 
15 any safety deficiencies, seek public input and report to the Legislature for a period of 5 
16 years data on before and after motor vehicle speeds, pedestrian counts and bicycle counts.1
