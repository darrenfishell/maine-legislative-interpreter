4§318-A.  Prohibitions
51.  Previous employment.  A person may not register as a lobbyist or lobbyist associate 
6 or engage in activities that require registration as a lobbyist or lobbyist associate as defined 
7 by section 312‑A, subsections 10 and 10‑A, respectively, if that person has within the

11 be assessed a fine of $100 for every day the person engages in lobbying.

20SUMMARY
21 This bill prohibits a person from registering as a lobbyist or lobbyist associate or 
22 engaging in activities that require registration as a lobbyist or lobbyist associate if the 
23 person has contributed to the transitional committee or inaugural committee or expenses of 
24 a Governor-elect.  The prohibition is in effect for the length of the term served by the 
25 Governor-elect as Governor.21
