35.  Approved educational technician III training programs; certification.  The 
4 commissioner may approve training programs for educational technician III certification, 
5 as defined by rule, offered by an accredited postsecondary institution in this State. 
6 Programs approved under this subsection may include, but are not limited to, learning 
7 facilitator programs offered through the Maine Community College System.  An applicant 
8 who successfully completes a training program approved under this subsection may receive 
9 an educational technician III certificate if the applicant meets all other certification 
10 requirements established by department rule, except that the applicant is not required to 
11 meet the minimum 90 credits of approved study in an educationally related field.
12SUMMARY
13 This bill creates an alternative pathway to an educational technician III certification 
14 that does not require the minimum 90 credits of approved study in an educationally related 
15 field as required by Department of Education rule Chapter 115: The Credentialing of 
16 Education Personnel. The bill provides that the Commissioner of Education may approve 
17 training programs for certification as an educational technician III offered by accredited 
18 postsecondary institutions in this State, including but not limited to learning facilitator 
19 programs offered through the Maine Community College System. An applicant who 
20 successfully completes an approved program and meets all certification requirements other 
21 than the minimum 90 credits of approved study in an educationally related field may be 
22 certified as an educational technician III.13
