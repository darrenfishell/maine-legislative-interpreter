Page 2 - 131LR1261(01)1SUMMARY
2 This bill provides an exemption from the motor vehicle excise tax for recreational 
3 vehicles that have electrical systems of at least 30 amps, water storage and graywater and 
4 blackwater holding tanks and permits municipalities to impose fees on exempt recreational 
5 vehicles and campgrounds or parks that host those recreational vehicles. The fees must be 
6 calculated to produce annual revenues that would offset the revenue lost by the 
7 municipality due to the excise tax exemption. The bill also authorizes municipalities to 
8 impose a fee on campgrounds for permission to construct or to lease land to another person 
9 or entity to use docks, floats, piers, boat lifts and riprap that occupy space that is within 100 
10 feet from the shoreline of a great pond.2
