4CARDIOVASCULAR PROFESSIONALS
5ยง9731.  Practice protected
6 This Title may not be construed as preventing or restricting the practice, services or 
7 activities of a person who has been formally trained and issued credentials by a nationally 
8 recognized association of cardiovascular professionals to perform actions dictated by and 
9 performed in the presence of a physician while the physician is performing cardiac 
10 catheterization or electrophysiology procedures.
11SUMMARY
12 This bill ensures that cardiovascular professionals who have been formally trained and 
13 issued credentials by a nationally recognized association of cardiovascular professionals 
14 may perform actions necessary to assist a physician while the physician is performing 
15 cardiac catheterization or electrophysiology procedures.12
