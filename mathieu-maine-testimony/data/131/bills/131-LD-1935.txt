10 B. Housing;

12 D. Transportation; and

143.  Intake and case management.  In coordination with other state agencies, the 
15 commissioner shall develop and implement a system for intake of persons needing services 
16 offered by the program, including an application form, and a system for case management.
174.  Rules.  The commissioner shall adopt rules to implement this section, including 
18 rules regarding eligibility for the program.  Rules adopted under this section are routine 
19 technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
