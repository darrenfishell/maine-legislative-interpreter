2 Pharmacy shall conduct a study to review the work practices and treatment of pharmacists,

4Sec. 2.  Report. Resolved: That, by December 6, 2023, the Maine Board of

10SUMMARY
11 This resolve directs the Maine Board of Pharmacy to conduct a study to review the 
12 work practices and treatment of pharmacists, including, but not limited to, treatment by 
13 employers.  The board is required to submit a report based on its study by December 6, 
14 2023 to the Joint Standing Committee on Health Coverage, Insurance and Financial 
15 Services, which may report out a bill based on the subject matter of the report to the Second 
16 Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.11
