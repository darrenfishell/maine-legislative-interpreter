18SUMMARY
19 This bill changes the definition of "affordable housing development" in the laws 
20 governing affordable housing density to provide that an affordable housing development is 
21 a development in which a household whose income does not exceed certain levels can 
22 afford a majority of the units in the development without spending more than 30% of the 
23 household's monthly income on housing costs.19
