10SUMMARY
11 This bill allows for equitable tolling to prevent a period of limitations from barring a 
12 petition for post-conviction review.  Equitable tolling is a principle of law that allows a 
13 petition to be filed after a period of limitations if the petitioner proves that the petitioner 
14 pursued the petitioner's rights diligently and with reasonable care but failed to meet the 
15 period of limitations due to extraordinary circumstances.11
