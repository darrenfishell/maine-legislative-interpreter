3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill would establish the Whole Home Repairs Program at the Maine State Housing 
6 Authority to provide grants of up to $15,000 to homeowners and forgivable loans of up to 
7 $25,000 to certain landlords for the purpose of repairing homes and rental units, 
8 respectively, in order to make those homes and rental units eligible for weatherization 
9 programs.
10 The Whole Home Repairs Program would replace the authority's existing 
11 Weatherization Program.  Funding for the Whole Home Repairs Program would come from 
12 general appropriations and not, as for the Weatherization Program, from the Housing 
13 Opportunities for Maine Fund.4
