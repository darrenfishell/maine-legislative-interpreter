SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the majority report, clarifies that the ongoing funding to 
provide grants to municipalities to address ash tree disease and expand tree replacement 
efforts be deposited into the Community Forestry Fund, established under the Maine 
Revised Statutes, Title 12, section 8705. The amendment also provides ongoing funding 
for a full-time pennanent Entomologist I position to provide technical and educational 
assistance to municipalities and organizations to address forest pests and disea~es.

position.It includes Other Special Revenue Fund (OSR) allocations of $585 to ACF starting in fiscal year 2024-25 to

General Fund to the CFF to fund the allocation.
