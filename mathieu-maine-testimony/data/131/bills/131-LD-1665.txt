14Sec. 4.  24-A MRSA §2745-H is enacted to read:
15§2745-H.  Coverage for BRCA-related genetic counseling and testing
161.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
17 following terms have the following meanings.
18 A. "BRCA-related genetic counseling" means the provision of services to individuals, 
19 couples, families and organizations by one or more appropriately trained individuals to 
20 address the physical and psychological issues associated with the occurrence or risk of 
21 occurrence of BRCA-related cancers in an individual or family.  
22 B. "BRCA-related genetic testing" means all genetic laboratory testing of the BRCA1 
23 and BRCA2 genes for individuals assessed to be at an increased risk for developing 
24 BRCA-related cancers, recommended by a health care provider in accordance with the 
25 most recently published BRCA-related cancer screening guidelines of a national cancer 
26 society. 
272. Required coverage. All individual health policies must provide coverage for 
28 BRCA-related genetic counseling and BRCA-related genetic testing for individuals who 
29 are at increased risk for developing BRCA-related cancers according to the most recently 
30 published BRCA-related genetic counseling and BRCA-related genetic testing guidelines 
31 of a national cancer society. 
323. Prohibition of cost-sharing. An insurer may not impose a deductible, copayment, 
33 coinsurance or other cost-sharing requirement for the costs of BRCA-related genetic testing 
34 or BRCA-related genetic counseling.
