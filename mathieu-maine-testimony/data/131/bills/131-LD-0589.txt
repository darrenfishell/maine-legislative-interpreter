3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill proposes to require the Public Utilities Commission to:
6 1. Ensure that the State's electric grid is managed to benefit ratepayers by encouraging 
7 reduction of greenhouse gas emissions and providing opportunities for ratepayers to control 
8 costs and to implement local energy solutions;
9 2. Investigate technological advances to minimize the need for new grid infrastructure, 
10 including grid-enabling connection ports on water heaters, reconductoring transmission 
11 lines, dynamic line ratings and dynamic hosting capacity maps;
12 3. Order implementation of technological advances that the commission determines are 
13 cost-effective and in the public interest; and
14 4. Report to the Legislature on how the commission stays informed about new 
15 technology and requires the adoption of specific technology to benefit ratepayers.4
