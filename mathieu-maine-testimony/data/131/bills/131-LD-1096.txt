20SUMMARY
21 This bill changes the index used to calculate the cost-of-living adjustment for state 
22 employee and teacher retirement benefits to the same index that is used to calculate the 
23 cost-of-living adjustment for benefits under the United States Social Security Act.  This 
24 bill also removes the 3% cap on the cost-of-living adjustment for state employee and 
25 teacher retirement benefits.21
