16SUMMARY
17 This bill allows a regional school unit whose reorganization plan includes school 
18 choice for grade levels that had school choice in the prior school administrative units to 
19 hold a community referendum to amend the reorganization plan to opt out of the school 
20 choice provisions. The amended plan must include a transition period for students who are 
21 exercising the school choice option at the time of the referendum.17
