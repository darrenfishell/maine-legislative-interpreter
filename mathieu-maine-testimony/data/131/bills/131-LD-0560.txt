47.  County sheriff vehicles.  A vehicle owned by a county and used by a full-time law 
5 enforcement department may be issued special registration plates at the request of the chief

10 production and issuance of the plates.
11SUMMARY
12 This bill charges the chief law enforcement official of a county with the duty to design 
13 county law enforcement registration plates in consultation with the Secretary of State. It 
14 removes the duty of the Secretary of State to design county law enforcement registration 
15 plates and the duty to consult with the Maine Sheriffs' Association. This bill requires that 
16 the county sheriff reimburse the Highway Fund the cost associated with the production and 
17 issuance of the plates.12
