SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, changes the increase 
in reimbursement rates for foster care from 20% to 15%. Rates were increased by 5% in 
Public Law 2023, chapter 17. The amendment raises the rates by 10% over the rates under 
Public Law 2023, chapter 17. The amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations 
section.
