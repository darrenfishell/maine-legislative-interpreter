26SUMMARY
27 Current law provides that the standard time year-round for the State is the time 
28 commonly known as eastern daylight time, if federal law permits the year-round 
29 observation of eastern daylight time and all the states in the eastern time zone and the 
30 District of Columbia observe eastern daylight time year-round.  This bill removes the 
31 prerequisite that all the states in the eastern time zone and the District of Columbia observe 
32 eastern daylight time, which will allow Maine to observe eastern daylight time year-round 
33 if federal law permits it.27
