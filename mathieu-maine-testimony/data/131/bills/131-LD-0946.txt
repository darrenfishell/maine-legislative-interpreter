Page 2 - 131LR1416(01)1SUMMARY
2 This bill provides eligibility for unemployment benefits to a person who performed 
3 services in a public educational institution in an instructional capacity on a part-time basis 
4 for at least 6 consecutive academic semesters that constitute at least 3 academic years and 
5 is then not employed by the institution for the next academic semester.  It also provides 
6 eligibility if the person performed services in a public educational institution in an 
7 instructional capacity on a part-time basis and is terminated as a result of the institution 
8 cancelling the class for which the person was the instructor, as long as the termination 
9 occurs no later than 60 days after the start of the class.2
