SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill and incorporates a number of changes and 
clarifications to the Lake Restoration and Protection Fund under the Maine Revised 
Statutes, Title 38, section 480-N, including changing the name of the fund to the Lake 
Water Quality Restoration and Protection Fund, clarifying the types of projects that are 
eligible for 100% payment of eligible costs under the fund and the scope of fund 
expenditures not related to those projects. The amendment also provides allocations of 
$1,000,000 in fiscal year 2023-24 and $1,500,000 in fiscal year 2024-25 to help pay costs 
related to projects that improve or maintain the quality of lake waters.
