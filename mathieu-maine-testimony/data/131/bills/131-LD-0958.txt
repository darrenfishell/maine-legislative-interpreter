2Sec. 1.  12 MRSA §12663-B, as enacted by PL 2013, c. 372, §2, is amended to read:
3§12663-B.  Unlawful sale of lead sinkers and , bare lead jigs  and painted lead jigs
41.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
5 following terms have the following meanings.
6 A.  "Bare lead jig" means an unpainted lead jig that contains lead and that:
7 (1)  Weighs one ounce or less; or
8 (2)  Measures 2 1/2 inches or less in length.
9 B.  "Lead sinker" means a device that contains lead that is designed to be attached to a 
10 fishing line and intended to sink the line and that:
11 (1)  Weighs one ounce or less; or
12 (2)  Measures 2 1/2 inches or less in length.
13 "Lead sinker" does not include artificial lures, weighted line, weighted flies or jig 
14 heads.
15 C.  "Painted lead jig" means a painted lead jig that contains lead and that:
16 (1)  Weighs one ounce or less; or
17 (2)  Measures 2 1/2 inches or less in length.
182.  Sale of lead sinker or , bare lead jig  or painted lead jig.  This subsection governs 
19 the sale of lead sinkers and , bare lead jigs  and painted lead jigs.
20 A.  A person may not:
21 (1)  Sell a lead sinker; or
22 (2)  Beginning September 1, 2016, sell a bare lead jig. ; or
23 (3)  Beginning September 1, 2024, sell a painted lead jig.
24 B.  A person who violates this subsection commits a civil violation for which a fine of 
25 not less than $100 nor more than $500 may be adjudged.
26 C.  A person who violates this subsection after having been adjudicated as having 
27 committed 3 or more civil violations under this Part within the previous 5-year period 
28 commits a Class E crime.
293.  Offer lead sinker or , bare lead jig or painted lead jig for sale.  This subsection 
30 governs offering lead sinkers and , bare lead jigs and painted lead jigs for sale.
31 A.  A person may not:
32 (1)  Offer for sale a lead sinker; or
33 (2)  Beginning September 1, 2016, offer for sale a bare lead jig. ; or
34 (3)  Beginning September 1, 2024, offer for sale a painted lead jig.
35 B.  A person who violates this subsection commits a civil violation for which a fine of 
36 not less than $100 nor more than $500 may be adjudged.

31SUMMARY
32 This bill makes it illegal to sell or offer for sale painted lead jigs weighing one ounce 
33 or less or measuring 2 1/2 inches or less in length beginning September 1, 2024 and illegal 
34 to use them beginning September 1, 2026.32
