51.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
6 following terms have the following meanings.
7 A. "Hospital" means a hospital licensed and operating under Title 22, chapter 404 or 
8 405 or the parent of such institution, or a hospital subsidiary or hospital affiliate in this 
9 State that provides medical services or medically related diagnostic and laboratory 
10 services or engages in ancillary activities supporting those services.
11 B. "Maintenance of certification program" means satisfactory participation in a 
12 program beyond the continuing medical education requirements currently in place by 
13 the Board of Licensure in Medicine or Board of Osteopathic Licensure, or initial board 
14 certification by a national or regional medical specialty board.
15 C. "Outpatient clinical practice" means any medical practice, home care or hospice 
16 organization or other nonhospital entity providing medical care through physicians.
17 D. "Physician" means an individual licensed under Title 32, chapter 36 or 48.
182.  Discrimination prohibited.  A carrier offering a health plan in this State may not:
19 A. Deny reimbursement to a physician, hospital or outpatient clinical practice for 
20 services rendered because a physician does not participate in a maintenance of 
21 certification program;
22 B. Provide lower reimbursement for services rendered by a physician, hospital or 
23 outpatient clinical practice because a physician does not participate in a maintenance 
24 of certification program;
25 C. Prohibit a physician, hospital or outpatient clinical practice from participating in any 
26 of the carrier's provider networks because a physician does not participate in a 
27 maintenance of certification program; or
28 D. Determine insurance empanelment of a physician, hospital or outpatient clinical 
29 practice based on the status of a physician's, hospital's or outpatient clinical practice's 
30 participation in a maintenance of certification program.
31Sec. 2.  Application. The requirements of this Act apply to a carrier as defined in the 
32 Maine Revised Statutes, Title 24-A, section 4301-A that executes, delivers, issues for 
33 delivery, continues or renews an individual or group policy, contract or certificate in this 
34 State on or after January 1, 2024.
35SUMMARY
36 This bill prohibits carriers from conditioning reimbursement, provider network or 
37 empanelment status for a physician, hospital or outpatient clinical practice on a physician's, 
38 hospital's or outpatient clinical practice's participation in a maintenance of certification 
39 program.36
