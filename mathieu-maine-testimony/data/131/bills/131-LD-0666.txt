2Sec. 1.  5 MRSA §13056-K is enacted to read:
3§13056-K.  Community-based Volunteer Organization Support Program
4 The Community-based Volunteer Organization Support Program, referred to in this 
5 section as "the program," is established within the department to provide technical 
6 assistance and skill development training to small start-up nonprofit volunteer 
7 organizations. The department shall adopt rules necessary to implement the program, 
8 including, but not limited to, rules that establish:
91. Eligibility criteria.  Eligibility criteria that an organization must meet in order to 
10 receive technical assistance and skill development training under this section; and
112. Technical assistance and skill development training.  The kinds of technical 
12 assistance and skill development training that are available to an organization under this 
13 section.
14 Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules as defined in chapter 
15 375, subchapter 2‑A.
16SUMMARY
17 This bill establishes within the Department of Economic and Community Development 
18 a technical assistance and skill development training program for small start-up nonprofit 
19 volunteer organizations.17
