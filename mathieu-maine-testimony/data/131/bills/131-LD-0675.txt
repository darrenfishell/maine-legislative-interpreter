9 This bill requires that a newly constructed public venue or a public venue undergoing 
10 major renovations that has a capacity of over 100 individuals must include restroom 
11 facilities at a ratio of at least 55% women's occupancy, no more than 35% men's occupancy

13 the plumbing code and to include in the code a definition of "public venue."9
