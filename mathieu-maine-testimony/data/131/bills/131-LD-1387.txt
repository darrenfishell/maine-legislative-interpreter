3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill would create the Healthy Start Maine Commission to oversee a program to 
6 automatically provide single-payer health care insurance coverage to children who have 
7 not attained 19 years of age.  The coverage provided would be similar to benefits provided 
8 under the MaineCare program and would begin January 1, 2025.4
