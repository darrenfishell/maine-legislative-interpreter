22SUMMARY
23 This bill repeals the provisions of law that terminate all powers, duties and authority of 
24 the Maine School for Marine Science, Technology, Transportation and Engineering and 
25 that require the school to reorganize from a public magnet school to an educational 
26 program-based model 90 days after the adjournment of the First Regular Session of the 
27 131st Legislature.23
