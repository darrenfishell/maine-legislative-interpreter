21SUMMARY
22 This bill prohibits health care providers and health care practitioners from denying 
23 health care services or treatment based on the manner in which a patient intends to pay for 
24 health care services or treatment.  The bill also requires health care providers and health 
25 care practitioners to disclose the cash price of health care services or treatments and allow 
26 a patient to pay cash instead of filing an insurance claim.
27 The bill also prohibits a health insurance carrier from prohibiting a provider from 
28 providing an enrollee with the option of paying the provider's cash price for health care 
29 services and disclosing to an enrollee if the cash price is less than the enrollee's cost-sharing 
30 amount.22
