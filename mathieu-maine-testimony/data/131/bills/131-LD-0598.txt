2Sec. 1.  29-A MRSA §2258 is enacted to read:
3§2258.  Accidents involving cats, dogs or livestock
41.  Notification.  The operator of a motor vehicle involved in an accident with a cat, a 
5 dog or livestock shall, by the quickest means available, report the accident to a law 
6 enforcement officer or to the owner or custodian of the cat, dog or livestock. For purposes 
7 of this section, “livestock” has the same meaning as in Title 7, section 81, subsection 8.
82.  Violation.  A person who violates this section commits a civil violation for which 
9 a fine of not less than $100 nor more than $500 may be adjudged.  
103.  Multiple violations.  A person who violates this section after having been 
11 adjudicated as having committed 3 or more violations of this section within the previous 5-
12 year period commits a Class E crime.
13SUMMARY
14 This bill makes it a civil violation for an operator of a motor vehicle to fail to report an 
15 accident with a cat, a dog or livestock to a law enforcement officer or to the owner or 
16 custodian of the cat, dog or livestock by the quickest means available.  The bill provides 
17 that a violation after 3 or more violations within the previous 5-year period is a Class E 
18 crime.14
