16 petition government.
17SUMMARY
18 This bill amends the definition of "a party's exercise of its right of petition" in the law 
19 governing special motions to dismiss by including statements made in connection with 
20 complaints under the Maine Human Rights Act, the laws governing sexual violence, 
21 intimate partner violence and stalking policies at institutions of higher education and the 
22 so-called Title IX provisions of the federal Education Amendments of 1972.18
