Page 1 - 131LR1660(01)1Sec. 1.  Department study. Resolved:  That the Department of Professional and 
2 Financial Regulation shall study ways to give financial incentives to plumbing and 
3 electrical contractors to take more small jobs for consumers. Financial incentives 
4 considered may include scaled reductions in payroll, excise taxes or state income taxes and 
5 reductions in sales and use taxes or license fees. The department shall report on its findings 
6 to the Joint Standing Committee on Innovation, Development, Economic Advancement 
7 and Business by December 6, 2023. The joint standing committee may report out a bill to 
8 the Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.
9SUMMARY
10 This resolve directs the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation to study 
11 ways to give financial incentives to plumbing and electrical contractors to take more small 
12 jobs for consumers. The department is directed to report on its findings to the Joint Standing 
13 Committee on Innovation, Development, Economic Advancement and Business by 
14 December 6, 2023. The joint standing committee is authorized to report out a bill to the 
15 Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.10
