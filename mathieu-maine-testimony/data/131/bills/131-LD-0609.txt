23SUMMARY
24 This bill requires the Department of Corrections and county and municipal detention 
25 facilities to provide residents in detention facilities, correctional facilities and jails free 
26 access to telephone calls protected by the attorney-client privilege during regular business 
27 hours. These free telephone calls must be for the purpose of communicating with the 
28 resident's attorney regarding the criminal proceeding or proceedings on the offense or 
29 offenses for which the resident is incarcerated. The bill also directs the chief administrative 
30 officer of each detention facility and correctional facility and each county and municipal 
31 detention facility to draft, publish and implement guidelines governing access to free 
32 telephone calls.24
