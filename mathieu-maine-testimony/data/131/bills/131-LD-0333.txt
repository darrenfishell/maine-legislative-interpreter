15SUMMARY
16 This bill prohibits the Department of Health and Human Services from considering an 
17 individual's primary residence for the benefit of the individual's child in determining the 
18 individual's eligibility for long-term care if it excludes the primary residence from 
19 consideration for the benefit of the individual's spouse. The bill also directs the department 
20 to amend its rules accordingly.16
