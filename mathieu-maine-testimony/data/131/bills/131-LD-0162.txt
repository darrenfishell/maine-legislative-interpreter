6§1700-I. Substance use disorder hotline and consultation and clinical supervision 
7services
81.  Hotline.  The department shall establish a substance use disorder hotline staffed by 
9 volunteers and offering on-call assistance to health care providers seeking guidance in the 
10 treatment of patients with substance use disorder.  
112.  Consultation and clinical supervision program.  The department shall establish 
12 a consultation and clinical supervision program offering ongoing consultation and clinical 
13 supervision services to health care providers who provide care to patients with substance 
14 use disorder.
153.  Consultation.  In implementing the hotline and program established pursuant to 
16 subsections 1 and 2, respectively, the department shall consult with health care providers 
17 with expertise in the treatment of patients with substance use disorder.
184.  Funding.  The department may accept federal funds and grants for implementing 
19 the hotline and program established pursuant to subsections 1 and 2, respectively, and may 
20 contract for work with outside vendors or individuals. 
215.  Rules.  The department shall adopt rules to implement this chapter. Rules adopted 
22 pursuant to this chapter are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, 
23 subchapter 2‑A.
24SUMMARY
25 This bill directs the Department of Health and Human Services to establish a substance 
26 use disorder hotline staffed by volunteers and offering on-call assistance to health care 
27 providers seeking guidance in the treatment of patients with substance use disorder.  It also 
28 directs the department to establish a consultation and clinical supervision program offering 
29 ongoing consultation and clinical supervision services to health care providers who provide 
30 care to patients with substance use disorder. It directs the department to engage the services 
31 of health care providers with expertise in the treatment of patients with substance use 
32 disorder. It provides that the department may accept federal funds and grants to implement 
33 the hotline and the program and directs the department to adopt routine technical rules.25
