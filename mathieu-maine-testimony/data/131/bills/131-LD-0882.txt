26SUMMARY
27 This bill allows an ambulance service or nontransporting emergency medical service 
28 to participate in the State's Participating Local District Consolidated Retirement Plan as a 
29 local district so that its employees who are emergency medical services providers may 
30 receive state retirement benefits, death benefits and disability retirement benefits. The bill 
31 also allows these employees to be eligible for the state group health plan.27
