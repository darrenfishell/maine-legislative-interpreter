2regarding campgrounds.  Resolved: That the Department of Health and Human 
3 Services shall review its current rules regarding campgrounds, including the definition of 
4 "campground" and whether parking lots and other unlicensed facilities should continue to 
5 be regulated.  No later than December 6, 2023, the department shall report its findings to 
6 the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human Services.
7SUMMARY
8 This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to review its current 
9 rules regarding campgrounds and report its findings to the Joint Standing Committee on 
10 Health and Human Services.8
