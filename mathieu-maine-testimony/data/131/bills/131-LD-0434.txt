23SUMMARY
24 This bill increases the fee for resident all-terrain vehicle registrations from $70 to $100. 
25 This bill also increases the fee for nonresident all-terrain vehicle registrations from $100 to 
26 $130 for a 7-day registration and from $115 to $150 for a one-year registration. 
27 Additionally, this bill creates a registration fee of $70 for members of official all-terrain 
28 vehicle clubs, as determined by the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry. 
29 Finally, this bill increases the amount of fees collected that are deposited in the ATV 
30 Recreational Management Fund.24
