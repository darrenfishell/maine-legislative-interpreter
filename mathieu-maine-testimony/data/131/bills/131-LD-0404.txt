22Sec. 1.  Trail construction on Mountain Division Line.  Resolved:  That, based 
23 on the recommendation of the Mountain Division Rail Use Advisory Council and pursuant 
24 to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 23, section 7107, the Department of Transportation 
25 shall, subject to available funding resources, permitting and municipal agreements, remove 
26 31 miles of state-owned inactive existing railroad track in the Town of Standish and the 
27 Town of Fryeburg and replace the track with an interim 10-foot-wide bicycle and 
28 pedestrian trail, surfaced with either pavement or stone dust on the existing rail bed.
29SUMMARY
30 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation, subject to available funding

32 inactive existing railroad track in the Town of Standish and the Town of Fryeburg and
