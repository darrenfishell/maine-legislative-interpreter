Page 1 - 131LR1230(01)1Sec. 1.  Department of Health and Human Services to increase limit of 
2prepaid burial contracts considered unavailable for use as an asset for 
3purposes of MaineCare eligibility. Resolved:  That the Department of Health and 
4 Human Services shall amend its rule Chapter 332: MaineCare Eligibility Manual, Part 16 
5 to increase the amount of a prepaid burial contract from $12,000 to $18,000 that is 
6 considered unavailable for use as an asset in determining eligibility for MaineCare and 
7 further provide that the amount of a prepaid burial contract considered unavailable for use 
8 as an asset in determining eligibility for MaineCare must be increased on January 1, 2025, 
9 and every year thereafter, by the same percentage as the percentage increase of the 
10 Consumer Price Index for the preceding year.  For purposes of this section, "Consumer 
11 Price Index" has the same meaning as in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 17001, 
12 subsection 9.
13SUMMARY
14 This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its 
15 MaineCare rules to increase the amount of a prepaid burial contract, also known as a 
16 mortuary trust, unavailable for use as an asset in determining eligibility for MaineCare from 
17 $12,000 to $18,000 and increase that amount yearly by the same percentage as the 
18 percentage increase in the Consumer Price Index for the preceding year.14
