Page 1 - 131LR2204(01)1Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2Sec. 1.  30-A MRSA §9 is enacted to read:
3§9.  Firearms and ammunition
4 Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, a county officer may not assess 
5 a tax or fee on any firearms or ammunition sold or possessed in the State.
6Sec. 2.  30-A MRSA §2007 is enacted to read:
7§2007.  Firearms and ammunition
8 Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, a municipality may not assess a 
9 tax or fee on any firearms or ammunition sold or possessed in the State.
10Sec. 3.  36 MRSA §1766 is enacted to read:
11§1766.  Firearms and ammunition
12 Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the State Tax Assessor may not 
13 assess a tax under this Part on any firearms or ammunition sold or possessed in the State.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill prohibits the State Tax Assessor from assessing a sales or use tax on any 
16 firearms or ammunition sold or possessed in the State.  It prohibits a municipality or a 
17 county officer from assessing a tax or fee on any firearms or ammunition sold or possessed 
18 in the State.15
