31SUMMARY
32 This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to establish a liaison 
33 team within the department to provide education and outreach to children with intellectual 
34 disabilities receiving services at school and those children's families on transitional services 
35 and services available to adults with intellectual disabilities.  This team must provide 
36 educational and outreach services, including assistance with applications for services with 
37 waiting lists, at schools and other appropriate locations.  School administrative units are 
38 required to assist the liaison team with providing transitional services.  Education and 
39 outreach services must be provided on a regular basis, at least annually, beginning when 
40 the child receiving services at school is 15 years of age.32
