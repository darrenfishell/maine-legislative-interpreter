Page 1 - 131LR1245(01)1Preamble.  The Constitution of Maine, Article IX, Section 23 requires that real estate 
2 held by the State for conservation or recreation purposes may not be reduced or its uses 
3 substantially altered except on the vote of 2/3 of all members elected to each House.
4Whereas, the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the Department of 
5 Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may sell, lease or exchange lands with the approval 
6 of the Legislature in accordance with the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 12, sections 1814, 
7 1837 and 1851; now, therefore, be it
8Sec. 1.  Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands directed to convey 
9interest in certain land in the Newfield Wildlife Management Area. Resolved: 
10 That the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the Department of Agriculture, 
11 Conservation and Forestry shall, by quitclaim deed without covenant, convey to Raymond 
12 Dolbec and Patricia Dolbec for $1 in accordance with this section interest in a parcel of 
13 land in the Newfield Wildlife Management Area totaling approximately 12.6 acres that is 
14 disputed land between the bureau and Raymond Dolbec and Patricia Dolbec and described 
15 in a standard boundary survey dated May 20, 2002 of the lands of Raymond Dolbec and 
16 Patricia Dolbec.
17SUMMARY
18 This bill directs the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the Department 
19 of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to, by quitclaim deed without covenant, convey 
20 to an abutting private landowner for $1 interest in a parcel of land in the Newfield Wildlife 
21 Management Area totaling approximately 12.6 acres that is disputed land between the 
22 bureau and the private landowner.18
