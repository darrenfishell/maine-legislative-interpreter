13EMERGENCY RENTAL ASSISTANCE
14ยง4768.  Emergency Rental Assistance Fund and Program
151.  Creation.  The Emergency Rental Assistance Fund, referred to in this section as 
16 "the fund," is established within the Maine State Housing Authority.  The fund may receive 
17 money from any available state, federal or private source. The fund may not lapse, but must 
18 be carried forward to carry out the purposes of this chapter.
192.  Program.  The Emergency Rental Assistance Program, referred to in this section 
20 as "the program," is established within the Maine State Housing Authority.
213.  Uses of fund.  The fund may be used for supplementing or creating emergency 
22 rental assistance under the program similar to that under the federally funded emergency 
23 rental assistance programs established by Section 501 of Division N of the federal 
24 Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2021, Public Law 116-260 and Section 3201(a) of the 
25 federal American Rescue Plan Act of 2021, Public Law 117-2. 
264.  Reporting.  Beginning February 1, 2024, the Maine State Housing Authority shall 
27 provide a quarterly report of expenditures from the fund and the goals and achievements of 
28 the program to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over 
29 housing matters.
30Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
31 takes effect when approved.
32SUMMARY
33 This bill establishes the Emergency Rental Assistance Program to supplement or create 
34 emergency rental assistance in order to mitigate the end of the federally funded emergency 
35 rental assistance programs established by Section 501 of Division N of the federal 
36 Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2021, Public Law 116-260 and Section 3201(a) of the 
37 federal American Rescue Plan Act of 2021, Public Law 117-2.33
