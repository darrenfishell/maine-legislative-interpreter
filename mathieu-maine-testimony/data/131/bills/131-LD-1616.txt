7Sec. 2.  Redesign of human image on state flag.  The Secretary of State shall 
8 commission a redesign of the flag that contains a design similar to the coat of arms of the 
9 State currently on the flag except depicting one of the human images on the coat of arms 
10 as a female. By December 6, 2023 the Secretary of State shall submit a recommended 
11 design of the state flag to the Joint Standing Committee on State and Local Government. 
12 The committee may report out legislation based upon the recommended design to the 
13 Second Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill amends the law governing the state seal by making the descriptions of the 
16 figures appearing on the state seal gender-neutral by changing "husbandman" to "farmer" 
17 and "seaman" to "sailor." This bill also directs the Secretary of State to commission a 
18 redesign of the state flag depicting one of the human figures as a female.15
