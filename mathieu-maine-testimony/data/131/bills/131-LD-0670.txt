4public improvements
5 By December 31, 2024, the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, 
6 Bureau of General Services shall establish a lead-by-example initiative under which it 
7 develops guidance regarding integration of bird and wildlife risk management into public 
8 improvements. When making public improvements, the bureau shall include bird and 
9 wildlife risk management projections in project siting and design. The bureau shall 
10 incorporate relevant construction features and strategies into buildings that are newly 
11 constructed, acquired, substantially altered or otherwise undergoing necessary 
12 development so as to reduce bird fatalities and risk to wildlife. The bureau shall develop 
13 and make available for public use a related design guide and annually certify the design 
14 guide's active use and submit a report related to the design guide to the joint standing 
15 committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over public improvements matters.
16 This section does not apply to public buildings and sites or infrastructure determined 
17 by the Maine Historic Preservation Commission, created pursuant to Title 27, section 502, 
18 to be eligible for inclusion in the National Register of Historic Places, including but not 
19 limited to the State House and its grounds and the Blaine House and its grounds.
20SUMMARY
21 This bill requires, by December 31, 2024, the Department of Administrative and 
22 Financial Services, Bureau of General Services to develop guidance regarding integration 
23 of bird and wildlife risk management into public improvements. The requirement does not 
24 apply to public buildings and sites or infrastructure determined to be eligible for inclusion 
25 in the National Register of Historic Places, including the State House and the Blaine House.21
