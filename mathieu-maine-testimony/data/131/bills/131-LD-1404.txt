4SUMMARY
5 This bill requires health insurance carriers to provide coverage under an individual or 
6 group health insurance policy of an adoptive parent for the person giving birth to the child, 
7 including maternity and postpartum coverage, for the duration of the pregnancy and 
8 through the 12 months following a birth. The bill clarifies that an adoptive parent may pay 
9 for the health insurance coverage for the person giving birth to the child as part of an 
10 adoption proceeding. 1
