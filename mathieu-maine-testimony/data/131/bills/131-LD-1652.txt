13 therefore, be it
14Sec. 1.  Adoption.  Resolved:  That final adoption of portions of Chapter 119:  
15 Motor Vehicle Fuel Volatility Requirements, a provisionally adopted major substantive 
16 rule of the Department of Environmental Protection that has been submitted to the 
17 Legislature for review pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, 
18 subchapter 2-A outside the legislative rule acceptance period, is authorized.
19Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
20 takes effect when approved.
21SUMMARY
22 This resolve provides for legislative review of portions of Chapter 119:  Motor Vehicle 
23 Fuel Volatility Requirements, a major substantive rule of the Department of Environmental 
24 Protection that was filed outside the legislative rule acceptance period.22
