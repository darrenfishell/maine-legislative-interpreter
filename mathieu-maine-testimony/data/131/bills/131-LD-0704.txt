Page 1 - 131LR2000(01)1Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2Sec. 1.  29-A MRSA §453, sub-§2, as amended by PL 2021, c. 216, §11, is further 
3 amended to read:
42.  Fee.  The annual administrative fee for a vanity registration plate is $25 in addition 
5 to the regular motor vehicle registration fee.  A one-time administrative fee for a vanity 
6 registration plate of $50 must be paid at the time the plate is issued in addition to the regular 
7 motor vehicle registration fee.  The administrative fee must be credited to the General 
8 Highway Fund, except that $10 of the administrative fee must be transferred on a quarterly 
9 basis by the Treasurer of State to the TransCap Trust Fund established by Title 30‑A, 
10 section 6006‑G.  A sum sufficient to defray the cost of this program must be allocated 
11 annually from the General Highway Fund.
12SUMMARY
13 This bill removes the annual $25 administrative fee for a vanity registration plate and 
14 replaces it with a one-time administrative fee of $50.13
