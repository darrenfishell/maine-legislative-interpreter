Page 1 - 131LR0942(01)1Preamble.  The Constitution of Maine, Article IX, Section 23 requires that real estate 
2 held by the State for conservation or recreation purposes may not be reduced or its uses 
3 substantially altered except on the vote of 2/3 of all members elected to each House.
4Whereas, the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the Department of 
5 Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may sell or exchange lands with the approval of 
6 the Legislature in accordance with the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 12, sections 1814, 
7 1837 and 1851; now, therefore, be it
8Sec. 1.  Director of Bureau of Parks and Lands directed to convey certain 
9land used for community recreation areas along Piggery Road in Augusta, 
10Maine. Resolved: That the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the 
11 Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry shall convey by quitclaim deed 
12 without covenant to the Capitol Area Recreation Association for $1 on July 1, 2024 all of 
13 the State's interest in a parcel of land in Augusta, Maine comprising playing fields and other 
14 community recreation areas along Piggery Road, which is currently the subject of a lease 
15 dated January 1, 1992 between the Bureau of Parks and Lands and the Capitol Area 
16 Recreation Association.
