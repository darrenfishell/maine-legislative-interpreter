Page 1 - 131LR0911(01)1Preamble.  The Constitution of Maine, Article IX, Section 23 requires that real estate 
2 held by the State for conservation or recreation purposes may not be reduced or its uses 
3 substantially altered except on the vote of 2/3 of all members elected to each House.
4Whereas, the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the Department of 
5 Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may sell, lease or exchange lands with the approval 
6 of the Legislature in accordance with the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 12, sections 1814, 
7 1837 and 1851; now, therefore, be it
8Sec. 1.  Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands is authorized, but not 
9directed, to lease certain land within Upper Enchanted Township, Somerset 
10County.  Resolved: That the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the 
11 Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may lease to Central Maine Power 
12 Company, on such terms and conditions as the director may require, for existing radio 
13 telecommunication facilities on certain property of approximately 0.4 acres on the Coburn 
14 Mountain public reserved lands within Upper Enchanted Township, Somerset County 
15 originally subject to a lease signed by the bureau in 1999.
