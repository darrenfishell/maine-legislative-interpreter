20Sec. 2.  Property interests that may be conveyed. Resolved: That the state 
21 property authorized to be sold or leased is a parcel of land with improvements thereon, 
22 located in the Town of Machiasport consisting of approximately 9.58 acres of land, more 
23 or less, as conveyed to the State of Maine, Department of Corrections, on February 20, 
24 1985, and recorded in the Washington County Registry of Deeds as "Parcel 6 Family 
25 Housing Area" in Book 1314, Page 276, as identified on a survey dated September 21, 2022 
26 by Haley Ward, excepting and reserving to the State ownership of the well, water lines, 
27 electric power infrastructure and any other installations serving the well and operation of 
28 the well, as shown on the Haley Ward survey.  The well is located approximately 88 feet 
29 southerly of the northerly line of the property and 202 feet easterly of the westerly line. The 
30 State reserves an exclusive permanent easement that runs with the land and is binding on 
31 the purchaser and the purchaser's heirs, successors and assigns for access to the well, water 
32 lines, electric power infrastructure and any other installations serving the well, together 
33 with the right to maintain, repair or replace the well, water lines, electric power 
34 infrastructure and other installations serving the well and for access to a 30-foot buffer area 
35 surrounding the well together with the right to place a fence along the buffer line.
36Sec. 3.  Property to be sold "as is". Resolved: That the commissioner may 
37 negotiate and execute leases and purchase and sale agreements upon terms the 
38 commissioner considers appropriate; however, the state property described in section 2 
39 must be sold "as is," with no representations or warranties. Title must be transferred by 
40 quitclaim deed without covenant or release deed and executed by the commissioner.
41Sec. 4.  Appraisal. Resolved: That the commissioner shall have the current market 
42 value of the state property described in section 2 determined by a broker opinion of value 
43 and current comparative market analysis. The commissioner may list the state property for 
44 sale, lease or auction with a private real estate broker or auction company and negotiate 
Page 2 - 131LR0118(01)45 any sale or lease, solicit bids, sell directly to a purchaser or enter directly into leases with 
46 tenants. The commissioner may reject any offer.
3Sec. 5. Proceeds. Resolved: That any proceeds from the sale or lease of the state 
4 property described in section 2 must be used to reimburse the Department of Administrative 
5 and Financial Services administrative expenses and be deposited into the Bureau of General 
6 Services capital repair and improvement account for capital improvements, and the 
7 remaining proceeds must be deposited into the Department of Corrections capital account.
8Sec. 6.  Repeal. Resolved: That this resolve is repealed 5 years from its effective 
9 date.
10SUMMARY
11 This resolve authorizes the Department of Administrative and Financial Services to 
12 convey the interests of the State in a portion of the larger Downeast Correctional Facility 
13 parcel located in Machiasport.1
