Amend the bill by striking out all of section 1. 
Amend the bill by striking out all of section 3. 
Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section 
number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, removes the definition 
of "enclosure" from the bill. It also removes the proposed changes to the section that 
prohibits a minor from being admitted to any pari-mutuel sales area and instead retains the 
prohibition on a minor's being admitted to any pari-mutuel enclosure.
