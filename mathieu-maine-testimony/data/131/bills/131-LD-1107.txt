Page 1 - 131LR1084(01)1Sec. 1. Maine State Housing Authority to conduct 2-year rent payment 
2reporting pilot project. Resolved:  That the Maine State Housing Authority shall 
3 develop and administer through a contract with an appropriate vendor a 2-year pilot project 
4 to facilitate rent payment history reporting to credit reporting agencies for purposes of 
5 establishing or improving the credit ratings of low-income residential tenants through 
6 voluntary participation of residential landlords and tenants in the State in submitting rent 
7 payment histories of tenants. The project may not charge a fee to a landlord or a tenant for 
8 participation in the program. The Maine State Housing Authority shall seek to enroll as 
9 many residential tenants as possible who are persons of low income in the State and who 
10 broadly represent the various regions of the State, including urban, suburban and rural 
11 areas. For purposes of this section, "persons of low income" has the same meaning as in 
12 the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, section 4702, subsection 12.
13Sec. 2. Maine State Housing Authority to issue a request for proposals. 
14Resolved:  That the Maine State Housing Authority shall issue a request for proposals for 
15 a vendor to provide an automated subscription-based service to collect, process and submit 
16 to appropriate credit reporting agencies rent payment histories collected under the pilot 
17 project described in section 1. The request for proposals must favor proposals that provide 
18 value in services delivered per unit cost, equity and impartiality in its processes, compliance 
19 with federal and state laws and privacy protections for pilot project participants.
20Sec. 3. Report. Resolved:  That by December 3, 2025, the Maine State Housing 
21 Authority shall submit a report on the pilot project detailing the effectiveness of collecting 
22 and reporting rent payment histories and analysis of any improvement in the credit ratings 
23 of tenant participants along with any recommended legislation to the joint standing 
24 committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over labor and housing. The joint standing 
25 committee may report out legislation based upon the report to the Second Regular Session 
26 of the 132nd Legislature.

33SUMMARY
34 This resolve requires the Maine State Housing Authority to implement and administer 
35 a 2-year pilot project to collect and report tenant rent payment histories to appropriate credit 
36 reporting agencies with the purpose of improving the credit ratings of low-income 
37 residential tenants. The resolve includes a one-time appropriation of $250,000 to contract 
38 with a vendor and pay subscription costs to implement and administer the project.33
