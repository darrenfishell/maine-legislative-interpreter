6SUMMARY
7 This bill adds dental hygiene diagnosis to the procedures a dental hygienist, an 
8 independent practice dental hygienist and a public health dental hygienist may perform 
9 under the general supervision of a dentist.  The bill also authorizes a dental hygienist to 
10 administer local anesthesia or nitrous oxide analgesia under the general supervision of a 
11 dentist instead of under the direct supervision of a dentist.7
