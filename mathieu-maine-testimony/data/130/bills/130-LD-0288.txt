2Sec. 1.  36 MRSA §5122, sub-§2, ¶UU is enacted to read:
3 UU.  For tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2020, an amount equal to the 
4 amount of income attributable to paycheck protection program loan forgiveness

8 HH.  For tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2020, an amount equal to the

10 excluded from federal gross income under Section 1106(i) of the federal Coronavirus 
11 Aid, Relief, and Economic Security Act, Public Law 116-136.
12SUMMARY
13 This bill conforms Maine tax law to federal income tax treatment of loan forgiveness 
14 under Section 1106(i) of the federal Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security Act, 
15 Public Law 116-136, by providing an income tax exclusion for income attributable to 
16 paycheck protection program loan forgiveness that is excluded from federal gross income.
