3ยง1612.  Sale of certain plastic water bottles prohibited
41.  Definition.  As used in this section, "plastic" means a synthetic material made from 
5 fossil fuel or organic-based polymers, including polyethylene, polystyrene, polypropylene 
6 and polycarbonate, that can be molded or blown into specific shapes.
72.  Plastic water bottles prohibited.  A person may not sell or offer to sell or distribute 
8 for promotional purposes uncarbonated, unflavored drinking water in containers of one liter 
9 or less that are made of plastic.
103.  Penalty.  A person who violates this section commits a civil violation subject to 
11 section 349. 
124.  Exemption.  This section does not apply to a person who sells, offers to sell or 
13 distributes drinking water for the immediate preservation of public health or safety during 
14 an event for which a Governor's state of emergency proclamation has been issued pursuant 
15 to Title 37-B, section 742. 
16SUMMARY
17 This bill prohibits the sale, offering for sale and distribution for promotional purposes 
18 of uncarbonated, unflavored drinking water in containers of one liter or less that are made 
19 of plastic, except for the immediate preservation of public health or safety during an event 
20 for which a Governor's state of emergency proclamation has been issued.17
