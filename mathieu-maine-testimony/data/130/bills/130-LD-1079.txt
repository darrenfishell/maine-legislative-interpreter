Page 1 - 130LR0891(01)1Sec. 1.  Review concerning effects of electric industry deregulation.  
2Resolved:  That the Public Utilities Commission shall establish a commission to review 
3 the effects of policies enacted in the State in the late 1990s and early 2000s relating to the 
4 deregulation and restructuring of the State's electric industry.  The Public Utilities 
5 Commission shall report that commission's findings to the Joint Standing Committee on 
6 Energy, Utilities and Technology no later than December 1, 2021.  The Joint Standing 
7 Committee on Energy, Utilities and Technology may report out legislation on the subject 
8 matter of the report to the Second Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.
9SUMMARY
10 This resolve requires the Public Utilities Commission to establish a commission to 
11 review the effects of policies enacted in the State relating to the deregulation and 
12 restructuring of the State's electric industry.  The commission must report that 
13 commission's findings to the Joint Standing Committee on Energy, Utilities and 
14 Technology no later than December 1, 2021, and the committee may report out legislation 
15 on the subject matter of the report to the Second Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.10
