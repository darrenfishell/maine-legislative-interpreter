Page 1 - 130LR0009(01)1Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2Sec. 1.  29-A MRSA §2054, sub-§2, ¶F, as amended by PL 2017, c. 21, §2, is 
3 further amended by enacting a new subparagraph (5) to read:
4 (5)  Notwithstanding subsection 3, a municipality may authorize a harbor master 
5 or deputy harbor master appointed under Title 38, section 1 or 2, respectively, to 
6 use a siren and a combination of red and white auxiliary lights when responding to 
7 a watercraft emergency. 
8SUMMARY
9 This bill allows a municipality to authorize a harbor master or deputy harbor master to 
10 use a siren and a combination of red and white auxiliary lights on a vehicle when 
11 responding to a watercraft emergency.
