3ยง1483-B.  Local option exemption for volunteer firefighters
4 A municipality may by ordinance exempt from the annual excise tax imposed pursuant 
5 to section 1482 one vehicle owned or jointly owned by a resident volunteer firefighter.  To 
6 be entitled to an exemption, a resident volunteer firefighter must annually present 
7 documentation to a designated municipal official demonstrating that during the previous 
8 12 months the resident actively served as a volunteer firefighter and used the vehicle on 
9 which the exemption is being requested to respond to fire calls.  A municipal ordinance 
10 may enumerate additional eligibility criteria, including, but not limited to, duration of 
11 service, minimum call response and training requirements.  For the purpose of this section, 
12 "volunteer firefighter" means a member of a separately incorporated volunteer fire 
13 department or member of a municipal fire department who receives only nominal 
14 compensation or reimbursement for actual costs incurred for responding to fire calls or 
15 participating in required training and meetings.
16SUMMARY
17 This bill authorizes municipalities to adopt an ordinance extending a vehicle excise tax 
18 exemption to volunteer firefighters.
