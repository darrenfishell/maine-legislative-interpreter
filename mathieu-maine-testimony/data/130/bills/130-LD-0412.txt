33SUMMARY
34 This bill permits revenue from tax increment financing districts to be used for the 
35 construction or renovation of municipal offices or other buildings and limits the revenue 
36 that may be used to $1,000,000 during a 20-year period for any one tax increment financing 
37 district.  The bill also corrects a conflict in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, section 
38 5225, subsection 1, paragraph C, which was amended by Public Law 2019, chapter 604 
39 and chapter 625, by incorporating the changes made by both laws.
