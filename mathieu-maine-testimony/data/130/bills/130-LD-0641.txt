11ยง1064.  Contributions, expenditures and participation by foreign nationals prohibited
121.  Definition.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, "foreign 
13 national" means:
14 A.  An individual who is not a citizen of the United States; or
15 B.  A foreign entity, including a government, organization or business that holds, owns 
16 or otherwise has direct or indirect majority control of a foreign entity.
172.  Contribution prohibited.  A foreign national may not make, directly or indirectly, 
18 a contribution of money or anything of value to influence a referendum.
193.  Solicitation or acceptance of contribution prohibited.  A person may not solicit, 
20 accept or receive a contribution described in subsection 2 from a foreign national.
214.  Expenditure prohibited.  A foreign national may not make, directly or indirectly, 
22 an expenditure to influence a referendum.
235.  Participation prohibited.  A foreign national may not direct, dictate, control or 
24 directly or indirectly participate in the decision-making process of any person with regard 
25 to that person's activities to influence a referendum, such as decisions concerning the 
26 making of contributions or expenditures to influence a referendum.
276.  Violation; penalty.  Notwithstanding section 1004, subsection 1, a person that 
28 violates this section commits a civil violation for which a fine of not more than $100,000 
29 or twice the amount of the contribution or expenditure involved in the violation, whichever 
30 is greater, may be adjudged.
31Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
32 takes effect when approved.
33SUMMARY
34 This bill provides that a foreign national may not:
35 1.  Make, directly or indirectly, a contribution of money or anything of value to 
36 influence a referendum;
37 2.  Make, directly or indirectly, an expenditure to influence a referendum; or
38 3.  Direct, dictate, control or directly or indirectly participate in the decision-making 
39 process of any person with regard to that person's activities to influence a referendum, such 34
