25SUMMARY
26 This bill limits a state of emergency proclaimed by the Governor to 30 days.  It requires 
27 subsequent renewals of a state of emergency to receive approval from 2/3 of the 
28 membership of the Legislature.  It also amends the Governor's powers during a state of 
29 emergency.
