4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill would direct the Department of Environmental Protection to create a plan to 
6 compost all of the State's food waste and bagged grass clippings, leaves and other woody 
7 debris in convenient places in each of the counties of the State.  The department would be 
8 directed to submit this plan and implementing legislation for presentation to the Second 
9 Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.
