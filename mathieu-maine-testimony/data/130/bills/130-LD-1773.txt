3SUMMARY
4 This bill designates rules adopted by the Department of Health and Human Services 
5 before January 1, 2023 relating to implementing the federal requirements of Section 
6 1915(c) of the United States Social Security Act for certain home and community-based 
7 services as major substantive rules.  It prohibits the Department of Health and Human 
8 Services from adopting or enforcing the routine technical rules proposed on August 10, 
9 2021 relating to these home and community-based services.  The rules apply to an adult 
10 MaineCare member with a physical disability or an adult who is elderly who is receiving 
11 MaineCare services under a waiver granted by the federal Department of Health and 
12 Human Services, Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services and are intended to include 
13 individuals receiving services under the MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter II, Sections 
14 18, 19, 20, 21 and 29.4
