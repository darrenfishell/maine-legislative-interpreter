35SUMMARY
36 This bill authorizes a person participating in the State’s group health plan who becomes 
37 eligible for coverage through the federal military health insurance program to decline 
38 coverage and reenroll in coverage at a later date. The bill clarifies that the person remains 
39 eligible for coverage under the State’s group health plan as a retiree and applies similar 36
