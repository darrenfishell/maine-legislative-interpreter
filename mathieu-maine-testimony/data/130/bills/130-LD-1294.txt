27SUMMARY
28 This bill amends the Maine Human Rights Act to provide protections against 
29 discrimination in employment and housing against a person who has sought and received 
30 an order of protection under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 19-A, chapter 101.  All 
31 remedies currently available under the Maine Human Rights Act, including a private right 
32 of action and attorney's fees, are available for these persons.28
