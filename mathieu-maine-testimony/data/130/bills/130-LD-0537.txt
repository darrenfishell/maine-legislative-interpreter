3ยง1952.  Fallen State Trooper Dedication Program
4 The Fallen State Trooper Dedication Program is established.  The Department of 
5 Transportation and the Maine Turnpike Authority shall designate one mile of the Interstate 
6 95 highway system to a state police officer who dies in the performance of that officer's 
7 duty within one year of the death.  The one-mile section must be located as close as possible 
8 to the site of the officer's death, and the department or the authority shall post and maintain 
9 appropriate signs on both the northbound and southbound sides of the highway.
10 The Maine State Police shall notify the Department of Transportation and the Maine 
11 Turnpike Authority of a deceased state police officer who is eligible for designation under 
12 this section.
13Sec. 2.  Fallen State Trooper Dedication Program.  Notwithstanding the Maine 
14 Revised Statutes, Title 23, section 1952, the Maine State Police shall notify the Department 
15 of Transportation and the Maine Turnpike Authority of the identity of any state police 
16 officer who died in the performance of that officer's duty before the effective date of this 
17 Act and who is eligible for the Fallen State Trooper Dedication Program established under 
18 that section, and the department and the authority shall designate one mile of the Interstate 
19 95 highway system to that officer required under that program.
20SUMMARY
21 This bill establishes the Fallen State Trooper Dedication Program, which requires the 
22 Department of Transportation and the Maine Turnpike Authority to designate and place 
23 appropriate signs on one mile of the Interstate 95 highway system for each state police 
24 officer who died in the performance of that officer's duty.21
