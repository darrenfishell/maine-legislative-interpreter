6SUMMARY
7 This bill eliminates boards and commissions that did not file annual reports in either 
8 2020 or 2021 or that filed a report that indicated inactivity for 2020 and 2021. The boards 
9 and commissions being eliminated are:
10 1.  Animal Welfare Advisory Council
11 2.  Clean-up and Response Fund Review Board
12 3.  Combat Sports Authority of Maine
13 4.  Commission To End Student Hunger
14 5.  Driver Education and Evaluation Programs Appeals Board
15 6.  Maine State Film Commission
16 7.  Nutrient Management Review Board
17 8.  Potato Marketing Improvement Fund Committee
18 9.  Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics Council
19 10.  Seaweed Fisheries Advisory Council
20 This bill is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on State and Local 
21 Government pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 12006, subsection 2.  
22 As authorized by the law, the bill eliminates boards and commissions that have not reported 
23 on their activities to the Secretary of State during the preceding 24 months as required or 
24 have reported to the Secretary of State being inactive during the preceding 24 months.  
25 The committee has not taken a position on the substance of the bill.  By reporting out 
26 this bill the committee is not suggesting and does not intend to suggest that it agrees or 
27 disagrees with any aspect of this bill. The committee is reporting out the bill for the sole 
28 purpose of turning the draft into a printed bill that can be referred to the committee for an 
29 appropriate public hearing and subsequent processing in the normal course. The committee 
30 is taking this action to ensure clarity and transparency in the legislative review of the 
31 proposals contained in this bill.
32 This bill does not include any amendments to or repeal of sections of the Maine Revised 
33 Statutes necessary to correct and update any references and cross-references in the statutes 
34 to provisions of law repealed in this bill.7
