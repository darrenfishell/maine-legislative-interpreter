3ยง13027.  Lifetime teaching certificate
41.  Application, review and certificate issuance.  A person who meets the eligibility 
5 requirements of subsection 2 may apply for a lifetime teaching certificate by filing an 
6 application with the department.  The commissioner shall review the application, and if the 
7 commissioner approves the application, the commissioner shall reissue the applicant's 
8 professional teacher certificate as a lifetime teaching certificate.
92.  Eligibility.  To be eligible for a lifetime teaching certificate, a person must:
10 A.  Hold a valid professional teacher certificate issued pursuant to this Title; and
11 B.  Have taught in the State within the area of endorsement on the person's professional 
12 teacher certificate for at least 20 years while maintaining a teaching certificate issued 
13 pursuant to this Title.
143.  State and federal laws, rules and regulations.  A lifetime teaching certificate 
15 holder is subject to all state and federal laws, rules and regulations related to the holder's 
16 certification, including, but not limited to, laws, rules and regulations related to criminal 
17 history record checks and special education training.
184.  Proof of qualifications.  The department may not require a holder of a lifetime 
19 teaching certificate to provide proof of professional development, continuing education 
20 credits, education or other similar qualifications to maintain the certificate.
215.  Revocation.  A lifetime teaching certificate is subject to the same laws for 
22 revocation as any teaching certificate.
236.  Renewal not required.  A person who holds a lifetime teaching certificate is not 
24 required to renew the certificate.
257.  No annual fee.  The department may assess a one-time fee for a lifetime teaching 
26 certificate but may not require a holder of a lifetime teaching certificate to pay an annual 
27 fee to maintain the certificate.
28Sec. 2.  Rules.  The Department of Education shall adopt rules setting a reasonable 
29 one-time fee for a lifetime teaching certificate under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 
30 20-A, section 13027, subsection 7.  Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine 
31 technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
32SUMMARY
33 This bill authorizes the Commissioner of Education to issue lifetime teaching 
34 certificates to teachers in the State who have maintained a teaching certificate and taught 
35 in the State for at least 20 years.
