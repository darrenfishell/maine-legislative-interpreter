4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill proposes to require the Department of Health and Human Services to provide 
6 timely updates on the new rules for the Medicaid home and community-based services 
7 waiver spending plans for enhanced federal medical assistance percentages using funds 
8 provided under the American Rescue Plan Act.  The bill also proposes to require the 
9 Department of Health and Human Services to provide consistent and accurate information 
10 regarding the spending plans to providers through its communications portal.4
