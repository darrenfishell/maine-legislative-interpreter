41.  Establish criteria defining unserved and underserved areas.  The authority, by 
5 rule adopted pursuant to section 9205, subsection 3, shall establish criteria to define 
6 unserved and underserved areas with respect to broadband service.  Criteria established by 
7 the authority to define unserved areas must include any geographic area where broadband 
8 service is not available or where the speeds available are less than 100 megabits per second 
9 for downloads and less than 100 megabits per second for uploads.  Criteria established by 
10 the authority to define unserved and underserved areas must include the percentage of 
11 households with access to broadband service within a municipality or other appropriate 
12 geographic area.  The authority shall use these criteria to determine those areas of the State 
13 that are unserved or underserved.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill clarifies the meaning of "unserved area" within the State's broadband service 
16 laws by providing that the criteria established by the ConnectMaine Authority by rule to 
17 define that term must include any geographic area where broadband service is not available 
18 or where the speeds available are less than 100 megabits per second for downloads and less 
19 than 100 megabits per second for uploads.
