4 (6)  A wrecker may be equipped with a flashing red auxiliary light mounted on top

9SUMMARY
10 This bill authorizes wreckers to use a flashing red auxiliary light in conjunction with 
11 an amber light, as provided in current law, while servicing, freeing, loading, unloading or 
12 towing a vehicle on a public way or in a place where public traffic may reasonably be
