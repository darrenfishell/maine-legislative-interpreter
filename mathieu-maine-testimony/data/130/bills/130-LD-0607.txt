16SUMMARY
17 This bill annually raises the minimum salary that an employee who works in an 
18 executive, administrative or professional capacity must earn in order for that employee to 
19 be exempt from the laws governing the minimum wage and overtime pay until it is $55,224 
20 on January 1, 2024.  The bill provides for an annual adjustment, beginning January 1, 2025, 
21 based on the percentage annual increase in certain earnings as published by the United 
22 States Department of Labor, Bureau of Labor Statistics.
