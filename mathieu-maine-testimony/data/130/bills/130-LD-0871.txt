7SUMMARY
8 This bill makes the following changes to the marijuana excise tax provisions.
9 1.  It adds definitions of "wet marijuana flower" and "wet marijuana trim."
10 2.  It removes the "per pound or fraction thereof" calculation of excise taxes for mature 
11 marijuana plants and, beginning July 1, 2021, provides an excise tax of $35 per mature 
12 marijuana plant.
13 3.  It requires a cultivation facility licensee to calculate the taxable weight by reducing 
14 the total weight of wet marijuana flower or wet marijuana trim by 75%, to account for the 
15 water in the flower or trim, before applying the excise tax.
