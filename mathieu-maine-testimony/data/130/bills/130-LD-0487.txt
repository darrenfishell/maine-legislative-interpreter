15SUMMARY
16 This bill changes the title of the Business Services Manager position to Senior Assistant 
17 to the Public Advocate to clarify that a person in the position may perform duties aside 
18 from business-related duties.  The bill also clarifies that the Public Advocate may legally 
19 accept funds from sources other than an assessment on utilities for the purpose of carrying 
20 out its statutory duties.16
