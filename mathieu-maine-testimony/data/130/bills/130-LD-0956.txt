28SUMMARY
29 This bill amends the state retirement system laws governing creditable service for 
30 service in the United States Armed Forces to provide credit for service during operations 
31 in Lebanon, August 21, 1982 to February 26, 1984, and operations in Grenada, October 25, 
32 1983 to December 15, 1983.29
