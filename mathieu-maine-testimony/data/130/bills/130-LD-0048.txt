15SUMMARY
20 This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to apply to the 
21 United States Department of Health and Human Services, Centers for Medicare and 
22 Medicaid Services for a waiver from the requirements of federal law and regulations to 
23 allow Maine to provide Medicaid-funded direct support services to individuals 
24 experiencing homelessness.  In addition, the resolve provides ongoing funds to the Housing 
25 First Assistance Program established within the Maine State Housing Authority to be 
26 distributed to community action agencies to assist individuals on the verge of becoming 
27 homeless.15
