26SUMMARY
27 Resolve 2021, chapter 122 established the Commission To Develop a Paid Family and 
28 Medical Leave Benefits Program and requires the commission to submit a report no later 
29 than February 1, 2022.  This resolve extends the date for the commission to submit its 
30 report to November 2, 2022.  This resolve also provides that funds appropriated or allocated 
31 pursuant to Resolve 2021, chapter 122 do not lapse but must be carried forward into the 
32 next fiscal year to be used for the purpose for which the funds were provided.27
