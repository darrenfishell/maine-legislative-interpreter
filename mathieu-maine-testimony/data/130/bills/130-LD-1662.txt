22SUMMARY
23 This bill amends the law restricting the sale of residential upholstered furniture treated 
24 with flame-retardant chemicals to align Maine's requirements with standards enacted in 
25 California.  The bill also specifies that the Department of Environmental Protection is 
26 authorized, rather than required, to adopt rules to implement the section of law governing 
27 residential upholstered furniture.23
