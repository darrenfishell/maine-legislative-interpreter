3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill would provide a method to reduce or eliminate the effects of the federal 
6 windfall elimination provision, which reduces social security benefits for a person who 
7 qualifies for social security benefits and also earns a retirement or disability pension from 
8 an employer that did not withhold social security taxes from the earnings of that person.
