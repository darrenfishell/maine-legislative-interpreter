18SUMMARY
19 This bill requires the Secretary of State to change the driving record of a holder of a 
20 provisional license that is suspended for a motor vehicle violation from indicating the 
21 suspension of the license to indicating the motor vehicle violation committed that caused 
22 the suspension once the provisional license holder receives a permanent unrestricted license 
23 or the provisional license is restored.19
