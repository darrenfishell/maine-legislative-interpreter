Page 1 - 130LR1684(01)1Emergency preamble.  Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
2 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
3Whereas, this legislation must take effect before the end of the 90-day period so that 
4 the Department of Labor may adopt rules to implement this resolve; and
5Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
6 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
7 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
8 therefore, be it
9Sec. 1.  Unemployment program for self-employed workers and 
10independent contractors.  Resolved:  That the Department of Labor shall establish a 
11 state unemployment program for workers not covered by unemployment compensation in 
12 the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 26, chapter 13.  The program must cover self-employed 
13 workers and independent contractors as defined in Title 26, section 1043, subsection 11, 
14 paragraph E.  The program must include benefits for self-employed workers and 
15 independent contractors that are equal to those benefits received by employees eligible for 
16 unemployment compensation under Title 26, chapter 13.
17Sec. 2.  Funding.  Resolved:  That the Department of Labor shall use any funds 
18 received from the Federal Government, if available, for start-up costs for the program 
19 established under section 1.  Ongoing funding for the program must be provided through 
20 charges or assessments to an employer's account as described in the Maine Revised 
21 Statutes, Title 26, section 1221.
22Sec. 3.  Report; authority to submit bill.  Resolved:  That, by November 3, 
23 2021, the Department of Labor shall submit an implementation plan for the program 
24 established under section 1, including any necessary legislation and suggested rules, to the 
25 Joint Standing Committee on Labor and Housing.  The committee may submit a bill related 
26 to the report to the Second Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.
27Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
28 takes effect when approved.
29SUMMARY
30 This resolve directs the Department of Labor to create an unemployment compensation 
31 system for self-employed workers and independent contractors.
