17SUMMARY
18 This bill creates a Kennebec County public defender office, beginning January 1, 2022.  
19 As a first step in providing indigent legal services through an office of a public defender, 
20 this bill provides funding to the Maine Commission on Indigent Legal Services for 
21 positions to establish the office.  The bill also authorizes the commission's system for 
22 providing indigent legal services to employ attorneys.17
