2Sec. 1.  36 MRSA §653, sub-§1, ¶L is enacted to read:
3 L.  For property tax years beginning on or after April 1, 2022, the exemptions provided 
4 under paragraphs C and D are increased to $9,000 and the exemptions provided under

8SUMMARY
9 This bill increases by 50%, for tax years beginning on or after April 1, 2022, the 
10 property tax exemption for all categories of eligible veterans other than paraplegic veterans 
11 receiving a $50,000 exemption for specially adapted housing units.9
