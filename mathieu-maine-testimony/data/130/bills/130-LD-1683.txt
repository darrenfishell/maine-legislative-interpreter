Page 1 - 130LR0892(01)1Sec. 1.  Department of Corrections hazard duty stipend.  Resolved: That the 
2 Department of Corrections, and any entity providing services on behalf of the department, 
3 shall compensate employees eligible for a hazard duty stipend related to the COVID-19 
4 pandemic. The stipend must:
5 1.  Be paid retroactively from the time the previous hazard duty stipend was removed;
6 2.  Continue until the state of civil emergency due to the COVID-19 pandemic is 
7 terminated; and
8 3.  Be contingent upon the State's receiving COVID-19 pandemic relief funding from 
9 the Federal Government for which the hazard duty stipend for the Department of 
10 Corrections could be a qualifying expense.
11SUMMARY
12 This resolve requires the Department of Corrections to use federal funding to pay 
13 eligible employees a hazard duty stipend related to the COVID-19 pandemic.  The stipend 
14 must be paid retroactively from when the previous hazard duty stipend ended until the state 
15 of civil emergency due to the COVID-19 pandemic is terminated.12
