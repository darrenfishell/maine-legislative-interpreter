13 SUMMARY 
14 This amendment amends Committee Amendment "A" to change: 
15 1. From December 31, 2022 to March 1, 2023 the date by which the Department of 
16 Health and Human Services must provide an alternative, updated method for rebasing 
17 federally qualified health center prospective payment system rates; 
18 2. From December 31, 2022 to December 31, 2023 the date after which the department 
19 may change the base year used for rebasing federally qualified health center prospective 
20 payment system rates; 
21 3. From December 31, 2022 to February 1, 2023 the date by which the department must 
22 issue final rate letters implementing the reimbursement option chosen by each federally 
23 qualified health center; and 
24 4. From July 1, 2022 to March I, 2023 the date on which the reimbursement option 
25 chosen by each federally qualified health center is effective.
