3ยง2743-B.  Maternity and postpartum care
4 An insurer that issues individual contracts providing maternity benefits shall provide 
5 coverage for services related to postpartum care, including coverage for development of a 
6 postpartum care plan; contact with the patient within 3 weeks of the end of pregnancy; a 
7 comprehensive postpartum visit, including a full assessment of the patient's physical, social 
8 and psychological well-being; treatment of complications of pregnancy and childbirth, 
9 including pelvic floor disorders and postpartum depression; assessment of risk factors for 
10 cardiovascular disease; and care related to pregnancy loss.  Coverage must encompass the 
11 recommendations outlined in the "Optimizing Postpartum Care" opinion published May 
12 2018 by the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists and support postpartum 
13 care as an ongoing process rather than an isolated visit and include services and support 
14 necessary to transition a patient to a healthy and stable condition.
15Sec. 2.  Application. This Act applies to all policies, contracts and certificates 
16 executed, delivered, issued for delivery, continued or renewed in this State on or after July 
17 1, 2022.  For purposes of this Act, all contracts are deemed to be renewed no later than the 
18 next yearly anniversary of the contract date.
19SUMMARY
20 This bill requires insurers, in individual policies that cover maternity benefits, to 
21 provide coverage for postpartum care that meets the recommendations of the American 
22 College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists.20
