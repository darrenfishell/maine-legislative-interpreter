SUMMARY 
This amendment changes the mens rea requirement for the Class C crime of gross 
sexual assault under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 17-A, section 253, subsection 2, 
paragraph M to criminal negligence and makes the same change to the corresponding Class 
C and Class D crimes of unlawful sexual contact and the corresponding Class D crime of 
unlawful sexual touching.
