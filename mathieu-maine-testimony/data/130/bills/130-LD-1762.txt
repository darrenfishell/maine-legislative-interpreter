18SUMMARY
19 This bill removes the requirement for a disability plate or placard issued to a person 
20 with a permanent disability to expire with the applicant's driver's license or nondriver 
21 identification card issued by the State and provides that the disability plate or placard may 
22 be renewed for a period not to exceed 6 years. It authorizes the Secretary of State to adopt 
23 routine technical rules regarding the issuance and renewal of disability plates or placards 
24 issued to persons with a permanent disability.
25 This bill also removes the exemption from the fee for the issuance or renewal of a 
26 recycler license for a new car dealer, used car dealer or equipment dealer.19
