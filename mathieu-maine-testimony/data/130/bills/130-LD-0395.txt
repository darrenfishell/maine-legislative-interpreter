12 A person may not place, and the Department of Transportation may not issue a license 
13 or permit for, a temporary sign within the public right-of-way.
14Sec. 5.  23 MRSA §1917-B, as enacted by PL 2015, c. 403, §6, is repealed.
15SUMMARY
16 This bill removes the authorization for a person to place a temporary sign in the public 
17 right-of-way.  It also removes the civil violation that currently applies to persons who take, 
18 deface or disturb such signs.
