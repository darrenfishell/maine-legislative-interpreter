36SUMMARY
37 This bill reduces the maximum rating factor for geographic area that may be used by 
38 carriers to determine individual and small group health insurance premiums to 1.35 for plan 
39 year 2023 and to 1.25 for plan year 2024 and thereafter.  Under current law, the maximum 
40 rating factor for a geographic area is 1.5.
41 The bill also requires the Superintendent of Insurance to report on the difference in 
42 premium rates in each geographic rating area used by a carrier in this State to the joint 37
