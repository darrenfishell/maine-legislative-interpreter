16SUMMARY
17 This bill requires the presentation of proof of identity in the form of a photograph 
18 identification document when voting in person.  Acceptable photograph identification is a 
19 current and valid driver's license or nondriver identification card issued in this State, a 
20 United States passport, a military identification or a permit to carry a concealed handgun 
21 issued in this State if that permit includes a photograph.  An identification issued by a 
22 college or university in this State may not be accepted for voter identification. 17
