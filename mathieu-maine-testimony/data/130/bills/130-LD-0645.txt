Page 1 - 130LR1247(01)1Sec. 1.  Electronic titling work group.  Resolved:  That the Department of the 
2 Secretary of State, Bureau of Motor Vehicles shall convene a work group to study 
3 electronic titling in accordance with this section, referred to in this section as "the work 
4 group."
5 1.  The Secretary of State shall appoint to the work group:
6 A.  Staff of the Bureau of Motor Vehicles;
7 B.  A representative of the Secretary of State's computing and information services 
8 staff;
9 C.  A representative of state credit unions;
10 D.  A representative of state banks;
11 E.  A representative of other financing entities;
12 F.  A representative of a car dealership;
13 G.  A representative of a car dealership primarily selling used cars; and
14 H.  A representative of a national organization that develops standards and guidance 
15 for electronic titling systems.
16 The Secretary of State or the Secretary of State's designee shall serve on the work group.  
17 Members of the work group may not be compensated for their work on the work group.
18 2.  Duties of the work group include:
19 A.  Exploring efforts to develop a framework to clearly define electronic titling;
20 B.  Establishing standards to support and adopt electronic titling;
21 C.  Examining the benefits and challenges related to a paperless electronic titling 
22 program;
23 D.  Examining standards and systems developed by industry experts and exploring 
24 ways in which the State can prepare to adopt such standards and systems; and
25 E.  Reviewing and recommending improvements to the State's titling laws, rules and 
26 procedures, with an emphasis on creating a streamlined titling system that is easy to 
27 use.
28 3.   The Bureau of Motor Vehicles, at the direction of the chairs of the joint standing 
29 committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over transportation matters, shall provide 
30 an annual report to the committee.  The work group is dissolved on the adjournment of the 
31 Second Regular Session of the 132nd Legislature.
32SUMMARY
33 This resolve directs the Department of the Secretary of State, Bureau of Motor Vehicles 
34 to convene a work group to study electronic titling and make annual reports to the joint 
35 standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over transportation matters 
36 through the 132nd Legislature, at the end of which time the work group dissolves.33
