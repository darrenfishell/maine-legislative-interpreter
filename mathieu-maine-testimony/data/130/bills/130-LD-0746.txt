3ยง403-B.  Public proceedings of municipal agencies through remote access
41.  Remote access.  Notwithstanding any provision of law or municipal charter 
5 provision or ordinance to the contrary, any board, commission, agency or authority of a 
6 municipality subject to this subchapter may conduct a public proceeding through 
7 telephonic, video, electronic or other similar means of remote participation under the 
8 following conditions:
9 A.  The board, commission, agency or authority of a municipality determines that 
10 participation by the public may be through telephonic, video, electronic or other similar 
11 means of remote participation;
12 B.  Notice of the public proceeding has been given in accordance with section 406 and 
13 includes the method by which the public may attend in accordance with paragraph A;
14 C.  Each member of the board, commission, agency or authority of a municipality who 
15 is participating in the public proceeding is able to hear and speak to all the other 
16 members during the public proceeding, and members of the public attending the public 
17 proceeding in the location identified in the notice given pursuant to paragraph A are 
18 able to hear all members participating at other locations; and
19 D.  All votes taken during the public proceeding are taken by roll call vote.
202.  Application to other proceedings.  This section does not apply to town meetings 
21 held pursuant to Title 30-A, section 2524 or regional school unit budget meetings held 
22 pursuant to Title 20-A, section 1482-A.
23SUMMARY
24 This bill establishes in law the authorization for boards, commissions, agencies and 
25 authorities of municipalities to conduct meetings remotely.
