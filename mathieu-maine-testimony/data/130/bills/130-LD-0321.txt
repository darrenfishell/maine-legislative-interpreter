16 cumulative terms may continue to serve through the end of the 130th Legislature.
17SUMMARY
18 This bill provides that Legislators have a lifetime cumulative limit of 8 terms in the 
19 Legislature for a total of 16 years regardless of whether those terms are served in one 
20 chamber or both.
