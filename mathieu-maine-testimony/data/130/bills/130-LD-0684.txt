2 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and

11Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within

13 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
14 therefore, be it
15Sec. 1.  Department of Health and Human Services to amend payments to 
16nursing facilities with high MaineCare utilization.  Resolved:  That, by July 1, 
17 2021, the Department of Health and Human Services shall amend its rules in Chapter 101, 
18 MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 67, Principles of Reimbursement for 
19 Nursing Facilities, governing supplemental payments to nursing facilities where the 
20 number of MaineCare residents constitutes more than 80% of the total number of residents 
21 to remove the requirement that such nursing facilities have base year direct and routine 
22 aggregate costs per day that are less than the median aggregate direct and routine allowable 
23 costs for the facility's peer group.
24Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
25 takes effect when approved.
26SUMMARY
27 This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rules 
28 governing supplemental payments to nursing facilities where the number of MaineCare 
29 residents constitutes more than 80% of the total number of residents to remove the 
30 requirement that such nursing facilities have base year direct and routine aggregate costs 
31 per day that are less than the median aggregate direct and routine allowable costs for the 
32 facility's peer group.27
