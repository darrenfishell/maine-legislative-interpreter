24 SUMMARY 
25 This amendment extends the authority granted to qualified on-premises retailers and 
26 qualified distilleries by Public Law 2021, chapter 3 to sell liquor for off-premises 
27 consumption through March 30, 2025. The amendment similarly extends the authority 
28 granted to licensed Maine distilleries and small distilleries by Public Law 2021, chapter 91 
29 to sell cocktails containing samples of their spirits products for on-premises consumption 
30 through March 30, 2025.
