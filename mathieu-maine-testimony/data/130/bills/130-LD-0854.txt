17 The department shall provide school-based services in developmental preschools to 
18 children from birth to 5 years of age, including rehabilitative and community support 
19 services for children with cognitive impairments and functional limitations and behavioral 
20 health services.  The department may adopt rules to implement this section.  Rules adopted 
21 pursuant to this section are major substantive rules pursuant to Title 5, chapter 375, 
22 subchapter 2-A.
23 As used in this section, "developmental preschool" means a program that has been 
24 approved by the Department of Education as either a special purpose private school or a 
25 regular education public school program or a program operated or funded by the Child 
26 Development Services System in Title 20-A, section 7209.
27Sec. 2.  Stakeholder group; review of early childhood special education 
28services.  The Department of Health and Human Services shall convene a stakeholder 
29 group to participate in any rulemaking pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 34-B, 
30 section 1227, including the drafting of rules and any proposed changes to those rules after 
31 the public hearing process to the extent permitted by Title 5, chapter 375.  Stakeholders 
32 include representatives of providers, advocates, parents of children receiving services, the 
33 Child Development Services System, school administrative units and the Department of 
34 Education.  The Department of Health and Human Services also shall take into account the 
35 findings from the independent review of the State's early childhood special education 
36 services being carried out pursuant to Public Law 2019, chapter 343, Part VVVV.
37Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation
