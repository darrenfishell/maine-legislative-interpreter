21SUMMARY
22 This bill requires a candidate for sheriff or someone serving as sheriff to have met and 
23 continue to meet any statutory requirements of preservice, basic or in-service law 
24 enforcement training required for certification or continued certification as a law 
25 enforcement officer.  This bill also provides that any person who served as a full-time law 
26 enforcement officer employed by a state agency, including the University of Maine System, 
27 on or before July 1, 1990 is considered to have met the standards required by this bill.22
