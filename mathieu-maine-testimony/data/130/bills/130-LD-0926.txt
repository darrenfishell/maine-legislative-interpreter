15SUMMARY
16 This bill prohibits any student from joining or participating in an interscholastic or 
17 intramural athletic team or sports activity sponsored by an elementary or secondary school 
18 or postsecondary institution that is designated for "females," "women" or "girls" except for 
19 students of the female gender. A student prohibited from joining or participating in an 
20 athletic team or sport activity on the basis that the student is not a female may dispute the 
21 denial to the Commissioner of Education with a signed statement from a physician stating 
22 that the student is a female based upon the student's internal and external reproductive 
23 anatomy and naturally occurring level of testosterone and an analysis of the student's 
24 chromosomes.16
