15SUMMARY
16 This bill, beginning in 2023, limits eligibility for commercial menhaden fishing 
17 licenses to those individuals who held a license to fish commercially for menhaden in any 
18 year from 2016 to 2021 and landed 25,000 pounds or more of menhaden in at least one of 
19 those years. It provides that the Commissioner of Marine Resources may not issue a 
20 resident commercial menhaden fishing license or a nonresident commercial menhaden 
21 fishing license to any individual in any year subsequent to 2023 unless that individual 
22 possessed that license in the previous calendar year. 16
