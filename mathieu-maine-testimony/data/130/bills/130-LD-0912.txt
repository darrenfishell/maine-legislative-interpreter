21SUMMARY
22 This bill extends the entitlement to family medical leave to hourly employees of school 
23 administrative units who have been employed for at least 12 months by the school 
24 administrative unit and worked at least 1,250 hours during the previous 12-month period.  
25 Under current law, employees must have 12 consecutive months of employment with the 
26 same employer to be entitled to family medical leave.22
