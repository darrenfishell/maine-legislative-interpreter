36SUMMARY
37 This bill increases the annual cap on eligible investment in any one business eligible 
38 for the seed capital tax credit from $2,000,000 to $3,000,000 and the aggregate cap on 
39 eligible investment in any one eligible business from $3,500,000 to $6,000,000.
