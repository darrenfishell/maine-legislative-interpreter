3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill proposes to codify the 10-year economic development strategy for Maine that 
6 was developed by the Department of Economic and Community Development in 2019. The 
7 bill would provide additional clarity and include requirements for the Maine Economic 
8 Growth Council to fulfill its statutory mandate to develop, maintain and evaluate Maine’s 
9 long-range strategic economic and community development plan and build out the adopted 
10 10-year strategy. It would also include provisions to support the State’s achievement of the 
11 adopted goals and objectives by establishing requirements for the Governor, the Legislature 
12 and state agencies to engage in the ongoing evaluation, reporting and review of the strategy.
