24SUMMARY
25 This bill provides that if an employee is covered by a collective bargaining agreement, 
26 a dispute over the applicability or enforcement of the earned paid leave law is subject to 
27 the dispute resolution process in that collective bargaining agreement, with the remedies in 
28 the law governing family medical leave available to the employee if the employee 
29 demonstrates an employer violation.25
