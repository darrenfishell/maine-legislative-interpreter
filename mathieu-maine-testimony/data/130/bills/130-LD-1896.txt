21SUMMARY
22 This bill requires vendors of bulk deliveries of liquid fuel sold for heating residential, 
23 industrial or commercial space or water or motor vehicle fuel to provide to a purchaser a 
24 delivery ticket by e-mail upon the request of the purchaser.  It also makes a technical 
25 correction to the law governing bulk sales.22
