SENATE AMENDMENT" A "to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 130, 
L.D. 177, "An Act To Improve Labor Laws for Workers in Maine" 
Amend the amendment by inserting before section 1 the following: 
'Sec.1. Transfer from Medical Use of Marijuana Fund. Notwithstanding any 
provision of law to the contrary, the State Controller shall transfer $200,000 from the 
Medical Use of Marijuana Fund, established in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 22, 
section 2430, within the Department of Administrative and Financial Services to the 
unappropriated surplus of the General Fund no later than June 30, 2023.' 
Amend the amendment by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment transfers $200,000 from the Medical Use of Marijuana Fund within 
the Department of Administrative and Financial Services to the unappropriated surplus of 
the General Fund no later than June 30, 2023 to offset $200,000 of the General Fund 
appropriations in Committee Amendment "A."
