14SUMMARY
15 This bill defines the term "electronic lien titling program" under the Maine Motor 
16 Vehicle Certificate of Title and Antitheft Act as a program that permits the creation and 
17 exchange of an electronic record for maintaining lien information.  It also allows the 
18 Secretary of State to use an electronic lien titling program for the purposes of that Act.15
