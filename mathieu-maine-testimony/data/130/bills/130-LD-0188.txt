38SUMMARY
39 This bill requires that owners and managers of log yards and mill sites present a copy 
40 of a trip ticket to a forest ranger in the log yard or mill site upon request.  The bill adds a 
41 requirement related to the transportation of forest products by providing that a landowner 
42 of 50,000 or more acres of forest land in the State may not allow the transportation of forest 
Page 2 - 130LR0642(01)43 products harvested on the landowner's land from a location in the State to another location 
44 in the State in violation of federal law or regulation or an international trade agreement that 
45 prohibits the transportation of goods from a location in the United States to another location 
46 in the United States.  The bill also provides that a landowner with 2 prior violations of the 
47 new transportation requirement is ineligible for classification of the landowner's land under 
48 the Maine Tree Growth Tax Law and the landowner may not receive certain tax incentives 
49 or state grants or other state funding.1
