4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill proposes to direct the Department of Labor to work with the Department of 
6 Education, colleges and universities in the State and career and technical education centers 
7 to develop education and certification programs for direct care workers, including 
8 vocational education programs such as preapprenticeship programs at career and technical 
9 education centers, apprenticeship programs and so-called "earn as you learn" programs.
