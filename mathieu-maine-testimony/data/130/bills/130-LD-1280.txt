30SUMMARY
31 This bill establishes the Maine Rural Economy Future Development Program within 
32 the University of Maine System to promote future business development in rural Maine by 
33 providing course offerings on the economy of rural Maine.  The bill requires the University 
34 of Maine at Fort Kent, the University of Maine at Presque Isle, the University of Maine at 
35 Machias and the University of Maine at Farmington to each create and offer a program that 
36 provides a comprehensive study of the rural economy of Maine and that promotes the 
37 partnering of students with businesses in rural Maine.  The bill specifies that the program 30
