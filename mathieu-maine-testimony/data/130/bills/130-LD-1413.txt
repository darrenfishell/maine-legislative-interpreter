40SUMMARY
41 This bill establishes an income tax exemption for civil service retirement plan benefits 
42 for recipients who do not participate in the federal social security program in recognition 41
42
Page 3 - 130LR1471(01)43 of the fact that the current law provides an income exemption for all social security benefits 
44 in addition to a $10,000 pension exemption for retirement plan benefits.  The bill phases in 
45 greater parity in treatment of the 2 types of pension systems by increasing the civil service 
46 pension deduction amount by $5,000 per year until 2025 when it reaches the maximum 
47 annual benefit that an individual retiring at retirement age would receive under the federal 
48 Social Security Act.1
