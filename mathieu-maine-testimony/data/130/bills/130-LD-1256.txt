18Sec. 2.  5 MRSA ยง8063-C is enacted to read:
19ยง8063-C.  Climate impact
20 Every rule proposed by an agency must contain a climate impact note at the end of the 
21 rule. The note must be placed on the rule prior to any public hearing and, in the case of 
22 rules adopted without a hearing, prior to the sending of notice under section 8053. The 
23 climate impact note must describe the estimated climate impact on municipalities, counties, 
24 the State and the United States and globally of implementing or complying with the 
25 proposed rule. If the proposed rule will not impose any climate impact under this section, 
26 the climate impact note must state that fact.
27 This section does not apply to emergency rules.
28SUMMARY
29 This bill requires a climate impact note on every rule proposed by a state agency and 
30 on all legislation that would have an apparent significant climate impact if implemented.29
