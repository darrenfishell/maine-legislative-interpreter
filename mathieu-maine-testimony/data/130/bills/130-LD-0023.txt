9SUMMARY
10 This bill reinstates and increases the income tax deduction for contributions to so-called 
11 Section 529 education savings plans, providing a deduction up to $1,000 per designated 
12 beneficiary.  A deduction up to $250 was in effect from 2007 to 2015.
