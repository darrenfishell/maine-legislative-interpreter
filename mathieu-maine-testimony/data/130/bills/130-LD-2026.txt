14Sec. 1.  Adoption.  Resolved:  That final adoption of Chapter 180: Appliance 
15 Efficiency Standards, a provisionally adopted major substantive rule of the Department of 
16 Environmental Protection's bureau of air quality that has been submitted to the Legislature 
17 for review pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A 
18 outside the legislative rule acceptance period, is authorized.
19Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
20 takes effect when approved.
21SUMMARY
22 This resolve provides for legislative review of Chapter 180: Appliance Efficiency 
23 Standards, a major substantive rule of the Department of Environmental Protection's bureau 
24 of air quality that was filed outside the legislative rule acceptance period.22
