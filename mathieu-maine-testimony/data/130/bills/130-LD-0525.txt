55-A.  Use of shared facility for retail sale of adult use marijuana and adult use 
6marijuana products and sale of marijuana and marijuana products for medical use.  
7 A marijuana store licensee that is also a registered caregiver or a registered dispensary may 
8 sell or offer for sale to consumers adult use marijuana and adult use marijuana products 
9 pursuant to this chapter within the same facility or building in which the licensee also sells 
10 or offers for sale to qualifying patients marijuana and marijuana products for medical use 
11 pursuant to the Maine Medical Use of Marijuana Act, as long as the adult use marijuana 
12 and adult use marijuana products are sold using a different cash register than that used for 
13 sales of marijuana and marijuana products for medical use.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill allows the use of a shared facility for retail sale of adult use marijuana and 
16 adult use marijuana products and sale of marijuana and marijuana products for medical use, 
17 as long as the adult use marijuana and adult use marijuana products are sold using a 
18 different cash register than that used for sales of marijuana and marijuana products for 
19 medical use.
