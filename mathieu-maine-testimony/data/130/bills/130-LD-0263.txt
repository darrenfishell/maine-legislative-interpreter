30SUMMARY
31 This bill changes the expiration date of shellfish sanitation certificates from May 31st 
32 to March 31st and clarifies that an aquaculture license is not needed for holders of 
33 aquaculture leases or limited-purpose aquaculture licenses who are using the lease or 
34 license only for personal use or for research purposes.  The bill clarifies that a lobster trap 
35 tag issued by the Department of Marine Resources must be securely attached to the frame 
36 of the trap.  The bill also provides that the specific penalty for violation of a rule adopted 
37 by the department may be specified in that rule.
