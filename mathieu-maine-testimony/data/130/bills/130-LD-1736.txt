10SUMMARY
11 This bill amends Public Law 2021, chapter 459 to establish and provide funding for 
12 the costs of one Director position, one Public Service Coordinator II position, one Office 
13 Specialist II position and related costs for the new Office of Affordable Health Care 
14 beginning October 1, 2021.  These General Fund costs are offset by transfers to the General 
15 Fund pursuant to Public Law 2021, chapter 459 from the Department of Professional and 
16 Financial Regulation, Bureau of Insurance Other Special Revenue Funds account and from 
17 the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Medical Use of Marijuana Fund 
18 Other Special Revenue Funds account.
