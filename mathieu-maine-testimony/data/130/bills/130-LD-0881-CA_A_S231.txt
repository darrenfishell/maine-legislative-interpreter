12 SUMMARY 
13 This amendment makes the following changes to the bill: 
14 1. It adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause; 
15 2. It changes the definition of" seedling" to make it consistent between the medical use 
16 laws and the adult use laws; 
17 3. It removes provisions in the bill that require a registered caregiver operating a 
18 caregiver retail store to acquire a separate registration certificate for the caregiver retail 
19 store and to pay a separate registration fee for the caregiver retail store.
