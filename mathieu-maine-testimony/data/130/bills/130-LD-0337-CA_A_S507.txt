28 SUMMARY 
29 This amendment replaces the bill, which is a concept draft, and provides that the 
30 Efficiency Maine Trust, in carrying out its duties, has the power to make agreements, obtain 
31 certifications and insurance and guarantee performance of any obligation, contract for 
32 professional services and acquire and dispose ofreal or personal property. 
33 The amendment also makes changes to the Maine Clean Energy and Sustainability 
34 Accelerator finance program. The amendment allows the accelerator to lend money or 
3 5 otherwise extend credit through various mechanisms, including through leases. It also 
36 expands the description of the sources of capital for the program.
