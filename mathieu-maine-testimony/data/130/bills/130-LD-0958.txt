4 with the activities of daily living and the instrumental activities of daily living.  The review 
5 must include direct care services provided in the home by home health aides, certified 
6 nursing assistants, direct support professionals, personal support specialists and unlicensed 
7 assistive personnel.  The department shall include providers, consumers, direct care

3SUMMARY
4 This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to review rules 
5 that relate to direct care services provided in the home to assist individuals with the 
6 activities of daily living and the instrumental activities of daily living.  The review must 
7 examine how entities providing direct care services to affected individuals provide 
8 information, such as advance notice of staff absences, termination of services and training 
9 requirements, and ensure supervision and quality care and protection of vulnerable people 
10 receiving care.  The department is required to submit its findings and recommendations to 
11 the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human Services no later than January 1, 2023.1
