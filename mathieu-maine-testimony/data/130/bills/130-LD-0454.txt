9SUMMARY
10 This bill amends the laws concerning the shellfish depuration harvesting process to 
11 raise the rate of reimbursement paid to the municipality by the depuration certificate holder 
12 from 50 cents for each bushel of soft-shelled clams to $2.00 for each bushel.
