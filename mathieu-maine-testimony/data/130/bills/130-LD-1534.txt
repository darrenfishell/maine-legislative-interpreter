11SUMMARY
12 This bill directs the Department of Transportation to preserve and protect the rail 
13 corridors of the State for future railroad use and maintain all rail corridors in a condition 
14 that allows for their resuming railroad service.
15 The bill prohibits a rail corridor from being converted into or operated as a trail, used 
16 as a pathway or space for walking or biking, used for any other form of nonmotorized travel 
17 or recreation or used as a bus line without first being evaluated for passenger railroad 
18 service.12
