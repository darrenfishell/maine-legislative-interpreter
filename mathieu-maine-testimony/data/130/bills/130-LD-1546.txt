6§4767.  Rental relief program for persons having difficulty paying rent
71.  Rental subsidy program established.  A rental subsidy program, referred to in this 
8 section as "the program," is established within the Maine State Housing Authority.  The 
9 purpose of the program is to provide rental subsidies to persons having difficulty paying 
10 rent.
112.  Eligibility.  The Maine State Housing Authority shall determine the eligibility of 
12 and provide assistance under the program to a person having difficulty paying rent in the 
13 following order of priority:
14 A.  To any person whose personal or financial circumstances have changed as a result 
15 of the novel coronavirus disease referred to as COVID-19, including but not limited to 
16 a person whose:
17 (1)  Place of employment closed;
18 (2)  Hours of work were reduced;
19 (3)  Child's daycare or school is closed; or
20 (4)  Income is otherwise decreased due to COVID-19; and
21 B.  To any other person who is having difficulty paying rent.
223.  Rental subsidy.  A rental subsidy must be provided on behalf of a person eligible 
23 for the program pursuant to subsection 2 directly to a landlord by a community action 
24 agency as defined in Title 22, section 5321, subsection 2.  The Maine State Housing 
25 Authority shall adopt rules establishing the amount of the rental subsidy available to a 
26 person eligible for the program and the length of time the rental subsidy is available to a 
27 person eligible for the program.
284.  Fund.  The Rental Relief Fund, referred to in this section as "the fund," is 
29 established as a nonlapsing fund within the Maine State Housing Authority pursuant to the 
30 following conditions.
31 A.  All money appropriated for inclusion in the fund pursuant to Title 36, section 
32 4641-B, subsection 4-B, paragraph H and private donations for the program may be 
33 included in the fund.
34 B.  The Maine State Housing Authority may apply money in the fund for purposes 
35 authorized by this subchapter.
365.  Report.  Beginning January 15, 2022, and annually thereafter, the Maine State 
37 Housing Authority shall submit a report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature 
38 having jurisdiction over housing matters on the program, including the number of persons 
Page 2 - 130LR1346(01)39 eligible for the program pursuant to subsection 2 and the number of persons provided rental 
40 subsidies under the program pursuant to subsection 3.
36.  Rules.  The Maine State Housing Authority shall adopt rules to implement this 
4 section. Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules as defined in Title 
5 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.

16SUMMARY
17 This bill establishes a rental subsidy program within the Maine State Housing 
18 Authority for persons experiencing difficulty paying rent.  The rental subsidy program 
19 provides a rental subsidy on behalf of a person eligible for the program.1
