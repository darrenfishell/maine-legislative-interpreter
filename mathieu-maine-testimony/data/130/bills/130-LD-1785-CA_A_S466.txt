SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill with a resolve directing the Department of Labor, in 
consultation with the Maine Fire Protection Services Commission, the Maine Sheriffs' 
Association, the Maine Criminal Justice Academy, the Maine Ambulance Association, the 
Maine Municipal Association and the Finance Authority of Maine, to develop a definition 
for "public safety worker" and to examine programs and incentives for recruitment and 
retention of such public safety workers. The department is required to submit a report, 
including suggested legislation, to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 
jurisdiction over innovation, development, economic advancement and business matters 
and the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over labor and 
housing matters by January 15, 2023. The joint standing committees may each report out a 
bill based on the report to the First Regular Session of the 131 st Legislature.
