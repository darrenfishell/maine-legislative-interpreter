12Sec. 2.  35-A MRSA §4703, sub-§8 is enacted to read:
138.  Renewable natural gas.  Reasonable costs for gas include costs associated with the 
14 procurement of renewable natural gas.  The commission shall allow a gas utility to use 
15 renewable natural gas for not more than 2% of the natural gas it supplies to its customers 
16 starting in 2022 and shall allow a gas utility to use an additional 2% annually thereafter and 
17 allow a gas utility to include the costs of that renewable natural gas in its cost-of-gas 
18 adjustment rate under subsection 1.
19Sec. 3.  35-A MRSA §4703, sub-§9 is enacted to read:
209.  Definition.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, 
21 "renewable natural gas" means natural gas resulting from the decomposition of organic 
22 matter.
23SUMMARY
24 This bill requires the Public Utilities Commission to allow a gas utility to use renewable 
25 natural gas for no more than 2% of the natural gas it supplies to its customers starting in 
26 2022 and to allow a gas utility to use an additional 2% annually thereafter.  A gas utility 
27 also may include the costs of that renewable natural gas in its cost-of-gas adjustment rate.24
