41.  Verification of eligibility of benefits recipients.  The commissioner shall on a 
5 weekly basis review the rolls of recipients receiving unemployment benefits to verify 
6 eligibility.  In performing the verification review required under this section, the 
7 commissioner shall:
8 A. Utilize an integrity data hub administered by a nationally recognized national 
9 association of state workforce agencies;
10 B.  Review lists of individuals incarcerated in detention and correctional facilities as 
11 defined in Title 34-A, section 1001, subsection 6, and pursuant to Title 34-A, sections 
12 1208 and 1208-A; and
13 C. Check new hires records against the National Directory of New Hires created by the 
14 United States Department of Health and Human Services, Administration for Children 
15 and Families, Office of Child Support Enforcement or successor federal directory.
162.  Data sharing.  The commissioner may enter into a memorandum of understanding 
17 with any federal, state, county or municipal government, department or agency to obtain 
18 information required to be reviewed or collected under this section.
193. Action on review results.  If the commissioner determines through the verification 
20 review required under subsection 1 that information exists indicating a change of 
21 circumstances affecting the eligibility of a recipient of unemployment benefits, the 
22 commissioner shall review that recipient's eligibility.
234. Rules.  The commissioner may adopt rules to carry out the purposes of this section.  
24 Rules adopted under this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 
25 375, subchapter 2-A.
26SUMMARY
27 This bill requires the Commissioner of Labor to weekly review the rolls of recipients 
28 receiving unemployment benefits with reference to a federal national directory of new hires 
29 and to review lists of incarcerated individuals in county and state custody to determine if 
30 there is a change of circumstances affecting the eligibility of a recipient of unemployment 
31 benefits.27
