52.  Issuance of moose hunting permits.  In accordance with section 11552, the 
6 commissioner may issue moose hunting permits and may establish the number of moose 
7 hunting permits to be issued for each wildlife management district established by the 
8 commissioner by rule open to moose hunting. No more than 10% of the moose hunting
