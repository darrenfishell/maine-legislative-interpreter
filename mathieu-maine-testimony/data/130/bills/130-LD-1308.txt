12 B.  The funds needed to meet the obligations of the Maine National Guard in 
13 responding to the declared major disaster or emergency are in excess of available 
14 appropriations and any other funding sources at the time of the transfer.
15 This subsection is repealed 90 days after the adjournment of the First Regular Session of

17SUMMARY
18 This bill authorizes the State Controller to transfer up to $100,000 from the Maine 
19 Budget Stabilization Fund to the Maine National Guard based on amounts certified by the 
20 Adjutant General of the Maine National Guard to be necessary to respond to a major 
21 disaster or emergency declared by the President of the United States.  The transfer is 
22 authorized only if approved by the Governor, and the amount transferred must be returned 
23 to the stabilization fund once federal funding is available.  These provisions are repealed 
24 90 days after the adjournment of the First Regular Session of the 131st Legislature.18
