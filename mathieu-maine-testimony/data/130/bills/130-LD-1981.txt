16Sec. 1.  Director of Bureau of Parks and Lands authorized to allow certain 
17actions related to the lease of certain land within the Bald Mountain public 
18reserved lands in the Town of Rangeley.  Resolved:  That the Director of the Bureau 
19 of Parks and Lands within the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may 
20 authorize RCC Atlantic, Inc. and American Tower to take the following actions on certain 
21 land within the Bald Mountain public reserved lands in the Town of Rangeley on which 
22 American Tower operates a commercial telecommunications tower pursuant to a lease 
23 dated August 31, 2011 under which the bureau is the lessor, RCC Atlantic, Inc. is the lessee 
24 and American Tower is a sublessee:
25 1.  Construct an extension at the top of the existing tower, with the extension not to 
26 exceed the height of the existing tower by more than 18 feet; 
27 2.  Construct an additional custom shelter at the base of the tower with an associated 
28 generator; and 
29 3.  Allow the installation of communications equipment on the tower by an additional 
30 wireless communications company, with the goal of improving local wireless 
31 communications service and emergency telecommunications service in the region.
32 Except as provided in this section, any authorization granted to RCC Atlantic, Inc. or 
33 American Tower must be in accordance with the August 31, 2011 lease entered into 
34 between the Bureau of Parks and Lands and RCC Atlantic, Inc.
35SUMMARY
36 This resolve authorizes the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the 
37 Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to authorize the construction of an 
38 extension to a telecommunications tower on property located on Bald Mountain in the 
39 Town of Rangeley, construction of an additional custom shelter at the base of the tower 
40 with an associated generator and installation of communications equipment on the tower 
41 by an additional wireless communications company.36
