SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment requires the Department of 
Economic and Community Development to establish a program under which a regional 
community and economic development organization that serves an area located in a rural 
region of the State, Aroostook, Piscataquis, Somerset and Washington counties, may 
provide a grant to cover the cost of obtaining broadband services for up to 5 years to an 
individual who has moved to a rural region of the State served by the regional community 
and economic development organization from a location outside of the State within the 12 
months preceding application for the grant. A grant made under this program may not 
exceed $1,000 per year.
