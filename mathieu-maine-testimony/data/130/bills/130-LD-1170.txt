7 A.  A law enforcement officer, a corrections officer or a corrections supervisor engaged

133.  It is not a defense to a prosecution under this section that the person holds a valid 
14 permit to carry a concealed handgun issued under Title 25, chapter 252.
154.  Unauthorized possession of a firearm in a correctional facility is a Class D crime.
165.  For the purposes of this section, "chief administrative officer" and "correctional 
17 facility" have the same meanings as in Title 34-A, section 1001, subsections 1 and 6, 
18 respectively.
