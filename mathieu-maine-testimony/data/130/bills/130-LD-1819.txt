2Sec. 1.  35-A MRSA §3209-A, sub-§1, ¶A-1 is enacted to read:
3 A-1.  "Discrete electric generating facility" means a facility that:
4 (1)  Is not located on a lot or parcel or more than one adjoining lot or parcel created 
5 by a division on or after November 22, 2019, except when the commission 
6 determines that the primary purpose for such division was not the development of 
7 a discrete electric generating facility on the resulting lot or parcel;
8 (2)  Has its own point of interconnection with an investor-owned transmission and 
9 distribution utility separate from any adjacent facility, including distinct metering 
10 and the ability to be connected to or disconnected from the transmission and 
11 distribution utility's system;
12 (3)  Is fenced separately from any adjacent facility and not commingled with or 
13 connected to any adjacent facility other than by the investor-owned transmission 
14 and distribution utility's system;
15 (4)  Has generation components, including, but not limited to, panels and inverters, 
16 that are separate from the generation components of any adjacent facility but may 
17 share nonoperational infrastructure with an adjacent facility such as access roads, 
18 utility poles and other infrastructure necessary to provide physical access to each 
19 facility; and
20 (5)  Is located on a lot or a parcel or more than one adjoining lot or parcel where 
21 the total nameplate capacity of the facility does not exceed 5 megawatts.
22Sec. 2.  35-A MRSA §3209-A, sub-§1, ¶B, as enacted by PL 2019, c. 478, Pt. A, 
23 §3, is amended to read:
24 B.  "Distributed generation resource" means an a discrete electric generating facility 
25 that uses a renewable fuel or technology under section 3210, subsection 2, paragraph 
26 B‑3 and is located in the service territory of a transmission and distribution utility in 
27 the State.
28Sec. 3.  35-A MRSA §3481, sub-§4-A is enacted to read:
294-A.  Discrete electric generating facility.  "Discrete electric generating facility" has 
30 the same meaning as in section 3209-A, subsection 1, paragraph A-1.
31Sec. 4.  35-A MRSA §3481, sub-§5, as enacted by PL 2019, c. 478, Pt. B, §1, is 
32 amended to read:
335.  Distributed generation resource.  "Distributed generation resource" means an a 
34 discrete electric generating facility with a nameplate capacity of less than 5 megawatts that 
35 uses a renewable fuel or technology under section 3210, subsection 2, paragraph B‑3 and 
36 is located in the service territory of a transmission and distribution utility in the State.
37SUMMARY
38 This bill defines "discrete electric generating facility" for the purposes of net energy 
39 billing and distributed generation laws.38
