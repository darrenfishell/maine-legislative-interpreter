13SUMMARY
14 This bill is submitted by the Criminal Law Advisory Commission pursuant to the 
15 Maine Revised Statutes, Title 17-A, section 1354, subsection 2. The bill makes technical 
16 corrections to the law governing the sealing of juvenile case records to facilitate 
17 implementation of the law and reflect how information is transmitted by the court to the 
18 Department of Public Safety, Bureau of State Police, State Bureau of Identification.  The 
19 bill does not make any substantive changes to the law.14
