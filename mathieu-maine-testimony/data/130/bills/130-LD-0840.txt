3ยง760.  Indemnification
4 A person is liable to the owner or operator of the overhead high-voltage line and 3rd 
5 parties, if any, for all damages to facilities, injuries to persons and all costs, expenses and 
6 liabilities incurred by the owner or operator of the overhead high-voltage lines and 3rd 
7 parties, if any, as a result of any contact with an overhead high-voltage line if the person 
8 causes, permits or allows any work or activity in violation of a provision of this chapter 
9 and, as a result, a physical or electrical contact with an overhead high-voltage line occurs, 
10 except that if such damages, injuries and incurred costs, expenses and liabilities are caused 
11 by the combined fault of the person and the owner or operator of the overhead high-voltage 
12 line, the liability of the person is joint and several with the owner or operator of the 
13 overhead high-voltage line.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill amends the Overhead High-voltage Line Safety Act to provide an exception 
16 to indemnification by allowing for allocated liability if damages to facilities and injuries to 
17 persons is caused by the combined fault of a person and the owner or operator of an 
18 overhead high-voltage line.15
