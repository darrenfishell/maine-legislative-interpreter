17SUMMARY
18 This bill makes permanent the changes to the liquor laws made by Public Law 2021, 
19 chapters 3 and 91, which were to be repealed September 10, 2022, allowing qualified on-
20 premises retailers and qualified distilleries to sell liquor for off-premises consumption 
21 under certain conditions and the sale of cocktails for on-premises consumption at 
22 distilleries and small distilleries.18
