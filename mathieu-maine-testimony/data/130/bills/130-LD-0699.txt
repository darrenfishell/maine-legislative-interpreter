5ยง3931.  Kinship care navigator program
61.  Kinship care defined.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise 
7 indicates, "kinship care" means the care of a child on a temporary or permanent basis, by a 
8 person who is not the child's parent but who is either a relative or a person who has a close 
9 relationship with the child, whether or not the department was involved with the placement 
10 of the child with the person.
112.  Program established.  The kinship care navigator program, referred to in this 
12 chapter as "the program," is established within the department to provide resources and 
13 information to persons providing kinship care to children in the State.  The commissioner 
14 shall designate one person to administer the program.
153.  Services.  The program may:
16 A.  Identify and coordinate existing resources available to persons providing kinship 
17 care to children;
18 B.  Develop collaborative working relationships with state agencies, social services 
19 agencies and other organizations serving persons providing kinship care to children; 
20 and
21 C.  Provide to persons providing kinship care to children referrals to available resources 
22 and information about the means of obtaining services from the department.
23SUMMARY
24 This bill establishes within the Department of Health and Human Services the kinship 
25 care navigator program to provide resources and information to persons providing kinship 
26 care to children in the State.  It requires the Commissioner of Health and Human Services 
27 to designate one person to administer the program.24
