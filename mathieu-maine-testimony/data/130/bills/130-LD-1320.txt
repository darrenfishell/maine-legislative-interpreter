20SUMMARY
21 This bill allows corporators, members and holders of a proxy of a corporator or member 
22 of a mutual bank or holding company and shareholders and holders of a proxy of a 
23 shareholder of an investor-owned bank to hold meetings remotely and to participate in 
24 meetings by means of remote communication.21
