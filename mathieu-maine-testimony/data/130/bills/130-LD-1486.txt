23SUMMARY
24 This bill amends the Maine Criminal Code by providing that a person who poses as a 
25 minor is deemed a minor for the purposes of a sex crime that has as an element or 
26 aggravating factor of the crime that the victim or person other than the actor is a minor. 
27 This bill also adds as conduct punishable under the crime of patronizing prostitution of a 
28 minor that the person committing the crime believes that the person whose prostitution is 
29 sought is a minor, and the bill makes the new crime a Class C crime and changes the 
30 existing crime of patronizing prostitution of a person who has not in fact attained 18 years 
31 of age from a Class D crime to a Class C crime.24
