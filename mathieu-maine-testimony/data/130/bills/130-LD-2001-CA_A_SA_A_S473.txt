SUMMARY 
This amendment limits the notification by the Board of Trustees of the Maine Veterans' 
Homes to instances of discontinuation of services at or the closure of a public home for 
veterans. It shortens the time by which the joint standing committee having jurisdiction 
over veterans affairs must hold a public hearing to 10 days following notice by the board 
and removes the requirement that the board wait 90 days following adjournment of the 
legislative session to which the report was . submitted before taking action on the 
discontinuation of services or closure of the home.
