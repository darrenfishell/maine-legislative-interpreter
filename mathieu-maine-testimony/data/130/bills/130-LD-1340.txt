16SUMMARY
17 This bill provides that a municipality is ineligible to receive general purpose aid for 
18 local schools, municipal general assistance and state-municipal revenue sharing if that 
19 municipality prohibits or restricts, formally or informally, the exchange of information with 
20 federal immigration authorities or any other federal, state or local government entity 
21 regarding the immigration status, lawful or unlawful, of any individual or the maintenance 
22 of such information.17
