31SUMMARY
32 This bill makes the following changes to the laws governing marine port terminal 
33 facility development.
34 1. It limits the actions of the Maine Port Authority and the use of the proceeds of bonds 
35 issued by the Maine Port Authority to those actions and uses pertaining to marine port 
36 terminal facilities and railroad facilities that directly support marine port operations.
37 2. It expands the sources of funds that may be used to undertake projects that link 
38 marine port terminal facilities to freight networks within the State.
39 3. It expands the scope of the Maine Port Terminal Facilities Marketing Program.
40 4. It requires the Maine Port Authority to hire an executive director and authorizes the 
41 executive director to hire certain other employees.
42 5. It requires the Maine Port Authority to submit an annual report to the Legislature.32
