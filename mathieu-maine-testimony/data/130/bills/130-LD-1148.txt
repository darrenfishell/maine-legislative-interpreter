Page 1 - 130LR0637(01)1Sec. 1.  Broadband access.  Resolved:  That the Department of Health and Human 
2 Services shall work with providers of all levels of private nonmedical institution services 
3 to determine ways of providing no-cost broadband access to residents for medical, 
4 educational and recreational purposes through changes in MaineCare and grant-funded 
5 contracts.  The department shall submit a report, including suggested legislation, to the 
6 Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human Services by December 1, 2021.  The 
7 committee may submit a bill related to the report to the Second Regular Session of the 
8 130th Legislature.
9SUMMARY
10 This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to work with 
11 providers of all levels of private nonmedical institution services to determine ways of 
12 providing no-cost broadband access to residents for medical, educational and recreational 
13 purposes through changes in MaineCare and grant-funded contracts.  The department is 
14 required to submit a report, including suggested legislation, by December 1, 2021 to the 
15 Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human Services, which may submit a bill related 
16 to the report to the Second Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.10
