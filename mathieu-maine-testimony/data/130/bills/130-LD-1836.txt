34SUMMARY
35 This bill amends the laws governing certain processes pertaining to financial institution 
36 mergers and consolidations, increases the capital required to charter a new institution, 
37 replaces a United States Treasury target rate for mortgage escrows with an updated 
38 substantially equivalent rate and clarifies that the Superintendent of Financial Institutions 
39 may engage experts if needed to carry out regulatory functions.35
