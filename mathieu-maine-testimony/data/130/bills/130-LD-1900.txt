42.  Notice and name change; adults; notice.  Upon receipt of a petition filed under

31SUMMARY
32 This bill provides that notice of the filing of a petition to change an adult's name must 
33 be provided only to interested parties as required by law and that notice of a petition to 
34 change a minor's name must be provided only to any other parent, any guardian and any 
35 person or agency with legal custody of the minor; to the guardian ad litem, if any; and to 
36 the minor if the minor is 14 years of age or older.  The court may limit that notice to protect 
37 the safety of the adult or minor.32
