3Sec. 4.  Deed and restrictions.  Resolved:  That the property must be conveyed by 
4 quit claim quitclaim deed without covenants, subject to the following deed restrictions:  
5 The Town of Carrabassett Valley shall in perpetuity retain title to the property and may not 
6 sell or otherwise transfer any interest, in whole or in part, in the property except that the 
7 Town of Carrabassett Valley may lease portions of the property as long as the uses are 
8 consistent with the uses specified in this resolve.  The property must remain open and 
9 available for use and enjoyment by the public at large.  Use of the property must be 
10 dedicated for purposes of public outdoor recreation, including, but not limited to: natural 
11 history study; hiking; camping; cross-country skiing; hunting; fishing; fisheries and 
12 wildlife management; skating; timber management and harvesting under a management 
13 plan prepared by a licensed professional forester; and attendant roads and parking.  The 
14 property must be maintained in an essentially natural and undeveloped condition, except 
15 that up to 25 contiguous acres in the aggregate, including any development in existence on 
16 the effective date of this resolve, may be developed for any municipal facility.  The 
17 following uses are expressly prohibited: residential development of any type; and 
18 development for overnight accommodations, except camping, including commercial 
19 camping if development plans for commercial camping are approved by the State; 
20 development for any type of commercial service center, shops, restaurants or other 
21 commercial development; or development for any purpose that will change the natural 
22 character of the area, except that those uses presently made of the Outdoor and Touring 
23 Center located on the property may continue.  If the Town of Carrabassett Valley fails to 
24 comply with any of the conditions or restrictions, in whole or in part, contained in this 
25 resolve, the State may give written notice to the Town of Carrabassett Valley, and if the 
26 Town of Carrabassett Valley fails to comply within 30 days, then the title to the property 
27 reverts to the State; such a reversion may not be effective until the State records a notice of 
28 the reversion in the Franklin County Registry of Deeds.
29SUMMARY
30 This resolve amends the deed transferring land from the State to the Town of 
31 Carrabassett Valley to remove the prohibition against using the land for a commercial 
32 purpose.30
