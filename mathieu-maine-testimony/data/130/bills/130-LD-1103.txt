25Sec. 6.  Application.  Notwithstanding the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 
26 18252-C, subsection 1, an employee who has been employed for more than 5 years on the 
27 effective date of this Act may make a one-time election to join the Participating Local 
28 District Retirement Program by November 1, 2021. A person electing to join must be 
29 granted service as described in Title 5, section 18252-C, subsection 3 and must make 
30 contributions according to Title 5, section 18252-C, subsection 4.
31SUMMARY
32 This bill allows employees in a participating local district to enroll in the Participating 
33 Local District Retirement Program up to and including that employee's 5th-year 
34 employment anniversary through an annual open enrollment. It also provides a one-time 
35 election for participating local district employees whose 5-year work anniversary has 
36 passed to join the Participating Local District Retirement Program.1
