17 Notwithstanding any provision of this chapter to the contrary, the following provisions 
18 apply. 
191.  Issuance of license; provision of health care services in person or through 
20telehealth.  The board shall issue a license to any nurse who seeks immediate licensure 
21 who is licensed in good standing in another state and has not been subject to a disciplinary 
22 or adverse action in the past 10 years involving loss of license, probation or practice 
23 restriction or limitation.  A nurse licensed under this subsection may provide health care 
24 services within the scope of practice of the nurse's license through an in-person encounter 
25 in this State or across state lines into this State using telehealth, as defined in Title 24-A,

27 licenses issued pursuant to this subsection must be established by the board.
282.  Issuance of license for retired nurse.  Upon request, the board shall immediately 
29 issue a license to a nurse who retired in good standing in this State within 2 years of the 
30 effective date of this section and who has not been subject to a disciplinary or adverse 
31 action in the past 10 years involving loss of license, probation or practice restriction or 
32 limitation and has no outstanding complaints or open investigations.  The board may 
33 impose a fee for a license issued or renewed pursuant to this subsection.
343.  Permitted activity without license.  A nurse who is licensed in good standing in 
35 another state and who has patients residing in this State who travel to another state to 
36 receive ongoing health care from that nurse may provide those health care services to those

40 related to mandatory supervision or collaborative practice agreements for an otherwise

37SUMMARY
38 This bill makes permanent the authorization for licensing out-of-state and recently 
39 retired doctors, physician assistants and nurses that was provided through executive order 
40 during the declared state of civil emergency related to COVID-19.  The bill also expands 
41 the scope of health care services that can be provided though telehealth to include the use 
42 of audio-only telephone.38
