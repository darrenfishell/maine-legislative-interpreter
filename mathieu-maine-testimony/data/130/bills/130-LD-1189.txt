5SUMMARY
6 This bill does the following regarding the certification of education personnel.
7 1. It requires an educational technician to get an authorization rather than a certificate 
8 for serving in public schools or approved private schools.
9 2.  It amends the definition of "educational specialist."
10 3. It repeals the requirement for rules adopted by the State Board of Education to 
11 include that applicants furnish evidence of good moral character.
12 4.  It revises the qualifications for a professional teacher certificate.
13 5.  It provides that a criminal conviction for a criminal offense involving physical or 
14 sexual abuse or exploitation of a child within 5 years of an application for initial 
15 certification or renewal is grounds for a denial of a certificate.
16 6.  It amends the definition, qualifications and terms of issuance of a certification for a 
17 school psychologist.
18 7.  It directs the State Board of Education to amend its certification rules.  Rules 
19 amended for the purpose of implementing this legislation are designated as routine 
20 technical rules rather than major substantive rules.6
