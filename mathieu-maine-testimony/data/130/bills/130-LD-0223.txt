12SUMMARY
13 This bill requires payment of a $50 reinstatement fee to reinstate a suspended or 
14 revoked license, permit or registration under the inland fisheries and wildlife laws.  It 
15 includes language that is consistent with all-terrain vehicle registration requirements in 
16 regard to the form and display of snowmobile registration numbers and stickers and 
17 provides that a snowmobile registered after May 1st and prior to July 1st has a registration 
18 that is valid for the upcoming winter and expires June 30th of the following year.  It also 
19 allows a nonresident junior trapper to purchase a trapping license at the resident junior rate.
