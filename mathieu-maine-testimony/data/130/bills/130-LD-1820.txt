30SUMMARY
31 This bill adds 3 members each to the Board of Trustees of the University of Maine 
32 System and the Board of Trustees of the Maine Community College System: a faculty 
33 member, a nonfaculty staff member and an adjunct faculty member, notwithstanding the 
34 Maine Revised Statutes, Title 17, section 3104, which governs conflicts of interest and 
35 contracts made on behalf of the State.  The members are nonvoting members, serve 2-year 
36 terms and are appointed by the Governor from a list provided by their respective unions.  
37 The successive members for a seat may not come from the same campus in any 2 
38 consecutive terms.  They receive reasonable leave with pay at their university salaries for 
39 board meetings and other board business conducted during working hours, and their 
40 compensation as university employees is not otherwise altered.  The bill also requires that 
41 the Governor solicit lists of eligible candidates for initial appointments within 60 days of 
42 the effective date of this Act. 1
