3SUMMARY
4 This bill improves the employment tax increment financing program for calendar years 
5 beginning with 2022 by basing benefits on the gross wages of qualified employees rather 
6 than on income tax withholding for those employees.  The bill directs the Commissioner 
7 of Economic and Community Development and the State Tax Assessor to take actions, 
8 including the adoption of routine technical rules, to implement the change.1
