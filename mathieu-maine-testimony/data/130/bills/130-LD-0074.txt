Page 1 - 130LR0085(01)1Sec. 1.  Personal needs allowance for residents of nursing facilities.  
2Resolved:  That, by January 1, 2022, the Department of Health and Human Services shall 
3 amend the rules for the MaineCare program to increase the personal needs allowance for 
4 persons residing in nursing facilities.  The rules must provide for an increase in the personal 
5 needs allowance from $40 per month to $70 per month.  Rules adopted pursuant to this 
6 section are routine technical rules as defined in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 
7 375, subchapter 2-A.
8Sec. 2.  Personal needs allowance for residents of residential care facilities.  
9Resolved:  That, by January 1, 2022, the Department of Health and Human Services shall 
10 amend the rules for the MaineCare program to increase the personal needs allowance for 
11 persons residing in residential care facilities.  The rules must provide for an increase in the 
12 personal needs allowance from $70 per month to $100 per month.  Rules adopted pursuant 
13 to this section are routine technical rules as defined in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, 
14 chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
15SUMMARY
16 This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its 
17 MaineCare rules to provide for increases in the personal needs allowances of residents in 
18 nursing facilities and residential care facilities.  The rules are designated as routine 
19 technical rules.
