Page 1 - 130LR0350(01)1Sec. 1.  Department of Health and Human Services to amend rules. 
2Resolved:  That, notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, no later than 
3 December 1, 2021, the Department of Health and Human Services shall amend its rules in 
4 Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 29, Allowances for Support 
5 Services for Adults with Intellectual Disabilities or Autism Spectrum Disorder to increase 
6 reimbursement rates for professionals working with individuals with intellectual or 
7 developmental disabilities by a percentage equivalent to the cumulative increase in costs 
8 measured by the United States Department of Labor, Bureau of Labor Statistics Consumer 
9 Price Index medical care services index in each year from 2010 to 2020.
10SUMMARY
11 This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rules 
12 governing MaineCare reimbursement for professionals working with individuals with 
13 intellectual or developmental disabilities to provide an increase reflecting cost increases 
14 from 2010 to 2020.11
