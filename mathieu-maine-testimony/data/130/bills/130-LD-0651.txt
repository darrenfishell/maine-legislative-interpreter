14 §29, is further amended to read:
15 C.  Beginning in fiscal year 2018-19  2021-22:
16 (1)  The average of the pupil counts for October 1st of the 2 most recent calendar 
17 years prior to the year of funding, reported in accordance with section 6004, 
18 including the counts of students enrolled in an alternative education program made 
19 in accordance with section 5104‑A, except that if a school administrative unit 
20 experiences a decline in total school administrative unit student enrollment of 10% 
21 or more in a single year, the pupil count used for operating costs in this Act for that 
22 school administrative unit is the average of the pupil counts for October 1st of the 
23 3 most recent calendar years prior to the year of funding, reported in accordance 
24 with section 6004, including the counts of students enrolled in an alternative 
25 education program made in accordance with section 5104-A.
26Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
27 takes effect when approved.
28SUMMARY
29 This bill requires the Department of Education to use a 3-year average for pupil counts 
30 when a school administrative unit experiences a decrease of 10% or more of total school 
31 administrative unit student enrollment in a single year.29
