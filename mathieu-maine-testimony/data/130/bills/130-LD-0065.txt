Page 2 - 130LR0081(01)1SUMMARY
2 This bill allows a small portion of Land for Maine's Future program bond funds to be 
3 used to fund minor capital investments in the stewardship and management of properties 
4 acquired with funding from the Land for Maine's Future Fund or the Public Access to 
5 Maine Waters Fund, ensuring that these properties are maintained in perpetuity for the 
6 purposes for which the properties are conserved.
