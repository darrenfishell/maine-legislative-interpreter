5ยง220-A. Potato Farmers Disaster Relief Grant Program
61.  Grant program established.  The Potato Farmers Disaster Relief Grant Program, 
7 referred to in this section as "the program," is established in the department to assist potato 
8 farmers in the State to overcome the adverse effects of drought conditions by providing 
9 grants in accordance with this section.
102. Eligibility.  A potato farmer in the State may apply for a grant under the program if, 
11 as a result of drought conditions, the farmer's potato yield is significantly lower than that 
12 farmer's expected crop yield as determined by the most recent agricultural yield survey 
13 conducted by the United States Department of Agriculture's National Agricultural Statistics 
14 Service.
153.  Potato Farmers Disaster Relief Grant Program Fund.  The Potato Farmers 
16 Disaster Relief Grant Program Fund is established as a nonlapsing fund to provide funding 
17 to achieve the purposes of the program.  The fund consists of any funds received from 
18 private and public sources.  The fund must be held separate and apart from all other money, 
19 funds and accounts.  Any balance remaining in the fund at the end of any fiscal year must 
20 be carried forward to the next fiscal year.
214.  Rules.  The department shall adopt rules to implement the program.  The rules must 
22 include grant eligibility requirements, grant application and award procedures and grant 
23 funding limits.  Rules adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as 
24 defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
25SUMMARY
26 This bill establishes the Potato Farmers Disaster Relief Grant Program to assist potato 
27 farmers in the State to overcome the adverse effects of drought conditions by providing 
28 grants.26
