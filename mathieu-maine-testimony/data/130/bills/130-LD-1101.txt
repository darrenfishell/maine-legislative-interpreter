28SUMMARY
29 This bill allows civilian employees in the employment of the Department of Public 
30 Safety, Maine State Police Crime Laboratory or computer crimes unit whose job 
31 responsibilities include the handling, examination or analysis of digital or physical 
32 evidence to elect to participate in the 1998 Special Plan of the Maine Public Employees 
33 Retirement System.  Under that plan, a person may retire at 55 years of age with 10 years 
34 of creditable service or may retire before 55 years of age with 25 years of creditable service 
35 and at a reduced benefit.29
