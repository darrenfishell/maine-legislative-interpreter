36 property on or after April 1, 2023.
37SUMMARY
38 Current law provides an exemption from property taxes in an amount up to $6,000 of 
39 the just value of the property to veterans of the Armed Forces of the United States who are 
40 disabled by injury or disease during active military service.  This bill provides a property 
41 tax exemption up to 100% of the just value to a veteran who is totally and permanently 
42 disabled.38
