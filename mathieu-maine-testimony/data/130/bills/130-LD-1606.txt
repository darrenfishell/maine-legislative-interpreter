4 A corporation organized under this Title that enters into a rental agreement for dwelling

9SUMMARY
10 This bill requires a nonprofit corporation that provides dwelling units in a multi-unit 
11 residential building to include a current tenant on its board of directors.  It does not apply 
12 to a nonprofit corporation that only provides emergency shelter or short-term transitional
