3SUMMARY
4 Public Law 2019, chapter 274 enacted the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 22, section 
5 3196, which requires the Department of Health and Human Services to provide coverage 
6 to a MaineCare member for abortion services.  Abortion services that are not federally 
7 approved Medicaid services must be funded by the State.
8 This bill repeals Title 22, section 3196, removing the requirement that the department 
9 fund abortion services for MaineCare members and removing the requirement that the State 
10 pay when the abortion services are not covered by Medicaid.
