32SUMMARY
33 This bill expands the membership of the board of trustees of the Maine Criminal Justice 
34 Academy from 18 to 19 by adding a county commissioner who is not and has never been a 
35 sworn member of a law enforcement agency.  The bill also expands the total membership 
36 of the board's complaint review committee from 3 to 5 and the number of members who 
37 are citizens who have not and never have been sworn members of a law enforcement 
38 agency from one to 2.  The bill further allows deliberations when a majority of the members 
39 of the committee, including at least one citizen member, is present instead of requiring that 
40 all members be present. 33
