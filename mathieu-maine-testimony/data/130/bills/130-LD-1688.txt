15SUMMARY
16 This bill addresses inconsistencies in the protections provided in different areas of 
17 jurisdiction under the Maine Human Rights Act.  The bill provides more inclusive 
18 protection by:
19 1. Including adult family members dependent for care in the definition of "familial 
20 status";
21 2. Including familial status as a protected class in employment;
22 3. Including age as a protected class in public accommodations; and
23 4. Clarifying the scope of the Maine Human Rights Act application in education.
24 The bill also clarifies that the sexual orientation provisions in the Maine Human Rights 
25 Act extend to gender identity.16
