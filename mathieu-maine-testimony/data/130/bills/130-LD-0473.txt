5ยง4994-A.  Maine Rental Assistance and Voucher Guarantee Program 
6 The Maine Rental Assistance and Voucher Guarantee Program, referred to in this 
7 section as "the program," is established within the Maine State Housing Authority. The 
8 program must include a rental assistance component to assist individuals with the cost of 
9 rent and a rental voucher guarantee component to encourage landlords to work with the 
10 program and other rental assistance programs in the State. 
111. Rental assistance.  The rental assistance component of the program must be 
12 administered in accordance with this subsection. 
13 A. The Maine State Housing Authority shall establish and administer eligibility criteria 
14 and application requirements for tenants or prospective tenants to participate in the 
15 program.
16 B. Rental assistance payments for program participants must be paid from the fund 
17 established in section 4994-B directly to the landlord of the participant or to a property 
18 management company designated by the landlord. 
19 C. A landlord may receive rental assistance payments under the program only if the 
20 landlord agrees to:
21 (1)  Exclude credit score, rental history and last month's rent from the rental 
22 application;
23 (2)  Work with individuals referred to as "housing navigators" affiliated with 
24 housing authorities, general assistance programs or nonprofit organizations who 
25 assist tenants with locating housing and the rental process and who provide 
26 mentoring services to promote successful landlord-tenant relationships;
27 (3)  Waive tenant late fees for any month in which rental assistance is paid by the 
28 program; and
29 (4)  Not evict a program participant for nonpayment of rent during any month for 
30 which rental assistance is paid. 
312.  Rental voucher guarantee.  The rental voucher guarantee component of the 
32 program must be administered in accordance with this subsection. 
33 A. The purpose of the rental voucher guarantee program component is to encourage 
34 landlords to work with the program and other rental assistance programs available in 
35 the State, including but not limited to rental assistance programs administered by or 
36 available through the Maine State Housing Authority, local housing authorities, other 
37 state and local agencies, federal agencies, municipalities or tribal governments. 
Page 2 - 130LR1421(01)1 B. The Maine State Housing Authority shall annually dedicate $1,000,000 of the fund 
2 established in section 4994-B for the rental voucher guarantee component of the 
3 program.  These funds may be used as follows: 
4 (1)  To hire individuals referred to as "housing navigators" affiliated with local 
5 housing authorities, general assistance programs or nonprofit organizations to 
6 assist tenants with locating housing, to assist with the rental process and to provide 
7 mentoring services to promote successful landlord-tenant relationships;
8 (2)  To provide a letter of credit to landlords who enter into a 12-month rental 
9 agreement with a person receiving rental assistance through a municipal general 
10 assistance program under Title 22, chapter 1161. The letter of credit must guarantee 
11 up to one month of rental assistance and verified property damages, not to exceed 
12 $2,000, in the event the tenancy is unsuccessful;
13 (3)  To expand the availability of programs that assist landlords who participate in 
14 rental assistance programs available in the State, as described in paragraph A, with 
15 mitigation and repair of damages to units, mitigation of lost funds due to unpaid 
16 rent and tenant payment of security deposits; and 
17 (4)  To connect landlords with resources for the abatement of lead paint hazards in 
18 residential housing. 
193. Rules.  The Maine State Housing Authority may adopt rules to implement this 
20 section. Rules adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in 
21 Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
