61.  Recognition.  The Kineo Band of Maliseet Indians is recognized by the State as a 
7 Native American tribe.
82.  Effect of recognition.  Recognition of the Kineo Band of Maliseet Indians may not 
9 be construed to create, extend or form the basis of any right or claim to land or real estate 
10 in the State or any right to conduct gambling activities prohibited by law.  The Kineo Band 
11 of Maliseet Indians and the individual members of the band remain subject to all the laws 
12 of the State.
13SUMMARY
14 This bill provides for state recognition of the Kineo Band of Maliseet Indians as a 
15 Native American tribe.  Recognition does not create, extend or form the basis of any right 
16 or claim to land or real estate in the State or any right to conduct gambling activities 
17 prohibited by law.  The Kineo Band of Maliseet Indians and the individual members of the 
18 band remain subject to all the laws of the State.
