21 SUMMARY 
22 This amendment directs a vendor to provide a delivery ticket by e-mail if a purchaser 
23 of a bulk delivery of liquid fuel sold for heating residential, industrial or commercial space 
24 or water or motor vehicle fuel submits a written request to receive a delivery ticket by e-
25 mail instead of a paper delivery ticket. The amendment provides that delivery tickets 
26 provided by e-mail must be provided to the. purchaser by e-mail within 24 hours after 
27 delivery.
