3ยง13056-I.  Maine Municipal Carbon Reduction Initiatives Program
41.  Program established.  The Maine Municipal Carbon Reduction Initiatives 
5 Program, referred to in this section as "the program," is established within the department 
6 to provide grants to municipalities for technology and innovation projects that further the 
7 goals of the climate action plan adopted under Title 38, section 577.  Grants to any 
8 municipality under the program are limited to $50,000 each during any state budget 
9 biennium.
102.  Fund established.  The Maine Municipal Carbon Reduction Initiatives Fund, 
11 referred to in this section as "the fund," is established as a nonlapsing fund within the 
12 department.
133.  Criteria; applications.  The department shall solicit applications from 
14 municipalities for grants from the fund through a competitive application process 
15 established by the department by rule.  The process must require that an application include, 
16 at a minimum:
17 A.  A description of a proposed technology and innovation project that will further the 
18 goals of the climate action plan adopted under Title 38, section 577;
19 B.  Data that demonstrates that the proposed project will reduce carbon emissions; and
20 C.  A statement of project readiness.
214. Rulemaking. Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules as 
22 defined in chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
23SUMMARY
24 This bill establishes the Maine Municipal Carbon Reduction Initiatives Program for 
25 the purpose of providing competitive grants for towns and cities to help fund technology 
26 and innovation projects that will reduce carbon emissions and that are in line with Maine's 
27 climate action plan. 24
