16SUMMARY
17 This bill implements recommendations of the Right To Know Advisory Committee 
18 concerning the fees that an agency or official may charge to cover the costs of responding 
19 to public records requests.
20 The bill does the following.
21 1.  It extends the time period for which an agency or official may not charge a fee for 
22 searching for, retrieving and compiling a requested public record from the first hour of staff 
23 time per request to the first 2 hours of staff time per request.
24 2.  It increases the fee that may be charged after the first 2 hours of staff time per request 
25 for searching for, retrieving and compiling a requested public record from not more than 
26 $15 per hour to not more than $25 per hour. 
27 3.  It specifies that an agency may retain any fee or cost charged.17
