41.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
5 following terms have the following meanings.
6 A.  "Applicant" means an individual that applies for a grant under this section.
7 B.  "Fund" means the Satellite-based Broadband Grant Fund established in subsection 
8 2.
9 C.  "Satellite-based provider" means a broadband service provider that provides 
10 Internet access directly to consumers using satellite technology.
112.  Fund established.  The Satellite-based Broadband Grant Fund is established as a 
12 nonlapsing fund administered by the commission to provide grants to applicants in 
13 accordance with the purposes in subsection 3.  All money in the fund must be continuously 
14 applied by the commission to carry out this section.  The commission may receive and 
15 deposit in the fund funds from the following sources:
16 A.  Federal funds and awards that may be used for the purposes of this section; and
17 B.  Any other funds from public or private sources received in support of the purposes 
18 for which the fund is established.
193.  Purpose of fund.  The fund is established to address the need in the State for access 
20 to broadband services, particularly in the unserved or underserved areas of the State, by 
21 providing grants to eligible applicants for installation and other advance costs associated 
22 with the acquisition of broadband access from a satellite-based provider.  To the extent 
23 funds are available, the fund must be used to provide grants to applicants meeting the 
24 requirements of subsection 4 and in accordance with the award criteria in subsection 5.
254.  Grant requirements.  An applicant for a grant under this section must submit an 
26 application to the commission on a form prepared and made available by the commission.  
27 The commission shall establish application requirements for grants under this section, 
28 which must include, but are not limited to:
29 A.  A requirement that grants be awarded only to individuals applying on behalf of a 
30 residential household and that grants may not be awarded to business entities;
31 B.  A requirement that an applicant may not have previously received a grant under the 
32 program;
33 C.  A requirement that an applicant provide information regarding the satellite-based 
34 provider from which the applicant intends to acquire broadband access;
35 D.  A requirement that an applicant provide documentation of the installation and other 
36 advance costs required by the satellite-based provider for the applicant to initially 
37 acquire broadband access;
38 E.  A prohibition on the awarding of grants for broadband access costs not described 
39 in paragraph D, such as monthly service charges; and
Page 2 - 130LR0328(01)1 F.  A prohibition on the awarding of grants for broadband access costs associated with 
2 broadband providers that are not satellite-based providers.
35.  Awarding of grants; additional criteria.  To the extent funds are available and in 
4 accordance with this subsection, the commission shall award grants to applicants that have 
5 provided the required information under subsection 4 and meet any other eligibility criteria 
6 established by the commission.  
7 A.  The maximum amount of a grant that may be awarded to an applicant is $500.
8 B.  The commission may not award more than one grant under this section to the same 
9 residential household.
10 C.  The commission shall prioritize the awarding of grants to applicants located in 
11 unserved or underserved areas of the State.
126.  Collaboration with ConnectMaine Authority; technical assistance.  At the 
13 request of the commission, the authority shall provide to the commission all information 
14 and assistance necessary for the administration of this section.  The commission may 
15 provide technical assistance to applicants who request assistance with the grant application 
16 process under this section.
177.  Rules; application procedure.  The commission may adopt rules to implement this 
18 section, including rules governing the application process for grants under this section.  
19 Rules adopted under this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 
20 375, subchapter 2-A.

28SUMMARY
29 This bill establishes a satellite-based broadband grant program, to be developed and 
30 administered by the Public Utilities Commission, to provide grants of up to $500 to 
31 individuals for installation costs and other advance costs associated with the initial 
32 acquisition of broadband access from a satellite-based broadband provider.  Under the 
33 program, grant priority is to be given to applicants from unserved or underserved areas of 
34 the State.  This grant program is capitalized under the bill through a one-time General Fund 
35 appropriation of $1,000,000.28
