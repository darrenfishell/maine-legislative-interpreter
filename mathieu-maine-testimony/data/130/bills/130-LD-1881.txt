Page 2 - 130LR2438(01)1SUMMARY
2 This bill provides that:
3 1. A decision, lump-sum settlement or agreement under the Maine Workers' 
4 Compensation Act of 1992 may not prohibit an employee from engaging in conduct as a 
5 qualifying patient pursuant to the Maine Medical Use of Marijuana Act;
6 2. The Maine Medical Use of Marijuana Act may not be construed to require an 
7 insurance company providing workers' compensation insurance to reimburse a qualifying 
8 patient for costs associated with the medical use of marijuana; and
9 3. The Workers' Compensation Board must exclude evidence of an employee engaging 
10 in conduct as a qualifying patient authorized by the Maine Medical Use of Marijuana Act 
11 in any proceedings before the board.2
