SUMMARY 
2 This amendment is the majority report of the committee. The amendment changes the 
3 total homestead property tax exemption to $30,000 for property tax years beginning on or 
4 after April 1, 2022 and increases the percentage of state reimbursement of taxes lost by 
5 reason of the homestead exemption to municipalities by 3% per year up to 100%. It also 
6 adds an appropriations and allocations section.
