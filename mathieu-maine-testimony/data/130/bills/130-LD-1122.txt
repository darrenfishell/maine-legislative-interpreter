19SUMMARY
20 This bill authorizes the display of illuminated signs on delivery vehicles that transport 
21 customers or property as long as the only information on the sign is the name and telephone 
22 number of the company providing the delivery.  It also amends the law to clarify that an 
23 illuminated sign with the same information may be affixed to the roof of a vehicle for the 
24 conveyance of passengers. 20
