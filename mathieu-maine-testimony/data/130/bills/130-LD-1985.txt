Page 2 - 130LR2579(01)1SUMMARY
2 This bill provides an exemption to the required testing of adult use marijuana or a 
3 marijuana product if the marijuana or marijuana product has already undergone mandatory 
4 testing at the direction of another licensee and the marijuana or marijuana product has not 
5 undergone any further processing, manufacturing or alteration that would result in an 
6 increase in the concentration of any contaminants for which testing is required.2
