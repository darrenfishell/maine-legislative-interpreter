11SUMMARY
12 This bill allows the Maine State Cultural Affairs Council to conduct a public 
13 proceeding using telephonic, video, electronic or other means of remote participation when 
14 2 or more members are physically present at the location of the public proceeding identified 
15 in the notice for the meeting and the total number of members participating in the meeting, 
16 both physically present and participating remotely, constitute a quorum.12
