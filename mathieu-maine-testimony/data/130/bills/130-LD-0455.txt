11SUMMARY
12 This bill prohibits a municipality from enacting an ordinance governing the minimum 
13 hourly wage paid by an employer or requiring adjustments to the minimum hourly wage 
14 for hazard pay. The bill provides an exception to the prohibition that allows a municipality 
15 to regulate the minimum hourly wage and hazard pay of municipal employees.12
