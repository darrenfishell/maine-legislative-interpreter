7 SUMMARY 
8 This amendment increases the number of elected directors on the Board of Directors 
9 of the Maine International Trade Center from 7 to 11, rather than the 10 proposed in the 
10 bill. It removes the requirement that one of the 5 appointed directors be the International 
11 Trade Director at the Department of Economic and Community Development. It requires 
12 that the president of the Maine International Trade Center serve as an ex officio voting 
13 member of the board of directors.
