2Sec. 1.  36 MRSA §5122, sub-§2, ¶UU is enacted to read:
3 UU.  For tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2021, to the extent included in 
4 federal adjusted gross income, an amount equal to the amount of the taxpayer's 
5 contribution to an employee retirement plan or an individual retirement account, as 
6 those terms are defined in paragraph M-2, the taxpayer contributed to while the resident 
7 of another jurisdiction.  This paragraph does not apply to amounts deducted under 
8 paragraph M-2.  For purposes of this paragraph, "another jurisdiction" means another 
9 state or territory of the United States, a political subdivision of such a state or territory 
10 and the District of Columbia.
11SUMMARY
12 This bill exempts from Maine income tax the portion of retirement benefits attributable 
13 to a taxpayer's contribution to an employee retirement plan or an individual retirement 
14 account the taxpayer contributed to while the resident of another jurisdiction if those 
15 benefits are included in federal adjusted gross income.12
