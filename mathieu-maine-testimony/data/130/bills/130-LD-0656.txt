17§7057.  Employees of the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, office 
18of marijuana policy
19 Beginning January 1, 2022, a person may not be employed by or engage in a 
20 compensated activity for an entity registered under Title 22, chapter 558-C or licensed 
21 under Title 28-B, chapter 1 if that person has within the previous 24 months provided 
22 compensated or uncompensated services for or been employed as an officer or employee

24 service of this State by the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, office of 
25 marijuana policy.
26SUMMARY
27 This bill requires a person who was employed by or otherwise worked for the 
28 Department of Administrative and Financial Services, office of marijuana policy to wait at 
29 least 24 months before being employed by or engaging in compensated activities as a 
30 lobbyist or being employed by or engaging in compensated activities for an entity requiring 
31 licensure or registration under the adult use or medical marijuana provisions of the Maine 
32 Revised Statutes.27
