17SUMMARY
18 This bill requires the Department of Labor with the assistance of the Department of 
19 Health and Human Services to gather data regarding the number of persons who are 
20 employed by businesses in this State that have 50 or more employees and who receive 
21 public benefits, including but not limited to benefits under the federal Temporary 
22 Assistance for Needy Families program, the federal Supplemental Nutrition Assistance 
23 Program and the MaineCare program; municipal general assistance; Section 8 housing 
24 assistance; and the Bridging Rental Assistance Program.  The data gathering process must 
25 respect the privacy rights of individual employees, and the data gathered may not identify 
26 individual employees.  The department must report its findings to the joint standing 
27 committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over labor matters no later than December 
28 1st each year.18
