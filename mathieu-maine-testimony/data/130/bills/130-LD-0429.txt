221. Duties; priorities. The health and safety committee shall coordinate planning and 
23 response to health and safety matters affecting the students and staff of the school 
24 administrative unit.  For the duration of a state of emergency declared by the Governor 
25 pursuant to Title 37-B, section 742 due to the outbreak of coronavirus disease 2019, or 
26 COVID-19, a health and safety committee established under this section shall prioritize 
27 planning and response to health and safety concerns associated with the outbreak of 
28 COVID-19.
292. Membership. The health and safety committee must include as members:
30 A. Teachers and other school employees, who must make up a majority of committee 
31 members and must be selected by the local representative of the applicable collective 
32 bargaining unit if the teachers in the school administrative unit are covered by a 
33 collective bargaining agreement;
34 B. School administrators of the school administrative unit selected by the 
35 superintendent;
36 C. Members of the school board selected by that board; and
37 D. Parents of students attending schools in the school administrative unit selected by 
38 the unit's organized parent group or groups or, if no organized parent group exists, by 
39 the school board.

7SUMMARY
8 This bill requires each school administrative unit to establish a health and safety 
9 committee to coordinate planning and response to health and safety matters affecting 
10 students and staff of the school administrative unit.  It requires these committees to 
11 prioritize planning and response to health and safety concerns associated with the outbreak 
12 of COVID-19 while there is in effect a state of emergency declared by the Governor due 
13 to COVID-19. The bill specifies that each committee must include teachers and other 
14 school employees, school administrators, school board members and parents of students.8
