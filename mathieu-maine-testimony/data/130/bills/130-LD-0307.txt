5Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
6 takes effect when approved.
7SUMMARY
8 This bill clarifies that licensed Maine manufacturers of spirits, wine, malt liquor and 
9 low-alcohol spirits products may sell and ship their products to a person located in another 
10 state, as long as the sale and shipment are both authorized by and conducted in accordance 
11 with the requirements of the law of the state where the shipment is delivered.8
