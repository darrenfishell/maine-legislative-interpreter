15SUMMARY
16 This bill amends the law prohibiting the unauthorized practice of law to allow a marine 
17 patrol officer who is not an attorney to represent the Department of Marine Resources in a 
18 hearing under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 12, section 6374, subsection 2 for 
19 administrative suspension of a license or certificate.
