2Sec. 1.  28-B MRSA §504-A is enacted to read:
3§504-A.  Off-premises sales
4 Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the department may issue a 
5 permit to a marijuana store licensee to sell adult use marijuana or adult use marijuana 
6 products at a specified event located outside the licensed premises in accordance with the 
7 requirements of this section. Transportation of adult use marijuana and adult use marijuana 
8 products between the licensed premises and the specified event is subject to the 
9 requirements of section 505.
101. Limitations on off-premises sales. A permit issued by the department under this 
11 section for a specified event may not authorize:
12 A.  Sales at the specified event for a period greater than 3 days;
13 B.  Sales anywhere other than the specified event; or
14 C.  The consumption of adult use marijuana or adult use marijuana products at the 
15 location of sale at the specified event.
162. Municipal approval.  A marijuana store licensee applying for a permit under this 
17 section must obtain approval from the municipality in which the specified event is located 
18 to sell adult use marijuana or adult use marijuana products at the specified event before the 
19 department may issue a permit under this section.
203. Guidance. The department shall develop guidance documents to assist marijuana 
21 store licensees in applying for and complying with permits under this section and lawfully 
22 engaging in the off-premises sale of adult use marijuana or adult use marijuana products.
234. Rulemaking. The department shall adopt rules to administer this section. Rules 
24 adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 
25 375, subchapter 2-A.
26Sec. 2.  28-B MRSA §505, as enacted by PL 2017, c. 409, Pt. A, §6, is amended to 
27 read:
28§505.  Transportation of adult use marijuana and adult use marijuana products
29 A licensee and its employees may transport adult use marijuana and adult use marijuana 
30 products between the licensed premises of the licensee and the licensed premises of any 
31 other marijuana establishment and may transport adult use marijuana and adult use 
32 marijuana products between the licensed premises of the licensee and the location at which 
33 the licensee is authorized to sell adult use marijuana and adult use marijuana products under 
34 a permit issued under section 504-A.  All transportation of adult use marijuana and adult 
35 use marijuana products must be documented by the licensee or an employee of the licensee 
36 in accordance with rules adopted by the department.  The department shall adopt rules 
37 regarding the transportation of adult use marijuana and adult use marijuana products by 
38 licensees under this chapter.
Page 2 - 130LR2301(01)1SUMMARY
2 This bill authorizes the Department of Administrative and Financial Services to issue 
3 permits to marijuana store licensees for the off-premises sale of adult use marijuana or adult 
4 use marijuana products and provides certain limitations on those permits. The bill also 
5 requires the department to develop guidance documents for compliance with such permits 
6 and to adopt routine technical rules for the administration of the permits.2
