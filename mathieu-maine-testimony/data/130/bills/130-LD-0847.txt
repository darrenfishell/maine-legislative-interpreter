3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill proposes to create the Transition to Adulthood Diversion Program that mirrors 
6 the Maine Juvenile Code in terms of its goals of rehabilitation and diversion, including 
7 diversion to programs based in restorative practices.
8 This bill would require that a percentage of persons 18 years of age or older and under 
9 25 years of age who are black, indigenous or other persons of color currently in the criminal 
10 justice system be diverted into the Transition to Adulthood Diversion Program. 
11 This bill would provide to the Transition to Adulthood Diversion Program the same 
12 percentage of funding currently provided for juveniles in this age group who are in 
13 community-based diversion programs.4
