3ยง206.  State flag
4 The flag to be known as the official flag of the State shall be of blue, of the same color 
5 as the blue field in the flag of the United States, and of the following dimensions and 
6 designs; to wit, the length or height of the staff to be 9 feet, including brass spearhead and 
7 ferrule; the fly of said flag to be 5 feet 6 inches, and to be 4 feet 4 inches on the staff; in the 
8 center of the flag there shall be embroidered in silk on both sides of the flag the coat of 
9 arms of the State, in proportionate size; the edges to be trimmed with knotted fringe of 
10 yellow silk, 2 1/2 inches wide; a cord, with tassels, to be attached to the staff at the 
11 spearhead, to be 8 feet 6 inches long and composed of white and blue silk strands  must be 
12 buff, charged with the emblem of the State, a pine tree proper, in the center, and the North 
13 Star, a mullet of 5 points, in blue in the upper corner; the star to be equidistant from the 
14 hoist and the upper border of the flag, the distance from the 2 borders to the center of the 
15 star being equal to about 1/4 of the hoist, this distance and the size of the star being 
16 proportionate to the size of the flag. A flag made in accordance with the description given 
17 in this section shall must be kept in the office of the Adjutant General as a model.
18SUMMARY
19 This bill replaces the current State of Maine flag with the flag that was in use before
