28SUMMARY
29 This bill authorizes licensed emergency medical services persons to provide medical 
30 services in health care facility settings that are not hospital settings under delegated 
31 authority if certain criteria are met.  Current law authorizes licensed emergency medical 
32 services persons to provide medical services in hospital settings under delegated authority.
33 The bill also provides that the medical service must be rendered in the person's capacity 
34 as an employee or a contracted agent of the hospital or health care facility.  Current law 
35 provides that the medical service must be rendered in the person's capacity as an employee 
36 of the hospital.29
