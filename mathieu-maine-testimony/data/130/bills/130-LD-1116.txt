35SUMMARY
36 This resolve directs the Joint Standing Committee on Energy, Utilities and Technology 
37 to hold at least 3 public hearings during the First Regular Session of the 130th Legislature 
38 to hear testimony on lessons learned from the restructuring of Maine's electric utilities, the 
39 required divestiture of utility-owned generation and the creation of ISO-New England in 
40 2000.  It also requires the Public Utilities Commission to hire an independent consultant to 
41 conduct a high-level review of and report on current proposals to better align the clean 
42 energy and decarbonization objectives adopted by New England states with the emphasis 36
