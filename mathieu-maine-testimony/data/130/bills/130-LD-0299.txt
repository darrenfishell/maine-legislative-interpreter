3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill proposes to enact measures that provide clarity in the laws governing the 
6 profession of counseling.  It will provide statutory language that describes and 
7 differentiates between the licensed title of counselor and the practice of counseling, similar 
8 to that found in Vermont and New Hampshire law and in Maine law governing alcohol and 
9 drug counseling.
