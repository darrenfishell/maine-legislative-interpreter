25SUMMARY
26 This bill directs the State Board of Education to adopt rules to amend the credentialing 
27 of educational personnel to create a pathway for a teacher to obtain a conditional certificate 
28 and a pathway for a teacher to obtain an endorsement on a teaching certificate for a 
29 component of industrial arts with an experiential lab.26
