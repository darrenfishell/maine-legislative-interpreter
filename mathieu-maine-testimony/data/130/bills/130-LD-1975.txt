18SUMMARY
19 This bill extends the repeal date of Private and Special Law 2021, chapter 10 until 
20 October 1, 2024 with the following additional amendments:
21 1.  It permits the Town of Windham to submit a report to the joint standing committee 
22 of the Legislature having jurisdiction over state and local government matters by October 
23 1, 2024 and permits the committee to report out a bill to the First Regular Session of the 
24 132nd Legislature; and 
25 2.  It permits the Town of Windham, beginning October 1, 2024, to provide winter 
26 maintenance, for public health and safety purposes and certain environmental purposes, on 
27 private ways over which at least 50% of the abutting property owners have issued the town 
28 a public easement. 
29 The bill is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on State and Local 
30 Government pursuant to Private and Special Law 2021, chapter 10.  The committee has not 
31 taken a position on the substance of this bill.  By reporting this bill out the committee is not 
32 suggesting and does not intend to suggest that it agrees or disagrees with any aspect of this 
33 bill.  The committee is reporting the bill out for the sole purpose of having a bill printed 
34 that can be referred to the committee for an appropriate public hearing and subsequent 
35 processing in the normal course.  The committee is taking this action to ensure clarity and 
36 transparency in the legislative review of the proposals contained in the bill.19
