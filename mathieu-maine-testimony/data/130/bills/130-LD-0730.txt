24SUMMARY
25 This bill changes the date after which a qualified Pine Tree Development Zone business 
26 in a tier 1 location may not be certified from December 31, 2021 to December 31, 2024.  It 
27 also changes the date that all Pine Tree Development Zone benefits are terminated from 
28 December 31, 2031 to December 31, 2034 and makes corresponding changes in several 
29 sections of law.1
