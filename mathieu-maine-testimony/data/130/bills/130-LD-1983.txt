10Sec. 1.  Director of Bureau of Parks and Lands authorized to lease certain 
11land within the public reserved lands in Dennistown Plantation in Somerset 
12County.  Resolved:  That the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the 
13 Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may lease to Christian Camps and 
14 Conferences, on such terms and conditions as the director may direct, certain land within 
15 the public reserved lands in Dennistown Plantation in Somerset County described in the 
16 Transmission Line Lease entered into between the bureau and Christian Camps and 
17 Conferences in effect beginning June 1, 2016 to be used to replace, remove, maintain, 
18 operate, repair, upgrade and use telephone cable buried in conduit for the purpose of 
19 providing telephone service.
