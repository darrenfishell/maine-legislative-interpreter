9SUMMARY
10 This bill requires any action taken pursuant to an emergency proclamation of the 
11 Governor intended to abate, clean up or mitigate whatever danger may exist within an 
12 affected area to be approved by a 2/3 vote of the Legislative Council if the action would 
13 directly result in the temporary or permanent closure of a business or a civic or religious 
14 organization.10
