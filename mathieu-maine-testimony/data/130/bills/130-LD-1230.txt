2Sec. 1.  29-A MRSA §2063, sub-§2-B is enacted to read:
32-B.  Riding to right.  A person operating a bicycle upon a roadway shall operate in 
4 single file with other bicyclists when being overtaken by a motor vehicle.
5Sec. 2.  29-A MRSA §2063, sub-§7, as amended by PL 2015, c. 164, §6, is further 
6 amended to read:
77.  Penalties.  A person 17 years of age or over who violates subsection 2, 2-B, 3, 3-A, 
8 4, 6, 9, 10, 11, 12 or 13 commits a traffic infraction for which a fine of not less than $25 
9 and not more than $250 may be adjudged.  A person under 17 years of age is not subject to 
10 a fine under this section.
11SUMMARY
12 This bill requires bicyclists operating on a roadway to ride in single file while being 
13 overtaken by motor vehicles and makes the failure to do so a traffic infraction.12
