Page 1 - 130LR0795(01)1Sec. 1.  Data for racial and ethnic disparities in health care.  Resolved: That 
2 the Maine Health Data Organization shall determine the best methods and definitions to 
3 use in collecting data to assist in analyzing the origins of racial and ethnic disparities in 
4 health care in the State.
5Sec. 2.  Report.  Resolved: That, by October 1, 2021, the Maine Health Data 
6 Organization shall submit a report, including suggested legislation, based on its evaluation 
7 in section 1 to the Joint Standing Committee on Health Coverage, Insurance and Financial 
8 Services.  The Joint Standing Committee on Health Coverage, Insurance and Financial 
9 Services is authorized to submit legislation related to the report to the Second Regular 
10 Session of the 130th Legislature.
11SUMMARY
12 This resolve directs the Maine Health Data Organization to determine the best methods 
13 and definitions to use in collecting data to assist in analyzing the origins of racial and ethnic 
14 disparities in health care in the State and to submit a report with suggested legislation by 
15 October 1, 2021 to the Joint Standing Committee on Health Coverage, Insurance and 
16 Financial Services, which may submit legislation to the Second Regular Session of the 
17 130th Legislature.
