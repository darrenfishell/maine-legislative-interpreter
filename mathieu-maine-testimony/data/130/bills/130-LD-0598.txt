8SUMMARY
9 This bill amends the Maine Human Rights Act to prohibit discrimination in 
10 employment and education based on hair texture or hairstyle.  The bill provides that use of 
11 the term "race" includes traits that are associated with race, including hair texture, Afro 
12 hairstyles and protective hairstyles.  "Protective hairstyles" includes braids, twists and 
13 locks.  The text is based on language adopted in Maryland and referred to as the CROWN 
14 Act, which is an acronym for Creating a Respectful and Open World for Natural Hair.9
