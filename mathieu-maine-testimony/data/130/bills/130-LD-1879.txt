4SUMMARY
5 This bill repeals the provision of law repealing, on October 1, 2022, the Maine Revised 
6 Statutes, Title 39-A, section 201, subsection 3-A, paragraph B, which establishes a 
7 rebuttable presumption that, for law enforcement officers, corrections officers, E-9-1-1 
8 dispatchers, firefighters and emergency medical services persons diagnosed as having post-
9 traumatic stress disorder resulting from extraordinary and unusual work stress, the post-
10 traumatic stress disorder arose out of and in the course of employment.5
