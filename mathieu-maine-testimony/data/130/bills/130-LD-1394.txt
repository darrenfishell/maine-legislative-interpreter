3ยง5.  Participation while intoxicated
4 A member of the Senate or a member of the House of Representatives may not enter 
5 the chamber of the Senate or chamber of the House of Representatives or vote on any 
6 legislative matter while under the influence of intoxicants or while having an alcohol level 
7 of 0.08 grams or more of alcohol per 100 milliliters of blood or 210 liters of breath. For 
8 purposes of this section, "under the influence of intoxicants" means under the influence of 
9 alcohol, a drug other than alcohol, a combination of drugs or a combination of alcohol and 
10 drugs to an extent to which criminal operating under the influence of intoxicants or with an 
11 excessive alcohol level under Title 29-A, section 2411 would apply.
12SUMMARY
13 This bill prohibits a Legislator from entering the chamber of the Senate or chamber of 
14 the House of Representatives or voting on any legislative matter if the Legislator is 
15 intoxicated by drugs or alcohol at a level that would constitute criminal operating under the 
16 influence.13
