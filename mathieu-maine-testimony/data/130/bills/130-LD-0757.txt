35SUMMARY
36 This bill amends the State's laws concerning contracts and agreements for the large-
37 scale extraction and transportation of water by requiring any such contract or agreement to 
38 be approved by a vote of the legislative body of each municipality and township located 
39 within the watershed from which water is to be extracted and transported pursuant to that 
40 contract or agreement.  It also provides that a consumer-owned water utility may not enter
