18SUMMARY
19 This bill includes instruction on African American studies and Maine African 
20 American studies, including African American cultural systems and the experience of 
21 African Americans throughout American history, in the Department of Education's 
22 required units of instruction on American history and Maine studies.  The bill requires the 
23 department to convene a volunteer advisory committee to collect information and prepare 
24 materials for teaching African American studies and Maine African American studies and 
25 to develop and provide guidance, best practices and exemplar modules to local school 
26 administrative units to integrate African American studies and Maine African American 
27 studies into their curriculum.  The bill also requires the department to develop a process to 
28 enable a local school administrative unit to conduct an internal audit of its curriculum to 
29 ensure an equitable representation of African American voices and an accurate historical 
30 account of African American history and culture.  The bill also requires the department to 
31 submit a report on the progress of the implementation of this Act to the joint standing 
32 committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over education matters no later than April
