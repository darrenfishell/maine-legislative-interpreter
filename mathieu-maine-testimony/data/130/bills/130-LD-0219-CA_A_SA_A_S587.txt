SUMMARY 
This amendment amends Committee Amendment "A" to remove the provision that 
requires the State Controller to transfer $550,000 from General Fund undedicated revenue 
to the Agricultural Development Fund annually. It requires the transfer of $467,240 from 
the unappropriated surplus of the General Fund to the Department of Agriculture, 
Conservation and Forestry, Agricultural Development Fund Other Special Revenue Funds 
account by June 30, 2023. It replaces the ap ro riations and allocations section.

This amendment replaces the bill's ongoing transfer of $550,000 from the unappropriated surplus of the General Fund 
to the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Agricultural Development Fund, Other Special Revenue 
Funds account with a one-time transfer of $467,240 in fiscal year 2022-23. The amendment also changes the bill's 
ongoing Other Special Revenue Funds allocation of $550,000 for that account to a one-time allocation of $467,240 in 
fiscal year 2022-23. The bill's one-time Other Special Revenue Funds allocation of $150,000 in fiscal year 2022-23 to 
be funded by FAME transfers of the balance of interest in the Agricultural Marketing Loan Fund is unchanged by this 
amendment.
