32-C.  Prior authorization and step therapy of treatment for serious mental illness.  
4 A carrier may not require prior authorization or step therapy protocol as defined in section 
5 4320-N, subsection 1, paragraph G for the prescription of medication used to assess or treat 
6 an enrollee's serious mental illness.  For the purposes of this subsection, "serious mental 
7 illness" means a mental disorder, as defined in the most recent edition of the Diagnostic 
8 and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders published by the American Psychiatric 
9 Association, that results in serious functional impairment that substantially interferes with 
10 or limits one or more major life activities.  The superintendent may adopt rules to 
11 implement this subsection.  Rules adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical 
12 rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
13Sec. 2.  Application.  The requirements of this Act apply to all policies, contracts 
14 and certificates executed, delivered, issued for delivery, continued or renewed in this State 
15 on or after January 1, 2022.  For the purposes of this Act, all contracts are deemed to be 
16 renewed no later than the next yearly anniversary of the contract date.
17SUMMARY
18 This bill prohibits an insurance carrier from requiring prior authorization or step 
19 therapy protocol for the prescription of medication to assess or treat an enrollee's serious 
20 mental illness.18
