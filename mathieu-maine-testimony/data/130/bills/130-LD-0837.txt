2Sec. 1.  22 MRSA §4002, sub-§1, as amended by PL 2015, c. 360, §2, is further 
3 amended to read:
41.  Abuse or neglect.  "Abuse or neglect" means a threat to a child's health or welfare 
5 by physical, mental or emotional injury or impairment, sexual abuse or exploitation 
6 including under Title 17-A, sections 282, 852, 853 and 855, deprivation of essential needs 
7 or lack of protection from these or failure to ensure compliance with school attendance 
8 requirements under Title 20‑A, section 3272, subsection 2, paragraph B or section 5051-A, 
9 subsection 1, paragraph C, by a person responsible for the child.  "Abuse or neglect" also 
10 means a threat to a child's health or welfare caused by child sex trafficking by any person, 
11 regardless of whether or not the person is responsible for the child.
12Sec. 2.  22 MRSA §4002, sub-§3-B is enacted to read:
133-B.  Child sex trafficking.  "Child sex trafficking" means the recruitment, harboring, 
14 transportation, provision or obtaining of a child for the purposes of a commercial sex act.
15SUMMARY
16 This bill amends the definition of "abuse or neglect" and enacts a definition for "child 
17 sex trafficking" within the Child and Family Services and Child Protection Act to ensure 
18 the State is in compliance with the federal Child Abuse Prevention and Treatment Act, as 
19 amended by Public Law 115-271.
