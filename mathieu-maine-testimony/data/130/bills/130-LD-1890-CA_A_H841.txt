'An Act To Equalize Sales Tax Treatment of Certain Vehicles' 
Amend the bill by striking out all of Part A.

Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section

SUMMARY 
This amendment removes from the bill provisions that established a registration 
program for rental fleet vehicles. The amendment also provides that the term "retail sale" 
for sales tax purposes excludes the purchase of all trucks and vans with a gross vehicle 
weight of less than 26,000 pounds, including integral parts and accessories for those 
vehicles, by a rental company, if those vehicles will be used for short-term rental.
