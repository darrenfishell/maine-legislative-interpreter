30SUMMARY
31 This bill allows students whose access to on-site education is limited as a result of a 
32 state of emergency, such as that resulting from the COVID-19 pandemic, to be classified 
33 as having experienced education disruption and eligible for school work recognition plans 
34 that will demonstrate achievement under Maine's system of learning results.
