3Whereas, the State's COVID-19 response has delayed celebration of the State's

9Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within

12 therefore,
13Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
14Sec. 1.  29-A MRSA §454, sub-§2, as enacted by PL 1993, c. 683, Pt. A, §2 and 
15 affected by Pt. B, §5, is amended to read:
162.  Display.  A commemorative plate may be displayed to replace the front registration 
17 plate on a motor vehicle, except a truck tractor, including a motor vehicle registered outside 
18 this State and operated within it, from January 1st to December 31st of the year celebrated, 
19 except that a commemorative plate celebrating the bicentennial of the State may be 
20 displayed until December 31, 2021.

6Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation

8SUMMARY
9 This bill allows the sale, distribution and display of a commemorative vehicle 
10 registration plate celebrating the State's bicentennial until December 31, 2021.
