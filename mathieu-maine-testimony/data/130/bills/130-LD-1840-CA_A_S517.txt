This bill includes a one-time General Fund appropriation of $335,194 to the Retirement Allowance Fund within the 
Maine Public Employees Retirement System (MainePERS) for the increase in the unfunded actuarial liability created 
as a result of allowing the service retirement benefits for certain employees in the Department of Corrections earned 
under the regular state employee and teacher plan to be calculated under the 1998 Special Plan. Pursuant to the 
Constitution of Maine, Article IX, Section 18-A, unfunded liabilities may not be created except those that result from 
experience losses.
