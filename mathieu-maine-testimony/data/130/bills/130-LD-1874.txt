5SUMMARY
6 This bill amends the law requiring school administrative units to restore up to 15 sick 
7 leave days used by a public school employee affected by COVID-19 to instead grant up to 
8 a maximum of 15 days of paid leave for the purposes described in the law from January 1, 
9 2021 until the maximum has been reached.  The bill provides that a public school employee 
10 who used sick leave prior to the effective date of this legislation is entitled to request that 
11 that leave time be restored, and the school administrative unit is required to comply with 
12 the request.1
