4 created a public health emergency; and
5Whereas, public schools in the State have experienced significant declines in student

11 therefore,
12Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
13Sec. 1.  20-A MRSA §15674, sub-§1, ¶C, as amended by PL 2017, c. 284, Pt. C, 
14 §29, is further amended to read:
15 C.  Beginning in fiscal year 2018-19:
16 (1)  The average of the pupil counts for October 1st of the 2 most recent calendar 
17 years prior to the year of funding, reported in accordance with section 6004, 
18 including the counts of students enrolled in an alternative education program made 
19 in accordance with section 5104‑A, except that for the 2021-22 fiscal year, if a 
20 school administrative unit's student enrollment has declined from the previous 
21 allocation year, the pupil count from the previous allocation year must be used.
22Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
23 takes effect when approved.
24SUMMARY
25 This bill provides that for the 2021-22 fiscal year, if a school administrative unit's 
26 student enrollment has declined from the previous allocation year, the commissioner is 
27 required to use the pupil count from the previous allocation year for the determination of 
28 operating costs.25
