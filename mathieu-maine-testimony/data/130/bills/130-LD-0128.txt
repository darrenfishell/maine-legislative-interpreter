36SUMMARY
37 This bill allows the Commissioner of Transportation to enter into agreements and 
38 cooperate with the United States Department of Transportation as provided in 23 United 
39 States Code, Sections 325 to 327 and as authorized under the federal Moving Ahead for 
40 Progress in the 21st Century Act, the federal Safe, Accountable, Flexible, Efficient 
41 Transportation Equity Act: A Legacy for Users and the federal National Environmental 
42 Policy Act of 1969.  It allows the commissioner to adopt rules to implement an agreement, 
Page 2 - 130LR0131(01)43 carry out duties under an agreement and make expenditures in connection with an 
44 agreement.  Consistent with 23 United States Code, Sections 326 and 327, it provides for 
45 a partial waiver of immunity from civil suit in federal court regarding the compliance, 
46 discharge or enforcement of a responsibility assumed by the Department of Transportation 
47 upon the department's entering an agreement.1
