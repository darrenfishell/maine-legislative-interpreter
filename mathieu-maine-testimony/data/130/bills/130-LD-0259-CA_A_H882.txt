This bill increases the fee imposed on landfilled municipal solid waste and municipal solid waste ash from $1 per ton 
to $2 per ton. The Department of Environmental Protection has indicated that based on the current volume of material 
subject to this fee annual revenue will increase by $796,000 starting in fiscal year 2022-23 to the Maine Solid Waste 
Management Fund (MSWMF). The bill includes a corresponding allocation to the MSWMF to allow expenditure of 
the additional revenue.
