11SUMMARY
12 This bill defines the term "medical safety zone" and requires the demarcation of a 
13 medical safety zone around a building in which patients receive health services and 
14 prohibits a person from entering into, remaining in or creating an obstruction in that 
15 medical safety zone during the hours in which health services may be provided. The bill 
16 also makes violating that prohibition or intentionally interfering or attempting to interfere 
17 with the exercise of certain constitutional rights a Class E crime.12
