Page 1 - 130LR0901(01)1Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2Sec. 1.  26 MRSA §976 is enacted to read:
3§976.  Obligations during interim between contracts
4 During the interim after the expiration of a collective bargaining agreement and before 
5 the effective date of any subsequent collective bargaining agreement, public employees 
6 covered by the expired collective bargaining agreement remain eligible for and must 
7 receive step increases in accordance with the terms and conditions set forth in the expired 
8 collective bargaining agreement.
9Sec. 2.  26 MRSA §1038 is enacted to read:
10§1038.  Obligations during interim between contracts
11 During the interim after the expiration of a collective bargaining agreement and before 
12 the effective date of any subsequent collective bargaining agreement, regular employees 
13 covered by the expired collective bargaining agreement remain eligible for and must 
14 receive step increases in accordance with the terms and conditions set forth in the expired 
15 collective bargaining agreement.
16Sec. 3.  26 MRSA §1296 is enacted to read:
17§1296.  Obligations during interim between contracts
18 During the interim after the expiration of a collective bargaining agreement and before 
19 the effective date of any subsequent collective bargaining agreement, judicial employees 
20 covered by the expired collective bargaining agreement remain eligible for and must 
21 receive merit or step increases in accordance with the terms and conditions set forth in the 
22 expired collective bargaining agreement.
23SUMMARY
24 This bill provides the same protections to municipal, judicial and public higher 
25 education employees that are provided to state employees upon the expiration of labor 
26 contracts by requiring that, during an interim between the expiration of a public employee 
27 collective bargaining agreement and before the effective date of any subsequent collective 
28 bargaining agreement, those employees covered by the expired collective bargaining 
29 agreement remain eligible for and must receive merit or step increases in accordance with 
30 the terms and conditions set forth in the expired collective bargaining agreement.
