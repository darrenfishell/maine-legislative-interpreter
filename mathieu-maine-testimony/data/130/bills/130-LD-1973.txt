2Sec. 1.  Waiver of professional licensing fees.  Notwithstanding any provision of 
3 law to the contrary, professional licensing fees are waived for the following persons for the 
4 period from March 1, 2020 to December 31, 2022 and any professional licensing fees paid 
5 by such a person for that period must be refunded to that person:
6 1.  An emergency medical services person as defined in the Maine Revised Statutes,

8 2.  A registered nurse;
9 3.  A licensed practical nurse;
10 4.  A respiratory care practitioner as defined in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 32, 
11 section 9702, subsection 6;
12 5.  A midwife as defined in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 32, section 12501, 
13 subsection 6-B; and
14 6. A person associated with the practices in subsections 1 to 5 and who is required to 
15 pay a licensing fee professional licensing fee.
16Sec. 2.  Retroactivity. This Act applies retroactively to March 1, 2020.
17SUMMARY
18 This bill waives professional licensing fees for emergency medical services persons, 
19 registered nurses, licensed practical nurses, respiratory care practitioners, midwives and 
20 persons associated with those practices for the period from March 1, 2020 to December 31, 
21 2022.  It requires that any professional licensing fees paid by such a person for that period 
22 be refunded to that person.18
