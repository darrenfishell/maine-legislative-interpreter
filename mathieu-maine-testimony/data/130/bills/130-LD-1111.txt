6SUMMARY
7 This bill amends certain laws governing the Board of Trustees of the Maine Veterans' 
8 Homes.  It amends the composition of the board.  It requires the board to submit a list of 
9 nominees for appointment to the board to the Governor for the Governor's consideration.  
10 It requires the board to request certain organizations and individuals to submit to the board 
11 names of suggested nominees.  It limits board members to serving 3 full terms or no more 
12 than 9 consecutive years.  It changes the number of annual meetings from 6 to 4.  It requires 
13 the board to elect a vice-chair and treasurer, in addition to a chair and secretary.  It removes 
14 a requirement that the chief executive officer be an honorably discharged veteran.  It repeals 
15 a provision in law related to renovations undertaken by the Maine Veterans' Homes 
16 Augusta nursing facility.7
