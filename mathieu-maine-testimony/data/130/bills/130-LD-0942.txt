2Sec. 1.  Licensing and certification fees for 2020 waived.  All licensing and 
3 certification fees imposed pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 32 for calendar 
4 year 2020 are waived.  A person who has paid a licensing or certification fee for calendar 
5 year 2020 must be reimbursed.
6SUMMARY
7 This bill waives all licensing and certification fees for calendar year 2020 that are 
8 imposed pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 32, which governs professions and 
9 occupations.  A person who has paid a licensing or certification fee for calendar year 2020 
10 must be reimbursed.7
