18SUMMARY
19 This bill permits an insurance producer to refer clients to an authorized 3rd-party 
20 payment service provider for the purpose of accepting a credit card or debit card to pay the 
21 client's insurance premium.  The bill also authorizes the 3rd party to impose a surcharge 
22 for payments made with a credit card or debit card.
