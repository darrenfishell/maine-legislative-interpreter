Page 1 - 130LR1370(01)1Sec. 1.  Department of Public Safety to apply for an exemption from 
2background checks for holders of concealed weapons permits. Resolved: That 
3 the Department of Public Safety shall apply to the United States Department of Justice, 
4 Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives for an exemption for concealed 
5 weapons permit holders under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 25, section 2003 when 
6 purchasing a firearm from undergoing a background check under the national instant 
7 criminal background check system under 18 United States Code, Section 922(t).
8SUMMARY
9 This resolve directs the Department of Public Safety to apply to the United States 
10 Department of Justice, Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives for an 
11 exemption from background checks under the national instant criminal background check 
12 system for holders of concealed weapons permits when purchasing a firearm.9
