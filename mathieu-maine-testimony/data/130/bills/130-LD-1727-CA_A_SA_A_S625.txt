Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This amendment offsets the General Fund appropriations in the bill by transfering $363,500 from the Department of 
Administrative and Financial Services Medical Use of Marijuana Fund, Other Special Revenue Funds account and 
$500,000 from the Liquor Operation Revenue Fund within the Maine Municipal Bond Bank to the unappropriated 
surplus of the General Fund no later than June 30, 2023. As amended, the bill still includes General Fund 
appropriations totaling $863,500 per year beginning in fiscal year 2022-23.
