Page 1 - 130LR1002(01)1Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2Sec. 1.  Temporary waiver of educator performance evaluations.  
3 Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, a school administrative unit is not 
4 required to evaluate an educator as defined in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20-A, 
5 section 13701, subsection 1 pursuant to a performance evaluation and professional growth 
6 system developed in accordance with Title 20-A, chapter 508 until the 2022-2023 school 
7 year.
8SUMMARY
9 This bill temporarily waives, until the 2022-2023 school year, the requirement that 
10 school administrative units evaluate the performance of teachers and principals.9
