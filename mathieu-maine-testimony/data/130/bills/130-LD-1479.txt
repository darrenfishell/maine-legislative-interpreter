4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill proposes to make certain traffic infractions secondary offenses, including 
6 littering from a vehicle, failing to register a vehicle or properly display a vehicle 
7 registration, failing to display a valid and current vehicle inspection sticker, failing to wear 
8 a seat belt, making unnecessary noise, operating of a defective vehicle, operating a 
9 motorcycle without a headlight, operating a vehicle with an obstructed view, operating a 
10 vehicle in a 2-way or left lane, or operating a vehicle with a suspended license as a result 
11 of failure to pay a fine, license reinstatement fee or a dishonored check, and certain 
12 equipment violations under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 29-A, chapter 17, subchapter 
13 1.4
