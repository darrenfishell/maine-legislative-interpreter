8SUMMARY
9 This bill prohibits a person engaging in an activity governed by the inland fisheries and 
10 wildlife laws from allowing the person's hunting dog to enter or remain in any place in 
11 defiance of a lawful order not to enter or an order to vacate that was personally 
12 communicated to that person by the owner of the place or another authorized person.
