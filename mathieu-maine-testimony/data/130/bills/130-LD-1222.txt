2Sec. 1.  36 MRSA §656, sub-§1, ¶L is enacted to read:
3 L.  For property tax years beginning on or after April 1, 2021, that portion of taxable 
4 real estate owned by an individual who is 65 years of age or older the tax on which, if 
5 subject to the property tax of the municipality in which it is located, would equal the 
6 amount of the property tax on the individual's taxable real estate that the municipality 
7 would use to fund public schools.
8SUMMARY
9 This bill provides a property tax exemption for individuals 65 years of age or older 
10 equal in value to the amount of those taxes the municipality in which the real estate is 
11 located would otherwise use to fund its public schools.  The bill applies to property tax 
12 years beginning on or after April 1, 2021.9
