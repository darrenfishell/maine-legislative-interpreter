Page 1 - 130LR1700(01)1Sec. 1.  Restorative justice review.  Resolved:  That the Permanent Commission 
2 on the Status of Racial, Indigenous and Maine Tribal Populations, established in the Maine 
3 Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 12004-I, subsection 74-J, shall conduct a review of 
4 proposed and passed national restorative justice legislation and make recommendations in 
5 the areas of education, juvenile justice, adult diversion and correctional settings for 
6 advancing restorative justice legislation in this State.  In conducting the review, the 
7 commission shall pay particular attention to race and ethnicity equity and to concerns of 
8 confidentiality, victim and perpetrator rights, the readiness for engagement in restorative 
9 justice and restorative justice as a sustainable form of justice.
10Sec. 2.  Legislation.  Resolved:  That the Permanent Commission on the Status of 
11 Racial, Indigenous and Maine Tribal Populations is authorized to submit legislation based 
12 on its recommendations under section 1 to the Second Regular Session of the 130th 
13 Legislature.
14SUMMARY
15 This resolve directs the Permanent Commission on the Status of Racial, Indigenous 
16 and Maine Tribal Populations to conduct a review of proposed and passed national 
17 restorative justice legislation and make recommendations in the areas of education, juvenile 
18 justice, adult diversion and correctional settings for advancing restorative justice legislation 
19 in this State.  The commission is authorized to submit legislation based on its 
20 recommendations to the Second Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.15
