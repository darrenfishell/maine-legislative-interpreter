4 Notwithstanding the provisions of this chapter, Title 13, chapter 83 or Title 22, section 
5 707 to the contrary, a nonprofit corporation may conduct an open air cremation by pyre if 
6 the cremation is conducted on property owned or possessed by the corporation that is at 
7 least 20 acres in size.  Only one open air cremation may be conducted at a time.  The ashes 
8 constituting the remains of the deceased person who is cremated may be scattered on the 
9 property or otherwise disposed of pursuant to law.  The board shall adopt routine technical 
10 rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A to carry out the purposes of this 
11 section.
12SUMMARY
13 This bill allows a nonprofit corporation to conduct open air cremations by pyre if the 
14 cremation is conducted on property of at least 20 acres that is owned or possessed by the 
15 corporation, restricted to one cremation at a time.  The bill allows the ashes to be scattered 
16 on the property or otherwise disposed of pursuant to law.13
