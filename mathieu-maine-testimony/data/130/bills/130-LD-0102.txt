7SUMMARY
8 Current law allows a municipality or jurisdiction that conducts its own elections to 
9 process absentee ballots beginning on the 4th day immediately prior to election day.  This 
10 bill allows a municipality or jurisdiction to process absentee ballots beginning on the 7th 
11 day immediately prior to election day.
