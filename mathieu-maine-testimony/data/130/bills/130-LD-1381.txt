11 This bill establishes the weekend of Indigenous Peoples Day as an annual sales-tax-
12 free weekend for sales of tangible personal property and prepared food and the rental of 
13 living quarters.  The exemption does not apply to tobacco products, boats, vehicles or any 
14 single item with a cost of more than $2,500. 11
