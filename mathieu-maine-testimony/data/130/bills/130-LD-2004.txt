2Sec. 1.  24-A MRSA §2910-C is enacted to read:
3§2910-C.  Selection of attorneys
4 An insurer may not require an insured person to select an attorney other than one of the 
5 insured person's own choosing to provide legal services in a dispute related to the casualty 
6 insurance contract in effect between the insurer and the insured person.
7Sec. 2.  24-A MRSA §2910-D is enacted to read:
8§2910-D.  Attorney's fees
9 In a dispute in which an insurer provides insurance to more than one of the parties to 
10 the matter under dispute, the insurer shall pay reasonable attorney's fees for all of the parties 
11 to that dispute that the insurer insures.
12Sec. 3.  24-A MRSA §3044 is enacted to read:
13§3044.  Selection of attorneys
14 An insurer may not require an insured person to select an attorney other than one of the 
15 insured person's own choosing to provide legal services in a dispute related to the property 
16 insurance contract in effect between the insurer and the insured person.
17Sec. 4.  24-A MRSA §3045 is enacted to read:
18§3045.  Attorney's fees
19 In a dispute in which an insurer provides insurance to more than one of the parties to 
20 the matter under dispute, the insurer shall pay reasonable attorney's fees for all of the parties 
21 to that dispute that the insurer insures.
22SUMMARY
23 This bill requires property and casualty insurers to allow insured persons to select an 
24 attorney in an insurance dispute and requires insurers to pay attorney's fees for the parties 
25 that an insurer insures in the event of a dispute in which the insurer insures more than one 
26 of the parties.23
