9SUMMARY
10 This bill provides that a new motor vehicle that has had an inspection is not required 
11 to have another inspection until 2 years from the last day of the month in which it was 
12 initially registered.  After this time period has passed, the motor vehicle is required to have 
13 an annual motor vehicle safety inspection.
