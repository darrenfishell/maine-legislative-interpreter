26 SUMMARY 
27 This amendment requires that the Office of Affordable Health Care, beginning in 2023, 
28 expand the scope of its annual public hearing on cost trends to include barriers to health · 
29 care affordability. The amendment specifies that the hearing must include an opportunity 
30 for public comment on these barriers. The amendment also extends the date for the office 
31 to report to the Legislature on its study of the effects of policies aimed at improving health 
32 care affordability and coverage from January 1, 2023 to January 1, 2024.
