3SUMMARY
4 This bill makes the following changes to the Maine Medical Use of Marijuana Act.
5 1.  It repeals and replaces the definition of "immature marijuana plant" and amends the 
6 definitions of "marijuana plant" and "seedling."
7 2.  It requires a registered caregiver operating a caregiver retail store to acquire a 
8 separate registration certificate for the caregiver retail store and to pay a separate 
9 registration fee for the caregiver retail store.  It changes the fee for caregiver registration 
10 based on the plant count to a fee for caregiver registration based either on plant count or 
11 plant canopy.4
