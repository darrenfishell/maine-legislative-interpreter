SUMMARY 
This amendment amends Committee Amendment "A" to change the title of the bill and 
to strike from the legislation all provisions except for the provision allocated to the Maine 
Revised Statutes, Title 35-A, section 6410-A allowing the Public Utilities Commission to 
appoint a receiver to oversee a consumer-owne ter utility lacking a sufficient number 
of duly elected trustees.
