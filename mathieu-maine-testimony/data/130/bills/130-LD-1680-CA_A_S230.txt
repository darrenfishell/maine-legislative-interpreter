SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill and changes the law governing reimbursements for 
Legislators to: 
1. Clarify that Legislators are entitled to reimbursement for meals and lodging while 
attending session and committee meetings; 
2. Prohibit a cap on mileage reimbursement other than the rate paid per mile and the 
detennination of mileage by the most reasonable direct route; 
3. Beginning with the 131st Legislature, require the meal allowance to be determined 
by the Legislative Council at the beginning of each first regular session; 
4. Beginning with the 131 st Legislature, require the lodging allowance to be 
determined by the Legislative Council at the beginning of each first regular session at no 
less than the average nightly rate of lodging in the Augusta area or the federal per diem 
rate, whichever is less; and 
5. Require a Legislator to submit a lodging receipt to receive lodging reimbursement. 
This amendment also makes several technical changes.
