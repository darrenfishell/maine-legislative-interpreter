10SUMMARY
11 This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to provide notice of a 
12 nursing facility's voluntary closure to all hospitals that are located in the same county as 
13 the nursing facility and to each hospital that is located in an adjacent county if the hospital 
14 is within 25 miles of the nursing facility.  The department must provide the notice within 2 
15 business days after receiving notice of the closure from the nursing facility.11
