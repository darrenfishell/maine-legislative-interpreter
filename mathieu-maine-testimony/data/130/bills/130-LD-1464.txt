142.  Emergency federal rental assistance.  If the Maine State Housing Authority 
15 receives emergency federal rental assistance, for the period of time the Maine State 
16 Housing Authority possesses and administers the assistance, the Maine State Housing 
17 Authority shall submit a monthly report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature 
18 having jurisdiction over labor and housing matters including:
19 A.  The number of applicants for assistance;
20 B.  The average amount of assistance awarded per application;
21 C.  The average amount of time between when assistance was applied for and when the 
22 assistance was delivered to the recipient; and
23 D.  If there is an excessive delay in application processing time under paragraph C, the 
24 reason, if available, for the delay.
25SUMMARY
26 This bill requires the Maine State Housing Authority to report annually to the 
27 Legislature information issued by the Maine State Housing Authority and local public 
28 housing authorities detailing the disbursement of United States Department of Housing and 
29 Urban Development tenant-based housing choice vouchers.  This bill also requires that, if 
30 the Maine State Housing Authority receives federal emergency housing rental assistance, 
31 the Maine State Housing Authority submit for the period of time the authority possesses 
32 and administers the assistance a report to the Legislature detailing information concerning 
33 the disbursement of the assistance and if there is an excessive delay in application 
34 processing time, the reason, if available, for the delay.26
