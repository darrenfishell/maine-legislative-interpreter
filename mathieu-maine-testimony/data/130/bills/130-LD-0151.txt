3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill proposes to allow persons working in agriculture to organize for the purposes 
6 of collectively bargaining for wages, hours, other working conditions and benefits without 
7 fear of reprisal.  The bill would exempt farms under a certain size and with a limited number 
8 of employees.
