9SUMMARY
10 This bill protects inmates in county jails and municipal detention facilities and 
11 prisoners or detainees in state correctional and detention facilities from excessive telephone 
12 and video communications charges.  The bill amends the law governing standards 
13 established by the Commissioner of Corrections for inmates of county jails and municipal 
14 detention facilities and establishes the right to communications 2 times per week without 
15 charge.  The bill contains similar rights for prisoners and detainees in Department of 
16 Corrections correctional and detention facilities.  The bill allows county jails and municipal 
17 detention facilities to contract for communications services and limits the charges that may 
18 be imposed and fees to be paid to the jails and municipal detention facilities for the calls.  
19 The bill includes similar provisions to protect prisoners and detainees in Department of 
20 Corrections correctional and detention facilities.
