Fiscal Note 
Current biennium revenue increase -Other Special Revenue Funds 
Current biennium cost increase -Baxter State Park 
This bill authorizes, but does not direct, the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry (ACF) to sell 
interest in certain parcels ofland held in common and undivided interest in T. 4, R. 8 in Penobscot County to Baxter 
State Park. This conveyance will increase revenue to the County Specific Reserved Lands Acquisition Fund in ACF. 
The amount will not be known until an appraisal is completed. It is anticipated that Baxter State Park has sufficient 
funding available to complete the purchase.
