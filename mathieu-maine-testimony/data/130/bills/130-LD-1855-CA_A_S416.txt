12 'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
13 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and 
14 Whereas, Public Law 2021, chapter 28 permitted delegation of COVID-19 vaccine 
15 administration at point-of-dispensing sites during the public health emergency declared by 
16 the Governor; and 
17 Whereas, although the public health emergency declared by the Governor has 
18 expired, public health measures to address COVID-19, such as point-of-dispensing sites, 
19 are still needed in this State; and 
20 Whereas, point-of-dispensing sites provide expanded access to vaccines; and 
21 Whereas, this legislation extends protections for permitted delegation of COVID-19 
22 vaccine administration at point-of-dispensing sites beyond a declared emergency; and 
23 Whereas, this legislation maintains requirements that point-of-dispensing sites must 
24 operate in accordance with a written memorandum of understanding with the Department 
25 of Health and Human Services, Maine Center for Disease Control and Prevention and meet 
26 certain requirements for oversight and training; and 
27 Whereas, having this legislation take effect as soon as possible will ensure point-of-
28 dispensing sites can continue to be established when necessary; and 
29 Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
30 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as
