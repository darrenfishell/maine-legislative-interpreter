25SUMMARY
26 This bill allows the Commissioner of Marine Resources to adopt routine technical rules 
27 to amend the minimum and maximum lobster size and the dimensions of vents in lobster 
28 traps when necessary to comply with changes to the Atlantic States Marine Fisheries 
29 Commission Interstate Fishery Management Plan for American Lobster. The bill also 
30 makes other technical changes. 26
