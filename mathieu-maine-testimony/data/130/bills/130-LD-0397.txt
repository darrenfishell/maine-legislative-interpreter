Page 1 - 130LR1321(01)1Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2Sec. 1.  Recalculation of benefits for certain Maine Public Employees 
3Retirement System retirees subject to merit pay freezes; retroactivity.  The 
4 changes to the law governing the calculation of retirement benefits for members of the 
5 Maine Public Employees Retirement System under Public Law 2015, chapter 267, Part 
6 CCCC, section 1 and Public Law 2015, chapter 385, which allowed members who retired 
7 on or after June 30, 2015 to pay the necessary member contributions to include in their 
8 retirement benefit calculations wages lost due to merit pay freezes and longevity pay 
9 freezes, apply retroactively to the date of retirement for a member who retired on or after 
10 May 1, 2014 and prior to June 30, 2014, except that such lost wages may include only 
11 frozen merit pay and not frozen longevity pay.
12SUMMARY
13 The law governing the calculation of retirement benefits for members of the Maine 
14 Public Employees Retirement System was amended by Public Law 2015, chapter 267, Part 
15 CCCC, section 1 and Public Law 2015, chapter 385 to allow members who retired on or 
16 after June 30, 2015 to pay the necessary member contributions to include in their retirement 
17 benefit calculations wages lost due to merit pay freezes and longevity pay freezes.
18  This bill authorizes a member of the Maine Public Employees Retirement System who 
19 retired on or after May 1, 2014 and prior to June 30, 2014 to include in the member's 
20 retirement benefit calculations wages lost due to merit pay freezes, retroactive to the date 
21 of the member's retirement. This provision does not include lost wages due to longevity 
22 pay freezes.
