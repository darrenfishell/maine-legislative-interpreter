2Sec. 1.  29-A MRSA §1904, sub-§6, as amended by PL 2005, c. 577, §25, is further 
3 amended to read:
46.  Motorcycle.  A motorcycle that does not have an adequate beam for headlights is 
5 restricted to daytime operation.  A motorcycle may be equipped with a low-beam headlight 
6 that is capable of flashing, the use of which is governed by section 2067, subsection 4.
7Sec. 2.  29-A MRSA §2067, sub-§4 is enacted to read:
84.  Motorcycles; flashing low beam.  Notwithstanding section 2054, subsection 2, 
9 paragraph A, the operator of a motorcycle equipped with a low-beam headlight that is 
10 capable of flashing may cause the headlight to flash during the daytime.
11SUMMARY
12 This bill allows the operator of a motorcycle to use a flashing low-beam headlight 
13 during the daytime.
