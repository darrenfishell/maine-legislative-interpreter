23Sec. 2.  Report.  By February 1, 2022, the commission shall submit a report on the 
24 progress of the comprehensive rental registry of multifamily housing to the Maine State 
25 Housing Authority.  On or before January 1, 2023 and annually thereafter, the Maine State 
26 Housing Authority shall report on the progress of the registry to the joint standing 
27 committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over labor and housing matters.
28SUMMARY
29 This bill directs the Maine State Housing Authority to create a commission to develop, 
30 implement and disseminate to municipalities and post on a publicly accessible website a 
31 comprehensive rental registry of multifamily housing in the State. The commission consists 
32 of representatives of large and small municipalities, owners and renters of multifamily 
33 housing and other interested parties invited by the authority.  The authority is directed to 
34 report annually to the Legislature on the progress of the registry.29
