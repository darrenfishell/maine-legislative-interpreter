15SUMMARY
16 This bill provides that the on-premises consumption of drink is not prohibited in a 
17 tobacco specialty store.  It requires a tobacco specialty store to provide notice to all 
18 applicants for employment and employees that working in a tobacco specialty store may 
19 cause serious negative health effects, including an increased risk of cancer and heart disease 
20 and that no level of exposure to environmental tobacco smoke is safe.16
