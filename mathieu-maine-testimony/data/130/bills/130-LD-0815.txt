30SUMMARY
31 Current law requires the Efficiency Maine Trust to develop and administer a school 
32 energy savings program.  This bill changes the program to a school decarbonization 
33 program and requires the trust to provide technical and financial support to help 
34 kindergarten to grade 12 schools become carbon neutral.  The bill also repeals the school 
35 solar energy program administered by the trust.
