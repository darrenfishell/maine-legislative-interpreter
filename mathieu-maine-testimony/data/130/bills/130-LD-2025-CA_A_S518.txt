Fiscal Note 
Legislative approval may avoid constitutional conflict 
This bill provides legislative approval for an existing lease of certain lands in Aroostook County. The lease was issued 
in 2019 to Aroostook Technologies, Inc and generates annual revenue of $1,000. Approval is occurring now to av, 
any potential conflict with certain constitutional requirements for real estate held by the State. Such approval will 
confirm the validity of the existing lease and there will be no impact on the Department of Agriculture, Conservation, 
and Forestry.
