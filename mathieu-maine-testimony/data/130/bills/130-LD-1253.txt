36SUMMARY
37 This bill adds 2 members to the Board of Trustees of the University of Maine System, 
38 a faculty member and a nonfaculty staff member.  The members are full voting members, 
39 serve 5-year terms and are appointed by the Governor from a list provided by their 
40 respective unions.  The member for each seat may not come from the same campus in any 
41 2 consecutive terms.  They receive reasonable leave with pay at their university salaries for 
42 board meetings and other board business conducted during working hours, and their 
43 compensation as university employees is not otherwise altered.37
