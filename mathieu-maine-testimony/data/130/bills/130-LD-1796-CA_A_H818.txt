The Maine Port Terminal Facilities Marketing Program is established to encourage and

SUMMARY 
This amendment provides that the proceeds of bonds, the use of funds and the scope of 
the marketing program include associated multimodal infrastructure facilities that directly 
support marine port operations. The amendment adds Portland and Searsport to the marine 
ports for which the Maine Port Terminal Facilities Marketing Program encourages and 
· promotes business opportunities.
