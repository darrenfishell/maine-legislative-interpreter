40SUMMARY

Page 9 - 130LR2058(01)1 Part A provides a bond issue, in the amount of $100,000,000, for the Department of 
2 Transportation to support its ongoing capital programs to preserve, improve and construct 
3 roads, bridges, railroads, airports, transit and ports.
4 Part B provides a bond issue, in the amount of $40,000,000, that will be used to provide 
5 funds for the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Land for Maine's 
6 Future Board.
7 Part C allows proceeds of the Land for Maine's Future Fund to be spent on property 
8 that is determined by the Land for Maine's Future Board to be for a community 
9 conservation project.  It defines "community conservation project" and amends the 
10 definition of "cooperating entities." It also provides that helping the State's natural 
11 ecosystems, wildlife and natural resources-based economies adapt to a changing climate is 
12 a priority of the board in making land acquisitions.
13 Part D provides a bond issue, in the amount of $6,800,000, that will be used to provide 
14 funds for the State's share of maintenance, repair, capital improvement, modernization and 
15 energy efficiency projects for Maine National Guard readiness centers and support 
16 facilities, to be used to match an estimated $7,600,000 in federal funds.
