3ยง13027.  Lifetime substitute teaching certificate
41.  Application, review and certificate issuance.  A person who meets the eligibility 
5 requirements of subsection 2 may apply for a lifetime substitute teaching certificate by 
6 filing an application with the department.  The commissioner shall review the application, 
7 and if the commissioner approves the application, the commissioner shall issue the 
8 applicant a lifetime substitute teaching certificate.
92.  Eligibility.  To be eligible for a lifetime substitute teaching certificate, a person 
10 must have held for at least 10 years a professional teacher certificate issued pursuant to this 
11 Title that has never been revoked or suspended.
123.  State and federal laws, rules and regulations.  A lifetime substitute teaching 
13 certificate holder is subject to all state and federal laws, rules and regulations related to 
14 criminal history record checks and special education training.
154.  Proof of qualifications.  The department may not require a holder of a lifetime 
16 substitute teaching certificate to provide proof of professional development, continuing 
17 education credits, education or other similar qualifications to maintain the certificate.
185.  Revocation.  A lifetime substitute teaching certificate is subject to the same laws 
19 for revocation as any teaching certificate.
206.  Renewal not required.  A person who holds a lifetime substitute teaching 
21 certificate is not required to renew the certificate.
227.  No annual fee.  The department may assess a one-time fee for a lifetime substitute 
23 teaching certificate but may not require a holder of a lifetime substitute teaching certificate 
24 to pay an annual fee to maintain the certificate.
25Sec. 2.  Rules.  The Department of Education shall adopt rules setting a reasonable 
26 one-time fee for a lifetime substitute teaching certificate under the Maine Revised Statutes, 
27 Title 20-A, section 13027, subsection 7.  Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine 
28 technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
29SUMMARY
30 This bill authorizes the Commissioner of Education to issue lifetime substitute teaching 
31 certificates to teachers in the State who have held for at least 10 years a professional teacher 
32 certificate that has never been revoked or suspended.30
