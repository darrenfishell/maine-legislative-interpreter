Page 1 - 130LR2464(01)1Sec. 1. Task force to coordinate data to improve water quality of Saco Bay. 
2Resolved:  That, no later than September 1, 2022, the Department of Environmental 
3 Protection shall convene a task force to integrate data from multiple studies of the water 
4 quality of Saco Bay to identify sources of pollution and develop recommendations to 
5 remove those sources or lessen the pollution from those sources with the overall goal of 
6 improving water quality and increasing access to recreational and economic activity in the 
7 bay.  The task force must include a broad range of interested persons, including, but not 
8 limited to, representatives of environmental groups, municipal, state and federal officials 
9 and persons with policy expertise in issues related to water quality improvement.  By 
10 October 1, 2023, the department shall submit a report including findings and 
11 recommendations of the task force to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 
12 jurisdiction over environmental protection matters.
13SUMMARY
14 This resolve directs the Department of Environmental Protection to convene a task 
15 force to integrate data from multiple studies of the water quality of Saco Bay to identify 
16 sources of pollution in the bay and develop recommendations to remove those sources or 
17 lessen the pollution from those sources.  The department must convene the task force by 
18 September 1, 2022 and submit a report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature 
19 having jurisdiction over environmental protection matters by October 1, 2023.14
