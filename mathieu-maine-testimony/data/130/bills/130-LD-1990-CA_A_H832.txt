9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A " to H.P. 1476, L.D. 1990, "An Act Allowing 
10 · Electric-powered School Buses To Have Distinctively Colored Bumpers, Wheels and Rub 
11 Rails" 
12 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
13 'An Act Allowing Electric-powered School Buses To Have Distinctively Colored 
14 Bumpers, Wheels and Rub Rails and Allowing Public Service Vehicles To Be 
15 · Equipped with a Flashing Green Auxiliary Light' 
16 Amend the bill by inserting before section 1 the following: 
17 'Sec. 1. 29-A MRSA §2054, sub-§2, 1C, as amended by PL 2021, c. 205, §1, is 
18 further amended by enacting a new subparagraph (2-B) to read: 
19 (2-B) A public service vehicle may be equipped with a flashing green auxiliary 
20 · light mounted on top of the vehicle in such a manner as to emit a green light over 
21 a 360-degree angle. A flashing green auxiliazy light on a public service vehicle 
22 equipped in accordance with this subparagraph may be used only when assisting 
23 members of the public or law enforcement officers with a disabled vehicle. loading 
24 a vehicle onto a wrecker. removing debris from a roadway or constructing, 
25 maintaining, inspecting or repairing utility infrastructure.' 
26 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section 
27 number to read consecutively. 
28 SUMMARY 
29 This amendment allows a flashing green auxiliary light to be mounted on top of a public 
30 service vehicle.
