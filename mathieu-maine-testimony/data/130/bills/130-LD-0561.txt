Page 1 - 130LR0966(01)1Sec. 1.  Department to adopt rules regarding animal rescue entities.  
2Resolved:  That the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry shall adopt 
3 rules that set standards for animal rescue entities, including but not limited to rescue dog 
4 operations, that specify staff-to-animal ratios and that govern animals that are returned 
5 multiple times and resold or placed in multiple homes.  Rules adopted pursuant to this 
6 section are routine technical rules as described in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, 
7 chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
8SUMMARY
9 This resolve requires the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to 
10 adopt rules that set standards for animal rescue entities, including but not limited to rescue 
11 dog operations, that specify staff-to-animal ratios and that govern animals that are returned 
12 multiple times and resold or placed in multiple homes.  9
