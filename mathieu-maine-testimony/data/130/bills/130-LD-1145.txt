13SUMMARY
14 Retirement benefits under state, local or federal government retirement plans that are 
15 based on employment compensation for which contributions are not made to the federal 
16 Social Security system result in reductions in the amount of Social Security benefits that a 
17 retiree is eligible to receive under the federal windfall elimination provision.  This bill 
18 provides an income tax exemption for those retirement benefits.14
