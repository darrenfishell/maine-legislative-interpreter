28SUMMARY
29 This bill allows holders of commercial lobster and crab fishing licenses to hold a 
30 marine harvesting demonstration license.  The commercial license holders are required to 
31 use different buoys and tags for the demonstration harvesting than they use for their 
32 commercial harvesting.29
