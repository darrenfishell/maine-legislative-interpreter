26SUMMARY
27 This bill amends the voting laws in the following ways. It:
28 1.  Prohibits incarcerated persons from voting;
29 2.  Requires ballots to be in paper form;
30 3.  Requires photo identification to vote;
31 4.  Prohibits accepting ballots after the polls close;
32 5.  Prohibits the buying of a vote by another person for money or any other thing of 
33 value; and
34 6.  Allows absentee voting only for a person who is unable to vote, the reason for which 
35 is certified by the person, and requires 2 other persons to collect and deliver the ballot, each 
36 of whom must certify the collection and delivery.27
