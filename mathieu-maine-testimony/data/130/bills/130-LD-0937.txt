10Sec. 2.  Maine Climate Council; report.  Resolved: That on or before December 1, 
11 2021, the chairs of the Maine Climate Council shall submit to the Joint Standing Committee 
12 on Environment and Natural Resources a report outlining the recommendations of the

15 Second Regular Session of the 130th Legislature.
16SUMMARY
17 This resolve directs the cochairs of the Maine Climate Council, established pursuant to 
18 the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 38, section 577-A, to convene a stakeholder group to 
19 develop recommendations for the establishment of programs and policies designed to 
20 incentivize the effective sequestration of carbon in soils by landowners and land managers 
21 in the State, including, but not limited to, through the identification and distribution of 
22 financial incentives for that purpose.  On or before December 1, 2021, the cochairs of the 
23 Maine Climate Council are required to submit to the Joint Standing Committee on 
24 Environment and Natural Resources a report outlining the stakeholder group's 
25 recommendations, including any proposed legislation.  After reviewing the report, the 
26 committee may report out related legislation to the Second Regular Session of the 130th 
27 Legislature.17
