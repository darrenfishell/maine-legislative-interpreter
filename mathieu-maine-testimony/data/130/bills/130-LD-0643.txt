16SUMMARY
17 This bill directs the Commissioner of Education to establish a working group to study 
18 and disseminate a program to improve the training of school boards in their role of hiring, 
19 evaluating and overseeing superintendents, which must include the development of a best 
20 practices module for diverse and inclusive executive hiring practices and a standardized, 
21 360-degree performance evaluation system that incorporates best practices and board 
22 member training tools.  The Commissioner of Education is also required to administer the 
23 program and make it available to all school boards.17
