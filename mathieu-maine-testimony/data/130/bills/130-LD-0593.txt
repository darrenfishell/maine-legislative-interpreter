4 In accordance with the requirements of this section and in consultation with the 
5 Department of Marine Resources, the department shall establish and administer an eelgrass 
6 mapping program.  For the purposes of this section, "eelgrass" means the flowering marine 
7 plant species Zostera marina that is primarily found in shallow, protected intertidal and 
8 subtidal locations in the State.
91.  Mapping procedures.  The department shall in accordance with the schedule in 
10 subsection 3 facilitate the production and update of eelgrass distribution maps for eelgrass 
11 beds within the State.  The data collected and the maps produced and updated in accordance 
12 with this section must, to the maximum extent practicable, be compatible with the State's 
13 geographic information system.
142.  Eelgrass Mapping Fund.  The Eelgrass Mapping Fund is created within the 
15 department as a nonlapsing dedicated fund to support the establishment and administration 
16 of the eelgrass mapping program required under this section.  The fund may accept revenue 
17 from grants, bequests, gifts or contributions from any source, public or private.
183.  Mapping schedule.  The mapping of eelgrass beds required under this section must 
19 be conducted in accordance with the following schedule, as department resources allow.
20 A.  No later than November 1, 2022, mapping must be completed for that portion of 
21 the coast from Phippsburg to St. George and must be updated every 5 years thereafter.
22 B.  No later than November 1, 2023, mapping must be completed for that portion of 
23 the coast from St. George to Brooklin and must be updated every 5 years thereafter.
24 C.  No later than November 1, 2024, mapping must be completed for that portion of 
25 the coast from Brooklin to Jonesport and must be updated every 5 years thereafter.
26 D.  No later than November 1, 2025, mapping must be completed for that portion of 
27 the coast from Jonesport to Calais and must be updated every 5 years thereafter.
28 E.  No later than November 1, 2026, mapping must be completed for that portion of 
29 the coast from Eliot to Phippsburg and must be updated every 5 years thereafter.
304.  Availability of data and maps.  The department shall make available on its publicly 
31 accessible website the data collected and maps produced and updated under this section.
325.  Report.  On or before March 1, 2023, and biennially thereafter, the department shall 
33 submit to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over 
34 environmental and natural resources matters a report summarizing the data collected and 
35 maps produced and updated under this section and including an analysis, if available, of 
36 the changes to the eelgrass beds within the State that are demonstrated by the data collected 
37 and maps produced under this section.
