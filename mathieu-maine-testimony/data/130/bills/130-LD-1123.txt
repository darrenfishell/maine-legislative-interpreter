2Sec. 1.  3 MRSA §902, sub-§1-D is enacted to read:
31-D.  Memorial to victims of COVID-19 pandemic.  Notwithstanding section 902‑A, 
4 subsection 2, paragraph B, the commission may arrange for and oversee the development

7SUMMARY
8 This bill allows the State House and Capitol Park Commission to arrange for and 
9 oversee the development and installation of a monument honoring the victims of the 
10 pandemic related to coronavirus disease 2019, or COVID-19.8
