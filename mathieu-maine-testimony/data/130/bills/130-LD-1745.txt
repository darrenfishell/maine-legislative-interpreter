19SUMMARY
20 This bill allows an employee license issued by the Department of Public Safety, 
21 Gambling Control Board to be renewed for either a one-year term or a 3-year term. The 
22 renewal fee for a one-year employee license is $25, and the renewal fee for a 3-year 
23 employee license is $50.20
