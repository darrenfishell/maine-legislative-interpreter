14SUMMARY
15 This bill changes the term "job outlook" to "job openings" in the law governing the 
16 Higher Opportunity for Pathways to Employment Program to better describe the criteria 
17 for participation to be identified by the Center for Workforce Research and Information 
18 within the Department of Labor.  It provides that department rules must specify at minimum 
19 the fields of health care, technology, engineering and education in the provision governing 
20 approved postsecondary undergraduate 4-year degree programs.
