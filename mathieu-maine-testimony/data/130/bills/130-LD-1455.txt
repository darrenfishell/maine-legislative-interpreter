11SUMMARY
12 This bill amends the law on the crime of prostitution by changing from an affirmative 
13 defense to a defense that a person engaged in prostitution because the person was compelled 
14 to do so and adding the affirmative defense that a person engaged in prostitution to prevent 
15 bodily harm, serious economic hardship, serious injury or another threat to the person or 
16 another person.12
