3ยง1127.  Maintaining drug-involved premises
41.  A person is guilty of maintaining drug-involved premises if the person:
5 A.  Knowingly opens, leases, rents, uses or maintains any place, whether permanently 
6 or temporarily, for the purpose of unlawfully manufacturing, storing, distributing or 
7 using a scheduled drug; or
8 B.  Manages or controls, whether permanently or temporarily, either as an owner, 
9 lessee, agent, employee, occupant or mortgagee, and knowingly and intentionally rents, 
10 leases, profits from or makes available for use, with or without compensation, any place 
11 for the purpose of unlawfully manufacturing, storing, distributing or using a scheduled 
12 drug.
132. Maintaining drug-involved premises is a Class C crime.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill creates the Class C crime of maintaining drug-involved premises, in which an 
16 occupant, user or owner owns, rents, leases, occupies or otherwise uses a place for the 
17 purpose of unlawfully manufacturing, storing, distributing or using a scheduled drug.15
