28SUMMARY
29 This bill provides for the exercise of judicial discretion beginning January 1, 2022 to 
30 change all mandatory terms of imprisonment for persons sentenced beginning on that date 
31 to maximum terms of imprisonment.  The bill applies to all Titles of the Maine Revised 
32 Statutes.  The bill directs the Revisor of Statutes to review the Maine Revised Statutes and 
33 compile all provisions that require the court in sentencing a person for a violation of law to 
34 impose a mandatory term of imprisonment.  The Revisor of Statutes is required to submit 
35 a report by January 15, 2022 to the Joint Standing Committee on Criminal Justice and 
36 Public Safety listing all provisions of all Titles that require the court to impose a mandatory 
37 term of imprisonment and providing amending language to change the mandatory term of 
38 imprisonment into a maximum term of imprisonment as determined in the discretion of the 
39 court.  After receiving and reviewing the report of the Revisor of Statutes, the Joint 
40 Standing Committee on Criminal Justice and Public Safety may introduce legislation to the 
41 130th Legislature.29
