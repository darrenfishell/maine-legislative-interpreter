25SUMMARY
26 This bill requires the Government Oversight Committee to create a system designed to 
27 monitor, on an ongoing basis, the Department of Health and Human Services, Office of 
28 Child and Family Services regarding the effectiveness of the office in protecting the safety 
29 of children in state care. The committee may create a working group that has the purposes 
30 of monitoring the policies and practices used by the office to maintain the safety of children 
31 in state care, reporting to the committee on a quarterly basis and providing an annual report 
32 to the committee and the Legislature.26
