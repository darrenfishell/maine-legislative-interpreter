20Sec. 3.  Adapt programs to assist veterans caring for children with special 
21needs.  The Director of the Maine Bureau of Veterans' Services within the Department of

23 by the State to veterans and adapt the programs as necessary to ensure the programs provide 
24 assistance specifically targeted to the needs of veterans who are caregivers of children with 
25 special needs.  In adapting programs under this section, the bureau shall ensure that 
26 program staff consult with doctors, therapists, educational professionals and other 
27 professionals in fields relevant to caring for children with special needs to ensure that the 
28 services provided to each veteran who is a caregiver of a child with special needs are 
29 adapted to serve the specific needs of that child.
