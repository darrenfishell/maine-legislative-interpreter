3SUMMARY
4 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill proposes to implement strategies relating to blockchain, cryptocurrency and 
6 other financial technology to promote regulatory efficiency, enable business organizational 
7 and governance structures that may expand opportunities in financial technology and 
8 promote education regarding and adoption of financial technology in the public and private 
9 sectors.  This bill also proposes to:
10 1.  Define "blockchain" and "blockchain technology";
11 2.  Enable the creation and regulation of personal information protection companies;
12 3.  Enable the creation of blockchain-based limited liability companies; and
13 4.  Create a study for the potential use of blockchain technology in government record 
14 keeping.4
