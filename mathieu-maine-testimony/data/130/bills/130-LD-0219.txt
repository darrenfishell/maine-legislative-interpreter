15SUMMARY
16 This bill allows the Commissioner of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, in 
17 consultation with the Finance Authority of Maine, to periodically review interest rates and 
18 establish an interest rate for agricultural marketing loans that varies with the prime rate of 
19 interest and is a competitive interest rate at the time of loan origination.  It prohibits the 
20 rate from being less than 1% per year.  It also allows participants in the Maine Farms for 
21 the Future Program to borrow at that rate.
