15Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
16Sec. 1.  Department of Administrative and Financial Services to submit 
17legislation allocating state fiscal recovery funds.  The Department of Administrative 
18 and Financial Services shall propose a bill to the Joint Standing Committee on 
19 Appropriations and Financial Affairs during the First Special Session of the 130th 
20 Legislature that allocates funds received from the State Fiscal Recovery Fund established 
21 in the federal American Rescue Plan Act of 2021, Public Law 117-2.  The legislation must 
22 accomplish the following goals.
231.  Immediate recovery from the COVID-19 pandemic.  The funding must provide 
24 new and renewed support for businesses in the State, particularly small businesses, to 
25 ensure their doors stay open and their workers are safe and on the job and the economy is 
26 secure in the short term.  In order to meet this goal, the legislation must:
27 A. Establish and provide funding for a program of grants and loan guarantees to 
28 businesses; 
29 B. Invest in heritage industries such as historic sites, buildings and museums for the 
30 purpose of encouraging tourism; 
31 C.  Lower health care costs for small businesses; 
32 D.  Replenish the State's account in the Unemployment Trust Fund; 
33 E.  Sustain new businesses and entrepreneurs; and 
34 F.  Encourage diversity in business ownership and management.
352.  Long-term economic growth.  Based on the recommendations of the Governor's 
36 Economic Recovery Committee and those in the Maine Economic Development Strategy 
37 2020-2029 from the Department of Economic and Community Development that call for 
38 robust investment in research and development, skills training and new worker attraction 
39 to spur new businesses and attract new residents to the State, the bill must provide funding 
40 to encourage long-term economic growth.  This must be accomplished by:

6Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
7 takes effect when approved.
8SUMMARY
9 This bill requires the Department of Administrative and Financial Services to submit 
10 legislation to the Joint Standing Committee on Appropriations and Financial Affairs that, 
11 using funding from the State Fiscal Recovery Fund as established by the federal American 
12 Rescue Plan Act of 2021, accomplishes the following goals:
13 1.  Immediate recovery from the COVID-19 pandemic; 
14 2.  Long-term economic growth; and
15 3.  Infrastructure revitalization.  
16 The joint standing committee is authorized to report out a bill, based on the legislation 
17 submitted by the department, to the 130th Legislature.9
