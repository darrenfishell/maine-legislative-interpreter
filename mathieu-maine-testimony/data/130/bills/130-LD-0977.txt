12SUMMARY
13 Current law allows a person who obtained an associate or bachelor's degree from an 
14 accredited Maine or non-Maine community college, college or university after December 
15 31, 2015 and a person who obtained a graduate degree from an accredited Maine college 
16 or university after December 31, 2015 to be eligible for the educational opportunity tax 
17 credit.  This bill, for tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2022, provides eligibility 
18 for the tax credit to a person who obtained an associate or bachelor's degree from an 
19 accredited Maine or non-Maine community college, college or university or a graduate 
20 degree from an accredited Maine college or university after December 31, 2011.13
