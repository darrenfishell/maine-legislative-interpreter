10SUMMARY
11 This bill requires that access to in-home and community support services for adults 
12 with long-term care needs be equitably distributed across geographic areas of the State and 
13 provides that, at a minimum, providers must be available for telephonic or in-person 
14 meetings in order to provide services in every county in the State.  The bill authorizes the 
15 Department of Health and Human Services to adopt routine technical rules to implement 
16 these provisions.11
