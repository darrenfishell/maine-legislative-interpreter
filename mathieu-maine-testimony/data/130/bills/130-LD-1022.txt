11SUMMARY
12 This bill provides that agricultural employees and seasonal employees are subject to 
13 the laws that place limits on mandatory overtime.  It also provides that agricultural 
14 employees are subject to the laws that set a minimum wage and overtime rate.  It provides 
15 that the laws that set an overtime rate apply to certain activities related to agricultural 
16 produce, meat and fish products and perishable foods.12
