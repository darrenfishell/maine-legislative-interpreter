32SUMMARY
33 This bill, which is being submitted by the Sex Offender Management and Risk 
34 Assessment Advisory Commission, amends the Sex Offender Registration and Notification 
35 Act of 2013 to fix inconsistencies in the law introduced when that Act was recently 
36 amended. The bill also provides that a violation of the statute prohibiting the promotion of 
37 prostitution of certain minors, added in 2021, is a Tier III offense.33
