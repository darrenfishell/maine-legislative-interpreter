10SUMMARY
11 This bill authorizes a public employer to deduct payroll deductions designated in 
12 writing by a public employee for disability insurance and life insurance offered in 
13 conjunction with the employee's membership in an employee organization recognized by 
14 the employer or designated by a collective bargaining agreement.
