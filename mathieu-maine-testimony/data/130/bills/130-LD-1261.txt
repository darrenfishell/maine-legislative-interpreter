32SUMMARY
33 This bill repeals the definitions of "tourist camp" and "trailer camp" and replaces those 
34 terms with the term "campground" in the sales and use tax law.  This bill exempts from 
35 sales and use tax rental fees charged to a person who resides for 28 days or longer at one 
36 campground.  This bill also exempts from the sales and use tax the rental of a site at a 
37 campground regardless of the number of days of residence as long as nothing else of value 
38 is provided by the owner of that campground to the renter.33
