3ยง11226-B.  Nonresident big game hunter; guide required
4 A nonresident may not hunt moose, deer or bear without being accompanied by a 
5 person who holds a valid guide license pursuant to chapter 927 authorizing that person to 
6 act as a hunting guide, except that a nonresident may hunt moose, deer or bear without a 
7 hunting guide if the nonresident is the resident of a state or a province of Canada that has 
8 a reciprocity agreement with the State allowing Maine residents to hunt moose, deer or bear 
9 without having to be accompanied by a guide.
10SUMMARY
11 The bill prohibits a nonresident from hunting moose, deer or bear without being 
12 accompanied by a licensed Maine guide if the nonresident is from a state or a province of 
13 Canada without a reciprocity agreement with the State allowing Maine citizens to hunt 
14 there without a guide.
