17SUMMARY
18 This bill allows for the treatment of human remains by alkaline hydrolysis or natural 
19 organic reduction as an alternate to burial in a casket or cremation.  Alkaline hydrolysis is 
20 the reduction of human remains to bone fragments and essential elements in a licensed 
21 alkaline hydrolysis facility using heat, pressure, water and base chemical agents and natural 
22 organic reduction is the contained accelerated conversion of human remains to soil.1
