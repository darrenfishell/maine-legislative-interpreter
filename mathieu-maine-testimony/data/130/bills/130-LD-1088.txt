4 further amended to read:
5 A.  All persons shall be silent at the moderator's command.  A person may not speak 
6 before that person is recognized by the moderator.  A person who is not neither a voter 
7 in the town nor the owner of residential real property in the town may speak at the 
8 meeting only with the consent of 2/3 of the voters present.  For the purposes of this 
9 paragraph, a mortgagor of residential real property is deemed the owner of that 
10 residential real property.
11 (1)  If any person, after a command for order by the moderator, continues to act in 
12 a disorderly manner, the moderator may direct that person to leave the meeting.  If 
13 the person refuses to leave, the moderator may have that person removed by a 
14 constable and confined until the meeting is adjourned.
15SUMMARY
16 This bill allows a nonresident individual who owns residential real property in a town 
17 to speak at a town meeting to the same extent as a resident of the town.16
