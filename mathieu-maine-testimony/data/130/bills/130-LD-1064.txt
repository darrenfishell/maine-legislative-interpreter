29SUMMARY
30 This bill directs the Department of Health and Human Services to provide 
31 reimbursement under the MaineCare program for palliative care for the entire 
32 interdisciplinary team as appropriate to the plan of care, regardless of setting, including 
33 hospitals, nursing homes, outpatient clinics and home care providers.  It also requires the 
34 department to adopt rules that support and standardize the delivery of palliative care in the 
35 State, including but not limited to strategies for the distribution of public educational 
36 documents and the distribution by health care providers of information regarding the 
37 availability of palliative care to patients.  It also requires the department to consult with the 
38 Maine Hospice Council, the Palliative Care and Quality of Life Interdisciplinary Advisory 
39 Council and other stakeholders when developing educational documents and rules related 
40 to palliative care.30
