17SUMMARY
18 This bill amends the laws governing school bus identifiers to allow electric-powered 
19 school buses to have bumpers, rub rails and wheels in the colors originally painted by the 
20 original manufacturer.  It also allows all school buses to display lettering from the original 
21 manufacturer and to have wheels and rub rails that are painted glossy black.18
