3ยง752-F.  Perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl substances
4 An action arising out of any harm or injury caused by a perfluoroalkyl or 
5 polyfluoroalkyl substance must be commenced within 6 years after the date the plaintiff 
6 discovers or reasonably should have discovered such harm or injury.  For the purposes of 
7 this section, "perfluoroalkyl or polyfluoroalkyl substance" means any member of the class 
8 of fluorinated organic chemicals containing at least one fully fluorinated carbon atom.
9 Nothing in this section may be construed to alter the notice period applicable to claims 
10 and causes of actions against governmental entities in section 8107 or the time to file an 
11 action against a governmental entity in section 8110.
12SUMMARY
13 This bill provides that an action arising out of any harm or injury caused by a 
14 perfluoroalkyl or polyfluoroalkyl substance must be commenced within 6 years after the 
15 plaintiff discovers or should have discovered such harm or injury.
16 It makes clear that the notice of claim and statute of limitations provisions of the Maine 
17 Tort Claims Act apply to actions against governmental entities that arise out of any harm 
18 or injury caused by a perfluoroalkyl or polyfluoroalkyl substance.13
