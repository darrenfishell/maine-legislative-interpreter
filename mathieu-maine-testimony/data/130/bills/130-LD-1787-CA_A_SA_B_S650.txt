SUMMARY 
This amendment amends Committee Amendment "A" to incorporate the provisions in 
Senate Amendment "A" to Committee Amendment "A" that changed: 
1. From December 31, 2022 to March 1, 2023 the date by which the Department of 
Health and Human Services must provide an alternative, updated method for rebasing 
federally qualified health center prospective payment system rates; 
2. From December 31, 2022 to December 31, 2023 the date after which the department 
may change the base year used for rebasing federally qualified health center prospective 
payment system rates; 
3. From December 31, 2022 to February 1, 2023 the date by which the department must 
issue final rate letters implementing the reimbursement option chosen by each federally 
qualified health center; and 
4. From July 1, 2022 to March 1, 2023 the date on which the reimbursement option 
chosen by each federally qualified health center is effective.
