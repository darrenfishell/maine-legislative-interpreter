2Sec. 1.  37-B MRSA §742, sub-§1, ¶E is enacted to read:
3 E.  Notwithstanding any provision of this subsection to the contrary, the Governor may 
4 not restrict the operation of any essential business, which is any private business that 
5 employs a person in this State, without restricting all essential businesses in the same 
6 manner.
7SUMMARY
8 This bill prohibits the Governor from restricting the operation of a private business 
9 during a state of emergency unless the Governor restricts all private businesses in the same 
10 manner.8
