SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces Committee Amendment "A." The amendment: 
1. Adds attendance at training events to the standards governing the delivery of indigent 
legal services; 
2. Establishes public defender positions to be dispatched in the State where needed; 
and 
3. Provides money for legal research the Maine Commission on Indigent Legal 
Services attorneys.
