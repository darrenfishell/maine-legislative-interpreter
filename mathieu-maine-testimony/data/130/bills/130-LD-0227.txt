18SUMMARY
19 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation, in consultation with the 
20 Northern New England Passenger Rail Authority and interested municipalities and 
21 planning organizations, to conduct a feasibility study and develop a plan to implement 
22 passenger rail service from Brunswick through Augusta and Waterville to Bangor along 
23 existing railroad corridors.  It provides an allocation of $300,000 in one-time funds in fiscal 
24 year 2021-22 to fund the feasibility study and plan.13
