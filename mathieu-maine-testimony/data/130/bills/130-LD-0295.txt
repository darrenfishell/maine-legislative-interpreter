6SUMMARY
7 This bill eliminates a provision in law requiring that a certified nurse practitioner who 
8 qualifies as an advanced practice registered nurse must practice, for at least 24 months, 
9 under the supervision of a physician or supervising nurse practitioner or be employed by a 
10 clinic or hospital that has a medical director who is a licensed physician.  The bill also 
11 removes a requirement that a certified nurse practitioner must complete 24 months of 
12 supervised practice to be a supervising nurse practitioner.
