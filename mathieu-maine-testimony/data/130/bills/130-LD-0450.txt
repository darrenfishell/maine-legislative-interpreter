3CHAPTER 408-B
4BONE MARROW DONATION
5ยง1981.  Educational materials
61.  Content.  The department shall develop informational and educational materials for 
7 dissemination to the public and the health care providers, as defined in section 1843, 
8 subsection 3 regarding bone marrow donation through the national marrow donor program.  
9 The department shall seek assistance from the national marrow donor program to ensure 
10 that the materials are updated periodically, fully disclose the risks involved in donating 
11 bone marrow and address the education and recruitment of minority populations.
12 The information and educational materials must include information on:
13 A.  The need for bone marrow donations;
14 B.  The patient populations that would benefit from bone marrow donation;
15 C.  How to join the bone marrow registry; and
16 D.  How to acquire a free buccal swab kit from the bone marrow registry.
172.  Distribution.  The department shall provide information and educational materials 
18 developed pursuant to subsection 1 to the public and to health care providers.  The 
19 department shall seek assistance from the national marrow donor program to establish a 
20 system to distribute the information and educational materials.
213.  Rules.  The department shall adopt routine technical rules pursuant to Title 5, 
22 chapter 375, subchapter 2-A to implement the provisions of this chapter, including the 
23 establishment of a distribution system and methods to ensure the use of the materials by 
24 health care providers.
25SUMMARY
26 This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to develop and 
27 disseminate, in coordination with the national marrow donor program, information and 
28 educational materials regarding bone marrow donation.  The information and materials 
29 must be distributed to the public and to health care providers.
