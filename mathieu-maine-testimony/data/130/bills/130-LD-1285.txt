41.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 
5 following terms have the following meanings.
6 A.  "Active duty combat" means performing any of the following services in the active 
7 federal military service of the United States:
8 (1)  Participation in an armed conflict;
9 (2)  Performance of a hazardous service in a foreign state; or
10 (3)  Performance of a duty through an instrumentality of war.
11 B.  "Official declaration of war" means an official declaration of war made by the 
12 United States Congress pursuant to the United States Constitution, Article I, Section 8, 
13 Clause 11.
142.  Release of guard.  Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the Maine 
15 National Guard and any member of the Maine National Guard may not be released from 
16 the State into active duty combat unless the United States Congress has passed an official 
17 declaration of war or has taken an official action pursuant to the United States Constitution, 
18 Article I, Section 8, Clause 15 to explicitly call forth the Maine National Guard and any 
19 member of the Maine National Guard to expressly execute the laws of the union, repel an 
20 invasion or suppress an insurrection.  The Governor shall take all actions necessary to 
21 comply with the requirements of this section.
22SUMMARY
23 This bill requires an official declaration of war or an action to call forth the state militia 
24 by the United States Congress before members of the Maine National Guard may be 
25 released from state control to participate in active duty combat.23
