2Sec. 1.  5 MRSA §17806, sub-§1, ¶A, as amended by PL 2015, c. 334, §1, is further 
3 amended to read:
4 A.  Except as provided in paragraphs A-1 and A-2, whenever there is a percentage 
5 increase in the Consumer Price Index from July 1st to June 30th, the board shall 
6 automatically make an equal percentage increase in retirement benefits, beginning in 
7 September, up to a maximum annual increase of 3%.  Effective July 1, 2011, the 
8 increase applies to that portion of the retirement benefit up to $20,000, which amount 
9 must be indexed in subsequent years by the same percentage adjustments granted under 
10 this paragraph and paragraph A-2.
11SUMMARY
12 This bill provides that cost-of-living increases for retired state employees and teachers 
13 and their beneficiaries apply to a retiree's entire retirement benefit instead of only to the 
14 first $20,000 of the retirement benefit.12
