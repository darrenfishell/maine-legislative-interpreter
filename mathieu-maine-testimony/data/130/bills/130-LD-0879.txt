17SUMMARY
18 This bill amends the Maine Human Rights Act to ensure that religious corporations, 
19 associations and organizations, whether or not they receive public funds, are exempt from 
20 discrimination prohibitions on the basis of employment, housing and educational 
21 opportunity.  Any for-profit organization owned, controlled or operated by a religious 
22 association or corporation remains subject to the discrimination prohibitions.18
