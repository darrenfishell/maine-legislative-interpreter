13Sec. 21.  Additional changes to law.  The Department of the Secretary of State, 
14 Bureau of Motor Vehicles shall submit a bill to the Second Regular Session of the 130th 
15 Legislature to make changes in the Maine Revised Statutes to fully abolish inspection 
16 requirements for motor vehicles registered in the State, with the exception of inspection 
17 requirements for commercial motor vehicles, trailers and semitrailers under Title 29-A, 
18 section 1753; inspection of fire trucks under Title 29-A, section 1755; and inspection by 
19 dealers and transporters under Title 29-A, section 1754, which must be retained.
20SUMMARY
21 This bill repeals statutory provisions requiring inspections for motor vehicles registered 
22 in the State, except that the provisions regarding inspection of commercial motor vehicles, 
23 trailers and semitrailers under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 29-A, section 1753; 
24 inspection of fire trucks under Title 29-A, section 1755; and inspection by dealers and 
25 transporters under Title 29-A, section 1754 are not repealed.21
