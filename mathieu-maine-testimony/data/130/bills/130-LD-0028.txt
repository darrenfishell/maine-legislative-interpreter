6ยง2231.  Alert system to notify the public of a missing person with an intellectual or 
7developmental disability
8 In accordance with this chapter and with the cooperation of the Department of 
9 Transportation, the Maine Turnpike Authority, a statewide organization representing 
10 broadcast groups in the State, the Office of the Governor and appropriate law enforcement 
11 agencies, the Department of Public Safety shall develop and implement an alert system to 
12 be activated on behalf of missing persons with intellectual or developmental disabilities. 
13 The alert system must include standards of procedure for local law enforcement agencies 
14 to determine that a missing person is a person with an intellectual or developmental 
15 disability and appropriately activate an alert to local or statewide law enforcement agencies 
16 and to the media, including print, radio, Internet-based communication systems and other 
17 methods of communicating information to the public; a plan for providing relevant 
18 information to the public through an existing system of dynamic message signs located 
19 across the State when necessary; and training for all law enforcement officers. The alert 
20 system must be developed and implemented using existing resources.
21SUMMARY
22 This bill requires the Department of Public Safety to develop and implement an alert 
23 system to be activated on behalf of missing persons with intellectual or developmental 
24 disabilities.
