SUMMARY  23 
This bill amends laws governing forensic examination kits to provide for the kits to 24 
be used for testing in relation to alleged crimes other t han gross sexual assault.  This bill 25 
also provides that rules that have been adopted by the Department of Public Safety to 26 
implement the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 25, sections 2915 and 3821 may be amended 27 
to reflect the changes set forth in this Act wi thout triggering the requirements set forth in 28 
the Maine Administrative Procedure Act.  29
