This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes directing the Public Utilities Commission to pursue the 4 5 
recommendations made by Emprimus LLC o f Minneapolis, MN in Emprimus LLC’s 6 
report to the Public Utilities Commission filed January 6, 2015, Docket Number 2013 - 7 
00145, regarding the effect of geomagnetic disturbances and electromagnetic pulses on 8 
the State’s electric grid.  These recommendations include:  9 
1.  Pursuing neutral blocking in the 18 transformers of the State’s electric grid with 10 
the highest geo -electric -induced currents;  11 
2.  Installing electromagnetic pulse and intentional electromagnetic interference 12 
detectors at key substations on the  State’s electric grid;  13 
3.  Installing electromagnetic pulse and intentional electromagnetic interference 14 
protective cabinets at key substations on the State’s electric grid; and  15 
4.  Monitoring the costs and benefits derived from protection of the State’s electric 16 
system from harmful effects of geomagnetic disturbances and support the cost recovery 17 
of supplemental reliability improvements to Central Maine Power’s Maine Power 18 
Reliability Program.  19
