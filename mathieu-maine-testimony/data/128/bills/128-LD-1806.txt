2 Sec. 1.  29-A MRSA §101, sub-§8-B  is enacted  to read:
3 8-B.  Battery-electric  motor vehicle.  "Battery -electric motor vehicle" means an 
4 automobile or pickup truck the primary motive power of which is an electric motor.  
5 "Battery- electric motor vehicle" includes an electric motor  vehicle  with an auxiliary fuel 
6 combustion engine for purposes  of charging a battery and operating  auxiliary equipment.   
7 "Battery- electric motor vehicle" does not include a low-speed vehicle.
8 Sec. 2.  29-A MRSA §101, sub-§27-B  is enacted  to read:
9 27-B.  Hybrid  motor vehicle.   "Hybrid motor  vehicle"  means  an automobile  or 
10 pickup truck powered by a  combination  of a fuel  combustion engine and electric motor.
11 Sec. 3.  29-A MRSA §501, sub-§§1-A and 1-B are  enacted to read:
12 1-A.  Hybrid motor vehicle  registration surcharge.  In addition to the fee in 
13 subsection 1, the annual surcharge for registering a hybrid motor vehicle is $150.  The 
14 surcharge collected pursuant to this subsection  must be deposited in the  Highway Fund.
15 1-B.  Battery-electric motor  vehicle registration  surcharge.  In addition to the fee 
16 in subsection 1, the annual surcharge for registering  a battery-electric motor vehicle is 
17 $250.  The surcharge  collected pursuant  to this subsection must be deposited in the 
18 Highway Fund.
19 SUMMARY
20 This bill imposes  a surcharge, dedicated to the Highway  Fund, on the annual  
21 registration of a hybrid  motor vehicle in the amount of $150 and of a battery -electric  
22 motor vehicle in  the amount  of $250.
