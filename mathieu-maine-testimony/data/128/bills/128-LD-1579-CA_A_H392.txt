Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Any additional costs to the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry as a result of a municipality 
choosing to elect or appoint a local sealer of weights and measures, or retaining enforcement authority if a 
municipality chooses not to elect or appoint a local sealer of weights and measures, are anticipated to be minor and 
can be absorbed within existing budgeted resources.
