11 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
12 summary and inserting the following: 
13 'Sec. 1. 21-A MRSA §1014, sub-§2-C is enacted to read: 
14 2-C. Communications distributed by mail. Whenever a person makes an 
15 expenditure to finance a communication described in subsection 1, and the 
16 communication is to be distributed by a mailing house, a copy of the communication 
17 must be sent to the commission by mail in the same manner and form and at the same 
18 time as it is sent to those persons the communication is intended to influence. As used in 
19 this subsection, "mailing house" means a company that prepares unsolicited advertising 
20 for submission to and mailing by the United States Postal Service.
