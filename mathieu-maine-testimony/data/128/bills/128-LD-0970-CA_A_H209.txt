This bill includes ongoing General Fund appropriations of $300,000 per year beginning in fiscal year 2017-18 to a 
newly established Housing First Assistance Program within the Maine State Housing Authority (MSHA) for 30 
housing units and 24-hour housing support services at those units at a "Housing First" development for chronically 
homeless individuals and families. The bill allows MSHA to use a portion of this funding for the administrative costs 
associated with administering the program.
