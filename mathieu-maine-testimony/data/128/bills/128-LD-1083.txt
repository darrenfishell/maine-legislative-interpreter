7-B.  Hunting deer over bait.   A hunting license of a person convicted of placing or 3

Sec. 2.  12 MRSA §11452, sub -§2, as enacted by PL 2003, c. 655, Pt. B, §171 7 
and affected by §422, is amended to read:  8 
2.  Penalty.   A person who violates subsection 1 commits a Class E crime for which a 9 
fine of $500 must be imposed.  Pursuant to section 10902, subsection 7 -B, the hunting 10 
license of a person convicted of violating subsection 1 must be revoked and that person  is 11 
ineligible to obtain a hunting license for a period of one year from the date of conviction . 12 
SUMMARY  13 
This bill makes the penalty for hunting over bait during an open season on deer a 14 
mandatory fine of $500.  It also provides for the one -year suspension  of a hunting license 15 
of a person convicted of doing so.  16
