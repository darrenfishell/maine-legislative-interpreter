 SUMMARY  1 
This bill makes polygraph examiner and professional investigator administrative 2 
licensing files confidential by law, except the final written decision of whether a license 3 
is issued or denied, or of whet her, in response to a complaint, adverse action is taken 4 
against a licensee's license, is publicly accessible and records may be disclosed for 5 
criminal justice purposes or to a government licensing agency of this State or another 6 
state.  In the case of the  issuance or denial of a license, the final written decision must 7 
state the basis for which a license is issued or denied, and, in the case of a complaint 8 
against a licensee's license, the final written decision must state the basis for which 9 
adverse actio n was or was not taken against the license.  The Private Security Guards Act 10 
also is amended to ensure consistency with the changes made to the Polygraph Examiners 11 
Act and Professional Investigators Act.  12
