19 SUMMARY 
20 This amendment is the majority report of the committee. The amendment specifies 
21 that the crime of falsifying health care records applies if a person intends to deceive a 
22 governmental entity, as well as another person. The amendment removes veterinary 
23 hospitals from the definition of "health care provider." The amendment requires that the 
24 type of bodily injury that is required to elevate the crime to Class C is serious bodily 
25 injury.
