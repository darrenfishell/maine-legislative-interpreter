COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "W,' to H.P. 110, L.D. 152, Bill, "An Act To 
Strengthen Intragovernment Communication" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in §201 by inserting after the first indented paragraph the 
following: 
'When contacted by a member of the Legislature regarding a legislative matter or a 
constituent matter, a commissioner or director of a state agency or the commissioner's or 
director's designee shall respond in a cooperative and timely manner.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment is the majority repo1t of the committee. The amendment adds a 
provision to the bill to require the commissioner or director of a state agency, or the 
commissioner's or director's designee, to respond in a cooperative and timely manner 
when contacted by a member of the Legislature regarding a legislative or constituent 
matter.
