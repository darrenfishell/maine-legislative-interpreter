SUMMARY  27 
This bill remo ves all the good cause exceptions that prevent a person from being 28 
sanctioned under the Additional Support for People in Retraining and Employment - 29 
Temporary Assistance for Needy Families, or ASPIRE -TANF, program or the Temporary 30 
Assistance for Needy Fami lies program for failure to participate in the ASPIRE -TANF 31 
program, with the exception of domestic violence.  32
