Fiscal Detail and Notes 
The bill lowers the estate tax exemption from $5,000,000 to $1,000,000 for deaths occurring on or after January 1, 
2018 and reduces General Fund revenue by $24,400,000 in fiscal year 2018-19. The bill includes General Fund 
appropriations of $167,855 in fiscal year 2017-18 and $297,675 in fiscal year 2018-19 to the Department of 
Administrative and Financial Services for 3 Senior Revenue Agent positions and related costs to audit and process 
estate tax returns.
