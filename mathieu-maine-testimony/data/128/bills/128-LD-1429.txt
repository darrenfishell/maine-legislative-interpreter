SUMMARY  13 
This bill address es the opiate crisis in Maine by:  14 
1.  Allowing funds from property forfeited pursuant to a criminal forfeiture action to 15 
be assigned by the court, upon the request of the investigating or prosecuting agency, to a 16 
law enforcement agency in this State that p rovides case management and other social 17 
services to persons with substance use disorders;  18 
2.  Clarifying that the Class B crimes of aggravated furnishing of scheduled drugs and 19 
aggravated trafficking or furnishing of counterfeit drugs are for the death of  another 20 
person, whose death was caused by drugs furnished by the defendant; and  21 
3.  Requiring the Department of Health and Human Services to provide an annual 22 
report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over health 23 
and hu man services matters regarding the Controlled Substances Prescription Monitoring 24 
Program, including the number of prescribers participating and trends in prescription 25 
practices.  26
