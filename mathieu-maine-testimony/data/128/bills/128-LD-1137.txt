SUMMARY  32 
This bill expands crossbow hunting for deer into the regular archery -only season on 33 
deer.  It removes the requirement that a person must have a valid hunting or archery 34 
hunting license or an apprenticeship hunter license or archery hunting license in order to 35 
hunt with a crossbow.  It also lowers from 70 years of age to 65 years of age the age at 36 
which a person may hunt a wild bird or wild anima l with a crossbow during any open 37 
season on that wild bird or wild animal, hunt in an expanded archery zone and hunt 38 
during muzzle -loading -only deer hunting season.  39
