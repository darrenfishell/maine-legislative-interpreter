SUMMARY  26 
This bill eliminates the requirement that a person obtain a permit to hunt wild 27 
turkeys.  This bill increases the number of wild turkeys a person may take during the 28 
spring open season for hunting wild turkeys from  2 wild turkeys of either sex to 3 male 29 
wild turkeys.  This bill increases the number of wild turkeys a person may take during the 30 
fall open seasons for hunting wild turkeys from 2 to 3 of either sex over both seasons.  31 
This bill allows a person to registe r wild turkeys electronically or by telephone.  32
