 Sec. 1.  County jail substance use rehabilitation pilot project.  Resolved:  1 
That the Department of Corrections shall by January 1, 2018 establish a 4 -month pilot 2 
project to assist in the rehabilitation of individuals incarcerated at county jails who are 3 
experiencing substance use issues or addiction, especially issues or addiction related to 4 
opioid use.  The pilot project must focus on the health of the individual and providing 5 
counseling and community service, employment and educational opportunities to th e 6 
individual.  The pilot project must be designed to include the involvement of a 7 
community -based program to ensure successful reintegration into the community and a 8 
significant reduction in recidivism; and be it further  9
