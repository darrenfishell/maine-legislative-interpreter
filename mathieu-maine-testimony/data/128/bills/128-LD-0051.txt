SUMMARY  36 
This bill adds requirements to an agreement for withdrawal of a single municipality 37 
from a reg ional school unit.  The additional requirements are a plan for providing child 38 
nutrition services and an anticipated budget for the first year of operation of schools 39 
operated by the petitioning municipality.  The bill requires the directors of a regional 40 
school unit board to respond to a proposed agreement of a withdrawing municipality 41
