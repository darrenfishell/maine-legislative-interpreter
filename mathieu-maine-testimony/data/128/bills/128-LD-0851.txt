 SUMMARY  1 
Part A of this bill repeals the 3% surcharge imposed on taxable individual income 2 
over $200,000 for tax years beginning in or after 2017 and the Fund to Advance Public 3 
Kindergarten to Grade 12 Education, both as enacted in Initiated Bill  2015, chapter 4.  4 
The repeal is retroactive to the effective date of the initiated bill.  5 
Part B of this bill enacts a 3% surcharge on taxable individual income over $200,000 6 
for tax years beginning in or after 2019 and it enacts the Fund to Advance Public  7 
Kindergarten to Grade 12 Education, but only if approved by the voters at referendum in 8 
November 2018.  9
