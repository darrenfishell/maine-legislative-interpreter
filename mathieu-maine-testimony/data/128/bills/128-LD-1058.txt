SUMMARY  12 
This bill removes the requirement that before a city election the clerk publish a 13 
composite specimen ballot containing the names of all the nominees in a newspaper 14 
having general circulation in the city.  It also removes the requirement that the clerk post 15 
multiple copies of the instruction poster and specimen ballot in the voting room outside 16 
the guardrail enclosure and instead specifies that at least one of each of t hese documents 17 
be posted.  18
