SUMMARY  29 
This bill makes t he following changes to the fish and wildlife licensing laws.  30 
1.  It eliminates the $50 examination fee for a taxidermy license.  31 
2.  It clarifies the length of the period of time during which a person who has 32 
obtained a moose hunting permit is ineligible t o obtain another permit.  33 
3.  It authorizes the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to issue a 34 
complimentary group fishing license to a person or entity that allows a group of persons 35 
with disabilities to fish in a location for a period of time as  specified on the license.  36
