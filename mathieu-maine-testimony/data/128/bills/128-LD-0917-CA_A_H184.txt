COMMITTEE AMENDMENT"(\, to H.P. 645, L.D. 917, "Resolve, To Require a 
Review of the State Employee and Teacher Retirement Plan" 
Amend the resolve by inserting before section 1 the following: 
'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and 
Whereas, this resolve establishes a working group to evaluate and design retirement 
plan options for all state employees and teachers; and 
Whereas, the working group must be convened before the 90-day period expires in 
order that the evaluation may be completed and a rep01i submitted in time for submission 
to the next legislative session; and 
Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
immediately necessaty for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
therefore, be it' 
Amend the resolve in section 1 in subsection 2 in paragraph E in the last line (page 1, 
line 22 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "; and" and inserting the following: ';' 
Amend the resolve in section 1 in subsection 2 in paragraph F in the last line (page 1, 
line 24 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "Association." and insetting the following: 
'Association; and' 
Amend the resolve in section 1 in subsection 2 by insetting after paragraph F the 
following: 
'G. One member appointed by the chair of the working group from nominations 
submitted by the Maine Association of Retirees, who serves as a nonvoting member 
of the working group.'
