§4730.  Capstone program  17 
A school administrative unit operating a secondary school shall establish a capstone 18 
program.  The capstone program must require each student to complete a capstone 19 
project.  The capstone project must be developed by a student  and must require the 20 
student to identify a problem, develop a solution to that problem and share that solution 21 
with the community.  The capstone program must require a student to communicate 22 
effectively orally and in writing, think critically, access info rmation from a variety of 23 
sources and express ideas creatively.  24 
SUMMARY  25 
This bill requires all high school students to complete a capstone project in which the 26 
student must identify a problem, develop a solution to that problem and share that 27 
solution with  the community.  28
