SUMMARY  23 
This bill creates an exemption to federal provisions regarding the observation of 24 
so-called Eastern Daylight Saving Time in the State and requires the Secretary of State to 25 
request that the United States Secretary of Transportation place the State in the Atlantic 26 
Standard Time Zone.  27
