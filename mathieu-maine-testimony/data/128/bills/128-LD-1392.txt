SUMMARY  7 
Under current law, all municipalit ies that have more than 4,000 residents must 8 
enforce the Maine Uniform Building and Energy Code.  9 
This bill requires a municipality that has more than 4,000 residents to adopt and 10 
enforce either the Maine Uniform Building Code, the Maine Uniform Energy Code  or the 11 
Maine Uniform Building and Energy Code.  12
