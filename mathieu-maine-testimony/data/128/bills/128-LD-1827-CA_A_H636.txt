SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies that the settlor of a trust has authority to waive one or more 
of the trustee's duties that are listed in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 18-B, section 
813, subsections 1, 2 and 3 with regard to giving notice, information and reports to 
qualified beneficiaries.
