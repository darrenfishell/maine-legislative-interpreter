28 SUMMARY
29 This bill, which is reported  out by the Joint  Standing Committee on Environment  and 
30 Natural Resources pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 3, section 955, 
31 subsection 4, implements the recommendations resulting  from the committee's State 
32 Government Evaluation Act  review of the Board of Environmental Protection  as follows:
33 1.  It clarifies that a member  of the board continues to serve until that member has 
34 been reappointed  or a successor has been appointed; and
35 2.  It clarifies  that the participation of the Public Utilities Commission  in appeals  to 
36 the board of license or permit decisions relating  to expedited wind energy development, 
37 offshore wind energy demonstration projects or tidal energy demonstration projects  is 
38 optional and at  the discretion of the commission's chair.
