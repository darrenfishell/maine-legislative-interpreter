SUMMARY  18 
This bill requires the Technical Building Codes and Standards Board to ensure that 19 
tiny house construct ion is permissible under the Maine Uniform Building and Energy 20 
Code. The bill also requires that the Technical Building Codes and Standards Board adopt 21 
standards for tiny house construction consistent with the Tiny House Appendix to the 22 
International Resid ential Code, as approved by the International Code Council in 23 
December 2016.  24
