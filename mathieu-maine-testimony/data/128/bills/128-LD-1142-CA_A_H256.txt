12 SUMMARY 
13 This amendment is the majority report of the committee. The amendment makes the 
14 following changes to the bill. 
15 1. It establishes the Board of Trustees of the Cross Insurance Arena in statute. 
16 2. It clarifies that current members of the Board of Trustees of the Cumberland 
17 County Recreation Center serve as initial members of the Board of Trustees of the Cross 
18 Insurance Arena for the balance of their terms. 
19 3. It specifies that the budget for the Cross Insurance Arena is developed by the 
20 board of trustees and presented by the board to the Cumberland County commissioners. 
21 It clarifies that budget approval and adoption is the responsibility of the county 
22 commissioners and that the county commissioners may increase or decrease the overall 
23 budget presented by the trustees but may not otherwise alter or amend the budget.
