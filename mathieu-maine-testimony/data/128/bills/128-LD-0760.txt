1.  Hand washing facilities.  A person who makes livestock or any other animal, 4

SUMMARY  26 
This bill requires a person who makes livestock or any other animal, including fowl, 27 
available for touching by the general public to provide suitable hand washing f acilities or 28 
disinfectant for members of the public to sanitize their hands.  The bill also requires that 29 
the person post signs at certain places informing the public of the necessity of washing or 30 
sanitizing hands after touching animals and of the locatio n of the hand washing facilities 31 
or disinfectant. This bill does not apply if the animal that is touched is a cat, dog or other 32 
animal commonly used as a domestic pet or if the touching of livestock or other animal is 33 
in a private setting by a guest or soc ial invitee of or someone who is familiar with the 34 
person who allows the touching of livestock or another animal.  35
