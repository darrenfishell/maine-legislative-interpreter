Sec. 3.  12 MRSA §10267 is enacted to read:  25 
§10267.  Maine Outdoor Programs and Activities Fund  26 
1.  Definitions.   As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 27 
following terms have the following meanings.  28 
A.  "Board" means the Maine Outdoor Programs and Activities Fund Board 29 
establishe d in Title 5, section 12004 -G, subsection 20 -C. 30 
B.  "Fund" means the Maine Outdoor Programs and Activities Fund.  31 
2.  Fund established.   There is established the Maine Outdoor Programs and 32 
Activities Fund.  The fund consists of revenues received pursuant to  section 12501, 33 
subsection 8 and any funds received as contributions from private and public sources.  34 
The fund, to be accounted within the department, must be held separate and apart from all 35 
other money, funds and accounts.  Eligible investment earnings credited to the assets of 36 
the fund become part of the assets of the fund.  Any unexpended balances remaining in 37 
the fund at the end of any fiscal year do not lapse and must be carried forward to the next 38 
fiscal year.  39

SUMMARY  7 
This bill creates the Maine Outdoor Programs and Activities Fund, administered by 8 
the Maine Outdoor Programs and Activities Fund Board, to carry out the mission of the 9 
Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife.  Th e fund provides on a regional basis 10 
funding and grants to departmental and other outdoor programs and activities including 11 
funding for snowmobile and all -terrain vehicle trail improvements, fish and game 12 
organizations and youth outdoor activity programs.  The Maine Outdoor Programs and 13 
Activities Fund is funded by adding $1 to a resident fishing license fee and $2 to a 14 
nonresident season fishing license fee for persons 16 years of age or older.  15
