SUMMARY  16 
This bill allows detectives in the office of investigation s within the Department of the 17 
Secretary of State, Bureau of Motor Vehicles to elect to participate in the 1998 Special 18 
Plan of the Maine Public Employees Retirement System.  Under that plan, a person may 19 
retire at 55 years of age with 10 years of creditab le service, or may retire before 55 years 20 
of age with 25 years of creditable service and at a reduced benefit.  21
