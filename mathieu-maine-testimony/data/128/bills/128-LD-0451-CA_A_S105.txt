3 SUMMARY 
4 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, requires the 
5 Department of Health and Human Services to submit a waiver request no later than 
6 January 1, 2018 to the United States Depatiment of Health and Human Services, Centers 
7 for Medicare and Medicaid Services to implement the provisions of the Maine Revised 
8 Statutes, Title 22, section 3174-ZZ. The depatiment is required to take all reasonable and 
9 necessaiy steps to seek approval of the waiver. Upon approval of the waiver, the 
10 department is directed to adopt rules no later than 180 days after receiving approval. The 
11 depa1iment is required to repoti its progress in seeking a waiver and implementing rules 
12 on a quarterly basis beginning October 1, 2017 until the process is complete. The 
13 amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section.
