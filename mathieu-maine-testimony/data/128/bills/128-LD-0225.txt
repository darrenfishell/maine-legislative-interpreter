SUMMARY  17 
This bill pro vides an additional penalty for operating under the influence of 18 
intoxicants or with an excessive alcohol level.  The bill allows the court, when sentencing 19 
a person for criminal operating under the influence, to impose an order prohibiting the 20 
person from  purchasing alcoholic beverages for the time period of the suspension.  The 21 
bill requires the Secretary of State, upon receiving an attested copy of the court order, to 22 
record the order and notify the person.  The bill directs the Secretary of State to ado pt 23 
rules regarding notice to the person and notation on the person's driver's license of the 24 
prohibition and designates the rules as routine technical rules.  25
