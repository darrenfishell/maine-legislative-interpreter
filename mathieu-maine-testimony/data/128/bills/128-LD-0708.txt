SUMMARY  12 
This bill increases the sales tax on nonmedical marijuana from 10% to 15% and the 13 
tax on cigarettes fro m $2.00 per pack of 20 cigarettes to $2.50 per pack.  The bill 14 
provides that revenue from these increases is deposited in the newly created Surcharge 15 
Relief Fund to be used to reduce the rate of the 3% surcharge on taxable income 16 
exceeding $200,000.  The b ill contains an effective date of October 1, 2017.  17
