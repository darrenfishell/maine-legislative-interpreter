This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
To protect waterbodies from nonpoint source pollution, this bill proposes to amend 5 
the laws governing shoreland zon ing to increase the vegetative buffer requirement from 6 
75 feet to 150 feet for certain areas in the shoreland zone.  The bill also proposes to 7 
remove provisions in law that grandfather certain parcels of land and that allow smaller 8 
vegetative buffers on th ose parcels.  9
