 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  12 MRSA §11205, sub -§1, as affected by PL 2003, c. 614, §9 and 2 
amended by c. 655, Pt. B, §137 and affected by §422, is further amended to read:  3 
1.  Prohibition.   A person may not:  4 
A.  Hunt wild animals or wild birds  on Sunday; or 5 
A-1.  Hunt wild birds on Sunday with any firearm other than a shotgun; or  6 
B.  Possess any wild animal or wild bird taken in violation of paragraph A or A -1 7 
except as otherwise provided in this Part.  8 
SUMMARY  9 
This bill allows licensed hunters to hunt wild birds on Sundays with a shotgun.  10
