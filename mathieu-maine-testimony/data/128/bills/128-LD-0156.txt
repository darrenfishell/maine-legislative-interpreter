SUMMARY  18 
This bill proposes to adopt the interstate compact th at is the agreement among the 19 
states to elect the President of the United States by national popular vote.  Under the 20 
compact and the bill, the presidential candidate who receives the most popular votes in all 21 
50 states and the District of Columbia will wi n the presidency.  Under this bill, all of the 22 
State's electoral votes would be awarded to the presidential candidate who receives the 23 
most popular votes in all 50 states and the District of Columbia.  This bill would take 24 
effect only when enacted by state s possessing a majority of the electoral votes, that is, 25 
enough electoral votes to elect a President, which is 270 of 538.  26
