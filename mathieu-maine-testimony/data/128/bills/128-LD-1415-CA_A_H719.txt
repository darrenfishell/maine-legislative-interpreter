Education, Mental Health Treatment and Substance Abuse Treatment Programs" 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
summary and insetting the following: 
'Sec. 1. 17-A MRSA §1253-A is enacted to read:

2. The earned deductions authorized under this section for educational achievements 
and completions of treatment are as follows: 
A. For achievement by an eligible person of a bachelor's degree from an accredited 
institution of higher education, a deduction of 216 days from the person's sentence; 
B. For achievement by an eligible person of an associate degree from an accredited 
institution of higher education, a deduction of 108 days from the person's sentence; 
C. For achievement by an eligible person of an adult high school diploma, a 
deduction of 3 6 days from the person's sentence; 
D. For completion by an eligible person of a general high school eguivalency 
diploma or test authorized and approved by the Department of Corrections, a 
deduction of36 days from the person's sentence; 
E. For completion by an eligible person of a vocational, career or technical training 
program authorized and approved by the Department of Corrections, a deduction of 
72 days from the person's sentence; 
F. For completion by an eligible person of a course of mental health programming 
and treatment recommended or approved by the Department of Corrections and 
specifically recommended for the person by clinical treatment staff of the department, 
a deduction of 18 days from the person's sentence; 
G. For completion by an eligible person of a course of substance abuse programming

specifically recommended for the person by clinical treatment staff of the department,

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Savings to the Department of Corrections as a result of reduced sentences from the completion of education 
achievement and treatment programs could be significant over time. The amount and timing of these savings in any 
given fiscal year will depend on participation in the programs and cannot be determined at this time.
