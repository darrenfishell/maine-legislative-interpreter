SUMMARY 
This amendment authorizes the issuance of $1,000,000 in General Fund bonds for 
improvements to, and remediation of, the Maine Maritime Academy1s waterfront pier in 
Castine and $5,000,000 in General Fund bonds for a competitive grant program that 
matches local funding for the upgrade of municipal culverts at stream crossings.
