 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  22 MRSA §4315, first ¶, as amended by PL 2015, c. 312, §1, is further 2 
amended to read:  3 
Whoever knowingly and willfully makes any false representation of a material fact to 4 
the overseer of any municipality or to the department or its agents for the purpose of 5 
causing that or any other person to be granted assistance by the municipality or by the 6 
State is guilty of a Class E crime and shall reimburse the municipality for that assistance.  7 
Further assistance may be denied until that person reimburses the municipality for the 8 
assistance or enters into a written agreement, which must be reasonable under the 9 
circumstances, to reimburse the municipality or that person has been ineligible for 10 
assistance for a period of 120 days  24 months , whichever period is longer.  11 
Sec. 2.  22 MRSA §4315, 3rd ¶, as amended by PL 2015, c. 312, §2, is further 12 
amended to read:  13 
If the overseer of any municipality or the fair hearing officer finds that a recipient 14 
made a false representation to the overseer in violation of this section, that recipient is 15 
required to reimburse the municipality for any assistance rendered for which that 16 
recipient was ineligible.  The recipient is ineligible from receiving further assista nce for a 17 
period of 120 days  24 months  or until that person reimburses the municipality for the 18 
assistance or enters into a written agreement, which must be reasonable under the 19 
circumstances, to reimburse that municipality, whichever period is longer.  20 
SUM MARY  21 
This bill increases the ineligibility penalty for falsifying a general assistance 22 
application from 120 days to 24 months.  23
