 SUMMARY  1 
This bill amends the law regarding the number of moose permits made avail able 2 
through a chance drawing to hunting outfitters, which is currently calculated as 10% of 3 
the number of permits made available in the public chance drawing above 3,140.  The bill 4 
sets the number of moose permits made available to hunting outfitters at 1 0% of the 5 
number of permits in excess of the average number of permits made available in the 6 
public chance drawing per year since 1982 or 100, whichever is greater.  7
