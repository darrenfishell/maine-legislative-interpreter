SUMMARY  32 
Current law allows former Legislators and certain former executi ve-level state 33 
employees to engage in compensated lobbying for up to 8 hours per calendar month for 34 
the first year after leaving office or employment.  This bill prohibits all compensated 35 
lobbying by such persons during that period of time.  36
