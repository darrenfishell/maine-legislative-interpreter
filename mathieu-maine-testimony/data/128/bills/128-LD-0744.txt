SUMMARY  31 
This bill provides a permanent seat on the  Maine Criminal Justice Academy Board 32 
of Trustees for a representative of the 5 Wabanaki tribal governments.  The tribal 33 
representative must be chosen for a 2-year term by a process determined by the tribal 34 
governments.  The process must require that the position rotate among the 5 tribal 35 
governments.  36
