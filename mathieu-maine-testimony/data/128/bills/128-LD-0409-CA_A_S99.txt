SUMMARY 
This amendment makes the following changes to the bill. 
1. It further clarifies the purposes for which the assets of the group life insurance 
program may not be used. 
2. It changes the board meeting requirements. 
3. It updates references to the Governmental Accounting Standards Board 
Statements that apply to postemployment benefits. 
4. It removes the provisions in the bill that make changes to how disability 
retirement benefits are calculated for part-time or seasonal employees.
