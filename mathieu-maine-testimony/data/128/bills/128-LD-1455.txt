SUMMARY  10 
This bill increases the fee for an elver fishing license by $100.  The revenue from the 11 
increase is deposited in the Eel and Elver Management  Fund.  The bill also reinstates a 12 
lottery for elver licenses based on the number of pieces of gear that are retired in the prior 13 
calendar year.  It allows the Department of Marine Resources to charge an annual fee of 14 
up to $25 for the elver transaction ca rd used by harvesters for electronic reporting.  It also 15 
authorizes the department to sell at auction a portion of the individual quota allocations 16 
that are associated with retired licenses to existing license holders, with the revenues of 17 
the sale to be d eposited in the Eel and Elver Management Fund.  18
