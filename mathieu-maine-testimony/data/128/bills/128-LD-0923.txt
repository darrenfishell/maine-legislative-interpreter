SUMMARY  12 
This bill provides that the Secretary  of State may issue a driver's license or nondriver 13 
identification card that meets the standards of the Western Hemisphere Travel Initiative 14 
developed by the United States Department of State and the United States Department of 15 
Homeland Security.  The bill  also establishes fees for these driver's licenses and 16 
nondriver identification cards.  17
