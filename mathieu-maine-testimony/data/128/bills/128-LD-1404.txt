SUMMARY  26 
This bill requires annual review of for -profit colleges by the State Board of Education 27 
to ensure that they are meeting adequate educational sta ndards.  If the board finds that a 28 
for-profit college is not meeting the standards, the board may terminate the 29 
degree -granting authority of a for -profit college.  30
