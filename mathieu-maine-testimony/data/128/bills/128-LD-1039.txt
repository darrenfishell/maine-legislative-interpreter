SUMMARY  34 
This initiated bill requires the State to provide federally approved Medicaid services 35 
through MaineCare to qualifying persons under 65 years of age with income equal to or 36 
below 133% p lus 5% of the nonfarm income official poverty line.  37 
The initiated bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to prepare 38 
and submit to the Federal Government any state plan amendments, no later than 90 days 39 
after the effective date of the ini tiated bill, necessary to implement the provisions of the 40 
initiated bill.  The initiated bill requires monthly reporting by the department to the 41 
appropriate joint standing committees of the Legislature on the status of a state plan 42 
amendment submission un til such an amendment is approved and reporting on the status 43
