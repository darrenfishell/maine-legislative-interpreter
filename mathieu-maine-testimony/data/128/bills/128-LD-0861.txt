SUMMARY  10 
This bill provides for a 15% upward adjustment of salary schedules in fiscal year 11 
2017 -18 for certain law enforcement positions in the Department of Cor rections; the 12 
Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry; Baxter State Park Authority; the 13 
Department of Defense, Veterans and Emergency Management, Maine Military 14 
Authority; the Office of the Attorney General; and the Office of the Secretary of State, 15 
Bureau of Motor Vehicles.  The bill requires that, beginning in fiscal year 2018 -19, 16 
revenue from a sales tax imposed on the retail sale of recreational marijuana is to be used 17 
to offset the cost of the salary increase for Adult Probation Officer an d Juvenile 18 
Community Corrections Officer positions in the Department of Corrections.  19
