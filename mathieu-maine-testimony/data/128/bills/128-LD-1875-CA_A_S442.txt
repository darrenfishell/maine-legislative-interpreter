25 SUMMARY 
26 This amendment makes the following technical changes to the bill. 
27 1. It adds a reference to a contract to clarify the application of the exception in the 
28 bill to any long-term benefits or health benefits provided through an annuity contract. 
29 2. It clarifies that the exception in current law for obligations that do not arise under 
30 the express terms of the policy or contract applies to misrepresentation of annuity 
31 contracts. 
32 3. It clarifies the specific provision that provides the guaranty association limits for 
33 long-term care insurance.
