SUMMARY  11 
This bill amends the law regarding em ployment practices by increasing the fine for a 12 
violation of certain state wage and benefits laws to $500 for the first violation and $2,500 13 
for each subsequent violation and by providing a private right of action for a person 14 
aggrieved of such a violation . 15
