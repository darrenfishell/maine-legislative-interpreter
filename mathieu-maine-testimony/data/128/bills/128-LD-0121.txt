SUMMARY  31 
This bill requires that a vote r provide proof of identity with photographic 32 
identification for the purpose of voting.  The bill specifies the types of photographic 33 
identification that may be used to verify the identity of a voter.  It provides that a person 34 
who does not present photogr aphic identification may cast a provisional ballot and 35 
establishes the process for provisional voting.  Under this process, if the person can verify 36 
the person's identity to the municipal clerk, deputy clerk or warden or an election clerk 37 
within 5 business  days of the election by presenting acceptable photographic 38 
identification, the ballot will be cast as a regular ballot. Through the general election of 39 
2018, a person who does not present acceptable photographic identification but is known 40 
to a municipal clerk, registrar or election official at the voting place may cast a regular 41
