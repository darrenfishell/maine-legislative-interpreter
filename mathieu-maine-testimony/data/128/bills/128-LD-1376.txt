SUMMARY  23 
This bill directs the Department of Health  and Human Services to increase the rates 24 
of reimbursement under the MaineCare Benefits Manual, rule Chapter 101:  Chapters II 25 
and III, Sections 13, 17, 23, 28, 65 and 97 by 20% over the rates in June 2016 by October 26 
1, 2017.  27 
The bill also makes the follow ing changes to the laws and rules regulating alcohol 28 
and drug counselors.  29 
1.  It requires reimbursement under the MaineCare program for the services of a 30 
licensed clinical professional counselor at the same rate as a licensed clinical social 31 
worker.  32 
2.  It allows a person to qualify as a licensed alcohol and drug counselor if that person 33 
holds a certified alcohol and drug counseling certificate and completes 2,000 hours of 34 
practice in alcohol and drug counseling.  35
