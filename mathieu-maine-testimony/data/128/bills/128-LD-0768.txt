SUMMARY  5 
This bill establishes resident and nonresident comprehensive hunting licenses that 6 
allow hunting of all legal species sub ject to the moose, pheasant, migratory waterfowl, 7 
special season deer and antlerless deer permit requirements.  The fee for the resident 8 
comprehensive hunting license is $38 and the fee for the nonresident comprehensive 9 
hunting license is $143.  The bill a lso establishes resident and nonresident comprehensive 10 
combination hunting and fishing licenses that allow fishing and hunting of all legal 11 
species, subject to the moose, pheasant, migratory waterfowl, special season deer and 12 
antlerless deer permit require ments.  The fee for the resident comprehensive combination 13 
hunting and fishing license is $55 and the fee for the nonresident comprehensive 14 
combination hunting and fishing license is $178.  The bill establishes January 1, 2018 as 15 
the effective date of thes e licenses.  16 
  This bill eliminates all alien licenses and permits and allows persons who are not 17 
citizens to purchase nonresident licenses and permits if they are not considered residents.  18 
This bill allows the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife or a clerk or other 19 
agent appointed by the commissioner who issues licenses to inquire of a license applicant 20 
which species the applicant intends to hunt under the license and limit the license to the 21 
species specified. A license with this limitation must clearly indicate the limitation.  22
