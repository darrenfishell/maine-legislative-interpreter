17 SUMMARY
18 Current law allows an eligible organization  that has registered with the Gambling 
19 Control Unit within the Department of Public Safety to conduct  one raffle in a 12-month  
20 period awarding noncash prizes, up to $75,000 in value.  This bill allows that raffle to 
21 include total cash prizes not exceeding $20,000 with no more than one $10,000 prize  for 
22 the holder  of a winning chance.
