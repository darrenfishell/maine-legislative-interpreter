 SUMMARY  1 
This bill changes the deadline for requestin g a recount in a municipal, local school or 2 
local referendum election to 10 business days after the election.  It also provides that in a 3 
municipality that counts all ballots by hand, a recount is automatically triggered in a 4 
municipal, local school or loc al referendum election if the total vote difference is 10 votes 5 
or fewer.  6
