Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill establishes a 6-day nonresident archery license with a fee of $26. The revenue impact to the General Fund is 
dependent upon the extent that nonresident hunters choose the $26 6-day nonresident archery license instead of the 
$75 annual nonresident arche1y license.
