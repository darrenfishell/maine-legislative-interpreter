COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "ff" to H.P. 866, L.D. 1243, Bill, "An Act 
Regarding the Maine Length of Service A ward Program Board of Trustees" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in paragraph C in subparagraph (4) in the 5th line (page 1, 
line 16 in L.D.) by striking out the following: 11chair of the Maine Fire Protection Services 
Commission" and inserting the following: 'Board of Trustees of the Maine Public 
Employees Retirement System' 
Amend the bill in section 2 in the 6th line (page 1, line 23 in L.D.) by striking out the 
following: 11chair of the Maine Fire Protection Services Commission" and inserting the 
following: 'Board of Trustees of the Maine Public Employees Retirement System' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment changes the bill by designating the Board of Trustees of the Maine 
Public Employees Retirement System as the alternate appointing authority when the 
Governor does not make appointments to the Maine Length of Service Award Program 
Board of Trustees.
