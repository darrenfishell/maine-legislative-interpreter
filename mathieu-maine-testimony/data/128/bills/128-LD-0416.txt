forth in this Part.  Any unencumbered balances remaining at th e completion of the project 21

 Sec. A -6.  Contingent upon ratification of bond issue.  Sections 1 to 5 do not 1

forth in this Part.  3 
Sec. A -7.  Appropriation balances at year -end.  At the end of each fiscal year, 4

Sec. A -8.  Bonds authorized but not issued.  Any bonds authorized but not 9

Sec. A -9.  Referendum for ratification; submission at election; form of 14 
question; effective date.  This Part must be submitted to the legal voters of the State at 15 
a statewide election held in the month of November following passage of this Act.  The 16 
municipal offic ers of this State shall notify the inhabitants of their respective cities, towns 17 
and plantations to meet, in the manner prescribed by law for holding a statewide election, 18 
to vote on the acceptance or rejection of this Part by voting on the following quest ion: 19 
"Do you favor a $5,000,000 bond issue for the purchase of federal 20 
northeast multispecies groundfish permits to enable Maine fishing 21 
vessels to land their catch in Maine?"  22 
The legal voters of each city, town and plantation shall vote by ballot on this question 23 
and designate their choice by a cross or check mark placed within a corresponding square 24 
below the word "Yes" or "No."  The ballots must be received, sorted, counted and 25 
declared in open ward, town and plantation meetings and returns made to the S ecretary of 26 
State in the same manner as votes for members of the Legislature.  The Governor shall 27 
review the returns.  If a majority of the legal votes are cast in favor of this Part, the 28 
Governor shall proclaim the result without delay and this Part becom es effective 30 days 29 
after the date of the proclamation.  30 
The Secretary of State shall prepare and furnish to each city, town and plantation all 31 
ballots, returns and copies of this Part necessary to carry out the purposes of this 32 
referendum.  33
