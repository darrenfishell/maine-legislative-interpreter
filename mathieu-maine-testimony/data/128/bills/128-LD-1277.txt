SUMMARY  16 
This bill requires that, beginning April 1, 2018, an applicant for a new commercial 17 
driver's license complete in person or online a nationally recognized training program on 18 
preventing human trafficking in  the trucking industry as a requirement to receive a 19 
license. It also requires that all new and renewed commercial driver's licenses be 20 
accompanied by a wallet -sized card that outlines signs of and how to report human 21 
trafficking and includes a telephone n umber for a national human trafficking hotline.  22
