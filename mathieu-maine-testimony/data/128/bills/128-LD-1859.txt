13 SUMMARY
14 Current law provides that a person commits the crime  of operating after habitual 
15 offender revocation if that person operates a motor  vehicle on a public way when that 
16 person's license  to operate  a motor  vehicle has been revoked under certain circumstances.  
17 This bill provides that a person also commits the crime of operating after habitual  
18 offender revocation if that person operates a motor vehicle  in a parking  area when that 
19 person's license  to operate  a motor vehicle has  been revoked under certain circumstances.
