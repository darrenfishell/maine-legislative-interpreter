 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 1

Whereas, Private and Special Law 2015, chapter 14 revised the charter of the 3 
Rumford Water District  and allows the district to contract with any person, district, 4

Whereas, this legislation amends the charter of the district to require that all 6

SUMMARY  27 
This bill requires that all proposed commercial contracts of the Rumford Water 28 
District for water extraction or water resale be subject to a vote b y the voters of the 29 
district.  For the vote to be valid, the total number of votes cast must be at least 60% of 30 
the number of votes cast for Governor by the voters of the district at the last gubernatorial 31 
election.  32
