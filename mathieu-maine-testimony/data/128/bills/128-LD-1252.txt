SUMMARY  5 
This bill allows the operation of an  all-terrain vehicle, or ATV, on a public way as 6 
long as the ATV is registered and meets specified safety equipment requirements and the 7 
operator is licensed and has insurance.  This bill establishes an annual registration fee of 8 
$33, the same as for ATVs under current law that are not permitted on a public way.  9 
This bill prohibits the operation of an ATV on a public way with a posted speed limit 10 
of more than 50 miles per hour.  The Department of Transportation may adopt rules or a 11 
municipality or county ma y adopt an ordinance to restrict or prohibit the operation of an 12 
ATV on a way if the department, county or municipality determines that the restriction or 13 
prohibition is necessary in the interest of public safety.  14
