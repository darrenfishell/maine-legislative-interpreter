9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A "to S.P. 22, L.D. 42, Bill, "An Act To Deter the 
10 Dealing of Dangerous Drugs" 
11 Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 1 by striking out all of paragraph D (page 1, 
12 lines 27 to 33 in L.D.) and inserting the following: 
13 'D. Trafficks in a drug with or furnishes a drug to another person in violation of 
14 chapter 45 and death is in fact caused by the use of the drug trafficked or furnished. 
15 Violation of this paragraph is a Class A crime.' 
16 SUMMARY 
17 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, clarifies the new 
18 Class A crime of illegal trafficking in a drug with or furnishing of a drug to another 
19 person when death is in fact caused by the use of the drug trafficked or furnished. It 
20 removes language stating that it is not a defense to prosecution of the new crime that the 
21 decedent contributed to the decedent's death by the decedent's use of or consent to the 
22 administration of a drug.
