SUMMARY  11 
This bill amends the statutory provisions regarding criminal post -conviction review 12 
to allo w the filing of a petition for post -conviction review claiming actual innocence at 13 
any time during the period of direct impediment, except that it may not be filed within 14 
one year of a judgment on a prior petition for post -conviction review on the same 15 
conviction.  This bill requires that a petition for post -conviction review claiming actual 16 
innocence receive at least one evidentiary hearing in which the petitioner may submit 17 
new evidence and evidence submitted in prior proceedings on the same matter.  18
