SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment establishes that the assessment to 
municipalities for the provision of correctional services in a county budget may not be 
less than the base assessment limit established by law. The amendment also specifies that 
if a county collects more in a year than the base amount established for that year, the base 
amount for the succeeding year is the amount collected in the prior year.
