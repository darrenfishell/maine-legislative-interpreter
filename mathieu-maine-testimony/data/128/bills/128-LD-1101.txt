SUMMARY  12 
This bill directs the Secretary of State to review and recommend updates to the 13 
motorcycle driver education provisions based up on changes of law and current trends and 14 
conditions and to report its findings to the Joint Standing Committee on Criminal Justice 15 
and Public Safety, which may report out a bill based upon the report to the Second 16
