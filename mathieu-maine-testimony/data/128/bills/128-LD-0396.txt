 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  8 MRSA §471, sub -§5-A is enacted to read:  2 
5-A.  Mobile or traveling housing facility.   "Mobile or traveling housing facility" 3 
means a transporting vehicle such as a truck, trailer or ra ilway car used to transport or 4 
house animals while traveling for exhibition or other performance.  5 
Sec. 2.  8 MRSA §471, sub -§7-A is enacted to read:  6 
7-A.  Performance.   "Performance" means any exhibition, public showing, 7 
presentation, display, exposition, fair, animal act, amusement show, circus, ride, trade 8 
show, petting zoo, carnival, parade, race or similar undertaking in which animals are 9 
required to perform tricks, give rides or participate as accompaniments for the 10 
entertainment, amusement or benefit of a live audience.  11 
Sec. 3.  8 MRSA §471, sub -§9 is enacted to read:  12 
9.  Traveling animal act.   "Traveling animal act" means any performance of animals 13 
when animals are transported to, from or between locations for the purpose of the 14 
performance in a mobil e or traveling housing facility.  15 
Sec. 4.  8 MRSA §477 is enacted to read:  16 
§477.  Use of elephants in traveling animal acts prohibited  17 
1.  Prohibition.   Notwithstanding any other provision of law, a person may not allow 18 
the participation of an elephant in a  traveling animal act.  19 
2.  Violation.   A person who violates this section commits a civil violation for which 20 
a fine of not less than $500 nor more than $1,000 for each violation may be adjudged.  21 
3.  Enforcement; rules.   An officer or agent authorized by t he commissioner or a law 22 
enforcement officer of the State or of any unit of local government within the State may 23 
enforce the provisions of this section.  The commissioner may adopt routine technical 24 
rules pursuant to Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2 -A relating to the implementation of 25 
this section.  26 
Sec. 5.  Effective date.   This Act takes effect January 1, 2018.  27 
SUMMARY  28 
This bill prohibits the use of elephants as performers in traveling animal acts, 29 
effective January 1, 2018.  30
