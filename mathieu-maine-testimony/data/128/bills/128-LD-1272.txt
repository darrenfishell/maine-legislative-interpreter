SUMMARY  23 
This bill moves the administration of the federal Child and Adult Care Food Program, 24 
which provides food assistance to children and adult participants in nonresident ial 25 
institutions that provide care, from the Department of Health and Human Services to the 26 
Department of Education.  It directs the Department of Education to enter into a written 27 
agreement with the United States Department of Agriculture to administer th e program.  28 
The Department of Health and Human Services is directed to work with the Department 29 
of Education to move the administration of the program.  The Department of Education is 30 
directed to report to the committee of jurisdiction during the Second Re gular Session of 31 
the 128th Legislature and the First Regular Session of the 129th Legislature and is 32 
authorized to submit suggested legislation and to adopt routine technical rules.  33
