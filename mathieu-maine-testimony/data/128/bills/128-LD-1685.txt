22 A.  Ten dollars  to The Barbara Bush Children's Hospital to support ongoing pediatric

24 B.  Nine dollars to the Highway Fund for administrative and production  costs; and
25 C.  One  dollar to the Specialty License Plate Fund established  under  section  469.

28 registration plates  is $15, which must  be deposited with the Treasurer  of State and 
29 credited as  follows:
30 A.  Ten dollars  to The Barbara Bush Children's Hospital to support ongoing pediatric 
31 programs;
32 B.  Four dollars to the Highway Fund for administrative  and production costs; and
33 C.  One  dollar to the Specialty License Plate Fund established  under  section  469.

4 6.  Transfer  of fees.  On a quarterly basis, the Secretary of State shall transfer the

8 7.  Duplicate plates prohibited.  The Secretary of State shall  issue The Barbara

12 8.  Weight limit.  The Barbara Bush Children's Hospital special registration plates 
13 may be issued for a motor vehicle that does not exceed  26,000 pounds.  A motor vehicle  
14 that exceeds 6,000 pounds is subject  to the fees  in section  504.
15 9.  Date  of first issue.  The Secretary of State shall  issue the first The Barbara Bush 
16 Children's Hospital special registration plate by October  1, 2018.
17 SUMMARY
18 This bill creates a new specialty registration plate that will generate  funds to support 
19 The Barbara Bush  Children's Hospital at Maine  Medical Center, the only full-service 
20 children's hospital in the State.
