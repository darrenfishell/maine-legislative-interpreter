SUMMARY  29 
This bill allows a taxpayer, either an individual or corporation, that operates a 30 
registered dispensary of medical marijuana pursuant to the Maine Medical Use of 31 
Marij uana Act to deduct from income the costs of operating that registered dispensary. 32 
The bill also allows a qualified patient to deduct from income the costs of medical 33 
marijuana purchased by that qualifying patient.  The bill also provides a statutory 34 
requir ement that registered primary caregivers under the Maine Medical Use of 35 
Marijuana Act register to collect sales tax.  36
