This bill includes a one-time General Fund appropriation of $2,284,245 in fiscal year 2017-18 to the Retirement 
Allowance Fund within the Maine Public Employees Retirement System for the increase in the unfunded actuarial 
liability of the State Employee and Teacher program as & result of allowing the service retirement benefits for certain 
corrections officers and capitol police officers earned under the regular state employee and teacher plan to be 
calculated under the 199 8 Special Plan. 
This legislation will also increase the employer's contribution to the normal cost component for the 1998 Special Plan 
from 5.92% to 5.97%, or .05% beginning in fiscal year 2017-18. This legislation will also increase the employer's 
contribution to the normal cost component for the 20 year/age 50 plan by 2.2% of the entire payroll of participants in 
the plan. The costs to individual State departments and programs within those departments can not be determined and 
will depend on where members affected by this legislation are currently employed. Those costs are not included in the 
appropriation shown above.
