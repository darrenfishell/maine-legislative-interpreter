14 SUMMARY 
15 Current law provides that a person who promotes prostitution of a person less than 18 
16 years old is guilty of aggravated sex trafficking, which is a Class B crime. The bill, as 
17 amended by Committee Amendment "A," increases the class of this crime to Class A and 
18 adds that the person must compel the minor to enter into, engage in or remain in 
19 prostitution to be guilty of aggravated sex trafficking. This amendment removes the 
20 added element of compulsion from the crime.
