SUMMARY  13 
Current law provides that a minor under 18 years of age may consent to treatment for 14 
abuse of alcohol or drugs or for emotional or psychological problems.  This bill provides 15 
that a minor who is 14 ye ars of age or older may consent to treatment for abuse of alcohol 16 
or drugs or for emotional or psychological problems and does not need the consent of a 17 
parent or guardian for such treatment.  It also provides that if the parent or guardian 18 
consents to suc h treatment of a minor 14 years of age or older, the minor may not 19 
abrogate that consent and that if a minor 14 years of age or older consents to such 20 
treatment, a parent or guardian may not abrogate that consent.  21
