16 SUMMARY
17 This bill authorizes  municipalities to enter into memoranda of agreement with the 
18 Secretary of State, the Department of Transportation and the Department  of Professional 
19 and Financial Regulation, Bureau of Insurance to develop, test and operate pilot programs  
20 for the use of autonomous vehicles  for public  transportation until March  31, 2022.  It 
21 requires any municipality that develops, tests or operates a pilot program to submit a 
22 report regarding  the pilot program to the joint standing committee of the Legislature 
23 having jurisdiction over transportation matters by December  1, 2021, and it authorizes the 
24 joint standing  committee  to report  out a bill to the Second  Regular  Session  of the 130th 
25 Legislature.
