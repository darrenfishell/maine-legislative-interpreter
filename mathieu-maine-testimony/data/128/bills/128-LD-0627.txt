SUMMARY  4 
This bill establishes a program in the Department of Health and Human Services, 5 
Maine Center for Disease Control and Prevention to collect data for th e purpose of 6 
monitoring the effect on the State from the legalization and regulation of retail marijuana.  7 
Reports must be published at least annually to provide data to the Legislature, the 8 
Governor's Office and appropriate state government departments ov erseeing the 9 
implementation of the retail marijuana laws to be used to inform needed adjustments, law 10 
changes and rule changes to minimize the financial and social costs to the people of this 11 
State.  12
