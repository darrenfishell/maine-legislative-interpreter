SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill. It requires the Department of Labor to issue an 
annual report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over 
labor matters concerning complaints received by the department regarding violations of 
state wage and hour laws that resulted in final action by the department.
