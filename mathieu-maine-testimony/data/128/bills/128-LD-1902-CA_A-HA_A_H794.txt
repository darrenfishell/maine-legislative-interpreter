Fiscal Note 
Eliminates Potential State Mandate -Unfunded 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Removing the requirement that all school administrative units enhance and upgrade the system of response to 
intervention for kindergarten to grade 12 to become an all-encompassing, tiered system of support for all general and 
special education students eliminates the unfunded state mandate.
