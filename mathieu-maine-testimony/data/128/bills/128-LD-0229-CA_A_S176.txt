9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" A "to S.P. 75, L.D. 229, Bill, "An Act To Prohibit 
10 an Electric Utility from Charging a Customer for Using an Electromechanical Meter" 
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
12 'Resolve, To Quantify and Ensure Actual Ratepayer Benefits from the Deployment 
13 of Advanced Metering Infrastructure' 
14 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and before the summary and 
15 inserting the following: 
16 'Sec. 1. Public Utilities Commission evaluation. Resolved: That the Public 
17 Utilities Commission shall quantify the total accrued costs and benefits to ratepayers and 
18 taxpayers of advanced metering infrastructure from the initial purchase and deployment 
19 of advanced metering infrastructure to the effective date of this resolve. The commission 
20 shall identify baniers to achieving benefits from the deployment of advanced metering 
21 technology and propose solutions to ensure that advanced metering infrastructure 
22 deployed by transmission and distribution utilities benefit ratepayers and taxpayers, 
23 reduce costs and advance the State's smart grid policy goals set out in the Maine Revised 
24 Statutes, Title 35-A, section 3143, subsection 3. The commission shall also evaluate the 
25 fee imposed on customers that decline the installation of advanced metering infrastructure 
26 and instead choose to continue to use an electromechanical meter. The commission shall 
27 consider a discount to be provided to customers that install advanced metering 
28 infrastructure rather than imposing a fee to those customers that decline the installation of 
29 advanced metering infrastruc_ture. 
30 The commission shall submit a report, including any recommendations, to the Joint 
31 Standing Committee on Energy, Utilities and Technology by January 30, 2018. The 
32 committee may report out a bill to the Second Regular Session of the 128th Legislature 
33 based on the report. 
34 For the purposes of this section, "electromechanical meter" means a meter used to 
35 measure electricity consumption that is not a wireless smart meter.'
