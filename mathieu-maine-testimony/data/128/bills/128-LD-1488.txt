Sec. 1.  36 MRSA §584 -B is enacted to read:  2 
§584 -B.  Increase in valuation  3 
If the State Tax Assessor increases the valuation of a parcel of land subject to the 4 
provisions of this subchapter, wit hin 90 days the State Tax Assessor shall send the 5 
landowner notice of the increase in valuation.  If an increase in valuation of a parcel of 6 
land under this section results in an increase in taxes under this subchapter and notice 7 
under this section is sent  within 90 days of when the increased taxes are due, the 8 
landowner shall pay by the due date an amount equal to the previous year’s taxes under 9 
this subchapter and may pay the amount of taxes equal to the increase in taxes from the 10 
prior tax year within 90  days of the date of the notice.  11 
SUMMARY  12 
This bill requires the State Tax Assessor, if the State Tax Assessor increases the 13 
valuation of a parcel of property under the Maine Tree Growth Tax Law, to notify the 14 
landowner of the increase within 90 days of the  determination.  If notice is sent within 90 15 
days of when the taxes on the parcel are due and the increase in valuation results in an 16 
increase in taxes on the parcel, the landowner has 90 days from the date of the notice to 17 
pay the amount representing the increase.  18
