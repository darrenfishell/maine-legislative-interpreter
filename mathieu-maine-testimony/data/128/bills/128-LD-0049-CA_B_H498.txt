29 SUMMARY 
30 This amendment is the minority report of the committee. The amendment strikes the 
31 provision of the bill that adds science to the content areas of English language arts, 
32 literacy and mathematics as part of the rating scale required to measure educator 
33 effectiveness as required under the performance evaluation and professional growth 
34 system. The amendment further directs the Commissioner of Education, in amending the 
35 Department of Education rules in order to include science standards as part of the State's 
36 system of learning results and assessment, to include the science standards adopted by the
