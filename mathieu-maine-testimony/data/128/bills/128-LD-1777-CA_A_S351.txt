Fiscal Note 
Current biennium cost increase -Maine Turnpike Authority 
This bill makes allocations from Maine Turnpike Authority funds of $45,291,372 for the authority's operating 
expenses for the calendar year ending December 31, 2019. Maine Turnpike Authority revenue of $147,023,810 is 
budgeted to fund those allocations. Included in the budgeted revenue but not allocated is $2,442,500 to pay off bondf 
authorized by the Legislature for specified Depatiment of Transportation projects. These bonds were issued by the 
Maine Turnpike Authority pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 23, section 1968, subsection 2-A. This bill 
also includes budgeted revenue of $40,000,000 for the Reserve Maintenance Fund, $34,475,890 for the Debt Service 
Fund and $24,814,048 for capital improvements at new interchanges. The funds that are not allocated in the bill are 
not subject to the transfer limitations in section 2 of the bill.
