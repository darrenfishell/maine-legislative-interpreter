18 SUMMARY 
19 This amendment replaces the bill and provides that if a person demonstrates that a 
20 use or structure that does not conform with Maine Land Use Planning Commission 
21 district standards has existed for at least 30 years, there is a rebuttable presumption that 
22 the use or structure is a nonconforming use or nonconforming structure, which is a use or 
23 structure that lawfully existed when district regulations were adopted but that does not 
24 currently conform.
