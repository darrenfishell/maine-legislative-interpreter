SUMMARY  10 
This bill amends the Maine Metallic Mineral Mining Act to prohibit the Department 11 
of Environmental Protection from approving an application for a mining permit if the 12 
applicant proposes to mine a metallic mineral ore deposit that contains a massive sulfide 13 
ore deposit.  The bill defines a "massive sulfide ore deposit" as a metal sulfide ore deposit 14 
that, as determined by the Maine Geological Survey,  contains in total 1,000,000 tons or 15 
more of metallic minerals.  16
