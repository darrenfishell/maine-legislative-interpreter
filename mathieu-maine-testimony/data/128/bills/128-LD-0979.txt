SUMMARY  8 
This bill allows for municipalities to conduct early voting in person during the same 9 
period as absentee voting.  Early voting takes place in the municipal offices with the same 10 
requirements and restrictions for polling places, voting booths, ballot boxes and ballot 11 
challenges as for regular voting.  At the end of early voting, the municipal clerk delivers 12 
the sealed ballot boxes to the polling place as presently required by law.  13
