SUMMARY  26 
This bill provides that the Board of Trustees of the University of Maine System, the 27 
Board of Trustees of the Maine Community College System and the Board of Trustees of 28 
the Maine Maritime Academy may adopt rules and establish po licies relating to firearms 29 
except that these rules and policies may not regulate or prohibit the otherwise lawful 30 
possession, carrying or transporting of firearms or ammunition by a person issued a 31 
permit to carry a concealed handgun under the Maine Revis ed Statutes, Title 25, chapter 32 
252 or a person who may carry a concealed handgun under Title 25, section 2001 -A, 33 
subsection 2, paragraph A -1.  The bill also provides, with certain exceptions, that a 34 
person issued a permit to carry a concealed handgun under  Title 25, chapter 252 or a 35 
person who may carry a concealed handgun under Title 25, section 2001 -A, subsection 2, 36 
paragraph A -1 may not carry a concealed weapon within a student dormitory or residence 37 
hall or within a building of a public entertainment fa cility at which signs are 38 
conspicuously posted at entrances notifying attendees of any restriction on the possession 39 
of firearms in the facility during a game or event.  40
