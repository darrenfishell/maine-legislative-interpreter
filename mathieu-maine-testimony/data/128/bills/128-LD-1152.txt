By January 1, 2018, each board, commission, office and agency within the 4 
department listed in section 8001 or affiliated  with the department under section 8001 -A 5 
shall adopt a process in accordance with this section to facilitate qualified work - 6 
authorized immigrants obtaining in an expeditious manner professional licenses granted 7 
by those boards, commissions, offices and ag encies.  For the purposes of this section, 8 
"work -authorized immigrant" means an alien who has been lawfully admitted to the 9

1.  Endorsement.  The board, commission, office or agency may permit a work - 13 
authorized immigrant who holds a comparable license in another state or country to 14 
acquire a license by endorsement i n this State for the remainder of the term of the license 15 
from the other state or country or until a license is obtained in this State, whichever 16 
occurs first.  17 
2.  Temporary license.  The board, commission, office or agency may permit a 18 
work -authorized imm igrant who holds a comparable license in another state or country to 19 
obtain a temporary license in this State for a period of time necessary to obtain a license 20 
in this State.  21 
3.  Education.  The board, commission, office or agency may not issue a license by 22 
endorsement under subsection 1 or a temporary license under subsection 2 to a work - 23 
authorized immigrant unless the work -authorized immigrant is enrolled in and 24 
participating in an education program approved by the board, commission, office or 25 
agency.  T he board, commission, office or agency may not approve an education program 26 
under this subsection unless successful completion of the education program allows the 27 
work -authorized immigrant to qualify either for licensure in this State or to take the 28 
examin ation required for licensure in this State.  29 
4.  Rules.  Each board, commission, office or agency affected by this section shall 30 
adopt rules establishing the criteria for work -authorized immigrants to obtain a license by 31 
endorsement or a temporary license u nder this section, including rules for determining 32 
whether a license issued in another country is comparable to a license issued by the 33 
board, commission, office or agency.  Rules adopted pursuant to this subsection are 34 
routine technical rules as defined i n Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2 -A. 35
