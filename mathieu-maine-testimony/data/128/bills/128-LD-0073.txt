SUMMARY  22 
This bill raises the homestead property tax exemption to $50,000 for persons who are 23 
75 years of age or older and claimed the homestead as a permanent residence for 10 or 24 
more years.  The bill requires the State to reimburse municipalities for 100% of the 25 
property taxes lost as a result of the increase in the exemption.  26
