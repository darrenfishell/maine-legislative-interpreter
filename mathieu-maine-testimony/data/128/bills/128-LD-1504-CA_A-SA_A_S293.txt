SUMMARY 
This amendment makes the following changes to Committee Amendment "A." 
1. It requires a net energy billing customer to receive 100% of the net energy of an 
eligible facility for the customer's transmission and distribution bill until December 31, 
2019 instead of December 31, 2021. 
2. It changes from 200 to 100 the limit on the number of eligible customers that may 
participate in a single shared interest in an eligible facility or the number of meters 
associated with a single shared interest, except in the service territory of a transmission 
and distribution utility located in an area administered by the independent system 
administrator for northern Maine. 
3. It changes the report date for the Public Utilities Commission to January 1, 2020 
for recommendations on how to transition from net energy billing to time-of-use rates, 
market-based rates or other rate design options.
