14 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and before the summary and 
15 inserting the following: 
16 'Sec. 1. York County Jail Drug Detoxification and Rehabilitation Pilot 
17 Program. Resolved: That the York County Jail Drug Detoxification and 
18 Rehabilitation Pilot Program, referred to in this section as "the pilot program," is 
19 established to provide state funding for the creation of drug detoxification and 
20 rehabilitation programs in therapeutic communities within the York County Jail or in 
21 facilities attached to or affiliated with the York County Jail. The pilot program must 
22 provide treatment services to inmates with substance use disorders in a facility or 
23 program that may be residential and that may also provide outpatient and 
24 community-based services. The pilot program must provide services to inmates of the 
25 York County Jail and may provide services to inmates :from other counties as agreed 
26 between the York County Sheriff and sheriffs from other counties in this State. 
27 Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, funds appropriated to the pilot 
28 program that are remaining at the end of a state fiscal year do not lapse but are carried 
29 forward into the next fiscal year. The pilot program terminates upon the expenditure of 
30 all funds appropriated to the pilot program; and be it further 
31 Sec. 2. Appropriations and allocations. Resolved: That the following 
32 appropriations and allocations are made.

11 This amendment is the majority report of the committee. The amendment changes 
12 the title and replaces the bill with a resolve that establishes the York County Jail Drug 
13 Detoxification and Rehabilitation Pilot Program to provide one-time state funding for the 
14 creation of drug detoxification and rehabilitation programs in therapeutic communities 
15 within the York County Jail or in facilities attached to or affiliated with the jail. The pilot 
16 program is required to provide services to inmates of the York County Jail and may 
17 provide services to inmates from other counties as agreed between the York County 
18 Sheriff and the sheriffs of other counties in this State. The amendment provides funding 
19 from the General Fund of $975,000 per year for state fiscal years 2017-18 and 2018-19. 
20 The funding does not lapse but carries forward from year to year.
