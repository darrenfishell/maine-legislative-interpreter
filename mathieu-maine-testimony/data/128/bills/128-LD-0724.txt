SUMMARY  9 
This bill transfers the budget approval authority for the Northern New England 10 
Passenger Rail Authority from the Department of Transportation to the Legislature.  It 11 
requires the authority to present its annual report to a joint meeting of the joi nt standing 12 
committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over transportation matters and the joint 13 
standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over appropriations and 14 
financial affairs.  It also requires the authority to hold timely and a ppropriate forums for 15 
public input and vetting of significant projects throughout the project selection, design 16 
and implementation processes.  Finally, the bill directs the passenger rail advisory council 17 
within the Department of Transportation to meet 4 t imes a year and to serve as a public 18 
forum for input on passenger rail planning and provide the public with updates on the 19 
operational, capital and financial structure of passenger and freight railroad operations in 20 
the State.  21
