SUMMARY  19 
This bill requires that all retail marijuana and retail marijuana products sold at a retail 20 
marijuana store be in tamper -evident packaging, which involves a device or process such 21 
as a seal, label or marking that makes unauthorized access to or tampering with a 22 
package, product or container easily detectable.  23
