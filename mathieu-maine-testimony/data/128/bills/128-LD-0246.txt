SUMMARY  7 
This bill requires research facilities that receive public funding to offer for adoption 8 
cats and dogs used for science, testing, education or research purposes when the cats and 9 
dogs are no longer needed for those purp oses instead of euthanizing them.  Adoption of 10 
the cats and dogs may be through an animal shelter, animal rescue organization or private 11 
placement.  12
