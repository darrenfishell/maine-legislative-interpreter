SUMMARY  17 
This bill removes the provisions of law that on August 1, 2017 repeal provisions 18 
enacted in Public Law 2013, chapter 434 that enable the Department of Corrections to 19 
establish an intensive mental health unit, which provides services to the department's 20 
prisoners and to prisoners of jails, and that enable the department to obtain court orders 21 
for the involuntary medication of prisoners with mental illness.  22
