SUMMARY  28 
This bill directs the Commissioner of Marine Resources to open and  keep operational 29 
from April 15th to June 30th annually the fishway on the Sheepscot River located at the 30 
outlet of Sheepscot Pond in the Town of Palermo and to investigate the conditions of the 31 
pond for the purpose of developing a management plan for anad romous fish species.  32
