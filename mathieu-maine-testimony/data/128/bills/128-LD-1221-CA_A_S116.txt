9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A " to S.P. 412, L.D. 1221, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Clarify and Amend Certain Provisions of Law Regarding Victim Services" 
11 Amend the bill in section 4 in paragraph B-1 by striking out all of subparagraphs (1) 
12 and (2) (page 2, lines 1 to 5 in L.D.) and inserting the following: 
13 '( 1) . The person has contact with a victim with whom the person has been ordered 
14 not to have contact as a condition of probation; 
15 (2) In the case of a person who has been committed to the Department of 
16 Corrections, the person has contact with any victim with whom the person has 
17 been prohibited to have contact by the Department of Corrections; or 
18 (3) In the case of a person who has been committed to a county or regional jail, 
19 the person has contact with any victim with whom the person has been prohibited 
20 to have contact by the county or regional jail.' 
21 SUMMARY 
22 This amendment amends the bill by providing that a court may revoke the probation 
23 of an offender who has contact with a victim during incarceration at a county or regional 
24 jail if contact has been prohibited by the county or regional jail. The bill provides that a 
25 court may revoke the probation of an offender who has contact with a victim during 
26 incarceration if contact has been prohibited by a condition of probation or by the 
27 Department of Corrections.
