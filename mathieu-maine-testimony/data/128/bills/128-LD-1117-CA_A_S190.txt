2 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, changes the 
3 provision in the resolve regarding staffing for the Commission To Study the Phase-out of 
4 Subminimum Wage. Instead of the Legislative Council providing all staffing for the 
5 commission, the Legislative Council is only authorized to provide staffing when the 
6 Legislature is not in session. The Legislative Council is authorized to contract for 
7 necessary staff support when the Legislature is in session. 
8 This amendment provides funds for the operating costs of the commission and for 
9 contracted staff support.
