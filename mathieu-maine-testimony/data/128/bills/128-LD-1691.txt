Page 1 - 128LR2677(01)-11 Sec. 1.  Secretary of State to review uniform legislation. Resolved:  That the 
2 Secretary of State shall review  the uniform law entitled  "Revised Uniform Law on 
3 Notarial Acts," which was approved and recommended for enactment  by the National  
4 Conference of Commissioners  on Uniform State Laws on November 15, 2010.  In 
5 conducting this review, the Secretary of State may consult with state and national  
6 associations for notaries public, as well as other experts in the field of notarial law and 
7 practice.  Based on that review, the Secretary  of State shall  submit a report including a 
8 recommendation regarding  whether the Legislature should  adopt  the uniform  law, with or 
9 without changes, together with any proposed implementing legislation, to the joint 
10 standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction  over judiciary matters  no later 
11 than January 15, 2019.  Following receipt and review  of the report,  the joint standing  
12 committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction  over judiciary matters  is authorized to 
13 report out a bill  to the  First Regular Session of  the 129th Legislature.
14 SUMMARY
15 This resolve directs the Secretary of State to review uniform  legislation  governing 
16 notarial practice  and to submit a report including a recommendation regarding adoption 
17 of the uniform  law, along with any proposed implementing  legislation,  to the joint 
18 standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over judiciary matters, which  is 
19 authorized to report out  a bill to the 129th Legislature.
