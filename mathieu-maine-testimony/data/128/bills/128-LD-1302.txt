SUMMARY  22 
This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to administer a 23 
program to provide financial assistance to families receiving Temporary Assistance for 24 
Needy Families benefits or statewide food  supplement program benefits for motor vehicle 25 
repairs, registration and inspection when the motor vehicle is needed for employment 26 
purposes.  Under the bill, the program must be supported with federal block grant funding 27 
under the Temporary Assistance for  Needy Families program.  28
