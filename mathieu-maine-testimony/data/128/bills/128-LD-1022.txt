SUMMARY  27 
This bill requires a person who tows a commercial motor vehicle without consent to 28 
provide an itemized invoice, including a notice to direct complaints about overcharging to 29 
the traffic division of the State Police, and  provides for a fine for failure to itemize an 30 
invoice.  It provides a definition of "commercial motor vehicle" in language modeled on 31 
49 Code of Federal Regulations, Section 390.5. It provides that a customer is not liable 32 
for a charge not included on the  itemized invoice.  It also prohibits a person holding or 33 
storing a commercial motor vehicle from holding the vehicle's cargo.  34
