Page 1 - 128LR2546(01)-11 Sec. 1.  Suicide prevention protocols.   Resolved:  That the Department of 
2 Education shall adopt for schools protocols  designed to help prevent  youth suicide.  The 
3 protocols must  include a process  for a school  to follow  when the school is made aware 
4 that a student made a suicidal comment or has otherwise been  identified as suicidal.  The 
5 process must  include without limitation:
6 1.  Notifying the parent or legal guardian  of the student, law enforcement  and the 
7 student's mental health or health care provider;
8 2.  In accordance with privacy  provisions of the federal Health Insurance  Portability 
9 and Accountability Act of 1996 and the federal Family  Educational  Rights and Privacy 
10 Act of 1974, interviewing the parent or legal guardian  of the student  and requiring the 
11 student to meet with a  mental  health or  health care provider;
12 3.  Allowing the student  to attend  school  only after the student's  mental health or 
13 health care provider  determines the student is not at risk of self-harm; and
14 4.  Regular follow-up with the student  by a school counselor and a mental health or 
15 health care provider.
16 The department may report to the joint standing  committee of the Legislature having  
17 jurisdiction over education  matters any recommended legislation relating to the protocols,  
18 and the joint standing committee may report out a bill to the First Regular Session of the 
19 129th Legislature  based on the report.
20 SUMMARY
21 This resolve directs the Department of Education  to adopt  for schools protocols  
22 designed to help prevent youth suicide.   It authorizes the department to report any 
23 recommended legislation  relating to the protocols  to the joint standing committee of the 
24 Legislature having  jurisdiction over education matters and authorizes the joint standing  
25 committee to report out a bill to the First Regular Session  of the 129th Legislature based  
26 on the report.
