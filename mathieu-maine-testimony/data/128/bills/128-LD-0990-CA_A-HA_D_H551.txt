4 SUMMARY 
5 This amendment removes the Class B crime added by Committee Amendment "A" of 
6 causing bodily injury to a law enforcement officer, emergency medical care provider or 
7 firefighter because of that person's status as a law enforcement officer, emergency 
8 medical care provider or firefighter. Instead, this amendment makes causing bodily 
9 injury to a law enforcement officer, emergency medical care provider or firefighter 
10 because of that person's status as a law enforcement officer, emergency medical care 
11 provider or firefighter an aggravating factor that must be considered by the court when 
12 sentencing a person convicted of assault of a law enforcement officer, emergency medical 
13 care provider or firefighter. 
14 This amendment also removes the Class C crime added by Committee Amendment 
15 "A" of intentionally or knowingly causing offensive physical contact to a law 
16 enforcement officer while t at officer is in the performance of the officer's official duties.
