6 SUMMARY 
7 This amendment makes changes to the Maine Educational and Attainment Research 
8 Navigation System Executive Council by designating as ex officio and nonvoting 
9 members the chairs of the joint standing committees of the Legislature having jurisdiction 
10 over workforce and education matters. It also adds an effective date of January 1, 2019 to 
11 the provisions of the bill that repeal the State Education and Employment Outcomes Task 
12 Force, in order to provide a more gradual transition of the duties of the task force to the 
13 duties of the executive council. Additionally, the amendment adds the State Workforce 
14 Investment Board as a recipient of the executive council's annual report and provides the 
15 Department of Labor the authority to adopt routine technical rules. 
16 This amendment increases the funding for the cost of the Legislators being added to 
17 the executive council.

This amendment adds ongoing annual General Fund appropriations of $2,500 beginning in fiscal year 2017-18 for the 
cost of Legislators participating on the Maine Educational and Attainment Research Navigation System Executive 
Council. The fiscal note also reflects the incremental cost in fiscal year 2017-18 only of delaying by one year the 
annual savings of $2,500 created by the bill's elimination of the State Education and Employment Outcomes Task 
Force.
