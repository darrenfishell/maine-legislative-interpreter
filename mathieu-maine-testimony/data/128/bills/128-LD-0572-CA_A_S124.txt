COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A " to S.P. 187, L.D. 572, Bill, "An Act To 
Amend the Laws Governing the Practice of Pharmacy" 
Amend the bill by adding after section 2 the following: 
'Sec. 3. Rule of construction. Nothing in this Act is intended to affect the scope 
of the term "health care provider" as that term is used in other provfsions of law, 
including the Maine Health Security Act.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, clarifies that the 
revisions to the definitions of "phannacist" and "practice of pharmacy" in the bill are not 
intended to affect the definition of "health care provider" in other provisions of law, 
including the Maine Health Security Act.
