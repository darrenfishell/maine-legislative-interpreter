SUMMARY  14 
This bill amen ds the law governing time shares in the following ways.  15 
1.  It grants a person who purchases a time share from a developer the right to sell the 16 
time share back to the developer at fair market value after expiration of the sale 17 
cancellation period.  18 
2.  It prohibits the developer or an entity wholly or partially owned by the developer 19 
from serving as the manager of the time -share project.  20 
3.  It limits the liability of a time -share owner for assessments levied against the time - 21 
share unit for maintenance, tax es or fines to the fair market value of the time share.  22
