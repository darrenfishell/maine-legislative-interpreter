SUMMARY  16 
This bill amends the 3% surcharge imposed on individ ual taxable income over 17 
$200,000 to increase the threshold, for tax years beginning after 2017, to $300,000 for 18 
taxpayers filing as heads of households and to $400,000 for taxpayers filing married joint 19 
returns or surviving spouses permitted to file a join t return.  Single individuals and 20 
married persons filing separate returns are still subject to the surcharge on taxable income 21 
over $200,000.  22
