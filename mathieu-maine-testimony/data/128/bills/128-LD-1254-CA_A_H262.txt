This bill increases the number of super cribbage tournaments per year from 3 to 15 and decreases the number of 
players required for super cribbage tournaments from 50 to 30. The license fee for a super cribbage tournament is $75, 
the additional 12 licenses will increase Other Special Revenue Funds revenue for the Department of Public Safety by 
$900 annually beginning in fiscal year 2017-18.
