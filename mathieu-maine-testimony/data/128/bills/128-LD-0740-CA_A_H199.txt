COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A-,, to H.P. 520, L.D. 740, Bill, "An Act To 
Promote Efficiency and Accountability to Taxpayers in Personal Services Contracting" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Promote Efficiency and Accountability to Taxpayers in State 
Contracting for Goods and Services' 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the 
following: 
'Sec. 1. 5 MRSA ยง1816-B is enacted to read: 
ยง1816-B. Contract review 
A department or agency of the State shall submit to the Attomey General for review 
and approval any proposed contract for the purchase of goods or services by that 
department or agency when the department or agency determines the contract may expose 
the State to substantial risk in the event of nonperformance or the contract could 
reasonably be expected to incur costs to the State in excess of $3,000,000.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment is the majority report of the committee. The amendment replaces the 
bill, which is a concept draft. The amendment requires a department or agency of the 
State to submit a proposed contract to the Attomey General for review and approval when 
the department or agency determines the contract may expose the State to substantial risk 
in the event of nonperfonnance or is expected to incur costs to the State in excess of 
$3,000,000.
