SUMMARY  27 
This bill requires that the political designation for an unenrolled  candidate who is 28 
nominated by petition be “unenrolled” and disallows the term “independent” for an 29 
unenrolled candidate. This bill also prohibits a candidate who intends to form a new party 30 
to be placed on the ballot by petition, but requires the candidat e to follow the procedures 31 
in the election laws for forming new parties.  32
