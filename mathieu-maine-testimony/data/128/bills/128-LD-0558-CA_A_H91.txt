SUMMARY 
This amendment provides that hunters under 10 years of age on the opening day of 
the open moose season are eligible to apply for a moose hunting pennit and may accrue 
points in the public chance drawing for moose permits but are not eligible to receive a 
moose hunting pennit. The amendment prohibits a person under 10 years of age on the 
opening day of the open moose season from being a subpennittee-designate or alternate 
su bpermittee-designate.

Minor cost increase -General Fund

This bill allows individuals younger than 10 to apply for, but not be eligible to be issued, an open moose season 
permit in order to accrue points for the public chance drawing. Any additional costs to the Department of Inland 
Fisheries and Wildlife as result of this bill are anticipated to be minor and can be absorbed within existing budgeted 
resources. Any additional revenue to the General Fund from additional application fees is expected to be minor.
