SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to address the problem of marine debris resulting from commercial 5 
activities such as commercial fishing and aquaculture.  The bill will use current 6 
knowledge, information and data generated by previous meetings and conferences and 7 
scientific publications along with accepted fishing and aquaculture practices to advance 8 
an approach to address marine de bris while recognizing that healthy commercial fishing 9 
and aquaculture industries are paramount for the economic health of this State.  10
