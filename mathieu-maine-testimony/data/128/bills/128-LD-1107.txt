SUMMARY  10 
This bill allows a municipality to extend the time for the sale or delivery of liquor by 11 
licensed establishments by one hour, by either opening earlier than 5 a.m. or closing later 12 
than 1 a.m., within that municipality.  Thi s bill also allows a municipality to extend by 13 
one hour, to 2:15 a.m., the permitted consumption or possession of liquor in licensed 14 
establishments.  In order to extend the time of operation, consumption or possession, the 15 
legislative body of that municipa lity must vote to extend by one hour the sale or 16 
consumption or possession of liquor at establishments licensed for on -premises 17 
consumption within the municipality; the municipality must have a full -time police 18 
department or contract with the county sherif f or State Police to provide coverage during 19 
the extended hour of operation; and the municipality must notify the Department of 20 
Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery 21 
Operations of the extended hour within 30 days of adoption.  22
