SUMMARY 
This amendment provides that the annual credit may include loan amounts paid in 
excess of the amount due during a taxable year. The amendment also provides that 
credits in excess of those that may be used during a taxable year may be carried over for 
the next succeeding 5 years. This amendment also allows for the refundability of the new 
credit for those loans for which a refundable credit was available under the current credit 
for educational opportunity, such as loans to obtain an associate1s or bachelor1s degree in 
science, technology, engineering or mathematics. The refundability for all loans ends 
beginning with the tax year beginning January 1, 2021. 
This amendment also makes technical con-ections.
