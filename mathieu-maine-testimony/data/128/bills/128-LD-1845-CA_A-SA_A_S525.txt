This amendment reduces the General Fund appropriations to the Live Fire Service Training Facilities Fund program 
within the Maine Community College System by $500,000 annually beginning in fiscal year 2018-19. This 
amendment also specifies that funding for this program may not continue beyond fiscal year 2020-21 without explicit 
legislative approval.
