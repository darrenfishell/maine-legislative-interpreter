SUMMARY  22 
This bill updates references to the United States Internal Revenue Code of 1986 23 
contained in the M aine Revised Statutes, Title 36 to refer to the United States Internal 24 
Revenue Code of 1986, as amended through December 31, 2016, for tax years beginning 25 
on or after January 1, 2016 and for any prior tax years as specifically provided by the 26 
United States  Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended.  This bill primarily affects the 27 
State's income tax laws.  28
