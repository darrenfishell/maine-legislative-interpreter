SUMMARY  11 
This bill provides for the deorganization of the Town of Atkinson in Piscataquis  12 
County subject to approval at local referendum and execution of a withdrawal agreement 13 
from School Administrative District No. 41, also known as Regional School Unit No. 41.  14 
It also provides that townships deorganized on or after July 1, 2019 continue to  receive 15 
school subsidies through general purpose aid to local schools, subject to approval at local 16 
referendum.  17
