SUMMARY  25 
Current law allows a person licensed for retail sale of wine or malt liquor for off - 26 
premises consumption to conduct taste testings on the licensee's premises if the licensee 27 
stocks at least 125 different labels o f wine or 100 different labels of malt liquor.  This bill 28 
removes the minimum stock requirements.  29
