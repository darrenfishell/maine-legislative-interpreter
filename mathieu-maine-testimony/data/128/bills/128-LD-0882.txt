SUMMARY  21 
This bill prohibits any public entity, including the State and any of its agencies or 22 
subdivisions, from boycotting the State of Israel or contracting with or giving a grant to a 23 
person that is boycotting the State of Israel and requires all contracts and grants by a state 24 
agency or subdivision to certify that the per son awarded the contract or grant will not 25 
boycott the State of Israel for the duration of the contract or grant. This bill does not 26 
apply to a contract if the person supplies the services, supplies or information technology 27 
for at least 20% less than the lowest eligible competitor or to a contract or grant with a 28 
total potential value of less than $1,000.  29
