1 Sec. 6. Application. The sections of the bill that amend the Maine Revised 
2 Statutes, Title 36, sections 2011 and 2555 apply to requests for credit or refund of sales 
3 tax paid to a retailer and service provider tax paid to a service provider for which 
4 administrative or judicial review is still available.' 
5 SUMMARY 
6 This amendment requires a person requesting a refund of sales and use tax to submit 
7 an affidavit to the State Tax Assessor stating that a refund or credit has not been and will 
8 not be requested from the retailer. The requirement applies to pending reconsiderations 
9 and administrative appeals. The amendment also revises the process for a customer of a 
10 service provider to obtain a refund or credit of tax that was erroneously or illegally 
11 computed.
