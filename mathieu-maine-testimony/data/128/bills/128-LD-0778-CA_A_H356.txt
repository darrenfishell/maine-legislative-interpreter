Fiscal Note 
Future biennium savings -General Fund 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Eliminating the requirement that the minimum hourly wage in effect on Janua1y 1, 2021 and each January 1st 
thereafter be increased by the percentage increase in the Consumer Price Index for Urban Wage Earners and Clerical 
Workers, CPI-W, for the Northeast region, or its successor index, will result in future savings to State Government as 
well as municipalities and other public and private employers. The amount of savings can not be estimated at this 
time.
