SUMMARY  19 
This bill amends the requirements for a high school diploma and adds new subjects to 20 
those requirements.  It r epeals the sunset and application provisions of the high school 21 
diploma standards in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20 -A, section 4722.  It requires 22 
the Department of Education to adopt rules establishing pathways by which students may 23 
demonstrate achie vement of high school diploma standards by documentation of college 24 
readiness assessment standardized test scores, combinations of GPA and academic and 25 
career indicators and advanced placement examinations and course grades.  26
