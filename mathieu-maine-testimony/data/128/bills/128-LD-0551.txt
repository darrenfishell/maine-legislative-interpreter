This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
Under current law, a naturopathic doctor may prescribe nonprescription medications, 5 
homeopathic remedies, vitamins and minerals, hormones, local anesthesia and 6 
immunizations that are designated by rule by a subcommittee of the Board of 7 
Complementary Health Care Providers.  This bill proposes to expand the authority of 8 
naturopathic doctors to prescribe certain naturally  occurring medications.  9
