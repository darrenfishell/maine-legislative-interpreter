fund the replacement  of malfunctioning septic systems, wastewater treatment facility 16 
planning, construction grants and hydrographic modeling in coastal watersheds, 17 
prioritizing areas with high -value shellfish resources, and assistance to homeowners 18 
whose homes are served by s ubstandard or malfunctioning wastewater treatment systems.  19
