 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  28-A MRSA §1355 -A, sub -§2, ¶K is enacted to read:  2 
K.  A licensee with multiple facilities licensed for the manufacture of liquor under 3 
this section may transfer product produced by the licensee from one of those facilities 4 
to another for the purposes of selling liquor for off -premises or on -premises 5 
consumption as permitted by laws administered by the United States Department of 6 
the Treasury, Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau, inc luding any 7 
requirements to maintain records regarding transfers between facilities.  8 
SUMMARY  9 
This bill permits a manufacturer who manufactures liquor at multiple licensed 10 
locations to transport the manufacturer's own product between those locations, as is 11 
allowed under federal law.  12
