14 SUMMARY
15 This bill requires  the Public Utilities Commission  to establish the statewide E-9-1-1 
16 surcharge and the prepaid wireless  E-9-1-1 surcharge  but limits the surcharges to no more  
17 than 45¢ per month per line or number for the statewide E-9-1-1 surcharge and 45¢ per 
18 retail transaction for the prepaid wireless E-9-1-1 surcharge,  which is the same amount as 
19 in the current law.
