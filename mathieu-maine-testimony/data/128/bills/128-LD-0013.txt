SUMMARY  29 
This bill requires the Board of Osteopathic Licensure, the Board of Licensure in 30 
Medicine, the State Board of Social Worker Licensure, the Board of Counseling 31 
Professionals Licensure and the Board of Dental Practice to report to a law  enforcement 32 
agency or the Department of Health and Human Services known or suspected incidents of 33 
sexual abuse of a client or patient by a professional regulated by the board or by an 34 
assistant to a professional regulated by the board.  35
