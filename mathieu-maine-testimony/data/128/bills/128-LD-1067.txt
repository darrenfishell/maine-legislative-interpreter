SUMMARY  26 
This bill limits the size of scallop drags allowed in the territorial waters of the State to 27 
a maximum measurement of 5 1/2 feet across when used within the island zone as 28 
defined by the Depart ment of Marine Resources and 10 feet across when used outside the 29 
island zone.  This bill also establishes penalties for exceeding the drag width limit.  30
