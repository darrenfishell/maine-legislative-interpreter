2 This amendment is the minority report. The amendment removes the bill's 
3 requirement that the local school budgeting approval process, which is currently done on 
4 an annual basis, must be a biennial process beginning with the biennium of fiscal years 
5 2019-20 and 2020-21. The amendment allows school administrative units to establish a 
6 local school budgeting approval process on an annual or biennial basis.
