COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 76, L.D. 108, Bill, "An Act To Allow a 
Law Enforcement Agency That Treats a Person with Naloxone Hydrochloride To Bill 
That Person for That Treatment" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 3-A in the 5th line (page 1, line 7 in L.D.) 
by inserting after the following: "provided" the following: 'or the legal guardian of that 
person' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, clarifies that the 
legal guardian of a person who receives naloxone hydrochloride may be billed for the 
cost.

Potential current biennium revenue increase -General Fund 
Potential revenue increase to municipalities

Additionally, municipalities could also bill people for reimbursement under this bill and could receive increased 
revenues.
