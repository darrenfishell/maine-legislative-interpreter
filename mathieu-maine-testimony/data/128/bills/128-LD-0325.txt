SUMMARY  21 
This bill requires the Commissioner of Inland Fish eries and Wildlife, if antlerless 22 
deer permits are issued, to issue antlerless deer permits to applicants who are chosen 23 
through a public chance drawing. The commissioner is required to adopt rules by 24 
November 1, 2017 establishing a public chance drawing f or antlerless deer permits, 25 
including provisions for awarding to applicants who are not selected for a permit points 26 
that increase the applicants' chances of being selected in a subsequent consecutive year.  27 
The bill requires the commissioner to issue antle rless deer permits to veterans, who 28 
are not required to participate in the public chance drawing.  The number of permits 29 
issued to veterans is determined by the commissioner annually based upon the deer 30 
population.  31
