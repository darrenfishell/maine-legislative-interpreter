SUMMARY  24 
This bill creates the Class E crime of unlawful damage by a tenant if at the 25 
termination of a tenancy a rental unit has sustained over $1,000 worth of damage over the 26 
amount of the security deposit and the tenant does not pay the landlord within 60 days' 27 
notice by the landlord of the damage. A 2nd or subsequent offense is a Class D cr ime. 28
