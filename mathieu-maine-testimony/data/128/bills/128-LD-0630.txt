SUMMARY  8 
This bill repeals the prohibition on the exchange f or consideration of a moose permit 9 
in a designated hunting area, zone or season for another moose permit in a different 10 
designated hunting area, zone or season.  The bill requires the Department of Inland 11 
Fisheries and Wildlife to establish an online trans fer system for moose permits through 12 
which transfers of designated hunting areas, zones or seasons may be accomplished.  13
