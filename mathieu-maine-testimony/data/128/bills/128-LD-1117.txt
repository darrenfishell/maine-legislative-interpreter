SUMMARY  28 
This resolve es tablishes the Commission To Study the Phase -out of the Subminimum 29 
Wage for the purpose of determining the effect of the law that requires the minimum cash 30 
wage paid to a tipped service employee to be increased by $1 each year until it reaches 31 
the same amou nt as the minimum wage.  32
