SUMMARY  16 
This bill enhances the penalty for an intentional or knowing violation of the law 17 
prohibiting the discharge of waste from water craft into the inland waters of the State from 18 
a Class E crime to a Class C crime.  The bill directs the Department of Inland Fisheries 19 
and Wildlife, in consultation with the Department of Environmental Protection, by July 1, 20 
2018, to ensure that signs des cribing this prohibition against the discharge of waste from 21 
watercraft, including the increased penalties for an intentional or knowing violation of the 22 
prohibition, are installed at all state boat launch facilities.  23
