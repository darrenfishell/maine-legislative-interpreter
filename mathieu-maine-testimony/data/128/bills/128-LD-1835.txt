12 Sec. 1.  Transfer;  Inland Fisheries and Wildlife carrying  account.  
13 Notwithstanding any other  provision of law, the State Controller shall transfer $600,000 
14 by June 30, 2018 from the Inland Fisheries and Wildlife  carrying account, General Fund  
15 account within the Department of Inland Fisheries  and Wildlife  to the License and 
16 Registration General  Fund account within the Department of Inland Fisheries  and 
17 Wildlife.  These funds may be allotted by financial  order upon recommendation of the 
18 State Budget Officer and  approval of the Governor.

21 SUMMARY
22 This bill directs the State  Controller to transfer $600,000 by June 30, 2018 from the 
23 Inland Fisheries and Wildlife  carrying account, General  Fund account within the 
24 Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to the License and Registration  General 
25 Fund account within the Department of Inland Fisheries  and Wildlife.
