ยง3174-JJ. MaineCare reimbursement for ambulance services 
The department shall reimburse for ambulance services under MaineCare at a level 
that is not less than the average allowable reimbursement rate under Medicare for such 
services or at the highest percent of that level that is possible within resources 
appropriated for those purposes. Beginning March 1, 2015, the department shall 
reimburse for ambulance services under MaineCare at a level that is not less than 65% of 
the average allowable reimbursement rate under Medicare for such services. Beginning 
March 1, 2018, the department shall reimburse for ambulance services under MaineCare 
at a level that is not less than 70% of the average allowable reimbursement rate under 
Medicare for such services. The department may not reduce any reimbursement rate in 
this section to a level that is less than the rate as of January 1, 2017.

11 This amendment removes the provisions in the bill that relate to community 
12 paramedicine services. It increases beginning March 1, 2018 the reimbursement rate for 
13 ambulance services under the MaineCare program under current law from 65% to 70% of 
14 the average allowable reimbursement rate under Medicare. It provides that the 
15 Department of Health and Human Services may not lower any reimbursement rates for 
16 ambulance services below the rates as of January 1, 2017. The amendment also adds an 
17 appropriations and allocations section.
