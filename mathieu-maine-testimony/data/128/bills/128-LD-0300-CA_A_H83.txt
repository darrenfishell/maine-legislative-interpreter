Eliminating Maine Clean Election Act funding for gubernatorial candidates will reduce anticipated spending by 
$4,572,626 in fiscal year 2017-18 and by $1, 725,24 7 in fiscal year 2018-19 from eliminating distributions to

and $60,000 in fiscal year 2018-19.
