9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A " to S.P. 170, L.D. 509, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Allocate at Least 10 Percent of Antlerless Deer Permits Available in Each Wildlife 
11 Management District to Hunters 70 Years of Age and Older" 
12 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
13 'An Act To Allocate at Least 5 Percent of Antlerless Deer Permits Available in Each 
14 Wildlife Management District to Hunters 70 Years of Age and Older' 
15 Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 9 in the first line (page 1, line 3 in L.D.) by 
16 striking out the following: 1110%11 and inserting the following: '5%' 
17 SUMMARY 
18 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, decreases the 
19 percentage of antlerless deer permits issued in a wildlife management district to eligible 
20 hunters 70 years of age and older from 10%, as provided in the bill, to 5%.

Fiscal Note 
Minor cost increase -General Fund 
Requiring that at least 5% of antlerless deer permits issued in wildlife management districts be reserved for eligible 
hunters 70 years of age and older may result in minor costs to the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife that 
can be absorbed within existing budgeted resources.
