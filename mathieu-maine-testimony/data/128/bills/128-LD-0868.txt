SUMMARY  14 
This bill requires the court to order the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife 15 
to issue a permit to take a deer or moose to a person found not to have violated the inland 16 
fisheries and wildlife laws when a deer or moose taken by the person and seized by the 17 
department  had been disposed of before that finding was made.  18
