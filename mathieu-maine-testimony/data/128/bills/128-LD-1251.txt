SUMMARY  30 
This bill requires a person who tows a motor vehicle or stores a motor vehicle that 31 
has been towed due to being  illegally parked or left standing, at the direction of a law 32 
enforcement officer or due to being left on property without permission as soon as 33 
practicable after receipt of the motor vehicle to notify the owner or lienholder of the need 34 
to retrieve the mo tor vehicle and pay all towing, storage and authorized repair charges or 35 
risk losing title to the vehicle. The bill requires waiver of any towing or storage fee if 36 
notification to the owner or lienholder has not been timely made or if the notification to 37 
the Secretary of State has not been made within 14 days after receipt of the vehicle as 38 
required by law unless the person who tows or stores the towed vehicle makes a good 39 
faith attempt to notify the owner or lienholder and notify the Secretary of State.  40
