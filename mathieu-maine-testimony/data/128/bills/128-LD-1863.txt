7 Sec. 2.  22 MRSA  §8107, sub-§1-A is enacted  to read:
8 1-A.  Number; placement in family foster home.  The limitation on the number of 
9 children in a family foster  home does not prohibit the placement of more  than the allowed  
10 number if  the department determines it to be in the best interest of a  child.
11 SUMMARY
12 This bill allows a family foster home to exceed the limitation on the total number  of 
13 children in care if the Department of Health and Human Services  determines it to be in 
14 the best  interest  of a child.
