SUMMARY  6 
This bill requires a school administrative unit with a public school in which at least 7 
50% of students q ualified for a free or reduced -price lunch during the preceding school 8 
year to operate an alternative breakfast delivery service that provides breakfast after the 9 
start of the school day.  It also provides a process for a school administrative unit to opt 10 
out of the alternative breakfast delivery service. This bill requires the Department of 11 
Education to adopt rules to develop an application process and to adopt standards to 12 
address evaluation criteria based on need for funding assistance for alternative br eakfast 13 
delivery services in school administrative units. It also requires the department to develop 14 
a means to track health and academic outcomes of students and schools that participate in 15 
alternative breakfast delivery services.  16
