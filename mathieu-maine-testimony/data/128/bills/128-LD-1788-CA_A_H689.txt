COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "/) " to H.P. 1235, L.D. 1788, Bill, "An Act To 
Enhance Safety for Victims of Sexual Assault and Stalking and To Amend the Laws 
Governing Harassment and Protection from Abuse" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in paragraph C in the last line (page 1, line 7 in L.D.) by 
striking out the following: "506," 
Amend the bill by strildng out all of sections 2 and 4. 
Amend the bill in section 5 in subsection 1 in the 2nd blocked paragraph in the 3rd 
and 4th lines from the end (page 2, lines 24 and 25 in L.D.) by striking out the following: 
"or harassment as described in Title 17-A, section 506, subsection 1, paragraph A" 
Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment removes the expansion of the crime of harassment by telephone or 
by electronic communication device as proposed in the bill. It also removes the cross­
references to the expanded crime in the protection from harassment and protection from 
abuse laws.
