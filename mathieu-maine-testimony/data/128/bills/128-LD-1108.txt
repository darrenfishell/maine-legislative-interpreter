SUMMARY  20 
This bill is emergency legislation.  This bill enumerates the types of nursing services 21 
that must be provided by the Public Health Nursing Program within the Department of 22 
Health and Human Services.  It specifies required staffing in the program.  It sets 23 
deadlines for staffing and requires the Public Health Nursing Program by September 1, 24 
2017 and October 15, 2017 to report on progress in achieving full staffing.  The bill 25 
requires the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human Services to conduct a 26 
review of public health nursing services including types of public health needs of persons 27 
who have recently moved to the State, services being provided to meet those needs and 28 
any unmet needs.  The bill requires the committee to provide to the Legislature a written 29 
report of its findings and recommendations for any future action by January 1, 2018.  30
