SUMMARY  35 
This bill amends the provisions of law regarding insurers' initial communications 36 
with an insured or claimant filin g a claim for collision damage to an automobile in the 37 
following ways.  38

 1.  It clarifies that an insurer may not intimidate, coerce, threaten or provide incentive 1 
or inducement to the insured or claimant to use any particular specified place of business 2 
to repair the automobile.  3 
2.  It requires an insurer to submit to and have approved by the Superintendent of 4 
Insurance the script or talking points the insurer requires to be read or told to an insured 5 
or claimant upon receiving the claim and to include in th e script or talking points an 6 
advisory that the insured or claimant has the legal right to choose the motor vehicle repair 7 
service to fix the vehicle.  8 
3.  It makes a violation of the provisions of this bill and the other provisions 9 
regarding free competiti on in insurance for motor vehicle repairs an unfair trade practice 10 
and a civil violation with graduated fines from $1,000 for a first offense to $5,000, a 11 
requirement for the 2nd and subsequent offense that the insurer provide proof of 12 
corrective measures and, for a 4th or subsequent offense within a 12 -month period, 13 
possible suspension or revocation of the insurer's certificate of authority.  14 
4.  It allows the Attorney General to enforce the provisions of this bill and the other 15 
provisions regarding free co mpetition in insurance for motor vehicle repairs.  16
