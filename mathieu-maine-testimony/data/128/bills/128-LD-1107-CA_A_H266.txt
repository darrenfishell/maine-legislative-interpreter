COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A " to H.P. 781, L.D. 1107, Bill, "An Act To 
Allow Municipalities To Adjust Times of Operation for Establishments That Serve 
Alcohol" 
Amend the bill in section 2 in subsection 2 in paragraph A-1 in the first line (page 1, 
line 31 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "Notwithstanding paragraph A, on­
premises" and inserting the following: 'On-premises' 
Amend the bill in section 2 in subsection 2 in paragraph A-1 in subparagraph (1) in 
the first line (page 1, line 33 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "extend by one hour" 
and inserting the following: 'permit' 
Amend the bill in section 2 in subsection 2 in paragraph A-1 in subparagraph (1) in 
the last line (page 1, line 35 in L.D.) by inserting after the following: "municipality" the 
following: 'until 2 a.m.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment makes technical changes to the bill to clarify that an on-premises 
licensee may sell liquor until 2 a.m. if the municipality in which the licensee is located 
approves liquor sales until 2 a.m.
