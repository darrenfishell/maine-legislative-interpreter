SUMMARY  11 
This bill allows a municipality that issues a property tax b ill to a taxpayer to include 12 
in the tax bill other fees or assessments owed by the taxpayer to the municipality if the 13 
other fees and assessments are itemized and distinct from the information on the tax bill 14 
that is required by law.  15
