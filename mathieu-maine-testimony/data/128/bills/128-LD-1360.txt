SUMMARY  16 
This bill replaces all references to the federal Workforce Investment Act of 1998 with 17 
the federal reauthorization of that act, the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act, 18 
and changes the name of the State Workforce Investment Board to the State Workforce 19 
Board.  The bill updates the duties and responsibilities of the State Workforce Board to 20 
conform to those articulated in the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act.  It 21 
changes the reporting requirements to the Legislature to ensure the Legislature receives 22 
the same outcome measurements and expenditure information as is reported to the 23 
Federal Gover nment under the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act.  Finally, it 24 
requires the Center for Workforce Research and Information within the Department of 25 
Labor to publish annually a list of high -priority occupations that meet certain specified 26 
criteria, w hich is the same process used to create the list of high -wage, in -demand jobs 27 
produced by the Center for Workforce Research and Information and used by the 28 
Competitive Skills Scholarship Program.  29
