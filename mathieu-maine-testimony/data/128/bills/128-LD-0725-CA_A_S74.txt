COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A " to S.P. 242, L.D. 725, Bill, "An Act To 
Recognize Local Control Regarding Food and Water Systems" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Recognize Local Control Regarding Food Systems' 
Amend the bill in section 2 in §201-B in the first line (page 1, line 9 in L.D.) by 
striking out the following: "water and" 
Amend the bill in section 2 in §201-B in the indented paragraph in the 4th and 5th 
lines (page 1, lines 13 and 14 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "and the transport of 
water for commercial purposes beyond the boundaries of the municipality" 
SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the majority report, strikes the language in the bill 
authorizing municipal governments to regulate by ordinance the transport of water for 
commercial purposes.
