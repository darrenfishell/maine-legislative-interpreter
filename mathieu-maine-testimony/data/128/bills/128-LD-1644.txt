SUMMARY  16 
This bill implements the cost items in the collective bargaining agreements reached 17 
between the State and the American Federation of State, County and Municipal 18 
Employees, the Maine State Troopers A ssociation, the Maine State Law Enforcement 19 
Association and the Maine State Employees Association if tentative agreements are 20 
ratified and provides for equitable treatment for confidential employees and certain other 21 
employees excluded from collective barg aining.  22 
The bill specifies the costs from the General Fund and Highway Fund to fund salary 23 
increases and authorizes the transfer by financial order of available General Fund and 24 
Highway Fund balances as necessary.  25 
The bill provides for the adjustment of ce rtain salary schedules in fiscal year 2017 -18 26 
and in fiscal year 2018 -19.  The bill provides for similar and equitable treatment of 27 
confidential employees, probationary employees and other employees excluded from 28 
collective bargaining.  29
