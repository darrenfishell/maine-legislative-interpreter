SUMMARY  3 
This bill provides funding to expand the use of electronic monitoring as part of a 4 
coordinated response to domestic violence, sexual assault and stalking.   It provides funds 5 
for victim advocates and case management and funds to support the cost of electronic 6 
monitoring for indigent offenders and for victims who may choose to carry a 7 
corresponding device.  8
