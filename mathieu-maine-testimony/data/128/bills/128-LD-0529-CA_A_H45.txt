COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "~" to H.P. 373, L.D. 529, Bill, "An Act To 
Ensure Resiliency of the Maine Electrical Grid" 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
summary and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. 35-A MRSA §3144 is enacted to read: 
14 §3144. Mitigation measures to protect from geomagnetic disturbances and 
15 electromagnetic pulse 
16 Beginning December 1, 2017, and annually thereafter, the commission shall direct an 
17 investor-owned transmission and distribution utility to submit to the commission a report 
18 detailing what steps, if any, the investor-owned transmission and distribution utility has 
19 taken to protect its bulk transmission systems from geomagnetic disturbances and 
20 electromagnetic pulse, including activities undertaken to meet any federal 
21 recommendations made to address geomagnetic disturbances and electromagnetic pulse. 
22 The commission shall require an investor-owned transmission and distribution utility to 
23 include neutral direct current monitoring information from autotransformers where 
24 monitoring equipment is installed, collected by the investor-owned transmission and 
25 distribution utility. The commission, as part of its annual report pursuant to section 120 
26 shall compile reports it has received pursuant to this section in the prior year and submit 
27 this information to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction 
28 over utilities and energy matters.' 
29 SUMMARY 
30 This amendment is the majority report of the committee and it strikes and replaces 
31 the bill. It requires an investor-owned transmission and distribution utility to report to the 
32 Public Utilities Commission annually on its activities regarding protection from 
33 geomagnetic disturbances and electromagnetic pulse. It also requires an investor-owned 
34 transmission and distribution utility to include in its report the Public Utilities 
35 Commission neutral direct current monitoring information from autotransformers where
