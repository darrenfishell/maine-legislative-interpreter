§6087.  Se aweed Advisory Council  7 
1.  Appointment; composition.  The Seaweed Advisory Council, established by 8 
Title 5, section 12004 -I, subsection 57 -H and referred to in this section as "the council," 9 
consists of 5 members appointed by the commissioner as follows:  10 
A.  Two members who each hold either a seaweed permit or seaweed buyer's license;  11 
B.  Two members who are seaweed aquaculture lease holders; and  12 
C.  One member of the scientific community.  13 
The commissioner shall make appointments so that the composition of the council 14 
reflects the diversity of the State's seaweed operations.  15 
2.  Purpose.  The council shall:  16 
A.  Make recommendations to the commissioner and the joint standing committee of 17 
the Legislature having jurisdiction over marine resources matters on all  matters 18 
concerning the health of the seaweed resource, its ecosystem and the industry it 19 
supports.  20 
B.  Make recommendations to the commissioner regarding expenditures from the 21 
Seaweed Management Fund as described in section 6806.  22 
3.  Term.  The term of a member appointed to the council is 3 years, except that a 23 
vacancy during an unexpired term must be filled in the same manner as for the original 24 
member for the unexpired portion of the member's term.  A member may not serve more 25 
than 2 consecutive terms.  26 
4.  Officers.  The officers of the council are the chair, vice -chair and secretary.  The 27 
term of the officers is one year.  The council shall elect a member of the council for each 28 
officer position at the first regular meeting of each year.  29 
5.  Meeting.  The council shall meet at least once a year.  It may also meet at other 30 
times at the call of the chair or the commissioner.  31
