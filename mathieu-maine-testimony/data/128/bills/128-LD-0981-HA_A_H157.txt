HOUSE AMENDMENT "A "to S.P. 320, L.D. 981, Bill, "An Act Regarding State 
Investments and the Dakota Access Pipeline" 
Amend the bill by striking out all of section 3. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment removes the provision in the bill that requires the Maine Public 
Employees Retirement System to divest holdings in any corporation or company that is 
constructing or funding construction of the so-called Dakota Access pipeline.
