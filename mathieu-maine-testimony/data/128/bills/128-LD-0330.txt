SUMMARY  16 
This bill provides an income tax credit to an employer that makes up to $1,000 in 17 
matching contributions on behalf of an employee to a qualified tuition program, 18 
sometimes referred to as a college savings program, established by the employee under 19 
Section 5 29 of the federal Internal Revenue Code.  20
