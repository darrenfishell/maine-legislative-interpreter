SENATE AMENDMENT" A" to H.P. 1275, L.D. 1833, Bill, "An Act To Facilitate 
Compliance with Federal Immigration Law by State and Local Government Entities" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in §25003 in subsection 4 in the 3rd line (page 3, line 3 in 
L.D.) by inserting after the following: "immigration detainer" the following: 'that includes 
written confirmation that the immigration detainer is based on probable cause' 
SUMMARY 
Under the bill, a government entity may not be limited or restricted from complying 
with an immigration detainer. This amendment limits that prohibition to immigration 
detainers that include written confirmation that the immigration detainer is based on 
probable cause. ~ -~
