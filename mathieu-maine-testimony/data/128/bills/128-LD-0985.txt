 SUMMARY  1 
This bill allows a person who holds a license to  practice medicine and surgery or 2 
osteopathic medicine and surgery in another state who accompanies an athletic team from 3 
that state to provide medical services to members and supporting personnel of the team 4 
without a license from this State but restricts  the person from providing medical services 5 
in a health care facility in the State.  6
