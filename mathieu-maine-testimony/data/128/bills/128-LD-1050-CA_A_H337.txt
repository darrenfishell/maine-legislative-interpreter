COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "/-J" to H.P. 733, L.D. 1050, Bill, "An Act To 
Protect the Safety of Emergency Medical Services Personnel and Patients" 
Amend the bill by striking out all of section 2 and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 2. 25 MRSA §2011, sub-§3-A is enacted to read: 
3-A. Exception for emergency medical services vehicle. This section does not 
prohibit an order, ordinance, rule or regulation of any political subdivision that regulates 
possession of a firearm while inside an emergency medical services vehicle that is owned 
or operated by or on behalf of the political subdivision.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment is one of 2 minority reports from the committee. The amendment 
does not prohibit a political subdivision of the State from adopting an order, ordinance, 
rule or regulation that regulates possession of a firemm while inside an emergency 
medical services vehicle that is owned or operated by or on behalf of the political 
subdivision.
