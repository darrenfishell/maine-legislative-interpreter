SUMMARY  10 
This bill authorizes the State to accept federal funds to provide health insurance 11 
coverage to adults with incomes equal to or below 133% plus 5% of the nonfarm income 12 
official federal poverty line for the applicable family size.  Persons with incomes equal to 13 
or below 100% of the nonfarm income official poverty line receive coverage through the 14 
MaineCare program.  The Commissioner of Health and Human Services is authorize d to 15 
seek approval to provide that persons with income levels over 100% of the federal 16 
poverty line and up to 133% of the federal poverty line may receive coverage from a 17 
private health insurance plan or other mechanisms. In the event that the commissioner  18 
uses this authority, any funds made available from unexpended state matching funds must 19 
be used to help ensure that those persons with income levels between 100% and 133% of 20 
the federal poverty line receive affordable and comprehensive health coverage.  21 
The bill provides for measures to be taken in the event that federal funding, as 22 
established under federal law, is reduced, so that the Legislature may act promptly to 23 
address the consequences of this action. The bill also authorizes the Department of Health  24 
and Human Services to contract with health insurance plans to provide coverage in order 25 
to maximize savings and to ensure access to and quality of services.  26 
The bill sets forth time standards for the start of the expansion of health care 27 
coverage, for sub mission of requests to obtain necessary federal approvals and for 28 
ongoing reporting to the Legislature during the start -up phase of this expansion.  Further 29 
reporting is required on the status of any savings generated to state -funded programs as a 30 
result o f this expansion.  It requires the Department of Administrative and Financial 31 
Services, Maine Revenue Services to report no later than 60 days following the end of the 32 
first 12 months of enrollment under the expansion on revenues generated as a result of 33 
expanded coverage.  It requires that savings and revenues be verified by the Office of 34 
Fiscal and Program Review within 90 days after the end of the first 12 months of 35 
enrollment under the expansion.  It transfers any savings to the MaineCare Stabilization 36 
Fund prior to the next fiscal year.  It requires the Office of Fiscal and Program Review to 37 
report its findings to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction 38 
over health and human services matters and to the joint standing committe e having 39 
jurisdiction over appropriations and financial affairs.  40 
The bill authorizes the Department of Health and Human Services to apply for and 41 
accept private foundation grants to be used to cover the cost of preparing and submitting 42

 any waivers and stat e plan amendments to the Federal Government required as a result of 1 
expanding health care coverage.  2
