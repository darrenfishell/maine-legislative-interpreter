SUMMARY 
This amendment is the majority report of the committee. The amendment removes 
from the bill the provision that allows the Maine Children's Growth Council to request 
staff assistance from the Department of Health and Human Services and the Child 
Development Services System. The bill changes a reporting requirement from every 2 
years to annually; the amendment removes that change so that the reporting requirement 
remains every 2 years. The amendment provides funding for a part-time staff person to 
support the activities of the Maine Children's Growth Council. 
The amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section.
