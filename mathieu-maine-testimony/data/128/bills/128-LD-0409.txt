SUMMARY  24 
This bill makes the following changes to the laws governing the Maine Public 25 
Emplo yees Retirement System:  26 
1.  It replaces the term "physician" with "medical provider" and defines "medical 27 
provider" to include physicians and clinical psychologists, which permits clinical 28 
psychologists to be included in the composition of the medical boar d and for other 29 
medical consultations;  30 
2.  It clarifies that the board member who represents participating local districts may 31 
be either an active or retired member.  32 
3.  It clarifies the basis on which disability benefits for members of the Legislative 33 
Retirement Program are determined;  34 
4.  It clarifies that any property owned by the retirement system is tax exempt;  35 
5.  It gives the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over 36 
public employee retirement matters the authority to repor t out legislation as 37 
recommended by the board of trustees to improve the retirement system;  38 
 
 Page 10 - 128LR0678(01) -1 
 6.  It replaces obsolete language regarding the retirement system budgeting process;  1 
7.  It corrects a reference to the legislative jurisdiction that pertains to ma tters relating 2 
to the retirement system;  3 
8.  It clarifies the treatment of retirement system assets;  4 
9.  It clarifies the disposition of funds appropriated to subsidize the purchase of 5 
military service credit for specific members who subsequently decline t o purchase service 6 
credit or are able to purchase service credit without subsidy;  7 
10.  It changes the manner in which disability retirement benefits are calculated for 8 
less than full -time members;  9 
11.  It clarifies that assets of the group life insurance p rogram may only be used for 10 
benefits for participants of employers for which the assets are attributable; and  11 
12.  It removes obsolete language.  12
