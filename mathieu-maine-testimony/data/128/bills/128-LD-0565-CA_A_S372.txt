implement this revision when updating, publishing or republishing the statutes.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill, which is a concept draft. It repeals the provisions 
of Public Law 2017, chapter 249 that prevent pharmacists from prescribing and 
dispensing naloxone hydrochloride after July 1, 2019 to an individual at risk of an 
overdose or to a family member or friend of an individual at risk of an overdose.
