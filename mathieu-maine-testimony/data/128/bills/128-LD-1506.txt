SUMMARY  13 
This bill specifies the rights and obligations of parties to a guaranteed asset protection 14 
waiver.  A guaranteed asset protection waiver is a contractual agreement in which a 15 
creditor agrees for a separate charge t o cancel or waive all or part of the amount due on a 16 
borrower's finance agreement for a motor vehicle in the event of a total physical damage 17 
loss or unrecovered theft of the motor vehicle.  18
