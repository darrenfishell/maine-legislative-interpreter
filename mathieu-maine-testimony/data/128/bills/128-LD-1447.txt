SUMMARY  10 
This bill authorizes  the Department of Public Safety, Gambling Control Board to 11 
accept an application for a casino operator license from the Houlton Band of Maliseet 12 
Indians to operate 350 slot machines and 10 table games at a casino on Houlton Band 13 
Trust Land located on the North Road property of the Houlton Band of Maliseet Indians 14 
in the Town of Houlton.  A casino operated by the Houlton Band of Maliseet Indians is 15 
not subject to a referendum.  The bill raises the limit on the number of slot machines 16 
allowed in the State fr om 3,000 to 3,350 to accommodate the casino operated by the 17 
Houlton Band of Maliseet Indians.  A casino operated by the Houlton Band of Maliseet 18 
Indians would be subject to the oversight of the Gambling Control Board.  19
