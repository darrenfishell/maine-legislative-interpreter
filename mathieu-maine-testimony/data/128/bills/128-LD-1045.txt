 SUMMARY  1 
This bill requires the Board of Trustees of the Maine Public Employees Retirement 2 
System to prepare and present an actuarial report on normal teacher retirement costs to a 3 
joint meeting of the j oint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over 4 
education matters and the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction 5 
over appropriations and financial affairs matters by May 31st of even -numbered years.  It 6 
also req uires the board to rely on this biennial actuarial report in its determination of the 7 
percentage of total teacher compensation required to fund normal teacher retirement 8 
costs.  9 
This bill also requires the Joint Standing Committee on Appropriations and Fina ncial 10 
Affairs to conduct a review of the historical and projected future actuarial value of normal 11 
teacher retirement costs.  It requires the committee to report the results of its review to the 12 
Second Regular Session of the 128th Legislature.  13
