5 SUMMARY 
6 This amendment amends the laws governing forensic examination kits to provide for 
7 the kits to be used for testing in relation to alleged sexual assault crimes other than gross 
8 sexual assault, making that change in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 3360-M 
9 and Title 24, section 2986. The amendment changes the tracking number used on the 
10 completed kit from a number assigned by the hospital or health care practitioner to the 
11 number assigned by the kit manufacturer. The amendment requires the Victims' 
12 Compensation Board to amend its rules regarding forensic examination test kits.
