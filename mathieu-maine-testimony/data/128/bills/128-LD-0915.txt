SUMMARY  25 
This bill requires payments beginning in fiscal year 2018 -19 to a municipality of an 26 
amount, as determined by the State Tax Assessor, equal to 5% of the sales and use tax 27 
revenue attributable to the municipality in the previous fiscal  year. The amount received 28 
may not reduce any other state aid or revenue sharing received by that municipality, and 29 
the municipality must use the amount received to reduce property taxes by the same 30 
amount. The Unorganized Territory Education and Services Fund receives the local sales 31 
tax payment for the unorganized territory and is subject to the same requirement to use 32 
the revenue to reduce property taxes.  33
