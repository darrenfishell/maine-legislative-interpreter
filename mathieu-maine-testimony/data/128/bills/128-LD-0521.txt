SUMMARY  8 
This bill provides that a veteran who is a member of the Maine Public Employees 9 
Retirement System and who has been awarded a disability pension under 38 Code of 10 
Federal Regulations, Section 3.3 (2003) for a service -connected disability is qualified for 11 
disability retirement benefits under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 423, 12 
article 3.  The provisions of the bill apply to all applications for disability retirement 13 
benefits filed on or after January 1, 2018.  14
