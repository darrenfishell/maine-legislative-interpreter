COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 478, L.D. 1400, Bill, "An Act To 
Create the Bar Harbor Port Authority" 
Amend the bill by striking out all of the emergency preamble (page 1, lines 1 to 9 in 
L.D.). 
Amend the bill by striking out all of section 5 and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 5. Referendum; effective date. This Act takes effect when approved only 
for the purpose of permitting its submission to the legal voters of the Town of Bar Harbor 
at an election called for that purpose and held within 2 years after the effective date of 
this Act. The election must be called, advertised and conducted according to the law 
relating to municipal elections. The registrar shall make a complete list of all the eligible 
voters of the Town of Bar Harbor as described in this Act. The list prepared by the 
registrar governs the eligibility of a voter. For the purpose of registration of voters, the 
registrar of voters must be in session the next regular workday preceding the election. 
The subject matter of this Act is reduced to the following question: 
"Shall 'An Act To Create the Bar Harbor Port Authority,' as passed by the 
Second Regular Session of the 128th Legislature, be accepted?" 
The voters shall indicate by a cross or check mark against the word ''Yes" or "No" 
their opinion of the same.
