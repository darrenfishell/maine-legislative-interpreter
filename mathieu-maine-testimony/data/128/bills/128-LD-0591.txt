 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  30-A MRSA §3015 is enacted to read:  2 
§3015.  Ordinances regarding bus traffic  3 
Notwithstanding Title 29 -A, section 2053 or any other provision of law to the 4 
contrary, a municipality may a dopt an ordinance that requires the operator of a vehicle to 5 
yield the right -of-way to a bus.  For the purposes of this section, "bus" has the same 6 
meaning as in Title 29 -A, section 101, subsection 11; "operator" has the same meaning as 7 
in Title 29 -A, sect ion 101, subsection 48; and "vehicle" has the same meaning as in Title 8 
29-A, section 101, subsection 91.  9 
SUMMARY  10 
This bill allows municipalities to adopt local ordinances requiring vehicle operators 11 
to yield the right -of-way to a bus.  12
