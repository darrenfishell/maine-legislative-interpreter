SUMMARY  31 
This bill amends the municipal general assistance laws to provide a different method 32 
of determining the residence of an applicant and the municipality responsible for 33 
providing general assistance to that applicant, including an applica nt relocating from 34 
another municipality.  The bill provides that the municipality of record, which is defined 35 
as the municipality in which the applicant resided immediately prior to applying for 36 
assistance, is the responsible municipality.  37
