9 on the motor  vehicle.
10 SUMMARY
11 This bill provides that the prohibition  in current law on the possession of a firearm on 
12 public school  property or the property of an approved private school does not apply  to a 
13 person who possesses  a firearm in a motor vehicle as long as the person is dropping off or 
14 picking up a student and remains in the vehicle and, in accordance with the federal Gun-
15 Free School Zones Act of 1990,  the firearm is not loaded and is in either  a locked 
16 container or a locked  firearms rack.
