SUMMARY  19 
This bill prohibits a provider of broadband Internet access service from using, 20 
disclosing, selling or permitting access to customer personal info rmation unless the 21 
customer expressly consents to that use, disclosure, sale or access.  The bill provides 22 
other exceptions under which a provider may use, disclose, sell or permit access to 23 
customer personal information.  The bill prohibits a provider fro m refusing to serve a 24 
customer, charging a customer a penalty or offering a customer a discount if the customer 25 
does or does not consent to the use, disclosure, sale or access.  The bill requires providers 26 
to take reasonable measures to protect customer pe rsonal information from unauthorized 27 
use, disclosure, sale or access.  The provisions of the bill apply to providers operating 28 
within the State when providing broadband Internet access service to customers that are 29 
billed for service received in the State and are physically located in the State.  30
