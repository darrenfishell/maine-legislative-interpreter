Hunters Whose Religion Prohibits Wearing Hunter Orange Clothing To Instead Wear 
Red" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 1-A in the first line (page 1, line 3 in L.D.) 
by inserting before the following: "red" the following: 'bright' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment provides that a person with a religious opposition to wearing hunter 
orange may substitute articles of bright red clothing, instead of articles of red clothing as 
the bill provides, for the required articles of hunter orange clothing.
