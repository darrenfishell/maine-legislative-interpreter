SUMMARY  22 
This bill creates an exception to the limit on the number of agency liquor stores 23 
allowed by population threshold to allow the Department  of Administrative and Financial 24 
Services, Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations to license an agency 25 
liquor store in a municipality without an agency liquor store as long as an agency liquor 26 
store is not located within 3.5 miles of the muni cipality.  With regard to such a store, it 27 
allows the bureau to adopt rules regarding hours, seasons of operation and inventory.  It 28 
also allows the bureau to license stores in municipalities along interstate highways and at 29 
major airports in the State and  requires the bureau to enter into contracts for the 30 
management of those stores.  31
