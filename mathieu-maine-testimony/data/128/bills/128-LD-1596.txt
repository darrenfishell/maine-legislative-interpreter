§1363.  Duties  13 
1.  The commission shall review laws and rules pertaining to the use, possession, 14 
transportation and consumption of cannabis as d efined by Title 7, section 2422 and any 15 
other provision of law or rule pertaining to cannabis, including but not limited to laws and 16 
rules regarding public health, public safety, juvenile and adult criminal and civil offenses, 17 
workplace drug testing, workp lace safety, motor vehicles, alcohol, landlords and tenants, 18 
the medical use of marijuana and taxes.  19 
2.  The commission shall submit to the Legislature such changes to the laws as it 20 
considers appropriate to preserve the public safety and well -being of the  citizens of the 21 
State and the intent of citizens as expressed in passage of the Marijuana Legalization Act.  22 
3.  The commission may hold public hearings at such times and at such places as the 23 
commission considers appropriate in order to take testimony con cerning the use, 24 
possession and distribution of cannabis and the agreement of the Marijuana Legalization 25 
Act with other provisions of law.  26 
4.  The commission shall issue a report of its findings and concerns to the Legislative 27 
Council by January 15th annua lly. 28 
§1364.  Organization; staff  29

the commission shall organize, elect a chair, vice -chair and secretary -treasurer and adopt 34

2.  Within the limits of its budget, the commission may contract with and employ 36
