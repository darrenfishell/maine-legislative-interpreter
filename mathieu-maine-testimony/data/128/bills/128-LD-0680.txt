SUMMARY  25 
This bill allows a spouse of a school board member to be employed or serve in a 26 
volunteer position in a public school within the jurisdiction of the school board to which 27 
the member is elected or in a contract high school or academy located within a 28 
supervisory union in which the member is a representative on the union committee upon a 29 
2/3 vote of the school board or committee.  30
