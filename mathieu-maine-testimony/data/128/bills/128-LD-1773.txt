10 Sec. 1.  Director of Bureau of Parks and Lands to convey land.  Resolved:  
11 That the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the Department of Agriculture, 
12 Conservation and Forestry shall  by quitclaim deed without covenant, for negotiated 
13 value, and on such other  terms and conditions as the director may direct, convey or 
14 release to the First Congregational Church of Pittston a portion with a total of not more  
15 than .30 acre of a parcel of land situated  on Arnold Road  in the Town of Pittston, County 
16 of Kennebec and  recorded on the Town of  Pittston property tax map  U-13, Lot 9.
17 SUMMARY
18 This resolve  directs  the Director of the Bureau of Parks  and Lands  within the 
19 Department of Agriculture,  Conservation and Forestry to transfer a portion of a parcel of 
20 land situated on Arnold  Road in the Town of Pittston to the First Congregational  Church 
21 of Pittston.
