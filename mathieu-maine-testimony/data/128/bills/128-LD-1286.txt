SUMMARY  28 
This bill requires every school administrative unit to obtain a criminal history 29 
background check of an individual before hiring or placing that individual within the 30 
school administrative unit except for educational personnel currently required to undergo 31 
a criminal history background check.  This bill also requires every school administrative 32 
unit to conduct an audit to determine if the school administrative unit possesses a criminal 33 
history background check for all current employees of the school administrative unit and 34 
to obtain criminal history background checks for those employees for whom t he school 35 
administrative unit does not have a criminal history background check.  36
