SUMMARY 
2 This amendment is the minority report of the committee, changes the bill to a resolve 
3 and requires the University of Maine System to study the existing and emerging 
4 engineering workforce, applied research and technical assistance needs of southern Maine 
5 businesses and industry. The study must include recommendations to increase the 
6 engineering capacity specifically in southern Maine by strengthening academic and 
7 related nonacademic programs at the University of Southern Maine. The University of 
8 Maine System is required to report its findings and recommendations to its board of 
9 trustees by November 20, 2017 and to submit a report to the Joint Standing Committee on 
10 Education and Cultural Affairs and the Joint Standing Committee on Labor, Commerce, 
11 Research and Economic Development by December 31, 2017. After reviewing the 
12 report, the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs may report out a 
13 bill to implement recommendations contained in the report to the Second Regular Session 
14 of the 128th Legislature. The cost of the study will be absorbed using existing University 
15 of Maine System resources.
