SUMMARY  16 
This bill eliminates the requirement that the State Fire Marshal inspect a family foster 17 
home and certify that it meets all elements of the fire safety code before the Department 18 
of H ealth and Human Services may issue a license to operate as a family foster home.  19 
The bill moves the inspection responsibility to the Department of Health and Human 20 
Services, which is directed to adopt rules governing the method of inspection.  21
