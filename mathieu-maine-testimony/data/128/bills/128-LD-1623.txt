SUMMARY  14 
This bill responds to the Law Court decision regarding State of Maine v. Dale M. 15 
Pinkham, Sr.,  2016  ME 59.  The bill clarifies that when the term "heroin" is used in the 16 
chapter of the Maine Criminal Code regarding drug offenses, that term includes heroin 17 
that is mixed with other substances and that the total weight of that compound, mixture or 18 
preparat ion is defined as "heroin."  19
