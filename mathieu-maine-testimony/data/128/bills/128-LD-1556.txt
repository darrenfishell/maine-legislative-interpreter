SUMMARY  34 
Currently, ce rtain mandated reporters of child abuse are required to report to the 35 
Department of Health and Human Services when they know or suspect that an infant had 36 
been exposed to drugs or alcohol prior to birth.  This bill extends that mandate to all 37 
mandated repo rters of child abuse, adds substance abuse addiction treatment providers to 38 
the list of mandated reporters and requires all mandated reporters to report to the 39
