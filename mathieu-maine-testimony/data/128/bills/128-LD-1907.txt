Fiscal Detail and Notes
Additional costs to the Maine Public Employees Retirement System (MainePERS) to continue the review of the State 
Employee and Teacher Retirement Plan, including staffing the existing working group and preparing the required report, can be absorbed within its existing budgeted resources.  The additional costs to continue serving as a member of the working group can be absorbed by the Department of Administrative and Financial Services.Fiscal Note Required: Yes
