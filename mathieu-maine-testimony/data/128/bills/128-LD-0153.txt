SUMMARY  21 
This bill prohibits the operating of a motor vehicle by a person who is receiving 22 
methadone for treatment of opioid dependency or for use as a pain medication.  The 23 
Secretary of State shall amend its Chapter 3 rules, "Physical, Emotional and Mental 24 
Competence to Operate a Motor Vehicle," to require a health care practitioner or facility 25 
that is treating an individual with methadone to report to the Department of the Secretary 26 
of State, Bureau of Motor Vehicles.  These rules are routine tec hnical rules.  27
