COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" A,, to H.P. 524, L.D. 744, Bill, "An Act To Create 
a Pennanent Wabanaki Law Enforcement Seat on the Maine Criminal Justice Academy 
Board of Trustees" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in §2802 in the last paragraph in the 3rd and 4th lines 
(page 1, lines 25 and 26 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "except that the Wabanaki 
law enforcement member serves for a term of 2 years" 
SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the committee. The amendment strikes 
from the bill the provision that limits to 2 years the term of the representative of the 
Wabanaki tribal governments.
