16 SUMMARY 
17 This amendment provides that the Department of Defense, Veterans and Emergency 
18 Management, Bureau of Maine Veterans' Services is required to represent all veterans in 
19 filing their initial claims for service-related benefits to the United States Department of 
20 Veterans Affairs. The bill applies this requirement to all claims for service-related 
21 benefits and includes claims made to other veterans' service organizations.
