SUMMARY  18 
This bill amends the law regarding municipal shellfish conservation programs to 19 
clarify that the intertidal zone extends from the high -water mark to the extreme low -water 20 
mark and that the shellfish conservation ordinances apply only within the intertidal zones 21 
of municipa lities.  22
