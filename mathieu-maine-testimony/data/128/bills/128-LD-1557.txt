SUMMARY  12 
This bill requires that when health care services are provided to a covered person by a 13 
nonparticipating facility -based provider at a participating facility and the covered person 14 
was unaware or could not reasonably be expected to have been aware that the services 15 
were being provided by a nonparticipating facility -based provider, the covered person 16 
may not be billed for those health care services.  The bill requires the imple mentation of 17 
associated notification and mediation processes. The bill also requires health carriers to 18 
make available provider directories.  19
