 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  P&SL 1971, c. 86, as amended by P&SL 1991, c. 36, is repealed.  2 
Sec. 2.  Transition provisions.   The following provisions govern the transition of 3 
the Cumberland County Recreation Center  to Cumberland County.  4 
1.  Cumberland County is the successor in every way to the powers, duties and 5 
functions of the Cumberland County Recreation Center, referred to in this section as "the 6 
center."  7 
2.  All existing rules, regulations and procedures of th e center continue in effect 8 
unless rescinded, revised or in conflict with Cumberland County rules and regulations or 9 
amended by the county commissioners of Cumberland County.  10 
3.  All real and personal property of the center, including but not limited to fi xtures, 11 
records and equipment owned or leased by the center, is transferred to Cumberland 12 
County and becomes the property of Cumberland County.  13 
4.  All existing contracts, agreements and obligations of the center, including bonds 14 
or other debt instruments and management agreements, continue in effect, and the rights 15 
and obligations of the center become the rights and obligations of Cumberland County.  16 
5.  The Cumberland County commissioners shall adopt the budget of and act as 17 
funding authority for the opera tions of the Cross Insurance Arena, an enterprise fund 18 
under the oversight of the county commissioners.  The county commissioners shall meet 19 
with the Board of Trustees under subsection 6 as needed, but at least annually, to address 20 
the needs of the Cross I nsurance Arena.  21 
6.  There is established a 9 -member Board of Trustees, the members of which are 22 
appointed by the Cumberland County commissioners, which shall review and make 23 
recommendations to the county commissioners on financial, contractual and policy 24 
issues.  25 
The Board of Trustees consists of 2 members appointed from the City of Portland, 2 26 
members who are at large and one member from each of the following sets of towns: 27 
South Portland and Cape Elizabeth; Scarborough, Westbrook and Falmouth; Gorham, 28 
Wind ham, Standish, Baldwin and Sebago; Brunswick, Freeport, Harpswell, Pownal and 29 
New Gloucester; and Yarmouth, Cumberland, North Yarmouth, Gray, Casco, Raymond, 30 
Naples, Harrison and Bridgton. Trustee members are appointed for a term of 3 years and 31 
may not ser ve more than 2 consecutive terms.  32 
The Board of Trustees shall propose and recommend policy and procedures for the Cross 33 
Insurance Arena.  The Board of Trustees shall serve as the budget review panel for the 34 
Cross Insurance Arena.  The Board of Trustees sha ll meet monthly or as workload 35 
requires.  The Board of Trustees shall meet with the county commissioners as needed, but 36 
at least annually, to address the needs of the Cross Insurance Arena.  37
