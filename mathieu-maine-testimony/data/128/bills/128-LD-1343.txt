SUMMARY  4 
This bill establishes the Locating Businesses Downtown Loan Program within the 5 
Communities for Maine's Future Program to provide forgivable loans for businesses 6 
seeking to initially locate or t o relocate in a downtown area, village area or along a main 7 
street within the State.  Applications for loans under the program are evaluated by the 8 
Department of Economic and Community Development in conjunction with a loan 9 
review panel.  Successful applic ants must execute a loan agreement prepared by the 10 
department specifying the terms and conditions of the loan, including the length of time 11 
that a business must remain in the downtown area, village area or along a main street 12 
within the State for the loan to be forgiven.  13
