 Sec. 1.  Motorcycle rider education rules.  Resolved:  That the Department of 1 
the Secretary of State shall amend its rules on motorcycle rider education to:  2 
1.  Remove the restriction preventing a motorcycle instructor from teaching more 3 
than one class per  day; 4 
2.  Remove the requirement that a motorcycle school have a training range and a 5 
classroom at the same site;  6 
3.  Allow no more than one unannounced inspection of a motorcycle school per 7 
month; and  8 
4.  Increase the allowed maximum number of students in  a motorcycle rider 9 
education class to 24 and in a driver education class to 35.  10 
SUMMARY  11 
This resolve directs the Department of the Secretary of State to amend its rules on 12 
motorcycle rider education regarding instructor teaching hours, motorcycle school 13 
requirements and inspections and the number of students allowed in a class.  14
