SUMMARY  12 
This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to reimburse 100% 13 
of the child care expenses incurred by an  adult relative guardian of a minor if the 14 
guardian is 60 years of age or older and has an annual income of $50,000 or less.  The bill 15 
also prohibits the department from requiring the guardian to be employed a minimum 16 
number of hours per week in order to r eceive the reimbursement.  17
