SUMMARY  15 
This bill makes it a Class C crime if a person who is required to maintain proof of 16 
financial responsibility operates a vehicle, or knowingly allows the operation of that 17 
vehicle, on a public way without such proof and that vehic le is involved in an accident 18 
that results in serious bodily injury while being operated on the public way.  This bill also 19 
increases from a Class E to a Class D crime the penalty for such a person operating a 20 
vehicle or knowingly allowing the operation of  a vehicle without proof of financial 21 
responsibility.  22
