COMMITTEE AMENDMENT ",i,, to H.P. 903, L.D. 1306, Bill, "An Act To 
Create the Small Communities Tourism Fund" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Create a Small Communities Tourism Grant Program' 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
summary and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. 5 MRSA § 13090-K, sub-§4 is enacted to read: 
4. Small communities tourism grant program. To the extent funds are available, a 
minimum of $10,000 of the funds received annually by the Tourism Marketing 
Promotion Fund in accordance with subsection 2 must be used for a small communities 
tourism grant program, referred to in this subsection as "the program." Program funds 
must be used to award grants of $1,000 to $2,000 to municipalities or nonprofit agencies 
to match public and private funds to promote tourism in small communities and events 
that are designed to attract individuals who reside at least 50 miles from those small 
communities. Grant recipients may not receive a 2nd or subsequent award from the 
program for the same event but may receive grants from other programs funded by the 
Tourism Marketing Promotion Fund for that event. For the purposes of this subsection, 
"small community" means a municipality with a population of fewer than 7,000 
individuals.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, replaces the bill. 
The amendment creates a small communities tourism grant program to issue matching 
grants to small communities to promote tourism and events. The $10,000 annual funding
