 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 1 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and  2 
Whereas, this legislation requires the upward adjustment of the salary schedules of 3 
judicial marsha ls and sergeants beginning on or about July 1, 2017; and  4 
Whereas, the 90 -day period may not expire until after the date the upward salary 5 
adjustment is required to begin; and  6 
Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency with in 7 
the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 8 
immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 9 
therefore,  10 
Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  11 
Sec. 1.  A djustment of salary schedules for judicial marshals and 12 
sergeants in fiscal year 2017 -18.  Notwithstanding any other provision of law, 13 
effective at the beginning of the pay period commencing closest to July 1, 2017, the 14 
salary schedules must be adjusted up ward by 20% for a position classified as Judicial 15 
Marshal or Judicial Sergeant in the judicial branch that is included in the law enforcement 16 
bargaining unit or the supervisory services bargaining unit.  17 
Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 18 
legislation takes effect when approved.  19 
SUMMARY  20 
This bill provides for a 20% upward adjustment in the salary schedules of judicial 21 
marshals and judicial sergeants in the judicial branch beginning with the pay period 22 
commencing closest to  July 1, 2017.  23
