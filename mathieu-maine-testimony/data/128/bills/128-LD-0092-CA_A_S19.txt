24 SUMMARY 
25 This amendment is the minority report of the committee. The amendment replaces 
26 the bill and changes the title. The amendment provides that in using a sentencing 
27 alternative involving a term of imprisonment for a person convicted of unlawful 
28 furnishing of a scheduled drug in which death was in fact caused by the use of the 
29 scheduled drug or unlawful trafficking in a scheduled drug in which death was in fact 
30 caused by the use of the scheduled drug, a court must assign special weight to this 
31 objective fact in determining the basic term of imprisonment as the first step in the 
32 sentencing process.

Fiscal Note 
Court shall assign special weight to certain facts 
This bill requires a court to assign special weight to certain facts during sentencing. Given that judges may already be 
weighing this infonnation in establishing sentences, it is not possible to predict whether future sentences and related 
costs will be changed by this bill.
