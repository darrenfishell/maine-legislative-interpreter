5.  Game of chance.  "Game of chance" means a game, contest, scheme or device in 4

A.  A person stakes or risks something of value for the opportunity to win something 6

B.  The rules of operation or play require an event the result of which  outcome  is 8

SUMMARY  29 
This bill specifies, for the purposes of the laws governing games of chance, that 30 
games of chance ar e those games in which the outcome is solely determined by chance, 31 
outside the control of the contestant or participant.  32
