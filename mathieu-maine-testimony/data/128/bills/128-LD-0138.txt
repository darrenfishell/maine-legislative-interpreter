SUMMARY  5 
This bill requires a person to re gister on the registry of sex offenders maintained by 6 
the Department of Public Safety, Bureau of State Police, State Bureau of Identification 7 
when that person is a teacher, employee or other official having instructional, supervisory 8 
or disciplinary author ity who is convicted of a Class D or Class C offense of gross sexual 9 
assault, unlawful sexual contact or unlawful sexual touching and the victim, regardless of 10 
the victim's age, is a student over whom that person has control.  Class D sex offenses are 11 
considered Tier I offenses, requiring the offender to register for up to 10 years, and Class 12 
C sex offenses are considered Tier II offenses, requiring the offender to register for up to 13 
25 years.  14
