15 §353-A.  Maine National Guard Postsecondary  Fund
16 The Maine National  Guard Postsecondary  Fund, referred to in this section as "the 
17 fund," is established in the Military Bureau as a nonlapsing account in the General Fund  
18 to provide  tuition benefits for eligible Maine  National  Guard  members  to state 
19 postsecondary education institutions and private nonprofit postsecondary education 
20 institutions.  Deposits to the fund may come from sources  including but not limited to: the 
21 Armory Rental Fund  established in section 152; the Capital Repair, Maintenance, 
22 Construction and Acquisition Account established in section  154; the reimbursement fund 
23 established in section 155; revenue  generated  from the Maine  Military Authority; and 
24 rental income fees under Title 5, section 1742, subsection 26, paragraph  B.  The Adjutant 
25 General is responsible  for oversight and allocation of these  funds in accordance with this 
26 subchapter.  The Adjutant  General shall provide a report to the Commissioner of 
27 Education on the first day of January  each calendar year accounting for the use of all 
28 funds in the fund.

Page 3 - 128LR2746(01)-11 SUMMARY
2 This bill broadens  the tuition benefit program that is available to members of the 
3 Maine National  Guard who attend a state postsecondary  education institution  to provide  a 
4 tuition benefit  for members who attend a private  nonprofit postsecondary education 
5 institution in the State.  The bill limits  the tuition benefit at private nonprofit 
6 postsecondary education  institutions to the tuition  at the Maine  Maritime Academy  
7 during the  prior academic year.
