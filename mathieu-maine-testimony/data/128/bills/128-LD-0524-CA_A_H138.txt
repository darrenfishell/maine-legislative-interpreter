COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A " to H.P. 368, L.D. 524, Bill, "An Act To 
Amend the Laws on Domestic Violence " 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and before the summary and 
inserting the following: 
'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and 
Whereas, to ensure the necessary and proper prosecution of crimes in the State 
involving domestic violence and to enhance the protection of victims of domestic 
violence from their abusers, this legislation must take effect immediately; and 
Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
therefore, 
Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows: 
Sec. 1. 17-A MRSA §§208-D, 208-E and 208-F are enacted to read: 
§208-D. Domestic violence aggravated assault 
1. A person is guilty of domestic violence aggravated assault if: 
A. The person violates section 208; and 
B. The victim is a family or household member as defined in Title 19-A, section 
4002, subsection 4. 
2. Violation of this section is a Class B crime.
