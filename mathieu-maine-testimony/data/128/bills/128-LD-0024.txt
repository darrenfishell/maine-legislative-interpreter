 Mandate preamble. This measure requires one or more local units of government 1 
to expand or modify activities so as to necessitate additional expenditures from local 2 
revenues but does not provide funding for at least 90% of those expenditures.  Pursuant to 3 
the Constitution of Maine, Article IX, Section 21, 2/3 of all of the members elected to 4 
each House have determined it necessary to enact this measure.  5 
Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  6 
Sec. 1.  Sinclair.   Wherever the designatio n Township 17, Range 4, WELS, appears 7 
or reference is made to that unorganized territory, that designation or reference means 8 
Sinclair.  9 
Sec. 2.  Referendum for ratification.   This Act must be submitted to the legal 10 
voters residing in the unorganized territ ory of Township 17, Range 4, WELS, in 11 
Aroostook County. The date of the submission must be determined by the Aroostook 12 
County Board of Commissioners but may not be later than 6 months after adjournment of 13 
the First Regular Session of the 128th Legislature.  The Aroostook County commissioners 14 
are authorized to expend such funds as necessary to implement the referendum.  15 
The county commissioners shall cause the preparation of the required ballots, on 16 
which they shall state the subject matter of this Act in the following question:  17 
"Do you favor changing the name of Township 17, Range 4, WELS, to 18 
Sinclair?"  19 
The voters shall indicate by a cross or a check mark placed against the word "Yes" or 20 
"No" their opinion of the question.  21 
The result of the election must be de clared by the Aroostook County commissioners 22 
and due certificate filed with the Secretary of State.  23 
Sec. 3.  Effective date.   The name change in section 1 of this Act takes effect 24 
immediately upon its acceptance by a majority of the legal voters voting at the election 25 
held pursuant to section 2.  26 
SUMMARY  27 
This bill authorizes the Aroostook County Board of Commissioners to seek approval 28 
from the residents of Township 17, Range 4, WELS, to rename the township to Sinclair.  29
