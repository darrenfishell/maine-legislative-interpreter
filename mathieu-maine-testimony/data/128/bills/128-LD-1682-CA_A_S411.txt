COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A "to S.P. 618, L.D.1682 
1 SUMMARY 
2 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, adds definitions of 
3 "person recovering from a substance use disorder" and "recovery residence" and directs 
4 the Department of Health and Human Services to establish a voluntary certification 
5 process for recovery residences. It also directs the Maine State Housing Authority to 
6 create a pilot project to provide a short-term rental subsidy to a person recovering from a 
7 substance use disorder to reside in a certified recovery residence. 
8 It also adds recovery to substance abuse prevention and treatment in the context of 
9 activities and services under the laws addressing alcohol and drug abuse. It also adds an 
10 appropriations and allocations section.
