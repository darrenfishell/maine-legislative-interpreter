SUMMARY  10 
This bill provides that if an employee is actively participating in a rehabilitation plan 11 
ordered by the Workers' Compensation Board or en tered into voluntarily with the 12 
employer, there is a conclusive presumption that work is unavailable to the employee for 13 
as long as the employee continues to actively participate in employment rehabilitation, 14 
and during the employee’s active participation in the rehabilitation plan, benefits must be 15 
paid to the employee.  16
