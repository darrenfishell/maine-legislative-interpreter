 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  12 MRSA §6856, sub -§§1-A and 2 -A are enacted to read:  2 
1-A.  Certificate fee.   The fee for a shellfish sanitation certificate is $100.  3 
2-A.  Express authorization fees.   The commissioner  shall charge a fee for each 4 
additional authorized activity that is provided to the holder of a shellfish sanitation 5 
certificate through the issuance of a permit.  The fee for each additional authorized 6 
activity is $50.  7 
Sec. 2.  12 MRSA §6856, sub -§9 is enacted to read:  8 
9.  Disposition of fees.   The commissioner shall deposit fees collected pursuant to 9 
this section into the Shellfish Fund under section 6651.  10 
SUMMARY  11 
This bill establishes a fee of $100 for a shellfish sanitation certificate and a fee of $50 12 
for each additional authorized activity provided to the holder of a shellfish sanitation 13 
certificate.  The fees collected are deposited into the Shellfish Fund.  14
