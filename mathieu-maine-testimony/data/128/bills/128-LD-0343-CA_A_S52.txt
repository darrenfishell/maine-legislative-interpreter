9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 109, L.D. 343, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Prohibit the Discharge of a Firearm within 300 Feet of a State-owned Boat Launching 
11 Ramp" 
12 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
13 summary and inserting the following: 
14 'Sec. 1. 12 MRSA §11209-A is enacted to read: 
15 §11209-A. Discharge of firearm within 300 feet of state-owned boat launching ramp 
16 1. Prohibition. A person may not discharge a firearm within 300 feet of a state-
17 owned boat launching ramp that is posted in accordance with subsection 2. 
18 2. Posting. An agency of the State having jurisdiction over a state-owned boat 
19 launching ramp may post notice, in a prominent location at the boat launching ramp, that 
20 the discharge of a firearm is prohibited within 300 feet of the boat launching ramp. 
21 3. Exception. This section does not apply to a law enforcement officer in the 
22 performance of the law enforcement officer's official duties. 
23 4. Penalty. A person who violates subsection 1 commits a Class E crime.' 
24 SUMMARY 
25 This amendment replaces the bill and provides that an agency of the State having 
26 jurisdiction over a state-owned boat launching ramp may post notice, in a prominent 
27 location at the boat launching ramp, that the discharge of a firearm is prohibited within 
28 300 feet of the boat launching ramp. A person who discharges a firearm within 300 feet 
29 of a boat launching ramp that is posted in accordance with this provision commits a Class 
30 E crime.
