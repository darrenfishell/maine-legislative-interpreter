SUMMARY  22 
This bill removes the sunset provision from the consolidated law governing children's 23 
guardians ad litem in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 4, chapter 32.  24 
This bill also requires a guardian ad li tem appointed as the court’s agent to represent 25 
the best interests of one or more children to prepare reports to the court and the parties 26 
using court -approved forms.  Each report must demonstrate that the guardian ad litem 27 
fulfilled each of the specific d uties assigned to the guardian ad litem in the court’s order 28 
of appointment.  29
