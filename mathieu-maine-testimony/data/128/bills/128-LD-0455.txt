 SUMMARY  1 
This bill amends the laws governing the practice of pharmacy to expressly include 2 
tobacco cessation counseling services and th e furnishing of nicotine replacement products 3 
approved by the federal Food and Drug Administration.  It also requires pharmacists to be 4 
reimbursed by the MaineCare program for these services.  It requires the Department of 5 
Health and Human Services to adop t rules by January 1, 2018 for the reimbursement of 6 
pharmacists for counseling services.  It also requires the Department of Professional and 7 
Financial Regulation, Maine Board of Pharmacy to adopt rules by January 1, 2018 in 8 
consultation with the Board of Licensure in Medicine for the provision of tobacco 9 
cessation counseling services by pharmacists and the furnishing by pharmacists of 10 
nicotine replacement products that are approved by the federal Food and Drug 11 
Administration.  12
