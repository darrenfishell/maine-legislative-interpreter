 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  24-A MRSA §2902 -C, as enacted by PL 1991, c. 106, is repealed.  2 
Sec. 2.  24-A MRSA §2902 -H is enacted to read:  3 
§2902 -H.  Actions based on age of applicant or insured prohibited  4 
1.  Prohi bited actions based on age.  An insurer may not do any of the following 5 
solely because an applicant or insured is a certain age or has reached a certain age:  6 
A.  Refuse to issue a motor vehicle insurance policy;  7 
B.  Impose a surcharge on or otherwise incre ase the rate for a motor vehicle insurance 8 
policy;  9 
C.  Cancel or refuse to renew a motor vehicle insurance policy; or  10 
D.  Limit coverage under a motor vehicle insurance policy.  11 
2.  Other factors.  This section does not prohibit an insurer from refusing to issue, 12 
imposing a surcharge on or otherwise raising the rate for, cancelling or refusing to renew 13 
or limiting coverage under a motor vehicle insurance policy based upon factors other than 14 
the age of an applicant or insured.  15 
Sec. 3.  24-A MRSA §2916, as ena cted by PL 1973, c. 339, §1, is repealed.  16 
SUMMARY  17 
This bill repeals 2 provisions in current law related to the issuance of motor vehicle 18 
insurance and consideration of an insured's age by the insurer and incorporates them into 19 
one provision.  20 
This bill proh ibits an insurer from taking certain actions against an applicant or 21 
insured under a motor vehicle insurance policy based solely upon the age of the applicant 22 
or insured.  23
