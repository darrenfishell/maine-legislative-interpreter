SUMMARY  10 
This bill doubles the number of moose permits for auction by conservation 11 
organizations from 10 permits to 20 permits and directs the Commissioner of Inland 12 
Fisheries and Wildlife to disburse at least 50% of the revenue from the auction to 13 
conservation organizations for the purpose of operating youth day camps.  14
