Sec. A -9.  Referendum for ratification; submission at election; form of 3 
question; effective date.  This Part must be submitted to the legal voters of the State at 4 
a statewide election held in the month of Novemb er following passage of this Part.  The 5 
municipal officers of this State shall notify the inhabitants of their respective cities, towns 6 
and plantations to meet, in the manner prescribed by law for holding a statewide election, 7 
to vote on the acceptance or rejection of this Part by voting on the following question:  8 
"Do you favor a $75,000,000 bond issue to build Maine's workforce 9 
development capacity by modernizing and improving the facilities and 10 
infrastructure of Maine's public universities?"  11 
The legal vot ers of each city, town and plantation shall vote by ballot on this question 12 
and designate their choice by a cross or check mark placed within a corresponding square 13 
below the word "Yes" or "No."  The ballots must be received, sorted, counted and 14 
declared i n open ward, town and plantation meetings and returns made to the Secretary of 15 
State in the same manner as votes for members of the Legislature.  The Governor shall 16 
review the returns.  If a majority of the legal votes are cast in favor of this Part, the 17 
Governor shall proclaim the result without delay and this Part becomes effective 30 days 18 
after the date of the proclamation.  19 
The Secretary of State shall prepare and furnish to each city, town and plantation all 20 
ballots, returns and copies of this Part nece ssary to carry out the purposes of this 21 
referendum.  22
