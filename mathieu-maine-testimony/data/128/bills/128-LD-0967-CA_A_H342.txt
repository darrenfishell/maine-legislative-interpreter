3. Major substantive rules. Rules adopted pursuant to this section are major 
substantive rules as defined in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, 
subchapter 2-A. 
Sec. 3. Savings efforts. The Department of Health and Human Services and 
representatives of organizations of providers of community support services for 
individuals with intellectual disabilities and autism shall examine current reimbursement 
rates, costs of providing services, labor costs and administrative overhead costs to 
determine opportunities for efficiencies and savings. The department and providers shall 
present a report to the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human Services with 
findings no later than January 1, 2018.

18 This amendment directs the Depruiment of Health and Human Services to increase 
19 reimbursement rates by 10% over the reimbursement levels implemented in 2007 
20 pursuant to Public Law 2005, chapter 12, Part CCCC, section 1 for services provided 
21 under Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Sections 21 and 29 no later 
22 than October 1, 2017. The amendment narrows the services that are reimbursed to codes 
23 that provide direct support services to the MaineCare members receiving services under 
24 the waiver programs. It also directs the department and representatives of organizations 
25 of providers of community support services for individuals with intellectual disabilities 
26 and autism to examine reimbursement rates, costs of providing services and other costs to 
27 determine opportunities for efficiencies and savings. The department and the providers 
28 are required to report findings to the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human 
29 Services no later than January 1, 2018. This amendment also adds an appropriations and 
30 allocations section.
