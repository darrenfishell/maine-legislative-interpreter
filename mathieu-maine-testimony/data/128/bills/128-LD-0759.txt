SUMMARY  10 
This bill clarifies the authority of sewer districts and sanitary districts to administer 11 
financial res ources.  It allows sewer districts and sanitary districts to invest their funds in 12 
the same way as municipalities.  It clarifies that sewer districts have full authority to 13 
collect rates and fees.  14
