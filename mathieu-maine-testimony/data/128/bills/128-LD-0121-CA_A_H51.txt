14 Under the bill, a person who does not have proper photographic identification 
15 required to cast a ballot on election day has 5 days to provide photographic proof of 
16 identity to the municipal clerk, deputy clerk, warden or election clerk. This amendment, 
17 which is the minority report of the committee, reduces that time period to 3 days to reflect 
18 the number of days by which municipal election clerks must report election results to the 
19 Secretaiy of State. The amendment also adds a requirement that provisional ballots must 
20 be retained in tamper-proof containers separately from provisional ballot affidavits and 
21 the provisional ballot log. The amendment requires that rejected provisional ballots, 
22 provisional ballot logs and provisional ballot affidavits must be retained in the same 
23 manner as regular ballots and election materials. The amendment also adds a mandate 
24 preamble and an appropriations and allocations section.
