11 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 12, L.D. 11 
'"Do you favor amending the Constitution of Maine to provide that the 
right of the people of this State to hunt, trap, fish and harvest wildlife and 
fish may not be infringed, subject to reasonable laws and rules, and to 
provide that public hunting, trapping and fishing are a preferred means of 
managing and controlling wildlife?"' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, references wildlife 
instead of game and provides that trapping is included in the right to hunt, fish and 
harvest wildlife and fish.
