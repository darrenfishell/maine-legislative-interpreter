13 SUMMARY 
14 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, changes the amount 
15 in the bill that an employer may pay a person who is under 18 years of age to a minimum 
16 hourly wage that is 80% of the minimum hourly wage instead of 75% as proposed in the 
17 bill.
