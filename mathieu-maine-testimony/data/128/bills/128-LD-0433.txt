SUMMARY  30 
This bill allows a municipality that approves the location of a retail marijuana store or 31 
a retail marijuana social club to impose a local option sales tax.  Revenue from the local 32 
option sal es tax after deduction for the cost of state administration is distributed to the 33 
municipality.  34
