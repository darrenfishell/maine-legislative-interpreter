9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "/!;" to H.P. 183, L.D. 250, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Increase the Penalty for Aggravated Sex Trafficking" 
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
12 'An Act Regarding the Penalties for Aggravated Sex Trafficking' 
13 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
14 summary and inserting the following: 
15 'Sec. 1. 17-A MRSA §852, sub-§1, as amended by PL 2013, c. 407, §2, is further 
16 amended to read: 
17 1. A person is guilty of aggravated sex trafficking if the person knowingly: 
18 A. Promotes prostitution by compelling a person to enter into, engage in or remain in 
19 prostitution. Violation of this paragraph is a Class B crime; 
20 B. Promotes prostitution of a person less than 18 years old. Violation of this 
21 paragraph is a Class A crime; or 
22 C. Promotes prostitution of a person who suffers from a mental disability that is 
23 reasonably apparent or known to the actor and that in fact renders the other person 
24 substantially incapable of appraising the nature of the conduct involved. Violation of 
25 this paragraph is a Class A crime.
