SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. The amendment requires the 
election clerk to post a conspicuous notice in the voting place indicating that the citizen's 
guide to the referendum election is available for voters to read and that it provides helpful 
information regarding the referendum questions on the ballot.
