30 SUMMARY
31 This bill repeals the provisions of law relating to proficiency-based high school 
32 diploma standards.
33 The Joint Standing Committee on Education  and Cultural Affairs has not taken a 
34 position on the substance of the bill and by reporting out this bill the committee is not 
35 suggesting and does not intend to suggest that it agrees or disagrees  with any aspect  of 
36 this bill.  The committee is reporting the bill out for the sole purpose of the bill's being 
37 referred to the committee for an appropriate public hearing and subsequent processing in 
38 the normal course.
