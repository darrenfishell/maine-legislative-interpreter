SUMMARY  8 
This b ill amends the definition of "subdivision" in the laws regarding planning and 9 
land use regulation for subdivisions to specify that dwelling units are not subject to 10 
subdivision review if the municipal reviewing authority has determined that the units are 11 
otherwise subject to municipal review that is at least as stringent.  Current law specifies 12 
that leased dwelling units are not subject to subdivision review.  13
