SUMMARY  24 
This bill increases the classification of the crimes of assault on an officer, assault on 25 
an emergency medical care provider and assault on a firefighter from Class C to Class B 26 
crimes.  It also extends the application of the hate crime sentencing laws to a defendant 27 
who selects the person against whom a crime is committed because of that person's status 28 
as a law enforcement off icer, emergency medical care provider or firefighter or who 29 
selects property to damage because of such status of the owner or occupant of that 30 
property.  31
