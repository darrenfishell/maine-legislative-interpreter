SUMMARY  16 
This bill provides an exception to the law that requires a license to conduct games of 17 
beano.  The bill sta tes that certain campgrounds are not required to get a license to 18 
operate beano games if the games are offered to campground patrons and their guests 19 
only and the value of a prize awarded for winning a game does not exceed $25.  20
