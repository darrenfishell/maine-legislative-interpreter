 6.  Limitations.   The pane l coordinator may not proceed with reviews of medical 1

10.  Rulemaking.   The department shall adopt rules to implement this section, 18 
including rules on collecting information and data, selecting members of the panel, 19 
collecting and using individually identifiable health information and con ducting reviews 20

SUMMARY  27 
This bill changes the name of the maternal and infant death review panel to the 28 
maternal, fetal and infant mortality review panel and allows the panel coordinator access 29 
to medical records for the purposes of conducting a review without having to obtain 30 
permission in all cases.  31
