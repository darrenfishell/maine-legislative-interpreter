 SUMMARY  1 
This bill makes it unlawful for a member of  the clergy who is in a position of trust or 2 
authority over another person to use that clergyperson's position of trust or authority to 3 
engage in a sexual act, sexual contact or sexual touching with the other person.  The bill 4 
amends the laws on the crimes  of gross sexual assault, unlawful sexual contact and 5 
unlawful sexual touching.  6
