 SUMMARY  1 
This bill changes the state licensing authority under the Marijuana Legalization Act 2 
from the Department of Agricultur e, Conservation and Forestry to the Director of the 3 
Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations within the Department of 4 
Administrative and Financial Services.  It also establishes the Retail Marijuana 5 
Regulatory Coordination Fund to be used by th e director for rulemaking and other duties 6 
related to implementation of a regulatory scheme for the cultivating, distribution and sale 7 
of retail marijuana.  This bill also transfers $1,600,000 from unappropriated General Fund 8 
surplus to the fund.  9
