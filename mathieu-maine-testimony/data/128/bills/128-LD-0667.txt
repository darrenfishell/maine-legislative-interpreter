SUMMARY  12 
This bill repeals the Marijuana Legalization Act, which allows the commercial 13 
cultivation, sale, purchase, manufacture, possession and use of marijuana and marijuana 14 
products and the personal cultivation, possession and use of marijuana and marijuana 15 
products, and the tax that is imposed on the sale of marijuana and marijuana products, if 16 
approved by the voters at a referendum held in November 2018.  The bill also amends 17
