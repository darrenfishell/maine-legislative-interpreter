SUMMARY  11 
This bill creates an excise tax on wind energy producers for the privilege of using 12 
wind power to produce electricity from wind resources for sale or trade beginning 13 
January 1, 2018.  The tax is  equal to $1 per megawatt -hour of electricity produced as 14 
measured at the point of interconnection with an electric transmission line.  The tax first 15 
applies 3 years after the turbines producing the electricity first begin operation.  The tax 16 
does not appl y to the Federal Government, the State, a county or municipality in the State 17 
or any individual producing electricity from wind resources for that individual's personal 18 
use. 19 
Revenue from the tax is transferred to a newly created Wind Energy Impact Fund to 20 
be administered by the Public Utilities Commission.  The commission is required to 21 
establish a process for distribution of revenues to ratepayers in areas affected by the wind 22 
energy production that is subject to the tax.  23
