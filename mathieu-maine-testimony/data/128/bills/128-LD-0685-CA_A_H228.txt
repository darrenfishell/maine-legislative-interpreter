The bill includes General Fund appropriations to the Legislature of $13,350 in each of fiscal years 2017-18 and 2018-
19 for the operating and support costs of the Mining Advisory Panel. It also includes an Other Special Revenue Funds 
allocation of $500 in the event the panel receives grants and other nongovernmental funds. As required in the bill, the 
funds provided to the panel are appropriated and allocated to a new account separate from all other funds of the 
Legislature. 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Any additional costs to the Department of Environmental Protection to participate in the Mining Advis01y Panel are 
anticipated to be minor and can be absorbed within existing budgeted resources.
