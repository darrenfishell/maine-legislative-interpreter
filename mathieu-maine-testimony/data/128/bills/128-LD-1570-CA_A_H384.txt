SUMMARY 
This amendment makes the following changes. 
1. It removes from the bill a provision requiring a person filing a statement of appeal 
with the Board of Tax Appeals or a petition for review with the Superior Court to serve 
the statement or petition by certified mail, return receipt requested, upon the Attorney 
General and the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of 
Revenue Services. 
2. It removes from the bill language restricting the disclosure to and use of 
confidential information by persons for whom disclosure is pe1mitted. 
3. It removes the repeal of a section relating to prepaid wireless fees and reverses 
provisions in the bill intended to clarify certain sales and use tax refund procedures.
