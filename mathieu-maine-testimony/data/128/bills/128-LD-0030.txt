SUMMARY  15 
Current law provides that dancing and some other forms of entertainment are 16 
prohibited on the premises of an establishment  licensed to sell liquor for consumption on 17 
the premises unless the licensee has been issued a special amusement permit from the 18 
municipality where the establishment is located.  This bill provides that dancing does not 19 
include dancing done in conjunction with the singing activity commonly known as 20 
"karaoke."  21
