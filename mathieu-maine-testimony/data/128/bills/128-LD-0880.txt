SUMMARY  15 
This bill provides that a writ of possession may not issue in a foreclosure action that 16 
involves a residential mortgage until the mortgagor's period of redemption expires.  The 17 
period of redemption exp ires 90 days from the date of judgment for mortgages executed 18 
on or after October 1, 1975 and one year from the date of judgment for mortgages 19 
executed prior to October 1, 1975.  20
