SUMMARY  12 
This bill extends from 9 months to 15 months the time within which the percentage of 13 
germination test described under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 7, section 1043 must 14 
have been performed on cool -weather  lawn and turf seed in order for that seed to be sold.  15 
"Cool -weather lawn and turf seed" is defined to include Kentucky bluegrass, red fescue, 16 
Chewing's fescue, hard fescue, tall fescue, perennial ryegrass, intermediate ryegrass, 17 
annual ryegrass, colonial  bentgrass and creeping bentgrass.  18
