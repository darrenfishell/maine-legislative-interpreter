SUMMARY  25 
This bill specifies that a person is eligible to attend schools in the school 26 
administrative unit where the person's parent or surrogate resides and defines a surrogate 27 
as an adult related to a minor and from whom the minor receives th e ongoing care and 28 
support expected of a parent or, if no such relatives exist, an adult with whom the minor 29 
resides and who has provided the minor with the ongoing care and support expected of a 30 
parent.  31
