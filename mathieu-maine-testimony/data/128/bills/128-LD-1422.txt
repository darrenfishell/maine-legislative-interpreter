SUMMARY  7 
This bill requires that, in order for a candidate for President or a candidate for Vice 8 
President to appear on a general election ballot, the candidate for President or the 9 
candidate for Vice President must disclose the previous 5 years of that candidate's fe deral 10 
income tax returns, which may be redacted to remove personally identifying information 11 
other than the candidate's name.  It directs the Secretary of State to post the tax returns on 12 
the Secretary of State's website and to include in the secretary's r eport regarding 13 
presidential primaries pursuant to Public Law 2015, c. 474, section 5 suggested 14 
legislation requiring primary candidates to disclose federal income tax returns.  15
