 SUMMARY  1 
This bill creates an owner -operator requirement for a scallop dra gging license and sea 2 
urchin dragging license.  It changes from major substantive rules to routine technical 3 
rules the rules to allow entry in the scallop fishery.  It specifies that the prohibition 4 
against fishing for or taking scallops from April 16th to  November 30th applies only to 5 
scallop dragging.  It also allows the Commissioner of Marine Resources to amend by rule 6 
the boundaries of sea urchin Zone 1 and Zone 2.  7
