Fiscal Note 
Reduced distribution of benefits -Unemployment Compensation Trust Fund 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This legislation may result in less benefits being distributed from the Unemployment Compensation Trust Fund as a 
result of fewer individuals being eligible for the benefit. The savings to the Fund will depend on the number of labor 
disputes over time and the number of individuals that would have been eligible for benefits under current law but wil. 
no longer be as a result of this legislation.
