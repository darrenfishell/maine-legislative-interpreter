SUMMARY  5 
This bill amends the statutes to reflect that Mountain View Youth Development 6 
Center is now largely being used to house adult prisoners, though there are still retained 7 
provisions related to the juvenile detention unit.  It also re peals the provisions related to 8 
the Charleston Correctional Facility.  9
