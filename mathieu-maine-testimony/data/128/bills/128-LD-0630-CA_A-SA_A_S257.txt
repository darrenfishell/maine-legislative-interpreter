SENATE AMENDMENT" iA/' to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 446, 
L.D. 630, Bill, "An Act To Expand Opportunities for Moose Permit Winners To Swap 
Their Permits" 
Amend the amendment in section 1 in § 11110 in subsection 3 in the 2nd line (page 1, 
line 35 in amendment) by inserting after the following: "facilitate" the following: 'for 
consideration' 
Amend the amendment in section I in § 11110 in subsection 3 in the 3rd line (page 2, 
line 1 in amendment) by inserting after the following: "subsection 1." the following: 'For 
purposes of this subsection, "facilitate for consideration" means to directly receive 
compensation or something of value solely as part of the exchange of moose permits.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment defines the term "facilitate for consideration" as it relates to the 
exchange of moose permits. This amendment clarifies that the prohibition established by 
Committee Amendment "A" is only on facilitating the exchange of moose permits for 
compensation or something else of value.
