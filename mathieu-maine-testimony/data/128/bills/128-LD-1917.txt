18 SUMMARY
19 This bill provides  that a person who served on active duty in the medical corps  of any 
20 branch of the Armed  Forces of the United States  and spent an aggregate of at least 12 
21 months rendering bedside  patient care, completed the basic course of instruction in 
22 nursing required  by the branch  of the Armed  Forces  of the United  States in which the 
23 person served and was honorably discharged is eligible to take the examination  for 
24 licensure as  a licensed practical nurse.
