COMMITTEE AMENDMENT " A" to S.P. 503, L.D. 1457, Bill, "An Act To 
Rename and Repurpose the Mountain View Youth Development Center as the Mountain 
View Correctional Facility and To Eliminate the Charleston Correctional Facility as a 
Facility Separate from Mountain View" 
Amend the bill by striking out all of sections 5 and 6. 
Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment strikes those sections of the bill that address payment by the 
Department of Education for special education services and coordination of services for 
juvenile offenders in the custody of or under the supervision of the Department of 
Corrections.
