COMMITTEE AMENDIYJENT "A" to H.P. 348, L.D. 485, Bill, "An Act To Allow 
Migratory Game Birds To Be Hunted on Sundays" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Allow Hunting of Migratory Game Birds on Sundays and Prohibit 
Hunting of Migratory Game Birds on Wednesdays' 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
summary and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. 12 MRSA §11205, sub-§1, 1A, as enacted by PL 2003, c. 414, Pt. A, §2 
and affected by c. 614, §9, is amended to read: 
A. Hunt wild animals or wild birds on Sunday, except that a person may hunt 
migratory game birds, other than the American woodcock in accordance with this Part 
as long as any hunting on agricultural land is with the landowner's permission; or 
Sec. 2. 12 MRSA §11205-A is enacted to read: 
§11205-A. Hunting migratory birds on Wednesday 
1. Prohibition. A person may not: 
A. Hunt migratory game birds on Wednesday; or 
B. Possess any migratory game bird taken in violation of paragraph A except as 
otherwise provided in this Part. 
2. Penalties. A person who violates subsection 1 commits a Class E crime.'
