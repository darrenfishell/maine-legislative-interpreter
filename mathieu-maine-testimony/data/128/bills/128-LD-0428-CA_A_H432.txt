SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the Joint Standing Committee on Judiciary. 
It amends the resolution to repeal all of Article X, Section 7, resulting in the inclusion of 
the omitted sections, Sections 1, 2 and 5 of Article X, when a printed copy of the 
Constitution of Maine is prefixed to the Laws of Maine.
