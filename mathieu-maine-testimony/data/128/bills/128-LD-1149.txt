SUMMARY  14 
This bill does the following for the purpose of increasing the revenue to the Highway 15 
Fund.  16 
1.  It increases by $3 to $10 ce rtain fees charged by the Secretary of State for driver’s 17 
license examinations, temporary license plates, nondriver identification cards, issuance of 18 
duplicate registrations, titles, licenses and nondriver identification cards and transfers of 19 
registration s. 20 
2.  It imposes a $200 surcharge, dedicated to the Highway Fund, on the registration of 21 
hybrid motor vehicles, battery -electric motor vehicles and hydrogen fuel cell motor 22 
vehicles.  23 
3.  It requires 10% of the sales tax imposed on transportation -related i tems, such as 24 
motor vehicles and the products for the repair and maintenance of motor vehicles, such as 25 
motor oil, batteries and tires, to be transferred to the Highway Fund on a monthly basis.  26 
4.  It increases the tax imposed on motor vehicle fuel by 7¢ p er gallon, to 36.5¢ per 27 
gallon, beginning October 1, 2017.  28
