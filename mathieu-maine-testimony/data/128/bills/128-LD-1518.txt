§1407.  Maine Educational and Attainment Research Navigation System  6 
1.  Establishment.  The Maine Educational and Attainment Research Navigation 7 
System, referred to in this section as "the system," is established within the department 8 
for the maintenance and disseminati on of information and data by the department and 9 
partner organizations and must include, but is not limited to, information and data on 10 
education and training results, program completion, graduation, credentials earned and 11 
employment and earnings outcomes for those who complete postsecondary education and 12 
other training programs in the State over time.  13 
2.  Executive council.  The Maine Educational and Attainment Research Navigation 14 
System Executive Council, referred to in this section as "the council," is e stablished.  15 
Membership of the council must include, but is not limited to, the commissioner or the 16 
commissioner's designee, the Commissioner of Education or the commissioner's 17 
designee, the Chancellor of the University of Maine System or the chancellor's designee, 18 
the President of the Maine Community College System or the president's designee and 19 
the directors of the Bureau of Employment Services, the Bureau of Unemployment 20 
Compensation and the Bureau of Rehabilitation Services.  The council shall provide 21 
executive oversight of the development and direction of the system, including the 22 
establishment and refinement of policies and procedures for governance of the system, 23 
and shall set the priorities of the system, approve all proposed policies and rules and 24 
review annual reports.  25 
3.  Report.  The department shall report annually to the Governor and the joint 26 
standing committees of the Legislature having jurisdiction over workforce and education 27 
matters on the development, use and plans for the expansion of th e system.  28 
SUMMARY  29 
This bill establishes in statute the Maine Educational and Attainment Research 30 
Navigation System, a longitudinal data series of education and training outcomes, within 31 
the Department of Labor and creates the Maine Educational and Attainme nt Research 32 
Navigation System Executive Council to provide oversight.  It repeals the provisions 33 
establishing the State Education and Employment Outcomes Task Force, which currently 34 
develops procedures for the data series.  35
