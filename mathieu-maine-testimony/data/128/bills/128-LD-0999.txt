SUMMARY  26 
This bill provides that rules adopted by the Department of Health and Human 27 
Services for child care facilities and family child care providers must include rules 28 
pertaining to physical activity and recreational screen time and the provision of nutritious 29 
foods that contribute to the wellness, healthy growth and development of young children.  30
