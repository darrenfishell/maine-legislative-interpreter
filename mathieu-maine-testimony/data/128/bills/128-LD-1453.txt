The department shall adopt rules regulating hydraulic fracturing in order to prevent 18 
threats to drinking water resources from  hydraulic fracturing. Rules adopted or amended 19 
pursuant to this section are major substantive rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, 20 
subchapter 2 -A. A person may not engage in hydraulic fracturing in violation of rules 21 
adopted under this section.  22 
For t he purposes of this section, "hydraulic fracturing" means a stimulation technique 23 
used to increase production of oil and gas involving the injection of fluids under pressures 24 
great enough to fracture the oil -producing and gas -producing rock formations.  25 
Sec. 2.  Rules.  The Department of Environmental Protection shall provisionally 26 
adopt rules pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 38, section 490 -WW and submit 27 
those rules to the Legislature by December 31, 2017.  28 
Emergency clause.  In view of the emer gency cited in the preamble, this 29
