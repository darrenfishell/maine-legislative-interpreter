SUMMARY  17 
This bill prohibits the imposition of corporal punishment in elementary and 18 
secondary schools and preschool programs.  "Corporal punishment" means any form of 19 
discipline or punishment involving offensive physical contact with or inflicting physi cal 20 
pain or discomfort on a student.  "Corporal punishment" does not include the use of 21 
physical restraint to protect the student or others from immediate harm.  22
