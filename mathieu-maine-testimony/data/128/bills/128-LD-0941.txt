SUMMARY  17 
This bill amends the Maine State Grant Program, which is a grant program 18 
administered by the Finance Authority of Maine to award grants to Maine residents who 19 
are students at postsecondary institutions of higher education in this State based on 20 
financial need.  Under the program, the minimum grant is $1,000 per year.  21 
This bill allows the authority, using the same need and residency criteri a as for the 22 
grants, to award up to an additional $500 grant for textbooks per year.  23
