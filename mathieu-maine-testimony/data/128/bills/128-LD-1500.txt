 SUMMARY  1 
This bill conforms the laws identifying the responsibilities of the Commissioner of 2 
Labor relating to the collection and use of data with the Department of Labor's current 3 
practices of collecting and publishing data and allows for this info rmation as well as 4 
information on labor laws to be made available in various formats, including in electronic 5 
formats.  The bill adds the Director of the Bureau of Unemployment Compensation to the 6 
list of employees who are appointed by the Commissioner of Labor and who serve at the 7 
commissioner's pleasure. The bill repeals sections of law regarding the availability of 8 
information to trainees.  The bill also repeals the Nontraditional Occupation Act.  9
