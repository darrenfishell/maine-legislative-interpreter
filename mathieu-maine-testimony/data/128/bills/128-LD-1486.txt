 SUMMARY  1 
This bill requires the Superintendent of Insurance to maintain as confidential a 2 
document or information received from the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority or 3 
the National Association of Registered Agents and Brokers if the document or 4 
information has been provided to the superintendent with notice that it is confidential 5 
unde r the laws of the jurisdiction that is the source of the document or information.  6 
The bill authorizes the Bureau of Insurance to enter into agreements for the sharing of 7 
otherwise confidential information with the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority an d 8 
the National Association of Registered Agents and Brokers if the recipient of the 9 
information agrees to maintain the same level of confidentiality as is available under 10 
Maine law and has demonstrated that it has the legal authority to do so.  The bill se ts 11 
license fees for nonresidents acting in this State as insurance producers pursuant to a 12 
national nonresident insurance producer license issued by the National Association of 13 
Registered Agents and Brokers and requires them to be appointed by the insurers  they 14 
represent according to the procedures otherwise applicable to appointment of producers, 15 
including the payment of appointment fees.  16
