This bill establishes the Fund for Municipalities To Improve Pedestrian Safety as a 23 
program account in the Highway Fund within the Department of Transportation.  The bill 24 
provides that the fund must be used for pedestrian safety improvements, such as lights, 25 
paint, signs, speed bumps and reconstruction of intersections.  The bill also provides that 26 
a municipality or a group of municipalities may apply for funding from the fund.  Up to 27 
2/3 of project costs for pedestrian safety improvements may be finan ced from the fund 28 
with the remainder of the costs provided by the municipality or group of municipalities.  29
