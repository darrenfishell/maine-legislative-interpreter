3 SUMMARY 
4 This amendment changes the title and replaces the bill. The bill provides that a permit 
5 is required to import exotic wildlife or possess wildlife in the State or receive exotic 
6 wildlife and stipulates the requirements for that permit. The amendment provides that a 
7 person who allows wildlife in captivity to escape that person's possession or control in 
8 violation of rules governing keeping wildlife in captivity or an additional permit 
9 condition or requirement commits a Class E crime.
