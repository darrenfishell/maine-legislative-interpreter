 §1080.  Tobacco retail establishments  1 
1.  Issuance of licenses.   The bureau may issue licenses under this section for the sale 2 
of spirits, wine and malt liquor to be consumed on the premises to tobacco retail 3 
establishments as defined in section 2, subsection  15, paragraph T -2. 4 
2.  Minors not allowed on premises.   Minors are not permitted to remain on the 5 
premises of a tobacco retail establishment licensed under this section except when:  6 
A.  The minor is accompanied by a parent, legal guardian or custodian as defined in 7 
Title 22, section 4002, subsection 5;  8 
B.  The minor is employed under section 704; or  9 
C.  The licensee does not permit consumption of liquor on the premises for a specific 10 
period of time or event.  11 
SUMMARY  12 
This bill allows the Department of Admin istrative and Financial Services, Bureau of 13 
Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations to issue licenses to tobacco retail 14 
establishments to sell spirits, wine and malt liquor for consumption on the premises of 15 
those establishments.  16
