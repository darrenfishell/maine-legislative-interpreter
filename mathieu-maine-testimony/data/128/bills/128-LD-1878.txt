21 SUMMARY
22 This bill increases  the Governor's  salary from  $70,000 per year to $150,000 per year 
23 beginning January 2019.  The bill also increases the Legislative meal and housing 
24 allowances from $32 and  $38 to $50 and  $75, respectively.
