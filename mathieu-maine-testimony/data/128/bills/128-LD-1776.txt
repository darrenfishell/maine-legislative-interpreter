7 SUMMARY
8 This bill enacts a definition of "civil deputy," codifies the designation and duties of 
9 civil deputies,  requires payment  to civil deputies at a reasonable rate of compensation  
10 established by the county  commissioners  and authorizes sheriffs  to adopt  rules, 
11 procedures and requirements applicable to civil  deputies.
