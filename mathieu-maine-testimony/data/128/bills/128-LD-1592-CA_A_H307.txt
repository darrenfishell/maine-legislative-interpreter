SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies that examination fees, not examinations, may be waived for 
applicants for licensure who are military veterans.

Minor revenue decrease -Other Special Revenue Funds 
Additional costs to the Office of Professional and Occupational Regulation within the Department of Professional and 
Financial Regulation, to implement the requirements of this legislation can be absorbed within existing budgeted 
resources. Any reduction in revenue from waiving examination fees and license fees for applicants who identify 
themselves as veterans with military service, experience and training will be absorbed by the various boards where 
such waivers occur.
