 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  32 MRSA §15208 -A, as amended by PL 2013, c. 70, Pt. D, §13, is further 2 
amended by adding at the end a new paragraph to read:  3 
This section does not apply to a person, firm, partnership, association, corporation or 4 
company whose primary business is the installation or servicing of overhead garage doors 5 
or overhead garage door openers that also engages in the business of servicing, repairing, 6 
altering or installing dumbwaiters used only for  the transportation of inanimate goods.  7 
Sec. 2.  32 MRSA §15213, first ¶, as amended by PL 2013, c. 70, Pt. D, §20, is 8 
further amended to read:  9 
A person may not service, repair, alter or install any elevator unless that person is 10 
licensed as an elevator or  lift mechanic under this chapter , except that a person who 11 
installs or services overhead garage doors or overhead garage door openers may service, 12 
repair, alter and install dumbwaiters used only for the transportation of inanimate goods 13 
without being so l icensed .  Elevator work in industrial plants and manufacturing plants 14 
may be performed by plant personnel who are not licensed under this chapter if the work 15 
is supervised by the plant engineer and performed in compliance with rules adopted by 16 
the director . 17 
SUMMARY  18 
This bill allows a person who installs or services overhead garage doors or openers to 19 
install and repair dumbwaiters used only for the transportation of inanimate goods 20 
without being licensed as an elevator or lift mechanic and exempts a company  that 21 
installs or services overhead garage doors or openers from having to register as an 22 
elevator contractor in order to install or service dumbwaiters used only for the 23 
transportation of inanimate goods.  24
