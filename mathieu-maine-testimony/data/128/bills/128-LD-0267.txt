SUMMARY  8 
This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rules 9 
in Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 15 to increase 10 
reimbursement rates for chiropractic services for manipulative treatments under 11 
procedure codes 98940, 98941 and 98942 to no less than $30 per treatment.  The rules are 12 
routine technical rules and must be amended no later than January 1, 2018.  13
