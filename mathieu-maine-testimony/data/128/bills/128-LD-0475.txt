CONCEPT DRAFT  2 
SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to require a law enforcement officer who has administered 5 
naloxone hydrochloride to a person to provide the name and contact information for that 6 
person to the nearest publicly funded mental health treatment provider.  It requires a 7 
treatment provider receiving that information to contact the person and inform the person 8 
of available options regardin g drug addiction treatment.  9
