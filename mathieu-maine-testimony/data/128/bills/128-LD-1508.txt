SUMMARY  7 
This bill establishes a cat license that is optional for cat owners. This bill also directs 8 
the Commissioner of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to develop strategies to 9 
encourage owners of dogs and cats to license their pets, including an advertising and 10 
public outreach campaign promoting licensure; offering incentives such as coupons and 11 
rewards cards donated by participating businesses; developing an exclusive logo or 12 
certificate that may be displayed by supporting businesses; and coordinating with 13 
municipalities and other entities authorized to license dogs and cats to disseminate 14 
information, notices and flyers promoting licensure.  15
