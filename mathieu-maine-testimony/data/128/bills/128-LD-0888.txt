SUMMARY  19 
This bill requires that for property to be forfeited under the criminal forfeiture laws 20 
the owner of the property be con victed of a crime in which the property was involved, 21 
and it prohibits a law enforcement agency or other entity from circumventing the law by 22 
an agreement to transfer or refer the property seized to a federal agency.  This bill also 23 
requires that the recor ds of forfeited property be posted by the Department of Public 24 
Safety on a publicly accessible website.  25
