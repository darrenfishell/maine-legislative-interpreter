Page 1 - 128LR3054(01)-11 Emergency preamble. Whereas,  acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
2 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as  emergencies; and
3 Whereas, this resolve names a bridge in honor of a law enforcement  officer who 
4 was killed in the line of  duty; and
5 Whereas, it is important to the local community to honor  the late Corporal  Eugene 
6 Cole and to keep his memory  alive through  the naming of this bridge  over the Kennebec  
7 River in the Town of Norridgewock and to honor  his memory for all his contributions  to 
8 the safety of  Somerset County; and
9 Whereas, in the judgment  of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
10 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine  and require  the following legislation as 
11 immediately necessary  for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety;  now, 
12 therefore, be it
13 Sec. 1.  Bridge over the Kennebec River  in Town of Norridgewock named.   
14 Resolved:  That the Department of Transportation shall designate Bridge 2187 on Route  
15 201A, which crosses the Kennebec River in the Town of Norridgewock, the Corporal 
16 Eugene Cole  Memorial Bridge.
17 Emergency clause.  In view  of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
18 legislation takes effect when  approved.
19 SUMMARY
20 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation  to designate Bridge  2187 on 
21 Route 201A, which crosses the Kennebec River in the Town of Norridgewock, the 
22 Corporal Eugene  Cole Memorial  Bridge.
