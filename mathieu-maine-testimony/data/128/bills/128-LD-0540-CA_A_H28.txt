COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "It" to H.P. 384, L.D. 540, Bill, "An Act To Help 
Municipalities Prepare for Sea Level Rise" 
Amend the bill by striking out all of section 6. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment removes from the bill the prov1s10n that directs government 
agencies responsible for regulating, planning, developing or managing coastal resources 
to conduct their activities consistent with the policy of encouraging the assessment of and 
planning for the effects of the rise in sea level.

Fiscal Note 
Minor cost increase -Other Special Revenue Funds 
Any additional costs to the Department of Environmental Protection or the Department of Agriculture, Conservation 
and Forestry to encourage the assessment of and planning for the effects of a change in sea level on relevant state, 
regional, municipal or privately held infrastructure, property or resources are expected to be minor and can be 
absorbed within existing budgeted resources. 
Municipalities may incur costs if they choose to rewrite existing comprehensive plans to incorporate the assessment of 
and planning for the effects of a change in sea level. Such rewrites would be voluntary and any related costs are not 
expected to be significant.
