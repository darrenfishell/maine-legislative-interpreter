SENATE AMENDMENT" A" to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 536, 
L.D. 756, Bill, "An Act To Clarify the Authority of an Affiliate of a Utility To Own 
Power Generation outside of the Utility's Territory" 
Amend the amendment in section 1 in subsection 11 by inserting after the first 
blocked paragraph a new blocked paragraph to read: 
'Any affiliate generation or generation-related assets permitted under this subsection that 
do not have a commission-approved long-term contract or term sheet under this Title as 
of July 1, 2017 are ineligible to participate in a long-term contract under this Title.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment provides that any affiliate generation or generation-related assets 
that are permitted under the bill but do not have a long-term contract or term sheet 
approved by the Public Utilities Commission as of July l, 2017 are ineligible to 
participate in a long-term contract under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 35-A.
