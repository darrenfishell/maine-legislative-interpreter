SUMMARY  28 
This bill adds employees of the Maine Corr ectional Center, Long Creek Youth 29 
Development Center, Downeast Correctional Facility, Mountain View Youth 30 
Development Center and Charleston Correctional Facility to the retirement laws 31 
governing employees of the Maine State Prison, including the 1998 Speci al Plan.  32 
This bill provides that service retirement benefits for a Capitol Police officer in the 33 
employment of the Department of Public Safety must be computed on the basis of all of 34 
the member's creditable service, regardless of when that service was earn ed. 35
