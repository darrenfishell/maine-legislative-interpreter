ยง2936.  Sharing of training costs for dispatchers  3 
Whenever a full -time dispatcher has received the basic training provided to full -time 4 
dispatchers by the bureau at the expense of a particular public safety answering point and 5 
is subsequently hired by another public safety answering point as a full -time dispatcher 6 
within 5 years of the end of the dispatcher's training, the public safety answering point 7 
shall reimburse the first public safety answering point according to the formula under this 8 
section, unless a mutual agreement is reached.  9 
1.  Full amount of cost.   If the dispatcher is hired by the other public safety 10 
answering point during the first year after the end of the dispatcher's training, that public 11 
safety answering point shall reimburse the first public safety answering point the full 12 
amount of the training costs.  13 
2.  Eighty percent of costs.   If the dispatcher is hired by the other public safet y 14 
answering point during the 2nd year after the end of the dispatcher's training, that public 15 
safety answering point shall reimburse the first public safety answering point 80% of the 16 
training costs.  17 
3.  Sixty percent of costs.   If the dispatcher is hired by the other public safety 18 
answering point during the 3rd year after the end of the dispatcher's training, that public 19 
safety answering point shall reimburse the first public safety answering point 60% of the 20 
training costs.  21 
4.  Forty percent of costs.   If the dispatcher is hired by the other public safety 22 
answering point during the 4th year after the end of the dispatcher's training, that public 23 
safety answering point shall reimburse the first public safety answering point 40% of the 24 
training costs.  25 
5.  Twenty percent of costs.   If the dispatcher is hired by the other public safety 26 
answering point during the 5th year after the end of the dispatcher's training, that public 27 
safety answering point shall reimburse the first public safety answering point 20% of the 28 
training costs.  29 
6.  No obligation.   If the dispatcher ended training more than 5 years before 30 
subsequently being hired by the other public safety answering point, that public safety 31 
answering point is not obligated to reimburse the first public safety answering point.  32 
If the dispatcher is subsequently hired by additional public safety answering points 33 
within 5 years of the end of the dispatcher's training, each of those public safety 34 
answering points is liable to the public safety answering point immedi ately preceding it 35 
for the training costs paid by that public safety answering point under this section.  The 36 
extent of financial liability must be determined according to the formula established by 37 
this section.  38

 SUMMARY  1 
This bill provides for the reimburs ement, in full or in part, of a public safety 2 
answering point's training costs for a dispatcher who finds employment at another public 3 
safety answering point within 5 years of completion of training by the Emergency 4 
Services Communication Bureau within the  Public Utilities Commission.  5
