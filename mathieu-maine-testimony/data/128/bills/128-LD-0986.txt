SUMMARY  16 
This bill provides for a mandatory one -year suspension of a driver's license for a 17 
person convicted of terrorizing if the terrorizing causes the evacuation of a public 18 
elementary or secondary school.  If the person does not have a driver's license, the bill 19 
specifies a one -year delay in the issuance of the driver's license from the date of the 20 
conviction of the offense or, if the person is not eligible for a driver's license, a one -year 21 
delay from the date  that the person is eligible for the license.  This bill also requires that a 22 
notice of the provisions of the law be conspicuously posted in at least one location in 23 
every public middle and secondary school.  24
