11 SUMMARY 
12 This amendment increases the maximum annual income tax pension deduction 
13 amount for nonmilitary retirement pensions from $10,000 to $35,000 over a 5-year period 
14 beginning with the 2017 tax year. The $35,000 pension deduction amount that applies 
15 after 2021 is subject to an annual inflation adjustment.

This bill increases the pension deduction from $10,000 to $35,000 over a 5 year period. It would result in a loss of 
General Fund revenue of $20,251, 700 in fiscal year 2017-18 and $28, 118, 160 in fiscal year 2018-19 and a loss of 
Local Government Fund revenue of $413,210 in fiscal year 2017-18 and $574,050 in fiscal year 2018-19. It would 
also reduce revenue to the Fund to Advance Public Kindergarten to Grade 12 Education by $190,000 in fiscal year 
2017-18 and $500,000 in fiscal year 2018-19.
