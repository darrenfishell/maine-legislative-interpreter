3 SUMMARY
4 This bill directs the Department of Health and Human Services to establish standards 
5 for recovery residences based  on standards established  by the National  Alliance for 
6 Recovery Residences.  It also authorizes the Bridging Rental Assistance  Program to assist 
7 persons with substance use disorders involving opioids with housing placement in 
8 recovery residences,  including residences in which residents  share rooms.
