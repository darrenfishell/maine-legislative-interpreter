SUMMARY  25 
This bill directs the Director of the Bureau of Maine Veterans’ Services within the 26 
Department of Defense, Veterans and Emergency Management to station at each of the 27 
bureau’s field offices a social worker to provide direct mental health care  to veterans, to 28 
coordinate mental health care for veterans and to work with the Federal Government and 29 
state and local mental health service providers to provide mental health care to veterans.  30 
It also requires the director to administer a program to pro vide grants to providers of 31 
mental health services to veterans and appropriates ongoing funds of $1,000,000 annually 32 
for the program.  33
