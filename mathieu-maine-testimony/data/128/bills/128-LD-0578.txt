SUMMARY  33 
This bill prohibits in a divorce action the awarding of spousal s upport or retirement 34 
benefits to a party if the party has been convicted of or proved by a preponderance of the 35 
evidence to have committed domestic violence assault, domestic violence criminal 36 
threatening, domestic violence terrorizing or domestic violence  stalking and the other 37 
party was the victim.  The bill also prohibits the party from continuing to use the victim's 38 
name unless the names of the parties were identical before the marriage.  39
