 SUMMARY  1 
This bill provides that a person 5 years of age or older who is enrolled in public day 2 
school is required to attend during the time it is in regular sessio n.  The bill provides that 3 
a person 5 years of age or older and under 7 years of age may withdraw from school at 4 
any time within the 45 days after enrollment and may withdraw from school after 45 days 5 
after enrollment after consultation with the school boa rd or its designee.  The bill 6 
provides that students who are at least 5 years of age and enrolled in a public day school 7 
are subject to the same provisions regarding truancy as students who are at least 7 years 8 
of age and have not completed grade 6 who are  required to attend a public day school.  9 
The bill provides that a person 5 years of age or older and under 7 years of age is not 10 
required to meet the alternatives to attendance requirements set forth in the Maine 11 
Revised Statutes, Title 20 -A, section 5001 -A, subsection 3.  12
