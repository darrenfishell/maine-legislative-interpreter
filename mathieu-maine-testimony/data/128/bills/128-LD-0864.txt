Sec. 2.  Plan to implement statewide collective bargaining for teachers.   11 
The Department of Administrative and Financial Services, referred to in this section as 12

public school teachers beginning January 1, 2021.  14 
1. Policy goals.  The plan developed by the department must be designed to:  15

teacher e mployment;  17

SUMMARY  5 
This bill provides for statewide collective bargaining for teac hers beginning January 6 
1, 2021.  The bill directs the Department of Administrative and Financial Services to 7 
develop a plan to implement statewide collective bargaining for teachers and requires that 8 
the plan be designed to permit school administrative uni ts to maintain local control and 9 
direction of teacher employment; to include for all public school teachers a uniform 10 
compensation system that is based upon performance results that are competitive and fair 11 
throughout the State; and to maximize opportuniti es for certain economies of scale 12 
through the statewide negotiation of teachers' benefits, including, but not limited to, 13 
health insurance.  The department is directed to submit its plan, together with proposed 14 
implementing legislation, for introduction to  the Second Regular Session of the 128th 15 
Legislature.  16
