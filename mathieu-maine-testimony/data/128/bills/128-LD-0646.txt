SUMMARY  29 
This bill limits the authority of a shellfish conservation ordinance to the intertidal 30 
zone within a municipality.  It also allows the Commissioner of Marine Resources to 31 
grant regional shellfish management programs additional powers beyond those powers 32 
grant ed to municipal shellfish management programs.  33
