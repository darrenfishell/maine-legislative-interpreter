COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 957, L.D. 1378, Bill, "An Act To 
Create the Maine Family First Employer Award" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'Resolve, Directing the State Workforce Investment Board To Create the Maine 
Family First Employer Program' 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and before the summary and 
inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. State Workforce Investment Board to create program. Resolved: 
That the State Workforce Investment Board, established under the Maine Revised 
Statutes, Title 26, section 2006, shall create the Maine Family First Employer Program as 
an award program to recognize businesses whose policies and practices address the 
unique needs of working families in the State. The board or its designee shall establish a 
procedure for businesses to apply for the award designation with an open application 
period each year and shall review applications and award Family First Employer status to 
eligible selected businesses. The board or its designee may adopt criteria to guide its 
evaluation of businesses under the program. The criteria may include flexible work 
schedules, leave and vacation policies, workplace accommodations, flexible benefit 
packages, job-sharing, child care options and any policy that promotes the hiring or 
retention of working parents. 
The board or its designee may create a logo for the designation that may be used by 
businesses that receive the award. The board or its designee may determine the length of 
the award designation and may allow businesses to reapply for the designation. The 
board or its designee may post on its publicly accessible website a list of all designated 
Family First Employers.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment strikes the bill and replaces it with a resolve directing the State 
Workforce Investment Board to create the Maine Family First Employer Program to
