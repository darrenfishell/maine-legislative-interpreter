SUMMARY  25 
This bill accomplishes the following.  26 
1.  It makes food supplement program benefits and Temporary Assistance for Needy 27 
Families, or "TANF," benefits payable on a semimonthly sc hedule.  28 
2.  It requires the Department of Health and Human Services to provide recipients of 29 
food supplement program benefits and TANF benefits with information about online and 30 
community resources on money management.  31
