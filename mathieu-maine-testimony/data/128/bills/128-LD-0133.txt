SUMMARY  24 
Current law reduces the amount transferred to the Local Government Fund for state - 25 
municipal revenue sharing from 5% to 2% for fiscal years 2015 -16, 2016 -17, 2017 -18 26 
and 2018 -19.  This bill gradually restores the percentage transferred for state -municipal 27 
revenue sharing by increasing the percentage transferred to the Local Government Fund 28 
to 3% for fiscal year 2017 -18 and to 4% for fiscal year 2018 -19.  Following fiscal year 29 
2018 -19, the percentage of state -municipal revenue sharing is scheduled to return t o 5%.  30
