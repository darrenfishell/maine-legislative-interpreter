4.  Electronic benefits transfer system or EBT.   "Electronic benefits transfer 4

Sec. 2.  22 MRSA §23, sub -§§1-A and 1 -B are enacted to read:  9 
1-A.  Cash withdrawals prohibited.  A recipient may not redeem benefits received 10 
through the EBT system for cash.  11 
1-B.  Point of sale restrictions.  The department by rule shall establish a  system to 12 
electronically block the purchase through the EBT system of the products and services 13 
listed in this section and in section 3763, subsection 11 at the point of sale.  14 
SUMMARY  15 
This bill provides that benefits received through the electronic benefi ts transfer 16 
system may be redeemed only through purchase by electronic transfer and may not be 17 
redeemed for cash.  This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to 18 
establish a rule to create a system that would limit purchases at the point  of sale to those 19 
goods and services allowed by law.  20
