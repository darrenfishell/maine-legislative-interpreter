SUMMARY  17 
This bill allows an island community to require an emergency vehicle that is garaged 18 
and operated only on the island to be inspected every 5 years or every 500 miles of use or 19 
annually by a licensed inspection mechanic at the location where the vehicle is 20 
customarily kept.  21
