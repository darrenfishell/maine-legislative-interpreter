This bill increases the exemption from attachment and execution for a motor vehicle 30 
from $5,000 to $7,500 and provides a full exemption for a payment or account under an 31 
individual retirement plan or account that was rolled over from a plan or ac count under 32 
the United States Internal Revenue Code of 1954, Section 401(k) or 403(b) or from a 33
