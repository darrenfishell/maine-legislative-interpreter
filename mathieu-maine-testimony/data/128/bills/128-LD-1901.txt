15 SUMMARY
16 This resolve requires the Department of the Secretary  of State to work with the 
17 American Legion  Department of Maine to secure  a plaque for display  on the immediate 
18 grounds of the State House to honor  the 100th Anniversary of the American Legion.   The 
19 department is authorized to accept from  outside  sources, including the American Legion  
20 Department of Maine,  donated goods,  services  and funding for the design  and 
21 construction of the plaque.  Prior to the creation of the plaque,  the proposed design and 
22 location of the plaque must be submitted to the Executive  Director of the Legislative  
23 Council for approval by the Legislative Council, and final authorization  for the placement 
24 of the plaque  must be  made by  the Legislative Council.
