9 Legislature's position on final adoption of the rule; and

11 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine  and require  the following legislation as

13 therefore, be it
14 Sec. 1.  Adoption.  Resolved:  That final adoption  of portions of Chapter  308:  
15 Standards of Conduct for Transmission  and Distribution  Utilities and Affiliated 
16 Generators, a provisionally adopted major  substantive rule of the Public Utilities 
17 Commission that has been submitted to the Legislature  for review pursuant to the Maine 
18 Revised Statutes,  Title  5, chapter 375, subchapter  2-A, is  authorized.
19 Emergency clause.  In view  of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
20 legislation takes effect when  approved.
21 SUMMARY
22 This resolve provides for legislative review of portions  of Chapter 308:  Standards of 
23 Conduct for Transmission  and Distribution Utilities and Affiliated Generators,  a major  
24 substantive rule of the Public Utilities Commission.
