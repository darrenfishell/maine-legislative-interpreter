SUMMARY  11 
This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to continue to 12 
provide MaineCare cover age to a parent who is a MaineCare member and who is 13 
participating in rehabilitation and reunification efforts in accordance with a rehabilitation 14 
and reunification plan.  The bill requires the department to continue coverage until the 15 
discontinuance of re unification efforts or parental rights are terminated, whichever is 16 
earlier.  17
