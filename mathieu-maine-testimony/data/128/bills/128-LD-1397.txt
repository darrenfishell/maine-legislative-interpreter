SUMMARY  16 
This bill makes the followi ng changes to the Maine Clean Election Act and other 17 
related laws.  18 
1.  It changes references to the Maine Clean Election Act to the Maine Publicly 19 
Financed Election Act and removes specific references to the Maine Clean Election Fund.  20 
2.  It changes the ta x revenue amounts required to be deposited in the fund by the 21 
State Controller from $3,000,000 to $2,000,000.  22 
3.  It limits participation in the Maine Publicly Financed Election Act to first -time 23 
candidates for Governor, State Senator or State Representat ive and provides that seed 24 
money for participating candidates running for State Senator or State Representative may 25 
come only from the participating candidate's district.  It also revises seed money 26 
contribution limits for legislative candidates and elimin ates the provision allowing 27 
participating candidates to collect additional qualifying contributions.  28 
4.  It changes funding distributions under the Maine Publicly Financed Election Act 29 
to require the Commission on Governmental Ethics and Election Practices  to determine 30 
the funding amounts for legislative candidates at least every 2 years and sets a specific 31 
distribution for gubernatorial elections.  It also eliminates supplemental fund 32 
distributions.  33 
5.  It removes a provision requiring that communications that are independent 34 
expenditures include a conspicuous statement listing the top 3 funders of the entity 35 
making the independent expenditure. It also specifies that reports of independent 36 
expenditures aggregating in excess of $250 during any one candidate' s election are 37
