Fiscal Note 
Potential current biennium cost increase -All Funds 
Potential current biennium cost increase -Higher educational institutions 
Potential current biennium cost increase -Municipalities 
This legislation amends the labor relations laws governing public employees to require that the determinations of 
arbitrators with respect to controversies over all subjects including salaries, pension and insurance, be binding. This 
may result in increased personnel and other direct and indirect costs to the employers of these public employees. The 
impact cannot be determined at this time.
