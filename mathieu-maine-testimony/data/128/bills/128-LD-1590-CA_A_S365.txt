Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Cunently, the Penobscot Nation and the Passamaquoddy Tribe each receive half, or 2%, of 4% of the net slot machine 
income from the Oxford Casino. This bill would add the Houlton Band ofMaliseet and the Aroostook Band of 
Micmacs to the distribution. The amount distributed would remain at 4 %, resulting in each tribal government 
receiving 1 % of net slot machine income.
