SUMMARY  20 
This bill makes it a crime for a person, knowing the person is infected with an 21 
aggressive blood -borne pathogen, to intentionally, knowingly or recklessly cause any of 22 
the person's body fluids to be exposed to a first responder while the first responder is 23 
performing official duties.  24 
The bill also requires a person who has exposed that person's body fluids to a first 25 
responder in the course of the first resp onder's official duties to submit to a blood -borne 26 
pathogen test to test for aggressive blood -borne pathogens.  If there is reasonable cause to 27 
suspect that the person's body fluids might contain such a pathogen, a justice, judge or 28 
justice of the peace ma y issue a search warrant to compel the person to submit to testing.  29 
Subsequent testing arising out of the same incident of exposure may also be conducted.  30
