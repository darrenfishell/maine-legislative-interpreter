SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is one of 2 minority reports, removes the provisions of the 
bill and instead provides that a primary airport as designated by the Federal Aviation 
Administration that annually has at least 20,000 enplaned passengers may charge a 
transportation network company reasonable pick-up fees consistent with fees charged to 
other reserved transportation providers at the airport and may negotiate and enter into an 
operating agreement with a transportation network company.
