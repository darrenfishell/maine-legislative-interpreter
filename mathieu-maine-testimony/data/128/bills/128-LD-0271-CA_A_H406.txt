10 SUMMARY 
11 This amendment replaces the bill, which is a concept draft. It requires the Director of 
12 the Bureau of Maine Veterans' Services within the Department of Defense, Veterans and 
13 Emergency Management to establish a program to supp01t a community-based veteran 
14 service referral program. The program provides written and Internet-based materials and 
15 literature to state agencies, quasi-governmental agencies, local government service 
16 providers and private organizations, including a checklist of questions the service 
17 provider should ask an individual seeking assistance, including "Have you or a member 
18 of your family ever served in the military?" The materials must also include a 
19 comprehensive list of services for which a veteran may be eligible.
