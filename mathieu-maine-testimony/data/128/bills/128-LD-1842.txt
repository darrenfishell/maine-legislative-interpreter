9 SUMMARY
10 This bill requires  Legislators, legislative staff and lobbyists to attend and complete a 
11 course of in-person education and training regarding  harassment, including sexual  
12 harassment, at the beginning of each regular session  of the Legislature. It requires  the 
13 Legislative Council to develop  and implement the course.
14 This bill is reported  out by the Joint Standing Committee on State and Local 
15 Government pursuant  to Joint  Order 2017, Senate Paper 685.  The committee  has not 
16 taken a position  on the substance of the bill and by reporting  this bill out the committee is 
17 not suggesting and does not intend  to suggest that it agrees or disagrees with any aspect 
18 of this bill.  The committee  is reporting  the bill out for the sole purpose  of turning the 
19 draft into a printed bill that can be referred to the committee for an appropriate public 
20 hearing and subsequent  processing  in the normal course.  The committee is taking this 
21 action to  ensure clarity and transparency in the  legislative review of this bill.
