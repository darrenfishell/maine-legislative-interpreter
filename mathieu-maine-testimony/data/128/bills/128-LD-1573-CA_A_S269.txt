COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 551, L.D. 1573, Bill, "An Act To

Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Encourage Development in the Logging Industry by Requiring State and 
Local Government Agencies To Give Preference to Timber Harvested in the State' 
Amend the bill in section 1 in § 1778 by striking out all of subsection 2 (page 2, lines

'2. Residue. "Residue'' has the same meaning as in Title 12, section 8881, 
subsection 8.

'5. Timber. "Timber" means roundwood and residue.' 
Amend the bill in section 1 in § 1778 by renumbering the subsections to read

14 SUMMARY 
15 This amendment is the majority report of the committee. The amendment changes 
16 certain term,inology and definitions in the bill to clarify the types of wood to which the 
17 provisions of the bill apply. The amendment replaces the terms "lumber" and "solid wood 
18 products" in the bill with the term "timber," which is further defined as "roundwood," 
19 meaning logs, bolts · and other round sections of wood, and "residue,'' meaning 
20 by-products of a processed log. The amendment also adds a reference to state contracts 
21 for timber harvesting.
