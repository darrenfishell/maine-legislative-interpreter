SUMMARY  9 
This bill restores an income tax deduction for contributions to a qualified tuition 10 
program established under Section 529 of the United States Internal Revenue Code of 11 
1986 that was repealed in 2015 and sets the deduction at a maximum of $1,000 annually 12 
per designated beneficiary.  The change applies to tax years beginning on or after January 13 
1, 2017.  14
