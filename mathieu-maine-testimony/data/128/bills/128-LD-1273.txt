 Sec. 1.  Donation and redispensing of unused prescription drugs.  1 
Resolved:  That the Maine Board of Pharmacy, established in the Maine Revised 2 
Statutes, Title 32, chapter 117, shall adopt rules to allow a nongovernmental organization 3 
in the State to coord inate both the donation of unused prescription drugs by nursing 4 
homes, hospitals, wholesalers and other institutional pharmacies and the subsequent 5 
redispensing of these prescription drugs at no cost to low -income residents of the State, 6 
subject to the fol lowing provisions.  7 
1.  Prescriptions for donated drugs under this section must be filled by pharmacists 8 
licensed in the State for patients in the State who are prequalified by the coordinating 9 
organization.  10 
2.  Donated prescription drugs must be evaluated for suitability for redispensing and 11 
then stored, redispensed or destroyed in accordance with existing laws and Maine Board 12 
of Pharmacy rules.  13 
3.  An extended hospital pharmacy, as described in Maine Board of Pharmacy rule 14 
Chapter 35, may accept, store, re dispense or destroy donated prescription drugs 15 
prescribed for patients in the State.  16 
Rules adopted pursuant to this resolve are routine technical rules as defined in the 17 
Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2 -A. 18
