 SUMMARY  1 
This bill temporarily incre ases by $1 the annual fee for the lake and river protection 2 
sticker required under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 12, section 13058.  The 3 
additional revenue from this increased fee may be used to fund a study of the State’s 4 
efforts to prevent the infest ation of, to control, to eradicate and to otherwise manage 5 
invasive aquatic plants and nuisance species in the waters of the State through the 6 
program implemented under Title 38, chapter 20 -A.  The bill directs the Commissioner of 7 
Environmental Protection and the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife, in 8 
cooperation with the Interagency Task Force on Invasive Aquatic Plants and Nuisance 9 
Species, to facilitate this study, which must, among other things, identify the additional 10 
amount of funding, if a ny, necessary to ensure the long -term viability and success of the 11 
statutory program as well as recommendations for acquiring that additional amount of 12 
funding.  13
