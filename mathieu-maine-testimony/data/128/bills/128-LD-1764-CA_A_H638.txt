This bill transfers the Special Advocate position from the Department of Secretary of State to the Department of 
Economic and Community Development. The bill includes a General Fund deappropriation of $64,849 and a 
Highway Fund deallocation of $68,322 to the Department of Secretary of State in fiscal year 2018-19. This bill 
includes a General Fund appropriation of $104,384 in fiscal year 2018-19 to the Office of Business Development 
within the Department of Economic and Community Development for one Public Service Manager I position to serve 
as the small business Special Advocate. 
Overall, transferring this position will result in a net General Fund cost of $39,535 in fiscal year 2018-19 and a 
Highway Fund savings of$68,322 in fiscal year 2018-19. This assumes an August 1, 2018 effective date.
