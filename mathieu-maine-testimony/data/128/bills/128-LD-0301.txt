 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 1 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and  2 
Whereas, in November 2016, the citizens of the State by majority vote approved the 3 
Marijuana Legali zation Act, which provides for the legalization of marijuana for 4 
recreational use and retail sale; and  5 
Whereas, the Marijuana Legalization Act does not include restrictions on the 6 
placement and operation of retail marijuana establishments or retail marijua na social 7 
clubs in close proximity to public and private schools; and  8 
Whereas, the Marijuana Legalization Act goes into effect January 30, 2017; and  9 
Whereas, to ensure that restrictions are in place to prevent the placement and 10 
operation of retail marijuan a establishments and retail marijuana social clubs in close 11 
proximity to public and private schools, this legislation needs to take immediate effect; 12 
and 13 
Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 14 
the meaning of the  Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 15 
immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 16 
therefore,  17

 SUMMARY  1 
This bill restricts the licensing authority of the Department of Agriculture, 2 
Conservation and Forestry under the Marijuana Legalization Act by prohibiting the 3 
issuance of a license under that Act to operate a retail marijuana establishment or a retail 4 
marijuana social club that is to be located within 2,000 feet of the property line of a 5 
preexisting school.  6
