SUMMARY 
This amendment does the following. 
1. It changes the mill expectation from 8.49 to 8.48. 
2. It increases the allocation for career and technical education costs by $2,000,000. 
3. It decreases the allocation for alignment of career and technical education 
programs with national industry standards by $2,000,000.

Fiscal Note 
Amends 2018-2019 allocations for funding total cost ofK-12 public education 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This amendment amends the allocations for funding the total cost ofK-12 public education in 2018-2019 by 
increasing the allocation for career and technical education costs by $2,000,000 and decreasing the allocation for 
national industry standards for career and technical education by the same amount. This amendment does not change 
the total cost of funding K-12 public education in fiscal year 2018-19 or the State's share of that cost.
