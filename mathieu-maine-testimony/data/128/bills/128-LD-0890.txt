 SUMMARY  1 
This bill makes the statutory changes necessary t o include a representative of the 2 
Aroostook Band of Micmacs in the Maine House of Representatives beginning with the 3 
129th Legislature.  It requires the Tribal Clerk of the Aroostook Band of Micmacs to 4 
furnish the outgoing Clerk of the House of Representat ives with a certification of the 5 
name and residence of the Representative -elect of the Aroostook Band of Micmacs to the 6 
Legislature.  It also authorizes the Representative of the Aroostook Band of Micmacs to 7 
be compensated in the same manner as other membe rs of the House of Representatives.  8 
It also provides that the changes do not take effect unless the Aroostook Band of 9 
Micmacs approves them and provides certification of that approval to the Secretary of 10 
State within 90 days of the adjournment of the Firs t Regular Session of the 128th 11 
Legislature.  12
