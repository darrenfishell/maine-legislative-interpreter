1 SUMMARY 
2 This amendment makes changes to the bill to reflect the designation of the 
3 Depattment of Administrative and Financial Services as the state licensing authority 
4 pursuant to Public Law 2017, chapter 278. 
5 This amendment also removes the General Fund appropriations from the bill, 
6 replaces them with Other Special Revenue Funds allocations and requires the State 
7 Controller to transfer from the Retail Marijuana Regulatoty Coordination Fund within the 
8 Depattment of Administrative and Financial Services $91,032 to the Marijuana 
9 Regulation and Licensing Fund program within the Department of Agriculture, 
10 Conservation and Forestry and $84, 722 to the Maine Center for Disease Control and 
11 Prevention program within the Depattment of Health and Human Services.

This amendment eliminates the General Fund cost of the bill by removing the fiscal year 2017-18 General Fund 
appropriations of$91,032 to the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry (ACF) and $84,722 to the 
Department of Health and Human Services (DHHS). Instead, the amendment transfers the same amounts in fiscal year 
2017-18 only from the Retail Marijuana Regulatory Coordination Fund within the Department of Administrative and 
Financial Services to the Marijuana Regulation and Licensing Fund within ACF and to the Maine Center for Disease 
Control and Prevention program in DHHS.
