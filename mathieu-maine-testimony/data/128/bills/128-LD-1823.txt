9 SUMMARY
10 This bill is reported out by the Joint  Standing Committee on Inland Fisheries and 
11 Wildlife pursuant to Joint Order 2017, H.P. 1249.  The bill removes the provision of law 
12 that repeals  on September 15, 2018 the law that allows a nonresident  who owns 25 or 
13 more acres  of land in the State and leaves  that property  open to hunting, who holds a 
14 valid hunting license and who is not otherwise prohibited from hunting deer to hunt deer 
15 on the Saturday preceding  the first  day of the open  season on deer.
16 The committee has not taken a position on the substance of this bill.  By reporting  
17 this bill out, the committee is not suggesting and does not intend to suggest that it agrees 
18 or disagrees with any aspect  of this bill.  The committee  is reporting the bill out for the 
19 sole purpose of having a bill printed that can be referred to the committee for an 
20 appropriate public hearing and subsequent  processing  in the normal  course.  The 
21 committee is taking this action to ensure clarity  and transparency  in the legislative review  
22 of the proposals contained  in the bill.
