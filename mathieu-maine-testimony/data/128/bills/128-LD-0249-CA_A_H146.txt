COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 182, L.D. 249, Bill, "An Act To Fund 
and Enhance the Maine Diversion Alert Program" 
Amend the bill by striking out all of section 1 and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. Report. The Department of Public Safety, in consultation with the Maine 
Diversion Alert Program, shall review opportunities for collaboration with the Controlled 
Substances Prescription Monitoring Program established in the Maine Revised Statutes, 
Title 22, chapter 1603 and the United States Department of Veterans Affairs to improve 
the quality of health care and decrease the prevalence of substance use disorders. The 
Department of Public Safety shall report in writing on the results of the review by 
December 6, 2017 to the Joint Standing Committee on Criminal Justice and Public 
Safety. 
Sec. 2. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and 
allocations are made.
