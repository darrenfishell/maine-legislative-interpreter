SUMMARY  33 
This bill requires the Aroostook County Commissioners to re name Captain Ambrose 34 
Bear Stream in and near the Town of Houlton in Aroostook County to B Stream.  The bill 35 
also requires the county commissioners to notify the appropriate authorities and agencies 36 
of the name change.  The Department of Transportation is r equired to change any existing 37 
road signs designating Captain Ambrose Bear Stream to refer to B Stream.  The bill also 38
