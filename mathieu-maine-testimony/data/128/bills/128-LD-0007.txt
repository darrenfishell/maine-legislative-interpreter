SUMMARY  25 
This bill gives the Town of Bridgton the ability to convey nonwaterfront portions of a 26 
piece of property held for public use if the town determines th e portion to be conveyed 27 
has no identifiable public use. It also requires that all revenues received by the town from 28 
the conveyance of the nonwaterfront properties be held in a trust fund for parks and 29 
recreational purposes.  30
