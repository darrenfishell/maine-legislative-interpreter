COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 1055, L.D. 1535, Bill, "An Act To 
Modernize the State's Procurement Laws" 
Amend the bill in section 27 in § 1825-E by striking out all of subsection 4. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the committee. The amendment removes 
the provision in the bill that provides that costs for an unsuccessful appeal of a contract or 
grant award decision must be borne by the person requesting the appeal.
