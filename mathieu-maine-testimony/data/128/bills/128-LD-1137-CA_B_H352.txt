Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill lowers the age from 70 years or older to 65 years or older that a hunter can hunt for wild birds or animals 
using a crossbow during certain hunting seasons. This may increase the number of crossbow permits issued by IFW 
and increase General Fund revenue by a minor amount.
