1. Prohibiting a person holding a Class I, Class II or Class III lobster and crab 8

zone that is not the license holder’s declared lobster zone;  11 
2. Prohibiting a person holding a Class I, Class II or Class III lobster and crab 12 
fishing license from fishing more than 25% of the license holder’s lobster traps in a 13 
limited -entry zone that is not the license holder’s declared lobster zone;  14
