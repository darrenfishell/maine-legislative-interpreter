SUMMARY  27 
This bill amends the Freedom of Access Act by making collective bargaining 28 
meetings between public employers and a collective bargaining unit conducted pursuant 29 
to the municipal public employees labor relations laws, the state employees labor 30 
relations laws or the judicial employees labor relations laws public proceedings under the 31 
Act and therefore subject to the open meeting and notice requirements of the Act.  32
