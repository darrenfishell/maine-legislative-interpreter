 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  7 MRSA §3953, as amended by PL 2003, c. 414, Pt. B, §13 and affected 2 
by Pt. D, §7 and c. 614, §9, is further amended to read:  3 
§3953.  Stealing, injuring or killing dogs  4 
Except as provid ed in section 3951 and Title 12, section 12404, and unless the killing 5 
is justified to protect persons or property, a person who steals, confines or secretes, 6 
willfully or negligently injures or willfully or negligently kills a dog is liable in damages 7 
to the dog's owner in a civil action.  Damages may include, but are not limited to, actual 8 
damages, including veterinary fees and the value of the dog; damages for loss of 9 
companionship; damages for pain and suffering; and punitive damages.  The prevailing 10 
party in any action under this section may recover attorney's fees from the nonprevailing 11 
party.  12 
SUMMARY  13 
This bill allows damages for the willful or negligent injury or death of a dog to 14 
include, but not be limited to, actual damages, including veterinary fe es and the value of 15 
the dog; damages for loss of companionship; damages for pain and suffering; and 16 
punitive damages.  The prevailing party in any action may recover attorney's fees from 17 
the nonprevailing party.  18
