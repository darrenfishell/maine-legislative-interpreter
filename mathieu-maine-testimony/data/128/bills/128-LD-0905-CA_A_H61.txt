Fiscal Note 
Potential current biennium cost increase -Maine Tumpike Authority 
Potential current biennium revenue increase -Maine Turnpike Authority 
This bill requires the Maine Turnpike Authority (MTA) to construct a connector from South Gorham to an 
interchange on the Maine Turnpike after completing an evaluation of reasonable alternatives. The MTA would incur 
costs to plan, design and construct this project. The bill allows the MTA to issue bonds in an amount not to exceed 
$150,000,000 for these costs. The bonds would be paid off from tolls levied by the MTA.
