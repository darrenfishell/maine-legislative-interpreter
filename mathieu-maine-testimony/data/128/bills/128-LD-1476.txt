 SUMMARY  1 
This bill incorporates current requirements under the federal Patient Protection and 2 
Affordable Care Act for coverage of preventive health services, including services for 3 
women, into state la w.  The bill also requires coverage of certain contraceptive methods 4 
and services.  The bill directs the Superintendent of Insurance to annually review the 5 
recommendations and guidelines for coverage of preventive health services to identify 6 
any gaps in th e minimum coverage provided by health plans and authorizes the joint 7 
standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over health insurance matters to 8 
introduce legislation to update the requirements for minimum coverage.  The 9 
requirements apply t o all individual and group health insurance policies and contracts 10 
issued or renewed on or after January 1, 2018.  11
