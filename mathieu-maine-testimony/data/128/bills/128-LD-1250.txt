SUMMARY  3 
This bill requires a law enforcement officer to enforce disability parking restrictions 4 
on public property and on private property open to public use.  The bill provides that the 5 
court may susp end up to 1/2 of the fine for a violation of disability parking restrictions 6 
and impose a nonmonetary sanction meant to educate the offender about accommodating 7 
persons with disabilities.  The bill also provides that fines collected for violations of 8 
disab ility parking restrictions must be remitted to the municipality in which the violation 9 
occurred.  10
