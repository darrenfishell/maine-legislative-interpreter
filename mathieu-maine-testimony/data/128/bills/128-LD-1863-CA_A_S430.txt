9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "I\" to S.P. 708, L.D. 1863, Bill, "An Act 
10 Regarding the Limit on the Number of Children Who May Be Placed in a Single Foster 
11 Home" 
12 Amend the bill by inserting after the title and before the enacting clause the 
13 following: 
14 'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
15 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and 
16 Whereas, the emergency enactment of this legislation is necessary to allow the 
17 Department of Health and Human Services to place in appropriate foster homes children 
18 who are currently awaiting such placement; and 
19 Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
20 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
21 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
22 therefore,' 
23 Amend the bill by striking out all of section 2 and inserting the following: 
24 'Sec. 2. 22 MR.SA §8107, sub-§1-A is enacted to read: 
25 1-A. Number; placement in family foster home. The limitation on the number of 
26 children in a family foster home does not prohibit the placement of more than the allowed 
27 number in an individual case involving unusual circumstances if the department 
28 determines that placement to be appropriate. 
29 Emergency clause. In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
30 legislation takes effect when approved.'
