2 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as  emergencies; and
3 Whereas, the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A requires 
4 legislative authorization before major  substantive agency rules may be finally  adopted  by 
5 the agency; and
6 Whereas, the above- named major  substantive  rule has been  submitted to the 
7 Legislature outside the legislative rule acceptance period; and
8 Whereas, immediate enactment of this resolve is necessary to record the 
9 Legislature's position on final adoption of the rule; and
10 Whereas, in the judgment  of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
11 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine  and require  the following legislation as 
12 immediately necessary  for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety;  now, 
13 therefore, be it
14 Sec. 1.  Adoption.   Resolved:  That final adoption  of portions of Chapter 115: the 
15 Credentialing of Educational Personnel,  a provisionally adopted major  substantive rule of 
16 the Department of Education that has been submitted to the Legislature for review  
17 pursuant to the Maine  Revised  Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A outside the 
18 legislative rule acceptance  period, is authorized.
19 Emergency clause.  In view  of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
20 legislation takes effect when  approved.
21 SUMMARY
22 This resolve  provides for legislative  review  of portions  of Chapter 115: the 
23 Credentialing of Educational Personnel, a major  substantive  rule of the Department of 
24 Education that  was filed outside  the legislative  rule acceptance period.
