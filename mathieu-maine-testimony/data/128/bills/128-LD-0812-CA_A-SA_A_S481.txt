SENATE AMENDMENT "A" to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 257, 
L.D. 812, "Resolve, To Establish a Pilot Project To Save Lives and Support People with 
Substance Use Disorder in Washington County" 
Amend the amendment in section 2 in the 3rd line (page 2, line 5 in amendment) by 
striking out the following: "no later than October 1, 201811 and inserting the following: 
'when federal grant funding is obtained' 
SUMMARY 
Committee Amendment 11A11 requires the Department of Health and Human Services 
to work with Healthy Acadia to establish a central coordinating telephone system located 
in Washington County no later than October 1, 2018. This amendment requires that the 
central coordinating telephone system be established when federal grant funding is 
obtained.
