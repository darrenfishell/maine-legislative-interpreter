COMMITTEE AMENDMENT ,tt, to H.P. 263, L.D. 357, Bill, "An Act To Create 
a Penalty for the Discharge of Septic Waste from Watercraft into the Inland Waters" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Increase Penalties for the Discharge of Sewage, Septic Fluids, Garbage, 
Sanitary Waste or Other Pollutants from Watercraft into Inland Waters' 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
summary and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. 38 MRSA §423, sub-§5 is enacted to read: 
5. Penalty. Notwithstanding section 349, subsection 2, a person who is charged with 
a civil violation of this section is subject to a civil penalty, payable to the State, of not less 
than $500 and not more than $10,000 for each day of that violation.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment changes the title, replaces the bill and increases the minimum 
penalty from not less than $100 to not less than $500 for the discharge of sewage, septic 
fluids, garbage or other pollutants from watercraft into inland waters or for operating on 
inland waters a watercraft that has a permanently installed sanitary waste disposal system 
if it does not have a holding tank to prevent the discharge of sanitary waste into inland 
waters.
