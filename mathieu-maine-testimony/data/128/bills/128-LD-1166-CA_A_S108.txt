COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 387, L.D. 1166, Bill, "An Act 
Regarding Anesthesia Care in Rural Maine" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in §2211 by inserting at the end the following: 
'4. Rules. The board shall adopt rules to implement this section. Rules adopted 
pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined in the Maine Revised 
Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.' 
Amend the bill by striking out all of section 2. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment allocates to the Maine Revised Statutes the language in the bill 
granting rulemaking authority to the State Board of Nursing.
