15 §908. Home construction or repair fraud 
16 1. A home construction or repair seller is guilty of home construction or repair fraud 
17 if the seller knowingly enters into an agreement or contract, written or oral, with any 
18 person for home construction or repair services and the seller, at the time of entering into 
19 that agreement or contract: 
20 A. Intentionally misrepresents a material fact relating to the terms of the agreement 
21 or contract or misrepresents a preexisting or existing condition of any portion of the 
22 property that is the subject of the home construction or repair services. Violation of 
23 this paragraph is a Class D crime; 
24 B. Intentionally creates or reinforces an impression relating to the terms of the 
25 agreement or contract that is false and that the seller does not believe to be true or 
26 fails to correct such an impression that the seller had previously created or reinforced. 
27 Violation of this paragraph is a Class D crime; 
28 C. Intentionally promises performance under the terms of the agreement or contract 
29 that the seller does not intend to perform or that the seller knows will not be 
30 performed. Violation of this paragraph is a Class D crime; 
31 D. Intentionally uses or employs deception, false pretense or false promise in 
32 securing the agreement or contract. Violation of this paragraph is a Class D crime; 
33 E. Knows that the property that is the subject of the home construction or repair 
34 services was previously damaged or destroyed by the seller with the intent to obtain 
3 5 the agreement or contract. Violation of this paragraph is a Class D crime;

3. As used in this section, unless otherwise indicated, the following terms have the 
following meanings. 
A. 11Home construction or repair services11 means building or constructing a 
residence and fixing, replacing, altering, converting, modernizing, improving or 
making an addition to real property primarily designed or used as a residence. 
11Home construction or repair services11 includes not only structural work but also the 
construction, installation, replacement, improvement and cleaning of driveways, 
swimming pools, porches, kitchens, chimneys, chimney liners, garages, fences, 
fall-out shelters, central air conditioning, central heating, boilers, furnaces, hot water 
heaters, electric wiring, sewers, carpeting. plumbing fixtures, storm doors, storm 
windows, siding or awnings and other improvements to structures within the 
residence or upon the land adjacent to the residence, including tree trimming. 
B. 11Home construction or repair seller11 or 11seller11 means a person, partnership, 
corporation, business, trust or other legal entity that sells or provides home 
construction or repair services. 
C. 11Residence11 means a single-family or multifamily dwelling, including a single­
family home, apartment building, condominium, duplex or townhouse that is used or 
intended to be used by its occupants as a dwelling place.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the unanimous report of the committee, strikes and 
replaces the bill. The amendment expands the crime of home repair fraud to include 
fraud that occurs in connection with initial construction of a residence.
