SUMMARY  3 
This bill prohibits a denial of coverage or an increase in insurance premiums for life 4 
insurance, disability insurance or long -term care ins urance for living organ donors.  The 5 
bill also requires the Department of Health and Human Services to establish, maintain 6 
and operate an information service to educate the public on live organ donation.  7
