SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to further regulate the cultivation, transportation, sale and 5 
possession of marijuana and mariju ana products by:  6 
1.  Establishing tiered licenses, issued by the State, for possession, cultivation and 7 
sale based on quantity.  8 
A.  Tiers for personal possession would be set at up to one ounce, more than one 9 
ounce to no more than 2 ounces and more than 2 ounces to no more than 3 ounces.  10 
B.  Possession of more than 3 ounces would require a commercial license, which 11 
would also be tiered, based on the quantity above 3 ounces.  12 
C.  Tiers for personal cultivation would be established at one to 50 plants and 51 t o 13 
100 plants.  14 
D.  Cultivation of more than 100 plants would require a commercial license, which 15 
would also be tiered, based on the quantity above 100 plants.  16 
E.  Tiers for engaging in the sale of marijuana and marijuana products would be 17 
based on the quant ity of marijuana in the products sold.  18 
The cost of a tiered license would increase progressively based on the quantity possessed, 19 
cultivated or sold;  20 
2.  Requiring a state -issued license for the transportation of marijuana or marijuana 21 
products by motor ve hicle, watercraft or airplane within the State;  22 
3.  Prohibiting from possessing or using marijuana or marijuana products and 23 
subjecting to random drug testing certain persons, including health care workers such as 24 
physicians and nurses, commercial truck dr ivers, passenger or commercial vessel 25 
operators, taxi drivers, law enforcement officials, correctional officers and emergency 26 
first responders; and  27 
4.  Allowing nonresidents to purchase a temporary possession license.  28
