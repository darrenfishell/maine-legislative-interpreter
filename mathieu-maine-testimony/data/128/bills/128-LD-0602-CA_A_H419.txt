SUMMARY 
This amendment changes the membership of the Advisory Committee on College 
Savings. It also specifies that members appointed by the chair of the board of directors of 
the Finance Authority of Maine are appointed to one-year terms; those appointed by the 
Governor serve 4-year terms.
