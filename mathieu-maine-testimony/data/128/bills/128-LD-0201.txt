SUMMARY  24 
This bill adds an exception to the lobster and crab fishing trap tag limits, allowing a 25 
holder of a Class I, Class II or Class III lobster and crab fishing license to purchase up to 26 
the maximum number of trap tags the license holder purchased under that license in any 27 
previous year.  28
