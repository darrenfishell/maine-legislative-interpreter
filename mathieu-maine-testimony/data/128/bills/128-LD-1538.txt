SUMMARY  11 
This bill exempts veterans who are otherwise eligible for assistance under the federal 12 
Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program from time limits that would otherwise apply 13 
to the veter ans.  It also requires 5% of the Bridging Rental Assistance Program funding to 14 
be used to assist individuals who have been homeless for long periods of time.  The 15 
Department of Health and Human Services is directed to establish 5 case management 16 
positions to provide services to individuals receiving housing assistance and community 17 
mental health services under MaineCare.  18
