SUMMARY  6 
This bill establishes a program within the Department of Defense, Veterans and 7 
Emergency Management, Bureau of Maine Veterans' Services to encourage employers in 8 
the State to establish veteran -friendly wor kplaces.  The bill provides criteria for certifying 9 
a workplace as a veteran -friendly workplace and requires the Director of the Bureau of 10 
Maine Veterans' Services to conduct regular trainings for employers who voluntarily 11 
decide to establish such a workpl ace.  The bill also directs the Department of Labor to 12 
establish a manner for recognizing employers whose workplaces have been certified as 13 
veteran -friendly workplaces.  14 
This bill also requires an employer to allow an employee who is a veteran to take 15 
paid leave to attend scheduled appointments at a medical facility operated by the United 16 
States Veterans Administration.  The employee must make reasonable efforts to consult 17 
with the employer before scheduling such an appointment in order to accommodate, so 18 
far as is reasonable, the needs of the employer.  The employer may require reasonable 19 
advance notice and verification from the veteran of such an appointment.  20
