CONCEPT DRAFT  2 
SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to enact measures designed to stabilize the price of lobster bait.  5 
These measures may include, but are not limited to:  6 
1. Authorizing an increase in the number of people that may fish for bait;  7 
2. Amending the limits on specific species of bait; and  8 
3. Amending restrictions on the types of species that may be used as bait.  9
