4 SUMMARY
5 This bill grants the Maine Developmental Services Oversight and Advisory Board  
6 direct access  to the personal  planning and other records of a person receiving adult 
7 developmental services, subject to appropriate safeguards to protect  the person's right to 
8 confidentiality, and grants  the board direct access to the records  of an investigation  into 
9 the suspicious death of or the records of a mortality review pertaining to a person with 
10 intellectual disabilities or autism,  subject  to appropriate  safeguards for the privacy  of the 
11 deceased person.   It also requires the Department of Health  and Human Services to notify 
12 the board of any report made to a medical examiner regarding a mandated reporter's 
13 knowledge or reasonable suspicion that an adult receiving adult developmental services 
14 has died  as a result of abuse or neglect.
