SUMMARY  7 
This bill permits a rabbit producer to sell rabbit products without inspection at the 8 
farm on which the rabbits were raised, at farmers' markets, by delivery to c onsumers' 9 
homes, to members of community -supported agriculture farms with which the rabbit 10 
producer has a direct marketing relationship, to locally owned grocery stores and to 11 
locally owned restaurants, as long as the farm slaughters fewer than 1,000 rabbi ts 12 
annually.  13
