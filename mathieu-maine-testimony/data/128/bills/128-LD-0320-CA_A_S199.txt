13 'Sec. 1. 22 MRSA §3174-ZZ is enacted to read: 
14 §3174-ZZ. Chiropractic services reimbursement 
15 1. Reimbursement. The department shall reimburse under the MaineCare program 
16 for chiropractic evaluation and management examinations performed by a chiropractic 
17 doctor licensed under Title 32, chapter 9 that are within the scope of practice of 
18 chiropractic doctors. This subsection does not limit reimbursements under the MaineCare 
19 program that may be available for other chiropractic services or affect any limits that may 
20 apply to reimbursements such as limits relating to numbers of visits. 
21 2. Rulemaking. The department may adopt rules to implement this section. Rules 
22 adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine technical rules as defined by Title 5, 
23 chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
