SUMMARY  25 
This bill allows the operator of a lightweight vehicle facing a steady and circular red 26 
light, after stopping for a reasonable amount of time, to proceed caut iously through the 27 
intersection before the light on the traffic -control device turns green if no other vehicles 28 
are present at the intersection to actuate the traffic -control device.  "Lightweight vehicle" 29 
means a motorcycle, moped or bicycle, including a motorized bicycle, motorized tricycle 30 
or motorized scooter or a motor vehicle not heavy enough to actuate a traffic -control 31 
device sensor.  32
