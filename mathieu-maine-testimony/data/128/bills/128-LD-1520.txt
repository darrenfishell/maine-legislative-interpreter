SUMMARY  26 
This bill creates an aquacul ture license that exempts the holder from certain 27 
requirements in law to hold a separate license for the removal, possession, transport or 28 
sale of cultured marine organisms and authorizes the holder to remove, possess, transport 29 
or sell cultured marine org anisms.  30
