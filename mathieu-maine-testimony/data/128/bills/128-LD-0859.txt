1.  Definitions.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 4

B.  "Health care provider " means:  9 
(1)  A hospital licensed under Title 22, chapter 405; or  10 
(2)  A health care practitioner who is licensed by this State to provide or is 11 
otherwise lawfully providing health care or a partnership or corporation made up 12 
of such health care practition ers. 13 
C.  "Law enforcement agency" means the State Police, a county law enforcement 14

D.  "Sexual assault" means a gross sexual assault as described in Title 17 -A, section 16 
253, sexual abuse of minors as described in Title 17 -A, section 254 or unlawful 17 
sexual contact as described in Title 17 -A, section 255 -A. 18 
2.  Process forensic examination kits within 180 days.  The State Police shall 19 
process a forensic examination kit within 180 days of receipt of a kit or within  180 days 20 
of receipt of consent to analysis of that kit by the alleged victim of the sexual assault, 21 
whichever occurs later.  22 
3.  Report.  The State Police shall conduct annual audits of forensic examination kits 23 
and shall report to the general public and t o the Legislature by July 1, 2018 and annually 24 
thereafter regarding the status of analyses of kits in the possession of the State Police.  25 
The report must include, for the preceding 12 -month time period, the number of test kits 26 
on which analyses have been completed and the number of test kits on which analyses 27 
have not been completed.  28
