1 SUMMARY 
2 The bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to disclose 
3 information in its records under the Adult Protective Services Act to personal 
4 representatives of the estates of incapacitated or dependent adults who die while under 
5 public guardianship or public conservatorship. 
6 This amendment changes the title and replaces the bill to give the Department of 
7 Health and Human Services the option of disclosing information in its records under the 
8 Adult Protective Services Act to personal representatives of the estates of incapacitated or 
9 dependent adults who die while under public guardianship or public conservatorship.
