SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to recruit and retain professionals in Maine by:  5 
1.  Creating or enhancing certain tax credits, such as the dental care access credit and 6 
the primary care access credit, which provide a tax credit for dentists and medical 7 
professionals practicing in an underserved area of the State; and  8 
2.  Creating or enhancing loan and loan forgiveness programs, su ch as the Educators 9 
for Maine Program and the Health Professions Loan Program, which provide loans to 10 
students in the areas of education or health who then agree to complete a certain length of 11 
service in Maine in return for forgiveness of those loans.  12
