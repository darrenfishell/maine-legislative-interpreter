13 §1820.  Fee sharing
14 Seven percent  of all day use and camping fees derived  from any lands  classified by 
15 the director as parks or historic sites under jurisdiction of the bureau  must  be apportioned 
16 and paid to the all municipalities  having those that have  any lands classified by the 
17 director as parks or historic sites under jurisdiction of the bureau within their boundaries.   
18 In determining the payment to each municipality, the bureau shall assign one unit per 
19 front foot for each foot of lake, pond, ocean or major river frontage and 5 units for each 
20 acre of all such lands  within  the municipality.  Frontage  and acreage must be determined 
21 as of April 1st for the year in which revenue is being apportioned and computed  to the 
22 nearest whole unit.
23 Emergency clause.  In view  of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
24 legislation takes effect when  approved.
25 SUMMARY
26 This bill clarifies that 7% of all day use and camping fees derived from  any lands  
27 classified by the Director  of the Bureau  of Parks and Lands within the Department of 
28 Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry as parks or historic sites under jurisdiction of the 
29 bureau must be apportioned and paid to all municipalities that have any lands classified 
30 by the director  as parks or historic sites under jurisdiction of the bureau within their 
31 boundaries.
