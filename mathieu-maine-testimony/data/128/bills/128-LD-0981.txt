 SUMMARY  1 
This bill prohibits the Treasurer of State from deposi ting funds in any bank that is 2 
providing funds, extending credit or otherwise engaged in financing the construction of 3 
the Dakota Access pipeline. This bill also prohibits the Treasurer of State from investing 4 
in the securities of a corporation or company or other entity that is providing funds, 5 
extending credit or otherwise engaged in financing the construction of the Dakota Access 6 
pipeline.  7 
This bill requires the Maine Public Employees Retirement System to divest its 8 
holdings in any corporation or company  that is constructing or funding construction of 9 
the so -called Dakota Access pipeline.  The bill requires divestment to be completed by 10 
January 1, 2019.  11
