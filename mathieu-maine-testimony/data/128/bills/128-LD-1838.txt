27 SUMMARY
28 This bill provides that a person is guilty of the Class  E crime of indecent conduct  if 
29 the actor exposes  the actor's  genitals with the intent to create images that the actor  
30 transmits to another person by mechanical or electronic means under  circumstances that 
31 in fact are likely to cause affront  or alarm.   It also provides that a subsequent violation by 
32 a person who has 2 or more  prior convictions for indecent conduct  or visual sexual  
33 aggression against  a child is a Class D crime.
