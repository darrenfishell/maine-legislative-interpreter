SUMMARY  5 
This bill supports employee -owned businesses and cooperatives in the following 6 
ways.  7 
1.  It excludes from Maine income tax the amount of gain recognized by a business 8 
owner in transferring the business to an employee stock ownership plan, eligible worker - 9 
owned cooperative, consumer cooperative or affordable housing cooperative.  10 
2.  It excludes from Maine income tax interest from loans that finance transfers of 11 
ownership  from a business to an employee stock ownership plan, eligible worker -owned 12 
cooperative, consumer cooperative or affordable housing cooperative.  13
