8 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A "to H.P. 1059, L.D. 1538 
SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, removes the sections 
of the bill related to the Bridging Rental Assistance Program and retains only the section 
related to the eligibility of veterans for the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program. 
This amendment also provides funding to the Department of Health and Human Services 
for technology changes necessary to implement the bill.
