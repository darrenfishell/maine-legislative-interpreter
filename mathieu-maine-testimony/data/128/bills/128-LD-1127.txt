 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  12 MRSA §1819, as amended by PL 2013, c. 1, Pt. J, §1 and c. 405, Pt. A, 2 
§24, is further amended by adding at the end a new paragraph to read:  3 
A person displaying on the person's motor vehicle gold star family registration plates 4 
issued in accordance with Title 29 -A, section 524 -B and any passengers in that vehicle 5 
are not required to pay a fee for admission to any state -owned park, camping area or 6 
beach.  7 
SUMMARY  8 
This bill provides for f ree entry to state parks for those persons who have gold star 9
