26 E. Is directing traffic in an intersection with a marked crosswalk on a public way. 
27 2. Obeying school crossing guards. An operator of a motor vehicle shall obey a 
28 hand signal or handheld traffic control device of a school crossing guard qualified under

30 3. Assisting pedestrians. This section does not prohibit a school crossing guard

4 SUMMARY 
5 This amendment clarifies that failure to obey a school crossing guard is a traffic 
6 infraction. The amendment provides that school crossing guards are under the control of 
7 a local law enforcement agency only and not a school administrative unit. The 
8 amendment specifies that a school crossing guard must complete training approved by the 
9 Department of Labor, Bureau of Labor Standards in order to control traffic on a public 
IO way. The amendment also provides that a school crossing guard may direct traffic only 
11 in an intersection with a marked crosswalk on a public way. Finally, the amendment 
12 provides that a school crossing guard may not contradict or override a lighted traffic 
13 control device or pedestrian control device.
