Notwithstanding section 2449, section 2454 or any other provision of law to the 4

Sec. 3.  30-A MRSA §3015 is enacted to read:  14 
§3015.  Ordinances regarding marijuana cultivation  15 
As provided under Title 7, section 2455 and Title 22, sec tion 2430 -C, a municipality 16

areas within the municipality.  19 
SUMMARY  20 
This bill allo ws a municipality to restrict the cultivation of marijuana for recreational 21 
or medical use to certain areas in the municipality or to prohibit cultivation entirely 22 
within the municipality.  The bill applies to cultivation of marijuana under the Marijuana 23 
Legalization Act and the Maine Medical Use of Marijuana Act.  24
