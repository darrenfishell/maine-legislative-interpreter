SUMMARY  14 
This resolve establishes the Task Force To Implement Ranked -choice Voting.  The 15 
task force is charged with making recommendations for rules and changes to the law to 16 
implement ranked -choice voting.  The task force is required to sub mit a report containing 17 
its findings and recommendations to the Joint Standing Committee on Veterans and Legal 18 
Affairs by December 6, 2017.  The Joint Standing Committee on Veterans and Legal 19 
Affairs may report out legislation concerning the report to the Second Regular Session of 20 
the 128th Legislature.  21
