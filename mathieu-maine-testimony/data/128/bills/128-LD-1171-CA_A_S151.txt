14 SUMMARY 
15 This amendment is the majority report of the committee. The amendment strikes the 
16 section of the bill that establishes a liaison position with respect to the Job Creation 
17 Through Educational Opportunity Program and replaces it with a section appropriating 
18 $70,000 in ongoing annual funds for the Finance Authority of Maine to hire and pay a 
19 full-time customer service employee for the purposes of promoting the Job Creation 
20 Through Educational Opportunity Program and assisting interested individuals, 
21 businesses and schools. The amendment also provides that the Finance Authority of 
22 Maine is not required but may contract with a private, nonprofit corporation to help 
23 market the program.
