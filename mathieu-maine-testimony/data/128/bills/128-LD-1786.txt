34 SUMMARY
35 This bill clarifies  provisions in existing law authorizing  a licensed  manufacturer  to 
36 hold a license for a retail establishment  where  liquor  is consumed on the premises.  The 
37 bill also repeals a provision that requires the Department of Administrative  and Financial
