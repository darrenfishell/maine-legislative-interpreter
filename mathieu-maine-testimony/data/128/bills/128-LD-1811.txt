31 SUMMARY
32 This bill prohibits the Department of Health and Human Services from adopting  or 
33 enforcing rules  that are not identical  in substance to the rules in effect on January 31, 
34 2018 governing  the medical  use of marijuana.  The prohibition is repealed 90 days after 
35 the adjournment of the Second Regular Session  of the 128th  Legislature.  The bill further 
36 directs the department to adopt rules  governing the medical use of marijuana  program
