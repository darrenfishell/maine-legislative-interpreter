This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to implement programs to facilitate the transition of persons from 5 
rehabilitation for drug or al cohol addiction or economic dependency to self -sufficiency 6 
by providing employment opportunities while allowing their employers to reduce their 7 
tax liability.  8 
This bill would create a program, modeled on the federal Work Opportunity Tax 9 
Credit program oper ated by the United States Department of Labor, to encourage 10 
employers to hire from certain population segments, such as MaineCare and Medicare 11 
recipients, veterans, persons in the Temporary Assistance for Needy Families program or 12 
receiving general assista nce, persons convicted of a crime who have been released from 13 
prison, persons recently out of residential treatment or detoxification for substance use 14 
disorder or who recently began medication -assisted treatment and disadvantaged teens.  15 
The incentives wo uld consist of tax credits based on the wages paid to qualified persons 16 
and financial assistance for training costs.  17
