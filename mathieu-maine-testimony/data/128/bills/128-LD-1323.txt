SUMMARY  31 
This bill authorizes the Secretary of State to suspend, revoke or refuse to renew the 32 
commission of a notary public who has allowed another person to use that notary public's 33 
commission.  It also authorizes the Secretary of State to invalid ate a petition for a direct 34 
initiative or people's veto if the Secretary of State is unable to verify the notarization of 35 
that petition.  The bill directs the Secretary of State to establish an online reporting 36
