SUMMARY  15 
This bill requires the Secretary of State to conduct a recount paid for by the State for 16 
a referendum if the percentage difference between the yes and no votes as shown by the 17 
official tabulation of the  referendum votes is 0.25% or less.  It allows a group of voters to 18 
request a recount for a referendum in which the percentage difference between the yes 19 
and no votes as shown in the official tabulation of the referendum votes is greater than 20 
0.25%.  The p arty requesting a recount for a referendum in which the percentage 21 
difference between the yes and no votes as shown by the official tabulation is greater than 22 
0.25% must pay for the recount.  23
