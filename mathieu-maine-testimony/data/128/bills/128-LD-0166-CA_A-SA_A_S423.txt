SENATE AMENDMENT "A " to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 58, 
L.D. 166, Bill, "An Act To Increase Reimbursement for Child Care Services" 
Amend the amendment by inserting after the title the following: 
'Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Increase Reimbursement for Child Care Services and To Allow for the 
Payment of Outstanding Child Care Costs' 
Amend the bill by inserting after the enacting clause and before section 1 the 
following: 
'Sec. 1. 22 MRSA ยง3736, sub-ยง4 is enacted to read: 
4. Cost of child care. If the cost of child care exceeds the amount of subsidy for a

pay the outstanding balance of child care costs after subtracting the amount of subsidy 
without losing the subsidy.' ' 
Amend the amendment by inserting after section 6 the following: 
'Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter _or

SUMMARY 
This amendment allows a parent who receives a child care subsidy to make up the 
difference between the amount of the subsidy and the total cost of child care without 
losing the subsidy.
