Sec. 2.  25 MRSA §2934 -A is enacted to read:  24 
§2934 -A.  Emergency calling from multiline telephone systems  25 
1.  Direct dialing of 911.   A pu blic or private entity that installs or operates a 26 
multiline telephone system shall ensure that the system is connected to the public 27 
switched telephone network in such a way that when an individual using the system dials 28 
911, the call connects to the publ ic safety answering point without requiring the user to 29 
first dial any other number or set of numbers.  This subsection does not apply to any local 30 
unit of government if complying would necessitate additional expenditures from local 31 
revenues.  32 
2.  Complianc e period.   A public or private entity shall comply with subsection 1 33 
within one year after the effective date of this section or, if the public or private entity 34 
does not have a multiline telephone system capable of complying with subsection 1, by 35 
the date  that the multiline telephone system is next upgraded to a system capable of 36 
complying with subsection 1.  37
