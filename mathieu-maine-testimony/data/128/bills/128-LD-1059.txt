SUMMARY  16 
This bill requires the Department of Transportation to enter into an agreement 17 
regarding a bridge when the town way carried by the bridge is being discontinued by the 18 
municipality, or county in the case of a n unorganized territory.  The agreement must be 19 
made with the municipality or county or with another state agency and must require the 20 
municipality or county or state agency to be responsible for the ownership and possible 21 
maintenance of the bridge or to u nderstand that the department may choose to remove the 22 
bridge.  The bill also provides that an inspection pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, 23 
Title 23, section 563 may occur only on a bridge or minor span on a public way.  24
