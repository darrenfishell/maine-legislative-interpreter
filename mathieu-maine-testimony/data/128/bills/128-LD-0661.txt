Upon cremation of the body of a deceased person, the crem atory shall prepare a 4

Human remains may not be removed, transported or shipped to a crematory unless 13

SUMMARY  17 
This bill requires crematories to prepare a certificate of cremation in order to certify 18 
the identity of the body that was cremated.  The bill requires crema tories to label the 19 
container containing the cremated remains with the name of the deceased person.  20
