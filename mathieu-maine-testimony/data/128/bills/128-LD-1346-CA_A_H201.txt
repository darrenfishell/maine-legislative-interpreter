COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 936, L.D. 1346, Bill, "An Act To 
Clarify Access to Landlocked Ancient and Family Burying Grounds and To Clarify the 
Definition of Ancient Burying Ground" 
Amend the bill in section 3 in subsection 1-A in the 2nd paragraph in the 2nd and 3rd 
to last lines (page 2, lines 7 and 8 in L.D.) by striking out the following: 11municipal clerk 
of the municipality11 and inserting the following: 1registty of deeds in the county1 
Amend the bill in section 3 in subsection 1-A in the last paragraph in the 2nd line 
(page 2, line 11 in L.D.) by striking out the following: 11municipal clerk of the 
municipality11 and inserting the following: 1registry of deeds in the county1 
SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the committee. The amendment requires 
owners of land surrounding ancient or family burying grounds to file a notarized listing of 
obstructions with the county registry of deeds rather than the municipal clerk as in the 
bill.
