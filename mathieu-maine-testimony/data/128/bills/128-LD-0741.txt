 SUMMARY  1 
This bill allows a municipality that is a member of a school administrative unit 2 
receiving the minimum state share for education funding to impose by referendum a local 3 
option sales tax of up to 1% to be used for funding kindergarten to grade 12 education 4 
and reducing the property tax rate.  This bill also repeals a provision of the education 5 
funding laws calculating a minimum state share of costs based on a percentage of special 6 
education costs.  7
