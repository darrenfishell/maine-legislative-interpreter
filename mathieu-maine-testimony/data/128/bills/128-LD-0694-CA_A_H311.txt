§11701. Authority of commissioner; wild turkey hunting 
The commissioner may establish open seasons for hunting wild turkeys, designate 
areas that are open to the taking of wild turkeys in any part of the State, prescribe the 
fonn and regulate the number of permits to be issued, determine the number and sex of 
the birds to be harvested, establish bag limits, establish permit eligibility requirements,

1. Spring season. The commissioner shall establish by rule a spring open season for 
hunting wild turkeys. Unless the commissioner determines that sound management 
principles require otherwise, the rule must allow during the spring open season for 
hunting wild turkeys:

the 2nd wild turkey; 
B. A person to hunt from 30 minutes before sunrise to 30 minutes after sunset; and 
C. A person to hunt wild turkeys using a crossbow, in addition to any other weapons 
allowed by the rule.

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill directs the commissioner to establish annual weekends prior to the bear, deer, wild turkey and migratory 
waterfowl hunting seasons during which a youth hunter may hunt on Sundays with the landowners written permission. 
Any additional costs to the Department ofinland Fisheries and Wildlife as a result of the youth hunting weekends 
establishment and the Sunday hunting provisions are anticipated to be minor and can be absorbed within existing 
budgeted resources.
