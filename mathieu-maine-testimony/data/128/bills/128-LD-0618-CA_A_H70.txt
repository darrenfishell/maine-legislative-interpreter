COMMITTEE AMENDMENT «A "to H.P. 434, L.D. 618, "Resolve, Authorizing 
the Mount Hunger Area of the Town of Gray To Proceed with the Secession Process" 
Amend the resolve by striking out everything after the title and insetting the 
following: 
'Sec. 1. Mount Hunger area of the Town of Gray authorized to proceed 
with the secession process. Resolved: That, pursuant to the Maine Revised 
Statutes, Title 30-A, section 2171-C-1, the Mount Hunger area of the Town of Gray is 
authorized to proceed with the secession process in accordance with Title 30-A, chapter 
113, subchapter 2.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the committee. The amendment replaces the 
bill, which is a concept draft. The amendment authorizes the Mount Hunger area of the 
Town of Gray to proceed with the secession process in accordance with the Maine 
Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, chapter 113, subchapter 2.
