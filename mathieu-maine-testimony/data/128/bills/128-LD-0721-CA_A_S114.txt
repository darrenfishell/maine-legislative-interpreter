9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A " to S.P. 238, L.D. 721, Bill, "An Act To

12 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
13 summary and inserting the following: 
14 'Sec. 1. 17-A MRSA ยง517 is enacted to read: 
15 ยง517. Failure to report a planned act of terrorism 
16 1. A person is guilty of failure to report a planned act of terrorism if that person 
17 knows that another person is planning to act with terroristic intent and, in fact, the person 
18 does not report that planned act of terrorism to an appropriate law enforcement agency. 
19 2. Failure to report a planned act of terrorism is a Class C crime.' 
20 SUMMARY 
21 This amendment replaces the bill. It creates a new strict liability Class C crime for 
22 failure to report a planned act of terrorism. A person is guilty of this crime if the person 
23 knows that another person is planning to act with terroristic intent and does not report it 
24 to law enforcement.
