1.  Educational materials.  The Department of Education shall develop and post on 23 
its publicly accessible website guidelines and other relevant materials to inform and 24

an acknowledgment of receipt and review of the sudden cardiac arrest symptoms 33 
informational and educational guidelines under subsection 1.  34 
3.  Removal from participation.  An athletic director, coach or athletic trainer:  35

SUMMARY  32 
This bill enacts the Sudden Cardiac Arrest Prevention Act, which provides for 33 
education regarding sudden cardiac arrest in students engaged in athletics, the removal 34 
from athletic events of students who exhibit symptoms of cardiac arrest, education and 35 
training for athletic directors, coaches and athletic trainers and penalties for violations of 36 
the Act.  37
