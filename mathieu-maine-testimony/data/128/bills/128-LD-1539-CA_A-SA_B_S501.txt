SUMMARY 
This amendment requires a caregiver designated to possess and administer medical 
marijuana for use by a qualifying patient who is enrolled in primary or secondary school 
to submit to the same background check applicable to education personnel. The 
background check required includes finge rinting.

The amendment requires a caregiver, who is not the patient's parent or guardian, designated to possess and administe· 
medical marijuana for use by a qualifying patient who is enrolled in a primary or secondary school to pass the same 
background check applicable to educational personnel, including fingerprinting. The additional costs to the 
Department of Public Safety (DPS) to process additional fingerprint checks are expected to be minor. The new fees 
received by DPS for fingerprint checks are expected to be minor.
