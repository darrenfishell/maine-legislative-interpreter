SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, replaces the bill and 
changes the title. The amendment allows a municipality that has more than 4,000 
residents to affumatively opt out of the Maine Uniform Energy Code by adopting the 
Maine Uniform Building Code.
