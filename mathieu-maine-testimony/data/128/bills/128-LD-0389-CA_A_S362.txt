The bill includes an ongoing Other Special Revenue Funds allocation to the Department of Professional and Financial 
Regulation of $146, 149 beginning in fiscal year 2018-19 to hire and outfit one additional Senior Bank Examiner and 
one part-time Consumer Outreach Specialist to manage the increased workload required from changes in this bill. 
The bill will also generate $22,000 in Other Special Revenue Funds revenue to the Department of Professional and 
Financial Regulation. $21,000 of this is from examination fees for an anticipated increase in examiners and $1,000 is 
from an estimated increase in assessment revenue from institutions based on assets.
