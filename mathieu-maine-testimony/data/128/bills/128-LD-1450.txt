SUMMARY  15 
For the purpose of establishing the film industry  as a permanent component of the 16 
economic base of Maine; developing a pool of trained professionals and businesses in 17 
Maine to supply and support the film industry in Maine; increasing employment of Maine 18 
residents; improving the economic success of existi ng businesses in Maine; and 19 
developing the infrastructure in Maine necessary for a thriving film industry, this bill 20 
makes the following changes to the visual media production certification process, 21 
reimbursement and credit:  22 
1.  It specifies that a visual media production expense must be for preproduction, 23 
production and postproduction work performed in Maine;  24 
2.  It increases the cap on wages that can be included as a visual media production 25 
expense from $50,000 per individual to $75,000 per individual;  26 
3.  It increases the reimbursement rate from 12% of certified production wages that 27 
are paid to Maine residents and 10% of certified production wages paid to non -Maine 28 
residents to 25% of all visual media production expenses, including wages, and specifies 29 
that the reimbursement for wages is for all personnel, including filmmakers, cast 30 
members and the technical production and postproduction staff of a visual media 31 
production company as long as the company has at least $75,000 in visual media 32 
production expen ses from the certified production;  33 
4.  It repeals the certified visual media production tax credit and caps reimbursement 34 
of expenses at $750,000;  35 
5.  It specifies that, in order to be eligible for the visual media production 36 
reimbursement, at least 25% of  the above -the-line personnel, as well as 50% of the 37 
below -the-line personnel, not including extras, must be Maine residents, and either the 38 
visual media production expenses exceed 50% of the total production expenses or at least 39 
75% of the total principal  photography days occur in Maine; and  40

 6.  It specifies that a person claiming the Pine Tree Development Zone tax credit is 1
