36 SUMMARY
37 This bill implements the recommendation of the tax expenditure report of the Joint 
38 Standing Committee on Taxation  and the evaluation report of the Office of Program  
39 Evaluation and Government Accountability with regard  to the new markets  capital 
40 investment credit by excluding from the definition of "qualified  low-income community 
41 investment" a capital or equity  investment if more than 5% of the investment is used to 
42 refinance costs, expenses or investments incurred  or paid by the qualified active low-
43 income community business or a party related to the qualified active low-income 
44 community business prior to the date of the qualified low-income community investment;
