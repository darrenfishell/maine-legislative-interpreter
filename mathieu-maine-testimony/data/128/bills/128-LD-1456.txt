SUMMARY  29 
This bill amends the provisions of law regarding the State Compensation 30 
Commission, which makes recommendations for compensation for legislators and 31 
constitutional officers, by returning the  commission to its previous form in the following 32 
ways:  33 
1.  Changing membership requirements;  34 
2.  Changing report requirements and reporting dates; and  35 
3.  Expanding the scope of the commission to include making recommendations for 36 
compensation of the Gove rnor, justices and judges, the Secretary and Assistant Secretary 37 
of the Senate and the Clerk and Assistant Clerk of the House of Representatives.  38
