Sec. 2.  20-A MRSA §1312 -A is enacted to read:  23 
§1312 -A.  Reserve fund for operating costs  24 
School administrative districts may establish a reserve fund for operating costs as 25 
follows.  26 
1.  Establishment.   A school administrative district may establish a reserve fund for 27 
up to 3 months' operating costs, including salary and benefits.  The board of directors is 28 
the trustee of the reserve fund.  The reserve fund must be deposited or invested by the 29 
treasurer under the direction of the board.  30 
2.  Deposit or investment.   All district funds, including reserve funds, must be 31 
deposited or invested by the treasurer under the direction of the board of directors 32 
according to the requirements for the deposit or investment of municipal funds contained 33 
in Title 30 -A, chapter 223, subchapter 3 -A. 34 
3.  Expending money from reserve funds.   The board of directors may expend the 35 
sum in the reserve fund when authorized to do so by a vote of the district  at a district 36 
meeting or a district budget meeting, when an article for that purpose is set out in the 37 
warrant calling the meeting.  38
