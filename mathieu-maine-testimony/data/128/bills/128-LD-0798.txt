SUMMARY  25 
This bill amends the Marijuana Legalization Act to require the state licensing 26 
authority, when issuing a license under the Act, to give preferenc e to individuals who are 27 
residents of this State and to businesses that are organized or incorporated in this State.  28 
This bill also requires any licenses issued for retail marijuana cultivation facilities to be 29 
issued in an equal number between those faci lities with 3,000 square feet or less of plant 30 
canopy and those with more than 3,000 square feet of plant canopy.  31
