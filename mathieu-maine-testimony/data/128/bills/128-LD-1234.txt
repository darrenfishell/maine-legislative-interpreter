SUMMARY  3 
This bill changes the age range for compulsory school attendance from 7 years of age 4 
to under 17 years of age to 6 years of age to under 18 years of age and  changes the 5 
number of absences required for a student to be considered truant.  It establishes a fine for 6 
parents adjudged for repeated violations of the truancy laws and allows the 7 
superintendent to inform the Department of Health and Human Services that  the student 8 
is a victim of abuse or neglect based on the parent's failure to ensure compliance with 9 
school attendance requirements.  It also establishes a fine and possible driver's license 10 
suspension for truant students.  11
