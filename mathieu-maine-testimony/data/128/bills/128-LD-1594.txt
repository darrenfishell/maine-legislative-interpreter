SUMMARY  34 
This bill provides that a pharmacist may prescribe and dispense naloxone 35 
hydrochloride to an individual at risk of experiencing an opioid -related drug overdose or 36 
to a person in a position to assist such an ind ividual.  Current law provides that a 37 
pharmacist may dispense naloxone hydrochloride to such an individual or person.  The 38
