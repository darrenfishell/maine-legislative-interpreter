SUMMARY  33 
The purpose of this bill is to protect the environment and wildlife of the State and to 34 
reduce the increasing costs of litter collection and solid waste disposal by prohibiting the 35 
sale or distribution of disposable polystyrene food service containers, which constitute a 36 
sizable portion of nondegradable litter and solid waste in the State and which frag ment 37
