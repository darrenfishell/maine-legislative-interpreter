COMMITTEE AMENDMENT,{+" to H.P. 1069, L.D. 1551, Bill, "An Act To 
Amend the Maine Tax Laws" 
Amend the bill in Patt B by striking out all of sections 1 and 8. 
Amend the bill in Part B in section 11 by striking out all of the first sentence (page 9, 
lines 32 to 34 in L.D.). 
Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment removes from the bill a provision requiring that a prepaid wireless 
fee be remitted in the same manner as the service provider tax and a provision that 
changes the application of the penalty in current law for a retailer knowingly charging or 
collecting excess sales tax.
