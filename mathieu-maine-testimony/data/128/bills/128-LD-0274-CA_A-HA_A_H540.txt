SUMMARY 
This amendment strikes and replaces the sections of the majority report describing 
which child care staff members and providers are required to undergo a criminal 
background check that meets the requirements of 42 United States Code, Section 
9858f(b). It replaces thes sections with the bill's versions of those provisions.
