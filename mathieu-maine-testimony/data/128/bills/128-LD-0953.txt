SUMMARY  27 
The funds provided by this bond issue, in the amount of $20,000,000, will be used to 28 
provide support for the biomass industry in this State through the esta blishment of a 29 
revolving loan fund for capital investment in biomass generators or similar facilities.  The 30 
fund is administered by the Public Utilities Commission.  31
