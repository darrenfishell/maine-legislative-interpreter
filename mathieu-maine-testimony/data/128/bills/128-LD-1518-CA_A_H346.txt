Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Additional costs to the Department of Labor to establish the Maine Educational and Attainment Research Navigation 
System and to serve on the Maine Educational and Attainment Research Navigation System Executive Council can be 
absorbed within existing budgeted resources. Additional costs to the Department of Education, the University of 
Maine System and the Maine Community College System to serve on the Council can be absorbed within existing 
budgeted resources.
