This bill clarifies under the liquor laws that "spirits" does not include a food flavo ring 15 
or similar product used for cooking that has a high alcohol content and that a "low - 16 
alcohol spirits product" does not include malt liquor or malt liquor flavored with a food 17

the malt liquor from being taxed as a low -alcohol spirits product.  19
