SUMMARY  17 
This bill requires that, beginning October 1, 2017, Maine Care coverage for adult 18 
dental services include an annual comprehensive oral examination and preventive 19 
services, including prophylaxis, topical fluoride, sealants, oral hygiene instruction, 20 
behavior management and smoking cessation counseling.  21
