4 Sec. 2-A.  Sale of electricity; municipally  owned electricity provider.

10 The owner of the hydropower facilities may offer to a municipally  owned  electricity

12 electricity generated by the hydropower  facilities.  Electricity generated by the 
13 hydropower facilities that is not under contract to be sold to another entity may be offered 
14 for sale by the owner of the hydropower  facilities  to the owner  or tenant  of a rural 
15 manufacturing or industrial site.  For the purposes of this section, "municipally  owned 
16 electricity provider" means a municipally owned entity  authorized under  applicable law 
17 to buy and sell electricity.
18 SUMMARY
19 This bill allows the owner of certain hydropower  facilities to offer for sale to the 
20 owner or tenant of a rural manufacturing  or industrial site electricity generated by those 
21 hydropower facilities that  is not under contract to be sold to  another entity.
