SUMMARY 
This amendment provides that the Department of Health and Human Services may 
finally adopt portions of Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 
21, Allowances for Home and Community Benefits for Adults with Intellectual 
Disabilities or Autism Spectrum Disorder, a provisionally adopted major substantive rule, 
only if the rule is modified. 
The required modification relates to documentation for the audit of services provided. 
The proposed rule requires documentation showing the hours and the name of the direct 
care staff scheduled to work with the member. The required modification requires that the 
documentation show the hours and the name of the direct care staff scheduled to work at 
the facility. This conforms with the requirements of Resolve 2017, chapter 15.
