22 SUMMARY
23 This bill provides a waiver of the fee charged for the basic training course at the 
24 Maine Criminal Justice Academy for honorably discharged veterans of the United States  
25 Armed Forces who served as military police officers and who have  been hired  for or have  
26 received conditional offers of full-time  employment as law enforcement  officers  if the 
27 veterans' military experience and education meet certain criteria.
