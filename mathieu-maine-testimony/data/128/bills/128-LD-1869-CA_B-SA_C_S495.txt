20 SUMMARY 
21 This amendment does the following. 
22 1. It changes the mill expectation from 8.49 to 8.48. 
23 2. It increases the allocation for career and technical education costs by $2,000,000. 
24 3. It decreases the allocation for alignment of career and technical education 
25 programs with national industry standards by $2,000,000. 
26 4. It makes changes to the total cost of funding public education from kindergarten to 
27 grade 12 and to the local contribution to the total cost of funding public education from 
28 kindergarten to grade 12. 
29 5. It provides that for the purposes of calculating the total allocation for a career and 
30 technical education center or career and technical education region any cap on the total 
31 allocation does not apply for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2018 and ending June 30, 
32 2019 only.

Fiscal Note 
Amends 2018-2019 allocations for funding total cost ofK-12 public education 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This amendment amends the allocations for funding the total cost ofK-12 public education in 2018-2019 by 
increasing the allocation for career and technical education costs by $2,000,000 and decreasing the allocation for 
national industry standards for career and technical education by the same amount.
