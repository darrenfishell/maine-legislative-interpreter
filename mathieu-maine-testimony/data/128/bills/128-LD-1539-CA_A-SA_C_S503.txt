SUMMARY 
This amendment adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause to the bill. It 
provides that a registered caregiver may operate one retail store to sell harvested 
marijuana to qualifying patients. It provides that a municipality may not prohibit 
registered caregiver retail stores, registered dispensaries, marijuana testing facilities and 
manufacturing facilities that are operating in the municipality prior to the effective date of 
this legislation and that a municipality may not authorize registered caregiver retail 
stores, registered dispensaries, marijuana testing facilities and manufacturing facilities 
that are not operating on the effective date of this legislation to operate in the 
municipality unless the municipal legislative body has voted to adopt or amend an 
ordinance or approve a warrant article allowing registered caregiver retail stores, 
registered dispensaries, marijuana testing facilities or manufacturing facilities to operate 
within the municipality.
