SUMMARY  4 
This bill authorizes the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau 5 
of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations to issue licenses to public  service 6 
berthing vessels for the sale of spirits, wine and malt liquor on overnight voyages of at 7 
least one night.  The bill establishes requirements and fees for such licensing.  8
