HOUSE AMENDMENT" A" to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 323, 
L.D. 456, Bill, "An Act To Increase Access to Vaccinations" 
Amend the amendment in section 1 in subsection 2 in paragraph A in the last line 
(page 1, line 28 in amendment) by inserting after the following: "subsection 3." the 
following: 'A pharmacist who administers a vaccine pursuant to this paragraph shall 
report the administration of that vaccine to the immunization information system 
established by the Maine Center for Disease Control and Prevention.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment requires that a pharmacist who administers a vaccine to a person 
who is under 18 years of age report the administration of that vaccine to the Maine Center 
for Disease Control and Prevention for inclusion in the immunization information system 
referred to as "ImmPact." ~
