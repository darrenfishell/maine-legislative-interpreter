SUMMARY  28 
This bill amends the law regarding holding or storing a vehicle and its accessories, 29 
contents and equipment to make it apply to any vehicle that has been towed and limits the 30 
amount of money that may be charged for storage of the vehicle to 3 d ays' storage fees 31 
unless notice is given to the Secretary of State no sooner than 3 days following receipt of 32 
the vehicle and no later than 5 days following receipt.  It prohibits a person holding or 33 
storing the vehicle from charging a total amount more th an $900 for a 30 -day period or a 34 
prorated amount for a greater or lesser period of time and provides that that person may 35 
not give notice of an abandoned vehicle to the Secretary of State until the vehicle has 36 
been in the person's possession for 30 days.  37
