2 Sec. 1.  30-A MRSA §3014,  sub-§2,  ¶B, as amended by PL 2013, c. 161, §1, is 
3 further amended  to read:
4 B.  A municipality may prohibit residence by a sex offender up to a maximum 
5 distance of 750 feet surrounding  the real property comprising a public or private 
6 elementary, middle or secondary school or up to a maximum distance of 750 feet 
7 surrounding the real property owned by the municipality  or the State, including, but 
8 not limited to, real property comprising a municipally owned  or state-owned property 
9 that is leased  to a nonprofit organization for purposes  of a park, athletic field or 
10 recreational facility that is open to the public where children are the primary users.
11 SUMMARY
12 Current law allows a municipality  to prohibit a sex offender  from  residing within 750 
13 feet of municipally  owned or state-owned  property that is leased to a nonprofit 
14 organization for use as a park, athletic field or recreational facility  open to the public 
15 where children are  the primary users.
16 This bill allows a municipality to prohibit a sex offender  from residing within 750 
17 feet of any municipally owned or  state-owned property, regardless of its use.
