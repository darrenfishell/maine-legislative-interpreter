SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to establish a statewide commission to study housing insecurity in 5 
the context of aging in place .  The commission created under this bill would be tasked 6 
with exploring:  7 
1.  The long -term implications associated with the State's aging population;  8 
2.  The status of available affordable housing options in the State;  9 
3.  The availability of weatherizati on and repair programs that would allow older 10 
individuals to remain in their homes;  11 
4.  Various models of affordable and appropriate housing opportunities for older 12 
citizens that are in place in other states; and  13 
5.  The establishment of a statewide progra m facilitating the building for older 14 
persons of small accessory dwelling units that are located on the same grounds as, or 15 
attached to, a primary residence belonging to a family member of the older person.  16
