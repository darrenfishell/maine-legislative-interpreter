COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 139, L.D. 412, Bill, "An Act To 
Require the Completion of Courses of Study in Home Economics and Industrial Arts 
Education Prior to Graduation from High School" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'Resolve, To Direct the Development of After-school Programs' 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and before the summary and 
insertjng the following: 
'Sec. 1. After-school programs; working group. Resolved: That the 
Department of Education shall work with the Maine Afterschool Network to create a 
working group consisting of members of the Maine Afterschool Network and 
representatives of interested and appropriate business entities to design and develop after­
school programs, including but not limited to industrial arts and family and consumer 
sciences after-school programs, that schools can adopt to meet the interests and needs of 
students; and be it further 
Sec. 2. Report. Resolved: That the Department of Education by January 15, 2018 
shall report to the Joint Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs on the 
progress of the working group created pursuant to section 1 along with any 
recommendations to promote success of the effort to design and develop after-school 
programs.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill with a resolve. This amendment, which is the 
minority report of the committee, directs the Department of Education to work with the 
Maine Afterschool Network to create a working group consisting of members of the 
Maine Afterschool Network and representatives of interested business tntities to design 
and develop after-school programs that schools can adopt to meet the interests and needs 
of students. The department is directed to report by January 15, 2018 to the Joint 
Standing Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs on the progress of the working
