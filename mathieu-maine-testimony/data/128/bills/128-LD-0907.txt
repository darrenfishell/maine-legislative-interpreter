SUMMARY  18 
This bill requires that the Board of Trustees of the Maine Public Employees 19 
Retirement System invest and reinvest retirement system funds in well -diversified asset 20 
classes that will generate the maximum return and prohibits investment in narrowly 21 
tailored asset classes.  22
