24 resource.
25 SUMMARY
26 This bill allows a hydropower facility  licensed  after January 1, 2018 with a licensed 
27 capacity of no more  than 30 megawatts  that is interconnected with an electric distribution 
28 system located  in Maine to qualify as a new renewable capacity resource for purposes of 
29 meeting renewable energy portfolio standard requirements.
