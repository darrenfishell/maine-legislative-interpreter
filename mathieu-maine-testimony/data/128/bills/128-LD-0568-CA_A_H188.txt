SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title to reflect the content of the 
amendment. 
This amendment provides that the party or political designation of a candidate of an 
unqualified party is limited to one word and may not include additional numbers or 
symbols. Presidential and vice-presidential candidate designations remain subject to the 
current law, which provides that such a party or political designation must be no longer 
than 3 words.
