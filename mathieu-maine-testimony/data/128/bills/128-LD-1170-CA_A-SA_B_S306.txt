This amendment adds an exception to raising the legal age to purchase cigarettes and tobacco products from 18 to 21 
for individuals who are 18 years old as of July 1, 2018. The incremental impact compared to committee amendment 
A is to increase General Fund revenue by $3,587,017 in fiscal year 2017-18 and by $4,594,128 in fiscal year 2018-19 
and to increase Local Government Fund revenue by $6,616 in fiscal year 2017-18 and by $8,546 in fiscal year 2018-
19. 
As amended, the bill would have no revenue loss in fiscal year 2017-18, a loss of General Fund revenue of $106,075 
in fiscal year 2018-19 and a loss of Local Government Fund revenue of $111 in fiscal year 2018-19.
