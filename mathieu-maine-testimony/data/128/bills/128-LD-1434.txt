SUMMARY  20 
This bill clarifies the following state laws related to education in the unorganized 21 
territories.  22 
1.  It requires the Department of Education to collect and publish academic outcome 23 
data for any public schoo l located in an unorganized territory.  The data must be collected 24 
and published in accordance with existing kindergarten to grade 12 educational reporting 25 
guidelines.  26 
2.  It requires the Department of Education to develop and publish budget data for 27 
any p ublic school located in an unorganized territory.  The data must be collected and 28 
published in accordance with existing kindergarten to grade 12 educational reporting 29 
guidelines.  30 
3.  It eliminates the Commissioner of Education's power to appoint agents and  special 31 
attendance officers in the unorganized territories and requires that the Department of 32 
Education contract for school administrative services for the unorganized territories with 33 
neighboring school administrative units.  34 
4.  It provides that public schools located in unorganized territories are subject to the 35 
requirements of the system of learning results.  36
