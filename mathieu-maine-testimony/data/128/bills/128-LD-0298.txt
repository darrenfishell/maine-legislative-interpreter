SUMMARY  15 
This bill prohibits state agencies from expending public resources to influence the 16 
outcome of a direct initiative of legislation, a people's veto referendum or other statewide 17 
referendum except to provide an im partial factual summary regarding what is at issue.  It 18 
also extends the law governing the involvement of executive branch employees in 19 
elections to include direct initiatives of legislation, people's veto referenda and other 20 
statewide referenda.  21
