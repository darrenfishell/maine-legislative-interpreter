SUMMARY  24 
This resolve establishes a pilot project in Washington County to provide treatment 25 
and recovery services for substance use disorders.  It provides $1,600,000 in funding over 26 
the 2018 -2019 fiscal biennium.  The Department of Health and Human Serv ices is 27 
required to report on the planning and implementation of the pilot project to the Joint 28 
Standing Committee on Health and Human Services no later than November 30, 2018, 29 
and the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over he alth and 30 
human services matters may report out legislation to the First Regular Session of the 31 
129th Legislature.  32
