25 SUMMARY
26 This bill establishes  for each year from  2021 to 2030 the amount  of allowances the 
27 State can auction  as a participant in the regional greenhouse gas initiative.  It also 
28 establishes the adjustments for banked allowances  that must be made to the base annual  
29 carbon dioxide  emissions  budgets for  2021 to  2025.
