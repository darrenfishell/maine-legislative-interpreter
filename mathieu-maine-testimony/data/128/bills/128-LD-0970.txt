ยง4766.  Housing First Assistance Program  5 
1.  Establishment; purpose.  The Housing First Assistance Program, referred to in 6 
this subchapter as "the program," is established under the jurisdiction of the Maine State 7 
Housing Authority, referred to in this subchapter as "the authority," to provide permanent 8 
housing to people experiencing homeless ness in those areas of the State where the need is 9 
greatest and to homeless individuals addicted to opiates.  10 
2.  Requirements.  The authority shall coordinate with emergency shelters, street 11 
outreach providers and other crisis responders in those areas the  authority determines 12 
have the largest population of chronically homeless individuals and families in order to 13 
provide those persons with permanent housing through the program.  The program must 14 
meet the core elements for a Housing First program as delinea ted by the United States 15 
Interagency Council on Homelessness, or a successor organization, including, but not 16 
limited to, the following.  17 
A.  The program may not exclude applicants from participation in the program based 18 
solely on:  19 
(1)  Low income or a lack  of income or income source;  20 
(2)  A poor credit or financial history;  21 
(3)  A poor rental history or lack of a rental history;  22 
(4)  Abuse of drugs or alcohol or a failure to participate in or complete a 23 
substance abuse program or similar behavior modificati on program;  24 
(5)  A criminal record; or  25 
(6)  Other behavior that indicates a lack of readiness for housing.  26 
B.  The program must provide support services for drug and alcohol addiction as a 27 
resource to participants.  Participation in the services must be vo luntary and not a 28 
condition of acceptance into the program or continued residency.  29 
C.  Conditions and terms for payment of rent must be reasonably flexible and allow 30 
for the use of individualized payment arrangements and financial assistance.  31 
D.  Eviction from the program, if such eviction would result in a participant's 32 
homelessness, must be avoided when possible.  33 
3.  Administration; operation by outside entity; grants; costs.  The authority shall 34 
administer the program.  The authority may contract with en tities not in State 35 
Government to provide housing under the program and award grants to such entities 36

SUMMARY  16 
This bill establishes the Housing First Assistance Program.  The program is 17 
established in and administered by the Maine State Housing Authority.  The authority is 18 
required to work with emergency shelte rs and other crisis responders in the neediest areas 19 
of the State to provide permanent housing and support services for chronically homeless 20 
individuals and families and to homeless individuals addicted to opiates.  The program 21 
must meet the core elements for a "Housing First" program as delineated by the United 22 
States Interagency Council on Homelessness, including not disqualifying or evicting a 23 
resident due to alcohol or substance abuse, a criminal record, poor financial history or 24 
lack of an income.  The  authority may contract with outside entities to operate the 25 
housing units and award grants to those entities under a competitive bid process.  26 
This bill provides ongoing funding necessary to fund 30 housing units and provide 27 
support services at those units  for chronically homeless individuals including opiate - 28 
addicted homeless individuals.  29
