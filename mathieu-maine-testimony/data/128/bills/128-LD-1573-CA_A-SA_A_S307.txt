SENATE AMENDMENT" A" to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 551, 
L.D. 1573, Bill, "An Act To Encourage Development in the Logging Industry by 
Requiring State and Local Government Agencies To Give Preference to Lumber and 
Solid Wood Products Harvested in the State" 
Amend the amendment by striking out all of the 5th and 6th indented paragraphs 
(page 1, lines 22 and 23 in amendment) and inserting the following: 
'Amend the bill in section 1 in § 1778 by striking out all of subsection 4 (page 2, lines 
8 to 11 in L.D.) and inserting the following: 
'4. Timber. "Timber" means roundwood and residue.'' 
Amend the amendment by striking out all of the 2nd occurrence of subsection 2 (page 
1, lines 33 and 34 and page 2, lines 1 to 7 in amendment). 
Amend the amendment by inserting before the 11th indented paragraph (page 2, line 
8 in amendment) the following: 
'Amend the bill in section 1 in § 1779 by renumbering the subsections to read 
consecutively.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment removes the requirement that, if the State provides a subsidy to a 
person who purchases timber, the person give preference to timber harvested in the State 
by an in-state contractor.
