This bill includes a one-time General Fund appropriation of $4,000 in fiscal year 2017-18 to the Maine Labor 
Relations Board for the costs associated with the rulemaking process. Of this amount, $1, 750 is for the per diem for 
board members and $2,250 is for printing and publication of notices and other related expenses. 
The Maine Labor Relations Board has also indicated that it may require additional General Fund appropriations for 
staff and other technical resources prior to implementing the recettification elections. The amount can not be 
estimated at this time and will depend on the methods and procedures for conducting the elections adopted by the 
board during the rulemaking process.
