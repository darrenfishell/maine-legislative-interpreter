SUMMARY  7 
This bill restr icts the use of pesticides on school grounds. It allows their use only in 8 
situations that pose a health threat to a student or staff member, in response to the 9 
presence of animals or insects identified as a public health nuisance or on agricultural 10 
fields in accordance with the manufacturer's instructions.  It permits their use on athletic 11 
fields when determined necessary by the school for the health and safety of the field and 12 
students.  It requires the Commissioner of Education to adopt rules to implement  13 
landscaping design that minimizes or avoids the necessity of the use of pesticides on 14 
school grounds for new construction of school facilities.  15
