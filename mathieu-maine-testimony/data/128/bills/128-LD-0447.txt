SUMMARY  13 
Part A of this bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to fund, 14 
through a request for proposals, a position to coordinate substance use disord er 15 
prevention, advocacy, education and community outreach statewide and a position to 16 
coordinate peer recovery support services efforts for substance use disorder recovery 17 
statewide.  The request for proposals must be issued no later than January 1, 2018 a nd the 18 
funds awarded beginning no later than July 1, 2018.  19 
Part B of this bill provides funding for the request for proposals in Part A.  Part B 20 
also provides ongoing funding for an annual in -state conference regarding substance use 21 
disorder prevention, tr eatment and recovery.  22 
Part C of this bill requires the transfer of funds to the unappropriated surplus of the 23 
General Fund from the Medical Use of Marijuana Fund for the purposes of this bill.  24
