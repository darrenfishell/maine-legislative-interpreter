SUMMARY  29 
This bill allows the Commissioner of Agriculture, Conservation and For estry in cases 30 
of alleged cruelty to animals to waive costs and fees associated with the surrender of 31 
animals, and to decide that the case should not be sent to the attorney for the State, as 32 
long as the person who is subject to the investigation has volun tarily surrendered the 33 
animals to the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry and has not 34 
previously been charged with cruelty to animals or charged with animal neglect.  If the 35 
commissioner waives such costs or fees or decides that the case s houldn't be sent to the 36 
attorney for the State, the commissioner shall require the person to sign an affidavit 37 
stating that the person will never again possess or own animals of the same species as the 38
