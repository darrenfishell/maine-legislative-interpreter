SUMMARY  16 
This bill expands the causes for a 7 -day notice of termination of tenancy to include:  17 
violence, a threat of violence or sexual assault ag ainst other tenants, their guests, the 18 
landlord or the landlord's employees or agents; an unauthorized occupation of the 19 
premises; and the provision of false information on the tenant's rental application.  20
