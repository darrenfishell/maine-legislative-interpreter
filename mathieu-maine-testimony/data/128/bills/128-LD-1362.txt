SUMMARY  11 
The purpose of  this bill is to amend the definitions and service descriptions of the 12 
Department of Labor, Bureau of Rehabilitation Services in order to conform with the 13 
reauthorization of the federal Rehabilitation Services Administration through the 14 
Workforce Innovatio n and Opportunity Act of 2014. It eliminates the use of the term 15 
"disadvantaged individuals" and repeals provisions of law regarding personal care 16 
services because such services are not provided by the bureau.  17
