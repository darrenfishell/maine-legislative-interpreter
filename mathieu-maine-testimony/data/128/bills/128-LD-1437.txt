 SUMMARY  1 
This bill directs the Chief Justice of the Supreme Judicial Court to establish a youth - 2 
in-care court with jurisdiction over child protective and termination of parental rights 3 
proceedings as well as adoption and child custody proceedings involving youth in the 4 
foster care system as a pilot project within one or more judicial districts of the District 5 
Court.  The bill further directs the judicial branch to evaluate the effectiveness of the pilot 6 
project and to submit a report an d recommendations regarding the pilot project to the 7 
joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over judiciary matters by 8 
February 15, 2019.  The joint standing committee may report out a bill related to the 9 
report to the First Regular  Session of the 129th Legislature.  10
