SUMMARY  3 
This bill limits statewide assess ment program testing requirements to grades 3, 5, 7, 9 4 
and 11 and limits any use of alternative measures of student achievement to grades 9 and 5 
11. It also limits the statewide system of learning results testing requirements to students 6 
in grades 3, 5, 7, 9 and 11.  The bill authorizes the Commissioner of Education to adopt 7 
rules to implement the provisions of the bill.  8
