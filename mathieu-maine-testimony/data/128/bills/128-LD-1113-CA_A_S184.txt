COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 367, L.D. 1113, Bill, "An Act To 
Improve Antihunger Programs in Maine Schools" 
Amend the bill by striking out eve1ything after the enacting clause and before the 
summaty and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. 20-A MRSA ยง6601-A is enacted to read: 
ยง6601-A. National School Lunch Program; Internet-based school meal applications 
The depatiment shall make infonnation available to public schools regarding 
Internet-based applications for eligible students for free or reduced-price meals under the 
National School Lunch Program under 7 Code of Federal Regulations, Part 210. A public 
school may make an Internet-based application available for school meal applications. If 
a public school implements an Internet-based application process under this section, the 
public school shall make available a paper application for school meals to any student, 
parent or legal guardian who requests one.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment is the majority repoti of the committee. The amendment requires 
the Depatiment of Education to make infonnation available to public schools regarding 
Internet-based applications for eligible students for free or reduced-price meals under the 
National School Lunch Program as in the bill, but removes the requirement that the 
depatiment develop the application. The amendment specifies to whom a paper 
application must be made available if a public school implements an Internet-based 
application process.
