12 SUMMARY 
13 This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment provides that a campground may 
14 conduct beano without a license if games are conducted only for campground patrons and 
15 their guests, prizes do not exceed $25 per game and proceeds from the games are used 
16 only for prizes and to cover the actual costs of conducting the game. The amendment 
17 also adds an emergency preamble and an emergency clause.

Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill creates an exemption allowing licensed campgrounds to conduct beano or bingo without a gaming license as 
long as the game is exclusively for patrons of the campground, prizes are less than $25 in value and proceeds from the 
game are only used to cover the cost of prizes. Any additional costs to the Department of Public Safety associated 
with rulemaking as a result of the provisions of the bill are expected to be minor and can be absorbed within existing 
budgeted resources.
