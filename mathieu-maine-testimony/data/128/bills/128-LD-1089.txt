SUMMARY  14 
This bill prohibits a person from using a mobile telephone or handheld electronic 15 
device while operating a motor vehicle unless the mobile telephone or handheld 16 
electronic device allows for hands -free operation and the hands -free feature of the mobile 17 
telephone or electronic ha ndheld device is in fact being used by a person 18 years of age 18 
or older or the mobile telephone or handheld electronic device is being used in order to 19 
communicate with law enforcement or emergency services personnel under emergency 20 
circumstances.  21 
The bil l retains the prohibition in current law against the use of a mobile telephone or 22 
handheld electronic device, both handheld and hands -free use, for a person who has been 23 
issued an intermediate driver's license or for a person under 18 years of age.  24 
The bil l provides that the penalty for a first offense of the prohibition against using a 25 
mobile telephone or handheld electronic device while operating a motor vehicle is $75.  26 
The bill also provides that the penalty for a 2nd or subsequent offense within a 3 -year 27 
period is $150.  28
