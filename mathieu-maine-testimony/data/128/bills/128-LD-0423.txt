 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 1 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and  2 
Whereas, the Maine State Library received a 2 -year grant in 2015 to provide mobile 3 
wireless hot spo t devices to libraries in Washington County; and  4 
Whereas, funding for the 2 -year project ends in January 2017; and  5 
Whereas, it is necessary that this Act become effective immediately to offset the 6 
loss of funding so that mobile wireless hot spot devices ca n continue to be provided to 7 
libraries in Washington County; and  8 
Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 9 
the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 10 
immediately necessary for  the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 11 
therefore,  12
