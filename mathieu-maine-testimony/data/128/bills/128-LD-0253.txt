SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to repeal the Maine Metallic Mineral Mining Act, as contained in 5 
the Maine Revised Statutes, Tit le 38, chapter 3, subchapter 1, article 9, which was 6 
enacted in 2012 under Public Law 2011, chapter 653.  In addition to repealing the Maine 7 
Metallic Mineral Mining Act, this bill would repeal any other statutory provisions 8 
enacted as part of Public Law 20 11, chapter 653 and would reenact any statutory 9 
provisions that were repealed under that same legislation.  This bill would further direct 10 
the Department of Environmental Protection and the Department of Agriculture, 11 
Conservation and Forestry, Maine Land U se Planning Commission to engage in separate 12 
rule-making processes to remove any changes to their respective rules that were 13 
implemented in accordance with Public Law 2011, chapter 653.  14
