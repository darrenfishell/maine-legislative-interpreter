SUMMARY  5 
This bill, which is a recommendation of the Right To Know Advisory Committee in 6 
response to the decision of the Superior Court in Flanders v. State, et al. , BELSC -CV-15- 7 
12 (Me. Super. Ct., Waldo Cty., Aug. 12, 2016), is intended to clarify that under Maine's 8 
Freedom of Acc ess Act, an agency or public official may require payment of all costs 9 
before providing a public record to a requester.  10
