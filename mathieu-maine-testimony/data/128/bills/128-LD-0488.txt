Sec. 2.  17 MRSA §3210 is enacted to read:  25 
§3210.  Local option; grocery stores  26 
A munici pality may by ordinance allow grocery stores with no more than 10,000 27 
square feet of interior customer selling space to be open on Easter Day, Thanksgiving 28 
Day and Christmas Day.  For purposes of this subsection, "grocery store" has the same 29 
meaning as in Title 22, section 2491, subsection 7 -B. 30 
SUMMARY  31 
This bill allows a municipality by ordinance to allow grocery stores with no more 32 
than 10,000 square feet of interior customer selling space to be open on Easter Day, 33 
Thanksgiving Day and Christmas Day.  34
