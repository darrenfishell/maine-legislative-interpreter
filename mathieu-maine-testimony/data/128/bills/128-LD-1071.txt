SUMMARY  3 
This bill amends the development district law by capping project costs for services 4 
and equipment to provide skills development and training at 20% of total project costs 5 
and by requiring funding priority to be given to services, equipment and training for jobs 6 
created or retained in the municipality, plantation or unorganized territory.  7
