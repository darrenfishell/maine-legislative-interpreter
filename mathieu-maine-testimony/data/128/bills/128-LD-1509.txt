 SUMMARY  1 
This bill prohibits retired state employees or retired teachers from returning to 2 
employment after retirement, either as a state employee or as a teacher, while c ollecting 3 
retirement benefits from the Maine Public Employees Retirement System. This 4 
prohibition does not apply to retired state employees or retired teachers who are hired as 5 
independent contractors pursuant to a service contract lasting less than one ye ar or as a 6 
substitute teacher.  7
