SUMMARY  19 
This resolution proposes to amen d the Constitution of Maine to increase the length of 20 
the terms of State Senators from 2 years to 4 years beginning in 2020 and members of the 21 
House of Representatives from 2 years to 4 years beginning in 2022 and to limit the 22 
number of consecutive terms m embers of the State Senate and the House of 23 
Representatives may serve from 4 terms to 2 terms.  24
