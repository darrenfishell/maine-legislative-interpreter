SUMM ARY  10 
This bill establishes a limited microgrid program in northern Maine.  The bill directs 11 
the Public Utilities Commission to establish by rule a program that would allow the 12 
creation of small transmission or distribution systems connecting an electricity generator 13 
with one or more industrial consumers located in close proximity to the generator, 14 
allowing for the sale or transfer of electricity from the generator to those consumers 15 
without use of the system of the transmission and distribution utility.  The  commission is 16 
directed to report annually to the Legislature on the program and whether it should be 17 
continued, expanded, limited or discontinued.  18
