SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, removes the 
provision in the resolution that changes apportionment to a 12-year cycle and leaves it at 
10 years. The amendment also removes those provisions of the resolution that change 
references to biennial elections in the Constitution of Maine, as biennial elections will 
continue.
