Proceeds from the potential sale or lease of these properties will generally be credited to the Department of 
Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of General Services -Capital Construction and Improvement Reserve 
Fund. The proceeds of the sale of the Limestone property will be provided to the Maine School of Science and 
Mathematics. The proceeds of the sale of the Brookton Township property and a portion of the Rockwood Strip 
property will be credited to the Unorganized Tenitory Education and Services Fund.
