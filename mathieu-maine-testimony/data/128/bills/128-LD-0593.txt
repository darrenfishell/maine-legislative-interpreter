SUMMARY  27 
This bill removes certain explicit notification requirements of the Board of Licensure 28 
in Medicine to a physician whose license is about to expire or has expired.  This bill 29 
allows the board to reinstate the license of a physician whose license has expire d if, 30 
within 90 days following the expiration of the license, the physician pays a renewal fee 31 
and a late fee.  32
