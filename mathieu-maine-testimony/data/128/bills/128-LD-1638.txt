SUMMARY  16 
This bill adds supporting an attainment goal of increasing the percent of working -age 17 
adults holding a high -value certificate, college degree, vocational education or other 18 
industry -recognized credential to 60%  by 2025 with a focus on meeting future workforce 19 
needs to the list of responsibilities of the State Workforce Investment Board.  The board 20 
may seek information from the Maine Workforce and Education Coalition in regards to 21 
an attainment goal.  It also dir ects the Workforce Investment Board to track the State's 22 
progress toward the attainment goal and to annually report on the progress to the joint 23 
standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over education and cultural 24 
affairs and the joint sta nding committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over labor, 25 
business, research and economic development matters.  26
