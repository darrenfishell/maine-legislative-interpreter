SUMMARY  22 
This bill distributes 25% of tax revenues from the sales tax on retail marijuana and 23 
retail marijuana products to the counties to support county jail operations.  The funds 24 
distributed to the counti es under this bill must be distributed according to the formula 25 
established for funds in the County Jail Operations Fund and must be used to supplement 26 
and not to supplant funding provided to the counties for jail operations from other 27 
funding sources.  28
