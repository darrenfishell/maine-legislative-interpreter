SUMMARY  18 
This bill, using language modeled on the law regarding permits to carry concealed 19 
handguns, requires a person attempting to purchase a firearm to present the seller with 20 
proof of having successfully co mpleted military firearms training or an approved firearm 21 
safety course.  The bill also provides that a person may meet this requirement by 22 
presenting proof of having satisfied the handgun safety course requirements for a permit 23 
to carry concealed handguns . 24
