ยง1299.  Cancellation of subscriptions  5 
1.  Definitions.   As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates,  the 6 
following terms have the following meanings.  7 
A.  "Automatic renewal subscription" means an agreement to provide any of the 8 
following goods or services for a specified time and price that is automatically 9 
renewed at the end of a definite term for a sub sequent term unless the consumer 10 
cancels the agreement:  11 
(1)  Online magazines, journals and periodicals;  12 
(2)  Online media players;  13 
(3)  Mobile apps;  14 
(4)  Social networking services; and  15 
(5)  Internet game services.  16 
B.  "Internet game service" means an onl ine service that provides information, 17 
software, data, text, photographs, graphics, audio or video that may be accessed by a 18 
consumer on a paid subscription basis for the purpose of allowing that consumer to 19 
play a single -player or multiplayer game through  the Internet or to download a game 20 
for that consumer to play offline.  "Internet game service" does not include online 21 
gambling or other gaming in which a person participates to win money.  22 
C.  "Mobile app" means a software application designed to be opera ted on a mobile 23 
device such as a smartphone.  24 
D.  "Online media player" means an online service that delivers audio or video 25 
content.  26 
E.  "Social networking service" means an online service that facilitates the building of 27 
social relations and the sharing o f information among specified groups of people.  28 
2.  Method of cancellation of automatic renewal subscription.  A business that 29 
makes an automatic renewal subscription offer to a consumer in this State shall present 30 
that consumer with an easily accessible d isclosure of the methods that consumer may use 31 
to cancel the subscription.  One of the methods of cancellation must provide for online 32 
cancellation of the subscription by any means of communicating information over a 33 
computer network.  34 
3.  Exception.  This section does not apply to an entity that provides the host 35 
platform on the website of an Internet game service.  36

SUMMARY  6 
This bill requires a business that makes an automatic renewal subscription offer of an 7 
online magazine, journal or periodical, online media player, mobile app, social 8 
networking service or Internet game service to present a consumer in this State with an 9 
easily accessible disclosure of the methods that consumer may use to cancel the 10 
subscrip tion, which must include online cancellation.  11 
The bill only applies to agreements entered into or renewed after January 1, 2018 and 12 
does not apply to an entity that provides the host platform on the website of an Internet 13 
game service.  14
