SUMMARY  15 
This bill extends the eligibility period for closure costs for a landf ill that currently 16 
applies only to a landfill licensed on or before September 1, 1989.  The bill provides that 17 
if a license application for a landfill was received on or before September 1, 1989 and the 18 
application was approved by the Commissioner of Envir onmental Protection within one 19 
year of receipt, the landfill is eligible for payment of 75% of certain closure costs by the 20
