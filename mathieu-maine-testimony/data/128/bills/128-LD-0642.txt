 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 1 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and  2 
Whereas, the Task Force To Identify Special Education Cost Drivers and 3 
Innovative Approaches to Ser vices will work to address the rising cost of special 4 
education while maintaining high -quality services that accommodate the needs of all 5 
children; and  6 
Whereas, the study must be initiated before the 90 -day period expires in order that 7 
the study may be com pleted and a report submitted in time for submission to the next 8 
legislative session; and  9 
Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 10 
the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 11 
immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 12 
therefore, be it  13

Sec. 3.  Chairs.  Resolved:  That the Senate member is the Senate chair and the 3 
House of Representatives member is the House chair of the task force; and be it further  4 
Sec. 4.  Appointments; convening of task force.  Resolved:  That all 5 
appointments must be made no later than 30 days following the effective date of this 6 
resolve.  The appointing authorities shall notify the Executive Director of the Legislative  7 
Council once all appointments have been completed.  After appointment of all members, 8 
the chairs shall call and convene the first meeting of the task force.  If 30 days or more 9 
after the effective date of this resolve a majority of but not all appointment s have been 10 
made, the chairs may request authority and the Legislative Council may grant authority 11 
for the task force to meet and conduct its business; and be it further  12 
Sec. 5.  Duties.  Resolved:  That the task force shall address the rising cost of 13 
special education and the maintenance of high -quality services that accommodate the 14 
needs of all children by identifying cost drivers and recommending innovative 15 
approaches to serving students; and be it further  16 
Sec. 6.  Staff assistance.  Resolved:  That the Legislative Council shall provide 17 
necessary staffing services to the task force; and be it further  18 
Sec. 7.  Report.  Resolved:  That, no later than December 6, 2017, the task force 19 
shall submit a report that includes its findings and recommendations, incl uding suggested 20 
legislation, for presentation to the Second Regular Session of the 128th Legislature.  21 
Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 22
