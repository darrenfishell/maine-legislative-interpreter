Fiscal Note 
Expansion of solemn contractual commitments of the State -retirement pension COLA 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
Adding cost-of-living adjustments (COLA) to retirement benefits to the provisions that constitute solemn contractual 
commitments of the State may limit the State's future ability to decrease or eliminate the COLA provisions.
