This bill allows a motor vehicle operator to make a left turn at a red light after 29 
stopping if not prohibited by a sign such as "NO LEFT TURN ON RED" or "NO TURN 30 
ON RED" and the operator yields the right -of-way to pede strians on a crosswalk and to a 31
