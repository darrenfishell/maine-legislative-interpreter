SUMMARY  32 
This bill requires municipal satellite collection systems to be licensed by the 33 
Department of Environmental Protection as subpermittees under the licenses of publicly 34 
owned treatment works.  It requires the department to is sue a license to a municipal 35 
satellite collection system if the municipal satellite collection system meets operation and 36 
maintenance standards established in rules adopted by the department.  37
