23 SUMMARY 
24 This amendment is the majority report of the Joint Standing Committee on Education 
25 and Cultural Affairs. The amendment strikes a provision in the bill that removes the 
26 requirement in cmTent law requiring that student learning and growth be included as part 
27 of the multiple measures of educator effectiveness. The amendment replaces that 
28 provision to clarify that the use of student learning and growth data to inform instruction 
29 is included as part of the multiple measures of educator effectiveness. The amendment 
30 also requires the Depaitment of Education to submit a provisionally adopted rule to the 
31 Legislature by January 12, 2018.
