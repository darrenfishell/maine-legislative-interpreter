SUMMARY  31 
This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to increase 32 
reimbursement rates for home -based  care services consistent with the recommendations 33 
made by Burns & Associates, Inc. in its report "Rate Review for Personal Care and 34 
Related Services: Final Rate Models" dated February 1, 2016.  The first half of this 35 
increase was ratified by the 127th Leg islature through Public Law 2015, chapter 267.  36
