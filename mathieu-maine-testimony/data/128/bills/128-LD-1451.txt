SUMMARY  20 
This bill prohibits certain fish, amphibians and aquatic invertebrates from being 21 
introduced, imported or possess ed by a person in the State unless a permit to do so has 22 
been issued by the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife.  The bill provides that 23 
a permit is not required to introduce, import or possess other fish species kept in an 24 
aquarium.  The bill pr ohibits the introduction, importation, transportation and possession 25 
of certain aquatic plants in the State.  26
