17 SUMMARY
18 This bill allows a private school that enrolls fewer  than 60% publicly  funded  students 
19 to operate as an approved private school if it is accredited by a commission on 
20 independent schools of a New England association of schools and colleges; in current 
21 law, such a private school  is subject to the Department  of Education's basic school 
22 approval process.
