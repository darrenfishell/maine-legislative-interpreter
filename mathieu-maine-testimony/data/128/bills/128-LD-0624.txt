Transporting a dog in a motor vehicle is governed by this section.  5 
1.  Definitions.   As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the 6 
following terms have the following meanings.  7 
A.  "Open vehicle" means a motor vehicle with a portion of the vehicle not enc losed 8 
by a top and windows or sides. "Open vehicle" includes, but is not limited to, pickup 9 
trucks and convertibles.  10 
B.  "Occupant" means a person in a motor vehicle.  "Occupant" includes the driver 11 
and a passenger.  12 
C.  "Passenger compartment" means the po rtion of a motor vehicle designed to be 13 
occupied by an occupant.  14 
2.  Restrictions.  A person driving a motor vehicle may not:  15 
A.  Transport a dog in the open portion of an open vehicle on a public way unless the 16 
dog is protected in a manner that prevents t he dog from falling out of or jumping out 17 
of or being thrown from the vehicle;  18 
B.  Transport a dog in the passenger compartment unless the dog is restrained by a 19 
tether or harness that is secured in a manner that would minimize an injury to an 20 
occupant or the dog in the event of an accident;  21 
C.  Allow a dog in the front seat of the motor vehicle between an occupant and the 22 
steering wheel or between an occupant and the dashboard; or  23 
D.  Allow a dog to put the dog's head outside of the passenger compartment a t any 24 
time that the motor vehicle is in motion.  25 
3.  Exceptions.   Notwithstanding subsection 2, this section does not apply to:  26 
A.  A dog being transported by a farmer or a farm employee who is engaged in 27 
agricultural activities requiring the services of a dog; 28 
B.  A hunting dog at a hunting site or being transported between hunting sites by a 29 
licensed hunter who is in possession of all applicable licenses and permits for the 30 
species being pursued during the legal season for that activity; or  31 
C.  A dog used for law enforcement purposes while on duty in a motor vehicle used 32 
for law enforcement purposes.  33
