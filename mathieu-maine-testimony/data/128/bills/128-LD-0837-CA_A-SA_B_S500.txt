17 SUMMARY 
18 This amendment: 
19 1. Establishes the MaineCare Expansion Fund within the Department of Health and 
20 Human Services to provide the funds estimated to be necessary for expanded coverage 
21 under the MaineCare program required by Initiated Bill 2017, chapter 1, "An Act To 
22 Enhance Access to Affordable Health Care"; 
23 2. Limits the use of the fund to funding needed for the implementation and 
24 continuation of expanded access to health care as required by Initiated Bill 2017, chapter 
25 1; 
26 3. Requires that funds in the fund lapse to the unappropriated surplus of the General 
27 Fund if not expended by June 30, 2019; 
28 4. Directs the State Controller to transfer $31,159,210 from the unappropriated 
29 surplus of the General Fund to the fund no later than 10 days following the effective date 
30 of this legislation; and 
31 5. Provides that the Governor, on or before June 30, 2019 and upon the written 
32 request of the Commissioner of Health and Human Services in consultation with the State 
33 Budget Officer, may transfer up to $23,540,000 from the unexpended balance of the Fund 
34 for a Healthy Maine to the fund for the amount of medical costs of the MaineCare 
35 program that, as a result of expansion of the MaineCare program, will within the 15 days

This amendment transfers $31,159,210 from the unappropriated surplus of the General Fund to the newly established 
MaineCare Expansion Fund for MaineCare services and payments to providers. Any unused balance in the fund as of 
June 30, 2019 will lapse back to the General Fund. This amendment also authorizes transfers from the Fund for a 
Healthy Maine up to $23,540,000 for the same purposes in the event of a shortfall due to the expansion of eligibility 
for MaineCare.
