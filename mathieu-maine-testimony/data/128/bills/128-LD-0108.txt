SUMMARY  8 
This bill provides that, when a member of  a law enforcement agency or a municipal 9 
fire department administers naloxone hydrochloride, the law enforcement agency or 10 
municipal fire department may seek and receive reimbursement for the cost of that 11 
treatment from the person to whom the treatment was  provided.  12
