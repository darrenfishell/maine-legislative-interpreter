Sec. 5.  Duties.  Resolved:   That the commission shall:  20 
1.  Identify workforce needs in the State that could be met by the recruitment of 21 
veterans and service members who are transitioning out of the military;  22 
2.  Identify similari ties and differences between military occupational specialty 23 
training and state licensing and certification requirements for those fields of work 24 
identified by the commission;  25

veterans' training and experience;  29

relevant licensure or certification; an d 31

certification; and  34

SUMMARY  25 
This resolve creates the  Commission To Streamline Veterans' Licensing and 26 
Certification to study and address military service members' and veterans' transition from 27 
military service to the civilian workforce.  The commission is also charged with making 28 
recommendations to streamli ne licensing and certification processes for service members 29 
and veterans.  30
