SUMMARY  23 
This bill allows the Department of Transportation and the Maine State Police to 24 
establish speed limits within the limits of the property owned by or unde r the control of 25 
the Midcoast Regional Redevelopment Authority in Brunswick and the Loring 26 
Development Authority of Maine in Limestone.  The speed limits must be posted by the 27 
Midcoast Regional Redevelopment Authority and the Loring Development Authority o f 28 
Maine in accordance with written directions or policies of the Department of 29 
Transportation.  30
