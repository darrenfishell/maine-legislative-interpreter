SUMMARY  25 
This bill is submitted pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 3, section 1001, 26 
subsection 2.  The purpose of this bill is to make changes to the law to improve the 27 
efficiency and effectiveness of tax expenditure evaluations conducted by the Office of 28 
Program Evaluation and Government Accountability, or OPEGA, for the Legislature. The 29 
bill removes statutory deadlines to provide more flexibility in scheduling, completion and 30 
reporting on full eval uations to allow for a better fit with legislative schedules and to 31 
ensure OPEGA can complete a comprehensive and quality review of each program, 32 
within OPEGA's available capacity.  33
