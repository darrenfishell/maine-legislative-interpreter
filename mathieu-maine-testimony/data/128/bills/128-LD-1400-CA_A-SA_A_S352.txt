SENATE AMENDMENT "A "to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 478, 
L.D. 1400, Bill, "An Act To Create the Bar Harbor Port Authority" 
Amend the amendment by striking out all of section 5 (page 1, lines 14 to 33 in 
amendment) and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 5. Referendum; effective date. This Act takes effect when approved only 
for the purpose of permitting its submission to the legal voters of the towns of Bar 
Harbor, Tremont, Southwest Harbor, Cranberry Isles, Mount Desert, Lamoine, Hancock, 
Sorrento, Gouldsboro, Trenton, Sullivan and Winter Harbor at an election called for that 
purpose and held within 2 years after the effective date of this Act. The elections must be 
called, advertised and conducted according to the law relating to municipal elections. 
The registrars shall make a complete list of all the eligible voters of the towns of Bar 
Harbor, Tremont, Southwest Harbor, Cranberry Isles, Mount Desert, Lamoine, Hancock, 
Sorrento, Gouldsboro, Trenton, Sullivan and Winter Harbor as described in this Act. The 
list prepared by the registrars governs the eligibility of a voter. For the purpose of 
registration of voters, the registrars of voters must be in session the next regular workday 
preceding the election. The subject matter of this Act is reduced to the following 
question: 
11Shall 1An Act To Create the Bar Harbor Port Authority,' as passed by the 
Second Regular Session of the 128th Legislature, be accepted?" 
The voters shall indicate by a cross or check mark against the word "Yes" or 11No11 
their opinion of the same.

of votes cast for Governor in those towns at the last gubernatorial election. Another 
election may be held, if the total number of votes cast in the first election in a town does 
not equal or exceed 20%.

SUMMARY 
This amendment extends to the towns of Bar Harbor, Tremont, Southwest Harbor, 
Cranberry Isles, Mount Desert, Lamoine, Hancock, Sorrento, Gouldsboro, Trenton, 
Sullivan and Winter Harbor the right to vote on the creation of the Bar Harbor Port 
Authority. Under the amendment, in order for the authority to be created it must be 
approved by the voters in the Town of Bar Harbor and in at least 6 of the 11 other towns 
to which the question is submitted.
