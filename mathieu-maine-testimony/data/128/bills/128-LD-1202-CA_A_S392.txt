31 SUMMARY 
32 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, replaces the bill and 
33 amends the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 15, chapter 310, which provides a motion 
34 process for the imposition of special restrictions on the dissemination and use of criminal 
3 5 history record information for a single Class E criminal conviction committed by an 
36 individual 18 to 20 years of age. The amendment does the following. 
37 1. It authorizes a court to impose special restrictions on the dissemination and use of 
38 criminal history record information for a single Class D or Class E criminal conviction 
39 committed by an individual of any age when at least 4 years have passed since the person
