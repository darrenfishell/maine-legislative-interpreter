 SUMMARY  1 
This bill cr eates a process by which a state agency can enter a privatization contract 2 
with a nongovernmental entity to perform basic agency services for up to 5 years if a 3 
number of criteria are met, including:  4 
1.  Wages and benefits for employees of the contractor a re comparable to state 5 
employees performing the same services;  6 
2.  The contractor endeavors to hire agency employees terminated due to the 7 
privatization;  8 
3.  The agency provides an estimate of its costs in providing the subject services in 9 
the most efficie nt manner;  10 
4.  The agency provides support and resources to allow agency employees to submit a 11 
competing bid to provide the privatized services;  12 
5.  The agency considers as a contract cost any income tax revenue lost to the State as 13 
a result of services to  be performed out of state under the contract; and  14 
6.  A review by the State Auditor to determine that all of the requirements of the 15 
bidding and privatization contract have been met.  16
