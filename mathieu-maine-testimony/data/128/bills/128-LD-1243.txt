SUMMARY  25 
This bill amends the laws governing the Maine Length of Service Award Program 26 
Board of Trustees to require the chair of the Maine Fire Protection Services Commissio n 27 
to appoint initial members to the board of trustees if those appointments have not been 28 
made within 30 days of the effective date of this legislation.  The bill also requires the 29 
chair of the Maine Fire Protection Services Commission to make an appointme nt to fill a 30 
vacancy on the board of trustees if the appointment is not made within 60 days of the 31 
vacancy.  32
