6 SUMMARY
7 Current law provides  that a person who is convicted a 2nd time of hunting deer over 
8 bait during  an open season  on deer is permanently ineligible to obtain a hunting license.  
9 This bill changes that permanent ineligibility to an ineligibility  period  of 2 years.
