11 SUMMARY 
12 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, replaces the bill and 
13 changes the title. It provides that a new motor vehicle that has had an inspection is not 
14 required to have another inspection until 2 years from the last day of the month in which 
15 it was initially registered. The amendment also provides that the inspection sticker fee 
16 for the one-time 2-year inspection of a new motor vehicle is $7. After this time period 
17 has passed, the motor vehicle is required to have an annual motor vehicle safety 
18 inspection.

Fiscal Note 
No net fiscal impact 
The bill provides that a new motor vehicle that has had an inspection does not need another inspection for two years. 
It also increases the cost of the inspection stickers for new motor vehicles from $3.50 to $7.00. There is no net 
increase or decrease to Highway Fund revenue.
