SUMMARY  10 
This bill changes the name of the Maine Community College System's advisory 11 
council of college presidents from the administr ative council to the presidents' council.  It 12 
allows the Board of Trustees of the Maine Community College System to encourage the 13 
development of innovative education delivery methods, course schedules, student support 14 
services and prior learning assessment s that help expand access to both credit and 15 
noncredit programming, to support the development of campus security plans and policy 16 
and to develop and administer programs of campus employment.  It makes changes to 17 
promote the regular use of facility master planning and removes the requirement that the 18 
Legislature must approve real estate transactions selling or permanently transferring any 19 
interest in real estate.  It also imposes a number of constraints on contracts the board of 20 
trustees may enter into, oth er than employment contracts.  21
