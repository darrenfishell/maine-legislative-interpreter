following:

prevents individuals who are licensed as electricians in other jurisdictions from obtaining

SUMMARY 
This amendment adds provisions that allow a limited electrician to supervise licensed 
helper and apprentice electricians to the same extent as under current law and that allow 
applicants for a master electrician license to count hours worked as an apprentice 
electrician toward the 12,000-hour experience requirement, as is pennitted in current law. 
This amendment also makes technical changes to the bill and adds an emergency 
preamble and emergency clause.
