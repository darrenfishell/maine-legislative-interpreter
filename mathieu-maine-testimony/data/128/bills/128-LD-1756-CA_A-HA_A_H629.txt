Fiscal Note 
Pennanent renewal of temporary lease jeopardized 
Loss of revenue from leasing of state property -Other funds 
Delaying the effective date of this legislation may result in Disability Rights Maine being forced to vacate newly 
renovated facilities it leases from the Department of Administrative and Financial Services (DAFS) at the Maine 
Educational Center for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing and the Governor Baxter School for the Deaf 
(MECDHH/GBSD). The Office of the Attorney General had indicated this legislation would be required in order for 
Disability Rights Maine to continue leasing this space after the current temporary lease expires on May 1, 2018. 
Should the leased facility be vacated> DAFS and MECDHH/GBSD would lose the lease revenue.
