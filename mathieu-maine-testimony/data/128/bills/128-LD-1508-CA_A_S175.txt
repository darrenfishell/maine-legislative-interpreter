'Resolve, To Improve Animal Control in Maine'

'Sec. 1. Development of animal licensure incentives. Resolved: That the 
Commissioner of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestty shall, within existing resources, 
develop strategies to encourage owners of dogs to license their dogs under the Maine 
Revised Statutes, Title 7, chapter 721. The strategies may include, but are not limited to:

disseminate information, notices and flyers promoting licensure in municipal offices, on 
publicly accessible municipal websites and in other public buildings.'
