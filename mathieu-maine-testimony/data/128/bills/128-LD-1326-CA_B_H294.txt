§1125. Exemption from criminal liability for person who seeks medical assistance

1108, 1111 or 1111-A or chapter 49 if the grounds for the arrest, prosecution or detention

6 SUMMARY 
7 This amendment is the minority report of the committee. It removes the provisions in 
8 the bill that decriminalize possession of a hypodermic apparatus. It returns to the 
9 Department of Health and Human Services rule-making authority over establishing 
10 community-based drug overdose prevention programs, which is removed in the bill. It 
11 directs the department to provisionally adopt rules regarding community-based drug 
12 overdose prevention programs by January 15, 2018. It removes from the bill the 
13 appropriation for syringe exchange programs and naloxone hydrochloride distribution.
