Sec. 1.  37-B MRSA §159 is enacted to read:  2 
§159.  Authority to provide space to certain nonprofit organizations  3 
The Military Bureau may provide space in a military facility under the jurisdicti on of 4 
the Military Bureau to a statewide nonprofit organization established for the purpose of 5 
providing assistance to members or families of members of the Maine National Guard.  A 6

SUMMARY  10 
This bill authorizes the Military Bureau to provide space in military facilities to 11 
nonprofit organizations that provide assistance to memb ers or families of members of the 12 
Maine National Guard.  Under the bill, space provided to a nonprofit organization by the 13 
Military Bureau may be used only to provide assistance to members or families of 14 
members of the Maine National Guard.  15
