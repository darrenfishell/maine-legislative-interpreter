SUMMARY  7 
This bill removes the provisions of law that allow driver’s license suspensions for 8 
failure to pay a fine in offenses not related to driving. This bill provides that the statutory 9 
exemptions from attachment and execution for certain property apply to the enforcement 10 
of fines owed to the S tate. 11
