SUMMARY  7 
This bill pr ohibits a retailer from using plastic bags to bag products at the point of 8 
retail sale or otherwise make plastic bags available to customers, with exceptions for 9 
certain types of plastic bags.  This prohibition is effective September 1, 2020.  A retailer 10 
may provide recyclable paper bags to bag products at the point of sale and must provide 11 
reusable bags for purchase by a customer.  The bill also makes changes to existing law 12 
regarding retailer collection and recycling of used plastic bags to ensure consist ency with 13 
the implementation of the plastic bag prohibition.  14
