SUMMARY  21 
This bill provides an exemption from property tax for a recreational trail crossing a 22 
parcel of land as long as the trail is a recognized trail that is ope n to public recreational 23 
uses, such as snowmobiling, hiking or all -terrain vehicle use, and meets certain width and 24 
length requirements, depending on the use.  The exemption is limited to that portion of 25 
the parcel of land that is actually used for the rec reational purpose.  26
