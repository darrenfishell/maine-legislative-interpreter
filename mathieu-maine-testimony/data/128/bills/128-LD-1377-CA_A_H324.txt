Amend the bill in section 1 in ยง 1500-H in subsection 2 by striking out all of 
paragraph D (page 1, lines 28 to 39 and page 2, lines I and 2 in L.D.) and inserting the 
following: 
'D. Offer a document that is available free of charge or at a lesser price from a 
governmental entity without conspicuously disclosing that availability in a manner 
that is clearly visible to a consumer.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the unanimous report of the committee, replaces the 
requirement in the bill that a specifically worded notice be displayed on every offer of the 
sale of a governmental document that can be obtained from the applicable governmental 
entity for free or at a lesser charge with a more general notice requirement.
