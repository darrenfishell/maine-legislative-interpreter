COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 778, L.D. 1104, Bill, "An Act To 
Exempt School Resource Officers from Department of Education Background Check and 
Fingerprinting Requirements" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 11 in the first line (page 1, line 3 in L.D.) by 
striking out the following: "A law" and inserting the following: 'An active duty law' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment specifies that the exemption in the bill from school personnel 
background checks for law enforcement officers applies only to active duty law 
enforcement officers.
