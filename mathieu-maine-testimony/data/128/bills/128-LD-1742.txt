22 SUMMARY
23 This resolve provides increased funding  for the provision of assisted living services at 
24 facilities currently  operating at a loss, including,  but not limited to, facilities  in Bangor,  
25 Millinocket, Camden and Sanford. It directs the Department of Health and Human 
26 Services to conduct  a review of possible ways to stabilize funding  for affordable  assisted 
27 living facilities that contract with the office  of aging and disability services within the 
28 Department of Health  and Human Services, including permanent increases to existing 
29 funding levels, paying  the medical costs of certain residents until they are eligible  for 
30 MaineCare coverage,  a practice known as Rate Code 53 spending, and designating  
31 facilities as private nonmedical institutions. It directs the department to report  back with 
32 its recommendations to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 
33 jurisdiction over health and  human services  matters  by January 11, 2019.GENERAL FUND 2017-18 2018-19
