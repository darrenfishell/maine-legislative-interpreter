This bill creates an exemption from the sales and use tax and the service provider tax for organizations operating a 
retreat for combat-injured veterans and their families. It would result in a reduction in General Fund revenue of 
$20,286 and a reduction in Local Government Fund revenue of $414 in fiscal year 2018-19. The bill includes a one 
time General Fund appropriation to the Department of Administrative and Financial Services of $2,500 in fiscal year 
2018-19 for computer programming costs for the exemption.
