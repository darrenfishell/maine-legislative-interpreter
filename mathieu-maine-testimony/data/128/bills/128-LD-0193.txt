SUMMARY  8 
In the laws regarding exemption from attachmen t, pension assets are exempted from 9 
attachment by creditors to the extent reasonably necessary to the support of a debtor and 10 
any dependents; a similar exemption for assets in individual retirement accounts is 11 
capped at $15,000. This bill removes the cap o n the amount exempted from attachment 12 
by creditors for an individual retirement account.  13
