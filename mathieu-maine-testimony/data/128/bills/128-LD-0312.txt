SUMMARY  13 
This bill provides $400,000 in fiscal year 2017 -18 and $400,000 in fiscal year 2018 - 14 
19 to the Maine Economic Improvement Fund to strengthen the University of Maine 15 
System's engineering programs by seeding the development of competencies in areas 16 
such as industrial engineering, environmental engineering and biomedical engineering to 17 
meet the applied research and workforce needs of existing, growing and emerging 18 
industrial activity in southern Maine.  The funds will support the acquisition of critica l 19 
infrastructure at the University of Southern Maine such as laboratory equipment and 20 
personnel that are necessary to strengthen the University of Southern Maine's engineering 21 
program while promoting synergy with the University of Maine's engineering progr am.  22 
Ongoing equipment maintenance and personnel costs will be absorbed within the 23 
University of Southern Maine's budgeted resources.  24
