SUMMARY 
2 This amendment provides that a registered caregiver may operate one retail store to 
3 sell harvested marijuana to qualifying patients. It provides that a municipality may not 
4 prohibit registered caregiver retail stores, registered dispensaries, marijuana testing 
5 facilities and manufacturing facilities that are operating with municipal approval in the 
6 municipality prior to the effective date of this legislation and that a municipality may not 
7 authorize registered caregiver retail stores, registered dispensaries, marijuana testing 
8 facilities and manufacturing facilities that are not operating on the effective date of this 
9 legislation to operate in the municipality unless the municipal legislative body has voted 
10 to adopt or amend an ordinance or approve a warrant article allowing registered caregiver 
11 retail stores, registered dispensaries, marijuana testing facilities or manufacturing 
12 facilities to operate within the municipality.
