SUMMARY  22 
This bill amends the criteria for adopting a development program as part of a 23 
development district by requiring that 80% of the area within the district is designated for 24 
development by an entity engaged in a qualified business activity that is directly related 25 
to financial services, manufacturing or targeted technologies.  This provision applies to 26 
develo pment programs approved by the Commissioner of Economic and Community 27 
Development on or after April 1, 2018.  28
