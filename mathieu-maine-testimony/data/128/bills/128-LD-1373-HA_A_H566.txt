Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Clarify Net Energy Billing' 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
summary and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. 35-A MRSA §3209-B is enacted to read: 
§3209-B. Application of excess generation 
In adopting rules pursuant to section 3209-A for net energy billing that relate to 
reducing the percentage of net energy to be included in determining a net energy billing 
customer's transmission and distribution bill, the commission shall apply the percentage 
only to the excess energy generation subject to the net energy billing arrangement. 
For purposes of this section, "excess energy generation" means the amount of energy 
subject to a net energy billing arrangement that is greater than the amount of energy 
consumed by the customer subject to that net energy billing a1Tangement.'
