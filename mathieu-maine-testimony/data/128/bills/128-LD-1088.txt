SUMMARY  12 
This bill requires the Department of Labor, Bureau of Labor Standards to provide a 13 
poster or notice to employers that states that employees may be eligible for the earned 14 
income tax credit.  This bill requires employers to post the poster or notice in a place that 15 
is accessible to the employer's employees.  16
