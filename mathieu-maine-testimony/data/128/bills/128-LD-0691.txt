SUMMARY  24 
This bill provides that as part of the Department of Health and Human Services' 25 
educational and publicity program concerning lead poisoning, the home visiting program 26 
established by the department is required to  provide free home lead test kits to parents of 27 
young children living in homes built before 1978.  28
