SUMMARY  11 
Current law prohibits the tran sfer or assignment of a casino operator license.  This 12 
bill requires that a corporation applying for a license must have had the same principal 13 
officers for at least 5 years prior to the issue of that license and must retain those officers 14 
for at least 10 years after the issue or renewal of the license.  15
