18 SUMMARY 
19 This amendment amends the bill by basing the fee charged for assistance from the 
20 Department of Environmental Protection in submitting a voluntary response action plan 
21 on 1% of the assessed property value. The amendment also caps the fee at $15,000 and 
22 adds a delayed effective date of January 1, 2018.

Fiscal Note 
Minor savings -Other Special Revenue Funds 
This bill simplifies the fee charged for assistance from the Department of Environmental Protection (DEP) in 
submitting a voluntary response action plan by basing the fee on 1 % of the assessed value of the property up to a 
maximum of $15,000, starting Janua1y 1, 2018. This upfront fee rather than a cost recovered fee will result in amino 
cost savings to the Uncontrolled Sites Fund within the DEP.
