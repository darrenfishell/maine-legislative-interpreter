SUMMARY  33 
This bill creates a new violation under the law defining the crime of gross sexual 34 
assault in which an actor is guilty of gross sexual assault if the actor engages in a sexual 35 
act with another person who has not consented to the s exual act; this violation is a Class 36 
C crime.  The bill also amends the crime of gross sexual assault by repealing the defense 37
