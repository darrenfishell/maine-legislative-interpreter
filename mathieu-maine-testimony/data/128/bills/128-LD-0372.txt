SUMMARY  18 
This bill amends existing law requiring the inspection of a subsurface wastewater 19 
disposal system on a property located within the shoreland area of the State that is to be 20 
transferred and expands these requirements to apply to the transfer of any property in the 21 
State on which a subsurface wastewat er disposal system is located.  The bill also amends 22 
other provisions of the Maine Revised Statutes as necessary to incorporate these changes.  23
