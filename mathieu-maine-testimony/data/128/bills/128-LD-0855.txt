SUMMARY  21 
This bill amends the Marijuana Legalization Act to require the state licensing 22 
authority to develop rules that prohibit the manufacture, distribution an d sale of edible 23 
retail marijuana products in packaging or shapes that appeal to children.  Under current 24 
law, the possession of any edible retail marijuana product is prohibited until February 1, 25 
2018, so the effective date of this legislation is delayed until then.  26
