 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  20-A MRSA §6103, sub -§11 is enacted to read:  2 
11.  Exemptions.   A law enforcement officer from a local law enforcement agency 3 
with jurisdiction over a school’s premises who assists with school security, safety, 4 
emergency preparedness or emergency response or has been assigned other 5 
responsibilities concerning the school by the school or the local law enforcement agency 6 
is exempt from the provisions of this section.  7 
SUMMARY  8 
This bill exemp ts from the school personnel background check and fingerprinting 9 
requirements law enforcement officers from a local law enforcement agency with 10 
jurisdiction over a school’s premises, commonly called school resource officers, who 11 
assist with school security , safety, emergency preparedness or emergency response or has 12 
been assigned other responsibilities concerning the school by the school or the local law 13 
enforcement agency.  14
