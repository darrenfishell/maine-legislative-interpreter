SUMMARY  31 
This bill establishes a voluntary method of establishing that a person is not 32 
disqualified from possessing a firearm without the need for a criminal background check 33 
at the time of the sale.   This bill all ows an individual who is not a licensed firearm 34 
dealer, when selling a firearm, to request proof that the buyer is not disqualified from 35 
possessing a firearm.  The buyer may present a Maine driver's license or nondriver 36 
identification card that has been is sued by the Secretary of State that contains indicia of 37 
the holder's qualification to possess a firearm as acceptable proof, without the need for a 38 
criminal background check.  39
