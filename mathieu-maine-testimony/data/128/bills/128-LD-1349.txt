SUMMARY  3 
This bill enacts the Appraisal Management Company Registration Act.  Und er the 4 
bill, appraisal management companies must obtain registration by the Board of Real 5 
Estate Appraisers.  Appraisal management companies are entities that provide appraisal 6 
management services to creditors or secondary mortgage market participants with  7 
appraisers who are employees of the entity or part of an appraiser panel made up of 8 
independent contractors, that provide services in connection with valuing a consumer's 9 
principal dwelling as security for a consumer credit transaction or incorporating su ch 10 
transactions into securitizations and that oversee appraiser panels in one or more states.  11 
Appraisal management services provided by appraisal management companies include 12 
recruiting, selecting and retaining appraisers; contracting with appraisers to p erform 13 
appraisal assignments; managing the process of having an appraisal performed; and 14 
reviewing and verifying the work of appraisers.  15
