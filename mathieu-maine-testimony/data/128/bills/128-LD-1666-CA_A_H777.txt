This legislation removes the requirement that local school administrative units (SAU's) award proficiency-based 
diplomas and instead allows SAU's to choose between awarding diplomas based on either proficiency-based standards 
or high school diploma standards. 
This bill includes General Fund appropriations totaling $593,353 in fiscal year 2018-19 to the Department of 
Education for the costs of supporting 2 different systems of awarding high school diplomas. Of this total, $3 87, 129 is 
provided to the Learning Systems Team program for 2 Regional Education Representative positions and 2 
Management Analyst I positions and related All Other costs to provide the necessary technical assistance to schools 
and $206,224 is provided to the School Finance and Operations Team for 2 Public Service Coordinator positions and 
related All Other costs to support the additional monitoring, data tracking, school approval requirements and other 
reporting requirements.
