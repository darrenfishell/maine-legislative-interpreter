SUMMARY  32 
This bill requires a court to provide justification when participation in  a batterers' 33 
intervention program is not ordered in sentencing a person for certain crimes against a 34 
current or former spouse, domestic partner, sexual partner or dating partner or an 35 
individual with whom the person is living or lived as a spouse.  The bi ll adds an 36 
additional purpose to the sentencing provisions of the Maine Criminal Code.  The 37 
additional purpose recognizes domestic abuse as a serious crime and recognizes certified 38
