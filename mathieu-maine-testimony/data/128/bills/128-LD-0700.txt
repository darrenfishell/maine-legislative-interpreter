SUMMARY  3 
This bill creates an exemption from the eligibility requirements for unemployment 4 
benefits dealing with work search for an individual otherwise eligible for unemployment 5 
benefits when that individual has been temporaril y laid off with a definite recall date of 6 
not more than 12 weeks from the date of the individual's temporary layoff.  7
