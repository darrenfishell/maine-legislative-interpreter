32 SUMMARY
33 This bill is reported out by the Joint  Standing Committee on Marine  Resources 
34 pursuant to the Maine  Revised Statutes, Title 12, section  6455, subsection 8.  The bill 
35 removes the October 1, 2018  repeal of the section of law that establishes the Maine 
36 Lobster Marketing Collaborative and that sets the surcharges  on various types of licenses  
Page 2 - 128LR2843(01)-11 to fund the collaborative.  The bill continues through 2020 the surcharges that have been 
2 in place since 2016.
3 The committee has not taken a position on the substance of this bill.  By reporting  
4 this bill out, the committee is not suggesting and does not intend to suggest that it agrees 
5 or disagrees with any aspect  of this bill.  The committee  is reporting the bill out for the 
6 sole purpose of having a bill printed that can be referred to the committee for an 
7 appropriate public hearing and subsequent  processing  in the normal  course.  The 
8 committee is taking this action to ensure clarity  and transparency  in the legislative review  
9 of the proposals contained  in the bill.
