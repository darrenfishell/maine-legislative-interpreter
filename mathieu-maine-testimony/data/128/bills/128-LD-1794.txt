5 ยง1777.  State property acquisition and sale  authority
6 1.  Acquisition of property.  The Department of Administrative  and Financial  
7 Services, Bureau of General  Services, on behalf of the State,  may acquire such property 
8 as it determines necessary  to:
9 A.  Lay out and establish, construct, improve or maintain, or provide a change of 
10 location or alignment to, the  egress  and ingress  of existing state facilities;
11 B.  Provide  land for buildings, garages, parking areas or roadside and landscape 
12 development for the  preservation of planned development areas;
13 C.  Provide for the  health, safety and  welfare  of the public using a state  facility;
14 D.  Secure materials, with necessary ways and access, for the construction,  
15 improvement and maintenance of state facilities;
16 E.  Relocate,  remove  or dispose of state landfill facilities;
17 F.  Erect administrative and storage buildings; or
18 G.  Construct,  improve  and maintain  public  infrastructure projects  as directed by law 
19 and provide mitigation for existing or potential environmental effects of public  
20 infrastructure projects.
21 2.  Sale and disposal of property.  The Department of Administrative and Financial 
22 Services, Bureau of General Services, on behalf  of the State,  may sell, demolish or 
23 dispose of  state properties as it determines necessary to:
24 A.  Maximize the financial return to the State;
25 B.  Ensure the efficient disposition of property when the property is no longer  
26 considered essential for  agency objectives;  or
27 C.  Manage the long- term planning needs of  the State.
28 The bureau  may sell, demolish or dispose of property only in a manner  that is transparent, 
29 competitive, orderly, timely and cost-effective.
30 3.  Opinion of value.  When property is to be purchased or sold by the State pursuant 
31 to this section, the Department of Administrative and Financial Services,  Bureau  of 
32 General Services  shall obtain a current  opinion of value by a real estate appraiser 
33 pursuant to Title  32, chapter 124 or a broker  or associate  broker pursuant  to Title 32, 
34 section 14004.  The purchase price  or sale price of the property must reflect the market 
35 conditions and  opinion of value at the time of sale or purchase.
Page 2 - 128LR2766(01)-11 SUMMARY
2 This bill authorizes the Department of Administrative and Financial  Services,  Bureau  
3 of General Services to acquire real estate determined necessary to meet the needs  of the 
4 State.  It authorizes the sale or disposition of real property determined necessary to 
5 maximize financial return and to manage the long- term planning  needs  of the State.  It 
6 requires that a current  opinion  of value by a real estate  appraiser be obtained  and that the 
7 purchase or sale price reflect the opinion and current  market conditions when the State 
8 enters into a real estate transaction.
