The bill includes General Fund appropriations to the Department of Health and Human Services of $7,927,093 in 
fiscal year 2017-18 and $32,467,412 in fiscal year 2018-19 to fully fund the waiting list for community-based 
services provided under the MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapters II and III, Section 21 relating to home and 
community benefits for members with intellectual disabilities or autistic disorder. Federal Expenditures Fund 
allocations are also included for the FMAP match and Other Special Revenue Funds allocations are also included for 
the service provider tax.
