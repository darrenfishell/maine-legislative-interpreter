SUMMARY  17 
This bill requires the Department of Transportation to install center line markings o n 18 
all state and state aid highways and to establish a system by which certain state and state 19 
aid highways are designated as having a low traffic count.  Center line markings installed 20 
on those highways are required to be repainted every 2 years.  21
