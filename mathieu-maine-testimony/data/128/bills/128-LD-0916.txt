SUMMARY  26 
This bill amends the laws governing spousal support in divorce actions by:  27 
1.  Repealing the provision allowing the awarding of general supp ort; 28 
2.  Unless agreed to by the parties, limiting the amount of support a court may order 29 
to no more than 30% of the obligor's income;  30 
3.  Limiting the term of a support order to 4 years;  31
