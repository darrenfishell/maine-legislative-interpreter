SUMMARY  26 
The funds provided by the bond issue in Part A, in the amount of $20,000,000, will 27 
be used to provide funds for the development of multidistrict, consolidated and integrated 28 
prekindergarten through 16 school facilities.  29 
Part B es tablishes the Maine Innovative Regional School Facilities Finance Program 30 
to promote efficient capital financing activities for innovative regional school facilities 31 
projects. It also establishes the Innovative Regional School Facilities Revolving Fund to 32 
support local efforts related to the program.  33
