HOUSE AMENDMENT "A " to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 568, 
L.D. 1613, Bill, "An Act To Authorize a General Fund Bond Issue To Assist in the 
Commercialization of Maine Products and Services" 
Amend the amendment by striking out all of the 2nd blocked paragraph (page 1, lines 
30 to 33 and page 2, lines 1 to 5 in amendment) and inserting the following: 
'Provides funds, to be awarded through a competitive process, to small nonprofit 
research institutions located in the State engaged in tissue repair and regeneration 
biotechnology commercialization activity that, as of the effective date of this Act, 
have operating budgets less than $15,000,000, to repurpose, develop and use existing 
infrastructure, laboratory spaces and equipment required to assist start-up life science 
companies in order to grow the State's economy and create new jobs. These funds 
will be used to leverage an additional $5,000,000 in public and private grants.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment revises the description of the disbursement of $5,000,000 in bond 
proceeds to institutions specializing in tissue repair and regeneration and specifies that 
such funds must be awarded through a competitive process to small nonprofit research 
institutions in the State that have operating budgets of less than $15,000,000.
