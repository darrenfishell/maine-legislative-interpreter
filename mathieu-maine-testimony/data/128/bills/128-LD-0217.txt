SUMMARY  17 
This bill requires that the Commissioner of Corrections determine whether to transfer 18 
to a correctional facility or retain at a county jail a person who is imprisoned at a county 19 
jail for longer than 9 months.  If the person is retained at the county jail, the bill requires 20 
the commissioner to reimburse the county jail on a per diem basis for the cost of 21 
imprisonment beyond 9 months.  The bill requires that when a previously suspend ed 22 
sentence of imprisonment for a Class A, Class B or Class C crime is vacated, in whole or 23 
in part, as the result of a probation revocation, the court must respecify as the place of 24 
imprisonment the place in which the person was imprisoned prior to releas e on probation.  25
