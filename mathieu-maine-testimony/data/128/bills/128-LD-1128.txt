SUMMARY  21 
This resolve establishes the Committee To Study the Processing of Evidence from 22 
Sexual Assault Test Kits.  The resolve provides for an 11 -member committee that will 23 
study the processing of evidence from sexual  assault test kits and make recommendations 24 
and submit suggested legislation in a report to the Joint Standing Committee on Criminal 25 
Justice and Public Safety by December 6, 2017.  The resolve authorizes the committee to 26 
seek funding contributions to parti ally or fully fund the costs of the study but does not 27 
make the work of the committee contingent on outside funding.  28
