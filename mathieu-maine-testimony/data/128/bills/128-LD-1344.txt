SUMMARY  15 
This bill provides that the Commissioner of Education may not consider financial 16 
hardship as a basis for granting a school administrative unit a waiver from physical 17 
education requirements.  18 
The bill also directs the Department of Education to conduct  a comprehensive 19 
assessment of physical education offered to kindergarten to grade 12 students in public 20 
schools.  As part of the comprehensive assessment, the department is required to work 21 
with school administrative units to complete self -assessments.  22
