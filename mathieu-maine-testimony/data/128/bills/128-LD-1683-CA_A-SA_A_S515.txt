'An Act To Extend the Term of Guide Licenses'

SUMMARY 
This amendment removes the prov1S1on in Committee Amendment 11A11 that 
establishes a Class E crime for failing to notify the Department of Inland Fisheries and 
Wildlife of a conviction or a finding of not criminally responsible that is grounds for the 
revocation, suspension or denial of a guide license within 30 days of the conviction or 
finding. It retains the provisions of Committee Amendment 11A11 that extend the term of 
and increase the fee for a guide license. It also removes the emergency preamble and 
clause.
