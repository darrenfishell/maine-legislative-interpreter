2.  Sales prohibition.  Except as otherwise provided in section 1609, subsection 4, 16 
effective January 1, 2018, a person may not sell or offer to sell or distribute for 17 
promotional purposes upholstered furniture containing more than 0.1% of a flame - 18 
retardant chemical or containing more than 0.1% of a mixture that includes flame - 19 
retardant chemicals.  20 
3.  Exemptions.  The restrictions in subsection 2 do not apply to the following 21
