This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to facilitate the implementation of mobile narcotic treatment 5 
programs in rural counties in the State in order to ensure access to treatment for patients 6 
who reside remotely from, or lack transportation to, other narcotic treatment programs.  7
