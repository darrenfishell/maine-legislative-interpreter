1-A.  Exceptions.   Notwithstanding subsection 1, paragraph B, a person may:  11

C.  Use a spear or speargun to fish for northern pike in Sebago Lake.  14 
SUMMARY  15 
This bill allows the use of a spear or speargun to fish for northern pike in Sebago 16
