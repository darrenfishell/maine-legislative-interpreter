CONCEPT DRAFT  2 
SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to direct the Department of Education to establish a program that 5 
provides incentives to parents  or guardians of students in kindergarten to 3rd grade who 6 
are prone to absenteeism from school to encourage the parents or guardians to reduce or 7 
eliminate the absenteeism.  The parent or guardian of a student who has been determined 8 
to have excessive abs enteeism would be required to meet with a school administrator and 9 
social worker.  Incentives may be offered, for example, cash rewards or gift cards to 10 
grocery stores.  The program would be a public -private partnership, and the Department 11 
of Education wou ld seek private or corporate partners to help provide or donate the 12 
incentives.  13
