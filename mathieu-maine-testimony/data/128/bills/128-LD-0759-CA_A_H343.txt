SUMMARY 
Instead of simply stating that a sewer or sanitary district may invest its funds to the 
same extent as a municipality, this amendment includes the applicable provisions from 
the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, chapter 223, subchapter 3-A in the statutes 
governing sewer and sanitary districts.
