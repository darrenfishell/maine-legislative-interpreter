Sec. 5.  Duties.  Resolved:  That the advisory committee shall study health care 22 
issues, including but not limited to the issues of quality, affordability and accessibility of 23 
health care throug hout the State; the prevention of illness; fairness and equity of health 24 
care delivery and accessibility in different geographic regions of the State; various 25 
funding options; and methods of cost containment.  Through the use of public meetings, 26 
questionna ires, polling and other sampling techniques, the advisory committee shall 27 
solicit information and concerns from the public.  The advisory committee shall review 28 
strategies employed by other states and, based on the information it gathers, shall develop 29 
recommendations to present to the Legislature regarding the delivery and financing of 30 
health care in the State; and be it further  31
