SUMMARY  18 
This resolve creates the Commission To Study the Needs, Opportunities and 19 
Efficiencies Associated with the Production of Salmonid Sport Fish in Maine.  The 20 
commission is req uired to submit its report and any accompanying legislation to the Joint 21 
Standing Committee on Inland Fisheries and Wildlife by January 30, 2018.  The 22 
Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife is prohibited from making any expenditure 23 
of over $150,000 to  repair or upgrade its salmonid fish hatcheries until the report is 24 
received and its recommendations acted upon.  25
