13 §299-A.  Harness  Racing Promotional Fund
14 1.  Fund created.  The Harness Racing Promotional Fund, referred to in this section  
15 as "the fund," is established to assist with the promotion of harness racing in the State.  
16 The fund consists of any money received  through  the commission on wagers pursuant  to 
17 section 286 and any contributions, grants or appropriations from private and public  
18 sources.  The fund, to be accounted  for within the commission, must be held separate  and 
19 apart from all other money,  funds and accounts.  Any balance remaining in the fund at the 
20 end of a  fiscal  year does  not lapse but must be carried forward to the next  fiscal year.
21 2.  Expenditures.  The commission may expend revenue or award grants from the 
22 fund consistent with the  purposes of this section.
23 Sec. 5.  Transfer.   The State Controller  shall transfer all unexpended balances of 
24 the Harness  Racing Promotional Board to the Harness  Racing Promotional Fund 
25 established in the Maine  Revised Statutes, Title 8, section 299-A no later than 30 days 
26 following the effective date of  this Act.
27 SUMMARY
28 This bill adds promotion of harness  racing to the existing responsibilities of the State 
29 Harness Racing  Commission.  The bill also repeals the Harness  Racing Promotional 
30 Board and transfers the funds  held by the board to the Harness Racing Promotional Fund,  
31 which will be administered by the State Harness Racing  Commission  and used to 
32 promote harness  racing.
