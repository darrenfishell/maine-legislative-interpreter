§604.  Posting of legal notices  12 
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, beginning July 1, 2018, a legal notice 13 
may not be required to be published in a newspaper but must be posted on a publicly 14 
accessible website that must be maintained by the Department of Administrative and 15 
Financial Services.  16 
SUMMARY  17 
This bill amends the law regarding legal notices to prohibit, beginning July 1, 2018, 18 
any requirement that a legal notice  must be published in a newspaper and to require, 19 
beginning on that date, a legal notice to be posted on a publicly accessible website 20 
maintained by the Department of Administrative and Financial Services.  21
