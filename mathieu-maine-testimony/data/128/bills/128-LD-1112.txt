SUMMARY  13 
This bill gives the maternal and infant death review panel the power to request the 14 
Commissioner of Health and Human Services to issue subp oenas to require disclosure of 15 
records and information.  This is the same authority allowed the child death and serious 16 
injury review panel.  It requires the panel to meet twice per year and to study the causes 17 
of the increase in infant mortality in the St ate within the past 10 years.  The panel is 18 
required to report its findings by February 7, 2018 to the Department of Health and 19 
Human Services and the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human Services in the 20 
Second Regular Session of the 128th Legislat ure. 21
