 Sec. 1.  Development of proposals to streamline laws regarding beano and 1 
games of chance.  Resolved:  That, notwithstanding Joint Rule 353, the Joint 2 
Standing Committee on Veterans and Legal Affairs, or a subcommittee of its members as 3 
jointly appointed by  the chairs, shall hold up to 4 meetings beginning no later than 4 
October 1, 2017 to develop proposals to streamline the laws governing beano and games 5 
of chance with the purpose of simplifying licensure, maximizing revenue to licensed 6 
charitable organizati ons that are eligible to conduct beano and games of chance and 7 
easing administrative burdens of the Chief of the State Police with regard to enforcement.  8 
The Chief of the State Police, or the chief's designee, shall provide information to the 9 
committee or  subcommittee with regard to the number of licenses issued, revenue from 10 
license fees and the general costs of administration and enforcement of the laws 11 
governing beano and games of chance.  The joint standing committee shall complete its 12 
work, including drafting implementing legislation, by December 6, 2017 for the 13 
consideration of the Joint Standing Committee on Veterans and Legal Affairs during the 14 
Second Regular Session of the 128th Legislature.  15 
SUMMARY  16 
This resolve directs the Joint Standing Committee  on Veterans and Legal Affairs to 17 
meet to develop proposals to streamline the laws governing beano and games of chance 18 
with the purpose of simplifying licensure, maximizing revenue to licensed charitable 19 
organizations and easing the administrative and enfo rcement burdens of the Chief of the 20 
State Police.  The resolve requires the chief to provide certain information regarding 21 
licensing, revenues and costs of administration to the committee.  22
