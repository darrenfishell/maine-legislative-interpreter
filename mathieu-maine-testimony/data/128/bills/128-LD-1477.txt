 SUMMARY  1 
This bill amends the law to specify the roles and responsibilities of the Department of 2 
Labor and the Maine Human Rights Commissio n in the development, execution and 3 
enforcement of the existing workplace training requirements for sexual harassment.  It 4 
adds penalties for employers who have not met the posting, notification, education and 5 
training requirements and requires that the co ntent of the training be developed and 6 
updated annually by the Maine Human Rights Commission.  7
