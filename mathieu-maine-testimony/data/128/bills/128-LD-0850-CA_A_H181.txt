COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A .. to H.P. 599, L.D. 850, Bill, "An Act To 
Prohibit a Person from Providing False Testimony to a Committee of the Legislature" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in §491 in subsection 1 in paragraph B by inserting after 
subparagraph (3) the following: 
'(4) Member of the Legislature;' 
Amend the bill in section 1 in §491 in subsection 1 in paragraph B by renumbering 
the subparagraphs to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment is a minority report of the committee. The amendment adds 
members of the Legislature to the definition of "person," making Legislators subject to 
the provisions of the bill.
