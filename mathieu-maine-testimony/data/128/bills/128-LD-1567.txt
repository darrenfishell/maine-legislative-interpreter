SUMMARY  32 
This bill makes the following changes to the archives and records management laws:  33 
1.  Adds language to specify that it is the policy of the State to ens ure that 34 
nonpermanent records are preserved for the time required by an approved records 35 
retention schedule;  36 
 
 Page 8 - 128LR0676(01) -1 
 2.  Adds language to include the advice from the Archives Advisory Board in the 1 
State Archivist's consideration of what constitutes an archival rec ord, to change the 2 
definition of state agency or agency to include all government agencies that transmit 3 
records to the Maine State Archives and to change the definition of electronic records;  4 
3.  Adds language to specify the 2nd organizational unit within  the Maine State 5 
Archives is records management and adds language to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 6 
5, section 94 that was stricken from Title 5, section 95 regarding powers and duties of the 7 
State Archivist;  8 
4.  Changes the laws governing the State Arc hivist to reflect the 2 organizational 9 
units of the Maine State Archives: archives services and records management.  It adds 10 
language to strengthen the records management practices for all state and local 11 
government agencies by using 4 criteria in the deve lopment of a guiding records retention 12 
schedule: administrative use, legal requirements, fiscal and audit requirements and 13 
historical and research value;  14 
5.  Specifies when local government records may be destroyed;  15 
6.  Repeals and replaces the laws govern ing the Archives Advisory Board to change 16 
the expertise required of members, to provide that members are appointed by the 17 
Secretary of State and to provide 3 -year terms for members; and  18 
7.  Removes the requirement that the Maine Historical Records Advisory  Board 19 
report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over 20 
education and cultural affairs but retains the requirement that the board report to the joint 21 
standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over state and  local government 22 
matters, which is the committee of oversight for the Maine State Archives.  It also 23 
removes a reference to funding a full -time position that was eliminated in Public Law 24 
2015, chapter 480.  25
