SUMMARY  6 
The funds provided by this bond issue, in the amount of $2,373,000, will be used to 7 
protect public health while preserving access to marine resources for commercial and 8 
recreational harvest, ensure effective enforcement of the marine resources laws and 9 
achieve cost savings at state facilities of the Department of Marine Resources.  10
