SUMMARY  22 
This bill removes the time restriction within which an applicant for independent 23 
practice dental hygienist authority must complete a specified number of hours of clinical 24 
practice.  The bill also provides an applicant for independent practice dental hygienist 25 
authority an alternative method of meeting the s pecific hours by allowing the applicant to 26 
provide proof of at least 5 years of clinical practice, which may include part -time work, 27 
as a licensed dental hygienist.  28
