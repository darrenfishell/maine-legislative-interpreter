SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the committee. The amendment reduces 
the maximum rate differential in premium rates for individual and small group health 
insurance policies on or after January 1, 2019 on the basis of geographic area from 1.5 to 
1.2 and tobacco use as dete1mined by ratio from 1.5 to 1 to 1.2 to 1 and maintains the rate 
differential due to age and group size at 3 to 1. The bill prohibits insurance carriers 
providing individual health plans or small group health plans from varying premium rates 
based on age, geographic location or tobacco use on or after January 1, 2018.
