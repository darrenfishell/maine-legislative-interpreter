SUMMARY  13 
This bill provides additional clarity, requirements and resources for the Maine 14 
Economic Growth Council's efforts to fulfill its current statutory mandate to develop, 15 
monitor and maintain a long -range strategic economic improvement plan for the State. It 16 
also includes provisions to support the State's achievement of the goals and objectives in 17 
that plan by establishing requireme nts for the Governor, Legislature and agencies with 18 
relevant programs and activities to consider the long -range strategic economic 19 
improvement plan and provide information to the Maine Economic Growth Council at the 20 
request of the council.  This bill would  increase the annual General Fund appropriation to 21 
the Maine Economic Growth Council from its current $55,000 to $175,000 and would 22 
provide a one -time additional appropriation of $150,000 for development of the initial 23 
plan in order to meet the timelines r equired in this bill.  24
