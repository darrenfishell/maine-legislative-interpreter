Sec. 5.  Duties.  Resolved:  That the commission shall create a strategic, regionally 23 
focused economic development plan for the State, including:  24 
1.  An inventory and analysis o f the State's competitive advantages and an inventory 25 
and analysis of factors that tend to retard economic development in the State;  26 
2.  Identification and analysis of state, national and international economic trends 27 
affecting the State's economic develop ment;  28 
3.  Development of strategies for the promotion of business formation, expansion, 29 
recruitment and retention through aggressive marketing, international development and 30 
export assistance and any other strategies identified as advantageous that lead to  more 31 
and better jobs and higher wages for all geographic regions and disadvantaged 32 
communities and populations of the State, including rural areas;  33 
4.  Development of policies and programs to further economic diversity in the State, 34 
its regions and their associated industries;  35 
5.  Stimulation of economic development and job creation in rural areas and smaller 36 
economic regions of the State, including strategies for rural marketing and the 37 
development of infrastructure, both physical and electronic, in rural  areas;  38
