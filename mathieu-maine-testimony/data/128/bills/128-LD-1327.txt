SUMMARY  33 
This bill allows a former United States Ar my medic, United States Air Force medical 34 
technician, United States Navy corpsman or United States Coast Guard health services 35 
technician to perform certain medical services under the supervision of a person holding a 36 
license to practice medicine, osteopat hic medicine or podiatry or at a health care facility 37 
under a medical practice agreement approved by the respective licensing board.  38
