SUMMARY  3 
This bill provides for a mental health liaison in each county or regional jail to oversee 4 
inmates with serious mental illness and connect them to the services for which they 5 
qualify, to work with t he court system to ensure that they receive due process and speedy 6 
trials and to assist inmates who qualify for MaineCare to apply for and receive 7 
MaineCare benefits and services.  This bill directs the Department of Health and Human 8 
Services to issue a re quest for proposals to private providers of behavioral health services 9 
and advocacy to replace intensive case manager positions within the department to staff 10 
the 15 mental health liaison positions in the county and regional jails.  11
