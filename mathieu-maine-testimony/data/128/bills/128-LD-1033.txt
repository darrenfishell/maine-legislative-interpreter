SUMMARY  28 
This bill removes the requirement for political candidates, party committees and 29 
political action committees to report to the Commission on Gov ernmental Ethics and 30 
Election Practices within 24 hours of receiving certain contributions or making certain 31 
expenditures after the 14th day before an election.  32
