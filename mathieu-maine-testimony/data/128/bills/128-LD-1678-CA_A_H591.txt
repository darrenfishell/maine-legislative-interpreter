COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 1166, L.D. 1678, Bill, "An Act To 
Amend the Laws Affecting the Judicial Branch Regarding Railroad Trespass Civil 
Violations and Fines for Civil Violations" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 2 in the next to the last line (page 1, line 10 
in L.D.) by inserting after the following: "and surcharges" the following: ', within the 
limits prescribed by law,' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment is the report of the Joint Standing Committee on Judiciary. It 
clarifies that when the Chief Judge of the District Court establishes the level of fines for 
civil violations for persons charged who wish to waive a court appearance, those fines 
must be within the limits prescribed by law.
