COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 871, L.D. 1248, Bill, "An Act To 
Improve Public Transportation in Maine" 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
summary and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. Requirements. The Department of Transportation shall administer funds 
in section 2 to local nonprofit volunteer driver networks that coordinate with recipients of 
the United States Department of Transportation, Federal Transit Administration funds, 
seek financial assistance from municipalities and other sources, charge modest fares and 
serve the general public for the purpose of leveraging the providers' experience, mobility 
management systems and other resources. The Department of Transportation shall 
administer funds for a pilot purchase of service program funded in section 2 in selected 
regions of the State to provide vouchers to senior citizens and persons with disabilities to 
purchase their own transp01tation services. The Department of Transportation shall 
distribute to recipients of Federal Transit Administration funds the funding provided in 
section 2 using the same formula as current state public transportation funding is 
distributed: 50% distributed to urban areas following the Federal Transit Administration 
formula and 50% distributed to rural areas following the standards governing the Federal 
Transit Administration formula grants for rural areas under 49 United States Code, 
Section 5311, which include the number of square miles in the service area, the 
population of the service area and the percentage of low-income individuals in the service 
area. 
Sec. 2. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and 
allocations are made. 
TRANSPORTATION, DEPARTMENT OF 
Multimodal -Transit 0443 
Initiative: Provides funding to create a pilot purchase of service program in selected 
regions of the State to provide senior citizens and persons with disabilities vouchers to 
purchase their own transportation services.
