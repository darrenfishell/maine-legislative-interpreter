SUMMARY  20 
This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to disclose 21 
information in its records under the Adult Protective Services Act to personal 22 
representatives of the estates of incapacitated or dependent adults who die while under 23 
public guardianship or public conservatorship.  24
