SUMMARY  29 
This bill expressly authorizes a council of governments to provide municipal services 30 
to a municipality pursuant to a contract between the council of governments and the 31 
municipality.  The bill amends the Maine Tort Claims Act to include in the definition of 32 
"political subdivision" a council of governments when it is providing municipal services 33 
pursuant to a contract between the council of governments and the municipality.  34
