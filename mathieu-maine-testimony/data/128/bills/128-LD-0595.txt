SUMMARY  27 
This bill prohibits a law enforcement officer from ordering a subordinate officer or 28 
other officer under the supervision of the law enforcement officer, pur suant to the 29 
authority of a federal law or regulation or executive order of the President of the United 30 
States, to seize or confiscate a firearm or firearm accessory or ammunition in the 31 
possession of a resident of this State.  The bill designates a first violation of the law as a 32 
civil violation, punishable by a fine of $1,000, and a second violation as a Class D crime.  33 
The provisions of the bill do not apply to a law enforcement officer who engages in the 34 
prohibited conduct if the firearm or firearm acce ssory or ammunition is being used in the 35 
commission of a Class A, Class B or Class C crime or if the law enforcement officer is 36 
assisting a federal law enforcement office or agency in conducting an investigative or 37 
enforcement action against suspected traf ficking in scheduled drugs under the Maine 38
