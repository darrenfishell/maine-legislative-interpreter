SUMMARY  7 
This bill  directs the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, the State 8 
Liquor and Lottery Commission to develop an early childhood education lottery game.  9 
Proceeds from the lottery game will be used to fund the Early Childhood Education Fund, 10 
which i s established for the purpose of providing grant funding for public preschool 11 
programs.  12
