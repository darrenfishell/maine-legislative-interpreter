SUMMARY  21 
This bill requires that instruction involving affirmative consent, communication and 22 
decision making regardi ng sexual activity be a mandatory part of secondary school 23 
instruction.  "Affirmative consent" is defined as consent to sexual activity that can be 24 
revoked at any time and does not include silence, lack of resistance or consent given 25 
while intoxicated.  Th is bill also requires a postsecondary educational institution to make 26 
a notation on a student's transcript if the student has been convicted of sexual assault 27 
committed on the campus of the postsecondary educational institution.  28
