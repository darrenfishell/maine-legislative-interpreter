This bill includes ongoing General Fund appropriations of $4.0 million per year beginning in fiscal year 2017-18 to 
the General Purpose Aid for Local Schools program within the Department of Education to be distributed as start-up 
grants for expanded access to public preschool programs for children 4 years of age. 
The bill also includes a General Fund appropriation of $75,436 in fiscal year 2018-19 to the Learning Systems 
program within the Depaiiment to continue 2 Education Specialist II positions that currently provide administrative 
and technical suppoti to the public preschool program that was implemented utilizing funds from a federal four-year 
$14.8 million Preschool Expansion Grant received by the Department in January of 2015. These positions were 
established as limited-period positions that will end when the federal grant expires in December of 2018. This bill 
includes General Fund supp01i for the 2 positions beginning in January of 2019. The General Fund cost to fully fund 
these positions after that is estimated to be $159,141 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $164,994 in fiscal year 2020-21. 
This bill includes an Other Special Revenue Funds allocation of $23,456 in fiscal year 2018-19 to the Leadership 
Team to suppoti a potiion of the cost of one of the 2 Education Specialist II positions that is funded by a transfer 
from the Child Development Services program.
