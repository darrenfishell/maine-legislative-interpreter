COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 661, L.D. 1776, Bill, "An Act To 
Establish Requirements for Civil Deputies" 
Amend the bill in section 5 in subsection 6 in the last line (page 2, line 3 in L.D.) by 
inserting after the following: "qualifications" the following: 'and training' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment specifies that a sheriff may adopt rules, procedures and 
requirements related to the training of a civil deputy, as well as related to the 
qualifications of a civil deputy as provided in the bill.
