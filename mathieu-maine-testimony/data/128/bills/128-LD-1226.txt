SUMMARY  11 
This bill identifies 3 revenue sources to increase funding to the Highway Fund for 12 
transportation infrastructure.  13 
This bill:  14 
1.  Imposes an annual registration fee of $250 on hybrid vehicles and $350 on electric 15 
vehicles instead of the annual $35 fee imposed on other passenger vehicles;  16 
2.  Requires municipalities to spend revenue they collect from excise taxes on 17 
transportation projects within their municipality; and  18 
3.  Diverts from municipalities to the Highway Fund the excise tax collected on 19 
trucks and truck tractors that haul trailers.  20
