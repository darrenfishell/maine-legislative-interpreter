Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill would authorize the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry (ACF) to amend a property lease 
at Long Falls Dam Road to allow a sublease for recreational purposes and the sale of ce1iain state-owned lands 
located in Oxford and Aroostook Counties. The bill does not specify the financial tenns of the authorized transactionc 
but does include a provision that any proceeds from sales be rep01ied back to the Committee on Agriculture, 
Conservation and Forestry. The bill also directs ACF to amend the annual reports on the Public Nonreserved Lands 
Acquisition Fund and the Public Reserved Lands Acquisition Fund to report the amount of funds in each by county.
