SUMMARY  16 
This bill amends the laws governing expenditures made for political communications 17 
advocating the election or defeat of a candidate or the election or defea t of a ballot 18 
measure.   The bill requires communications made by mail to be posted for delivery 19 
within 14 days of the expenditure being made.  The bill also requires that a copy of the 20 
communication be sent to the Commission on Governmental Ethics and Ele ction 21 
Practices in the same manner and form and at the same time as it is sent to those persons 22 
the communication is intended to influence.  23
