SUMMARY  13 
Current law provides an exception to the requirement to check prescription 14 
monitoring information when a health care professional orders or administers the 15 
medication to a person in an emergency room, an in patient hospital, a long -term care 16 
facility or a residential care facility.  This bill expands the exception to include when a 17 
health care professional orders, prescribes or administers medication to a person suffering 18 
from pain associated with cancer trea tment or end -of-life, palliative or hospice care.  19
