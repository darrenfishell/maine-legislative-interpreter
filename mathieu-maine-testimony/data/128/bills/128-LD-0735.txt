SUMMARY  10 
The funds provided by this bond issue, in the amount of $50,000,000, will be used to 11 
assist in the provision of energy -efficient affordable multifamily housing to low -income 12 
Maine seniors.  Forty million dollars of the  bond proceeds will be used to construct new 13 
multifamily housing for seniors and to adaptively reuse existing structures to provide 14 
multifamily housing for seniors.  At least one housing facility will be located in each of 15 
Maine's 16 counties.  Preference will be given to housing locations that have access to 16 
health care services and other essential goods and services.  Five million dollars will be 17 
used to rehabilitate existing affordable senior housing facilities.  Five million dollars will 18 
provide for hom e repair and weatherization of the existing homes of low -income seniors.  19 
The bond proceeds will leverage an estimated $75,000,000 in private and other funds.  20
