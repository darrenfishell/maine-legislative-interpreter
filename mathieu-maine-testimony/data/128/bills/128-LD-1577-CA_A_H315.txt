SUMMARY 
This amendment strikes the provision in the bill that reduces most violations of the 
International Fuel Tax Agreement laws from a Class E crime to a traffic infraction. The 
amendment also strikes the requirement that a vehicle offered for sale by a motor vehicle 
dealer must be accompanied by a valid certificate of title. 
The bill creates an additional fee for expediting the issuance of driver's licenses and 
for expediting duplicates of driver's licenses and nondriver identification cards. The 
amendment also creates an additional fee for expediting the issuance of nondriver 
identification cards.
