5 SUMMARY 
6 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, clarifies that the 
7 funding for the new child welfare information system is one-time funding. It requires the 
8 Department of Health and Human Services to report on the progress of the department in 
9 implementing the provisions of the legislation to the joint standing committee of the 
IO Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human services matters by January 31, 
11 2019. It also replaces the appropriations and allocations section.

The bill includes General Fund appropriations to the Department of Health and Human Services of $18,245,081 in

Federal Expenditures Fund allocations and Other Special Revenue Funds allocations for the federal match.
