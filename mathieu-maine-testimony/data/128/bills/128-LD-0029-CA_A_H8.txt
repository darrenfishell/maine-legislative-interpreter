COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" A" to H.P. 30, L.D. 29, Bill, "An Act To Reduce 
Penalties for Violations of the Motor Vehicle Inspection Laws" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Reduce Penalties for Operation of a Vehicle without a Certificate of 
Inspection' 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and before the summary and 
inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. 29-A MRSA §103, sub-§3, as amended by PL 1995, c. 584, Pt. B, §2, is 
further amended to read:

Sec. 2. 29-A MRSA §1768, sub-§7, as enacted by PL 1993, c. 683, Pt. A, §2 and 
affected by Pt. B, §5, is amended to read: 
7. Operation of vehicle without certificate of inspection. An owner or operator of 
a vehicle required to be inspected commits a traffic infraction if that person operates that 
vehicle or permits that vehicle to be operated without displaying a current and valid 
certificate of inspection or producing the certificate on demand of a police officer. The 
fine for a violation of this subsection may not be less than $25 nor more than $100 for the 
first offense and may not be less than $25 nor more than $250 for each subsequent 
offense.
