SUMMARY 
This amendment makes the following changes to the bill. 
1. It removes the provision creating the position of student loan ombudsman under 
the Superintendent of Consumer Credit Protection within the Depaitment of Professional 
and Financial Regulation, Bureau of Consumer Credit Protection. 
2. It clarifies the definition of "student education loan."
