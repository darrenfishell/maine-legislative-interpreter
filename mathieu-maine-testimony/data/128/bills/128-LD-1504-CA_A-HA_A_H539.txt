22 SUMMARY 
23 This amendment makes the following changes to Committee Amendment "A." 
24 1. It requires a net energy billing customer to receive 100% of the net energy of an 
25 eligible facility for the customer's transmission and distribution bill until December 31, 
26 2019 instead of December 31, 2021. 
27 2. It changes from 200 to 100 the limit on the number of eligible customers that may 
28 participate in a single shared interest in an eligible facility or the number of meters 
29 associated with a single shared interest, except in the service territory of a transmission 
30 and distribution utility located in an area administered by the independent system 
31 administrator for northern Maine.
