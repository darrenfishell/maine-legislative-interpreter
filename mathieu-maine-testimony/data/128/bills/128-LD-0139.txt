SUMMARY  31 
The funds provided by this bond issue, in the amount of $15,000,000, will be used to 32 
strengthen and expand business and industry training, upgrade and renovate  instructional 33 
technology facilities and laboratories at Maine's 7 community colleges in order to 34 
strengthen educational programming and workforce development and install energy 35 
efficiency upgrades across the system.  36
