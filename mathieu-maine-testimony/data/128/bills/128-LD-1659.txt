25 SUMMARY
26 This bill reduces  the Class I lobster and crab fishing license fee for applicants under 
27 18 years of age from  $65 to $60; clarifies lobster  license surcharge fees to avoid  conflicts;  
28 fixes an error  regarding resident  apprentice  licenses for applicants 18 years of age or 
29 older; and repeals  conflicting language  regarding sea urchin  and scallop diving tender  
30 license surcharges  to reflect  current practice.
