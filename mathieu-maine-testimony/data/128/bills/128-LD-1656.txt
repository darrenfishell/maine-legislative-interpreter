15 Sec. 2.  37-B MRSA §8-A  is enacted to read:
16 §8-A.  Issuance of free admission  pass to Maine State Museum to veterans
17 The Commissioner of Defense, Veterans  and Emergency  Management, in accordance 
18 with a memorandum of agreement  entered into with the Maine State Museum 
19 Commission under Title  27, section 83, subsection 5 and this section, shall issue a free 
20 admission pass to the Maine State Museum to eligible veterans pursuant  to subsection 1.
21 1.  Eligibility.   The department shall determine a person's eligibility for a pass under 
22 this section  by establishing  that the person  is a veteran and:
23 A.  The  person is a resident of this State; and
24 B.  The person received an honorable discharge  or general discharge under honorable 
25 conditions.
26 2.  Responsibilities of commissioner.  The Commissioner of Defense, Veterans and 
27 Emergency Management shall  identify a point of contact  within the department to issue 
28 free admission passes in accordance  with this section  and the memorandum  of agreement 
29 entered into with the Maine State Museum Commission pursuant to Title  27, section 83, 
30 subsection 5.  The Commissioner of Defense,  Veterans  and Emergency  Management  
31 shall periodically report to the Maine  State  Museum Commission a list of the names  and 
32 addresses of all persons receiving passes to the Maine  State  Museum and the beginning 
33 dates for those passes.  A pass issued under this section does not expire  and is valid for 
34 the lifetime of the  holder.
35 SUMMARY
36 This bill provides for  free admission  to the Maine State Museum for veterans.
