SUMMARY 
This amendment requires the court to treat the age of the person promoted into 
prostitution as an aggravating sentencing factor. This amendment also increases the class 
of crime for sex trafficking if the person who is trafficked is less than 18 years of age 
from a Class C crime to a Class B crime.
