22.  Pharmacist.  "Pharmacist" means an individual provider of health care services  4 
licensed by  this State to engage in the practice of pharmacy.  5 
A.  "Chain pharmacist" means an individual who is engaged in the practice of 6 
pharmacy within a chain; that is, where there is a corporate grouping of 4 or more 7 
pharmacies.  8 
B.  "Hospital pharmacist" means a n individual who is engaged in the practice of 9 
pharmacy in a hospital setting.  10 
C.  "Independent pharmacist" means an individual who is engaged in the practice of 11 
pharmacy in an independent pharmacy; that is, where there are fewer than 4 12 
pharmacies under th e same ownership.  13 
D.  "Qualified assistant pharmacist" means an individual licensed by this State as a 14 
qualified assistant apothecary, qualified assistant or assistant pharmacist, provided 15 
that the license is in full force and effect, except for the right to serve as a pharmacist 16 
in charge.  17
