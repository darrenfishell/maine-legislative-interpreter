not deny coverage on the basis that the health care s ervice is provided through telehealth 25 
if the health care service would be covered if it was provided through in -person 26 
consultation between an enrollee and a provider.  Coverage for health care services 27

SUMMARY  21 
This bill does the following.  22 
1.  It provides immunity from liability to health care practitioners who voluntarily 23 
provide health care services through telehealth in the same manner as immunity is 24 
provided to health care practitioners who v oluntarily provide health care services in 25 
person.  26 
2.  It requires insurers to provide professional liability insurance for health care 27 
services provided through telehealth services in the same manner as the coverage is 28 
provided through face -to-face contac t between a health care practitioner and a patient.  29 
3.  It requires carriers that offer health plans in this State to provide coverage for 30 
health care services provided through telehealth services in the same manner as coverage 31 
is provided for services pro vided in person and sets forth certain standards for coverage 32 
of telehealth services.  33
