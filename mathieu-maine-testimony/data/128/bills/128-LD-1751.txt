10 SUMMARY
11 Current law provides that compensation  may not be paid from the Victims' 
12 Compensation Fund to any claimant who does not fully  cooperate with the Victims' 
13 Compensation Board  or with the reasonable requests of law enforcement  officers or 
14 prosecution authorities  or to or on behalf of any person who violated  a criminal  law that 
15 caused or contributed to the injury or death for which compensation is sought.  This bill 
16 provides that the board may deny  compensation  in whole or in part in these 
17 circumstances.
