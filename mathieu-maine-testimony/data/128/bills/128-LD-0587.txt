SUMMARY  8 
This bill allows a person who has lawfully killed a wild animal to sell the meat of the 9 
animal at a wild game dinner or to the organizer of a wild game dinner.  The bill defines 10 
"wild game dinner" as a single event where meat from a lawf ully taken animal is cooked 11 
and sold to persons at the event for immediate consumption.  12
