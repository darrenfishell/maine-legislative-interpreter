SUMMARY  16 
Current law allows a brewery, winery or wholesaler to provide to a retail licensee 17 
samples of products, limited to 9 gallons of malt liquor and 9 liters of wine annually.  18 
This bill increases the annual limit to 18 gallons of malt liquor and 18 liters of wine.  19 
Current law allows a licensed sales representative for a distilled spirits manufacturer 20 
or suppler to give to a retail licensee a maximum of 3 liters per year per distiller 21 
represented by the sales repres entative.  This bill increases the annual limit to 6 liters per 22 
distiller.  23
