SENATE AMENDMENT "A " to S.O. 28, Order, "Senate Order, Authorizing the 
President of the Senate To Represent the Interests of the Senate To Preserve the Integrity 
of the Election Process" 
Amend the Senate Order in the Ordered paragraph in the last line by striking out the 
following: "them. 11 and inserting the following: 1them; and be it further1 
Amend the Senate Order by adding at the end the following: 
10RDERED, that any action by the President of the Senate authorized by this order 
must be approved by at least 2/3 of the elected members of the Senate, prior to the 
President taldng such action; and be it further 
ORDERED, that the authorization granted to the President of the Senate by this 
order expires 45 days after passage by the Senate.1 
SUMMARY 
This amendment requires that any action authorized by the order have the prior 
approval of at least 2/3 of the elected members of the Senate. This amendment also 
specifies that the authorization granted by the order expires 45 days after passage of the 
order by the Senate.
