SUMMARY  21 
This bill provides that a parent of a student requesting a transfer from one school 22 
administrative unit to another school administrative unit may not request t he 23 
Commissioner of Education to review a decision to not approve a transfer if both 24 
superintendents find a transfer is not in the student's best interest.  The bill also 25 
establishes the Commission To Study the Student Transfer Process to conduct a review o f 26 
the appeal process under the laws governing the transfer of a student from one school 27 
administrative unit to another school administrative unit, including, but not limited to, a 28 
review of the criteria used by the Commissioner of Education and the State B oard of 29 
Education to make a decision regarding a parent's request to review a decision to not 30 
approve a transfer.  31
