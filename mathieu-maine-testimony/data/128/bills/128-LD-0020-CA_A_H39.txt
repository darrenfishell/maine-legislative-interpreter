year 2017-18 and $636,217 in fiscal year 2018-19 to reimburse nursing homes for the losses of coinsurance and 
deductibles for skilled nursing beds under rules adopted by the Department of Health and Human Services as required 
in Public Law 2013, chapter 368, including an additional cost for Office oflnformation Technology development and
