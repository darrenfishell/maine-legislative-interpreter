SUMMARY  25 
This bill makes an applicant for general assistance who voluntarily abandons or 26 
refuses to use an available resource without just cause ineligible to receive general 27 
assistance to replace the abandoned or refused resource f or a period of 120 days from the 28 
date the applicant abandons or refuses the resource.  The bill defines "available resource" 29 
as a resource that is immediately available or can be secured without delay.  The bill also 30 
makes an applicant who forfeits an avai lable resource due to fraud, misrepresentation or 31 
intentional violation or refusal to comply with rules without just cause ineligible to 32 
receive general assistance to replace the forfeited resource for the duration of the sanction 33 
imposed on the applicant for violation of a rule or 120 days, whichever is greater.  The 34 
bill identifies circumstances relating to use of an available resource under which just 35 
cause must be found.  36
