SUMMARY  16 
This bill removes the general requirement that an insurer pr ovide written notice of its 17 
information practices on an annual basis or when a policy is reinstated or benefits are 18 
changed.  This bill requires that the notices must be provided if a change is made in the 19 
insurer's information practices.  20
