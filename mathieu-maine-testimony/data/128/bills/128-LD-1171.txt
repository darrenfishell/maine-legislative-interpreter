SUMMARY  20 
This bill establishes a position within the Finance Authority of Maine to assist Maine 21 
residents in communicating with secondary school guidance counselors, college career 22 
counseling services staff and the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, 23 
Maine Revenue Services and in other issues related to the Maine resident's participat ing 24 
in the Job Creation Through Educational Opportunity Program.  This bill also increases 25 
the amount to market the program throughout the State from $20,000 to $50,000.  26
