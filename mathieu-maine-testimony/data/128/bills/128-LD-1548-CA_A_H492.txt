10 SUMMARY 
11 This amendment changes administration of the proposed Let's Grow Maine Program 
12 from the Finance Authority of Maine to the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and 
13 Forestry, deletes provisions increasing taxes on certain tobacco products and provides 
14 funding for the program from Temporary Assistance for Needy Families block grant 
15 funds.

This bill establishes the Let's Grow Maine Pro.gram within the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry 
(ACF). The bill requires a transfer of $10,000,000. annually, beginning July l, 2019, from the Temporary Assistance 
for Needy Families program, Federal Block Grant Fund, to the Let's Grow Maine program within ACF. 
ACF will require a one-time allocation of $1,500,000 in fiscal year 2019-20 for renovations and expansion to their 
existing cold storage facility to accommodate the additional storage of procured produce. Starting in fiscal year 2020-
21 annual allocations of $50,000 will be required for operating costs of the renovated cold storage facility. ACF will 
also require allocations of $225,63 7 in fiscal year 2019-20 and $23 8, 114 in fiscal year 2020-21 for one Planning and 
Resource Associate I position, one Planning and Resource Associate II position, one Office Associate fI position, one 
pool vehicle and associated all other costs.
