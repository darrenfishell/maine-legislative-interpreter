SUMMARY  22 
This bill allows children 26 years of age and younger to remain o n their parents' 23 
health insurance policy.  It clarifies that carriers offering individual or group health plans 24 
may not establish lifetime or annual limits on the dollar value of benefits.  It clarifies that 25 
individual, group and blanket health plans may n ot impose a preexisting condition 26 
exclusion on any enrollee.  27
