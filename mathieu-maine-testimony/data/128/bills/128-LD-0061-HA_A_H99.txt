HOUSE AMENDMENT "A "to H.P. 48, L.D. 61, Bill, "An Act To Allow Bird 
Hunting on Sundays by Licensed Hunters Using a Shotgun" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Allow Bird Hunting on Sundays on Certain Lands by Licensed Hunters 
Using a Shotgun' 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
summary and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. 12 MRSA §11205, sub-§1, as affected by PL 2003, c. 614, §9 and 
amended by c. 655, Pt. B, §137 and affected by §422, is further amended to read: 
1. Prohibition. A person may not: 
A. Hunt wild animals or wild 1:Jirds on Sunday; er 
A-1. Except as provided in subsection 1-A, hunt wild birds on Sunday: or 
B. Possess any wild animal or wild bird taken in violation of paragraph A or A-1 
except as otherwise provided in this Part. 
Sec. 2. 12 MRSA §11205, sub-§1-A is enacted to read: 
1-A. Exception. A person may hunt wild birds on Sunday with a shotgun if the 
person is carrying a map of the land on which the person is hunting and written 
petmission to hunt on that land from the landowner.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment allows a licensed hunter to hunt 
wild birds on Sundays with a shotgun if the hunter is carrying a map and the landowner's 
written permission to hunt.
