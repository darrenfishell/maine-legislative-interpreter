SUMMARY  22 
This bill directs the Department of Health and Human Services to provide 23 
reimbursement to hospitals other than critical access hospitals for each day after the 10th 24 
day that a MaineCare -eligible individual is in th e care of a hospital while awaiting 25 
placement in a nursing facility.  The reimbursement is to be paid prospectively at the 26 
statewide average rate per MaineCare member day for nursing facility services.  The 27 
department is directed to implement this reimburs ement for days awaiting placement for a 28 
period limited to 5 years.  Reimbursement is limited to a maximum of $500,000 of 29
