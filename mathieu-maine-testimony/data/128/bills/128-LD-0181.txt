SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
The purpose of this bill is to attract and retain new teachers in the State.  This bill 5 
proposes to create incentiv es to attract students to teacher education programs in colleges 6 
and universities in the State and to retain those students to teach in schools in the State 7 
after graduation.  The bill also proposes to identify components of effective teacher 8 
preparation p rograms in colleges and universities, including, but not limited to, educating 9 
teachers to use technology in the classroom in new ways.  To encourage new teachers to 10 
stay in the State, the bill also proposes to establish a tuition reduction and loan repaym ent 11 
program for new teachers who teach in the State for 5 years after graduation.  12
