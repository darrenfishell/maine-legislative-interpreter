SENATE AMENDMENT" A" to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 634, 
L.D. 1735, Bill, "An Act To Authorize Regional Medical Control Committees To Have 
Access to Maine Emergency Medical Services Data for Purposes of Quality 
Improvement" 
Amend the amendment in section 3 by striking out all of paragraph H (page 1, lines 
32 to 34 in amendment) and inserting the following: 
'H. Confidential information submitted to Maine Emergency Medical Services by 
any entity must be easily accessible by that entity in accordance with rules adopted 
by the board that enable compliance by the entity with federal and state laws 
regarding patient information privacy and access.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces language in Committee Amendment "A" requiring that 
information submitted to the Department of Public Safety, Emergency Medical Services' 
Board by an entity be accessible by that entity without charge for a period of 4 years from 
the date of submission with language requiring that such information be accessible by 
that entity in accordance with rules adopted by the board that enable compliance with
