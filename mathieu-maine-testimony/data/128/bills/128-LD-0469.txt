SUMMARY  15 
This bill amends the State's coastal management laws to require the Department of 16 
Environmental Protection to apply to activities under its jurisdiction a generally 17 
applicable regulatory standard that addresses the  impacts of sea level change and 18 
increased storm surges on coastal resources within the coastal area of the State.  The bill 19 
directs the department to adopt rules establishing that standard on or before November 1, 20 
2018.  21
