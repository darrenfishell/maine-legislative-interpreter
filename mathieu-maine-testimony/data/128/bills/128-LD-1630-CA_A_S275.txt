COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A " to S.P. 580, L.D. 1630, Bill, "An Act To 
Improve the Effectiveness of Notices Required by State Law" 
Amend the bill by adding after section 4 the following: 
'Sec. 5. Calculation and transfer. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, 
the State Budget Officer shall calculate the amount of savings from no longer requiring 
legal notices to be published in a newspaper that applies against each General Fund 
account for all depmiments and agencies of the executive branch and shall transfer the 
amounts by financial order upon the approval of the Governor. These transfers are 
considered adjustments to appropriations in fiscal year 2018-19. 
Sec. 6. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and 
allocations are made.
