SUMMARY  6 
This resolve establishes the Commission To Study the Salaries of the Governor, 7 
Legislato rs and Executive and Legislative Staff.  The commission is charged with 8 
studying the salaries of the Governor, Legislators and executive and legislative staff 9 
members and making recommendations for any necessary changes.  The commission is 10 
required to subm it a report containing its findings and recommendations to the Second 11 
Regular Session of the 128th Legislature by December 6, 2017.  12
