9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A " to S.P. 125, L.D. 384, Bill, "An Act To 
10 Strengthen Maine Children's Mental Health" 
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
12 'Resolve, To Clarify Reimbursement for Parent-only Programs under the 
13 MaineCare Program' 
14 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and inserting the following: 
15 'Sec. 1. Department of Health and Human Services to reimburse for 
16 parent-only programs when allowable. Resolved: That the Department of Health 
17 and Human Services shall amend its rules in Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, 
18 Chapters II and III, Sections 28, 65 and 90 to reimburse for services provided to a parent 
19 or guardian of a child who qualifies for the MaineCare program but who is not present 
20 when the service is being provided, as long as the service relates to the child's plan of care 
21 and is permitted by the United States Department of Health and Human Services, Centers 
22 for Medicare and Medicaid Services. These services may be provided to parents or 
23 guardians individually or in groups as long as the service is allowable under federal law. 
24 Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules pursuant to the Maine 
25 Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.' 
26 SUMMARY 
27 This amendment replaces the bill, which is a concept draft, with a resolve that 
28 requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rules in Chapter 101: 
29 MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapters II and III, Sections 28, 65 and 90 to clarify that 
30 reimbursement is allowable for services provided to parents or guardians of children who 
31 are eligible for the MaineCare program but are not present when the service is being 
32 provided, as long as the service relates to the .child's plan of care and is permitted by the 
33 United States Department of Health and Human Services, Centers for Medicare and 
34 Medicaid Services. These services may be provided to parents or guardians individually
