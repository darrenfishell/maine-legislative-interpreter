SUMMARY 
The bill prohibits destroying, tearing down, defacing or othetwise damaging property 
posting signs by hunters and provides for the one-year suspension of the hunting license 
of a person who is convicted of doing so. This amendment provides for a one-year 
suspension of the person's fishing license as well.
