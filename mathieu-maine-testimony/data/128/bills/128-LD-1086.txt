SUMMARY  33 
This bill amends the election laws to create a nonpartisan primary process for the 34 
election of L egislators, United States Senators, Representatives to Congress and other 35 
state and county officers.  36 
The bill also amends the laws on the conduct of elections by requiring that a sample 37 
ballot be provided with an absentee ballot and that a sample ballot be  posted in or near 38

 each voting booth on election day.  It also removes the requirement that a voter must file 1 
an application to change party enrollment prior to January 1st to be eligible to file a 2 
petition as a candidate in that election year.  3
