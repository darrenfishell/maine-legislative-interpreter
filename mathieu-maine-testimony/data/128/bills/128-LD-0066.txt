SUMMARY  35 
Current law allows a public employer to deduct service fees owed by an employee to 36 
a collective bargaining agent pursuant to a lawful collective bargaining agreement.  This 37 
bill prohibit s a public employer from collecting those fees or collecting member dues.  38
