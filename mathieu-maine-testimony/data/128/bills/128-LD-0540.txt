 SUMMARY  1 
This bill amends the State's growth planning and land use laws to reflect that 2 
addressing the effects of sea level rise is a state planning and regulatory goal.  The bill 3 
amends the laws regarding the State's coastal management policies to direct state, local 4 
and certain federal agencies responsible for regulating, planning, developing or managing 5 
coastal resources to condu ct their activities affecting the coastal area consistent with the 6 
policy of encouraging the assessment of and planning for the effects of the rise in sea 7 
level.  The bill provides that a coastal municipality or multimunicipal region that includes 8 
a coasta l municipality, if the municipality or region adopts a growth management 9 
program under the State's growth planning and land use laws, may include in its 10 
comprehensive plan projections regarding sea level changes and the potential effects of 11 
the rise in sea  level and may develop a coordinated plan for addressing the effects of the 12 
rise in sea level.  The bill also provides that "coastal municipality" means a municipality 13 
or township in the coastal zone as identified by the Department of Agriculture, 14 
Conserva tion and Forestry, Maine Coastal Program.  15
