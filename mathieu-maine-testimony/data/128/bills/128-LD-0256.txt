 SUMMARY  1 
This bill changes the limit on the amount collected on prepaid wireless 2 
telecommunications service from a percentage to a flat amount.  This bill also requires 3 
the Public Utilities Commission to convene a stakeholder group to evaluate an d provide 4 
recommendations regarding the method of collection of funds to support 5 
telecommunications access at libraries and public schools.  The commission is required to 6 
report to the Joint Standing Committee on Energy, Utilities and Technology by January  7 
30, 2018, and the committee is authorized to submit a bill based on the report.  8
