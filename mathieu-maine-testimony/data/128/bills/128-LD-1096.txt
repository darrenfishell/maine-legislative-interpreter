SUMMARY  10 
This bill amends the laws relating to the State's regulation of the shoreland zone as 11 
follows.  12 
1.  It requires the Department of Economic and Community Development, Office of 13 
Community Development to provide both basic and advanced training t o code 14 
enforcement officers in the technical and legal aspects of code enforcement.  Current law 15 
only requires that the office provide basic training to code enforcement officers.  16 
2.  It increases the maximum per day civil penalty for a specific violation of a 17 
municipal land use law or ordinance from $2,500 to $5,000 and increases the maximum 18 
per day civil penalty for a specific violation of a municipal shoreland zoning ordinance 19 
occurring within an area zoned for resource protection from $5,000 to $10,000.  20 
3.  It provides that municipal shoreland zoning ordinances must require an applicant 21 
for a permit for development within the shoreland zone to provide to the municipal 22 
permitting authority preconstruction and postconstruction photographs of the shoreline 23 
vegetation and development site.  24
