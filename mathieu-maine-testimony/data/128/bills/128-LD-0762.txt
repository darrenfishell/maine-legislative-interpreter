SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to dedicate a percentage of the funds derived from the Medical Use 5 
of Marijuana Fund established  in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 22, section 2430 to a 6 
medical marijuana research fund.  Under the bill, hospitals and other health care facilities 7 
may apply for grants to fund research proposals to study the medical efficacy of medical 8 
marijuana.  9
