SUMMARY  33 
This bill makes the reimbursement rate for transfers of inmates from a county jail to 34 
another county jail or to the Department of Corrections equal to the federal 35 
reimbursement rate for housing prisoners, but no le ss than $90 per diem per prisoner.  36
