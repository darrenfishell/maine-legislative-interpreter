SUMMARY  18 
This bill requires a health care practitioner or facility to provide health care 19 
information of a deceased person to an immediate family member upon reques t of the 20 
family member, including the parent or guardian of a minor child of the deceased when 21 
there is documented need for parental medical history for the health and well -being of 22 
that minor child.  23
