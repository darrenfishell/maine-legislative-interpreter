ยง264.  Living kidney donor grant program  3 
The department shall establish a grant program to encourage living kidney donation 4 
by removing financial barrie rs for living kidney donors.  The program must make grants 5 
available to living kidney donors to cover lost wages and unreimbursed travel, lodging 6 
and medical expenses of the donor associated with the kidney donation.  A living kidney 7 
donor may be awarded a  grant under this section of not more than $5,000.  For purposes 8 
of this section, "living kidney donor" means an individual who is not deceased from 9 
whom a kidney is removed for transplant to another individual.  10 
SUMMARY  11 
This bill directs the Department of Health and Human Services to establish a grant 12
