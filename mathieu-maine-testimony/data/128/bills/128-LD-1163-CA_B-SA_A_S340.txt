SUMMARY 
This amendment increases the General Fund obligation amount from $25,000,000, as 
proposed in Committee Amendment "B," to $40,000,000.

This amendment increases the minority report by $15,000,000. As amended the total amount of the bond is

Increasing the bond amount from $25,000,000 to $40,000,000 will increase the estimated cost to the Finance 
Authority of Maine to establish and operate the Student Debt Cancellation and Refinance Program by between 
$45,000 and $60,000 per year, or between 3% and 4%, assuming additional annual bond activity of $1,500,000 per 
year over the 10 year period.
