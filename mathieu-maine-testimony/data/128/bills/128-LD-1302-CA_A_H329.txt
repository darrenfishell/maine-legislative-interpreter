COMMITTEE AMENDMENT «A" to H.P. 899, L.D. 1302, Bill, "An Act To 
Increase Workforce Participation in the Temporary Assistance for Needy Families 
Program and Other Assistance Programs" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Increase Workforce Participation in the Temporary Assistance for 
Needy Families Program' 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
summary and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. 22 MRSA §3782-A, sub-§5-A is enacted to read: 
5-A. Transportation assistance. The department shall provide assistance with 
motor vehicle repairs when the vehicle is necessary for a participant in the 
ASPIRE-TANF program to engage in employment, educational or training activities 
required by the program. The department may provide assistance under this subsection in 
an amount that is no more than $2,000 per family in each calendar year. Assistance under 
this subsection must be supported with federal block grant funds provided under the 
United States Social Security Act, Title N, Part A. The department shall adopt rules 
relating to proof of employment. proof of an employment-related need for a motor 
vehicle, household income requirements and verification of motor vehicle repairs. 
Sec. 2. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and 
allocations are made. 
HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF (FORMERLY DHS) 
Temporary Assistance for Needy Families 0138 
Initiative: Provides funding up to $2,000 to families receiving Tempormy Assistance for 
Needy Families benefits for motor vehicle repairs when the motor vehicle is needed for 
employment purposes.
