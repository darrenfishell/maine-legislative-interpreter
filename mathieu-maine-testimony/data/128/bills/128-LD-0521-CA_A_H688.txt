SUMMARY 
This amendment is the majority report of the committee and replaces the bill. The 
amendment provides that a veteran who is a member of the Maine Public Employees 
Retirement System and who has been awarded disability compensation by the United 
States Department of Veterans Affairs for a service-connected disability based on a 
determination of individual unemployability is presumed to be disabled under the Maine 
Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 17921, subsection 1 and section 18521, subsection 1. 
This presumption may be rebutted only by evidence not considered by the United States 
Department of Veterans Affairs. The provisions of the amendment apply to any 
application for a disability retirement benefit filed on or after October 1, 2018.
