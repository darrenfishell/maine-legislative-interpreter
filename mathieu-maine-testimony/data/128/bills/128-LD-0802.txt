SUMMARY  21 
This bill establishes the Public Advocate Advisory Board.  The board consists of 3 22 
members, one appointed by the Governor, one ap pointed by the President of the Senate 23 
and one appointed by the Speaker of the House of Representatives.  The board appoints 24 
the Public Advocate to a 6 -year term, subject to confirmation by the Legislature, and 25 
provides policy guidance to the Public Advoca te.  The Public Advocate's first 6 -year term 26 
begins February 1, 2018.  27
