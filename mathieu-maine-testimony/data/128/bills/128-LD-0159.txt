SUMMARY  11 
This bill amends the laws regarding mining on state lands to prohibit the issuance of a 12 
mining lease that authorizes the mining of a massive sul fide ore deposit located on state 13 
lands. The bill defines a "massive sulfide ore deposit" as a metal sulfide ore deposit that, 14 
as determined by the Maine Geological Survey, contains in total 1,000,000 tons or more 15 
of metallic minerals.  16
