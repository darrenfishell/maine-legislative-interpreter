SUMMARY  15 
This bill provides an additional income tax deduction for intere st paid on qualified 16 
education loans that exceeds the amount allowable under the United States Internal 17 
Revenue Code.  The maximum deduction is increased from $2,500 to $5,000 and the 18 
modified adjusted gross income thresholds for phase -out of the deduction  are increased 19 
by $15,000 for taxpayers filing as single individuals and $30,000 for individuals filing a 20 
joint return.  21
