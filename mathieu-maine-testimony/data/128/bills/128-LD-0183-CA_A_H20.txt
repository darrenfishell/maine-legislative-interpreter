SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies that a person authorized to complete or file a certificate of 
death, except for a certificate filed by a family member or domestic partner of the 
deceased, must use the electronic death registration system maintained by the State 
Registrar of Vital Statistics. This amendment also adds an effective date.
