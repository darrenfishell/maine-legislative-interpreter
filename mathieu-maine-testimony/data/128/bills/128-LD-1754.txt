Page 1 - 128LR2657(01)-11 Sec. 1.  Funding for rural roads.  Resolved:  That the Department of 
2 Transportation shall prioritize and increase funding  for the construction  and repair  of 
3 roads classified as part of a rural road system under rules adopted by the department 
4 pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 23, section 53, subsection 1.  The 
5 department shall give the highest priority  to rural roads that provide  the greatest 
6 economic benefit to the State,  including, but not limited  to, roads heavily used by the 
7 forestry and agricultural industries.
8 SUMMARY
9 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to prioritize and increase  
10 funding for the construction and repair of rural roads,  giving the highest  priority  to rural  
11 roads that  provide  the greatest economic benefit to the  State.
