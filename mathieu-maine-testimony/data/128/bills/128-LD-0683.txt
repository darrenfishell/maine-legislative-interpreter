SUMMARY  3 
This bill phases out beverage containers 46 ounces and greater in capacity from the 4 
bottle redemption laws beginning December 1, 2017.  It ends the liability of redemption 5 
centers and dealers to consum ers for deposits January 1, 2018 and the liability of 6 
manufacturers and distributors February 1, 2019.  The bill requires manufacturers of 7 
certain refillable containers and distributors of certain nonrefillable containers to pay a 8 
fee, beginning December 1 , 2017 and ending December 1, 2022, at the rate of $0.005 per 9 
beverage container delivered for sale or distribution during any month.  All of the 10 
revenue from the fee is deposited into the Maine Solid Waste Management Fund for use 11 
by the Maine Solid Waste Diversion Grant Program.  12
