SUMMARY  14 
This bill extends through 2026 the income tax credit for eligible dentists who practice 15 
in underserved areas.  With respect to the primary care access credit, the bill increases the 16 
number of primary care professionals who practice in underserved areas who may be 17 
certified for the credit and allows the credit for primary care professionals to remain in 18 
effect beyond January 1, 2019.  19
