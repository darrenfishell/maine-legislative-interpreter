SUMMARY  6 
This resolve establishes the Commission To Study the Adequacy of Housing Safety 7 
Laws.  The commission is charged with studying the housing safety laws, including 8 
landlor d and tenant relations and rights, the eviction process, bank foreclosure actions on 9 
abandoned property and rental housing fire safety issues, and making recommendations 10 
for any necessary changes.  The commission is required to submit a report containing i ts 11 
findings and recommendations to the Joint Standing Committee on Veterans and Legal 12 
Affairs by December 6, 2017.  13
