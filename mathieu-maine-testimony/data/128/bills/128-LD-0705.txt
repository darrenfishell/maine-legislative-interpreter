SUMMARY  9 
This bill prohibits a person on a vessel fishing for lobster or crab from having in that 10 
person's possession materials used to package lobster or crab bait for sale, including the 11 
plastic wrapping and cardboard cartons in which bait is sold.  It also prohibits a person 12 
from discarding or abandoning into any waters the materials used for packaging lobster 13 
and crab bait for sale.  Violation of this provision is a civil violation, with a fine of $250.  14
