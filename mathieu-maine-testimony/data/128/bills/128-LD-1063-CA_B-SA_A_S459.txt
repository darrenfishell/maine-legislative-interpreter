SENATE AMENDMENT " A " to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "B" to H.P. 746, 
L.D. 1063, Bill, "An Act To Protect Substance-exposed Infants" 
Amend the amendment in section 1 in the 3rd line (page 1, line 16 in amendment) by 
inserting after the following: "organization" the following: ', but not including an 
organization that provides abortion services,' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment prohibits the Department of Health and Human Services from 
contracting with an organization that provides abortion services to develop outreach and 
educational programs as required by the committee amendment.
