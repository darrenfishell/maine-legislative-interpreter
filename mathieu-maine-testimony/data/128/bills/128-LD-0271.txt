This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes that the Department of Health and Human Services conduct an 5 
effort similar to the effort conduct ed by the New Hampshire Department of Health and 6 
Human Services to coordinate public and private service providers in the areas of aging 7 
and senior services, children’s services and school systems, employment and vocational 8 
services, faith -based groups and  health care and medical services to inquire about former 9 
military service of clients and customers to identify veterans and direct them and their 10 
families to resources and benefits of which the veterans may not be aware or are reluctant 11 
to receive.  12
