4 ยง11251.  Open  and closed seasons
5 1.  Open season on bear; commissioner's authority.  This subsection governs the 
6 open and closed seasons  on bear.
7 A.    There  is an open  season on hunting bear from the first Monday preceding 
8 September 1st to November 30th annually.  The commissioner may, pursuant  to 
9 section 10104, subsection 1, adopt  rules prohibiting the use of bait to hunt black  bear 
10 during any portion of the open bear hunting season.  Rules  adopted  pursuant to this 
11 paragraph are routine technical rules  as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 
12 2-A.
13 B.    There  is an open  season on using a dog or dogs  in conjunction with bear hunting 
14 from the first Monday preceding September  1st to the day preceding the open  firearm 
15 season on deer provided in sections 11401 and  11402.
16 3.  Youth bear hunting day.  The commissioner may establish by rule a youth 
17 hunting day for hunting bear. An adult  supervisor, parent  or guardian accompanying  a 
18 youth on a youth  hunting  day for hunting bear may possess  a firearm while  the youth is 
19 hunting bear.
20 Rules adopted pursuant to this section  are routine technical rules  as defined in Title 5, 
21 chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
22 SUMMARY
23 This bill allows the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to establish a 
24 youth hunting  day for hunting bear.   An adult supervisor, parent  or guardian 
25 accompanying the  youth is allowed to possess  a firearm while  the youth is hunting bear.
