 SUMMARY  1 
This bill replaces the Board of Trustees of the University of Maine System, the Board 2 
of Trustees of the Maine Community College System and the Board of Trustees of the 3 
Maine Maritime Academy with the Board of Trustees of Higher Education to provide a 4 
unified body for the governance of t hose institutions.  5 
Part A makes changes to the law to replace some select references to the individual 6 
boards of trustees with references to the unified board and enacts a new chapter in the 7 
Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20 -A with provisions that establish  the new unified board.  8 
Part B establishes a transition team to recommend further changes to the law to fully 9 
accomplish the transition to the unified board.  The transition team will recommend 10 
specific changes to determine the qualifications of trustees a nd the powers and duties of 11 
the unified board in a report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 12 
jurisdiction over education and cultural affairs.  The report of the transition team must 13 
also recommend changes to the administration or or ganization of and the existing law 14 
governing the University of Maine System, the Maine Maritime Academy and the Maine 15 
Community College System to ensure the efficient operation under the unified board and 16 
to avoid any potential conflicts with the law estab lishing the unified board.  The joint 17 
standing committee is authorized to submit a bill to the Second Regular Session of the 18 
128th Legislature based upon the report.  19
