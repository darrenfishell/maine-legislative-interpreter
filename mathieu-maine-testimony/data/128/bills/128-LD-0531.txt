This bill requires a person 13 years of age or older who is receiving medical services 13 
in a hospital or from a primary care provider to be offered an HIV test unless that person 14 
is being treated for a life -threatening  emergency, has previously been offered or been the 15 
subject of an HIV test or lacks the capacity to provide informed consent to an HIV test.  16
