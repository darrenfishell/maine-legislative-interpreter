Fiscal Note 
Potential current biennium cost increase -Other Special Revenue Funds 
Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill extends the time that the Public Utilities Commission (PUC) can execute an energy cost reduction contract 
(ECRC) from December 31, 2018, to December 31, 2020. If the PUC were to execute an ECRC within the extended 
timeframe, it is anticipated that any additional costs to the PUC can be managed within existing budgeted resources.
