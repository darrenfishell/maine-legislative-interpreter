SUMMARY  17 
This bill repeals the prohibition against the withdrawal of the Town of Frye Island 18 
from School Administrative Di strict No. 6, which then allows the Town of Frye Island 19 
the same statutory municipal power of any other municipality in Maine, as long as the 20 
Town of Frye Island joins another school administrative unit within 2 years of the 21 
withdrawal.  22
