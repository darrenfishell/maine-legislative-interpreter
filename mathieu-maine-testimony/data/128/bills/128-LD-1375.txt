 SUMMARY  1 
This bill directs the Department of Health and Human Services to certify 2 facilities 2 
in the State to provide safe and secure location s for people to self -administer previously 3 
obtained drugs.  The bill requires the facilities to have health care personnel and other 4 
trained staff, to provide information concerning drug overdoses and diseases associated 5 
with drug use, to administer first aid or other medications in case of an overdose and to 6 
provide referrals to other services clients of the facilities may need.  The bill immunizes 7 
from arrest or prosecution clients and staff members acting in accordance with the 8 
provisions of the bill and  creates a tolerance zone with 0.5 miles of the facility.  The 9 
facilities are directed to report certain demographic and other information to the 10 
department, which is directed to analyze the information and report to the joint standing 11 
committee of the Leg islature having jurisdiction over health and human services matters. 12 
The department is also directed to review the effectiveness of the facilities to determine 13 
whether to open additional facilities.  14
