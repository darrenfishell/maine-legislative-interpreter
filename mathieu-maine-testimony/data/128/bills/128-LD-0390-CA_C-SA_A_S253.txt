SENATE AMENDMENT" A" to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "C" to H.P. 281, 
L.D. 390, Bill, "An Act Making Unified Appropriations and Allocations for the 
Expenditures of State Government, General Fund and Other Funds and Changing Certain 
Provisions of the Law Necessary to the Proper Operations of State Government for the 
Fiscal Years Ending June 30, 2018 and June 30, 2019" 
Amend the amendment by striking out all of Part D. 
Amend the amendment by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter 
or section number to read consecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment strikes that Part that eliminates the 3 % income tax surcharge 
imposed on taxable income in excess of $200,000.
