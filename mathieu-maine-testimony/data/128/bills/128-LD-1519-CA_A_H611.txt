COMMITTEE AMENDMENT .. ft .. to H.P. 1043, L.D. 1519, Bill, "An Act To 
Define the Intertidal Zone for the Management and Enforcement of Shellfish 
Conservation Ordinances" 
Amend the bill by striking out all of section 2 (page 1, lines 14 to 17 in L.D.) and 
inserting the following: 
'Sec. 2. 12 MRSA §6671, as amended by PL 2013, c. 301, §14; c. 468, §31; and c. 
517, § 1, is further amended by adding at the end a new paragraph to read: 
For the purposes of this section, "intertidal zone" means the shores. flats or other land 
below the high-water mark and above subtidal lands.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment provides that, for the purposes of municipal shellfish conservation 
programs, the intertidal zone is the area below the high-water mark and above subtidal 
lands, instead of the area between the high-water mark and the extreme low-water mark, 
as the bill provided.
