This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to establish a working group that will include representatives of the 5 
judicial branch to review mandatory minimum sentences that are currently required under 6 
state law and to assess whether any of those mandatory minimum sentence requirements 7 
should be amended or repealed.  8
