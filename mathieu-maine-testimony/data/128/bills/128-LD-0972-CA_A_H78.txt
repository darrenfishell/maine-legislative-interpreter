SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the committee. The amendment makes the 
following changes to the bill. 
1. It provides for 3-year terms of the advisory committee voting members, instead of 
one-year terms as proposed in the bill. 
2. It makes the legislative member a nonvoting member, except in the case of a tie 
vote.
