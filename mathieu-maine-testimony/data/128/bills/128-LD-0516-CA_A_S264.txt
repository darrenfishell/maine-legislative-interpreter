21 SUMMARY 
22 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. The amendment does the 
23 following. 
24 1. The amendment provides that if a probationer is committed to jail without bail 
25 pending a probation revocation hearing, the date of the hearing must be set no later than 
26 45 days from the date of the initial appearance. The amendment eliminates the provision 
27 that allows the court in its discretion to allow the hearing to be held after 45 days. 
28 2. The amendment requires that, beginning November 1, 2017, the county jails and 
29 regional jail report to the Department of Corrections regarding interjail boarding rates. 
30 The amendment requires that by January 15th each year, beginning January 15, 2018, the 
31 Department of Corrections report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature 
32 having jurisdiction over criminal justice and corrections matters regarding interjail 
33 boarding rate information and any recommend.ations. 
34 3. The amendment requires that, beginning November 1, 2017, the county jails and 
35 regional jail report to the Department of Corrections regarding financial audits performed 
36 for the jails. The amendment requires that by January 15th each year, beginning January 
37 15, 2018, the Department of Corrections report to the joint standing committee of the 
3 8 Legislature having jurisdiction over criminal justice and corrections matters regarding 
39 data derived from the financial audit information and any recommendations. 
40 4. The amendment requires that, beginning November 1, 2017, each county jail and 
41 the regional jail report twice per month to the Unified Criminal Docket in the judicial
