 SUMMARY  1 
This bill provides that the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands within the 2 
Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry may lease observation stands and 3 
shelters and convey trail easements on public lands for private use, in addition to 4 
campsites, garages and other structures that currently m ay be leased.  It provides that the 5 
director must lease up to one observation stand or shelter and convey up to a mile of trail 6 
easement for each lodging room at a commercial sporting camp. It specifies that the site 7 
location and trail easement must be as requested by the commercial sporting camp owner 8 
unless the director determines the location is inconsistent with other uses of the public 9 
reserved land or with applicable laws or rules. It provides for a minimum annual 10 
commercial sporting camp observation stand or shelter lease fee of $100.  11
