SUMMARY  32 
This bill prohibits insurance carriers providing individual health plans or small group 33 
health plans from varying premium rates based on age, geographic location or tobacco 34 
use on or after January 1, 2018.  The bill a lso reduces the variation based on group size to 35 
1.5 to 1 for small group plans over time.  36
