 SUMMARY  1 
This bill increases the income tax credit for child care expenses from 25% to 50% of 2 
the federal tax credit allowable for child and dependent care expenses and increases the 3 
amount that is refundable from $500 to $750.  The increase applies to tax years beginning 4 
in or after 2017.  5
