SUMMARY  20 
This bill requires the form for opening a joint account at a financial institution to have 21 
for each party to the account the question, "Do you intend for the sum remaining upon 22 
your death  to belong to the surviving party or parties?    Yes    No." Each party to the 23 
joint account must answer the question in writing on the form prior to opening the 24 
account.  25
