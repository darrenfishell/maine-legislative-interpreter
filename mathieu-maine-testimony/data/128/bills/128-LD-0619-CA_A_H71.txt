COMMITTEE AMENDMENT./},, to H.P. 435, L.D. 619, "Resolve, Authorizing 
the Area of the West Side of Little Sebago Lake of the Town of Gray To Proceed with the 
Secession Process" 
Amend the resolve by striking out everything after the title and before the summary 
and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. West side of Little Sebago Lake in the Town of Gray authorized 
to proceed with the secession process. Resolved: That, pursuant to the Maine 
Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, section 2171-C-l, the west side of Little Sebago Lake in the 
Town of Gray is authorized to proceed with the secession process in accordance with 
Title 30-A, chapter 113, subchapter 2.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the committee, The amendment replaces the 
resolve, which was a concept draft. The amendment authorizes the west side of Little 
Sebago Lake in the Town of Gray to proceed with the secession process in accordance 
with the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, chapter 113, subchapter 2.
