COMMITTEE AMENDMENT " If,, to H.P. 890, L.D. 1277, Bill, "An Act To 
Combat Human Trafficking by Requiring Prevention Training for Commercial Drivers" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Require the Secretary of State To Inform Commercial Drivers about 
Human Trafficking Prevention' 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
summary and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. 29-A MRSA ยง1253, sub-ยง6 is enacted to read: 
6. Human trafficking prevention; information. A commercial driver's license 
issued or renewed by the Secretaiy of State must be accompanied by information that 
outlines how to recognize human trafficking and how to report human trafficking and 
includes a telephone number for a national human trafficking hotline.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment strikes the requirement in the bill that an applicant for a new 
commercial driver's license complete a nationally recognized training program on 
preventing human trafficking in the trucking industry. It retains the provision in the bill 
that requires that all new and renewed commercial driver's licenses be accompanied by 
information that outlines how to recognize human trafficking and how to report human 
trafficking and includes a telephone number for a national human trafficking hotline.
