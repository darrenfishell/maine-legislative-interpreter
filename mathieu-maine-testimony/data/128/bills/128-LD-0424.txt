SUMMARY  28 
This bill authorizes the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to allow 29 
hunting on Sunday and directs the commissioner to allow hunting on 5 Sundays for the 30 
respective hunting seasons for bear, moose, deer and wild turkey in coastal wildlife 31 
management areas on public land and private land of 5 acres or more with the 32 
landowner's permission.  33
