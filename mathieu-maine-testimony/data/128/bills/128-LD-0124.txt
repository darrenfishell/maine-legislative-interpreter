SUMMARY  14 
This bill increases the total amounts the State may loan from the Potato Marketing 15 
Improvement Fund from 45% of project cost to 55% of project cost for projects that cost 16 
more than $50,000 but less than $150,000.  Loans for projects of $50,000 and under 17 
remain unchanged at 55% and loans for projects tha t exceed $150,000 remain unchanged 18 
at 45%.  19
