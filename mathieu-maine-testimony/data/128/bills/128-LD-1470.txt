SUMMARY  18 
This bill enacts chapter 113 -B in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 20 -A to facilitate 19 
voluntary collaboration and cooperation among school administrative units and career 20 
and technical education regions.  It repeals chapter 113 -A, "Regional Education 21 
Cooperatives," and chapter 114, "Regional Collaboration," and enacts a statute modeled 22 
on Title 30 -A, chapter 115, "Interlocal Coop eration."  Under this new chapter, school 23 
administrative units and career and technical education regions are authorized to exercise 24 
any of their powers on a joint or cooperative basis, including the undertaking of joint 25 
school construction projects and th e issuance of bonds or notes.  26
