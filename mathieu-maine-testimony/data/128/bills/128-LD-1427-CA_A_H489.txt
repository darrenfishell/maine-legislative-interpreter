lines (page 1, lines 14 and 15 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "a community 
paramedicine project" and inserting the following: 'community paramedicine services' 
Amend the bill by striking out all of section 2. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment changes the term in the bill "community paramedicine projects" to 
"community paramedicine services." The amendment strikes the provision of the bill that 
prohibits the use of state funds for the purposes of the bill.
