Fiscal Note 
Continuation ofrevenue from leasing of state property -Other funds 
This legislation authorizes the Department of Administrative and Financial Services to lease school property at the 
Maine Educational Center for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing and the Governor Baxter School for the Deaf 
(MECDHH/GBSD) on Mackworth Island to the State's protection and advocacy agency for persons with disabilities. 
State law currently allows for the leasing of unused school property at MECDHH/GBSD on Mackworth Island for 
certain other activities.
