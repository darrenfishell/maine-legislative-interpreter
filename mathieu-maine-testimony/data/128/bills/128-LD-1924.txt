2 This bill requires that a school entity notify the Department of Education  when  it 
3 opens an investigation into a holder  of a department-issued  credential  and provide all 
4 final reports  on that investigation to the department.  It requires that the department notify  
5 a school entity when it is investigating a holder of a department-issued credential who 
6 works for the school entity and requires  the department to provide  all final reports  on the 
7 outcome of that investigation to all school entities for which that credential  holder works.  
8 When a school entity  notifies the department of an investigation  into a credential  holder, 
9 the department must then notify all the school entities for which  that credential holder 
10 works of the investigation and provide  to them all final reports the department receives.
