SUMMARY  23 
This bill requires the Department of Education to include the so -called Next 24 
Generation Science Standards for kindergarten to grade 12 in the State's system of 25 
learning results and assessment and directs the Commissioner of Education to amend 26 
Department of Education rules on or before December 31, 2017 in order to include the 27 
science standards as part of the State's system of learning results and assessment 28 
beginning with the 2019 -2020 school year.  29
