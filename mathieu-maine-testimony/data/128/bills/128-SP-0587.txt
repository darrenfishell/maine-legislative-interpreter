Joint Orders 
 
 
(4-1)  On motion by Senator MIRAMANT  of Knox, the following Joint Order: 
S.P. 587 
 
STATE OF MAINE  
128 TH  MAINE LEGISLATURE 
 
ORDERED, the House concurring, that the Joint Standing Commi ttee on Environment and 
Natural Resources report out a bill to the Senate t hat requires the State to: 
1.  Implement measures designed to meet, as practic able and on a state level, the broad goals 
of the Paris Agreement on climate change signed by the United States on April 22, 2016 and 
entered into force on November 4, 2016, referred to  in this order as "the Paris Agreement," 
including measures to reduce carbon emissions withi n the time limits proposed in the Paris 
Agreement; or 
2.  Join with any other states that have formed a c oalition to implement and comply with the 
goals of the Paris Agreement.
