SUMMARY  16 
This bill updates the law reg arding interstate and intrastate telecommunications 17 
access rates.  The bill also repeals obsolete provisions of law requiring persons providing 18 
intrastate interexchange service to reduce their intrastate long -distance rates in the 19 
absence of effective comp etition, prohibiting mandatory local measured telephone 20 
service, prohibiting the Public Utilities Commission from requiring a local exchange 21 
carrier to consider competitive bids on the construction of an interexchange facility, 22 
prohibiting a telephone util ity offering intrastate toll service from increasing prices 23 
without filing a tariff revision with the commission and providing subscribers written 24 
notice and prohibiting a provider of prepaid calling service from increasing rates on 25 
already purchased servi ce and requiring notice to prepaid calling service consumers about 26 
differences between in -state and interstate service rates.  27
