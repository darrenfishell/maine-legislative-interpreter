SUMMARY  27 
This bill changes the selection process for the position of Commissioner of 28 
Education.  Instead of being appointed by the Governor, under this bill, the commissioner 29 
is selected by the State Board of Education in consultation with the Governor.  The 30 
candidate selected by the State Board of Education would be subject to legislative review 31 
and confirmation.  32
