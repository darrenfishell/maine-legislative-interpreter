SUMMARY  3 
This bill adds duties for the Maine Children's Growth Council.  The bill requires the 4 
Maine Children's Growth Council to include additional information in the council's report 5 
to the Governor and certain legislative committees.  It changes the reporting requirement 6 
from every 2 years to annually and includes the Legislative Council as a recipient of the 7 
report.  The bill directs the Maine Children's Growth Council to develop an evaluation 8 
process and to periodically p erform an evaluation of its effectiveness in performing its 9 
duties.  The bill authorizes the Maine Children's Growth Council to receive staff 10 
assistance from the Child Development Services System and the Department of Health 11 
and Human Services.  12
