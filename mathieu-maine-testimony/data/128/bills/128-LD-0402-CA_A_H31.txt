This bill includes General Fund appropriations of $287,132 in fiscal year 2017-18 and $259,919 in fiscal year 2018-19 
to the Business Development program within the Department of Economic and Community Development to establish 
a year-round program to promote Maine Buy Local Month. 
Of that amount, General Fund appropriations of $107,132 and $109,919 in fiscal year 2017-18 and fiscal year 2018-
19, respectively, are included for one Development Project Officer position and related all other costs to administer 
the program anticipated to start October 1, 2017. Additionally, General Fund appropriations of $180,000 in fiscal 
year 2017-18 and $150,000 in fiscal year 2018-19 are included for the research, marketing, advertising and other 
costs to promote the Maine Buy Local Month program.
