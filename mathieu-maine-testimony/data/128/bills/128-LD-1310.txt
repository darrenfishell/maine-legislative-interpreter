SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to establish a program in which a person who has outstanding 5 
traffic tickets with unpaid fines t hat have resulted in the suspension of that person's 6 
driver's license is eligible, for a limited time, to pay a reduced fine per ticket in order to 7 
reinstate that person's driver's license.  It is based on a similar program in Vermont.  8 
This bill will estab lish criteria for eligibility for participation in the program, 9 
including which offenses qualify, the amount that an eligible person must pay and when 10 
the program will be held.  11
