 SUMMARY  1 
This bill amends the laws concerning the shellfish depuration harvesting process to 2 
clarify communication between the Department of Marine Resources, municipalities, 3 
depuration certificate applicants and ho lders and licensed commercial shellfish harvesters 4 
during the certification process.  It also raises the rate of reimbursement paid to the 5 
municipality by the depuration certificate holder from 50¢ for each bushel of soft -shelled 6 
clams to 25% of the curren t market value for each bushel.  The bill establishes a penalty 7 
for noncompliance with any provision of the law regulating the soft -shelled clam 8 
depuration harvesting process; depuration certificate holder found by the department to 9 
be in noncompliance is prohibited from engaging in depuration activities under this 10 
subsection for a period of one year from the date of the department's finding.  11
