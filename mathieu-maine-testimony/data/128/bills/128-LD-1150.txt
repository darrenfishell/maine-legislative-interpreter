 Sec. 1.  Task force established.  Resolved:  That the Road Usage Charge Task 1

2.  Duties.  The task force shall study road usage charge alternatives to the gas tax, 21 
make recommendations on the design of a pilot program to test alternative road usage 22 
charge approaches, gather public comment on issues and concerns re lated to the proposed 23

recommendations on the design of a pilot program to test altern ative road usage charge 26 
approaches, the task force shall take into consideration the following:  27 
A.  The availability, adaptability, reliability and security of methods that might be 28 
used in recording and reporting highway use;  29 
B.  The necessity of protecti ng all personally identifiable information used in 30 
reporting highway use;  31 
C.  The ease and cost of recording and reporting highway use;  32 
D.  The ease and cost of administering the collection of taxes and fees as an 33 
alternative to the current system of taxin g highway use through motor vehicle fuel 34 
taxes;  35 
E.  Effective methods of maintaining compliance with the proposed usage charge 36 
alternatives;  37
