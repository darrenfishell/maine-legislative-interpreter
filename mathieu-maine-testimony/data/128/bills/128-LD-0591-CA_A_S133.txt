COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" A" to S.P. 206, L.D. 591, Bill, "An Act To Allow 
Municipalities To Adopt Bus Traffic Ordinances" 
Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following: 
'An Act To Require Motorists To Yield to Transit Buses' 
Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and before the 
summaiy and inserting the following: 
'Sec. 1. 29-A MRSA §2053, sub-§9 is enacte.d to read: 
9. Yield to transit buses. An operator of a vehicle must yield the right-of-way to a 
transit bus traveling in the same direction if the transit bus has signaled and is reentering 
the traffic flow from a bus stop or shoulder of the roadway. This subsection does not 
relieve the operator of a transit bus from the duty to drive with due regard for the safety 
of all persons using the roadway. For purposes of this subsection, "transit bus" means a 
bus operated by a public mass transit operator and does not include a school bus.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment strikes and replaces the bill and changes the title. The amendment 
requires an operator of a vehicle to yield the right-of-way to a transit bus traveling in the 
same direction if the transit bus has signaled and is reentering the traffic flow from a bus 
stop or shoulder of the roadway.
