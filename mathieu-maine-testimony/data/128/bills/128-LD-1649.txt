Fiscal Detail and Notes
The bill provides appropriations of $675,770 in fiscal year 2017-18 and $680,097 in fiscal year 2018-19 for the Office 
of Geographic Information Systems and the Maine Library of Geographic Information. The bill also provides allocations for this purpose for the Highway Fund, Other Special Revenue Funds and Federal Expenditures Fund.128th MAINE LEGISLATURE
