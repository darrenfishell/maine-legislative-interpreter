Fiscal Note 
Potential current biennium cost increase -Highway Fund 
Potential current biennium cost increase -Other Special Revenue Funds 
This bill changes the amount paid by the Maine Department of Transportation to displaced individuals due to propert) 
acquisition for projects funded with nonfederal funds from a set amount to the amount required for federally funded 
projects. As the federal amount is currently higher than the state amount there will be increased costs for nonfederal 
projects. The amount of additional cost will depend on the number of such projects going forward and the federal rate 
at the time of the acquisition of property.
