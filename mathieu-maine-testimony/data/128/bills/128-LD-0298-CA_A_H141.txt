COMMITTEE AMENDMENT 
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A "to H.P. 231, L.D. 298 
SUMMARY 
2 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, removes the 
3 provisions of the bill that prohibit political subdivisions in the State from expending 
4 public resources to influence the outcome of a direct initiative of legislation, a people's 
5 veto referendum or other statewide referendum. The amendment clarifies that resources 
6 spent by an agency on a factual summary of the issues may not be used to express support 
7 or opposition for the ballot measure.
