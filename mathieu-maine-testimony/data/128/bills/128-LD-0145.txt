 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  9-B MRSA §428, as amended by PL 2001, c. 211, §13, is further amended 2 
to read:  3 
§428.  Inactive or unclaimed accounts  4 
All moneys  money  in unclaimed accounts in each financial institution  authorized to 5 
do business in this State must be disposed of according to Title 33, chapter 41.  Before 6 
disposing of money in an unclaimed account or closing an inactive account, a financial 7 
institution authorized to do business in this State shall send a written notice by registered 8 
mail to the holder of the unclaimed or inactive account at the holder’s last known address 9 
informing the account holder of the disposition of the money or the pending closure of 10 
the account.  11 
SUMMARY  12 
This bill requires a financ ial institution authorized to do business in this State to 13 
notify a holder of an inactive or unclaimed account by registered mail before the closure 14 
of the account or the disposition of the money of that closure or disposition.  15
