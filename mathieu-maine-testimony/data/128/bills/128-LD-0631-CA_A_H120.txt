Amend the bill by inserting after the enacting clause and before section 1 the 
following: 
'Sec. 1. 12 MRSA §13152, sub-§1, as enacted by PL 2003, c. 414, Pt. A, §2 and 
affected by c. 614, §9, is amended to read: 
1. License. An operator's license is not required for the operation of an ATV, except 
as required by section 13157-A, subsection 6, paragraph Hand Title 29-A.1 
Amend the bill by inserting after section 1 the following: 
'Sec. 2. 29-A MRSA §2080, as amended by PL 2005, c. 577, §32, is repealed and 
the following enacted in its place: 
§2080. Operation of all-terrain and off-road vehicles 
1. All-terrain vehicles and off-road vehicles subiect to motor vehicle 
requirements. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, except as provided in 
subsection 2, whenever an all-terrain vehicle or off-road vehicle is operated on a way, the 
vehicle and operator are subject to the provisions of this Title. 
2. Exceptions. Notwithstanding subsection 1, whenever an all-terrain vehicle or off-
road vehicle is operated on a way, the vehicle and operator are not subject to: 
A. Chapters 5, 7, 13 and 15; and 
B. Chapter 11, except as provided in Title· 12, section 13157-A, subsection 6, 
paragraph H.1
