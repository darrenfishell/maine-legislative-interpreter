COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "Pr" to H.P. 140, L.D. 184, Bill, "An Act To Allow 
Hospitals To More Efficiently Monitor the Prescribing of Controlled Substances by 
Amending the Laws Governing Access to Prescription Monitoring Information" 
Amend the bill in section 2 in paragraph Kin the first line (page 1, line 12 in L.D.) by 
striking out the following: "similar individual" and inserting the following: 
'administrative prescriber' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies that an administrative prescriber employed by a licensed 
hospital may access prescription monitoring information.
