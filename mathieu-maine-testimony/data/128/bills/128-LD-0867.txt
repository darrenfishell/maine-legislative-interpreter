Sec. 1.  Town of Monmouth to restrict access to Wilson Stream; gate; 2 
recording.   The Town of Monmouth shall construct a gate with an electronic locking 3 
mechanism to restrict motor vehicle access  at the public access point for Wilson Stream.  4 
The gate may allow access only to those persons who enter the correct code.  The town 5 
shall record an educational voice message regarding invasive milfoil, the damage milfoil 6 
causes to the environment and the  ways to prevent the spread of milfoil.  The message 7 
must end with the code needed to unlock the electronic locking mechanism on the gate.  8 
The town shall make this message available by telephone.  9 
The town shall post a sign at the gate that includes a warn ing about milfoil and 10 
instructions, including the telephone number to call for an educational message regarding 11 
milfoil and how to obtain the code in order to gain access at the gate.  12

SUMMARY  27 
This bill provides $15,000 to the Town of Monmouth for the construction of a gate 28 
with an electronic locking mechanism to restrict motor vehicle access at  the public access 29 
point to Wilson Stream.  The bill also requires the town to record an educational voice 30 
message that explains the dangers of invasive milfoil and how to obtain the code to 31 
unlock the gate.  This message must be made available by telephon e. 32
