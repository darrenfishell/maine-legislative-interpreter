SENATE AMENDMENT" A" to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to S.P. 327, 
L.D. 988, Bill, "An Act Regarding Possession of a Firearm on School Property" 
Amend the amendment in section 1 by striking out all of paragraph D (page 1, lines 
14 to 19 in amendment) and inserting the following: 
'D. The prohibition on the possession of a firearm does not apply to a person who 
possesses a firearm in a motor vehicle or in the vicinity of a motor vehicle as long as 
the following conditions are met: 
(1) The person is dropping off or picking up a student and remains within 5 feet 
of the motor vehicle; and 
(2) The firearm is not loaded and is concealed either in the motor vehicle or on 
the person's person.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment removes the requirements in Committee Amendment "A" that the 
person who possesses a firearm on school property has to remain in the motor vehicle and 
that the firearm needs to be in a locked container or firearms rack. Instead, this 
amendment requires a person who posesses a firearm on school property to remain within 
5 feet of the vehicle the firearm to be concealed either in the vehicle or on the 
person's person. L
