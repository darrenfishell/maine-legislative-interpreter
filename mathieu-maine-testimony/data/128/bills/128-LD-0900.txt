Sec. 5.  Application.  The requirements of this Act apply to all policies, contracts 26 
and certificates executed, delivered, issued for delivery, continued or renewed in this 27 
State on or after January 1, 2018.  For purposes of this Act, all contracts are deemed to be 28

SUMMARY  30 
This bill prevents insurers and health maintenance org anizations offering individual 31 
and group coverage from limiting dental fees that are not covered by the insurer or the 32 
portion of a dental fee that exceeds the amount covered.  The provision applies to all 33 
individual and group health insurance policies, co ntracts and certificates issued or 34 
renewed on or after January 1, 2018.  35
