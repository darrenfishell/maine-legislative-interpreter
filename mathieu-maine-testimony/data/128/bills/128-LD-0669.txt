This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to enact a comprehensive package of proposals designed to address 5 
the unmet workforce needs of e mployers and to improve the economic future of workers.  6 
These proposals may include, but are not limited to, proposals to:  7 
1.  Identify workforce needs;  8 
2.  Connect businesses with trained workers;  9 
3.  Maximize the benefits of educational assessment fundi ng by focusing the funds on 10 
the students;  11 
4.  Provide incentives for educational institutions receiving state training funds to 12 
place graduates into the employment or school of the graduates' choice;  13 
5.  Strengthen the ability of the Maine Quality Centers,  established in the Maine 14 
Revised Statutes, Title 20 -A, section 12725, to fulfill their mission of meeting the 15 
workforce education and training needs of new and expanding businesses in the State and 16 
providing new employment and career advancement opportuni ties for Maine people;  17 
6.  Design diverse programs to meet the needs of Maine employers;  18 
7.  Prepare untrained workers to meet immediate and long -term needs;  19 
8.  Allow military training to count towards required training for certain certifications 20 
and lice nses;  21 
9.  Establish a clearinghouse to connect workers with employers;  22 
10.  Identify educational programs necessary for specific types of employment and 23 
create a process to preemploy prospective workers while they are being trained;  24 
11.  Provide to people receiving public assistance the opportunity to permanently 25 
leave poverty behind by providing skills training that offers academic degrees, 26 
certifications or credentials and leads to employment with wages that can sustain 27 
families;  28 
12.  Establish workforce training programs targeted exclusively at individuals who 29 
are on public assistance or whose incomes meet certain criteria and provide transitional 30 
benefits including child care, health care, transportation supports and educational 31 
supports;  32
