Sec. 5.  Duties.  Resolved:  That the commission shall create a plan for a p robate 16 
court system with full -time judges.  The commission may consider for inclusion in the 17 
plan any features that the commission determines relevant, including, but not limited to, 18 
features that will ensure timely, convenient and meaningful access to jus tice, promote 19 
judicial responsibility and adherence to the code of judicial responsibility, provide for 20 
qualified full -time judges, provide adequate professional staff, reflect efficient practices 21 
in scheduling and case management throughout the system, al low for convenient and 22 
consumer -friendly processing of matters that are not contested and reflect economies of 23 
scale in all appropriate operational aspects.  The commission shall describe how the 24 
system would be funded; and be it further  25 
Sec. 6.  Staff ass istance.  Resolved:  That the Legislative Council shall provide 26 
necessary staffing services to the commission; and be it further  27 
Sec. 7.  Information and assistance.  Resolved:  That the Administrative Office 28 
of the Courts and registers of probate shall pr ovide to the commission information and 29 
assistance requested by the commission and required for the commission to perform its 30 
duties; and be it further  31 
Sec. 8.  Report.  Resolved:  That, no later than December 6, 2017, the commission 32
