SUMMARY  19 
This bill prohibits a hospital from billing an uninsured patient or a patient not covered 20 
under a health plan operating under a network agreement between the hospital and the 21 
patient's health plan  for any inpatient or outpatient service or procedure at a level that 22 
exceeds 120% of the average allowable reimbursement rate under Medicare for that 23 
service or procedure. The bill requires a carrier to disclose to a prospective enrollee prior 24 
to enrollme nt if a health plan has a provider network that operates under a provider 25 
agreement between the participating provider and carrier that subjects an enrollee to the 26 
terms of the agreement upon enrollment and that requires reimbursement for any hospital 27 
inpatient and outpatient services and procedures at a level that exceeds 150% of the 28 
average allowable reimbursement rate under Medicare for that service or procedure. For 29 
an enrollee enrolled in that type of health plan, a carrier may not deny the enrollee 30 
covered by a health plan the right to audit any hospital bill or explanation of benefits 31 
form.  32
