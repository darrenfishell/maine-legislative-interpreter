Amend the bill in section 1 in ยง1602-122 by striking out all of the first paragraph 
(page 1, lines 4 to 6 in L.D.) and inserting the following: 
'A unit owner may install a backup electricity generator for the unit in a limited 
common element space associated with the unit.' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the Joint Standing Committee on Judiciary. 
It allows a condominium unit owner to install an electricity generator in a limited 
common element space associated with the unit instead of in a common element space, 
which is open to all units.
