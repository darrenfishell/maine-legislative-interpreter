SUMMARY  26 
Current law requires schools that take drinking water from sources other than public 27 
water s ystems to have that water tested.  This bill requires all schools and nursery schools 28 
that take drinking water from any source to have the same tests performed on that water, 29 
including, but not limited to, tests for lead and arsenic.  The bill requires the  Department 30 
of Health and Human Services to make all test results available on the department's 31 
publicly accessible website and to inform the parents and guardians of all students at a 32 
school or nursery school if the water is found to violate the state pri mary drinking water 33 
regulations.  34
