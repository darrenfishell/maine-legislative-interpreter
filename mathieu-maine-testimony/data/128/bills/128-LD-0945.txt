Sec. 6.  Effective date.   This Act takes effect November 1, 2017.  24 
SUMMARY  25 
This bill increases the cigarette tax from $2.00 to $3.50 per pack of 20 cigarettes, 26 
beginning November 1, 2017.  This bill also provides increased funding in the amount of 27 
$8,100,000 per year in ongoing funding to the Maine Centers for Disease Control and 28 
Prevention for tobacco use prevention and cessation in order to align with United States 29 
Department of Health an d Human Services, Centers for Disease Control and Prevention 30 
recommendations.  Finally, this bill provides $1,000,000 in fiscal year 2017 -18 and 31 
$750,000 thereafter for MaineCare members for tobacco cessation medications and 32 
counseling.  33
