loss of Local Government Fund revenue of $250 in fiscal year 2018-19.

Development program within the Department of Economic and Community Development for the costs associated with 
updating tax incentive softvvare to allow for expanded data collection in order to meet the reporting requirements of

in fiscal year 2019-20 for one Tax Examiner position and related All Other costs to review and process sales tax 
reimbursement requests.
