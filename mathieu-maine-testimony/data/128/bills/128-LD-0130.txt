SUMMARY  8 
This bill includes Other Special Revenue Funds allocations totaling $76,365 in fiscal 9 
year 2017 -18 and $26,985 in fiscal year 2018 -19 to the Department of Professional and 10 
Financial Regulation for the costs associated with requiring individuals practicing 11 
midwifery in the State to be licensed by January 1, 2020.  12 
This bill also includes corresponding transfers of funds  from the General Fund 13 
unappropriated surplus to the Licensing and Enforcement, Other Special Revenue Funds 14 
account within the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation in fiscal years 15 
2017 -18, 2018 -19 and 2019 -20 to support these costs.  16
