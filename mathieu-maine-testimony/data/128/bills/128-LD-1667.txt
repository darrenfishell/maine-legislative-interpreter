15 SUMMARY
16 This bill requires the Commissioner  of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife  to prohibit the 
17 entry of anadromous fish into Sheepscot  Pond  in the Town of Palermo through June 30, 
18 2021.  The bill also requires the commissioner,  with the assistance of the Commissioner 
19 of Marine  Resources and the Commissioner  of Environmental Protection,  to study  the 
20 consequences of the presence  of anadromous fish species in Sheepscot  Pond, including  
21 any changes resulting  from  the prohibition through  June 30, 2021, and to report the yearly  
22 findings of that study to the joint standing  committee of the Legislature having 
23 jurisdiction over inland fisheries and wildlife matters by January 15th annually through  
24 2022; a report of the cumulative  effects of  the prohibition is due by January 15, 2022.
