 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  1 
Sec. 1.  12 MRSA §6721 -B is enacted to read:  2 
§6721 -B.  Scallop harvesting limit  3 
A person may not harvest more than 90 pounds of scallops per day.  4 
Sec. 2.  12 MRSA §6722, sub -§1, as repealed and replaced by PL 2007, c. 695, 5 
Pt. I, §6, is amended to read:  6 
1.  Scallop fishing season.  Unless modified by rules adopted under section 6171 -A, 7 
a person may not fish for or take scallops in the territorial waters from April 16th 23rd to 8 
November 30th, both  days inclusive.  9 
SUMMARY  10 
This bill creates a 90 -pound per day, per person harvesting limit on scallops and adds 11 
a calendar week to the scallop season.  12
