SUMMARY  15 
This bill creates the crime of domestic violence aggravated assault, which a person 16 
commits if the person commits aggravated assault, elevated aggravated assault or 17 
elevated aggravated assault on a pregnant person and the victim is a family or household 18 
member.  This bill also makes a violation of a protection from abuse order a Class C 19 
crime if the  person has 2 or more prior convictions for violating a protection from abuse 20 
order.  21
