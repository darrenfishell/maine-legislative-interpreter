SUMMARY  28 
This bill requi res prizes for state lotteries to have an annual aggregate payback of 29 
90% of gross revenues or greater.  The bill also states that rules adopted by the State 30 
Liquor and Lottery Commission must prohibit marketing, advertising and promotion of 31 
state lotterie s except at locations where lottery tickets are sold.  Finally, the bill requires 32 
the commission to adopt rules governing payback percentages and advertising no later 33 
than December 1, 2017.  34
