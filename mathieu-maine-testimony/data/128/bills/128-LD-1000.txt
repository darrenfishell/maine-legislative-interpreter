SUMMARY  23 
This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to increase the 24 
rates for service s provided to MaineCare members receiving Home Support (Residential 25 
Habilitation) Level I under the brain injury waiver, rule Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits 26 
Manual, Chapter III, Section 18, to no less than $8.63 per quarter hour.  It also allows up 27 
to 400  units of care coordination each year rather than only in the first year of receiving 28 
services under the waiver.  The Department of Health and Human Services is directed to 29 
explore opportunities to provide additional telehealth services, including care 30 
coordination services, provided by both licensed medical personnel and nonlicensed 31 
personnel.  32
