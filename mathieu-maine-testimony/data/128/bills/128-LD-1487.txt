SUMMARY  29 
This bill changes the requirement regarding the development, implementation, 30 
operation and management of nontransmission alternatives for proposed transmission 31 
lines and proposed transmission projects.  This bill requ ires that a smart grid coordinator 32 
appointed by the Public Utilities Commission develop a nontransmission alternative to a 33 
proposed transmission line or proposed transmission project.  34 
The bill provides that a smart grid coordinator must be an entity incor porated in the 35 
State; may not be a transmission and distribution utility located in the State or an affiliate 36 
of a transmission and distribution utility located in the State; and must have a 37 
demonstrated record of developing, operating and managing nontran smission alternatives.  38
