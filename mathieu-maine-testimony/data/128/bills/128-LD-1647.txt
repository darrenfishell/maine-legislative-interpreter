22 Sec. 1.  Director  of the Bureau of Parks and Lands authorized,  but not 
23 directed, to exchange  certain land in the Town  of Washburn, Aroostook 
24 County.  Resolved:  That the Director  of the Bureau of Parks and Lands  within the 
25 Department of Agriculture,  Conservation and Forestry may by quitclaim  deed convey to 
26 the Town of Washburn the property  southwest of the intersection of Main  Street,  Route 
27 164, and Gardiner Creek Road constituting a segment  of the Aroostook Valley Trail from 
28 where it joins  the Bangor and Aroostook Trail,  both being  state-owned trails used for 
29 motorized recreation, in exchange  for conveyance of the property  west and north of the 
30 intersection of Main Street, Route 164, and Riverside Drive, now owned by the Town of 
31 Washburn, constituting a new stretch of trail corridor 60 feet wide,  on which the Town  of 
32 Washburn must have built a new trail making  a connection between the 2 trails according 
33 to specifications  provided  by the Bureau of  Parks and  Lands prior to this conveyance.
34 Emergency clause.  In view  of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 
35 legislation takes effect when  approved.
36 SUMMARY
37 This resolve authorizes the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, 
38 Bureau of Parks  and Lands  to exchange  land along  Main Street, Route 164, in the Town 
Page 2 - 128LR2418(01)-11 of Washburn  for a nearby piece of land in order  to improve the safety of recreational 
2 travel on certain state-owned trails  used for motorized  recreation and allow the town to 
3 sell a parcel of land  it owns to a national business for  development.
