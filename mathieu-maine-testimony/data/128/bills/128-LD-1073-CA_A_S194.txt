I SUMMARY 
2 This amendment clarifies that scholarships, not awards, granted by the board of 
3 trustees of the Maine Community College System must be based on evidence of 
4 individual need and worth. It also clarifies qualifications students must meet in order to 
5 be selected for a scholarship or campus employment. It also provides that the board of 
6 trustees of the Maine Community College System may make hardship distributions from 
7 any of the accumulated assets in a defined contribution retirement plan account in 
8 accordance with federal regulations.
