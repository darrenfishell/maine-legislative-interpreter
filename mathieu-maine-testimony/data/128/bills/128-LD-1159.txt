§637.  Paid sick leave  11

1.  Eligibility.   An employee who works for 30 or more days in a one -year period is 14

2.  Accrual.   An employee entitled to paid sick leave under this se ction accrues such 16 
leave at a rate of no less than one hour of sick leave for every 30 hours worked.  Accrual 17 
begins at the start of employment, but the employer is not required to permit use of the 18 
leave before an employee has been employed for 90 days.  The employer shall permit an 19 
employee to carry forward at least 40 hours of accrued sick leave to the following year, 20 
but an employer is not required to allow the use of more than 40 hours of paid sick leave 21 
in one year.  22

SUMMARY  7 
This bill creates a right to paid sick leave for employees not covered by a collective 8 
bargaining agreement who are employed by an employer that employs 50 or more 9 
employees.  It also creates a right to unpaid sick leave for employees of an employer that 10 
employs fewer than 50 employees.  This bill takes effect January 1, 2019.  11
