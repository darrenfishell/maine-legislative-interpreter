14 SUMMARY 
15 Current law prohibits a person from selling, displaying, raffling, giving away or 
16 offering for sale to the public any live fowl, turtles or rabbits under 8 weeks of age in lots 
17 of less than 6. This amendment removes the changes made to that law by the bill and

19 sale to the public any live fowl, turtles or rabbits under 8 weeks of age in lots of less than
