SUMMARY  15 
This bill prohibits the State, as an employer , from inquiring about a prospective 16 
employee's prior compensation history until after an offer of employment that includes all 17 
terms of compensation has been negotiated and made to the prospective employee.  The 18 
bill also prohibits the State from requirin g that a prospective employee's prior 19 
compensation history meet certain criteria.  A state government entity that violates this 20 
law is subject to a fine of not less than $100 and not more than $500 per intentional 21 
violation.  22
