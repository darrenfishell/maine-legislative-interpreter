SUMMARY  28 
This bill authorizes the Department of Public Safety, Gambling Control Board to 29 
accept an application from the Passamaquoddy Tribe  to operate 50 slot machines at a 30 
gaming facility in Washington County at which high -stakes beano is conducted by the 31 
Passamaquoddy Tribe. The bill raises the limit on the number of slot machines allowed in 32 
the State by 50 to accommodate the slot machines that may be operated by the 33 
Passamaquoddy Tribe.  Slot machines operated by the Passamaquoddy Tribe would be 34 
subject to the oversight of the Gambling Control Board and subject to the same laws and 35 
rules as other slot machines operated in this State.  36 
It als o reduces the annual license fee for high -stakes beano from $50,000 to $25,000 37 
and allows the Passamaquoddy Tribe to operate 50 slot machines in the tribe's high -stakes 38 
beano facility.  39
