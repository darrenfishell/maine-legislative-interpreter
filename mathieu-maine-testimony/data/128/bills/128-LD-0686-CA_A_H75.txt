SUMMARY 
This amendment strikes and replaces the bill. It defines "water-related entity" as a 
water utility or a municipal or quasi-municipal entity owning, controlling, operating or 
managing sewer, sanitary or storm water works. It allows for a water-related entity to be a 
member of a regional water council and requires that one of the members of a regional 
water council be a water utility. It requires that a regional water council provide a 
statement of its financial activities to the member water-related entities and requires that
