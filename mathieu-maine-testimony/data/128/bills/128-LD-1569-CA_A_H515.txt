2 1. Requirement. An educational specialist certificate is required for employment as

7 3. Endorsements. The educational specialist ce1iificate must be issued with an

9 qualified. A holder of an educational specialist cetiificate may not work outside the

16 §13019-H. Educational technician certificate 
17 1. Requirement. An educational technician cetiificate is required for employment 
18 as an educational technician at a public school or a private school approved for 
19 attendance purposes pursuant to section 2901, subsection 2, paragraph B, if the employed 
20 individual will: 
21 A. Introduce new learning plans developed in consultation with the classroom 
22 teacher or appropriate content specialist; and 
23 B. Supervise small groups of students in community-based programs. 
24 2. Qualifications. The state board shall adopt rules establishing the qualifications 
25 and supervision for an educational technician ce1iificate.
