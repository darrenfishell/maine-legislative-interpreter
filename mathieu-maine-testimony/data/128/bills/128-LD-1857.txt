18 SUMMARY
19 This bill is reported  out by the Joint  Standing Committee on Labor,  Commerce, 
20 Research and Economic  Development pursuant to Resolve  2017, chapter 27.  The bill 
21 implements a recommendation  of the Commission To Streamline Veterans' Licensing  and 
22 Certification regarding licensure of military medics  and corpsmen  as licensed  practical 
23 nurses.  Pursuant  to the bill, a person  who served  on active  duty in the medical corps  of 
24 any branch of the Armed  Forces of the United States,  completed the basic course  of 
25 instruction in nursing required by the branch  of the Armed Forces of the United States in 
26 which the applicant served  and was honorably discharged is eligible to take the 
27 examination for  licensure as a licensed practical  nurse.
28 The Joint Standing Committee on Labor, Commerce, Research and Economic 
29 Development has not taken a position on the substance of the bill and by reporting out 
30 this bill the committee  is not suggesting and does not intend  to suggest that it agrees or 
31 disagrees with any aspect of this recommendation  from the Commission To Streamline  
32 Veterans' Licensing and Certification.  The committee is reporting the bill out for the sole 
33 purpose of turning the commission's  recommendation  into a printed bill that can be 
34 referred to the committee for an appropriate public hearing and subsequent processing in 
35 the normal course.   The committee  is taking  this action to ensure clarity  and transparency 
36 in the legislative review of the commission's recommendation regarding licensure of 
37 military medics and corpsmen as  licensed  practical nurses.
