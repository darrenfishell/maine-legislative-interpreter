SUMMARY  26 
This emergency bill amends and enacts new statutory provisions in the school 27 
funding formula to address the high costs for certain in -district and out -of-district special 28 
education students beginning in the 2017 -2018 school year.  The b ill provides school 29 
administrative units with additional state funding for certain special education students 30 
under the Essential Programs and Services Funding Act.  31 
The bill amends the calculation of special education costs that are included in the 32 
amount of state subsidy that is allocated to school administrative units for the placement 33 
of special education students, including high -cost in -district and high -cost out -of-district 34 
students, under the essential programs and services school funding formula for those 35
