 Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 1 
become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and  2 
Whereas, this legislation must take effect before the expiration of the 90 -day period 3 
to ensure the  law regarding super cribbage tournaments is amended before September 30, 4 
2017, allowing the law to remain in effect; and  5 
Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 6 
the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and requi re the following legislation as 7 
immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 8 
therefore,  9 
Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:  10 
Sec. 1.  17 MRSA §1836, sub -§4-A, as enacted by PL 2015, c. 163 , §1, is 11 
amended to read:  12 
4-A.  Exception for super cribbage tournament.  Notwithstanding any provision of 13 
this section to the contrary, the Chief of the State Police may issue up to 3 licenses per 14 
year for the conduct of a super cribbage tournament.  For the purposes of this subsection, 15 
"cribbage" means a card game that uses a board and pegs to keep score and of which the 16 
characteristic feature is a crib into which players discard cards from their dealt hand to 17 
create a crib of 4 cards unseen by other play ers that will be ultimately part of the dealer's 18 
hand.  The license fee for a super cribbage tournament is $75.  A super cribbage 19 
tournament must be conducted in the same manner as prescribed for a tournament game 20 
by this section except as follows.  21 
A.  The super cribbage tournament may be conducted by a nationally chartered 22 
organization that organizes tournament -style cribbage games and that is exempt from 23 
taxation under the United States Internal Revenue Code, Section 501(c)(3) so long as 24 
the principal org anizer has been a member of that organization for a period of not less 25 
than 3 years.  26 
B.  The minimum number of players required is 50.  27 
C.  The maximum entry fee allowed is $100 per player.  28 
D.  The super cribbage tournament need not be held on premises owne d by the 29 
licensee.  30 
E.  The super cribbage tournament may be conducted over a period of up to 72 hours.  31 
F.  Notwithstanding subsection 2, 50% of the proceeds of the super cribbage 32 
tournament after prizes are paid must be paid to a bona fide charitable organ ization, 33 
other than the licensee, listed on the tournament application submitted to the Chief of 34 
the State Police.  35 
This subsection is repealed September 30, 2017.  36 
Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this 37 
legislation takes eff ect when approved.  38
