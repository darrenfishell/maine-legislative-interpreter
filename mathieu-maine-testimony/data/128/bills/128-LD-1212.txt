SUMMARY  26 
This bill changes the statutory location of the tax exemption for personal property 27 
occupied or used solely for its own purposes by an incorporated benevolent and 28 
charitable organization that is exempt from taxation under section 501 of the Code and 29 
the primary purpose of which is the operation of a hospital licensed by the Department of 30 
Health and Human Services, a health maintenance organization or a blood bank.  This bill 31 
moves the exemption from the statu tes regarding exemption of real and personal 32 
property, since the exemption no longer applies to real property, to the definition of 33 
"eligible business equipment" for the purposes of defining personal property that is 34 
exempt from property tax under the busi ness equipment tax exemption, or "BETE," 35 
program.  36
