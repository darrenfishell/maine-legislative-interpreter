Fiscal Note 
No net fiscal impact to State Retirement System 
Minor cost increase -General Fund 
Minor cost increase -local school administrative units 
Amending the definition of "eamable compensation" under the laws governing the State Retirement System to change 
how retrospective payments for compensation earned by employees are to be included in the calculation of earnable 
compensation may benefit some members, while others will be disadvantaged. There will be no net fiscal impact on 
the System. 
Additonal costs to the State and to local school administrative units and local units of government to provide 
information on retrospective payments to the Maine Public Employees Retirement System can be absorbed within 
existing budgeted resources.
