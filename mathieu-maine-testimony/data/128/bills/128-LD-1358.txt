SUMMARY  28 
This bill:  29 
1.  Amends the labor relations laws governing municipal public employees and 30 
University of Maine System em ployees to provide that determinations by arbitrators with 31 
respect to controversies over all subjects, including salaries, pensions and insurance, are 32 
final and binding on the parties;  33 
2.  Amends the labor relations laws governing state employees to provid e that, with 34 
respect to controversies over salaries, an arbitrator's determinations are final and binding 35 
on the parties; and  36 
3.  Amends the labor relations laws governing judicial employees to provide that an 37 
arbitrator's determinations with respect to co ntroversies over all subjects, including 38 
salaries, pensions and insurance, are final and binding on the parties and that, with respect 39
