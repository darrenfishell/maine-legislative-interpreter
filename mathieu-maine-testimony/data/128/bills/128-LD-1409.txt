SUMMARY  9 
This bill allows small nonalcoholic beverage producers that manufacture or bottle no 10 
more than 50,000 gallons of beverages a year to clean and sanitize reusable beverage 11 
containers by washing by hand and bottle and carbonate the containers by hand as long as 12 
all other cleaning and sanitizing requirements are met.  13
