13 SUMMARY
14 This bill is reported  out by the Joint  Standing Committee on Labor,  Commerce, 
15 Research and Economic Development  pursuant  to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 32, 
16 section 14049-K, subsection 2.
17 The bill repeals the March 15, 2018 sunset provision in the laws governing  the 
18 licensure of appraisal  management companies in the Maine Revised  Statutes, Title  32, 
19 chapter 124-A.
