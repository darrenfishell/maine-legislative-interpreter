SUMMARY  18 
This bill requires that, when a child is ordered into the custody of the Department of 19 
Health and Human Services, the department must provide to the foster home in which the 20 
child is placed an overview of the child's medical condition and the name and contact 21 
information of the child's health care provider at the time of placement. The bill also 22 
requires the department to ensure that a child receives an appointment for a medical 23 
examination with in 3 working days of when the department's custody commences, 24 
instead of within 10 days as in current law, and requires that the department inform the 25 
foster parents of the appointment.  26
