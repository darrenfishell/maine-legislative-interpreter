SUMMARY  5 
This bill accomplishes th e following with regard to the statewide content standards, 6 
statewide assessment programs and the comparison of recent and proposed content 7 
standards and statewide assessment programs.  8 
With regard to content standards, the bill:  9 
1.  Prohibits the Departmen t of Education from adopting and implementing the 10 
common core state standards, or any standards developed by any similar initiative process 11 
or program, as the State's content standards for English language arts and mathematics 12 
and voids any prior actions t aken to adopt or implement the common core state standards;  13 
2.  Requires the Department of Education, within 90 days after the bill's effective 14 
date, to replace the content standards in English language arts and mathematics with new 15 
standards that are cons istent with the standards adopted by Massachusetts prior to that 16 
state's adoption of the common core state standards so that Maine's standards are, as 17 
much as possible, identical to those adopted by Massachusetts, except when a Maine 18 
context requires other wise;  19 
3.  Specifies that the content standards that are based on the Massachusetts standards 20 
are effective for the 2018 -2019, 2019 -2020 and 2020 -2021 school years only;  21 
4.  Requires the Department of Education, by June 30, 2019, to adopt new content 22 
standa rds for kindergarten and for each of grades 1 to 12 in English language arts and 23 
mathematics that are distinct and independent from the standards previously adopted by 24 
the Department of Education so that they are in place beginning with the 2021 -2022 25 
schoo l year;  26 
5.  States that a school administrative unit is not required to use all or any part of the 27 
content standards adopted by the Department of Education;  28 
6.  Prohibits the Commissioner of Education from adopting any model curricula that 29 
are aligned with  the content standards; and  30 
7.  Prohibits the Department of Education, the State Board of Education and any 31 
other state official, board or agency from adopting or revising any content standards in 32 
English language arts or mathematics until the new or revis ed standards are approved in 33 
accordance with the bill.  34
