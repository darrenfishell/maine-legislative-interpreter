11 (f)  Undertaking other health, safety and compliance repairs, including  
12 installations or improvements necessary to increase  school  facility security .
13 (2)  Repairs  and improvements related  to a school building  structure, windows

15 (3)   Repairs and improvements related  to energy  and water conservation receive 
16 Priority 3 status.
17 (4)  Upgrades of learning spaces in school buildings receive Priority 4 status.
18 (5)  The Commissioner of Education  may approve  other necessary repairs;
19 SUMMARY
20 This bill amends the law governing  the School Revolving Renovation  Fund  to specify 
21 that Priority 1 status  loans made to school administrative units for school repair and 
22 renovation include loans for the installations or improvements necessary to increase 
23 school facility  security.
