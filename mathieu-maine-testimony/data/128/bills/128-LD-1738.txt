2 This bill allows the sale of alcohol to persons who are on municipally owned property 
3 that is not contiguous to the licensed premises of a person  licensed  to sell spirits, wine  or 
4 malt liquor  for on-premises consumption as long as certain  conditions are met, such as 
5 approval by the municipality, the exercise of control  over the noncontiguous  real estate 
6 by the licensee, restricting to employees  of the licensee the sale of alcohol  and limiting 
7 the time during which  alcohol may be sold on the noncontiguous real estate.  The bill also 
8 permits the public to travel between the  licensed  premises and  noncontiguous real estate.
