COMMITTEE AMENDMENT ,A" to H.P. 339, L.D. 476, Bill, "An Act To 
Clarify the Authority for Cremation" 
Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 2 in the last blocked paragraph in the 4th 
line (page 1, line 31 in L.D.) by inserting after the following: "remains or dead body" the 
following: ', except that if the funeral director or practitioner of funeral service has 
knowledge that cremation conflicts with the deceased person's religious tenets and 
practices, the funeral director or practitioner of funeral service may not cremate the 
remains or dead body' 
SUMMARY 
This amendment is the minority report of the committee. It prohibits a funeral 
director or practitioner of funeral service from cremating abandoned remains or an 
abandoned dead body if the funeral director or practitioner of funeral service has 
knowledge that cremation conflicts with the deceased person's religious tenets and 
practices.
