7 SUMMARY 
8 This amendment clarifies that noncontiguous real estate near an establishment 
9 licensed for the sale of liquor for consumption on the premises that is approved by the 
10 Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages 
11 and Lottery Operations as a proper place for exercise of the licensee's privilege is 
12 considered part of the licensed premises. The amendment also clarifies that only an 
13 employee of the licensed establishment may transport liquor between the licensed 
14 establishment and the noncontiguous real estate.
