20 SUMMARY 
21 This amendment delays the date by which neurobehavioral beds must be provided 
22 from September 1, 2018 to July 1, 2019. It requires the Department of Health and 
23 Human Services to provide beds in 2 or more different centers rather than one or more 
24 centers as in the resolve. It restricts the population served to individuals with 
25 neurobehavioral issues or dementia and accompanying behavioral issues and removes 
26 individuals with brain injury, intellectual disabilities or autism.
