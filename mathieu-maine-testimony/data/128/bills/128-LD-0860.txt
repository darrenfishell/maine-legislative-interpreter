SUMMARY  33 
This bill directs the State Court Administrator to establish a secure system for the 34 
application, issuance and return of arrest warrants and search warrants that is electronic 35 
and that provides access to author ized users statewide.  The bill amends the law on search 36 
warrants to require electronic application and issuance once the electronic system has 37 
been established.  The bill directs the Supreme Judicial Court to adopt amendments to the 38 
Maine Rules of Unified  Criminal Procedure, Rule 4, Arrest Warrant or Summons, 39 
effective on the date of the establishment of the statewide electronic warrant system, to 40 
provide for the application, issuance and return of arrest warrants and search warrants 41 
through electronic mea ns. 42
