determine the performance of  certain programs administered by the department under this 11 
subtitle by examining the outcomes of families and individuals participating in those 12

SUMMARY  11 
This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to collect data 12 
related to performance measures of the Temporary Assistance for Needy Families, or 13 
TANF, program, including reducing ch ild poverty and food insecurity and improving 14 
educational attainment, employment and income levels.  It also requires the department to 15 
set benchmarks to measure improvement and success of the TANF program.  16 
It establishes the Independent Citizens Oversight  Committee to monitor the 17 
Department of Health and Human Services' progress with respect to meeting benchmarks 18 
for success of the TANF program.  19
