SUMMARY  9 
This bill requires manufacturers and distributors of food or beverage products to 10 
submit to the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry written notice if an 11 
ortho -phthalate or any other chemical the department determines poses a human health 12 
hazard  is present in an amount greater than a de minimus level in the product's container 13 
or packaging or the food or beverage within the container or packaging.  It requires the 14 
department to make information received from manufacturers and distributors availab le 15 
to the public.  It authorizes the department to assess a fee on manufacturers and 16 
distributors.  17
