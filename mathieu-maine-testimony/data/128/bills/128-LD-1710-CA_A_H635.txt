COMMITTEE AMENDMENT "A" to H.P. 1190, L.D. 1710, Bill, "An Act To 
Restore Maine's School-based Health Centers" 
Amend the bill by inserting after the enacting clause and before section 1 the 
following: 
'Sec. 1. Ongoing funding. During the First Regular Session of the 129th 
Legislature, the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over 
health and human services matters may report out legislation to provide ongoing funding 
for school-based health centers.' 
Amend the bill in section 1 in the initiative in the first line (page 1, line 16 in L.D.) 
by inserting after the following: "Restores funding" the following: 'on a one-time basis' 
Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or 
section number to read c~msecutively. 
SUMMARY 
This amendment provides that funding for school-based health centers is a one-time 
appropriation from the Fund for a Healthy Maine and authorizes the joint standing 
committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human services matters 
to report out legislation to provide ongoing funding for school-based health centers in the 
First Regular Session of the 129th Legislature. ·
