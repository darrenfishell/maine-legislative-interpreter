SUMMARY  12 
The funds provided by this bond issue, in the amount of $6,000,000, will be used to 13 
provide partnership funds and matching grants for geospatial data acquisition to 14 
communities that are creating or improving digital parcel maps to accurately identify 15 
existing boundaries and land use, identify potentia l community development areas and 16 
protect environmental resources.  17
