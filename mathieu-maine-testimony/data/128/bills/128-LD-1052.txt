SUMMARY  4 
This bill allows the Department of Health an d Human Services to require an applicant 5 
for Temporary Assistance for Needy Families, or TANF, benefits to complete a written 6 
screening tool to determine the applicant's likelihood of current illegal drug or controlled 7 
substance use.  Based on the results of the screening tool or interactions with a TANF 8 
applicant or recipient, the department may require the applicant or recipient to submit to a 9 
drug test as a condition of receiving benefits if the department has a reasonable suspicion 10 
that the applicant or  recipient is using an illegal drug or controlled substance.  11
