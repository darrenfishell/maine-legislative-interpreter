SUMMARY  19 
This bill replaces language regarding the bonding authority of the Madison 20 
Department of Electric Works to provide that it is the electric works that issues bonds for 21 
its purposes and not the Town of Madison and to remove the $1 ,200,000 cap on the 22 
issuance of bonds or notes for current operating expenses.  23
