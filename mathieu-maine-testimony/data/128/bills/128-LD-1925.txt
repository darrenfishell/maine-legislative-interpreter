24 SUMMARY
25 This bill, which is the report of the Joint Standing Committee on Appropriations  and 
26 Financial Affairs, provides  additional one-time funds to the Secretary  of State for the 
27 administration of the general election in November  2018.  The additional funds are 
28 necessary due to the  need to include a separate  ballot page.
