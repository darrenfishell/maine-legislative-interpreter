SUMMARY  25 
This bill exempts a person under 18 years of age from the requirement that the person 26 
wear a helmet while operati ng or being carried as a passenger on an all -terrain vehicle 27 
when the person is wearing a seat belt and the all -terrain vehicle is equipped with roll 28 
bars or bracing welded or attached to the frame in a permanent manner, with a roof or 29 
with a protective en closure.  30
