SUMMARY  23 
This bill allows municipalities or regional shellfish management committees to 24 
designate conservation areas where harvesting any marine organism is proh ibited. In 25 
aggregate the conservation areas may not exceed 10% of the total harvestable area in the 26 
intertidal zone. A designated conservation area must be clearly marked with signs that 27 
identify the municipality or regional shellfish management committee that designated the 28 
area and indicate the purpose of the area.  29
