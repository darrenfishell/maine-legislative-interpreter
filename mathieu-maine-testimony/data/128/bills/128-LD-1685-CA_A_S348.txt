This bill creates the Barbara Bush Children's Hospital specialty license plate. It includes a one time Highway Fund 
allocation of $51,962 to the Department of Secretary of State in fiscal year 2017-18 for the cost to design and 
manufacture the plates and an Other Special Revenue Funds allocation to the Department of Secretary of State of 
$66,600 in fiscal year 2018-19 to remit the funds to the Barbara Bush Children's Hospital. Highway Fund revenue 
would increase by $50,000 in fiscal year 2017-18 and by $56,640 in fiscal year 2018-19. Other Special Revenue 
Funds revenue to the Department of Secretary of State would be increased by $73 ,260 in fiscal year 2018-19.
