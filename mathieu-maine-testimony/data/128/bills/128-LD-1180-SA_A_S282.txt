8 SENATE AMENDMENT" A_,, to S.P. 401, L.D. 1180, Bill, "An Act To Provide a 
9 Definition of "Primary Residence" for Purposes of Property Tax Abatements Based on 
10 Hardship or Poverty" 
11 Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 2 by striking out all of the 2nd paragraph 
12 (page 1, lines 11 to 13 in L.D.) and inserting the following: 
13 'As used in this subsection, "primary residence" means the home, appurtenant structures 
14 necessary to support the home and acreage sufficient to satisfy the minimum lot size as 
15 required by the municipality's land use or building permit ordinance or regulations or, in 
16 the absence of any municipal minimum lot size requirement, as required by Title 12, 
17 section 4807-A.' 
18 SUMMARY 
19 This amendment revises the definition of "primary residence" as provided in the bill 
20 for the purpose of determining abatement of property taxes based on hardship or poverty 
21 by specifying that the acreage must satisfy the minimum lot size required by the 
22 municipality or, if the municipality has no minimum lot size requirement, the minimum 
23 lot size required under the realty subdi laws.
