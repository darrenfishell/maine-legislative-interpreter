Future biennium savings -General Fund 
This bill would result in savings to the State Employee Health and Benefits Program and the Legislative Retirement 
Program. The amount and timing of these savings cannot be determined at this time. While the bill does not 
specifically reference health plan benefits for Legislators, participation in the health plan requires participation in the 
Legislative Retirement Program.
