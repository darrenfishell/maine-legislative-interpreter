SUMMARY  18 
This bill requires anyone harvesting edible wild food to have written permission or a 19 
bill of sale from the landowner before harvesting or transporting.  The permission 20 
requirements and enforcement provisions of this bill are the same as current ly exist in 21 
Maine law for commercial harvesting of Christmas trees and boughs for wreaths. The bill 22 
also authorizes the Department of Health and Human Services to decline to certify or to 23 
revoke the certification under the Maine Wild Mushroom Harvesting Ce rtification 24 
Program of any person found in violation of the prohibition.  25
