SUMMARY  17 
This bill provides that a permit is r equired to import exotic wildlife into or possess 18 
wildlife in the State or receive exotic wildlife. The Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and 19 
Wildlife is required to adopt rules that specify the criteria for issuing such a permit. The 20 
bill allows the commis sioner to adopt rules to require permitted exotic wildlife to carry or 21 
be embedded with a tracking device. The bill requires the holder of a permit for exotic 22 
wildlife to provide notice to the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife, Bureau of 23 
Warden S ervice and all landowners within the range of travel of the wildlife in the event 24 
of an escape of the wildlife.  25
