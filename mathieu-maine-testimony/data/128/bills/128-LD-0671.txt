SUMMARY  15 
This bill provides that if the Secretary of State administratively suspends a person's 16 
license for operating a motor vehicle with an excessive alcohol level or for  operating a 17 
motor vehicle under the influence of drugs, the amount of time the license was 18 
administratively suspended must be deducted when suspending that person's license after 19 
a conviction.  20
