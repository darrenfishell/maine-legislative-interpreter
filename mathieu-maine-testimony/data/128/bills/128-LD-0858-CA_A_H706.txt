Fiscal Detail and Notes 
This bill amends current laws regarding dangerous dogs and animal welfare by updating various definitions, defining 
1mmicipal authority and responsibilities and creating a dangerous dog licensing program. Because the Department of 
Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry (ACF) already maintains an online dog licensing program, any additional 
costs as a result of the additional dangerous dog licensing requirements in this bill are anticipated to be minor and can 
be absorbed within existing budgeted resources. The dangerous dog licensing requirements will also result in a minor
