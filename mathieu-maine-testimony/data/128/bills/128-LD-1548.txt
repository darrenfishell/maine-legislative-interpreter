SUMMARY  27 
This bill establishes the Let's Grow Maine Program, which will be administered by 28 
the Finance Authority of Maine with the purpose of purchasing fruits and vegetables from 29 
farm businesses in the State for distribution to schools, senior citize ns and individuals 30 
with low income through a public and private partnership.  The program is funded 31 
through a newly created Food and Farm Fund that will receive dedicated revenue, starting 32 
in July 2019, based on 1/2 of the tax revenues from noncigarette sm oking tobacco 33 
products.  The bill increases the tax for noncigarette smoking tobacco products from 20% 34 
of the wholesale sales price to 47%, effective January 1, 2018.  If the tax on cigarettes is 35 
increased on or after January 1, 2018, this tax, as well as the taxes on smokeless tobacco, 36 
will be adjusted by a rate that is equivalent to the percentage change in the tax rate for 37 
one cigarette.  38
