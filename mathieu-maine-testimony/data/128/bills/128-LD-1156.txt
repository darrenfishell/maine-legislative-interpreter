SUMMARY  26 
This bill amends provisions regarding compensation and length of service of retired 27 
public school admin istrators who return to service by requiring for the first year of the 28 
return to service 100% of the compensation established for the position to be filled and 29 
for the subsequent 4 years annually reducing the compensation incrementally until 30 
compensation i s 75% of the compensation established for the position to be filled.  31 
Current law sets the compensation at 75% of the compensation established for the 32 
position filled.  33
