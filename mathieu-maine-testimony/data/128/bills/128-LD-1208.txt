SUMMARY  11 
This bill makes the following changes to the laws governing veterans' services.  12 
1.  It changes the name of the Bureau of Maine Veterans' Services to the Maine 13 
Bureau of Veterans' Services.  14 
2.  It removes the authority of the bureau to provide grants of temporary and 15 
emergency cash assistance to veterans.  It authorizes the bureau to accept money to 16 
provide financial assistance to veterans and to award grants to organizations that provide 17 
assistance  to veterans such as housing or emergency assistance.  18
