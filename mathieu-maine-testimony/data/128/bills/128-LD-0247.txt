SUMMARY  3 
This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to implement changes to the Participating Local District 5 
Consolidated Retirement Plan administer ed by the Maine Public Employees Retirement 6 
System; the changes would be based on recommendations of the Participating Local 7 
District Advisory Committee.  The committee, which represents both employee and 8 
employer interests, is authorized by statute to pro vide proposals for changes to the plan.  9 
Pursuant to that authority, the committee is reviewing possible plan adjustments to 10 
improve future funding levels and is expected to issue its recommendations prior to the 11 
end of the First Regular Session of the 128 th Legislature.  12
