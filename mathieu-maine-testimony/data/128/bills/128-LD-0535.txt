This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.  4 
This bill proposes to make the activity of flying an unmanned aerial vehicle into the 5 
airspace over or crossing the  boundary of another person's property and recording or 6 
videotaping or otherwise violating the privacy of the property owner either civil or 7 
criminal trespass.  8
