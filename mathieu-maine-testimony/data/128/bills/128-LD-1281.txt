SUMMARY  11 
This bill eliminates the requirement that compensation set by the Attorney General 12 
for assistant attorneys general, staff attorneys, the secretary to the Attorney General, 13 
District Attorneys and de puty and assistant district attorneys be approved by the 14 
Governor.  It also allows the Attorney General and the Secretary of State to approve 15 
financial orders for transfers and revisions of and increases to allotments within the 16
